</pre><p id="p_1">                     Ashok Rai
                                                                        .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                     Versus
                     The State Of Bihar
                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                     =============================================

3     11-11-2011                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                     counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   Finding the petitioner sole assailant, his earlier prayer

                     has been refused by this Court. Trial is not concluded and

                     petitioner is in custody is made ground for bail to the petitioner

                     which I do not find justified to bail when prosecution is producing

                     witnesses.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Taking the same into consideration, prayer of the

                     petitioner for bail is rejected.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   However, Trial Court is directed to expedite the trial.
