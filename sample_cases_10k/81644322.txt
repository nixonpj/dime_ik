      Heard Mr. A. Ahmed, learned counsel for the appellant.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      In view of office note dated 26.09.2019, it appears that respondents are avoiding
service. Hence, the appellant is directed to take steps for service of notice upon the
respondents in a substituted manner by publishing the same in one English and one Bengali
daily having wide circulation in Cachar. Steps within 2 (two) days. The publication shall be
made within 02.12.2019. The date of appearance shall be indicated in the notice as
03.01.2020. Before the next date, the appellant shall file an affidavit of service and enclose
thereto the photocopies of the publications and also the 2 (two) original newspapers.
</p><p id="p_3">      List on 03.01.2020.
