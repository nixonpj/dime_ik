<div class="doc_title">Pankaj Bansal vs State Of Punjab And Others on 5 September, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                        AT CHANDIGARH

Sr. No.129                                      CWP No.24454 of 2019
                                                DECIDED ON: 05.09.2019

DR. PANKAJ BANSAL

                                                                ..PETITIONER

                                       VERSUS

STATE OF PUNJAB AND OTHERS

                                                            ...RESPONDENTS


CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ARUN MONGA


Present:         Mr. Virinder K. Shukla, Advocate, for the petitioner.

                 Ms. Ambika Bedi, AAG, Punjab.

                 *****

ARUN MONGA, J. (ORAL)
</pre>

<p id="p_1">                 Petitioner herein seeks issuance of a writ in the nature of

mandamus directing the respondents to grant him 4/9 years placement in

higher pay-scales under Assured Career Progression Scheme as per Punjab

Govt. Policy dated 03.11.2006 (P-2) and to grant him annual increments for

the year 2015 to 2018. Petitioner also caused a leal notice dated 8.7.2019

(Annexure P-4), but the same was not adverted by the respondent. Hence,

this petition.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.               Notice of motion.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.               On advance service of the petition, Ms. Ambika Bedi, AAG,

Punjab appears and accepts notice on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_4">4.               Given the nature of order being passed, there is no necessity to

seek return by the respondents as no further proceedings and/or pleadings

are required.</p><pre id="pre_2">
                                  1 of 2

      CWP No.24454 of 2019                                             --2--

</pre><p id="p_5">5.           Without commenting on the merits of the case, the writ petition

is disposed of with a direction to the respondents to objectively consider the

legal notice dated 08.07.2019 (Annexure P-4) and also by keeping in view

the contentions stated in the present writ petition by treating it as a

supplementary representation and pass a speaking order, in accordance with

law.
</p>
<p id="p_6">6.           Let the needful be done within a period of 3 months from the

date of receipt of a certified copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_7">7.           Disposed of in above terms.
</p>
