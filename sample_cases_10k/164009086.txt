<p id="p_1">                                This petition has been filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. for a

            direction to the official respondents to transfer the enquiry to some

            adjoining District or to some senior police official or to some independent

            agency for free and fair investigation.

</p><p id="p_2">                                Learned counsel has argued that no action has been taken till

            date on a report lodged by him. Learned counsel has further argued that in

            this connection the petitioner has also moved representation dated

            19.06.2015 (Annexure P-3) to the respondent No.3-S.S.P., Ferozepur and at

            this stage he would be satisfied if a direction is given to respondent No.3 to

            look into the matter and decide his representation within any reasonable

            time.

<p id="p_3">                                I find this to be a fair request.     The respondent No.3-the

            Sr. Superintendent of Police, Ferozepur is directed to look into the

            matter/representation (Annexure P-3) and take whatever action he deems

            necessary as per law. .
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                Petition stands disposed of in the above terms.
