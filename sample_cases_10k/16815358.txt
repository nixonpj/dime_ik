     THIS CRL.P IS FILED U/S.482 OF CR.P.C., PRAYING

COURT IN THE BAIL ORDER, DATED 15.03.2016 IN
                                2


C.C.NO.7717/2014 ON THE FILE OF                   HON'BLE      II

     THIS PETITION COMING ON FOR ADMISSION, THIS

                            ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        Learned counsel for petitioner has filed a memo

along    with   certified   copy of the     Memorandum        of

Settlement dated 15.10.2018 in Crl.P.No.3718/2014.

Memo is placed on record.


</p><p id="p_2">        2.   Learned    counsel    for   petitioner   has   filed

another memo for withdrawal of the petition. The memo

reads as under:
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                            "MEMO
             It is respectfully submitted that the
        petitioner approached this Hon'ble Court for
        permission to travel abroad &amp; report to job &amp;
        this Hon'ble Court was please to allow him.
        In the mean time all disputes between
        petitioner/husband &amp; wife were referred to
        mediation &amp; amicably settled. As such in the
        light of settlement, this Hon'ble Court was
        please to close all the cases.       Hence this
        petition does not survive for consideration.
        Hence this memo may be taken on record &amp;
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              3</span>


     close this petition as withdrawn, in the
     interest of justice."</blockquote><p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">     In view of the said memo, petition is dismissed as

withdrawn.
</p>
