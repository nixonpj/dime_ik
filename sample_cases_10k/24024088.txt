       The High Court Of Madhya Pradesh
                  WP-5960-2019
             (YAGYA NARAYAN SHUKLA Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


Jabalpur, Dated : 19-03-2019

      Shri Rajesh Sharma, Advocate for the petitioner.

      Shri   K.N.Bundela,            Government       Advocate         for   the
respondents/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      With the consent of learned counsel for the parties, the matter is
heard finally.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      The petitioner before this Court is aggrieved by the order of
transfer dated 08.03.2019 (Annexure P/1) passed by the respondent
No.3- Collector, Satna, Districtg Satna (M.P.) whereby the petitioner
has been transferred from Patwari Halka Majhgawan Tehsil
Raghurajnagar, District Satna to Tehsil Amarpatan, District Satna.
</p><p id="p_3">      Against the aforesaid order, the petitioner has already preferred
a representation on 09.03.2019 (Annexure P/8) but till date no orders
have been passed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      In the circumstances, the petition is disposed of with a direction
to the respondent No.3 to decide the petitioner's representation
(Annexure P/8) in accordance with law within a period of four weeks
from the date of receipt of certified copy of this order.
</p><p id="p_5">      The petitioner is also free to file an application for stay before
the respondent No.3 and if such an application for stay is filed by the
petitioner within a period of one week from today, the same shall be
decided by the respondent No.3 within a period of one week from the
date of submission of application for stay along with certified copy of
this order, by speaking and reasoned order.
</p><p id="p_6">      Till the application for stay is decided, the petitioner shall be
                                      2                        WP-5960-2019
allowed to continue at the present place of posting i.e. Patwari Halka
Majhgawan Tehsil Raghurajnagar District Satna (M.P.).
</p><p id="p_7">            Accordingly, the writ petition is disposed of.
            C.c. as per rules.
</p>
