<p id="p_1">2   18-04-2019                 The petitioners claim that their land has been acquired

                   for construction of a road by National Highway Authority of

                   India. They have grievance against determination of amount of

                   compensation against the acquisition. According to them,

                   whereas the land is commercial in nature, their compensation

                   amount has been determined treating the land to be agricultural.

                   Other grievances are also there, in respect of the determination

                   of the amount of compensation.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               In my opinion, the petitioners have a remedy under

                   Section 3-G (5) of the <a href="/doc/1222415/" id="a_1">National Highways Act</a>, 1956 (in short
            Patna High Court CWJC No.7422 of 2019(2) dt.18-04-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     'the Act'), which the petitioners may avail.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 This application is disposed of with the aforesaid

                     observation. If the petitioners raise any claim under <a href="/doc/120401589/" id="a_2">Section 3-G</a>

                     (5) of the Act, it goes without saying that their claim shall be

                     considered and disposed of expeditiously.



</p><p id="p_4">                                                             (Chakradhari Sharan Singh, J)
Ashish/-
</p>
