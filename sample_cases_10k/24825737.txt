                          ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Sri   R.B.Sathyanarayana       Singh,     the   learned

Government Pleader is directed to take notice for the

respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      2.    The petitioner has called into question the

Tahsildar's order, dated 28.6.2012 (Annexure-P) turning

down their request for the grant of the land.


</p><p id="p_4">      3.    I am disinclined to entertain this petition on the

short ground of the availability of an alternative remedy.


</p><p id="p_5">      4.    Keeping all the contentions open and reserving

the liberty to the petitioner to avail of the remedy of filing

an appeal before the Assistant Commissioner, this petition

is rejected. No order as to costs.



</p><p id="p_6">                                           Sd/-
