<p id="p_1">     The writ petition is filed with the following prayer:-

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                  (A)  Issue a writ of mandamus or direction
           directing the 3rd respondent to consider the Form I
           application submitted by the petitioner before the
           S.R.O., Ernakulam on 29-3-2005 along with the copy of
           Ext.P1 sale deed and effect mutation of the property in
           the name of the petitioner in the Transfer of Registry,
           under the provisions of Transfer of Registry Rules,
           1966.
</blockquote>
<p id="p_2">In case no action has yet been taken in the matter the petitioner

may produce a copy of this judgment along with a copy of the

writ petition before the third respondent in which case

appropriate action in accordance with law will be taken in the

matter within a period of three months from the date of

production of a copy of this judgment.

</p><p id="p_3">     The writ petition is disposed of as above.
