                              JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">     This writ petition is filed by the petitioner complaining

that 1st respondent has not accepted Ext.P2 application

submitted by the petitioner seeking regular permit. Further it

is contended that Ext.P3 application for temporary permit is

accepted by the 1st respondent. It is in this background, this

writ petition is filed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     2. Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

Senior Government Pleader.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3. Learned counsel for the petitioner reiterated the

contentions raised in the writ petition. Per contra learned

Senior Government Pleader on instructions submitted that if

the petitioner is submitting Ext.P2 application, the same will

be accepted by the 1st respondent and consider the same in

accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     The submission is recorded. Therefore, there will be a

direction to the 1st respondent to consider the Ext.P2

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">W.P.(C). No.23005 of 2016       2</span>




application in accordance with law. Since the petitioner has

submitted Ext.P3 application seeking temporary permit, there

will be a direction to the 2nd respondent to consider the same

in accordance with law and take a decision on the same within

a period of three weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of

this judgment.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      Writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
