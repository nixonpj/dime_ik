<div class="doc_title">A.Sekar vs The Superintendent Of Police on 30 April, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                                1

                                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

                                                        DATED 30.04.2019

                                                              CORAM

                                 THE HONOURABLE Mr.JUSTICE G.K.ILANTHIRAIYAN

                                                 Crl.O.P.No.12037 of 2019
                     A.Sekar                                                             .. Petitioner

                                                                Vs
                     1.The Superintendent of Police,
                       Vellore, Vellore District-632 001.
                     2.The Inspector of Police,
                       Kurisilapattu Police Station,
                       Vaniyambadi Taluk,
                       Vellore District-635 702.                                        .. Respondents

                     Prayer:      Criminal Original Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of Criminal
                     Procedure Code, to direct the respondent to grant permission to perform the
                     program of 'Adal Padal' on 08.05.2019 at about 06.00 PM to 11.00 PM at “Sri
                     Mariamman Temple Festival” is situated at Jalagamparai Village, Perumapattu
                     Post, Vaniyambadi Taluk, Vellore District and to provide adequate police protection
                     based on the petitioner's representations dated 13.04.2019.


                                          For Petitioner        : Mr.S.Mayilnathan
                                          For Respondents       : Mr.M.Mohamed Riyaz
                                                                  Additional Public Prosecutor

                                                              ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                            Seeking permission     to conduct cultural    programme in the event of Sri

                     Mariamman     Temple    Festival    at   Jalagamparai   Village,   Perumapattu      Post,

                     Vaniyambadi Taluk, Vellore District on 08.05.2019, the petitioner made a

                     representation dated 13.04.2019 to the respondent. As the same has not been

                     considered, the present Criminal Original petition has been filed.
</p>


<p id="p_2">http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                 2</span>

</p><p id="p_3">                            2. Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the learned

                     Additional Public Prosecutor appearing for the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                              3. The learned Additional Public Prosecutor, on instructions, would submit

                     that there is likelihood of arising law and order problem.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                              4. A mere apprehension cannot be a ground to reject the representation.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                     There is absolutely no material before this Court to hold that there will be a law

                     and order problem in the event of permission being granted. In such view of the

                     matter, the petitioner is permitted to conduct cultural programme in the event of

                     Sri Mariamman Temple Festival at Jalagamparai Village, Perumapattu Post,

                     Vaniyambadi Taluk, Vellore District on 08.05.2019, however with the following

                     conditions:-
</p>


<p id="p_7">                              (a) The petitioner shall pay a sum of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand
                     only) as charges, to the respondent Police and on receipt of the same, the
                     respondent Police is directed to issue receipt and proceed further.
</p><p id="p_8">                              (b) The cultural programme in connection with the event of Sri
                     Mariamman      Temple     Festival   at   Jalagamparai   Village,   Perumapattu   Post,
                     Vaniyambadi Taluk, Vellore District on 08.05.2019, between 06.00 p.m. and
                     10.00 p.m.
</p><p id="p_9">                              (c)There should not be any kind of obscene dance or vulgar dialogues
                     during the performance, by anyone of the participants.
</p><p id="p_10">                              (d) Double meaning songs should not be played so as to spoil the minds
                     of students and youths.
</p>

<p id="p_11">http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                                 3</span>

</p><p id="p_12">                                (e) No dance or songs, touching upon any political party or religion or
                     community or caste shall be played.
</p><p id="p_13">                                (f) No flex boards in support of any political party or communal leader,
                     shall be erected at the premises of the programme.
</p><p id="p_14">                                (g) The function shall not affect either religious or communal harmony
                     and shall be conducted without any discrimination based on caste.
</p><p id="p_15">                                (h) If there is violation of any one of the conditions imposed, the
                     concerned police officer is at liberty to take necessary action, as per law and stop
                     such performance forthwith; and
</p><p id="p_16">                                (i) Similarly, the police is directed to stop the dance programme, if it is
                     played beyond the permitted time limit.
</p><p id="p_17">                                (j)   The   respondent   is   directed   to   issue   necessary    permission,
                     incorporating the above conditions.
</p>

<p id="p_18">                                5. With the above directions, this Criminal Original Petition stands
                     allowed.
</p>
