<div class="doc_title">Mohameed Fathima Beevi vs The District Collector on 2 March, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 02.03.2017  

Coram 

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.RAVICHANDRA BAABU               

W.P.(MD)No.3692 of 2017  


Mohameed Fathima Beevi                    : Petitioner      

vs.

1.The District Collector,
Trichy District.
Trichy.

2.The Tashildar,
Manaparai, 
Trichy District.                                        : Respondents

PRAYER: Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India for the
issue of a Writ of Mandamus or any other  order or direction in the nature of
Writ, directing the 1st Respondent to direct the 2nd respondent to grant
patta for the petitioner's lands situated in Old Survey No. 304/2, Natham
Survey No.571/14 and its extent of 4 + cents= 0.0125 sq. mtrs of puthanatham,
Manaparai Taluk, Trichy District by considering the petitioner's
representation dated 19.09.2016 with in a period of stipulated time.

!For Petitioner : Mr.S.Muthalraj
^For Respondents   : Mr.C.Selvaraj
                                         Special Government Pleader                     
        


:ORDER  
</pre>

<p id="p_1">         The Petitioner seeks for a direction to  the first respondent to
direct the second respondent to grant patta for the petitioner's lands
situated in Old Survey No. 304/2, Natham Survey No.571/14 and its extent of 4
+ cents= 0.0125 sq. mtrs of Puthanatham, Manaparai Taluk, Trichy District by
considering his representation dated 19.09.2016.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.  Mr.C.Selvaraj Special Government Pleader takes notice for the
respondents. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.By consent of both parties, the main Writ Petition  is taken up for
disposal,  at the admission stage itself.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4. The grievance of the petitioner is that even though he has given the
representation to the first respondent on 19.09.2016, the same has not been
considered sofar.
</p><p id="p_5">        5.In view of the above, without going into the merits of the claim made
by the petitioner, I only direct the first respondent to consider the
representation of the petitioner, dated 19.09.2016 and pass orders on the
same and in accordance with law, also by hearing  other rival  parties, if
any, within a period of twelve weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of
this order.
</p>
<p id="p_6">        6.With the above direction, the Writ Petition is disposed of. No costs.
</p>


<p id="p_7">To
</p><p id="p_8">1.The District Collector,
Trichy District.
</p><p id="p_9">Trichy.
</p>
<p id="p_10">2.The Tashildar,
Manaparai, 
Trichy District. .
