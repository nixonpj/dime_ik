<div class="doc_title">Kc vs The Hooghly Chinsurah ... on 30 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                             1



     20                         W.P. No. 15139(W) of 2018
30.08.2018
                                         Probodh Chowbey
    KC
                                                Vs.
                              The Hooghly Chinsurah Municipality &amp; Ors.

             Mr. Sukumar Bhattacharya
             Ms. Piyali Shaw
             Ms. Sayani Bhattacharya.
                   ... for the petitioner.

             Mr. A. C. Bagchi
             Ms. M. Patra.
                  ... for the respondent no. 3.</pre>

<p id="p_1">             Mr. Tapas Kumar Ghosh
             Mr. T. Chowdhury.
</p><p id="p_2">                  ... for the Municipality.
</p>
<p id="p_3">


</p><p id="p_4">                             The petitioner complains of unauthorised construction.

                             The Municipality and private respondents are represented.

                             Learned advocate for the private respondents submits that,

             the property in question belongs to the diety and that such diety is not a

             party in the proceedings.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Learned advocate for the petitioner submits that, the

             petitioner is one of the 'sebaits'.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                             Be that as it may, a Writ Court need not enter into the

             disputes with regard to title. The complaint is with regard to unauthorised

             construction.    It is for the Municipality to report as to whether there

             subsist any construction, which is unauthorised or not.

</p><p id="p_7">                             In such circumstances, the concerned officer of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                   2</span>

Municipality will visit the locale, upon notice to the private parties and

submit a report to the Court as to whether there subsists any

unauthorised construction or not. Let such report be filed on the next date

of hearing.
</p>
<p id="p_8">               List the writ petition on September 26, 2018 under the same

heading when the report as called for be filed.

</p><p id="p_9">               Urgent certified website copies of this order, if applied for,

be made available to the parties upon compliance of the requisite

formalities.
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">                                             (Debangsu Basak, J.)
