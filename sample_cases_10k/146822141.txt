                          JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     The petitioner who is working as an empanelled driver

seeks regularisation in the services of the Kerala State Road

Transport Corporation (KSRTC for short). The petitioner has

submitted Ext.P7 representation in this regard in the light of

Ext.P2 memorandum of KSRTC and the Government Orders in

vogue as regards regularisation.

</p><p id="p_2">     2.   I  direct   the    respondent     to   consider Ext.P7

representation on merits with notice to the petitioner within a

period of two months from the date of receipt of a copy of this

judgment.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     The Writ Petition is disposed of.




</p><p id="p_4">                                      V. CHITAMBARESH
