<p id="p_4">                               CMP No. 10342 of 2018

                               The prayer made in the application is allowed.
</p>




<p id="p_5">                 In view of the verdict pronounced by this Court upon FAO

                 No.   478   of   2017,   the   excess   amount   of   compensation,

                 deposited   by   the   applicant   be,   in   accordance   with   law,

                 refunded to the applicant/appellant. 
</p>
<p id="p_6">                               Also, subject to ascertaining the entitlement of


                 the applicants, in all respects, inclusive of the fact of their

                 having attained majority, at this stage, as, also whether the




                 award has not been stayed by the Hon'ble the Apex Court,





                 the   amount   falling   to   the   shares   of   the   applicants,   be

                 released   in   their   favour,   by   remission,   in   their   Savings





                 Bank   Accounts   against   proper   receipt   and   identification.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                 The   particulars   of   the   bank   account,   if   not   already

                 furnished, be furnished within a period of one week.  The

                 application stands disposed of. </p><pre id="pre_1">
