<div class="doc_title">Surendra Mahto vs State Of Bihar &amp; Anr on 17 April, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.16238 of 2015
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -1501 Year- 2005 Thana -SARAN COMPLAINT CASE District-
                                                            SARAN
                 ======================================================
                 1. Surendra Mahto S/o Achchelal Mahto Resident of Village Jajoli, Police
                 Station Mashrak, District Saran.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar.
                 2. Sona Devi W/o Surendra Mahto, D/o Madodar Mahto Opp. Party No. 2 is
                 Resident of Village Dumri Chhapia, Police Station Taraiya, District Saran.

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Najmul Hoda, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Dr.Ravindra Kumar(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   17-04-2015                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                     State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The      petitioner      being      the     husband         of    the

                     complainant is apprehending his arrest in a complaint

                     case in which processes have been directed to be issued

                     after cognizance being taken for the offences punishable

                     under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Section 498A</a> of the Indian Penal Code and 3 and 4

                     of the <a href="/doc/1763444/" id="a_2">Dowry Prohibition Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  The basic accusation is of torture for non-

                    fulfillment of dowry demand.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  Statement has been made in para-9 of the

                    petition that the complainant has already married to one

                    Acchelal Mahto and has got children from his second
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.16238 of 2015 (2) dt.17-04-2015                       2</span>




                          marriage. The Surpanch of Gram Kutchery, Sutihar has

                          issued a certificate to that effect. The same has been

                          brought on record vide Annexue-2.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                       Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above

                          named petitioner be released on provisional anticipatory

                          bail for six months in the event of his arrest or surrender

                          before the learned court below within a period of twelve

                          weeks from today, on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.

                          10,000/- (Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like

                          amount each to the satisfaction of learned Sub-Divisional

                          Judicial Magistrate, Chapra in connection with Complaint

                          Case No. 1501 of 2015, subject to conditions as laid down

                          under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_3">Section 438(2)</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_6">                                       It is expected from the learned court below to get

                          the certificate contained in Annexure-2 verified and if it is

                          found that complainant has performed second marriage,

                          then the learned court below confirm the provisional bail

                          of the petitioner, otherwise petitioner will surrender and

                          pray for regular bail.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">                                                 (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
P.K./-
</p>
