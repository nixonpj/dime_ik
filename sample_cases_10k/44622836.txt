       CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE MOHIT KUMAR SHAH

       ORAL JUDGMENT

         Date : 06-05-2019

</p><p id="p_52">         1.              The learned counsel for the parties are in

         agreement that the dispute involved in the present case has

         been resolved amicably, inasmuch the decision taken by the

         Eastern Regional Committee, National Council for Teachers

         Education for withdrawal of the recognition granted to the

         B.Ed. Course to the Petitioners-college has been rectified and

         the recognition has been restored from the Academic Session

         2019-20.
</p>
<p id="p_53">         2.              At this juncture, the learned counsel for the

         Nalanda Open University submits that the University shall be

         taking appropriate steps in accordance with the notification

         issued by the N.C.T.E., by which the aforesaid recognition of

         the Petitioners-college, which was withdrawn earlier, has been

         restored as also in light of the interim order passed by this

         Court earlier.
</p>
<p id="p_54">         3.              Accordingly, the present writ petition stands

         disposed off with a direction to the Nalanda Open University

         to act as per the order of restoration of the recognition of the
              Patna High Court CWJC No.6830 of 2019 dt.06-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_5">                                                        6/6</span>




                      Petitioners-college, issued by the               Eastern Regional

                      Committee, National Council for Teachers Education.
</p>
