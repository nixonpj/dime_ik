<pre id="pre_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                   BENCH AT JAIPUR

                    S.B. Civil Writs No. 25222/2018
Pushpendra Khandelwal S/o Shri Chandra Prakash Gupta, Aged
About 34 Years, Resident Of Ward No. 12, Behind Police Station,
Sikandara Road, Bandikui (Raj.)
                                                         ----Petitioner
                                Versus
1.      State Of Rajasthan Through The Principal Secretary,
        Department Of Local Self Government, Govt. Secretariat,
        Jaipur (Raj.).
2.      Director Department Of Local Bodies, G-3, Rajmahal
        Residency, Near Civil Lines Phatak, Jaipur.
3.      The Commissioner, Nagar Parishad, Dausa (Raj.)
                                                      ----Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">For Petitioner(s)        :   Ms. Sara Parveen for Mr. Tanveer
                             Ahamad
For Respondent(s)        :   Mr. Ashish Sharma for Mr. Rajendra
                             Prasad, AAG


         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ALOK SHARMA
                       Order
20/11/2018



           Counsel for the petitioner submits that in view of the


letter dated 15.11.2018 under the hand of Director and Joint


Secretary, Local Self Department, Government of Rajasthan,


Jaipur, this petition has been rendered infructuous. She has


submitted a copy of the letter dated 15.11.2018 which is taken on


record and it bears out the submission of counsel for the


petitioner. Counsel for the petitioner further submitted that it be


clarified that no detriment to the petitioner flowing from the letter
                                    dated 2/5.11.2018 would follow as admittedly the said letter was


                                   an error rectified by the subsequent letter dated 15.11.2018


                                   aforesaid.
</p>

<p id="p_2">                                                Counsel for the respondents Mr. Ashish Sharma is not in


                                   a position to submit to the contrary with regard to the petitioner


                                   being saved from any detriment flowing from the letter dated


                                   2/5.11.2018 in view of the fact that it was indeed an error, as


                                   reflected from the letter dated 15.11.2018 under the hand of the


                                   Director and Joint Secretary, Local Self Department, Government


                                   of Rajasthan, Jaipur.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                                                Resultantly this petition is disposed of as infructuous. It


                                   is however made clear that no detriment to the services of the


                                   petitioner would follow from the letter dated 2/5.11.2018 on any


                                   count whatsoever in view of the said letter ousting him to work as


                                   Sanitary Inspector is a consequence of error, which has been since


                                   rectified vide letter dated 15.11.2018.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                Petition stands disposed of accordingly.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                                                                                       (ALOK SHARMA),J
