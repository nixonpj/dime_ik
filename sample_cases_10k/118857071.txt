<div class="doc_title">B.O.C.A. vs Raman Sharma on 26 December, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">Suppl. List No. 1
Case No. 100


                 HIGH COURT OF JAMMU AND KASHMIR
                             AT JAMMU

OWP No. 2639/2018, IA No.1/2018.
                                                     Date of Order: 26.12.2018.

B. O. C. A.                               Vs                      Raman Sharma
Coram:
             Hon'ble Mr. Justice Dhiraj Singh Thakur, Judge

     For the appellant(s)/petitioner(s)   : Mr. Sachin Gupta, Advocate.
     For the respondent(s)                :</pre>
<pre id="pre_2">i.        Whether approved for                 :             Yes/No
          reporting in Press/Media
ii.       Whether to be reported in            :             Yes/ No.
          Digest/Journal

</pre><p id="p_1">          This is an appeal preferred against an interim order dated
14.12.2018, passed by the J&amp;K Special Tribunal, Jammu. The only
ground on which the order impugned has been challenged is that
while ordering maintenance of status-quo, notice as per Section 13
of the J&amp;K Control of Building Operation Act, 1988 was not issued.
</p>
<p id="p_2">          Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">          On a perusal of the order impugned, it is clear that the said
order is only an interim order and no final judgment has been
passed. Learned counsel for the petitioner admitted that an
application for vacation of order has since been filed before the
learned Special Tribunal, Jammu, it will be in the fitness of things to
allow the Tribunal to consider the same on its own merit. This
petition at this stage is found to be premature and is, accordingly,
dismissed along with connected IA.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                       (Dhiraj Singh Thakur)
                                                              Judge
Jammu
26.12.2018
(Muneesh)
