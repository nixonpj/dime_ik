<p id="p_1">     The disputes between the parties in this second appeal

have been settled and a compromise petition as I.A.No.862

of 2016 is filed. It is seen that the terms of the compromise

are lawful. In the circumstances, the impugned judgments

are set aside and the suit O.S.No.100 of 1998 on the file of

the Sub Court, Thiruvananthapuram is decreed in terms of

the compromise. A copy of the compromise petition shall be

appended to the judgment and the original shall be

preserved.
