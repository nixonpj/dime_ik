<div class="doc_title">Sandeep Kumar Shrivastava vs Shri Illyaraja T. Collector on 4 January, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">       THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                 CONC-1475-2016
       (SANDEEP KUMAR SHRIVASTAVA Vs SHRI ILLYARAJA T. COLLECTOR)


2
Gwalior, Dated : 04-01-2018
     Shri Mahesh Goyal, learned counsel for the applicant.
     None for the respondent, though served and</pre>
<p id="p_1">represented by a counsel as mentioned by the office in its
note dated 10.10.2017.
</p><p id="p_2">     As nobody is appearing for the respondent, let a bailable
warrant for a sum of Rs.25,000/- be issued against
respondent Ilayaraja T., Collector, Bhind, for his personal
presence on 16.01.2018.
</p><p id="p_3">     List the case on 16.01.2018.
</p><p id="p_4">                                         (VIVEK AGARWAL)
                                                   JUDGE

     Later on:
</p><p id="p_5">            Shri B.P.S. Chauhan appears and prays that
personal presence of the Collector may be made conditional
and if the order is not complied with then he be directed to
remain personally present. This request of Shri B.P.S.
Chauhan is accepted. It is directed that if the order is not
complied with on or before the next date, then the Collector
shall remain personally present on 16.01.2018.
</p><p id="p_6">     List the case on 16.01.2018.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                                 (VIVEK AGARWAL)
                                                       JUDGE
