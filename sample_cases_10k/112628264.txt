     THIS WRIT PETITION IS FILED UNDER <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">ARTICLE 226</a> AND <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_2">227</a>
OF THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA PRAYING TO DIRECT THE R2 TO
OPEN THE BID DATED 22.2.2014 AND PROCEED TO ALLOCATE THE

     THIS W.P. COMING ON FOR ORDERS THIS DAY, THE COURT
MADE THE FOLLOWING:
                       ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       The petitioner is seeking a direction to the respondent

No.2    to   open    the   bid   in   Tender    No.ICDS/Jlr/Food/

Tran/Tend/2013-14 dated 22.02.2014.


</p><p id="p_2">       2.    Sri    H.V.Manjunatha,     the    learned   Additional

Government Advocate appearing for the respondent Nos.1

to 3 submits that four responses are received pursuant to

the tender notification in question. The technical bids are

opened. Three bids are rejected.


</p><p id="p_3">       3.    The learned Additional Government Advocate

further submits that only one bid, that is of the petitioner is

found to be eligible technically.      The resultant position is

that the impugned tender process is to be treated as

non-responsive.      He submits that, that is why the fresh

tender process is being initiated.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              3</span>



</p><p id="p_4">      4.   Recording   the   submissions    of   the   learned

Additional Government Advocate, this petition is disposed

of. No order as to costs.
</p>
