                              THE GAUHATI HIGH COURT
   (HIGH COURT OF ASSAM, NAGALAND, MIZORAM AND ARUNACHAL PRADESH)

                               Case No. : Bail Appln. 1834/2019

            1:DIDUL MECH
            S/O LT. DINARAM MECH, R/O HALWATING MADHAB NAGAR KAPAHTOLI
            MICHINGGAON, P.O. MADHABNAGAR, P.S.HALWATING, DIST. SIVASAGAR,
            ASSAM, PIN-785681

            VERSUS

            1:THE STATE OF ASSAM
            REP. BY THE PP, ASSAM

Advocate for the Petitioner   : MR. P J SAIKIA

Advocate for the Respondent : PP, ASSAM




                                   BEFORE
                 HONOURABLE MRS. JUSTICE RUMI KUMARI PHUKAN

                                           ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 10-07-2019

      A prayer for regular bail has been sought for by this application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>
of Code of the Criminal Procedure, for and on behalf the petitioner, namely, Didul Mech in
connection with Halwating PS Case No.49/2018 registered under <a href="/doc/263398/" id="a_2">Sections 22</a> of Narcotic
Drugs and Psychotropic Substance Act corresponding to Special (NDPS) Case
No.02/2019.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       Heard Mr. PJ Saikia, learned counsel for the petitioner and Mr. BJ Dutta, learned
Additional Public Prosecutor, Assam for the State/respondent.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                                       Page No.# 2/2

       I have perused the record. It is contended that the petitioner was surrendered before
the trial court on 03.06.2019 as he was shown absconder and the case is at the trial stage.
The learned trial court has refused to grant bail to the petitioner-accused considering his
conduct.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       I have considered the submissions advanced by the learned counsel for the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       As it appears that the learned trial court was reluctant to grant bail to the accused-
petitioner considering his conduct and the case is now proceed for trial.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       Considering all entirety of the matter, particularly, the order dated 17.06.2019 passed
by the learned Special Judge, Sivasagar, the present bail application stands disposed of with a
direction to the petitioner to move the trial court with appropriate bail application after
recording the evidence of his witnesses on the next date fixed and the learned trial court shall
consider the application for bail of the petitioner in accordance with law, if so filed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">       With the above direction this bail application stands disposed of.
</p>



<p id="p_8">                                                                         JUDGE



Comparing Assistant
