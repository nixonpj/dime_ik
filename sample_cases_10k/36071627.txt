<p id="p_1">                                This appeal has been filed by the appellant Rajesh Kumar

               who is the father of the prosecutrix and also the complainant in the case.

               The appellant is aggrieved against the judgment and order dated 9.3.2015

               passed by the learned Additional Sessions Judge, Bhiwani whereby Ajay

               @ Lalu (respondent No.2) has been acquitted.

</p><p id="p_2">                                According to the appellant, the date of birth of the

               prosecutrix is 4.12.1996. The learned Additional Sessions Judge has held

               the date of birth of prosecutrix to be 20.10.1995. Be that as it may, the

               appeal has been filed in this Court on 2.5.2015. By that time, the

               prosecutrix had attained the age of majority. Therefore, Rajesh Kumar

               Code of Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Learned counsel for the appellant when confronted with this

               position submits that he may be allowed to withdraw this appeal so as to

               file an appeal on behalf of the 'victim', if she so desires.

</p><p id="p_4">                                The appeal is, accordingly, dismissed as withdrawn.
