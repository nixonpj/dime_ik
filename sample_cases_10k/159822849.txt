<p id="p_1">     Learned counsel for the petitioner would submit that the

issue involved in this civil petition is similar to the one

involved in C.P. KLRA. Nos.2, 3 and 4 of 2016, which were

dismissed by this Court by separate orders of even date i.e.,

26.02.2019. He would further submit that in the light of the

aforesaid   civil   petitions   being   already   dismissed   on
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    -3-</span>



26.02.2019,      this   Petition    also   does   not   survive    for

consideration.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      3.    Accordingly, placing the submission of learned

counsel for the petitioner on record, I.A. No.1/2017 seeking

condonation of delay of 9309 days in filing this petition is

hereby rejected as the same does not merit consideration and

consequently, this Civil Petition is dismissed on the same lines

on which CP.KLRA. Nos.2, 3 and 4 of 2016 were dismissed by

this Court by separate orders of even date i.e., 26.02.2019.



</p><p id="p_4">      4.    Learned      High      Court   Government    Pleader    is

directed to file memo of appearance within two weeks from

today.
