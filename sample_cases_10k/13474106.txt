<div class="doc_title">Vipin @ Golu @ Ravan Sakle vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 16 April, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                  M.Cr.C. No. 3599/2013
16/04/2013
      Shri Sharad Pandey, Advocate for the applicant.
      Shri Vivek Lakhera, PL for the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard finally.
</p><p id="p_2">      This is the first application filed by applicant under
<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Cr.P.C for grant of bail.
</p><p id="p_3">      Applicant    has      been    arrested   on    25/02/13    in
connection with Crime No. 220/12 registered at P.S.
GRP    Itarsi,   District    Hoshangabad       for   the   offence
punishable under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_2">section 379</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_4">      Learned counsel for the applicant submits that
applicant has been falsely implicated in the case.              The
offence is triable by Magistrate. Charge sheet has
already been filed and trial would take considerable
time to be disposed of finally         therefore, he prays for
bail to the applicant.
</p><p id="p_5">      Learned     counsel     for    the   State     opposes    the
application.
</p><p id="p_6">      Considering the overall facts and circumstances of
the case alongwith the nature of allegation made
against this applicant, I am of the considered view that
it is a fit case to release him on bail. Therefore, without
commenting on the merit of the case, this application is
allowed and it is directed that applicant Vipin @ Golu @
Ravan Sakle be released on bail on his furnishing a
personal bond in a sum of Rs. 25,000/- (Rs. Twenty
Five Thousand only) with one surety in the like
 amount to the satisfaction of Committal Court/trial
Court, for securing his presence before the said Court
on all the dates of hearing fixed in this regard during
trial.
</p><p id="p_7">         Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                                        (G.S.SOLANKI)
                                            Judge
navin
