<p id="p_2">              Sri.S.Sanal   Kumar,   learned   counsel   appearing   for   the   appellants,


submits   that   as   on   today,   the   relief   sought   for   in   this   writ   appeal   does   not


survive for consideration of this Court.
</p>

<p id="p_3">            2.  Taking note of the submission made by the learned counsel, the writ


appeal is disposed of as having become infructuous.
</p>

<p id="p_4">            3.   In view of the order passed in the writ appeal, the reliefs sought in


C.M.P.No.5775   of   2001  need   not   be   considered    by  this   Court.     Accordingly,


the said C.M.P. is also  rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_5">            Ordered accordingly.
</p>
