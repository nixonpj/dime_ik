<div class="doc_title">An Application For Bail Under ... vs In Re:- Chada @ Chadan Mallick @ ... on 17 April, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                    1

   21
17.04.2017</pre>
<p id="p_1">   sm
Allowed
                              CRM No.3128 of 2017

              In the matter of an application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the
              Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 11.04.2017 in connection with
              Kaliganj Police Station Case No.49 of 2012 dated 30.01.2012 under
              <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_3">452</a>/<a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_4">325</a>/<a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_5">326</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_6">307</a>/<a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_7">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_2">                                         And

             In Re:- Chada @ Chadan Mallick @ Chand Mohammad Molla

                                                                     .. Petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Mr.Prabir Majumder
             Mr.Snehansu Majumder        ... for the petitioner

             Mr.Neguive Ahmed, Ld.APP
             Ms.Mayukhi Mitra     ... for the State.


</p><p id="p_4">                      Leave is granted to correct the cause title.


                      Heard the learned advocates appearing on behalf of the

             parties.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                      The claim of the learned advocate for the petitioner is that his

             client is standing on same footing with the co-accused, who has

             been granted bail. Such fact has not been disputed by the learned

             advocate for the State.           The petitioner absconded for 5 and half

             years.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                      We have gone through the case diary and find no valid reason

             to take a different view. Accordingly, this application for bail stands

             allowed on parity.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  2</span><p id="p_9">


       Let the petitioner be released on bail to the satisfaction of the

learned Chief Judicial Magistrate at Nadia upon furnishing a Bond of

Rs.50,000/-, with two sureties of Rs.25,000/- each, one of whom

must be local and on further condition that after release, the shall not

leave the jurisdiction of the concerned police station and shall report

to the Officer-in-Charge of that police station thrice in every week

until further order.
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">       Accordingly, this application for bail is disposed of.




</p><p id="p_12">                                               (Ashim Kumar Roy, J.)



                                             (Amitabha Chatterjee, J.)
