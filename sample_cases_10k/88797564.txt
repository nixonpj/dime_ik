<p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
   S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 11895/2019

Mumtaj Khan @ Mantaj S/o Noor Ahmad, Aged About 22 Years,
B/c Musalman, R/o Jantawali, Police Station Naimandi Gharsana,
District Sri Ganganagar (Raj.). (At Present Lodged In Sub Jail
Anoopgarh).
</p><p id="p_2">                                                                    ----Petitioner
                                     Versus
State, Through P.p.</p><pre id="pre_1">
                                                                  ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)          :     Mr. D.S. Thind
For Respondent(s)          :     Mr. Shrawan Bishnoi, P.P.



             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

                                      Order

27/09/2019

</pre><p id="p_3">     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as learned

Public Prosecutor and also perused the material on record.


</p><p id="p_4">     The petitioner has been arrested in FIR No.181/2019 of

Police   Station    Ramsinghpur,         District      Sriganganagar      for   the

offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">Sections 379</a> and <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_2">414</a> of IPC. He has

preferred this bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_5">     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

     Having regard to the totality of the facts and circumstances

of the case, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail to the accused

petitioner under Section 439 Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      (Downloaded on 27/09/2019 at 08:54:02 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                       (2 of 2)                     [CRLMB-11895/2019]



                                        Accordingly, this bail application filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">Section 439</a>

                                   Cr.P.C. is allowed and it is directed that petitioner Mumtaj Khan @

                                   Mantaj S/o Noor Ahmad shall be released on bail in connection

                                   with FIR No.181/2019 of Police Station Ramsinghpur, District

                                   Sriganganagar provided he executes a personal bond in a sum of

                                   Rs.50,000/- with two sound and solvent sureties of Rs.25,000/-

                                   each to the satisfaction of learned trial court for his appearance

                                   before that court on each and every date of hearing and whenever

                                   called upon to do so till the completion of the trial.



</p><p id="p_8">                                                                                            (VIJAY BISHNOI),J

                                    113-Taruna/-
</p>
