  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 11986 of 2010(W)


1. BHASKARAN, GOVERNMENT CONTRACTOR,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. DISTRICT COLLECTOR, PATHANAMTHITTA.
                       ...       Respondent

2. REVENUE DIVISIONAL OFFICER, ADOOR.

                For Petitioner  :SRI.K.MADHUSOODHANAN NAIR

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice T.R.RAMACHANDRAN NAIR

 Dated :06/04/2010

 O R D E R
                 T.R. RAMACHANDRAN NAIR, J.
               ---------------------------------------
                  W.P.(C) No.11986 OF 2010
               ---------------------------------------
             Dated this the 6th day of April, 2010.


                         J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      The petitioner is a Government Contractor who has been

entrusted with the earthwork of Arattupuzha Panchayath on the

Manivelikkadavu, Kochi Jetty Road under the Coastal Road

Development Scheme. The work has to be executed by filling up

of road with gravel earth.       The Assistant Engineer, Harbour

Engineering Section II has already applied to the 1st respondent

to grant necessary permit to the petitioner to transport the gravel

from Pathanamthitta District to the place of work in Alappuzha

District as evidenced by Exhibit P1.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2. After getting consent from the owner of the property in

survey No.953/16 of Pallickal village, the petitioner has moved

the 2nd respondent for permission to extract the gravel from the

above property and to transport the same to the work spot at

Alappuzha District, as per Exhibit P2. Aggrieved by the delay in

the matter, this writ petition has been filed.

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">W.P.(C) No.11986/2010               2</span>


</p><p id="p_3">       There will be a direction to the 2nd respondent to consider

Exhibit P2 and pass appropriate orders within a period of two

weeks since it is submitted that the petitioner will have to

execute the work before the onset of monsoon. The       petitioner

will produce a copy of this writ petition along with a copy of the

judgment for compliance.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       This writ petition is disposed of as above.




</p><p id="p_5">                                   T.R. RAMACHANDRAN NAIR
                                              JUDGE

</p>
