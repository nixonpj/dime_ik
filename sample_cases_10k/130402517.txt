</pre><p id="p_1">	Instant writ petition has been jointly filed by the petitioners who are Ex-Servicemen and holding academic qualification but not having one year's experience of working to their credit, which is one of the condition of eligibility under the scheme of Rules, 2015 for a candidate to participate in the selection process initiated by the respondent-State pursuant to the advertisement dt.21.07.2015.
</p><p id="p_2">	The controversy has been decided by this court in the batch of writ petitions (D.B.Civil Writ Petition No.12228/2015) decided on 09.09.2015. 
</p><p id="p_3">	In the light of the judgment dt.09.09.2015 supra, the instant writ petition stands dismissed.
</p>
