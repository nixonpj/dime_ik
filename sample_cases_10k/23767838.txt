                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.10320 of 2016
                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -12 Year- 2015 Thana -SURSAND District- SITAMARHI
                 ======================================================
                    1. Md. Fakre Alam @ Gulab @ Fakre Alam son of Md. Masum
                    2. Ifrat @ Hasmukh wife of Md. Fakre Alam.
                                                                    .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Uday Kumar, Adv.
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Dr.Indiwar Kumari (App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   01-03-2016               Heard learned counsels for the petitioners and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                             The petitioners being the brother and brother's wife of

                 the husband of the informant are apprehending arrest in a case

                 registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/140515/" id="a_3">313</a> and <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_4">498A</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             The basic accusation is of torture. It is also alleged that

                 the father of the husband of the informant assaulted the informant

                 on her stomach causing bleeding injury.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that

                 there is no injury report of the informant on record suggesting the

                 bleeding injury. The accusation against the petitioner is omnibus

                 and general and thrust of accusation is against the husband of the

                 informant.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above named
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.10320 of 2016 (2) dt.01-03-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




                     petitioners be released on anticipatory bail, in the event of arrest or

                     surrender before the learned Court below within a period of 12

                     weeks from today, on furnishing bail bond of `10,000/- (ten

                     thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                     satisfaction of the learned J.M., Pupri at Sitamarhi in connection

                     with Sursand P.S. Case No. 12 of 2015, subject to the conditions

                     as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_6">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                  (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Amrendra/-
