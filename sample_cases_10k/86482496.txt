<p id="p_1">3   16-11-2019             Heard learned counsel for the parties.

</p><p id="p_2">                           Petitioner is apprehending his arrest in connection with

                   Bairgania PS Case No.41/19 (GR No.882 of 2019) , registered for

                   offences punishable under <a href="/doc/508426/" id="a_1">Sections 494</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_3">498A</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a>,

                   <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                           As per FIR, petitioners are said to be mother-in-law and

                   Nanad of the informant and there is allegation against them

                   that they abused and assaulted the informant and ousted her

                   from the house and also taken away her children.

</p><p id="p_4">                           Submission of the learned counsel for the petitioner is

                   that there is general allegation against the petitioners as well
         Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.56304 of 2019(3) dt.16-11-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                    2/2</span>




                  as the husband and considering the same they had been

                  granted provisional bail earlier.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                          Heard learned A.P.P. and the learned counsel for the

                  informant, who has opposed the prayer for bail stating that she

                  had made several representations to the authorities concerned

                  against the action of the accused persons , as they are not

                  allowing the informant to go to the school even. .

</p><p id="p_6">                          Having heard both sides and in view of the facts and

                  circumstances, as stated above, this application is allowed and

                  the provisional bail granted to the petitioner, vide order dated

                  5.9.2019 is confirmed on condition that they         shall file an

                  undertaking in the court below that they will not try to restrict

                  the movement of the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">                                          (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
chn/-
