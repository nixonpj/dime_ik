 (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE JAYANT PATEL)


</p><p id="p_2">  1. The order is clear in its own language.                     If the

     party-in-person is unable to follow, we cannot

     help it.
</p>
<p id="p_3">  2. The learned AGP states that the copies of all the




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   Page 1 of 2</span>
              C/CA/13875/2014                                          ORDER



        documents with the affidavit-in-reply are already

        supplied.              However, the party-in-person states

        that        the        enclosure          or      attachment      to       the

        affidavits are not there in some cases or such

        documents are not certified as 'true copy' or the

        authenticity thereof is also not known.

</p><p id="p_4">    3. In our view, such cannot be examined on a 'note'

        filed by the party-in-person.                        If the party-in-

        person has any doubt about the genuineness of the

        documents              or    authenticity           thereof      or        non-

        certification as 'true copy', the said aspect can

        be     contended            in   the     reply.      Further,         if   the

        documents are not supplied, which are referred to

        in     the       affidavit          of      the    State   authorities,

        grievance for the same can also be raised in the

        affidavit-in-reply.                    Time is now granted up to

        26.2.2015.
</p>
<p id="p_5">    4. Office note is disposed of accordingly.
