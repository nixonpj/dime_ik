<div class="doc_title">Mukesh Sharma @ Mukky Sharma vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 2 November, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                           MCRC-10540-2015
      (MUKESH SHARMA @ MUKKY SHARMA Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


02-11-2015

Shri Arshad Ali, counsel for the applicant/accused.

Smt. Sangeeta Pachouri, Public Prosecutor, for the respondent/State.</pre>

<p id="p_1">The case-diary is available and the Investigating Officer Shri
Chetan Arya, Inspector, is also present before this Court.
This is first application filed by the applicant/accused under
<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C. for grant of bail in connection with Crime
No.213/2012, registered by P.S. Karera, District Shivpuri (M.P) for
commission of offences under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of IPC and under
<a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_4">Section 25</a>, <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_5">27</a> of the Arms Act.
</p><p id="p_2">The learned counsel for the applicant-accused submits that the
allegations made against the applicant-accused are more or less
the same as those against the co-accused Umesh Sharma who is
the brother of the applicant/accused and who has been enlarged
on bail by this Court today vide order passed in Mcrc
No.10528/2015 having considered the merits of the case, hence,
on the basis of parity, the learned counsel for the applicant-
accused has prayed for grant of bail.
</p><p id="p_3">The learned Public Prosecutor opposing the submissions made on
behalf of the applicant/accused has prayed for rejection of the bail
application.
</p><p id="p_4">Heard the arguments of learned counsel for both the parties and
perused the case diary.
</p><p id="p_5">It transpires from the case-diary that the allegations against the
applicant-accused are the same as those against the co-accused
Umesh Sharma who has been granted bail by this Court today
 having considered the merits of the case in Mcrc No.10528/2015.
Therefore, on the basis of parity, without expressing any opinion
on the merits, the application is allowed. It is directed that the
applicant be released on bail on his furnishing a personal bond in
the sum of Rs.1,00,000/- (Rs. One Lac Only) with one solvent
surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial
court/committal court with a condition that he shall remain
present before the court concerned during trial and shall also
comply with the conditions enumerated in <a href="/doc/259682/" id="a_6">Section 437(3)</a> of
Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_6">Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                  (M.K. MUDGAL)
                                                      JUDGE
