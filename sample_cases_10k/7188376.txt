                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Criminal Appeal (DB) No.521 of 2012
                  ======================================================
                  Rakesh Paswan S/O Late Tetar Paswan R/V- Murgiyachak, P.S.- Vena
                  (Rahui), Distt.- Nalanda (Bihar)....................... .... Appellant/s
                                                   Versus
                  The State Of Bihar ..................................... .... Respondent/s
                  ======================================================
                  Appearance :
                  For the Appellant/s      : Mr. Ramakant Sharma, Sr. Advocate
                  For the Respondent/s      : Mr. APP
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SHYAM KISHORE
                  SHARMA
                            and
                            HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE AMARESH KUMAR LAL
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">                  (Per: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SHYAM KISHORE SHARMA)

5   21-11-2012                       I. A. No. 1869 of 2012

                                     Through the instant interlocutory application the

                   appellant has prayed for enlarging him on provisional bail for a

                   period of one month, so that he could properly solemnize the

                   marriage of his second daughter Anupam Kumari which is

                   scheduled to be held on 28.11.2012.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                     In support of the contention that the marriage has

                   been fixed, a photo stat copy of the invitation card as well as the

                   photo stat copy of the certificate granted by the Mukhiya of Gram

                   Panchayat Raj Supasang, Block- Rahui, Nalanda has been filed.

                   The aforesaid documents mention that the appellant's daughter is

                   to be solemnized. The affidavit has been sworn in by the wife of

                   the appellant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                     Considering the aforesaid facts, I. A. No. 1869 of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court CR. APP (DB) No.521 of 2012 (5) dt.21-11-2012</span>




                      2012 is allowed. During the pendency of this appeal, the appellant

                      above named is directed to be released on provisional bail for a

                      period of one month from the date of his release on furnishing of

                      bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the

                      like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Additional

                      Sessions Judge, F.T.C. No. IV, Nalanda at Biharsharif in Sessions

                      Trial No. 231 of 2012.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                        The appellant is directed to surrender before the

                      learned court below after one month from the date of his release

                      on provisional bail and file a surrender certificate on affidavit

                      before this Court.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                              (Shyam Kishore Sharma, J)


avin/-                                          (Amaresh Kumar Lal, J)
 </pre>
