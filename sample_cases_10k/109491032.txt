<div class="doc_title">Akbar Khan vs State Of Rajasthan on 3 September, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Vijay Bishnoi, Vijay Bishnoi</div>

<p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
  S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous II Bail Application No. 10250/2019

Akbar Khan S/o Lt. Sh. Sakoor Khan, Aged About 27 Years, By
Caste Mohammedan, R/o Fulad Road, Sojat Road, P.s. Sojat
Road, Distt. Pali (Raj.) (Presently Lodged In Distt. Jail, Pali)
</p><p id="p_2">                                                                     ----Petitioner
                                   Versus
State Of Rajasthan, Through Pp
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                   ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)        :     Mr. Sikander Khan

For Respondent(s)        :     Mr. Shrawan Bishnoi, PP

                               Mr. Javed Hussain, for complainant



            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

                         Judgment / Order

03/09/2019

     Heard learned counsel for the parties and perused the

material available on record.
</p><p id="p_4">    The petitioner(s) has/have                    been          arrested   in   FIR

No.77/2019 of Police Station Bagdi Nagar, Pali for the

offence(s) punishable under Section(s) 498-A, 323 and 306

<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>. He/she/they has/have preferred this/these second bail

application(s) under Section 439 Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_5">     Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that

after rejection of the first bail application of the petitioner,

charge-sheet has been filed. It is further submitted that as a

matter of fact, wife of the petitioner committed suicide on

account of some dispute with her mother-in-law. It is


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    (Downloaded on 03/09/2019 at 08:56:05 PM)</span>
                                                                      (2 of 2)                     [CRLMB-10250/2019]


                                   submitted that allegation of demand of dowry, levelled

                                   against the petitioner, is false.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                        Learned Public Prosecutor as well as learned counsel for

                                   the complainant have opposed the bail application(s).

</p><p id="p_7">                                        Having    regard     to     the      totality       of    the   facts   and

                                   circumstances of the case, without expressing any opinion on

                                   the merits of the case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail

                                   to the petitioner(s) under Section 439 Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_8">                                        Accordingly, this/these second bail application(s) filed

                                   under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is/are allowed and it is directed that

                                   petitioner(s) - Akbar Khan S/o Lt. Sh. Sakoor Khan shall be

                                   released on bail in connection with FIR No.77/2019 of Police

                                   Station Bagdi Nagar, Pali provided he/she/they execute(s) a

                                   personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two sound and

                                   solvent sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of

                                   learned trial court for his/her/their appearance before that

                                   court on each and every date of hearing and whenever called

                                   upon to do so till the completion of the trial.



</p><p id="p_9">                                                                                           (VIJAY BISHNOI),J

                                    9-msrathore/-
</p>
