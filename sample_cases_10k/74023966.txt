<pre id="pre_1">                            ORDER SHEET
                        APO NO.154 OF 2013
                         AP NO.247 OF 2013
                  IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                      Civil Appellate Jurisdiction
                            ORIGINAL SIDE


                           ANIL KUMAR &amp; ORS.
                                  Versus
                           L &amp; T FINANCE LTD.



   BEFORE:
  The Hon'ble JUSTICE ASHIM KUMAR BANERJEE

  The Hon'ble JUSTICE MRINAL KANTI CHAUDHURI

  Date : 4th June, 2013.

                                     Mr.Lal Ratan Mondal, Advocate
                                     for the appellant.

                                     Mr.Souvik Chowdhury, Advocate
                                     for the respondent.</pre>


<p id="p_1">   The Court : At the time of passing of the judgment and order impugned

Rs.3.04 lakhs was due on account of defaulted instalments. Subsequent

defaults have also been committed. The Receiver has already taken

possession. The appellants would contend, their application for setting

aside is pending and awaiting disposal. We would request the learned

single Judge to dispose of the same at an early date, subject to His

Lordship's preoccupation and convenience.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         2</span>


</p><p id="p_2">         We permit the appellants to pay the defaulted amount by weekly

instalment of Rs.1 lakh in addition to the current instalments. Mr.Mondal

appearing for the appellants assures, he would make payment of the first

instalment by tomorrow (05.06.2013). So long the instalments are paid as

per the agreement and the defaults are met in terms of the foregoing order,

the Receiver would not take any further steps to sell the vehicle.

</p><p id="p_3">         The appeal is disposed of without any order as to costs.




</p><p id="p_4">                                      (ASHIM KUMAR BANERJEE, J.)



                                     (MRINAL KANTI CHAUDHURI, J.)
