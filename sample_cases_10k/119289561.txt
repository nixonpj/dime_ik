<div class="doc_title">Sant Lal Sah vs The State Of Bihar on 18 September, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.25906 of 2014
                              Arising Out of Complaint Case No. -157 Year- 2010 District- SIWAN
                  ======================================================
                  Sant Lal Sah, Son of Ram Jatan Sah, Resident of Village - Dumri, P.S. -
                  Daraunda, District Siwan.

                                                                                   .... ....      Petitioner/s
                                                         Versus
                  The State of Bihar.

                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                  ======================================================
                  Appearance :
                  For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Surendra Kishore Thakur, Advocate
                  For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Awadhesh Kr. Singh (App)
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE AMARESH KUMAR LAL
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2    18-09-2014                 Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                  learned counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  The petitioner apprehends his arrest in Complaint

                  Case No. 157 of 2010 registered for the offence punishable under

                  <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections 323</a> and <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">498A</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  It is submitted that the petitioner has no criminal

                  antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  This application stands disposed of with a liberty to

                  the petitioner to move before Sri R.K. Singh, learned Judicial

                  Magistrate 1st Class, Siwan/ court concerned who will pass the

                  order in view of the direction given by the Hon'ble Supreme Court

                  in the case of <a href="/doc/2982624/" id="a_3">Arnesh Kumar vs. State of Bihar &amp; Anr</a>., reported

                  in 2014(3) PLJR 314 (SC).
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                                      (Amaresh Kumar Lal, J)
V.K. Pandey/-
</p><pre id="pre_2">  U         T
