<div class="doc_title">Narendra Singh @ Nicky vs State on 14 June, 2018</div><p id="p_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                        JODHPUR
              S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail No. 5552 / 2018
Narendra Singh @ Nicky Son of Shri Bhanwar Singh, By Caste
Rajput, Resident of Ward No.5, Milkman Colony, Lane No.1,
Shastri Nagar Police Station, Jodhpur, Presently Resident of
Maderna Colony, Mahamandir Police Station, Jodhpur, District
Jodhpur Metro. (Lodged in Central Jail, Jodhpur)

</p><p id="p_2">                                                         ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State of Rajasthan
</p><p id="p_3">                                                     ----Respondent
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)    : Sh. Bhanwar Singh
                       (Father of the petitioner present in person)
For Respondent(s) : Mr. Deepak Choudhary, Public Prosecutor
_____________________________________________________
       JUSTICE VINIT KUMAR MATHUR, VACATION JUDGE
                               Order
14/06/2018

       The present bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

Cr.P.C. on behalf of the petitioner who is in custody in connection

with F.I.R. No.67/2018, Police Station Mandore, District Jodhpur

for the offences under <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_2">Sections 143</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1243353/" id="a_4">342</a>, <a href="/doc/520270/" id="a_5">327</a> and <a href="/doc/886598/" id="a_6">365</a> of

IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       Since the lawyers are abstaining from work, heard, Sh.

Bhanwar Singh (Father of the petitioner present in person) and

learned Public Prosecutor.     Perused the material available on

record.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">       It is submitted on behalf of the petitioner that the co-

accused Savai Singh @ Yashpal Singh has already been granted
                                            (2 of 2)
                                                                  [CRLMB-5552/2018]

          bail by this Court vide order dated 10.05.2018.        The accused-

          petitioner is in judicial custody and the trial of the case will take

          sufficiently long time to be concluded and the case of the present

          petitioner is not distinguishable than the case of the co-accused

          who has already been released on bail by this Court.


</p><p id="p_7">                  Learned Public Prosecutor opposes the bail.


                  Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case and

          upon a consideration of the arguments advanced and considering

          the fact that the case of the present petitioner is identical to co-

          accused aforesaid who has been enlarged on bail by this Court,

          this Court is of the opinion that the petitioner is also entitled for

          the grant of bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_7">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_8">                  Consequently, the bail application is allowed. It is ordered

          that the accused-petitioner Narendra Singh @ Nicky Son of Shri

          Bhanwar Singh arrested in connection with F.I.R. No.67/2018,

          Police Station Mandore, District Jodhpur shall be released on bail;

          provided he furnishes a personal bond of Rs.50,000/- with two

          sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial

          court with the stipulation to appear before that Court on all dates

          of hearing and as and when called upon to do so.




</p><p id="p_9">                                                  (VINIT KUMAR MATHUR), V.J.
</p>
<p id="p_10">Solanki Sunil/-
</p><p id="p_11">S-102
