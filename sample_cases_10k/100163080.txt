                                      -------
</pre><p id="p_1">                Krishna Prasad Shah                         ... Petitioner

                                               Versus

                The State of Jharkhand &amp; Ors.                ... Opposite Parties

</p><p id="p_2">                                      -------
</p><p id="p_3">            CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SHREE CHANDRASHEKHAR
</p><p id="p_4">                                      -------
</p><p id="p_5">             For the Petitioner            : Miss Apoorva Singh, Advocate
             For the Opposite Party-State : Mr. Atanu Banerjee, G.A
                                             Mr. Suman Kr. Ghosh, A.C to G.A
                                             Ms. Aprajita Bhardwaj, A.C to A.G
</p><p id="p_6">                                      ------
</p>
<p id="p_7">07/21.12.2018            The learned State counsel tenders a copy of memo
                no. 932 dated 23.07.2018, a copy thereof served to the learned
                counsel for the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_8">                         Miss Apoorva Singh, the learned counsel for the
                petitioner, however, submits that payment to the petitioner has
                yet not been made.
</p><p id="p_9">                         It is stated that the petitioner who himself is the
                Drawing and Disbursing Authority is in possession of his service
                book.   Accordingly, he is directed to produce his service book
                before the District Superintendent of Education, Latehar on or
                before 04.01.2019.
</p><p id="p_10">                         Let an affidavit disclosing payment to the petitioner in
                terms of order dated 07.12.2017 passed in W.P.(S) No. 408 of
                2014 be filed before 18.01.2019.
</p><p id="p_11">                         The       Director,    School   Education   and   Literacy
                Department, Government of Jharkhand, Ranchi shall ensure
                compliance of the writ Court's order.
</p><p id="p_12">                         Let a copy of the order be given to the learned counsels
                for the parties.
</p>

<p id="p_13">                                                         (Shree Chandrashekhar, J.)
   Amit/
