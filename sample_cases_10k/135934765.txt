<div class="doc_title">Bajrang Lal Bairwa vs State And Ors on 1 March, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 S.B.Cr. Misc. Petition No. 872/2008
Bajrang Lal Bairwa    vs. State  of Rajasthan &amp;  ors.  
 ---- 
</pre>
<p id="p_1">1.3.2012
    HONBLE MR. JUSTICE MAHESH CHANDRA SHARMA
None present for the petitioners
Mr. Pradeep Shrimal, Public Prosecutor.   
</p>
<p id="p_2">                      ---            
</p>
<p id="p_3">      This misc. petition has been filed by the petitioner under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">section 482</a> Cr.P.C. for  change of investigation of FIR No. 99/2008 lodged at Police Station Mansarover Jaipur  for offence under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_2">sections  363</a>, <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_3">366</a> and <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_4">376</a> IPC and insertion of names of non-petitioners 3 to 5 in the FIR. 

     Nobody present on behalf of the petitioner. The change of investigation of FIR of 2008 has been    made in the instant petition. The police must have already investigated the matter and challan has been filed.  The misc. petition which was filed in the year 2008 stands dismissed. However, the petitioner shall be at liberty to raise all the points raised in  the criminal misc. petition  at the appropriate stage before the investigating agency  if the challan has not been filed or at the appropriate stage before the trial court, if the challan has been  filed. 

      This criminal misc. petition  is dismissed  with the aforesaid liberty.    
</p>
<p id="p_4"> (Mahesh Chandra Sharma) J.
