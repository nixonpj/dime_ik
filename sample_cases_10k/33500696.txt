<div class="doc_title">Tara Chand vs State Of Haryana And Others on 22 April, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                           IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT
                                         CHANDIGARH.

                                           CWP No.15991 of 2013 (O&amp;M)
                                           Date of decision: 22.4.2014

            Tara Chand                                                      ...Petitioner

                                               Versus

            State of Haryana and others                                     ...Respondents


            CORAM:             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJAN GUPTA

            Present:           Mr. Shalender Mohan, Advocate for the petitioner.
                               Mr. Saurabh Mohunta, DAG, Haryana.
                               Dr. Parveen Hans, Advocate for respondents No.5 to 8.

            Rajan Gupta, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                               Only grievance of the petitioner is that his revision petition was

            not entertained by Superintending Canal Officer despite power contained in

            Section 20 (3) of Haryana Canal and Drainage Act, 1974. Learned counsel

            for the petitioner has relied upon judgment of this court rendered in

            <a href="/doc/14060556/" id="a_1">Darbara vs. Superintending Canal Officer &amp; others, CWP No</a>.1201 of 2013,

            decided on 15.5.2013 in support of this plea.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Ratio of aforesaid judgment is not disputed by learned State

            counsel.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               In view of above, impugned order (Annexure P-6) is hereby

            set-aside. Petitioner is relegated to remedy of filing revision before the

            Superintending Canal Officer who shall decide the issue on merits after

            hearing both the parties. Needful be done expeditiously, in any case not

            later than four months.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                                                                  (RAJAN GUPTA)
                                                                      JUDGE
            22.4.2014
