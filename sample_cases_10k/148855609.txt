<div class="doc_title">Kumari Lalmati vs The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors on 17 February, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.3301 of 2013
                 ======================================================
                 Kumari Lalmati
                                                                      .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                 The State of Bihar &amp; Ors
                                                                     .... .... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s    :    Mr. Satyabir Bharti
                 For the Respondent/s      : Mr. Md. N. Hoda Khan Sc18
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SHIVAJI PANDEY
                                              ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">8   17-02-2016                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                                 Admittedly, the private respondent has better marks

                 than the petitioner but claim has been made by the petitioner that

                 the private respondent neither applied for the post of Panchayat

                 Teacher nor she appeared in person, which has been denied by the

                 learned counsel for the private respondent.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Learned counsel for the private respondent submits

                 that the private respondent has applied for the post of Panchayat

                 Teacher and has annexed the receipt of his application and the

                 Niyojan Panji.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                In such view of the matter, it will be necessary that

                 the original record be called for. Therefore, the learned counsel for

                 the State is directed to produce the original record with respect to
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court CWJC No.3301 of 2013 (8) dt.17-02-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                       second phase selection of Panchayat Teacher of Gram Panchayt

                       Raj Kataria, District-Bhojpur.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                       Let this case be listed after four weeks under the

                       same heading.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">                                                    (Shivaji Pandey, J)
pawan/-
</p>
