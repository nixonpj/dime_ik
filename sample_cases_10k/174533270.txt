<div class="doc_title">Shambhu Singh @ Bhuta vs The State Of Bihar on 17 August, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.30428 of 2012 (2) dt.17-08-2012




                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Criminal Miscellaneous No.30428 of 2012
                   ======================================================
                   Shambhu Singh @ Bhuta
                                                                        .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                     Versus
                   The State Of Bihar
                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     : Mr.
                   For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr.
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR
                   SRIVASTAVA
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   17-08-2012               Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                             Petitioner seeks bail in Barahiya P.S.Case No.198 of 2009

                   registered under <a href="/doc/1947545/" id="a_1">sections 448</a>,<a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">341</a>,<a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_3">326</a>,<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a> and <a href="/doc/1183069/" id="a_5">386</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of I.P.C.

                   and <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_7">section 27</a> of the Arms Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             There is specific allegation against the petitioner that he

                   demanded extortion money from the informant and when he

                   refused to obey the command of the petitioner, he opened fire of

                   his firearm causing injury on the right leg of the informant.

                   Impugned order reveals that the petitioner carries criminal

                   antecedent of several cases.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             Regard being had to the facts and circumstances of the case

                   as well as submissions of the parties, I am not inclined to grant

                   bail to the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Accordingly, his prayer for bail in connection with
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.30428 of 2012 (2) dt.17-08-2012</span>




                      Barahiya P.S. Case No.198 of 2009 pending in the court of C.J.M.,

                      Lakhisarai, is, hereby, rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_6">AnilKrSinha/-                               (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
