                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -146 Year- 2014 Thana -MUFFASIL District- AURANGABAD
                   ======================================================
                   1. Pintu Kumar S/o Arbind Kumar Singh R/o Village Pandit Bigha, P.S.
                   Barun, District Aurangabad.

                                                                                 .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                         Versus
                   1. The State of Bihar

                                                        .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR
                   SRIVASTAVA
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2     11-09-2015                     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                     The name of petitioner came in this case in

                   confessional statement of co-accused, Rakesh Kumar Gupta who

                   was later on, identified by the witness in TIP but admittedly, the

                   petitioner has not been put on TIP as yet and except criminal

                   antecedent as well as the above-said confessional statement, there

                   is nothing against him.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                     Accordingly, petitioner named above, is directed to

                   be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs 10,000/- (ten

                   thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each in connection

                   with Aurangabad (M) P.S. Case No. 146 of 2014 to the

                   satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Aurangabad.

</p><p id="p_4">                                        (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
