Kurukshetra, ('the Tribunal', for brevity) vide award dated

03.03.2012.
</p>
<p id="p_2">           The learned counsel for the appellant contends that the

appellant was 28 year old at the time of accident and remained

<p id="p_4">           I have heard the learned counsel for the appellant and

perused the record.
</p>
<p id="p_5">           As per the record, the appellant aged 28 years at the

time of accident, suffered disability to the extent of 10% in the

accident in question. The amount towards disability and loss of

future income and amenities of life has been assessed at

Rs.20,000/- by the learned Tribunal, which in view of the age and

avocation of the appellant is inadequate. Therefore, another

Rs.25,000/- under this head would sufficiently meet the ends of

justice.
</p>
<p id="p_6">           Further, keeping in view the age, nature of injury and

period of hospitalization, this Court feels that another increase of

of Rs.15,000/- towards pain and suffering would meet the ends of

justice.
</p>
<p id="p_7">           In view of the above, the injured-appellant is held

entitled to the enhanced compensation of Rs.40,000/- [Rs.25,000/-

(enhancement towards disability and loss of future income and

amenities) + Rs.15,000/- (enhancement towards pain and
