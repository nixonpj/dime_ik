(S.R.)     Mr. Debjit Mukherjee
           Mr. P. Basu

<p id="p_1">                   The subject matter of challenge in the instant writ application is

           an order dated 28th July, 2015 passed by the First Appellate Authority.


</p><p id="p_2">                   Heard Mr. Munsi, learned advocate appearing for the petitioner

           and Mr. Kar, learned advocate appearing for the respondent.


</p><p id="p_3">                   The petitioner is directed to implead Shri Atanu Sengupta

           whose application under the provisions of the Right to <a href="/doc/1965344/" id="a_1">Information

           Act</a>, 2005 was heard and rejected by the petitioner herein.


</p><p id="p_4">                   The learned advocate on record of the petitioner is directed to

           carry out the necessary correction in the cause title of the writ

           application.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                   The petitioner is directed to serve a copy of the writ application

           upon the added respondent intimating that the matter will be taken up

           for further consideration on 25th August, 2015 and to file an affidavit

           of service on the returnable date.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                   List this matter for further consideration under the same

           heading in the Daily Supplementary List of this Court on 25th August,

           2015.
</p>
<p id="p_9">
</p><p id="p_10">                                                     (Tapabrata Chakraborty, J.)
