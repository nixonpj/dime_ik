	Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India, praying for the issuance of a Writ of mandamus to direct the respondents to appoint the petitioner as P.G.Assistant Commerce in any of the Government Higher Secondary Schools in Tamil Nadu by recruitment by transfer against 2% of the post reserved for ministerial staff.


		For Petitioner	  :  Mr.V.Selvaraj

		For Respondents 	  :  Mr.M.S.Ramesh
					     Additional Government Pleader 


O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	The petitioner seeks a direction to the respondents to promote him as Post Graduate Assistant in Commerce in any one of the  Government Higher Secondary Schools in Tamil Nadu by recruitment by transfer against 2% of the posts reserved for ministerial staff.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2.  Learned counsel for the petitioner fairly submitted that during the currency of the writ petition, the petitioner retired from service on attaining the age of superannuation.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.  In view of the subsequent development, culminating in the retirement of the petitioner, nothing survives in this writ petition.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4.  The writ petition is disposed of with the above observation.  No costs.  Consequently, the connected miscellaneous petition is closed.
</p>
