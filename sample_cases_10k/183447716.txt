<p id="p_5">                  Having heard the learned advocates for the parties and upon
             perusing the instant application, this Court directs the District
             Primary School Council, Purulia, to file a report in the form of an
             affidavit on the next date, stating therein specifically as to whether
             the said Council initiated any proceedings against the petitioner
             due to her prolonged absence from service for seven years prior to
             issuance of the dismissal order which is impugned in the instant
             proceeding.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                  Let this matter appear for further consideration under the
             same heading a fortnight hence.
