<div class="doc_title">Bhagwanta @ Bhagwan Singh And Anr vs State And Ors on 18 December, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No.1298 of 2009
(Suspension of Sentence)
In
S.B. Criminal Appeal No.1233 of 2009
Bhagwanta @ Bhagwan Singh &amp; Others Vs. State 

Date of Order      ::::      18/12/2009

Hon'ble Mr. Justice Dalip Singh

Mr. Yogesh Singhal, Counsel for the accused-appellants
Mr. Sanjeev Kumar Mahala, Public Prosecutor for State 
				    ***						
</pre><p id="p_1">		Heard learned counsel for the accused-appellants, learned Public Prosecutor for the State and also perused the relevant record as well as the judgment of the learned trial Court .
</p><p id="p_2">		Learned counsel for the accused-appellants has submitted that the sentence of imprisonment awarded to the accused-appellants by the learned trial Court has been suspended by the learned trial Court for a period of one month.	
</p><p id="p_3">		In the facts and circumstances of the present case, I deem it just and proper to suspend the sentence of imprisonment awarded to the accused-appellants during the pendency of this criminal appeal.
</p><p id="p_4">		Accordingly, the application for suspension of  sentence is allowed and it is ordered that the sentence passed by the learned trial Court against the accused-appellants namely; (i) Bhagwanta @ Bhagwan Singh son of late Shri Manphool, (ii) Gordhan son of late Shri Manphool; and (iii) Munshi son of Shri Dhooriya, in Session case No.11/2008 shall remain suspended till the final disposal of aforesaid criminal appeal, provided each of them furnish a personal bonds in the sum of Rs.20,000/- (rupees twenty thousand only), along with two sureties of Rs.10,000/- (rupees ten thousand only) each to the satisfaction of the learned trial Court for their appearance before this Court on 19.01.2010 and whenever called upon to do so.				
</p><p id="p_5">                                   (Dalip Singh), J.</p><pre id="pre_2">
   ashok/
