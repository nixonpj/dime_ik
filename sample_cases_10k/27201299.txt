        Counsel for the petitioner states that the petitioner would be

satisfied if the writ petition is disposed of with a direction to the

Director, Ayurveda Department, Ajmer to address the petitioner's

representation now to be made seeking reimbursement of his

expenses for the official tours by his personal vehicle particularly

when permission to use the personal vehicle for official tour had

also been granted by the department.

</p><p id="p_2">        In view of the above submissions made, the writ petition is

disposed of. In the event a representation is made by the

petitioner before the Director, Ayurveda Department, Ajmer,
                                (2 of 2)

ventilating his grievances, same shall be decided by a reasoned

order within a period of 6 weeks of its receipt. If aggrieved,

thereafter the petitioner shall be free to take his remedy suitable

to the dispute in accordance with law.

</p><p id="p_3">      The writ petition stands disposed of accordingly.
