                                           HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                                                       Crl. Misc. No.M-33597 of 2013
                                                       Date of Decision: December 12, 2013

                      Shabnam and another
                                                                                   ...Petitioners
                                                        Versus
                      State of Punjab and others

                                                           ...Respondents
                      CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AMOL RATTAN SINGH

                      Present:-      Mr. Nonish Kumar, Advocate
                                     for the petitioners.

                                      ***
</pre><p id="p_1">                      Amol Rattan Singh, J.(Oral)
                                In response to notice issued, Mr. Arshdeep Singh Kler,

                      AAG, Punjab, puts in appearance for the official respondents and has

                      filed the reply of the Deputy Superintendent of Police, Sub-Division

                      Sangrur, which is taken on record.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                     Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that presently

                      the petitioners are receiving no threat to their lives and liberty and , as

                      such, the present petition be disposed of as having been rendered

                      infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                     Ordered accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                     However, in case at any subsequent stage, the petitioners

                      apprehend any threat to their lives and liberty, they would be free to

                      approach the police authorities concerned, who will take appropriate

                      action and would grant due protection to the petitioners.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                                  (AMOL RATTAN SINGH)
                      December 12, 2013                                   JUDGE
