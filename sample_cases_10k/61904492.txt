02/ 09.10.2017</pre><p id="p_1">        The petitioner is challenging the Revised Results of the
          Preliminary Examination for Combined Civil Services-2016 conducted
          by the Jharkhand Public Service Commission.
</p><p id="p_2">                       Learned Counsel appearing for the petitioner submits
          that reservation policy has not been followed in proper perspective.
</p><p id="p_3">                       Mr. Sanjay Piprawal, learned Counsel appearing for the
          J.P.S.C. and Mr. H.K.Mehta appearing for the State vehemently
          opposes the contention of the learned Counsel for the petitioner and
          submits that on earlier occasion the same issue was raised in this
          Hon'ble   Court   by   filing   separate   writ   petition   being   W.P.
          (S)No.1864/2017 disposed of on 25.07.2017 with a direction to the
          respondents to publish a fresh result in terms of the circular of the
          State Government. J.P.S.C. after order of this Hon'ble Court has
          already published the results taking into consideration the revised
          circular of the State Government.
</p><p id="p_4">                       However, the respondents are directed to file counter-
          affidavit within a period of four weeks.
</p><p id="p_5">                       Let this case be listed after four weeks.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                     [Dr.S.N.Pathak,J.]
          P.K.S.
</p>
