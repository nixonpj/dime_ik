  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 16550 of 2011(P)


1. SIDHARTH T
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE UNIVERITY OF CALICUT
                       ...       Respondent

2. THE CONTROLLER OF EXAMINATIONS,

                For Petitioner  :SRI.T.MADHU

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice P.R.RAMACHANDRA MENON

 Dated :21/06/2011

 O R D E R
                   P.R. RAMACHANDRA MENON J.
                     ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
                    W.P (C) No . 16550 of 2011
                     ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
                    Dated, this the 21st June , 2011

                               JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">       The prayer of the petitioner is to direct the respondents to publish

the revaluation results of the 4th Semester Supplementary Examination

of the B.Tech course of the petitioner, as requested by him vide Ext. P1

</p><p id="p_2">       2.   Heard the learned standing counsel appearing for the

respondents as well, who submits that, a minimum time of eight weeks

is necessary to have the revaluation result published in respect of the

papers concerned.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       In the said circumstances, the Writ Petition is disposed of

directing the respondents to consider Ext. P1 application preferred by

the petitioner for revaluation and finalize the proceedings by publishing

the results, as expeditiously as possible, at any rate, within 'eight

weeks', from the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment, if the

application is otherwise in order.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                              Sd/-
</p>
<p id="p_5">                          P. R. RAMACHANDRA MENON, JUDGE
