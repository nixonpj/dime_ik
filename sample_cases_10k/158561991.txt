<p id="p_1">                            This writ petition has been filed seeking a direction to the

                      respondents to consider the petitioner's representation dated 18.08.2018

                      and pass orders.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

</p><p id="p_3">                            2. The grievance of the petitioner is that he is residing in

                      Lakkapuram Village, Modakkurichi Block, Erode District and       a sewage

                      canal is running along with the road in front of the petitioner's building.

                      Now, the sewage water drainage has been blocked and over flowing.

                      Hence, he made a representation to the authorities to clean the sewage.

                      But, so far no action has been taken. Hence, the present writ petition

                      has been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                            3</span>

</p><p id="p_5">                               3. I have heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner

                      and the learned Special Government Pleaders appearing for the

                      respondents and perused the materials available on record.



</p><p id="p_6">                               4. Considering the limited prayer sought in the writ petition, the

                      second respondent/Block Development Officer is directed to consider the

                      petitioner's representation dated 18.08.2018 and pass suitable orders on

                      merits and in accordance with law within a period of six weeks from the

                      date of receipt of a copy of this order.



</p><p id="p_7">                               5.With the above directions, the Writ Petition is disposed of. No

                      costs.
</p>
