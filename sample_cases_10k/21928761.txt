Hon'ble V.K. Bist, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">CLMA No.4942 of 2012 (Delay Condonation Application)


      Shri Vikas Budhwar and Shri Dinesh
Gahtori, learned counsel for the appellant.
</p><p id="p_2">      Shri    Ramji    Srivastava,   learned
counsel for the respondent.
</p><p id="p_3">      Heard learned counsel for the
parties.
</p><p id="p_4">      Office has reported that there is one-
day delay in filing the appeal.
</p><p id="p_5">      Office report is incorrect. The appeal
is within time. Same is treated to be filed
within time.
</p><p id="p_6">      Delay condonation application is,
accordingly, disposed of.
</p><p id="p_7">      List this appeal on 11.06.2012. On
that day, the counsel for the appellant will
inform the Court about the proposal for
unloading of petrol from the tank trucks.
</p><p id="p_8">      In the meantime, it is provided that
in case application is moved for
adjournment, the learned Court below
shall consider the application for grant of
adjournment till 11.06.2012 only.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                      (V.K. Bist, J.)
Arpan                                      23.05.2012
