          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

                    COMPANY PETITION NO. 164 of 2014

================================================================
         MUKESH NATVARLAL BRAHMBHATT....Petitioner(s)
                           Versus
     MALHOTRA STEEL ROLLING PRIVATE LIMITED....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
MR TUSHAR P HEMANI, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
MS VAIBHAVI K PARIKH, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
NOTICE UNSERVED for the Respondent(s) No. 1
================================================================

         CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N.V.ANJARIA

                              Date : 10/09/2014

                                  ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">             In this petition the respondent company is
not getting served with the Notice of this Court,
despite       best          possible      efforts.          Learned    advocate
Ms.Vaibhavi Parikh pointed out that the address of the
Registered        Office        of     the       company      Malhotra       Steel
Rolling     Private          Limited      is     at    Malhotra      Road,   Opp.
Ashish Cinema, Odhav, Ahmedabad-382 415. She further
stated     that      statutory         notice         was   served    upon    the
company on the very address and the said address is
also on the record of the Registrar of Companies.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             The above address being the last known and
registered address of the company's Registered Office,
let the service be effected by substituted mode of
service by affixing copy of notice at the address



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                       Page 1 of 2</span>
         O/COMP/164/2014                                         ORDER



under Rule 28 of the Company Courts Rules, 1959 read
with   Order      V       Rule   20(1)       of   the    Code     of    Civil
Procedure, 1908. The notice shall inter alia mention
that the matter shall be next posted on 08th October,
2014   before     the      Court    for      further     proceedings and
appropriate orders.
</p>
<p id="p_3">           The petitioner shall take necessary steps to
effect service of notice by affixing as above.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                        (N.V.ANJARIA, J.)
Anup
