<div class="doc_title">Jitendra Singh Marskole vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 25 October, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">       THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                 WP-16722-2017
       (JITENDRA SINGH MARSKOLE Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


Jabalpur, Dated : 25-10-2017
           Shri Pramod Singh Tomar, learned counsel for the
petitioner.
      Shri N.S. Chouhan, learned Govt. Advocate for the
respondents/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">       The petitioner has filed the present petition praying
for a direction to the respondent No.4 to consider and decide
the application preferred by the petitioner vide Annexure-
P/13.
</p><p id="p_2">          The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that
during the course of inquiry, the petitioner has submitted an
application Annexure-P/13 to the respondent No.4 raising
certain objections regarding pendency of departmental
enquiry. He, therefore, prays that the appropriate direction
may be issued to the respondent No.4 to consider and decide
the said application before proceeding with the departmental
enquiry.
</p><p id="p_3">      The learned Govt. Advocate appearing on behalf of the
respondents/State, on advance copy, has submitted that the
suitable action shall be taken on the application submitted by
the petitioner vide Annexure-P/13.
</p><p id="p_4">         In view of the aforesaid submissions made by the
learned counsel for the parties, the petition filed by the
petitioner is disposed of with a direction to the respondent
No.4 to consider and decide the application submitted by the
petitioner vide Annexure-P/13 in accordance with law before
 proceeding with the departmental enquiry within a period of
30 days' from the date of receipt of certified copy of this
order, by speaking order. It is made clear that this Court has
not expressed any opinion on the merits of the case.
</p><p id="p_5">          With the aforesaid observations, the writ petition is
disposed of with no order as to costs.
</p><p id="p_6">           Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                (MISS VANDANA KASREKAR)
                                          JUDGE




sp/-
</p>
