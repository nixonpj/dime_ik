CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE BIRENDRA KUMAR
ORAL JUDGMENT
Date: 24-08-2018

                   Heard learned counsel for the parties.

                   This is an appeal under <a href="/doc/50707649/" id="a_1">Section 14-A(2)</a> of the Scheduled

   Castes and <a href="/doc/25085007/" id="a_2">Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act</a>, 1989

   (hereinafter referred to as the "SC/<a href="/doc/53524/" id="a_3">ST Act</a>") against the refusal of

   prayer for anticipatory bail vide order dated 04.04.2018 in A.B.P. No.

   105 of 2018 passed by the learned 1st Additional Sessions Judge,

   Sheikhpura in connection with SC/ST Case No. 05 of 2018 arising out

   of Sheikhpura (Mahila) P.S. Case No. 01 of 2018 registered under

   <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_4">Sections 354</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_5">354B</a> of the Indian Penal Code, <a href="/doc/1973522/" id="a_6">Section 08</a> of the

   Protection of Children from Sexual Offenses Act, 2012 as well as

   Sections 2(1)(w) of the SC/<a href="/doc/53524/" id="a_7">ST Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                   Considering the nature of allegation in the FIR against

   the appellant, in my view, this is not a fit case for grant of anticipatory
         Patna High Court CR. APP (SJ) No.1553 o f 2018 dt.24-08-2018

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                   2/2</span>




                bail.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                  Accordingly, this appeal against the refusal of prayer for

                anticipatory bail stands dismissed.

</p><p id="p_8">                                                                       (Birendra Kumar, J)
     Kundan/-
