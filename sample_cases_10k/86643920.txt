<div class="doc_title">Sunil vs State Of Haryana on 12 October, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT
              CHANDIGARH

                           Crl. Misc. No.M-27629 of 2012(O&amp;M)
                           Date of Decision:.12.10.2012

Sunil
                                                       ... Petitioner
                           vs.

State of Haryana
                                                     ... Respondent

CORAM:HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJENDER SINGH MALIK

Present:- Mr. Vikram Singh, Advocate
          for the petitioner.

            Mr. Anupam Sharma, AAG Haryana.
            Mr. V.K. Jindal, Advocate for the complainant.
                  ---
</pre>
<p id="p_1">VIJENDER SINGH MALIK,J(ORAL)

            Sunil, the petitioner seeks regular bail in a case

registered by way of FIR No. 311 dated 3.5.2012 at Police

Station Civil Lines, Karnal, for an offence punishable under

<a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_1">sections 148</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_2">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_4">325</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_5">341</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_6">506</a> IPC read with <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_7">section

149</a> IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

Jagdish @ Jagga, a co-accused of the petitioner who is identically

placed to the petitioner has been granted bail by this court vide

orders dated 31.8.2012 passed in Crl. Misc. No. M-18570 of 2012.

According to him, for this reason, the petitioner is entitled to bail

on the ground of parity.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            Learned State counsel as well as learned counsel for

the complainant do not dispute the fact that the case against the

petitioner and Jagdish is identical.
</p><p id="p_4"> Crl. Misc. No.M-27629 of 2012                    =2=



            In these circumstances, on the ground of parity, the

petitioner is entitled to bail. Therefore, the petition is allowed and

the petitioner is ordered to be released on bail on his furnishing a

personal bond in a sum of Rs.30,000/- with one surety in the like

amount to the satisfaction of learned trial court.</p><pre id="pre_2">



October 12,2012                (VIJENDER SINGH MALIK )
Jiten                                  JUDGE
