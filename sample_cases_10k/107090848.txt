<div class="doc_title">Md. Manjur Alam &amp; Anr vs The State Of West Bengal &amp; Ors on 25 March, 2014</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Biswanath Somadder</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                                        1


    103
25.03.2014
    pg.
                        WP No. 4815 (W) of 2014
                         Md. Manjur Alam &amp; Anr.
                                    Vs.
                      The State of West Bengal &amp; Ors.


                     Mr. Jahangir Alam
                     Mr. Madhusudan Sur
                                ... For the petitioners

                     Mr. Anami Sikdar
                     Ms. Tanima Sengupta
                                ... For the State


                     Having heard the learned advocates for the parties and

             upon perusing the instant application as well as the report in

             the form of an affidavit, it appears that the petitioners have

             approached this Court being aggrieved by the inaction on the

             part of the concerned respondent authorities to engage them as</pre>

<p id="p_1">             Social Audit Volunteers for Kharba Gram Panchayat, situated in

             the district of Malda.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                     From the report in the form of an affidavit filed on behalf

             of the Block Development Officer, Chanchal-I Development

             Block, it appears that preparation of panel wherein the

             petitioners'   name      appear   was   made   by   the   concerned

             respondent authority without following any government order

             and/or norms for selection. Subsequently, the District Nodal

             Officer, MGNREGS, Malda, directed the local Block Development
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                          2</span>


Officer to follow the guidelines of the Principal Secretary,

Government of West Bengal, Panchayats and Rural Development

Department, vide Government Order dated 28th October, 2013,

in its true letter and spirit so far as it relates to the selection of

Social Audit Volunteers. However, from the report, it does not

appear as to how a fresh selection process was initiated in

respect of Kharba Gram Panchayat since the report focuses only

on Kaligram Gram Panchayat.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">        As such, let this matter stand adjourned for a fortnight

so that the learned advocate for the State can obtain necessary

clarification from the concerned Block Development Officer as to

what happened in respect of the selection process of Social

Audit Volunteers for Kharba Gram Panchayat, situated in the

district of Malda.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                       (Biswanath Somadder, J.)
