<p id="p_1">             The petitioners make a grievance that even though the matter

has been argued and short order supplied, detailed order is not forth coming

even though they have applied for a copy of the order.

</p><p id="p_2">             However, we find that the petitioners have not approached the

concerned authorities in the first instance. Therefore, we do not intend to

interfere in the writ proceedings but grant liberty to the petitioners to make a

representation to the competent authority apprising him of their grievance

within a period of one week from today. In case such a representation is

filed, the competent authority would look into it and take appropriate

decision thereon.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Needless to say that looking at the nature of the request made,

the expeditious disposal would be a natural expectation.

</p><p id="p_4">             Disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_2">
