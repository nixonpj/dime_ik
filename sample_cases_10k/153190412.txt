<div class="doc_title">M/S Century Machines And Others vs Allahabad Bank on 27 March, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                               AT CHANDIGARH

135                                                 Civil Writ Petition No.8243 of 2019
                                                    Decided on : 27.03.2019

M/s Century Machines (Partnership Firm) and others
                                                                          . . . Petitioners
                                          Versus
Allahabad Bank
                                                                        . . . Respondent

CORAM:          HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AJAY KUMAR MITTAL
                HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE MANJARI NEHRU KAUL

Present:        Mr. Manuj Nagrath, Advocate for the petitioners.
                                             ****

AJAY KUMAR MITTAL, J. (Oral)
</pre><p id="p_1">                The challenge in this petition filed under Articles 226/227 of the
Constitution of India, is to the demand notices dated 12.12.2016, 20.04.2017
(Annexures P-1 &amp; P-4 respectively) and notices dated 04.03.2017 and
21.06.2017 (Annexures P-3 &amp; P-5 respectively) issued under Sections 13(2) and
13(4) of the Securitization and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and
Enforcement of Securities Interest Act, 2002 as well as to the auction notice
dated 02.02.2019 (Annexure P-11) issued by the respondent-Bank vide which
the properties of the petitioners were put on sale.
</p><p id="p_2">2.              After arguing for sometime, learned counsel for the petitioners
submitted that he may be allowed to withdraw the present writ petition with liberty
to the petitioners either to approach the respondent-Bank or take recourse to the
alternative remedies as may be available to them, in accordance with law.
</p><p id="p_3">3.              Dismissed as withdrawn. It shall, however, be open to the petitioners
to take recourse to the remedies as may be available to them, in accordance with
law.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                         (AJAY KUMAR MITTAL)
                                                                JUDGE




                                                        (MANJARI NEHRU KAUL)
March 27, 2019                                                 JUDGE
sonia gugnani
