<p id="p_1">(BY S.S.BADAWADAGI, ADV.)

      THIS MFA IS FILED UNDER SECTION 173(1) OF THE MOTOR
VEHICLES ACT, 1988 AGAINST THE JUDGMENT AND AWARD DATED
27.06.2011 PASSED IN MVC NO.753/2010 ON THE FILE OF THE
PRESIDING OFFICER, FAST TRACK COURT-II &amp; MEMBER ADDL. MACT,
BELGAUM,    PARTLY   ALLOWING   THE   CLAIM  PETITION  FOR
COMPENSATION AND SEEKING ENHANCEMENT OF COMPENSATION.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   2</span>




<p id="p_2">     THIS MFA COMING FOR CONCILIATION BEFORE LOK ADALAT
AFTER BEING REFERRED BY THE COURT, THE FOLLOWING
CONCILIATION ORDER IS PASSED:
</p>
<p id="p_3">                     CONCILIATION ORDER

        The learned counsel for the appellant, the learned counsel

for the respondent are present.
</p>

<p id="p_4">        2. After due deliberations in the matter, the parties have

arrived at a settlement.    The respondent has agreed to pay a

sum of Rs.75,000/- without interest, in addition to the amount

already awarded by the Tribunal. The appellant has agreed to

receive the said amount in full and final settlement of the claim.
</p>
<p id="p_5">A Joint memo has been filed to that effect.      Accordingly, the

appeal stands disposed of. The entire amount to be released in

favour of the claimant.
</p>

<p id="p_6">        3. Draw up the award accordingly.
</p>
