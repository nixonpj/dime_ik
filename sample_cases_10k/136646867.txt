<p id="p_1">    rrc
(Allowed)
                                        C.R.M. 6711 of 2017
             In Re:- An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the
             Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973.

             In the matter of : Abantika Mukherjee @ Chakraborty
                                                             ...        petitioner
             Mr. Sabyasachi Chatterjee
             Md. A. Ansari
             Mr. Pintu Karar
                                                    .....For the petitioner

             Mr. Rudradipta Nandy
                                                    ......For the State


                   Apprehending arrest in course of investigation of A.J.C. Bose

             B. Garden Police Station F.I.R. No. 861 of 2017 dated 11th June,

             2017 under <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_2">Sections 325</a>/<a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>/<a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_4">406</a>/<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_5">420</a>/<a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_6">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_7">34</a> of the Indian Penal

             Code read with <a href="/doc/902835/" id="a_8">Sections 23</a> and <a href="/doc/902835/" id="a_9">24</a> of the Maintenance and Welfare

             of Parents and Senior Citizen Act, 2007, the petitioner has applied

             for anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                   We have heard learned advocates for the parties and perused

             the materials in the case diary. Having regard to the level of

             complicity of the petitioner, as revealed therefrom, we are of the

             considered opinion that her custodial interrogation is not necessary

             for taking the investigation to its logical conclusion; hence, she is

             entitled to direction as prayed for.

</p><p id="p_3">                   The application, thus, stands allowed with the direction that

             in the event of arrest, the petitioner shall be released on bail upon

             furnishing bond of Rs.5,000/-, to the satisfaction of the arresting
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              2</span>


officer and also subject to the conditions as laid down in sub-section

(2) of <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_10">Section 438</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.


</p><p id="p_4">                                            (Dipankar Datta, J.)


                                           (Debi Prosad Dey, J.)
