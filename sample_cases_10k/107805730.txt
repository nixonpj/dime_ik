<p id="p_1">2   13-08-2019                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                   respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               The grievance of the petitioner in the present writ

                   application is indecisiveness on the part of the respondent

                   Aryabhatta Knowledge University in the matter of grant of

                   recognition to the institution.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

                   case of the petitioner institution was considered by the State
 Patna High Court CWJC No.16366 of 2019(2) dt.13-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           2/3</span>




          Government and vide order as contained in Memo No. 67(6)

          dated 17.01.2019 decision was taken for grant of recognition for

          B.Sc. Nursing with 60 intake. The letter was also communicated

          to the Registrar of the Aryabhatta Knowledge University for

          information and necessary action. Unfortunately, for the last

          seven months, no decision was taken by the Aryabhatta

          Knowledge University, which compelled the petitioner to

          approach this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                      The Aryabhatta Knowledge University is affiliating

          University but due to the indecisiveness on the part of the

          University the matter of affiliation/recognition of the institution,

          in question, is pending notwithstanding granting recognition by

          the appropriate authority including the Directorate, Health

          Department, Government of Bihar.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                      Mr. Arun Kumar, learned counsel for the petitioner

          submits that in addition to the communication of the order dated

          17.01.2019 by the Directorate, Health Department, Government

          of Bihar, the petitioner has also applied in prescribed formal

          after depositing the fee, but the matter is pending at the level of

          the Aryabhatta Knowledge University.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                      Considering the aforesaid, the writ application is

          disposed of with a direction to the Aryabhatta Knowledge
          Patna High Court CWJC No.16366 of 2019(2) dt.13-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    3/3</span>




                   University to take appropriate decision in the matter of grant of

                   recognition of the petitioner institution for B.Sc. Nursing within

                   a   maximum         period      of     30   days   from   the   date   of

                   receipt/production of a copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_7">                               With the aforesaid, this application stands disposed of.



</p><p id="p_8">                                         (Anil Kumar Upadhyay, J)
uday/-
</p>
