<p id="p_1">            At the outset learned State counsel has stated that refund

of Rs. 75,000/- has been paid to the petitioner and the writ petition

has been rendered infructuous. However, learned counsel for the

petitioner states that interest as contemplated by Section 12(3) of the

Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, on the delayed payment of

refund, has not been paid. Accordingly, we direct the respondents to

calculate the interest and pay the same to the petitioner within a
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> C.W.P. No. 4233 of 2009                                       2</span>




period of two weeks from today.
</p>
