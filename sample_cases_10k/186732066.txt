<div class="doc_title">Vishwamitra Nonia @ Vishwamitra ... vs The State Of Bihar on 24 September, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.33920 of 2014
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -161 Year- 2012 Thana -PAKARIBARAW District- NAWADA
                 ======================================================
                 1. Vishwamitra Nonia @ Vishwamitra Noniya S/o Sadhu Nonia Resident of
                 Village Silaur, P.S. Pakaribarawan, District Nawada.

                                                                              .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                                  .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Krishna Deo Raj, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Anita Kumari(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ASHWANI KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   24-09-2014                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The prayer for bail of the petitioner has already been

                 rejected earlier by this Court on 7th February, 2014 in Cr. Misc.

                 No. 53889 of 2013. He has renewed his prayer for bail. The matter

                 is under trial for the charge under Section 304B read with 34<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of

                 the Indian Penal Code</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 According to the prosecution case, the wife of the

                 petitioner was killed in her matrimonial home within seven years

                 of her marriage for non-fulfillment of demand of dowry. From the

                 record, it appears that witnesses are not turning up for examination

                 in course of trial.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Having regard to the nature of allegation as well as
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.33920 of 2014 (2) dt.24-09-2014                    2</span>




                    facts and circumstances of the case, I am not inclined to grant bail

                    to the petitioner Vishwamitra Nonia @ Vishwamitra Noniya.

                    Accordingly, his prayer for bail is rejected.

</p><p id="p_5">                                     However, the prosecution is directed to produce

                    witnesses expeditiously. The court below is directed to take all

                    possible steps to conclude the trial without further delay.


</p><p id="p_6">                                             (Ashwani Kumar Singh, J.)
KKSINHA/-
</p>
