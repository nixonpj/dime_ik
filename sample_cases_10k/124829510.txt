<div class="doc_title">Anita Pal (Maity) vs The State Of Jharkhand &amp; Anr on 3 October, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">         IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI

                               Cr. Revision No. 148 of 2001
         Anita Pal (Maity)                         ...... Petitioner
                                  Versus
         The State of Jharkhand &amp; Anr.             .........Opposite Parties

         CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
                                   ---------
</pre><p id="p_1">         For the Petitioner :Mrs. J. Mazumdar, Advocate
         For the State        :A.P.P
                      ........
</p><p id="p_2">         11/Dated: 03/10/2018
                It appears that under order dated 22.06.2018 explanation was called from the court of

         S.D.J.M, Chaibasa as to whether what circumstances, the required report          from his end,

         whereas a report has been directly submitted to this Court by the O/C Goelker Police Station.

</p><p id="p_3">                Perused the explanation dated 04.07.2018 submitted by Sri Rajesh Kumar-II, S.D.J.M,

         Porahat at Chaibasa in which it has been mentioned that an explanation/show cause was called

         from the Office Clerk, Uday Shankar Prasad, who has submitted reply mentioning therein that

         due to heavy work pressure he could not put up the direction of Hon'ble High Court before the

         then P.O.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                It appears that the explanation report is not satisfactory.

                Under these circumstance, let a copy of this order be transmitted to the Court of learned

         Principal District &amp; Sessions Judge, Chaibasa and learned Principal District &amp; Sessions Judge,

         Chaibasa is directed to personally look into the matter and hold enquiry thereafter, initiate

         departmental proceeding against the erring staff/officer and submit report to this Court on or

         before 12.02.2019.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                It appears that this application has been filed on 26.04.2001 and under order dated

         25.09.2001, this application has been admitted for hearing and L.C.R was called for, which has

         been received.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                Though, the counsel for the O.P. No. 2 has appeared by filing vakalatnama but today

         nobody appeared on behalf of O.P. NO. 2.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                Let a copy of this order be communicated to the court concerned and to the court of

         Principal District &amp; Sessions Judge, Chaibasa through FAX for compliance.



</p><pre id="pre_2">Satyarthi/-                                                          (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
 </pre>
