<div class="doc_title">Sayed Faroq Ali vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 20 June, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                                 Writ Petition No.5047/2014
20.6.2014
                   Shri     Naman            Nagrath,        Senior     Counsel
            assisted by Shri Nikhil Tiwari, counsel for the
            petitioner.
                   Shri P.K. Kaurav, Deputy Advocate General
            for respondents/ State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                   Shri     M.K.         Agrawal,        counsel       for     the
            interveners.
</p><p id="p_2">                   Shri S.A. Wakil, counsel for Respondent
            No.4.
</p><p id="p_3">                   Counsel for the State on the instructions,
            submits that notwithstanding the pendency of
            the review petition, the State is in the process
            of complying with the directions given by this
            Court as reiterated in the order dated 8.5.2014.
            That    statement           is    placed    on     record.         The
            consequence of not complying with the direction
            before the time specified in the order dated
            8.5.2014 can be considered on the next date.
</p><p id="p_4">                   In view of the aforesaid statement, we have
            no     hesitation       in       observing       that   the      State
            Government cannot take excuse of pendency of
            review petition and inform us on the next date
            that    final   decision          cannot    be    taken     on    that
            account.         In     other       words,       irrespective        of
            pendency        of    the        review    petition,      the    State
            Government           shall       proceed    in    the     matter     in
            conformity with             the directions given            by     this
            Court as reiterated in the order dated 8.5.2014
 and report compliance in that behalf on the next
date.
</p><p id="p_5">        Stand over till 1.7.2014.
</p><p id="p_6">        To be listed under caption "Directions".
        The intervention application shall be taken
along with the leading matter on that day.</p><pre id="pre_2">


        (A.M.Khanwilkar)            (Alok Aradhe)
         Chief Justice                 Judge
