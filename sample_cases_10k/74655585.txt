           It is reported that during the pendency of this application, charge 
sheet is filed.
</p>

<p id="p_3">           In   view   of   the   statement   made   by   Mr.N.J.Shah,   learned   APP 
appearing   for   the   respondent   -   State,   the   learned   advocate   for   the 
applicants   seeks   permission   to   withdraw   the   present   application   with 
liberty to file appropriate application before the Trial Court. Permission, 
as   prayed   for,   is   granted.   The   present   application   is   disposed   of   as 
withdrawn. 
</p>

<p id="p_4">           It   is   made   clear   that   the   present   application   is   not   decided   on 
merits and the trial Court shall decide the application on its own merits.
</p>
