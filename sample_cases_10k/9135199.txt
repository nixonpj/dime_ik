<div class="doc_title">Kishore Balwani vs Usman Khan on 15 October, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                           CRA-237-2010
                     (KISHORE BALWANI Vs USMAN KHAN)


15-10-2015
Shri K. L. Gupta, counsel for the appellant.
None for the respondent no.3.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Learned counsel for the appellant prays for some more time to submit
the service report of the notice issued to the respondent no.2.
It appears that on request of the appellant humdust notice was given of
the respondent no.2 for service whereas, the Police has given a report
that the respondent no.2 has vacated the premises shown as his
address in the cause title of the appeal and new address is not known.
If Humdast notice is given to the appellant then it should be returned
served or unserved along with an affidavit. One opportunity was given
on 28.9.2015 to get the report of that Humdast notice issued to the
respondent no.2.
</p><p id="p_2">Under these circumstances, it appears that the appellant could not
serve the notice of this case to respondent no.2. The appellant is
directed to submit the present correct address of the respondent no.2
within two weeks and thereafter, steps will be taken for issuing notice
to the respondent no.2.
</p><p id="p_3">Case be again listed after four weeks.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                          (N.K. GUPTA)
                                                             JUDGE
