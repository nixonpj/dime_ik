<div class="doc_title">An Application For Anticipatory ... vs Biswanath Ghosh &amp; Ors on 5 June, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                        1


   91 05.06.2018
   ss Rejected
                            C.R.M. 3309 of 2018


In the matter of : An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">section 438</a> of
the Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 04.06.2018 in connection with
Bharatpur P.S. Case No. 49 of 2018 dated 03.05.2018 under <a href="/doc/1328656/" id="a_2">sections
325</a>/<a href="/doc/1264755/" id="a_3">326</a>/<a href="/doc/647828/" id="a_4">307</a>/<a href="/doc/387648/" id="a_5">34</a></pre><p id="p_1"> of<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_6"> the Indian Penal Code</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                      And

In the matter of : Biswanath Ghosh &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                ... ... petitioners
Mr. Manas Kumar Das
                                                        ... ... for the petitioners
Mr. Arijit Ganguly,
Mr. Sanjib Kr. Dan
                                                              ... ... for the State

       Having considered the materials in the case diary prima facie

disclosing involvement of the petitioners in assaulting of the victim

resulting in grievous injury and hospitalization, we are of the opinion that

this is not a fit case to grant anticipatory bail to the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_4">       The application for anticipatory bail is, thus, rejected.



(Ravi Krishan Kapur, J.)                    (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
