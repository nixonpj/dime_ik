IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED: 26.05.2016
CORAM:
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE B.GOKULDAS
Crl.O.P.No.11058 of 2016

N.Veera Kumar 						Petitioner 
Vs
1.State Rep. By its
Commissioner of Police,
Coimbatore City,
Coimbatore

2. State rep. By its
The Inspector of Police (L&amp;O)
Kuniamuthur Police Station,
Coimbatore  						...Respondents 

Prayer:- Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C to direct the  2nd respondent to register the petitioner's complaint dated 30.04.2016 and consequently direct the 2nd respondent to investigate and file final report within the time fixed by this Court.

		For Petitioner	: 	Mr.G.Ramar

		For Respondents	: 	Mr.K.Prabakar 
						Government Advocate (Crl.Side)

O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	The petitioner has come forward with this petition to direct the 2nd respondent to register the petitioner's complaint dated 30.04.2016 and consequently to investigate and file the final report within the time fixed by this Court.
B.GOKULDAS,J.,
ssd
</p><p id="p_2">	2.Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the learned Government Advocate (Crl.Side) appearing for the respondent-police and perused the materials placed on record.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. Though the learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the petitioner has lodged a complaint before the 2nd respondent police on 30.04.2016, there is no proof of acknowledgement to that effect has been annexed in the typed set of papers. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">	In view of the same, no direction can be given, as sought for, by the petitioner.  Hence the petition is dismissed.
26.05.2016
ssd/kal				
To				
</p><p id="p_5">1.State Rep. By its
Commissioner of Police,
Salem city, Salem.
</p><p id="p_6">2. State rep. By its
The Inspector of Police (L&amp;O)
Azhagarpuram Police Station,
Salem.	
</p>
<p id="p_7">3. The Public Prosecutor,
High court, Madras.
</p><p id="p_8">Crl.O.P.No.11058 of 2016
