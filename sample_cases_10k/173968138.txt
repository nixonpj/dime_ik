                                                           ORDER

<p id="p_1">                             This Criminal Original Petition has been filed seeking a direction to direct

                      the first respondent to transfer the investigation in Crime No.68 of 2014 on the

                      file of the third respondent police to the fifth respondent and file final report

                      before the concerned Court.
</p>


<p id="p_2">                             2. The learned counsel for the petitioner would submit that the mother of

                      the petitioner was residing at Paul Street, Neyoor, Kanyakumari District with two

                      servants. On 31.01.2015 she was found murdered. Further the accused persons

                      have stolen jewels and therefore it is a murder for gain. On the complaint given

                      by the petitioner, the third respondent registered a case in Crime No.68 of2014

                      for the offences under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Sections 302</a> and <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_3">380</a> of IPC. Thereafter the third

                      respondent did not take any steps to secure the accused persons and as such

                      the petitioner filed direction petition before this Court in Crl.O.P(MD) No.19530 of


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      2/6</span>

http://www.judis.nic.in
                                                                                 Crl.O.P.(MD)No.3806 of 2018


                      2015 and this Court by an order dated 09.10.2015 directed the third respondent

                      to file final report as expeditiously as possible. Even then the third respondent

                      did not conclude the investigation and filed final report. Hence he has filed the

                      present petition.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                              3. The learned Government Advocate(Crl.Side) filed the status report and

                      submitted that so far they have examined some of the witnesses and also

                      deputed Assistant Superintendent of Police, Kolachel to               monitor the

                      investigation. Further special team were formed by three Sub Inspector of Police

                      to investigate the murder case and investigation is under progress, but

                      unfortunately no useful clues could be obtained from the investigation.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                              4. Though the third respondent conducted investigation, till today they

                      could not find out the real accused persons. Under such circumstances fresh

                      investigation may be ordered to find out the real accused persons in the murder

                      case.    Therefore the investigation in crime No.68 of 2014 pending on the file of

                      the third respondent is hereby withdrawn and the same is transferred to the file

                      of the fifth respondent for fresh investigation.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                              5. The third respondent is directed to hand over the entire case records

                      to the fifth respondent forthwith and on receipt of the same, the fifth respondent


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                      3/6</span>

http://www.judis.nic.in
                                                                                  Crl.O.P.(MD)No.3806 of 2018


                      is directed to complete the investigation and file final report before the

                      concerned jurisdictional Magistrate within a period of twelve weeks from the date

                      of receipt of case records from the third respondent.
</p>


<p id="p_6">                             6. With the above direction, the Criminal Original Petition is disposed of.
</p>
