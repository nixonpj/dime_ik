</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 04-02-2019
       Heard Mr. AT Sarkar, learned counsel for the petitioner and Mr. B Gogoi, learned
       counsel for National Health Mission (NHM).
         Mr. Gogoi, learned counsel for the NHM submits on the basis of instruction that as per
interpretation of NHM, respondent No.4 fulfills the eligibility criteria.

</p><p id="p_2">         Be that as it may, let notice be issued, returnable 2 weeks.

         Mr. Gogoi, learned counsel accepts notice on behalf of respondent Nos.1, 2 and 3.
Extra copies within 3 days.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         Mr. Gogoi further submits that Court may direct service of notice upon respondent
No.4 through the office of the Mission Director, NHM since Mission Director has the particulars
of respondent No.4 as an agreement has been entered into by the Mission Director with
respondent No.4.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         In that view of the matter, let respondent No.4 be served through the office of
respondent No.3 whereafter respondent No.3 may file affidavit of service.

</p><p id="p_5">         Till the next date, interim order passed on 30.01.2019 shall continue.

         By the next date, respondents shall file affidavit(s).

         List on 20.02.2019.
</p>
