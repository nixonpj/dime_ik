<div class="doc_title">Raju @ Rajkumar Sugnomal ... vs Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation ... on 9 May, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">           C/SCA/7253/2014                                            ORDER




     IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
               SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION  NO. 7253 of 2014

=============================================
</pre><p id="p_1">      RAJU @ RAJKUMAR SUGNOMAL MAHERCHANDAI....Petitioner(s)
                             Versus
       AHMEDABAD MUNICIPAL CORPORATION  &amp;  1....Respondent(s)
=============================================
Appearance:
</p><p id="p_2">MR P P MAJMUDAR, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
=============================================

           CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI
 
                                Date : 09/05/2014
 
                                   ORAL ORDER


</p><p id="p_3">1.     By way of the present petition under Articles 14, 19(1)(g), 21 and 
       226   of   the   Constitution   of   India,   the   petitioner   has   prayed   as 
       under:
</p>
<p id="p_4">      "[A] Your   Lordships   may   be   pleased   to   issue   a   writ   of 
              mandamus or a writ in the nature of mandamus or 
              any   other   appropriate   writ,   order   or   directions 
              directing   the   respondent   -   Corporation   to 
              expeditiously   hear   and   decide   application   dated 
              19.08.2013 made by the petitioner (at Annexure - A 
              hereto)

       [B]    During the pendency and final disposal of the present 
              petition, Your Lordships may be pleased to direct the 
              respondent authorities not to take any coercive action 
              with   regard   to   the   unauthorized   construction,   for 
              which   application   dated   19.08.2013   made   by   the 
              petitioner (at Annexure - A hereto);
</p>


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                       Page 1 of 2</span>
<p id="p_5">                  C/SCA/7253/2014                                          ORDER




</p><p id="p_6">2.         Mr.P.P.Majmudar, learned advocate appearing for the petitioner 
           would   submit   that   though   the   petitioner   has   made 
           representation   /   application   to   the     respondent   authority   to 
           consider   the   representation   /   application   for   regularizing   the 
           unauthorized   construction,   the   concerned   authority   has   not 
           decided the same, therefore, the authority be directed to decide 
           the representation / application as expeditiously as possible.
</p>
<p id="p_7">3.         Having heard learned advocate for the petitioner and considering 
           the facts of the case, I am of the opinion that the following order 
           would meet the end of justice:
</p>
<p id="p_8">           [a]      The   concerned   authority   is   hereby   directed   to   decide   the 
                    representation / application dated 19.08.2013 made by the 
                    petitioner  as expeditiously  as possible  preferably within  a 
                    period of six months from the date of receipt of this order, 
                    on its own merits and in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_9">4.         It is hereby made clear that this Court has not gone into the merits 
           of the case.
</p>
<p id="p_10">5.         In view of the above direction, the present petition is disposed of. 
</p><p id="p_11">           Direct service is permitted.
</p>

<p id="p_12">                                                                          (A.J.DESAI, J.) 
*Kazi...
</p>
