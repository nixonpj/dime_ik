<div class="doc_title">Anup Tiwari @ Anup Kumar Tiwari vs The State Of Bihar on 4 March, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.4665 of 2013
                 ======================================================
                 Anup Tiwari @ Anup Kumar Tiwari son of Sri Raghav Tiwari
                                                                   .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                Versus
                 The State Of Bihar
                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                                          ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   04-03-2013                   Petitioner is apprehending his arrest in a case

                 registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_6">325</a>, <a href="/doc/1440821/" id="a_7">332</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_8">333</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_9">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1243353/" id="a_10">342</a>, <a href="/doc/1208971/" id="a_11">353</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_12">379</a>, <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_13">427</a> of the

                 I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 It is alleged that one lady was being kept captive

                 in a room of the school since she was suspected to be child

                 lifter and when the informant and other police officials came

                 there then mob pelted stones and made assault.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioner that petitioner was not named in the F.I.R. and his

                 name subsequently sprang up as one of the instigators and at

                 earlier point of time the police jeep crushed two people and in

                 order to save their skin, the present case has been lodged.

</p><p id="p_4">                                 Considering the same, let the petitioner, above

                 named, in the event of his arrest or surrender before the Court
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4665 of 2013 (2) dt.04-03-2013</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                     below within a period of 12 weeks from today, be released on

                     anticipatory bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-(ten

                     thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                     satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Siwan in

                     connection with Basantpur P.S. Case No. 158 of 2011.




</p><p id="p_5">                                               (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
