<div class="doc_title">Sushma Santosh Patel vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 22 June, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                         WP-11440-2016
         (SUSHMA SANTOSH PATEL Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


22-06-2017

       Shri Abhijeet A. Awasthi, learned counsel for the
petitioners.
       Ms. Surabhi Nigam, learned P.L. for the State.
       Shri Samdarshi Tiwari, learned Additional Advocate
General for the respondents No.2 to 4.</pre>
<p id="p_1">       During course of arguments it has been pointed out that
against the order dated 27-5-2014, Annexure-P/6 in the present
writ petition passed by the Deputy Director, M.P. State
Agriculture Marketing Board, Bhopal as well as against
subsequent demands various writ petitions have been filed by
different firms, forming the subject-matter of W.P.
Nos.4362/2016, 7743/2016, 4363/2016, 8512/2016 and W.P.
No.21790/2015 which are pending.
</p><p id="p_2">       This Court is of the considered view that the present writ
petition requires to be heard and decided along with the
aforementioned writ petitions, as in the present writ petition
subsequent action and order after the Annexure-P/6, dated
27-5-2014 is under challenge.
</p><p id="p_3">       List this matter along with the aforementioned writ
petitions on 13-7-2017.
</p><p id="p_4">       In the meanwhile counsel appearing for the respondents
No.2 to 4 may complete the pleadings in the other connected writ
petitions also.
</p><p id="p_5">       Counsel for the petitioners submits that after supercession
of the Mandi Committee, bye-election is going to be held on
 25-6-2017. Counsel for the petitioners will have the liberty to
address this Court on the impact and effect of the bye-election in
the final outcome of the present writ petition, at the time of
hearing of the case on the date fixed.
</p>


<p id="p_6">                                     (VIJAY KUMAR SHUKLA)
                                             JUDGE
