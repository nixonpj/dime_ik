<div class="doc_title">Markanday Rai &amp; Ors vs State Of Bihar &amp; Ors on 4 October, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 CR. WJC No.845 of 2008
                                MARKANDAY RAI &amp; ORS
                                           Versus
                                STATE OF BIHAR &amp; ORS
                                         -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">7.   04.10.2010.              Petitioners have prayed for quashing F.I.R. of Kargahar

                   (Sidhi) Police Station Case No. 93 of 2008 registered u/s 25(1-B)A

                   and 35 of othe <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_1">Arms Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              On 25.12.2007 petitioner no.2 lodged police case being

                   Kargahar (Sidhi) Police Station Case No. 196 of 2007 u/s 25(1-

                   B)A, 26 and 35 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_2">Arms Act</a> alleging that on 24.12.2007 he

                   along with other petitioners were going to their house, 4-5 persons

                   who were hiding nearby fired upon them which missed and upon

                   hulla co-villagers gathered and one person, namely, Bir Bahadur

                   Kharwar @ Biru Kahar was caught with country made Katta along

                   with two misfired cartridges. As such,    Kargahar (Sidhi) Police

                   Station Case No. 196 of 2007 was instituted.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

                   Superintendent of Police, Rohtas supervised the case and found the

                   same to be true against accused persons. However, the IO on

                   30.6.2008 submitted final report no. 22/08 showing the entire case

                   false on the direction of the Deputy Inspector General of Police,

                   Sahabad Range. Initiation of criminal proceedings u/s 182 and 211

                   <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">I.P.C</a>. was also recommended. Thereafter, the IO instituted fresh

                   F.I.R. against the petitioners for the occurrence dated 2.7.2008

                   bearing Kargahar (Sidhi) Police Station Case No. 93 of 2008 u/s
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                2</span>




          25(1-B)A, 35 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_4">Arms Act</a>. It is relevant to mention that on

          26.12.2007, a counter case was also registered against the petitioners

          as Kargahar (Sidhi) Police Station Case No. 197 of 2007. Petitioner

          no. 2 has also filed a protest petition in Kargahar (Sidhi) Police

          Station Case No. 196 of 2007.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                      Contention of the petitioner is that the IO could have

          filed charge sheet against the petitioner in Kargahar (Sidhi) Police

          Station Case No. 196 of 2007 or even in the counter case namely,

          Kargahar (Sidhi) Police Station Case No. 197 of 2007 lodged by Bir

          Bahadur Kharwar @ Biru Kahar itself, instead of filing a fresh case.

</p><p id="p_5">                      It appears that the protest petition filed by the petitioner

          no.2 is pending before the Magistrate, where final report has been

          submitted. In the view of the matter, this Court directs the

          Magistrate concerned to dispose of the protest petition filed by the

          petitioners in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><pre id="pre_2">Shashi.                            ( Samarendra Pratap Singh, J.)
