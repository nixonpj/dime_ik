</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">43</span><p id="p_2">
as
(Rejected)
                                 C.R.M. 6541 of 2019

              In Re:- An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of
              the Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 22.07.2019 in connection
              with Kaliachak P. S. Case No.358 of 2008 dated 08.10.2008 under
              <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

              In the matter of : Md. Badshah Sekh &amp; Anr.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                              ... Petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_4">              Ms. Minoti Gomes,
              Mr. Kalidas Saha.
</p><p id="p_5">                                                             ...for the Petitioners.
</p><p id="p_6">              Mr. Rana Mukherjee,
              Ms. Sukanya Bhattacharyya.
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                  .....for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
                   Heard the learned Advocates appearing for the parties.

                   Having considered the materials on record prima facie

              disclosing involvement of the petitioners in murdering the victim

              and as he had absconded for more than a decade and in view of

              the gravity of the offence and the conduct of the petitioners, we are

              of the opinion that this not a fit case to grant anticipatory bail to

              the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                   Accordingly, the prayer for anticipatory bail of the petitioners

              is rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                   However, it is open to the petitioners to appear before the

              court below and pray for regular bail within fortnight from date

              and in the event, they appear before the court below within the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                          2</span>


time frame as aforesaid, their prayer for regular bail shall be

considered independently and in accordance with law without

being swayed by any observation made by this Court in this order.

</p><p id="p_11">     The application, being C.R.M.6541 of 2019, is disposed of.



(Manojit Mandal,J.)                 (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
 </p>
