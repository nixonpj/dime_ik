<div class="doc_title">State Of Haryana vs Nasir Ahmed on 23 September, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">Criminal Appeal No.383-DBA of 2001                                        1


        IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT
                        CHANDIGARH.



                                          DATE OF DECISION:-23.9.2009



State of Haryana                                         ...APPELLANT



                                     VERSUS


Nasir Ahmed                                              ...RESPONDENT



CORAM:- HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE MEHTAB S.GILL
        HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE JITENDRA CHAUHAN


PRESENT:-Mr.S.S.Randhawa, Additional Advocate General, Haryana.
            Mr.Sukhwinder Singh Kamboj, Advocate for the respondent.
MEHTAB S.GILL, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            Prosecution case is that Shiv Kumar, father of the prosecutrix

got recorded FIR that her daughter Pushpa was getting treatment from

respondent-Nasir Ahmed, as she suffered from fits. Respondent gave her

medicines. On 7.3.1999 at about 9 A.M. grand-father of the prosecutrix took

her to respondent-Nasir Ahmed. Respondent then again called prosecutrix

on 14.3.1999 at 9 A.M. When both the prosecutrix and her grand-father

returned after taking medicine from the respondent, prosecutrix started

crying and told her father and grand-father that she had been raped by Nasir

Ahmed and that he had threatened her not to tell this to any one, otherwise

she will be physically harmed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Prosecution to prove its case examined Dr.Ramesh Kumar as
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Criminal Appeal No.383-DBA of 2001                                        2</span>


PW1, HC Dayal Chand as PW2, ASI Sunder Singh as PW3, Surinder

Mohan Patwari as PW4, C.Balbir Singh as PW5, Dr.SS Punia as PW6,

Dr.Shanta Gupta as PW7, SI Jai Singh as PW8, Pushpa Rani as PW9, Shiv

Kumar as PW10, Prabhu Dayal as PW11, ASI Harbhajan Singh as PW12,

HC Rameshwar Dass as PW13, C.Jai Pal as PW14 and C.Chamela Ram as

PW15.
</p>
<p id="p_3">              Respondent also brought into the witness box Ram Singh

DW1, Hanif DW2 and Jebu Nissa DW3.
</p>
<p id="p_4">              Learned counsel for the State has argued that rape had been

committed on a minor and Dr.Shanta Gupta PW7, as per the medical report

has stated that the rape could have been committed on the prosecutrix. Other

prosecution witnesses have proved the case of the prosecution.

</p><p id="p_5">              Learned counsel for the respondent has argued that going

through the statements of the prosecutrix and Dr.Shanta Gupta PW7, it

comes out that rape could not have been committed on the prosecutrix.

</p><p id="p_6">              We have heard the learned counsel for the parties and perused

the record.
</p>
<p id="p_7">              The prosecutrix appeared as PW9. She has stated in her

statement that when rape was committed by the respondent on her, some

ladies were there. Her grand-father was sitting outside the hut. She did not

narrate anything to her grand father after coming out from the hut.

Dr.Shanta Gupta PW7 has stated after medically examining the prosecutrix

that the possibility of prosecutrix being habitual to sexual intercourse

cannot be ruled out.
</p>
<p id="p_8">              Prosecutrix could not have been raped with ladies sitting

outside the hut where rape was being committed. The prosecutrix would
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> Criminal Appeal No.383-DBA of 2001                                         3</span>


have shouted and cried.
</p>
<p id="p_9">            We do not find any infirmity in the judgment of the learned trial

Court.
</p>
<p id="p_10">            Appeal is dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_11">

</p><p id="p_12">                                                  (MEHTAB S.GILL)
                                                       JUDGE




                                               (JITENDRA CHAUHAN)
                                                        JUDGE
September 23,2009
