<p id="p_1">                          (Order of the Court was made by S.S.SUNDAR, J.)


                          This Writ Petition is filed for issuance of a Writ of

                 Mandamus directing the respondents 1 to 5 to remove the Ave Ice

                 Cream Kiosk put up by the sixth respondent over the storm water

                 channel falling along the road margin of entire bye-pass road

                 (SH75A) on the stretch of Vannarpettai area, Tirunelveli and

                 remove all other obstructions made on the above said storm water

                 channel on the entire road margin of said bye-pass (SH75A)

                 situated at Vannarpettai area, Tirunelveli City and restore the entire

                 stretch of storm water channel to its original position, so as to
http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                      3</span>

                 ensure free water flow and also take appropriate action against the

                 responsible persons and officers etc., within the stipulated time.



</p><p id="p_2">                            2.The   grievance   of   the   petitioner   is   that the   sixth

                 respondent has put up some obstructions in the water channel

                 which is going along the road margin of the bye-pass (State

                 Highways 75A) situate at Vannarpettai area, Tirunelveli City. The

                 petitioner has also annexed some photographs which would show

                 that a channel has been obstructed by dumbing sand to fill up the

                 channel.     A bunk shop has been put up after the channel is

                 completely filled upto the level of ground.



</p><p id="p_3">                            3.In the above circumstances, the first respondent District

                 Collector, Tirunelveli, is directed to ensure that the encroachment

                 by obstruction of water channel to be removed forthwith after

                 holding a preliminary enquiry as to the existence of water channel.

                 The encroachment appears to be a recent one.                  In case, the

                 obstruction is only in the water channel, the District Collector,

                 Tirunelveli and the revenue officials are directed to remove the

                 obstruction forthwith and report before this Court on or before

                 23.04.2019. If it is a land of State Highways, then this direction will

                 be read as a direction to respondents 4 and 5 who may report
http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    4</span>

                 compliance of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                          4.With the above direction, this Writ Petition is disposed of.

                 No costs.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                          5.Post the matter for reporting compliance on 24.04.2019.
