                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.18102 of 2013
                 ======================================================
                 Rakesh Pandey son of Sanjay Pandey
                                                                   .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                Versus
                 The State Of Bihar
                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                                      ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   16-05-2013                 Petitioner is apprehending his arrest in a case

                 registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_2">379</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               It is alleged that the informant fixed the marriage of

                 his sister with the petitioner but subsequently the petitioner

                 refused and then petitioner called the informant and kept his

                 motorcycle.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

                 that the accusation has not been supported by any of the

                 independent witnesses.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  Considering the aforesaid facts, let the petitioner,

                 above named, in the event of his arrest or surrender before the

                 Court below within a period of 12 weeks from today, be

                 released on anticipatory bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                 10,000/-(ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.18102 of 2013 (2) dt.16-05-2013</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                     each to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                     Jehanabad in connection with Kelar P.S. Case No. 06 of 2013,

                     subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_4">Section 438(2)</a> of

                     the Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                               (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
