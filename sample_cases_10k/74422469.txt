         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

        R/SPECIAL CRIMINAL APPLICATION NO. 6881 of 2019

==========================================================
MINABEN D/O NANJIBHAI LIMBANI W/O PRAVINKUMAR KARSHANBHAI
                           PATEL
                           Versus
                    STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
HCLS COMMITTEE(4998) for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MS AMI N BHATT(3372) for the Applicant(s) No. 1
for the Respondent(s) No. 2
PUBLIC PROSECUTOR(2) for the Respondent(s) No. 1
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.H.VORA

                           Date : 28/06/2019

                            ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.   By way of this petition under Articles 226 and 227 of the
Constitution of India, the petitioner has prayed for following
relief as prayed for in para 9(b):-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">          "That the Hon'ble Court may be pleased to direct the
          respondent No.2 Husband to obey the order passed by
          the Ld. Lower Court in Criminal Misc. Application
          No.1907 of 2016 order dated 10/9/2018 passed by Ld. 3rd
          Family Court, Ahmedabad. And to pay the petitioner all
          the arrears of maintenance which are pending according
          to the order and of her rights."
</blockquote>

<p id="p_2">2.   Having heard the submissions made at bar and looking
to the nature of prayer, as prayed for, by the petitioner
herein, the petitioner can very well avail the remedy u/s
125(3) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 and therefore,
proceedings initiated before this Court is not maintainable



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               Page 1 of 2</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   Downloaded on : Sun Jun 30 01:35:46 IST 2019</span>
           R/SCR.A/6881/2019                              ORDER




and therefore, it is hereby rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3.     However, the petitioner is at liberty to move appropriate
application for recovery of arrears of maintenance amount
from the respondent husband in accordance with provisions of
section 125(3) of the Code.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4.     It is clarified that this Court has not examined the merits
of the present petition.
</p>
