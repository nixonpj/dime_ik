<div class="doc_title">Lallan Rai vs The State Of Bihar on 24 March, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              Cr.Misc. No.8102 of 2011
                                     LALLAN RAI
                                          Versus
                               THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                        -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2.   24.3.2011                 Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               On search of the house of the petitioner, two

                 country made pistols were found kept inside the bed spread over

                 in a room. The pistols were found loaded with cartridges also.

                 The arms were seized and the petitioner was remanded in the

                 case.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               The plea of the petitioner is that he was ill and in

                 support of his contention he has enclosed a certificate granted by

                 a local Doctor.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               The Court does not find any reason for false

                 implication of the petitioner. Finding that the recovery of the

                 arms was made from the petitioner, the prayer for bail is

                 dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               However, if the trial is not concluded within one

                 year from the date the petitioner was remanded in Bakhtiarpur

                 P.S.case No. 205 of 2010, the court below, i.e., the Additional

                 Chief Judicial Magistrate, Barh, Patna, shall release the

                 petitioner on execution of a bond to his own satisfaction in the

                 above noted case.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><pre id="pre_2">        Kanth                                        ( Dharnidhar Jha, J.)
 </pre>
