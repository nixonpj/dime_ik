<div class="doc_title">Rajkishori Khakha vs Human Resources Department on 31 August, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                              W.P. (S) No. 4201 of 2012.
                                               ---
                  Rajkishori Khakha       ...  ...   ... ... ...           ...        Petitioner

                                                 Versus

                  The State of Jharkhand and others ... ...      ...   ...            Respondents
                                                ---
                  CORAM:      HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ALOK SINGH
                                                ---
                  For the Petitioner     : Mr. O.P. Tiwari, Advocate.
                  For the Respondents : Mr. Ajit Kumar, J.C. to G.P. IV.
                                               ---

02. 31.08.2012</pre><p id="p_1">.         The grievance of the petitioner is that her husband Imanuel Khakha
                  was working as Teacher in the Primary School, Sarangloya, Lapung,
                  Ranchi, which is a aided minority school, who has expired on 18.12.2011.
                  However, till date, family pension has neither been fixed nor has been
                  released in favour of the petitioner. The further grievance of the
                  petitioner is that in view of the death of the husband of the petitioner,
                  who died in harness, her son Biraj Khakha should be given compassionate
                  appointment.
</p><p id="p_2">                        Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that although a
                  representation      was   moved   on    24.05.2012,     however,        a   detailed
                  representation shall be moved before respondent no. 4 within 30 days
                  from today.
</p><p id="p_3">                        Mr. Ajit Kumar, J.C. to G.P. IV, appearing on behalf of the State-
                  respondents, submits that if such a representation is moved, respondent
                  no. 4-District Education Officer, Ranchi shall examine as to whether the
                  school wherein husband of the petitioner was working is aided school and
                  shall pass order thereon within 60 days in accordance with law. Learned
                  counsel   further    submits   that    on   the   question     of   compassionate
                  appointment of the son of the petitioner, respondent no. 3 shall examine
                  all the Rules and Regulations and shall proceed in accordance with law.
</p><p id="p_4">                        The present writ petition is disposed of with the direction that the
                  petitioner shall move a detailed representation within 30 days from today
                  before respondent no. 4-District Education Officer, Ranchi. On receiving
                  the representation, respondent no. 4 shall take decision thereon in
                  accordance with law within 60 days positively. Any monetary amount
                  found due and payable to the petitioner shall be paid to the petitioner
                  within 60 days thereafter positively.
</p><p id="p_5">                        On the question of compassionate appointment, respondent no. 4
                  shall examine all the aspects and shall proceed in accordance with law.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                                           (Alok Singh, J.)


    AKS.Cp.2.
</p>
