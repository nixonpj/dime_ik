<p id="p_6">CC No.193/17                                                     1/3
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                       2</span>

</p><p id="p_7">  1. A fresh complaint was filed in this case against the accused 
     namely Balbir Singh, Veena Dhama and Jitender Tyagi before 
     this   court   on   14.10.2016   for   offence   punishable   U/s 
     135/138/150   of   the   Electricity   Act,   2003   for   taking   action 
     against the accused persons as per law.                             
</p><p id="p_8">  2. In   pre­summoning   evidence,   CW1   Sh.   Mukesh   Sharma   and 
     CW2 Sh. Indra Pal Singh examined themselves and thereafter 
     the   remaining   pre­summoning   evidence   of   the   complainant 
     company was closed.         
</p><p id="p_9">  3. After perusal of the complaint and other documents relied upon 
     in support of the complaint supported with evidence of CW1 &amp; 
     CW2 respectively, a cognizance was taken against the accused 
     persons   for   the   offence   punishable   U/s   135/138/150   of   the 
     Electricity Act, 2003 and the accused was directed to appear 
     before this court to face the trial of this case.  
</p><p id="p_10">  4. It   was   stated   by   Sh.   Mukesh   Sharma,   A.R.   the   complainant 
     company before this court today i.e on 01.09.2017 that matter 
     has   been   amicably   settled   between   the   parties   through   Lok 
     Adalat   held   on   14.01.2017   as   per   terms   and   conditions 
     contained   in   the   order   of   concerned   Lok   Adalat   dated 
     14.01.2017 and as such he wanted to withdraw this case against 
     the accused persons.
</p>



<p id="p_11">CC No.193/17                                                                2/3
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                         3</span>

</p><p id="p_12">  5. Statement   of   Sh.   Mukesh   Sharma,   A.R.   of   the   complainant 
     company   was   recorded   today   separately   regarding   the 
     settlement to the above effect.  
</p><p id="p_13">  6. In   view   of   the   amicable   settlement   arrived   at   between   the 
     parties   and   in   view   of   the   above   mentioned   statement   of 
     Sh. Mukesh Sharma, A.R. of the complainant company, there is 
     no need now to proceed further in this case against the accused 
     persons  and the  matter   is  hereby dismissed  as  settled giving 
     liberty to the complainant company to file execution petition 
     against   the   accused   persons   in  future   for   recovery   of   settled 
     amount, if the accused makes default in payment of the same in 
     future,   if   any.     With   this   order,   all   the   accused   are   hereby 
     acquitted in this case. Bail bond and surety bond of accused, if 
     any, is discharged / cancelled.  Judicial file be consigned to the 
     record room as per rules after necessary compliance.        
</p>
