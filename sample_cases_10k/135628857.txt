<p id="p_1">  (PER
: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.P.BHATT)
 


 


 


</p><p id="p_2">1.		The present
application is filed through jail seeking temporary bail for a period
of 30 days for the purpose of attending medical treatment and surgery
of his brother, which is planned on 22.05.2011.  In support of the
application, medical certificate issued by CMO, Govind-Prabha Arogya
Sankul dated 11.05.2011 is annexed.
</p> 

 


 


<p id="p_3">1.1		The applicant has
been arrested in connection with Inquiry Case No.91/2007 of Rander
Police Station for the alleged offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1317063/" id="a_1">Sections 465</a>,
<a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_2">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_4">471</a>, <a href="/doc/1667388/" id="a_5">419</a>, <a href="/doc/1128948/" id="a_6">120(B)</a>, <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_7">114</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.  The
applicant has been arrested on 24.09.2009 and since then, he is in
jail.
</p> 

 


 


<p id="p_4">2.		 RULE. 
</p><p id="p_5">Mr.L.B.Dabhi, learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives service of
Rule on behalf of the respondent-State.
</p> 

 


 


<p id="p_6">3.		For the contents of
the application, the application is partly allowed.  The applicant
is ordered to be released on temporary bail for a period of two (2)
weeks from the date of his release, on his executing a personal
bond of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand Only) to the satisfaction of
the Jail authorities.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_7">4.		The applicant shall
surrender to the Jail authorities on expiry of the temporary bail
period.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_8">5.		Rule is made
absolute. 
</p>
 

<p id="p_9">		Direct
service is permitted.
</p> 


 


 

 


 


					
 

							
 

<p id="p_10">(P.P.Bhatt,
J.)
