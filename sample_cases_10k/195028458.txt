<div class="doc_title">Rungati Jha @ Runi Jha vs The State Of Bihar on 19 January, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                        Criminal Miscellaneous No.551 of 2012
                    Rungati Jha @ Runi Jha, son of Binodanand Jha
                                           Versus
                                   The State Of Bihar
                              ----------------------------------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">02.   19.01.2012           Petitioner is languishing in custody since

                   11.11.2011 in a case registered for the offences

                   under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_2">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_5">325</a>, <a href="/doc/1402213/" id="a_6">337</a>, <a href="/doc/1721129/" id="a_7">338</a>,

                   <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_8">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1183069/" id="a_9">386</a>, <a href="/doc/1208971/" id="a_10">353</a> of the I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                           It is alleged that accused inside Lakhisarai

                   jail, for non-fulfilment of the extortion demand the

                   contractor   and   two   security   personnel    were

                   assaulted.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                           It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                   petitioner that the accusation is general against

                   accused kept in jail and the fact is that the accused

                   inside jail were protesting against the irregularities

                   committed by the jail authorities, when the present

                   case has been lodged and other similarly situated

                   accused persons have been granted bail by this

                   Court vide Cr. Misc. No. 44205 of 2011.

</p><p id="p_4">                           Considering the aforesaid facts, let the

                   petitioner above named, be released on bail on

                   furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-(ten thousand)

                   with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                   satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate,
           Lakhisarai in connection with Kabaiya(Lakhisarai)

          P.S. Case No. 432 of 2011.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><pre id="pre_2">Shageer                            ( Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
 </pre>
