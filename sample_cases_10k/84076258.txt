                                                        ORDER

<p id="p_1">                             The relief sought for the in the present writ petition is to direct

                      the third respondent to comply with the order of the first respondent

                      dated 22.11.2011.
</p>


<p id="p_2">                             2. The petitioner-in-person appeared and made a submission

                      that in respect of his complaint, no enquiry was conducted by the

                      Tahsildar regarding the transfer of patta. The application filed under

                      the Right to <a href="/doc/1965344/" id="a_2">Information Act</a>, in this regard, also was not responded.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                      The Tahsildar has to conduct an enquiry and provide information to the

                      writ petitioner.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                             3.    The   learned    Additional   Government   Pleader   made    a

                      submission that the enquiry was conducted and accordingly, an order

                      was passed on 14.11.2019, by the Tahsildar, Cheyyur. The writ

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      2/4</span>


http://www.judis.nic.in
                                                                           W.P.No.22892 of 2019

                      petitioner has also participated in the process of enquiry, regarding the

                      transfer of patta and the enquiry was conducted under the Tamil Nadu

                      Patta Pass Book Act, 1983.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                             4. In view of the fact that the second respondent, Tahsildar

                      conducted an enquiry and passed an order in proceeding dated

                      14.11.2019 and the said copy of the order is served to the writ

                      petitioner today, this Court is of an opinion that it is left open to the

                      writ petitioner to redress his grievances, if any exists, in a manner

                      known to law.
</p>


<p id="p_6">                             5. With the above observation, the writ petition stands disposed

                      of. No costs.
</p>
