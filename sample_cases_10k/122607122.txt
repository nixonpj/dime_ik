      THIS CP IS FILED UNDER SECTION 24 OF CPC PRAYING
TO ORDER THAT THE CASE IN M.C.NO.2189/2008 PENDING
BEFORE THE FIRST ADDITIONAL PRINCIPAL JUDGE, FAMILY
COURT AT BANGALORE BE WITHDRAWN AND TRANSFERRED

                               -2-

                            ORDER

<p id="p_1">H.G.RAMESH, J. (Oral):
</p>
<p id="p_2">         This petition is by the wife seeking transfer of the

proceeding in M.C.No.2189/2008, pending before the

I Additional Principal Judge, Family Court, Bangalore,

to the Family Court, Shimoga.


</p><p id="p_3">2.       I have heard the learned Counsel for the

petitioner and perused the averments made in the

petition. The respondent though served with the notice

has remained unrepresented.


</p><p id="p_4">3.       It is stated that the petitioner is residing in

Shimoga and it will cause great hardship to her to

travel     to   Bangalore   to   attend   to   the   aforesaid

proceeding filed by the respondent-husband for grant

of a decree of divorce.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">4.       Having regard to the hardship that is likely to be

caused to the petitioner, I deem it appropriate to
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                           -3-</span>
                                       C.P.No.236/2011



transfer the aforesaid proceeding to the Family Court,

Shimoga. Ordered accordingly.

</p><p id="p_7">      Petition allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">                                       Sd/-
</p><p id="p_10">                                     JUDGE

Yn/ata.
