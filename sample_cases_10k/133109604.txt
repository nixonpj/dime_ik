<div class="doc_title">Sarvesh Pal Singh vs Executive Engineer First ... on 16 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">      IN THE HIGH COURT OF UTTARAKHAND AT
                    NAINITAL

            Writ Petition No.3028 of 2016 (M/S)

Sarvesh Pal Singh                                    ......Petitioner
                               Versus

Executive Engineer &amp; another                        ....Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">Mr Arvind Kumar Sharma, Advocate for the petitioner.
Mr. Lalit Samant, Brief Holder for the State of Uttarakhand/
respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                                Dated:16.11.2016


Hon'ble V.K. Bist J.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Present     petition    has     been    filed     for   the
following reliefs:-
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">      "i     Issue a writ, order or direction in the nature
             of mandamus to direct the respondents
             authorities to make payment of `3,47,727/- to
             the petitioner as he has completed his work.
      ii.    Issue a writ, order or direction in the nature
             of mandamus to direct the respondents
             authorities to make payment of `3,47,727/- to
             the petitioner with 18% interest."
</blockquote>
<p id="p_4">2.           On 31.05.2016, this Court has passed an
order in Writ Petition No.1487 of 2016. The order was
passed in following manner:-
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_2">             "It has been stated that the work was granted
             to the petitioner for civil work, the details of
             which are given in Paras 3 and 4 of the writ
             petition."
</blockquote>
<p id="p_5">3.           Submission        of   learned     counsel        for   the
petitioner is that in spite of the several requests and
reminders, payment is not being made.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                2</span>




<p id="p_6">4.         While    deciding   the   writ   petition     at   the
admission stage itself regarding the payment of dues,
the following order is passed, with the consent of learned
counsel for the parties:-
</p><p id="p_7">           The     respondents     are   directed   to    make
payment of the admitted dues, if any, to the petitioner
within a period of three months from the date of
production of certified copy of this order.
</p>

<p id="p_8">5.         Writ petition stands disposed of.
</p>



<p id="p_9">                                               (V.K.Bist, J.)
                                                16.11.2016
Arti
