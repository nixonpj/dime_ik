<div class="doc_title">Thaan Singh vs State Of Uttarakhand on 19 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">BA1 No.977 of 2017
Hon'ble V.K. Bist, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Ms. Vandana Singh, Advocate for
the applicant.
</p><p id="p_2">      Mr.      J.S.      Virk,     Assistant
Government Advocate for the State of
Uttarakhand.
</p><p id="p_3">      Heard learned counsel for the
parties.
</p><p id="p_4">      This is first bail application moved
by the applicant seeking regular bail in
F.I.R. No. 200 of 2016, under <a href="/doc/704277/" id="a_1">Section
8</a>/<a href="/doc/919170/" id="a_2">20</a> N.D.P.S. Act and under <a href="/doc/1440821/" id="a_3">Section
332</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1672685/" id="a_4">352</a> I.P.C., registered at Police
Station Vanbhulpura, Haldwani.
</p><p id="p_5">      Learned counsel for the applicant
submitted that the applicant has falsely
been implicated in the instant crime;
has no criminal history and is
languishing in jail since 24.12.2016.
She submitted that there is no
independent witness of the alleged
recovery. She submitted the alleged
recovery of Charas from the applicant
is   152     gram, which         is below
commercial        quantity.    She      also
submitted that the co-accused, namely,
Sahib @ Manpreet Singh @ Jasmeet
Singh has already been enlarged on
bail by the coordinate Bench of this
Court on 24.03.2017 and this is case of
parity. She further submitted that now-
a-days still cameras and video cameras
are available; but, despite this fact,
videography of the alleged recovery
showing the involvement of the
applicant has not been done. Learned
 counsel for the applicant submitted
that the applicant is ready to furnish
reliable and verified sureties to the
satisfaction     of   the     Magistrate
concerned, if he is directed to be
enlarged on bail.
</p><p id="p_6">      Considering the entire conspectus
of things, a case of bail is made out in
favour of the applicant on the ground of
parity.
</p><p id="p_7">      The     bail    application     is,
accordingly, allowed.
</p><p id="p_8">     Let the applicant Than Singh be
released on bail, on executing
personal bond and furnishing two
reliable sureties, each of the like
amount, to the satisfaction of
Magistrate concerned.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                           (V.K. Bist, J.)
Arpan                           19.07.2017
 </pre>
