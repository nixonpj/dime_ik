<div class="doc_title">Sanjay @ Natiya vs State Of U.P. And Another on 6 December, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Manju Rani Chauhan</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 76
 

 
Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 42547 of 2019
 

 
Applicant :- Sanjay @ Natiya
 
Opposite Party :- State of U.P. and Another
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Mithilesh Kumar Shukla,Avanish Kumar Shukla
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.
 

 
Hon'ble Mrs. Manju Rani Chauhan,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">1. Heard Mr. Mithlesh Kumar Shukla and Mr. Avanish Kumar Shukla, learned counsel for the applicant and learned A.G.A. for the State.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">2. The present application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. has been filed to quash the impugned order dated 1st February, 2013 passed by the Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate, Court No.6, Agra in Criminal Case No. 51 of 2011 (State Vs. U.P. Vs. Sanjay @ Natiya) arising out of Case Crime No. 135 of 2009, under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_2">Sections 363</a>/<a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_3">366</a> I.P.C., Police Station-Jagdishpura, District-Agra as well as the entire proceedings of the aforesaid criminal case. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">3. Before considering the present application on merits by the Court, Mr. Prashant Kumar, learned A.G.A. has raised preliminary objection to the maintainability of the present application by submitting that this is the second application filed by the applicant under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. for quashing of the aforesaid criminal case. Though in paragraph-10 of the affidavit accompanying the present application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., it has been stated that the applicant had earlier approached this Court by means of Criminal Misc. Application U/s 482 <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_6">Cr.P.C</a>. No. 14064 of 2012 (Smt. Sonam &amp; Another Vs. State of U.P. &amp; Another), which has been disposed vide order dated 26th April, 2012, a copy of which has been enclosed as Annexure-4 to the affidavit filed in support of the present application, but in paragraph no.1, it has been stated that this is the First Criminal Misc. Application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_7">Section 484</a> Cr.P.C. on behalf of the above named applicant before this Hon'ble Court for the same relief. In view of the aforesaid, the learned A.G.A. submits that this second application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_8">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. is not maintainable and is liable to be dismissed. 
</p> 
<p id="p_4">4. In the first application filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_9">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. by the applicant, on 26th April, 2012, following order has been passed by a Coordinate Bench of this Court:
</p> 
<p id="p_5">"Heard learned counsel for the applicants and learned A.G.A. for the State. 
</p> 
<p id="p_6">The applicant, through this application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_10">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., has invoked the inherent jurisdiction of this Court with a prayer to quash the proceedings of the Case No. 51 of 2011, State Vs. Sanjay @ Natiya, arising out of Case Crime No. 135 of 2009, under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_11">Sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_12">366</a> I.P.C., P.S. Jagdishpura, district Agra pending in the court of A.C.J.M VIth, Agra. 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">It is contended that the impugned criminal prosecution has been launched against the applicant no. 2 at the behest of opposite party no. 2 on account of his sister Km. Sonam, having married the applicant no. 2, Sanjay @ Natiya against his wish. 
</p> 
<p id="p_8">It is further contended that Km. Sonam has married the applicant no. 2, Sanjay @ Natiya on her own accord and is living with him as his wife and bless with a child, yet the Investigating Officer has illegally submitted chargesheet against the applicant no. 2 without recording the statement of the prosecutrix, Km. Sonam either under Section 161 or 164 Cr.P.C. 
</p> 
<p id="p_9">In this view of the matter it is directed that the applicants shall produce the prosecutrix Km. Sonam before the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Agra within a period of four weeks from today, the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Agra shall upon proper identification get her medically examined by C.M.O., Agra. Whereafter, he shall record her statement under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_13">Section 164</a> Cr.P.C. in the presence of the Invesigating Officer and decide the question of her custody after affording opportunity of hearing to the opposite party no.2. The entire exercise indicated hereinabove shall be completed by the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Agra within a period of four weeks from the date of production of the prosecutrix Km. Sonam before him. 
</p> 
<p id="p_10">For a period of ten weeks from today, no coercive action shall be taken against the applicants in the aforementioned case. 
</p> 
<p id="p_11">Subject to the aforesaid directions, the application is finally disposed of. "
</p> 
<p id="p_12">5. In view of the aforesaid, this second bail application for quashing of the same criminal case is not maintainable and is dismissed accordingly. 
</p> 

 
<p id="p_13">(Manju Rani Chauhan, J.)
 
Order Date :- 6.12.2019
 
Sushil/-
</p>
