<pre id="pre_1">                           -1-




IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA AT BANGALORE

        DATED THIS THE 3rd DAY OF MAY 2013

                       BEFORE

 THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.N.NAGAMOHAN DAS

        CRIMINAL PETITION NO.2675/2013

BETWEEN :

Suran Kumar,
S/o.Madappa,
Aged about 32 years,
R/at I Stage,
Gayathripuram,
Mysore - 570 019.                     ...PETITIONER

     (By Sri.L.Sudharshan, Adv.)

AND :

State of Karnataka,
By Krishnaraja Police Station,
Represented by SPP,
High Court, Bangalore - 1.           ...RESPONDENT

     (By Sri.G.M.Srinivasa Reddy, Adv.)
                       . . . .

      This Criminal petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a>
Cr.P.C. praying to enlarge the petitioner on bail in the
event of his arrest in Crime No.146/2010 of Krishnaraja
P.S., Mysore City, for the offences punishable under
<a href="/doc/664789/" id="a_2">Section 406</a>, <a href="/doc/1099980/" id="a_3">420</a> read with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">Section 34</a> of IPC.

      This criminal petition coming on for orders, this
day, the Court made the following:
                                  -2-




                            ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">         The   petitioner   is    accused         No.4    in   Crime

No.146/2010 for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/664789/" id="a_5">Section

406</a> and <a href="/doc/1099980/" id="a_6">420</a> read with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_7">Section 34</a> of IPC.


</p><p id="p_2">         Investigation is completed and charge sheet is

filed.    This Court in Criminal Petition No.1727/2013

vide order dated 5th April, 2013 enlarged accused Nos. 1

to 3 on bail. On parity, this petitioner is also entitled

for bail. Accordingly, the following :

</p><p id="p_3">                            ORDER

         The petition is hereby allowed. The petitioner is

enlarged on bail on the following conditions:-

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">               i)   In   the     event     of     respondent-police

                    arresting the petitioner, he shall be

                    enlarged     on      bail    on   executing    a

                    personal       bond         for   a    sum    of

                    0Rs.25,000/- to the satisfaction of the

                    arresting authority;
</blockquote><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                -3-</span><blockquote id="blockquote_2">




</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">           ii)    Whenever the       I.O. calls upon the

                  petitioner, he shall be present before

                  him;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">           iii)   The petitioner shall co-operate with the

                  IO     for     completion     of   further

                  investigation and in no manner shall

                  tamper with the prosecution witnesses.

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_5">           iv)    On all hearing dates the petitioner

                  shall keep present before the Trial

                  Court.</blockquote><p id="p_4">


</p><p id="p_5">      Violation of any one of the conditions above will

result in cancellation of bail order. Ordered accordingly.




</p><p id="p_6">                                         Sd/-
</p><p id="p_7">                                       JUDGE




SPS
 </p>
