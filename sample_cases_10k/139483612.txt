<div class="doc_title">Raju Sharma vs State Of Raj &amp; Ors on 8 September, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
BENCH AT JAIPUR
O R D E R

D. B. Habeas Corpus Petition No. 107/2010

Raju Sharma Vs State of Rajasthan &amp; Others.

08.09.2010


HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE NARENDRA KUMAR JAIN
HON'BLE DR. JUSTICE MRS. MEENA V. GOMBER  



Mr. Raju Sharma, petitioner-detenu present in person.
Mr. Sanjay Sharma, SHO PS Mansarover, Jaipur, present in person.
Mrs. Param Jyoti, S.P.(Legal), Crime Branch, CID(C.B.), Rajasthan, Jaipur, present in person.   
		
		Since the detenu Raju Sharma is present in person and he is no more in illegal detention of the respondents, therefore, we do not intend to continue this habeas corpus petition and the same is, accordingly, dismissed.</pre>
<p id="p_1">		However, it will be open for the        detenu-Raju Sharma to seek any other remedy with regard to his so-called illegal detention, which has been denied by the respondents in the Court, in accordance with law. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">(MEENA V. GOMBER),J.   (NARENDRA KUMAR JAIN),J.                                 
</p><p id="p_3">     Manoj
     Item No.3.
