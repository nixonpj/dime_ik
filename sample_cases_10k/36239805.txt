<div class="doc_title">Smt Anita &amp; Anr vs State &amp; Ors on 17 November, 2017</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
             S.B. Criminal Misc(Pet.) No. 4099 / 2017
</p><p id="p_2">1. Smt. Anita W/o Banwari Lal D/o Maniram aged about 26 years,
B/c Meghwal R/o Kaliyasar Tehsil Malsisar District Jhunjhunu at
present residing at Village Bholoosar Post Udasar Loder Tehsil
Sardarshahar District Churu.
</p><p id="p_3">2. Banwarilal S/o Bhanwar Lal aged about 31 years, B/c Jat R/o
Village Bholoosar Post Udasar Loder Tehsil Sardarshahar District
Churu.
</p><p id="p_4">                                                         ----Petitioners
                                  Versus
</p><p id="p_5">1. State of Rajasthan through the Secretary, Department of Home
Affairs, Govt of Rajasthan, Jaipur.
</p><p id="p_6">2. The Director General of Police, Rajasthan, Jaipur, Rajasthan.
</p><p id="p_7">3. That Superintendent of Police, Churu.
</p><p id="p_8">4. The Station House Officer, Police Station Sardarshahar, District
Churu.
</p><p id="p_9">                                                       ----Respondents
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)    : Mr. Pritam Joshi.
For Respondent(s) : Mr. MS Panwar PP.
_____________________________________________________
     HON'BLE DR. JUSTICE PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI
                                  Order
17/11/2017

</p><p id="p_10">1.         The      petitioners    have    preferred    this   criminal

misc.petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. for protection of life and

liberty from the relative of the petitioner No.1.
</p>
<p id="p_11">2.         After hearing the learned counsel for the parties and on

an overall consideration of the facts and circumstances of the

case, this Court deems it proper to direct the petitioners to appear

before the Superintendent of Police, Churu, who shall hear the

grievance of the petitioners, and if necessitated, provide adequate
                                (2 of 2)


security and protection to the petitioners. It is reflected from the

Mark-sheet issued by Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan

(Annexure-1) produced by the learned counsel for the petitioner

that the petitioner No.1 is major.
</p>
<p id="p_12">3.         Consequently,    the      present   misc.petition   stands

disposed of. The stay application also stands disposed of.
</p>



<p id="p_13">                              (DR. PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI)J.
</p>
