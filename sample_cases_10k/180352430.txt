<div class="doc_title">Yogendra Kumar Sharma vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh Thr on 20 August, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                W.P. No.5512/2015
    (Yogendra Kumar Sharma Vs. State of M.P. and
                      others)

20/08/2015
      Shri D.P.Singh, Advocate for the petitioner.
      Shri R.P.Gupta, Dy. Government Advocate for the
respondents-State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard on admission.
</p><p id="p_2">      By     this   petition,   under   <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article   226</a>   of   the
Constitution of India, petitioner has approached this
Court with a grievance that though while serving as Sub
Engineer he had acquired Engineering degree in the
month of January, 2012, yet the option exercised by him
to be included in the list of Sub Engineer (Degree
holders) has not so far been considered despite there
being a recommendation (Annexure P/1) made in that
behalf by the Executive Engineer way back on 11/8/2014.
</p><p id="p_3">      It is submitted that the matter is resting with the
Chief Engineer and so far no final decision has been taken
thereupon.     Under such circumstances, it is submitted
that the respondents be commanded to take a final
decision as regards placement of the petitioner in the
gradation list of Sub Engineers (Degree Holders).
</p><p id="p_4">      In view of the aforesaid innocuous prayer, it is
considered apposite to dispose of this writ petition with
                         W.P. No.5512/2015
            (Yogendra Kumar Sharma Vs. State of M.P. and
                              others)

        the following directions:-
</p><p id="p_5">           (I) The petitioner is set at liberty to file a detailed
              representation   before respondent no.2 seeking
              redressal of his greivances, as marashalled in this
              writ petition.
</p><p id="p_6">           (II)     If such a representation is filed, along with
              certified copy of this order,      within fifteen days
              from today, respondent no.2 through competent
              Authority shall ensure decision thereupon in the
              light of relevant service rules within four weeks
              from the date of submission of the representation.
              In the event, petitioner is held entitled to be
              included in the list of Sub Engineers (Degree
              holders), his name may accordingly be adjusted in
              the said list.
</p><p id="p_7">              It is made clear that this Court has not expressed
        any opinion on the merits of the case.
</p><p id="p_8">              Petition disposed of, accordingly.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                   (Rohit Arya)
(and)                                                Judge
 </pre>
