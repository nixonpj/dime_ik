<p id="p_1">	Heard
Mr.Dipak R. Dave, learned
advocate for the petitioner. It is submitted by
him that respondent No.1 has abandoned the work of his own and his
services have not been terminated by the petitioner.   On the
contrary, in the written statement filed before the Labour Court, the
petitioner has clearly stated that they are in need of the services
of respondent No.1 and have made efforts to ask him to resume his
duties. It
is further submitted that even after passing of
the impugned award dated 23.09.2011, respondent No.1 has not resumed
duties.
</p> 

 
 


 

<p id="p_2">	Issue
Rule and Notice as to interim relief,
returnable on 20.08.2012.
</p> 

<p id="p_3">	By
way of ad-interim relief, the direction in the impugned award
regarding payment of 20%      back-wages only, shall remain stayed
till then. 
</p>
 

 
 


 

<p id="p_4">(Smt.
</p><p id="p_5">Abhilasha Kumari, J.)
