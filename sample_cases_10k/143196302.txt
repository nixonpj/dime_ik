<div class="doc_title">Shanti Prasad Agarwala vs Unknown on 3 May, 2019</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                          1</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">19</span>


                   C.R.M. 3010 of 2019
                                               with
                                         CRAN 1605 of 2019

                                       Re: CRAN 1605 of 2019


     In re: An application for extension of interim bail.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                                     And

     In the matter of: Shanti Prasad Agarwala ...Petitioner.
</p>

<p id="p_3">            Shanti Prasad Agarwala... For the Petitioner(In Person)




             The petitioner, Shanti Prasad Agarwala, appears in person.
</p>

<p id="p_4">             The Sub-Inspector of police, Mr. Soumitra Das, who happens to be the
     investigating officer in connection with Hare Street P.S Case No. 154 of 2015
     dated 14.3.2015 under <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_1">Sections 120B</a>/<a href="/doc/1317063/" id="a_2">465</a>/<a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>/<a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>/<a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal
     Code is also present in Court along with the records.
</p>

<p id="p_5">            This Court by an order dated 26.3.2019 after perusal of the medical report was pleased to
     grant the petitioner interim bail for a period of four weeks. Subsequently, by an order dated
     22.4.2019 the interim bail was further extended for a period of two weeks.
</p>

<p id="p_6">            Considering the prevailing situation and the fact that the interim bail would expire on
     06.5.2019 we take up the matter for consideration on urgent basis with consent of the
     investigating agency.
</p>

<p id="p_7">            The investigating officer submits that as the petitioner is cooperating with the
     investigating agency he has no objection if the interim bail extended earlier is further extended.
</p>

<p id="p_8">            Considering the fact that the petitioner has been cooperating with the investigating officer
     and there is an earlier report relating to his health, we are inclined to extend the interim bail till
     14th June, 2019, as the summer vacation intervenes from 25th May, 2019 to 9th June, 2019.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                      2</span>



<p id="p_9">        Let the CRM 3010 of 2019 appear on 12.6.2019.
</p>

<p id="p_10">        With the aforesaid observation, CRAN 1605 of 2019 is disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_11">        Urgent photostat copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the parties upon compliance
of all formalities.
</p>


<p id="p_12">                                         (Md. Mumtaz Khan, J.)



                                         (Tirthankar Ghosh, J.)
 </p>
