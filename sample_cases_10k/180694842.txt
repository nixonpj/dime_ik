IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED   02.07.2015
CORAM
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.N.PRAKASH
Crl.OP.No.16256 of 2015  
Arumugam			...   	Petitioner
Vs
1.The Deputy Superintendent of Police,
Arakkonam,
Vellore District.

2.The Sub Inspector of Police,
Avalur Police Station,
Avalur, 
Arakkonam Taluk, 
Vellore District. 			 ...	Respondents

Prayer:- Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure to direct the respondents to register a case on the petitioner's complaint dated 17.06.2015 and file a final report in accordance with law. 
	For Petitioner	 :Mr.P.Krishnan 
	For Respondents	 :Mr.C.Emalias
			  Additional Public Prosecutor

			   ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">	Seeking a direction to the respondents to register a case on the petitioner's complaint dated 17.06.2015 and file a final report in accordance with law, the petitioner has come up with this petition. 
</p><p id="p_2">	2.Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the respondent and perused the material placed on record.  
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.The learned Additional Public Prosecutor submitted that based on the complaint lodged by the petitioner, the petition enquiry is pending in CSR.No.145 of 2015 before the respondent.  
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4.Recording the submission of the learned Additional Public Prosecutor, the respondent police is directed to conduct enquiry expeditiously and during the course of enquiry, if any  cognizable offence is made out, a regular case shall be registered, following the decision laid down by the Hon'ble Supreme Court in Lalita Kumari v. Government of Uttarpradesh and Others reported in (2014) 2 SCC 1.  
</p>
<p id="p_5">	With the above direction, this Criminal Original Petition is closed.	
02.07.2015

mps




To

</p><p id="p_6">1.The Deputy Superintendent of Police,
Arakkonam,
Vellore District.
</p>
<p id="p_7">2.The Sub Inspector of Police,
Avalur Police Station,
Avalur, 
Arakkonam Taluk, 
Vellore District. 
</p>
<p id="p_8">3.The Public Prosecutor,
High Court, Chennai.         
</p>














<p id="p_9">P.N.PRAKASH,J,
mps
                  











Crl.OP.No.16256 of 2015

















02.07.2015
