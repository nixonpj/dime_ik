<div class="doc_title">Pintu Kumar Das vs The State Of Jharkhand on 30 November, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                 Cr.M.P. No. 3634 of 2018

1.Pintu Kumar Das
2.Ajay Kumar Bharti @ Ajay Das
3.Diwakar Das
4.Pradip Kumar Das @ Pradip Das         ..... Petitioners
                        Versus
The State of Jharkhand                  ..... Opp. Party
                         ---------
</pre><p id="p_1">CORAM:      HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
</p><p id="p_2">                           ---------
</p><p id="p_3">For the Petitioners     : Mr. Venkateshwar Gopal, Advocate.
</p><pre id="pre_2">For the State           : A.P.P.
                     ---------
03/Dated: 30/11/2018

</pre><p id="p_4">         The defect as pointed out by the office is ignored for
the present.
</p><p id="p_5">         The instant application has been filed for modification
of order dated 06.09.2018 passed in A.B.A. No. 2199 of 2018
and also to extend the period of surrender of the petitioners
by further four weeks.
</p><p id="p_6">         It   appears   that   under   order   dated   06.09.2018,
petitioners were admitted on anticipatory bail, but due to
typographical error, in place of "Sarath P.S. Case No. 199 of
2017, corresponding to G.R. No. 1067 of 2017", it has been
wrongly typed as "Chandil (Kapali) P.S. Case No. 13 of 2018,
corresponding to G.R. No. 89 of 2018".
</p><p id="p_7">         Accordingly, the order dated 06.09.2018 is modified to
the extent that in place of "Chandil (Kapali) P.S. Case No. 13
of 2018, corresponding to G.R. No. 89 of 2018", it be read as
"Sarath P.S. Case No. 199 of 2017, corresponding to G.R.
No. 1067 of 2017".
</p><p id="p_8">         The period of surrender of the petitioners in the court
below is extended by further six weeks from today.
</p><p id="p_9">         Rest part of the order shall remain intact.
         Cr.M.P. No. 3634 of 2018 stands allowed.
         Let a copy of order be sent to the court below.
</p>



<p id="p_10">                                       (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
Sunil/
