<div class="doc_title">Rambali Sahni vs The State Of Bihar on 8 April, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                           Criminal Miscellaneous No.50656 of 2012
            ======================================================
            Rambali Sahni, Son of Late Fakira Sahni
                                                               .... .... Petitioner/s
                                             Versus
            The State Of Bihar
                                                          .... .... Opposite Party/s
            ======================================================
            CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE AKHILESH CHANDRA
            ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">4   08-04-2013                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                  Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner seeks bail in a case registered under

                  <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1947545/" id="a_4">448</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_5">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_6">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_7">324</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_8">307</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_9">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_10">120B</a> of the

                  Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_11">Section 27</a> of the Arms Act. In this case,

                  instituted against a few named, petitioners's name appears emerging

                  during investigation as leader of the group making attempt to kill the

                  informant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Submission is of false implication and other co-

                  accused persons facing trial have already been acquitted.           The

                  petitioner's name appears emerging during investigation and he had no

                  knowledge.    Further, the petitioner is in custody four about nine

                  months only because of carrying one criminal case against,

                  wherein, he is on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 If it is so, having regard to the facts and circumstances

                  of the case, the petitioner above named is directed to be released on

                  bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Rs. Ten Thousand) with

                  two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                  Chief Judicial Magistrate, Muzaffarpur, in connection with Gaighat

                  P.S. Case No. 158 of 2002, subject to condition to remain physically
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.50656 of 2012 (4) dt.08-04-2013                                 2</span>




                            present before the court below on each and every date till disposal of

                            the case and in case of failure on two consecutive dates without giving

                            any reasonable explanation, the privilege granted shall be deemed to

                            be cancelled.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                                  (Akhilesh Chandra, J)
Praveen-II/-
</p>
