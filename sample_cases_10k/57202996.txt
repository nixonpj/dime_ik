<div class="doc_title">An Application For Anticipatory ... vs Santosh Dalui &amp; Ors on 7 October, 2013</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Nishita Mhatre</div>

<p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                           1</span>


2013.
</p><p id="p_2">             C.R.M. 12776 of 2013

        In the matter of: An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the
        Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 13.09.2013.
</p><p id="p_3">                              And
        In the matter of: Santosh Dalui &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_4">                                       Petitioners.
</p><p id="p_5">                           -versus-
</p><p id="p_6">                        The State of West Bengal
                                      Opposite party
        Mr. Saumya Bandyopadhyay
                      For the petitioners.
</p><p id="p_7">        Mr. Subrata Roy
                               For the State.
</p>

<p id="p_8">             The petitioner, apprehending arrest in connection with Jagatballavpur Police

        Station Case No.182 of 2013 dated 26.06.2013 under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_2">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_3">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_4">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>,

        <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_6">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_7">325</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_8">326</a>, <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_9">427</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_10">379</a>, <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_11">354</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_12">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_13">302</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_14">354B</a> of the Indian Penal Code, have come

        to this Court for anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_9">             We have heard the learned Advocates for the parties and have seen the

        case diary and other material on record. The petitioners do not deserve to be

        granted anticipatory bail since they have been implicated in the incident of

        assault.
</p>
<p id="p_10">             The application for anticipatory bail is, thus, rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_11">                                    (Nishita Mhatre, J.)



                               (Ranjit Kumar Bag, J.)
