<div class="doc_title">Ramji Lal Saini vs R H B And Anr on 15 February, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
BENCH AT JAIPUR
1. S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.449/2013
Smt. Ganga Bai vs. Rajasthan Housing Board &amp; Anr.
2. S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.450/2013
Mohd. Ayub Khan vs. Rajasthan Housing Board &amp; Anr.
3. S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.452/<a href="/doc/15003215/" id="a_1">2013
Ramji Lal Saini vs. Rajasthan Housing Board &amp; Anr</a>.
4. S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.451/<a href="/doc/66664638/" id="a_2">2013
Smt. Mohini Devi vs. Rajasthan Housing Board &amp; Anr.

Date</a> of order 			:	               15/02/2013.

		HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE MOHAMMAD RAFIQ

Shri Rizwan Ahmed for the petitioners.
Shri Anuroop Singh for the respondents. 

******
</pre><p id="p_1">	Prayer in these writ petitions is that the petitioners may be declared eligible in pursuance of booklet form Self Finance Scheme Year 2012 in Sector 8 &amp; 10 of Pratap Nagar, Sanganer, Jaipur and respondents be directed to include the name of the petitioners in the lottery scheduled to be held on 16.1.2013. 
</p><p id="p_2">	The respondents have filed reply to the writ petition contending that the petitions have been filed without verifying the fact as, in fact, the name of petitioners already existed in the list of registered applicants to be included in the draw of lottery. 
</p><p id="p_3">	The respondents have filed on record copy of the list at Annexure-R/1 wherein name of the petitioners have been included. It was argued that because of interim order passed by this Court, the draw of lottery was deferred and now the same would again take place.
</p><p id="p_4">	In the light of the aforesaid statement by learned counsel for the respondents, the petitions are disposed of as having become infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                  		    	(MOHAMMAD RAFIQ), J.
</p><p id="p_6">RS/73-76
All corrections made in the judgement/order have been incorporated in the judgement/order being emailed. (Ravi Sharma,P.A.
</p>
