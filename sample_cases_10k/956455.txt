O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	Under order dated 19.12.2009, this Court had directed the transfer of C.C.No.129 of 2009 on the file of Judicial Magistrate No.II, Salem to the Court of Judicial Magistrate No.III, Salem with a further direction that the transferee Court shall complete the trial within a period of three months from the date of receipt of a copy of the order.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2.The matter stands posted today since the learned Judicial Magistrate No.III, Salem has expressed certain difficulty in hearing the matter.  For reasons, found acceptable, it becomes necessary to transfer the case to some other Court.  This Court finds it appropriate to direct the transfer of C.C.No.129 of 2009 on the file of the Judicial Magistrate No.II, Salem to the Court of Judicial Magistrate No.IV, Salem.  Upon transfer, the learned Judicial Magistrate No.IV, Salem shall complete the trial within a period of three months from the date of receipt of a copy of this order.  This Criminal Original Petition is ordered accordingly. Consequently, the connected miscellaneous petition is closed.
</p>
