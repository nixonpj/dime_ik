<div class="doc_title">Gopal Kumar Mahto @ Gopal Kumar ... vs The State Of Bihar on 28 March, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.14850 of 2018
                 ======================================================
                 Gopal Kumar Mahto @ Gopal Kumar Prasad @ Gpal Kumar Mahto, Son
                 of Sri Madan Kumar Prasad @ Madan Mahto, residence of Village-
                 Bhadariya, Police Station- Khaira District- Saran at Chapra.

                                                                      .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                 The State of Bihar.

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Shambhu Prasad Yadav
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Sri Kanhaiya Kishore
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">02/ 28-03-2018                         The present application has been filed for

                 modification of the order dated 17.02.2018 passed in Cr. Misc.

                 No. 9404 of 2018 since the father's name of the petitioner Gopal

                 Kumar Mahto @ Gopal Kumar Prasad @ Gpal Kumar Mahto has

                 been recorded as Sri Raj Nath Mahto instead of Sri Madan Kumar

                 Prasad @ Madan Mahto.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                       The said mistake perpetuated in the order since

                 the name of petitioner's father was recorded in the Vakalatnama

                 as well as in the main petition as Sri Raj Nath Mahto.

</p><p id="p_3">                                       Learned counsel for the petitioner is permitted

                 to make correction in the main petition as well as in Vakalatnama

                 attached therewith with regard to the father's name of the

                 petitioner.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">       Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.14850 of 2018 (2) dt.28-03-2018                       2</span><p id="p_4">




</p><p id="p_5">                                              Let the order dated 17.02.2018 passed in Cr.

                    Misc. No. 9404 of 2018 is modified to the extent that the father's

                    name of the petitioner Gopal Kumar Mahto @ Gopal Kumar

                    Prasad @ Gpal Kumar Mahto be read as Sri Madan Kumar Prasad

                    @ Madan Mahto and the period of surrender is extended by three

                    weeks from the date of receipt/production of a copy of this order

                    in connection with Khaira P.S. Case No. 275 of 2017 pending in

                    the Court of learned ACJM-X, Saran.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                              Accordingly, this modification application is

                   disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                              (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
    DKS/-
</p>
