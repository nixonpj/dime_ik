<div class="doc_title">Surinder Kumar vs State Of Punjab And Anr on 29 January, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                               AT CHANDIGARH.
                  224                                  CRM-M-2701-2016
                                             Date of Decision: January 29, 2016

                  SURINDER KUMAR                                     ..........PETITIONER
                                        VERSUS
                  STATE OF PUNJAB AND ANR                            ........RESPONDENTS</pre>

<p id="p_1">                  CORAM:- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE M.M.S.BEDI

                  Present:     Mr.Gurmeet Singh Guri, Advocate for the petitioner

                               Mr.Sidakmeet Sandhu, AAG, Punjab

                  M.M.S.BEDI, J.(Oral)

                               Petitioner is facing proceedings under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_1">Section 138</a> of the

                  Negotiable Instruments Act on account of cheque of Rs.85,000/- issued by

                  the petitioner to the complainant to discharge the liability, having been

                  dishonoured. The petitioner was declared proclaimed offender on 1.8.2014.

                  The petitioner was granted regular bail on 6.2.2015 but during the course of

                  trial he again did not appear on 24.9.2015 as a result of which he was again

                  declared proclaimed offender.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               As the proceedings against the petitioner are under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_2">Section 138</a>

                  of the Negotiable Instruments Act and the petitioner has been in custody

                  w.e.f. 16.12.2015, striking a balance between the right of the personal

                  liberty of the petitioner and the right of the complainant to have expeditious

                  disposal of the case, I deem it appropriate to grant concession of bail to the

                  petitioner by simultaneously        compensating      the complainant for

                  unnecessary harassment in        proceedings under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_3">Section 138</a> of the

                  Negotiable Instruments Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               The petition is allowed. It is ordered that the petitioner will

                  appear before the trial Court(successor Court) and on doing so he will be

                  released on bail on his furnishing bail bond and surety bond to the
