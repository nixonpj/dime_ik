<p id="p_1">                                  Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of

              the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking regular bail in

              FIR No.30 dated 09.02.2015, under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_2">Sections 392</a>/<a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_3">427</a> of Indian

              Penal Code, 1860 ('<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a>' for short) (<a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_5">Section 395</a> IPC added and

              <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_6">Section 392</a> IPC deleted later on), registered at Police Station

              Shimlapuri, District Ludhiana.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted

              that co-accused Harpreet Singh and Daljeet Singh who were

              similarly situated have been granted bail by this Court.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on

              merits on the case, this petition is allowed.                Petitioner be

              admitted to bail, subject to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial

              Magistrate, Ludhiana.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                                                        (SABINA)
