<pre id="pre_1">                                1


          IN THE COURT OF MS. RAVINDER BEDI:
        METROPOLITAN MAGISTRATE, NEW DELHI

STATE VERSUS SONU
                        FIR NO. 637/1996
                        U.S. 25 OF ARMS ACT
                        P.S. Hauz Khas

JUDGMENT
</pre>
<pre id="pre_2">1.Sr. No. of the Case               : 649/2

2.Name of complainant               : SI Babu Lal

3.Date of incident                  : 6.9.96

4.Name of the accused               :Sonu son of Roshan Lal R/o D-
</pre><p id="p_1">                                    4/179, Sector 20, Rohini, Delhi

</p><p id="p_2">5.Offence complained of             :U/S 25 of <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_1">Arms Act</a>

</p><p id="p_3">6.Plea of accused                   : Pleaded not guilty

</p><p id="p_4">7. Date on which the Judgment : 23.10.2007
   has been reserved

</p><p id="p_5">8.Final Order                       : Convicted

</p><p id="p_6">9.Date of such Order                : 23.10.2007

  BRIEF REASONS FOR SUCH DECISION:

</p><p id="p_7">           Genesis of the prosecution case is that on

6.9.1996 at 5.40 PM in front of Park Uphar Cinema

Accused Sonu was          found      in possession of one

buttandar knife without any permit or licence in

contravention    to     notification,    issued     by   Delhi

Administration. Sketch of knife was prepared and

seized. Site plan of the spot was prepared. Accused

was arrested and his personal search was conducted.

On completion of investigation, charge sheet was filed.

</p><p id="p_8">            Charge for the offences u/s 25 of <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_2">Arms Act</a>

was framed against accused on, to which, he pleaded

not guilty and claimed trial.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             2</span><p id="p_9">


</p><p id="p_10">            The Prosecution in order to substantiate its

case examined PW1 HC Joginder Singh; PW2 HC Jasbir

Singh PW3 Ct Prem Singh Kalyan Singh; PW4 HC Harpal

Singh; PW5 SI Babu Lal.
</p>
<p id="p_11">            Statement of the accused was recorded u/s

313 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_3">Cr.P.C</a>., wherein the accused has denied all the

incriminating evidence and has stated that he was

lifted from his house by police and has been falsely

implicated. However, he has not led any evidence in

his defence.
</p>
<p id="p_12">             Today i.e 23.10.2007 when the case was

fixed for final arguments, accused came forward and

plead guilty to the offence. He has prayed for leniency

in the matter. Statement of accused in this respect was

recorded.
</p>
<p id="p_13">            In view of the statement of accused, I hold

the accused guilty of the offence U/S 25 <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_4">Arms Act</a>. Let

he be heard on the point of sentence.

Announced in the open
Court on 23.10.2007               (RAVINDER BEDI)
                                METROPOLITAN MAGISTRATE
                                  NEW DELHI
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                              3</span>


          IN THE COURT OF MS. RAVINDER BEDI:
        METROPOLITAN MAGISTRATE, NEW DELHI

STATE VERSUS SONU
                      FIR NO. 637/1996
                      U.S. 25 OF ARMS ACT
                      P.S. Hauz Khas


ORDER ON SENTENCE:
</p>
<p id="p_14">           I have heard arguments on point of sentence.

           Ld. APP for the State request for a strict view in

terms of judgment of conviction of even date.

</p><p id="p_15">           On the other hand, the convict submits that

he is 29 years of age. He submits that he has no

previous conviction. He has prayed for a lenient view.

He intends to rehabilitate himself in Society.

</p><p id="p_16">           Keeping in view the circumstances and the

age of the convict and that accused remained in

custody w.e.f 6.9.96 to 20.9.1996, he is sentenced to

undergo for the period already undergone and also to

pay fine of Rs. 1500/-. Fine paid.

</p><p id="p_17">           Copy of the judgment and order on point of

sentence to be provided to him free of cost forthwith.

File be consigned to record room.

Announced in the open court.
</p>
<p id="p_18">

</p><p id="p_19">                                     M.M.N.D.23.10.2007
