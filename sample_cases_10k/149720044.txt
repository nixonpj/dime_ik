<div class="doc_title">7 18.9.2019 Heard Learned Counsel ... vs Unknown on 18 September, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                         W.P.(C) NO. 6520 OF 2007




7   18.9.2019                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.
                             It appears, this writ petition involves an order
                involving M.A.C. No.8/2001. Involving such order appeal lies.
                In such view of the matter, this Court finds, the writ petition
                is not maintainable. The petitioner taking a ground that the
                order     impugned    involves   a    disposed     of   writ    petition
                compelling the petitioner to challenge such order in filing
                another writ petition has no legal force. It is at this stage of
                the     matter,   learned   counsel    for   the   petitioner     seeks
                permission for withdrawal of the writ petition to move the
                appellate forum.
                             The writ petition is permitted to be withdrawn.
                However, keeping in view that the petitioner was bona fidely
                pursing a writ remedy and further the writ petition was also
                entertained, liberty is granted to the petitioner to file appeal.
                Let the appeal be filed within one month. Certified copy of
                Annexure-5 be returned to the learned counsel for the
                petitioner after substituting the photocopy thereof.




                                                 ..............................
                                                 Biswanath Rath, J.
</pre>
