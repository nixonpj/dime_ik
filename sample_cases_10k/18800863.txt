<div class="doc_title">Vinod Verma vs State Of Rajasthan Through P P on 4 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1"> HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN BENCH AT
                      JAIPUR
         S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail No. 8733 / 2017
Vinod Verma S/o Shri Suwalal B/c Balai, Aged About 25 Years, R/o

4-B, Manu Vatika Third, Bajri Mandi Road, Panchyawala, Jaipur

(Raj.)


                                                       ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State of Rajasthan Through PP
                                                     ----Respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)   : Mr. Sanjay Verma
For Respondent(s) : Mr. Prakash Thakuriya, PP
_____________________________________________________
             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PANKAJ BHANDARI
                        Judgment / Order
04/07/2017

</p><p id="p_2">1.    Petitioner has filed this bail application under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of

Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">2.    F.I.R. No.215/2017 was registered at Police Station, Karni

Vihar, Jaipur West (Raj.) for offence under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>, <a href="/doc/175699/" id="a_4">336</a>,

<a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_5">354</a> I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_4">3.    It is contended by counsel for the petitioner that the dispute

arose with regard to parking of the vehicle. Cross F.I.R. has been

lodged by the petitioner in this case. No injury has been sustained

by the complainant party and injuries have been sustained by the

petitioner party.
</p>
<p id="p_5">4.    Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

</p><p id="p_6">5.    Considering the arguments put forth by the counsel for the
                                          (2 of 2)
                                                                      [CRLMB-8733/2017]

petitioner, I deem it proper to allow the anticipatory bail

application.
</p>
<p id="p_7">6.     The     Anticipatory       Bail       Application      is     allowed.     The

S.H.O./I.O./Arresting Authority, Police Station Karni Vihar, Jaipur in F.I.R.

No.215/2017, is directed that in the event of arrest of the petitioner he

shall be released on bail, provided he furnishes a personal bond in

the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two sureties in the sum of

Rs.25,000/- each to his satisfaction on the following conditions:-

(I).   that    the   petitioner     shall           make   himself   available     for

interrogation by a police officer as and when required;

</p><p id="p_8">(ii). that the petitioner shall not directly or indirectly make any

inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted with the

facts of the case so as to dissuade him from disclosing such facts

to the Court or any police officer, and

</p><p id="p_9">(iii). that the petitioner shall not leave India without previous

permission of the Court.
</p>
<p id="p_10">


</p><p id="p_11">                                                       (PANKAJ BHANDARI), J.
</p>
<p id="p_12">Arti/33
