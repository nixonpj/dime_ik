<div class="doc_title">Mohan Singh Kushwah vs M.P. State Central Region ... on 15 June, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                              CRA-858-2012
(MOHAN SINGH KUSHWAH Vs M.P. STATE CENTRAL REGION ELECTRICITY DISTRIBUTION COMP.
                                     LTD.)


15-06-2016

Shri Ishtiyak Hussain, counsel for the appellant.
None for the respondent even in the second round.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Appellant Mohan Singh is present in person. He is duly
identified by the learned counsel for the appellant.
Learned counsel for the appellant has filed acknowledged
copy of I.A.No.11707/2016 for condonation of non-appearance
of appellant Mohan Singh before the Registry on 10.5.2016
on the ground of illness.
</p><p id="p_2">For the reasons stated in the application, I.A.No.11707/2016
is allowed.
</p><p id="p_3">The non-appearance of the appellant Mohan Singh before the
Registry on 10.05.2016 stands condoned.
Let appellant Mohan Singh now appear before the Registry

on 28th September, 2016 and on such other dates as may
be fixed in this regard.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                                                        (C V SIRPURKAR)
                                                             JUDGE
