                              Shri Pratyush Tripathi, Govt. Advocate for the respondent/State on</pre>
<p id="p_1">                     advance copy.
</p><p id="p_2">                              They are heard.
</p><p id="p_3">                              This petition has been filed by the petitioner under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the

                     Constitution of India against the order dated 05.12.2018 (Annexure P/5)
                     whereby the petitioner was sought to be retired on attaining the age of 60
                     years.
</p><p id="p_4">                              Admittedly, the petitioner has already been retired.
                              Counsel for the petitioner has drawn the attention of this Court to the
                     absorption list dated 09.08.2016 which was also subject matter of W.P. No.
                     15705/2016. In the order dated 27.09.2016 passed in W.P. No. 15705/2016
                     when a query was put to the counsel for the State regarding the status of the
                     aforesaid absorption list, counsel for the State has admitted that it is not a

                     proposal but it is a final decision taken by the State Government. Thus,
                     counsel has submitted that when the petitioner has already been absorbed by
                     the respondents vide order dated 09.08.2016, in view of the same all the
                     benefits emanating from his absorption vide order dated 09.08.2016 be
                     accorded to him at the earliest.
</p><p id="p_5">                              Counsel for the respondents/State has not refuted the aforesaid
                     submission and submitted that State Govt. shall pass appropriate orders
                     taking into account the aforesaid undertaking given by the State Govt. in W.P.
                     No. 15705/2016 dated 27.09.2016.
</p><p id="p_6">                              In view of the aforesaid submission, this petition is disposed of with a
                     direction to the petitioner to submit a fresh representation along with all the
                     relevant documents before the respondent no.2 within two weeks' time and
                     the respondent no.2 shall decide the same taking into account the aforesaid

                     of the order.
