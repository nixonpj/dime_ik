                         ORDER

        S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail Cancellation Appl. No.119/2016

Raj Gopal Tak Son of Late Shri Jagannath Tak, by caste Kalal, resident of
Sanjay Colony, Ward No. 20, In front of Railway Station, Gangapur City,
Police Station Gangapur City, Sawaimadhopur, Rajasthan.
Versus.
1. State of Rajasthan through P.P.
2. Shyam Gopal Tak Son of Late Shri Jagannath Tak, by caste Kalal,

Rajasthan.

Date of order :                              September 27, 2016

                  HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA,J.

Mr. Anoop Agarwal, for the petitioner.
Mr. V.S. Godara, PP for the State.


            Petitioner has filed this petition under Section 439

(2) Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking Cancellation of

regular bail in F.I.R. No.503/2015 registered at Police Station

Gangapur City, District Sawai Madhopur for offences under

<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_2">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a>, <a href="/doc/570574/" id="a_4">166</a>, <a href="/doc/1815602/" id="a_5">167</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">120-B</a> Indian Penal Code,

1860 granted to respondent No.2.</pre>

<p id="p_1">            Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            No ground for cancellation of bail is made out.

            Dismissed.
</p>
