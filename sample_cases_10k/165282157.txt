          THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE B.SUDHEENDRA KUMAR

     WEDNESDAY, THE 22ND DAY OF MAY 2019 / 1ST JYAISHTA, 1941

                   Bail Appl..No. 3307 of 2019

    CRIME NO. 388/2019 OF Kilikolloor Police Station , Kollam



PETITIONER/S/ACCUSED:


             SURESH
             AGED 40 YEARS,
             S/O. CHELLAPPAN ACHARY, MANNACHERI VADAKATHIL, TKMCE
             P.O, KOLLAM
             NOW RESIDING AT MANNACHERI VADAKATHIL,
             TKMCE P.O, KOLLAM.

             BY ADVS.
             SRI.M.KIRANLAL
             SRI.MANU RAMACHANDRAN
             SRI.R.RAJESH (VARKALA)
             SRI.T.S.SARATH


RESPONDENT/S/COMPLAINANT:
       1     STATE OF KERALA
             REPRESENTED BY PUBLIC PROSECUTOR ,
             HIGH COURT OF KERALA, ERNAKULAM-682031.

      2      SUB INSPECTOR OF POLICE,
             KILIKOLLOOR POLICE STATION, KOLLAM-691003.


             SMT.V.SREEJA,PP


THIS BAIL APPLICATION HAVING COME UP FOR ADMISSION ON 22.05.2019,
THE COURT ON THE SAME DAY PASSED THE FOLLOWING:
 Bail Appl..No. 3307 of 2019

                              2




                       O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">     The petitioner is the first accused in Crime No.388 of

2019 of Kilikolloor Police Station, registered for the

offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_2">324</a> and <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_3">308</a> IPC.

</p><p id="p_2">     2.   The petitioner has filed this application under

Section 438 Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3.   Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     4.   The learned Public Prosecutor has submitted

that the petitioner is not involved in any other offence of

similar nature. It has been further submitted by the

learned Public Prosecutor that the petitioner did not use

any weapon to inflict any injury on the injured. It appears

that no recovery is to be effected from the petitioner.

Therefore, the custodial interrogation of the petitioner is

not necessary in this case. That apart, the petitioner is not
 Bail Appl..No. 3307 of 2019

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  3</span>

having any criminal antecedent. In the said circumstances,

an order under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C. in favour of the

petitioner will be justified.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     In the result, this application stands allowed and the

respondents are directed to release the petitioner on bail

in the event of his arrest in the above said crime, on

condition of the petitioner executing a bond for

Rs.40,000/- (Rupees Forty Thousand only) with two

solvent sureties, each for the like sum to the satisfaction of

the second respondent, before whom the petitioner shall

surrender within ten days, if not arrested in the meantime

and subject to the following further conditions:

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">    (i) The    petitioner       shall   report   before   the
    Investigating Officer on every Saturday between
    9.00 a.m. and 11.00 a.m. for two months and
    thereafter,   as   and       when    required   by    the
 Bail Appl..No. 3307 of 2019

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                              4</span>

     Investigating Officer for interrogation.
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_2">      (ii) The petitioner shall not intimidate or
      influence the witnesses or in any way tamper with
      the investigation.
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_3">      (iii) The petitioner shall not get involved in any
      other offence during the pendency of this case.
</blockquote>


<blockquote id="blockquote_4">                                          Sd/-</blockquote><p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                              B.SUDHEENDRA KUMAR
                                         JUDGE
