                              ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      This writ petition was listed on earlier occasions.

There was no representation on behalf of the learned

counsel for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      2. Learned counsel for respondent-Bank submits that

petitioner is a tenant of respondent Nos.2 and 3 herein and

that if the petitioner is aggrieved by the action of the

respondent-Bank, he may approach the Debts Recovery
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 3</span>


Tribunal under Section 17(4A) of the Securitisation and

Reconstruction of Financial Asset and <a href="/doc/190229/" id="a_2">Security Act</a>, 2002.

He   further   submits   that   petitioner's   counsel   is   not

appearing in the matter after filing the application seeking

vacating of the interim order granted by this Court.


</p><p id="p_4">      3. I have perused the order sheet and I find that

there is no appearance on behalf of the petitioner. Even

other wise, if the petitioner is aggrieved by the action

initiated by the respondent - Bank, he has an alternative

and efficacious remedy under <a href="/doc/40409/" id="a_3">Section 17(4A)</a> of the Act

before the Debts Recovery Tribunal.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">      4. In the circumstances, the writ petition is not

entertained and is dismissed reserving liberty to the

petitioner to avail an alternative statutory remedy before

the Tribunal, if so advised. Petitioner is also at liberty to

seek interim order or protective order before the Tribunal.

If any such application is filed, the Tribunal shall consider

the same expeditiously and in accordance with law.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                  4</span>


</p><p id="p_7">        In the event there is any delay in approaching the

Tribunal, the pendency of the writ petition before this

Court may be taken note of.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">        The direction sought by the petitioner restraining

respondent No.1 to take physical possession of the

schedule premises pursuant to the order passed by the

Chief      Metropolitan        Magistrate,   Bengaluru    in

Crl.Misc.8803/16, cannot be issued in the instant case.


</p><p id="p_10">        Interim order dated 28/04/2017 is recalled.

        I.A.1/18 is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_11">

</p><p id="p_12">                                              Sd/-
</p><p id="p_13">                                             JUDGE
