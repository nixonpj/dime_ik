            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE M.N. BHANDARI
                                  Order
14/09/2017

     These writ petitions were heard to a great length. The

arguments on merit of the case have been raised in reference to

not only allotment of plots by the society but even applications for

conversion of land with deposition of fee and lastly even approval

of the layout plan.

</p><p id="p_68">     Learned counsel for respondents made a reference of the

order under Section 90B of the Rajasthan Land Revenue Act, 1956

regarding resumption of land. The argument ultimately led to the

issue of shifting of central line so that minimum damage is caused.

To find out the aforesaid, the map prepared after PT survey was

seen by the court. If central line is shifted then one side of the
                                    (9 of 9)
                                                             [ CW-15238/2017]



road would sustain more damage. To find out and to have

minimum      damages    of   the    shops,    the   Jaipur     Development

Authority is directed to mark the shops to find out as upto what

extent, it would go in the road. It would be kept in mind that

width of the road is to be maintained 160 ft. The effort would also

be made to have least damages to the shops, if possible. The

aforesaid exercise be undertaken. The order aforesaid has been

passed with the consent of the parties. A report pursuant to this

order would be submitted. Let the exercise directed above

complete within two days.

</p><p id="p_69">        List these cases on 18th September, 2017 on the top of the

list.
</p>
<p id="p_70">                                                     (M.N. BHANDARI)J.

FRBOHRA
