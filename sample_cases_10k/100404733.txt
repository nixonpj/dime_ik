<div class="doc_title">Ravindra Kumar Ojha @ Ravindra ... vs The State Of Bihar on 3 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.18144 of 2018
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -257 Year- 2017 Thana -KANTI THARMAL POWER District-
                                                      MUZAFFARPUR
                 ======================================================
                 1. Ravindra Kumar Ojha @ Ravindra Ojha S/o Sri Brij Kishore Ojha
                 resident of Village - Madhukar Chhapra, P.S. - Kanti, District -
                 Muzaffarpur.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar.

                                                              .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Chandra Shekhar Anand, Adv.
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Sri Sanjay Kumar Tiwary 1, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE BIRENDRA KUMAR
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   03-04-2018                        Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                 learned APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                      Petitioner seeks bail in a case registered for

                 the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_1">Sections 307</a>, <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_2">384</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_3">379</a>, <a href="/doc/1402213/" id="a_4">337</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_5">427</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_6">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_7">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_9">Section 27</a> of

                 Arms Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                      Petitioner had lodged Kanti P.S.Case No.260

                 of 2017 against informant of this case for offence under

                 <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_10">Section 307</a> of the Indian Penal Code and other allied

                 sections<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_11"> of the Penal Code</a> and thereafter the present FIR

                 against the petitioner and others with general and omnibus

                 allegation of demand of ransom and fire from the pistol.

                 However, no injury was caused to anyone.

</p><p id="p_4">                                      It appears that similarly situated co-accused
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.18144 of 2018 (2) dt.03-04-2018</span>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                         2</span>




                        Indrajeet Kumar Ojha has already been allowed bail by a

                        Coordinate Bench of this Court vide Annexure-4.

</p><p id="p_5">                                          Considering           the        aforesaid   facts,   let   the

                        petitioner, above named, be released on bail on furnishing

                        bail bond of Rs.20,000/- (Twenty Thousand) with two

                        sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                        Court below where the case is pending in connection with

                        Kanti Police Station Case No.257                       of 2017, subject to the

                        condition that the petitioner shall fully cooperate with the

                        trial of the case, failing which the court below shall be at

                        liberty to cancel the bail bond of the petitioner.


</p><p id="p_6">                                                                                  (Birendra Kumar, J)
Nitesh/-
</p>
