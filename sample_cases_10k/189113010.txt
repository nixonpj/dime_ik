<div class="doc_title">Saukat Ali vs The State Of Jharkhand on 20 September, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">          IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                        B. A. No.6837 of 2017
                                  -----
          Saukat Ali.                       .......... Petitioner.
                               -Versus-
          The State of Jharkhand.           .......... Opposite Party.
                                 ------
          CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJESH SHANKAR
                                 ------
          For the Petitioner :   Mrs. Neetu Singh, Advocate
          For the State      :   A.P.P.
                                 ------
02/20.09.2017</pre><p id="p_1">: Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p id="p_2">                 The petitioner is an accused in a case registered under
           <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_6">325</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_7">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_8">379</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_9">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1947545/" id="a_10">448</a>
           and <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_11">504</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_3">                 Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the
           petitioner has been falsely implicated in the present case and
           has not committed any offence as alleged in the first
           information report. It is alleged that the petitioner assaulted
           on the head of Ali Imam Ansari (elder brother of the
           informant) by sword, however, Para-12 of the impugned oder
           clearly suggests that the said injury was found to be simple in
           nature. Co-accused-Masuk Ali has already been granted bail
           by a co-ordinate Bench of this Court vide order dated 19th
           June, 2017 in B.A. No.4831 of 2017. The petitioner is in
           judicial custody since 7th July, 2017 and, therefore, he may be
           given the privilege of regular bail.
</p><p id="p_4">                 Learned APP opposes the petitioner's prayer for bail.
                 Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, I
           am inclined to enlarge the petitioner, above named, on bail.
           Accordingly, the petitioner is directed to be released on bail
           on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (rupees ten thousand)
           with two sureties of the like amount, each, to the satisfaction
           of learned Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class, Bokaro, in connection
           with Sector-12 P.S. Case No.101 of 2016, corresponding to
           G.R. No.1685 of 2016.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                                                  (Rajesh Shankar, J.)
Sanjay/
