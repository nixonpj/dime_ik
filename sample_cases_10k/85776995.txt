<div class="doc_title">Subhas Verma &amp; Ors. vs The State Of Bihar on 14 December, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                          Criminal Miscellaneous No.42319 of 2011
           ======================================================
</pre><p id="p_1">              1. Subhas Verma
</p><p id="p_2">              2. Prem Verma
</p><p id="p_3">              3. Chandra Prakash Verma
</p><p id="p_4">              4. Shailesh Verma
</p><p id="p_5">              5. Moti Lal Verma
                                                               .... .... Petitioners
                                           Versus
           The State Of Bihar
                                                           .... .... Opposite Party
           ======================================================


2   14-12-2011             Heard learned counsels for the petitioners and the

                  State.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                           The petitioners are apprehending their arrest in a

                  case registered under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_4">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_5">325</a>,

                  <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_6">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_7">379</a> and <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_8">504</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_7">                           The accusation is of making assault to the

                  informant. One of the injuries has been fond to be

                  grievous and others are simple. There is counter versions

                  of the occurrence also and from the petitioners' side one

                  person also received grievous injury.

</p><p id="p_8">                            Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above

                  named petitioners be released on anticipatory bail in the

                  event of their arrest or surrender before the learned court

                  below within a period of twelve weeks from today, on

                  furnishing the bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand)
                    each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                   satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Bagaha,

                   West Champaran in connection with Dhanaha P.S. Case

                   No. 87/2011 subject to the conditions as laid down under

                   <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_9">Section 438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">                                 (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)


Amrendra Kumar/-
</p>
