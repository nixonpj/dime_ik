<div class="doc_title">Satendra And 2 Others vs State Of U.P. And Another on 15 November, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Rajiv Gupta</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 72
 

 
Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 39845 of 2019
 

 
Applicant :- Satendra And 2 Others
 
Opposite Party :- State of U.P. and Another
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Gyan Prakash Mishra
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.
 

 
Hon'ble Rajiv Gupta,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Heard learned counsel for the applicants, learned A.G.A. and perused the record. 
</p> 
<p id="p_2">The present application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. has been filed for quashingthe order dated 3.9.2019 and 19.2.2015 as well as entire criminal proceedings of Complaint Case No. 710 of 2014 (Priyanka @ Kavita Vs. Satendra and others), under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Sections 498-A</a>, <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_3">354</a> IPC and 3/4 of D.P. Act, P.S. Kotwali Dehat, District- Bulandshahr, pending in the court of Addl. Chief Judicial Magistrate, Court No. 1, Bulandshahr. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">As per the allegations made in the complaint, it is alleged that opposite party no. 2 was married to applicant no. 1 on 9.12.2012, however after the said marriage the applicants started demanding additional dowry and for non-fulfilment of demand of additional dowry, they started torturing and maltreating her and also assaulted her and turned her out of her matrimonial house. On the basis of the said allegations made in the complaint and after making requisite enquiry by recording the statements of the witnesses u/s 200 and 202 <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">Cr.P.C</a>, the learned Magistrate summoned the applicants to face trial vide order dated 19.2.2015.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">Against the summoning order, the applicant filed a revision, which too has been rejected by the court below vide order dated 3.9.2019. 
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Learned counsel for the applicants has submitted that from the perusal of the allegations made in the complaint and the material collected during the course of inquiry, no offence is disclosed against the applicants and the present prosecution has been instituted with a malafide intention for the purposes of harassment. He has pointed out certain documents and statements in support of his contention. 
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Per contra, learned AGA has submitted that from the perusal of the allegations made in the complaint and the material collected during the course of inquiry, prima facie offence is clearly made out against the applicants and as such, entire proceedings cannot be quashed. 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">At this stage only prima facie case is to be seen in the light of the law laid down by the Supreme Court in the cases of R.P. Kapur Vs. State of Punjab, A.I.R. 1960 S.C. 866, State of Haryana Vs. Bhajan Lal, 1992 SCC (Cr.) 426, State of Bihar Vs. P.P. Sharma, 1992 SCC (Cr.) 192 and lastly Zandu Pharmaceutical Works Ltd. Vs. Mohd. Saraful Haq and another, (Para-10) 2005 SCC (Cr.) 283. 
</p> 
<p id="p_8">The disputed defence of the accused cannot be considered at this stage. Moreover, the applicant has got a right of discharge under <a href="/doc/1572967/" id="a_5">Section 239</a>/<a href="/doc/901796/" id="a_6">245</a> or 227/228 <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_7">Cr.P.C</a>. as the case may be through a proper application for the said purpose and he is free to take all the submissions in the said discharge application before the Trial Court. 
</p> 
<p id="p_9">The prayer for quashing the impugned orders as well as proceedings of the aforementioned case is refused. 
</p> 
<p id="p_10">The present application u/s 482 Cr.P.C. is accordingly disposed of. 
</p> 
<p id="p_11">Order Date :- 15.11.2019
 
KU
