<div class="doc_title">Amrendra Tiwari vs The State Of Jharkhand on 5 November, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">             IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                           B.A. No. 3556 of 2014

Amrendra Tiwari                                 ...      ...      ...     Petitioner

                                  Versus
The State of Jharkhand. ...         ...    ...        ...      ...      Opposite Party


Coram:              HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAVI NATH VERMA

For the Petitioner   : Mrs. Jashodhara Tripathy
For the Opposite Party: Additional Public Prosecutor.

08/ 05.11.2014</pre><p id="p_1">             The sole petitioner namely Amrendra Tiwari, has been accused in
connection with Patratu (Barkakana) P.S. Case No. 104 of 2009 ( G. R. No. 1534 of 2009) under
<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_1">sections 307</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and also under sections 3/4 of the Explosive
Substance Act.
</p><p id="p_2">                   Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned counsel for
the State.
</p><p id="p_3">                    The prosecution case as it appears from the FIR is that on 27.05.2009, at
about 10.45 A.M., two miscreants came on a Hero Honda motorcycle at MGCPL company
and one of the miscreants took out three bombs from the bag and exploded the same near the
informant who was Chowkidar of the said MGCPL company and as a result, informant
sustained injuries and thereafter, miscreants fled away by exploding the bombs.
</p><p id="p_4">             Learned counsel appearing for the petitioner submitted that the petitioner was
made accused in this case on the confessional statement of one co-accused Gayasuddin who
has already been granted bail by order dated 03.02.11 passed in BA No. 9147 of 2010.
Learned counsel further submitted that other similarly situated co-accused persons have also
been granted bail by different Benches of this Court and the petitioner has been languishing
in the jail custody since 16.01.14 and charges have also been framed against him.
</p><p id="p_5">                    Learned Addl. PP opposed the prayer, but fairly submitted that several
co-accused persons have been granted bail by this court.
</p><p id="p_6">                  Considering the aforesaid facts, the aforesaid sole petitioner is directed to
be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- ( Rupees Ten Thousand) with two
sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned Judicial Magistrate, 1 st Class,
Hazaribagh in connection Patratu (Barkakana) P.S. Case No. 104 of 2009 ( G. R. No. 1534 of
2009) on the condition that the petitioner shall remain physically present in the Court below
on each and every date till conclusion of the trial and in exceptional circumstances, the Court
below will consider the prayer for adjournment of the case.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                                           (R.N.Verma, J.)
Smita/-
</p>
