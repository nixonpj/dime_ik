<div class="doc_title">Narayan vs Govindram on 27 June, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                                 1

                   S.A.No.254/2015
27/6/2016

       Shri Vinay Gandhi, learned counsel for the
appellant.
       Heard on the question of admission
       The     appeal is admitted on the following
substantial questions of law:-
  1. Whether the suit filed by the respondent No.1 for mere</pre>
<p id="p_1">     possession and injunction was maintainable without
     claiming declaration, when the appellant disclosed in
     his defence/written statement the details of the right or
     title claimed by him, which raises a serious dispute or
     cloud over respondent's title to the disputed property,
     in view of the ratio laid down by the Hon'ble Apex
     Court in the case of <a href="/doc/540361/" id="a_1">Anathula Sudhakar vs. P. Buchi
     Reddy</a> 9AIR 2008 SC 2033)?
</p><p id="p_2">  2. Whether the learned lower courts below committed a
     grave error of law in not considering that the sale deed
     dated 12 September 1978 (Exhibit P/3), as duly proved
     by the evidence on record?
</p>

<p id="p_3">       Issue notice to the respondents on payment of
process by registered as well as ordinary mode within
a weeks.      Notices be made returnable within four
weeks.
</p><p id="p_4">       Interim relief to continue.
</p><p id="p_5">       C.c. as per rules.
</p><p id="p_6">                                    (Ms.Vandana Kasrekar)
                                           Judge
moni
