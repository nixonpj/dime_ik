<pre id="pre_1">                                                    1


02.8.10
 (SKB)
   27
                                          W.P. No. 20733(W) OF 2009


                                              Subrata Kumar Dutta
                                                      Versus
                                         The State of West Bengal &amp; Ors.


                      Mr. Prosenjit Mukherjee,
                                                 ....for the petitioner.

                      Mr. Subir Sanyal ...........for the Council.



                Having heard the learned advocates for the parties, it appears that the writ

          petitioner has been empanelled, subsequent to his participation in the selection

          process initiated by the District Primary School Council, Birbhum.         However,

          learned advocate for the District Primary School Council, Birbhum, submits that

          the process of verification of documents and other formalities as required under

          law is going on and it will take a while for grant of final approval of the panel-in-

          question by the Director of School Education, Government of West Bengal.

                Having regard to the submissions made by the learned advocate for the

          District Primary School Council, Birbhum, and in the facts and circumstances of

          the instant case this Court is of the view that no further order is required to be

          passed in the matter.

                In the event final approval is granted by the Director of School Education,

          Government of West Bengal, in respect of the panel wherein the writ petitioner's
                                          2


name figures, the District Primary School Council, Birbhum, may expedite the

issuance of necessary appointment letter in favour of the writ petitioner.

      Since no affidavits have been called for, allegations made in the writ

petition are deemed to be not admitted by the answering respondents.</pre>

<p id="p_1">      With the above directions, the writ petition stands disposed of.

      Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the

learned advocates for the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">


</p><p id="p_3">                                                         (Biswanath Somadder, J.)
