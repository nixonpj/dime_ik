<div class="doc_title">Manoj Kumar Sahani vs State Of Bihar on 14 May, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              Cr. Misc. No.10263 of 2009
            Manoj Kumar Sahani, Son of Sri Brahmdeo Sahani, Resident of Village- Bakhari
              Goriyari, P.S.-Bakhari, District- Begusarai.         . . . . Petitioner.
                                        Versus
                                  STATE OF BIHAR
                                       -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">2/    14.05.2009              Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                              Petitioner seeks bail in a case which has been registered for

                    the offences under Sections420, 386, 411/34<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>

                    and 3/11 of <a href="/doc/1824663/" id="a_2">Gambling Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              The informant is a police officer. The police had

                    apprehended the petitioner with one locket of victim and Rs. 1500/- in

                    cash. The petitioner has denied the recovery of any locket from his

                    possession.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                              Considering the facts and circumstances of the case,

                    petitioner, named above, is directed to be released on bail on

                    furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties

                    of the like amount each to the satisfaction of Chief Judicial

                    Magistrate, Begusarai / concerned court in connection with Bakhari

                    P.S. case no. 142 of 2008.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                                  (Shyam Kishore Sharma, J.)
     Uday/
 </p>
