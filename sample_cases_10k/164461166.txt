<div class="doc_title">Matang Singh vs The State Of Bihar on 5 August, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.21000 of 2016
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -400 Year- 2015 Thana -LAKHISARAI District- LAKHISARAI
                 ======================================================
                 1. Matang Singh, son of Bachchu Singh, Resident of village- Sharma, P.S.
                 &amp; District Lakhisarai.
                                                                      .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                 Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s      :  Mr. Kamal Kishore Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s  : Mr. Yogendra Kumar(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA MISHRA
                                             ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">4   05-08-2016           Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                         The petitioner apprehends his arrest in connection with a

                 case registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections

                 341</a>/<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>/<a href="/doc/1672685/" id="a_3">352</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>/<a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_5">379</a>/<a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_6">504</a>/<a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_7">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                         Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

                 allegation against these petitioners is that while the informant was

                 going on his motorcycle, the accused persons brought him down

                 from the motorcycle by wrapping towel around his neck and also

                 attacked him with the butt of pistol. However, simple injury was

                 found on the informant. It is further submitted that actually some

                 fight had occurred on the previous day in a Barat party and the

                 present case has been lodged out of vendetta keeping in mind the

                 earlier incident.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.21000 of 2016 (4) dt.05-08-2016</span><p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                                                   P2/2




                               Considering the aforementioned facts and circumstances

                      and that the petitioner has no criminal antecedent,          let the

                      petitioner, in the event of his arrest / surrender within a period of

                      four weeks from the date of receipt/production of a copy of this

                      order, be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/-

                      (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                      satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Lakhisarai in

                      connection with Lakhisarai P.S. Case No. 400/2015         subject to

                      the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_9">Section 438(2)</a> of the Cr. P.C.

</p><p id="p_6">                                                     (Anjana Mishra, J)
Saif/-
</p>
