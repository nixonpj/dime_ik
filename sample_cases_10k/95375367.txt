<div class="doc_title">S.M.Mayandi vs The Superintendent Of Police on 4 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 04.08.2017  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.S.SUNDAR           

W.P.(MD) No.14514 of 2017  

S.M.Mayandi                                                 ... Petitioner

                                            -Vs-

1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Tirunelveli District.

2.The Inspector of Police,
   Surandai Police Station,
   Tirunelveli District.                           ... Respondents


Prayer: Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India,
to issue a Writ of Mandamus, to direct the respondents to grant permission to
the petitioner to perform various events including cultural programme on
16.08.2017 during the celebration of Mulapari festival to Sri Santhipillaiyar
temple, <a href="/doc/27857091/" id="a_2">Keelasurandai Village, V.K.Pudur Taluk, Tirunelveli District</a> in
pursuance of the petitioner's representation dated 21.07.2017.

!For Petitioner   : Mr.S.Palani Velayutham

For Respondents : Mr.A.K.Baskara Pandian,  
                                            Special Government Pleader.



:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        This Writ petition has been filed for issuing a Writ of Mandamus, to
direct the respondents to grant permission to the petitioner to perform
various events including cultural programme on 16.08.2017 during the
celebration of Mulapari festival to Sri Santhipillaiyar temple, <a href="/doc/27857091/" id="a_3">Keelasurandai
Village, V.K.Pudur Taluk, Tirunelveli District</a> in pursuance of the
petitioner's representation dated 21.07.2017.
</p>        
<p id="p_2">        2. Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the
learned Special Government Pleader appearing for the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3. The learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the villagers
jointly decided to conduct ?cultural programme? programme on 16.08.2017 
during the celebration of Mulapari festival to Sri Santhipillaiyar temple,
<a href="/doc/27857091/" id="a_4">Keelasurandai Village, V.K.Pudur Taluk, Tirunelveli District</a>. He further
submitted that in this connection, a representation was given to the
respondent on 21.07.2017, with a request to grant permission and protection
to conduct the programme on the above said date. However, the respondent 
police has not yet considered the said representation and hence, the
petitioner is before this Court with this Writ Petition.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4. The learned Special Government Pleader appearing for the respondent
submitted that this Court may grant permission for ?cultural programme?
programme subject to stringent conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_5">        5. Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, the Writ
Petition is disposed of with the following directions:
</p><p id="p_6">        The second respondent/The Inspector of Police, Surandai Police Station,
Tirunelveli District, is directed to consider the representation of the
petitioner, dated 21.07.2017 and to give permission and to provide police
protection for ?cultural programme?  during the celebration of Mulapari
festival to Sri Santhipillaiyar temple, <a href="/doc/27857091/" id="a_5">Keelasurandai Village, V.K.Pudur
Taluk, Tirunelveli District</a>, scheduled to be held on on 16.08.2017, subject
to the following conditions;
</p><p id="p_7">        (a) the ?cultural programme? programme in connection with the a
festival in Sri Santhipillaiyar temple is situated at <a href="/doc/27857091/" id="a_6">Keelasurandai Village,
V.K.Pudur Taluk, Tirunelveli District</a>, scheduled to be held on 16.08.2017
should be completed before 10.30 p.m. 

</p><p id="p_8">        (b) there should not be any kind of obscene dance or vulgar dialogues
during the performance, by any one of the participants;
</p>
<p id="p_9">        (c) double meaning songs should not be played so as to spoil the minds
of students and the youth;
</p>
<p id="p_10">        (d) no dance or songs, touching upon any political party or religion,
community or caste be played; 
</p>
<p id="p_11">        (e) no flex boards in support of any political party or religious
leader be erected;
</p>
<p id="p_12">        (f) the function should not affect either religious or communal harmony
and shall be conducted without any discrimination based on caste;
</p>
<p id="p_13">        (g) if there is any violation of any one of the conditions imposed, the
concerned Police Officer is at liberty to take necessary action, as per law
and stop such performance; 
</p><p id="p_14">        (h) similarly, the Police  is empowered to stop the programme, if it
exceeds beyond the permitted time; 
</p><p id="p_15">        (i) the participants of the programme shall not intake any kind of in-
toxic substance or liquor during the programme;   and   
</p><p id="p_16">        (j) if any untoward incident takes place, the organizers of the
programme be made responsible for the same.     
</p>        
<p id="p_17">        6. It is open to second respondent police to put any further
restrictions or to impose any conditions purely in the interest of preserving
public order and tranquillity.  There can be a total ban for putting up any
Flex Boards representing any community. No costs. 
</p>        

<p id="p_18">To

</p><p id="p_19">1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Tirunelveli District.
</p>
<p id="p_20">2.The Inspector of Police,
   Surandai Police Station,
   Tirunelveli District.
</p>
