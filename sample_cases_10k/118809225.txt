<div class="doc_title">Smt. Shradhha Gupta vs Rajesh Kumar Gupta 33 ... on 13 December, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                              1

                   HIGH COURT OF CHHATTISGARH, BILASPUR

                                      TPC No. 70 of 2018

    Smt. Shradhha Gupta W/o Rajesh Kumar Gupta, Aged About 32 Years C/o
     Shailesh Gupta, R/o Vinayak Mobile, Parshuram Chowk, Raj Kishore Nagar,
     Bilaspur Tahsil And District Bilaspur Chhattisgarh

                                                                           ---- Petitioner

                                           Versus

    Rajesh Kumar Gupta S/o Late Shri Mohan Lal Gupta, Aged About 35 Years R/o
     Chetan Chowk, Near Play School, Pendra Tahsil Pendra, District Bilaspur
     Chhattisgarh

                                                                         ---- Respondent</pre>



<p id="p_1">For Petitioner             :     Shri S.K. Dewangan, Advocate
None for the Respondent, though served.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                          Hon'ble Shri Justice Goutam Bhaduri

                                       Order On Board

13/12/2018

</p><p id="p_4">   1. Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">   2. The present petition has been preferred by the wife for transfer of a case

        bearing   civil   suit/case     No.36A/2017,    which   is   preferred    by   the

        husband/respondent under <a href="/doc/1284729/" id="a_1">Section 13</a> of the Hindu Marriage Act for seeking

        divorce from the wife, which is pending before the Upper District Judge, Pendra

        Road to the Family Court, Bilaspur.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">   3.   Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the petitioner is a lady and out

        of the wedlock a child was born and it is extremely difficult for the petitioner to

        travel altogether from Bilaspur to Pendra Road as she is residing at Bilaspur

        and no maintenance has yet been granted to her. He further submits that no
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                2</span>

          prejudice or inconvenience would be caused to the husband/respondent if the

          case is transferred to the Family Court, Bilaspur, therefore, he prays that the

          case may be transferred to the Family Court, Bilaspur.


</p><p id="p_9">       4. No representation is made on behalf of the respondent despite service of

          notice.
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">       5. Perused the documents, contents of the petition and the order-sheet of the

          Court below. It shows that till date no maintenance has been granted to the

          wife.     Considering the same and also taking into that it is always the

          convenience of the wife has to be considered. In the opinion of this Court, in

          the facts of this case, no prejudice would be caused to the husband if the case

          is transferred to the Family Court Bilaspur from the Pendra Road which is 120

          km away only. In view of this, considering the convenience of the wife it is

          directed that the civil suit/case No.36A/2017 pending before the Upper District

          Judge, Pendra Road shall stand transferred to the Family Court, Bilapsur. The

          parties shall appear before the Family Court, Bilaspur on 11 th February, 2019.

          In absence of representation on behalf of the respondent on that date, the

          Family Court, Bilaspur shall issue notice to the respondent for his appearance

          before the Family Court, Bilaspur.
</p>
<p id="p_12">
</p><p id="p_13">       6. With such observation, the petition stands disposed of.


</p><p id="p_14">                                                                      Sd/-
</p>
<p id="p_15">                                                                    Goutam Bhaduri
                                                                        Judge
Ashu
