<p id="p_1">10   05-09-2014                    Heard in respect of Interlocutory Application No. 7834

                    of 2013 and Interlocutory Application No. 2278 of 2014.

</p><p id="p_2">                                   I.A. No. 2278 of 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   This interlocutory application is filed for condoning

                    the delay in filing the substitution petition bearing I.A. No. 7834

                    of 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   The reason for delay in filing the substitution is

                    condoned. Accordingly, the interlocutory application no. 2278 of

                    2014 is disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   I.A. No. 7834 of 2013

                                   This interlocutory application is filed for substitution

                    of legal heirs of sole opposite party, who died leaving behind his

                    legal heirs and representatives as set out in paragraph 3 of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court C.R. No.1221 of 2004 (10) dt.05-09-2014</span>




                     interlocutory application.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                    In spite of issuance of notice upon proposed legal heirs

                     none has appeared.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                    In view of the aforesaid submission, this interlocutory

                     application is allowed. Let the name of the opposite party be

                     deleted from the array of the party and his legal heirs and

                     representatives be substituted as set out in paragraph 3 of the

                     interlocutory application.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                    Learned counsel for the petitioners is directed to take

                     steps for issuing notice to the newly substituted legal heirs as set

                     out in paragraph 3 of interlocutory application no. 7834 of 2013,

                     under registered cover with A/D as well as ordinary process in the

                     admission matter, for which requisite etc. must be filed within two

                     weeks, failing which this civil revision shall stand rejected without

                     further reference to a Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_9">


</p><p id="p_10">                                                              (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J)
Bhardwaj/-
</p>
