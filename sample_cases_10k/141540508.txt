<p id="p_1">	Mr.R.S.
</p><p id="p_2">Sanjanwala, learned Senior Counsel with Mr.Manav Mehta appears for
the respondent and Mr.B.S. Raju, learned counsel appears for the
petitioner.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_3">	It
is pointed out by the learned counsel for the respondent that Company
Petition No.25 of 2011 is already admitted against the
respondent-Company and the respondent-Company, against the said order
of admission of the petition, has filed appeal which is pending for
hearing and incidentally today.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_4">	Hence,
it is requested to defer hearing of this petition.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_5">	In
view of the above, even though it is objected by learned counsel
Mr.Raju, in the interest of justice, the petition is adjourned to
08.05.2012.
</p>
