                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE JYOTI SARAN
                  ORAL ORDER

3   08-08-2016                 A counter affidavit has been filed in CWJC No.11550

                     of 2016 by Mr. Sarwesh Kumar Singh, learned AAG-11.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                     Counter affidavits are also filed in some other cases but as it is

                     informed by the learned State Counsel appearing in the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_9">             Patna High Court CWJC No.11550 of 2016 (3) dt.08-08-2016                       10</span>




                           respective cases that the stand is common hence the counter

                           affidavit filed in CWJC No.11550 of 2016 would be governing

                           all the cases unless any counter affidavit would be discussing

                           any material which is not discussed in the counter affidavit

                           filed in CWJC No.11550 of 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                     Let copies of the counter affidavit be served on the

                           learned counsel for the petitioners to enable them to file their

                           rejoinder by the next date.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                     The appearing State Counsel in the writ petitions

                           where counter affidavit have not yet been filed, may file their

                           counter affidavit in case they wish to bring some fresh material

                           on record.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                     Any action taken by the State Government during the

                           pendency of the matters would be governed by the final

                           outcome of the present proceedings.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                     Put up these matters in the same position on 29.8.2016.
</p>
