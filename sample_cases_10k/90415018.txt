                        JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">     This writ petition is filed seeking the directions to the

6th respondent not to register any documents in respect of

the property covered by Ext.P2 before vacating the orders of

attachments. The petitioner claims to have obtained an

order of attachments in a Civil Suit against the property. The

contention is that proceedings under the Securitisation and

Reconstruction of Financial Assets and Enforcement of

Securities Interest Act had been initiated in respect of the

same property. The question, therefore, would be as to when

the mortgage, which has resulted in the securitisation

proceedings initiated against the property was created.

Since the notice to party respondents 3 and 4 is not

complete, no orders can be passed in this writ petition.


</p><p id="p_2">     In the above view of the matter, this writ petition is

closed leaving open the contention of the petitioner, if any,

which survive for consideration. In case the petitioner has

any subsisting grievance, the same can be agitated in

appropriate proceedings, in accordance with law.
