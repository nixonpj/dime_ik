<div class="doc_title">Vijendra Meshram vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 8 September, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                         M.Cr.C. No.14383 of 2017
                                   08.09.2017
                                          Shri Shishir K. Soni, learned counsel for the applicant.
                                          Shri   D.K.    Paroha    learned    Govt.   Advocate   for   the
                                   respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                          This is first application for grant of bail in connection with
                                   Crime No.01/2017 registered at Police Station Kirnapur District
                                   Balaghat for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">Sections 379</a> and <a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_2">411</a> of
                                   the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/170198/" id="a_3">Section 41</a> (1+4) of the <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_4">Cr.P.C</a>.
</p><p id="p_2">                                          At the very outset learned counsel for the applicant has placed
                                   before this Court the order dated 28.08.2017 passed in M.Cr.C.
                                   No.14132/2017 by which the co-accused Lokchand and others of the
                                   same crime number have been granted the benefit of bail by this
                                   Court. The facts and circumstances of the case of the applicant fall
                                   squarely within the facts and circumstances of the case of the
                                   applicants in M.Cr.C.No.14132/2017.
</p><p id="p_3">                                          Under the circumstances on the ground of parity and the fact
                                   that the applicant has been in judicial custody since 01.08.2017, I am
                                   inclined to allow the application and direct that the applicant - Pitam
                                   be enlarged on bail upon his furnishing personal bond in the sum of
                                   Rs.50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand Only) with one solvent surety
                                   in the like amount to the satisfaction of the Trial Court.
</p><p id="p_4">                                          Accordingly, the application is allowed.
                                          Cc as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                                                  (Atul Sreedharan)
                                                                                        Judge
