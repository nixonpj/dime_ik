<div class="doc_title">Shanti Devi @ Shanti vs The State Of Bihar &amp;Amp; Ors on 7 March, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                     CWJC No. 7210 of 2006
SHANTI DEVI @ SHANTI, W/o Late Banarsi Choudhary, Retired as maid Servant from
Sundarvati Mahila Mahavidyalaya, Bhagalpur under Tilka Manjhi University, Bhagalpur,
Resident of Mohalla - Kathalbari (Surkikal), P.S. - Barari, P.O. - Tilka Manjhi, District -
Bhagalpur.
                                                                       ............... Petitioner
                                             Versus
1. THE STATE OF BIHAR
2. Vice Chancellor, Tilkamanjhi University, Bhagalpur.
3. Finance Officer, Tilkamanjhi University, Bhagalpur.
4. Registrar, Tilkamanjhi University, Bhagalpur.
5. Principal, Sundarwati Mahila Mahavidalaya, Bhagalpur.
                                                               ................. Respondents
                                            -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">3         07.03.2011                    Heard Sri Binoy Kumar, learned counsel for the petitioner,

                         learned counsel appearing on behalf of respondent nos. 2 to 4 / Tilka Manjhi

                         Bhagalpur University and Sri Shashi Bhushan Kumar, learned A.C. to

                         Government Pleader No. 15.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                        The petitioner, who retired long back on 31.10.2003 as Class

                         IV employee from Sundarvati Mahila Mahavidyalaya, Bhagalpur has prayed

                         for directing the respondents to make payment of following dues:

</p><p id="p_3">                                        (i) To pay the petitioner the amount of gratuity which is

                         legally due.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                        (ii) To pay the petitioner the amount of Earn leave salary.

</p><p id="p_5">                                        (iii) To pay the petitioner the Balance amount from group

                         Insurance Scheme which is legally due.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                        (iv) To pay to petitioner the balance amount from Staff

                         welfare Fund.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                        (v) To pay the Balance amount of Provident Fund.

</p><p id="p_8">                                        (vi) To grant and sanction the pension to petitioner and

                         also pay the arrear of the pension.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                        (vii) To pay the petitioner due amount of Arrear of salary
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   2</span>




            since February, 2001 to 31-10-2003.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                          (viii) To pay the Interest at the market rate on delayed

            payment of post retirement benefit and on other dues also and also pay

            the cost of litigation.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                          The writ petition was filed on 27.6.2006. However, till date no

            counter affidavit has been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_12">                          After hearing the parties the petition stands disposed of with

            liberty to the petitioner to file a detailed representation before respondent

            no. 2 i.e. Vice Chancellor, Tilka Manjhi University, Bhagalpur within a

            period of six weeks from today. If such representation is filed, respondent

            no. 2 is directed to examine the same and pass appropriate order in

            accordance with law within a period of two months from the date of filing

            of such representation.
</p>
<p id="p_13">                          The respondent no. 2 is further directed to clear all the

            admitted dues of the petitioner within the aforesaid time.

</p><p id="p_14">                           It is made clear that petitioner shall also be entitle to get

            admissible interest on delayed payment.

</p><p id="p_15">                           In case of refusal, respondent no. 2 is directed to pass

            speaking order within two months from the date of filing of such

            representation.
</p>
<p id="p_16">                          The petition stands disposed of with above observation and

            direction.
</p>
<p id="p_17">
</p><p id="p_18">                                                  ( Rakesh Kumar, J.)


Praful /-
</p>
