                      CHANDIGARH

                                       Crl. Misc. No.M-28599 of 2011 (O &amp; M)
                                       Date of decision: 19.09.2011

Rehana and another
                                                              ..... Petitioners
                       Versus


State of Haryana and others
                                                             ..... Respondents

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJAN GUPTA

Present: Mr. Ravinder Hooda, Advocate,
         for the petitioners.

             ****
</pre>
<p id="p_1">RAJAN GUPTA, J.: (Oral)

             The petitioners have filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C.

seeking protection, as they apprehend danger to their life and liberty at the

hands of respondents No.4 to 6.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             The petitioners claim that both of them are major and have

married each other against the wishes of their parents. Reliance is placed

on judgment reported as Fiaz Ahanger &amp; Ors. State of J &amp; K, 2009(3)

RCR (Civil) 217.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Notice of motion to official respondents.

             On asking of the court, Mr. Sidharth Sarup, Deputy Advocate

General, Haryana, accepts notice.
</p>
<p id="p_4">            Without expressing any opinion with regard to age or validity of

marriage of the petitioners, the instant petition is disposed of with a

direction to the Superintendent of Police, Palwal, District Palwal/respondent

No.2 to look into the representation, Annexure P-6, preferred by the

petitioners and take appropriate steps if circumstances so warrant.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                     (RAJAN GUPTA)
                                                         JUDGE
September 19, 2011
sukhpreet
  </p>
