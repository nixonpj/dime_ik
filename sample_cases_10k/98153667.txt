                   In CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. 734 of 2007

     SURESHKUMAR @ SURIYO DEVENDRABHAI PARMAR....Applicant(s)

                 and
                 HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.J.THAKER

                             Date : 14/03/2014


                              ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">        (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE KS JHAVERI)


</p><p id="p_2">1.           By   way   of   this   application,   the   applicant 

has prayed for the following reliefs:­



       (A) this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to  
       admit and allow this application;


       (B) this Hon'ble Court may kindly be pleased to  
       quash   and   set   aside   the   order   passed   by   the 
       Hon'ble Sessions Court and further be pleased to  



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 Page 1 of 3</span>
        R/CR.MA/3691/2014                            ORDER



     modify   the   order   passed   by   this   Hon'ble   Court 
     dated 3/7/2013 in Criminal Appeal no.734 / 2007  
     to the extent that to grant time of one week to  
     the applicant to enable him to pay the fine of  
     Rs.10,000/­ before the Hon'ble Sessions Court and 
     further   be   pleased   to   release   the   applicant   on  
     appropriate terms and conditions in the interest  
     of justice.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
     (C) Pending   admission,   hearing   and   final 
     disposal of the present application, this Hon'ble 
     Court   may   kindly   be   pleased   to   direct   the  
     respondent to release the applicant from jail in  
     the interest of justice.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
     (D) Xxx

     (E ) xxx".
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">2.         From   the  perusal   of   the  record,   it   appears 

that this Court vide judgment dated 03.07.2013 passed 

in  Criminal   Appeal   No.   734   of   2007  reduced   the 

conviction of the present applicant to the period of 

imprisonment   already   undergone   by   him,   however,   the 

fine imposed by the Trial Court was enhanced by this 

Court   from   Rs.   500/­   to   Rs.10,000/­.   This   Court 

directed the applicant to pay the fine within a period 

of   eight   weeks   from   the   date   of   passing   of   the 



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                            Page 2 of 3</span>
         R/CR.MA/3691/2014                            ORDER



judgment. However, the applicant could not comply the 

judgment of this Court and could not  pay the fine of 

Rs.10,000/­ before the competent Court.



</p><p id="p_7">3.          In that view of the matter, this application 

requires   to   be   allowed   and   the   same   is   accordingly 

allowed.   The   applicant   shall   pay   the   fine   of 

Rs.10,000/­   before   the   Competent   Court   and   upon 

depositing   the   said   amount,   the   applicant   will   be 

released   pursuant   to   the   earlier   judgment   passed   in 

Criminal   Appeal   No.734   of   2007.     Direct   service   is 

permitted. 
</p>
