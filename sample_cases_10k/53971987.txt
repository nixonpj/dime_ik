<div class="doc_title">Sridhar Singh &amp; Ors vs Sri Sri 108 Laxmi Narain &amp; Ors on 4 July, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                       SA No.284 of 2004
                                   SRIDHAR SINGH &amp; ORS
                                              Versus
                             SRI SRI 108 LAXMI NARAIN &amp; ORS
                                            -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1"> 06/   04.07.2008             Heard learned counsel for the appellants.

</p><p id="p_2">                              This appeal will be heard. Admit.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              Following     substantial   questions   of      law   arise   for

                    consideration in this appeal :-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                              (i)           Whether the assumption of the learned court of

                                            appeal below that the suit premises was an

                                            endowed property was based on any valid

                                            material ?
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">                              (ii)          Whether the learned lower appellate court

                                            misconstrued and misread Ext. 3 and Ext. 3/A

                                            with perversity while reversing the judgment

                                            and decree of the trial court ?
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">                              (iii)         Whether while setting aside the entries in the

                                            revisional survey khatian, learned lower

                                            appellate court did not give any reason for the

                                            same ?</blockquote><p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                               Issue notice to the respondents under ordinary process on

                    Talbana etc. being filed within one week, failing this, this appeal shall

                    stand dismissed without further reference to a Bench.

</p><p id="p_6">                              Call for the lower court records.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><pre id="pre_2">MPS/                                          ( S. N. Hussain, J. )
