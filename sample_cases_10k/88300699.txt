<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">Ct.No. 13</span>
aa.
</p><p id="p_2">                               CRM No. 11734 of 2014

              In re : Prahlad Ghosh and Others
                                                                  ... Petitioners
(Allowed
Partly)
              Re: An application under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Code of Criminal
                   Procedure filed on 07.09.2014 in connection with Nabadwip
                   Police Station Case No. 33 of 2011 dated 18.02.2011 under
                   <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_2">Sections 147</a>/<a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_3">148</a>/<a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_4">149</a>/<a href="/doc/488479/" id="a_5">186</a>/<a href="/doc/1208971/" id="a_6">353</a>/<a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_7">324</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_8">307</a> of the Indian Penal
                   Code read with <a href="/doc/1056565/" id="a_9">Sections 3</a> /<a href="/doc/895566/" id="a_10">4</a> of the Explosive Substances Act.

             Mr. Sudip Ghosh
             Mr. Apurba Kr. Datta
                                                           ...for the petitioners
             Mr. Angsuman Chakraborty
                                                                ... for the State



                     It is submitted on behalf of the petitioners that the

             petitioners are innocent of the allegations made against them.

</p><p id="p_3">                     We have heard the learned counsel for the State and

             perused the materials available in the case diary. Chargesheet

             has been submitted and investigation is complete.

</p><p id="p_4">                     On consideration of the materials available in the case

             diary, we are inclined to grant anticipatory bail to the petitioner

             no. 2 ( 2. Dipankar Ghosh ) and we are not inclined to grant

             anticipatory bail to the other petitioners.

</p><p id="p_5">                     In such circumstances, it is ordered that in the event of

             arrest the petitioner no. 2     ( 2. Dipankar Ghosh ) shall be
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                        2</span>


released on bail upon furnishing a bond of Rs. 5,000/- (Rupees

Five thousand only) with one surety of like amount to the

satisfaction of the learned Judicial Magistrate (1st Class),

Nabadwip, subject to the condition as laid down under <a href="/doc/1568384/" id="a_11">Section

438</a> (2)<a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_12"> of the Criminal Procedure Code</a>.

</p><p id="p_6">       The prayer for anticipatory bail of the petitioner nos. 1, 3

and 4 is rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_7">       The application for anticipatory bail is, thus, disposed of.



</p><p id="p_8">                             ( Tapan Kumar Dutt, J. )



                                      ( R. K. Bag, J. )
