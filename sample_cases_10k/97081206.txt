<div class="doc_title">Nawaj Ahmad vs State Of Bihar &amp; Anr on 15 May, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.57200 of 2015
                   Arising Out of PS.Case No. -390 Year- 2014 Thana -MADHUBANI COMPLAINT CASE District-
                                                           MADHUBANI
                   ======================================================
                   Nawaj Ahmad, son of Abu Zabar, Resident of Village- Damla, P.S.- Bisfi,
                   District- Madhubani.
                                                                        .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                   1. The State of Bihar.
                   2. Arshabari @ Daizi Wife of Md. Zakir Hussian, Resident of Mohalla-
                      Singhania Chowk, P.S.- Nagar Town, District- Madhubani.
                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Kripa Nand Jha
                   For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Shailendra Kumar No.1(App)
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                   SINGH
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">02/   15-05-2017                             Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                   Mr. J.N. Thakur, learned counsel for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                             The present application has been filed for

                   quashing the order dated 05.08.2014 passed by learned Sub-

                   divisional Judicial Magistrate, Madhubani in C.R. No. 6054 of

                   2014/390 of 2014, whereby process has been directed to be issued

                   after cognizance being taken for the offences punishable under

                   <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_2">379</a>, <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_3">498A</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and

                   <a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_6">Section 4</a> of Dowry Prohibition Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                             It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                   petitioner that the petitioner filed Complaint Case No. 245 of 2014

                   as contained in Annexure-3 against the complainant on 03.03.2014

                   levelling accusation of assault and one month thereafter the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.57200 of 2015 (2) dt.15-05-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                    present complaint was filed by the complainant on 03.04.2014.

                    The complainant-opposite party no. 2 has performed second

                    marriage with one Md. Zakir Hussain on 15.06.2013 and the said

                    Zakir Hussain has filed Matrimonial Suit No. 113 of 2014 in the

                    court of learned Principal Judge, Family Court, Madhubani as

                    contained in Annexure-4 for restitution of conjugal life.

</p><p id="p_4">                                               A statement has been made in paragraph 3 of

                    the supplementary affidavit dated 15.05.2017 that the charges

                    have not been framed as yet.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                               Issue notice to opposite party no. 2 by

                    ordinary process as well as by registered cover with A/D for which

                    requisites must be filed within two weeks, failing which this

                    application shall stand dismissed without further reference to the

                    Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                                 In the meantime, further proceedings of C.R.

                    No. 6054 of 2014/390 of 2014 pending in the court of learned

                    Sub-divisional Judicial Magistrate, Madhubani with regard to the

                    petitioner shall remain stayed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                               (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
    DKS/-
</p>
