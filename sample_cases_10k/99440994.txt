<div class="doc_title">Kanwarpal Singh Grewal &amp; Another vs State Of Punjab &amp; Others on 8 February, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">   IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                                             CRM-M-2515-2018
                                             Date of decision : 8.2.2018

Kanwarpal Singh Grewal alias Kanwar Grewal and another
                                                                     ... Petitioners

                               VERSUS

State of Punjab and others
                                                                   ... Respondents

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJ SHEKHAR ATTRI

Present:         Mr. Gaurav Chopra, Advocate,
                 for the petitioner.

        Mr. V.G.Jauhar, Sr. DAG, Punjab
             *****
</pre><p id="p_1">RAJ SHEKHAR ATTRI, J.(Oral)


                 This petition has been filed for grant of permission to the

petitioners to travel abroad.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                 However, learned State counsel has informed that FIR No.97

dated 26.10.2016 under <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_1">Sections 384</a>, <a href="/doc/1185693/" id="a_2">511</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_3">120-B</a> Indian Penal Code was

registered against the petitioner but after investigation, cancellation report

was submitted which is pending.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                 In view of the above, this petition is allowed and the petitioners

are granted permission to travel abroad for four months starting from

15.2.2018 subject to the conditions, if any, to be imposed by the

Investigating Officer.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                                             ( RAJ SHEKHAR ATTRI)
February 8, 2018                                     JUDGE
Paritosh Kumar
