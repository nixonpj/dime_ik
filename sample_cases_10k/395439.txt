ORAL

<p id="p_1">	This
application has been preferred by the applicants for fixing
peremptorily date of final hearing of the Criminal Appeal against the
order of conviction and sentence passed in Sessions Case No.97 of
2002 passed by the learned Fast Track Judge, Modasa.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">	Mr.
</p><p id="p_3">Pandit, learned advocate for the appellant No.3 - Balawantbhai
Vadaji Chauhan submitted that there is a question of survival of the
applicant and his family as the applicant has got appointment order
on compassionate ground from the Govt. of Gujarat. But due to order
of conviction and sentence, the Govt. Department made question in
connection of the posting of the applicant No.3 as the Appeal is
pending and the applicant is simply involved due to rioting case.
Therefore, he prays to allow this application by fixing the Criminal
Appeal.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_4">	I
have heard Mr. Pandit, learned advocate as well as Mr. H.L. Jani,
learned APP for the State. I have perused the contents of the
application along with papers. After considering the facts and
circumstances of the case, I am of the view that the Criminal Appeal
is required to be listed for final hearing on 27.12.2010. Hence, the
said Criminal Appeal be listed for final hearing on 27.12.2010. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_5">	In
view of the above, the application stands disposed of.
</p> 

 


 

 


 

<p id="p_6">(Z.K.SAIYED,J.)
