<p id="p_1">             Present petition has been filed praying for protection to the

life and liberty of the petitioners, in view of the marriage solemnized by

them against the wishes of respondents No. 4 to 7.

</p><p id="p_2">2.           Notice of motion to respondents No. 1 to 3.

</p><p id="p_3">3.           Mr. H.S. Sandhu, AAG, Punjab, accepts notice on behalf of

respondent Nos.1 to 3 and three copies of the petition have been supplied

to him in the Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.           The documents annexed by the petitioners including the

Aadhar Cards shows that both are major and are present in Court also
