<p id="p_1">This review petition has been filed by the applicants for
correction in the order dated 27/03/2017 passed by this
Court in W.P. No.10853/2012.
</p><p id="p_2">Learned counsel for the applicants submits that due to
inadvertent in the second line of para-1 of the order after
the word permanent employee in place of 19.11.1993 it
should be 27/07/1993, in the third line of this para in
place of 27/07/1993, it should be 13/01/2000 and
01/04/1999 and in sixth line of this para after word
arrears of pay, the word 'till date' has wrongly been
mentioned which needs to be deleted. Similarly, in
second paragraph of the order, in first line in place of
01/04/1999 and 29/11/2000, it should be 13/01/2000 and
01/04/1999. Also in last paragraph of the order, in place
of Annexure-P/1, it should be Annexures-P/3 and P/4. He,
therefore, prays that the aforesaid clerical error may be
corrected.
</p><p id="p_3">In view of aforesaid, the MCC is allowed and in the the
