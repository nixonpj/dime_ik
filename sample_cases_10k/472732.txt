                     Dated this the   27th  day of September, 2007




                                         AWARD





        Counsel for  the appellant, counsel for the Insurance Company and


Officers   of   the   Insurance   Company   are   present.     After   elaborate


discussion, it is agreed by both parties that the Insurance Company will pay


a   sum   of   Rs.60,000/-   (Rupees   Sixty   thousand   only)   over   and   above   the


amount already deposited, within two months in full settlement of the claim.


If the amount is not deposited within the time the amount will carry interest


at   9%   per   annum.     The   appeal   is   disposed   of   as   settled   between   the


parties.
