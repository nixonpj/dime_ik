<p id="p_1">1.		Learned
Advocate, Mr.G Ramakrishnan, for respondent No.2 - company
states that there is another petition being SCA No.1835 of 2009 filed
by the Provident Fund Department, against the company on the same
issue of damages and interest on the PF contribution.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.		In
the present matter, though Notice was served to respondent
No.1-Regional Provident Fund Commissioner Gujarat State, nobody
appears for respondent No.1. Learned Advocate, Mr.B T Rao, to take
instructions in this regard.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_3">3.		
Learned Advocate, Mr.G Ramakrishnan, for respondent No.2 -
company states company is not able to dispose of the land in question
as this Court has already injuncted them from disposing of the same
without furnishing Bank Guarantee. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_4">4.		Registry
is directed to notify this matter alongwith SCA no.1835 of 2009 for
perusal on 23/08/2011.
</p>
