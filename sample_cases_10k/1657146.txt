<p id="p_1">      In terms of Annexure I judgment, the petitioner was to pay

three instalments of Rs.2,00,000/- each, the last among which

was to be paid on 31.3.2009, The intention was, such amounts

ought to be with the bank before the end of that financial year.

Admittedly, the first instalment was paid only on 4.4.2009. I do

not find any ground of contempt being made to charge the

respondents of having not carried out the further directions in

the judgment. This case is hence closed, without prejudice to

other remedies, if available in law.
</p>
