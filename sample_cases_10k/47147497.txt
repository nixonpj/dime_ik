 sl.26.
           Mr. Kajal Ray
                              ----- for the petitioners.

           Mr. Srijon Nayak
                          ----- for the respondent-licensee.</pre>

<p id="p_1">           Mr. Tapas Kumar Ghosh
           Mr. Tanmoy Chowdhury
</p><p id="p_2">                         ----- for the respondents no.3 &amp; 4.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                  The writ petitioners are admittedly in occupation of two shop rooms for which
           they have applied for electricity connection. The ground of objection of the private
           respondents/landlords is that although the lease was granted to the writ petitioners to
           run a shop room they are proposing to run an hotel there which is not permissible.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                  It will be fully open to the landlords to pursue this ground in any other forum
           if they are so advised, but this objection cannot be a ground for denial of electricity to
           the writ petitioners. Under those circumstances I do not think that affidavits are called
           for in the matter as the ground of objection is just what I have enumerated above.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                  I dispose of this writ application by directing the respondent licensee to grant
           electricity connection to the writ petitioners as per their application and upon
           compliance with all formalities within a period of two weeks from the date of
           communication of this order, keeping all other issues like unlawful trade and
           occupation open.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                  Since the respondent licensee has not used any affidavit, the allegations
           contained in the writ petition are deemed not to be admitted.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                  Let photocopies of this order, duly countersigned by the Assistant Registrar
           (Court), be given to the parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.
</p>



<p id="p_8">                                                (I. P. Mukerji, J.)
