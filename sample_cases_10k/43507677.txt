<div class="doc_title">Bharat Bhushan And Another vs State Of Punjab And Another on 5 December, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">Criminal Misc. No.M-6040 of 2011                                      1


IN THE HIGH COURT FOR THE STATES OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                    AT CHANDIGARH.


                                   Criminal Misc. No.M-6040 of 2011
                                   Date of Decision: December 05, 2011



Bharat Bhushan and another

                                                            ....Petitioners
                                 Versus
State of Punjab and another
                                                         .....Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">CORAM:     HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE NARESH KUMAR SANGHI


Present:   Mr. Parshotam Lal Singla, Advocate
           for the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_2">           Mr. Munish Bansal, Asstt. Advocate General, Punjab

           Mr. Onkar Rai, Advocate
           for respondent No.2.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
NARESH KUMAR SANGHI, J (ORAL)


           On February 25, 2011 the following order was passed:

           "The present petition under Section 482, <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>. has been

           filed by the petitioners, namely, Bharat Bhushan and Chander

           Prakash for quashing of FIR No.243 dated 25.12.2010

           registered under <a href="/doc/1099980/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>/<a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_3">120-B</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a> at Police Station

           Phillaur, District Jalandhar, on the basis of compromise

           (Annexure P-2).
</p>
<p id="p_4">           Notice of motion to the respondents for 07.04.2011.

           Meanwhile, parties are directed to be present before the Illaqa

           Magistrate/trial Court on 09.03.2011 or any other date

           convenient to the Court for recording their statements with
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Criminal Misc. No.M-6040 of 2011                                           2</span>


            regard to compromise.      The Illaqa Magistrate/trial Court is

            directed to record the statements of both the parties to its

            satisfaction to know its genuineness that the statements are

            not the result of any pressure or coercion in any manner. The

            Illaqa Magistrate/Trial Court is also directed to send a report

            along with statements of the parties with regard to validity or

            otherwise of the compromise effected between the parties and

            also intimate whether any case is pending against either of the

            parties or not before the next date of hearing."




            However, the parties could not appear before the learned trial

Court in compliance of the above order and hence they were relegated to

appear on 08.09.2011. In compliance of order dated 29.07.2011, passed

by this Court, the report from learned Sub-Divisional Judicial Magistrate,

Phillaur through learned District and Sessions Judge, Jalandhar has been

received.   According to which complainant Darshan Ram and the

petitioners had appeared and admitted the factum of compromise.

</p><p id="p_5">            Learned    counsel   for   the   petitioners   and   counsel   for

respondent No.2 have admitted that the compromise was effected and

the parties had appeared before the learned trial Court and made their

respective statements.
</p>
<p id="p_6">            The offences under <a href="/doc/1099980/" id="a_5">Sections 420</a> and <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_6">120-B</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_7">IPC</a> allegedly

committed by the petitioners are compoundable and the parties have

amicably settled the dispute.
</p>
<p id="p_7">            In view of the above, FIR No.243 dated 25.12.2010 registered

under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_8">Sections 420</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_9">120-B</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_10">IPC</a> at Police Station Phillaur, District
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> Criminal Misc. No.M-6040 of 2011                               3</span>


Jalandhar and consequent proceedings emanating therefrom are hereby

quashed.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><pre id="pre_2">05.12.2011                          (NARESH KUMAR SANGHI)
vcgarg                                  JUDGE
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3"> Criminal Misc. No.M-6040 of 2011   4</span>
