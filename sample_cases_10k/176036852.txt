<div class="doc_title">Arun Kumar Gupta @ Bablu vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 14 September, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                       MCRC-15754-2015
       (ARUN KUMAR GUPTA @ BABLU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


14-09-2015
Shri Ashok Kumar Gupta, Advocate for the applicant.
Shri Ajay Shukla, Public Prosecutor for the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Case diary is not available, however, learned counsel for the
applicant submitted copies of challan papers.
Heard finally.
</p><p id="p_2">This is first bail application on behalf of the applicant under
<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_3">The applicant is in custody since 06/08/2015 in connection with
Crime No.146/2015 registered at Police Station Mauganj, District
Rewa (M.P.) for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_2">Section 379</a> of IPC.
It is submitted by learned counsel for the applicant that the
applicant is innocent and has been falsely implicated in the case. It
is further submitted that the applicant is a young youth of 26
years having no criminal antecedent. The applicant is in custody
and conclusion of trial would take considerable time. On the
aforesaid grounds, it is prayed that the applicant be released on
bail.
</p><p id="p_4">Learned Public Prosecutor for the State vehemently opposed the
application.
</p><p id="p_5">Considering the totality of the facts and circumstances of the case
coupled with the material available on record, without commenting
upon the merits of the case, the application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_3">Section 439</a> of
Cr.P.C. on behalf of applicant deserves to be and is hereby
allowed.
</p><p id="p_6">It is directed that applicant Arun Kumar Gupta @ Bablu be
released on bail on furnishing a personal bond in the sum of
Rs.25,000/-(Rupees Twenty Five Thousand Only) with one
solvent surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the
 committal Court/trial Court securing applicant's presence before
the trial Court on all the dates of hearing fixed in this regard
during trial.
</p><p id="p_7">     Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                                              (SUBHASH KAKADE)
                                                    JUDGE
