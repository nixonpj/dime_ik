                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD


             CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR QUASHING &amp; SET ASIDE
                                  FIR/ORDER) NO. 1381 of 2017

         ==========================================================
                  MOINKHAN ATAULLAKHAN PATHAN &amp; 1....Applicant(s)
                                     Versus
                       STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MR MM TIRMIZI, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1-2
         MS SHRUTI PATHAK, APP for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
         ==========================================================

                CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE J.B.PARDIWALA

                                       Date : 23/01/2017
                                         ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">               It is too early for this Court to take the view, whether any
         offence under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_1">Section 307</a> of the Indian Penal Code is made
         out or not ?
</p>
<p id="p_2">               The investigation is still in progress. It is for the
         Investigating Officer to consider all the relevant aspects of the
         matter before filing the charge-sheet for a particular offence.
         Ultimately, if charge-sheet is filed for the offence under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_2">Section
         307</a> of the Indian Penal Code and no case is made out of any
         attempt to commit murder, it shall be open for the applicants
         to challenge the charge-sheet before the appropriate forum in
         accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_3">               With the above, this application is disposed of. Direct
         service is permitted.</p><pre id="pre_2">
