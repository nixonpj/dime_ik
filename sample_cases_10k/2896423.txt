                          JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">     The petitioner applied for a temporary permit in

respect of a stage carriage bearing registration No.

KL-25/A-911 on the route Anayadi-Punalur. The petitioner

submits that it is an inter-district route and the

concurrence has already been obtained. Ext.P3 is the

application   for   temporary    permit    preferred    by the

petitioner in this connection.


</p><p id="p_2">     If that be so, there shall be a direction to the third

respondent to consider Ext.P3 application within two weeks

from the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment and if

there is no legal impediment, the permit shall be granted

within time.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     The writ petition is disposed of as above.
