                      HAZARI, DELHI

                                             Date of Institution : 03.02.2012
                                              Date of decision : 24.03.2015


KAMLA DEVI
W/o Late Jagan Nath

9.       BUM BUM HARI SHANKAR

         Respondent nos. 3 to 5 are daughters and
         6 to 9 are sons of late Jagan Nath,
         All are R/o 397, T-Huts, Rakhi Market,
         Rama Road, Zakhira, Delhi.                      ... Respondents




SC No. 12/2012                                                     Page 1 of 4
 JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">1.       The succession petition is filed by Smt. Kamla Devi for grant of
     succession certificate in respect of debts and securities of deceased who
     died on 04.09.2010. As per the petition, deceased has left behind his wife
     Smt. Kamla Devi, his four sons namely Ram Shankar Gupta, Sh. Prem
     Shankar Gupta, Sh. Sada Shiv Shankar Gupta, Bum Bum Hari Shankar
     Gupta(minor) and three daughters namely Smt. Gudia Devi, Smt. Meena
     Devi, Ms. Kanchan(minor) only as Class-I legal heirs.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.       After filing the petition, notice of the petition was given to the general
     public by publication in the newspaper 'Veer Arjun' dated 17.09.2013 but
     none appeared from general public to oppose or contest the petition.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3.       Statement of petitioner Smt. Kamla Devi was recorded on
     24.03.2015 wherein she stated that the deceased Sh. Jagan Nath was
     her husband who expired on 04.09.2010. His original death certificate is
     Ex.PW1/1. Parents of deceased also expired long back. Deceased has
     left behind herself, his four sons namely Ram Shankar Gupta, Sh. Prem
     Shankar Gupta, Sh. Sada Shiv Shankar Gupta, Bum Bum Hari Shankar
     Gupta(minor) and three daughters namely Smt. Gudia Devi, Smt. Meena
     Devi, Ms. Kanchan(minor) only as Class-I legal heirs. There is no other
     class-I legal heir except as stated above. Deceased had not executed
     any Will during his life time.
</p><p id="p_4">         Deceased was having one SB account bearing No. 188694 with
     Punjab National Bank, Shastri Nagar Branch, New Delhi. Photocopy of
     passbook in respect of aforesaid bank account is Ex.PW1/2. Original of
     the same was returned after perusal. Petitioner produced her Election I-
     card for identification. Photocopy of the same is Ex.PW1/3. Original of
     the same was returned after perusal.
</p>


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">SC No. 12/2012                                                           Page 2 of 4</span>
<p id="p_5"> 4.       Statement of respondent no. 2 Sh. H. L. Soni, Officer, PNB, Shashtri
     Nagar, Delhi was recorded on 31.07.2012 who produced the details in
     respect of SB Account bearing No. 1398000100188694 in the name of
     Sh. Jagannath Gupta which carries closing balance of Rs.3,19,491/- as
     on 04.03.2012. Statement of account in respect of aforesaid SB account
     is Ex.RW2/1 bearing signatures of Sh. T.R. Verma, Manager, Sh. Suresh
     Kumar, Chief Manager and seal of the bank.
</p>

<p id="p_6">5.       Statements of respondent no. 6 to 8 namely Sh. Ram Shankar, Sh.
</p><p id="p_7">     Prem Shankar, Sh. Sada Shiv Shankar were recorded on 21.11.2012
     wherein they filed their respective NOCs Ex.RW6/1, Ex.RW7/1 and
     Ex.RW8/1 in favour of petitioner in respect of debts and securities of
     deceased. Photocopies of their election I-card and Aadhar cards are
     Ex.RW6/2, Ex.RW7/2 and Ex.RW8/2. Originals of the same were
     returned after perusal.
</p><p id="p_8">         Statements of respondent no. 3 and 4 namely Gudia Devi and Munia
     Devi were recorded on 29.05.2012 wherein they filed their respective
     NOCs Ex.RW3/1 and Ex.RW4/1 in favour of petitioner           in respect of
     debts and securities of deceased.
</p>

<p id="p_9">6.       It is pertinent to mention here that objections have not been filed on
     behalf of general public despite publication in the newspaper 'Veer Arjun'
     dated 17.09.2013.
</p>

<p id="p_10">7.       In the facts and circumstances on record, petitioner Smt. Kamla Devi
     only shall be entitled in respect of 6/8th share of debts and securities of
     the deceased as per Ex.PW1/2 and Ex.RW2/1.
</p>

<p id="p_11">1.       In view of the matter as discussed above, I am of the considered
     opinion that there is no impediment in grant of succession certificate in
     favour of petitioner Smt. Kamla Devi in respect of 6/8th share of debts


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">SC No. 12/2012                                                        Page 3 of 4</span>
    and securities of the deceased as per Ex.PW1/2 and Ex.RW2/1. I
   accordingly direct that succession certificate be issued in the name of
   Smt. Kamla Devi in respect of 6/8th share of debts and securities of the
   deceased as per Ex.PW1/2 and Ex.RW2/1. Remaining 1/8th share each
   of Ms. Kanchan and Bum Bum Hari Shankar stands reserved and shall
   be deposited in a nationalized bank in the form of FDR and the same be
   released to them after their attaining majority.
</p><p id="p_12">         Court fees and indemnity bond with one surety shall be filed by the
   parties qua their respective share within 15 days. No further order is
   required to be passed. File be consigned to the Record Room.
</p>

<p id="p_13">Announced in the open court
On this 24th day of March, 2015
                                          (Sumedh Kumar Sethi)
                                          ACJ/CCJ/ARC(West)
                                          Tis Hazari, Delhi
