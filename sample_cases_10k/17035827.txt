<pre id="pre_1">        C/SCA/5608/2018                              ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

          R/SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 5608 of 2018

==========================================================
                      ZAVERBHAI PACHABHAI VARIYA
                                Versus
                          STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
MS. KRUTI M SHAH(2428) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1
for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1,2,3,4
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J. SHASTRI

                            Date : 10/04/2018

                             ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.   Present petition under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of
India is filed for the purpose of seeking following reliefs:
</p>

<blockquote id="blockquote_1">     "9(A) This Hon'ble Court may be pleased to issue
     appropriate writ, order or direction to the respondents
     for reconstruction of general register No.2 and 3 at
     Serial No.1172 to 3654 maintained by Khadadhar
     Primary School and further be pleased to issue direction
     to issue school leaving certificate to the petitioner.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">     9(B) Grant such other and further relief as thought fit in
     the interest of justice."
</blockquote>
<p id="p_2">2.   At the outset, Ms. K. M. Shah, learned advocate for the
petitioner has stated that despite the fact that on number of
occasions the petitioner had approached various authorities
but, so far, request of reconstruction of school leaving
certificate has not been considered.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3.   Considering the communications which are attached to


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                Page 1 of 2</span>
         C/SCA/5608/2018                                      ORDER



the petition compilation, it appears that there is some
difficulty as the record is old.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4.    However, it would be open for the petitioner to approach
the District Primary Education Officer to make appropriate
request by submitting the application preferably within a
period of ten days from today and as and when such request is
made,    the     authority   is     directed    to    take   appropriate
measurement to see that the institution can provide such
school leaving certificate           within a period of six weeks
thereafter in reconstructed form as sufficient powers are
vested with the authorities to direct the institution to take
effective steps for the purpose of reconstruction of school
leaving certificate.
</p>

<p id="p_5">5.    With this observation, the present petition stands
disposed of as not pressed.
</p>



<p id="p_6">                                                     (A.J. SHASTRI, J)
ila
