<div class="doc_title">WP(C)/9061/2014 on 7 May, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                        W.P.(C) No.9061 of 2014




3.     7.05.2018            Heard Shri S.K. Das, learned counsel for the petitioner
                   and Shri D. Mohapatra, learned Standing Counsel for the
                   School &amp; Mass Education Department.
                            The dispute raised herein appears to be set at rest by
                   virtue    of   the   judgment    of   this   Court   in   W.P.(C)
                   Nos.7504/3453/1593 of 2014, but however, supporting the
                   case of the petitioner. The only thing left herein to be worked
                   out is that the claim of the petitioner is required to be
                   considered in the light of the judgment passed in W.P.(C)
                   Nos.7504/3453/1593 of 2014.
                            With this observation only, this writ petition stands
                   disposed of directing the opposite parties to prepare a fresh list
                   keeping in mind the reservation of 50% at the maximum and
                   the direction contained herein; be worked within a period of
                   one month from the date of communication of a certified copy
                   of this order by the parties concerned.
                            The writ petition stands disposed of with the above
                   direction.
