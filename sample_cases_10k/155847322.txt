                      IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA

                       Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction

                                ORIGINAL SIDE



                            UTKARSH INDIA LIMITED
                                    Versus
                            THE STATE OF MIZORAM


  BEFORE:

  The Hon'ble JUSTICE ARIJIT BANERJEE</pre>

<p id="p_1">  Date : 4th April, 2019.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                 APPEARANCE:
</p><p id="p_4">                                                  Mr. Suddhasatva Banerjee,Adv.
</p><p id="p_5">                                                         Mr. Chayan Gupta,Adv.
</p><p id="p_6">                                                              Mr. A. Dutta,Adv.
</p><p id="p_7">                                                           Mr. Dipraj Basu,Adv.

<p id="p_8">                                                         Mr. Bikash Kr. Ray,Adv.
</p><p id="p_9">                                                                 Mr. Arif Ali,Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_10">      The Court :- In the last two orders dated 20th February, 2019 and 14th

March, 2019 it was erroneously recorded that the amount admitted to be payable

to the decree-holder by the Government of Mizoram is Rs. 92,17,470/-. The

actual admitted amount is Rs. 1,04,91,006 as would appear from the letter of

State of Mizoram dated 1st November,2018 (page 66 of the execution petition).

</p><p id="p_11">      In spite of the earlier orders, no money has been paid by the judgment-

debtor to the decree-holder. I was minded to issue a warrant of arrest since even
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                              2</span>


an affidavit of asset in proper format has not been filed. However, being

persuaded by the elocution of Mr. Arif Ali, learned Advocate, I grant a final

opportunity to the State of Mizoram to pay the admitted sum of Rs. 1,04,91,006

to the decree-holder/petitioner within a fortnight from date. The acceptance of

the said amount by the decree-holder will be without prejudice to its rights and

contentions in the pending proceeding. This time period is peremptory. Affidavit

of assets in proper format shall also be filed on the returnable date. In default of

either, warrant of arrest is likely to be issued against the responsible officer of the

Government of Mizoram.
</p>
<p id="p_12">            List the matter under the same heading on 25th April, 2019.




</p><p id="p_13">                                                      (ARIJIT BANERJEE, J.)




S.Chandra
