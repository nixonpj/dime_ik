          CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 27807 of
                                                 2017

         ==========================================================
          IMDADULLAH ABDULKARIMBHAI SHAIKH THROUGH VISHWASKUMAR
                     SURENDRAKUMAR YADAV....Applicant(s)
                                   Versus
                     STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MS GAYATRIBA B JADEJA, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR PRANAV TRIVEDI, ADDL. PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the Respondent(s)
         No. 1
         ==========================================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.H.VORA

                                      Date : 17/11/2017


                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.   Rule. Learned APP waives service of notice of rule for
         the respondent State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.   By way of this application, the applicant seeks temporary
         bail of 10 days' without police surveillance in respect of the
         offences registered with Ganagajadiya Police Station being
         C.R. No.72 of 2016.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.   Upon      hearing      the      submissions            made         at     bar      and
         considering the order dated 30.10.2017 passed by the
         Division Bench of this Court in Criminal Misc. Application (for
         Temporary Bail) No.26166 of 2017, present application
         requires consideration for grant of temporary bail for a period

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                              Page 1 of 2</span>

HC-NIC                                     Page 1 of 2      Created On Sat Nov 18 01:44:28 IST 2017
                      R/CR.MA/27807/2017                                            ORDER




         of 10 days on the same terms and conditions as imposed by
         the Division Bench of this Court.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.        Accordingly, the application is allowed. The applicant is
         ordered to be released on temporary bail for a period of 10
         days from the date of his actual release on his furnishing
         personal bond of the amount of Rs.5000/- and on usual terms
         and conditions, which may be imposed by the jail authority.
         The applicant shall surrender before the jail authority
         immediately after expiry of the aforesaid period, without fail.
         Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent. Direct service
         is permitted today.
</p>
