<div class="doc_title">Kok Singh vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 2 May, 2019</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                            1</span>

              HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH

                           MCRC No.12961/2019

                   (Kok Singh Vs The State of M.P.)

Gwalior, Dated : 02.05.2019

      Shri Pawan Vijaywargiya, learned counsel for the applicant.

      Shri Aditya Sharma, learned Panel Lawyer for the State.

      Shri Rajiv Upadhyay, learned counsel for the complainant.

      The applicant has filed this third bail application u/S.439,

<a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>. for grant of bail after rejection of earlier one which was

rejected by order dated 06.03.2019 passed in M.Cr.C.No.9447/2019

      The applicant has been arrested by Police Station Gohad,

District Bhind in connection with Crime No.190/2018 registered in

relation to the offences punishable u/Ss. 323, 294, 506, 34 of <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a> and

further added <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">Sections 324</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_4">326</a> and <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">307</a> of IPC.

</p><p id="p_2">      Learned counsel for the applicant submits that the applicant has

been falsely implicated. He is in custody since 26.12.2018. Charge

sheet has been filed and the investigation has been completed.

Allegation against the applicant is that he has caused injury to the

complainant by kicks and fists. The trial will take time to conclude.

There is no possibility of his absconding or tampering with the

prosecution case. Under these circumstances, counsel for the applicant

prays for grant of bail.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      Learned Panel Lawyer for the State as well as counsel for the

complainant opposed the prayer on the ground that the statements of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                          2</span>

                             HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH

                                       MCRC No.12961/2019

                                  (Kok Singh Vs The State of M.P.)

           victim have not been recorded. As per M.L.C. report, doctor has given

           opinion that injury was caused to the complainant by sharp cutting

           weapon. Hence, prayed for dismissal of the application.

</p><p id="p_4">                      Heard learned counsel for the parties and perused the case diary.

                      Since the second bail application was rejected because the

           statements of victim were not recorded but in this repeat application, it

           is not reflected that any statement has been recorded nor the same has

           been filed by the applicant. No new ground is pointed out for grant of

           this third bail application. Hence, the repeat bail application under

           <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_6">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C. is hereby rejected.

</p><p id="p_5">                                                     (Rajeev Kumar Shrivastava)

                                                                  Judge

AK/-
