<div class="doc_title">Babluram Meena vs State Of Rajasthan Through Pp on 4 May, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR 
RAJASTHAN BENCH AT JAIPUR.

ORDER

	S.B. CRIMINAL MISC.BAIL APPLICATION No.4477/2016.

		Babluram Meena     Vs.     State of Rajasthan 

Date of order :	                                  		             May 4, 2016. 

HON'BLE SMT.JUSTICE SABINA

Shri H. S. Sinsinwar for the petitioner. 
Shri N.S. Dhakad, Public Prosecutor for the State. 
	******	
</pre><p id="p_1">		Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Code of Criminal Procedure 1973 seeking regular bail in F.I.R. No. 195/2016 registered at Police Station Gangapur City, District Sawai Madhopur for offence under <a href="/doc/1092263/" id="a_2">Section 3</a>/<a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_3">25</a> Arms Act,1959 (hereinafter referred to as Act).
		Heard.
</p><p id="p_2">		As per the prosecution case country made 315 bore pistol and three live cartridges were recovered from the pocket of the petitioner. When the vehicle was searched, two country-made 315 bore pistols were recovered.
		Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, this petition is allowed. Petitioner be admitted to bail subject to satisfaction of the Area Magistrate.
</p><p id="p_3">                            						(SABINA), J 
Sudha/34
All corrections made in the judgment/order have been incorporated in the judgment/order being emailed.
MRG/PS
</p>
