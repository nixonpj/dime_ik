                      1.The Superintendent of Police,

                      PRAYER : Criminal Original Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of
                      Cr.P.C., to issue a direction to the respondents not to harass the
                      petitioner under the guise of enquiry.


                                  For Petitioner   : Mr.N.V.Nagenthiran
                                  For R1 to R3     : Mr.R.Anandharaj
                                                     Additional Public Prosecutor




http://www.judis.nic.in

                                                    ORDER
</pre>


<p id="p_1">                            This Criminal Original Petition has been filed seeking for a

                      direction to the respondent police not to harass the petitioner under

                      the guise of enquiry.
</p>


<p id="p_2">                            2.The learned Additional Public Prosecutor would submit that

                      on a complaint given by the fourth respondent a case has been

                      registered against the petitioner and her relatives for the offences

                      punishable under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_2">Sections 406</a> and <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_3">420</a> of I.P.C in Crime No.393 of

                      2018 on the file of the Thirumangalam Town Police Station,

                      Thirumangalam, Madurai District.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                            3.Recording the said submissions, this Criminal Original

                      Petition is closed.
</p>
