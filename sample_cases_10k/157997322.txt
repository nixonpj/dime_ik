<div class="doc_title">Rajesh @ Shera Kachhi vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 4 May, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                            MCRC-6041-2016
             (RAJESH @ SHERA KACHHI Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


04-05-2016

     Shri Ajay Mishra, learned counsel for the applicant.
      Shri Pradeep Singh, learned Government Advocate for
the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     This is first application filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the

Cr.P.C. for grant of bail to the applicant who has been

arrested in connection with Crime No. 82/2008, registered at
P.S.Dheemarkheda, District- Katni, for the offence punishable

under <a href="/doc/704277/" id="a_2">Sections 8</a>/<a href="/doc/919170/" id="a_3">20</a> NDPS Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     The allegation against the applicant is that the applicant

along with other co-accused was going on a motor bike and

when the bike was inspected, from the possession of the

accused persons 14 Kg. Ganja (Cannabis) was seized. The

applicant was absconding. Another co-accused Rinku @

Akhilesh was tried by the trial Court and acquitted vide

judgment dated 26/11/2015 passed in Special Trial No.

26/2010. The applicant was arrested on 22/07/2015 since

then he is in jail. There is no previous criminal history of the

applicant as verified by the learned Government Advocate.

</p><p id="p_4">     The prayer for bail is opposed by learned Government
 Advocate.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      Looking to the nature and facts of the case but without

expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, this

application is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">      It is directed that on furnishing a personal bond in the

sum of Rs. 50,000/- (Rs. Fifty Thousand Only) by the

applicant along with one solvent surety in the like amount to

the satisfaction of the trial court, the applicant be released on

bail with a direction to appear before the trial court on the
date of the trial.
</p>
<p id="p_7">      The applicant shall abide by the following conditions of

437 (3) of <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_4">Cr.P.C</a>. as under:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">    (a) that such person shall attend in accordance with the

    conditions of the bond executed under this Chapter,

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">    (b) that such person shall not commit any offence similar

    to the offence of which he is accused, or suspected, of

    the commission of which he is suspected, and

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">    (c) that such person shall not directly or indirectly make

    any inducement, threat or promise to any person

    acquainted with the facts of the case as to dissuade him

    from disclosing such facts to the Court or to any police

    officer or tamper with the evidence.
</blockquote><p id="p_8">   Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">                                 (S.K. GANGELE)
                                      JUDGE
MISHRA
