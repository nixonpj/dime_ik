<div class="doc_title">Debaprasad Bera vs The State Of West Bengal &amp; Ors on 2 September, 2016</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         1</span>


28    02.09.                       C.O. 3262 of 2016
AGM    2016


                                   Debaprasad Bera
                                          Vs
                            The State of West Bengal &amp; Ors

                   Mr. Prasanta Behari Mahata,
                                       For the Petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
                     This is an application under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the

               Constitution of India at the instance of the petitioner in a

               Land Acquisition proceeding seeking direction upon the

               Court of Appeal to dispose of Misc Appeal No. 17 of 2005

               expeditiously.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                     Upon perusing the certified copy of the order sheets

               annexed to this revisional application, this Court finds

               that a interlocutory application is pending in the said

               Misc Appeal and, therefore, the same has not ripened for

               final disposal.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                     However, considering the nature of the proceeding

               as well as the pendency of the said application since 30th

               July 2013, this Court requests the Trial Court to take up

               the said application on the next date, so fixed, and shall

               make efforts to dispose of the same as expeditiously as

               possible.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                     After the disposal of the same, the Trial Court shall
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                           2</span>


make all endeavour to dispose of the Misc Appeal as

expeditiously as possible without granting unnecessary

adjournments to either of the parties.

</p><p id="p_6">      With these observations the revisional application

stands disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">      There shall be no order as to costs.



</p><p id="p_8">                                    ( Harish Tandon, J.)
 </p>
