                   Cr.M.P. No.3133 of 2017
                             ------
    Ajay Kumar Lokesh                .... .... .... Petitioner
                             Versus
    The State of Jharkhand          ....  .... .... Opposite Party
                             ------
CORAM         : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR CHOUDHARY
                                       ------
     For the Petitioner          : Mr. Santosh Kumar, Advocate
     For the State               : Addl.P.P.
                                       ------
     03/01.12.2017</pre><p id="p_1"> This criminal miscellaneous petition has been filed under
     <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure, at the instance of the petitioner
     with a prayer for modification of the order dated 10.10.2017 passed in A.B.A.
     No.4628 of 2017.
</p><p id="p_2">                    Heard learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and learned
     Addl.P.P. appearing for the State.
</p><p id="p_3">                    It is submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioner that vide
     order dated 10.10.2017 passed in A.B.A. No.4628 of 2017, the petitioner was
     granted anticipatory bail. In the anticipatory bail application inadvertently the
     petitioner wrongly mentioned the name of the court where the case is pending
     consequently in the order of anticipatory bail In this criminal miscellaneous
     petition, the petitioner has made two-fold prayer which are as follows:-
</p><p id="p_4">              a). In the impugned order, the name of the court where the case has been
     pending, has been mentioned as A.D.J. 1st Bermo at Tenughat in place of Sri
     S.N. Kujur, J.M. 1st Class Bermo at Tenughat.
</p><p id="p_5">              b). Further extension of 'two weeks' time to surrender before the court
     below.
</p><p id="p_6">                    Learned Addl.P.P appearing for the State has no objection.
                    Considering the aforesaid submissions of the learned counsel for
     the petitioner, the prayer for modification in the order dated 10.10.2017 passed
     in A.B.A. No.4628 of 2017 is allowed. The order dated 10.10.2017 passed in
     A.B.A. No.4628 of 2017 is modified to the extent that the name of the court in
     which the petitioner was directed to surrender be read as 'Sri S.N. Kujur, J.M. 1st
     Class Bermo at Tenughat' instead of 'A.D.J. 1st Bermo at Tenughat', and the
     time period for surrendering in the court below is extended by "two weeks"
     from the date of this order instead of the time period mentioned in the order
     dated 10.10.2017 passed in A.B.A. No.4628 of 2017.
</p><p id="p_7">                         The order dated 10.10.2017 passed in A.B.A. No.4628 of 2017 is
          modified to the aforesaid extent only.
</p><p id="p_8">                        Accordingly, this criminal miscellaneous petition is disposed of.
                        Let a copy of this order be communicated to the concerned court
          below through 'FAX' at the cost of Rs.50/- per page to be borne by the
          petitioner.
</p>


<pre id="pre_2">Animesh                                            (Anil Kumar Choudhary, J.)
