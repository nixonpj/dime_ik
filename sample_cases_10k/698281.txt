  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

Bail Appl..No. 546 of 2011()


1. NAJEEB KHAN I, S/O.IBRAHIM KUTTY,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. STATE OF KERALA,
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.PHILIP T.VARGHESE

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice V.RAMKUMAR

 Dated :01/02/2011

 O R D E R
                        V. RAMKUMAR, J.
                     - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

                      - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
           Dated this the 1st day of February, 2011.

                              O R D E R
</pre>


<p id="p_1">      The petitioner seeks anticipatory bail on the allegation that

the Konni police are at his heels at the instance of one <a href="/doc/33632/" id="a_1">Anitha

V.S</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      The learned Public Prosecutor, on instructions, submitted

that the petitioner is the accused in Crime No. 5 of 2011 of Konni

Police Station involving offences under <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_2">Sections 294(b)</a> and <a href="/doc/1255223/" id="a_3">507</a>

I.P.C., both of which are bailable offences. If so, the invocation

of <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_4">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C is misconceived.                 This petition is

accordingly dismissed without prejudice to the right of the

petitioner to seek regular bail from the Magistrate concerned.



</p><p id="p_3">                                              V.RAMKUMAR, JUDGE.
</p>
