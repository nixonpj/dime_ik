<div class="doc_title">P.Veerappudayar vs The Superintendent Of Police on 10 June, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">       

  

   

 
 
 BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT

DATED: 10.06.2015

CORAM

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.M.SUNDRESH

W.P(MD)No.9320 of 2015

P.Veerappudayar			       		... Petitioner

Vs.

1.The Superintendent of Police,
  Tiruchirappalli District,
  Tiruchirappalli

2.The Sub-Inspector of Police,
  Kallagudi  Police Station,
  Kallagudi, Trichirappalli District.			... Respondents

Prayer
 	Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the
Constitution of India for the issuance of Writ of Mandamus, directing the
respondents to grant permission and protection for Aadal Paadal programme
performed by ?Madurai Abinaya Group? in Thiurther Thiruvizha Alaippithal?
scheduled to be held on 12.6.2015 at Arulmighu Sri Selliamman Thirukkoil at
South Street, Kallagam Vil.lage, Lalgudi Taluk, Tiruchirappalli District.

!For Petitioner  : M/s.R.Karthic Rajan

For Respondents : Mr.R.Karthikeyan
 		   Addl.Govt.Pleader


:ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	This Writ Petition has been filed seeking for the issuance of Writ of
Mandamus, directing the respondents to grant permission and protection for
Aadal Paadal programme performed by ?Madurai Abinaya Group? in Thiurther
Thiruvizha Alaippithal? scheduled to be held on 12.6.2015 at Arulmighu Sri
Selliamman Thirukkoil at South Street, Kallagam Vil.lage, Lalgudi Taluk,
Tiruchirappalli District.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2.Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the
learned Additional Government Pleader appearing for the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.Learned counsel for the petitioner would submit that the petitioner
is a member  of the Vizha Committee and a devotee of Arulmighu Sri Selliamman
Thirukkoil at South Street, Kallagam, Lalgudi Taluk, Tiruchirappalli District
District and every year Thiruther Thiruvizha Alaippithal is being conducted
by the villagers in the month of Vaigasi and this year also, the villagers
jointly decided to conduct the said  temple festival from 11.06.205 to
13.06.2015 for three days, with "Aadal Padal  programme" on 12.06.2015. In
this connection, a representation was given on 6.6..2015 to the second
respondent police, with a request to grant permission to conduct "Adal Padal
Programme" on 12.06.2015, however, no order was passed by the respondents on
the said representation and hence the petitioner is before this Court with
this writ petition. The learned counsel for the petitioner further submitted
that as the temple festival is celebrated in a peaceful manner by the
villagers,  there will be no untoward incident by the people of the village.
</p>
<p id="p_4">		4.Mr.R.Karthikeyan, learned Additional Government Pleader
appearing for the respondents, on instructions, would submit that the
permission sought for by the petitioner may be granted with stringent
conditions for maintaining law and order problem and also to avoid any
untoward incident during performance of such programme.
</p><p id="p_5"> 		5.Having considered the facts and circumstances and the
submissions made by the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned
Government Advocate appearing for the respondents,  this court is of the view
that necessary permission shall be granted to the petitioner and his
villagers to conduct 'Adal Padal programme" in connection with the temple
festival of Arulmighu Sri Selliamman Thirukkoil at South Street, Kallagam
Vil.lage, Lalgudi Taluk, Tiruchirappalli District.
</p>
<p id="p_6">		6.Accordingly, the writ petition is disposed of with the
following directions:
</p><p id="p_7">	(a)The cultural programme in connection with temple festival at
Arulmighu Sri Selliamman Thirukkoil at South Street, Kallagam Vil.lage,
Lalgudi Taluk, Tiruchirappalli District  to be held on 12.06.2015 between
06.00 p.m. and 10.30 p.m.
</p><p id="p_8">	(b)There should not be any kind of obscene dance or vulgar dialogues
during the performance, by anyone of the participants;
</p><p id="p_9">	(c)Double meaning songs should not be played so as to spoil the minds
of students and youths.
</p><p id="p_10">	(d)No dance or songs, touching upon any political party or religion or
community or caste be played.
</p><p id="p_11">	(e)No flex boards in support of any political party or communal leader
be erected.
</p><p id="p_12">	(f)The function should not affect either religious or communal harmony
and shall be conducted without any discrimination based on caste.
</p><p id="p_13">	(g)If there is any violation of any one of the conditions imposed, the
concerned police officer is at liberty to take necessary action, as per law
and stop such performance; and
</p><p id="p_14">	(h)Similarly, the police is entitled to stop the dance programme, if it
exceeds beyond the permitted time limit.
</p><p id="p_15">	(i)The second respondent is directed to issue necessary permission,
incorporating the above conditions.
</p><p id="p_16">		 No costs.
</p>
					
<p id="p_17">Note: Issue order copy on 11.06.2015

To

</p><p id="p_18">1.The Superintendent of Police,
  Tiruchirappalli District,
  Tiruchirappalli

</p><p id="p_19">2.The Sub-Inspector of Police,
  Kallagudi  Police Station,
  Kallagudi, Trichirappalli District.	
</p>
