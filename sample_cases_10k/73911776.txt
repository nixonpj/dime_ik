<div class="doc_title">Sandeep Prasad Kunwar @ Sonu Singh vs The State Of Jharkhand on 23 September, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                             Cr. M. P. No. 2222 of 2019
  Sandeep Prasad Kunwar @ Sonu Singh            .....   .... Petitioner
                         Versus
  The State of Jharkhand                        .... .... Opp. Party
                              ------
</pre><p id="p_1">  CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANDA SEN
</p><p id="p_2">                         ------</p><pre id="pre_2">
  For the Petitioner           : Mr. Pran Pranay, Advocate
  For the State                : A.P.P.
                                  ------
         Order No. 03                     Dated 23rd September, 2019

</pre><p id="p_3">         Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned A.P.P. for the
State.
</p><p id="p_4">         The petitioner confines his prayer in this application only in respect
of the order dated 22.2.2019 passed by the learned S.D.J.M., Madhupur
in connection with Chitra P.S. Case No. 10 of 2019 by which coercive
steps have been ordered to be taken against the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_5">         Perusal of the impugned order dated 22.2.2019 does not reveal any
appropriate reasons necessitating issuance of proclamation and process
under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 82</a> Cr.P.C. and accordingly the said order is, hereby,
quashed and set aside.
</p><p id="p_6">         This application is allowed.
</p><p id="p_7">         However, the learned Magistrate is at liberty to proceed further in
accordance with law.
</p>


<p id="p_8">                                                (Ananda Sen, J.)
AKT/Rajnish
