<p id="p_1">                                             Re: C.A.N. 2184 of 2018

               This is an application filed by the respondent/decree-holder praying for

        occupational charges at reasonable rate during pendency of the appeal.

</p><p id="p_2">               Copy of the application has been served upon the appellant, Director, West Bengal

        Fire Services and he admits receipt of the same. However, the appellant/judgment debtor

        prays for time to file affidavit-in-opposition.

</p><p id="p_3">               Let affidavit-in-opposition be filed within 10 days and reply, if any, thereto by 3

        days thereafter.
</p>
<p id="p_4">               Considering the urgency involved in this matter, let the matter appear at the top of

        the list under the heading "Application" on April 30, 2018.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                                       (Sahidullah Munshi, J.)
