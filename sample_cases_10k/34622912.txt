<pre id="pre_1">                                                           1

21.06.2013</pre>
<pre id="pre_2">  ad                               C.R.M 7973 of 2013

                       In re: Kakali Majhi
                                                    ......... petitioner

</pre><p id="p_1">             Re: An application under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C filed on 4th June, 2013 in connection with
               Burdwan Police Station Case No. 487 of 2012 dated 21.06.2012 under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Sections
               498A</a>/<a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_3">302</a>/<a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_4">304B</a>/<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120B</a> of the Indian Penal Code.</p><pre id="pre_3">
             Mr. Kamakshya Prasad Mukherjee
                                    .........              For the petitioner

               Mr. Shabir Ahmed                .........             For the State

</pre><p id="p_2">                        Heard the learned Counsels appearing on behalf of the petitioner
               and the State.
</p><p id="p_3">                        It is submitted on behalf of the petitioner that there is a substantial
               change of the circumstance inasmuch as the charge sheet has been filed
               under <a href="/doc/92983/" id="a_6">Section 306</a> of the Indian Penal Code whereas the F.I.R was
               registered, inter alia, under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_7">Sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_8">304B</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_4">                        This is a case where the housewife allegedly committed suicide
               within two years of her marriage.            We are not impressed upon by the
               submission of the learned Counsel for the petitioner that the aforesaid
               circumstance is a substantial change necessitating review of our earlier
               order of rejection of anticipatory bail particularly when charge sheet had
               already been filed prior to the rejection of the earlier prayer for anticipatory
               bail.
</p><p id="p_5">                         This application is accordingly rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                    (Joymalya Bagchi,J.)


                                             (Anindita Roy Saraswati,J.)
