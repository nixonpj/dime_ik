<div class="doc_title">Rajesh @ Raju Rameshbhai Bhatti ... vs State Of Gujarat on 24 December, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">         R/CR.MA/21619/2014                                 ORDER




          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

 CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 21619 of
                                    2014

================================================================
       RAJESH @ RAJU RAMESHBHAI BHATTI THRO' BHATTI RUSHI
                    RAMESHBHAI....Applicant(s)
                             Versus
                 STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
PARTY-IN-PERSON, PERSONAL CAPACITY for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MR NJ SHAH ADDITIONAL PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the Respondent(s)
No. 1
================================================================

         CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI

                              Date : 24/12/2014


                               ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.0    Rule. Mr.  NJ Shah,  learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives 

service of notice of rule on behalf of the respondent. 



2.0    By way of present application, the  applicant through his brother 

Bhatti Rushi Rameshbhai has prayed  to release  him on temporary bail 

on the ground of sickness of his mother.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

3.0      I have considered  the gravity of the offence  committed  by the 

undertrial prisoner. He is arrested in connection with the FIR registered 

as C.R. No. I­74 of 2014 with  Visavadar Police Station. 


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  Page 1 of 2</span>
</p><pre id="pre_2">           R/CR.MA/21619/2014                                          ORDER




4.0     Considering the peculiar facts and circumstances of the case , I 

</pre><p id="p_3">am of the opinion  that  the  application  requires  consideration  and  the 

same is accordingly allowed. The applicant is ordered to be released on 

temporary   bail  for   a   period   of  three  weeks  from   date   of   his   actual 

release  on   executing   a   personal   bond   of   Rs.10000/­(   Rupees   Ten 

Thousand   Only)   to   the   satisfaction   of   the   concerned   Jail   Authority. 

During the period of temporary bail, the under­trial prisoner shall also 

mark   his   presence   once   in   a   week   with   concerned   Police   Station 

between 9.00 a.m and 2.00 p.m.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

5.0     Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.   Direct service is 

permitted. 
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                                                    (A.J.DESAI, J.)

niru*
