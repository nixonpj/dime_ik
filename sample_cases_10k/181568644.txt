<div class="doc_title">Bajaj Allianz General Insurance ... vs Bani Barman (Singha) &amp; Ors on 16 November, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                            1


16.11.2017
 85. AJ.
                                                C.A.N. 9576 of 2017
                                                        with
                                           C.A.N. 10127 of 2017
                                             in
                                     F.M.A.T. 955 of 2017

                       Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company Limited
                                            -Vs-
                                   Bani Barman (Singha) &amp; Ors.

                   Mr. Rajesh Singh.
                             .....for the appellant/applicant.



                        C.A.N. 9576 of 2017 is an application under <a href="/doc/100581/" id="a_1">Section 5</a> of the Limitation Act,

             seeking condonation of delay in presentation of the appeal (F.M.A.T. 955 of 2017).

             C.A.N. 10127 of 2017 is an application for stay filed in such appeal.

                       By the impugned award, the relevant motor accident claims tribunal allowed

             an application under <a href="/doc/136948773/" id="a_2">Section 166</a> of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 and awarded

             compensation of Rs.45,000/- to be paid to the claimant by the insurance company

             together with interest @ 6% per annum from the date of presentation of the application.

                       We have heard Mr. Singh, learned advocate for the applicant/appellant in

             support of the applications.

                       There shall be an unconditional stay of operation of the impugned award till 7th

             December, 2017. Should the appellant/applicant in the meanwhile deposit the sum

             awarded by the tribunal plus interest with the Registrar General of this Court, the ad-

             interim order of stay shall continue till disposal of the application for stay.

                       In case of default of payment as directed above, the ad-interim order of stay

             shall stand lapsed with effect from 8th December, 2017.

                       Upon deposit of the sum awarded by the tribunal by the appellant/applicant, the

             Registrar General shall ensure that the said sum is invested in a short-term auto-

             renewable fixed deposit account of a nationalized bank, until further orders of this Court.
                             2

          Copies of the applications shall be served on the claimants/respondents within</pre>

<p id="p_1">two weeks from date with an intimation that the same shall be listed once again in the

monthly list of January, 2018.
</p>



<p id="p_2">                                 ( Dipankar Datta, J. )




                            ( Protik Prakash Banerjee, J. )
