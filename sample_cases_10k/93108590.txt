<div class="doc_title">Anirudh Prasad @ Anirudh Prasad ... vs The State Of Jharkhand Through Acb ... on 9 May, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">         IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                           B.A. No. 2024 of 2018

         Anirudh Prasad @ Anirudh Prasad Singh                ....    .... Petitioner(s).

                                       Versus

         The State of Jharkhand through ACB (Vigilance)     .... .... Opp. Party(s)
                                       ------
</pre><pre id="pre_2">         CORAM :           HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANDA SEN.
                                       ------
         For the Petitioner(s)         : Mr. Anjani Kumar, Advocate
         For the State                 : A.P.P.
                               .........

           05/09.05.2018
                Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</pre><p id="p_1">                The petitioner is an accused for allegedly committing an offence
   punishable under <a href="/doc/324254/" id="a_1">Sections 7</a>/<a href="/doc/1259316/" id="a_2">13(2)</a> of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988.
</p><p id="p_2">                It is alleged that petitioner being Amin of Tishri Block accepted Rs.
    3000/- as bribe.
</p><p id="p_3">                Counsel for the petitioner submits that charge-sheet has been
    submitted in this case. He remained in custody since 10.2.2017. Petitioner has
    been put under suspension.
</p><p id="p_4">                Learned A.P.P. opposes the prayer for bail and submits that charge-
    sheet has been submitted in this case.
</p><p id="p_5">                In view of the fact that charge-sheet has been submitted in this case,
   I am inclined to allow this application. Accordingly, the petitioner above
   named is directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.
   10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to
   the satisfaction of learned Additional Sessions Judge-IX-cum-Special Judge (
   ACB), Dhanbad in connection with Dhanbad (ACB) Police Station Case No. 01
   of 2018 (G.R. No. 5551 of 2018), corresponding to Special (ACB) Case No. 01 of
   2018, subject to the condition that petitioner shall appear before the trial court
   on each and every day during trial, failing which his bail bond shall stand
   cancelled and trial court shall take all possible steps for his apprehension.
</p>



<p id="p_6">                                                         (ANANDA SEN , J)
anjali/ C.P 3
