DATED: 17.04.2018  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE C.T.SELVAM           
AND  
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.M.BASHEER AHAMED               

H.C.P.(MD).No.476 of 2018 

K.Balamurugan                                           : Petitioner
                        
Vs.

1. The Ministry of External Affairs,
    Represented by,
    The Secretary to Government of India,
    South Block Building,
    Raisina Hill,
    New Delhi.
        
2. The Superintendent of Police,
    Sivagangai District,
    Sivagangai.

3.  The Inspector of Police,
     Alagappapuram Police Station,
     Karaikudi,
     Sivagangai District.

4.Ilangeshwaran 

5.Sakthi                                                        : Respondents 

PRAYER: Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India to
issue a Writ of Habeas Corpus, directing the  respondents 1 to 3 to secure
the detenu and produce the body or person of the detenu,   Karnamoorthy, S/o.
Karundhan, aged about 27 years, before this Court, illegally detained by the
4th and 5th  respondents and set him at liberty.
                

!For Petitioner          : Mr.K.Ramanathan  

For Respondents 1 to 3 : Mr.K.Dinesh Babu        
                                                Additional Public Prosecutor            


:ORDER  
</pre>

<p id="p_1">[Order of the Court was made by C.T.SELVAM, J.]  
        The learned counsel for the petitioner sought permission of this Court
to withdraw the Habeas Corpus Petition. He has also made an endorsement to  
that effect.
</p>

<p id="p_2">        2. In view of the above, the Habeas Corpus Petition is dismissed as
withdrawn.
</p>
