                   ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">    (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE MOHINDER PAL)


          Through this petition, the petitioner has sought custody of his
wife, who is stated to be with her parents. The petitioner has also
placed on record Marriage Certificate, according to which, marriage
has taken place in the year 2014 and the couple is having a child
out of the wedlock.
</p><p id="p_2">          In order to ascertain genuineness of the petitioner, he is
directed to deposit a sum of Rs.15,000/- with the registry of this
Court before the next date.
</p><p id="p_3">          Matter is adjourned to 04/12/2018.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                        Sd/-
