SUDIP AHLUWALIA J. (ORAL)


              Ld. counsel for the petitioners draws attention to the specific

averment made in ground 'F' on Page No. 17 to the effect that an application

for exemption was actually filed in the Ld. Court below, even though the

Ld.   Trial   Magistrate    in    her      impugned    order   dated    02.08.2017

(Annexure P-1) had observed:
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  "no        such   application   for   exemption

                    application. Application stand dismissed."

</p><p id="p_4">2.            Visibly such order appears to have been passed in a mechanical

manner, considering the manifest grammatical errors in the entire order.

</p><p id="p_5">3.            Ld. counsel for the petitioners undertakes that all his clients

will positively appear before the Ld. Trial Court on the next date fixed, i.e.
