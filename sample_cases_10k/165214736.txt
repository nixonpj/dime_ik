      This writ petition is filed under Articles 226 and
227 of the Constitution of India praying to direct
respondents   to   consider    the   representation   of   the
petitioner dated 6.6.2014 at Annexure-K, etc.


     This writ petition coming on for preliminary hearing
in 'B' Group this day the Court made the following:-


                        ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     The petitioners seek for a writ of mandamus to the

respondents to consider the legal notice dated 6.6.2014

vide Annexure-K.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               -3-</span><p id="p_2">




</p><p id="p_3">        2. I am of the considered view that no writ of

mandamus would lie seeking enforcement of a legal

notice issued by a lawyer.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">        Hence,   the   writ    petition        is     dismissed    as

misconceived.    However,      liberty    is        granted   to   the

petitioners to approach the respondents and make a

necessary application in a manner known to law.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                      SD/-
</p><p id="p_7">                                                     JUDGE
