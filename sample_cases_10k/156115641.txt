<p id="p_1">9     11-02-2013                      Criminal Revision No. 488 of 2002 alongwith Criminal

                     Revision No. 489 of 2002 is called out. While Sri Jitendra Narayan

                     Sinha, learned counsel appears on behalf of petitioners in Criminal

                     Revision No. 489 of 2002 but none has turned up on behalf of petitioners

                     in Criminal Revision No. 488 of 2002.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                      Since both the criminal revision petitions are arise out of

                     same impugned judgment, therefore, Mr. Jitendra Narayan Sinha,

                     learned counsel, is appointed as amicus curiae to assist the court in

                     Criminal Revision No. 488 of 2002.
