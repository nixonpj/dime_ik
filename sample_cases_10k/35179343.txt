<div class="doc_title">Hemant Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 24 December, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                               Criminal Miscellaneous No.40685 of 2013
                  ======================================================
                  HEMANT KUMAR, SON OF LATE MAHENDRA PRASAD,
                  RESIDENT OF MOHALLA - NAYABAJAR, WARD NO 3, NARIYAR
                  ROAD, P.S. AND DISTRICT - SAHARSA.
                                                               .... .... PETITIONER/S
                                              VERSUS
                  THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                                         .... .... OPPOSITE PARTY/S
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ADITYA KUMAR TRIVEDI
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2.   24-12-2013                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

                  Additional Public Prosecutor.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               Issuance of Kishan Credit Card in favour of Sudish

                  Yadav, cousin brother of complainant with regard to the land

                  claimed by the complainant to be his exclusive property on

                  account of partition having effected in his family wherein active

                  involvement of petitioner, being a Karamchari, has also been

                  identified and accordingly a complaint case has been brought up

                  at the instance of complainant Lallan Prasad @ Laliteshwar

                  Prasad Yadav.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               From the complaint petition itself, the inter se

                  relationship amongst the complainant along with Sudish Yadav

                  who has already been granted anticipatory bail vide Cr. Misc.

                  No.38992 of 2013 is admitted one along with the fact that there

                  happens to be no partition by meets and bound, the certificate so

                  granted in favour of Sudish Yadav cannot be said to be under evil
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.40685 of 2013 (2) dt.24-12-2013                  2</span>




                     design.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                     Consequent thereupon, petitioner Hemant Kumar is

                    directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of

                    Rs.10,000/-(ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                    each, to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                    Saharsa in connection with Saharsa P.S. Case No.578 of 2011 in

                    the event of his arrest or surrender within four weeks in terms of

                    <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_1">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                              (Aditya Kumar Trivedi, J)
PN/-
</p>
