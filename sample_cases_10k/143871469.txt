                                DATE OF DECISION: SEPTEMBER 27, 2018


L/NK RAMESH KUMAR ARMY NO 14929060                           ...PETITIONER

                                      VERSUS

SUSHMA RANI &amp; ANR.                                           ...RESPONDENT</pre>

<p id="p_1">CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.S. MADAAN.

PRESENT: MR. SANDEEP SIWACH, ADVOCATE
         FOR MR. RAJEEV DEV SHARMA, ADVOCATE
         FOR THE PETITIONER.

<p id="p_2">               MR. RAHUL DEV SINGH, ADVOCATE
               FOR THE RESPONDENTS.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
H.S. MADAAN, J.(ORAL)

               Learned counsel for the petitioner states that both the spouses

have filed a petition for divorce by mutual consent in terms of <a href="/doc/439618/" id="a_1">Section 13-B</a>

of the Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 wherein their statements have been

recorded in 1st motion.      Copies of statements of the petitioners therein

alongwith copies of their affidavits have been placed. It comes out that the

dispute has been settled between the parties for a sum of `10,50,000/-

payable by petitioner Ramesh Kumar to the wife Sushma Rani.

</p><p id="p_4">               It being so, this petition does not survive and is dismissed

accordingly.</p><pre id="pre_2">


September 27, 2018                                       (H.S. MADAAN)
Gulati                                                       JUDGE

Whether Reportable                :      Yes

Whether Speaking/Reasoned         :      No
