</p><p id="p_8">28/18.12.2018       Heard Mr. Arshad Hussain, learned counsel for the petitioners,
            Mr. Abhay Prakash, learned A.C. to A.A.G. appearing for the
            Respondent-State as well as Mr. Nikhil Kumar Mehta, learned counsel
            for the Respondents-Ranchi University.
</p><p id="p_9">                   Inspite of the order dated 30.10.2018, no affidavit seems to
            have been filed by the respondent nos. 10 and 11.
</p><p id="p_10">                   Learned counsel for the petitioner has filed an affidavit, stating
            that till now no payment has been made to any of the petitioner in this
            case despite the required fund has been made available with clear cut
            direction of payment of the same within 2 days.
</p><p id="p_11">                   Learned counsel for the Respondents-University seeks short
            adjournment, as a last indulgence, for filing of the affidavit.
</p><p id="p_12">                   However, with much reluctance, the matter is being adjourned
            to 15.01.2019 for filing of the affidavit, failing which, the Registrar,
            Kolhan University, Chaibasa (Respondent No. 10) and the Finance
            officer, Kolhan University, Chaibasa (Respondent No. 11) shall
            appear in person.
</p>

<p id="p_13">                                                          (Pramath Patnaik, J.)
