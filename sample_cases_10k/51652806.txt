<p id="p_1">      This criminal petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C. by the

petitioner/A4 in Crime No.54 of 2018 of Hayathnagar Police Station,

Ranga Reddy District, registered for the offences punishable under

<a href="/doc/1404646/" id="a_2">Sections 8(c)</a>    r/w 20(b)(ii)(c) of the Narcotic Drugs and

<a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_3">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>, 1985 (for short 'the Act').

</p><p id="p_2">2.    Despite posting the matter under the caption 'for

dismissal', there is no representation for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_3">3.    Accordingly,    the   Criminal   Petition   is   dismissed   for

non-prosecution.
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.    Consequently, miscellaneous petitions, if any shall stand

closed.
</p>
