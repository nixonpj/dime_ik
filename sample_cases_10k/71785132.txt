                  2. Ravindra Prasad @ Lalwa, son of late Ram Narayan Prasad, both
                     resident of Mungeriganj, P.S.-Town, District- Begusarai

                                                    Versus
                  1. The State of Bihar &amp;
                  2. Vijay Kumar Soni, son of late Laxman Prasad Soni, resident of

                  CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA PRAKASH
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">3/   11-09-2012                  The case has been listed for priority hearing on the

                  prayer of the counsel for Opposite Party No.2 on the ground that

                  trial has been held up on account of interim order dated

                  25.04.2012 passed by this Court by which it had directed the Trial

                  Court not to proceed against the Petitioners till further orders.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The Petitioners seek quashing of the order dated

                  29.02.2012 for recall of P.W.11, by which the Additional Sessions

                  Judge-II, Begusarai, in Sessions Trial No.692 of 2010 arising out

                  of Town P.S. Case No.212 of 2009 has refused to recall P.W.11,

                  Shakuntala Devi.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Having gone through the order impugned, I find that

                  it is a fit case where such a prayer should be allowed.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   In view of such, the Trial Court is directed to fix

                  one date for examination of P.W.11 after due notice to the parties.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.15083 of 2012 (3) dt.11-09-2012</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_5">                                     Counsel for the Informant-Opposite Party No.2 is

                    directed to cooperate and produce P.W.11, Shakuntala Devi, on

                    the date fixed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                     With the aforesaid observation/ direction, the

                    application stands allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                     Let this order be communicated to the Additional

                    Sessions Judge-II, Begusarai, in connection with Sessions Trial

                    No.692 of 2010 through FAX at the cost of the Informant-

                    Opposite Party No2.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                                                (Anjana Prakash, J)
    JA/-
</p>
