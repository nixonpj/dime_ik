<div class="doc_title">Rajan Ram vs The State Of Jharkhand on 28 January, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">         IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                      B.A. No.7400 of 2014

         Rajan Ram @ Rajan Kumar S/O Kanhai Ram, R/O of village-Chhath
         Talab, Dibha Mohalla PO &amp; PS-Sadar District-Chatra
                                                     .... Petitioner
                                Versus
         The State of Jharkhand                        ...O.P.

         Coram : The Hon'ble Mr. Justice D.N.Upadhyay

         For the Petitioner       : Mr. A.K.Chaturvedi, Advocate
         For the O.P.            : APP
                              -----

03/28.01.2015</pre><p id="p_1">        Heard the parties.
</p><p id="p_2">                     The petitioner is an accused in connection with Sadar
         Chatra P.S. Case no.12 of 2014, corresponding to G.R. no.84 of 2014,
         for the offence under sections 25(1-b)a and 26 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_1">Arms Act</a>.
</p><p id="p_3">                     The petitioner has been prosecuted for having been found
         in possession of country made pistol and two live cartridges.
</p><p id="p_4">                     It is submitted that the petitioner has remained in custody
         for more than one year against recovery of country made pistol and
         live cartridges. As a matter of fact, the recovery of fire arm and
         ammunition has been planted by the police.
</p><p id="p_5">                     Learned counsel for the State has opposed the prayer.
                     Considering the period of detention of the above named
         petitioner in jail custody, he is directed to be released on bail on
         furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-(ten thousand) with two sureties of
         the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial
         Magistrate, Chatra in connection with Sadar Chatra P.S. case no.12 of
         2014, corresponding to G.R. no.84 of 2014.
</p>



<p id="p_6">                                                    ( D.N.Upadhyay, J.)
s.b.
</p>
