                   1. Anant Kumar S/O Indreo Prasad Resident Of Village Katarpur, P.S. Katri

                   1. The State Of Bihar

                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Niraj Kumar @ Sanidh,Adv

                   ======================================================

                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">5   15-09-2014                      Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                     learned APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                    2. The petitioner apprehends his arrest for the

                     offences alleged under <a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_1">Sections 409</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a> IPC registered

                     in connection with Karja P.S. Case No. 22 of 2011.

</p><p id="p_3">                                    3. It is submitted that the petitioner has been

                     falsely implicated at the instance of one Ramesh

                     Thakur.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                    4. The petitioner is the Panchayat Rozgar

                     Sevak who happens to be the son of the Mukhiya. There

                     is accusation of defalcation of huge amount of more than

                     one crore rupees against these persons among others.

</p><p id="p_5">                                    5. Having regard to the nature of accusations
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.48350 of 2012 (5) dt.15-09-2014</span>




                          and the gravity of the offence, this Court is not inclined

                          to grant privilege of anticipatory bail to the petitioner.

                          The bail petition stands dismissed.</p><pre id="pre_2">




                                                                          (Vikash Jain, J)
Chandran
