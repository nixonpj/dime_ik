DATED : 19.12.2016
CORAM

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.MAHADEVAN

Crl.O.P.No.14667 of 2013
and
M.P.No.1 of 2013
1.Malathi
2.Ekavalli
3.Abdul Kathar
4.Malini								..  Petitioners

					          Vs

1.The Inspector of Police,
   N-3 Muthialpet Police Station, 
   Chennai. 

2.V.Lakshmi Narayanan					.. Respondents


	Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Criminal Procedure Code, to call for the records and quash the FIR No.839 of 2012 dated 19.05.2012 on the file of the first respondent police. 

		For Petitioners    : Mr.R.Santhanam
		For R1 	        : Mr.C.Emalias, APP

ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">	The petitioners have filed this Criminal Original Petition to call for the records relating to the FIR in Crime No.839 of 2012 pending on the file of the first respondent police and quash the same.

R.MAHADEVAN, J.
</p>
<p id="p_2">rk
</p><p id="p_3">	2. Today, when the matter came up for hearing, learned Additional Public Prosecutor submits that after completing the investigation of the case in Crime No.839 of 2012, charge sheet has been filed against the petitioners before the XVI Metropolitan Magistrate Court, George Town, Chennai. 

</p><p id="p_4">	3. Since the charge sheet has already been filed against the petitioners, nothing survives for adjudication in this petition. Hence, recording the submission so made by the learned Additional Public Prosecutor, this Criminal Original Petition is closed.  Consequently, connected Miscellaneous Petition is also closed.

</p><pre id="pre_2">Index		: Yes/No					           19.12.2016
rk 

To
</pre><p id="p_5">1.The XVI Metropolitan Magistrate Court, George Town, Chennai. 
</p>

   N-3 Muthialpet Police Station, 
   Chennai. 
</p>
