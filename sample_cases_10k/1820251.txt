<p id="p_1">	Rule.
</p><p id="p_2">Learned APP waives service of Rule. 
</p>
 

<p id="p_3">	This
application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal
Procedure in connection with first information report registered at
C.R.No.II-142 of 2011 with Godhra Taluka police station for the
offences punishable under <a href="/doc/158335608/" id="a_2">Sections 3</a> and <a href="/doc/361626/" id="a_3">7</a> of the Essential
Commodities Act, 1955 read with Sections 9, 10 and 14 of the Gujarat
Essential Commodities [licensing to fair price shops] Order, 2004 and
Sections 2, 6(4), 7(3) and 7 of P.D.S. (Control) Order, 2011.
</p> 

<p id="p_4">	Heard
learned counsel for the parties and perused the record.
</p> 

<p id="p_5">	Learned
counsel for the applicant submits that co-accused with similar
allegation of divesting essential commodities on the basis of
comparison drawn with the
'on-line' voters' list and that of the ration-card holders is
enlarged on bail by this Court and, on the ground of parity, the
applicant may be granted bail.
</p> 

<p id="p_6">	In
the facts and circumstances of the case and considering the nature of
allegations, role attributed to the applicant, and co-accused with
similar allegations is granted bail,
without
discussing the evidence in detail, prima facie, this Court is of the
opinion that this is a fit case to exercise the discretion to enlarge
the applicant on bail.
</p> 

<p id="p_7">	Hence,
the application is allowed and the applicant is ordered to be
released on bail in connection with first information report
registered at C.R.No.II-142 of 2011 with Godhra Taluka police station
on his executing a bond of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees five thousand only)
with one surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial
court and subject to the conditions that he shall
 

    
 i.	not
take undue advantage of his liberty or misuse his liberty;
</p> 
	  

<p id="p_8">not
	act in a manner injuries to the interest of the prosecution;
</p>	  

<p id="p_9">surrender
	his passport, if any, to the lower court within a week;
</p>	  

<p id="p_10">not
	leave the State of Gujarat without prior permission of the Sessions
	Judge concerned;
</p>	  

<p id="p_11">mark
	his presence at the concerned police station on the first Sunday of
	every month between 10 a.m. and 3 p.m for three months only;
</p>	  

<p id="p_12">furnish
	the present  address of his residence to the I.O. and also to the
	Court at the time of execution of the bond and shall not change the
	residence without prior permission of this Court;
</p>
 

<p id="p_13">	The
Authorities will release the applicant only if he is not required in
connection with any other offence for the time being. If breach of
any of the above conditions is committed, the Sessions Judge
concerned will be free to issue warrant or take appropriate action in
the matter. Bail bond to be executed before the lower court having
jurisdiction to try the case. At the trial, the trial court shall not
be influenced by the observations of preliminary nature, qua the
evidence at this stage, made by this Court while enlarging the
applicant on bail. Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
D.S. Permitted.
</p> 

<p id="p_14">(Anant
S. Dave, J.)
 

(swamy)
