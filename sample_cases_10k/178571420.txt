<p id="p_3">
                        This matter shall be listed at 10.30 A.M. on 9th July, 2013.

                        On that date, the petitioners shall apprise this Court the total

              quantity of iron ore transported by them categorised in the form of lump

              and fines, during the subject period.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                        This direction is being passed as this is the query of the Special

              Officer which the Special Officer submits is not being answered to. Mr.

              Mukherjee, learned senior counsel for the petitioners wants to take

              instruction from his clients on this issue.

</p><p id="p_5">                        The interim order already passed in this matter shall stand

              extended till 10th July, 2013 or until further order, whichever is earlier.

</p><p id="p_6">                        As a special case, urgent photostat plain copy of this order, duly

              countersigned by the Assistant Registrar (Court), be given to the parties

              on usual undertaking.
