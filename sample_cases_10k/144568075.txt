<div class="doc_title">Pinku @ Fatku vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh Thr on 15 February, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                       1
                                                      Cr.A. 648/2015

       15.02.2016
             Shri Tajuddin Khan, Advocate for the appellant.
             Shri   Mohd     Irshad,       Panel   Lawyer   for   the
       respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">             I.A. No. 562/16 has been filed for grant of further time
       to furnish appropriate bail bonds in terms of the order passed
       on 31.08.2015 suspending the sentence of appellant.
</p><p id="p_2">             It is contended by the learned counsel for appellant
       that appellant due to unavoidable reasons could not procure
       appropriate surety and security and therefore seeks
       extension of time for the said purpose.
</p><p id="p_3">             Reasons assigned in I.A. No. 562/16 appears to be
       bonafide and therefore the same is allowed.
</p><p id="p_4">             Let bail bonds as directed by this Court on 31.08.2015
       be furnished within a period of thirty working days, failing
       which the order passed on 31.08.2015 by this Court shall
       become ineffective.
</p><p id="p_5">             In case, the appellant furnishes bail bonds within the
       said period, then the next date of appearance before the
       CJM, Morena      would be 04.04.2016        and all such other
       subsequent dates as may be fixed by the concerned CJM.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                       (Sheel Nagu)
sh/-                                                      Judge
