                                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

                                                     DATED 25.03.2019

                                                          CORAM

                                THE HONOURABLE Mr.JUSTICE G.K.ILANTHIRAIYAN

                                                 Crl.O.P.No.7856 of 2019

                     L.Janardhanan                                                          .. Petitioner

                                                              Vs
                     The State rep by
                     The Inspector of Police,
                     T-1, Ambattur Police Station,
                     Tiruvallur District.                                                 .. Respondent

                     Prayer: Criminal Original Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of Criminal Procedure
                     Code, to direct the respondent police viz., the Inspector of Police, T-1, Ambattur
                     Police Station, Chennai to investigate the case in Crime No.1923 of 2017 dated
                     29.07.2017 and file the final report within a stipulated time before the competent
                     Court of law.

                                         For Petitioner       : M/s.T.K.Viswanath
                                         For Respondent       : Mr.C.Raghavan
                                                                Government Advocate (Crl.Side)

                                                         ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">                                This Criminal Original Petition has been filed seeking for a direction to

                     the respondent to complete the investigation and to file a final report in Crime No.

                     1923 of 2017 dated 29.07.2017, pending on the file of the respondent police.



</p><p id="p_2">                           2.     Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned

                     Government Advocate (Crl.Side) for the respondent.



http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                  2</span>

</p><p id="p_3">                            3.        Whenever any information in relation to commission of cognizable or

                     non cognizable offence is received, the police officer shall adhere to the procedure

                     contemplated under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Sections 154</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">155</a> of the Criminal Procedure Code and after

                     conducting necessary enquiry/investigation, file final report under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">Section 173</a> of

                     Cr.P.C. Such investigation under<a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5"> Chapter XII of the Criminal Procedure Code</a> shall

                     be completed without any unnecessary delay. The delay in filing a final report in

                     the present case is inordinate and unjustified. Hence, it would be appropriate to

                     direct the Investigating Officer to file a final report within a stipulated time, if not

                     already filed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                            4.        Considering the facts and circumstance of the case, this Court directs

                     the respondent police to complete the investigation in Crime No. 1923 of 2017

                     dated 29.07.2017, pending on his file and file a final report within a period of

                     three months from the date of the receipt of a copy of this Order.



</p><p id="p_6">                            5.        This Criminal Original Petition is disposed of accordingly.
