<p id="p_14">

           HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SANGEET LODHA
            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ARUN BHANSALI

                                      Order

18/11/2019

     By way of this writ petition (PIL), the petitioners are seeking

directions to the respondents for sanction of Sub Health Centre at

Village-Berasar.
</p>
<p id="p_15">     Reply to the writ petition has been filed on behalf of the

respondent No.6-Sarpanch Gram Panchayat, Pindiya as also on

behalf of the respondent-State.
</p>
<p id="p_16"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      (Downloaded on 19/11/2019 at 08:44:47 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_17">                                                                              (2 of 2)               [CW-9735/2018]



                                        As per the resolution adopted by the Gram Panchayat,

                                   Pindiya in its meeting held on 05.11.2017, the establishment of

                                   Sub Health Centre is proposed on the land measuring 0.16 biswas

                                   comprising Khasra No.140, alleged to have been encroached upon

                                   by unscrupulous elements. However, as per the reply of the State,

                                   the land measuring 5 biswas comprising Khasra No. 495/330 has

                                   already been allotted for establishment of Sub Health Centre at

                                   Village-Beraser. But the fact remains that even after lapse of more

                                   than six years since the allotment of the land, no steps

                                   whatsoever have been taken by the State for establishing the Sub

                                   Health Centre at Village-Beraser.
</p>
<p id="p_18">                                        In this view of the matter, the writ petition is disposed of

                                   with the directions that the dispute, if any regarding the location

                                   of the Sub Health Centre at Village-Beraser, shall be resolved by

                                   the State Government within a period of one month from the date

                                   of this order. The necessary budget for the establishing the Sub

                                   Health Centre at Village-Beraser, shall be sanctioned within a

                                   period of one month thereafter and the constructions of the Sub

                                   Health Centre at the appropriate place, shall be completed within

                                   a period of six months thereafter.
</p>
<p id="p_19">                                        After completion of work within stipulated time frame as

                                   aforesaid, the compliance report shall be filed by the respondents

                                   before this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_20">
                                   (ARUN BHANSALI),J                                       (SANGEET LODHA),J

                                    37-PKS/-
</p>
