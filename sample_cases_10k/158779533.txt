<p id="p_1">     The respondent-husband filed a petition under

Section-13(1)(ia)(ib)    of    the      <a href="/doc/590166/" id="a_1">Hindu   Marriage   Act</a>,

before    the   family   court     at    Davangere,   seeking

dissolution of the marriage. Seeking its transfer to the

family court at Hospet, the present petition is filed.


</p><p id="p_2">     2.     The learned counsel for the respondent has

no objection to allow the petition.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                           3</span>




</p><p id="p_3">     3.    Consequently, the petition is allowed. The

proceedings in M.C.No.292/2014, on the file of Family

Court at Davangere is transferred to the court at

Principal Senior Civil Judge, Hospet to be disposed off

in accordance with Law.
</p>
