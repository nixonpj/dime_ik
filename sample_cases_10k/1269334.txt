<p id="p_1">3   01-02-2016                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

                 learned A.P.P. for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 Accusation against the petitioner is that he took huge

                 amount from informant on the pretext to provide him government

                 job in railway but submission on behalf of the petitioner is that in

                 support of the aforesaid accusation, except oral evidence, there is

                 no documentary evidence with the informant nor the same has

                 surfaced in course of investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances as

                 well as submissions of the parties, let the above named petitioner

                 be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten

                 thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.45151 of 2015 (3) dt.01-02-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                       satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Nawada in

                       connection with Nawada Town P.S. Case No. 447/2015.



</p><p id="p_4">                                                      (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
Dilip/-
