<div class="doc_title">Lalji (Since Deceased) Through ... vs Gram Panchayat Jhinna on 30 April, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                          MCC-1208-2016
   (LALJI (SINCE DECEASED) THROUGH LRS SMT. PHOOLMATI Vs GRAM PANCHAYAT
                                 JHINNA)


30-04-2016</pre>

<p id="p_1">Shri Rajneesh Patel, Advocate for the applicants.
</p><p id="p_2">       Heard.
</p><p id="p_3">      Seeking restoration of Second Appeal No.1077/2005 which
was dismissed on account of non-compliance of peremptory order
dated 28.01.2014, this petition has been filed along with an
application seeking condonation of delay in filing the M.C.C.
being I.A.No.5870/2016.
</p><p id="p_4">      After hearing learned counsel appearing on behalf of the
applicant, subject to deposit of cost of Rs.500/- with the M.P. High
Court Legal Services Committee, the delay in filing of this
restoration petition is hereby condoned and Second Appeal
No.1077/2005 is restored to its file.
</p><p id="p_5">        The amount of cost be deposited in the M.P. High Court
Legal Services Committee within two weeks from today failing
which, this order shall not be given effect to and the Second
Appeal No.1077/2005 be again treated as dismissed on account of
non-compliance of peremptory order .
</p><p id="p_6">       M.C.C. is allowed.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                                        (J. P. GUPTA)
                                                            JUDGE
