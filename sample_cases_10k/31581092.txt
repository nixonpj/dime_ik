                                     PLA No.320 of 2013
                             IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                            Testamentary and Intestate Jurisdiction
                                       ORIGINAL SIDE


                                  IN THE GOODS OF:
                            DEBENDRA CHANDRA PODDAR (DEC)



         BEFORE:
        The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>
<p id="p_1">        Date : 18th January, 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                                  Appearance:
</p><p id="p_4">                                                            Ms. Hasnuhana Chakraborty, Adv.
</p><p id="p_5">                                                                 Mr. Ranjit Kumar Basu, Adv.
</p><p id="p_6">                                                                            ...for the petitioner

                                                                Mr. Subhasish Sengupta, Adv.
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                     Mr. Bikas Goswami, Adv.
</p><p id="p_8">                                                                         Mr. Amit Halder, Adv.
</p><p id="p_9">                                                                  ...for Mr. Debabrata Poddar


           The Court : Pursuant to the summons served upon Sri Debashis Poddar who

appears to be one of the witnesses, Sri Poddar has appeared and stated on oath that the

signatures appearing in the document said to be a Will of his father are the signatures of

his father. The witness has also identified the signatures of Smt. Manisha Poddar and

Sri Debashis Poddar. The said signatures duly identified in the said document by Sri

Debashis Poddar marked as 'A1' to 'A6' are of Debendra Chandra Poddar, 'B' is of

Debashis Poddar and 'C' is of Manisha Poddar.

</p><p id="p_10">           In view of an application filed by Sri Debabrata Poddar, one of the sons of the

deceased testator, further hearing of this application is deferred.

</p><p id="p_11">           The matter is adjourned for three weeks.

           Sri Debashis Poddar has signed once in a ball pen and once in an ink pen on two

pages. The said to pages are kept with the record.



</p><p id="p_12">                                                                         (SOUMEN SEN, J.)
B.Pal
