<div class="doc_title">3 vs The State Of West Bengal &amp; Ors on 2 March, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                1




Ct-05   02.3.2017                       WP 4713(w) of 2017

                                       Noni Gopal Das &amp; Anr.
13
                                                 Vs.
                                   The State of West Bengal &amp; Ors.


                       Mr. Kallol Kumar Maity
                                    ... For the Petitioners

ar                     Mr. Asish Guha
                       Mr. Naren Ghosh Dastidar
                                    ... For the State


                       Let the affidavit of service filed in Court today
                    be kept with the record.


                       The petitioners file the present writ petition
                    for a direction upon the respondent no. 4, the</pre>
<p id="p_1">                    Block    Development              Officer,   Contai-III,    to
                    consider the representation of the petitioners
                    received on 24th January, 2017.
</p>

<p id="p_2">                       Mr. Maity, learned advocate appearing for the
                    petitioners, submits that the petitioners had
                    made     a     representation          before    the    Block
                    Development            Officer,      Contai-III,       thereby
                    ventilating their grievance that the respondent
                    authority is going to construct Vat in front of the
                    petitioners' dwelling house for collection of soil
                    waste at plot no. 219, Mouza-Guagechhia under
                    police       station       Marishda,         District-Purba
                    Medinipur, which would create health pollution
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                            2</span>


as well as harzard of the family members of the
petitioners.
</p>

<p id="p_3">   Mr. Ghosh Dastidar, learned advocate for the
respondent authorities, submits that there is no
document regarding construction of the Vat in
front of the petitioners' house.
</p>

<p id="p_4">   Considering the submissions advanced by
the learned advocates and after perusing the
record, I direct the respondent no. 4, the Block
Development         Officer,   Contai-III,       to   take   a
decision       in    respect     of        the    petitioners'
representation received on 24th January, 2017
within six weeks from the date of communication
of the order, after giving the petitioners or their
authorised      representative        an    opportunity      of
hearing and thereafter communicate the decision
to the petitioner within one week.
</p>

<p id="p_5">   The writ petition is accordingly disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_6">   There shall be no order as to costs.
</p>

<p id="p_7">   Urgent photostat certified copy of this order,
if applied for, be given to the parties on usual
undertaking.
</p>



<p id="p_8">                       (Samapti Chatterjee, J.)
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> 3</span>
