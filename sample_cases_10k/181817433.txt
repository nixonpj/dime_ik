                   however, it is the grievance of the petitioner that some amount

                   has been recovered from the gratuity amount for which the

                   petitioner seeks liberty to approach the departmental authority

                   by filing appropriate representation.
</p>

                   of as not pressed, however, with liberty to the petitioner to

                     has already submitted his application. In case any representation

                     is filed within a period of four weeks from today, the respondent

                     no.6 shall dispose of the same within a period of eight weeks

                     thereafter by passing a reasoned order.
