<p id="p_1">                                               -------------

<p id="p_2">13   03-04-2015                The petitioners have filed the present writ petition
                  seeking a direction to the respondents to remove the alleged
                  encroachment from the private land belonging to them. They have
                  further prayed for restraining the respondents from constructing
                  public road over the private land of the petitioners under Pradhan
                  Mantri Gramin Sadak Yojna.
</p><p id="p_3">                               By order dated 10.12.2014 passed by a Bench of this
                  Court, the respondent State of Bihar as also Union of India were
                  directed to file their counter affidavits. In compliance of the
                  aforesaid order, a counter affidavit has been filed on behalf of the
                  respondent no.2 on 05.01.2015. In paragraph 5 of the aforesaid
                  counter affidavit it has been stated that the lands in question were
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court CWJC No.8497 of 2007 (13) dt.03-04-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                       measured by Anchal Amin, Begusarai and it was found that the
                       public road has been constructed over the government's land. On
                       the basis of measurement taken, a report dated 17.12.2014 was
                       submitted by the Anchal Adhikari, Begusarai to the District
                       Collector, Begusari which has been brought on record as
                       Annexure-A to the aforesaid counter affidavit.
</p><p id="p_4">                                      The facts stated in the aforesaid counter affidavit has
                       not been controverted by the writ petitioners.
</p><p id="p_5">                                      In above view of the matter, no relief can be granted
                       to the petitioners in the present proceeding.
</p><p id="p_6">                                      Consequently, the petition has to fail and is,
                       accordingly, dismissed.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                              (Birendra Prasad Verma, J)
Tahir/-
