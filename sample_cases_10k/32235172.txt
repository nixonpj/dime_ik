<div class="doc_title">Prabhu Dayal Sagar @ Sagar Yadav vs The State Of Bihar on 4 August, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                        IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                       Criminal Miscellaneous No.2738 of 2014
                       Arising Out of PS.Case No. -173 Year- 2010 Thana -NAYA RAM NAGAR District- MUNGER
                     ======================================================
                     1. Prabhu Dayal Sagar @ Sagar Yadav S/O Late Ramdhani Yadav R/O
                     Mohalla- Laldarwaja, P.S.- Kotwali, District- Munger .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                     1. The State Of Bihar
                     2. Naresh Yadav S/o late Chaturi Yadav, R/o village Milkichak, P.S.
                     Nayaramnagar, Distt. Munger                      .... .... Opposite Party/s
                     ======================================================
                     CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA PRAKASH
                     ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">4       04-08-2014                 The Petitioner seeks quashing of the order of

                     cognizance dated 23.4.2013 passed by the Chief Judicial

                     Magistrate, Munger in Naya Ram Nagar P.S. case No.173 of 2010

                     (G.R. No.2145 of 2010).
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   It has been contended on behalf of the Petitioner that in

                     the facts of the case no offence under <a href="/doc/409589/" id="a_1">Section 304</a> I.P.C. is made

                     out and there is no material to suggest that it was the Petitioner

                     who was getting the contractual work done. In fact the Petitioner

                     happens to be a Constable in the Bihar Government and, therefore,

                     he should not be put on trial.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Considering that the Petitioner seeks quashing on

                     disputed questions of fact which cannot be gone into at this stage,

                     I am not inclined to interfere in the matter.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   The application is dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                  (Anjana Prakash, J)
Narendra/-
</p>
