<div class="doc_title">Omana K. vs The State Of Kerala on 29 January, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">            IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

                               PRESENT

            THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SUNIL THOMAS

     TUESDAY ,THE 29TH DAY OF JANUARY 2019 / 9TH MAGHA, 1940

                      Bail Appl..No. 484 of 2019

 AGAINST THE ORDER IN CMP 41/2019 of JUDICIAL MAGISTRATE OF FIRST
               CLASS -I,HOSDRUG DATED 15-01-2019

 CRIME NO. 6/2019 OF NEELESWARAM EXCISE RANGE OFFICE, KASARGOD



PETITIONER/ACCUSED:


             OMANA K., AGED 61 YEARS
             W/O.KUNHIKANNAN, RESIDING AT KAROTI HOUSE,
             MAYYANGANAM, KARINTHALAM VILLAGE, VELLARIKUNDU
             TALUK, KASARAGOD DISTRICT.

             BY ADVS.
             SRI.T.MADHU
             SMT.C.R.SARADAMANI




RESPONDENT/STATE:

             THE STATE OF KERALA, THROUGH THE EXCISE RANGE
             OFFICER, NILESHWAR EXCISE RANGE OFFICE, KASARAGOD
             DISTRICT, REPRESENTED BY THE PUBLIC PROSECUTOR, HIGH
             COURT OF KERALA, ERNAKULAM.




             PP AJITH MURALI


THIS BAIL APPLICATION HAVING COME UP FOR ADMISSION ON 29.01.2019,
THE COURT ON THE SAME DAY PASSED THE FOLLOWING:
 Bail Appl..No. 484 of 2019
                                 2



                             ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">         The petitioner herein stands arrayed as the accused

   in Crime No.6/2019 of Nileshwar Excise Range Office,

   Kasaragod District, for offence punishable under section

   55(g) of the Kerala Abkari Act.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">         2.   The allegation of the prosecution is that on

   9.1.2019 at 10.45 am., 30 litres of wash was seized from

   the house of the petitioner herein.        Alleging that the

   petitioner was in the process of distilling of arrack, crime

   was registered and the petitioner was arrested. She is in

   custody since then. She seeks bail.


</p><p id="p_4">         3.   It seems that the investigation has substantially

   progressed.    The petitioner is not involved in any other

   crime.      Though    substantial   quantity   of   wash   was

   recovered, considering the quantity of arrack that could

   distill from it, the age of the petitioner herein and also the

   fact that the petitioner is a women and has no criminal

   antecedents, I am inclined to grant bail to the petitioner

   herein on the following conditions:
 Bail Appl..No. 484 of 2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         3</span>

</p><p id="p_5">           (i) Petitioner shall execute a bond for a sum of

   Rs.40,000/-      (Rupees      forty       thousand    only)    with   two

   sureties for the like sum each to the satisfaction of the

   jurisdictional Court.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">          (ii)   She shall appear before the investigating officer

   as     and    when   called    for        and   co-operate     with   the

   investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_8">          (iii) She shall not threaten, coerce or intimidate the

   defacto complainant and the witnesses nor shall she

   interfere in the process of investigation.

</p><p id="p_9">          (iv) She shall not get involved in any other crime

   and involvement of it, will be a ground for cancellation of

   bail in this case.
</p>
<p id="p_10">          Bail application is allowed as above.



</p><p id="p_11">                                                      Sd/-
</p>
<p id="p_12">                                             SUNIL THOMAS, JUDGE
   R.AV

                                  //True Copy//


                                                    PA to Judge
