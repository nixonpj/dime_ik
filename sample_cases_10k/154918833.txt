PETITION FOR GRANT OF SUCCESSION CERTIFICATE UNDER SECTION 372
               OF THE INDIAN SUCCESSION ACT, 1925

</p><p id="p_8">1. Date of Institution of Petition         : 09.10.2018
</p><p id="p_9">2. Date of Judgment                        : 15.10.2019
</p><p id="p_10">3. Decision                                : Petition Allowed




Vaibhav Mittal Vs. State etc.        SC No.96/2018     (15.10.2019)        Page No.1 of 6
</p><p id="p_11"> 1.              Vide this Judgment this Court shall dispose­off petition filed by Sh.

Vaibhav Mittal for grant of succession certificate in his favour in respect of debts and

securities i.e. 50 shares of Bajaj Finserv Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE918I01018 of

Rs.7,439.40 each, 100 shares of Bharti Airtel Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE397D01024 of

Rs.361.55 each, 200 shares of Infosys Ltd. ISIN No. INE009A01021 of Rs.774.55 each

and 100 shares of Shriram Transport Fin. Company Ltd. bearing ISIN No.

INE721A01013 of Rs.1,032.15 each which existed in the name of late Sh. Anil

Aggarwal.
</p>
<p id="p_12">2.              In the petition, it is inter­alia pleaded that petitioner is the son of late Sh.

Anil Aggarwal who expired on 07.11.2016.              At the time of death, late Sh. Anil

Aggarwal was ordinarily residing within the territorial jurisdiction of this Court.

Petitioner has averred that he is son of the deceased late Sh. Anil Aggarwal and Smt.

Sudha Rani and Smt. Upasana are other legal heirs of deceased late Sh. Anil Aggarwal

along­with petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_13">3.              The record of Court file reflects that the notice of this petition was given

to the general public by way of publication in the newspaper 'Veer Arjun' on

20.11.2018. In response to the notice, no person from the general public had come

forward to offer any objection(s) qua grant of succession certificate to the petitioner Sh.

Vaibhav Mittal.
</p>
<p id="p_14">4.              During the summary proceedings conducted as per <a href="/doc/994957/" id="a_1">Section 373</a> of the

Indian Succession Act, 1925, five persons were examined viz. PW1 Mr. Vaibhav




Vaibhav Mittal Vs. State etc.         SC No.96/2018         (15.10.2019)      Page No.2 of 6
 Mittal, RW1 Sh. Satyapal (Patwari), RW2 Ms. Sudha Rani, RW3 Ms. Upasna and

RW4 Sh. Rajesh Kumar.
</p>
<p id="p_15">5.              During the testimony of petitioner, PW1 Mr. Vaibhav Mittal had deposed

in line with the petition. In support of her testimony, PW1 had relied upon documents

viz. Copy of Aadhar Card of petitioner Ex.PW1/1 (OSR), death certificate of deceased

Sh. Anil Aggarwal Ex.PW1/2, copy of voter ID card of deceased Ex.PW1/3 (OSR),

statement of latest value of debt and security in original generated from internet

Ex.PW1/4.
</p>
<p id="p_16">6.              During the testimony of RW1 Sh. Satyapal, Patwari, SDM Office Vivek

Vihar, Delhi deposed that the legal heirs of the deceased namely late Sh. Anil

Aggarwal who had expired on 07.11.2016 are namely Sh. Vaibhav Mittal (son), Smt.

Sudha Rani (wife) and Smt. Upasana (daughter). Reply of the Executive Magistrate

(Vivek Vihar), Delhi being Ex.RW1/1 and his identity proof being Ex.RW1/2 (OSR).

</p><p id="p_17">7.              RW2 Ms. Sudha Rani has filed his no objection in the form of affidavit

Ex.RW2/1 wherein she has submitted/deposed that she has no objection if the

succession certificate with regard to debts and securities of deceased is granted in

favour of petitioner. Her identity proof was exhibited as Ex.RW2/2 (OSR) on record.

</p><p id="p_18">8.              RW3 Ms. Upasna has filed her no objection in the form of affidavit

Ex.RW3/1 wherein she has submitted/deposed that she has no objection if the

succession certificate with regard to debts and securities of deceased is granted in

favour of petitioner. Her identity proof was exhibited as Ex.RW3/2 (OSR) on record.




Vaibhav Mittal Vs. State etc.      SC No.96/2018      (15.10.2019)     Page No.3 of 6
</p><p id="p_19"> 9.              RW4 Sh. Rajesh Kumar, Manager Legal, of respondent no. 4 has filed

client master and holding statement of DMAT account No. 10344608 maintained by

late Sh. Anil Aggarwal with them.          He exhibited the client master and holding

statement (3pages) as Ex.RW1/4 and his ID proof as Ex.RW4/2 (OSR) in his evidence.

</p><p id="p_20">10.             I have heard Ld. Counsel for the petitioner and he/she had referred to the

testimonies of PW1 Sh. Vaibhav Mittal, RW1 Sh. Satyapal (Patwari), RW2 Ms. Sudha

Rani, RW3 Ms. Upasna and RW4 Sh. Rajesh Kumar and have submitted that they are

sufficient to conclude that the petitioner is entitled to grant of succession certificate in

respect of 50 shares of Bajaj Finserv Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE918I01018 of

Rs.7,439.40 each, 100 shares of Bharti Airtel Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE397D01024 of

Rs.361.55 each, 200 shares of Infosys Ltd. ISIN No. INE009A01021 of Rs.774.55 each

and 100 shares of Shriram Transport Fin. Company Ltd. bearing ISIN No.

INE721A01013 of Rs.1,032.15 each which existed in the name of late Sh. Anil

Aggarwal.
</p>
<p id="p_21">11.             I have considered the submissions made by Ld. Counsel for the petitioner

and have perused the record of the Court file, particularly the testimonies of PW1 Sh.

Vaibhav Mittal, RW1 Sh. Satyapal (Patwari), RW2 Ms. Sudha Rani, RW3 Ms. Upasna

and RW4 Sh. Rajesh Kumar which show that petitioner is the son of late Sh. Anil

Aggarwal and Ms. Sudha Rani and Ms. Upasana are other legal heirs of deceased late

Sh. Anil Aggarwal along­with petitioner because no one came forward to oppose

succession petition despite publication in newspaper. Death certificate of late Sh. Anil




Vaibhav Mittal Vs. State etc.       SC No.96/2018        (15.10.2019)      Page No.4 of 6
 Aggarwal has also been produced in Court by petitioner and as per witnesses deceased

late Sh. Anil Aggarwal had 50 shares of Bajaj Finserv Ltd. bearing ISIN No.

INE918I01018 of Rs.7,439.40 each, 100 shares of Bharti Airtel Ltd. bearing ISIN No.

INE397D01024 of Rs.361.55 each, 200 shares of Infosys Ltd. ISIN No.

INE009A01021 of Rs.774.55 each and 100 shares of Shriram Transport Fin. Company

Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE721A01013 of Rs.1,032.15 each. Therefore, succession

certificate be issued in favour of petitioner Sh. Vaibhav Mittal with respect to 50 shares

of Bajaj Finserv Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE918I01018 of Rs.7,439.40 each, 100 shares

of Bharti Airtel Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE397D01024 of Rs.361.55 each, 200 shares

of Infosys Ltd. ISIN No. INE009A01021 of Rs.774.55 each and 100 shares of Shriram

Transport Fin. Company Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE721A01013 of Rs.1,032.15 each

which existed in the name of late Sh. Anil Aggarwal.

</p><p id="p_22">12.             The succession certificate shall be considered only as an entitlement of

the petitioner to get transferred said shares in his name/to receive the amount due

towards late Sh. Anil Aggarwal with respect to 50 shares of Bajaj Finserv Ltd. bearing

ISIN No. INE918I01018 of Rs.7,439.40 each, 100 shares of Bharti Airtel Ltd. bearing

ISIN No. INE397D01024 of Rs.361.55 each, 200 shares of Infosys Ltd. ISIN No.

INE009A01021 of Rs.774.55 each and 100 shares of Shriram Transport Fin. Company

Ltd. bearing ISIN No. INE721A01013 of Rs.1,032.15 each which existed in the name

of late Sh. Anil Aggarwal. The succession certificate shall not be considered directions

as such to the concerned officials/manager of concerned offices to transfer said shares




Vaibhav Mittal Vs. State etc.      SC No.96/2018       (15.10.2019)      Page No.5 of 6
 in name of petitioner/to release the aforesaid amounts to the petitioner and the

manager/concerned authority(ies) of concerned office(s) shall be competent to seek

compliance of formalities, if any, to be fulfilled by the petitioner for transfer of shares

in his name/release of the aforesaid amount and further transfer should be

made/amount should be released only as per rules. Upon filing of requisite Court fees

and indemnity bond(s) with surety bond(s) by petitioner, succession certificate be

issued. File be consigned to record room after due compliance.</p><pre id="pre_2">
