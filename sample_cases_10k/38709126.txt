<div class="doc_title">Nawal Kishore Mishra vs State Of Bihar &amp; Anr on 4 November, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.51265 of 2015
                 Arising Out of PS.Case No. -1382 Year- 2014 Thana -GOPALGANJ COMPLAINT CASE District-
                                                         GOPALGANJ
                 ======================================================
                 Nawal Kishore Mishra
                                                                      .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                 State of Bihar &amp; Anr
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Ranjeet Kumar Pandey
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Madan Kumar(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   04-11-2015               Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                             The petitioner being the father of the husband of the

                 complainant is apprehending arrest in a complaint case wherein

                 process has been directed to be issued after cognizance being

                 taken for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Sections 498A</a> and <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_2">406</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a>

                 of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             The basic accusation is of torture.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that

                 the husband of the complainant has been granted bail on readiness

                 to make payment of maintenance amount of `5,000/- and the same

                 is being paid by the husband of the complainant. statement to that

                 effect has been made in para 10 of the petition which reads as

                 follows:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                 "That it is also humbly stated and submitted that the
                                  husband of the complainant is also paying `5,000/-
                                  per month for maintenance of the complainant in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.51265 of 2015 (2) dt.04-11-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




                                       view of the undertaking given before the learned
                                       Sessions Judge."
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">
                                    Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above named

                     petitioner be released on anticipatory bail, in the event of arrest or

                     surrender before the learned Court below within a period of 12

                     weeks from today, on furnishing bail bond of `10,000/- (ten

                     thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                     satisfaction of the learned C.J.M., Gopalganj in connection with

                     Complaint Case No. 1382 of 2014, subject to the conditions as

                     laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_4">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.</blockquote><p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                                                (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Amrendra/-
</p><pre id="pre_2"> U         T
