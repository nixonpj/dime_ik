<p id="p_1">           TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.J

                               It is pleaded that the petitioner who was a member of the master

           cadre was promoted as Lecturer in the year 2008. The seniority of the

           master cadre has been determined in the light of Rule 11 of the Punjab State

           Education Class III (School Cadre) Service Rules, 1978 and a seniority list

           was issued on 23.09.2012.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               Counsel would argue that a number of officials namely

           Rajinder Kumar, Balihar Chand, Baldev Singh, Amarjit and Paramjit Singh

           have been shown junior to the petitioner in the master cadre. Counsel would

           advert to order dated 06.08.2001 at Annexure P-4, whereby all the

           aforenoticed juniors to the petitioner have been granted promotion to the

           post of Lecturer w.e.f. the year 2001. Counsel contends that in the light of

           fixation of seniority in the year 2012, the petitioner is vested with a right to

           be promoted to the post of Lecturer, be it on a notional basis w.e.f. the date

           his juniors have been so promoted.
</p>

           petition is disposed of with a direction to respondent No.1 to consider the
