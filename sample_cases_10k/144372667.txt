                 Sahab Kumar @ Sahab Kumar Paswan son of Narayan Singh @ Narayan

                 For the Opposite Party :      Mr. Bhanu Pratap Singh(App)
                 (In Cr.Misc. No.43135 of 2014)
                 For the Petitioner        :   Mr. Swapnil Kumar Singh
                 For the Opposite Party :      Mr. Rajesh Kumar(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                                                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   13-02-2015                           The present applications are being taken up

                 out of turn since a supplementary affidavit has been filed to the

                 effect that father of petitioner Sahab Kumar @ Sahab Kumar

                 Paswan has died and as petitioner is the only son, hence he

                 has to perform last rituals.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                         The petitioner Sahab Kumar @ Sahab Kumar

                 Paswan      being      appointed        as    panchayat         teacher      whereas

                 petitioner Praveen Kumar is Panchayat Secretary who are
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.42692 of 2014 (3) dt.13-02-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                           2/3</span>




             languishing in custody since 08.06.2014 and 09.09.2014 in a

             case registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>,

             <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_2">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_4">471</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120B</a> of the I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                      It   is    alleged       that   Panchayat         Secretary

             Praveen       Kumar        completed        the    appointment       process       of

             panchayat teacher without signature of Panchayat Mukhiya

             though only with signature of Ram Badan Yadav, a teacher of

             the middle school. It is further alleged that Sahab Kumar @

             Sahab Kumar Paswan and others appointed on the post of

             teacher on the basis of forged Teacher Eligibility Test certificate

             submitted by them.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                      It   is    submitted       on   behalf    of      petitioner

             Praveen Kumar that proceeding book reflects the presence of

             Panchayat Mukhiya and other persons during the meeting held

             for appointment. Moreover for submitting a forged Teacher

             Eligibility Test certificate this petitioner cannot be held

             responsible and as soon as it was verified that certificates are

             forged, all such appointments were cancelled.

</p><p id="p_5">                                      It is submitted on behalf of petitioner Sahab

             Kumar @ Sahab Kumar Paswan that petitioner, in pursuance

             to appointment never received any monetary benefits. A

             statement to that effect has been made in para 9 of the petition.

             Moreover other similarly situated co-accused has been granted

             bail vide Cr. Misc. No. 996 of 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                      Considering         the    aforesaid     facts,    let   the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">             Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.42692 of 2014 (3) dt.13-02-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                                       3/3</span>




                         petitioners, above named, be released on bail on furnishing bail

                         bond of Rs. 10,000/-(ten thousand) each with two sureties of

                         the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned Chief

                         Judicial Magistrate, Munger in connection with Bariyarpur P.S.

                         Case No. 71 of 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">                                                                           (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
