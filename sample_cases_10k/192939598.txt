                                Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.15217 of 2014
                 ======================================================
                 Jagdishpuri
                                                                       .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                 The State of Bihar &amp; Ors
                                                                      .... .... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr. Ashish Giri
                 For the Respondent/s       : Mr. Kundan Bhadur Singh
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">02/ 07-08-2017                             The present writ application has been filed

                 for a direction to the respondent authorities to get the

                 encroachment removed over the ancestral agricultural land of the

                 petitioner appertaining to khata no. 57, Survey plot no. 1329,

                 measuring an area of 03 bigha 19 katha situated in village Sirisia,

                 Police Station - Navtan, District - Siwan, whereupon respondent

                 no. 4, the Principal of Government Primary School, Sirisia has

                 illegally constructed the school building without any direction or

                 without there being any acquisition of the land in question.

</p><p id="p_2">                                           It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioner that the land is recorded in the Khatiyan in the name of

                 forefather of the petitioner and representation to that effect has

                 been submitted to the District Magistrate and other authorities,

                 hence after acquisition of the land in question and payment of

                 compensation, the construction will be made.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court CWJC No.15217 of 2014 (2) dt.07-08-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                 2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_3">                                                It is expected from the counsel for

                    respondent nos. 1 to 3 to file counter affidavit within a period of

                    four weeks.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                              Issue notice to respondent no. 4 by ordinary

                    process as well as by registered cover with A/D for which

                    requisites must be filed within four weeks, failing which this

                    application shall stand dismissed without further reference to the

                    Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                             (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
    DKS/-
</p>
