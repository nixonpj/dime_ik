           IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

        R/SPECIAL CRIMINAL APPLICATION NO. 3759 of 2019
==========================================================
              KIRPALSINH JUNGBAHADURSINH CHHABDA
                              Versus
                        STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
JAY R SHAH(8428) for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MR JINESH H KAPADIA(5601) for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MR PRANAV TRIVEDI, APP (2) for the Respondent(s) No. 1
==========================================================
 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.H.VORA

                            Date : 13/09/2019

                             ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.    By way of present petition under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> and <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_2">227</a> of
the Constitution of India read with <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">section 482</a> of the Criminal
Procedure Code, the petitioner has made following prayer in
terms of para - 9(A) :-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">      "9(A) That this Hon'ble Court be pleased to issue
      appropriate writ, order or direction quashing and setting
      aside the condition no.3 and 4 imposed in order dated
      27.04.2018 passed on Criminal Misc. Application No.2745
      of 2018 and further be quashed the order dated
      17.10.2018 passed on Criminal Misc. Application No.6094
      of 2018 and order to released the passport of the
      petitioner."
</blockquote>

<p id="p_2">2.    Having heard submissions made at bar and considering
the fact that the petitioner obtained clean acquittal from this
Court being Criminal Appeal No.1740 of 2012, there is no any
reason / ground to impose any conditions, as imposed by the
impugned order more particularly, condition no.3 and 4 in the
order dated 27.04.2018 in Criminal Misc. Application No.2745
of 2018.
</p>

</p><p id="p_4">3.       Accordingly, this petition deserves to be accepted and it
is hereby allowed. Relief in terms of para 9(A) is granted.
</p><p id="p_5">         Direct service is permitted.
</p>
