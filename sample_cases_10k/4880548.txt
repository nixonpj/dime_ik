<div class="doc_title">Aasifhusen Iqbalahmed Siddiki vs State Of on 2 April, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">  
	 
	 AASIFHUSEN IQBALAHMED SIDDIKI....Applicant(s)V/SSTATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	

 
 


	 


	R/CR.MA/1510/2013
	                                                                    
	                           ORDER

 
	  
	  
		 
			 

IN
			THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
		
	

 


CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR REGULAR BAIL) NO. 1510 of 2013
 

===========================================================
 


AASIFHUSEN IQBALAHMED
SIDDIKI....Applicant(s)
 


Versus
 


STATE OF
GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
 

================================================================
 

Appearance:
 

MRS
SHILPA R SHAH, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
 

MS.M.D.
MEHTA, ADDITIONAL PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the Respondent(s) No. 1
 

================================================================
 


	 
		  
		 
		  
			 
				 

CORAM:
				
				
			
			 
				 

HONOURABLE
				MR.JUSTICE ANANT S. DAVE
			
		
	

 


 

 


Date : 02/04/2013
 


 

 


ORAL ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">1.	This
application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal
Procedure, 1973 for regular bail in connection with First Information
Report registered at C.R.No.I-29/2012
with DCB Police Station, Vadodara for
the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1374258/" id="a_2">Sections 364A</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_3">120B</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506(2)</a> of
the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1961417/" id="a_6">Section 25(1)(A)</a>,(B) of the Arms Act.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_2">2.	Learned
advocate for the applicant submits that chargesheet is filed, but
applicant will be available at the time of trial. Considering above
aspects, the applicant may be enlarged on bail by imposing suitable
conditions. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_3">3.	Ms.M.D.
</p><p id="p_4">Mehta, learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the respondent-State
has opposed grant of bail to the applicant on the ground that the
applicant has no doubt taken complainant at various places, but
manner and method in which the amount was extracted from him keeping
the complainant in his custody and sufficient material exist on
record to show that the amount received from the complainant was
transferred in the account of the brother of the applicant-accused
and therefore, no bail be granted to the applicant. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_5">4.	Having
heard the learned advocates for the parties and considering the
overall facts and circumstances of the case and now, investigation is
over and the applicant is in jail since 23.10.2012 and nature of
allegations, role attributed to the applicant and punishment
prescribed for the alleged offences, by imposing suitable conditions
so as to secure his presence at the stage of trial, the applicant is
ordered to be enlarged on bail.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_6">5.	In
the facts and circumstances of the case, the application is allowed
and the applicant is ordered to be released on bail in connection
with first information report registered at C.R.No.I-29/2012
with DCB Police Station, Vadodara,
 on executing a bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand
only) with one surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the
trial court and subject to the conditions that he shall;
</p> 


<p id="p_7">(a)
not take undue advantage of liberty or misuse liberty;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_8">(b)
not act in a manner injurious to the interest of the prosecution; 
</p>
 


 


 
	  


<p id="p_9">	surrender
	passport, if any, to the lower Court within a week;
</p>	 


	

 
	  


<p id="p_10">	not
	leave the State of Gujarat without prior permission of the Sessions
	Judge concerned;
</p>	 


	

 


<p id="p_11">(e)
mark presence at the concerned police station on the first Sunday of
every month between 10.00 a.m. And 3.00 p.m. for three months only; 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_12">(f)
furnish the present address of residence to the I.O. and also to the
Court at the time of execution of the bond and shall not change the
resident without prior permission of this Court;
</p> 


 


 
	 


<p id="p_13">	6.	The
	Authorities will release the applicant only if not required in
	connection with any other offence for the time being.
</p>
 


 


 


 


 
	 


<p id="p_14">	7.	If
	breach of any of the above conditions is committed, the Sessions
	Judge concerned will be free to issue warrant or take appropriate
	action in the matter.
</p>
 
	 


	
	 


<p id="p_15">	8.	Bail
	bond to be executed before the lower Court having jurisdiction to
	try the case.
</p>	 


	

 
	 


<p id="p_16">	9.	For
	modification and/or deletion of any of the conditions hereinabove,
	the applicant/s will be at liberty to approach the concerned Court
	and such Court shall decide the application for modification and/or
	deletion of any of the conditions of this order in accordance with
	law.
</p>	 


	

 
	 


<p id="p_17">	10.	At
	the trial, the trial Court shall not be influenced by the
	observations of preliminary nature, qua the evidence at this stage,
	made by this Court while enlarging the applicant on bail.
</p>	 


	

 


<p id="p_18">11.	Rule
is made absolute to the aforesaid extent. 
</p>
 


<p id="p_19">	Direct
Service is permitted.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_20">(ANANT S.DAVE, J.) 

 

chandresh
