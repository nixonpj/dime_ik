                                 [[




                                               Ms. Smita Pal
                                               Mr. Syamal Kumar Das
                                               Ms. Debarati Bag
                                                     .....For the Petitioner
                                               Mr. Robiul Islam
                                                     .....For the State

                    Petitioner complains of police inaction.

                    Learned advocate appearing for the petitioner submits that, the petitioner

             possesses requisite permission to erect a mobile tower. The private respondents are</pre>

<p id="p_1">             preventing the petitioner from doing so.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                    Learned advocate appearing for the State submits that, the complaint of the

             petitioner was enquired into. On enquiry the police learnt that, not only the private

             respondents but also the entire locality, were objecting to the erection of the mobile

             tower in view of the health hazards.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                    In the facts of the present case, it would be appropriate to permit the parties to

             file affidavits.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                    Let affidavit in opposition be filed within one week after vacation, reply thereto,

             if any, be filed two weeks thereafter.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                    The writ petition will be treated as ready for hearing immediately on

             completion of the time stipulated for filing affidavits.

</p><p id="p_6">                    Liberty to the parties to mention for early hearing.

                    Urgent certified website copies of this order, if applied for, be made available to

             the parties upon compliance of the requisite formalities.

</p><p id="p_7">                                                                             (Debangsu Basak, J.)
