             IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT
                        CHANDIGARH

                                           CRM-M-49018 of 2017.
                                           Decided on:-December 21, 2017.


Parminder Singh and others
                                                             .........Petitioners.
                                  Versus
State of Punjab and others

                                                             .........Respondents.


CORAM:       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE HARI PAL VERMA.

             *****

Present:-    Mr. Ramesh Sharma, Advocate
             for the petitioners.

HARI PAL VERMA, J. (ORAL)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Prayer in this petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.PC is for

issuance of a direction to respondents No.2 to 4 to protect the life and liberty

of the petitioners and their family members at the hands of private

respondents No.5 to 8.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioners states that for the protection

of life and liberty of the petitioners, they have already made a representation

dated 19.11.2017 (Annexure P-1) to Senior Superintendent of Police, Fazilka,

and at this stage, the petitioners would be satisfied if a direction is issued to

respondent No.2 to consider the said representation viz. a viz. their prayer for

protection of the life and liberty only.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Notice of motion to respondents No.1 to 3 only.




</p><p id="p_4">                                  1 of 2
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">               ::: Downloaded on - 23-12-2017 22:55:30 :::</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> CRM-M-49018 of 2017                                                        2</span>




             On asking of this Court, Mr. M.S. Nagra, AAG, Punjab accepts

notice on behalf of these respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             Let requisite number of copies of paper book be given to learned

State counsel during the course of the day.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_7">             Without commenting anything on the merits of the case and

considering the restricted prayer of the petitioners seeking protection of their

life and liberty, the present petition is disposed of with a direction to the

respondent No.2 to look into the contents of representation (Annexure P-1)

qua protection of life and liberty of the petitioners only and to act in

accordance with law.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                      (HARI PAL VERMA)
