HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD, LUCKNOW BENCH
 
 

?Court No. - 8
 

 
Case :- U/S 482/378/407 No. - 4126 of 2007
 

 
Petitioner :- Smt. Narmrata Rastogi
 
Respondent :- State Of U.P.
 
Petitioner Counsel :- Salil K. Srivastava
 
Respondent Counsel :- Govt. Advocate
 

 
Hon'ble Ravindra Singh,J. 
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">List is revised.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">Learned counsel for the applicant is not present.
</p> 
<p id="p_3">Heard learned A.G.A. and perused the record.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">This application has been filed with a prayer to expedite the proceedings of case No. 53 of 2005 under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">section 498-A</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_3">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506</a>, <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_5">406</a> IPC and section 3/4 D.P. Act, pending in the court of learned C.J.M. Lucknow.
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Considering the facts and circumstances of the case it is directed that proceedings of the above mentioned shall be expedited, in case the proceedings are stayed by any other court.
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Accordingly this application is finally disposed of. 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">Order Date :- 9.12.2010
