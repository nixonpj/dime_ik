<p id="p_1">AJAY KUMAR MITTAL, J.: (Oral)

            This order shall disposed of a bunch of 13 cases bearing

COCP Nos.2373, 2374, 2375, 2376, 2377, 2378, 2379, 2380, 2381,

2382, 2383, 2384 and 2385 of 2011            as common violations are

involved in these cases. For brevity, the facts are being taken from

COCP No.2373 of 2011, which are given hereunder:-

</p><p id="p_2">            The grievance of the petitioners, in this petition, is that the

respondent had violated order dated 06.10.2010 passed by this

Court in RFA No.3841 of 2001 whereby directions were issued to the

respondent to disburse the compensation after seeking the account

numbers of the petitioners and depositing the same therein.

</p><p id="p_3">            Learned counsel for the respondent states that the

amount has been received by the respondent and shall be deposited

in the accounts of the petitioners within a week.

</p><p id="p_4">            In view of the above, the present petitions are rendered

infructuous and the same are disposed of as such.
</p><p id="p_5"> COCP No.2373 of 2011 (O &amp; M)                                        ::5::
</p>
<p id="p_6">            However, in case, the petitioners find that insufficient claim

has been granted, they shall be entitled to take recourse to the legal

remedies available to them, in accordance with law.



</p><p id="p_7">                                               (AJAY KUMAR MITTAL)
                                                      JUDGE
November 30, 2011
sukhpreet
