<div class="doc_title">Ranjeet Mahto vs The State Of Bihar on 1 February, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.49299 of 2015
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -248 Year- 2014 Thana -LAKHISARAI District- LAKHISARAI
                 ======================================================
                 Ranjeet Mahto, son of Madan Mahto, Resident of village- Oraiya, Police
                 Station and District- Lakhisarai.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar.

                                                             .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Rabi Bhushan
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. M.K. Khare(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE JITENDRA MOHAN
                 SHARMA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   01-02-2016                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 A.P.P. representing the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               The petitioner seeks bail in connection with Lakhisarai

                 P.S. Case No. 248 of 2014 registered for the offence punishable

                 under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Section 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                               Allegedly, in the occurrence the petitioner and Subodh

                 Mahto opened fire with pistol, out of which the fire made by

                 Subodh Mahto, hit chest of Gopal Mandal and the fire made by the

                 petitioner, hit right arm of Gopal Mandal resulting Gopal Mandal

                 died at Lakhisarai Sadar Hospital.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               Submission is of false implication and that allegedly

                 the firing made by the petitioner hit right arm of the deceased

                 which is not vital part of the body and, as such, the petitioner who
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.49299 of 2015 (3) dt.01-02-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        is suffering in custody since 05.07.2014, deserves sympathetic

                        consideration to which the learned A.P.P. opposes by submitting

                        that the petitioner is also one of the assailant and further he was

                        earlier convicted in an offence under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_3">Section 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> IPC vide

                        Lakhisarai P.S. Case No. 142 of 1999.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                     In     the     facts    and      circumstances   stated   above,

                        this Court is not inclined to enlarge the petitioner on bail and, as

                        such, prayer stands rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                  (Jitendra Mohan Sharma, J)
sushma/-
</p><pre id="pre_2">  U           T
