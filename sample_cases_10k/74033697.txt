<div class="doc_title">Rijwan @ Rizwan Akhter @ Md. Chand vs The State Of Jharkhand on 23 March, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                         B.A. No. 1271 of 2018
           Rijwan @ Rizwan Akhter @ Md. Chand         .....    Petitioner
                                    -V e r s u s-
           The State of Jharkhand                 .....     Opposite Party
                                    .....
</pre><p id="p_1">           CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANDA SEN
                                    .....</p><pre id="pre_2">
            For the Petitioner      : Mr. Syed Ramiz Zafar, Advocate
            For the State           : A.P.P
                                    .....
3/23.03.2018      Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</pre><p id="p_2">                      The petitioner is an accused for allegedly committing
            offence punishable under <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_1">Sections 366</a> and <a href="/doc/886598/" id="a_2">365</a> of the Indian Penal
            Code, in connection with Lohardaga, Police Station Case No.104 of
            2017, corresponding to G.R. No. 369 of 2017 (S.T. No. 113 of 2017),
            pending in the Court of learned Principal District Sessions Judge, at
            Lohardaga.
</p><p id="p_3">                      The victim has been recovered and her statement was
            recorded under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_3">Section 164</a> of the Cr. P.C.
</p><p id="p_4">                      Learned Addl. P.P. opposes the prayer for bail and after
            going through the case diary submits that there is a direct allegation
            against the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_5">                      I have gone through the statement of the victim girl
            recorded under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_4">Section 164</a> of the Cr. P.C. Since there is a direct
            allegation against the petitioner, I am not inclined to grant privilege
            of bail to the petitioner at this stage. Accordingly, the prayer for bail
            of the petitioner, namely, Rijwan @ Rizwan Akhter @ Md. Chand is
            hereby rejected.
</p><p id="p_6">                      The petitioner is at liberty to renew his prayer for bail after
            examination of the victim girl during trial.
</p><p id="p_7">                      This application stands dismissed.
</p>


<p id="p_8">                                                            (Ananda Sen, J.)
Amar/cp3
