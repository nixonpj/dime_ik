<div class="doc_title">Chandan Singh vs The State Of Bihar on 23 January, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.1151 of 2013
                   ======================================================
                   Chandan Singh
                                                                       .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                   The State Of Bihar
                                                                  .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                   SINGH
                                         ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2     23-01-2013                    Petitioner    is     languishing      in    custody        since

                   17.10.2012 in a case registered for the offences punishable

                   under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_1">Sections 392</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_2">120B</a> of the I.P.C.

</p><p id="p_2">                                    Accusation      is    of    robbing        cash     from    the

                   informant when petitioner was not named in the F.I.R. and his

                   name sprang up on confession of co-accused Shankar Yadav @

                   Suddha who has been granted regular bail vide Cr. Misc. No.

                   34496 of 2012.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                    Considering        the     aforesaid       facts,    let    the

                   petitioner, above named, be released on bail on furnishing bail

                   bond of Rs. 10,000/-(ten thousand) with two sureties of the like

                   amount each to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial

                   Magistrate, Katihar in connection with Berari P.S. Case No. 182

                   of 2012.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


</p><p id="p_5">                                              (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
