<pre id="pre_1">ORDER SHEET

                                    CA No.220 of 2014
                              IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                                 Original Jurisdiction



                                      IN THE MATTER OF :
                                      GALORE SUPPLIERS PVT.LTD. &amp; ORS


      BEFORE:
      The Hon'ble JUSTICE PATHERYA</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Date : 28th March, 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                                             Ms.Manju Bhuteria, Advocate for the
                                             applicants.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
              The Court : In this application under <a href="/doc/1518484/" id="a_1">Sections 391(1)</a> and <a href="/doc/506042/" id="a_2">393</a> of

the Companies Act, 1956, Summons has been taken out for dispensing with the

meetings of the shareholders of the applicant nos.1 to 7.

</p><p id="p_5">              Such prayer is allowed, in view of the written consent given by all

the   shareholders   of    the   applicants    which    are    annexed   in   original   to     the

affidavit in support of the Summons.

</p><p id="p_6">              Summons be signed as on date.
</p>
<p id="p_7">              The application is thus disposed of.

              The applicants are directed to file a petition under <a href="/doc/108996/" id="a_3">Section 391(2)</a>

and <a href="/doc/301194/" id="a_4">394</a> of the Companies Act in respect of the proposed scheme within four weeks

from date.
</p>
<p id="p_8">              Urgent      certified    photocopy   of   this    order,   if   applied    for,   be

supplied to the parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.



</p><p id="p_9">                                                              ( PATHERYA, J.)
