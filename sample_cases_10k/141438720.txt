Smt.Ningamma,

            (By Sri B.Sharanabasava, Advocate)

1. The Managing Director,
Rajeeva Gandhi Grameena

                                 2




3. The Secretary,
Grama Panchayath Hosalli,
Tq. &amp; Dist: Koppal.                          ... Respondents

           (R1 - R3 are served and unrepresented)

      This writ petition is filed under Articles 226 &amp; 227 of the
Constitution of India praying to quash the impugned order
dated 26.4.2007 passed by the 2nd respondent vide Annexure-
F in so far as petitioner is concerned, etc.,

     This writ petition, coming on for preliminary hearing in
B Group, this day, the Court made the following:


                           ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     The petitioner has called into question the second

respondent's order, dated 26.04.2007 (Annexure-F) cancelling

the allotment of the house site to the petitioner.


</p><p id="p_2">     2. Sri Sharanabasava, the learned counsel for the

petitioner submits that the impugned order is passed without

putting the petitioner on notice.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">     3. The respondents are served but have remained un-

represented.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                3</span><p id="p_5">




</p><p id="p_6">       4. Before passing the impugned order, the petitioner is

not put on notice, much less heard in the matter. The

impugned order is liable to be quashed on the short ground of

non-observance of the principles of natural justice. The

impugned order states that the allotment is cancelled because

it is not in keeping with the revised guidelines. What is

involved is the petitioner's interest in the immovable property.

I therefore, quash the impugned order in so far as it pertains

to the petitioner. Liberty is reserved to the respondent No.2

to take a decision in the matter after observing the principles

of natural justice and in accordance with law.


</p><p id="p_7">       5. This petition is accordingly allowed. No order as to

costs.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">                                              Sd/-
</p><p id="p_10">                                             JUDGE
