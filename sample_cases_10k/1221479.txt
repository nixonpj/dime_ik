: 
MR
VIJAL P DESAI for

1, 
=========================================================


 
	  
	 
	  
		 
			 

CORAM
			: 
			
		
		 
			 

HONOURABLE
			MR.JUSTICE Z.K.SAIYED
		
	

 

 
 


 

Date
: 25/08/2010 

 

 
 
ORAL
ORDER</pre> 

 

 


 


<p id="p_1">	 In the facts
and circumstances of the case and by consent of both the sides, this
application is taken up for hearing today.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_2">	This
application is preferred under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal
Procedure for regular bail in connection with FIR registered as CR
No.I   73 of 2010 registered with Limdi Police Station,  for the
offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_2">Sections 376</a>, <a href="/doc/886598/" id="a_3">365</a> and <a href="/doc/1243353/" id="a_4">342</a> of the Indian
Penal Code. 
</p>
 


	
 


<p id="p_3">	Parties do not
press for further reasoned order.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_4">	I have also
perused the averments made in the application as well as the FIR
produced on the record and prima faice, there is no case made out
against the applicant. Considering the above, I am of the view that
the applicant is required to be enlarged on regular bail at this
stage, without discussing the evidence in detail.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_5">	In the facts
and circumstances of the case, the application is allowed and the
applicant is ordered to be enlarged on bail in connection with CR
No.I   73 of 2010 registered at Limbdi Police Station, on executing
a bond of Rs.10,000/- [Rupees ten thousand only] with one surety of
the like amount to the satisfaction of the Trial Court and subject to
the conditions that he shall:
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_6">[a]	not take
undue advantage of his liberty or abuse his liberty;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_7">[b]	not act in
a manner injurious to the interest of the prosecution;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_8">[c]	surrender
his passport, if any, to the lower Court within a week;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_9">[d]	not leave
the State of Gujarat without the prior permission of the Sessions
court concerned;
</p> 


 


 


 


 


<p id="p_10">[e]	furnish the
present address of his residence to the I.O. and also to the Court at
the time of execution of the bond and shall not change his residence
without prior permission of this Court;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_11">[f]	maintain
law and order.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_12">	If breach of
any of the above conditions is committed, the Sessions Judge
concerned will be free to issue warrant or to take appropriate action
in the matter.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_13">	Bail bond to
be executed before the lower Court having jurisdiction to try the
case.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_14">	At the trial,
the Trial Court shall not be influenced by the observations of
preliminary nature, qua the evidence at this stage, made by this
Court while enlarging the applicant on bail.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_15">	Rule is made
absolute to the aforesaid extent.	Direct Service is permitted.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_16">(Z.K.SAIYED,
J.)
