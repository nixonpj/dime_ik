<p id="p_1">1.   RULE.   Learned   APP,   waives   service   of   notice   of 
rule on behalf of the respondent - State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.   By way of the present application, through jail, 
the   applicant   has   prayed   to   release   him   on   parole 
leave on the ground of repairing of his house. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.   I   have   heard   learned   Additional   Public 
Prosecutor   for   the   respondent   and   considered   the 
averments made in this application. The jail remarks 
attached   with   the   application   do   not   inspire   any 
confidence   and,   therefore,   looking   to   the   jail 
remarks,   no   case   is   made   out   for   grant   of   parole 
leave.
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.   Considering the peculiar facts and circumstances 
of the case, I do not find any sufficient reason to
