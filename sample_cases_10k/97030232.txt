<div class="doc_title">Shishram Garhwal vs State Of Rajasthan Through Pp on 4 August, 2016</div><p id="p_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                          AT JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR

           S.B. CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION No.8688/2016.
</p><p id="p_2">                                      ::
</p><p id="p_3">                   Shishram Garhwal Vs. State of Rajasthan
                                      ::
</p><p id="p_4">                         Date of Order : 04/08/2016

               HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PRASHANT KUMAR AGARWAL

Mr. Surendra Kuri, for the petitioner.
Mr. Rajendra Raghav, Public Prosecutor for the State.


</p><p id="p_5">                Heard learned counsel for the parties.

                On consideration of submissions made on behalf of the respective

parties and the material made available for my perusal and more particularly in

view of the fact that the injury was not caused in the incident, but without

expressing any final opinion on the merit and de-merit of the case, I am inclined

to grant benefit of bail to the accused-petitioner.

</p><p id="p_6">                Consequently, the bail application filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is

allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                It is ordered that the accused-petitioner Shishram Garhwal S/o Shri

Girdhari Lal Garhwal in F.I.R. No.95/2016, registered at Police Station Khandela,

District Sikar, shall be released on bail; provided he furnishes a personal bond of

Rs.50,000/- and two surety bonds of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the

learned trial court with the stipulation to appear before that Court on all dates of

hearing and as and when called upon to do so.



</p><p id="p_8">                                               (PRASHANT KUMAR AGARWAL),J.
</p><p id="p_9">A.Arora/-
</p><p id="p_10">Item No.38.
</p>
