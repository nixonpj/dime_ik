                                        Criminal Revision No.326 of 2018
                               Arising Out of PS. Case No.- Year- Thana- District- Buxar
                 ======================================================
                 Sudama Prasad Gupta @ Suadama Shah, Son of Late Ram Laxman Sah,
                 Resident of House No.25, Village- Narbatpur, Chausa P.S.- Buxar (Muffasil),
                 District- Buxar.

                                                                                    ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
            1. The State of Bihar.
            2. The Sub Divisional Magistrate, Buxar.
            3. Sr. Krishna Kant Tiwary, S/o Ayodhya Kant Tiwary, Resident of Village-
               Chaus, P.S.- Buxar (Muffasil), District- Buxar.

                                                           ... ... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s    :        Mr. Vijay Kumar
                 For the Respondent/s    :        Smt. Meena Singh
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">6   02-11-2018                 This revision application has been preferred against

                  the order dated 05.10.2017 passed by the Sub Divisional

                  Magistrate, Buxar in Case No. 648(M) of 2013 under <a href="/doc/1405190/" id="a_1">Section

                  145</a> Cr.P.C., by which, Circle Officer Chausa reciever and has

                  seized the disputed land appertaining to Chak Khata No. 51,

                  Khesra No. 1258, area 1.75 acres under mauza Chausa.

</p><p id="p_2">                              It has been submitted by learned counsel for the

                  petitioner that title suit being Title Suit No. 101/1984 has been

                  decided in favour of the petitioner against which, opposite party

                  no. 3 has preferred First Appeal before this Court and the

                  Hon'ble Supreme Court in the case of <a href="/doc/1861271/" id="a_2">Ram Sumer Puri

                  Mahant vs. The State of Uttar Pradesh</a> reported in AIR
            Patna High Court CR. REV. No.326 of 2018(6) dt.02-11-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                    1985(1) SC 472 has already held that when a civil suit with

                    respect to a land is pending between the parties,           criminal

                    proceeding under <a href="/doc/1405190/" id="a_3">Section 145</a> Cr.P.C. is not maintainable.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 In view of the above facts and circumstances, issue

                    notice to opposite party no. 3 under both process i.e. ordinary as

                    well as registered cover with A/D for which requisites etc. must

                    be filed within a period of two weeks after upcoming Deepawali

                    Holidays.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 List this case on valid service of notice or on

                    appearance of opposite party no. 3, whichever is earlier.

</p><p id="p_5">                                 In the meantime, status quo shall be maintained.



</p><p id="p_6">                                             (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
sunilkumar/-
</p>
