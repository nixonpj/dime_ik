<div class="doc_title">Sharafat Ansari @ Sharafat ... vs The State Of Jharkhand &amp; Others on 17 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                           Cr. Misc. Petition No. 1219 of 2018
                                  ---
</pre><p id="p_1">              Sharafat Ansari @ Sharafat Hussain                 .....Petitioner
                                 vs.-
</p><p id="p_2">              The State of Jharkhand &amp; others                    ... Opp. Parties
</p><p id="p_3">                                 ---
</p><p id="p_4">              CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE APARESH KUMAR SINGH
                            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RATNAKER BHENGRA
</p><p id="p_5">                                 ---
</p><p id="p_6">              For the Petitioner      : Mr. P. C.Tripathi, Sr. Adv.
</p><p id="p_7">                                        Mr. Jai Shankar Tripathi, Adv.
</p><p id="p_8">              For the Opp. Parties    : Mr. Tapas Roy, A.P.P
</p><p id="p_9">                                  ---
</p><p id="p_10">3/17.4.2018         Learned Senior Counsel for the petitioner seeks permission to
              convert the instant criminal miscellaneous petition into Acquittal Appeal
              and also seek leave to appeal in terms of <a href="/doc/613293/" id="a_1">Section 378(3)</a> of the Code of
              Criminal Procedure as the instant impugned judgment dated 2nd February,
              2018 arises out of Sessions Trial no. 90 of 2004 rendered by learned
              Additional       Sessions   Judge-XI,     Dhanbad       whereunder      the
              accused/opposite party nos. 2 and 3 have been acquitted of the charges
              under <a href="/doc/695990/" id="a_2">Sections 364</a>/<a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_3">302</a>/<a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_4">201</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120B</a> of the Indian Penal Code. Learned
              Senior Counsel for the petitioner submits that the instant petition has been
              filed within 90 days period from the date of the impugned judgment.
</p><p id="p_11">                    In view of what has been held by us in Criminal Miscellaneous
              Petition nos. 321 of 2018 and 637 of 2018, permission to convert this
              miscellaneous petition to acquittal appeal is granted. Office to undertake
              fresh stamp reporting.
</p><p id="p_12">                    Let necessary corrections be made by learned counsel for the
              petitioner in the body of petition for converting it into acquittal appeal
              within a week.
</p><p id="p_13">                    After removing the defects, if any, pointed out by stamp reporter,
              the matter be listed before the appropriate Bench.
</p>



<p id="p_14">                                                            (Aparesh Kumar Singh,J)


                                                            (Ratnaker Bhengra, J)
         JK
