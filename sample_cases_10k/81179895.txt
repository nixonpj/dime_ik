IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

DATED: 3/8/2015

C O R A M

THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SATISH K. AGNIHOTRI
and
	  	THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE K.K.SASIDHARAN

W.P. No.22682 of 2015
 and 
M.P. No.1  of 2015

M. Suresh			...	Petitioner 

Vs

The Authorised Officer 
State Bank of India
RASMECC-cum-SARC
208 Anna Salai 
(Vignesh Complex)
Pondicherry 605 001.		...	Respondent
	
	Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India seeking a writ of certiorarified mandamus calling for the records pertaining to possession notice issued dated 26/12/2014 in respect of the property situated at old Door No.42 New Door No.2 at Jayaram Chettiar Thottam, Vanarampettai, Pondicherry comprised in Ward FBlock No.16, T.S.No.58, R.S.No.354/1pt, Cadastre No.206/2 pt, Patta No.169 in Village No.40, Pondicherry Revenue Village, Vanarampettai, Ulandai hamlet, Pondicherry Commune and Municipality, Pondicherry Sub R.D and Pondicherry R.D and quash the same and to direct the respondent to re-assess the loan account No.10831629723 of the petitioner by considering the loan amount repaid by the petitioner with costs.
			For petitioner	: Mr.S.S.Jhothi Vani		
- - - - -

O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	(Order of the Court was made by SATISH K. AGNIHOTRI,J.,)
	Learned counsel appearing for the petitioner seeks permission to withdraw the writ petition with liberty to move the statutory forum and makes an endorsement to that effect, as under:
</p>
<p id="p_2">May be permitted to withdraw the petition with liberty to file application before the Debts Recovery Tribunal.

</p><p id="p_3">	2.  In view of the above, the writ petition is dismissed as withdrawn with liberty to take recourse to the statutory forum, if so advised, under the provisions of law.  Consequently, M.P. No. 1 of 2015 is dismissed.  No costs. 
</p>
