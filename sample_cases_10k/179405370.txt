     Date of institution                  :     13.02.2012

     Date of Passing of Award             :     10.01.2013


Ref. F.24(443)/11/SWD/Lab./8484­8487 dated 29.12.2011

AWARD

           Having satisfied regarding existence of an industrial dispute 

between  the parties, the Deputy  Labour Commissioner, Government of 


LIR No. 213/2012                                                Page 1/2
 NCT  of Delhi in exercise of powers conferred by section 10(1)(c) and 

<a href="/doc/347928/" id="a_1">section   12(5)</a>  of   the   Industrial   Disputes   Act,   1947   Notification   No. 

F­1/31/616/Estt./2008/7458   dated   the   03­03­2009   of   Govt.   of   NCT   of 

Delhi   referred   the   present   dispute   to   Labour   Court   No.   XII   for 

adjudication with the following terms of reference:

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">           "Whether services of Sh. Vijay Mandal S/o Sh. 
           Haldar have been illegally and/or unjustifiably 
           terminated   by   the   management   and   if   yes,   to 
           what relief is he entitled?"
</blockquote>

<p id="p_5">2.            Notice of the reference was sent to the workman however, 

despite opportunities given no one has appeared on behalf of workman 

nor statement of claim filed on his behalf even today despite repeated calls 

despite last opportunity on the last date. Therefore, it is presumed that he 

is not interested in prosecuting the claim as he has no dispute with the 

management. Accordingly, No Dispute Award is passed. 

</p><p id="p_6">              Reference accordingly stands answered. Copies of award be 

sent for publication and case file be consigned to Record Room. 

Announced in the open court
Dated : 10th January 2013
                                                 (SANJAY SHARMA)
                                 PRESIDING OFFICER, LABOUR  COURT­XIX,
                                       KARKARDOOMA COURTS, DELHI
