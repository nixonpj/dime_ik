<p id="p_1">03/   05-07-2013               These matters are running on the list since several

                   dates but none is appearing.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               It is pointed out by learned counsel appearing for

                   one of the victims that the bail application of this petitioner was

                   earlier rejected in the present case being Bikram P.S. Case No.

                   183 of 1996 (Sessions Trial No. 242 of 1998) where the

                   petitioner got his name recorded in the petition as Babloo Singh

                   @ Ranvijay Singh Son of Ram Bhawan Singh, which stood

                   dismissed on 07.03.2013 vide Cr. Misc. No. 5510 of 2013 by a

                   co-ordinate Bench of this Court, but now the present application
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">  Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.15368 of 2013 (3) dt.05-07-2013</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                            2/2</span>




                 has been filed vide Cr. Misc. No. 15368 of 2013 in which

                 criminal antecedent of the petitioner was called for vide order

                 dated 28.05.2013.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  The other application of this petitioner has been in

                 connection with Bikram P.S. Case No. 80 of 1996. It is further

                 pointed out by learned counsel for the victim that in another

                 case the petitioner disclosed his antecedent by way of filing

                 supplementary affidavit that the petitioner is involved in 10

                 other cases but in that list these two cases are not mentioned.

</p><p id="p_4">                                  Considering the fact that earlier bail application was

                 dismissed by a co-ordinate Bench of this Court on 07.03.2013,

                 let both the maters be placed before the appropriate Bench

                 under the orders of Hon'ble the Chief Justice.



</p><p id="p_5">                                         (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J.)

DKS/
