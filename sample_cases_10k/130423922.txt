<div class="doc_title">Anil Kevat vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 3 December, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                                -: 1 :-

          Misc. Criminal Case No.10577 of 2015.
03.12.2015 :-
     Shri M.K.Sharma, learned counsel for the applicant.

     Shri Peeyush Jain, learned Deputy Govt. Advocate for

the Non-applicant/State.

     Heard with the aid of the case-diary.

                                          O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            THIS is first bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

of the Code of Criminal Procedure for grant of bail. The

applicant   is   in   custody      in     connection   with   Crime

No.799/2015 registered at Police Station Banganga, Indore

for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/436339/" id="a_2">Sections 381</a>, <a href="/doc/764237/" id="a_3">394</a>, <a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_4">397</a>,

<a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_5">302</a> and <a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_6">201</a> of IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Learned counsel for the applicant submits that the

applicant is a young person and permanent resident of

District Indore having no criminal antecedents. He has been

falsely implicated in this case and a false memorandum has

been drawn and on that basis, Chappal, handkerchief and

clothes of the deceased have been recovered. Surprisingly no

dead body of deceased has been recovered till date. There is

no legal evidence against the present applicant to connect
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              -: 2 :-</span>

him with the crime. The applicant is detained in this case

since 26.07.2015. Final report has been filed. The trial will

take considerable time to conclude. In such circumstances,

the applicant be released on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_3">           On the other hand, learned Deputy Govt.

Advocate for the Non-applicant/State vehemently opposes

the prayer. He submits that there is overwhelming evidence

against the present applicant. Apart from the memorandum

and recovery of incriminating articles, there are CCTV

footage recovered from the Toll Barrier.                 In such

circumstances he prays for dismissal of the application.

</p><p id="p_4">           I   have   considered       the   overall   facts   and

circumstances of the case. I am of the view that at this

juncture the applicant has failed to make out a case for

granting bail. Thus, the application is hereby dismissed.



</p><p id="p_5">                                         [ Jarat Kumar Jain ]
                                                 JUDGE
(AKS)
 </p>
