<div class="doc_title">Shaila Devi &amp; Anr vs State Of Bihar on 18 February, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                         Cr.Misc. No.5761 of 2009
                            SHAILA DEVI &amp; ANR
                                  Versus
                            THE STATE OF BIHAR
                               -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2/   18/2/2009                Heard learned counsel for the petitioners

                  and learned counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              The prayer in this application for extension

                  of the time for surrender is based on the premise that

                  the FAX cost could not be deposited in time.

</p><p id="p_3">                              The   application     is    disposed     with   the

                  observation that any application for regular bail shall be

                  considered by the court below on the date that the

                  petitioners surrender, without being prejudiced by the

                  refusal of this Court to extend the time for surrender,

                  keeping   in   mind   that      they    had   been     granted

                  anticipatory bail, but could not surrender in time.

</p><p id="p_4">                              Let the order be communicated through FAX

                  to the court of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Jamui in

                  connection with Sikandra P.S. Case No.121/2008.


</p><pre id="pre_2">KC                                                       ( Navin Sinha, J.)
 </pre>
