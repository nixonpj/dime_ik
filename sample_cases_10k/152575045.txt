<div class="doc_title">Jitendra Singh Kushwaha vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 27 November, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                           W.P. No. 11804/2007
    27.11.2014
         Ms.      Shanti   Tiwari,    learned   counsel    for    the
    petitioner.
         Shri Aditya Khandekar, leaned counsel for the
    respondents No. 2 and 3.</pre>
<p id="p_1">         It is seen that the order impugned is issued by
    the Managing Director of the M.P. Warehousing and
    Logistic Corporation, Bhopal, accepting the mutual
    transfer of the employee working in the Corporation at
    Chhattisgarh with the employee working in the
    Corporation at M.P. In terms of the provisions of
    Section 68 of the M.P. Reorganization Act, 2000, such
    a prayer could not have been accepted by the
    authority     situated   within    the   State   of   M.P.,   as
    interstate transfer is to be ordered only and only by
    the Central Government.
</p><p id="p_2">         Learned counsel for the respondents to address
    this Court on this question on the next date.
</p><p id="p_3">         List the petition on 1st December, 2014.
</p>
