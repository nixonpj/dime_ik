<div class="doc_title">Mandeep Mahto vs The State Of Bihar on 28 September, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                            Criminal Miscellaneous No.21093 of 2011
                             Mandeep Mahto, S/O-Late Rawan Mahto
                                               Versus
                                      The State Of Bihar
                                  ----------------------------------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">04    28.09.2011             Supplementary affidavit is filed on behalf of the petitioner

                   bringing the present stage of trial of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">                             Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as learned

                   Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             Although, the allegation against this petitioner is very

                   serious in nature but admittedly, petitioner is in jail custody since

                   16.07.2007 and up-till-now his trial could not be concluded.

</p><p id="p_4">                             Taking into consideration the period of detention of the

                   petitioner in jail custody as well as present stage of trial, let the

                   petitioner be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/-

                   (Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                   satisfaction of 9th Additional Sessions Judge, Saran at Chapra in

                   connection with Sessions Trial No. 553 of 2007 arising out of Parsa

                   P.S. Case No. 68 of 2007.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><pre id="pre_2">     SHAHZAD                       (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
