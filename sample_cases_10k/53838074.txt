<p id="p_3">

                           The learned Advocate appearing on behalf of the petitioner

              submits that due to inadvertence a copy of the order dated 8th March, 2011

              passed by this Court in CRM No. 10360 of 2010 cancelling the bail granted

              earlier to the petitioner has not been annexed to this revision petition. He

              submits that the petitioner would like to file a supplementary affidavit

              incorporating a copy of the same and he prays for a week's time to do so.

</p><p id="p_4">                           Let this matter appear under the same heading 'Listed Motion' after one week.

                           Urgent photostat certified copy of this order may be given to the parties

             expeditiously, if applied for.
