<div class="doc_title">Anjlo Mahesh Baxla vs The State Of Jharkhand And Ors on 11 July, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                     W. P. (C) No. 3038 of 2014

            Anjlo Mojesh Baxla                ....  ... Petitioner
                                    Versus
           The State of Jharkhand and others ...   ... respondents
                                 -----
           CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE R.R. PRASAD
                                 -----

           For the Petitioner : Mr. Sanjeev Thakur, Advocate
           For the State      : Md. Shamim Akhtar, Advocate
           For the JPSC       : Mr. Sanjay Piprawall, Advocate
                                  -----

4/11.07.2014</pre><p id="p_1">       When it was submitted on behalf of Jharkhand Public Service

      Commission (for short 'JPSC') that the caste certificate, issued by the

      competent authority-Sub Divisional Officer of the Sub Division, was

      required to be submitted for taking benefit of reserved category, but the

      petitioner    had   submitted   caste   certificate   issued   by   the     Block

      Development Officer of the concerned Block and, therefore, the petitioner

      was not treated as a candidate of reserved category, rather he was treated

      as a candidate of general category, learned counsel for the petitioner

      submits that it may be that the petitioner may not have submitted the

      caste certificate, issued by the Sub Divisional Officer, but in such

      situation, JPSC had given an opportunity to the number of candidates for

      rectifying their mistakes but in the case of the petitioner that opportunity

      was never given to him and that the petitioner does have caste certificate,

      issued by the competent authority, photo-copy of which he will be filing

      along with the supplementary affidavit, which should be filed to the effect,

      as stated above, in course of day or at best by 14.7.2014.

</p><p id="p_2">               As prayed for, post this case on 15.07.2014.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">                                                              (R.R. Prasad, J.)
    AKT
