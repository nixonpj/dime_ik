                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.20962 of 2013

                   1. Sagar Choudhary S/O Tribeni Choudhary R/O Village- Noorjamapur,

                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   20-08-2013                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as learned

                   Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                              The petitioner was neither caught at the spot nor anything

                   was recovered from his conscious possession and according to

                   prosecution case itself, the pick-up van was being driven by son-

                   in-law of the petitioner and except the aforesaid material, there

                   appears to be nothing against the petitioner. Moreover, paragraph-

                   11 of the petition reveals that the petitioner does not have any

                   criminal antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              Accordingly, this anticipatory bail petition is allowed and

                   it is ordered that the petitioner, named above, in the event of his

                   arrest or surrender within four weeks from today in the court

                   below, shall be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.20962 of 2013 (3) dt.20-08-2013</span>




                        10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each

                        in connection with Khagaria (Muffasil) P.S. Case No. 543 of 2012

                        to the satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Khagaria subject to

                        conditions as laid down u/S 438(2) of the <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>.

</p><p id="p_4">                                              (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
</p>
