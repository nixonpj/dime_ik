<div class="doc_title">Sanjay Sharma vs Unknown on 7 December, 2011</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Sh. Daya Prakash</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                                           ­1­

 IN THE COURT OF SH. DAYA  PRAKASH: PRESIDING OFFICER LABOUR
              COURT NO. XVI: KARKARDOOMA COURTS : DELHI


                                      ID No.77/10



Sanjay Sharma 
s/o Late Sh. Shyam Sunder Sharma
c/o Rashtriya Jata Dal Mazdoor Prakoshth
C­35, Satsang Colony, Wazirpur Industrial Area
Delhi­52.                                                         ...... Workman


VERSUS


M/s Khera Auto Industries
B­1005, Shastri Nagar,
Delhi­51                                                     ...... Management



                                                     Date of Institution  :  27.03.10
                                                     Judgment reserved :  07.12.11
                                                     Date of decision     :  07.12.11




                              COMPROMISE  AWARD



1.</pre><p id="p_1">     The   National     Capital   Territory   of   Delhi,   through   its  Dy.  Labour

Commissioner   vide   reference   no.   F­24(48)/ND/112/2009/Lab./622­26   Dated

10.02.10   referred   the   dispute   for   adjudication   between   the   Management  M/s

Khera Auto Industries  and its  workman Sh. Sanjay Sharma  in the following

terms of reference:  
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><pre id="pre_2">ID No.77/10                                                                               1/3
                                                   ­2­

</pre><p id="p_3">      "Whether   the   services   of   Sh.   Sanjay   Sharmaa   s/o   Sh.   Shyam
      Sunder   Sharma   have   been   terminated   illegally   and/or
      unjustifiably by the management; and if yes, to what relief is he
      entitled and what directions are necessary in this respect?"


</p><p id="p_4">2.       As per claim, workman stated that he was working with the management

for the last 10 years on the last drawn wages of Rs. 7000/­ per month.   It is

alleged   by   the   workman   he   was   illegally   terminated   by   the   management   on

26.04.09.  It is stated that he is entitled to reinstatement in service with full back

wages and other consequential benefits.  
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">3.       Notice of claim was issued to management. AR for management appeared

and filed WS denying the allegations levelled by the workman in his statement of

claim.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">4.       Subsequently, Rejoinder to the W.S. of the management was filed wherein

the   workman   has   denied   the   allegations   made   in   the   WS   and   reiterated   the

averments contained in the claim.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">5.       After completion of pleadings, issues were framed on 01.12.10 and case

was adjourned for WE.
</p>
<p id="p_11">

</p><p id="p_12">6.       Perusal   of   file   shows   that   during   the   course   of   proceedings   i.e.   at   the

stage of WE, both the parties have apprised that they wanted to settle the matter

and   hence,   wanted   the   case   file   be   referred   to   Mediation   Cell,   Karkardooma

Courts, Delhi. 
</p>
<p id="p_13">
</p><pre id="pre_3">ID No.77/10                                                                                         2/3
                                                 ­3­

</pre><p id="p_14">7.        File received from the Mediation Cell, Karkardooma Courts with the order

that the workman has agreed to settled his dispute against the management for a

total sum of Rs. 50,000/­  which shall be paid in two installments of Rs. 25,000/­

each.
</p>
<p id="p_15">

</p><p id="p_16">8.        Today, statement of workman has been recorded separately  wherein it is

stated that the workman  has already received Rs. 25,000/­ in cash out of which

and   a   post   dated     cheque   of   Rs.   25,000/­   has   been   received   by   him   today.

Statement   of   Sh.   Pawan   Khera,   Proprietor   of   management   has   also   been

recorded regarding payment.
</p>
<p id="p_17">

</p><p id="p_18">9.        Since   the   workman   has   settled   his   dispute   with   the   management   and

nothing   remains   for   adjudication,   compromise   award   is   passed   as   per

statements. File be consigned to the record room after necessary compliance by

Ahlmad.
</p>
<p id="p_19">

Announced in the Open Court                                 (DAYA  PRAKASH)
     th
on 7  December, 2011                        Additional District &amp; Session Judge
                                                     Presiding Officer labour Court XVI
                                                              Karkardooma  Courts  : Delhi.
</p>
