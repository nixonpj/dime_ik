</p><p id="p_3">1.        The present application is moved by the applicant inter alia praying

for fixing the early date of hearing of Special Civil Application

No.11719/2013.
</p>
<p id="p_4">2.        In the main matter, Rule was made returnable on 05.05.2014, but

thereafter, the main matter could not be heard finally. Therefore, request is

made to fix the early date of final hearing of the main matter.
</p>
<p id="p_5">3.        Heard the learned advocates for the respective parties and perused the

present application.
</p>
<p id="p_6">4.        For the reasons stated in the application, the present application is

allowed and Special Civil Application No.11719/2013 is ordered to be listed

for final hearing on 30.04.2018.
