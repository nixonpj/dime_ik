<div class="doc_title">Baidnath Yadav &amp; Ors vs The State Of Bihar on 29 July, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.29653 of 2015
                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -345 Year- 2014 Thana -SABAUR District- BHAGALPUR
                 ======================================================
                 1. Baidnath Yadav @ Baij Nath Yadav
                 2. Inder Yadav, Both Sons of Vishu Yadav
                 3. Umesh Yadav Son of Buti Lal Yadav
                 4. Bhola Yadav Son of Khantar Yadav
                    All Residents of Village Ali Nagar, Police Station- Sabour District-
                    Bhagalpur.
                                                                           .... .... Petitioners
                                                    Versus
                   The State of Bihar
                                                                       .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioners      : Mr. Sanjay Kumar Tiwary, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party :     APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VIKASH JAIN
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   29-07-2015                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned

                  APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                2. The petitioners apprehend their arrest for the

                  offences alleged under Sections 341, 323, 458, 307, 379, 504,

                  506 and 354 read with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_1">Section 34</a> of the Indian Penal Code

                  registered in connection with Sabour P.S. case No.345 of 2014.

</p><p id="p_3">                                3. It is submitted that the petitioners have been falsely

                  implicated and in any event accusation of assault against the

                  petitioners is general and omnibus. The injuries upon the persons

                  of the informant's side are attributed to other accused persons.

</p><p id="p_4">                                4. Having regard to the entirety of the facts and

                 circumstances of the case, in the event of the petitioners' arrest or
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.29653 of 2015 (2) dt.29-07-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




                     surrender before the court below within six weeks from the date of

                     communication of this order, let the above named petitioners be

                     released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (ten

                     thousand) each with two sureties of like amount each to the

                     satisfaction of Sri Md. Salim, learned Judicial Magistrate, Ist

                     Class, Bhagalpur in connection with Sabour P.S. Case No.345 of

                     2014 , subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_2">Section 438</a> (2)

                     <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_3">Cr.P.C</a>. and also subject to the following further conditions:-

</p><p id="p_5">                                      (i)    The     petitioners       shall   cooperate   with   the

                     investigation and make themselves available as and when so

                     required and in case of failure, the State shall be at liberty to move

                     for cancellation of bail.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                    (ii) The petitioners shall remain physically present on

                     each and every date during trial and in the event of failure on two

                     consecutive dates, their bail bonds shall be liable to be cancelled

                     by the learned Court concerned.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                                                        (Vikash Jain, J)
B.T/-
