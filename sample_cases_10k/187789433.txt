<div class="doc_title">No One Appears On Behalf Of The vs Unknown on 29 June, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
BENCH AT JAIPUR
O R D E R
D.B. CIVIL CONTEMPT PETITION NO.632/2015
MANSORAM SHARMA Vs. SHRI NARESH PAL GANGWAR &amp; ANR.

DATE:29.06.2015

HON'BLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. SUNIL AMBWANI
HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE BANWARI LAL SHARMA
            
                                          *****
</pre><p id="p_1">1.		No one appears on behalf of the applicant.
</p><p id="p_2">2.		This contempt petition seeks to draw attention of the Court the purported non-compliance of the order dated 19.11.2014, which reads as follows:-
</p><p id="p_3">	Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	After considering the facts of the case and on perusal of the impugned order dated 18.11.2013, the appeal has been admitted. Therefore, operation of the impugned order dated 18.11.2013 shall remain stayed till the disposal of the appeal. However, it is made clear that any order passed in consequence of the impugned order dated 18.11.2013, shall not come in operation.
</p>
<p id="p_5">	Accordingly, the stay application stands disposed of.
</p><p id="p_6">3.		By the letter dated 26.11.2014, the Principal of the Government Senior Secondary School, Roopbas, District Bharatpur, has sought directions from the Director, Secondary Education, Rajasthan, Bikaner, for compliance of the order dated 19.11.2014 for relieving the petitioner/applicant to join at his old place of posting.
</p>
<p id="p_7">4.		We do not find that any order has been passed by the Director, which may amount to deliberate disobedience or non-compliance of the order dated 19.11.2014, nor there is any assertion that any such order has been passed.
</p><p id="p_8">5.		The contempt petition is dismissed.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">(BANWARI LAL SHARMA),J.                                  (SUNIL AMBWANI),C.J.


/KKC/


Certificate:

</pre><p id="p_9">All corrections made in the judgment/order have been incorporated in the judgment/order being emailed.
</p>
<p id="p_10">KAMLESH KUMAR
P.A
