<p id="p_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                       JODHPUR

                   S.B. Criminal Misc(Pet.) No.4672/2019

Rukshana Bee &amp; Anr.
</p><p id="p_2">                                                                          ----Petitioner
                                         Versus
State of Rajasthan &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                        ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)              :     Mr. Manoj Purohit
For Respondent(s)              :     Ms. Rajlaxmi Choudhary, PP



     HON'BLE DR. JUSTICE PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI

                                          Order

03.9.2019
</p><p id="p_4">1.           The petitioners have preferred this criminal misc.

petition under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. for issuance of necessary

directions    to    the     official     respondents          to      provide   adequate

security and protection to the petitioners on the ground that they

are facing grave threat of life and liberty at the hands of private

respondent no.4 in view of staying in live-in relationship.

</p><p id="p_5">2.           After hearing the learned counsel for the parties and on

overall consideration of the facts and circumstances of the case,

this Court is of the view that nobody can be permitted to take law

in his own hands merely because the live-in relationship is not

acceptable.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">3.           Consequently,           the     present        misc.       petition   stands

disposed of by issuing direction to the SHO, Police Station

Rajiyasar, District Ganganagar to ensure necessary vigil that no

harm is caused to the life and liberty of the petitioners at the

hands of those, who are opposed to the their relationship.

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                          (Downloaded on 06/09/2019 at 08:35:02 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_8">                                                          (2 of 2)                                  [CRLMP-4672/2019]


</p><p id="p_9">                                   4.         It is made clear that this order is not a proof of age and

                                   the marriage of the petitioners and any observation in this order

                                   shall not affect any criminal and civil proceedings initiated against

                                   the petitioners at the instances of their relatives.


</p><p id="p_10">                                                                     (DR. PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI)J.
</p>
