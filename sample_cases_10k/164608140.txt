<div class="doc_title">Natthi Lal vs State Of Uttarakhand on 19 March, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">CRLA No. 93 of 2015


U.C. Dhyani, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">        Mr. Sandeep Kothari, Advocate for the
appellant.
</p><p id="p_2">        Mr. K.S. Rautela, Government Advocate with
Mr. Raman Kumar Shah, Dy. Advocate General for the
respondent State.
</p><p id="p_3">        Present criminal appeal has been preferred by
the appellant being aggrieved against the judgment and
order dated 02.03.2015, passed by learned Sessions
Judge, Tehri Garhwal, whereby the appellant was
convicted under <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_1">Sections 354</a> and <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_2">325</a> IPC. Convict
was directed to undergo rigorous imprisonment for one
year alongwith a fine of Rs. 3,000/- under <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_3">Section 354</a>
IPC and one year's rigorous imprisonment alongwith a
fine of Rs. 2,000/- under <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_4">Section 325</a> IPC. Both the
sentences were directed to run concurrently.
</p><p id="p_4">        Heard.
</p><p id="p_5">        Admit.
</p><p id="p_6">        Summon the lower court record.
</p><p id="p_7">        List after the lower court record is received.
        Bail application is taken up at the instance of
learned counsel for the appellant.
</p><p id="p_8">        Appellant is in judicial detention since
05.08.2014, which means that he has already served out
seven months out of one years' rigorous imprisonment,
which has been awarded to him by the court below.
</p><p id="p_9">        Taking into consideration this aspect of the case,
it is provided as an interim measure, that the appellant
be released on bail, during the pendency of present
criminal appeal, on his executing a personal bond and
furnishing two sureties each of like amount to the
satisfaction of court concerned.
</p><p id="p_10">        It is made clear that this Court has not stayed the
fine which has been imposed upon the appellant by the
court below.
</p>

<p id="p_11">                                       (U.C. Dhyani, J.)
                                          19.03.2015
