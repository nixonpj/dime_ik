<div class="doc_title">Sumirak Yadav &amp; Ors vs State Of Bihar on 23 November, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                         IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                          Cr.Misc. No.34851 of 2009
                1. SUMIRAK YADAV
                2. Sudhir Yadav
                   Both are sons of Jaypal Yadav, resident of vill.-dharam Bigha, P.S.-Nimchak
                   Bathani, Distt.-Gaya
                3. Raghunandan Chauhan, son of Bhola Chauhan, resident of vill.-Kamalpur
                   Beldariya, P.S.-Nimchak Bathani, Distt.-Gaya---------------------Petitioners.
                                                       Versus
                                              STATE OF BIHAR .
                                                     -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2.   23.11.2009               Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                              Submission of the learned counsel for the petitioners is

                      that differences arose in between the parties on the point of

                      payment or non-payment of wages of labourers which petitioner

                      no.3 had supplied. Allegation under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">section 379</a> of the Indian

                      Penal Code is super-addition and no case under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_2">section 406</a> of the

                      Indian Penal Code is made out.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case,

                      in the event of arrest or surrender within one month from the date

                      of communication of this order the above named petitioners shall

                      be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-(Ten

                      thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                      satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Gaya, in connection

                      with Nimchak Bathani P.S. case no.9 of 2009, subject to the

                      conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_3">section 438(2)</a> of the Code of

                      Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


</p><pre id="pre_2">        Sudip                               ( Mandhata Singh,J )
