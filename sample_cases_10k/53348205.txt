                                                                       .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                  1. The State Of Bihar

                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                  ======================================================
                  Appearance :
                  For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Aditya Kumar Singh, Advocate

                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ASHWANI KUMAR
                  SINGH
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">4    20-05-2013                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned
                    counsel for the State.
</p><p id="p_2">                               The petitioner seeks anticipatory bail in connection
                    with Dihri (Town) P.S. Case No. 157 of 2011, instituted under
                    <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">section 379</a> of the Indian Penal Code which was registered
                    against unknown accused persons. The name of the petitioner
                    transpired in course of investigation.
</p><p id="p_3">                               Taking into consideration the materials available in
                    the case diary, I am not inclined to grant anticipatory bail to
                    the petitioner. It is dismissed accordingly.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                          (Ashwani Kumar Singh, J)

M.Rahman/-
</p>
