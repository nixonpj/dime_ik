<div class="doc_title">Sitaram Yadav vs The State Of Bihar on 2 December, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.46584 of 2013
                 ======================================================
                 1. Sitaram Yadav Son Of Late Mohan Yadav Resident Of Village-Tiresat
                 Tarh, P.S.-Barhat, District-Jamui

                                                                      .... ....   Petitioner
                                                 Versus
                 1. The State Of Bihar

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner     : Mr. Satya Prakash Parasar, advocate
                 For the Opposite Party : Mr. Navin Kr. Pandey, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE CHAKRADHARI
                 SHARAN SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   02-12-2013                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 Additional Public Prosecutor appearing on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  The petitioner apprehends arrest in a case registered

                 for the offences punishable under section 47(a) of the Bihar Excise

                 Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  As per prosecution case, in course of raid, Excise

                 officials seized 20 liter of country made liquor and 50 kg. of

                 fermented Jawa Mahua in five pouches from the house of the

                 petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

                 alleged recovery was made on 23.08.2011 and the report to the

                 court concerned was sent on 22.10.2011 which casts serious doubt

                 on the prosecution story. He submits that petitioner has been made
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.46584 of 2013 (2) dt.02-12-2013</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




                     accused first time in a case registered for the offence under section

                     47(a) of Bihar Excise Act.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                        In view of submission, as above, let the petitioner,

                     above named, in the event of his arrest/ surrender within a period

                     of six weeks from today be released on bail on his furnishing bail

                     bond of Rs. 10,000/-(ten thousand) each with two sureties of the

                     like amount each to the satisfaction of Sri S. Kumar, learned

                     Judicial Magistrate, 1st class, Jamui in complaint case No. 326-

                     C2/2011, subject to conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_1">section 438(2)</a>

                     of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                           (Chakradhari Sharan Singh, J)


BKS/-
</p>
