<p id="p_1">      The petitioner's complaint in this writ petition is that the

petitioner's application for a building permit is not being

considered by the 1st respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2.    The learned Standing Counsel for the 1st respondent

submits that if the application for permit has been received by

the 1st respondent and is pending, the same can be considered.

</p><p id="p_3">      In the above circumstances, this writ petition is disposed of

with a direction to the 1st respondent to consider and pass orders

on the application, if submitted by the petitioner and is pending,

as expeditiously as possible, at any rate, within one month from

the date of receipt of a certified copy of this judgment.
