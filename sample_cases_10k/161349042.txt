<div class="doc_title">Mitthu vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 14 May, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                   1
            HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                         Cr.A.No.3622/2018
                (MITTHU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)




  Jabalpur, dated: 14.05.2018
      The case is taken upon mention.
      Shri R.S. Patel, counsel for the appellant.
      Shri Vivek Mishra, Government Advocate for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard on admission.
</p><p id="p_2">      Admit.
</p><p id="p_3">      Let record of the Court below be requisitioned.
      Learned Government Advocate accepts notice on
behalf of respondent/State; as such, no further notice is
required.
</p><p id="p_4">      Heard     on    I.A.No.7447/2018         for   suspension     of
sentence and grant of bail under <a href="/doc/1116025/" id="a_1">Section 389(1)</a> of the Code
of Criminal Procedure filed on behalf of appellant Mitthu.
</p><p id="p_5">      A perusal of record reveals that appellant stands
convicted under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_3">471</a> and <a href="/doc/756306/" id="a_4">474</a> of the IPC. He
has been sentenced to undergo rigorous imprisonment of
three years and fine in the sum of Rs.1,500/- with default
stipulations.
</p><p id="p_6">      Learned counsel for the appellant submits that the
appellant has been released on bail by the trial Court under
<a href="/doc/970675/" id="a_5">section 389</a> (3)<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_6"> of the Code</a> of Criminal Procedure till
30.05.2018;     therefore,    it   has   been     prayed    that   the
substantive jail sentence of the appellant be suspended.
</p><p id="p_7">      Learned        Government           Advocate         for     the
respondent/State on the other hand has opposed the
application.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    2</span>
<p id="p_8">             HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                         Cr.A.No.3622/2018
                (MITTHU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)




        Keeping in view the short duration of sentence
imposed upon the appellant, in the opinion of this Court, the
appellant deserves to be released on bail.
</p><p id="p_9">        Consequently, I.A.No.7447/2018 is allowed.
        It is directed that on depositing the fine amount, if not
already deposited, and furnishing a personal bond in the sum
of      Rs.50,000/- with one solvent surety in the same amount
to the satisfaction of the trial Court for his appearance before
the Registry of this Court on 05.12.2018 and on all other
subsequent dates fixed by the Registry in this regard, the
remaining part of the substantive jail sentence imposed upon
the appellant shall stand suspended and he shall be released on
bail.
</p><p id="p_10">        Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                                              (C.V. Sirpurkar)
                                                  Judge
vai
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                      3</span>
HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
           Cr.A.No.3622/2018
  (MITTHU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)
 </p>
