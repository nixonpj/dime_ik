<div class="doc_title">Ramesh Kumar &amp; Ors. vs The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors. on 7 September, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                      Miscellaneous Jurisdiction Case No.3407 of 2010
                                   Ramesh Kumar &amp; Ors.
                                             Versus
                                The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors.
                                ----------------------------------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">08/   07.09.2011               In compliance of my order dated 9.8.2011,

                   the Commissioner, Tirhut Division and the Collector,

                   Muzaffarpur are present.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               It is submitted by them that in compliance

                   of the orders of the High Court, roster with regard to

                   Class IV vacancies in the Collectorate and the

                   attached offices has been cleared pursuant whereto 22

                   persons including petitioner no.1 have been issued

                   appointment letter. Appointment in regard to the

                   other Line Department could not proceed as the

                   District Animal Husbandry Officer, Phylaria Officer,

                   Phylaria Control Unit, Executive Engineer, N.H.

                   Division No. II, Superintending Engineer, Minor

                   Irrigation Circle, Executive Engineer, N.H. Division,

                   Executive      Engineer,      Irrigation   (Mechanical)

                   Division,    Civil     Assistant   Surgeon-cum-    Chief

                   Medical     Officer,     Factory   Inspector,   Assistant

                   Director, Labour and Employment Department,
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                     2</span>




Deputy Director, Panchayati Raj, District Sub-

Registrar, District Agriculture Officer, Executive

Engineer, PHED, District Malaria Officer, Divisional

Forest Officer, Superintending Engineer, Rural

Works      Department,     Circle      No.    II,     Executive

Engineer, Drainage Division, Executive Engineer,

Building     Division,    Joint     Director,       Agriculture,

Principal,     Superintendent,       SKMCH,           Principal,

Industrial    Training    Institute,    Additional        Chief

Medical Officer, Superintending Engineer, North

Bihar, Road Circle and District Statistical Officer, all

situate within the district of Muzaffarpur have not

responded      to   the   request      of     the     Collector,

Muzaffarpur to submit their Class IV vacancies for

roster clearance before the Commissioner, Tirhut

Division and on account of their non-cooperation, full

compliance of the orders of the High Court dated

1.9.2009,     Annexure-4     read      with     order     dated

20.5.2011 passed in the instant case                  to make

appointment on Class IV post in the Collectorate and

the attached office is not being made.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Issue notice of contempt to the aforesaid
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                            3</span>




         officers to submit the vacancies available in their

         offices for roster clearance before the Commissioner,

         Tirhut Division by 21.9.2011, failing which they

         should appear before this Court on 28.9.2011.

</p><p id="p_4">                   The notice to the aforesaid officers should

         be served by making available the web copy of this

         order to them by the Collector, Muzaffarpur.

</p><p id="p_5">                   Put up on 28.9.2011 maintaining its

         position under the same heading when Collector,

         Muzaffarpur shall appear before this Court.




</p><pre id="pre_2">Arjun/                                   ( V. N. Sinha, J.)
 </pre>
