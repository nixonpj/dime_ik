<div class="doc_title">Shravan Keswani vs Ashok Kumar on 16 April, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Pushpendra Singh Bhati</div>

<p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
              S.B. Civil Writ Petition No. 5465/2019

Shravan Keswani S/o Shri Sivandas Keswani, Aged About 61
Years,   Resident   Of    17-A-17,         Bapunagar,           District   Bhilwara
(Commercial Address - Pesh Area, Opposite Mahaveer Park,
Bhilwara).
</p><p id="p_2">                                                                     ----Petitioner
                                   Versus
Ashok Kumar S/o Shri Rooplal Birani, Resident Of 7-A-9, R.c.
Vyas Colony, Bhilwara (Rajasthan).
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                  ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)        :     Mr.TRS Sodha.
For Respondent(s)        :     Mr.Rakesh Arora.


</p><p id="p_4">     HON'BLE DR. JUSTICE PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI

                                    Order

16/04/2019

</p><p id="p_5">1.        After arguing to some length, learned counsel for the

petitioner urged that he wants to withdraw this writ petition, with

permission to the petitioner to retain the premises in question for

a term upto 31.12.2020.
</p>
<p id="p_6">2.        Learned counsel for the respondent does not object to

the aforesaid prayer, if the reasonable check is imposed.

</p><p id="p_7">3.        In view of the aforesaid submission, the present writ

petition is dismissed as withdrawn with permission to the

petitioner to retain the premises in question for a term upto

31.12.2020, subject to the condition that the petitioner shall

submit an undertaking before the learned trial court within a

period of three weeks from today to the following effect that :




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    (Downloaded on 27/06/2019 at 11:53:20 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_8">                                                                              (2 of 2)               [CW-5465/2019]


                                   (1)-   The petitioner will handover complete vacant possession of

                                          the premises in question to the respondent on or before

                                          31.12.2020.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                   (2)- The petitioner will not part with the possession of the

                                          premises or any portion thereof to anybody else.

                                   (3)- The petitioner will make the payment of rent to the

                                          respondent month by month, that is before 7th day of every

                                          month.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                                   (4)- The arrears of rent and also the mesne profit as determined

                                          and awarded shall be paid to the respondent within a period

                                          of one month from today.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                                   4.          In the event, the petitioner does not submit the

                                   undertaking aforesaid or violate any condition thereof, then the

                                   respondent would be entitled to get the decree executed.


</p><p id="p_12">                                   5.          Stay Petition No.5379/2019 also stands dismissed

                                   accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_13">

</p><p id="p_14">                                                                  (DR. PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI),J

                                    225-Sphophaliya/-
</p>
