: 07/11/2013
 


 

 


ORAL
ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">1.		Learned
advocate Mr.Yunus U. Malek, relies on oral judgment dated 01/04/2011
in Uday Narendrabhai Bhatt V/s. Shivangi Narendrabhai Shastri
being Special Civil Application No.2854 of 2011 and another oral
judgment dated 22/04/2011 in Maulin Girishchandra Pandya V/s.
Riddhi w/o Maulin G. Pandya and D/o Mahendra Shantilal Bhatt being
Special Civil Application No.4877 of 2011, both of this Court,
and submits that in those decisions, this Court exercised powers to
waive period of six months contemplated under Section-13 B (2) of the
<a href="/doc/590166/" id="a_1">Hindu Marriage Act</a>, 1955 in peculiar facts and circumstances, so as
to grant the decree of divorce by mutual consent.     
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_2">2.		It
was submitted that in the present case, if the divorce is not granted
by insisting upon the passage of six months, the prospects of
continuance of student visa of petitioner no.2 would be seriously
jeopardized as the period of visa expires on 31/12/2013.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_3">3.		The
petition is to be notified next on 13/11/2013.  On that date, the
petitioner shall remain personally present before this Court.  It was
stated that the petitioner no.2 has been presently staying in U.K. 
</p>
 


 


 


 


 


<p id="p_4">(N.V.ANJARIA,
J.) 

 

Rathod...
</p>
