                      CHANDIGARH

Rajesh
                                                      ....Petitioner

                                        V/s

                                                      ....Respondent</pre>

<p id="p_1">BEFORE : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJAN GUPTA

Present:     Mr. Kunal Dawar, Advocate for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">             Mr. Chetan Sharma, AAG Haryana.
</p>
<p id="p_3">RAJAN GUPTA J.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             This is a petition filed under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Code of

Criminal Procedure seeking pre-arrest bail in a case registered against the

petitioner vide FIR No. 166 dated 04.05.2017 under sections 7(1)&amp; 10 of

Haryana Development and Regulation of Urban Area Act, 1975 (<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Section

420</a> IPC added later) at Police Station Bhupani, District Faridabad.

</p><p id="p_5">             Learned counsel for the State submits that pursuant to order

dated December 08, 2017, petitioner has joined the investigation and is no

longer required for custodial interrogation.

</p><p id="p_6">             In view of the above, order dated December 08, 2017 is made

absolute subject to the conditions envisaged under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_3">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_7">             Disposed of.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">January 18, 2018                                      (RAJAN GUPTA)
Ajay                                                       JUDGE
