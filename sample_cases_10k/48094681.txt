            IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA
               CIRCUIT BENCH AT GULBARGA

       DATED THIS THE 10TH DAY OF SEPTEMBER, 2012

                          BEFORE

        THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A.S. PACHHAPURE

            CRIMINAL PETITION No.15897/2012

BETWEEN:

VINOD @ VINAYKUMAR
S/O RAJENDRA GIRANI
AGE: 24 YEARS, OCC: STUDENT
R/O RAJAPUR, GULBARGA
                                               ... PETITIONER

[BY SRIYUTHS. USTAD SADAT HUSSAIN AND
    USTAD ZAKIR HUSSAIN, ADVOCATES]

AND:

THE STATE THROUGH
UNIVERSITY PS, GULBARGA
                                              ... RESPONDENT

[BY SRI. SUBASH MALLAPUR, HCGP]

                             ***

       THIS CRIMINAL PETITION IS FILED UNDER SECTION 439
OF CR.P.C. PRAYING TO, ENLARGE THE ACCUSED/PETITIONER
ON BAIL IN CRIME NO.151/2012 OF UNIVERSITY POLICE
STATION    GULBARGA,    WHICH      IS   REGISTERED   FOR   THE
OFFENCE P/U/S. 504, 147, 148, 149, 307, 324 OF <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> &amp; 3(1) (10)
OF SC &amp; ST (PREVENTION OF ATROCITIES) ACT.

       THIS PETITION COMING ON FOR ORDERS, THIS DAY THE
COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING:
                                2




                           ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      The petitioner has approached this Court for grant of

regular bail having been arrested in Crime No.151/2012

registered for the offence under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Section 302</a> of IPC and other

allied offences.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      2.    The facts reveal that on 04.07.2012 at about

10.30 p.m. when the complainant was with his two friends

Praveen (deceased) and one Harshavardhan, it is alleged that

the petitioner and other accused having formed an unlawful

assembly said to have caused assault on Praveen (deceased),

complainant and Harshavardhan and in this regard a case

and counter case were registered against the persons

associated with the first accused and the deceased with his

friends. The petitioner submits that he is innocent and he has

not committed any offence and therefore, has approached this

Court for grant of regular bail.     The learned High Court

Government Pleader has opposed the application.



</p><p id="p_4">      3.    It is the submission of the learned counsel for the

petitioner that accused No.4 has been granted bail by this
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 3</span>




Court in Crl.P.No.15751/2012 vide order dated 24.08.2012

and the petitioner is also a student and he submits that he

has not assaulted the deceased and the only allegation

against him is that he caused assault on the complainant.

The material placed on record reveals that a counter case was

registered in Crime No.152/2012 in which accused No.4 i.e.,

was also assaulted.     Any how taking into consideration the

fact that the petitioner is a student and that there were

criminal cases against the complainant and the deceased, the

benefit extended to accused No.4 will have to be granted to

the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">      4.    In the result the petition is allowed. The petitioner

is ordered to be released on bail on his executing personal

bond for `50,000/- with two solvent sureties for the likesum

to the satisfaction of the Sessions Court with the further

following conditions:
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">      i)    That the petitioner shall attend the police
            station on every Sunday in between 10.00
            a.m. to 11.00 a.m. until further orders.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">      ii)   The petitioner shall not tamper with the
            prosecution witnesses in any manner.
</blockquote><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                 4</span>




<blockquote id="blockquote_3">      iii)   He shall not jump bail.</blockquote><p id="p_8">


</p><p id="p_9">      If any of the conditions are violated, the bail granted

entails cancellation.
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">      Intimate the concerned authority.
</p>
<p id="p_12">


</p><p id="p_13">                                        Sd/-
</p><p id="p_14">                                       JUDGE
