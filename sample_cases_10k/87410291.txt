<div class="doc_title">Sahil Rajpal vs State Of Rajasthan Through Pp on 10 October, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1"> HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN BENCH AT
                      JAIPUR
        S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail No. 13638 / 2017
Sahil Rajpal S/o Shri Virendra Rajpal, Aged About 34 Years, R/o
82, G-block, Sri Ganganagar, Rajasthan. (accused in Central Jail At
Jaipur)
                                                      ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State of Rajasthan Through P.P.
                                                    ----Respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)   : Mr. Jitendra Mitrucka
For Respondent(s) : Mr. Sudesh Saini-P.P.
_____________________________________________________
                HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA
                               Order
10/10/2017

     Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Code of

Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking regular bail in F.I.R. No.

215/2017 registered at Police Station ACB INT., Distt. Jaipur, for

offences under Sections 8, 9, 13(1) D and 13(2) of <a href="/doc/1331755/" id="a_2">Prevention of

Corruption Act</a>, 1988 and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_3">Sections 120-B</a>, <a href="/doc/159157/" id="a_4">170</a>, <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_5">384</a>, <a href="/doc/1667388/" id="a_6">419</a> and <a href="/doc/1255223/" id="a_7">507</a>

of Indian Penal Code, 1860 and Section 66 (C) (D) of Information

Technology Act, 2008.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
     As per the prosecution story, petitioner took money from the

complainant by posing as a police official. Petitioner is in custody

since 09.08.2017. Challan has already been presented in the

Court and conclusion of trial may take time.
</p><p id="p_4">                                  (2 of 2)
                                                       [CRLMB-13638/2017]

     Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on merits of the

case, this petition is allowed. Petitioner be admitted to bail subject

to satisfaction of the Trial Court.




</p><p id="p_5">                                                          (SABINA)J.
</p>
<p id="p_6">Sudha/221
