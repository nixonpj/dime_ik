<p id="p_1">5   21-08-2018                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel appearing on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                              Since LPA filed by Amrita Singh being LPA No. 1237

                 of 2017, arising out of the present writ had been filed before this

                 Court stating therein that after removal of the writ petitioner, she

                 had been appointed in the year 2012 itself and was not made party

                 although she was a necessary party, the order dated 31.07.2017

                 was quashed in LPA vide order dated 20.06.2018 and matter was

                 remanded for fresh consideration before learned Single Judge after
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court CWJC No.20511 of 2012 (5) dt.21-08-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                     giving an opportunity of hearing to the appellant Amrita Singh.

                     The order was passed in LPA in which I was one of the member.

</p><p id="p_3">                                   Hence, the matter be heard before another Bench for

                     which necessary permission be taken from Hon'ble the Chief

                     Justice.
