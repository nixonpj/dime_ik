<pre id="pre_1">                              ORDER SHEET
                             EC 282 of 2015
                     IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                 Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                             ORIGINAL SIDE




              TATA CAPITAL FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED

                                 Versus

                      RUBU CONSTRUCTION &amp; ANR.



       BEFORE:

       The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>

<p id="p_1">       Date : 14th July, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                             Mr.Paritosh Sinha, Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                                   Ms.S.Sharma, Adv.
</p><p id="p_4">                                                    Mr.A.Singh, Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
                 The Court: This is an execution application for

enforcement of an award dated 15th July, 2014 which is a

deemed decree.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                 It is submitted on behalf of the decree-holder

that    the   award   is   not   under    challenge.   The   time   to

challenge the award has expired. The award has now become

enforceable. There shall be an order of injunction over and

in respect of the equipment which is the subject matter

herein.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                        2</span><p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">            In    view   of    the     aforesaid,        Mr.Nirmalya         Dhara,

Advocate,     Bar    Association           Room    No.02,      High     Court   is

appointed as a Receiver for the purpose of taking symbolic

possession of the asset in question after making inventory

thereof.    The     Receiver       shall    be    entitled      to    an    initial

remuneration of 600 GMs.
</p>
<p id="p_9">            The Officer-in-Charge of the local Police Station

is directed to render assistance to the Receiver in the

event   the      Receiver     approaches          the   said    authority       for

assistance.
</p>
<p id="p_10">            The     matter    is    made     returnable        on    11th   August,

2015.
</p>
<p id="p_11">


</p><p id="p_12">                                                               (SOUMEN SEN, J.)
