<div class="doc_title">Sri Devendra Prasad Singh vs The Food Corporation Of India &amp; Ors on 9 March, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                        IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.8223 of 2014
                     ======================================================
                     Sri Devendra Prasad Singh                          .... .... Petitioner/s

                                                      Versus
                     The Food Corporation of India &amp; Ors                .... .... Respondent/s
                     ======================================================
                     Appearance :
                     For the Petitioner/s :       Mr. Syed Asgher Najmi, Advocate.
                     For the Respondent/s :       Mr. Prabhakar Tekriwal, Advocate.
                     ======================================================
                     CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE MIHIR KUMAR JHA
                     ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">3       09-03-2015                 Heard in part.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   Mr. Prabhakar Tekriwal, learned counsel appearing on

                     behalf of the Food Corporation of India (hereinafter referred to as 'the

                     Corporation') prays for and is allowed four weeks time to produce not

                     only the copy of the Food Corporation of India (Staff) Regulations,

                     1971, as updated till now, but also the statutory/executive instructions

                     relating to grant of retirement benefits to an employee of the

                     Corporation       governed by the Food Corporation of India (Staff)

                     Regulations, 1971.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Additionally, the authorities of Corporation in the

                     meantime are also directed to file their compact counter affidavit

                     addressing not only to the facts in the writ application but also as with

                     regard to the averments made in the supplementary affidavit filed

                     today.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   Put up this case for further hearing after four weeks under

                     the same heading.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                                            (Mihir Kumar Jha, J)
        Sujit/-
