<div class="doc_title">Balmiki Mahto vs The State Of Bihar on 8 February, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.49763 of 2016
                         Arising Out of PS.Case No. -163 Year- 2013 Thana -BHELDI District- SARAN
                 ======================================================
                 Balmiki Mahto, son of Lakshman Mahto, Resident of Village- Madarpur,
                 P.S. Bheldi, District- Saran.

                                                                                 .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                             .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Mukesh Kumar Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Smt. Renuka Ratnakar
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ARVIND SRIVASTAVA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   08-02-2017                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 Petitioner is accused in connection with Bheldi P.S.

                 Case     No.       163       of      2013       registered         under       <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Sections

                 498A</a>/<a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_2">304B</a>/<a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_3">201</a>/<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_4">120B</a> of the I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 The informant alleged that his daughter was married

                 to the petitioner but immediately after the marriage, her husband

                 and other in-laws began to torture her. The informant got

                 information that his daughter and her six months child were

                 poisoned to death.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioner

                 submits that the deceased died of burn injury accidentally during

                 the course of cooking. The informant also filed a petition that he
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.49763 of 2016 (3) dt.08-02-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                       filed the case on wrong information. It is also submitted that

                       similarly situated co-accused persons have already been allowed

                       bail by a Bench of this Court vide Cr. Misc. No. 4378 of 2015.

                       Further submission is that petitioner is in custody since

                       23.08.2016.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                        Having considered the facts and circumstances of the

                       case, let the above named petitioner be released on bail on

                       furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with two

                       sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of A.C.J.M.,

                       Chapra, Saran in connection with Bheldi P.S. Case No. 163 of

                       2013.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                                    (Arvind Srivastava, J)
Shail/-
</p>
