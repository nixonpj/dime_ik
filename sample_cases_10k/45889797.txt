BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 19.06.2018  

CORAM   

THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE D.KRISHNAKUMAR           
                                                                        
Crl.O.P.(MD) No.9827 of 2018 
K.Ponnandhi                                                             ... Petitioner
        
-Vs-


1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Pudukkottai District,
   Pudukkottai.

2.The Inspector of Police,
   All Women Police Station,
   Keeranor, Pudukkottai District.                                              ...
Respondents     

Prayer: Criminal Original petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of Code of
Criminal Procedure, to direct the 1st respondent to direct the 2nd respondent
to register the case on the basis of the petitioner's complaint dated
06.10.2017 within a period of stipulated time.

!For Petitioner             : Mr.A.Prasanna Rajadurai
                                                for Muthalraj
For Respondents     : Mr.K.K.Ramakrishnan, 
                                               Additional Public Prosecutor             

:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        This petition has been filed to direct the first respondent to direct
the second respondent to register the case on the basis of the petitioner's
complaint dated 06.10.2017 
</p><p id="p_2">        2. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned
Additional Public Prosecutor appearing for the respondents.
</p><p id="p_3">        3.Today, when the matter is taken up for hearing, the learned
Additional Public Prosecutor submitted that the during enquiry, both the
parties appeared before the second respondent and gave a statement that they
will approach the family Court to seek their remedy and the petitioner has
also requests not to proceed further in the complaint. In the light of the
above said statement, the second respondent closed the complaint. The closure
report is also produced before this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4. Recording the submission made by the learned Additional Public
Prosecutor, this Criminal Original Petition is dismissed with liberty to the
petitioner to approach before the appropriate forum to seek her remedy.
</p>                        

<p id="p_5">To
</p><p id="p_6">1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Pudukkottai District,
   Pudukkottai.
</p>
<p id="p_7">2.The Inspector of Police,
   All Women Police Station,
   Keeranor, Pudukkottai District.
</p>
<p id="p_8">3.The Additional Public Prosecutor,
   Madurai Bench of Madras High Court,
   Madurai.
