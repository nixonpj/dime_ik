<p id="p_1">2   13-02-2018            Both the bail applications (Cr. Misc. No. 8431 of 2018 and
                 Cr. Misc. No. 2322 of 2018) arise out of same FIR therefore are
                 being disposed of by this common order.
</p><p id="p_2">                          Heard learned Counsel for the petitioners and the State.
                          Petitioners, already in custody, seek bail in Naubatpur PS
                 Case No. 275 of 2017 registered under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>,
                 <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_5">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_6">325</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_7">326</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_8">307</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_9">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_10">506</a>, <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_11">302</a> of the IPC and <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_12">Section 27</a> of the
                 Arms Act.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.8431 of 2018 (2) dt.13-02-2018                            2</span>




<p id="p_3">                               The allegation in brief is that the accused persons armed with
                     lathi, danda and pistol assaulted informant's brother Nanle Yadav
                     and his nephew Nitish Yadav while they were doing agricultural
                     work in the paddy field.
</p><p id="p_4">                               Learned Counsel for the petitioners submits that there is case
                     and counter case between both sides, there is land dispute and from
                     the prosecution side two persons were killed whereas from the
                     petitioners' side one person was killed. All the family members have
                     been implicated and there is general and omnibus allegation, nothing
                     is specific against any one and the death is said to be caused by hard
                     blunt substance, moreover title of the land was decided in favour of
                     the petitioners in the Title Suit and charge-sheet has been submitted.
</p><p id="p_5">                               Learned Counsel for the State submits that there is general
                     and omnibus allegation against all and even in the case diary there is
                     no specific allegation against any one.
</p><p id="p_6">                               Having considered the said facts and circumstances, the
                     petitioners, namely, Dinesh Yadav and Bhun Bhun Yadav @ Sudhir
                     Yadav are directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of
                     Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) by both of them with two sureties of the
                     like amount each to the satisfaction of ACJM II, Danapur, Patna in
                     connection with Naubatpur PS Case No. 275 of 2017.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                     (Arun Kumar, J.)
Snkumar/-
