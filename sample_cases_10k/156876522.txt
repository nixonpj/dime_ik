<p id="p_2">PER COURT:
</p>

<p id="p_3">                Heard learned counsel for the applicant and learned APP
for the respondent State of Maharashtra.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                Admittedly, the applicant is convicted for the ofence
punishable under <a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_1">Section 409</a> and <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_2">468</a> of IPC.                          Both the courts of
learned Magistrate and learned Sessions Judge, expressed the
concurrent        fnding         about   the     guilt   of    the     accused/applicant.
Obviously, there are legal issues arguable in nature for detail haring
of the proceeding on merit, to see the legality and propriety of the
impugned order by exercising revisional jurisdiction under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">section
397</a> of Cr.P.C. Therefore, "ADMIT".
</p>

<p id="p_5">                Issue           notice   of      revision       application           to      the
respondent/State of Maharashtra.                     Learned APP waives notice for
respondent sole.           R &amp; P. is already received to this court.                        After
compliance of procedural formalities, list the present revision for
fnal hearing on merit on 11th December, 2019, as the private paper
book of the evidence adduced before the trial court is available in
