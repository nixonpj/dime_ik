<p id="p_1">	Rule.
</p><p id="p_2">Learned APP Mr. Sejpal waives service of rule for respondent State.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_3">	The
applicant has been convicted by the learned Magistrate which
conviction has been upheld by learned Sessions Court. He wishes to
approach High Court against his conviction and sentence of three
years. He has already been in jail for a period of more than two
years and one month including while as an under-trial prisoner.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_4">	Under
the circumstances, applicant is ordered to be released on parole for
a period of 30 days on his depositing an amount of Rs.5,000/-(Rupees
five thousand only) before the jail authorities. Upon completion of
the said parole period, he shall surrender before the jail
authorities. Rule made absolute accordingly.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_5">	Order
shall be communicated to the prisoner in jail by the Registry.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_6">(Akil
