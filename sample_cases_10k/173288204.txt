        The writ petitioner has approached this Court claiming compensation due to the death of her

</p><p id="p_2">        Issue notice of motion returnable in six weeks.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        Mr. P.S. Deka, learned Standing Counsel, Revenue Department, Assam, has entered appearance

service of notice is deemed to be complete in this case. However, the petitioner to furnish extra copies of

the writ petition, in requisite numbers, to the learned counsel representing the respondents within three

days from today, enabling them to obtain instruction facilitating disposal of the writ petition.

</p><p id="p_4">        List accordingly.
