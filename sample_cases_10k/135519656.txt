<p id="p_3">           S.J. VAZIFDAR, ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE (Oral)

                               The learned counsel appearing on behalf of the respondent

           states that the notifications issued under the <a href="/doc/7832/" id="a_1">Land Acquisition Act</a>, which

           have been challenged in the writ petition, have been withdrawn. The

           petitioner's grievance, therefore, stands redressed. It is not necessary,

           therefore, to grant any relief in this writ petition.

</p><p id="p_4">           2.                  Mr. Puri stated that fresh notification has been issued and the

           petitioner is always at liberty to challenge the same.

</p><p id="p_5">           3.                  Writ petition is accordingly disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_2">
