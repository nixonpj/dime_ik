<p id="p_1">(Per
: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.M.KAPADIA)
 

 


 


		Mr.
</p><p id="p_2">K L Pandya, Ld. AGP states that two reply affidavits have been filed,
one by Mr. Dalsukhbhai Patel, P.I., Patan Police Station and other by
Mr. Govindbhai Visani, P.S.I., A Division Police Station, Rajkot
City. It has been inter-alia stated therein about the effective steps
taken by the police authorities to find out the corpus -
missing girl Hetal, who is in allegedly illegal custody of
respondents nos. 3 and 4, who allegedly kidnapped corpus and kept her
in the State of Andhra Pradesh. It
is also stated in the reply affidavits that one team consisting of
one P.S.I and other constable of Patan Police Station has already
been sent to Andhra Pradesh to trace out the missing girl and second
team  of the Police Officers of Rajkot Police Station will be sent
soon to the Andhra Pradesh to find out missing girl. It would require
more time to trace out the corpus. He, therefore, requests to adjourn
the matter after 4 weeks. 
</p>
 


<p id="p_3">2.		The
prayer made by Mr. Pandya has not been opposed by Mr. Mahesh Pujara,
learned advocate for Mr. Ashish Dagli, learned advocate for the
petitioner. 
</p>
 


<p id="p_4">3.		Hence,
by consent, matter is adjourned to 07/10/2010. 
</p>
 

 


 

 


 

<p id="p_5">[
A.M. KAPADIA, J. ]
 

 


 
	 

[
	J.C. UPADHYAYA, J. ]

Pansala.
