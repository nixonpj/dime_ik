<p id="p_1">The
	present application is for interim injunction pending the First
	Appeal.
</p>	  

<p id="p_2">Heard
	Mr.Nanavati, learned Counsel for the applicant.  Mr.Mehta has filed
	his appearance for respondent No.1 ?  original claimant.
</p>	  

<p id="p_3">The
	ad-interim relief was granted by this Court on condition to deposit
	the entire awarded amount as per the order dated 26.3.2007.
	Mr.Nanavati, learned Counsel for the applicant states that the
	amount has been deposited and the condition is complied with. 
	Hence, the ad-interim relief granted earlier is confirmed.
</p>	  

<p id="p_4">Considering
	the facts and circumstances, the amount shall be invested by the
	Tribunal for a period of five years with the further clause of
	renewal in the event the appeal is not finalized before the due date
	and with the condition that the claimant shall not be entitled to
	any loan over such investment, but shall be entitled to the interest
	periodically as and when it becomes due.
</p>	  

<p id="p_5">The
	application is allowed to the aforesaid extent.  Rule made absolute
	accordingly.
</p>
