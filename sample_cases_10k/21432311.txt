<p id="p_1">      Petitioner has filed Ext.P2 appeal before the 3rd respondent

and along with the appeal, petitioner has also filed an application for

stay. It is submitted that during the pendency of the appeal, steps

are being taken by the respondent authorities for recovering the

amount in terms of Ext.P4.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2. Having regard to the fact that an appeal is pending before

the competent authority, I do not think it necessary to consider the

matter on merits. Suffice to say that the appellate authority has to

consider the stay petition on merits within a specified time and in

the meantime, the recovery can be kept in abeyance.

</p><p id="p_3">      Accordingly, this writ petition is disposed of as under:

</p><p id="p_4">      (i)    The 3rd respondent shall consider the stay petition filed

along with the appeal within a period of one month from the date of

receipt of a copy of the judgment.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      (ii)   In the meantime, the demand made in terms of Ext.P4

shall be kept in abeyance.
</p>
