<p id="p_1">3   20-11-2014                     Heard learned counsel for the Petitioners and the

                 State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  The Petitioners who are the in-laws seek quashing of

                 the First Information Report of Khajouli P.S. Case No. 26 of 2013,

                 G.R. No. 810 of 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  It has been submitted that after initial investigation,

                 charge sheet was submitted as against the husband of the deceased

                 who was put on Trial. None of the witnesses have supported the

                 Prosecution case and, hence, the husband was acquitted itself on

                 this ground.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   The      Petitioners       seek      quashing        of    the     First

                 Information Report since the investigation should be rarely
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.40680 of 2014 (3) dt.20-11-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                2/2</span>




                  interfered with, I am not inclined to interfere in the matter.

</p><p id="p_5">                                   The Investigating Officer is directed to look into the

                  record of the case and such other materials and conclude the

                  investigation positively within a period of two months from the

                  date of the receipt of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   Let this order be communicated to the Superintendent

                  of Police, Madhubani, through FAX at the cost of the Petitioner

                  for its onward transmission to the Investigating Officer in

                  connection with Khajouli P.S. Case No. 26 of 2013, G.R. No. 810

                  of 2013 pending before the Court of Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                  Madhubani.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                   The application stands disposed off.




</p><p id="p_8">                                                                    (Anjana Prakash, J)
    S.Ali/-
</p>
