<div class="doc_title">BA1/1954/2019 on 26 September, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">BA1 No.1954 of 2019
Hon'ble R.C. Khulbe, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">        Mr. Naveen S. Bisht, Adv. for the applicant.
        Mr. Lalit Miglani, B.H. for the State.
        I.A. 12833/19 is disposed of by taking on
record the counter affidavit filed by the State.
</p><p id="p_2">        Heard on the bail application.
        Applicant Shawez Kureshi has sought his
release on bail in connection with FIR no.225 of
2019 u/s 3/5/6/11(1) of Uttarakhand Protection of
Cow Progeny Act, registered at P.S.
Banbhoolpura, Distt. Nainital.
</p><p id="p_3">        It is argued that the applicant has been
falsely implicated; he was not arrested at the spot;
he is languishing in jail since 20.7.2019; and there
is no criminal history against him.
</p><p id="p_4">        Learned State Counsel opposed the bail.
        Accused is in jail since 20.7.2019; he was
not arrested at the spot and nor is there any
recovery made from him; and the case is triable by
Magistrate.
</p><p id="p_5">        Considering the above facts, at this stage, it
is a fit case for bail. The bail application is thus,
allowed. The applicant shall be enlarged on bail on
executing a personal bond and furnishing two
sureties, each of the like amount, to the satisfaction
of the Court concerned.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                           (R.C. Khulbe, J.)
                             26.09.2019
Rdang
