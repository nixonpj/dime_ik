                      B.A. No. 10452 of 2018
     1.    Lakho Devi
     2.    Munna Singh                   .....     Petitioners
                             Versus
     The State of Jharkhand                    .....      Opp. Party
                                -----
</pre><p id="p_1">    CORAM:        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
</p><p id="p_2">                                 -----</p><pre id="pre_2">
     For the Petitioners     : Mr. O.P.Singh, Advocate
     For the State           : A.P.P
                                -----
04/Dated: 05/04/2019

</pre><p id="p_3">      The petitioner has been made accused for the offence under
<a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>,<a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">341</a>,<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>,<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a> and <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_5">302</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_4">      It appears that under order dated 13.03.2019, petitioner no.1
namely, Lakho Devi was admitted on bail and it was directed that the
bail application of petitioner no.2, namely, Munna Singh will be
considered after framing of charge.
</p><p id="p_5">      Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that charge
has been framed.
</p><p id="p_6">      Learned   counsel   for   the     petitioners   is   directed   to   file
supplementary affidavit bringing on record the copy of order framing
charge.
</p><p id="p_7">      List this case after Two weeks.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                              (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
Raman/
