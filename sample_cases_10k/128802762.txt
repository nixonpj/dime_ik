<div class="doc_title">Bheemsain Kushpup vs State Of Uttarakhand on 14 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">Urgency Application No. 3388 of 2017
In
BA1 No. 802 of 2017
Hon'ble V.K. Bist, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">      Ms. Reeta Saxena, Advocate for
the applicant.
</p><p id="p_2">      Mr. Raman Kumar Sah, Deputy
Advocate General for the State of
Uttarakhand.
</p><p id="p_3">      Heard learned counsel for the
parties and considered the grounds
taken up in the bail application.
</p><p id="p_4">      This is the first bail application
moved by the applicant.
</p><p id="p_5">      The      applicant-    Bheemsain
Kushyup is seeking regular bail in
connection with Case Crime No. 156 of
2016, relating to offences punishable
under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">Section 379</a> and <a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_2">411</a> of I.P.C.,
registered at P.S. Lalkuan, District
Nainital.
</p><p id="p_6">      It is submitted that an F.I.R. was
lodged against the unknown thief,
alleging therein, that some unknown
thief committed theft of the motorcycle
of the complainant. The F.I.R. was
lodged on 08.11.2016.
</p><p id="p_7">      It is contended by the learned
counsel for the applicant that the
applicant is not named in the F.I.R;
has no previous convict and is in jail
since 09.11.2016. It is also submitted
that if bail is granted, the applicant
will not misuse the same.
</p><p id="p_8">      Learned Deputy Advocate General
submitted that the applicant hails
 from Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh and will
flee away from justice, if he is granted
bail. To this, learned counsel for the
applicant submitted that the applicant
is ready to furnish local and reliable
sureties to the satisfaction of the
Magistrate concerned, if he is directed
to be enlarged on bail.
</p><p id="p_9">       Considering the submission of
learned counsel for the parties, and
without expressing any opinion as to
final merits of the case, this Court is
of the view that the applicant deserves
bail at this stage.
</p><p id="p_10">      The bail application is allowed.
      Let the applicant be released on
bail, on executing personal bond and
furnishing two reliable local sureties,
each of the like amount to the
satisfaction     of    the     Magistrate
concerned.
</p><p id="p_11">      Urgency      application     stands
disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_12">                                    (V.K. Bist, J.)
                                     14.07.2017
Navin                            
 </p>
