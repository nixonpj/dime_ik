 (PER : HONOURABLE THE ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE ANANT S. DAVE)
        This application under <a href="/doc/100581/" id="a_1">section 5</a> of the Limitation Act is
preferred for condonation of delay of 21 days occurred in filing
the letters patent appeal against the judgment dated 22.2.2018
passed by learned Single Judge in Special Civil Application No.
12035 of 2006.
</p><p id="p_3">        Heard learned advocates for the parties.
        Considering the averments made in the application on
oath, we are satisfied that sufficient cause is made out for
condonation of delay. Hence, delay is condoned. Application is
allowed. Rule is made absolute.
</p><p id="p_4">        Letters patent appeal be listed on 7th May, 2019.
