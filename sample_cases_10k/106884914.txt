<div class="doc_title">Tribeni Prasad Singh vs State Of Bihar on 19 February, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">            IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                      CR.MISC. NO.599 OF 2009
TRIBENI PRASAD SINGH, SON OF RAGHUNANDAN PRASAD SINGH, RESIDENT
   OF VILLAGE LAHTHUA, POLICE STATION MOHANPUR, DISTRICT GAYA
                              VERSUS
                          STATE OF BIHAR
                              *********
</pre><p id="p_1">2    19.02.2009                Heard learned Counsel for the petitioner and

                   the A.P.P. appearing on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner apprehends his arrest in a case

                   under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_2">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_4">120B</a> of the Indian Penal

                   Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 The petitioner has executed a sale deed with

                   respect to plot no. 1232 and by mistake the scribe has

                   entered plot no. 1231 of khata no.             204. It is further

                   submitted that the petitioner has also filed a title suit in

                   which he has prayed that his name which has been entered in

                   the records of right with respect to plot no. 1231 should be

                   cancelled.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Considering     the       aforesaid    facts   and

                   submission, the above named petitioner is directed to be

                   released on anticipatory bail in the event of arrest/surrender

                   before the Court below within a period of six weeks from today

                   in Complaint Case No. 439 of 2008 on furnishing bail bond of

                   Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like

                   amount each to the satisfaction of Shri R. K. Singh, Judicial

                   Magistrate, Sherghati, district Gaya.


</p><pre id="pre_2">Anand                                                   ( Sheema Ali Khan, J. )
 </pre>
