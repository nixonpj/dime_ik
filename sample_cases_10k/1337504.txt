<div class="doc_title">Shalendra Sharma vs The State Of Bihar on 25 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.24005 of 2017
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -3 Year- 2017 Thana -ALAMNAGAR District- MADHEPURA
                 ======================================================
                 Shalendra Sharma, Son of late Ramdeo Sharma, Resident of Village-
                 Chaubasa Itahari, P.S. Alamnagar, District- Madhepura.
                                                                         .... .... Petitioner
                                                   Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                    .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s    :   Mr. Bhola Prasad, Adv.
                 For the Opposite Party : Mr. Binod Kumar no.3, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SHIVAJI PANDEY
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   25-08-2017                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                In this case, the petitioner is seeking anticipatory

                 bail in connection with Alamnagar P.S. Case No.03 of 2017,

                 registered for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_1">Sections 376(D)</a> of

                 the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Allegation has been made that while the informant

                 was returning from his basa after feeding the cattle, in the mid-

                 way the brother of the petitioner and two unknown persons have

                 intercepted and at the behest of the brother of the petitioner said

                 two unknown persons have perpetrated rape on her.

</p><p id="p_4">                                In the FI.R. as well as other parts of the case diary,

                 allegation has been made against the petitioner that he has

                 threatened the informant to repeat the same offence against her,
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.24005 of 2017 (3) dt.25-08-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                       but there is no offence.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                        Looking to the aforesaid facts, let the petitioner-

                       Shalendra Sharma, be released on anticipatory bail in the event

                       of his arrest or surrender before the learned Court below within a

                       period of six weeks from today, on furnishing bail bonds of

                       Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand Only) with two sureties of

                       the like amount each to the satisfaction of the S.D.J.M.

                       Udakishunganj, District- Madhepura, in connection with

                       Alamnagar P.S. Case No.03 of 2017, subject to the conditions as

                       laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_2">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal

                       Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                         (Shivaji Pandey, J)
pawan/-
</p>
