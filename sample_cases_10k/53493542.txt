<div class="doc_title">Prakash Roy @ Prakash Ray vs The State Of Bihar on 1 April, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.5798 of 2014
                 ======================================================
                 Prakash Roy @ Prakash Ray, Son of Sri Jagarnath Ray, Resident of Village
                 - Sikandarpr, District - Balia (U.P.) At Present Residing Kacharapada
                 Railway, Plot No. 160/A Police Station - Bijpur District - 24, P.G.S.C.N
                 (West Bangal).

                                                                        .... ....   Petitioner
                                                  Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner     : Mr. Navin Kumar, Advocate.
                 For the Opposite Party : Mr. Ramesh Chandra, APP.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SHYAM KISHORE
                 SHARMA
                                               ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   01-04-2014                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioner seeks bail in a case which has been

                 registered under <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_1">Sections 467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_2">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_3">471</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_4">420</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian

                 Penal Code. The case has been registered on the basis of written

                 report of the Branch Manager, State Bank of India against

                 Mahavir Singh, the account holder.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Submission is that the petitioner's name has come

                 during investigation on the confession of Prakash Rai. The

                 petitioner has been falsely framed in the present case. The

                 petitioner is in custody since 15.7.2013. After clearance of the

                 cheque of Rs. 2,98,700/- the payment was made in favour of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.5798 of 2014 (3) dt.01-04-2014                      2</span>




                    Mahavir Singh at Bank of India Bhardeshwar Branch Hoogly,

                    West Bengal and some of the amount was withdrawn. The I.O. has

                    failed to locate the person who withdrew the money.

</p><p id="p_4">                                     Considering the facts and circumstances of the case,

                    let the above named petitioner be released on bail on furnishing

                    bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the

                    like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial

                    Magistrate/court concerned, Jehanabad in connection with Arwal

                    P.S. Case No. 54 of 2012, subject to the condition that the bailors

                    should be local and substantial.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                            (Shyam Kishore Sharma, J.)

KKSINHA/-
</p>
