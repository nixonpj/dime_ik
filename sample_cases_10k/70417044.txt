 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED  :  29.01.2015
CORAM
THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE R.SUBBIAH
Contempt Petition No.3306 of 2014

K.Elangovan								... Petitioner

Vs.

Mr.A.Muruganandam
Deputy Registrar of Co-op. 
     Societies (Housing)
Vellore  9.								... Respondent


Prayer : Petition filed under <a href="/doc/974692/" id="a_1">Section 11</a> of Contempt of Courts Act, to punish the respondent for his disobedience of this Court's order dated 14.02.2014 and made in W.P.No.4246 of 2014 under the <a href="/doc/1396751/" id="a_2">Contempt of Courts Act</a>.

	For Petitioner	:	Mr.S.Sairaman
	For Respondent	:	Mr.R.Bala Ramesh
					Additional Government Pleader


ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	This Court, by order dated 14.02.2014, directed the respondents to consider and dispose of the representation of the petitioner dated 18.04.2013, by passing appropriate orders in accordance with law and on merits, within a period of six weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of the order.  Since the order has not been complied with by the respondent within the stipulated period, the present Contempt Petition has been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2.  Today, when the matter is taken up for consideration, the learned Additional Government Pleader submitted that by considering the representation of the petitioner, the Joint Registrar of Co-operative Societies. Vellore, has passed appropriate order on 14.11.2014.  
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. Since the order of this Court dated 14.02.2014 has been complied with, nothing survives in this contempt petition.  Hence, this Contempt Petition is closed. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">   29.01.2015
ogy
To

Mr.A.Muruganandam
Deputy Registrar of Co-op. 
</p><p id="p_5">     Societies (Housing)
Vellore  9.
</p>
<p id="p_6">R.SUBBIAH, J.
</p><p id="p_7">ogy
	













Contempt Petition No.3306 of 2014
