<div class="doc_title">Astak S/O Shri Abdul Majid @ Abduli ... vs State Of Rajasthan on 15 April, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">        HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                    BENCH AT JAIPUR

     S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 4688/2019

Astak S/o Shri Abdul Majid @ Abduli B/c Mev, R/o Padasali Police
Station Tijara Distt. Alwar Raj. (At Present Confined In Jail In
Alwar)
                                                                    ----Petitioner
                                   Versus
State Of Rajasthan, Through P.p.
                                                                  ----Respondent</pre>


<p id="p_1">For Petitioner(s)         :    Mr. Vijay Narayan Sharma
For Respondent(s)         :    Mr. Sumer Singh Ola, PP



            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE INDERJEET SINGH

                                    Order

15/04/2019

</p><p id="p_2">1.    The present bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

Cr.P.C. The petitioner has been arrested in connection with FIR No.

194/2018 Registered at Police Station Tijara, District Alwar for the

offence(s) under <a href="/doc/1982060/" id="a_2">Sections 450</a>, <a href="/doc/9545/" id="a_3">376-D</a> IPC, Section 5/6 of the

Protection of Children From Sexual Offences Act, 2012.

</p><p id="p_3">2.    Counsel for the petitioner submits that co-accused Sabir has

already been released on bail by this court vide order dated

11.4.2019    passed     in     S.B.       Criminal        Misc.     Second    Bail

No.4687/2019 and his case is similar to Sabir.

</p><p id="p_4">3.    Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

</p><p id="p_5">4.    Considering the contentions put-forth by the counsel for the

petitioner and taking into account the facts and circumstances of

the case and without expressing any opinion on the merits of the




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    (Downloaded on 28/06/2019 at 12:36:51 AM)</span>
                                                                         (2 of 2)                   [CRLMB-4688/2019]


                                   case, this court deems it just and proper to enlarge the petitioner

                                   on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   5.    Accordingly, the bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C. is

                                   allowed and it is ordered that the accused-petitioner Astak S/o

                                   Shri Abdul Majid @ Abduli shall be enlarged on bail provided he

                                   furnishes a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two

                                   sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial

                                   Judge for his appearance before the court concerned on all the

                                   dates of hearing as and when called upon to do so.




</p><p id="p_7">                                                           (INDERJEET SINGH),J

                                   ANAND TANWAR /85
