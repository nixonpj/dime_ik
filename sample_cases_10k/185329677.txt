<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 73</span>
<p id="p_1"> Allowed
md.
</p><p id="p_2">                                CRM No. 1398 of 2017
                In Re:- An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">section 438</a>
            of the Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 17.2.2017 in
            connection with G.R. No. 4944 of 2016 arising out of Barasat
            Women P.S. Case No. 100 dated 13.12.2016 under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Sections
            498A</a>/<a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_3">406</a>/<a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_4">376</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">307</a>/<a href="/doc/1185693/" id="a_6">511</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_7">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code, 1860 now
            pending in the Court of the Learned Chief Judicial Magistrate at
            Barasat ;
</p><p id="p_3">                      And
               In the matter of:-
</p><p id="p_4">               Tanmoy Gain
                                                                    ... Petitioner
               Mr. Samrat Goswami
                                                             ..for the Petitioner

              Mr. Madhusudan Sur, A.P.P.,
              Mr. Manoranjan Mahata
                                                                 .. for the State


                On scrutiny of the Case Diary, the contents of the F.I.R. and
           the 164 statement of the victim lady, so also the injury report,
           warrants grant of anticipatory bail to the petitioner herein.
           Accordingly, the petitioner, namely, Tanmoy Gain, in the event of
           his arrest be released on anticipatory bail upon furnishing bond
           of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties of Rs.5,000/- each, to the
           satisfaction of the arresting authority and subject to the
           conditions imposed under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_8">Section 438</a> (2) <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_9">Cr.P.C</a>.
</p><p id="p_5">                In view of the aforesaid, this application is allowed and
           disposed of.
</p><p id="p_6">                 Certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the
           parties on priority basis upon compliance of all formalities.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                                      (Patherya,J.)
