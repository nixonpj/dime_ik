              CONTEMPT CASE No.1410 of 2018

                                             ..     Petitioner

Chandrasekhar, Station House Officer,

Kushaiguda, Cyberabad and another.
                                        ..        Respondents




The Court made the following :
                                   2




            THE HON'BLE SRI JUSTICE P.NAVEEN RAO

                CONTEMPT CASE No.1410 of 2018

ORDER :
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        This contempt case is filed alleging willful disobedience of

the direction issued by this Court in W.P.No.29853 of 2016 by the

respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">2.      Learned Government Pleader for Home produced a copy of

order dated 05.04.2017 of the Station House Officer, Kushaiguda,

whereunder the petitioner's request for protection to undertake

construction of her building was rejected and she was advised to

obtain appropriate orders from the competent authority for the

same.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">3.      In view of the fact that an order was already passed on the

representation made by the petitioner, which is placed on record, it

cannot be said that the respondents have deliberately disobeyed

the order of this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">4.      Hence, the Contempt Case is closed. The respondents are

discharged from the contempt proceedings.


</p><p id="p_8">        Consequently, Miscellaneous Petitions, if any, pending in

this Contempt Case shall stand closed.


</p><p id="p_9">                                          __________________________
                                          JUSTICE P.NAVEEN RAO

Date : 25.02.2019
ssp
