<div class="doc_title">M/S Deepak Advertisers Thr vs Municipal Corporation Thr on 2 January, 2019</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                 1</span>

                             THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                                         WP 29502/2018
      M/s Deepak Advertisers through its <a href="/doc/139061857/" id="a_1">Proprietor vs. Municipal Corporation, Gwalior &amp; Anr</a>.
</p>

<p id="p_2">      Gwalior, dtd. 02/01/2019
              Shri Pawan Dwivedi, counsel for the petitioner.
              This petition under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_2">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India
      has been filed against the order dated 29/11/2018 (Annexure P-1)
      by which the petitioner has been directed to pay Rs.31,11,000/- by
      way    of   permanent          amount     and    Rs.11,92,320/-         by    way     of
      advertisement charges.
</p><p id="p_3">            The impugned order has been passed by Commissioner,
      Municipal Corporation, Gwalior, which is an appealable order.
      Accordingly, this writ petition is dismissed with liberty to the
      petitioner to avail statutory remedy available to it under the law.
</p>
