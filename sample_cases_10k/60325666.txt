M/s Sadbhav Engineering Ltd.                           .. Petitioner

vs.

Coram:       Hon'ble Mr. Justice Rajesh Bindal

             for the petitioner.

             Mr. Ankur Mittal, Addl. A.G., Haryana.

<p id="p_1">             The only prayer made by the petitioner is that during the

pendency of the appeal filed by it before the First Appellate Authority

against the order of assessment, the Bank guarantee furnished, in terms of

the demand raised, should not be encashed.

</p><p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the

respondents be directed to release the bank account, which was attached

vide communication dated 25.07.2016, as the interest of the department is

secured.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Learned counsel for the State, on instructions from Dr.

V.K.Shastri, ETO, Sirsa, stated that during the pendency of the appeal

before the First Appellate Authority, the bank guarantee furnished by the

petitioner shall not be encashed. He further submitted that the necessary

direction shall be issued to the bank releasing the attachment of the bank

account of the petitioner.
</p>
