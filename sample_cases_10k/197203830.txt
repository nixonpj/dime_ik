                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.28466 of 2012
                   ====================================================
                   ==
                   Dina Nath Roy @ Dina Nath Rai &amp; Anr.
                                                                  .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                   The State Of Bihar
                                                             .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ====================================================
                   ==
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr.

                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR

                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   06-08-2012                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and

                   learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Petitioners are named in the first information

                   report on the allegation that they opened fire causing fire-

                   arm injury on the elbow of the injured, Sitaram Yadav.

</p><p id="p_3">                               Learned counsel for the petitioners drew my

                   attention towards Annexure-2 to this petition and submitted

                   that only one fire-arm injury has been found on the person

                   of the injured, Sitaram Yadav and it is not clear as to which

                   of the petitioners, is author of the aforesaid injury. It is

                   further pointed out by him that there is case and counter

                   case between the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.28466 of 2012 (2) dt.06-08-2012</span>




                        of the case as well as submission of the parties, let the

                        petitioners, namely, Dina Nath Roy @ Dina Nath Rai and

                        Ram Babu Roy be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds

                        of Rs 10,000/- (ten thousand) each with two sureties of the

                        like amount each in connection with Dumariyaghat P.S.

                        Case No. 81 of 2008 to the satisfaction of Chief Judicial

                        Magistrate, Motihari.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                             (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
</p>
