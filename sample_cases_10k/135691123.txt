<div class="doc_title">Prabhawati vs State Of U.P. on 20 November, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Rajeev Singh</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD, LUCKNOW BENCH
 
 

?Court No. - 11
 
Case :- BAIL No. - 11116 of 2019
 
Applicant :- Prabhawati
 
Opposite Party :- State of U.P.
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Lakshmana Singh
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.
 

 
Hon'ble Rajeev Singh,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Heard, learned counsel for the applicant, learned A.G.A for the State of U.P. and perused the record.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">The present bail application has been filed on behalf of the applicant in Case Crime No.139 of 2018 under <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_1">Sections 366</a> and <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_2">376</a> D I.P.C, Police Station Kumarganj, District-Faizabad, with the prayer to enlarge him on bail. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">The submissions of learned counsel for the applicant are that the applicant is innocent person and has been falsely implicated in the case, he is having no previous criminal history and in jail since 11.10.2019. It is further submitted on behalf of applicant that the applicant has been falsely implicated in FIR No.139 of 2018, under <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_3">Sections 366</a> and <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_4">376</a> D I.P.C. which was lodged by informant on 09.07.2018. He has further submitted that the statement of prosecutrix was recorded in which she levelled allegation against the Aditya Singh and Ankit for molestation, they have been granted bail by this Court, vide orders dated 12.03.2019 and 12.03.2019 passed in Criminal Misc. Bail Application No.04 of 2019 and 10959 of 2018, in these circumstances, the applicant is entitled for bail. In case of being enlarged on bail, he will not misuse the liberty of bail. 
</p> 
<p id="p_4">Learned A.G.A. has opposed the prayer for grant of bail to the applicant but has fairly conceded that the specific allegation have been levelled against the co-accused who have already been granted bail by this Court. 
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Considering the rival submissions of learned counsel for parties, material available on record as well as totality of fact and circumstances, and without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, I am of the view that the applicant is entitled to be released on bail. 
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Let applicant -Prabhawati- be released on bail in Case Crime No.139 of 2018 under <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_5">Sections 366</a> and <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_6">376</a> D I.P.C, Police Station Kumarganj, District-Faizabad, on his furnishing personal bond and two reliable sureties each of the like amount to the satisfaction of the court concerned subject to following conditions:- 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">(1) Applicant will not try to influence the witnesses or tamper with the evidence of the case or otherwise misuse the liberty of bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_8">(2) Applicant will fully cooperate in expeditious disposal of the case and shall not seek any adjournment on the dates fixed for evidence when witnesses are present in the Court. 
</p> 
<p id="p_9">(3) Applicant shall remain present, in person, before the trial court on the dates fixed for (a) opening of the case, (b) framing of charge; and (c) recording of statement under Section 313 Cr.P.C. 
</p> 
<p id="p_10">Any violation of above conditions will be treated misuse of bail and learned Court below will be at liberty to pass appropriate order in the matter regarding cancellation of bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_11">Order Date :- 20.11.2019
 
Amit/-
</p>
