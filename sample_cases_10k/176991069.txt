<p id="p_2"> 1.                 The present matter was referred for mediation and 

 I am happy to note that, the mediation has become successful. 

 The learned Mediator has forwarded his report along with the 

 terms of settlement, which are signed by the parties to the  lis. 

 The learned Counsel appearing for the parties submitted that, 

 the present appeal be disposed of in terms of settlement.  



</p><p id="p_3"> 2.                 I   have   gone   through   the   terms   of   settlement.   The 

 settlement, which has been arrived at is within four corners of 

 law, it has, therefore, to be accepted.  It is accordingly accepted.

 settlement.   The   Award   be   drawn   accordingly.     The   terms   of 

 settlement shall form the part of the Award. 



</p><p id="p_6"> 4.                 The   appellant  -  Insurance   Company   is  entitled  for 

 the refund of the Court Fee in accordance with law.  



</p><p id="p_7"> 5.                 After the amount is deposited, it would be open for 

 the Original Claimant No.1  to withdraw the same. 



</p><p id="p_8"> 6.                 The   Registry   shall   permit   the   withdrawal   in   the 

 name of Original Claimant No.1, if he submits the consent of all 

 other claimants, by verifying the said position.
