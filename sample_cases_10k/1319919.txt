ORAL

<p id="p_1">1.	Heard
learned advocate Mr.A.V.Nair for learned advocate Mr.Ashish Shah for
the petitioner, who vehemently submitted that the learned first
appellate Court has committed an error in allowing Civil Misc.Appeal
No.107 of 2007 by judgment and order dated 16.07.2009 and has
committed an error in quashing and setting aside the order passed
below application Exh.5 in Regular Civil Suit No.3 of 2007 by the
learned Principal Senior Civil Judge, Mehsana dated 26.04.2007.	
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_2">2.	The fact
that the case of the present petitioner   original plaintiff is
that the 'urinal' constructed by the petitioner is already handed
over to the Panchayat, the petitioner cannot have any grievance if
the same is ordered to be removed by the authorities.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_3">2.1	Taking into
consideration the fact that the 'urinal' is constructed and it is
already handed over to the Government, the authorities shall take
this fact in the consideration before effecting demolition. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_4">3.	With these
observations, the petition is disposed of.
</p> 


 


 

 


 

<p id="p_5">(Ravi
R.Tripathi, J.)
