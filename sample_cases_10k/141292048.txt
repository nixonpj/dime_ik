<p id="p_6">
      The Court: This is an application by the added defendants namely 1A, 1B

and 1C for extension of time to file the written statement. Mr. Reetobroto Mitra,

the learned Counsel appearing on behalf of the plaintiff, submits that the original

defendant has already filed a written statement and the said defendants cannot

make any inconsistent pleading and, accordingly, no leave may be given to the

said defendant to file a fresh written statement. Mr. Sabhyasachi Chowdhury

submits that the cause of action against the present defendants do not survive.

Ordinarily the substituted defendants are bound by the pleading filed by the

original defendant. However, if there are anything personal which required to be

canvassed the defendants may do so and the same can only be decided at the

trial of the suit after the pleadings are filed. In such circumstances reserving the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           2</span>


objections raised by Mr. Reetobroto Mitra the time to file the written statement is

extended by four weeks from date.
</p>

supplied to the parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.
