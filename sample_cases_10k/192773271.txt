<p id="p_1">  (PER : HONOURABLE THE
CHIEF JUSTICE MR. BHASKAR BHATTACHARYA)
 


 


 


	By this Public
Interest Litigation, the petitioners have payed for issue of 
mandamus or any other appropriate writ, order or direction to the
respondent Nos. 1 to 3 for starting external and distance learning
courses in the North Gujarat University.
</p> 


<p id="p_2">	It appears from
the affidavit given on behalf of the respondent-University that the
issue of starting external/distance learning courses at graduation
level was discussed in a meeting of  the Academic Council convened on
2nd July, 2012 and it was resolved  to authorize the Vice
Chancellor of the University to constitute a Committee to consider
the issue and to submit a detailed report of the same.
</p> 


<p id="p_3">	Consequently, a
Committee was constituted to discuss the  pros and cons of starting
external/distance learning courses at graduate level on 16th
January, 2013 and  a detailed report was forwarded to the Academic
Council of the University. According to the affidavit, in the
meeting, it was unanimously resolved not to start external/distance
learning courses for B.A. and B. Com at present. 
</p>
 


<p id="p_4">	Thus, after
considering the report of the Committee dated 16th
January, 2013, the Academic Council of the University, in its meeting
held on 25th March, 2013 resolved to postpone the issue of
starting of external/distance learning courses at present.
</p> 


<p id="p_5">	In view of the
aforesaid affidavit affirmed on behalf of the University, we find
that there is no scope of passing any direction to start
external/distance learning courses at graduation level when the
appropriate authority under the Statute has decided not to start such
course. Copy of the resolution has been annexed to the  affidavit.
</p> 


<p id="p_6">	We, thus, find
that this application is devoid of any merit and is consequently
dismissed. Notice is discharged. No orders as to costs.
</p>
