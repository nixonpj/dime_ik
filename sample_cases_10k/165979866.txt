                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.20760 of 2015
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -36 Year- 2010 Thana -JAYNAGAR District- MADHUBANI
                 ======================================================
                 1. Md. Ansarul, Son of Md. Dukhi Resident of Village- Jainagar, P.S.
                 Jainagar, District- Madhubani.

                                                                               .... ....    Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Kripa Nand Jha
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Prem Kumar Jha (App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR
                 SRIVASTAVA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   08-10-2015                           Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                 State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                         According to the prosecution case the petitioner

                 was working with the informant and informant handed over to him

                 two lakh Nepali Currency to convert the aforesaid currency into

                 Indian Currency but the petitioner did not return the aforesaid

                 amount to the informant. It would appear from the perusal of the

                 case diary that except oral statement of witnesses there is nothing

                 in the entire case diary to prove the aforesaid accusation.

                 Moreover, the submission on behalf of the petitioner is that

                 petitioner has been implicated in this false case by the informant

                 on account of dispute of wages.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                         Considering         the      aforesaid            facts     and
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.20760 of 2015 (3) dt.08-10-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                    circumstances as well as submissions of the parties , let the above

                    named petitioner be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                    10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of like amount each to

                    the satisfaction of           Chief Judicial Magistrate, Madhubani in

                    Jaynagar P.S. Case no. 36 of 2010.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                                          (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
M.Rahman/-
</p>
