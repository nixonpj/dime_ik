<div class="doc_title">Vikash Kumar @ Ashish Sharma vs The State Of Bihar on 3 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.47418 of 2018
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -371 Year- 2018 Thana -ARA NAWADA District- BHOJPUR
                 ======================================================
                 1. Vikash Kumar @ Ashish Sharma, Son of Asapujan Sharma, Resident of
                 Mohalla- Karman Tola, P.S.- Ara Nawada, District- Bhojpur.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar.

                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr. Bhavesh Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Ram Sumiran Roy
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRAKASH CHANDRA
                 JAISWAL
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   03-08-2018           Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned APP

                 for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                         The petitioner seeks bail in a case registered for the offence

                 punishable under Section 30 (a) of the Bihar Prohibition and

                 <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                         27.75 liters of foreign liquor is said to have been recovered

                 from the possession of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                         It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that no

                 incriminating article has been recovered from the conscious

                 physical possession of the petitioner. He has no concern with the

                 seized liquor. He has been falsely implicated in this case at the

                 instance of his enemy by planting the aforesaid recovery from his

                 possession. There is violation of <a href="/doc/1137396/" id="a_2">Section 100</a> Cr.P.C. Though one
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.47418 of 2018 (2) dt.03-08-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                Page 2 of 2</span>




                      more case under the <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_3">Excise Act</a> has been lodged against the

                      petitioner but he is on bail in the said case. He has been

                      languishing in custody since 03.06.2018.

</p><p id="p_5">                                         In the facts and circumstances of the case, the above

                      named petitioner is directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing

                      bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the

                      like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Special Judge

                      Excise Act, Bhojpur Ara in connection with Ara Nawada P.S.

                      Case No. 371 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                              (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
Kr. Uday/-
</p>
