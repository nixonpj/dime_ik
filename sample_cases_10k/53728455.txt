                             GA No.87 of 2010
                            APOT No.23 of 2010
                                   With
                             AP No.274 of 2009
                     IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                              ORIGINAL SIDE



                                                                In the matter of:
                                                          L &amp; T Finance Limited
                                                                         Versus
                                                             Ravi Kumar &amp; Anr.



Before:
The Hon'ble Justice Banerjee
And
The Hon'ble Justice Kishore Kumar Prasad
Date: 03.02.2010


                                                                   Appearance:
                                                   Mr. Utpol Bose Sr. Adv., Mr.
                                                    Paritosh Sinha Adv., for the
                                                                      applicant
                                               Mr. Pradeep Kumar Adv., for the
                                                                   respondent.</pre>


<p id="p_1">            The Court:- The learned Judge has dismissed the application made

under <a href="/doc/1120409/" id="a_1">Section 9</a> of the Arbitration and <a href="/doc/1306164/" id="a_2">Conciliation Act</a>., 1996.


</p><p id="p_2">      Being aggrieved, the appellant has come up for appeal. This application

would be heard. Mr. Pradip Kumar appears for all the respondents.         Hence

service of notice of appeal upon the respondents is dispensed with. Requisite

number of informal paper book incorporating all papers used before the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           2</span>


learned Single Judge must be filed within three weeks from date. In default,

place it for final order. As soon as paper book is filed, place it for final hearing.


</p><p id="p_3">      The vehicle is lying at Josipur Police Station in the District of

Mayurbhanj, in the State of Orissa.       Let it not be removed from the Police

Station without express leave being obtained from this Court.


</p><p id="p_4">      Since no affidavit is called for, the allegations contained in the stay

application are deemed to have not been admitted.


</p><p id="p_5">      GA No.87 of 2010 is disposed of without any order as to costs.


      Urgent certified copy of this order, if applied for, be supplied to the

parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                    (Banerjee, J.)




                                                    (Kishore Kumar Prasad, J.)


      SP/
