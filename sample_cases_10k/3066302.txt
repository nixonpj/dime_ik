<p id="p_3">                                  Mr. Partha Sarathi Hazra      ...for the Petitioners.




                          Learned Counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioners submits that the

                   entire amount of the cheque / cheques that were purportedly issued by the accused

                   bank statements.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                          Let the petitioners serve a copy of this application upon the

                   opposite party by speed post with acknowledgement due and file an

                   affidavit of service to that effect on the next date of hearing.

</p><p id="p_5">                          Let this matter appear as "Contested Application" two weeks hence.

                          The petitioners shall be at liberty to pray for an interim order of stay after

                   service of notice upon the other side.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                          Urgent Photostat Certified copy of this order, if applied for, be supplied

                   expeditiously after complying with all necessary legal formalities.
