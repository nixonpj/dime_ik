<div class="doc_title">Virendra Singh Solanki vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 27 June, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                    W.P.No.10076/2016
27.06.2016


                     Shri V.K. Shukla, learned counsel for the petitioner.
                     Shri Sudeep Chatterjee, learned Govt. Advocate for the
             respondents/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                     Shri Shukla advanced singular contention.       He submits
             that against the promotion order dated 29.10.2015, petitioner's
             representation Annexure P/7 dated 10.11.2015 is pending
             consideration before respondentNo.1 and 2 and the said
             authority be directed to decide it within reasonable time. He
             further submits that petitioner is going to retire in August,
             2016.
</p><p id="p_2">                     Accordingly, this petition is disposed of by directing the
             petitioner to resubmit the said representation alongwith copy of
             this order to the said respondents.             In turn, the said
             respondents are directed to decide the representation by a
             reasoned order in accordance with law within 60 days
             preferably before 31st August, 2016.        The outcome shall be
             communicated to the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_3">                     The petition is disposed of without expressing any view
             on merits.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                             (Sujoy Paul)
                                                                Judge
  YS/
