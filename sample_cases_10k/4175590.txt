<pre id="pre_1">           HON'BLE SRI JUSTICE ABHINAND KUMAR SHAVILI

W.P.Nos.794, 798, 799, 800, 801, 805, 808, 809, 810, 812, 813, 815, 816,
 818, 822, 823, 824, 825, 827, 828, 829, 830, 831, 832, 833, 834, 835,
     837, 844, 849, 852, 880, 892, 896, 898, 906, 922, 923 of 2019

COMMON ORDER:

      When these matters are taken up for hearing, learned

counsel for the petitioners submitted that the petitioners are

challenging the Circular dated 17.10.2011 in restricting their age to

refer to the Medical Board.

      Learned Standing Counsel appearing for the respondents has</pre>

<p id="p_1">informed this Court that the grievance of the petitioners has been

settled in view of issuance of fresh Circular dated 21.1.2019 and in

terms of the Circular dated 21.1.2019, the case of the petitioners will

be considered for referring to the Medical Board.

</p><p id="p_2">      Learned counsel appearing for the petitioners contends that

in view of issuance of latest circular by the respondents, the

grievance of the petitioners has been resolved. He further contends

that the case of the petitioners be considered in terms of Circular

dated 21.1.2019.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      This Court having considered the submissions made by the

parties is of the considered view that the grievance of the

petitioners has been resolved with the issuance of Circular dated

21.1.2019. It is needless to state that the respondents shall consider

the case of the petitioners in terms of Circular dated 21.1.2019.

</p><p id="p_4">      With these observations, the Writ Petitions are closed. No

order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      Miscellaneous petitions pending, if any, shall stand closed.


</p><p id="p_6">                                        _____________________________
                                         ABHINAND KUMAR SHAVILI, J
Date: 22/01/2019
