<p id="p_1">                                   By filing the present petition under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Code

                     of Criminal Procedure, the petitioner has sought pre-arrest bail in case

                     FIR No.53 dated 28.8.2013, registered under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a>,

                     <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">120-B</a> of the Indian Penal Code at Police Station Joga.

</p><p id="p_2">                                   Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the petitioner

                     is not the beneficiary. An FIR with regard to the same property has

                     already been lodged at Police Station Maur, in which, the petitioner was

                     taken into custody and subsequently, he was admitted to regular bail by

                     the learned Additional Sessions Judge, Bathinda. He further submits that

                     no recovery is to be effected.
</p>

                                  On the other hand, the learned State counsel opposes the

                     prayer of the petitioner. He further submits that the petitioner in

                     connivance with other co-accused forged the Will on fake documents and

                     got mutated the land of Ujagar Singh, who has since died in the year

                     1971, in his name and thereafter, sold the same to one Baldev Singh. One

                     Harnek Singh impersonated himself as Ujagar Singh. He further informs

                     that FIR No.71 is with regard to the forging of power of attorney of one

                     Nicky.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                  There are specific allegations against the petitioner and other

                     co-accused that they in connivance with each other prepared the fake

                     documents and forged the Will in favour of the petitioner. One Harnek

                     Singh impersonated himself as Ujagar Singh. The Will was registered on

                     21.5.2013 and thereafter, Ujagar Singh was shown to be died on

                     23.5.2013. Thereafter, the land in question, which was in the name of

                     deceased Ujagar Singh, who has died in the year 1971, was got

                     transferred in the name of the petitioner on the basis of the forged Will

                     and subsequently, sold to Baldev Singh and others.

</p><p id="p_6">                                  The Hon'ble Supreme Court in State rep. <a href="/doc/48179074/" id="a_7">By the CBI vs.

                     Anil Sharma</a>, reported in (1997) 7 Supreme Court Cases 187 has held

                     as under:-
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                        "The custodial interrogation is qualitatively more

                                        elicitation oriented than questioning a suspect who is

                     petitioner is required. Accordingly, no case is made out to grant pre-

                     arrest bail to the petitioner.
