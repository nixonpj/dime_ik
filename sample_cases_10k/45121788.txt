<p id="p_2">4   26-03-2015                 Heard the parties.
</p><p id="p_3">                               The matter at issue is allotment of LPG distributorship
                 at Jalalpur in the district of Saran by the respondent Bharat
                 Petroleum Corporation Limited.
</p><p id="p_4">                               Learned     counsel     appearing     on    behalf    of   the
                 respondent nos. 1 to 5 submits that LPG distributorship at the
                 location in question was commissioned way back on 25.03.2010
                 and since then it is operational/functional.
</p><p id="p_5">                               In view of the aforesaid statements, learned counsel
                 appearing on behalf of the petitioner seeks permission to withdraw
                 the present writ petition.
</p><p id="p_6">                               Permission is accorded.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court CWJC No.11733 of 2010 (4) dt.26-03-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




<p id="p_7">                                      The writ petition stands dismissed as withdrawn.
</p>
