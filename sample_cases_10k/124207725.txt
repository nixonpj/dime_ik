<p id="p_1">Surendra Mohan, J.

<p id="p_2">          The counsel on both sides submit that the issue in

this case has been settled out of court.      The parties have

agreed that an amount of Rs.22,052/-        remaining in Fixed

Deposit with the Federal Bank on which a lien has been

marked in favour of the Registrar General, High Court of

Kerala be paid to the second respondent herein.       The Fixed

Deposit is in the name of the petitioner. The deposit has been

made pursuant to the interim order dated 9.3.2017 passed by

this Court. Since the parties have agreed that the amount so

remaining in Fixed Deposit could be paid to the second

respondent herein, the petitioner as well as the Registrar

General shall ensure that the said amount is paid to the

second respondent, as agreed between them.            No further
W.P.(C) No. 7883 of 2017.
</p>
<p id="p_3"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 2</span>

orders are required to be passed in this case, according to the

counsel appearing for the respective parties.

</p><p id="p_4">            In view of the above, this writ petition is disposed of

with the above direction.
