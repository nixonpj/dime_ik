                      W.P (C) No. 4307 OF 2011
                     ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
             Dated, this the 10th day of February, 2011

                              JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       The petitioner is stated as aggrieved of the alleged callous

inaction on the part of the concerned respondent in considering Ext. P4

application and granting appropriate relief with regard to the license

sought for setting up an 'explosive magazine' for storing the explosives

in connection with the      operation of the quarry being run by the

petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       2. The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that all the

requisite documents have been furnished by the petitioner, in spite of

which, the respondents are       simply sleeping over the issue, which

made the petitioner to approach this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       3. The learned Government Pleader appearing on behalf of the

respondents submits that the matter requires consideration at different

levels and that the second respondent, who is the competent authority

under the relevant provision of law has to get various reports from

different corners as prescribed, before finalization of the proceedings.

It is also stated that earnest efforts are being taken to have the matter

finalized within the shortest possible time.

W.P. (C) No. 4307 of 2011
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    : 2 :</span>

</p><p id="p_4">      4.     After hearing both the sides, this Court finds it fit and

proper to direct the second respondent to consider Ext. P4 application

submitted by the petitioner and pass appropriate orders in accordance

with law, as expeditiously as possible, at any rate, within two months

from the date of receipt of copy of the judgment. It is ordered

accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      The petitioner shall produce a copy of the judgment along with a

copy of writ petition before the second respondent for further steps.

</p><p id="p_6">      The Writ Petition is disposed of as above.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                               P. R. RAMACHANDRA MENON, JUDGE
