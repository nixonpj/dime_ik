BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 11.10.2018  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.M.SUNDRESH             
AND  
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N.SATHISH KUMAR             

H.C.P(MD)No.1341 of 2018  

Kamatchi                                : Petitioner
                                Vs.
        
1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Ramanathapuram,  
   Ramanathapuram District.

2.The Inspector of Police,
   Kenikarai Police Station,
   Ramanathapuram District.

3.Saranya                                         : Respondents

PRAYER: Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India praying
to issue a Writ of Habeas Corpus, directing the 2nd respondent to produce the
body or person of the petitioner's son namely Saransingh, S/o.Kamatchi, aged
about 22 years, before this Court and set het at liberty.

!For Petitioner           : Mr.R.Kathiresaperumal
^For R1 &amp; R2      : Mr.K.Dinesh Babu
                            Additional Public Prosecutor


:ORDER  
</pre>

<p id="p_1">   [Order of the Court was made by M.M.SUNDRESH, J.]   
        When the matter is taken up for hearing today, there is no
representation on the side of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.The learned Additional Public Prosecutor submitted that it is case of
elopement. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.In view of the aforesaid submission, we do not find any merit in this
petition. Hence, this Habeas Corpus Petition stands dismissed.
</p>
                        
<p id="p_4">To
</p><p id="p_5">1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Ramanathapuram,  
   Ramanathapuram District.
</p>
<p id="p_6">2.The Inspector of Police,
   Kenikarai Police Station,
   Ramanathapuram District.
</p>
<p id="p_7">3.The Additional Public Prosecutor,
   Madurai Bench of Madras High Court,
   Madurai.
