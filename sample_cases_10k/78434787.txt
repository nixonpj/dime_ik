<div class="doc_title">Mahalakshmi vs K.V.Kumaresan on 29 June, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
 DATED : 29.06.2017
CORAM :
THE HONOURABLE MS. JUSTICE V.M.VELUMANI
Tr.C.M.P. Nos.210, 211 and 393 of 2017
and C.M.P.Nos.3878, 3879 and 7787 of 2017


Mahalakshmi     		           ... Petitioner in Tr CMPs.210,211/17
						Respondent in Tr.CMP 393/17 														
Vs.

K.V.Kumaresan			 ... Respondent in Tr CMPs 210,211/17
                                                   Petitioner in Tr CMP 393/17

	 Petitions have been filed under Section 24 of C.P.C., with the following prayers:
	
Prayer in Tr CMP 210/17:-to withdraw G.W.O.P No.7 of 2016, pending on the file of the Principal District Court, Vellore and transfer the same to Family Court, Chennai.
Prayer in Tr CMP 211/17:-to withdraw F.C.O.P No.245 of 2016, pending on the file of the Family Court, Vellore and transfer the same to Family Court, Chennai.
Prayer in Tr CMP 393/17:-to withdraw H.M.O.P No.538 of 2017, pending on the file of the II Additional Family Court, Chennai and transfer the same to Family Court, Vellore.
		For Petitioner   		: Mr.N.R.Chandran, SC
           					  for M/s.Geeta Ramaseshan

		For Respondent		: Mr.S.Navaneetha Krishnan, SC
           					  for Mr.S.G.Ramesh Kumar


O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	Tr.C.M.P.No.210 of 2017 is filed by the petitioner/wife for transferring G.W.O.P No.7 of 2016, filed by the respondent/husband seeking for the custody of minor son, pending on the file of the Principal District Court, Vellore  to Family Court, Chennai.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2. Tr.C.M.P.No.211 of 2017 is also filed by the wife seeking transfer of F.C.O.P No.245 of 2016, filed by the respondent/husband for divorce, pending on the file of the Family Court, Vellore  to the Family Court, Chennai.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.  The respondent/husband filed Tr.C.M.P.No.393 of 2017 to transfer H.M.O.P No.538 of 2017, filed by the petitioner/wife  for restitution of Conjugal Rights, pending on the file of the II Additional Family Court, Chennai  to Family Court, Vellore.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4. The learned Senior Counsel appearing for the petitioner/wife and the learned Senior Counsel appearing for the respondent/husband agreed for transfer all the three matters to one and the same Court at Chengalpattu and on instructions  given by the parties, they pray for transfer of the cases to the Family Court, Chengalpattu. The wife is present before this Court and she has also agreed  for the same.  
</p>
<p id="p_5">	5. Having regard to the submissions made by the learned Senior counsels appearing for the petitioner and respondent to transfer all the three cases, namely G.W.O.P No.7 of 2016, pending on the file of the Principal District Court, Vellore, F.C.O.P No.245 of 2016, pending on the file of the Family Court, Vellore and H.M.O.P No.538 of 2017, pending on the file of the II Additional Family Court, Chennai,  to the Family Court, Chengalpattu, the learned Principal District Judge, Vellore, the learned Judge of the Family Court, Vellore, and II Additional Judge, Family Court, Chennai are directed to transmit all the records pertaining to G.W.O.P No.7 of 2016 and F.C.O.P No.245 of 2016 and HMOP No.538 of 2017 to the 
V.M.VELUMANI,J.
</p><p id="p_6">jv
file of the Family Court, Chengalpattu, within a period of two weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of this order. The learned Judge, Family Court, Chengalpattu is directed to dispose of all the three cases within a period of two months from the date of receipt of the case bundles.
</p>	
<p id="p_7">	4. Accordingly, these Transfer Civil Miscellaneous petitions are ordered. No costs. Consequently, connected Miscellaneous Petitions are closed. 
</p>
<p id="p_8">29.06.2017
Index:Yes/No
speaking order/non speaking order
jv

To

</p><p id="p_9">1. The  Principal District Court, 
    Vellore.
</p>
<p id="p_10">2  The Family Court, 
    Vellore.
