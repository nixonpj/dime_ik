ORAL ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">		Learned
advocate Mr.Patadiya submits that as the applicant is poor widow, she
could not get the certified copy of the bill of costs in time and so
office objections could not be removed by the present applicant in
time and and the appeal was dismissed for non-removal of office
objections. He submits that at present, he has received the copy of
bill of costs and so application for restoration of appeal is filed
along with this delay condonation application. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_2">		Considering
the submissions made in this application, delay caused in filing the
restoration application is condoned. 
</p>
 


 


 


 


 


 


 


<p id="p_3">(M.D.SHAH,
J.)
