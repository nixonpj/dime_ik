<div class="doc_title">Manoj Kumar vs The State Of Jharkhand And Anr on 22 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                        A.B.A. No. 3992 of 2016
     Manoj Kumar                                 ...... Petitioner
                            Versus
     The State of Jharkhand &amp; Anr.          ...... Opposite Parties
                           -----
     CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
                           -----
     For the Petitioner          : Mr. R.K.Mahtha, Advocate
     For the State               : A.P.P
     For the O.P. No.2           : Mr. Santosh Kumar, Advocate
                           -----
     04/Dated: 22/08/2017
           Pursuant to order dated 07.03.2017 both the parties are physically
     present before this Court.
           Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the petitioner
     has complied the order dated 07.03.2017 and deposited the amount
     within the stipulated time as directed by this Court. Learned counsel fro
     the O.P. No.2 has submitted that the aforesaid has been received by the
     O.P. No.2.
           The petitioner is directed to continue the payment of ad interim
     maintenance @ Rs.2,000/- per month to O.P. No.2 from August, 2017
     onwards.
           After some persuasion, both the parties are agreed to resolve their
     dispute through the process of mediation.
           Learned counsel for both the parties have requested that the case
     may be referred to DLSA, Bermo at Tenughat for resolving the disputes
     between the parties through the process of mediation.
           With the consent of the parties, the matter is referred to the
     Secretary-cum-Coordinator, DLSA, Bermo at Tenughat. Both the parties
     are directed to appear before the Secretary-cum-Coordinator, DLSA,
     Bermo at Tenughat, on 06.09.2017 at 11:30 a.m. The Coordinator himself
     or nominate two mediators, who will try to explore the possibility of
     settlement of disputes and will submit a report to this court on or before
     12.10.2017</pre><p id="p_1">.
</p><p id="p_2">           List this case on 12.10.2017. On that date after going through the
     mediation report, final order may be passed.
</p><p id="p_3">           Till then, interim order dated 04.10.2016 shall continue.
           Let a copy of this order be communicated to the court below and the
     Secretary-cum-Coordinator, DLSA, Bermo at Tenughat.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                    ( Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
Raman/Shahid
