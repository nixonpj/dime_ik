                                 Mr. Narayan Chandra Mandal
                                 Ms. Mousumi Chatterjee

                                                                 .. for the petitioner

                                 The petitioner complains of the rejection of an

                    application for amending the plaint.

</p><p id="p_5">                                 It is the admitted position that the evidence

                    had been concluded and the matter was at the argument

                    stage in the court below.

</p><p id="p_6">                                 In such a situation, the merits of the

                    application are not as relevant as the reason for the

                    delay in making the application.

</p><p id="p_7">                                 No    explanation   is    forthcoming    from    the

                    plaintiff in such regard.

</p><p id="p_8">                                 Accordingly,   C.O.      3613   of   2016    stands

                    dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                 There will be no order as to costs.
