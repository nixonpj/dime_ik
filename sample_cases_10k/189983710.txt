<p id="p_1">CORAM: HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE JASWANT SINGH

Present:-   Mr.Amandeep Chhabra,Advocate for the petitioners

Jaswant Singh,J.(Oral)

            Prayer in this petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., is for

seeking protection to life and liberty of both the petitioners, who are

stated to be major in age and have married against the wishes of

father/uncles etc. (respondents no.4 to 13) of petitioner no.1-wife.

They apprehend threat to their life at the hands of said respondents. It is

further stated that the couple is residing at Adarsh Nagar, Gali No.5,

Malout,Distt.Sri Muktsar Sahib,whereas father of wife is residing at

Adarsh Nagar,Street No.1,Malout,Distt.Muktsar.

</p><p id="p_2">            After hearing the learned counsel for the petitioners and

keeping in view the directions issued by the Hon'ble Supreme Court in

the case of <a href="/doc/1364215/" id="a_2">Lata Singh v. State of U.P. And another, JT</a> 2006(6) SC 173,

the present petition is disposed of with a direction to petitioners to

present themselves before SSP, Sri Muktsar Sahib who shall                      take
                                                                      RAJINDER PRASHAD JOSHI
                                                                      2015.12.04 17:01
                                                                      I attest to the accuracy and
                                                                      authenticity of this document
                                                                      High Court, Chandigarh.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> CRM-M 41186/2015                                                         114</span><p id="p_3">

appropriate action, if so warranted in accordance with law.

</p><p id="p_4">           This order shall not be construed to be conferring the

legitimacy or authenticity to the factum of marriage having been

performed as well as the age, as the Court is clearly deprived of any

means to determine the aforesaid facts.
</p>
<p id="p_5">           Petition stands disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_2">


04.12.2015.                                        (Jaswant Singh)
joshi                                                   Judge
