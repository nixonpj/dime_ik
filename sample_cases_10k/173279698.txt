<p id="p_1">14-08-15
Item no.56
   aks


                                        C.R.A. 551 of 2014

             Mr. Anand Keshari ... for the State.


</p><p id="p_2">                   State is represented by Mr. Keshari, learned Advocate.

             None is present to represent the appellant.

</p><p id="p_3">                   This appellant was released on bail on 29.01.2015. I am

             sorry to note the conduct of the accused- appellant, Dinesh

             Kumar.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                   Issue administrative notice to the said accused appellant,

             Dinesh Kumar, Son of Madan Lal, residing at Ahamadganj,

             Police Station - Kotwali, District - Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh, PIN

</p><p id="p_5">             -212 601 through the Superintendent of Police, Fatehpur and

             also through the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Fatehpur, Uttar

             Pradesh.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                   Superintendent, Criminal Section of this court is directed

             to send copy of this order to both the Superintendent of Police

             and the Chief Judicial Magistrate by Speed Post. The accused

             appellant be apprised that if nobody turns up on the date fixed,

             then warrant of arrest will be issued against him and surety

             bonds will be forfeited without any further reference.

</p><p id="p_7">                   This has reference to Sessions Trial No.XIIA(8)2011 of the

             Additional Sessions Judge, Nabadwip, Nadia, West Bengal

             within   Sadar   Sub-Division   at   Krishnagar   arising   out   of

             Nabadwip P.S. Case No. 182/10 dated 18/07/2010.
      The notice is returnable on or before 17th September,

2015. Place this order immediately to the Superintendent for

compliance.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                    (Indrajit Chatterjee, J.)
