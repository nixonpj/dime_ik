                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

              MISC. CIVIL APPLICATION (FOR RESTORATION) NO. 1858 of 2016
                                             In
                       SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 7787 of 2016
         ==========================================================
                     VALIBEN GOIVINDBAI D/O DECEASED MADHABHAI
                                 VIRABHAI....Applicant(s)
                                        Versus
                          STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 4....Opponent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MR KARNA H DHOMSE, ADVOCATE for MR RAMESH N JADAV,
         ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR PP BANAJI, AGP for State Government authorities
         ==========================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.M.CHHAYA
                               Date : 07/07/2016
                                     ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">         1.     Heard Mr. Karna H. Dhomse, learned advocate for 
                Mr. Ramesh N. Jadav for the applicant - original 
                petitioner and Mr. P.P. Banaji, learned AGP for 
                the State Government authorities.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.     Considering   the   issue   involved   in   the   present 
                application   and   with   consent   of   the   learned 
                advocates   appearing   for   the   parties,   this 
                application   is   taken   up   for   final   disposal 
                forthwith. 
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.     It appears from the record that by an order dated 
                6.5.2016, this Court (Coram: C.L. Soni, J.) has 
                granted   time   to   the   applicant   -   original 
                petitioner to remove the office objections latest 
                by 20.5.2016. It appears from the averments made 

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                        Page 1 of 2</span>

HC-NIC                                Page 1 of 2     Created On Sat Jul 09 01:56:02 IST 2016
               C/MCA/1858/2016                                                 ORDER


              in the application that the applicant - original 
              petitioner   could   not   adhere   to   the   said   time 
              limit   granted   by   this   Court   and   therefore,   the 
              main   writ   petition   being   Special   Civil 
              Application No.7787 of 2016 came to be dismissed 
              for non­prosecution. 
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.   Mr. Dhomse undertakes before this Court that if 
              sometime is granted, the office objections shall 
              be removed. 
</p>

<p id="p_5">         5.   Having heard the learned advocates appearing for 
              the parties and considering the averments made in 
              the   application,   the   application   deserves   to   be 
              accepted.   The   main   matter   being   Special   Civil 
              Application   No.7787   of   2016   is   restored   on 
              condition that the applicant-original petitioner 
              shall   remove   all   the   office   objections   on   or 
              before   22.7.2016.   In   case   if   the   applicant-
              original   petitioner   fails   to   remove   the   office 
              objections,   the   main   writ   petition   shall   stand 
              dismissed automatically. 
</p>

<p id="p_6">         6.   Accordingly,   this   application   is   allowed   in   the 
              above terms only. There shall be no order as to 
              costs. 
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                                      (R.M.CHHAYA, J.)
