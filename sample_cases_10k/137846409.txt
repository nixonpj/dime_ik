                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.18156 of 2019
                    Arising Out of PS. Case No.-60 Year-2019 Thana- KUCHAIKOTE District- Gopalganj
                 ======================================================
           1.     TUFANI SAH Son of Suraj Sah Resident of Village- Barai Patti, P.s.-
                  Yadopur, District- Gopalganj.
           2.    Rakesh Kumar Son of Suneshwar Rai Resident of Village- Barai Patti, P.s.-
                 Yadopur, District- Gopalganj.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Sushil Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Upendra Kumar
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE MOHIT KUMAR SHAH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   02-04-2019                   Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                   learned APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner seeks regular bail in connection with

                   Kuchaikote P.S. Case No. 60 of 2019 for the offences

                   punishable under Sections 30(a), 32(2), 38(1), 41(1) of the Bihar

                   Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>, .
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 The allegation is regarding recovery of 49.5 liters of

                   foreign liquor from the motorcycle being driven by the

                   petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

                   petitioner has been falsely implicated in the present case. It is

                   further submitted that the petitioner is having a clean antecedent
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.18156 of 2019(2) dt.02-04-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     and is languishing in custody since 18.02.2019.

</p><p id="p_5">                                  Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the

                     case, the petitioner, above named, is directed to be enlarged on

                     regular bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- with two

                     sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                     Additional District and Sessions Judge-2 cum Special Judge

                     Excise Act, Gopalganj in connection with Kuchaikote P.S. Case

                     No. 60 of 2019.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                             (Mohit Kumar Shah, J)
Tiwary/-
</p>
