<div class="doc_title">Vijay @ Sethi @ Suresh vs State Of Rajasthan on 4 February, 2019</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
              S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail No. 1371/2019

Vijay @ Sethi @ Suresh S/o Shri Mahaveer, Aged About 32
Years, By Caste Jat, R/o Adarsh Nagar, Hanumangarh Town,
Tehsil And District Hanumangarh.
(Presently Lodged In District Jail, Hanumangarh)
</p><p id="p_2">                                                       ----Petitioner
                               Versus
The State Of Rajasthan
</p><p id="p_3">                                                     ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)       :   Mr. Ranjeet Singh
For Respondent(s)       :   Mr. A.S. Rathore, P.P.



</p><p id="p_4">            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

                        Judgment / Order

04/02/2019


     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as learned

Public Prosecutor and also perused the material on record.

</p><p id="p_5">     The petitioner has been arrested in FIR No.5/2019 of Police

Station Talwada, District Hanumangarh for the offence punishable

under <a href="/doc/1555874/" id="a_1">Section 4</a>/<a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_2">25</a> of Arms Act. He has preferred this bail

application under Section 439 Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_6">     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

     Having regard to the totality of the facts and circumstances

of the case, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail to the accused

petitioner under Section 439 Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_7">     Accordingly, this bail application filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">Section 439</a>

Cr.P.C. is allowed and it is directed that petitioner - Vijay @ Sethi
                                                                     (2 of 2)                [CRLMB-1371/2019]


                                   @ Suresh S/o Mahaveer shall be released on bail in connection

                                   with   FIR   No.5/2019     of   Police      Station   Talwada,    District

                                   Hanumangarh provided he executes a personal bond in a sum of

                                   Rs.50,000/- with two sound and solvent sureties of Rs.25,000/-

                                   each to the satisfaction of learned trial court for his appearance

                                   before that court on each and every date of hearing and whenever

                                   called upon to do so till the completion of the trial.




</p><p id="p_8">                                                                                    (VIJAY BISHNOI),J
                                   Abhishek Kumar
                                   S.No.40
