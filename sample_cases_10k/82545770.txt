<p id="p_2">               Pleadings on record have been perused.
</p>
<p id="p_3">               Writ petition is disposed of with a direction to respondent No.3

i.e. SHO, Police Station Thermal, District Bathinda that in case any

representation is filed seeking protection of life and liberty, the contents

thereof be duly verified and if necessary, requisite steps be taken strictly in

accordance with law for grant of protection of life and liberty to the

petitioners. It is clarified that this order shall not be treated as a stamp of this

Court for marriage of the parties.
</p>
