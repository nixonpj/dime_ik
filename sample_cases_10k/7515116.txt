           HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SANDEEP MEHTA
                          Judgment / Order
03/04/2017

     The instant writ review petition has been filed by the State of

Rajasthan seeking review and recalling of the order dated

22.11.2016 whereby the Writ Petition No.8139/2016 preferred by

Shishpal Meghwal non-applicant herein was allowed and the order

of suspension passed against him was quashed and set aside.

</p><p id="p_8">     I have heard and considered the arguments advanced by

learned counsel for the parties and have gone through the order

under review as well as the record of the writ petition.

</p><p id="p_9">     This Court quashed the order of suspension observing that
                                            (2 of 2)
                                                                     [WRW-170/2016]

           no inquiry under Section 38(1) of the Rajasthan Panchayati Raj

           Act had been instituted against the petitioner. It appears that the

           said observation made by this Court was made inadvertently and

           is factually incorrect. There admittedly is available on record, copy

           of the inquiry proceedings initiated against the petitioner under

           Section 38(1) of the Rajasthan Panchayati Raj Act well before

           issuance of the impugned order of suspension. Reference to

           initiation of such inquiry is made in the order dated 6.7.2016

           whereby the writ petitioner was placed under suspension.

</p><p id="p_10">                In this background and having regard to the entirety of facts

           and circumstances as available on record, this Court is of the firm

           opinion that the order under review suffers from a factual error

           apparent on the face of record as the same was passed in

           ignorance of material facts which escaped notice of the Court

           owing to inadvertence.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                Consequently, the review application is allowed. The order

           dated 22.11.2016 is hereby recalled. The writ petition be placed

           before the concerned Bench for consideration.




</p><p id="p_12">                                                         (SANDEEP MEHTA)J.
</p>
