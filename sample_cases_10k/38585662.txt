                                     ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 28-02-2019

       Up-to-date case diary was called for vide order dated, 8.2.2019. But, the case
diary received by this court contains the entry up-to July, 2018 only.
</p><p id="p_2">      This is clearly in violation of the order, dated 8.2.2019 passed by this court. Therefore,
the learned state counsel will take steps to return the case diary to be re-submitted on
5.3.2019.
</p><p id="p_3">      In the event the investigating officer does not furnish the up-to-date case diary on the
date fixed, he shall have to appear personally and explain as to why he has not obeyed the
order of this court.
</p><p id="p_4">      List the matter on 5.3.2019.
</p><p id="p_5">     A copy of this order be furnished to the learned state counsel during the course of the
day.
</p>
