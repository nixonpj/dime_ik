      IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                     AT CHANDIGARH


                                 CRM-M-2129-2019
                                 Date of decision-24.05.2019


Sejal Suman                                   ...Petitioner

                   Vs.

State of Punjab and others                    ...Respondents

CORAM:- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MANOJ BAJAJ

Present:     Petitioner in person with
             Mr. Gaurav Gaur, Advocate for
             Mr. Manoj Kumar Sharma, Advocate.

             Mr. Kirat Singh Sidhu, DAG, Punjab.

             Mr. S.D.Bansal, Advocate for respondent Nos.4 and 5.
             ***

MANOJ BAJAJ, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Petitioner-Sejal Suman has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a>

of Cr.P.C for issuance of directions to official respondents to protect her life

and liberty from the hands of respondent Nos.4 and 5 as she has solemnized

marriage on 24.12.2018 against the wishes of private respondents.

</p><p id="p_2">             Learned counsel appearing on behalf of respondent Nos.4 and 5

i.e. parents of the petitioner has apprised the Court that the petitioner is

residing with the parents and her husband is already in custody in two FIRs

i.e.FIR No.117 dated 26.07.2018 registered under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_2">Sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/1559723/" id="a_3">366-A</a>, <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_4">376</a>

and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120-B</a> of Indian Penal Code and FIR No.177 dated 15.12.2018

registered under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_6">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_7">452</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_8">506</a>, <a href="/doc/886598/" id="a_9">365</a>,<a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_10">148</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_11">120-B</a> read with

<a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_12">Section 149</a> IPC. The petitioner who is present in person in the Court has
