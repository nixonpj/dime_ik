<div class="doc_title">Cwp No.25236 Of 2013 vs State Of Punjab And Others on 18 November, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">            107                IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                                          AT CHANDIGARH

                                                            CWP No.25236 of 2013
                                                  Date of Decision: November 18, 2013
            Kulwinder Singh and another                                     --- Petitioners
                       vs.
            State of Punjab and others                                      --- Respondents</pre>

<p id="p_1">            CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AJAY KUMAR MITTAL
                   HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MEHINDER SINGH SULLAR

            Present:           Mr. Gurcharan Singh, Advocate for the petitioners.


            AJAY KUMAR MITTAL.J(ORAL)

</p><p id="p_2">            1.                 Challenge in this writ petition filed by the petitioners is to the

            Public Notice issued by Jalandhar Improvement Trust, Jalandhar on

            07.03.2013 (Annexure P-4).
</p>
<p id="p_3">            2.                 Learned counsel for the petitioners submitted that the

            petitioners have filed objections (Annexure P-5) to the said Public Notice, in

            which the hearing is fixed for today itself.
</p>
<p id="p_4">            3.                 In view thereof, he seeks permission to withdraw the present

            writ petition with liberty to approach this Court, in case objections are

            decided against the petitioners by the Improvement Trust, Jalandhar.

</p><p id="p_5">            4.                 Dismissed as withdrawn. In case the objections filed by the

            petitioners do not find favour with the Improvement Trust, Jalandhar, it shall

            be open to the petitioners to take recourse to the remedies available to

            them, in accordance with law.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                               (AJAY KUMAR MITTAL)
                                                                     JUDGE



                                                         (MEHINDER SINGH SULLAR)
            November 18, 2013                                     JUDGE
Rani Sarita sarita
2013.11.20 10:24
