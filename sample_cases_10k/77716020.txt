                              JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">1. Petitioners have filed the above writ petition, on the ground that, the

   petitioners were not granted the disbursal of the amounts deposited

   by the Insurance Company, in pursuance to the Award made by the

   Motor Accidents Claims Tribunal, Alappuzha.         Non-disbursement

   was said to be, on account of the fact that, no details were found

   regarding the payment of court fee. This Court had called for a

   report and now it is revealed that, the Registry of the Tribunal has

   been able to trace out the details, regarding deposit of court fee and

   the application also was allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2. Petitioner has prayed for an early disbursal of the amounts. It is

   stated that four interlocutory applications, numbered as I.A.Nos.665,

   666, 667 and 668 of 2015, are pending before the MACT. In such

   circumstance,     the   said   applications   shall  be    considered,

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">   O.P.(MAC)No.191 of 2014            -:2:-</span>




   expeditiously, at any rate, within a period of two months from today.

   The disbursal shall also be made, in accordance with law,

   expeditiously. The enquiry, initiated on the orders of this Court,

   shall be closed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">   Original Petition is disposed off.
</p>
