<div class="doc_title">Banaras Prasad &amp; Ors vs The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors on 11 December, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                CWJC No.2196 of 2008
                                BANARAS PRASAD &amp; ORS
                                       Versus
                              THE STATE OF BIHAR &amp; ORS
                                    -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">7.   11/12/2008           Inspite of repeated adjournments and order

                   dated   10.11.2008   to   file   show   cause,   neither

                   counter affidavit has been filed in compliance to

                   the order dated 15.9.2008 by which order dated

                   31.7.2008 passed in another batch of cases was

                   made applicable in the case        of   the petitioner

                   also, nor show cause has been filed in compliance

                   to the order dated 10.11.2008.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                           In the circumstances, contempt proceeding

                   is initiated against the Vice-Chancellor and the

                   Registrar of the University. The Vice-Chancellor

                   and the Registrar of the University shall show

                   cause as to why they be not punished for contempt

                   on account of disobedience of the directions of

                   this Court issued by order dated 31.7.2008. Show

                   cause should be filed within four weeks.

</p><p id="p_3">                           Let this case be listed on 27th January,

                   2009 on which date the vice-Chancellor and the

                   Registrar of the University shall be personally

                   present in Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                           Let a copy of this order be handed over to

                   learned counsel for the University.

</p><pre id="pre_2">        Pradeep/                              (J. N. Singh, J.)
