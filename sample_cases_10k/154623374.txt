<p id="p_1">		The
facts of the case show that there was a total mess created by the
indiscreet decision on the part of the employee and the Management.
This frustrated the inquiry. Now it is ordered that the inquiry
should continue with the appointment of a fresh Inquiry Committee, if
need be. Substitution, if required will be made in the Committee. The
inquiry will proceed forthwith with utmost expediency. During the
period of inquiry from April, 2008 the employee will be paid
subsistence allowance and would be treated to be under suspension.
Suspension allowance will be paid by the Government for future. Put
up after two months and in the meanwhile all attempts should be made
to conclude the inquiry.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">		Notice
returnable 16.6.2008.
