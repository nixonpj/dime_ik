CWP Nos.24147, 24148, 24606, 24848, 24307, 24309, 24501, 24506,

24655, 24666, 24705, 25102, 25139 of 2016 and CWP Nos.7133 &amp;

9546 of 2017, as the issues involved in all the petitions are common.
