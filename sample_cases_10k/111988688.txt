<p id="p_1">2   20-08-2018                     Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned

                 APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   The petitioners seek bail in a case registered for the

                 offence punishable under Sections 272 and 273/34 of the Indian
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.50981 of 2018 (2) dt.20-08-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                            2/3</span><a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">




             Penal Code</a> and Section 30(a) of the Bihar Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_2">Excise

             Act</a>, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                603 litres of foreign liquor is said to have been

             recovered from the Maruti Van and petitioners and another

             accused managed to escape taking benefit of darkness.

</p><p id="p_4">                                It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioners

             that no incriminating article has been recovered from the

             conscious physical possession of the petitioners. They have no

             concern either with the seized liquor or with the vehicle in

             question or any trade of liquor. They have been falsely remanded

             in the case from Jaynagar P.S. Case No. 201 of 2018 blaming

             them as trafficker of the liquor. They have been enlarged on bail in

             the aforesaid case by learned lower court. They were not

             apprehended on the spot. There is violation of <a href="/doc/1137396/" id="a_3">Section 100</a> Cr.P.C.

             Barring the aforesaid case petitioners have no criminal antecedent.

             They have been languishing in custody since 15.05.2018.

             Similarly situated co-accused, namely, Shambhu Yadav and Kari

             Yadav have been enlarged on bail by this court vide order dated

             03.08.2018 and 07.08.2018 passed in Cr. Misc. No. 47600 of 2018

             and Cr. Misc. No. 48110 of 2018 respectively.

</p><p id="p_5">                               Considering the facts aforesaid, the above named

             petitioners are directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">          Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.50981 of 2018 (2) dt.20-08-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                                     3/3</span>




                      bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) each with two sureties of the

                      like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Additional

                      District &amp; Sessions Judge-Ii cum Special Judge, <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_4">Excise Act</a>,

                      Madhubani in connection with Jainagar P.S. Case No. 197 of

                      2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                              (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
Kr. Uday/-
