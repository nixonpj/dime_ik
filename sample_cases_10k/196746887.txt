<p id="p_6">                           ****

Ramendra Jain, J.(Oral)

             Through this petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of

Criminal Procedure, prayer has been made for quashing F.I.R. No.92 dated

29.10.2014 under <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_2">Sections 326</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_4">324</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_5">148</a> and <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_6">149</a> I.P.C. pertaining to

Police Station Rangar Nangal, District Batala, on the basis of compromise

dated (Annexure-P2).
</p>
<p id="p_7">             On presentation of the petition, parties were directed to appear

before the trial Court on 21.12.2015 to get their statements recorded for

compromise with a direction to the trial Court to furnish a report alongwith

the recorded statements qua veracity of the compromise, if any, between the

parties.
</p>
<p id="p_8">             In compliance of the order dated 04.12.2015 of this Court,

report from learned Judicial Magistrate Ist Class, Batala has been received

vide her letter bearing No.45 dated 27.01.2016 duly forwarded by learned

District and Sessions Judge, Gurdaspur vide letter No.1639/RKT dated

29.01.2016.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                  According to the report of Judicial Magistrate Ist Class, Batala,

the compromise between the parties is with their free consent, without any

pressure and coercion. No P.O. Proceeding is pending against the parties.

</p><p id="p_10">                  In view of the totality of the facts and circumstances and

considering the fact that the compromise will bring harmony in relations

between the parties, the aforesaid F.I.R. No.92, is hereby quashed.
