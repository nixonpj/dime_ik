                                                     ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                           The relief sought for in the present writ petition is for a

                     direction to direct the first and second respondent, not to entertain or

                     recognizing any of the respondents 4 to 12 in the capacity of office

                     bearers on behalf of the petitioner's association namely All India

                     Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes/Buddhists LIC Employees Welfare

                     Association, South Zone in any manner as per the undertaking made

                     by   the   second   respondent       in   their   counter   affidavit   filed   in

                     W.P.No.26953 of 2018.
</p>


<p id="p_2">http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                     3</span>
                                                                          W.P.No.26090 of 2019




</p><p id="p_3">                           2. The learned counsel for the writ petitioner argued the matter

                     for the considerable length of time.     Then, this Court, pointed out

                     that the earlier order of this Court dated 21.02.2019 states that

                     “Without expressing any view on the merits of the matter, this writ

                     petition is disposed of by granting liberty to the parties to agitate the

                     matter before the Competent Civil Court and the writ petition cannot

                     be entertained for the purpose of dissolving the internal disputes

                     arose between the office bearers of the Employees Association”. The

                     writ petitioner has chosen to withdraw the writ petition. He has also

                     made an endorsement to that effect.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                           3. Accordingly, the writ petitioner is permitted to withdraw the

                     writ petition and consequently, the writ petition is dismissed as

                     withdrawn. No costs. Connected miscellaneous petition is closed.
</p>
