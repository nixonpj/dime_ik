<div class="doc_title">Prabhu Yadav @ Prabhunath Yadav vs The State Of Bihar on 12 March, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.15336 of 2019
                      Arising Out of PS. Case No.-2 Year-2019 Thana- PANCHRUKHI District- Siwan
                 ======================================================
                 PRABHU YADAV @ PRABHUNATH YADAV Son of Jang Bahadur Yadav
                 Resident of Village- Mahpur, P.S.- Sarai, O.P., District- Siwan.

                                                                                 ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 The State of Bihar Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr.Ajay Kumar Tiwary
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mr.Ansar Ul Haque
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE MOHIT KUMAR SHAH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   12-03-2019                    Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                   learned APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioner seeks regular bail in connection with

                   Pachrukhi (Sarai) P.S. Case No. 02 of 2019 for the offences

                   punishable under <a href="/doc/471092/" id="a_1">Sections 272</a>, <a href="/doc/285163/" id="a_2">273</a> of the Indian Penal Code

                   and Section 30(a) of the Bihar Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_3">Excise Act</a>, 2016.

</p><p id="p_3">                                The allegation is regarding recovery of 69.10 liters of

                   illicit liquor from the motorcycle of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                                The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

                   neither the motorcycle nor the illicit liquor belongs to the

                   petitioner and he has been falsely implicated in the present case.

                   It is further submitted that though the petitioner is an accused in

                   one other case, but he is on bail in the said case. It is further

                   submitted that the petitioner has been remanded from the said
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.15336 of 2019(2) dt.12-03-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     case on 28.1.2018 and since then he is languishing in custody.

</p><p id="p_5">                                  Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the

                     case, the petitioner, above named, is directed to be enlarged on

                     regular bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- with two

                     sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                     Additional Sessions Judge 2nd cum Special Judge Excise,

                     Siwan in connection with Pachrukhi (Sarai) P.S. Case No. 02 of

                     2019.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                             (Mohit Kumar Shah, J)
Tiwary/-
</p>
