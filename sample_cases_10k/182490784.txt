<p id="p_1">  (PER : HONOURABLE

1.00.		Present
application has been preferred by the applicants herein   original
petitioner  No.1 and
original respondent No.5, respectively, in Special Criminal
Application No.325 of 2013, for modification of the order dtd.
8/3/2013 passed by this Court in  Special Criminal Application No.325
of 2013 in view of the subsequent development. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_2">2.00.		To
ascertain the wish of the  applicant No.1 - corpus  - Darji Roshniben
Sunilkumar and to satisfy ourself whether there is any compulsion
upon the corpus by her parents or not, we have taken the matter in
the chamber  and the corpus  - Darji Roshniben Sunilkumar  has stated
that as per her wish and will, her parents have found out a good
match for her and  she is ready and willing to marry with the boy
selected by her parents without any force or compulsion and at her
wish and will.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_3">3.00.		However,
as  the learned advocate appearing
on behalf of the respondent No.4 herein   original
respondent No.4 is not present,  only for that purpose, S.O. to
15/4/2013. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_4">4.00.		Let
Mr.Buch, learned advocate appearing
on behalf of the applicants herein furnish copy of the present
application to Mr.Kirit Chaudhary, learned  advocate, who earlier
appeared on behalf of the original respondent No.4.
</p>
