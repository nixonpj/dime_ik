<div class="doc_title">Prakash Sahu Alias Prakash Gupta ... vs The State Of Jharkhand on 1 April, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND, RANCHI
                                 A. B. A. No. 4575 of 2013

                 1.Prakash Sahu @ Prakash Gupta
                 2.Anand Sahu        ...    ... ...      Petitioners
                                         Versus
                 The State of Jharkhand ... ...          Opp. Party

                CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PRASHANT KUMAR
                                 ............

                For the Petitioners    : Mr. B.M. Tripathy, Sr. Advocate
                                         Mr. Navin Kumar Jaiswal
                For the State          : Mr. D.K. Chakraverty, A.P.P.
                                       ........

 4/01.04.2014</pre><p id="p_1">               It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that he
                has applied for no objection certificate from Pollution Control Board in
                the year 2008 for running of crusher machine, but his application has
                not been disposed as yet. It is also submitted that school has been
                opened near his crusher machine in the year 2013, whereas,
                petitioner's crusher machine was running at the present place from the
                year 2005. It is also submitted that admittedly on the date of inspection
                crusher machine was not running. Accordingly, he submits that
                question of polluting the air does not arise. In the counter-affidavit
                aforesaid fact has not been replied properly.
</p><p id="p_2">                            Thus, Additional PP is directed to file supplementary
                counter affidavit and properly reply all the aforesaid submissions made
                by learned counsel for the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_3">                            Put up this case after one week, as prayed by learned
                Additional PP.
</p><p id="p_4">                            In the meantime, interim order passed on 05.03.2014 will
                continue.
</p><p id="p_5">                            Let a copy of this order be given to Sri D.K. Chakraverty,
                learned Additional PP.
</p>



<p id="p_6">                                                                  (Prashant Kumar, J.)
Binit/Tarun
