<div class="doc_title">Sita Devi vs The State Of Bihar on 16 July, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.43483 of 2019
                      Arising Out of PS. Case No.-235 Year-2019 Thana- SAHARSA District- Saharsa
                 ======================================================
                 SITA DEVI, W/o Dinesh Paswan, Resident of Village- Aguwanpur, P.S.-
                 Saharsa, District- Saharsa.
                                                                        ... ... Petitioner
                                             Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                 ... ... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Pawan Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Mukesh Kumar Singh
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   16-07-2019                  Petitioner seeks bail in anticipation of her arrest in

                   connection with Saharsa Sadar P.S. Case No. 235 of 2019

                   registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_1">Sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/1559723/" id="a_2">366A</a>

                   and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Written report disclosed the allegation of kidnapping

                   of daughter of informant against one Ajay Paswan and others

                   and it further disclosed that when informant went to the house

                   of Ajay Paswan in search of his daughter, he was abused and

                   threatened by the accused persons, including the petitiioner.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 Submission of learned counsel for the petitioner is

                   that FIR does not disclose allegation of kidnapping against the

                   petitioner, who is a lady and furthermore the victim girl in her

                   statement recorded under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">Section 164</a> Cr.P.C. had stated only

                   this much that she has made some gesture in her kidnapping

                   against the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_4">              Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.43483 of 2019(2) dt.16-07-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                         2/2</span>




                                    Heard learned APP also.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                    In view of above facts and circumstances, let

                       petitioner, in the event of arrest or surrender before the court

                       below within a period of six weeks from the receipt of this

                       order, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.25,000/-

                       (Rupees twenty five thousand) with two sureties of the like

                       amount each to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                       Saharsa, in connection with Saharsa Sadar P. S. Case No. 235 of

                       2019, subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5">Section

                       438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C. and further condition is that one of the

                       bailors of the petitioner shall be a local person having sufficient

                       immoveable property within the jurisdiction of the court

                       concerned.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                               (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
spal/-
</p>
