                          WPC No. 2881 of 2016

     1. Kumari Bai W/o Late Guman Singh Aged About 37 Years Caste-
        Gond, Panch Of Ward No. 10, Gram Panchayat- Kukdur, Tahsil-
        Pandariya, District- Kabirdham, Chhattisgarh, R/o Ward No. 10,
        Gram Panchayat- Kukdur, Tahsi;-Pandariya, District- Kabirdham,
        Chhattisgarh

                                  Versus

     1. State Of Chhattisgarh Through Secretary, Department Of
        Panchayat And Rural Welfare, Mahanadi Bhawan, Mantralaya,
        Naya Raipur, District- Raipur, Chhattisgarh

     2. Director, Panchayat, Indrawati Bhawan, Naya Raipur, District-
        Raipur, Chhattisgarh

     3. Collector, Kabirdham, Chhattisgarh

     4. Janpad Panchayat, Pandariya, Through Its Chief Executive Officer,
        Pandariya, District- Kabirdham, Chhattisgarh

                                                           ---- Respondent</pre>



<p id="p_1">For Petitioner       Shri Malay Shrivastava, Advocate
For Respondent/State Shri R.K. Mishra, Dy. Adv. General


               Hon'ble Shri Justice Prashant Kumar Mishra

                              Order On Board

23/11/2016

</p><p id="p_2">1.     Petitioner would assail the order dated 18-10-2016 and would

       further pray for a direction to the respondent to decide the appeal at

       the earliest.
</p><p id="p_3">   2.    Referring the order sheets of the appeal proceedings recorded by

        the Director (Panchayat) it is brought to the notice of this Court that

        the Director (Panchayat) heard the appellant on 4-10-2016 and

        posted the appeal for orders on 18-10-2016, however, instead of

        passing the order the matter was dismissed for want of prosecution.


</p><p id="p_4">  3.    Bare perusal of the order sheets would reveal that the Director

        (Panchayat) has committed serious error of procedure by

        dismissing the appeal for want of prosecution on a date which was

        fixed for orders and not for hearing of the appeal.


</p><p id="p_5">  4.    In view of the above, the order dated 18-10-2016 deserves to be

        and is hereby quashed at the admission stage itself. The matter is

        remitted back to the Director (Panchayat) for hearing the appeal on

        merits and for deciding the same at the earliest preferably within a

        period of three months from the date of appearance of the petitioner

        before the appellate authority.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">  5.    The petitioner shall appear before the Director (Panchayat) on

        5-12-2016.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">  6.    As an upshot, the writ petition is allowed to the extent indicated

        supra.                                                Sd/-
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">                                                          Judge
                                                  Prashant Kumar Mishra
Gowri
