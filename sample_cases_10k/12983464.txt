<p id="p_1">        This writ petition is filed by the Bank stating that the amount of

Rs.94,500/- as recorded in the judgment was the amount in default as

on 19.2.2019 and that the said amount was liable to be paid with

accrued interest along with the regular installments.

</p><p id="p_2">        2. Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned

counsel appearing for the 1st respondent.

</p><p id="p_3">        In view of the fact that the amount of Rs.94,500/- is said to be

the defaulted amount due in the loan availed by the 1st respondent and

the total amount due is said to be Rs.3,99,976/-, the judgment which is

vitiated by errors of fact is liable to be reviewed. The review petition is

allowed and the judgment in W.P(C).No.3549 of 2019 dated 19.2.2019

is recalled.
</p>
