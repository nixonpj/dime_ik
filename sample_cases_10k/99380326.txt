<div class="doc_title">Dinesh Prasad Verma @ Dinesh Verma vs State Of Jharkhand on 13 February, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">        IN   THE       HIGH   COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                             B. A. No. 5928 of 2011
        Dinesh Prasad Verma @ Dinesh Verma             ..... ...     Petitioner
                                      Versus
        The State of Jharkhand                           ..... ...     Opposite Party
                                   --------
               CORAM       :    HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H. C. MISHRA
                                   ------
        For the Petitioner         :        M/s Mahesh Tewari &amp; S.N.Roy, Advocates
        For the State              :        A.P.P.
                                    ------

4/ 13.02.2012</pre><p id="p_1">          Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned A.P.P. for the
        Prosecution.
</p><p id="p_2">                       The petitioner has been made accused for the offence under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Section
        498A</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/751411/" id="a_2">Sections 3</a> and <a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_3">4</a> of the Dowry Prohibition
        Act, in connection with Complaint Case no. 1246 of 2009 corresponding to T.R.
        No. 1373 of 2009.
</p><p id="p_3">                       Though provisional bail was granted to the petitioner by order dated
        8.11.2011, but it is submitted that the petitioner is still in custody as no
        compromise could be reached between the parties.
</p><p id="p_4">                       In the facts of this case, I am inclined to enlarge the petitioner,
        Dinesh Prasad Verma @ Dinesh Verma, on bail. Accordingly, the petitioner,
        named above, is directed to be released on bail, on furnishing bail bond of Rs.
        10,000/- (ten thousand), with two sureties of the like amount each, to the
        satisfaction of Sri S.N. Sikdar, Judicial Magistrate 1st Class, Giridih, or his
        successor, in connection with Complaint Case no. 1246 of 2009 corresponding to
        T.R. No. 1373 of 2009.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                               ( H. C. Mishra, J.)
R.Kr.
</p>
