<p id="p_1">Prathiba M. Singh, J.
</p><p id="p_2">1.     The present petition has been filed seeking enforcement of the award
dated 3rd September, 2013, passed by a three-member arbitral tribunal by a
2:1 majority.
</p><p id="p_3">2.     O.M.P. 2/2014 is filed by the State Trading Corporation of India Ltd.
(hereinafter, 'STC') under <a href="/doc/1722761/" id="a_1">Section 34</a> of the Arbitration and <a href="/doc/1306164/" id="a_2">Conciliation
Act</a>, 1996 (hereinafter 'the Act') challenging the award dated 3rd September,
2013 passed by a three member Arbitral Tribunal by a 2:1 majority. The
majority award has been upheld with modifications in terms of the

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">OMP (ENF.) (COMM.) 45/2018                                          Page 1 of 2</span>
 judgement passed today in OMP 2/2014. Directions in respect of the
payment to be made are contained in the said judgement.
</p><p id="p_4">3.     The present OMP is disposed of in terms of the judgement passed
today in OMP 2/2014.
