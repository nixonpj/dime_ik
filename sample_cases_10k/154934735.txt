ORAL
ORDER</pre> 

 
	  

<p id="p_1">By
	way of present application, filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of
	Criminal Procedure, 1973, the applicant has prayed to release him on
	regular bail in connection with CR No.I-4 of 2012  registered with
	Surat City ACB Police Station, for the offence punishable under
	<a href="/doc/324254/" id="a_2">Sections 7</a>, <a href="/doc/1281473/" id="a_3">13(1)(D)</a> and <a href="/doc/1259316/" id="a_4">13(2)</a> of Prevention of Corruption Act.
</p>	  

<p id="p_2">Heard
	learned counsel for the applicant and Mr.L.B.Dabhi, learned
	Additional Public Prosecutor for respondent-State.
</p>	  

<p id="p_3">In
	the facts and circumstances of the case and considering the nature
	of offence and the fact that the applicant is suspended from service
	and also the fact that investigation is almost complete and the
	applicant is in custody since 9-3-2012,  without entering into the
	merits of the case, prima facie, this Court is of the opinion that
	this is a fit case to exercise the discretion to enlarge the
	applicant on bail.
</p>	  

<p id="p_4">Hence,
	the applicant is ordered to be released on bail in connection with
	CR No.I-4 of 2012  registered with Surat City ACB Police Station for
	the offence alleged against him in this application on his executing
	a bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand only) with one solvent
	surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial Court and
	subject to the conditions that he shall -
</p>
 

 


 


<p id="p_5">a)	not
take undue advantage of his liberty or	abuse 	his liberty;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_6">b)	not
to try to tamper or pressurise the 	prosecution 	witnesses or
complainant in any 	manner;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_7">c)	maintain
law and order and should cooperate 	the 	Investigating Officer;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_8">d)	not
act in a manner injurious to the interest of	the prosecution;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_9">e)	not
leave the State of Gujarat without prior permission of the Sessions
Judge concerned;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_10">f)
	furnish the address of his residence to the Investigating
Officer	and also to the Court with documentary proof of residence at
the time of execution of the bond and shall not change the residence
without prior permission of this Court;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_11">g)	surrender
his passport, if any, to the lower Court within a week.
</p> 


 


 
	  

<p id="p_12">If
	the breach of any of the above conditions is committed, the
	concerned Court will be free to issue warrant or take appropriate
	action in the matter.
</p>	  

<p id="p_13">Bail
	before the lower Court having jurisdiction to try the case. It would
	be open to the trial Court concerned to give time to furnish the
	solvency certificate if prayed for. Rule is made absolute. Direct
	service is permitted.
</p>	  


<p id="p_14">	At
	the trial, the trial court shall not be influenced by the
	observations of preliminary nature, qua the evidence at this stage,
	made by this Court while enlarging the applicant on bail.
</p>	 


	 

		

 

<p id="p_15">								[M.D.SHAH,J.]
