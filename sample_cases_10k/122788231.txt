<div class="doc_title">Gita Devi vs The State Of Bihar on 4 September, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 CRIMINAL APPEAL (SJ) No.3194 of 2019
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-252 Year-2019 Thana- RAJIVNAGAR District- Patna
                 ======================================================
                 Gita Devi Wife of Sri Suresh Sharma Resident of Chandra Vihar Colony,
                 Nepali Nagar, Ashiyana Digha Road, P.O.- Ashiyana Nagar, P.S.- Rajeev
                 Nagar, District- Patna, Pin Code- 800025 (Bihar).


                                                                                 ... ... Appellant/s
                                                      Versus
                 The State of Bihar.

                                                           ... ... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Appellant/s     :       Mr. Dhanendra Chaubey
                 For the Respondent/s    :       Mr. Sadanand Paswan
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRAKASH CHANDRA
                 JAISWAL
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   04-09-2019                    Heard learned counsel for the appellant and learned

                   Special Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                   This is an appeal under Section 14(A)(2) of the

                   Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of

                   Atrocities) Act, 1989 (hereinafter in short referred to as the

                   'SC/ST Act') against the refusal of prayer of anticipatory bail

                   vide order dated 03.07.2019 passed by learned Additional

                   Sessions Judge-XIII-cum Special Judge SC/ST Act, Patna in

                   connection with Rajeev Nagar P.S. Case No. 252 of 2019

                   (Special Case No.241 of 2019) registered under Sections 341,

                   323, 504 &amp; 506/34 of the Indian Penal Code and Section 3(1) (r)

</p><p id="p_3">                   (s) of the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of
 Patna High Court CR. APP (SJ) No.3194 of 2019(3) dt.04-09-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                             2/3</span>




          Atrocities) Act.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                            On getting information about construction of the

          house on the land of the Bihar State Housing Cooperative

          Federation Limited by the accused persons, when the informant

          along with others arrived at the site and tried to get the work

          stopped, appellant along with two other ladies started slating

          him in the name of caste.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                            It is submitted by learned counsel for the appellant

          that no such occurrence as alleged ever took place. Appellant

          has been falsely implicated in the case merely because she

          happens to be wife of Suresh Sharma against whom the

          informant has lodged false and frivolous case on earlier

          occasions. The appellant does not know the caste name of the

          informant, hence no question of slating the informant in his

          caste name in order to humiliate him arises. Appellant is a lady

          and has no criminal antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                            Learned Spl. PP for the State opposed the prayer

          for bail.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                          In the facts and circumstances of the case, the

          above named appellant, in the event of his arrest or surrender

          before the learned Court below within a period of six weeks

          from today, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.
             Patna High Court CR. APP (SJ) No.3194 of 2019(3) dt.04-09-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                         3/3</span>




                      10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like

                      amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Additional

                      Sessions Judge-XIII-cum Special Judge SC/ST Act, Patna in

                      connection with Rajeev Nagar P.S. Case No. 252 of 2019

                      (Special Case No.241 of 2019), subject to the condition as laid

                      down under Section 438 (2) of the Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                   Accordingly, the impugned order is set aside and this

                      appeal is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">                                          (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
Trivedi/-
</p>
