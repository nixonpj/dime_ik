                 the photocopy of the communication dated 06.03.2017 
                 addressed by the learned Deputy Secretary, Forest &amp; 
                 Environment Department of the State of Gujarat. The 
                 same is taken on record.

                 that the minimum width is prescribed  at 50 microns 
                 keeping   in   mind   the   provisions   of   Rules   4(c)   and 
                 4(d)   of   the   Plastic   Waste   (Management   &amp;   Handling), 
                 Rules,   2011.     It   is   further   provided   in   the   said
