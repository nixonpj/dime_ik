<div class="doc_title">Durgesh vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 13 June, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                           MCRC-9320-2016
                  (DURGESH Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


13-06-2016

Shri Baldev Patel, Advocate for the applicants.
Ms. Surbhi Nigam, Government Advocate for the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Heard finally.
</p><p id="p_2">Perused the case diary.
</p><p id="p_3">This is first bail application filed on behalf of the applicants Durgesh
and Arvind under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_4">The applicant are in custody since 05.05.2016 in connection with Crime
No.97/2016 registered at Police Station Excise Dept. Circle Saunsar,
Chhindwara (M.P.), for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/387648/" id="a_2">Sections 34</a> (1)
(A) and 41 (1) (2) of M.P. Excise Act.
</p><p id="p_5">Learned counsel for the applicant submits that the applicants are
innocent and have been falsely implicated in this case. It is further
submitted that the applicants are earning members of their family and
if they remain in jail for a long time, great hardships would be fall on
them. The applicants are in custody, having no past criminal
antecedents and trial would take considerable time to conclude. In
view of the aforesaid the prayer is made to release the applicants on
bail.
</p><p id="p_6">Learned Government Advocate for the respondent-State vehemently
opposed the application for grant of bail.
</p><p id="p_7">On due consideration of the contentions raised in the application,
nature of allegation against the applicants, and overall facts and
circumstances of the case, I am of the considered view that it is a fit
case to release the applicants on bail, therefore, without expressing
any view on the merits of the case, the application is allowed and it is
directed that applicant No.1 Durgesh and No.2 Arvind shall be
 released on bail on their furnishing a personal bond in a sum of Rs.
20,000/- (Rupees Twenty Thousand only) each with one surety
each in the like amount to the satisfaction of the committal Court/trial
Court for securing their presence before the said Court on all the dates
of hearing fixed in this regard during trial.
Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                                                 (SUBHASH KAKADE)
                                                      JUDGE
