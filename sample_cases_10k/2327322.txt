<div class="doc_title">The State Of Madhya Pradesh vs Radu on 7 April, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                           -1-

                    First Appeal No.219/2016
07/04/2016
      Shri Yogesh Mittal, learned counsel for the appellant / State.
        Shri V. A. Katkani, learned counsel for the respondent.</pre>
<p id="p_1">        I.A.No.1251/2016, is an application for condonation of delay.
        Arguments heard and I.A. is allowed. The delay in filing the
present appeal stands condoned.
</p><p id="p_2">        Also heard on I.A.No.1252/2016 which is an application for
stay.
</p><p id="p_3">        The award dated 12/11/2014 reflects that the respondents is a
tribal with no other source of income. The land of the respondent
has been acquired and he has been granted compensation and the
same has been enhanced after considering various sale deeds by
the Court below to Rs.1,20,000/-.
</p><p id="p_4">        This Court after careful consideration of the impugned order,
does not find any reason to stay the operation of the impugned
award.
</p><p id="p_5">        I.A.No.1252/2016 stands rejected. Learned counsel appearing
for the State is granted eight weeks time to deposit the amount in
question and the respondent while withdrawing the same shall
furnish solvent surety in respect of the compensation amount.
</p><p id="p_6">        The appeal is admitted for final hearing.
        List the same in due course.
</p><p id="p_7">                                                    (S. C. SHARMA)
                                                        JUDGE
Tej
