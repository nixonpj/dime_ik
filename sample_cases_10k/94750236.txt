<div class="doc_title">Rinku Yadav @ Sharad Yadav vs State Of U.P. on 25 September, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Ajit Singh</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 80
 

 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION No. - 36755 of 2019
 

 
Applicant :- Rinku Yadav @ Sharad Yadav
 
Opposite Party :- State Of U.P.
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Mohammad Khalid
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.,Hardev Prajapati
 

 
Hon'ble Ajit Singh,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Heard learned counsel for the applicant and the learned A.G.A.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">This is a bail application on behalf of the applicant Rinku Yadav @ Sharad Yadav in connection with Case Crime No.123 of 2018, under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_2">406</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_3">504</a> I.P.C., P.S. Amapur,, District Kasganj. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">The first information report of the incident was lodged in pursuance of an application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">Section 156(3)</a> Cr.P.C. It was alleged in the first information report that the complainant had taken a loan from Magma HDI General Insurance Company Ltd, Kolkatta for tractor. It is further mentioned that for sometime he had deposited the installments regularly but after that he was not able to deposit the installments and the tractor was taken by Rinku Yadav, the agent of aforesaid finance company and thereafter, the same had been given to another co-accused, Mukesh. 
</p> 
<p id="p_4">The submission of learned counsel for the applicant is that the applicant is quite innocent and has been falsely implicated in the present case with the ulterior motive. He submitted that the tractor which is said to have been taken by the complainant when the installments of the aforesaid loan was not deposited by the applicant and the tractor was taken from the complainant legally. He further submitted that in an auction proceedings, the aforesaid finance company had sold the alleged tractor to one Mukesh and no offence has been committed by the applicant. He submitted that all the offences are triable by the court of Magistrate. He submitted that the applicant is languishing in jail since 28.08.2019 and in case, he is released on bail he will not misuse the liberty of bail and will cooperate in the trial. 
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Learned A.G.A. has opposed the bail plea. 
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Considering the overall facts and circumstances, the nature of allegations, the gravity of offence, the severity of the punishment, the evidence appearing against the accused, but without expressing any opinion on merits, this Court finds it to be a fit case for bail. 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">Accordingly, the bail application stands allowed. 
</p> 
<p id="p_8">Let the applicant Rinku Yadav @ Sharad Yadav involved in the aforesaid crime be released on bail on executing a personal bond and furnishing two sureties each in the like amount to the satisfaction of the court concerned with the following conditions: 
</p> 
<p id="p_9">i) The applicant shall not tamper with the prosecution evidence.
</p> 
<p id="p_10">ii) The applicant shall not threaten or harass the prosecution witnesses;
</p> 
<p id="p_11">iii) The applicant shall appear on the date fixed by the trial court;
</p> 
<p id="p_12">iv) The applicant shall not commit an offence similar to the offence of which the applicant is accused, or suspected of the commission;
</p> 
<p id="p_13">v) The applicant shall not directly or indirectly make any inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted with the facts of the case so as to dissuade such person from disclosing facts to the Court or to any police officer or tamper with the evidence.
</p> 
<p id="p_14">Order Date :- 25.9.2019
 
R./
