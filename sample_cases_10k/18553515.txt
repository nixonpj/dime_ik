<pre id="pre_1">      IN THE COURT OF SURINDER KUMAR SHARMA
      ADDITIONAL  DISTRICT JUDGE, EAST DISTRICT 
            KARKARDOOMA COURTS, DELHI


                         PC No. 05/13

    1. Smt. Pinki
       W/o Late Sh. Lalit Mohan

    2. Master Gaurav Tanwar
       S/o Late Sh. Lalit Mohan
       Through Petitioner No.1 his mother
       (being minor)
       Both petitioners are
       R/o 158, Gagan Vihar Extension
       Gali no. 3, Delhi - 110 051.

                                        ........ Petitioners

                         Vs. 

    1. The State Government of NCT of Delhi
       5, Sham Nath Marg, New Delhi.

    2. The Manager
       United Bank of India
       Gandhi Nagar Branch, Delhi.


                                        ........ Respondents

Date of filing of petition              :  09.04.2013
Date of Arguments                       :  31.03.2014
Date of Judgment                        :  21.04.2014

JUDGMENT
</pre>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">PC No. 05/13                                           Page 1 of 5</span>
<p id="p_1">                The   petitioners   have   filed   the   present   petition 
under <a href="/doc/105862/" id="a_1">section 372</a> of Indian Succession Act 1925 for grant of 
Succession   Certificate   in   respect   of   Bank   Account   No. 
0965010031941/01537221   and   for   issuing   Letter   of 
Administration   in   respect   of   immovable   properties,   which 
belonged to Late Sh. Lalit Mohan. 
</p><p id="p_2">               It is submitted in the petition that petitioner No.1 
is the wife and petitioner No.2 is the son of Late Sh. Lalit 
Mohan,   who   expired   intestate   on   11.03.2011   without 
executing any Will regarding his properties.     Late Sh. Lalit 
Mohan left behind the following Class­I legal heirs:­
</p><p id="p_3">    1. Smt. Pinki                         Wife/petitioner No.1.
</p><p id="p_4">    2. Master Gaurav Tanwar               Son/petitioner No.2.
</p><p id="p_5">               It is stated that the deceased Sh. Lalit Mohan was 
the owner of the properties bearing Nos. 158, 1st floor, Khasra 
No. 29/12, Village Khureji, Gagan Vihar Extension, Gali No.3, 
Delhi   -   110051,   Shop   vide   Unit   No.   26,   G.F,   Vardhman, 
Mayur Vihar, Phase­III, Kondli Gharoli, Delhi and Shop No. 
G­26, Ground floor, Vadhman Appensell Plaza, C.S.C. Mayur 
Vihar, Phase­III, Kondli Gharoli, Delhi. 
</p><p id="p_6">               Notice   of   the   petition   was   issued.     Valuation 
Report of the properties was also called from the concerned 
SDM.     Publication   was   also   made   in   newspaper   "Maha 
Medha". 
</p>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">PC No. 05/13                                                   Page 2 of 5</span>
<p id="p_7">                Thereafter in support of their case, the petitioners 
examined two witnesses.
</p><p id="p_8">               PW1   is   Sh.   Praveen   Sharma,   Manager,   United 
Bank   of   India,   Gandhi   Nagar,   Delhi.     He   proved     the 
Statement   of   Account   in   respect   of   saving   bank   A/C   No. 
0965010031941 of Sh. Lalit Mohan.   The bank statement is 
Ex. PW1/A. 
</p><p id="p_9">               Ms.   Pinki,   Petitioner   No.1   examined   herself   as 
PW­2 and filed her affidavit Ex. PW2/A supporting her case. 
She   also   proved   documents   Ex.   PW2/A   to   PW2/K.     The 
petitioner also filed her additional affidavit.
</p><p id="p_10">               I have heard the Ld. Counsel for the petitioners 
and I have also perused the file.
</p><p id="p_11">               As per the case of the petitioners,   deceased Sh. 
Lalit   Mohan   acquired   property   i.e.   Flat   bearing   No.   158, 
Gagan Vihar Extn., Gali no.3, Delhi - 51.   The sale deed of 
the same is Ex. PW2/B.  The copy of Voter Card of PW2 is Ex. 
PW2/C.    Copy of  Ration Card is Ex. PW2/D.   It is further 
stated that deceased  Sh. Lalit Mohan also acquired one shop 
bearing No. G­26, Ground floor,  Vardhman, Mayur Market, 
CSC   Mayur   Vihar,   Phase­III,   Gharoli,   Kondli,   Delhi. 
Possession Letter of the same is Ex. PW2/E.  One other shop 
was also acquired by the deceased Sh. Lalit Mohan at G­26, 
Ground flloor, Vardhman Appenzal Plaza CSC, Mayur Vihar 
III, Kondli, Gharoli, Delhi.   Possession Letter of the same is 

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">PC No. 05/13                                                   Page 3 of 5</span>
 Ex. PW2/F.  Deceased Sh. Lalit Mohan was also having bank 
A/c No. 0965010031941 with United Bank of Inida, Gandhi 
Nagar   Branch   and   Bank   passbook   is   Ex.   PW2/G.     The 
Valuation report of Flat bearing No. 158, Gagan Vihar Extn., 
is Ex. PW2/I, Valuation Report of Shop No. G­26, Vardhman 
Appenzal Plaza, Kondli Gharoli, Mayur Vihar Ph­III, Delhi is 
Ex. PW2/J and Valuation report of another shop No. G­26, 
Vardhman Kondli Gharoli, Mayur Vihar Ph­III is Ex. PW2/K. 
In her additional affidavit, the petitioner Pinki stated that her 
mother in law Smt. Gango Devi expired on 09.08.1958 and 
her death certificate is Ex. P­1.  She also stated that the shop 
bearing No. G­26, Ground floor, Vardhman Appenzell Plaza. 
CSC, Mayur Vihar Ph­III, Kondli Gharoli, Delhi, was originally 
alloted to Sh. Harshvardhan Kapoor, Sh. S.K. Kapoor and Sh. 
Anil Kapoor and the said shop was purchased by deceased 
Sh. Lalit Mohan vide agreement Ex. P­2.  
</p><p id="p_12">               There is no impediment in granting the relief as 
prayed  by   the   petitioners.   The   petitioners  have   proved  the 
relevant documents. The objections from the public at large 
have not been received despite the publication made in the 
newspaper.     Therefore,   Succession   Certificate   in   respect   of 
the Bank Account of deceased Late Sh. Lalit Mohan in the 
United   Bank   of   India   and   Letters   of   Administrations   in 
respect of properties bearing Nos. 158, 1st  floor, Khasra No. 
29/12,   Village   Khureji,   Gagan   Vihar   Extension,   Gali   No.3, 

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">PC No. 05/13                                                  Page 4 of 5</span>
 Delhi   -   110051,   Shop   vide   Unit   no.   26,   G.F.   Vardhman, 
Mayur Vihar, Phase­III, Kondli, Gharoli, Delhi and Shop No. 
G­26, Ground floor, Vadhman Appensell Plaza, C.S.C. Mayur 
Vihar,   Phase­III,   Kondli   Gharoli,   Delhi   are   issued   on 
furnishing the requisite Court fee by the petitioners.  
</p><p id="p_13">               The   petitioners   shall   furnish   Administration/ 
surety bond.
</p><p id="p_14">               It is clarified that the question of right, title and 
ownership of the properties referred above is not decided by 
the grant of this Letters of Administration.  
</p><p id="p_15">               File be consigned to Record Room.
</p>

<p id="p_16">Announced in Open Court
on 21.04.2014
                                      (Surinder Kumar Sharma)
                                      Addl. District Judge, East
                                      Karkardooma Courts, Delhi.
</p>
