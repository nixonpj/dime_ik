<p id="p_7">Suit for recovery of Rs. 1,64,659/-
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">Date of institution:                            24.09.2007.
Date on which reserved for judgment:            26.03.2008.
Date of decision:                               26.03.2008.



Judgment (u/o 37 CPC).


1            The present suit for recovery of Rs. 1,64,659/- has been
filed   by   plaintiff   bank   against   the   defendants     through    its
</pre><p id="p_8">constituted attorney Sh. S.C. Budhwar.
</p>

<p id="p_9">2.           The case of the plaintiff is that defendants approached
the plaintiff bank for grant of personal loan of Rs. 1,35,000/-. On the
request of defendant no. 1, the plaintiff bank granted a personal
loan of Rs. 1,35,000/- to defendant no. 1 vide loan account no.
1124.    Defendant no. 2 stood as a guarantor/co-obligant for
defendant no. 1.         In consideration of the loan, the defendants
executed original loan application dated 14.09.2005, original take
delivery letter to DPN dated 15.09.2005, original pronote dated
15.09.2005 and several other usual documents.                 The loan was
agreed to be repaid in 36 equal monthly installments of Rs. 4500/-.
Thereafter, the defendants did not adhere to the financial discipline
of the loan.      Despite repeated requests, defendant no. 1 and 2
failed to repay the loan.       Finally, the plaintiff sent a legal notice
dated 25.06.2007 to the defendants          asking them       to clear their
dues.    Neither the defendants no. 1 and 2 paid the amount nor
gave any reply to the notice.       Hence, the present suit.
</p>

<p id="p_10">3.           The defendants were served with summons u/o 37 CPC
but they did not file any appearance within stipulated time as
mandated under order 37 CPC.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                    3</span>

<p id="p_11">4         The suit is based upon written agreement.            Same is
covered u/o 37 CPC.     Suit is within limitation.   As no appearance
has been filed by the defendant within stipulated time as per
provision u/o 37 CPC, the contents of the plaint are deemed to
have been admitted by the defendant. Plaintiff has accordingly
become entitled to a decree. Suit of the plaintiff is accordingly
decreed for an amount of Rs. 1,64,659/- along with interest @ 6% per
annum from the date of filing of the suit till the date of decree.
Costs of the suit are also awarded in favour of plaintiff.     Decree
sheet be prepared accordingly.         File be consigned to Record
Room.
</p>

<p id="p_12">Announced in the open                         (Gaurav Rao)
court on 26.03.2008.                          Civil Judge/Delhi.
</p>
