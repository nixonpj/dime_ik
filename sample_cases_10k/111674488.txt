                                                   Heard Shri Narnaware, learned Counsel
                                       appearing for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                                   We have gone through the impugned order
                                       passed by the Scrutiny Committee invalidating the caste
                                       claim of the petitioner for "Halba" (Scheduled Tribe).
                                       On pages 167 and 168 of the paper book, which are
                                       parts of the impugned order, we find ten entries of
                                       "Koshties" said to be in the names of blood relatives of
                                       the petitioner.     The petitioner          has denied his
                                       relationship with these persons. Reference is also made
                                       to the caste validity certificates issued in the name of
                                       Ramchandra Narendra Deoghare and Deepak Vithal
                                       Deoghare, who are real uncles of the petitioner. The
                                       Scrutiny   Committee    has     held   that    caste     validity
                                       certificates were issued prior to constitution of Police
                                       Vigilance Cell in the year 1996.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                   The petitioner is undergoing B.D.S. course
                                       and has to appear in the final year B.D.S. Summer
                                       Examination of 2019.
</p>
