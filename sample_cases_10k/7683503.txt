           IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

    CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 3810 of
                                    2015
              In CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. 1301 of 2014
===========================================================
=====
            SABIRMIYA RASULMIYA MALEK....Applicant(s)
                            Versus
             STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
THROUGH JAIL for the Applicant(s)
MR. HARDIK SONI, APP,for the Respondent(s)
================================================================
          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE Z.K.SAIYED

                              Date : 25/02/2015
                               ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">       Rule.  Learned   APP   waives   service   of   notice   of 
rule on behalf of the respondent­State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2      The present application  has  been  filed by  the 
applicant­prisoner   seeking   temporary   bail   for   a 
period of 30 days on the ground of agriculture.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3      Heard   Mr.Hardik   Soni,   learned   Additional   Public 
Prosecutor for the respondent­State.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4      I   have   gone   through   the   jail   record,   which 
indicates   that  in  the   month   of   September,  2014,  the 
applicant was released on temporary bail for a period 
of   28   days   from   23.09.2014   to   20.10.2014   he   had 
surrendered   before   the   jail   authority   in   time.   The 
conduct of the prisoner in the jail is also reported 


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  Page 1 of 2</span>
               R/CR.MA/3810/2015                     ORDER



to be good. He has already undergone sentence of  year 
3 months and 15 days. 
</p>
<p id="p_5">5           In view of the above and keeping in mind the jail 
record   of   the   prisoner,   the   applicant,   prisoner,   is 
ordered   to   be   released   on   temporary   bail   for   the 
period   of  15   (fifteen)   days,   on   usual   terms   and 
conditions from the date of his actual release, upon 
on the prisoner executing a personal bond in the sum 
of Rs.10,000/­ (Rupees Ten thousand only) before the 
jail   authority.   He   shall   surrender   before   the   jail 
authority   immediately   after   the   aforesaid   period   is 
over.
</p>

<p id="p_6">            Rule is made absolute.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                 (Z.K.SAIYED, J.)


siddharth
