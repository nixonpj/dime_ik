<div class="doc_title">D.Vairavamoorthy vs The Superintendent Of Police on 27 April, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 27.04.2017  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.SELVAM           
and 
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N.AUTHINATHAN            

W.P(MD)No.8047 of 2017   

D.Vairavamoorthy                                ..  Petitioner

Vs.

1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Sivagangai District, Sivagangai.

2.The Inspector of Police,
   Mathagupatti Police Station,
   Sivagangai District.                                 ..  Respondents

PRAYER: Writ Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India
praying for the issuance of a Writ of Mandamus, directing the respondents to
grant permission to the organizers of the festival to Arulmigu Sri Bharatha
Vinayagar Temple at Paganeri Village, Kalayarkoil Taluk, Sivagangai District
on 05.05.2017 for conducting Dance and Entertainment programs as possible 
under law.


!For Petitioner                 : Mr.V.Kannan 

For Respondents         : Mr.K.Guru,        
                                  Additional Government Pleader.
        

:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">[Order of the Court was made by A.SELVAM, J.]  
        This Writ Petition has been filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_2">Article 226</a> of the Constitution
of India praying to direct the respondents to give necessary permission for
conducting the programmes mentioned therein, by way of issuing a Writ of
Mandamus.  
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.Mr.K.Guru, learned Additional Government Pleader, has taken notice
for the respondents.    
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.It is seen from the records that on 20.04.2017 a representation has
been given, but the same has not been considered.  Under the said
circumstances, this Court is inclined to pass the following order.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4.In fine, this Writ Petition is allowed in part without costs and the
second respondent is directed to look into the representation dated
20.04.2017 and dispose of the same within a period of two days.
</p>

<p id="p_5">To

</p><p id="p_6">1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Sivagangai District,  Sivagangai.
</p>
<p id="p_7">2.The Inspector of Police,
   Mathagupatti Police Station,
   Sivagangai District..
