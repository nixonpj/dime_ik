                                                      Crl. Misc. No. M-34302 of 2014
                                                         Date of decision:31.03.2015

            Gurdeep Singh                                .... Petitioner

                                                   Versus

            State of Punjab and another                   .... Respondents

            CORAM: HON'BLE MS. JUSTICE NAVITA SINGH

            Present:            Mr. J.S. Bhatti, Advocate for the petitioner.

                                Mr. K.S. Pannu, DAG, Punjab.
                                            *****

            Navita Singh, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                As per report from the Office, service is complete.

                                State Counsel submits that according to his information, the

            petitioner appeared on all dates but was absent on one date on which bail

            was granted.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Counsel for the petitioner submits that after passing of the

            order dated 23.12.2014, the petitioner appeared before the Appellate Court

            and was released on interim bail.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Since the petitioner has appeared and has been granted bail,

            though interim, it is made clear that he shall keep appearing regularly before

            the Appellate Court in all normal circumstances and the interim bail granted

            to him shall continue. However, if he misuses the concession, the Court

            shall proceed as per law.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                Petition is disposed of accordingly.
</p>
