                 CHANDIGARH


           CRL WRIT PETITION NO.1203 OF 2012(O&amp;M)
                   DECIDED ON : 31.07.2012



Court on its own motion
                                                 ...Petitioner
                versus

State of Punjab
                                                 ...Respondent



CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE K. C. PURI



Present : Mr. S. S. Chandumajra, DAG, Punjab.



K. C. PURI, J. (ORAL)
</pre>

<p id="p_1">           Telegram regarding illegal custody of Salim Gill was

sent by the complainant to Hon'ble the Acting Chief Justice, which

was taken on judicial side.
</p>
<p id="p_2">           Reply has been filed by respondent-State wherein it is

mentioned that Salim Gill is not in illegal custody. He was

summoned on complaint moved by Narinder Kaur. Now the

matter has been compromised between them.

</p><p id="p_3">           In these circumstances, the present petition stands

dismissed as having been rendered infructuous.



</p><pre id="pre_2">JULY 31, 2012                              (K. C. PURI)
shalini                                       JUDGE
