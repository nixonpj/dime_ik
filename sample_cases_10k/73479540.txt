<div class="doc_title">Sunil Thakur vs The State Of Bihar on 17 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.26383 of 2017
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -144 Year- 2016 Thana -BABUBARHI District- M ADHUBANI
                 ======================================================
                 1. Sunil Thakur Son of Late Suresh Thakur, Resident of Village- Sitapatti,
                 P.S.- Phulpras, District- Madhubani.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                             .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Gagan Deo Yadav, Ad vocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Sri Anand Mohan Prasad Mehta, A.P.P.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SHIVAJI PANDEY
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   17-08-2017                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               In this case, the petitioner is seeking anticipatory bail

                 in connection with Babubarhi P.S. Case No. 144 of 2016,

                 registered for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1293807/" id="a_1">Sections 461</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_2">379</a>, <a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_3">411</a>

                 and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_4">120B</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               As per the allegation, Police has intercepted a Tempo

                 from there an old Parvati statue was recovered. The petitioner

                 along with others was sitting in the Tempo.

</p><p id="p_4">                               As there is direct allegation against the petitioner, this

                 Court is not inclined to grant bail to the petitioner. Accordingly,

                 the prayer for anticipatory bail is rejected. If the petitioner

                 surrenders before the Court below within four weeks from today,
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.26383 of 2017 (3) dt.17-08-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                      the court below will pass the order without being prejudiced to the

                      order passed by this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                      (Shivaji Pandey, J)
Vats/-
</p>
