                                                                         ...Petitioners
                                  Versus

                                                                     ...Respondents

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH

Present:     Mr. Yesh Paal Malik, Advocate
             for the petitioners.

<p id="p_1">             In compliance with the order dated 29.03.2016, respondent
No.2-Professor     Kavita     Chakravarthy,        Registrar,   B.P.S.    University,
Khanpur Kalan, is present in person in Court.
</p><p id="p_2">             Counsel for respondent No.1 has placed on record copy of order
dated 25.07.2016 passed by the Supreme Court in Special Leave to Appeal
(C) No.18246 of 2016 titled as State of Haryana Versus Dr. B.C. Arya and
others, according to which after issuance of notice, the operation of the
judgment has been stayed as also the contempt proceedings.
</p><p id="p_3">             In view of the above referred to order dated 25.07.2016 passed
by the Supreme Court, the present contempt petition does not survive at this
stage. The same is disposed of as such.
</p><p id="p_4">             Liberty is, however, granted to the petitioner to revive the same
in case the stay as granted by the Supreme Court is vacated or any other
cause of action arise.
</p><p id="p_5">             Rule discharged.</p><pre id="pre_2">
July 26, 2016                                (AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH)
Puneet                                               JUDGE
