                               JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">     The limited prayer of the petitioners is for a direction to the

1st respondent, before whom they have preferred Ext.P6 complaint

alleging certain illegal constructions in the Apartment complex

wherein they are residing, to consider and pass orders on the

same, expeditiously, after hearing the petitioners and other persons

who are likely to be affected by any decision to be taken by the 1 st

respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

</p><p id="p_3">     2.    I have heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and

learned Standing Counsel for the respondents.



</p><p id="p_4">     On a consideration of the facts and circumstances of the case,

and submissions made across the Bar, I dispose the writ petition

with a direction to the 1st respondent to consider and pass orders

on Ext.P6 complaint, within a period of six weeks from the date of

receipt of copy of this judgment, after hearing the petitioners and

others likely to be affected by any orders to be passed by the 1 st

respondent. The petitioners shall produce a copy of this judgment
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  -2-</span>
W.P.(C) No.11566 of 2018



along with a copy of the writ petition before the 1 st respondent

for further action.
</p>
<p id="p_5">        This writ petition is disposed as above.
