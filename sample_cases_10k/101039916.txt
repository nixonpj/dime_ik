                                  ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">                                 (12-05-2016)


      Keeping in view the judgment rendered by a Coordinate Bench
of this court in W.P.No.10946/2015 and W.P.No.19397/2015
(Annexure P-1) on 14-01-2016 and the directions issued therein,
which clearly shows that the University cannot refuse consideration
or request of the institute for affiliation only on the ground that the
NOC from the State Government is not produced. It has been held by
a Coordinate Bench of this court in the aforesaid cases that once the
National Council for Teachers Education has granted recognition in
accordance to the        requirement of the statute, the University is
required to consider the application for affiliation and process it in
accordance with law and take a decision thereof without insisting
upon the institute submitting any NOC from the State Government.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.    Keeping in view the aforesaid legal principles laid down by
the Coordinate Bench of this court and the time limit fixed by Hon'ble
the Supreme Court          in the matter of grant           of recognition and
affiliation to such institute, recognition by the NCTE upto 02-05-
2016 and consideration of the case for affiliation by the University
upto 30-05-2016, we dispose of these petitions in the following
directions:
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">      "In case the petitioners herein have been granted recognition
      by the NCTE and they               submit any applications            to the
      University for grant of             affiliation in accordance to the
      requirement of law on or before 18-05-2016, the University
      shall process the applications for affiliation in accordance to
      the provisions as applicable to the University, process it and
      take a decision well before starting of next academic session
        or cut off date fixed by the Supreme Court, as the case may be
       without insisting upon the institute submitting NOC from the
       State Government. Action be taken and decision be
       communicated to the petitioners as directed hereinabove.
</blockquote>
<p id="p_3">3.     With the aforesaid these petitions stands disposed of.
</p>
