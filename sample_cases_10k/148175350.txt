<p id="p_1">     By this petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_2">Section 482</a> of Cr.P.C.,
petitioners are praying for issuance of direction to police to
conduct the investigation in a fair and impartial manner.
</p><p id="p_2">     As under the Law, it is the duty of the investigating
agency to conduct the investigation in a fair and impartial
manner, therefore, no further direction in this regard is
required. This petition is disposed off accordingly.
