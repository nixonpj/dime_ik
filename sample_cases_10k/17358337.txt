<p id="p_1">                                    Instant writ petition has been filed under Articles 226/227 of

                   the Constitution of India for issuance of a writ in the nature of mandamus

                   directing the respondents to release the pensionary benefits of the mother

                   of the petitioners including pension from 01.12.2014 till 09.01.2015, who

                   retired from the service of respondent no.4 on 30.11.2014.

</p><p id="p_2">                                    The mother of the petitioners, namely, Smt. Bimla, was a

                   Safai Sewak in the Municipal Council, Dhariwal. She joined the abovesaid

                   Class IV post on 23.09.1981 and retired on 30.11.2014. The mother of the

                   petitioners has expired on 09.01.2015. The pensionary benefits of the

                   mother of the petitioners have not been paid. The petitioners have

                   approached this Court after representing the respondents to which no

<p id="p_3">                                Having heard learned counsel for the petitioners and after

                   perusing the paper-book, however, without giving notice to the

                   respondents, instant writ petition is disposed of with a direction to the

                   Executive Officer of the Municipal Council, Dhariwal to get all the

                   pension papers of the mother of the petitioners completed, if so required

                   after visiting the petitioners within seven days and release the due amount

                   payable by the Municipal Council, Dhariwal within seven days thereafter.

                   The pension papers complete in all respects shall be handed over to the

                   concerned office through a special messenger the very next day of

                   completion of those papers and respondents shall release the due amount

                   of pension along with arrears within three weeks from the date of

                   submission of the papers. In case, the petitioners are not paid the due

                   amount before 08.04.2015, there shall be cost of Rs.200/- per day on the

                   Executive Officer of the Municipal Council, Dhariwal which shall be

                   personally recoverable from him and in that event the petitioners shall also

                   become entitled for interest 9% per annum.
</p>
