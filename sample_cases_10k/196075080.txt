           IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA
                     GULBARGA BENCH

         DATED THIS THE 1st DAY OF APRIL, 2014

                             BEFORE

       THE HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE RATHNAKALA

              CRL.PETITION No.200137/2014

BETWEEN

ASHOK S/O BASAVARAJ AKAMANCHI,
AGE: 22 YEARS, OCC: STUDENT,
R/O: SHANTI NAGAR, GULBARGA.
                                             ...PETITIONER

(BY SRI MAHANTESH DESAI &amp; SMT ANURADHA.M.DESAI
  AND SRI PANCHAYYA.C.SWAMY, ADVS.)


AND:

THE STATE OF KARNATAKA
THROUGH ASHOK NAGAR P.S.,
REPRESENTED BY ADDL.SPP,
HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA,
GULBARGA BENCH.
                                           ...RESPONDENT

(BY SRI S.S.ASPALLI, HCGP)

     THIS CRL.P. IS FILED UNDER SECTION 439 OF CR.P.C.
PRAYING TO RELEASE THE PETITIONER ON BAIL IN CRIME
NO.124/2013 OF ASHOK NAGAR POLICE STATION, GULBARGA,
WHICH IS REGISTERED FOR THE OFFENCES P/U/S. 364-A OF <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>.


    THIS PETITION COMING ON FOR ORDERS, THIS DAY, THE
COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING:
                                  2




                           ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     This petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_2">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C.

seeking bail in Crime No.124/2013 of Ashok Nagar Police

Station Gulbarga, registered for the offences punishable

under <a href="/doc/872363/" id="a_3">Sections 143</a>, <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_4">364-A</a>, <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_5">506</a> r/w <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_6">Section 149</a> of IPC.


</p><p id="p_2">     2.       The co-accused Nos.5 and 6 have been enlarged

on bail by this Court in Criminal Petition No.200199/2014

and connected case. This petitioner also stands in the same

footing with that of 5th and 6th accused who were enlarged

on bail. Under the circumstance there is no impediment to

enlarge the petitioner on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">     3.       Accordingly, petition is allowed. The petitioner is

enlarged on bail in Crime No. 124/2013 of Ashok Nagar

police    station,   Gulbarga,       subject   to   the   following

conditions:
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                3</span><p id="p_5">



</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">      (i)     He shall execute a self bond for a sum of
              Rs.50,000/- each with one surety for the like
              sum:
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">      (ii)    He shall appear before the Investigating Officer
              whenever called for during further course of
              investigation.
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_3">      (iii)   He shall not prevail upon or threaten the
              prosecution witnesses.
</blockquote>


<blockquote id="blockquote_4">                                           Sd/-</blockquote><p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                          JUDGE

msr
