THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE S.VAIDYANATHAN

Crl.O.P.(MD)NO.21695 of 2014


A.Mani							...   Petitioner		
				
Versus

1.State Rep.by its
  The Superintendent of Police,
  Tirunelveli District,
  Tirunelveli.

2.State Rep.by its
  The Inspector of Police,
  Karivalamvanthanallur Police Station,
  Tirunelveli District.				...   Respondents

PRAYER

Criminal Original Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of
Criminal Procedure praying to lay final report in Crime No.159 of 2013 dated
07.08.2013 on the file of the second respondent police within a stipulated
time fixed by this Court.



!For Petitioner	:Mr.M.Sathiamoorthy

For Respondents	:Mr.A.P.Balasubramani				
				Govt Advocate (Crl. Side)
:ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	This petition is filed seeking for a prayer to lay final report in
Crime No.159 of 2013 dated 07.08.2013 on the file of the second respondent
police within a stipulated time fixed by this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2.The learned Counsel for the petitioner would submit that even though
the case has been registered in Crime No.159 of 2013 on 07.08.2013, the
second respondent police has not taken any steps for investigating the matter
and filing the charge sheet.  Hence, he come forward with the present
petition for direction.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.The learned Government Advocate (Crl. Side) sought two months time
for filing final report.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4.In view of the above submission made by the learned Government
Advocate (Crl.Side), I am inclined to direct the second respondent to
investigate the matter as expeditiously as possible and to file the charge
sheet within a period of four months from the date of receipt of a copy of
this order.
</p><p id="p_5">	5.With the above direction, this Criminal Original Petition is disposed
of.
</p><p id="p_6">02.12.2014
