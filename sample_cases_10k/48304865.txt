                                                    ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">                               The present Criminal Original Petition has been filed to call for

                      the entire records pertaining to the Crime No.106 of 2014 pending before

                      the respondent and to quash the same.



</p><p id="p_2">                               2. From the records, it is seen that when the matter was taken

                      up “for admission” on 14.07.2014, the learned counsel appearing for the

                      petitioners took an adjournment and the matter has been adjourned to

                      21.07.2014 and thereafter, at his request, the matter has been adjourned

                      to 04.08.2014 and 01.09.2014. Further, for the past five years, the

                      learned counsel has not taking any steps to list the matter. It shows that

                      the petitioners are not interested to pursue the matter.




http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                             3</span>




</p><p id="p_3">                                3. In view of the above, this petition cannot be considered now

                      and   accordingly,   this   Criminal       Original   Petition   is    dismissed.

                      Consequently, connected miscellaneous petition is closed.
