<div class="doc_title">WPCRL/1961/2019 on 6 November, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">WPCRL No. 1961 of 2019
Hon'ble Manoj K. Tiwari, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">        Heard Mr. P.K. Chauhan, learned counsel for the
petitioners, Mr. Pratiroop Pandey, learned A.G.A. for the
State of Uttarakhand/respondent Nos. 1 &amp; 2.
</p><p id="p_2">       Admit.
</p><p id="p_3">       Issue notice to respondent no. 3 returnable within a
period of four weeks, by registered post, acknowledgment
due.
</p><p id="p_4">       List this matter on 10.12.2019 along WPCRL No.
1960 of 2019.
</p><p id="p_5">       Meanwhile, learned counsel for State shall file
counter affidavit.
</p><p id="p_6">       Mr. P.K. Chauhan, learned counsel for the
petitioners submits that it is a matrimonial dispute and
there are chances of amicable settlement between the
parties.
</p><p id="p_7">       Under the aforesaid circumstance, petitioner No. 1
and respondent No. 3 are directed to appear before this
Court for mediation on 10.12.2019.
</p><p id="p_8">       After considering the submission of the learned
counsel for the parties and after going through the material
available on record, it is directed that, till the next date of
listing, no coercive steps shall be taken against the
petitioners in connection with Case Crime No. 808 of
2019, relating to offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections 323</a>,
<a href="/doc/1672685/" id="a_2">352</a>, <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_3">498-A</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a> of I.P.C., registered at Police Station
Kotwali Haridwar, District Haridwar, provided they
cooperate with the investigating agency in the
investigation of the case. Petitioners shall appear before
the Investigating Officer on or before 14.11.2019 and such
further dates, as required.
</p><p id="p_9">       Stay application (CLMA No. 15010 of 2019) stands
disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                                  (Manoj K. Tiwari, J.)
Aswal                                     06.11.2019
 </pre>
