                             IA ORDER

</p><p id="p_3">1.     By    this         application,        which   has    been        filed
through his wife, the applicant­convict seeks to
be enlarged on temporary bail for a period of 30
days    for       the       purpose      of      providing      financial
assistance to his family members.
</p>

<p id="p_4">2.     A perusal of the jail record of the applicant
reveals         that         the    applicant         has       undergone
approximately 3½ years imprisonment. During such
period, he has availed of parole on 2 occasions,
i.e. in the months of March and June 2018. The
applicant has availed of temporary bail twice in
the current year, once in February ­ March 2019

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       Downloaded on : Sat Sep 07 03:53:40 IST 2019</span>
          R/CR.A/641/2018                                           IA ORDER



for a total period of 18 days and thereafter, in
June 2019, for a period of 15 days.
</p>

<p id="p_5">3.     Having regard to the fact that the applicant
has availed of temporary bail for more than 30
days     in      the       current      year,         the    court         is      not
inclined to exercise discretion in his favour.
The      application,           therefore,               fails         and         is,
accordingly, summarily rejected.
</p>
