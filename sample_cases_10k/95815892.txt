<p id="p_1">                                          ***

                                Respondent is present in person and has brought an

            amount of ` 5 lacs, which has been handed over to the counsel for

            the petitioner in Court today. Respondent states that now an amount

            of ` 31,55,861/- is due, which he will pay within a period of four

            months. He further states that the share amount be adjusted in the

            last instalment and he would pay interest @ 8% for the delayed

            payment i.e. w.e.f. 30.07.2013 till the date of deposit.

</p><p id="p_2">                                This statement of the respondent is accepted by the

            counsel for the petitioner subject to the rights, which have been

PRERNA DATTA
            granted to him in the order dated 30.07.2013 passed in CWP No.
2014.11.11 09:23
I attest to the accuracy and
authenticity of this document
High Court, Chandigarh
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             COCP No. 3338 of 2013                                                 2</span>

            5712 of 2011, non-compliance of which is asserted in the present

            contempt petition.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Respondent states that the proceedings under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_1">Section

            138</a> of the Negotiable Instruments Act have been initiated by the

            bank against the respondent for dishonour of cheque of ` 5 lacs. In

            the light of the payment of the amount in Court today, he states that

            the bank should not pursue the said petition under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_2">Section 138</a> of the

            Negotiable Instruments Act.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                Counsel for the petitioner states that if that be so, he

            would instruct the petitioner-bank to withdraw the said petition filed

            against the respondent on the next date of hearing, which is fixed

            before the trial Court.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                In the light of the statement made by the respondent, the

            present contempt petition is disposed of binding the respondent by

            the undertaking/statement given in Court. Liberty is granted to the

            petitioner for revival of the contempt petition in case of breach of the

            undertaking/statement made by the respondent.</p><pre id="pre_2">
