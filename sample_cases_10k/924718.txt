<div class="doc_title">Sukan Rai &amp;Amp; Ors vs The State Of Bihar on 29 October, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Cr.Misc. No.32612 of 2010
                                      SUKAN RAI &amp; ORS
                                              Versus
                                    THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                            -----------
</pre>


<p id="p_1">2       29.10.2010              Heard learned counsel for the petitioners as well

                     as learned counsel for the state assisted by the complainant.

</p><p id="p_2">                                It would appear from perusal of the contents of

                     complaint petition that a dispute took place between the

                     parties on the point of payment of wages and in that course,

                     the petitioner abused the complainant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                In my view, the application of provision of S.C. &amp;

                     S.T. (Prevention of Atrocities) Act appears to be doubtful.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Accordingly, this anticipatory bail petition is

                     allowed. In the event of arrest or surrender before the court

                     of Sub Divisional Judicial Magistrate, Sitamarhi within 30

                     days from the date of receipt of the order, the petitioners,

                     namely, Sukan Rai, Shivjee Rai, Bala Rai, Jagarnath Rai

                     and Sanjay Rai shall be released on bail on furnishing bail

                     bond of Rs 10,000/- (ten thousand) each with two sureties of

                     the like amount each in connection with Complaint Case No.

                     23 of 2010 subject to condition as laid down u/S 438(2) of

                     the <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><pre id="pre_2">AKV/-                                     ( Hemant Kumar Srivastava,J.)
