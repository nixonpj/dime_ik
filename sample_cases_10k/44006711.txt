<div class="doc_title">Rambilas Mehta vs The State Of Bihar on 9 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.30716 of 2019
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-812 Year-2017 Thana- FORBESGANJ District- Araria
                 ======================================================
                 Rambilas Mehta S/O Ramashish Mehta, R/O Village- Pachhiyari Tola Jhirua,
                 Ward No. 9, Verma Colony, P.S. Simraha, District- Araria.


                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar


                                                                            ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Bidhan Chandra Jha
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Vinod Shanker Modi
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRAKASH CHANDRA
                 JAISWAL
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   09-05-2019                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                   learned A.P.P. for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   The petitioner apprehends his arrest in a case

                   registered under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_3">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_4">379</a> and <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_5">504</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the

                   Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Petitioner along with other accused persons are

                   said to have assaulted the informant, his wife and his daughter-

                   in-law by means of lathi making them injured.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

                   that no such occurrence as alleged ever took place. Co-accused

                   Lal Bahadur Mehta has filed a case bearing Forbesganj
 Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.30716 of 2019(2) dt.09-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            2/3</span>




          (Simraha) P.S. Case no. 811 of 2017 against the informant and

          others and in order to save his skin from the said case the

          informant has filed this false and frivolous case against the

          petitioner. There is land dispute between the parties. The

          allegation levelled against the petitioner is not specific rather

          general and omnibus in nature. Accused persons are said to

          have assaulted the daughter-in-law of the informant by means

          of lathi but the doctor has found incised wound which is simple

          in nature on the vertex of the said victim which creates serious

          doubt about the prosecution case. Petitioner has no criminal

          antecedent. Co-accused namely Nand Kumar Mehta has been

          enlarged on anticipatory bail by a co-ordinate Bench of this

          Court vide order dated 17.04.2019 passed in Cr. Misc. No.

          23938 of 2019.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                       Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the

          case, let the above named petitioner, be released on bail, in the

          event of his arrest or surrender before the learned Court below

          within a period of four weeks from today, on furnishing bail

          bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the like

          amount each to the satisfaction of the learned A.C.J.M.-V,

          Araria in connection with Forbesganj (Simraha) P.S. Case No.

          812 of 2017, subject to the condition as laid down under Section
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.30716 of 2019(2) dt.09-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       3/3</span>




                     438 (2) of the Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                      (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
mantreshwar/-
</p>
