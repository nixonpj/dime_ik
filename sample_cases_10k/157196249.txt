<div class="doc_title">Safir Alam vs Unknown on 30 October, 2014</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Pranab Kumar Chattopadhyay</div>

<pre id="pre_1">1

155   30.10.2014</pre><p id="p_1">                     CRM 12321 of 2014
         BD
                       Re: An application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the
                       Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 2nd September, 2014
                       in connection with Jagaddal Police Station Case No.
                       650/2013 dated 07.09.2013 under <a href="/doc/1557102/" id="a_2">Section 20(b)</a> of the
                       Narcotic Drugs and <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_3">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                            In the matter of : Safir Alam.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                        ... Petitioner
                            Mr. Dhananjay Banerjee.
</p><p id="p_4">                                                ... For Petitioner.
</p><p id="p_5">                            Mr. Bitosok Banerjee.
</p><p id="p_6">                                                ... For State.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                        The petitioner is seeking bail in connection with a case
                   relating to Jagaddal Police Station Case No. 650/2013 dated
                   07.09.2013 under <a href="/doc/1557102/" id="a_4">Section 20(b)</a> of the Narcotic Drugs and
                   <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_5">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>.
</p><p id="p_8">                            The   learned    advocate   representing    the   petitioner
                    submits that the petitioner is in custody for last 13 months and
                    charge sheet has already been submitted and two charge
                    sheeted co-accused persons have already been granted bail
                    earlier by this Court.
</p><p id="p_9">                            Learned advocate for the State submits that in the
                    present case 21 Kgs. 200 grams of ganja has been seized from
                    the joint possession of the three persons including the
                    petitioner herein.
</p><p id="p_10">                            Having considered the materials in the case diary and
                    specially considering the fact that the petitioner is in custody for
                    last 13 months and charge sheet has already been submitted

        Bail        and two charge sheeted co-accused persons have already been
                    granted bail earlier by this Court and furthermore, only 21 Kgs.
                    200 grams ganja has been allegedly seized from the joint
                    possession of three persons, which is virtually below the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2</span>


    commercial quantity in relation to each one of the persons
    apprehended including the petitioner herein, we are of the
    opinion that further detention of the accused/petitioner is not
    necessary.
</p>

<p id="p_11">           Therefore, the accused/petitioner namely, Safir Alam be
    released on bail upon furnishing bond of Rs. 10000/- (Rupees
    Ten Thousand) only with two sureties of like amount, one of
    whom must be local, to the satisfaction of the learned Judge,
    Special Court under the <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_6">N.D.P.S. Act</a> at Barrackpore.
        The application for bail, thus, stands allowed.
</p>

<p id="p_12">                                    (Pranab Kumar Chattopadhyay, J)


                                               (Sudip Ahluwalia, J)
 </p>
