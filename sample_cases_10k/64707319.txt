<div class="doc_title">Ajitsinh Navuji Chavda vs State Of Gujarat on 4 May, 2018</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: J.B.Pardiwala</div>

<pre id="pre_1">        R/CR.MA/8972/2018                                   ORDER




          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

           R/CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 8972 of 2018
==========================================================
                            AJITSINH NAVUJI CHAVDA
                                     Versus
                               STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR KEYUR A VYAS(3247) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1,2,3,4,5,6,7
for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 2
MS. MOXA THAKKAR, ADDL. PUBLIC PROSECUTOR(2) for the
RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE J.B.PARDIWALA

                               Date : 04/05/2018

                                 ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">      RULE returnable forthwith. Ms. Moxa Thakkar, the learned APP

waives service of notice of rule for and on behalf the respondent no. 1 -

State of Gujarat. Mr. Jignesh Nayak, the learned advocate has entered

appearance and waives service of notice of rule for and on behalf of the

respondent no. 2 - original complainant.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      By this application under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal

Procedure, 1973, the applicants seek to invoke the inherent powers of this

Court praying for quashing of the First Information Report being CR I-

No. 143 of 2017 registered with the Mansa Police Station, District:

Gandhinagar, for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_4">325</a>,

<a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_5">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_6">143</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_7">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_8">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_9">149</a> read with <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_10">Section 114</a> of the IPC and <a href="/doc/534021/" id="a_11">Section 135</a>


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   Page 1 of 4</span>
         R/CR.MA/8972/2018                                     ORDER



of the Gujarat Police Act, on the ground that the parties have amicably

resolved the dispute.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">      An affidavit dated 02.05.2018 has been filed on behalf of the

respondent no. 2 - original complainant, inter alia, stating as under:

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">             "I, Siddhrajsinh Keshuji Chavda, Age: 27 years,
             respondent no. 2 - original complainant herein file
             the following affidavit and state on solemn
             affirmation as under:-
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">             (1)    That I am the respondent no. 2 in the
             present petition, original complainant and that I
             am well conversant with the facts of the case and I
             have gone through the petition filed by the
             petitioner including the prayers made therein and I
             am competent to file this affidavit. I had filed FIR
             being CR -I no. 143 of 2017 dated 06.12.2017
             registered with Mansa Police Station under
             <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_12">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_13">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_14">325</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_15">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_16">143</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_17">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_18">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_19">149</a>
             and <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_20">114</a> of the Indian Penal Code, 1860 and
             Section 135 of the Gujarat Police Act.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">             (2)    It is humbly submitted that with passage of
             time the dispute between the parties i.e. the
             petitioners and the complainant-the respondent no.
             2 arrived at full and final amicable settlement in
             the present case with no grievance left between us.
             That the petitioners and complainant- deponent
             herein have resolved the dispute whatsoever and
             now no disputes are remaining to be left out
             between us.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">             (3)    I therefore, now not interested in
             prosecuting further in the criminal case. That the
             persecution would further harm the reputation of
             families of both the sides and, therefore, when the
             dispute is already resolved, the FIR as well as
             criminal case may be quashed and set aside since I
             do not intend to proceed in the present case.
</blockquote>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                  Page 2 of 4</span>
<pre id="pre_2">          R/CR.MA/8972/2018                                    ORDER




              (4)    I therefore humbly state and submit that the
</pre><blockquote id="blockquote_5">              impugned FIR in the present petition being CR - I
              no. 143 of 2017 dated 06.12.2017 registered with
              Mansa Police Station for the offence punishable
              under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_21">sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_22">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_23">325</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_24">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_25">143</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_26">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_27">148</a>,
              <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_28">149</a> and <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_29">114</a> of the Indian Penal Code, 1860 and
              section 135 of Gujarat Police Act as well as
              criminal case being no. 335 of 2018 pending
              before the learned Magistrate court at Mansa may
              be quashed and set-aside by this Honourable
              Court.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_6">              (5)    That the contents of the petition have been
              read over and explained to me in detail in
              vernacular language. That the contents of the
              petition are absolutely true and I support the same.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_7">              (6)   By way of this affidavit I give my consent for
              quashing of First Information Report being CR - I
              no. 143 of 2017dated 06.12.2017 registered with
              Mansa Police Station under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_30">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_31">324</a>,
              <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_32">325</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_33">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_34">143</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_35">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_36">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_37">149</a> and <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_38">114</a> of the Indian
              Penal Code, 1860 and Section 135 of Gujarat
              Police Act as well as criminal case being no. 335
              of 2018 pending before the learned Magistrate
              Court at Mansa.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_8">              Solemnly affirmed at Ahmedabad on this 2nd day of
              May, 2018."</blockquote><p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">      Taking into consideration the fact that the parties have amicably

resolved the dispute, no useful purpose would now be served to permit

the investigation to continue further.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">      In the result, this application is allowed. The First Information

Report being CR I-No. 143 of 2017 registered with the Mansa Police



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                  Page 3 of 4</span>
            R/CR.MA/8972/2018                                   ORDER



Station, District: Gandhinagar, is hereby ordered to be quashed. All

consequential proceedings pursuant thereto shall also stand terminated.

</p><p id="p_10">         Rule is made absolute. Direct service is permitted.




</p><p id="p_11">                                                       (J.B.PARDIWALA, J)
Bhoomi
