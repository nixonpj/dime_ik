<div class="doc_title">Rajendra Sahu vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 5 September, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                      Rajendra Sahu Vs. State of M.P. &amp; Ors.




                   Writ Petition No. 14212 / 2012
5.9.2012</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Shri D.S. Chauhan, learned counsel for the petitioner.
       Smt. D.K. Bohrey, learned Panel Lawyer for the State, on
advance notice.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       Petitioner is working as Panchayat Secretary in Gram
Panchayat Gadasaraie, Janpad Panchayat Bajag, District Dindori and
by the impugned order dated 13.7.2012 he has been transferred to
Gram Panchayat Khannat under Janpad Panchayat Karanjiya,
District Dindori. Challenge to the transfer order is made merely on
the ground of personal inconvenience of petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       As grounds raised by petitioner in this writ petition are not
such which permits judicial review of an administrative order of
transfer, petitioner should represent to the competent authority and it
would be for the competent authority to consider representation of
petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       Records indicate that representation Annexure P-5 has
already been submitted by the petitioner before the Chief Executive
Officer Janpad Panchayat Dindori, the said authority is directed to
consider and decide the representation of petitioner in accordance to
law by a speaking order within four weeks.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       Till the aforesaid exercise is not completed, status-quo in the
matter as is existing today shall be maintained.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       With the aforesaid the petition stands disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">       Certified copy as per rules.
</p><p id="p_8">                                                        (Rajendra Menon)
                                                              Judge
