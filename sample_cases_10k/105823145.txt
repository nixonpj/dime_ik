<pre id="pre_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                   BENCH AT JAIPUR

         S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail No. 14400/2018

Abid Sunsara S/o Rahim Sunsara B/c Musalman, R/o Masjid Bas
Laxmipura, Distt. Patan, Gujrat.
                                                        ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State Of Rajasthan Through PP
                                                      ----Respondent</pre>


<p id="p_1">For Petitioner(s)       :   Mr. Shantnu Bansal Adv.
For Respondent(s)       :   Mr. R.S. Raghav, P.P.



</p><p id="p_2">                HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA

                            Judgment

12/12/2018
     Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Code of

Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking anticipatory bail in F.I.R.

No.12/2018 registered at Police Station Sarund, District Jaipur

offences under <a href="/doc/1459475/" id="a_2">Sections 374</a>, <a href="/doc/1495318/" id="a_3">344</a> of Indian Penal Code, 1860 and

<a href="/doc/148942/" id="a_4">Section 75</a>, <a href="/doc/148942/" id="a_5">79</a> of Juvenile Justice (Care and <a href="/doc/1973522/" id="a_6">Protection of Children)

Act</a>, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the

petitioner is innocent and has placed reliance on affidavit

Annexure-4.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     On 14.11.2018 following order was passed by this court:-

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">              "List again on 12.12.2018.            In the
         meantime, in the event of arrest, petitioner
         be released on interim bail subject to the
         satisfaction of the Arresting Officer. Petitioner
         shall abide by the conditions envisaged under
         <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_7">Section 438(2)</a> Cr. P. C."
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                                                                        (2 of 2)                  [CRLMB-14400/2018]


                                         Learned State counsel has submitted that in pursuance to

                                   the above order, petitioner has joined investigation.

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                                         Accordingly, keeping in view the facts and circumstances of

                                   the case and the fact that the petitioner has joined investigation,

                                   without expressing any opinion on merits of the case, interim bail

                                   granted    by   this   Court   to    the       petitioner   vide   order   dated

                                   14.11.2018 is made absolute.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">                                         Petition stands disposed of accordingly.


</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_5">                                                                                                       (SABINA)J.</blockquote><p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                   S.Kumawat-20
