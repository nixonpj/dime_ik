                            ASHIM GUHATHAKURTA &amp; ORS.
                                      Versus
                          BINOY KUMAR CHATTERJEE &amp; ORS.


  BEFORE:
  The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>
<p id="p_1">  Date : 9th October, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                             Appearance:
</p><p id="p_4">                                                  Ms. Suchismita Chatterjee (Ghosh) Adv.

</p><p id="p_6">                                                                      Mr. A.K. Dey, Adv.

</p><p id="p_9">                                                                Mr. Paritosh Sinha, Adv.
</p><p id="p_10">                                                                      Mr. Ajay Roy, Adv.
</p><p id="p_11">                                                             Ms. Malashree Ghosh, Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_12">
      The Court: Pursuant to the order dated 29th September, 2015 the Joint

Administrators have filed a report. It appears from the report that little over Rs.5 crores

with accrued interest are being held by the Registrar, High Court, Original Side and a

sum of Rs.10,69,357.14 as on 21st September, 2015 is lying in the credit of the current

account maintained by the Joint Administrators with various banks.              The Joint

Administrators during the pendency of this proceeding has paid to all the depositors who

are having claimed between Rs.500/- and Rs.5000/- in full and 30% of the claim of the

depositors having claim between Rs.5500/- to Rs.53,000/-.

</p><p id="p_13">      Under such circumstances, I direct the Joint Administrators to pay all depositors

having claim up to Rs.25,000/- in full within six weeks from date.         This will be in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                               2</span>


addition to the depositors who could not collect their refund as stated in paragraphs 5

and 6 of the report. The Joint Administrators shall file a report two weeks after vacation.

The fund shall be disbursed by the Registrar, High Court, Original Side on a requisition

being sent by the Joint Administrators for release and disbursement of the fund in order

to enable the Joint Administrators to pay the depositors. It would be the responsibility

of the Joint Administrators to ascertain the veracity of the claim and such amount shall

be released on production of proper identification.

</p><p id="p_14">      The Joint Administrator shall file a report two weeks after vacation. The matter is

made returnable on 2nd December, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_15">      Urgent certified website copies of this order, if applied for, be urgently supplied to

the parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.




</p><p id="p_16">                                                  (SOUMEN SEN, J.)
