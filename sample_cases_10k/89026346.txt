<div class="doc_title">Satpal vs Kaithal Cooperative Sugar Mills ... on 5 April, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">             IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                         AT CHANDIGARH

Sr. No.259
                                        CWP No.15627 of 2010
                                        Date of decision: 05.04.2016

Satpal                                                      ....Petitioner

                           versus

The Kaithal Cooperative Sugar Mills Limited, Kaithal and others
                                                       ....Respondents

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE DEEPAK SIBAL

Present:     Mr. J.P. Dhull, Advocate for the petitioner.

             Mr. Surender Saini, Advocate, for respondents No.1 and 2.

             Mr. S.K. Sharma, Advocate, for respondent No.3.

                   * * *

DEEPAK SIBAL, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Through the present petition, the petitioner who is serving the

respondent-Society as Tubewell Operator/Attentdant seeks regularization of

his services from the date respondent No.3 his junior has been regularized.

</p><p id="p_2">             Today, learned counsel appearing on behalf of respondents

No.1 and 2 has produced an order dated 04.04.2016 through which the

services of the petitioner have been regularized w.e.f. 17.04.1997 i.e. the

date of regularization of services of his junior-respondent No.3, rendering

the present petition infructuous.

</p><p id="p_3">             Ordered accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             Copy of the order dated 04.04.2016 is ordered to be placed on

record and marked as Annexure 'A'.



</p><p id="p_5">                                               (DEEPAK SIBAL)
                                                   JUDGE
April 05, 2016
