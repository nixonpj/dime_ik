         ==========================================================
                    RAJ @ RAJA NAMDEVBHAI KADABE....Petitioner(s)

         MR DARSHAN P DAVE, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1

                                    Date : 03/01/2017
                                     ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">              Learned AGP is in receipt of communication  dated 
         03/01/2017   disclosing   that   till   date,   there   is   no 
         proposal for detaining the petitioner under PASA. Copy 
         of the same is taken on record.
</p>

<p id="p_2">              Therefore, this petition is disposed of with an 
         observation   that   henceforth   respondents   should   not 
         detain/book the present petitioner under PASA for the 
         same set of information which is available with them 
         in   connection   with   the   FIRs   being   C.R.   No.III­
         5112/2016,   CR   No.III­/5149/2016   and   C.R   No.   III­
         5198/2016   respectively   registered   with   Krishnanagar 
         Police Station, Ahmedabad.
</p>
<p id="p_3">              With   the   above   observation,   this   petition   is 
         disposed of. Direct service is permitted. </p><pre id="pre_2">
