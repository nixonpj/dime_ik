<div class="doc_title">Manoj vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 30 November, 2019</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Sunil Kumar Awasthi</div>

<pre id="pre_1">6
THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
              M.Cr.C. No.49549/2019
Indore dated :30/11/2019
     Shri Manish Yadav, learned counsel for the applicant.
     Shri Gaurav Kumar Verma, learned Public Prosecutor for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard. Case diary perused.
</p><p id="p_2">     This is repeat (second) application under Section 439, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>
for grant of bail in connection with Crime No.238/2019,
registered   at     Police   Station-Shajapur,      District-Shajapur
concerning offence under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_2">Sections 363</a> and <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_3">376</a> of the IPC
alongwith <a href="/doc/200125/" id="a_4">Section 3</a>/<a href="/doc/230360/" id="a_5">4</a> of Protection of Children from Sexual
Offences Act, 2012. Earlier application of the applicant has been
dismissed by this Court, vide order dated 16/09/2019 passed in
M.Cr.C. No. 37569/2019 granting liberty to renew his prayer after
recording the court statement of the prosecutrix.
</p><p id="p_3">     Learned counsel for the applicant has submitted that the
prosecutrix has already been examined before the trial Court and
from her court statement it appears that at the time of alleged
incident she was major and she was having love affairs with the
applicant, which clearly indicates that she was the consenting
party. The applicant is in custody since 07/08/2019 and conclusion
of trial will take sufficient long time. Under these circumstances,
learned counsel for the applicant prays for grant of bail to the
applicant.
</p><p id="p_4">     On the other hand, learned Public Prosecutor opposed the
application by contending that in her court statement, the
prosecutrix has categorically made allegation against the applicant
regarding commission of rape and at the time of deciding bail
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           6</span>
          application, detail appreciation of the statement of the prosecution
          witnesses is not permissible. Under these circumstances, he prays
          for rejection of the application.
</p><p id="p_5">                Considering the court statement of the prosecutrix, this court
          is of the view that no case for grant of bail to the applicant is
          made out. Accordingly, this application filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_6">Section 439</a> of
          the Cr.P.C. is dismissed.
</p><p id="p_6">                                                            (S.K. Awasthi)
                                                               Judge
          skt

Santosh Kumar Tiwari
2019.11.30 16:24:02 +05'30'
 </p>
