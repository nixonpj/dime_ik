        IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA
           CIRCUIT BENCH AT DHARWAD

 DATED THIS THE 19TH DAY OF OCTOBER 2012
                        PRESENT
     THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE K.L.MANJUNATH
                             AND
       THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE B.MANOHAR
                  R.P. NO.1534/2011

BETWEEN:

Arun G. Toppannavar,
3941, Basav Nagar,
Club Road, Belgaum.
                                          ... PETITIONER

(By Shri A.Shankar for Shri Shashank S.Hegde, Advocate)

AND:

1.     The Commissioner of Income Tax,
       Belgaum.

2.     Income Tax Officer,
       Ward 2(1), Belgaum.
                                         ...RESPONDENTS

(By Shri Y.V.Raviraj, Advocate)

     This RP is filed under Order 47 Rule 1 of CPC,
praying to review the order dated 08.03.2011 passed in the
ITA No.1424/2006 on the file of High Court of Karnataka,
Circuit Bench at Dharwad.

     This petition coming on for admission this day,
K.L.Manjunath J., delivered the following:-
                              -2-


                           ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">       Having heard the learned counsel appearing for the

petitioner. We do not see any ground to review the order

and if the appellant is aggrieved by the order passed in ITA

No.1424/2006, it is open for him to challenge the same

before the Hon'ble Apex Court.


</p><p id="p_2">       Accordingly, the review petition is dismissed.
