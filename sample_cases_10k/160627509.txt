     CORAM :                 HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE KURIAN JOSEPH

                              UPON hearing the counsel the Court made the following
                                                 O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">                         Leave granted.
</p><p id="p_2">                         Call for the records.
</p><p id="p_3">                         Having regard to the advanced age of the appellant, which is
     reported to be 84 years, having regard to his health conditions and
     considering the fact that he has spent around 50% of the term in
     jail, we are of the view that the appellant should be released on
     bail, in connection with Special Case No.3/87, on his execution of
     a bond to the tune of Rs.2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lacs only) with two
     solvent sureties of the like amount and subject to other conditions
     to be imposed by the Trial Court.                   Ordered accordingly.
