<p id="p_1">             This petition under Articles 226/227 of the Constitution of

India is for issuance of writ in the nature of mandamus directing the

respondents to release the payment of the works,executed by the petitioner

along with interest @ 18% p.a.

             In the year 2013, the petitioner was allotted the work vide work

order dated 06.08.2011 (p-1) and the work was executed by him, but the

amount has not been released to him till date. In this regard, the petitioner

has given a legal notice dated 13.01.2014 (Annexure P-1), but no action has

been taken thereon till date.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             In view of the above position, this petition, at this stage is being

disposed of without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, by

giving a direction to respondent No. 3 and 4 to look in to the legal notice of

the petitioner (Annexure P-1) and decide the same in accordance with law,

within a period of one month from the date of receipt of certified copy of
