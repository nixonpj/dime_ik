             WRIT PETITION NO.8846 OF 2002

ORDER:
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      On 13.8.2018 when this matter was called, there was no

representation on behalf of the petitioner. Hence, the office was

directed to list this matter under the caption of 'for dismissal'.

Today, when the matter was called in the forenoon session, there

was no representation on behalf of the petitioner and hence, the

matter was passed over and again taken up in the afternoon session.

But there was no representation on behalf of the petitioner.


</p><p id="p_2">      Therefore, the Writ Petition is dismissed for default. No

costs. Consequently, miscellaneous petitions pending, if any, shall

stand dismissed.
</p>
