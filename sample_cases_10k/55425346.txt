<div class="doc_title">Gopal Krishna vs The State Of Jharkhand on 7 September, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                   I.A. No. 7219 of 2017
                                             in
                             Cr. Appeal (SJ) No. 1594 of 2017

            Gopal Krishna                                ...         Appellant

                                           Versus
            The State of Jharkhand                       ...         Respondent


        Coram:    HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR CHOUDHARY


        For the Petitioner     : Mr. A.K. Sahani, Adv.
        For the State          : Addl. P.P.


03 /07.09.2017</pre><p id="p_1">           This criminal appeal is directed against the impugned
            judgment of conviction an the order of sentence dated 08.08.2017,
            passed by the learned Addl. Sessions Judge -VI, Garhwa in
            Sessions Trial No. 181 of 2010 whereby and whereunder the
            appellant has been convicted for the offences punishable under
            <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">sections 498A</a>/<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>/<a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_3">504</a> of the Indian Penal Code and has been
            sentenced to undergo rigorous imprisonment for three years with
            fine of Rs.1000/- under sections -498A<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>
            and in default, to undergo rigorous imprisonment for another
            three months and rigorous imprisonment for six months for the
            offences under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">section 323</a> of the Indian Penal Code and nine
            months of rigorous imprisonment under <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_6">section 504</a> of the Indian
            Penal Code with compensation of Rs.25,000/-.
</p><p id="p_2">                         Heard the learned counsel for the appellant and the
            learned Addl. P.P. for the State.
</p><p id="p_3">                         Admit.
</p><p id="p_4">                         Call for the L.C.R.
</p><p id="p_5">                         From perusal of the I.A. No. 7219 of 2017, it appears
            that the learned Additional Sessions Judge -VI, Garhwa, vide
            order dated 08.08.2017 enlarged the appellant on provisional bail.
              In the facts and circumstances of the case, the
 provisional bail earlier granted to the appellant vide order dated
 08.08.2017, passed by the learned Additional Sessions Judge -VI,
 Garhwa in S.T. Case No. 181 of 2010 is hereby confirmed.
</p><p id="p_6">             Accordingly, I.A. No. 7219 of 2017 is disposed of.
             Let a copy of this order be sent to the court below.
</p>

<pre id="pre_2">Sonu-                                   (Anil Kumar Choudhary, J.)
 </pre>
