     IN THE HIGH COURT FOR THE STATES OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT
                            CHANDIGARH
                               C.W.P. No.17600 of 2011 (O&amp;M)
                               Date of Decision.21.10.2011

Zenith Institute of Management &amp; Technology (ZIMT) SCF 26-27, Near
Papiha Park Fatehabad
                                            .....Petitioner
                               Versus

The Haryana Roadways through its Director General State Transport and
others                                       .....Respondents</pre>

<p id="p_1">Present: Mr. D.S. Bishnoi, Advocate
         for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Ms. Kirti Singh, DAG, Haryana.
</p>
<p id="p_3">CORAM:HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE K. KANNAN
</p><p id="p_4">1. Whether Reporters of local papers may be allowed to see the
   judgment ? No
</p><p id="p_5">2.     To be referred to the Reporters or not ? No
</p><p id="p_6">3.     Whether the judgment should be reported in the Digest? No
</p><p id="p_7">                                       -.-
</p><p id="p_8">K. KANNAN J.(ORAL)

</p><p id="p_9">1.         Learned counsel for the petitioner points out to the fact that

this Court has already taken a view in Guru Jambeshwar Institute of

I.T. and Management and another Vs. The Haryana Roadways and

others in C.W.P. No.10766 of 2010 allowing for grant of concessional

passes also to students at the institutes, which are recognized by the

University. It appears that a similar point is pending before the Hon'ble

Supreme Court but even then this Court has held that concessional

passes cannot be denied merely because the students are not attending

regular classes.    The students studying in the petitioner-institute will

have the same relief and the respondents shall be directed to issue

concessional passes. This is, however, subject to any decision that may

be passed by the Hon'ble Supreme Court in the case said to be pending
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> C.W.P. No.17600 of 2011 (O&amp;M)                               -2-</span>

before it.
</p>
<p id="p_10">2.           The writ petition is allowed. Copy of the order be given dasti

on payment of usual charges.
</p>

</p><p id="p_12">                                                  (K. KANNAN)
