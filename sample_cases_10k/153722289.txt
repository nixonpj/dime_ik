<p id="p_1">                                 This petition under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. has been filed by the

                   petitioner seeking regular bail in FIR No. 151 dated 23.11.2012,

                   registered under <a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_2">Section 304-B</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> IPC at Police Station Mukerian,

                   District Hoshiarpur.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 I have heard learned counsel for the petitioner, learned State

                   counsel and carefully gone through the record.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 Learned State counsel submits that out of total 16 witnesses,

                   6 witnesses have already been examined. Learned State counsel also

                   submits that all the private witnesses have been examined and remaining

                   are the official/formal witnesses.     The petitioner is in custody from

                   24.11.2012.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Learned counsel for the petitioner has referred to the

                   statement of PW-1 Yashpal (Annexure P-2), the complainant-father of the

                   was declined.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                In view of the above and without commenting on the merits

                   of the case, the instant petition is allowed and the petitioner be admitted

                   to bail on furnishing bail bonds to the satisfaction of trial Court.
