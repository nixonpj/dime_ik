<div class="doc_title">Dhanendra @ Gudda Pathak vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 11 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                          MCRC-19086-2016
         (DHANENDRA @ GUDDA PATHAK Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


11-11-2016

     Shri Mahendra Pateria, Advocate for the applicant.
     Shri Amit Pandey, PL for the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard.
</p><p id="p_2">    This is first bail application filed by the applicant/accused
under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of Cr.P.C for grant of anticipatory bail,
who is apprehending his arrest in connection with Crime
No.302/2016 registered at Police Station- Barhi, District-
Katni(MP) for offences punishable under <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_2">Section 354</a> of the
IPC.
</p><p id="p_3">      Having considered the contention advanced by the
learned counsel for the parties and on perusal of record, it is
evident that prima facie there is sufficient material to
connect the applicant with the crime.
</p><p id="p_4">     In view of the facts and nature of the case, applicant is
not entitled to be enlarged on anticipatory bail. Hence, the
application is rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_5">     CC as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                                          (J. P. GUPTA)
                                                              JUDGE
