<p id="p_1">ALOK SINGH, J (ORAL)
          Learned counsel for the petitioner states that petitioner's
candidature is not being considered despite of the fact that petitioner is
eligible for the allotment of ration depot.           He further states that
representation moved by the petitioner before the Director Food, Civil
Supplies and Consumer Matters Department, Punjab, was forwarded to
the District Controller, Food and Civil Supplies and Consumer Matter
vide Annexure P-4 and no action has been taken thereon also. Learned
counsel further states that let respondents No.2 and 3 take decision on
Annexure P-3 within such time as fixed by this Court.
</p><p id="p_2">          Without expressing any opinion on the merit of the case,
present petition is disposed of with a direction to respondents No.2 and
3 to take decision on Annexure P-3 within 90 days from the date
certified copy of this order is placed before them.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                                                       (Alok Singh)
                                                          Judge
January 11, 2012
R.S.
