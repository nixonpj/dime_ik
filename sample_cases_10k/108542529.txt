                         J U D G M E N T

<p id="p_1">           The petitioner seeks permission to operate his stage

carriage service on the basis of Ext.P1 proceedings settling the

timings. The Government Pleader submits that Ext.P3 timings is

not on the basis of any proceedings of the second respondent

and that it can safely be ignored.
</p>
<p id="p_2">           2. The petitioner is permitted to operate the stage

carriage service on the basis of Ext.P1 timings if the same

govern the field. The second respondent is however at liberty to

settle the timings with notice to all the operators on the route.

</p><p id="p_3">           The Writ Petition is disposed of.
</p>
