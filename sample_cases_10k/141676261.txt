<div class="doc_title">Nagina Bhagat vs The State Of Bihar on 4 December, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.41680 of 2012
                 ======================================================
                 Nagina Bhagat
                                                                     .... .... Petitioner.
                                                 Versus
                 The State Of Bihar
                                                                .... .... Opposite Party.
                 ======================================================

                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   04-12-2012              Heard learned counsels for the petitioner

                 and the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                            The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in

                 a case registered for the offences punishable under

                 <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_3">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_4">379</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal

                 Code     and    3(1)(x)       of     the    SC/ST    (Prevention        of

                 Atrocities Act).
</p>
<p id="p_3">                            The accusations are of making assault and

                 abusing by calling caste name.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                            It is submitted that the accusation under

                 SC/<a href="/doc/53524/" id="a_6">ST     Act</a>    is    omnibus       and    general       against    five

                 persons        when     for        occurrence        of     14.08.2012-

                 16.08.2012, F.I.R. was registered on 17.08.2012 when

                 there     is    admittedly         land    dispute    and    accusation

                 under SC/<a href="/doc/53524/" id="a_7">ST Act</a> is cosmetic one. The statement has

                 been made in paragraph no.12 of the petition that

                 the petitioner has no criminal antecedent.

</p><p id="p_5">                            Considering        the     aforesaid      facts,    let    the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.41680 of 2012 (2) dt.04-12-2012</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                  petitioner, above named, be released on bail in the

                  event      of     arrest       or     surrender       before   the   learned

                  court below within a period of twelve weeks from

                  today in connection with Vijaipur P.S. Case No. 138

                  of 2012 on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-(Ten

                  Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each

                  to the satisfaction of the learned C.J.M. Gopalganj,

                  subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_8">Section

                  438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.




</p><pre id="pre_2">U.K./-                                      (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
 </pre>
