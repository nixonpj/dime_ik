<div class="doc_title">Brij Bashi Singh S/O Feku Singh vs State Of U.P. Thru Principal ... on 17 June, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">Court No. - 3

Case :- SERVICE SINGLE No. - 4091 of 2010

Petitioner :- Brij Bashi Singh S/O Feku Singh
Respondent :- State Of U.P. Thru Principal Secretary Nagar Vikas &amp; Ors.
Petitioner Counsel :- Rajendra Singh Chauhan
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.

Hon'ble Shri Narayan Shukla,J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">Heard Mr.Rajendra Singh Chauhan, learned counsel for the petitioner and
Mr.I.P.Singh, learned counsel for the Jal Nigam as well as learned Standing
Counsel.
</p>
<p id="p_2">This order shall also govern the prayer for interim orders made in Writ
Petition Nos. 690 (S/B) of 2010 and 671 (S/B) of 2010 .
</p>
<p id="p_3">Indisputably, the petitioner is similarly situated like those in Writ Petition No.
168 (S/B) of 2010, therefore, the petitioner would be entitled to get the
benefits of order dated 22.02.2010.
</p>
<p id="p_4">Hence, I grant the interim relief in the same terms as granted in Writ Petition
No. 168 (S/B) of 2010 till the final decision on the issue in question.
</p>
<p id="p_5">List with connected writ Petition nos. 690 (S/B) of 2010 and 671 (S/B) of
2010.
</p>
<p id="p_6">Order Date :- 17.6.2010
Banswar
