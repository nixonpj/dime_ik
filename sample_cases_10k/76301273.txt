<p id="p_1"> IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR


D.B. CIVIL SPECIAL APPEAL(WRIT) NO. 1097/2012
IN
S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO. 1267/2010


ANIL KUMAR KHANDELWAL
VS.
</p><p id="p_2">URBAN IMPROVEMENT TRUST, ALWAR &amp; OTHERS.
Date: 27.08.2012
HON'BLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. ARUN MISHRA
HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE NARENDRA KUMAR JAIN-I

Mr. Prashant Sharma on behalf of Mr. S.K. Jindal, for the appellant.		
		Land acquisition, which was made in the year 1983, was sought to be questioned by way of amendment application.  Writ application was filed in the year 2010.  The amendment application has rightly been rejected by the Single Bench.
		We find no illegality in the order passed by the Single Bench.  Intra Court appeal is, hereby, dismissed.      
</p>
<p id="p_3">              (NARENDRA KUMAR JAIN-I),J.                    (ARUN MISHRA),CJ.
