<p id="p_2">                   The order has admittedly been complied with. There is, as
             such, no scope for entertaining the contempt application. We are
             told that after the order was complied with and the appointment
             was approved, the concerned District Inspector of Schools has
             at the instance of the Commissioner sought to reopen the
             matter. This exercise, by which the writ petitioners/appellants
             are aggrieved, may be the subject matter of a separate writ
             petition. This cannot be taken up in the contempt application.
             The cause of action is different. Therefore, there is nothing that
             can be done by us.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                   The contempt proceedings are therefore dropped.
</p>



<p id="p_4">     Bd.
