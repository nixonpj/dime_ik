<p id="p_1">            Exhibit P31 is a notice issued under Section 11(3) of the

Land Conservancy Act, 1957, by which the petitioner is directed to

vacate an alleged encroachment over 65.31 Ares of property

situated in Re-survey Nos.642/3 and 643/1 of Mararikulam North

Village. Against Exhibit P31, petitioner filed Exhibit P32 statutory

appeal, which is accompanied by Exhibit P33 stay petition; before

the 3rd respondent. The appeal and the stay petition are pending

before the 3rd respondent. In the meanwhile, it is alleged, coercive

proceedings have been threatened. It is in this context, the writ

petition has been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            2. In the above circumstances, it is directed that the

appellate authority, the 3rd respondent herein, shall          dispose of

Exhibit P33 stay application within a period of two months from

today and the coercive proceedings be stayed until such orders are

passed; which order shall determine the matter thereafter. The

petitioner shall also maintain status quo in the property.

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">WP(C) No.29283 of 2017          - 2 -</span>


</p><p id="p_3">           3. Petitioner shall produce a copy of this judgment

before the 3rd respondent for compliance.

</p><p id="p_4">           Writ petition disposed of as above.
