<div class="doc_title">Parmeshwar Mahto vs State Of Jharkhand on 7 September, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                         B.A No. 5806 of 2011
                                                -------
                   Parmeshwar Mahto                     ......    Petitioner

                                                -Versus-
                   The State of Jharkhand                    ...... Opposite Party
                                              ------
                   CORAM :        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.C.MISHRA
                                               ------
                   For the Petitioner :    Mr.Satya Narayan Prasad, Advocate.
                   For the State      :    Ms.Anita Sinha, A.P.P.



                                                  ------

2/ 07.09.2011</pre><p id="p_1"> Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned counsel for the State.
</p><p id="p_2">              The petitioner has been made accused in Angara P.S case No.26 of 2010
        corresponding to G.R Case No. 1592 of 2010, S.T No.364 of 2010 for the offence under
        <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_1">Section 376</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_3">              There is direct allegation against the petitioner to have sexually exploited the
        informant due to which, the informant became pregnant.
</p><p id="p_4">              In the facts and circumstances of the case, I am not inclined to release the
        petitioner Parmeshwar Mahto on bail. Accordingly, the prayer for bail of the petitioner is
        hereby rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                                      (H.C.Mishra, J.)
     B.S/
