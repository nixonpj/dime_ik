                            JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">  Ramachandra Menon, J.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       Non-implementation of the direction given by the learned
 Con.Case(C).No.1015 of 2015 IN WP(C).28477/2014
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            2</span>

   Single Judge as per the judgment dated 16.01.2015 but for

   passing Annexure-A2 order on 16.04.2015, holding that the

   petitioner is not entitled to get gratuity in view of the black and

   white    difference     in   the    factual    situation,   is   stated   as   a

   contumacious act on the part of the first respondent and hence

   sought to be proceeded against.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         2. When the matter came up for consideration before this

   Court on the last occasion, it was submitted that the first

   respondent, who was the Managing Director of the KSRTC, who

   passed Annexure-A2 order, is no more. In the said circumstance,

   the matter cannot be proceeded against the said respondent, as

   the charge sought to be fixed upon the first respondent would

   stand abated.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         3. The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

   petitioner might be permitted to proceed against the competent

   authority of the KSRTC as they have already implemented similar

   direction as contained in Annexure A1 in respect of other similarly

   situated persons.
</p>
<p id="p_5">         4. The learned standing counsel appearing for the KSRTC

   submits that steps are being taken to have a uniform stand and to

   release gratuity to the petitioner as well.            One month's time is

   sought for to implement the direction. In the said circumstance,
 Con.Case(C).No.1015 of 2015 IN WP(C).28477/2014
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                            3</span>

   no further orders are called for. The contempt proceedings stand

   closed, recording the submission as above.</p><pre id="pre_2">
