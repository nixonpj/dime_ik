                                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

                                                      DATED 04.03.2019

                                                           CORAM

                                   THE HONOURABLE Mr.JUSTICE G.K.ILANTHIRAIYAN

                                                 Crl.O.P.No. 5651 of 2019

                     M.Sampathkumar                                                      .. Petitioner

                                                             Vs
                     State Rep. by
                     The Sub-Inspector of Police,
                     Vellithiruppur Police Station,
                     Erode District.                                                    .. Respondent

                     Prayer: Criminal Original Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of Criminal
                     Procedure Code, to direct the respondent to grant permission and police
                     protection to conduct the cultural event viz., Adal Padal (Song and Dance)
                     program to be held on 06.03.2019 at 7.00 pm onwards in connection with the
                     Temple Festival Committee in Arulmigu Mariamman Swamy Thirukovil,
                     Pommanpatti Village, Anthiyur Taluk, Erode District by considering the
                     petitioner's representation dated 25.02.2019.


                                         For Petitioner      : Mr.S.Sathish

                                         For Respondent      : Mr.M.Mohamed Riyaz
                                                               Additional Public Prosecutor

                                                          ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                           Seeking permission to conduct cultural        programme in the event of

                     Arulmigu Mariamman Swamy Thirukovil Festival at Pommanpatti Village,

                     Anthiyur Taluk, Erode District, the petitioner made a representation dated

http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                               2</span>

                     25.02.2019 to the respondent.      As the same has not been considered, the

                     present Criminal Original petition has been filed.
</p>



<p id="p_2">                             2. Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the

                     learned Additional Public Prosecutor appearing for the respondent.
</p>



<p id="p_3">                              3. The learned Additional Public Prosecutor, on instructions, would

                     submit that there is likelihood of arising law and order problem.
</p>



<p id="p_4">                              4. A mere apprehension cannot be a ground to reject the

                     representation. There is absolutely no material before this Court to hold that

                     there will be a law and order problem in the event of permission being granted.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                     In such view of the matter, this petition is allowed and the petitioner is

                     permitted to conduct cultural programme in the event of Arulmigu Mariamman

                     Swamy Thirukovil Festival at Pommanpatti Village, Anthiyur Taluk, Erode District

                     however with the following conditions:-
</p>
<p id="p_6">                              (a) The cultural programme in connection with the event of Arulmigu
                     Mariamman Swamy Thirukovil Festival at Pommanpatti Village, Anthiyur Taluk,
                     Erode   District shall be conducted on 06.03.2019     between       06.00 p.m. and
                     10.00 p.m.
</p><p id="p_7">                              (b)There should not be any kind of obscene dance or vulgar dialogues
                     during the performance, by anyone of the participants.
</p><p id="p_8">                              (c) Double meaning songs should not be played so as to spoil the minds

http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                            3</span>

                     of students and youths.
</p><p id="p_9">                             (d) No dance or songs, touching upon any political party or religion or
                     community or caste shall be played.
</p><p id="p_10">                             (e) No flex boards in support of any political party or communal leader,
                     shall be erected at the premises of the programme.
</p><p id="p_11">                             (f) The function shall not affect either religious or communal harmony
                     and shall be conducted without any discrimination based on caste.
</p><p id="p_12">                             (g) If there is violation of any one of the conditions imposed, the
                     concerned police officer is at liberty to take necessary action, as per law and
                     stop such performance forthwith; and
</p><p id="p_13">                             (h) Similarly, the police is directed to stop the dance programme, if it
                     is played beyond the permitted time limit.
</p><p id="p_14">                             (i) The respondent is directed to issue necessary permission,
                     incorporating the above conditions.
</p>
