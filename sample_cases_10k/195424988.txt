<div class="doc_title">Dayanand Pandey &amp; Anr vs State Of Bihar on 7 December, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                       Cr.Misc. No.35875 of 2009
     1. DAYANAND PANDEY S/O -LATE JAGDEO PANDEY VILL- BADNA, P.S-
        RAGHUNATHPUR, DISTT- SIWAN.
    2. GOVIND SARAN PANDEY S/O- DAYANAND PANDEY VILL- BADNA, P.S-
       RAGHUNATHPUR, DISTT- SIWAN...................................................Petitoners.
                                                            Versus
       THE STATE OF BIHAR...................................................................Opposite Party.
                                                         -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2       07.12.2009.                           Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and

                               counsel appearing for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                             Petitioners are apprehending their arrest in a case

                               under <a href="/doc/1586755/" id="a_1">Sections 460</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_2">326</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_3">307</a>, <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_4">380</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal

                               Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                             Considering the allegation of assault in the First

                               Information Report and the injuries, I am not inclined to grant

                               anticipatory bail to the petitioners. The court below may

                               consider their regular bail application and disposed of the same

                               expeditiously if the petitioners surrender in the court below

                               without being prejudiced by this order.




</p><pre id="pre_2">             U.K.                                                                (Sheema Ali Khan,J)
