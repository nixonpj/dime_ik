                 Writ Appeal No.209/2017
               (Commissioner, Municipal Corporation, Ujjain
                                  Versus
         Mohammad Shakir Khan s/o Shri Mohammad Shabbir &amp; another)
Indore, Dated 22.11.2017
    Shri B.L. Jain, learned counsel for the appellant.
     Shri Sanjay Zamindar, learned counsel for
respondent No.1 / writ petitioner.
     Shri Rohit Mangal, learned Government Advocate
for respondent No.2 / State of Madhya Pradesh.
     Heard on the question of admission.
                             ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">      This intra court appeal under Section 2 (1) of
Madhya Pradesh Uchcha Nyalayaya (Khand Nyay
Peeth Ko Appeal) Adhiniyam, 2005 has been filed by
the appellant (respondent No.2 in writ petition)
against order dated 07.09.2016 passed in Writ Petition
No.1079/2016, whereby the learned Writ Court
allowed the writ petition.
</p><p id="p_2">2.   Learned counsel for the appellant - Ujjain
Municipal Corporation submits that there is no post of
Junior Mechanic in the Corporation and this question
was taken in the reply filed before the learned Writ
Court, but the same has not been considered.
</p><p id="p_3">3.   Considering the aforesaid, learned counsel for the
appellant prays for withdrawal of the writ appeal with
liberty to file a review petition before the learned Writ
Court.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                          2</span>


<p id="p_4">             4.   Prayer allowed.
</p><p id="p_5">             5.   With   the    aforesaid         liberty,                       Writ   Appeal
             No.209/2017 is dismissed.</p><pre id="pre_2">
