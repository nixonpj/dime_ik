<div class="doc_title">Pulivarthi Cornelius vs The State Of Andhra Pradesh on 24 July, 2018</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: M.Satyanarayana Murthy</div>

<pre id="pre_1"> THE HON'BLE SRI JUSTICE M.SATYANARAYANA MURTHY

               CRIMINAL PETITION No.6451 of 2018

ORDER:
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      This Criminal Petition under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. is filed to quash

the proceedings in C.C.No.665 of 2016 on the file of Additional

Judicial First Class magistrate, Gudiwada, Krishna District, Andhra

Pradesh, registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Sections 120-B</a>,

<a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5">471</a>, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_6">409</a>, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_7">420</a> r/w 34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_8">IPC</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      The case of the prosecution in brief is that the petitioner

fabricated/forged a Will dt. 14.10.2007 allegedly executed by the

father of the petitioner, but a written report was lodged on

07.04.2011 and after completion of investigation, the police

concerned filed charge sheet on 06.09.2016.


</p><p id="p_4">      The contention of the learned counsel for the petitioner before

this Court is that the De facto complainant also fabricated another

Will Dt. 06.10.2007, which is subject matter of C.C.No.16 of 2016

pending on the file of Additional Judicial First Class Magistrate,

Gudivawa.      Though the counsel raised several grounds in the

petition, he fairly requested this Court to direct the Magistrate

concerned to dispose of C.C.No.665 of 2016 and C.C.No.16 of 2016

pending    against   this   petitioner/accused    and    the   de   facto

complainant herein registered for various offences by contending

joint trial as the basis for registering the Charge sheet is only

forgery/fabrication of two different wills by this petitioner and the

complainant. But, this Court cannot direct the trial Court for joint trial

of both cases. However, this Court is competent to issue a direction
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   2</span>


for simultaneous trial and pronouncement of judgment on the same

day by exercising power under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_9">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C.,


       Learned Public Prosecutor for the State of Andhra Pradesh

reported no objection to issue such direction.


</p><p id="p_5">      Taking into consideration the fact and circumstances of the

case and nature of the offences, the Additional Judicial First Class

Magistrate is directed to dispose of both the cases i.e., C.C.No.665 of

2016 and C.C.No.16 of 2011 pending before him simultaneously and

decide on the same day by two independent judgments.


</p><p id="p_6">      With the above directions, this Criminal Petition is disposed of

without touching the grounds urged in the Petition.


</p><p id="p_7">      As a sequel, miscellaneous applications pending, if any, shall
stand closed.
</p><p id="p_8">                                       ______________________________
                                       M. SATYANARAYANA MURTHY,J

Dt: 24-07-2018
eha
