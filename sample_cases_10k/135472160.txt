<div class="doc_title">The Punjabi Cooperative L &amp; C ... vs A. Venu Prasad on 15 February, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">223          IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                      AT CHANDIGARH

                                         COCP No.2154 of 2018
                                         Date of Decision: 15.2.2019

The Punjabi Cooperative Labour and Construction Society Limited
                                               .....Petitioner
                                Versus
A. Venu Prasad                                  .....Respondent

CORAM: HON'BLE MS. JUSTICE NIRMALJIT KAUR

Present:Mr. Raman Sharma, Advocate, for the petitioner.
        Mr. Kanisth Ganeriwala, Assistant Advocate General, Punjab.
NIRMALJIT KAUR, J. (ORAL)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             The present contempt petition has been filed for non-

compliance of the order dated 9.10.2017, vide which direction was issued to

decide the legal notice of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Short reply by way of affidavit of A. Venu Prasad, Principal

Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Local Government,

Punjab dated 15.2.2019 filed in Court is taken on record. As per the reply,

the order has been complied with by way of passing a speaking order dated

28.1.2019 (Anneuxre R-1), vide which claim of the petitioner has been

accepted and direction has been issued to the Municipal Council,

Kapurthala to disburse the payment.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             In view of the above, the present contempt petition is

dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             Rule issued against the respondent stands discharged.

             The amount be deposited in the account of the petitioner within

two weeks from today.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                                                      (NIRMALJIT KAUR)
15.2.2019                                                 JUDGE
