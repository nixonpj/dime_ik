<div class="doc_title">Mahesh Yadav vs The State Of Jharkhand on 12 August, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE   HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND                AT     RANCHI

                           B.A. No. 4560 of 2014
                  Mahesh Yadav                    ... ...   Petitioner
                                      Versus
                 The State of Jharkhand     ..      Opposite Party

                                         ------
                 CORAM:      HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PRASHANT KUMAR
                                         ------
                 For the Petitioner:   Mr. K.P. Deo, Adv.
                 For the Opp. Party:   Md. Hatim, A.P.P.
                                             ------

3/12.08.2014</pre><p id="p_1">           Bail application filed by petitioner Mahesh Yadav is

                 moved by Sri K.P. Deo, learned counsel for the petitioner and

                 opposed by Md. Hatim, learned Additional P.P. for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                       This is a case under <a href="/doc/764237/" id="a_1">Section 394</a> of I.P.C. and under

                 <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_2">Section 27</a> of Arms Act, triable by a Court of Magistrate. It

                 appears from the impugned order that petitioner implicated in

                 this case on the basis of his own confessional statement made

                 before the police. Petitioner is in custody from 25.4.2014.

</p><p id="p_3">                       Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstance, I allow

                 this application and direct the court below to enlarge the

                 petitioner, above named, on bail on his furnishing bail bond of

                 Rs.10,000/-(Ten thousand) with two sureties of like amount

                 each to the satisfaction of learned Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class,

                 Godda in connection with Poraiyahat P.S. Case No. 49 of 2014,

                 corresponding to G.R. No. 402 of 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                                                     (Prashant Kumar, J.)



     Sudhir
