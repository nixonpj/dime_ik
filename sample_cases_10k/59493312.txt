<div class="doc_title">Rajinder Singh vs State Of Punjab on 9 May, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">CRM-M-15408-2017                                           -1-

     IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                  AT CHANDIGARH

                                         CRM-M No.15408 of 2017
                                         Date of decision: 09.05.2017

Rajinder Singh                                             ... Petitioner

                                   Versus

State of Punjab                                            ... Respondent

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JITENDRA CHAUHAN

Present:     Mr. Gulshan Sharma, Advocate
             for the petitioner.

          Mr. R.S. Nain, AAG, Punjab assisted by
          Mr. Satpal, ASI.
                               ****
Jitendra Chauhan, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             By filing the present petition under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the

Code of Criminal Procedure, the petitioner has sought regular bail in

FIR No.20 dated 17.01.2015, registered under <a href="/doc/1046310/" id="a_2">Sections 15</a>, <a href="/doc/325366/" id="a_3">18</a>, <a href="/doc/1566465/" id="a_4">21</a>

and <a href="/doc/263398/" id="a_5">22</a> of the Narcotic Drugs and <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_6">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>, 1985

(for short '<a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_7">NDPS Act</a>'), at Police Station City Ferozepur, District,

Ferozepur.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             It is contended that recovery of NDPS substance i.e. 300

grams of Alprazolam was not recovered from the conscious

possession of the petitioner. This recovery was effected in pursuance

of the secret information from the house of the petitioner which is

not in exclusive possession of the petitioner. The petitioner is 19

years of age who was admitted to interim bail awaiting the receipt of

the FSL report. The petitioner did not misuse the concession of bail.

</p><p id="p_3">                On the other hand, the learned State counsel opposes the

prayer of bail.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                I have heard learned counsel for the parties and perused

the record.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                Considering the fact that petitioner is not a previous

convict and the recovery was not effected from the conscious

possession; the FIR has been registered against all the seven persons

including family members and relatives of the petitioner.            The

challan stands presented, however, the charge has not yet been

framed; the conclusion of trial will take a long time, without

adverting to the merits of the instant case, this petition is allowed.

The petitioner be admitted to bail during the pendency of the trial, on

his furnishing bail bonds and surety bonds, to the satisfaction of the

Chief Judicial Magistrate/Duty Magistrate, Ferozepur.

</p><p id="p_6">                Anything said herein above shall not be construed as an

expression of opinion of the Court on the merits of the case.</p><pre id="pre_2">
