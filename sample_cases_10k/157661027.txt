<div class="doc_title">Harinder Dhaka vs . Praveen Kumar on 3 April, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE COURT OF MS. ADITI GARG, M.M­01 (NE)
                     KARKARDOOMA COURTS, DELHI

CC No. : 220/17
P.S : Bhajanpura
Harinder Dhaka Vs. Praveen Kumar

ORDERS ON APPLICATION U/S 156(3) CR.P.C.
03.04.2019
1.</pre><p id="p_1">    Briefly stated, it is the case of the complainant that the accused was known to
him and the accused was having an Innova car which was hypothecated with HDFC
Bank. In the year 2015, accused was in the need for money and therefore, he asked
the complainant to pay the installments towards the card assuring him that he would
transfer the car in the name of the complainant. Complainant submits that he had paid
the installment directly to the bank. However, thereafter, the accused did not transfer
the documents. It is further stated that the accused had put two different signatures on
form 29 and 30, hence, transport authorities are not transferring the vehicle in the
name of the complainant. It is further alleged that the accused is having two different
PAN cards.
</p><p id="p_2">2.    Status report has been received in which it is stated that the matter is primarily
of civil nature.
</p><p id="p_3">3.    Arguments heard on the application.
</p><p id="p_4">4.    Considering the allegations that the accused had cheated the complainant by
not transferring the car and also procuring two PAN, I direct the SHO to register the
FIR under relevant sections of law. Put up for status report on 25.04.2019.</p><pre id="pre_2">
