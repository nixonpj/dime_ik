<pre id="pre_1">                                                    1

           IN THE COURT OF SH.SANJAY GARG:JUDGE:MACT:
                            KKD: DELHI.
SUIT No. 336/06


                     NEPALI CHAUDHARY V/S.  SITARUDDIN   &amp; ORS. 
 
                                                        
AWARD 


1.                 Nepali Chaudhary received   injuries in a road side accident on  1.6.06

    involving       Motorcycle   No.   DL­7S­AG­2991   (hereinafter   called   the   offending

    vehicle)     .     The  plea   of   the  petitioner  is  that     accident  took place  due  to  sole

    negligence of the offending vehicle.  Petitioner  has claimed compensation of  Rs.  5

    lacs  from the respondents   being   owner and insurer of the offending vehicle.   </pre>

<p id="p_1">2.               R1     filed   appearance   through   counsel   and  was   suppose   to  file   written

    statement on the next date of hearing i.e. 6.3.07 but the claim stands compromised

    today.    R2, the ICICI  Lombard General Insurance Co. Ltd. filed written statement

    admitting that the offending vehicle was insured with it. 

</p><p id="p_2">3.             During conciliation   Sh.S.K. Tyagi, Ld. counsel for R2  offered lump sum

    compensation of Rs.50,000/­ to the petitioner   for full and final settlement of the

    claim.    Petitioner   agreed to that.   Statement of both the  parties recorded  to this

    effect.     The   compromise   being   voluntary   and     the   compensation   amount   being

    reasonable, it   is accepted.     I thereby pass an award for a sum of Rs. 50,000/­   in

    favour of the petitioner  and against the respondent No.2. 

</p><p id="p_3">4.             R1      not   being   party   to   compromise   has   no   liability   to   pay   any

    compensation amount.
</p>
<p id="p_4">5.             R­2   is, thereby, directed to deposit the award amount within one month

    from today by way of crossed cheque with the Tribunal. 

</p><p id="p_5">6.               The   award   amount   be   released   to   the   petitioner   on   deposit   with   the

    Tribunal.   
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">ANNOUNCED IN THE OPEN                                           (SANJAY GARG)
COURT ON   9.2.2007                                                JUDGE MACT:KKD:DELHI
(TWO CC ATTACHED)
