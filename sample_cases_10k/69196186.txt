                                         ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 02-04-2019

     Heard Mr. S. Ahmed, the learned counsel for the appellant, who submits that the
appellant being aggrieved with the Judgment &amp; Order dated 20.11.2018, passed by the
learned Member, Motor Accidents Claims Tribunal No. 3, Kamrup (M) in MAC Case No.
2451/2015 has filed the instant appeal seeking enhancement of the compensation. The
learned counsel submits that although the claimant exhibited receipt and vouchers, which
                                                                              Page No.# 2/2

were to the tune of Rs. 70,000/-, the learned Tribunal however has only awarded the lump
sum amount of Rs. 20,000/-. He further submits that the evidence led by the claimant's
witness also has not been falsified by the opposite party.

</p><p id="p_2">     In view of above, let appeal be admitted.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     Office to requisition the LCR from the Tribunal concerned.

     Issue notice to the respondent, returnable in 4 (four) weeks.

      Appellant to take steps for service of notice upon the respondent by Registered post

with A/D due within 3 (three) days.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     List the case again after a month.
</p>
