<div class="doc_title">Sunil Kumar @ Sunil Kumar Mishra vs The State Of Bihar on 6 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.11202 of 2019
                          Arising Out of PS. Case No.-98 Year-2017 Thana- DELHA District- Gaya
                 ======================================================
                 Sunil Kumar @ Sunil Kumar Mishra S/o Late Awdhesh Mishra resident of
                 village-Kharkhura, Bhat Bigha, P.S-Delha and Distt.-Gaya.

                                                                                   ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :        Mr. Manish Kumar No2, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s :        Mr. Manoj Kumarm, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE BIRENDRA KUMAR
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   06-05-2019                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                     The petitioner is languishing in custody for the

                   offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the Indian Penal

                   Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Marriage of this petitioner was solemnized in the

                   year 2005 with Sujata Kumari. Allegation is that the petitioner

                   committed dowry death on 29.04.2017.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  Submission of learned counsel for the petitioner is

                   that for the occurrence of death on 29.04.2017, FIR was lodged

                   on 12.05.2017. In fact the victim had sustained fire injury while

                   cooking the food on 12.03.2017. She was immediately rushed

                   to NMCH, Gaya and thereafter, Appolo Burns Hospital, Patna
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.11202 of 2019(3) dt.06-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     where she was undergoing treatment and after discharge, went

                     to her parent's house where she died.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                         Considering the aforesaid fact, let the petitioner,

                     above named, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of

                     Rs.20,000/- (Twenty Thousand) with two sureties of the like

                     amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Court-below

                     where the case is pending in connection with Delha P.S. Case

                     No. 98 of 2017, with following conditions:

</p><p id="p_6">                                   (a)      Both the bailors shall be the resident of

                     territorial jurisdiction of the learned Court-below having

                     immovable property.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                  (b) The petitioner shall fully cooperate with the

                     investigation/trial of the case, failing which the court below

                     shall be at liberty to cancel the bail bond of the petitioner.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                                          (Birendra Kumar, J)

Banti/Rajan

U      T
 </pre>
