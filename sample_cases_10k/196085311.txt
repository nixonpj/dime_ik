<p id="p_1">      Petitioner challenges Ext.P4 by which conditional order of

stay has been granted. Petitioner is called upon to remit 20% of

the balance tax and furnish security for the remaining amount.

</p><p id="p_2">      2.    Perusal of Ext.P4 would show that the appellate

authority had considered the matter in proper perspective and

had given appropriate concession to the petitioner while granting

stay. I do not find any illegality in the matter warranting

interference by this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      This writ petition is, hence, dismissed.             The petitioner is

however granted four weeks' time to comply with Ext.P4 order.
