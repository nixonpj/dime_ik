</p><p id="p_3">  1. RULE.      Learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives service of

     Rule on behalf of the respondentÂ­State.

</p><p id="p_4">  2. The present application is filed by the applicant seeking extension

     of temporary bail granted by this Court by an order dated

     18.01.2019 in Criminal Miscellaneous Application No.894/2019.

</p><p id="p_5">  3. On the ground of agricultural work, therefore prayed for

     reasonable extension and submitted under instructions that

     henceforth, no further extension will be sought for on this

     ground.
</p>
<p id="p_6">  4. Learned APP drew attention of this Court to the jail record to

     indicate that the applicant was recently released on temporary bail

     on the ground of death in family.
</p>

</p><p id="p_9">    5. It would be appropriate that as a last opportunity, extension is

         granted for a further period of FIVE(5)days.

</p><p id="p_10">    6. In view of the aforesaid,the application is partly allowed. The

         temporary bail granted earlier is extended for a further period

         of FIVE DAYS from today.          It is made clear that no further

         extension will be granted to the accused.

</p><p id="p_11">    7. The applicant shall surrender to the Jail authorities on expiry of the

         temporary bail period. Rule is made absolute. Direct service is

         permitted.
