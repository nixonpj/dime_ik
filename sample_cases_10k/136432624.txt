                             IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

                                                   DATED : 16.08.2019

                                                        CORAM

                               THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE P.D.AUDIKESAVALU

                                                   W.P.No.23065 of 2019

                      R.Gnanothayam                                                ... Petitioner

                                                          -Vs-

                      1. The District Collector,
                         Dharmapuri,
                         Dharmapuri District.

                      2. The Tahsildar,
                         Harur Taluk,
                         Dharmapuri District.                                   ... Respondents

                      Prayer : Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of
                      India, praying to issue a Writ of Mandamus, directing the Respondents
                      to include the name of the Petitioner in the Legal Heir Certificate
                      issued by the Second Respondent in proceeding No.Pa.Mu.1043/2016/A3
                      dated 05.04.2016 by considering the representation dated 21.06.2019.


                                  For Petitioner        : Mr.P.Ganesan
                                  For Respondents       : Mr. R. Venkatesh,
                                                          Government Advocate

                                                       ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                              The Writ Petition has been filed by the Petitioner to direct the

                      Respondents to include the Petitioner's name in the Legal Heirs


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      1/4</span>
http://www.judis.nic.in
                                                                               W.P.No.23065 of 2019

                      Certificate issued by the Second Respondent in proceedings No. Pa. Mu.

                      1043/2016/A3 dated 05.04.2016 by considering the representation dated

                      21.06.2019 made by the Petitioner.



</p><p id="p_2">                      2.      The Petitioner is the sister of the deceased R. Gnanapalam.

                      <a href="/doc/1968317/" id="a_2">Section 8</a> of the Hindu Succession Act, 1956, clearly envisages that only

                      when the mother of the deceased not being alive, the question of

                      considering the siblings as the Legal Heirs of the deceased would arise.

                      Since the mother of the deceased in the present case is alive, his

                      siblings could not be included as his legal heirs in the certificate issued

                      for that purpose. The Representation dated 21.06.2019 made by the

                      Petitioner has been rightly rejected by the Respondents and that

                      decision, which is impugned in this Writ Petition, does not warrant any

                      interference.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">                      3.      Accordingly, the Writ Petition is dismissed. No costs.
