<div class="doc_title">Dr. Pramod Tiwari vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 1 April, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                             WP-6183-2016
             (DR. PRAMOD TIWARI Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


01-04-2016

Shri Brindawan Tiwari, Advocate for petitioners.
Smt.J.Pandit, Deputy Government Advocate for respondent No.1.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Learned Deputy Government Advocate for respondent No.1 prays for
and is granted two weeks' time to file reply of the petition.
Issue notice to respondent Nos. 2 to 4 on payment of process fee by
tomorrow.
</p><p id="p_2">Notice be made returnable within two weeks.
</p><p id="p_3">Considered the prayer for grant of interim relief.
Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that the petitioners are on
the post of Guest Faculty for last 20 years. He submits that when the
earlier advertisement was issued in the year 2014 this Court had passed
the interim order on 7.10.2014 in WP No.15256/14, however, the said
advertisement was cancelled and therefore the petition could not be
decided finally.
</p><p id="p_4">Having considered the submissions made by learned counsel for the
parties and having gone through the averments made in the petition
and the grounds raised, I am inclined to grant the interim relief by
directing the respondents to accept the forms of the petitioners and to
allow them to appear in the examination for appointment of Assistant
Professor in pursuance to the advertisement dated 19.2.2016, if the
petitioners are otherwise qualified except in respect to the condition of
age and the condition of passing of NET/SLET. However, it is made clear
that the petitioners shall not be entitled to claim any equity on the basis
of their appearing in the examination on the basis of this interim order
and allowing them to appear in the examination will be subject to
further orders. It is also made clear that the petitioners' result will not be
declared without leave of this Court.
</p><p id="p_5">List on 20.4.2016 alongwith Writ Petition Nos.4992/2016, 4994/2016,
4996/2016.
</p><p id="p_6">Certified copy as per rules.
</p><p id="p_7"> (J.K. MAHESHWARI)
       JUDGE
