                                        ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.     Learned advocate Ms.Vasavdatta Bhatt for the applicant
         and learned advocate Mr.S.N. Kikani for the opponents (original
         claimants) state at bar that the applicant has deposited
         amount of compensation as per impugned judgment and
         award passed by the learned Tribunal and investment and
         disbursement is also effected as per directions issued in the
         impugned award.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.     It appears that opponent - owner is expired but, his
         presence is not necessary as legal heirs and representatives of

         the deceased - owner are not likely to be adversely affected by
         the order passed in the present application.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.       In light of the above position, learned Tribunal is directed
         to continue renewal of F.D.R. towards 60% amount from time
         to time till final disposal of appeal. However, it is clarified that
         the opponents (original claimants) will be at liberty to withdraw
         periodical interest that may accrue on such F.D.R. from time to
         time till final disposal of appeal. The learned Tribunal shall
         keep original F.D.R. in custody of Nazir of the learned Tribunal
         and in an unincumbent position till further orders.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.       The present application stands disposed of accordingly.
         Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p>
