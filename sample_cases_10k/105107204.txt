<p id="p_1">       The present application is filed seeking condonation of delay
caused in filing First Appeal No.5876 of 2019.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       Having regard to the submissions advanced by the learned
advocate for the applicant and the averments made in the application,
the delay of 23 days is required to be condoned and the same is hereby
condoned. The present application stands disposed of accordingly.
</p>
