_____________________________________________________

                        Mr.Vivek Goyal.

_____________________________________________________
          HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PANKAJ BHANDARI
                                Order
03/01/2018

</p><p id="p_5">1.   Petitioners have filed these IInd bail applications under

<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section     439</a> of Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">2.   F.I.R. No.281/2017 was registered at Police Station Anti-

Corruption Bureau, Jaipur, Chauki A.C.B. Tonk for offence under
                                 (2 of 2)
                                                     [ CRLMB-17703/2017]



Sections 7, 13(1) (D), 13 (2) of <a href="/doc/1331755/" id="a_2">Prevention of Corruption Act</a>,

1988 and 120-B <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">IPC</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_7">3.    It is contended by counsel for the petitioners that after

rejection of the first bail application, charge sheet has been filed.

The matter pertains to bribe amounting to Rs.4400/-. The trial

would consume time.
</p>
<p id="p_8">4.    Learned    Public   Prosecutor       has   opposed    these      bail

applications.
</p>
<p id="p_9">5.    I have considered the contentions.

</p><p id="p_10">6.    Considering the contentions put forth by counsel for the

petitioners, I deem it proper to allow the IInd bail applications.

</p><p id="p_11">7.    These IInd bail applications is accordingly allowed and it is

directed that accused petitioners shall be released on bail provided

they furnish a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees

Fifty Thousand only) together with two sureties in the sum of

Rs.25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand only) each                to the

satisfaction of the learned trial court with the stipulation that they

shall appear before that Court and any court to which the matter

is transferred, on all subsequent dates of hearing and as and when

called upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_12">8.    A copy of this order be placed in the connected file.




</p><p id="p_13">                                            (PANKAJ BHANDARI), J.
</p>
