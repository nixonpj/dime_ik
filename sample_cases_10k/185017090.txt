<div class="doc_title">Amit Kumar @ Gapu @ Amith Kr. @ Gappu vs The State Of Bihar on 13 September, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.46345 of 2018
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -167 Year- 2018 Thana -GANDHIMAIDAN District- PATNA
                 ======================================================
                 1. Amit Kumar @ Gapu @ Amith Kr. @ Gappu, S/o Vinod Kumar Yadav,
                 Resident of Mohalla- Salimpur Ahara, P.S.- Gandhi Maidan, District- Patna.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s    : Mr. Ranjan Kumar Sharma
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Sri Panchanand Pandit
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   13-09-2018               Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             Petitioner seeks bail in Gandhi Maidan P.S.Case No.167

                 of 2018 registered for the offences under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a>, <a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_2">201</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_3">120B</a>

                 of the I.P.C. and Section 27 of the Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             Petitioner is not named in the F.I.R. His name transpired

                 during course of investigation only on the basis of confessional

                 statement of co-accused Amar Kumar.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             Submission of the learned counsel for the petitioner is

                 that from confessional statement of co-accused it is apparent that

                 he has not participated in the occurrence. He is in custody since

                 31.5.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Heard learned A.P.P. also.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                             Having heard both sides and in view of the facts and

                 circumstances, as stated above, let the petitioner, above named, be
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.46345 of 2018 (2) dt.13-09-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                      released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.25,000/- with two

                      sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of A.C.J.M. VI,

                      Patna, in Gandhi Maidan P.S. Case No.167 of 2018 subject to the

                      conditions that (1) one of the bailors must be a local person having

                      sufficient immovable property within the jurisdiction of the

                      concerned court. (ii) the petitioner will not induce any witness or

                      tamper with the evidence and the petitioner shall co-operate in the

                      disposal of the trial and make himself available as and when

                      required by the court, otherwise, his bail bond shall be cancelled.



</p><p id="p_7">                                                  (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
AnilKrSinha/-
</p>
