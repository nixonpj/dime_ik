           application alleging violation of the earlier order of this Court.

</p><p id="p_5">                 Mr. Bhattacharyya, prays for extension of time to file the

           affidavit-in-reply. Such prayer is allowed.

           being CPAN 2516 of 2014, appear under the same heading in
