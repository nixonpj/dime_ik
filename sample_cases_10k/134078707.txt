<p id="p_1">6   02-09-2014                 Counter Affidavit filed today on behalf of the Opposite

                 Party No.2 be kept on the record.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               The Petitioner seeks quashing of the First Information

                 Report of Bibhutipur P.S. case No.237 of 2013 pending before

                 A.C.J.M., Rosera, Samastipur.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               It has been submitted that there is absolutely no

                 material against the Petitioner and, therefore, he should not be

                 proceeded against.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               Since the investigation should be rarely interfered with,

                 I am not inclined to interfere in the matter.

</p><p id="p_5">                               The application is disposed of with a direction to the

                 Investigating Officer to consider the aforesaid facts and conclude

                      the investigation positively within a period of three months from

                      the date of receipt of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                     Let this order be communicated to the Superintendent

                      of Police, Samastipur through FAX at the cost of the Petitioner for

                      its onward transmission to the Investigating Officer in connection

                      with Bibhutipur P.S. case No.237 of 2013 pending before the court

                      of A.C.J.M., Rosera, Samastipur.
</p>
