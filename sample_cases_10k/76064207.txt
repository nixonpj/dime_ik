<p id="p_1">     This petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Cr.P.C.

seeking to quash Annexures A1 and A2 which are the FIR

and Final Report respectively in C.R.No.32 of 2012 of the

Udumbanchola Excise Range. When the matter came up for

hearing it is submitted by the learned counsel that in view of

the settled pronouncement of this Court covering the issue,

the petitioner is not intending to prosecute the instant

petition.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     2. On account of the non appearance of the petitioner

before the learned Magistrate, the case against him was

split and was transferred to the Register of long pending

cases and entered as L.P.No.8 of 2015 on the file of the

Judicial First Class Magistrate, Nedumkandom. Non bailable

warrants of arrest are pending against the petitioner. This

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">Crl.M.C. No. 1886 of 2016      -2-</span>


Court will not by justified in directing the enlargement of the

petitioner on bail without ascertaining the circumstances

under which the petitioner remained absent before the

learned Magistrate.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3. Heard the learned Public Prosecutor as well.

        Accordingly  this Criminal    Miscellaneous   Case   is

disposed of permitting the petitioner to surrender before the

learned Magistrate and to file an application for regular bail

within a period of two weeks from          today with advance

notice to the Assistant Public prosecutor. In case he does so,

his bail application shall be considered and disposed of on

merits preferably on the same date on which the petition is

filed and uninfluenced by the warrants, if any, pending

against him.
</p>
