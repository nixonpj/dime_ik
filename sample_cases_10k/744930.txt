<div class="doc_title">Dharmveer Singh vs State Of U.P. on 29 July, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">Court No. - 28

Case :- BAIL No. - 5670 of 2010

Petitioner :- Dharmveer Singh
Respondent :- State Of U.P.
Petitioner Counsel :- Deep Singh,Dhirendra Singh
Respondent Counsel :- Govt.Advocate

Hon'ble Shri Kant Tripathi,J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">Heard the learned counsel for the applicant and the learned AGA and perused
the record.
</p><p id="p_2">The applicant, Dharmveer Singh has filed this bail application in case crime
no. 28 of 2010 under <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_1">sections 395</a>, <a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_2">397</a>, <a href="/doc/844968/" id="a_3">412</a>, <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_4">414</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_5">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_6">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_7">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_8">471</a> IPC,
P.S. Maanpur, District Sitapur.
</p>
<p id="p_3">The learned counsel for the applicant submitted that the recovery is false and
the applicant has been falsely implicated by the police. It was next submitted
that the engine number mentioned in the FIR does not tally with the engine
number mentioned in the recovery memo.
</p><p id="p_4">The learned AGA on the other hand submitted that there is no difference
between the engine number and chasis number mentioned in the FIR as well
as in the recovery memo.
</p><p id="p_5">Keeping in view the facts and circumstances of the case, complicity of the
applicant and nature of evidence, I do not find it proper to enlarge the
applicant on bail, therefore, the bail application is rejected.
Order Date :- 29.7.2010
MTA
 </p>
