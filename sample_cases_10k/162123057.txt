<p id="p_1">1.0      By way of present application, the applicants have prayed to condone 

the delay of 401 days in preferring the Second Appeal (Stamp) No.38 of 2015. 



2.0      I   have   heard   learned   advocate   appearing   on   behalf   of   the 

applicant and perused the reasons mentioned in the present application 

for   non­filing   the   appeal   in   time.   Considering   the   facts   and 

circumstances   of   the   case,   the   present   application   is   allowed.   Delay 

caused  in preferring  the Second  Appeal  is hereby  condoned.  Rule  is 

made absolute accordingly.
