      IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD


      FIRST APPEAL No 2174 of 2002
      				with
      		CIVIL APPLICATION NO.7646 OF 2002

      --------------------------------------------------------------
      KANJIBHAI JIVABHAI BARAVADA
 Versus
      HEIRS OF DECD.LILABHAI        BHIMABHAI KOLI
      --------------------------------------------------------------
      Appearance:
      1. First Appeal No. 2174 of 2002
           MR HRIDAY BUCH for Petitioner No. 1
           .......... for Respondent No. 1,1/4
           MS YAMINI J DESAI for Respondent No. 1/1-1/3
           MR HEMANT S SHAH for Respondent No. 2
           NOTICE SERVED for Respondent No. 3

      --------------------------------------------------------------

               CORAM : MR.JUSTICE N.G.NANDI
                                  and
                       MR.JUSTICE D.A.MEHTA

               Date of Order: 13/03/2003

 ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">                    (Per : MR.JUSTICE N.G.NANDI)

      FIRST APPEAL NO.2174 OF 2002.
</p>      
<p id="p_2">      1.	Heard.
</p>      
<p id="p_3">      2.	In the order dated 3.2.2003  through  error  word
      "Notice of  admission"  has  been  mentioned.  Vide order
      dated  24.1.2003  notice  to  the  respondents  has  been
      issued.   The  order  dated  3.2.2003 is modified and the
      order to be read as "Notice  to  respondent"  instead  of
      "Notice  of  admission  to  the  respondent." Rest of the
      order remains the same.
</p>      
<p id="p_4">      3.	Admit.
</p>      
      
      
      
<p id="p_5">      CIVIL APPLICATION NO.7646 OF 2002.
</p>      
<p id="p_6">      1.	Heard. 
</p>      
<p id="p_7">      2.	It is stated by Mr.H.C.Buch, learned advocate for
      the  applicant that opponent no.3 - Jaitabhai N. Rabari -
      the driver be deleted from the array of parties  in  this
      civil application. Opponent no.3 is accordingly deleted.
</p>      
<p id="p_8">      2.	Rule.  Ms.  Yamini Desai, learned advocate waives
      service  and  accepts  notice  on behalf of opponent nos.
      1/1/ to 1/4  and  Mr.H.S.Shah,  learned  advocate  waives
      service and accepts notice on behalf of opponent no.2.
</p>      
<p id="p_9">      3.	On applicant depositing the entire awarded amount
      together  with  cost  and  interest  within  4 weeks from
      today, operation / execution of the impugned award  shall
      remain stayed  till  the  next  date.    To  come  up  on
      22/4/2003.
</p>      
<p id="p_10">      4.	It is stated by Mr.H.C.Buch, learned advocate for
      the  applicant  that  the  amount  of  Rs.25,000/-  under
      <a href="/doc/147367599/" id="a_1">Section  173</a> of the M.V.Act, is deposited with the claims
      Tribunal.
</p>      
<p id="p_11">      5.	It  is stated by Mr.H.C.Buch, leaned advocate for
      the applicant that Insurance Company has filed  execution
      application  before  the  claims Tribunal for recovery of
      the amount of Rs.25,000/-, deposited under <a href="/doc/32775809/" id="a_2">Section 140</a> of
      the M.V.Act. 
</p>      
<p id="p_12">      6.	It is stated by Mr.H.S.Shah, learned advocate for
      the opponent no.2 - Insurance  company  that  he  is  not
      aware  of any such execution application filed before the
      claims Tribunal.  It is further stated that if  even  any
      such  execution  application  is  filed  by the Insurance
      Company, same shall not be proceeded further.
</p>      
      
       
<p id="p_13">        					(N.G.Nandi,J.)
        
        					(D.A.Mehta,J.)
        
      (vipul)



</p>
