<div class="doc_title">Nirmala Rathore vs State Of Rajasthan on 28 September, 2018</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Vinit Kumar Mathur</div>

<p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
      S.B. Criminal Misc. 2nd Bail Application No. 9054/2018

Smt. Nirmala Rathore D/o Shri Kalu Lal, aged about 34 years,
B/c Mali, R/o Near Baba Ramdev Temple, Kherwara, Distt.
Udaipur.
</p><p id="p_2">(Presently lodged in Central Jail, Udaipur.)
</p><p id="p_3">                                                      ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State of Rajasthan
</p><p id="p_4">                                                    ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)       :   Mr. Shambhoo Singh
For Respondent(s)       :   Mr. K.V. Vyas, Public Prosecutor



        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VINIT KUMAR MATHUR

                               Order

28/09/2018


     The present 2nd bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section

439</a> Cr.P.C. on behalf of the petitioner who is in custody in

connection with F.I.R. No.26/2018, Police Station P.B.I. M &amp; H

Jaipur for the offences under Sections 5(2), 23 &amp; 25 PCPNDT Act,

1994 and <a href="/doc/1637773/" id="a_2">Sections 315</a>/<a href="/doc/1185693/" id="a_3">511</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_4">420</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120-B</a> of IPC.

</p><p id="p_5">     The first bail application of the petitioner was rejected on

27.07.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned Public

Prosecutor. Perused the material available on record.

</p><p id="p_7">     It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that

after rejection of the first bail application of the petitioner, the

challan has been filed in the matter and the conclusion of trial will
                                                                    (2 of 2)             [CRLMB-9054/2018]


                                   take sufficiently long time, therefore, it is prayed that the

                                   petitioner may be enlarged on bail.

</p><p id="p_8">                                         The learned Public Prosecutor vehemently opposes the bail

                                   application.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                         Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case and

                                   upon a consideration of the arguments advanced, this Court is of

                                   the opinion that the 2nd bail application filed by the petitioner

                                   deserves to be accepted.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                                         Consequently, the 2nd bail application is allowed. It is ordered

                                   that the accused-petitioner Smt. Nirmala Rathore D/o Shri Kalu

                                   Lal, arrested in connection with F.I.R. No.26/2018, Police Station

                                   P.B.I. M &amp; H Jaipur shall be released on bail; provided she

                                   furnishes a personal bond of Rs.50,000/- with two surety bonds of

                                   Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial court with

                                   the stipulation to appear before that Court on all dates of hearing

                                   and as and when called upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_11">


</p><p id="p_12">                                                                              (VINIT KUMAR MATHUR),J




                                   Ramesh/63
