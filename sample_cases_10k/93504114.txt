<div class="doc_title">Ramsahay vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 31 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                           1
                                                       M.Cr.C. No.8426/2017

                           ( Ramsahay Vs. State of MP)
       31.07.2017
             Shri Brajesh Tyagi , Advocate for applicant.
             Shri J.M. Sahani , Panel Lawyer for Respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Case Diary is perused.
</p><p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the rival parties are heard.
             The applicant has filed this 3 rd repeat bail application u/S 439,
       <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>. for grant of bail. The applicant has been arrested by Police
       Station Bargava , District Sheopur       in connection with Crime No.
       6/2013 registered in relation to the offences punishable u/Ss. 307,
       399 &amp; 402      of <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>,   Sec 25/27 of <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_3">Arms Act</a> and Sec 11/13 of
       M.P.D.V.P.K. Act.
</p><p id="p_3">             Learned Panel Lawyer for the State opposed the application
       and prayed for its rejection by contending that on the basis of the
       allegations and the material available on record, no case for grant of
       bail is made out.
</p><p id="p_4">             This third repeat bail application has been filed after rejection
       of the earlier ones including the last one which was dismissed by
       order dated 16.03.2017 passed in M.Cr.C. No. 2820/17 on merits and
       also after taking into account, applicant having absconded prior to his
       arrest on 23.11.2016.
</p><p id="p_5">             The offence is of murder and due to applicant's absconsion,
       trial has got unnecessarily delayed and therefore the ground of
       prejudice is not available to the applicant to be raised.
</p><p id="p_6">             In view of the above, no case for grant of bail is made out.
             Accordingly, the present 3rd repeat bail application is hereby
       rejected with liberty to the applicant to repeat his prayer for bail in
       case trial gets further delayed for reasons not attributed to the
       applicant.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                                (Sheel Nagu)
                                                                   Judge
