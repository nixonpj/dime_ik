<p id="p_1">           A judgment of conviction under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_1">Section 138</a> of the

Negotiable Instruments Act is under challenge in this revision.

Pending the proceedings, the parties came to terms amicably out

of court, and accordingly filed a composition. The composition

was accepted and recorded as part of the proceeding.             The

dispute stands resolved forever. Hence this revision petition is

allowed in terms of the composition. Accordingly, the conviction

and   sentence     against     the    revision     petitioner in C.C

No.2045/2008 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court I,

Thrissur, confirmed in appeal by the appellate court in Crl.A

No.167/2009, will stand set aside, and the revision petitioner will

stand released from prosecution, on the benefit of acquittal

under <a href="/doc/1876710/" id="a_2">Section 320(8)</a> Cr.P.C. The bail bond, if any, executed by

the revision petitioner will stand discharged. The amount, if any,

deposited by the revision petitioner in the trial court can be

returned to him.
</p>
