<p id="p_1">                Notice of motion.
</p><p id="p_2">                On the asking of the Court, Mr.Vinod Sharma, Sr.Panel
Counsel, accepts notice on behalf of respondent No.1, Mr.D.K.Singal,
Advocate for respondent No.2 and Mr.Jagmohan Ghuman, DAG, Punjab for
respondent No.3. Copies furnished.
</p><p id="p_3">                Learned counsel for the parties are ad idem that the matter in
issue is squarely covered by an order and judgement dated 06.09.2017,
rendered by this Court in CWP No.17010 of 2017 (Vinod Kumar Vs. The
State of Haryana and others) and other connected matters. Therefore, this
petition too be disposed of in the same terms.
</p><p id="p_4">                That being so, the petition is disposed of, in terms of the
decision, rendered by this Court in the case of Vinod Kumar (supra).
