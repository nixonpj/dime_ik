<pre id="pre_1">        C/SCA/17985/2016                                          ORDER



           IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

           R/SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 17985 of 2016

==========================================================
            GUJARAT STATE WAREHOUSING CORPORATION
                             Versus
                     RAHIMABAI SIDDIK RAYMA
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR PJ KANABAR(1416) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1
HETAL V CHAVDA(9032) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 3
JEET Y RAJYAGURU(8039) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1,2,3,4
==========================================================
 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.M.THAKER

                            Date : 01/05/2018
                             ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">      Learned advocate for the petitioner is not
present. Learned advocate for the respondent is
present.
</p><p id="p_2">      It    has      emerged      from          the    record     that     the
petitioner did not attend the proceedings before
the   Commissioner          of     Workmen's           Compensation         in
respect      of     Workman's       Compensation            (Fatal)       Case
No.15       of      2010     and       also           during      execution
proceedings. Under the circumstances, the Court
passed orders in absence of present petitioner.
Even the application seeking recall of the order
dated      10.4.2013        came      to        be    rejected       by    the
Commissioner.
</p><p id="p_3">      Despite        such    facts,        even        at   the     time    of
hearing of this petition, no one is present for
the   petitioner.           Any    request            for   pass-over       or
adjournment is also not made. The petitioner has


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  Page 1 of 2</span>
           C/SCA/17985/2016                                            ORDER



preferred            to       ignore          and         neglect        present
proceedings.
</p><p id="p_4">       Further, though until now, any interim relief
is not granted in this petition, the petitioner
has not complied the order dated 10.4.2013 which
is still valid and yet not set aside by competent
Court or recalled by the Commissioner.
</p><p id="p_5">       In this view of the matter, even otherwise,
the     petitioner            does     not          deserve     any     audience
because the order passed by the Commissioner is
not complied by the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_6">       Since         despite       such       facts,        the     petitioner
neglected            the      proceedings,                the   petition      is
dismissed            on      the     ground          of    non-prosecution.
Notice is discharged.
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                  (K.M.THAKER, J)
KAUSHIK D. CHAUHAN
