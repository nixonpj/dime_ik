                          Judgment / Order
27/01/2017

      Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_8">      The intra-court appeal filed by the State being SAW

No.1479/2011,        of    which   restoration    is   sought,    was

dismissed     for    non-compliance      of      the   order     dated

27.04.2011.
</p><p id="p_9">                              (2 of 2)
                                                     [WRES-53/2015]

         Upon perusal of the defects pointed out by the

  office, the intra-court appeal itself was filed with a delay

  of 366 days. Thus, in view of the fact appeal itself was

  filed after a delay of 366 days, and other defects pointed

  out by the office were not removed despite per-emptory

  order passed by this Court dated 27.04.2011, therefore,

  there is no question to restore the said appeal.

</p><p id="p_10">         Accordingly and in view of above, the present

  restoration application is hereby dismissed.




(KAILASH CHANDRA SHARMA), J. (GOPAL KRISHAN VYAS) J.
