<p id="p_1">      The petitioner has filed this Writ Petition challening Ext.P2

amendment brought to Rule 28 of the Foreign Liquor Rules and

Condition No.20 of the statutory form of FL 3 licence. As per the

impugned amendment, restrictions were placed on the right of

the petitioner to open the Bar after sunrise and before 8 a.m.

The Bars are also to be closed at 11 p.m. With respect to Bars

functioning within the limits of Municipal Corporations, Ext.P2

amendment had taken away the right to open a Bar after sunrise

and before 9 a.m. According to the petitioner, a Division Bench

of this Court has already set aside the amendments.

</p><p id="p_2">      2.   The learned Government Pleader also submits that the

point is covered in favour of the petitioner in view of the

judgment in W.A No.1516 of 2012 dated 27.09.2012.

</p><p id="p_3">      In view of the above, this Writ Petition is allowed. The

petitioner shall also be entitled to the benefits of the judgment

dated 27.09.2012 in Writ Appeal No.1516 of 2012.



</p><p id="p_4">                                    (K.SURENDRA MOHAN, JUDGE)
