           CORAM : HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE SURYA
                                      SURYA KANT
                   HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE SURINDER GUPTA
                                                    ****
           Present:            Mr. PS Bajwa, Addl. AG Punjab for the appellants
                               Mr. Munish Dadwal, Advocate for the respondent
                                                    ****
           SURYA KANT J. (ORAL)

           (1).                The only effective direction issued by the learned Single
           Judge vide order dated 03.11.2012 which is under appeal is to direct the
           competent authority to take a fresh decision regarding regularization of
           the period of absence of respondent from duty including after she was
           ordered to be suspended and some previous periods, if no decision with
           regard thereto has been taken so far. It is obvious that if a positive
           decision is taken, it shall have consequential reflections for the purpose
           of counting the 'qualifying service' of the respondent for pensionary
           benefits.
</p><p id="p_3">           (2).                Let appropriate decision in accordance with the Rules be
           taken by the competent authority within a period of three months from
           the date of receipt of a certified copy of this order, more-so when the
           respondent is already said to have represented the competent authority.
           (3).                With these observations and clarifications, the appeal stand
           dismissed.
</p><pre id="pre_2">           (4).                Dasti.
