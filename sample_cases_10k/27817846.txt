<p id="p_1">      When this matter is taken up for hearing, the learned

Counsel for the petitioners submitted that the petitioners retired

from service. Therefore, this Court feels that no relief can be

granted at this stage.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      Accordingly, the Writ Petition is dismissed as infructuous.

No costs.   Consequently, miscellaneous petitions pending, if

any, shall stand closed.
