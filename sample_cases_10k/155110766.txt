</pre><p id="p_1">A.B. CHAUDHARI, J (Oral)

             Heard learned counsel for the rival parties.

             Petitioner seeks regular bail in FIR No.113 dated

30.07.2016, under <a href="/doc/325366/" id="a_1">Section 18</a> of NDPS Act, registered at Police

Station Rahon, District Shaheed Bhagat Singh Nagar.

</p><p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

petitioner is in jail since 30.07.2016.

</p><p id="p_3">             Allegation against the petitioner is that 4 Kg. opium was

recovered from him during naka at disclosed place held by the police.

</p><p id="p_4">             Upon instructions from ASI Balwinder Singh, learned

counsel for the State submits that there is no other case is pending

against the petitioner.     He further submits that the trial has been

completed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             In that view of the matter, I think that there is no point in

keeping the petitioner in the jail. Hence, the petitioner be released on
