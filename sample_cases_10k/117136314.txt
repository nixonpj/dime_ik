  IN THE COURT OF DR. SHAHABUDDIN : ADDL. DISTT. &amp;  
     SESSIONS JUDGE,  DELHI (PRESENTLY PRESIDING 
     OVER AS ADDL. SESSIONS JUDGE, SPECIAL COURT  
   ELECTRICITY­I, EAST DISTT., KARKARDOOMA COURTS,  
                         DELHI) 
C.C. NO. 422/09
P.S. KARAWAL NAGAR, DELHI
U/S 135/138/150 OF THE ELECTRICITY ACT, 2003
CASE I.D. NO. 02402R0211972009

B.S.E.S. YAMUNA POWER LIMITED,
SHAKTI KIRAN BUILDING,
KARKARDOOMA, 
DELHI.       
                VS.
JAYANT BANSAL,
S/O NARENDER BANSAL,
R/O KH. NO. 205, MUKUND VIHAR,
MAIN ROAD, KARAWAL NAGAR,
DELHI­110094.

DATE OF INSTITUTION OF THE CASE                                      :11.07.2009
DATE ON WHICH JUDGMENT WAS RESERVED                                 :25.10.2012
DATE OF PASSING OF JUDGMENT                                         :25.10.2012

JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     1. A fresh complaint was filed in this case against accused Jayant 
C.C. NO. 422/09, P.S. KARAWAL NAGAR, BSES (YPL) VS. JAYANT BANSAL            page 1 of 3
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                 2</span>

           Bansal   in   before   this   court   on   11.07.2009   for   offences 

           punishable   U/s   135/138/150   of   the   <a href="/doc/132967048/" id="a_1">Electricity   Act</a>,   2003   for 

           taking action against the accused as per law.

</p><p id="p_2">     2. In   pre­summoning   evidence,   CW1   Sh.   Jitender   Shankar   and 

           CW2   Sh.   K.P.Singh   examined   themselves   and   thereafter   the 

           remaining   pre­summoning   evidence   of   the   complainant 

           company was closed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3. After perusal of the complaint and other documents relied upon 

           in support of the complaint supported with evidence of CW1 &amp; 

           CW2   respectively,   a   cognizance   was   taken   against   accused 

           Jayant Bansal for the offences punishable U/s 135/138/150 of 

           the <a href="/doc/132967048/" id="a_2">Electricity Act</a>, 2003 and accused was directed to appear 

           before this court to face the trial of this case.

</p><p id="p_4">     4. This case was fixed for today i.e. on 25.10.2012 for appearance 

           of the accused before this court today.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     5. It   was   stated   by   Sh.   Mukesh   Sharma,   A.M.   (Legal)   of   the 

           complainant company before this court today i.e on 25.10.2012 

           that   matter   has   been   amicably   settled   between   the   parties 

           through   Lok   Adalat   held   on   13.10.2012   as   per   terms   and 

           conditions   contained   in   the   order   of   concerned   Lok   Adalat 

C.C. NO. 422/09, P.S. KARAWAL NAGAR, BSES (YPL) VS. JAYANT BANSAL               page 2 of 3
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                                 3</span>

           dated 13.10.2012 and as such he wanted to withdraw this case 

           against the accused.
</p>
<p id="p_6">     6. Statement   of   Sh.   Mukesh   Sharma,   A.M.   (Legal)   of   the 

           complainant company was recorded today separately regarding 

           the settlement to the above effect.
</p>
<p id="p_7">     7. In   view   of   the   amicable   settlement   arrived   at   between   the 

           parties  and  in  view of the above mentioned statement  of Sh. 

           Mukesh   Sharma,   A.M.   (Legal)   of   the   complainant   company, 

           there is no need now to proceed further in this case against the 

           accused   and   the  matter  is   hereby  dismissed   as  settled  giving 

           liberty   to   the  complainant  company to file execution petition 

           against the accused in future for recovery of settled amount, if 

           the accused makes default in payment of the same in future, if 

           any.  With this order, accused Jayant Bansal is hereby acquitted. 

           His bail bond and surety bond, if any, are discharged.  Judicial 

           file be consigned to the record room as per rules after necessary 

           compliance.
</p>
<p id="p_8">ANNOUNCED IN THE OPEN COURT
TODAY ON  25th OCTOBER, 2012         ( DR. SHAHABUDDIN )
                    ASJ/ SPECIAL COURT ( ELECTRICITY­I)
                                                KKD/DELHI

C.C. NO. 422/09, P.S. KARAWAL NAGAR, BSES (YPL) VS. JAYANT BANSAL             page 3 of 3
