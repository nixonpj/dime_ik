34   05-11-2015                   Heard learned counsel for the parties.

<p id="p_3">                                  Pursuant to the earlier order of this Court, the officials

                    of the Vigilance Department are present in person.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  Mr. Ramakant Sharma, learned senior counsel,

                    appearing on their behalf, has produced a photocopy of the

                    proceeding book of the Governing Body of the college, which is

                    taken on record. Mr. Sharma also assures this Court that a copy of

                    the First Information Report and the charge-sheet, submitted by

                    the Vigilance Department against the Principal, namely, Radha

                    Mohan Rai and one another person, namely, Shivcharan Rai, shall
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_7">           Patna High Court CWJC No.7246 of 2008 (34) dt.05-11-2015                          8</span>




                       be filed tomorrow.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                     That being so, for the time, there would be no need for

                       the appearance of the Superintendent of Police, Vigilance and

                       other authorities.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                     This Court also must notice that pursuant to the earlier

                       order of this Court, the Registrar of the Bihar University, namely,

                       Mr. Ratnesh Mishra along with two of the official dealing with the

                       Ayurvedic, namely, Dr. Anil Kumar Shahi (Medical Officer) and

                       Mr. Deepak Kumar (Law Assistant), present today, are directed to

                       discuss the issue with learned AAG-2 and again appear before this

                       Court tomorrow when these cases shall be taken up at 1 O'clock.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                     Put up all these cases tomorrow (6.11.2015) at 1

                       O'clock.
</p>
