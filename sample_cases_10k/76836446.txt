</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 11-11-2019

       Heard Mr. P.J. Saikia, learned counsel for the writ petitioner.
       Apprehending illegal eviction from the plot of land under occupation the petitioner
has approached this Court, inter-alia alleging that the Estate Officer is seeking to evict the
petitioner without following the due process of law.
</p><p id="p_2">       Mr. A.K. Purkayastha, learned Sr. standing counsel, N.F. Railway seeks a week's
time for obtaining instruction.
</p><p id="p_3">       Prayer allowed.
</p><p id="p_4">       Let this matter be listed again on 20-11-2019 enabling Mr. Purkayastha to obtain
instruction.
</p><p id="p_5">       One extra copy of the writ petition be furnished to the learned standing counsel.
       Let this matter be listed again by reflecting the name of Mr. A.K. Purkayastha in the
cause list.
</p><p id="p_6">       Till the next date, status quo as on today, be maintained.
