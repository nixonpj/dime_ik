</p><p id="p_3">          The Court: Papers are not traceable. None appears for the plaintiff. No

accommodation is sought for.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      From the cause title it appears that the suit was required to be transferred

to the Debts Recovery Tribunal established under the provisions of the Recovery

of Debts due to Banks &amp; Financial Institutions Act, 1993.

</p><p id="p_5">      The suit is dismissed without any order as to costs. This order of dismissal

will not preclude the parties to agitate their grievances in the event the suit stood

transferred to the Debts Recovery Tribunal.


</p><p id="p_6">                                                           (DEBANGSU BASAK, J.)
