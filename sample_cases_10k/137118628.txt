<div class="doc_title">Anil Jain vs Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. on 1 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                      1                                 WP-7438-2017
                               The High Court Of Madhya Pradesh
                                          WP-7438-2017
                                        (ANIL JAIN Vs INDIAN OIL CORPORATION LTD.)

                    6
                    Jabalpur, Dated : 01-08-2018
                          Shri Jubin Prasad, learned counsel for the petitioner.
                          Shri Aditya Adhikari, learned Senior Counsel with Shri Jagat
                    Singh, learned counsel for the respondent.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                          Shri Rohan Harne, learned counsel for the petitioner in other
                    connected writ petitions.
</p><p id="p_2">                          Parties are heard on I.A. No.10510/17, an application seeking
                    appropriate directions.
</p><p id="p_3">                           Shri Prasad, learned counsel for the petitioner contended that in
                    view of interim order directing the Corporation to maintain status-quo
                    passed in linked petitions, Field Verification Committee is not
                    proceeding further. The said order may be modified by directing the
                    Field Verification Committee to consider the eligibility of present
                    petitioner and thereafter the Corporation may be restrained from passing
                    a final order. If the present petitioner is not found as eligible/qualified by
                    said committee, this petition will render infructuous.
</p><p id="p_4">                           Shri Harne and Shri Adhikari have no objection.
                           Accordingly, I.A. No.10510/17 is allowed to the said extent. It is
                    clarified that the status-quo order passed in other connected writ
                    petitions shall stand modified and now Field Verification Committee of
                    Corporation can consider the eligibility of petitioner and decide the
                    aspect of eligibility. Beyond that no final order be passed by the
                    Corporation.
</p><p id="p_5">                           I.A. is disposed of.
</p><p id="p_6">                           The Corporation shall communicated the outcome of such
                    consideration by the Committee to the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_7">                           C.C. as per rules.
</p>
