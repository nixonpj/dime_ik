<div class="doc_title">Babun Das &amp; Anr vs The C.E.S.C. Ltd. &amp; Ors on 23 July, 2019</div><p id="p_1">23.07.2019
   ss
                 W.P. 12594 (W) of 2019

                   Babun Das &amp; anr.
</p><p id="p_2">                          Vs.
</p><p id="p_3">                 The C.E.S.C. Ltd. &amp; Ors.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
      Mr. S. Ghoshal
      Mr. Satyajit Senapati
                                  ... for the petitioners.
</p><p id="p_5">      Ms. Nandini Mitra
                                  ... for the C.E.S.C. Ltd.</p><pre id="pre_1">

      Mr. Malay Krishna De
      Ms. Neelam Singh            ... for the State


</pre><p id="p_6">      This is an application under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India

wherein the petitioners want supply of new electric connection to the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_7">      Counsel appearing on behalf of the licensing Company refers to a letter

dated February 9, 2019 whereby the licensing Company has written to the

petitioners appraising that the petitioners are already receiving electric

connection and the sole purpose for obtaining a new meter is with the intention

of splitting the load. Counsel further submits that the petitioners can take up

this grievance with the Grievance Redressal Officer (GRO) who is the competent

person to decide the issue.
</p>
<p id="p_8">      In the light of the above submissions made on behalf of the parties and

perused the materials, I am of the view that there is an alternative remedy

available to the petitioners with regard to the problem assailed in this writ

petition.
</p><p id="p_9">       In the light of the above, W.P. 12594 (W) of 2019 is dismissed with liberty

to the petitioners to move before the appropriate Forum for redressal of his

grievance.
</p>
<p id="p_10">      Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the

parties upon compliance of all necessary formalities.


</p><p id="p_11">                               (Shekhar B. Saraf, J.)
