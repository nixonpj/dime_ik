<p id="p_18">    Sanjay Karol, ACJ. (oral)




                                                             .
</p><p id="p_19">                In these appeals, so filed under <a href="/doc/151577964/" id="a_1">Section 54</a> of the





    Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (hereinafter referred to as the





    Act), the beneficiary of the acquisition proceedings has

    assailed the common award dated 22.11.2012, passed by

    learned District Judge, Bilaspur, H.P., in Land Reference No.





    16 of 2008/07, titled as Jagat Pal vs. Land Acquisition

    Collector, Kol Dam, Bilaspur, H.P. &amp; another, alongwith other

    connected reference petitions.
</p>
<p id="p_20">    2.          Undisputedly claimants' land situate in village

    Kasol, Tehsil Sadar, District Bilaspur, H.P. came to be



    acquired for public purpose namely construction of Kol Dam

    Hydro Electric Project. Acquisition proceedings commenced




    with the publication of notification issued under <a href="/doc/43654/" id="a_2">Section 4</a> of





    the Act on 23.11.2000. The Collector Land acquisition





    passed his award No. 17 dated 15.8.2004. It is a matter of

    record that market value of the acquired land came to be

    assessed at different rates, classification/category wise

    ranging    from    `1,04,416/-   to    `4,69,955/-          per      bigha.
</p>
<p id="p_21">    Aggrieved thereof, claimants filed land reference petitions

    under <a href="/doc/1517117/" id="a_3">Section 18</a> of the Act, seeking re-determination of the




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_6">                                          ::: Downloaded on - 19/12/2017 23:05:11 :::HCHP</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_7">                                    4</span>




    market value of the acquired land, which stand decided in

    terms of impugned award dated 22.11.2012, in terms




                                                          .
</p>
<p id="p_22">    whereof, the market value of the acquired land stands re-
</p>
<p id="p_23">    determined @ `4,69,955/- per bigha,            on uniform basis,





    irrespective of the classification and category of land.
</p>
<p id="p_24">    3.         It is not in dispute that appeals arising out of





    similarly situated claimants as also very same acquisition

    proceedings, arising out of very same notifications under

    <a href="/doc/43654/" id="a_4">Sections 4</a> and <a href="/doc/291273/" id="a_5">11</a> of the Act, already stand dismissed by

    this Court vide judgment dated 11.01.2017, passed in RFA

    No. 792 of 2012, titled as <a href="/doc/191226095/" id="a_6">NTPC Ltd. Kol Dam vs. Babu Ram


    &amp; another</a>. The present Appeals are squarely covered by the

    said decision. Such fact is not disputed. As such, as jointly




    prayed for, the present Appeals stand disposed of, in terms





    of the said judgment. Directions issued in the aforesaid

    decision shall mutatis mutandis apply to the instant cases





    also. Pending application(s) if any, also stand disposed of

    accordingly.
</p>
