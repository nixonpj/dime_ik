                                      CP No. 607 of 2001
                                IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                                    ORIGINAL JURISDICTION

                                IN THE MATTER OF:
            INDODEEP GENERAL FINANCE &amp; INVESTMENT CO. LTD. (IN LIQN.)
                                        AND
                               OFFICIAL LIQUIDATOR
                                      Versus
                        SRI PANKOJ KRISHNA DEBNATH &amp; ORS.


   BEFORE:
   The Hon'ble JUSTICE ANIRUDDHA BOSE
   Date : 22nd September, 2009.


             The Court : Service has been effected upon the respondent nos.2, 3,</pre>

<p id="p_1">    5, 6, 8, 9 and 11.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             As regards respondent nos.1 and 7, notice was sent but the same has

    been    returned     with    the   endorsement   made   by    the    postal     authorities,

    'Intimation Served' and 'Left without intimation' respectively.

</p><p id="p_3">             So far as respondent nos.4 and 10 are concerned, it is submitted

    that the acknowledgment due card has not been received though the same was

    despatched under registered post on 20th March, 2009.

</p><p id="p_4">             Under these circumstances, service on respondent nos.1, 4, 7 and 10

    is treated as proper service.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             Let   the    Official     Liquidator    file   the   points     of    claim   by    3rd

    November, 2009; points of defence may be filed by the respondents by 20th

    November, 2009.       Affidavit of documents may be filed within 7th December,

    2009;   inspection     forthwith.       Matter   will   appear      in   the   list    on   14th

    December, 2009 under the heading 'Misfeasance Application'.




</p><p id="p_6">                                          (ANIRUDDHA BOSE, J.)


bp.
