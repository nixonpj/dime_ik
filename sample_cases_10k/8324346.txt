<pre id="pre_1">        C/SCA/4490/2018                                  ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

          R/SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 4490 of 2018

==========================================================
                     BIPINKUMAR ISHWARBHAI PATEL
                                Versus
                           STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR GAJENDRA P BAGHEL(2968) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1
MS ASMITA PATEL, AGP for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
RULE SERVED BY DS(65) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 2,3
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J. SHASTRI

                           Date : 27/07/2018

                            ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.   The   present   petition   is   filed   under   apprehended 
action   of   detention   by   the   authority.   The   Court   on 
22.3.2018   was   pleased   to   issue   Rule   making   it 
returnable   on  9.4.2018   and   thereafter,   on  12.4.2018 
the   authority   was   directed   not   to   take   coercive 
steps.   Today,   when   the   matter   has   come   up   for 
hearing,  learned Assistant Government Pleader placed 
on record a communication dated 27.7.2018 indicating 
that there is no proposal of detention of the present 
petitioner under the PASA in respect of III­CR No.125 
of 2018 registered with Olpad Police Station, Dist.­
Surat   for   the   offences   mentioned   therein.   The   said 
communication   submitted   by  Police   Inspector,   Olpad 
Police   Station,         informing          the   office   of   the 
Government Pleader, is taken on record.
</p>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               Page 1 of 2</span>
<p id="p_2">            C/SCA/4490/2018                          ORDER



</p><p id="p_3">2.   Hence,   in   light   of   aforesaid   communication, 
learned   advocate   appearing   for   the   petitioner   does 
not press the present petition at this stage. It is 
clarified   that   in   view   of   aforesaid   instructions 
since   the   petition   is   withdrawn,   the   detaining 
authority shall not detain the petitioner under PASA 
in respect of complaints which are mentioned in the 
present petition. It is further clarified that this 
order is restricted to present FIR which is brought 
before   the   Court.   In   view   of   this,   petition   stands 
dismissed as not pressed.
</p>

<p id="p_4">3.     Rule   is   discharged.   Interim   relief   shall   stand 
vacated.   No   order   as   to   costs.   Direct   service   is 
permitted.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                 (A.J. SHASTRI, J)
V.J. SATWARA
