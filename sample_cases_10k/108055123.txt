<div class="doc_title">Nopa Ram vs State on 11 January, 2017</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
              S.B.Criminal Misc. Bail No. 11925 / 2016
Nopa Ram S/o Pura Ram, by caste Nayak, resident of Village 34
M.O.D.   VPO    Gurusar     Modiya   Tehsil   Suratgarh   District   Sri
Ganganagar.
</p>
<p id="p_2">(At Present lodged in Central Jail, Sri Ganganagar)

</p><p id="p_3">                                                          ----Petitioner
                               Versus
The State of Rajasthan

</p><p id="p_4">                                                      ----Respondent
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner : Mr. H.S. Sidhu

For Respondent : Mr. Ashok Upadhyay, P.P., for the State

_____________________________________________________
               HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE P.K. LOHRA
                                  Order
11/01/2017

     Being arrested in connection with FIR No.349/2016 of Police

Station ACB Chowki, Bikaner, for offences under Sections 7, 13(1)

</p><p id="p_5">(d) and 13(2) of the <a href="/doc/1331755/" id="a_1">Prevention of Corruption Act</a>, 1988 and

<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_2">Section 120-B</a> IPC, accused-petitioner has laid this bail application

under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_3">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">     After arrest, petitioner applied for bail before the learned

trial Court, but the said effort of the petitioner proved abortive

and the learned trial Court rejected his bail application on

16.12.2016.
</p>
<p id="p_7">     Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the co-

accused person, Anil Kumar Sharma in S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail
                                 (2 of 2)
                                                        [CRLMB-11925/2016]

Application No.99/2017 has already been enlarged on bail by this

Court vide order dated 06.01.2017.

</p><p id="p_8">     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application in

general.
</p>
<p id="p_9">     Having heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

learned Public Prosecutor and taking into account totality of

circumstances, without expressing any opinion on merits of the

case, I feel inclined to grant indulgence to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_10">     Consequently, the bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_4">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.

is allowed and it is ordered that the accused-petitioner Nopa Ram

S/o Pura Ram arrested in connection with F.I.R. No.349/2016,

Police Station ACB Chowki, Bikaner may be released on bail;

provided he furnishes a personal bond of Rs.50,000/- with two

surety bonds of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned

trial court with the stipulation to appear before that Court on all

dates of hearing and as and when called upon to do so.




</p><p id="p_11">                                                     (P.K. LOHRA)J.
</p>
<p id="p_12">Bharti/30
