<pre id="pre_1">IN THE COURT OF SHRI S.N. GUPTA :  ADDL. DISTRICT JUDGE
                  TIS HAZARI COURTS :  DELHI.



Suit No   : 131/07

Shri Joginder Singh Yadav 
S/o Shri Ram Singh Radav
R/o RZ­C­143, Nihar Vihar
Delhi­110041.                                     ... Plaintiff

VERSUS


1. Shri Rinku @ Satvir Singh Yadav
   S/o Shri Joginder Singh Yadav

2. Smt. Rinki Yadav
   W/o Shri Rinku @ Satvir Singh Yadav
   Both R/o RZ­C­143, Nihar Vihar
   Delhi­110041.                                  ... Defendants 

J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     This suit has been filed by the plaintiff for possession, Mesne

Profits/Damages &amp; Permanent Injunction. 



</p><p id="p_2">2.   Written statement was filed by the defendant. 
</p><p id="p_3"> 3.    From the pleadings of the parties following issues are framed

for disposal of the case. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">                     a)   Whether the plaintiff is entitled for 
                          possession?
</p><p id="p_5">                     b)   Relief. 
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">4.    However   during   the   course   of   proceedings   a   better   sense

prevailed. The defendants who are the   son and daughter in law of

the plaintiff has delivered the possession of the property in suit by

vacating it and handed over the  keys to the plaintiff. 



</p><p id="p_8">5.    The defendant made statement that  he has vacated the suit

property   and   handed   over   the   keys   of   the   said   house   to   the

plaintiff and he has no concern with the suit property in future. 



</p><p id="p_9">6.    The plaintiff who is the father of the defendant No. 1 also made

a statement that he has taken the possession of the suit property

and the keys of the said house is given to him by the defendant

in the court. Now he has no concern with the defendant in future

and he will not take any legal action against him regarding the

suit property.             
</p><p id="p_10"> 7.   The   statement   of   both   the   parties   recorded.   In   view   of   the

statements of the parties, the suit stands compromised/settled. File

be consigned to record room. Parties to bear their own cost. 




Announced in the Open Court                   ( S.N. GUPTA )
on  3 day of January'2008                     Addl. District Judge
     rd 


                                              Delhi.
</p>
