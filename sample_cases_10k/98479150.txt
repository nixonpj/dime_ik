<p id="p_3">
            RITU BAHRI, J (ORAL)


                               This petition has been entertained on a letter written

            by Gurdeep Singh, alleging that on 15.07.2013, Constable

            Mahender Singh, CIA Staff, had picked his brother Dilbagh Singh

            along with Charanjeet Singh and thereafter, his family members

            had not heard about him.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               On notice, reply on behalf of the respondent-State, in

            the shape of affidavit of Deputy Superintendent of Police (D),

            Ferozepur, has been filed, stating therein that Dilbagh Singh and

            Charanjeet Singh were not required by the Police of CIA,

            Ferozepur. However, the petitioner was arrested by ASI Baljinder

            Singh of Narcotic Control Cell, Ferozepur, when he was found in

            possession of stolen Innova and an FIR No.102 dated 21.07.2013,

            was registered against him under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">Section 379</a> and <a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_2">411</a> IPC at
Prasher Ajay
2013.09.30 14:32
I attest to the accuracy and
integrity of this document
High Court Chandigarh
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Crl. Writ Petition No. 1453 of 2013                                2</span>

            Police Station Ghall Khurd. Thereafter, he was produced before

            the Illaqa Magistrate on 22.07.2013. During investigation, the

            petitioner made disclosure statement about certain other thefts,

            pursuant to which, he got recovered ten more vehicles.                 At

            present, he has been lodged in Central Jail, Ferozepur, since

            25.07.2013.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               In view of the aforesaid reply, filed on behalf of the

            respondent-State, no further orders are required to be passed in

            this petition.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                               Dismissed.</p><pre id="pre_2">
