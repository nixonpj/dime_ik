                                    Vs.

             Mr. Sougata Bhattacharya ...for the Petitioner.

             Mr. Hiranmoy Bhattacharya,
             Mr. Suman Dey         ...for the Contemnor.


                     The contemnor is present pursuant to this court's order

           dated 25.11.2016. Pursuant to the order passed vide order dated

           22.08.2016 passed in W.P. No. 25386 (W) of 2015, the respondent</pre>

<p id="p_1">           authority has already given the benefit of gratuity and pension by

           issuing P.P.O., but the provident fund amount has not been paid to

           the petitioner as yet.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                     Learned counsel for the contemnor submits that since the

           petitioner has not furnished the bank account, the provident fund

           amount has not been deposited as yet. In the midst of hearing the

           petitioner furnishes the Savings Bank Account being No.

           50348953376 with the Allahabad Bank, Ultadanga Branch, bearing

           IFSC Code No. 3017229687-1 in the name of Ms. Shyamali

           Bhattacharya, the petitioner herein.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                     Let the provident fund amount be remitted in the aforesaid
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                        2</span>

account of the petitioner by the Additional District Inspector of

Schools, Secondary Education, Asansol Sub-Division, District

Burdwan. The D.I. of Schools, the contemnor herein, is personally

present and he should take note of the order and will take all

endeavour to see to it that provident fund amount is remitted to the

aforesaid account of the petitioner within a month.

</p><p id="p_4">         The personal appearance of the D.I. of Schools, the

alleged contemnor, Khagendra Nath Ray be dispensed with at this

stage.
</p>
<p id="p_5">         With this direction the W.P.C.R.C. 313 (W) of 2016

stands disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_6">         The parties to act on the basis of the server copy of the

order.
