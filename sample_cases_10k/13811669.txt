          Mr. Saibal Mondal.

</p>
<p id="p_7">
      Let this matter appear under the heading "Contested Application" one week after

reopening of the Court after the summer vacation.

</p><p id="p_8">      In the meantime, the petitioner is directed to serve copy of the application upon the

opposite parties by Registered Post with A/D and to file an affidavit of service on the next

date of hearing.
</p>
<p id="p_9">
</p><p id="p_10">      Let there be a stay of operation of the impugned order dated 17.12.2014 passed by

the learned Judicial Magistrate, 7th Court, Alipore in connection with Case No.C-2640 of

2012 till two weeks after reopening of the Court after the summer vacation.

</p><p id="p_11">      Liberty is granted to the parties to pray for extension and/or modification and/or

variation of this order upon notice to the other side.

</p><p id="p_12">      Urgent photostat certified copy of this Order, if applied for, be supplied expeditiously

after complying with all necessary legal formalities.


</p><p id="p_13">                                ( Siddhartha Chattopadhyay, J.)
