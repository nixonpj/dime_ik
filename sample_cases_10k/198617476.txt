<div class="doc_title">Mantu Bind vs State Of Bihar on 18 January, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">               IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                         Cr.Misc. No.42844 of 2009
MANTU BIND, SON OF LATE MAKHAN BIND, RESIDENT AT VILLAGE-ARMA, POLICE
STATION-SURAJGARHA (KAJRA), DISTRICT-LAKHISARAI.
                                                          .....Petitioner.
                                 Versus
THE STATE OF BIHAR                                     .....Opposite Party.
                                -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">03/   18.01.2010             Heard   learned         Counsel     for      the

                    petitioner and learned Counsel for the

                    State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             The petitioner seeks bail in a case

                    which was rejected earlier vide Annexure-

                    1 on 04.05.2009.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             A report has been called for and

                    according to the report it appears that

                    only charge has been framed.

</p><p id="p_4">                             Submission is that in this case,

                    the petitioner is in custody since 20th

                    April, 2008.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Considering        the       facts           and

                    circumstances       of    the    case,     the     above

                    named     petitioner       is     directed       to   be

                    released     from        custody     on      bail     on

                    furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten

                    thousand) with two sureties of the like

                    amount each to the satisfaction of the

                    learned      Chief        Judicial        Magistrate,
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                         2</span>




           Lakhisarai/concerned court in connection

           with Surajgarha (Kajra) P.S. Case No. 104

           of 2008.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
kksinha/                      ( Shyam Kishore Sharma, J.)
