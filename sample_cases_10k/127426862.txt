<div class="doc_title">Darshan Singh @ Darshi vs State Of Punjab on 26 November, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                         KAPIL
                                                         2015.11.26 16:36
                                                         I attest to the accuracy and
                                                         integrity of this document

  In the High Court of Punjab and Haryana at Chandigarh.

                                CRM-M-36383-2015
                                Date of Decision:26.11.2015

Darshan Singh @ Darshi                             ........Petitioner

                             Versus

State of Punjab                                ........Respondent

CORAM: HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA

Present:   Mr. S.S. Goraya, Advocate,
           for the petitioner.

           Mr. R.P.S. Sidhu, AAG, Punjab.
                          *****

SABINA, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">           Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of

the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 for grant of regular bail in

FIR No.30, dated 09.02.2015, under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_2">Sections 392</a> and <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_3">427</a> of the

Indian Penal Code, 1860      (<a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_4">Sections 395</a>, <a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_5">397</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">120-B</a> IPC

was added later on and <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_7">Section 392</a> IPC was deleted), registered

at Police Station Shimlapuri, District Ludhiana.

</p><p id="p_2">           Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_3">           Admittedly, co-accused of the petitioner who were

similarly situated have been granted bail by this Court.

</p><p id="p_4">           Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on the

merits of the case, this petition is allowed. Petitioner be

admitted to bail subject to the satisfaction of Chief Judicial

Magistrate, Ludhiana.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><pre id="pre_2">November 26, 2015                                         (SABINA)
kapil                                                      JUDGE
