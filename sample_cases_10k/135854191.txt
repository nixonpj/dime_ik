                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.29498 of 2019
                      Arising Out of PS. Case No.-171 Year-2018 Thana- CHANDAN District- Banka
                 ======================================================
                 Rajesh Kumar Singh, Son of Ashok Singh @ Ashok Kumar Singh, Resident
                 of Village- Lal Pokhar Bichchi, P.S.- Hajipur, District- Vaishali, at present
                 resident of Village- Jasidih (Teant of Ajay Rai), P.S.- Rambagh, District-
                 Deoghar (Jharkhand).
                                                                              ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                       ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr.Rajani Ranjan Pd. Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mr.Anand Kishore Choudhary
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR UPADHYAY
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   03-05-2019                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   counsel appearing on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                The petitioner is in custody in connection with

                   Chandan P.S. Case No.171of 2018 for the offences under

                   <a href="/doc/399026/" id="a_1">Sections 399</a>, <a href="/doc/1784492/" id="a_2">402</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1961417/" id="a_3">Sections 25</a>(1-

</p><p id="p_3">                   b)a, 26 &amp; 35 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_4">Arms Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                It is alleged that from the possession of the petitioner

                   one country made pistol with live cartridge were recovered.

</p><p id="p_5">                                Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

                   petitioner has got no criminal antecedent and he is in custody

                   since 19.10.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                Considering the fact that the petitioner has got no

                   criminal antecedent, the petitioner, named above, is directed to

                   be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/-
               Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.29498 of 2019(2) dt.03-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                          2/2</span>




                        (Rupees ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each

                        to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Banka,

                        in connection with Chandan P.S. Case No.171of 2018.



</p><p id="p_7">                                                            (Anil Kumar Upadhyay, J)
sunil/-
</p>
