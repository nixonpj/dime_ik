      Rule.       Mr.Rakesh         Patel,        learned      Additional

Rule on behalf of the respondent - State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      Corpus - Bijalben was produced before us on
12th September, 2019. Considering the mindset of
the Corpus, this Court passed following order on
12th September, 2019:-
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                  "Corpus - Bijal is produced before
             us. Considering the mindset of the Corpus
             and in the facts of the present case, we
             deem   it  fit   to   direct   the  police
             authorities to admit the Corpus in Nari
             Vikas Gruh, Paldi, Ahmedabad. The Corpus
             shall be produced again before this Court
             on 17.9.2019. Nari Vikas Gruh, Paldi,
             Ahmedabad is directed to not to permit
             anybody to meet Corpus - Bijal. If any
             medical treatment is required, it would be

<p id="p_3">        Corpus         -       Bijalben     has       been   again       produced
before us today. She has expressed her desire and
willingness to not to go with the petitioner.
Keshubhai - uncle of Corpus - Bijalben is present
before us. We have been informed by the Corpus
that she has lost her parents and has expressed
her desire to stay with her uncle - Keshubhai.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        In view of the aforesaid, the petition is not
entertained.               The     same       is      dismissed.         Rule         is
discharged.
