                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Criminal Miscellaneous No.11193 of 2011
                   ======================================================
                   Dr. Rajendra Prasad Mandal
                                                                        .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                     Versus
                   The State Of Bihar
                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     : Mr.
                   For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr.
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SHIVAJI PANDEY
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   06-07-2012                     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                       learned counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 This application has been filed for quashing the

                       order dated 24.7.2010 passed in Araria P.S. Case No. 65 of

                       2002, G.R. No.283 of 2002 by the Chief Judicial

                       Magistrate, Araria by which he has taken cognizance

                       against the petitioner under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the Indian

                       Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that this

                       application has been filed by a Doctor and he has nothing to

                       do with the family of the deceased. In the complaint

                       petition though he has mentioned about the involvement of

                       the Doctor in connection with the death of his daughter but

                       in the protest petition in paragraph 2 he has stated that this

                       petitioner has no connection in the death of his daughter.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.11193 of 2011 (2) dt.06-07-2012</span>




</p><p id="p_4">                                      Issue notice to opposite party nos. 2 under ordinary

                           process as well as registered cover with A/D for which

                           requisites etc. must be filed within a period of one week,

                           failing which this application as against him shall stand

                           rejected without further reference to the Bench.

</p><p id="p_5">                                     After appearance of opposite party no.2, let this

                           case be listed at the top of the list.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><pre id="pre_2">Vinay/-                                               (Shivaji Pandey, J)
