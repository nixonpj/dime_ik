                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.   It appears that at the relevant time of accident, one
         Maruti Car bearing registration No.GJ-7-A-34 was registered in
         the name of original claimant. At the time of accident, the said
         Maruti Car was driven by her husband and when her husband
         was trying to overtake S.T. Bus, the said car went below the
         bus and thus, accident in question has occurred.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.   In this factual background, the Insurance Company raised
         contention before the learned Tribunal that the owner of the
         vehicle is not made party respondent and as per contract of
         indemnity, the appellant promises to indemnify the owner if
         any liability arises from the use of vehicle. In the alternative,
         the appellant also contended that the Insurance Company's

</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.       In light of this position, notice for final disposal,
         returnable on 11.12.2015. Learned advocate Ms.Vasavdatta
         Bhatt waives service of notice for respondent No.3.

<p id="p_4">         Order in Civil Application

                  Notice returnable on 11.12.2015. Learned advocate
         Ms.Vasavdatta Bhatt waives service of notice for opponent
         No.3. It is stated at bar that the appellant has deposited the
         amount of compensation with interest and cost with the
         learned Tribunal but, as on date, the same is not disbursed and
         invested as per directions issued in the impugned award. In
         light of this position, the learned Tribunal is directed to invest
         the amount deposited by the appellant in cumulative F.D.R. in
         any nationalized bank in the name of Registrar of the learned
         Tribunal initially for a period of six months and thereafter, the
         same shall be renewed from to time till further orders. The
         learned Tribunal shall keep the original F.D.R. in custody of the
         Nazir of the learned Tribunal and in an unincumbent position.
</p>
