<div class="doc_title">Bimal Roy @ Bimal Krishna Roy &amp; Ors vs Kumaresh Sen &amp; Anr on 29 July, 2019</div><p id="p_1">29.07.19
Sl.no. 33
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">Ct. No. 34</span>
 P.M.
</p><p id="p_2">                               C. R. R. 3067 OF 2006

                      Bimal Roy @ Bimal Krishna Roy &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_3">                                    - vs -
</p><p id="p_4">                              Kumaresh Sen &amp; Anr.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

             None appears for the petitioners. No accommodation is sought for.

       The revisional application has been preferred against the order dated

       22.03.2006.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">             The records of this case reflect that on 24.08.2006 when the matter

       was taken up it was observed that the copy of the impugned order was not

       enclosed along with revisional application and the same was not furnished

       to the court on that date. On 30.08.2006 none appeared on behalf of the

       petitioner and as such there was a direction by this Court for the matter to

       go out of the list.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">             Today when the matter has been taken up none appeared to furnish

       the information regarding the Complaint Case No. 192 of 2004 pending

       before the learned Judicial Magistrate, 2nd Court at Barasat nor        the

       certified copy of the impugned order has been placed before this Court. As

       the outcome of the present stage of the case is not furnished by this Court

       I am reluctant to interfere at this belated stage.
           Accordingly the application being C.R.R. 3067 of 2006 is dismissed.


      Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the applicant

upon compliance of requisite formalities.


</p><p id="p_10">                                                                     ( Tirthankar Ghosh, J.)
