<p id="p_1">2   29-10-2018                  On last occasion, nobody had appeared on behalf of
                 the respondents. Today also none has bothered to appear on behalf
                 of the respondents. It is submitted by the learned counsel
                 appearing for the petitioner that the learned counsel appearing for
                 the respondents, namely, Shri Surya Narayan Sah was informed,
                 but he has failed to turn up before this Court. Hence, there is no
                 option but to proceed with the matter.
</p><p id="p_2">                                The short submission of the learned counsel for the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court CWJC No.1850 of 2018 (2) dt.29-10-2018</span>

                                                 2 /2




                     petitioner is that though the petitioner had supplied jute to the
                     respondents National Jute Manufactures Corporation Ltd.,
                     however, the outstanding admitted dues amounting to Rs.
                     15,82,609/-- has not been paid to the petitioner herein.
</p><p id="p_3">                                     Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the
                     case, the respondent no.2 is directed to pay the admitted
                     outstanding dues of the petitioner within a period of six weeks
                     from today, failing which the said amount shall carry an interest of
                     18% per annum for the entire period for which the due amount has
                     remained unpaid as well as the Respondent No.2 shall be liable for
                     penal consequences.
</p><p id="p_4">                                     The present writ petition is disposed of on the
                     aforesaid terms.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                                               (Mohit Kumar Shah, J)
