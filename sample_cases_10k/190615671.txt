COMMON ORDER
		Even though the petitioners'  counsel has undertaken to file an affidavit, seeking time to vacate the premises, the learned counsel for the petitioners is absent.  Hence, the writ petitions  are dismissed and the interim orders granted stand vacated.  The respondent Municipality is at liberty to take possession of the premises of the respective petitioners  within three months from today and file a compliance report, for  having taken possession, after a period of three months. No costs. Connected W.M.Ps are closed.

		2.	Post the writ petitions after three months for reporting compliance.
nv										23.08.2016
