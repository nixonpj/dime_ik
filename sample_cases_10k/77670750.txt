Court   8.10.2013                     W.P. 32225 (W) of 2013
No22
Sl 16
Ac                                           Raiganj Dalkhola Highways Ltd &amp; Anr
                                                            -vs-
                                                    State of West Bengal &amp; Ors


                    Mr. Boudhayan Bhattacharyya
                    Ms. Aritra Roy             ... For Petitioners

                    Mr. Soumitra Mukherjee      ... For Ld. Advocate General



                                 Affidavit-of-service filed today be kept with the
                    record.
                                 Despite service there is no appearance on behalf
                    of the State respondents.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                 Mr. Mukherjee appears on behalf of the learned
                    Advocate General.
</p><p id="p_2">                                 Learned advocate for the petitioner submits that
                    copy of the writ petition would be handed over to Mr.
                    Mukherjee in course of this day.
</p><p id="p_3">                                 Let copy of the writ petition be served upon Mr.
                    Mukherjee in course of this day.
</p><p id="p_4">                                 The petitioners have sought for declaration that
                    the provisions of the West Bengal Tax on Entry of Goods Into
                    Local Areas Act, 2012 and the Rules made thereunder are
                    ultra vires and be declared as null and void. In one of the
                    writ petitions, being W.P. 11407 (W) of 2012, the vires of the
                    provisions of the said Act was also challenged and by a
                    judgment dated 24.06.2013 the Hon'ble Single Bench
                    declared the provisions of the said Act as ultra vires.
</p><p id="p_5">                                 The State respondents, who are represented,
                    submit that the said judgment is carried in an appeal before
                    the Division Bench in M.A.T. 1107 of 2013 and the Hon'ble
                    Division Bench has directed the matter to be listed on 12th
                    November 2013 and by way of interim measure, directed the
                    authorities to proceed with the assessment proceeding and
                    they are not liable to refund the entry tax, already collected.
</p><p id="p_6">                                 This Court finds that the mater is sub-judice
                    before the Division Bench and the decision that would be
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    2</span>



taken therein would have impact on the determination,
which is required to be made in this writ petition.
</p><p id="p_7">            Accordingly, the writ petition is adjourned sine
die till disposal of the matter pending before the Hon'ble
Division Bench.
</p><p id="p_8">            The parties are at liberty to pray for inclusion of
this matter upon disposal of the appeal or if any order be
passed by the Division Bench which does not put any
impediment in deciding the instant writ petition.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                                        (Harish Tandon, J.)
