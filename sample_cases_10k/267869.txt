<div class="doc_title">Mahesh Sahani &amp; Anr. vs The State Of Bihar on 15 October, 2011</div><p id="p_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                          Criminal Miscellaneous No.32474 of 2011
                                   Mahesh Sahani &amp; Anr.
</p><p id="p_2">                                           Versus
                                    The State Of Bihar

</p><p id="p_3">2.   15.10.2011.             Heard       learned      counsels          for    the

                   petitioners and the State.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             The     petitioners          are       apprehending

                   their    arrest       in   a    case   registered          under

                   <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_4">379</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">307</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_6">504</a>,<a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_7">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a>

                   of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             The accusation of assault is against

                   Lal Babu Sahani whereas allegation against

                   the     petitioners        to     have       snatched       Rs.

                   40,000/-
</p>
<p id="p_6">                             It is submitted by learned counsel

                   for the petitioners that charge sheet was

                   submitted under bailable provisions whereas

                   the cognizance has been taken under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_9">Sections

                   307</a> and <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_10">379</a> of the I.P.C. also.

</p><p id="p_7">                             Considering the fact that prior to

                   the   present     case     petitioners'          side   lodged

                   the   complaint       case      against      the    informant

                   side, let the petitioners, Mahesh Sahani and

                   Vinay Sahani, be released on                  bail in the

                   event    of    arrest      or    surrender         before   the

                   learned       court    below     within      a     period    of

                   twelve weeks from today in connection with
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             2</span>




        Madhuban     P.S.       Case    No.       141   of   2010   on

        furnishing        bail    bond       of     Rs.10,000/-(Ten

        Thousand) each with two sureties of the like

        amount     each     to    the    satisfaction         of    the

        learned     C.J.M.       East    Champaran,          Motihari,

        subject to the conditions as laid down under

        <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_11">Section     438(2)</a>       of    the    Code      of   Criminal

        Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_8">


U. K.                                    ( Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
 </p>
