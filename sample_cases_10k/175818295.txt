<div class="doc_title">Ghuman Singh vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 29 July, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                     1                           MCRC-30062-2019
        The High Court Of Madhya Pradesh
                  MCRC-30062-2019
                     (GHUMAN SINGH Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


Gwalior, Dated : 29-07-2019
      Shri M.P.S. Raghuvanshi, counsel with Shri Vijay Kumar Jha, counsel

for the applicant.
      Shri Purushottam Rai, Panel Lawyer for the respondent / State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Case diary is available.
</p><p id="p_2">      This is third application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C. for grant of bail.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      The applicant has been arrested on 11.09.2016 in connection with
Crime No. 350/2016 registered by Police Station Isagarh District Ashok
Nagar for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Sections 302</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_3">307</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_4">      It is submitted by the counsel for the applicant that although the case is
fixed for examination of the witnesses but from 15.03.2019 onwards the case
has been adjournment for examination of the accused, therefore, he may be
granted bail.
</p><p id="p_5">      Per contra, it is submitted by the counsel for the State that from order-
sheet dated 29.04.2019, it appears that the case was fixed for examination of

the accused on 12.06.2019 and the applicant has not filed the order-sheets of
the Trial Court from 12.06.2019 onwards to show that whether the accused
has been examined or not. Verbal statement is made by the counsel for the
applicant that till today the accused statement has not been recorded so far.
</p><p id="p_6">      Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
      It is really surprising that when the case was fixed for the first time on
29.03.2019 for recording the accused statement then the case is being
adjourned for the same purposes. It is not known that whether the accused
statement has been recorded or not, therefore, in case, if the accused
statement has not been recorded so far, then the Trial Court is directed to
record the accused statement on the next date of hearing positively.
</p><p id="p_7">      The applicant is directed to file the certified copy of this order before
the Trial Court.
</p><p id="p_8">                                   2                          MCRC-30062-2019
       With aforesaid observations, the application is disposed of.
</p>
