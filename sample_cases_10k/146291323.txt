<div class="doc_title">Satyendra Pd,Sinha &amp; Ors vs Hare Krishna Prasad &amp; Ors on 20 March, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                     FA No.416 of 1980
                             SATYENDRA PD,SINHA &amp; ORS
                                             Versus
                            HARE KRISHNA PRASAD &amp; ORS
                                           -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">49   20.03.2009          Considered the request made in I.A. no. 5952 of 2008 to

                  restrain the heirs of respondent no.11 from selling the suit property. It

                  is submitted on behalf of the appellants that this Court under orders

                  dated 21.9.2000 directed respondent no.4 to seek permission from this

                  court before proceeding to sell any of the suit property but ignoring

                  such direction he sold part of the suit property under sale deed dated

                  3.11.2001. In the circumstances this court should restrain the heirs of

                  respondent no.4 from selling any other suit property. As Respondent

                  no.4 died on 17.1.2004 and this interlocutory application has been

                  filed four years thereafter, no action can be taken against respondent

                  no.4 except that the sale made by him contrary to the direction

                  contained in the orders dated 21.9.2000 shall be subject to the result of

                  this appeal. The heirs of respondent no.4 however, are directed not to

                  sell any other suit property without seeking permission from this court

                  as was ordered under order dated 21.9.2000.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             I.A. no. 5952 of 2008 is accordingly disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                            I.A. No.6816 of 2008 is taken up for orders and it is directed

                  that the vendees of the sale deed dated 3.11.2001, namely, Manoj

                  Mahto and Chandan Mahto both sons of Jago Mahto be impleaded as

                  respondents no.9 and 10 in the instant appeal.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                            Issue appeal notice to the newly added respondents no.9 and

                  10 by both the modes for which requisite etc must be filed within two
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                -2-</span>




         weeks, failing which the appeal shall stand dismissed against them

         without further reference to the Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                   I.A. no. 6816 of 2008 is accordingly disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><pre id="pre_2">shahid                                             (V.N.Sinha, J)
