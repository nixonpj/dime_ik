 29/09/2014
   ARDR

                                         CRM 13061 of 2014

              In Re: An application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal
              Procedure filed on 10th September, 2014 in connection with New Town
              P.S. Case no. 241 of 2014 dated 31/7/2014 under <a href="/doc/330464/" id="a_2">Sections 20(b)(ii)</a> of
              the Narcotic Drugs and <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_3">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>.

</p><p id="p_2">                                                  And

              In the matter of: Mamon Gazi
                                                                      ...Petitioner.
</p><p id="p_3">              Mr. Pronojit Roy,
                                                        ...for the Petitioner.
</p><p id="p_4">              Mr. Kaushik Biswas,
                                                        ...for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_5">ALLOWED

                    Heard the learned Counsel for the parties.

                    In view of the fact that only five kilograms of contraband was

              recovered from the possession of this Petitioner, let this Petitioner be

              enlarged on bail upon furnishing a bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten

              thousand only) with two sureties of like amount, one of whom must be

              local, to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Barasat.

</p><p id="p_6">                    The application for bail stands Allowed.



</p><p id="p_7">                                                        (Tapen Sen, J.)



                                                 (Indrajit Chatterjee, J.)
