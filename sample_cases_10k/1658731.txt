                                        Versus
                              STATE OF BIHAR &amp; ORS

<p id="p_1">5   22/09/2010            Let State of Jharkhand through its necessary

                  authority as well as Jharkhand State Electricity Board be

                  impleaded      as    opposite   parties   in   the   contempt

                  application.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                          Let copies of contempt application along with

                  annexures be served on the learned Standing Counsel for

                  the State of Jharkhand as well as learned counsel for the

                  Jharkhand State Electricity Board who shall seek

                  instruction in the matter and file counter affidavit.

</p><p id="p_3">                          Let this matter come up after six weeks.


</p><pre id="pre_2">    AMIN/                             (Ajay Kumar Tripathi, J.)
