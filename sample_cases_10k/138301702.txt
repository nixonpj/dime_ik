<div class="doc_title">Devashish vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 20 January, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                         MCRC-19436-2015
               (DEVASHISH Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


20-01-2016

      Shri P. S. Chouhan, and Manish Jain Shri learned counsel
for the petitioner.
      Shri Devendra Shukla, learned Public Prosecutor for
respondent/ State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">       This is first application filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Cr. P. C.
for grant of bail to the applicant who has been arrested on 27.09.2015,
in connection with Crime No.82/2015, registered at P. S. Chopana
District Betul for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_2">Section 304-B</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1868826/" id="a_3">498-</a>
A of IPC.
</p><p id="p_2">       Applicant is a husband.
</p><p id="p_3">       It is contended by the learned counsel for the applicant that date
of incident is 9.8.2015 and FIR was lodged on 26.9.2015 after delay
of near about forty five days and no explanation has been offered in
regard to delay in lodging the FIR.
</p><p id="p_4">      Learned Public Prosecutor admitted the fact that the date of
incident is 9.8.2015 and FIR was lodged on 26.9.2015.
</p><p id="p_5">       The deceased was wife of the applicant committed suicide by
consuming poison. The family members of the the deceased leveled
the allegation against the applicant that she was subjected to cruelty
and demand of dowry was made.
</p><p id="p_6">      However, looking to the nature of evidence and delay in
lodging the FIR and no possible explanation has been offered in
regard to lodging the FIR, without expressing any opinion on
merits of the matter petition is allowed.
</p><p id="p_7">      It is directed that on furnishing personal bond of Rs.50,000/-
(Rs. Fifty thousand) by the applicant along with one solvent surety in
 the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial Court the applicants
Devashish be released on bail.
</p><p id="p_8">       The applicants shall abide by the following conditions of
<a href="/doc/1682361/" id="a_4">Section 437</a> (3) of Cr. P. C. as under:
</p><p id="p_9">       (a) that such person shall attend in accordance with the
conditions of the bond executed under this Chapter;
</p><p id="p_10">       (b) that such person shall not commit any offence similar to
the offence of which he is accused, or suspected of the
commission of which he is suspected and;
</p><p id="p_11">       (c) that such person shall not directly or indirectly make
any inducement, treat or promise to any person acquainted with
the facts of the case as to dissuade him from disclosing such facts
to the Court or to any police officer or tamper with the evidence.
</p><p id="p_12">       C. C. as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_13">                                                (S.K. GANGELE)
                                                     JUDGE
