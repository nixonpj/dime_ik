Shankar Rao V. Desai

1.     The President
       Shanti Vardha Education Society

                                          ... Respondents

     Sri Ashok S. Kinagi, Adv., for R3)
                             -2-

                                           W.P.No.102252/2013


      This Writ Petition is filed under Articles 226 &amp; 227 of
the Constitution of India, praying to direct the respondents
No.1 &amp; 2 to pay salary arrears from dated 01.12.2000 to
10.01.2007, with interest @ 18% p.a. quarterly rests as per
Bank Rules.

      This petition coming on for Preliminary Hearing in 'B'
Group this day, the court made the following.


                          ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Petitioner   -   party-in-person   claims   to   be   a

Professor in English in the 1st respondent Private

Education Society. It is the allegation of the petitioner

that he has not been paid salary from 01.12.2000 to

10.01.2007.    Hence, has preferred this petition under

<a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India to issue a writ of

mandamus, directing respondent Nos.1 and 2 - Society

to pay the arrears of salary.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      2. Heard the petitioner - party-in-person and the

learned counsel for the respondents.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             -3-</span>

</p><p id="p_4">                                          W.P.No.102252/2013


</p><p id="p_5">      3. The claim of the petitioner is for recovery of

arrears of salary, which cannot be done by invoking the

extraordinary jurisdiction under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_2">Article 226</a> of the

Constitution of India. It is for the petitioner to institute

an appropriate civil suit and recover his salary and

wages, if any, and not come rushing to this Court by

invoking the extraordinary jurisdiction under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_3">Article

226</a> of the Constitution of India. Accordingly, petition is

rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                          Sd/-
</p><p id="p_8">                                         JUDGE
