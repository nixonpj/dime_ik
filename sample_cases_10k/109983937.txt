<div class="doc_title">Manglesh vs State Of U.P. on 20 November, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Bachchoo Lal</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 68
 

 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION No. - 50778 of 2019
 

 
Applicant :- Manglesh
 
Opposite Party :- State of U.P.
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Kundan Kumar,Rakhi Kumari
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.
 

 
Hon'ble Bachchoo Lal,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Sri Satya Prakash Singh, Advocate has filed vakalatnama on behalf of the complainant is taken on record.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">Heard learned counsel for the applicant, learned counsel for the complainant as well as learned A.G.A and perused the record.
</p> 
<p id="p_3">Learned counsel for the applicant submits that the FIR of the alleged incident was lodged against four persons including the applicant making general allegation for committing marpeet with the deceased and injured person with lathi, danda, kick and fist. No specific role has been assigned to the applicant. It has further been submitted that an FIR was also lodged from the side of the applicant and three persons have sustained injury from the side of the applicant.It has further been submitted that the FIR of the alleged incident was lodged under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">section 323</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_2">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_3">506</a> IPC and after the death of injured Bechan, the case was converted under <a href="/doc/409589/" id="a_4">section 304</a> IPC. In postmortem report three injuries have been found on the body of the deceased. The injuries no. 2 and 3 are not on vital part and the injury no. 1 is on head and the cause of death of the deceased has been shown coma as result of head injury but it has not been specified as to who has caused the head injury. The injury of injured personfrom the side of prosecution are simple in nature. There is cross version of the alleged incident. There is no criminal history of the applicant and is in jail since 26.7.2019.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">Per contra, learned counsel for the complainant as well as learned A.G.A opposed the prayer for bail and argued that the applicant is named in the FIR. In this incident four persons have sustained injury including the deceased. The applicant and other co-accused have committed the alleged offence, therefore, the applicant is not entitled for bail. 
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, I find it a fit case for bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Let the applicant Manglesh involved in Case Crime No. 288 of 2019, under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_6">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_7">506</a>, <a href="/doc/409589/" id="a_8">304</a> IPC, P.S. Machhlishahr, District Jaunpur be released on bail on his furnishing a personal bond and two sureties each in the like amount to the satisfaction of the court concerned with the following conditions:-
</p> 
<p id="p_7">1. The applicant will not tamper with the evidences.
</p> 
<p id="p_8">2. The applicant will not pressurize/intimidate the prosecution witnesses and co-operate with the trial.
</p> 
<p id="p_9">3. The applicant will appear on each and every date fixed by the trial court unless personal appearance is exempted by the court concerned. 
</p> 
<p id="p_10">In case of breach of any conditions mentioned above, the trial court shall be at liberty to cancel the bail of the applicant. 
</p> 
<p id="p_11">Order Date :- 20.11.2019
 
A.
</p>
