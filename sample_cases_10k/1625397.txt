                                  JUDGMENT
</pre>




<p id="p_1">      A   very   detailed   counter   affidavit   has   been   filed   raising


Several   contention   touching   on   the   merits   of   the   claims   of   the


petitioners   and   contentions   regarding   the   maintainability   of   the


writ   petition   as   well   as     Ext.P9   application   itself.     Though


elaborate submissions were addressed before me by Sri.Denizen


Komath counsel for the petitioners and by Sri.G.S.Raghunath, the


learned counsel for the contesting respondent,   I do not propose


to consider the merits of the matter.   The principal relief sought


for is that the court below be directed to dispose of Ext.P9 claim


petition,   early.     I   decline     that   relief   on   the   reason   that   the


petitioners   rushed   to   this   court   within   just   two   days   of   filing


Ext.P9   claim   petition   seeking   a   writ   of   mandamus   regarding


Ext.P9.  The writ petition will stand dismissed.   It is open to the


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">WPC No.10316/2007                2</span>



petitioners to pursue   the matter before the court below.
</p>




<p id="p_2">                               PIUS C. KURIAKOSE,

                                      JUDGE.
