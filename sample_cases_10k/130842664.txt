     THIS CRL.P FILED U/S.482 CR.P.C PRAYING TO
MODIFY THE ORDER DATED 02.09.2013 MADE IN
CRL.A.NO.289/2013 BY THE S.J., D.K., MANGALORE,
                             2


IN SO FAR THE CONDITION THAT THE APPELLANT
SHALL DEPOSIT RS.63,000/- OUT OF FINE AMOUNT.

     THIS CRL.P COMING ON FOR ORDERS THIS
DAY, THE COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING:

                         ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner. Perused

the records.   The Appellate Court while suspending

the sentence u/s.389(1) of <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.PC</a>, imposed a condition

on the petitioner to deposit an amount of Rs.63,000/-.


</p><p id="p_2">     2. It is contended by the learned counsel for the

petitioner that he has made all his efforts to deposit the

amount within time, but beyond his reach, he could not

deposit the said amount within time.      It appears, the

appellant has not made any Application for extension of

time before the Court below.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">     3.     Therefore,   looking   into   the   facts   and

circumstances of the case, I feel it is just and necessary

to extend further period of four weeks from today to

deposit the said amount of Rs.63,000/- before the Trial

Court, failing which, no further extension would be

granted.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             3</span><p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">     With the aforesaid observation, the Criminal

Petition stands disposed off accordingly.




</p><p id="p_7">                                        Sd/-
</p><p id="p_8">                                       JUDGE
