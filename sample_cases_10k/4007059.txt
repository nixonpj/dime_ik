CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.K.SASIDHARAN            
and 
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE G.R.SWAMINATHAN              

W.P.(MD)No.2762 of 2017  

Nellai R.Sollalagan                     : Petitioner
                                        
Vs.

1.The District Collector,
   Kanyakumari District.

2.The District Adi Dravidar &amp;
   Tribal Welfare Officer,
   Nagercoil, Kanyakumari District.

3.The Superintendent of Police,
   Kanyakumari District.

4.The Special Tahsildar,
   Adi Dravidar Welfare &amp;
   Land Acquisition Officer,

   Kanyakumari District.
5.Tmt.Gnanasoundari                     : Respondents 

PRAYER: Writ Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India
to issue a Writ of Mandamus, directing the respondents to remove the
encroachments in Sy.No.129/3A1, to an extent of 0.77 Hectares (1 Acre 90 
cents) of land in Mancode Village, (which has been converted into lay out as
46 plots) Neratankuzhi S.C.Colony, Vilavancode Taluk, Kanyakumari District,
which was allotted for Adi Dravidar vide the representation of the petitioner
dated 19.07.2016.

!For Petitioner      : Mr.D.Saravanan

For Respondents 1to4: Mr.M.Govindan,  
                        Special Government Pleader 
        For Respondent No.5: No Appearance   



:ORDER

        The learned counsel for the petitioner made an endorsement indicating
that he is withdrawing the Writ Petition with liberty to approach the
appropriate authority.  
</p><p id="p_2">        2. In view of the endorsement so made, the Writ Petition is dismissed
as withdrawn with liberty. No costs.
