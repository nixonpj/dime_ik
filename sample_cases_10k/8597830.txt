                               JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">     Petitioner has availed a Mortgage Loan from the

second respondent bank to a tune of Rs.10 lakhs.

Admittedly, repayment was defaulted.                     Consequent to

which, bank has initiated action under the <a href="/doc/52229129/" id="a_1">SARFAESI Act</a>,

evident from Ext.P1 sale notice.             The sale stands posted

to 3.5.2017 at 10.30 a.m. It is thus challenging Ext.P1

and seeking other related reliefs, this petition is filed.

</p><p id="p_2">     2.   Having heard               learned          counsel for the

petitioner and learned standing counsel appearing for

the respondents, I think it is only appropriate that this

Writ Petition is given a quietus by issuing appropriate

directions.   Therefore, there will be a direction to the

petitioner to pay the outstanding amount in ten equated

monthly instalments starting from 5.6.2017 onwards and

on the corresponding date of the succeeding months.

W.P.(C). No.14492 of 2017
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    2</span>



Needless to say, if any of the instalment is defaulted, bank

will be at liberty to resurrect the proceedings pertaining to

Ext.P1 and recover the amount in lump.

</p><p id="p_3">      Writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
