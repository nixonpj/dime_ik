<div class="doc_title">Pradeep Pandit vs The State Of Jharkhand on 13 February, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
               A.B.A. No. 582 of 2019
                          ------
</pre>
<pre id="pre_2">        Pradeep Pandit                      ...           Petitioner
                                        Versus
       1. The State of Jharkhand
       2. Mira Devi                          ...        Opposite Parties
                                        ------
</pre><p id="p_1">   CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR CHOUDHARY
</p><p id="p_2">                                         ------</p><pre id="pre_3">
       For the Petitioner          : Mr. A.K. Choudhary, Advocate
       For the State               : Addl. P.P.
                                         ------
       Order No.04 Dated- 13.02.2019

</pre><p id="p_3">             Apprehending his arrest, the petitioner has moved this
       Court for grant of privilege of anticipatory bail in connection with
       Complaint Case No. 680 of 2017 registered under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">sections 498A</a> of
       the Indian Penal Code read with section 3/4 of D.P. Act.
</p><p id="p_4">             Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and learned
       Addl. P.P. for the State.
</p><p id="p_5">             The Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the
       petitioner is the husband of the opposite party no.2-complainant
       and the allegation against the petitioner are false and are general
       and omnibus in nature. Hence, it is submitted that the petitioner be
       given the privilege of anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_6">             Learned Addl. P.P. opposes the prayer for grant of
       anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_7">            Let notice be issued to the opposite party no.2 under
      registered cover with A/D as well as under ordinary process for
      which requisites etc. must be filed within a week by the petitioner,
      failing which, this application shall stand dismissed without further
      reference to the Bench.
</p><p id="p_8">            List this case on 09.04.2019.
</p><p id="p_9">            Considering the submissions of the counsels and the fact as
      discussed above, I am inclined to pass an interim order of
      anticipatory bail provisionally till 09.04.2019. In case of the
      petitioner being arrested by the police on or before 09.04.2019, he
      shall be released on bail provisionally on furnishing bail bond of
 Rs.25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand) with two sureties of
like amount each to the satisfaction of the officer concerned in
connection with Complaint Case No. 680 of 2017, subject to the
conditions laid down under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal
Procedure.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                         (Anil Kumar Choudhary, J.)
Sonu/Gunjan-
</p>
