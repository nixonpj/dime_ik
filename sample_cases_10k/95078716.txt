                                     Shyamalendu Kundu
                                               v.
                                The State of West Bengal &amp; Ors.



      Mr. Manas Kumar Ghosh
      Ms. Susmita Dey Basu
      Mr. Arabinda Maji
                      ... for the petitioner.

      The report in the form of affidavit has been filed by the respondent no. 4.</pre>

<p id="p_1">      The stand taken by the said respondent is that the Corporation has taken

all steps in the matter as per the Government orders and directions issued from

time to time.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      The learned advocate representing the State respondents submits that the petitioner is not

entitled to the benefits in accordance with the Memorandum No. 3015-F dated March 13, 2001.

</p><p id="p_3">      The State respondents seek time to file an affidavit in the matter.

      The respondents are directed to file a short report in the form of affidavit

within December 5, 2019. The State respondents shall clearly specify whether the

aforesaid memo dated March 13, 2001 was at all brought to the knowledge of the

Corporation and when the same was forwarded to the Corporation.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         2</span>

List the matter on December 12, 2019.
</p>
