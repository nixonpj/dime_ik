<pre id="pre_1">                                            1                             State Vs. Kasim

                  IN THE COURT OF SHRI LALIT KUMAR, M.M
                          KARKARDOOMA COURTS: DELHI

                                                                     FIR No : 149/08
                                                                     U/s : 25 <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_1">Arms Act</a>
                                                                     P.S : Seema Puri
                                                                      State Vs. Kasim

JUDGEMENT
</pre><pre id="pre_2">1. Sl. No. of the case                                : 152/08
2. Date of institution of the case                    : 06.06.2008
3. Name of complainant                                : State
4. Date of commission of offence                      : 08.05.2008
5. Name of accused, parentage &amp; address               : Kasim s/o
                                                        Sh. Tohit Alam r/o Village
                                                        Gurgao, P.O Luhagra, PS
                                                        Bahadur Ganj, District Kishan
                                                        Ganj, Bihar.
6. Offence complained of or proved                    : 457/380/411 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>
7. Plea of accused                                    : Accused pleaded not guilty
8. Final order                                        : Convicted
9. Date of such order                                 : 02.08.2008


</pre><p id="p_1">              BRIEF REASONS FOR THE DECISION OF THE CASE


</p><p id="p_2">1.            The case of the prosecution is that on 08.05.2008 at about 2.00 pm at
     Old Seema Puri, Delhi on accused committed lurking house trespass in night
     into the house of complainant Sh. Khalil, and attempted to commit theft by
     removing his household articles, and subsequently caught red handed at the
     spot, and the present FIR was registered. On completion of other formalities,
     present challan was filed in the court by the IO through SHO.
</p>

<p id="p_3">2.            Copies of documents were supplied to the accused. Charge U/s
     457/380/511 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">IPC</a> was framed against the accused to which he pleaded not guilty
     and claimed for trial. Later, accused has expressed his intention to plead guilty.
     Separate plea of guilt of accused is recorded.
</p><p id="p_4">                                             2                           State Vs. Kasim

</p><p id="p_5">3.             The prosecution has examined only one witness in order to
     substantiate its case.
</p>

<p id="p_6">4.             I have heard arguments of Ld. APP for the State, and accused, and
     have perused the record very carefully.
</p>

<p id="p_7">5.             To prove its case, prosecution has examined first witness as PW1 Sh.
</p><p id="p_8">     Khalil, the complainant of the case. He has supported the prosecution story, and
     deposed on the lines of his statement ex. PW1/A.            He deposed that on
     08.05.2008, at about 2.00 am when he was sleeping in his house, he heard some
     noise of utensils. On this he woke up, and saw accused taking away their
     household items. He apprehended the accused, and called the police. He has
     proved site plan Ex.PW1/B, arrest memo of Accused ex.PW1/C, and personal
     search memo ex. PW1/D.
</p>

<p id="p_9">6.             However, during the course of prosecution evidence, accused pleaded
     guilty to the charge framed against him.    In the light of aforesaid evidence and
     the plea of guilt of the accused, he is convicted for the offence punishable u/s 25
     <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_4">Arms Act</a>.
</p>

<p id="p_10">DICTATED &amp; ANNOUNCED IN
THE OPEN COURT ON 02.08.2008

                                                         (LALIT KUMAR)
                                                  METROPOLITAN MAGISTRATE
                                                KKD COURTS, SHAHDARA, DELHI
 </p>
