<div class="doc_title">Akhatar Akbar Sayyad vs The State Of Maharashtra And Anr on 24 August, 2018</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: A.S. Gadkari</div>

<pre id="pre_1">  ssm                                                                        1                      906-revn29.16gp.doc

           IN THE  HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT BOMBAY

                        CRIMINAL APPELLATE JURISDICTION 

           CRIMINAL REVISION APPLICATION NO. 29 OF 2016

                                                       WITH

           CRIMINAL REVISION APPLICATION NO. 30 OF 2016

                                                       WITH

           CRIMINAL REVISION APPLICATION NO. 31 OF 2016

Akhatar Akbar Sayyad                                                                      ....Applicant

                  Vs.

State of Maharashtra &amp; Anr.                                                               ....Respondents. 

Mr. Satish Mishra for the Applicant.
Mr. A.R. Patil APP, for the Respondent-State.
Mr. Tushar Doshi, DCP, Crime Branch, Navi Mumbai.
Mr. P.G. Ghagare, PSI, Nerul Police Station, Navi Mumbai. 

                            CORAM  :  A. S. GADKARI, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">                                DATE  : 24th AUGUST, 2018.
</p><p id="p_2">P.C.:-
</p>
<p id="p_3">                  The   Applicant   is   convicted   for   an   offence   punishable

under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_1">Section 138</a> of the Negotiable Instruments Act by the learned

Judicial Magistrate, First Class, 5th Court, Vashi, District Thane by its

separate   Judgment   and   Order   dated   22nd  April,   2014   and   was

sentenced to suffer simple imprisonment for one year and to pay the

compensation as has been directed by the impugned Judgment and

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                                                                      1/5</span>
     ssm                                                                        2                      906-revn29.16gp.doc

Order   passed   in   the   aforesaid   matters.    The   Criminal   Appeals

preferred   by   the   Applicant   have   been   turned   down   by   the   learned

Second   Additional   Sessions  Judge,  Thane   by  its  separate  Judgment

and Order dated 23rd December, 2015. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">2                   Feeling aggrieved by the said Judgment and Orders, the

Applicant   preferred   the   present   Revision   Applications,   which   have

been   admitted   on   1st  February,   2016.     The   substantive   sentence

imposed   upon   the   Applicant   was   suspended   by   Order   dated   1 st

February 2016, passed by this Court, on the ground that the Applicant

had   expressed   his   desire   to   deposit   the   additional   amount   of

compensation before the Sessions Court, Thane, within six weeks from

1st February, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_5">3                   The record indicates that, the Applicant failed to comply

with his statement while getting an interim relief from this Court and

on the basis of his further statement, the said period to comply with it

was thereafter extended on 24th June, 2016 and 14th July, 2016 by this

Court. 
</p>
<p id="p_6">4                   On 18th  July, 2016, the Applicant filed an Affidavit-cum-

Undertaking before this Court. By the said Affidavit-cum-Undertaking,

the   Applicant   had   undertaken   to   pay   the   balance   sum   of


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                                                                                        2/5</span>
     ssm                                                                        3                      906-revn29.16gp.doc

Rs.50,00,000/- to the Respondent No.2 towards the settlement and

the said amount was to be paid on or before 18 th October, 2016.  This

Court,   therefore,   adjourned   the   matter   to   19th  August,   2016   for

compliance.  The said Affidavit-cum-Undertaking is on record. 

5                   The Applicant, thereafter, failed to comply with the said

undertaking   and   sought   extension   of   time   to   comply   with   it   and

therefore,   this   Court   by   its   Orders   dated   19 th  August,   2016,   23rd

August,   2016,   26th  August,   2016,   4th  October,   2016,   14th  October,

2016, 8th December, 2016, 14th December 2016, 23rd December 2016

extended the period to comply with the said Undertaking given to this

Court.
</p>
<p id="p_7">6                   On   28th  June,   2017,   this   Court   after   noticing   the   afore-

stated facts, issued directions to the Applicant to pay the said sum of

Rs.50,00,000/- to the Respondent No.2 on or before 5 th July, 2017 by

Pay Order of a Nationalized Bank and it was further directed that, if

the said payment is not made on or before 5 th July, 2017, the interim

relief/protection including suspension of sentence shall stand vacated

forthwith and the present Revision Applications shall stand dismissed

without further reference to the Court. </p><pre id="pre_2">

7                   The record further indicates that, on 9 th  November 2017


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                                                                                                        3/5</span>
     ssm                                                                        4                      906-revn29.16gp.doc

</pre><p id="p_8">and 12th February 2018, the learned counsel for the Applicant sought

adjournments   in   the   matters   and   therefore,   these   matters   were

adjourned to 26th February, 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_9">8                   By   self   operative   order   dated   26th  February   2018,   this

Court   issued   non-bailable   warrant   against   the   Applicant   to   be

executed through the Deputy Commissioner of Police in-charge, Sector

25, Nerul, Navi Mumbai and/or by the Deputy Commissioner of Police

in-charge Police Station, having jurisdiction over Section 19 of Nerul

Police Station, Navi Mumbai. 
</p>
<p id="p_10">                    In pursuance of the said issuance of non-bailable warrant

dated 26th February 2018, Mr. Tushar Doshi, DCP, Crime Branch, Navi

Mumbai has executed the said non-bailable warrant and produced the

Applicant before this Court today. 
</p>
<p id="p_11">9                   Today also, the learned counsel for the Applicant sought

adjournment   on   the   ground   that,   the   Applicant   is   still   ready   and

willing to comply with his undertaking dated 18 th  July, 2016.   The

facts narrated here-in-above and the record clearly indicates that, the

Applicant submitted the said Affidavit-cum-Undertaking only with a

view   to   procure   time   and   for   continuation   of   interim   relief   of

suspension of sentence and nothing else. 
</p>
<p id="p_12">
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                                                                                                        4/5</span>
</p><pre id="pre_3">   ssm                                                                        5                      906-revn29.16gp.doc

10                Though by a self operative order the Revision Applications

</pre><p id="p_13">were   dismissed,   the   Applicant  did not  surrender  himself before  the

Trial Court and therefore, this Court by an Order dated 26 th February

2018,   was   constrained   to   issue   non-bailable   warrant   against   the

Applicant.   The chronology mentioned here-in-above would make it

clear that, the Applicant has taken this Court and process of law for a

ride.  
</p>
<p id="p_14">                  As the Revision Applications are already dismissed on 24 th

June,   2016,   the   Applicant   is   remanded   to   the   judicial   custody   for

undergoing his balance sentence, as prescribed by the Trial Court by

its Judgment and Order dated 22nd April, 2014.

11                The concerned Police Officer, who is present in the Court,

to take necessary steps in that behalf. 
</p>
<p id="p_15">                  All the concerned to act on the basis of an authenticated

copy of this Order.                    </p><pre id="pre_4">

                                                                       (A.S. GADKARI, J.)


                                  Digitally signed
          SS                      by S S Mashalkar
          Mashalkar               Date: 2018.08.24
                                  17:50:15 +0530
