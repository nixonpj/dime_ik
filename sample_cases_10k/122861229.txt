                      HIGH COURT OF M.P. BENCH AT INDORE

                                  CRA No.8/2016
                                (Jagdish V/s. CBN)
                                  CRA No.291/2016
                                (Ghisibai V/s. CBN)
                                  CRA No.344/2016
                                (Badrilal V/s. CBN)
                                  CRA No.228/2016
                                (Labhchand V/s. CBN)
       DATED 13.4.2018
           Ms. Sonali Gupta, Advocate for the appellant (Jagdish)
             Shri M. Soni, Advocate for the respondent - Deptt.</pre>
<p id="p_1">             As per I.A.No.2152 of 2018, which is repeat application for
       suspension of jail sentence of the appellant Jagdish, on the ground of
       marriage of his son - Prahlad.
</p><p id="p_2">             As per report dated 12.4.2018 of the respondent, the date of
       marriage is 18.4.2018.
</p><p id="p_3">             On due consideration of the aforesaid, without expressing any
       opinion on merits of the case, we allow the prayer for suspension of jail
       sentence of the appellant (Jagdish) temporarily for a period of ten days,
       subject to his furnishing a bail bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two
       local sureties in the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court,
       appellant - Jagdish son of Devaji, be released temporarily for ten days
       only, with an undertaking that after the expiry of the aforesaid period of
       ten days, he will immediately surrender himself before the concerned trial
       Court. Failure to surrender before the concerned trial Court, the respondent
       shall be at liberty to take appropriate action, in accordance with law.
</p><p id="p_4">             With the aforesaid, I.A.No. 2152/2018 is disposed of.
</p>
