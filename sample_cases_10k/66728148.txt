<div class="doc_title">Narayan Agrawal @ Narayan Prasad ... vs The State Of Bihar on 16 May, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              Criminal Miscellaneous No.17196 of 2013
               ======================================================
               1. Narayan Agrawal @ Narayan Prasad Agrawal, son of Late Hanuman
                  Das Agrawal
               2. Sujeet Agrawal @ Sujeet Kumar Agrawal, son of Narayan Agrawal @
                  Noryan Prasad Agrawal
                                                                   .... .... Petitioner/s
                                               Versus
               The State Of Bihar
                                                              .... .... Opposite Party/s
               ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                                          ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">02/   16-05-2013                 Heard learned counsels for the petitioners

                    and the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioners are apprehending their arrest

                    in a case registered for the offences punishable under

                    <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_1">Sections 406</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_5">384</a>, <a href="/doc/1183069/" id="a_6">386</a> and <a href="/doc/695990/" id="a_7">364</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a> of the

                    Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 The accusation is of kidnapping the brother

                    of the informant in the background of some loan dispute

                    and the accused persons took the signature of the victim

                    on several papers including cheuqe.

</p><p id="p_4">                                 It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                    petitioners that the accusation was found false and final

                    form was submitted but subsequently differing with the

                    same, cognizance has been taken when the victim

                    returned immediately after institution of the case.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.17196 of 2013 (2) dt.16-05-2013</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                           2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_5">                                   Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above

                   named petitioners be released on anticipatory bail in the

                   event of their arrest or surrender before the learned court

                   below within a period of twelve weeks from today, on

                   furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) each

                   with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                   satisfaction of learned CJM, Munger in connection with

                   Kotwali P.S. Case No. 227 of 2011, subject to the

                   conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_9">Section 438(2)</a> of the

                   Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                  (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J.)
DKS/
 </p>
