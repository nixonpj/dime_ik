<div class="doc_title">M.Annadurai vs The Inspector Of Police on 16 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

				Dated     :  16.08.2017

CORAM

The Hon'ble Mr.Justice M.S.RAMESH

Writ Petition No.21578 of 2017 


M.Annadurai 								...Petitioner
Vs.

The Inspector of Police, 
Chinna Salem Police Station,
Villupuram District.   			  		                  ...Respondent


	Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India, praying to issue a writ of Mandamus to direct the respondent to give permission and protection to conduct cultural program(Dance and Music Program) in the Sri Mariyamman Temple situated at Nadu Street, Thottiyam Post, Kallkurichi Taluk, Villupuram District scheduled to be held on 26.08.2017 from 6 p.m to   11 p.m.
			 For Petitioner       :  Mr.N.Desinghu
   			 For Respondent     :  Mr.D.Vairamoorthy, 
  					            Special Government Pleader

O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	Seeking permission  to conduct cultural  programme in the event of           Sri Mariyamman Temple situated at Nadu Street, Thottiyam Post, Kallakurichi Taluk, Villupuram  District, scheduled to be held on 26.08.2017 between 6.00 p.m., to 11.00 p.m., the petitioner made a representation dated 03.08.2017 to the respondent.  As the same has not been considered, the present writ petition has been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	 2. Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the learned Special Government Pleader appearing for the respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. Learned  Special Government Pleader, on instructions, would submit that there is likelihood of arising law and order problem.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4. A mere apprehension cannot be a ground to reject the representation. There is absolutely no material before this Court to hold that there will be a law and order problem in  the event of permission being granted. In such view of the matter, the writ petition is allowed and the petitioner is permitted to conduct cultural programme in the event of Sri Mariyamman Temple situated at Nadu Street, Thottiyam Post, Kallakurichi Taluk, Villupuram  District, however, with the following conditions:-
</p><p id="p_5">	(a) The cultural programme in connection with in the event of Sri Mariyamman Temple situated at Nadu Street, Thottiyam Post, Kallakurichi Taluk, Villupuram  District, shall be conducted on 26.08.2017 between 06.00 p.m. and  11.00 p.m.
</p><p id="p_6">	(b)There should not be any kind of obscene dance or vulgar dialogues during the performance, by anyone of the participants.
</p><p id="p_7">	(c) Double meaning songs should not be played so as to spoil the minds of students and youths.
</p><p id="p_8">	(d) No dance or songs, touching upon any political party or religion or community or caste shall be played.
</p><p id="p_9">	(e) No flex boards in support of any political party or communal leader, shall be erected at the premises of the programme.
</p><p id="p_10">	(f) The function shall not affect either religious or communal harmony and shall be conducted without any discrimination based on caste.
</p><p id="p_11">	(g) If there is violation of any one of the conditions imposed, the concerned police officer is at liberty to take necessary action, as per law and stop such performance forthwith; and
</p><p id="p_12">	(h) Similarly, the police is directed to stop the dance programme, if it is played beyond the permitted time limit.
</p><p id="p_13">	  (i) The 2nd respondent is directed to issue necessary permission, incorporating the above conditions.
		 No costs.						
</p>
