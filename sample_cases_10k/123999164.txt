<pre id="pre_1">             IN THE COURT OF SH. MAHAVIR SINGHAL: POIT,
                    KARKARDOOMA COURTS, DELHI


I.D. No 509/10

Workman
Sh. Phool Chand s/o S. Mihi Lal, represented by Municipal Employees'
Union, Agarwal Bhawan, GT Road, Tis Hazari, Delhi.
                    Vs.

Management
M/s Municipal Corporation of Delhi, through its Commissioner, Town
Hall, Chandni Chowk, Delhi 110006.


Date of institution                   04.06.2010
Date of reserving award               07.10.2013
Date of award                         07.10.2013


Ref : F.24/(302)/ND/173/2008/Lab/4239-43 dated 02.06.2010


AWARD


1.</pre><p id="p_1">               Workman has raised the present industrial dispute through
     Union and on failure of conciliation proceedings, GNCT of Delhi referred
     the dispute to this Tribunal for adjudication in following terms of
     reference:-
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                  ''Whether demand of Sh. Phool Chand s/o Sh. Mihi
                  Lal, Electric Belar for regularisaton on the post of
                  Wireman Grade I with retrospective effect from
                  1.4.87 in proper pay scale and allowance alongwith
                  arrears of difference in wages as Beldar and Wireman

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">I.D. No 509/10                                                           Page 1 of 3</span>
                   Grade I, is justified, and if so, what directions are
                  necessary in this respect."
</blockquote><p id="p_2">2.               Pleadings were filed in the present case and on the basis of said
     pleadings, following issues were framed vide order sheet dated
     24.01.2012:
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                    1. Whether the present claim of the workman has been
                       properly espoused by the Union? OPW
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                    2. Whether any notice of demand was served upon the
                       management, if so, its effect? OPW
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">                    3. As per terms of reference.
</blockquote><p id="p_3">3.               Thereafter, case has been running at the stage of workman
     evidence. However, today, Ld. AR for workman appeared and submitted
     that the workman is not interested in pursuing the present matter.
     Statement of parties in this regard, have been recorded, which are being
     reproduced as under:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_5">                  Statement of Sh. Surender Bhardwaj, Ld. AR for workman
                               I am General Secretary of Municipal
                  Employees Union, for workman. I give statement on
                  behalf of the workman that he is not interested in pursuing
                  the present matter.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_6">                               It is, therefore, prayed that No Dispute Award
                  be passed accordingly.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_7">                  RO&amp;AC                       (MAHAVIR SINGHAL)
                                            POIT,KKD, DELHI 07.10.13

                  Statement of Sh. Abhinav Kumar, Ld. Proxy Counsel/AR
                  for Sh. Umesh Gupta, Ld. Counsel/AR for management.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_8">                             I have heard and read the statement of Ld. AR
                  for workman. Management has no objection if No Dispute
                  Award is passed in the present case, as prayed for
                  workman.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_9">                  RO&amp;AC                          (MAHAVIR SINGHAL)

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">I.D. No 509/10                                                                  Page 2 of 3</span>
                                            POIT,KKD, DELHI 07.10.13

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_10">4.               In view of statements of parties, nothing survives in the present
     matter to be adjudicated upon. Hence, No Dispute Award is passed in the
     present case and the reference is disposed of in these terms.
</blockquote><p id="p_4">5.               Copy of the award be sent to GNCT of Delhi for publication. File
     be consigned to Record Room.</p>
<p id="p_5">     Announced in open Tribunal
     on 07.10.2013                            (MAHAVIR SINGHAL)
                                       Presiding Officer, Industrial Tribunal
                                            Karkardooma Courts, Delhi.
</p>
