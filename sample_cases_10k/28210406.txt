<pre id="pre_1">        R/CR.MA/21945/2019                                            ORDER



          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

        R/CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 21945 of 2019
==========================================================
 SOHANSINH ARVINDSINH RAJPUT THRO CHIMANBHAI S/O RAVJIBHAI
                         THAKORE
                           Versus
                     STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR V B MALIK(5071) for the Applicant(s) No. 1
for the Respondent(s) No. 2,3
MS KRINA CALLA, AP (2) for the Respondent(s) No. 1
==========================================================
 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.H.VORA

                             Date : 27/11/2019

                              ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.    Learned advocate for the applicant states at bar that no
any other offence is registered against the applicant, except
present FIR.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.    Learned advocate Mr. Samir Khan states that he has
instructions to appear for respondent No.2 - complainant. He is
permitted to file his Vakalatnama.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.    Respondent no.2 - complainant is present before the Court
and admits correctness and genuineness of the affidavit filed by
her through her learned advocate. Learned advocate Mr.Khan
identifies   respondent      no.2      and        confirms   correctness            and
genuineness of the affidavit filed by her annexed at Annexure B.
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.    Rule. Learned A.P.P. Ms.Calla and learned advocate Mr.Khan
waive service of Rule for respondent Nos.1 and 2 respectively.
Learned APP objects quashment of present proceedings on the
premise of settlement.
</p>

<p id="p_5">         R/CR.MA/21945/2019                                ORDER



</p><p id="p_6">6.     With the consent of learned advocate for the applicant and
learned advocate for respondents, present application is taken up
for final disposal today.
</p>

<p id="p_7">7.     By way of the present application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the
Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (for short, the 'Code'), the
applicant pray for quashing and setting aside the F.I.R. being
C.R.No.I-101 of 2019 registered with Godhra 'A' Division Police
Station for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1535430/" id="a_2">Sections 328</a> and <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_3">380</a> of
IPC.
</p>

<p id="p_8">8.     Learned advocate for the applicant has taken this Court
through the factual matrix arising out of the present application.
</p>

<p id="p_9">9.     At the outset, it is submitted that the parties have amicably
resolved the dispute. In support of such submission made at bar
by the learned advocates appearing for the respective parties,
they have placed on record affidavit of settlement of dispute duly
signed by the respondent No.2 - complainant.
</p>
<p id="p_10">10.    Since now, the dispute with reference to the impugned
F.I.R. is settled and resolved by and between parties which is
confirmed by the original complainant through his learned
advocate, the trial would be futile and any further continuation of
proceedings would amount to abuse of process of law. Therefore,
the impugned F.I.R. is required to be quashed and set aside.
</p>
<p id="p_11">11.    Resultantly, this application is allowed. Impugned F.I.R.
being C.R.No.I-101 of 2019 registered with Godhra 'A' Division
Police Station and all other consequential proceedings arising out
of said FIR are hereby quashed and set aside qua the applicant
only. Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent. Direct
service is permitted.
</p>
