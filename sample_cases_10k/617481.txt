                                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">          Rule, returnable on 30.07.2018. Learned AGP waives service 
of   rule   on   behalf   of   respondent   No.1.   It   is   made   clear   that   the 
competent authority shall not pass any order of detention in case of 
solitary   offence   against   the   petitioner   since   the   same   is   not 
permissible   under   law.  However,   if  there   are   any   other   offences 
registered   in   near   past   or   hereinafter,   then   competent   authority 
may take appropriate steps in accordance with law irrespective of 
such protection. However, it is made clear that if at all petitioner is 
involved in any other similar offence and if the competent authority 
finds   that   there   is   sufficient   evidence   and   material   against   the 
petitioner   to   confirm   that   he   has   committed   such   offence, 
competent authority is free to pass appropriate orders under PASA 
Act, irrespective of this interim relief.  Direct Service is permitted 
upon the rest of the respondent/s. 
</p>


<p id="p_2">                                                                     (S.G. SHAH, J)
JIGNESH
