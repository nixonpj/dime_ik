<div class="doc_title">Hamiras Kishorkumar Rana vs State Of Gujarat on 19 October, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">               R/CR.MA/26094/2016                                                  ORDER


                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

                               CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION
                                (FOR ANTICIPATORY BAIL )
                                   NO. 26094 of 2016

         =============================================
                         HAMIRAS KISHORKUMAR RANA....Applicant
                                       Versus
                            STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent
         =============================================
         Appearance:
         MR HARDIK A DAVE, ADVOCATE for the Applicant
         MS MONALI BHATT, APP for the RESPONDENT
         =============================================

                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE PARESH UPADHYAY

                                    Date : 19/10/2016

                                      ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.     This is an application for anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2.     The applicant apprehends his arrest in connection with
         FIR being III-CR No.445 of 2016 registered with the Palasana
         Police Station, Dist. Surat for the offences punishable under
         <a href="/doc/1779880/" id="a_1">Sections 66(1)(B)</a>, <a href="/doc/26136/" id="a_2">65(A)(E)</a>, <a href="/doc/609127/" id="a_3">116(2)</a>, <a href="/doc/1159920/" id="a_4">98</a> and <a href="/doc/650803/" id="a_5">99</a> of the Indian
         Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         3.     Learned advocate for the applicant does not press this
         application.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.     This application is dismissed as not pressed. Rule is
         discharged. Interim protection is vacated.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                         (PARESH UPADHYAY, J.)
         M O Bhati/30
