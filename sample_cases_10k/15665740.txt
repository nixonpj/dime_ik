<div class="doc_title">Pappu Yadav vs The State Of Bihar on 17 April, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.15802 of 2019
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-186 Year-2018 Thana- PARBATTA District- Khagaria
                 ======================================================
                 Pappu Yadav Son of Late Gulab Yadav Resident of Village- Araria, P.S.-
                 Parbatta (Maraiya), Distt. Khagaria

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State Of Bihar Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Ranjit Kumar Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Kalyan Shankar
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   17-04-2019                     Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   Earlier the prayer for bail of the petitioner was

                   rejected by this Court, vide order dated 01.08.2018 passed in Cr.

                   Misc. No.44712 of 2018 and the allegation against the petitioner

                   is that they chased the deceased and made indiscriminate firing.

</p><p id="p_3">                                      Submission of the learned counsel for the

                   petitioner is that the specific allegation is against the co-accused

                   persons and he is in custody since 27.5.2018 and up-till-now

                   there is no progress in the trial. .
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   Heard learned A.P.P. and from perusal of the record

                   it appears that the charges have been framed and up till now

                   three prosecution witnesses have been examined and that shows

                   that the trial is going on.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   In such view of the matter, I am not inclined to
             Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.15802 of 2019(3) dt.17-04-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                      grant bail to the petitioner and the learned trial court is directed

                      to expedite the trial.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   With the aforesaid direction, this application is

                      dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">                                              (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
chn/-
</p>
