</pre><pre id="pre_2">                                     DATE      : 1st JULY 2019
P.C.:

1                   Heard Mr. Poojari and the following issues are framed :

                                         ISSUES

</pre><p id="p_1">                (1) Whether plaintiff proves proprietorship to trademark
                "Veer Chaap" or "ववर छछप " at Exhibit A to the plaint?

                (2) Whether the impugned mark at Exhibit G 1 and G 2 to
                the plaint is identical with or similar to plaintiff's mark at
                Exhibit A to the plaint?
</p>
<p id="p_2">                (3) Whether goods covered under the rival marks are same
                or similar?
</p>
<p id="p_3">                (4) Whether use of impugned mark at Exhibit G 1 and G 2
                to the plaint by defendants amounts to infringement of
                copyright and passing off goods?
</p>
<p id="p_4">                (5) Whether use of impugned mark at Exhibit G 1 and G 2
                to the plaint by defendants in relation to tobacco products
                or similar goods is liable for restraining under the law of
                infringement of copyright and passing off goods?

                (6) Whether plaintiff is entitled for accounts of profits?</p><pre id="pre_3">




Gauri Gaekwad
