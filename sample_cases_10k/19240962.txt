<div class="doc_title">M/S Shiv Shakti Medical Society vs Debts Recovery Appellate ... on 31 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1"> IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                             Civil Writ Petition No.16171 of 2017.
                             Date of Decision: July 31, 2017
M/s Shiv Shakti Medical Society                            ......Petitioner
                                versus
Debts Recovery Appellate Tribunal and another            .....Respondents

CORAM: HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE SURYA KANT.
         HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE SUDHIR MITTAL.
                     ***
Present: Mr.Anand Chhibbar, Senior Advocate with
         Mr.Vaibhav Sahni, Advocate, for the petitioner.
                      -.-

Surya Kant, J. (Oral)
</pre><p id="p_1">             Learned Debts Recovery Appellate Tribunal has passed the

impugned clarificatory order dated 18.07.2017 to the effect that there is no

interim stay operative after 18.02.2016. The main appeal has been fixed for

hearing on 04.08.2017. The bank has neither taken any step so far nor

appears to be inclined to take over the management of the Institutes being

run by the petitioner-Society.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             The instant writ petition is accordingly disposed of with a

request to the learned Tribunal to make an endeavour to hear the main case

on 04.08.2017.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Let management of the Institutes being run by the petitioner-

Society be not taken over by the Bank till 04.08.2017. This interim order

shall not be operative beyond 04.08.2017.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                                                  [SURYA KANT]
                                                      JUDGE


July 31, 2017                                   [SUDHIR MITTAL]
  mohinder                                          JUDGE

Whether Reportable                        :           Yes/No
