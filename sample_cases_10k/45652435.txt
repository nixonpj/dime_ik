<pre id="pre_2"> tkm/ct 28                            C.R.M. 9023 of 2019
sl no. 93
</pre><p id="p_1">             In Re : An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">section 438</a> of the Code of
             Criminal Procedure filed on 19.9.2019 in connection with Basirhat P.S case
             no. 87 of 2019 dated 24.1.2019 under <a href="/doc/1667388/" id="a_2">sections 419</a>/<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_3">420</a>/<a href="/doc/1317063/" id="a_4">465</a>/<a href="/doc/1053937/" id="a_5">466</a>/<a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_6">468</a>/<a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_7">471</a>
             of the Indian Penal Code and 14 of the <a href="/doc/27376/" id="a_8">Foreigners Act</a>
                                       And
Allowed
                  In Re : Dulal Roy                             ...... petitioner

                        Mr. D. Dandapath
                        Mr. P. Ghosh
                        Mr. A. K. Dutta
                                                          ...... for the petitioner
                        Mr. D. Deb
                                                          ...... for the State
                        Mr. Kallol Kr. Basu
                        Ms. D. Roy
                                                   ...... for the de facto complainant



                        Having considered the materials in the case diary and in
                  view of the fact that the Indian passport issued in favour of the
                  petitioner has not been cancelled as yet, we are inclined to grant
                  anticipatory bail to him.
</p><p id="p_2">                        Accordingly, we direct that in the event of arrest, the

                  petitioner shall be released on bail upon furnishing a bond of Rs.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                  10,000/- with two sureties of like amount each, to the

                  satisfaction of the Arresting Officer and also subject to the

                  conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_9">section 438(2)</a> of the Code of

                  Criminal Procedure, 1973 and on further condition that the

                  petitioner shall meet the investigating officer once in a week while

                  on bail until further orders and that the petitioner shall appear
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 2</span>


before the court below and pray for regular bail within four weeks

from date.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      The application being CRM 9023 of 2019 is disposed of.
</p>


<p id="p_5">(Jay Sengupta, J.)                        (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
