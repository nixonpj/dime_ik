           Ms. Archana Maheshwari, learned counsel for the

<p id="p_1">           Learned Counsel for the petitioner submits that Inquiry was
     concluded by the Team of Officers on 14.11.2013, but till today
     no action has been taken against the erring Doctors.
</p><p id="p_2">           Considering the aforesaid, petitioner is directed to file a
     representation before the respondent Nos.1 and 2 within a period
     of seven days from today. In case, such a representation is filed,
     the Competent Authority/respondent Nos.1 and 2 shall take
     appropriate action in the matter in accordance with law within a
     period of four weeks from the date of its receipt.
</p><p id="p_3">           With the aforesaid, Writ Petition No.1821/2016 stands
     disposed of.
</p><p id="p_4">           Certified copy, as per rules.
