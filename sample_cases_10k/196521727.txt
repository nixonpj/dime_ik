                                     JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">       This Second Appeal arises from the judgment and decree of the Sub

court, Thalassery in A.S.No.122 of 1994 confirming the final decree passed by

learned Additional Munsiff, Kannur on I.A.No.2110 of 1991 in O.S.No.119 of

1982.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

</p><p id="p_3">       2.     On the death of the appellant,               his legal representatives are

impleaded as appellants 2 to 5 and 8th respondent. Respondent/plaintiff is no

more and his legal representatives are impleaded as additional respondents 2 to

</p><p id="p_4">7.



</p><p id="p_5">       3.     Parties have settled the dispute between them outside court and

have executed an agreement dated 18.08.2011. Pursuant to that agreement,

they have filed I.A.No.473 of 2012 in this appeal requesting to pass a final

decree in terms of the settlement.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">       4.     Learned counsel on both sides requested that the compromise may

be accepted and a final decree may be passed in terms of the compromise and

in substitution of the final decree passed by the trial court on I.A.No.2110 of

1991 in O.S.No.119 of 1982 as confirmed by the first appellate court in

A.S.No.122 of 1994.
</p>
<p id="p_8"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">SA No.595/1996                            2</span>

</p><p id="p_9">       5.     Having regard to the request it is only to be allowed.



       Resultantly this Second Appeal is allowed of as under:



</p><p id="p_10">              i.    The compromise vide I.A.No.473 of 2012 is accepted and

recorded.
</p>
<p id="p_11">

</p><p id="p_12">              ii.   Second Appeal is allowed in terms of the compromise.



              iii.  In substitution of the final decree passed by the learned

Additional Munsiff, Kannur on I.A.No.2110 of 1991 in O.S.No.119 of 1982 and

confirmed by the learned Sub Judge, Thalassery in A.S.No.122 of 1994, final

decree is passed in terms of the settlement vide I.A.No.473 of 2012.



</p><p id="p_13">              iv.   The compromise and agreement annexed to it shall form

part of the decree of this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_14">

</p><p id="p_15">       All pending interlocutory applications will stand dismissed.




</p><p id="p_16">                                                 THOMAS P.JOSEPH,
                                                          Judge.
</p>
