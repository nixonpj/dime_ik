<p id="p_7">
      The Court : GA No.1528 of 2017 is an application filed by the judgment-debtor

nos.7 to 14 for substitution. A similar application has been filed by the decree-holder in

GA No.650 of 2017 for amendment of the execution application consequent upon the

disclosure of the names of the legal heirs and representatives of the deceased testator.

</p><p id="p_8">      In view thereof, GA No.650 of 2017 stands disposed of in terms of prayers (a) to

</p><p id="p_9">(h). The Department shall carry out the amendment as shown at page 20 onwards of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                              2</span>


supplementary affidavit affirmed on 21st April, 2017 by Ravi Kant Jajodia. This order

shall also dispose of GA No.1528 of 2017. No separate amendment need to be carried

out as prayed for in GA No.1528 of 2017. The service of notice upon the substituted

judgment-debtors shall be considered later on.

</p><p id="p_10">        GA no.1528 of 2017 and GA No.650 of 2017, accordingly, stand disposed of.

        However, there shall be no order as to costs.



</p><p id="p_11">                                                                    (SOUMEN SEN, J.)
B.Pal
