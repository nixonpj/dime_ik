<div class="doc_title">Dinesh Kumar Deo vs Kanhaya Lal Jalan &amp; Ors. on 18 November, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             C.R. No.1667 of 2009
                            DINESH KUMAR DEO
                                      Versus
                        KANHAYA LAL JALAN &amp; ORS.
                                    -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">11   18.11.2010   Re. I.A. Nos. 6553 of 2010 &amp; 8047 of 2010

                           Learned counsel      for the petitioner     seeks

                  permission to make necessary correction in provision

                  of law as well as in paragraph nos. 1, 2 and prayer

                  portion in aforesaid applications.

</p><p id="p_2">                           Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.

                            I.A. No. 6553 of 2010       has been filed for

                  expunging the name of the deceased opposite party no.

                  10, namely, Nandkari Devi, and for impleading her

                  legal representatives as fully described in paragraph no.

                  4 as opposite parties.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                            I.A. No. 8047 of 2010       has been filed for

                  condoning the delay in taking such steps.

</p><p id="p_4">                             It is submitted on behalf of the petitioner

                  that the opposite party no. 10, who was substituted as a

                  plaintiff in the court below during trial of the eviction

                  suit concerned. However, she died on 24.4.2005, but the

                  plaintiffs did not take any steps in the eviction suit for
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                          2</span>




        substitution   of her legal representatives. Thus, this

        petitioner also could not know about the factum of the

        same. Such fact could be known to the petitioner only

        on 11.5.2010 upon perusal of the report of the postal

        peon.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                   Learned counsel appearing for the opposite

        parties does not raise any objection.

</p><p id="p_6">                   As a result, I.A. No. 8047 of 2010 is allowed

        and delay in preferring I.A. No. 6553 of 2010 is

        condoned.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                   Upon hearing the parties and in the aforesaid

        facts and circumstances I.A. No. 6553 of 2010 is also

        allowed. Let the name of the deceased opposite party

        no. 10 be expunged from the array of the opposite

        parties.   Let her legal representatives   described   in

        paragraph no. 4 be impleaded as opposite party in this

        case.
</p>
<p id="p_8">


</p><pre id="pre_2">Spd/-                          ( Dr. Ravi Ranjan, J.)
 </pre>
