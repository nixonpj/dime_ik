<pre id="pre_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                   BENCH AT JAIPUR

          S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail No. 7692/2018

Gajendra Singh S/o Jaswant Singh , Ranjeet Coloni Sogariya P.s.
Railway Coloni Kota. (At Present Confined At Central Jail Kota)
                                                          ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State Of Rajasthan Through Pp , Jaipur
                                                        ----Respondent</pre>


<p id="p_1">For Petitioner(s)       :   Mr. Abdul Rahim Khan
For Respondent(s)       :   Mr. Sudesh Saini, P.P.



</p><p id="p_2">          HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PANKAJ BHANDARI

                                Order

05/07/2018

</p><p id="p_3">1.   Petitioner has filed this bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section

439</a> of Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">2.   F.I.R. No. 224/2018 was registered at Police Station Vigyan

Nagar Kota City for offence under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_2">Section 307</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of I.P.C.


</p><p id="p_6">3.   It is contended by counsel for the petitioner that there is no

allegation against the petitioner of use of fire arm.


</p><p id="p_7">4.   Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.


</p><p id="p_8">5.   I have considered the contentions.


</p><p id="p_9">6.   Considering the contentions put forth by counsel for the

petitioner, I deem it proper to allow the bail application.


</p><p id="p_10">7.   This bail application is accordingly allowed and it is directed

that accused petitioner shall be released on bail provided he

furnishes a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees Fifty
                                                                   (2 of 2)             [CRLMB-7692/2018]


                                   Thousand only) together with two sureties in the sum of

                                   Rs.25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand only) each          to the

                                   satisfaction of the learned trial court with the stipulation that he

                                   shall appear before that Court and any court to which the matter

                                   is transferred, on all subsequent dates of hearing and as and when

                                   called upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_11">
</p><p id="p_12">                                                                               (PANKAJ BHANDARI),J

                                   Amit/69
