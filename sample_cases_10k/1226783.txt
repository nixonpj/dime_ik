</p><p id="p_9">                 The application (I.A. No. 1414 of 2011) for condoning the delay of
      113 days in filing this appeal has been submitted. However, it appears that the
      learned Single Judge held that the order passed by the Additional Collector has been
      set aside by the Deputy Commissioner, Dhanbad, whereas he had no appellate
      jurisdiction to entertain the appeal. Since nothing has been shown in this L.P.A. that
      how the Deputy Commissioner has applied appellate jurisdiction without there being
      any statutory power, we are not inclined to condone the delay. Hence the application
      for condoning the delay (I.A. No. 1414 of 2011) as well as this appeal, both are
      dismissed.
</p>
