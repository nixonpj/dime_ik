</p><p id="p_2">            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ASHOK KUMAR GAUR

                                       Order

19/12/2019

     Learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the

controversy involved in the present writ petition is about treating

the petitioner as Disqualified Directors under <a href="/doc/310282/" id="a_1">Section 164(2)(a)</a> of

the Companies Act, 2013. The petitioner has also sought direction

to use their Director Identification Number (DIN) and Digital

Signature Certificate for the purpose of filing their returns.

</p><p id="p_3">     Learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the

Division     Bench   of    this    Court       in    D.B.Civil      Writ   Petition

No.4062/2019 (Narendra Kumar Jat Vs. Union of India) has

already passed interim order and directions have been given to

reactivate the Director Identification Number (DIN) and further

Digital Signature Certificate is required to be issued in the capacity

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                       (Downloaded on 20/12/2019 at 09:31:17 PM)</span>
                                                                              (2 of 2)                [CW-21812/2019]



                                   of the Director to file necessary annual returns to discharge

                                   statutory obligations of all the other companies, wherein the

                                   petitioner is Director.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                         This Court, as an interim measure, directs the respondents

                                   to reactivate the Director Identification Number (DIN) of the

                                   petitioner and Digital Signature Certificate to enable the petitioner

                                   to file necessary annual returns and also to discharge their

                                   statutory obligations.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                         The interim order, passed by this Court, will remain subject

                                   to final outcome of the present writ petition.

</p><p id="p_6">                                         Let the case be listed before the Division Bench.

                                         Learned counsel for the petitioner is directed to file additional

                                   copy of the writ petition enabling Registry to list the matter before

                                   the Division Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                                                                     (ASHOK KUMAR GAUR),J

                                   Monika/163
