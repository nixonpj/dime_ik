                                                      ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                             This Criminal Original Petition is filed to quash the FIR in Crime

                      No.288 of 2018 on the file of the first respondent police for the

                      offences under <a href="/doc/513074/" id="a_2">Sections 109</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_3">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_4">148</a>, <a href="/doc/594493/" id="a_5">294(B)</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_6">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_7">324</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_8">506(ii)</a> of

                      IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             2.By passage of time, the parties have decided to bury their

                      hatchet and compromise the dispute amicably among themselves.



</p><p id="p_3">                             3.A joint memo of compromise has been filed before this Court,

                      which has been signed by the petitioners and the second respondent

                      and also by their respective counsel. The petitioners and the second

                      respondent were also present in person before this Court and they

                      were identified by the respondent police.     This Court also enquired

                      both the parties and was satisfied that the parties have come to an

                      amicable settlement between themselves.

</p><p id="p_4">                             4.Under such circumstances, no useful purpose will be served

                      in keeping the FIR in Crime No.288 of 2018 pending. In the light of

                      the guidelines given by the Hon'ble Supreme Court reported in 2017

                      9 SCC 641-(Parbathbhai Aahir @ Parbathbhai Vs. State of

                      Gujrath), this Court in exercise of its jurisdiction under Section 482

                      Cr.P.C. quashes the FIR in Crime No.288 of 2018.



http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                           3</span>



</p><p id="p_5">                            5.This Criminal Original Petition stands allowed and as a

                      sequel, the FIR in Crime No.288 of 2018, on the file of the Sivagangai

                      Taluk Police Station, Sivagangai, is quashed and the terms of Joint

                      Memo of Compromise shall form part and parcel of this order. Each of

                      the petitioner shall pay a sum of Rs.1,000/- (Rupees One Thousand

                      only) as costs, to the credit of the Chief Justice Relief, within a period

                      of two weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of this order and file a

                      photocopy of the receipt along with a memo reporting compliance in

                      the Registry.
</p>
