<p id="p_1">                   The petitioner had sought a direction to respondents No. 1 to 4

      to hand over the investigation in FIR No. 0448 dated 17.11.2015 under

      <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_2">201</a> of the Indian Penal Code registered at Police Station

      Gohana City, District Sonepat, to the Crime Branch or Central Bureau of

      Investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                   On 03.04.2019, learned State counsel had sought time to seek

      instructions as to whether a Special Investigation Team can be constituted

      to investigate the matter.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                   Learned State counsel has handed over a copy of the

      communication dated 16.04.2019 from the Superintendent of Police,

      Sonepat, wherein it is mentioned that a Special Investigation Team headed

      by Smt. Susheela Devi, Deputy Superintendent of Police,Gohana, has been

      constituted to investigate the case thoroughly, fairly and impartially.

</p><p id="p_4">                   Learned counsel for the petitioner contends that a Special
