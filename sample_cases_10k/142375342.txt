                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.36259 of 2014
                  Arising Out of PS.Case No. -25 Year- 2014 Thana -SHAKURABAD District- JEHANABAD

               ======================================================
               1. Radhey Mohan Singh, son of Laljee Singh
               2. Girija Nand Singh, son of Bineshwar Mahto
               3. Vikash Kumar, son of Girija Nand Singh
               4. Gulshan Kumar, son of Vidya Singh @ Bidya Bhushan Singh
               5. Bilas Singh, Son of Laxman Singh ,
               All resident of village - Murhara , Police Station Shakurabad , District -
               Jehanabad .                                          .... .... Petitioners
                                                 Versus
               The State of Bihar.                                        .... .... Opposite Party
               ======================================================
               Appearance :
               For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Vijay Kumar, Advocate
               For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Anuj Kr. Srivastav, APP
               ======================================================
               CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ASHWANI KUMAR SINGH
               ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2 01-04-2015                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned

                counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              The petitioners seek anticipatory bail in connection

                with Shakurabad P.S. Case No. 25 of 2014 registered for the

                offences punishable under Sections 323, 353 and 504 read with

                34<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              It has been contended that there is no truth behind the

                allegation made in the FIR and as a matter of fact, the parties

                have compromised the case outside the Court and a petition to

                this effect has been filed in the Court on 7th July, 2014.

</p><p id="p_4">                              Regard being had to the facts and circumstances of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2              Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.36259 of 2014 (2) dt.01-04-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                        2/2</span>




                         the case, in the event of arrest or surrender in the court below

                         within a period of four weeks from today, let the petitioners,

                         above named, be released on anticipatory bail on furnishing bail

                         bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the

                         like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial

                         Magistrate, Jehanabad in connection with Shakurabad P.S. Case

                         No. 25 of 2014 subject to the conditions as laid down under

                         <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_2">Section 438(2)</a> Cr. P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                                 (Ashwani Kumar Singh, J.)
        Sanjeet/-
</p>
