      IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA
                   AT CHANDIGARH

                             Civil Writ Petition No. 18166 of 2018 (O&amp;M)
                             Date of Decision: 01.08.2018

Dr. Samrat Vikram Sahgal
                                                                 .......... Petitioner
                                         Versus
State of Punjab and others
                                                              .......... Respondents

CORAM:       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JASWANT SINGH

Present:      Mr. Ranjit Singh Ghuman, Advocate
              for the petitioner.

              Mrs. Anu Chatrath Kapur, Additional Advocate General, Punjab
              for the respondents/State and assisted by
              Mr. Ajay Sharma, Sr. Assistant, O/o Directorate of Ayurveda, Chandigarh

                                 ****

JASWANT SINGH, J. (ORAL)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">              Petitioner (Dr. Samrat Vikram Sahgal) is stated to be

completing his second year's extension in service in the rank of District

Ayurvedic and Unani Officer, Jalandhar on 31.10.2018.

</p><p id="p_2">              The grievance as set out in the petition is that vide impugned

order dated 19.07.2018 (Annexure P-15), the petitioner has been

transferred from his place of posting at Jalandhar to report at the Head

Quarter at Chandigarh.
</p>
<p id="p_3">              It is contended that such an action is totally motivated and at

the instance/political influence of one Dr. Pritpal Singh, Ayurvedic Medical

Officer (Retd.), who has become inimical on account of the petitioner

having asked him about his previous misconduct during his service, while

posting at the various Community Health Centres.

</p><p id="p_4">            Upon notice, learned State Counsel, on instructions from Mr.

Ajay Sharma, Sr. Assistant, O/o Directorate of Ayurveda, Chandigarh,

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> C.W.P. No. 18166 of 2018 (O&amp;M)                                            -2-</span>


submits that the transfer of the petitioner has been ordered on account of

certain complaints qua which the discreet enquiries were made and the

allegations found true.
</p>
<p id="p_5">               At this stage, counsel for the petitioner, on instructions from his

client, prays for withdrawal the present petition with liberty to pursue his

remedy before the authorities concerned.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                 Dismissed as withdrawn with the aforesaid liberty.
