                                     AWARD
                A reference no. F. 24 (411)/Lab./SD/09/1919 dated 17.02.2010 
was sent by Sh. Rajender Dhar, Under Secretary (Labour), Govt. of NCT of 
Delhi   for   adjudication   and   disposal   of   an   industrial   dispute   between   the 
aforesaid workman and the management by formulating the following issue:
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                      "Whether   the   services   of   Sh.   Mohd. 
                      Munish S/o Sh. Mohd. Hanish have been 
                      illegally   and/or   unjustifiably   terminated 
                      by the management; if yes, what relief he 
                      is   entitled   to   and   what   directions   are 
                      necessary in this respect?"
</blockquote>


<blockquote id="blockquote_2">ID No. 293/10                                                                              1/2
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3"> 2.              Notice   of   this   reference   was   sent   to   the   workman.   Today   the 
workman appeared in the court and stated that due to some personal reason, 
he does not want to pursue this case any further.  His statement is recorded in 
this regard separately.
</blockquote>

<p id="p_8">3.              In   view   of   statement   given   by   workman,   no   dispute   award   is 
passed against him and the reference is answered accordingly.
</p>
