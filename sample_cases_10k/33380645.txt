<p id="p_1">      The arguments presently advanced by the learned

counsel for the petitioner that the matters involved are purely

concerning a civil dispute, cannot be considered by this Court

at this stage. If the allegations can be proved, the offences

alleged against the petitioner will lie.

</p><p id="p_2">      With liberty to the petitioner to take up the very same

contentions before the court below, at appropriate stage, viz.

either at the stage of <a href="/doc/897083/" id="a_1">Section 239</a> Cr.P.C. or during trial, as the

case may be, this this Crl.M.C. is disposed of.
