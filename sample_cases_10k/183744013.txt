                 For the Petitioner       :    Mr. Shakil Ahmad Khan, Adv.

                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">4   06-09-2018                 Heard learned counsel for petitioner and learned

                  counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               Petitioner is apprehending his arrest in connection

                  with Complaint Case No. 57 of 2018 for the offences registered

                  under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_1">Sections 406</a> and <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a> of the Indian Penal Code and under

                  <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_3">Section 138</a> of N.I. Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               Allegation against the petitioner is that he has taken

                  friendly loan of Rs. 10,00,000/- for the purpose of business on

                  15.01.2016 and he has not returned the said amount within time.

                  It has been further submitted that petitioner gave a cheque of Rs.

                  10,00,000/- to the complainant which was dishonored due to

                  insufficient amount in the account of petitioner which is an
            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.33672 of 2018(4) dt.06-09-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                    offence under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_4">Section 138</a> of N.I. Act which is bailable .

</p><p id="p_4">                                 Informant in his complaint has stated that he had

                    provided a friendly loan as such no case of Criminal Breach of

                    Trust or fraudulent intention is made out. It has been further

                    stated that informant has somehow obtained the cheque of

                    petitioner and misused it. Informant has filed similar case

                    against 10 persons with similar allegation.

</p><p id="p_5">                                 Considering the facts and circumstances of the present

                    case, let petitioner, above named, in the event of arrest or

                    surrender within a period of four weeks from the date of

                    receipt/production of a copy of this order, be enlarged on bail on

                    furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand only) with

                    two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the

                    learned A.C.J.M. II, Aurangabad in connection with Complaint

                    Case No. 57 of 2018, subject to the condition as laid down

                    under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_5">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                                  (S. Kumar, J.)
Rajiv/manoj

U      T
