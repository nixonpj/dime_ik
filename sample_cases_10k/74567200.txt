<p id="p_1">                                After arguing for sometime, learned counsel for the

                    petitioners seeks and is granted permission to withdraw the instant

                    petition.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Dismissed as withdrawn.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                If the petitioner No.1 moves the trial Court seeking

                    exemption from personal appearance, she will be granted

                    exemption on furnishing following undertaking:-

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                1.    that her advocate shall appear on her behalf on
                                      each and every date of hearing and the evidence
                                      recorded in her absence would be acceptable to
                                      her;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">                                2.    that undertaking of her counsel to appear on each
                                      date of hearing to defend her case;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">                                3.    that she will not dispute her identity and she will
                                      appear before the trial Court as and when
                                      directed by the Court.
</blockquote>
