CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.S.RAMESH

and 
M.P(MD)No.1 of 2013  

Arumugavel                                                                ..Petitioner

Vs 

The Inspector of Police,
Kadaladi Police Station,
Ramanathapuram District.                                                  ..Respondents.

Prayer: Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India,

relating to the impugned notice issued  by the respondent in his proceedings
in Na.Ka.No.Nil, dated 28.10.2013 and to quash the same as illegal.

!For Petitioner     :Mr.R.Vignesh
                                              for M/s.R.Gandhi
                
For Respondent:  Mr.A.Muthukaruppan   
                                               Addl.Govt.Pleader

                        
:ORDER

<p id="p_1">        Mr.R.Vignesh, learned counsel representing  the learned counsel on

</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.In view of the same, the Writ Petition is dismissed as infructuous.
Consequently, connected Miscellaneous Petition is  dismissed. No costs.</p><pre id="pre_2">


To
