<div class="doc_title">Jakhra Samsubhai @ Samsuddin ... vs State Of Gujarat on 2 February, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                 R/CR.MA/2619/2016                                              ORDER



                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

          CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 2619 of
                                             2016
         ==========================================================
              JAKHRA SAMSUBHAI @ SAMSUDDIN DADABHAI....Applicant(s)
                                     Versus
                        STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         PARTY-IN-PERSON, PERSONAL CAPACITY for the Applicant.
         MS MD MEHTA, LD.ADDL.PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the Respondent.
         ==========================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI

                                     Date : 02/02/2016


                                      ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.          By way of the present application, party-in-person -
         wife of the undertrial prisoner has prayed to release her
         husband on temporary bail on the ground of her sickness i.e.
         sickness of the wife of the under-trial prisoner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2.          I have heard party-in-person and Ms.M.D.Mehta,
         learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the respondent- State
         of Gujarat and considered the averments made in this
         application. I have considered the fact that under-trial
         prisoner was recently released on temporary bail by this
         Court in the month of December,2015.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         3.          Considering the peculiar facts and circumstances
         of the case, I do not find any sufficient reason to entertain this
         application. Hence, the present application is rejected.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                                       [A.J.DESAI,J.]
