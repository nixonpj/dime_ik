<p id="p_1">                                  The writ petition was listed for hearing on 07.12.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                      None appeared for the writ petitioner. Thus, the case was directed to

                      be listed on 10.12.2018 under the caption “For Dismissal”.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                                  2. When the matter is taken up for hearing today i.e., on

                      10.12.2018, there is no representation on behalf of the writ petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                      Thus, it is to be construed that the writ petitioner is not interested in

                      pursuing the matter.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                                  3. Accordingly, the writ petition stands dismissed for non-
</p>
<p id="p_6">                      prosecution. However, there shall be no order as to costs.
