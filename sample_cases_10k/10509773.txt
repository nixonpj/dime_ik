<div class="doc_title">Antim vs Smt. Reena on 28 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                          HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                                1                                                  MCC No.660/18

                                                          MCC No.660/2018
                                                      (Antim Vs. Smt. Reena)
                                Indore, Dated : 28.8.2018
                                          Shri P. K. Vishwakarma, learned counsel for the
                                applicant.

                                          Shri   Ashish   Joshi,   learned   counsel   for   the
                                respondent.</pre>

<p id="p_1">                                          Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                          This application under Section 24 of the CPC has
                                been filed for transfer of HMA Case No.52/2016 from the
                                court of 15th Additional District Judge, Indore to the Court of
                                Addl. Principal Judge, Family Court, Indore.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                          Learned counsel for the applicant submits that the
                                applicant's divorce petition is pending before the Addl.
                                District Judge, whereas other two cases namely; the case
                                under <a href="/doc/1056396/" id="a_1">Section 125</a> of the Cr.P.C. being MJC No.1039/2016
                                and HMA Case No.1409/2016 under <a href="/doc/322349/" id="a_2">Section 9</a> of the Hindu
                                Marriage Act are already pending before the Family Court,
                                Indore, therefore, the pending case before the Addl. District
                                Judge, Indore be transferred to the 1st Addl. Principal
                                Judge, Family Court, Indore.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                          Learned counsel for the respondent fairly submits that
                                he has no objection in transferring the case.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                          Having regard to the consent of the parties and also
                                taking note of the fact that the case under <a href="/doc/322349/" id="a_3">Section 9</a> of the
                                Hindu Marriage Act is already pending before the 1 st Addl.
                                Principal Judge, Family Court, Indore, the present MCC is
                                allowed and the HMA Case No.52/2016 is transferred from
                                the Court of Addl. District Judge, Indore to the Court of 1 st

Date: 29/08/2018 18:21:09
                                            HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH

                                Addl. Principal Judge, Family Court, Indore where the other
                                two cases are pending, for its decision in accordance with
                                law.
</p>
