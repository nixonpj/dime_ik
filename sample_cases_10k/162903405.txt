<div class="doc_title">Uday Das vs The State Of West Bengal And Others on 22 June, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                  1




30
Suman
        22.06.16
                                               W.P. 10213 (W) of 2016

                                                     Uday Das
                                                        Vs.
                                        The State of West Bengal and others



                           Mr. N. I. Khan
                           Mr. Amlan Kumar Mukherjee
                                          ..for the petitioner

                           Mr. Pantu Deb Roy
                           Mr. Lal Mohan Basu
                                         ...for the State



                           Affidavit of service filed in Court today be kept with the record.

                           In the writ petition, it is the case of the petitioner that he has made an

                   application on 16th June, 2015 with requisite fees before the Regional Transport

                   Authority, Nadia being the respondent no.2 herein, for grant of contract carriage</pre>

<p id="p_1">                   permit in respect of the auto rickshaw in the route Taldaha Ghat to Shibnibash

                   Mandir Ghat in accordance with the provisions of <a href="/doc/770795/" id="a_1">Sections 66</a>, <a href="/doc/118713/" id="a_2">69</a>, <a href="/doc/1899504/" id="a_3">73</a> and <a href="/doc/1785632/" id="a_4">80</a> of

                   the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988. The said application has been made being

                   annexure "P-1" to the writ petition.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                           Since the authority has not yet considered the said application, I direct

                   the respondent no.2, the Regional Transport Authority, Nadia to consider and

                   dispose of the said application of the petitioner praying for grant of contract

                   carriage permit. The authority is directed to take a final decision by way of a

                   reasoned order, after giving an opportunity of hearing to the petitioner, within a

                   period of six weeks from the date of communication of this order and to
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 2</span>

communicate a copy of the said reasoned order to the petitioner within a period

of two weeks thereafter.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        With this direction, this writ petition is disposed of.

        However, there will be no order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        Urgent certified photostat copy of this order, if applied for, shall be

given to the parties as expeditiously as possible on compliance of all necessary

formalities.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                (Samapti Chatterjee, J.)
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> 3</span>
