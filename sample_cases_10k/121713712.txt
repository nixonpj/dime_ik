         Prosecutor waives service of notice of Rule on behalf of

         respondent State.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">         2.   This application, dated 26.07.2017, has been preferred

         by the applicant, through jail, with a prayer to enlarge him on

         temporary bail for a period of 30 days in order to provide his

         family with all means of livelihood and sustenance and

         financial help.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">         3.   The applicant is undergoing life imprisonment for the

         offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_3">506(2)</a> of the

<p id="p_7">

         as well as the jail remarks pertaining to the applicant which

         indicate that his conduct in jail is good. The applicant has

         surrendered on time on the previous occasions when he was

         enlarged on temporary bail.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">         5.   Considering the reasons stated in the application, we

         deem it proper to partly grant the prayer made in the

         application. Hence, we pass the following order.



</p><p id="p_11">              The applicant shall be released on temporary bail for a

              period of 10 days from the date of his actual release

              upon furnishing a personal bond of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees

              Five Thousand only) and upon the usual terms and

              conditions, to the satisfaction of the concerned Jail
