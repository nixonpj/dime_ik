<div class="doc_title">Arun Paswan @ Arun Kumar Paswan vs The State Of Bihar on 17 March, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Criminal Miscellaneous No.9202 of 2015
                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -73 Year- 2013 Thana -GAYGHAT District- MUZAFFARPUR
                   ======================================================
                   Arun Paswan @ Arun Kumar Paswan S/o Sri Chandradeep Paswan,
                   Resident of Village - Afjalpur Puraina, P.S. - Patepur, District - Vaishali.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                   The State of Bihar

                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Sunil Kumar Singh
                   For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Anuradha Singh(App)
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                   SINGH
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">02/   17-03-2015                             Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and

                   the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                             The petitioner is languishing in custody since

                   12.06.2014 in a case registered for the offences punishable under

                   <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_1">Sections 395</a> and <a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_2">397</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                             A truck loaded with hundred bundle of

                   rubber worth Rs.25,45,000/- was robbed by ten miscreants. The

                   FIR was lodged against unknown. The name of the petitioner

                   sprang up during investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                             A statement has been made in para 7 of the

                   petition that neither there is any recovery from the petitioner nor

                   the petitioner has been put on TIP till date.

</p><p id="p_5">                                            Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.9202 of 2015 (02) dt.17-03-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                    named petitioner, be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of

                    Rs.10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                    each to the satisfaction of learned Judicial Magistrate, Ist Class,

                    Muzaffarpur in connection with Gaighat P.S. Case No. 73 of 2013.

</p><p id="p_6">                                               Since the petitioner has serious criminal

                    antecedent, the learned court below will be at liberty to cancel the

                    bail bonds of the petitioner, if he substantially gets involved in

                    some serious nature of the offence or defaults for two consecutive

                    occasions.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">                                               (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
    DKS/-
</p>
