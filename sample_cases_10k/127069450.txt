                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -80 Year- 2010 Thana -CHENARI District- SASARAM (ROHTAS)

                    Arjun Paswan, son of Budhan Paswan, resident of village Sahpur Police
                    Station Chenari Distt. Rohtas

                                                           Versus
                    1. The State of Bihar

                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                    ======================================================
                    Appearance :
                    For the Petitioner/s    :  Mr. Dr. Shraddhanand Paswan-Advocate

                    CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ADITYA KUMAR TRIVEDI
                    ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">03   19-10-2016                        Learned Additional Public Prosecutor so assigned

                    with the case is absent. However, learned Additional Public

                    Prosecutor, Smt. Sharda Kumari submits that she be allowed to

                    represent the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                       Report has been received at the end of learned

                    Additional Sessions Judge-II, Rohtas at Sasaram wherefrom it is

                    evident that after framing of charge, up-till now, nine PWs have

                    already been examined. Subsequently thereof, the matter is

                    lingering for the evidence of Narendra Kumar Singh, I.O., who

                    has now retired.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                       That being so, the Superintendent of Police, Rohtas

                    at Sasaram is directed to procure attendance of the I.O. before the

                    Court for his evidence on the next date, positively, failing which
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4172 of 2016 (04) dt.19-10-2016                      2</span>




                        necessary order will follow.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                           A copy of the order should be served upon the

                        learned Advocate General for compliance.

</p><p id="p_5">                                           Office to communicate and procure report from the

                        Superintendent of Police, Rohtas at Sasaram within four weeks

                        and list accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                 (Aditya Kumar Trivedi, J)
Vikash/-
