<div class="doc_title">Kailash Modi @ Kailash Barnwal @ ... vs State Of Bihar on 2 September, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">            IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                      Cr.Misc. No.17502 of 2009
   KAILASH MODI @ KAILASH BARNAWAL @ KAILASH PRASAD BARNWAL
                                Versus
                        THE STATE OF BIHAR
                               -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">02   02.09.2009          Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                         Petitioner seeks bail in a case instituted under

                <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">Sections 379</a> and <a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_2">411</a> of the Indian Penal Code and 33,

                42 and 44 of the <a href="/doc/654536/" id="a_3">Indian Forest Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                         It is contended that neither the petitioner was

                arrested with the tractor or at the place of occurrence.

                The name of the petitioner was disclosed by the driver of

                the tractor. It is contended that the petitioner is neither

                the owner of the tractor nor is a license holder for

                excavation by the competent authority.

</p><p id="p_4">                         In the facts and circumstances of the case,

                above named petitioner, in the event of arrest or

                surrender in the court below within four weeks from the

                date of receipt of a copy of this order, shall be enlarged on

                bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten

                thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to

                the satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Jamui in

                connection with Sono P.S. Case No. 150 of 2008 , subject

                to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/654536/" id="a_4">Section 438</a> (2)<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_5"> of

                the Code</a> of Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">         P.K.                        (K.K. Mandal, J.)
