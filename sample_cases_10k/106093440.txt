         1      Heard   Ms.   Jirga   Jhaveri,   learned   Additional 

         Public Prosecutor, for the respondents. 



         2      This   is   an   application   filed   by   the   applicant 

         through jail, seeking temporary bail for a period of 21 

         days.   The   jail   record   of   the   applicant   indicates   that, 

         during   the   period   of   incarceration   of   four   and   a   half 

         years,   the   applicant   has   availed   of   temporary   bail   on
