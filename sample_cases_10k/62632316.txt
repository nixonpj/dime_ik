                         J U D G M E N T

<p id="p_1">      The petitioner claims that he is the owner of 12.1 Acres of

land in Kakkulissery and Kodungallur villages. He approached the

Taluk Land Board, Thrissur by Ext.P8. The main grievance raised

in Ext.P8 is that the Taluk Land Board, in the ceiling proceedings,

is attempting to club the land belonging to the petitioner as well

as a company named M/s.Fortune Gate Mansions Pvt.Ltd

together for the purpose of determination of ceiling.          The

petitioner's present request is that Ext.P8 may be directed to be

considered by the Taluk Land Board and till then, no coercive

steps shall be taken to take over the possession of the land.

</p><p id="p_2">      2. Considering the plea as above, there shall be a direction

to the Taluk Land Board, Thrissur to consider Ext.P8 within a

period of three months.     Till final order is passed on Ext.P8,

status quo as on today shall be maintained. Needless to say that

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">W.P.(C) No.39005/2017               2</span>



the petitioner and the aforesaid company shall be heard before

passing final order on Ext.P8.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       This writ petition is disposed of as above.
