CORAM:
THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE B.RAJENDRAN
W.P. No. 19567 of 2003
Ramachandran								.. Petitioner

Versus

The Superintendent (Registrar)
District Registrar 
North Madras, Rajaji Salai
Chennai - 600 001						 		.. Respondent

	Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India, praying for the issuance of a Writ of Mandamus directing the respondent to register the partition deed to be executed between the petitioner and the two sisters - Padmavathi and Jayalakshmi on the basis of the consent letter given by them on 10.07.2003.

For Petitioner		:	No appearance
For Respondent		:	Mr. A.N. Thambidurai
					Special Government Pleader

ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	When the writ petition was listed for hearing on 07.09.2016, there was no representation on behalf of the petitioner.  Therefore, this writ petition was directed to be posted today under the captioin 'for dismissal'.  Even today, there is no representation for the petitioner.  The writ petition is pending from the year 2003.  In the above circumstances, no useful purpose will be served by keeping the writ petition pending.  Accordingly, the writ petition is dismissed for non-prosecution.  No costs.  	
12.09.2016
rsh

To
The Superintendent (Registrar)
District Registrar 
North Madras, Rajaji Salai
Chennai - 600 001
B. RAJENDRAN, J
