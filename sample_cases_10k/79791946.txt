24/11/2014

       Shri   R.P.   Agrawal,   learned   Senior   Advocate   with   Shri
Pramod Sahu, learned counsel for the appellants.
       Heard on admission as well as for consideration of I.A.
No. 16052/14.
       Record of the courts below be called for.
       On   payment   of   process   fee   within   seven   days   by
registered AD as well as by ordinary mode, notice be issued to
the   respondents   for   I.A.   No.   16052/14,   be   made   returnable</pre>
<p id="p_1">within six weeks.
</p><p id="p_2">       On perusal of the record it is evident that the suit filed by
the   respondents/plaintiffs   for   possession   was   dismissed   vide
judgment   dated   07­10­08   whereas   the   appeal   filed   by   the
respondents was allowed vide judgment dated 07­10­14. 
</p><p id="p_3">       Hence,   it   is   ordered   that   the   proceedings   for   partition
shall remain continued. However, final judgment shall not be
passed.
</p><p id="p_4">       After receipt of the records list for admission as well as
for consideration of I.A. No. 16052/14.
</p><p id="p_5">       C.C. as per rules.
</p>
