<p id="p_1">CORAM: HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE JASWANT SINGH

Present:-    None for the petitioner

Jaswant Singh,J.(Oral)
             Judgment Debtor is in revision under Section 115 CPC
aggrieved against the order dated 6.5.2002 passed by the learned Civil
Judge (Senior Division)Patiala, whereby mesne profit in respect of
property in dispute has been assessed @ Rs.3500/- per month.
</p><p id="p_2">             The present revision petition was "Admitted" vide order
dated 16.7.2002.
</p><p id="p_3">             Thereafter the case has been listed for hearing on 9.7.2013,
24.4.2014 and 8.5.2014.          On these three dates none had put in
appearance on behalf of the petitioner. Same is the position today.
</p><p id="p_4">             It appears that the petitioner is not interested in pursuing
the present revision petition.
</p><p id="p_5">             Dismissed in default.
</p>
