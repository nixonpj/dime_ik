<p id="p_1">    The petitioners are accused in S.C.No.754 of 2012,

on the files of the court below. The offences alleged are

punishable under Sections 341, 323, 324 and 308 read

with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_1">Section 34</a> IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">    2.   The respondent Nos. 2 to 4 are the injured,

who filed separate affidavit stating that the matter has

been settled and hence they have no further grievance

against the petitioners. The learned Public Prosecutor,

on instructions, has submitted that the matter has

been settled by the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">    3.   Having heard the learned Counsel for the

petitioners and the learned Counsel for respondent
 Crl.MC.No. 121 of 2019

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 4</span>

Nos. 2 to 4, I am satisfied that the respondent Nos. 2 to

4 filed the affidavit voluntarily.


</p><p id="p_6">      4.        Considering the nature of the allegations, I

am of the view that no public interest is involved in the

dispute in this case. In the said circumstances, I am

inclined to quash the final report and further

proceedings against the petitioners in the above said

case, in exercise of the inherent power under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Section

482</a> Cr.P.C., to meet the ends of justice. It is ordered

accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">      In the result, this Crl.M.C. stands allowed as above.
