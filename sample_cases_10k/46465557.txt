<div class="doc_title">Sri Mrityunjoy Sarkar vs Sri Santiranjan Sarkar &amp; Ors on 19 June, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                              1


19.06.2017.                    S.A.T. 373 of 2010
Item No.57                              with
 Ct. No.14                        CAN 3992 of 2016
       dc.



                              Sri Mrityunjoy Sarkar
                                     versus
                           Sri Santiranjan Sarkar &amp; Ors.


              Mr. Ram Prokash Banerjee
                               ... For the Appellant/Applicant.



                    This second appeal will be heard on the following
              substantial questions of law :-


                    1.   Whether the learned courts below were justified in
                         decreeing the partition suit in preliminary form
                         without holding that some of the co-sharers who
                         have not been joined as parties in the partition suit
                         have no share and/or interest in the suit property ?


                    2.   When a decree will become nullity because of non-
                         impleadment of all the co-sharers in the partition
                         suit, whether the learned court below was justified
                         in refusing the plaintiffs to amend their pleadings for
                         disclosing the names of the co-sharers who are left
                         out, particularly when the facts regarding non-
                         impleadment of those co-shares had already been
                         brought on record before the learned Trial Court at
                         the time of recording evidence of the D.W.-1 ?


                    Call for the record.
                    Issue usual notice.
                 2




           Re: CAN 3992 of 2016 (Injunction)


      The appellant/applicant is directed to serve copy of the
application for injunction upon the respondents intimating</pre>
<p id="p_1">that this application will be listed for hearing eight weeks
hence. Such service will be effected by Registered Post and/or
Speed Post with A/D.
</p>

<p id="p_2">      The appellant/applicant is directed to file affidavit-of-
service on the next date of hearing.
</p>

<p id="p_3">      All final decree proceedings including the work of
commission will continue in the court below. However, no final
decree will be drawn up by the learned Trial Judge without the
leave of the Court during the pendency of this application.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                     (JYOTIRMAY BHATTACHARYA, J.)


                            ( ASHA ARORA, J. )
