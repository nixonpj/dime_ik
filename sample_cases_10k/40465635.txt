                        Vs

                       ...       Respondent

              Dated this the 27th July, 2011

                             ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1"> Thottathil B.Radhakrishnan, J


      We have seen the TDB Report No.117 of 2011 by the

learned Ombudsman generated on a complaint made

anonymous. Though the complaint about the contractor

who had the right to collect rice from the Sannidhanam was

found to be not substantiated on the enquiry by the

Vigilance Officer, a report has been made by the Vigilance

suggesting that the collection and accounting of rice and its

disposal to the contractor need to be more transparent. We

are therefore in complete agreement with the views

expressed by the learned Ombudsman that the report of the

Vigilance Officer needs appropriate attention of the Board,

so that needful directions could be issued. We direct the

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">DBP No.110 of 2011          2</span>

  Secretary, Travancore Devaswom Board to place relevant

  materials for the consideration of the Board at the earliest.

</p><p id="p_2">        Ordered accordingly.
</p>
