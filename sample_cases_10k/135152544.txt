<p id="p_1">CORAM : HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE RAJIVE BHALLA &amp;
        HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE G.S.SANDHAWALIA

Present:     Mr.Surinder Sharma, Advocate, for the petitioner,

             Mr.Ashok Giri, Advocate, for respondent No.3.
</p>
<p id="p_2">Rajive Bhalla J.(Oral)

             The prayer in the writ petition is to issue a writ of mandamus to
direct the Director, Rural Development &amp; Panchayats, Punjab, Mohali to
entertain the petitioner's application for stay in pending Appeal No.111 of
2012.
</p><p id="p_3">             Counsel for the Gram Panchayat submits that he has no
objection if the writ petition is allowed in terms of the prayer made in the
writ petition.
</p><p id="p_4">             After hearing counsel for the parties, we dispose of the writ
petition by directing the    Director, Rural Development &amp; Panchayats,
Punjab, Mohali to consider the appeal of the petitioner and decide the same
within one month from the receipt of a certified copy of this order.
</p><p id="p_5">             Parties shall maintain status quo with respect to the property in
dispute.</p><pre id="pre_2">




                                                    (Rajive Bhalla)
                                                      JUDGE


18.10.2012                                         (G.S.Sandhawalia)
sailesh                                                JUDGE
