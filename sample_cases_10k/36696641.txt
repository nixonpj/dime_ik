<div class="doc_title">Geethakumary vs The Authorised Officer on 16 February, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">       

  

  

 
 
  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 990 of 2011(W)


1. GEETHAKUMARY, W/O.J.GOPALAKRISHNAN NAIR,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE AUTHORISED OFFICER,
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.AYYAPPAN SANKAR

                For Respondent  :SRI.K.K.CHANDRAN PILLAI (SR.)

The Hon'ble MR. Justice C.K.ABDUL REHIM

 Dated :16/02/2011

 O R D E R
                      C.K.ABDUL REHIM, J
                ---------------------------------------
                  W.P(C) No.990 of 2011-W
                ----------------------------------------
         Dated this the 16th day of February, 2011.

                         J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Proceedings    initiated    under     Section     14(1) of the

Securitization and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and

<a href="/doc/198257891/" id="a_1">Enforcement of Security Interest Act</a>, 2002 <a href="/doc/52229129/" id="a_2">(SARFAESI Act</a>) is

under challenge in this writ petition. Considering the fact that

the petitioner had not availed the effective alternative remedy

available, this Court was not inclined to entertain the writ

petition. However, as a gesture of indulgence an interim stay

was granted against dispossession, subject to condition of the

petitioner remitting a sum of Rs.1,00,000/- on or before

25.1.2011.   Eventhough the petitioner sought extension for

payment of the amount till 14.2.2011, she had failed to comply

with the condition.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      Under the above circumstances, I am not inclined to

entertain this writ petition any further.        However, it is made

clear that the petitioner will be at liberty to invoke statutory

remedy, if any available under law.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      The Writ Petition is dismissed with the above observations.


</p><p id="p_4">                                   C.K.ABDUL REHIM
                                         JUDGE
ab
