</pre><p id="p_1">Suit   No.41   of   2013,   is   facing   corporate   insolvency   proceedings   before

Resolution Professional (RP) has been appointed.  Moratorium subsists till
December 2018.  
</p>

<p id="p_2">2        There   are   two   companion   notices   of   motion,   namely,   Notice   of
Motion Nos.970 of 2018 in Suit No.40 of 2013 and 971 of 2018 in Suit
No.41 of 2013, which have been taken out by RP.  These motions require
handing over of the property of the Defendants to the RP in pursuance of
the insolvency proceedings.   Since these motions will require hearing, all
the matters are adjourned to 31 October 2018.  
</p>

<p id="p_3">3        Notice of Motion No.971 of 2013, which is not on board today, shall
also appear along with Notice of Motion No.970 of 2013 on that date.
