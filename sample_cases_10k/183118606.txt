<div class="doc_title">Prasadi Mandal And Ors vs The State Of Jharkhand on 21 September, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                      Cr. Appeal (S.J.) No. 730 of 2015

                1. Prasadi Mandal, son of Chunni Mandal
                2. Bideshi Mandal, son of Chunni Mandal
                3. Chunni Mandal, son of Late Mangar Mandal
                   All residents of village Tekra, PO &amp; PS- Karon, District- Deoghar
                                                                          ....     ...     Appellant(s)
                                       -V e r s u s-
                State of Jharkhand                                        ....     ...   Respondent

                       CORAM: - HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAVI NATH VERMA

                For the Appellant(s)        : - Mr. A.K.Choudhary, Advocate
                For the State               : - A.P.P.

03/21.09.2015</pre><p id="p_1">          Admit.
</p><p id="p_2">                       Issue notice.
</p><p id="p_3">                       Call for the Lower Court Records from the court concerned.
                       Learned counsel appearing for the three appellants submitted that appellant
                no.1 Prasadi Mandal has been convicted under <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_1">Section 325</a> of I.P.C. and sentenced
                to undergo R.I. for three years with fine of Rs.5,000/- with default clause and
                further convicted under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">Section 341</a> of I.P.C. for which he has been imposed fine
                of Rs.500/- with default clause while the appellant no. 2- Bideshi Mandal and
                appellant no.3- Chunni Mandal were given the benefit of <a href="/doc/167447/" id="a_3">Probation of Offenders
                Act</a>. It was also submitted that appellant no.1 Prasadi Mandal has already been
                granted provisional bail by the court below vide order dated 31.08.2015 till
                30.09.2015.
</p><p id="p_4">                       The provisional bail granted to the appellant no.1-Prasadi Mandal is, hereby,
                confirmed.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                                                (R. N. Verma, J.)


Ritesh
