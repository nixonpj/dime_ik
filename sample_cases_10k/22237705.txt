<div class="doc_title">Sanjay Choudhary @ Gorelal ... vs The State Of Bihar on 21 August, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No. 52170 of 2019
                       Arising Out of PS. Case No.-527 Year-2018 Thana- NAWADA District- Nawada
                 ======================================================
                 SANJAY CHOUDHARY @ GORELAL CHOUDHARY Son of Late
                 Ramchandra Choudhary Resident of Village-Kadirganj, P.S.-Nawada,
                 District-Nawada.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Sheo Kumar Prasad, Adv
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Sanjay Kumar Singh, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE S. KUMAR
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   21-08-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the parties.

</p><p id="p_2">                                Petitioner who is in custody seeks bail in Nawada

                   (Kadirganj) P.S. Case No. 527 of 2018 registered for the

                   offence punishable under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_1">Sections 392</a> of the Indian Penal

                   Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               Earlier the bail of the petitioner was rejected vide order

                   dated 27.11.2018 in Criminal Miscellaneous No.63289 of 2018

                   with liberty to renew his prayer for bail after completing one

                   year of Jail custody.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioner that

                   petitioner is in custody since 03.08.2018 and he has completed

                   more than one year in Jail custody.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                 Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances of
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No. 52170 of 2019(2) dt.21-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     the case and considering the period of custody, let the petitioner

                     named above be released on bail upon furnishing bail bond of

                     Rs. 10,000/- with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                     satisfaction of learned court below where the case is pending, in

                     connection with Nawada (Kadirganj) P.S. Case No. 527 of

                     2018, with following conditions:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                                (1)Bailors should be local having sufficient
                                   immovable property within the jurisdiction of the court
                                   concerned.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                                                (2) Petitioner shall co-operate in the trial and shall
                                   be properly represented on each and every date fixed by the
                                   court and shall remain physically present as directed by the
                                   Court and his/her absence on two consecutive dates without
                                   sufficient reason, his/her bail bond shall be cancelled by the
                                   court below.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                                                  (3) If the petitioner tampers with the evidence or
                                   the witnesses of the case, in that case, prosecution will be at
                                   liberty to move for cancellation of bail of the petitioner.</blockquote><p id="p_6">




</p><p id="p_7">                                                                      (S. Kumar, J)
ranjan/-
</p>
