<div class="doc_title">Ashok Kumar Agarwal vs M/S. Srei Equipment Finance ... on 19 December, 2017</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           1</span>

S/L.139.                                C.R.R. No.4049 of 2017
December
19, 2017
    Bpg.   In the matter of :        Ashok Kumar Agarwal
                                         Versus
                                    M/s. Srei Equipment Finance Limited.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   Mr. Pawan Kumar Gupta,
                                Mr. Awadhesh Kumar Rai.
</p><p id="p_3">                                           ...for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                         This is an application under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal
            Procedure for quashing of the Complaint Case no.0054259/2016 pending in the
            Court of learned Metropolitan Magistrate, 14th Court, Calcutta under <a href="/doc/1834702/" id="a_2">Section 138</a>
            read with <a href="/doc/686130/" id="a_3">Section 141</a> of the Negotiable Instruments Act on the ground that the
            petitioner resigned from the company way back in the year 2014 and the cheque
            under reference was issued on August 3, 2015.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                         Having considered the submission of learned advocate for the
            petitioner and after going through the materials on record, I find sufficient
            justification to stay further proceedings of this case under reference in respect of
            the present petitioner. Let further proceedings of the aforesaid case be stayed for
            a period of four weeks after the Christmas Vacation in respect of the present
            petitioner only.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                         The petitioner is, thus, directed to serve a copy of the application
            along with annexures thereof to the opposite party by speed post with
            acknowledgement due within a period of two weeks from date and to file an
            affidavit-of-service to that effect on the next date of hearing.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                         List the matter under the heading "Adjourned Motion" three weeks
            after the Christmas Vacation.
</p>



<p id="p_8">                                                          (Debi Prosad Dey, J.)
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> 2</span>
