<div class="doc_title">Smt Neha Ojha vs State Of Rajasthan And Anr on 27 January, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 S.B. Criminal Revision Petition No.86/2013
(Smt. Neha Ojha Versus State of Raj. &amp; Anr.)
Date of Order			::		27th January, 2014
HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MAHESH CHANDRA SHARMA  

Mr. Bipin Gupta, counsel for the  petitioner
Mr. Mohan Chaudhary, counsel for the respondent no.2 </pre>
<p id="p_1">Mr. Peeyush Kumar, PP for the State 
		This revision petition has been filed by the petitioner against the order dated 11.10.2012 passed by Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate No.2, Bharatpur in Cr. Case No. 158/2009, whereby the application filed under <a href="/doc/1056396/" id="a_1">section 125</a> of CrPC has been partly allowed by the trial court.
		I have heard learned counsel for the parties and perused the impugned order dated 11.10.2012 passed by the trial court. 
		Having heard the learned counsel for the parties, I do not find any force in this revision petition and the same being bereft of any merit, deserves to be dismissed, which stands dismissed accordingly after confirming the order passed by the court below. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">  						  (Mahesh Chandra Sharma)J.
</p>
<p id="p_3">All corrections made in the judgment / order have been incorporated in the judgment / order being E-mailed. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">Dilip Khandelwal
P
