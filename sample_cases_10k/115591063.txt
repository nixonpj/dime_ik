<div class="doc_title">Chhedi Mallah vs The State Of Bihar on 1 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.14711 of 2019
                    Arising Out of PS. Case No.-67 Year-2018 Thana- PIPRASI District- West Champaran
                 ======================================================
                 CHHEDI MALLAH, Son of Late Jokhan Mallah, Resident of Village - Sripat
                 Nagar Bhaisahiya, P.S.- Piprasi, Distt - West Champaran.

                                                                                     ... ... Petitioner
                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                          ... ... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :        Mr.Mitali
                 For the Opposite Party/s :        Mr.Sanjay Kumar Sharma
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   01-05-2019                  Petitioner seeks bail in anticipation of his arrest in

                   connection with Piprasi P.S. Case No. 67 of 2018, registered for

                   the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_4">325</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">307</a>,

                   <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_6">504</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_7">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Allegation against the petitioner is of assault by

                   means of lathi and occurrence took place due to plucking of

                   Datun from the mango tree.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Submission of learned counsel for the petitioner is

                   that there is case and counter case between the parties and

                   injuries are simple in nature.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Heard learned APP also.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                Having heard both sides and in the facts and

                   circumstances, let petitioner, above named, surrender within a

                   period of four weeks from the receipt of this order and pray for
              Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.14711 of 2019(3) dt.01-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                         2/2</span>




                       regular bail, on which learned court below will verify the

                       submission that injuries are simple in nature and if it is so, he

                       shall be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.25,000/-

                       (Rupees twenty five thousand) with two sureties of the like

                       amount each to the satisfaction of Additional Chief Judicial

                       Magistrate-II, Bagha, West Champaran, in connection with

                       Piprasi P.S.Case No. 67 of 2018, subject to the conditions as laid

                       down under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_8">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure

                       and further condition that one of the bailors of the petitioner

                       shall be a local person having sufficient immoveable properties

                       within the jurisdiction of the court concerned, otherwise the

                       court below may pass any other order(s) as it may deem fit and

                       proper.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                    With the above observation, this application is

                       disposed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                               (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
spal/-
</p>
