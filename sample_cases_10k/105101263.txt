   rrc

                               W. P. 4264 (W) of 2015
               [Dainhat Joymatara P.T.T.I. (Additional B.Ed. Course) &amp; Anr.
</p><p id="p_3">                          Vs. University of Kalyani &amp; Ors.]


                Mr. Avijit Chakraborty
                                                 .....For the petitioners

                Mr. Amitava Chowdhury
                Ms. A. Kar Gupta
                                                 .....For the University


                Having heard learned advocates for the parties and considering

             the nature of grievance expressed by the petitioners, I find no

             reason to keep the writ petition pending. The same stands

             disposed of with a direction upon the Vice-Chancellor of the

             University    of   Kalyani     to   consider   and   dispose   of   the

             representation of the petitioner no. 2 dated 29th January, 2015 as

             early as possible but not later than 10 (ten) days from date of

             receipt of a copy of this order.

                No opinion is expressed on merits of the petitioners' claim and

             all points are left open for a decision by the Vice-Chancellor of the

             University.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                There shall, however, be no order costs.

                Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be

             given to the parties as expeditiously as possible.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                             ( Dipankar Datta, J. )
