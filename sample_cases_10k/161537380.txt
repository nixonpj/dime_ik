<p id="p_1">        Mr.R.Sundar Srinivasan, learned counsel appearing for the appellant
seeks permission to withdraw this Writ Appeal with liberty to raise the
contentions canvassed in this Writ Appeal at the time of hearing of W.P.(MD)
No.8728 of 2013, which is filed raising comprehensive contentions regarding
the order passed terminating the service of the appellant herein.
Accordingly, reserving the liberty, this Writ Appeal stands dismissed as
withdrawn.  No costs.  Consequently, the connected miscellaneous petitions
are closed.
