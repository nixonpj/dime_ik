            Md. Naseem                                      .....   Petitioner(s)

</pre><p id="p_9">            Jharkhand State Housing Board, Ranchi &amp; Ors.
</p><pre id="pre_4">                                                       ....           Respondent(s)

            CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE R. R. PRASAD

            For the Petitioner(s)       :     M/s Ayush Aditya, Advocate.
            For the Respondent(s)       :     G.P.II
                                              M/s Sumeet Gadodia, Advocate.
                                    -----

</pre><p id="p_10">06 /26.9.2011.    Heard learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and learned

            counsel for the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                  Learned counsel appearing for the petitioner submits that some portion

            of the land appertaining to Khata No.4, Plot Nos. 780-A, 780-B, 241, 777, 781

            was acquired vide Land Acquisition Case No.19 /1964-65. Since some of the

            lands of the aforesaid Khesras had not been acquired and application was

            filed before the Housing Board, praying therein to demarcate the land which

            had been acquired from the land which has not been acquired and then

            release it in favour of the petitioner, but nothing was done in this respect and,

            therefore, the petitioner has moved to this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_12">                  A counter-affidavit has been filed on behalf of the State, wherein,

            statements had been made with respect to the land which have been

            acquired. Thus, it is evident that the entire land of the aforesaid khesras,

            above-mentioned have never been acquired and then a decision is to be

            taken by the Housing Board for release of the land in favour of the petitioner

            of the land which has not been acquired by the Housing Board and hence,

            this writ application is disposed of, directing the petitioner to file a fresh

            representation before the Respondent No.2 (the Managing Director,

            Jharkhand State Housing Board, Ranchi), within a period of four weeks, from

            today so that decision be taken by the Respondent No.2, in the matter of

            release of land in favour of the petitioner, which has not been acquired, within

            a period of 8 weeks thereafter.
</p>
<p id="p_13">                  The Respondent No.2 will be taken a decision within a period of eight
            weeks if the authority comes to the conclusion that the land has not been

           acquired, the same be released in favour of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_14">                 Thus, this writ application stands disposed of,.
</p>


<p id="p_15">                                                                    (R. R. Prasad, J)
Sandeep/

             writ petition.

             between the petitioner and the State was cancelled and in spite of that the
             authority is intending to invoke the Bank guarantee and, therefore, the
             authority be restrained from invoking the Bank guarantee.
</p><p id="p_21">                    Learned counsel appearing for the respondents seeks time to file
