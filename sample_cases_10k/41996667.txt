<div class="doc_title">Arun Ram vs State Of Bihar on 14 September, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             Cr.Misc. No.22035 of 2009
                                   ARUN RAM
                                       Versus
                                STATE OF BIHAR
                                      -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">3.   14.09.2009                  Heard counsel for the parties.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner has approached this Court seeking bail in a

                      case instituted under diverse sections<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Penal Code</a>        including

                      <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">section 498A</a> IPC. The case arises out of a complaint.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 It is contended that the allegation is general and omnibus.

                                 The petitioner seems to be in custody since 18.05.2009.

                                  In the facts and circumstances of the case, let the petitioner

                      abovenamed be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-

                      with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of

                      Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate, Sheikhpura in connection with

                      Case No.5c/2009.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
              hr
                                             ( Kishore K. Mandal )
