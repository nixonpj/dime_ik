                NIZAMUDEEN,

              SR.PP C.N PRABHAKARAN


THIS BAIL APPLICATION HAVING COME UP FOR ADMISSION ON 03.12.2018, THE
COURT ON THE SAME DAY PASSED THE FOLLOWING:
 BA No.7175/2018                               2


                                      O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            Petitioner herein is the first accused in         Crime No.1567/2018 of

     Kadakkal     Police    station   for   offences    punishable     under     <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_1">sections

     294(b)</a>,<a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">341</a>,<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>,<a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_4">324</a>,<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">307</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            2. Allegation of the prosecution is that, on 2/9/2018 at about 8 p.m. the

     petitioner herein inflicted multiple injuries on the body of the defacto

     complainant along with the co-accused. According to the petitioner herein,

     he questioned one Binu for attacking the friend of the petitioner. It is stated

     that, the accused using a knife hit on the abdomen and thereafter when he

     attempted to hit again, the defacto complainant resisted.            He sustained

     multiple injuries on the finger. Wound certificate shows that extensive injuries

     have been inflicted on various parts of the body. 9 injuries are seen on

     various parts of the body which includes multiple incised wound on the right

     hand, one stab injury on the cheek and three other incised wounds.

</p><p id="p_3">            Considering the nature of the injuries, I feel that this is not a fit case for

     granting the benefit of pre arrest bail. Accordingly, the bail application is

     dismissed.
</p>
