<p id="p_1">2   29-08-2019                 The short prayer made in the present writ petition is to

                   direct the respondent authorities to remove the encroachment

                   made over the public road situated at Mauza- Chatiya, P.S.

                   Malahi, District East Champaran appertaining to Khata No. 952,

                   Khesra No.4173.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               This Court is of the view that the remedy of the

                   petitioner lies before the Circle Officer concerned under the

                   provisions of the Bihar Public Land Encroachment Act, 1956.

</p><p id="p_3">                               Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the

                   case, the present writ petition stands disposed of with liberty to

                   the petitioner to approach the Circle Officer, Areraj, East

                   Champaran for redressal of his grievances and in case a
            Patna High Court CWJC No.19583 of 2016(2) dt.29-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                      2/2</span>




                     complaint is made under the provisions of the Bihar Public Land

                     Encroachment Act, 1956, the respondent no.4 shall take

                     cognizance thereof, if found maintainable, under the provisions

                     of the said Act and take appropriate action.

</p><p id="p_4">                                 The writ petition stands disposed of on the aforesaid

                     terms.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                             (Mohit Kumar Shah, J)
Tiwary/-
