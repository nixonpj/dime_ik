                   IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                    Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                             ORIGINAL SIDE


                     M/S. AMBO EXPORTS LIMITED
                               Versus
                    M/S. NARASU'S EXPORTS &amp; ANR.

                                                              Appearance
                                                  Mr. D. Sharma, Advocate

                                                Mr. P.P. Banerjee, Advocate

  BEFORE:

  The Hon'ble JUSTICE SANJIB BANERJEE</pre>
<p id="p_1">  Date : 25th November, 2010.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">           The Court : Subject to the defendants paying costs assessed at

200 GM to the plaintiff, the time to file the affidavit-in-opposition to the

application under Chapter XIIIA of the Rules on the Original Side of this

Court in G.A. No. 42 of 2010 is enlarged by a week. Reply thereto, if any,

may be filed within a week thereafter. G.A. No. 42 of 2010 will appear as a

chamber application for final disposal in the monthly list of December,

2010.
</p>
<p id="p_4">           G.A. No. 1007 of 2010 is the defendants' application for

rejection of the plaint. It is fairly submitted on behalf of the defendants
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                        2</span>


that the grounds urged can be conveniently taken both in the opposition to

the Chapter XIIIA application and, if permitted, in the written statement to

be filed in the suit.
</p>
<p id="p_5">            Accordingly, the defendants are permitted to withdraw G.A. No.

1007 of 2010 without prejudice to their right to urge the same grounds at

the appropriate stage.
</p>
<p id="p_6">            There will be no order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_7">            Urgent certified photocopies of this order, if applied for, be

supplied to the parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.



</p><p id="p_8">                                                  (SANJIB BANERJEE, J.)


sg.
