<div class="doc_title">Baljit Singh And Another vs State Of Punjab And Others on 21 December, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1"> IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                                       CWP No. 25959 of 2012
                                       Date of Decision: 21.12.2012

Baljit Singh and another                                            ...Petitioners

                                  Versus

State of Punjab and others                                     ..Respondents.


CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A.K.SIKRI, CHIEF JUSTICE.
       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAKESH KUMAR JAIN.

Present :    Mr. Vishal Gupta, Advocate, for the petitioner.

                           ****
</pre><p id="p_1">A.K.SIKRI C.J.(Oral)

             The petitioner wants enquiry to be conducted against

respondent No.6 for alleged mis-utilization and embezzlement of panchayat

funds. The petitioner has himself enclosed a letter dated 18.10.2012 written

by the Government to the District Development Panchayat Officer on his

representation dated 23.08.2012, directing the District Development

Panchayat Officer to hold an enquiry and submit the report. The grievance

is that though 30 days time was given but he is sleeping over the matter and

is not conducting the enquiry into the matter. Since the Government itself

marked the enquiry to the District Development Panchayat Officer, the only

direction that can be passed is to direct the District Development Panchayat

Officer to expedite the enquiry and submit the report within a period of 30

days from today.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Writ petition is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                          (A.K.SIKRI)
                                                         CHIEF JUSTICE


21.12.2012                                        (RAKESH KUMAR JAIN)
'ravinder'                                               JUDGE
