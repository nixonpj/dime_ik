<pre id="pre_1">Sl 72                                                 Sudhir Kumar Ghosh
Ac                                                               -vs-
                                                            Sushil Kumar Bose


                     Mr. Kishore Mukherjee
                     Mr. Sankha Subhra Ray              ... For Petitioner



</pre><p id="p_2">                                  This is an application under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the
                     Constitution of India at the instance of the defendant no.
                     6/petitioner seeking an order for expeditious disposal of
                     the application under Order 12 Rule 6 of the Code of Civil
                     Procedure, pending before the learned Civil Judge, (Senior
                     Division), Sealdah.
</p><p id="p_3">                                  On perusal of the certified copy of the order
                     sheet, annexed to this revisional application, it appears
                     that hearing of the application under Order 12 Rule 6 of
                     the Code of Civil Procedure is being unnecessarily
                     dragged. Several dates are fixed but hearing of the said
                     application is adjourned on one pretext or the other.
</p><p id="p_4">                                  Since the order, which this Court proposes to
                     pass, would not in any way cause prejudice to the other
                     side, this Court takes up this revisional application
                     without serving copy of the revisional application upon
                     the opposite party.
</p><p id="p_5">                                  The trial Court is requested to make all
                     endeavour to dispose of the said application on the date
                     so fixed and in the event the same cannot be disposed of
                     on the same day, it would make efforts to dispose of the
                     same within two weeks therefrom.
</p><p id="p_6">                                  The revisional application is disposed of.
                           There shall, however, be no order as to costs.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                              (Harish Tandon, J.)
