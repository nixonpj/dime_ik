                                                                Judgment
                                   13/02/2019

                                         Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Code of

                                   Criminal    Procedure,   1973    seeking      regular    bail   in   F.I.R.

                                   No.308/2018 registered at Police Station Chandwaji, District

                                   Jaipur for offences under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">120-B</a>

                                   Indian Penal Code, 1860.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                         Mr. Rajendra Kumar has submitted that the petitioner is in

                                   custody since 3.11.2018. Challan has already been presented in

                                   the court and conclusion of trial may take time. Co-accused have

                                   been granted bail by this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                         Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on the merits of

                                   the case, this petition is allowed. Petitioner be admitted to bail

                                   subject to satisfaction of the Trial Court.

</p><p id="p_4">                                                                                               (SABINA)J.
