<div class="doc_title">Subodh Sharma @ Subodh Kant Akela vs The State Of Bihar on 1 April, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.19906 of 2019
                         Arising Out of PS. Case No.-41 Year-2018 Thana- KONCH District- Gaya
                 ======================================================
                 Subodh Sharma @ Subodh Kant Akela Son of Late Ragho Singh, Resident of
                 Village- Chabura, P.S.- Anti, District- Gaya.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Kiran Sinha
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Atul Chandra
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRABHAT KUMAR JHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   01-04-2019                  Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioner apprehends his arrest in Konch P.S.

                   Case No.41 of 2018 registered under <a href="/doc/1128948/" id="a_1">Sections 120(B)</a>, <a href="/doc/1208971/" id="a_2">353</a>, <a href="/doc/1183069/" id="a_3">386</a>

                   and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and under <a href="/doc/1546136/" id="a_5">Section 17</a> of

                   Criminal Law Amendment Act and under <a href="/doc/544006/" id="a_6">Sections 25</a>(1-a), (1-

                   aa), 26(2) and 35 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_7">Arms Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                The     police    on     confidential      information          about

                   movement of naxalites and putting arms for using against police

                   force raided the place and recovered huge quantity of arms and

                   ammunition.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that on

                   mere suspicion the name of petitioner has been figured in the

                   case. Nothing has been recovered from personal possession of

                   the petitioner, but it appears that the petitioner is an active
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.19906 of 2019(2) dt.01-04-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     member of extremist group and he has got criminal antecedent.

                     As many as nine cases of the same and similar nature are

                     pending against the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                  Having considered the facts aforesaid, I am not

                     inclined to enlarge the petitioner on anticipatory bail.

                     Accordingly, the same is rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                             (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J)
Harish/-
</p>
