<p id="p_1">               (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE KS JHAVERI)


</p><p id="p_2">1.            On 15.11.2011, this Court in Civil Application No.9805 of 

2011 has passed the following order:­




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  Page 1 of 2</span>
            C/CA/9805/2011                                          ORDER



              "It   is   stated   by   Mr.Pranav   Dave,   learned   Assistant  
              Government Pleader appearing on behalf of the appellants  
              that   the   respondent   No.1   having   died,   his   client   is  
              required to take appropriate steps for substitution of heirs  
              and legal representatives of such deceased respondent. To  
              enable his client to take such step, let the matter go out of  
              the   list   with   liberty   to   mention   after   filing   of   such  
              application."
</p>

<p id="p_3">2.            However, till date nothing is on record to show that any 

such   application   is   filed   by   the   applicant.   Hence,   the   present 

application stands dismissed for default. 
</p>


<p id="p_4">3.            In view of the order passed in Civil Application No.9805 

of  2011,  the  main  appeal  as  well  as  the  Civil  Application  for  stay 

being Civil Application (Stamp No.) No.8897 of 2011 do not survive 

and the same are disposed of accordingly. 
</p>



<p id="p_5">                                                                (K.S.JHAVERI, J.)




                                                                 (A.G.URAIZEE,J)
