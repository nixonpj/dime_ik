JUDGMENT :
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     1. A fresh complaint was filed in this case against accused namely 

           Mukesh   Dutt   Sharma   before   this   court   on   10.01.2014   for 

           offences punishable U/s 135/138 of the <a href="/doc/132967048/" id="a_1">Electricity Act</a>, 2003 for 

           taking action against the accused as per law.

</p><p id="p_2">     2. In   pre­summoning   evidence,   CW1   Sh.   Jitender   Shankar   and 

           CW2 Sh. Prabudh Kumar examined themselves and thereafter 

           the   remaining   pre­summoning   evidence   of   the   complainant 

           company was closed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3. Today Sh. Mukesh Sharma, A.R. of the complainant company 

           has submitted that the accused has already settled the dispute 

           with   the   complainant   company   by   making   payment   of   the 

           settled amount and further submitted that this case be disposed 

           of today as fully and finally settled amicably between both the 

           parties. He has also filed No Dues Certificate   Ex. C­1 today 

           regarding payment of entire settled amount to the complainant 

           company by the accused.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     4. Statement   of     Sh.   Mukesh   Sharma,   A.R.   of   the   complainant 

           company   was   recorded   today   separately   regarding   the 

           settlement.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     5. In   view   of   the   amicable   settlement   arrived   at   between   the 
C.C. NO.78/14, BSES (YPL) VS. MUKESH DUTT SHARMA       `                     page 2 of 3
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                     3</span>

           parties,  there is no need now to proceed further in this case 

           against the accused. Hence, the accused is hereby acquitted / 

           discharged in this case and the civil liability against him also 

           waived off. The case property of this matter is confiscated to 

           complainant   company to  be dealt   with  /  disposed  off  by the 

           complainant   company   as   per   law   /   rules.     Judicial   file   be 

           consigned   to   the   record   room   as   per   rules   after   necessary 

           compliance. 
</p>
