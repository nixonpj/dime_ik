<div class="doc_title">Vijay Yadav vs The State Of Bihar on 17 October, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.62184 of 2019
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-3 Year-2019 Thana- WARISLIGANJ District- Nawada
                 ======================================================
                 VIJAY YADAV Son of Late Sahdeo Yadav Resident of Village - Milki, P.S.-
                 Warisaliganj, Distt - Nawada.

                                                                                 ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr.Man Mohan Kumar, Adv
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mr.Pawan Kumar Chaurasia, APP
                 For the Informant        :      Mr,. Birendra Kumar, Adv
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE BIRENDRA KUMAR
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   17-10-2019                          Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                   learned APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                         Petitioner seeks bail in a case registered for the

                   offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>,<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>,<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_3">307</a>,<a href="/doc/1402213/" id="a_4">337</a>,<a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_5">302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of

                   the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                         Co-accused-Lalan Yadav assaulted with iron

                   rod, as a result whereof, husband of the informant died. Though

                   there is allegation of commission of assault against others also

                   including the petitioner, however, the postmortem report would

                   reveal that the Doctor has found single injury on the person of

                   the deceased. Petitioner is in custody since 19.03.2019.

</p><p id="p_4">                                         Learned counsel for the informant opposed the

                   prayer for bail.
</p><p id="p_5">                Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.62184 of 2019(2) dt.17-10-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                           2/2</span>




                                              Let the petitioner, above named, be released on

                         bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.20,000/- (Twenty Thousand)

                         with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of

                         learned Court below where the case is pending in connection

                         with Warisaliganj Police Station Case No.03 of 2019, subject to

                         the condition that both bailors shall be resident of territorial

                         jurisdiction of the learned court below and further the petitioner

                         shall fully cooperate with the trial of the case, failing which the

                         court below shall be at liberty to cancel the bail bond of the

                         petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                              (Birendra Kumar, J)

Nitesh/-
</p>
