04.02.2019, THE COURT ON THE SAME DAY PASSED THE FOLLOWING:
 Crl.MC.No. 767 of 2019

                             2




                         O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">     The petitioners were accused Nos. 16 and 23 in

C.C.No.986 of 2008 on the files of the trial court.     The

offences alleged are punishable under Sections 143, 147,

148, 452, 427, 324, 506 (1) and 153 A read with <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_1">Section

149</a> IPC. Annexure - A2 is the final report in the said case.


</p><p id="p_2">     2.   38 accused persons, who faced the trial in

C.C.No.986 of 2008, were acquitted by the trial court. The

proceedings against two other accused were quashed by

this Court as per the order in Crl.M.C.No.5854 of 2016.

Another accused, who faced the trial in C.C.No.267 of

2013, was also acquitted by the trial court. The

proceedings against five accused persons had been

quashed by this Court as per the order in Crl.M.C.No.563
 Crl.MC.No. 767 of 2019

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               3</span>

of 2018. The case of the petitioners is presently pending

as C.C.No.224 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">     3.   As all the other accused persons had been

acquitted, no purpose will be served even if the

prosecution against the petitioners is permitted to be

continued. In the said circumstances, I am inclined to

quash the final report and further proceedings against the

petitioners in the above said case, in exercise of the

inherent power under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., to meet the ends

of justice. It is ordered accordingly.


</p><p id="p_5">     In the result, this Crl.M.C.stands allowed as above.
