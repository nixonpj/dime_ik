      Harnaresh Singh Gill, J (Oral)

                               This is a petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal

     Procedure for issuance of directions to the officials respondents to protect the life

     and liberty of the petitioners at the hands of private respondent.

</p><p id="p_5">                               Notice to official respondents only at this stage.

                               On the asking of Court, Mr. Pawan Sharda, Senior Deputy

     Advocate General, Punjab, accepts notice on behalf of respondent - State. Copy of

     the petition be supplied to him during the course of the day.

</p><p id="p_6">                               The present petition is disposed of with a direction to respondent

     No.2 - Senior Superintendent of Police (Rural), Jalandhar, to look into the

     grievances of the petitioners unfolded by them in their representation/application

     dated 24.12.2018 (Annexure P-6) and if found necessary, take appropriate

     measures in accordance with law.
