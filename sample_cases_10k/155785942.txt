<div class="doc_title">Bhagwati @ Bantu vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 8 February, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                          MCRC-2532-2016
             (BHAGWATI @ BANTU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


08-02-2016</pre>

<p id="p_1">Shri Ashok Kumar Chourasiya, counsel for the applicant.
The case is posted on the question of maintainability on the
ground that in the same crime number in respect of the same
applicant another previously filed bail application being
M.Cr.C. No.21783/2015 is pending before this Court.
In this regard, learned counsel for the applicant submits that
the crime number has probably been erroneously mentioned
in this application, as no crime number has been mentioned
in the order of learned trial Court.
He submits that Session Trial number in both the cases is
different.
</p><p id="p_2">In aforesaid view of the matter, he prays for two weeks' time
to verify the situation.
</p><p id="p_3">Let the matter be listed after two weeks.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                                                      (C V SIRPURKAR)
                                                            JUDGE
