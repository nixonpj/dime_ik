<p id="p_84">          Dated this the 27th day of September, 2016


                              J U D G M E N T




      The petitioners, who are working as Amins and Clerks in the

Kerala Judicial Ministerial Subordinate Service were promoted to

the post of Amin from the post of Attender Grade-II directly. In

otherwords, they obtained a promotion from the post of Attender

Grade-II to the post of Amin without going through the post of

Attender Grade-I which is the feeder category to the post of Amin.
</p>
<p id="p_85">In the writ petition, the grievance of the petitioners is essentially

that, while granting promotion to the post of Amin, their notional

increment in the intermediary post of Attender Grade-I was not

added while fixing their pay. In the writ petition, they impugn

Ext.P4 official memorandum issued by the 3rd respondent which

mandates that fixation of pay in the intermediary post of Attender

Grade-I is permissible only if there is a vacancy in the intermediary

post of Attender Grade-I, and the next promoted post of Amin as

well, and double promotion is given on the same date.
</p>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   -2-</span>
<p id="p_86">W.P.(C).No.29850 of 2016




</p><p id="p_87">        2.    I have heard the learned counsel appearing for the

petitioners as also the learned Government Pleader appearing for

the respondents 1 and 2. In the nature of the orders I propose to

pass I do not deem it necessary to hear the 3rd and 4th respondents.
</p>



<p id="p_88">       3. It is pointed out by the learned counsel for the petitioners

that the issue involved in the writ petition has already been

decided by this Court through Ext.P5 judgment, and acting on the

said judgment, the 3rd respondent has since, by an official

memorandum dated 09.09.2016, directed District Judges to take

note of the judgment of this Court and to grant notional fixation in

the intermediary post of Attender Grade-I on direct promotion as

Amin from the post of Attender Grade-II irrespective of existence

of vacancy in the category of Attender Grade-I. Taking note of the

said submission, I find that this writ petition also needs to be

allowed in the light of Ext.P5 judgment of this Court.
</p>



<p id="p_89">       Resultantly, the writ petition is allowed and there will be a

direction to the 4th respondent to pass consequential orders in

respect of the petitioners herein, based on this judgment, and the

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                             -3-</span>
W.P.(C).No.29850 of 2016




official memorandum dated 09.09.2016 referred above, within a

period of two months from the date of receipt of a copy of this

judgment.
</p>
