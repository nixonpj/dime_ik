<p id="p_1">5/   22.04.2011                   Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  The petitioners have sought quashing of the order dated

                      24.04.2008 passed by the Additional Sessions Judge-cum-Fast Track

                      Court No. 2, Jehanabd, in Sessions Trial No.69 of 2006, by which an

                      application under <a href="/doc/1056165/" id="a_1">section 227</a> Cr. P.C. was disallowed.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  It has been submitted that the petitioners were named in

                      the First Information Report and subsequently charge sheet was

                      submitted against them. Upon the charge sheet cognizance was also

                      taken. However, subsequently the CID filed an application before

                      the Court for further investigation and submitted final report, but in

                      the meanwhile since cognizance had already been taken the court

                      did not pass any positive order in favour of the petitioners. The

                      petitioners have prayed for quashing the order of the trial court on

                      the ground that it should have considered the entire material and by

                      not considering the CID report it has committed illegality.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             2</span>




                   It has been repeatedly held by this Court as well as

       Hon'ble Supreme Court that at the stage of framing of charge no

       express order detailing the case of the accused has to be passed and

       it has merely to see that there has been application of mind which is

       evident from the order impugned.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                   In view of such, the application is dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><pre id="pre_2">JA/-                                          (Anjana Prakash,J.)
