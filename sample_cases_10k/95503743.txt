 BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT
DATED: 30.06.2014
CORAM
THE HONOURABLE Mr.JUSTICE G.M.AKBAR ALI
Crl.O.P.(MD)No.1194 of 2014
and
M.P.(MD)No.1 of 2014


Balasingam								...	Petitioner

Vs.

1.The Sub Inspector of Police,
   Uchipullai Police Station,
   Ramanathapuram.

2.T.Palanichamy Nadar 						...	Respondents

PRAYER
	Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure,
praying to call for the records in Crime No.208 of 2013 on the file of the
Sub Inspector of Police, Ramanathapuram for an offence punishable under
<a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_2">Sections 427</a>, <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_3">294(b)</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506(i)</a> of IPC., and quash the FIR as far as the
petitioner / accused is concerned.

For Petitioner 	: Mr.S.Srimathy

For Respondent-1	: Mrs.S.Prabha
			  Govt.Advocate (crl.side)

:ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	This Criminal Original Petition is filed praying to quash the Crime
No.208 of 2013 on the file of the Sub Inspector of Police, Ramanathapuram  in
so far as the petitioner / accused is concerned.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2. When the matter is taken up for hearing, the learned Government
Advocate (crl.side) submitted that after investigation the case was closed as
civil in nature.
</p>	
<p id="p_3">	3. Recording the submission made by the learned Government Advocate
(crl.side), this Criminal Original Petition is closed. Consequently connected
miscellaneous petition is also closed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">To

</p><p id="p_5">1.The Sub Inspector of Police,
   Uchipullai Police Station,
   Ramanathapuram.
</p>
<p id="p_6">2.The Additional Public Prosecutor,
   Madurai Bench of Madras High Court,
   Madurai.</p>
