:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">(Order of the Court was made by  R.SUDHAKAR,J.)   

        This writ petition has been filed in public interest, seeking  a Writ
of Mandamus to direct the respondents to remove the illegal encroachments on
the irrigation channel flowing through S.Nos.96/1, 97 at Nadiyambalpuram,
Pattukottai and flowing through S.Nos.21/9 and 20/3 at Kailasanathapuram,
Pattukkottai, Thanjavur District, within the stipulated time fixed by this
Court.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2. Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3. The grievance of the petitioner appears to be that there are some
encroachments on public revenue lands, more particularly, on the public water
bodies and therefore, he made a representation to the first respondent to
take action and remove the encroachments made by the encroachers. Since no   
final order has been passed, the present writ petition has been filed for the
relief stated supra.
</p>

<p id="p_4">        4. At the hearing, the learned Special Government Pleader appearing for
the respondents, on instructions, submits that appropriate action would be
taken as per Section 7 of the Tamil Nadu Protection of Tanks and Eviction of
Encroachment Act, 2007, (in short 'the Act') after issuing notice to the
alleged encroachers on the water bodies.
</p>
<p id="p_5">        5. In such view of the matter, the competent authority as specified  in
sub-section (2) of Section 6 of the Act, is directed to consider the claim of
the petitioner and initiate appropriate action as per Section 7 of the Act,
after affording due opportunity of hearing to the alleged encroachers on the
water bodies, as expeditiously as possible.
</p>
<p id="p_6">        6. With the above direction, this writ petition stands disposed of. No
costs.                                                                          
</p>
