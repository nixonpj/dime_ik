<div class="doc_title">Bhup Singh vs State Of Rajasthan on 26 October, 2018</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
             S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail No. 10466/2018

Bhup Singh S/o Sher Singh, By caste Jat, Aged About 40 Years,
R/o Village Bhangarh, Tehsil Bhadra, District Hanumangarh
(At present lodged in Sub Jail Bhadra)
</p><p id="p_2">                                                      ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State Of Rajasthan, Through Pp
</p><p id="p_3">                                                    ----Respondent


For Petitioner          :   Mr. R.S. Choudhary
For Respondent          :   Mr. A.S. Rathore, Public Prosecutor



            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

                        Judgment / Order

26/10/2018


     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as learned

Public Prosecutor and also perused the material on record.

</p><p id="p_4">     The petitioner has been arrested in FIR No.133/2016 of

Police Station Bhirani, District Hanumnagarh for the offences

punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a>, <a href="/doc/1243353/" id="a_2">342</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_4">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_5">148</a> and <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_6">149</a> IPC.

He has preferred this bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_7">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_5">     Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the

first bail application of the petitioner was dismissed as not pressed

while giving liberty to the petitioner to file fresh bail application

after recording of the statement of Ramesh Kumar and Vijay

Singh. It is submitted that now the statements of Ramesh Kumar

and Vijay Singh have been recorded and both of them have not

supported the prosecution story and turned hostile. It is also

submitted that co-accused person viz. Vrendra Kumar @ Mandiya
                                                                    (2 of 2)         [CRLMB-10466/2018]


                                   has been released on bail while taking into consideration the fact

                                   that two witnesses Ramesh Kumar and Vijay Singh have not

                                   supported the prosecution story.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                        Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

                                        Having regard to the totality of the facts and circumstances

                                   of the case, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

                                   case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail to the accused

                                   petitioner under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_8">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_7">                                        Accordingly, this bail application filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_9">Section 439</a>

                                   Cr.P.C. is allowed and it is directed that petitioner - Bhup Singh

                                   S/o Sher Singh shall be released on bail in connection with FIR

                                   No.133/2016 of Police Station Bhirani, District Hanumnagarh

                                   provided he executes a personal bond in a sum of Rs.50,000/-

                                   with two sound and solvent sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the

                                   satisfaction of learned trial court for his appearance before that

                                   court on each and every date of hearing and whenever called upon

                                   to do so till the completion of the trial.




</p><p id="p_8">                                                                                 (VIJAY BISHNOI),J
                                   Abhishek Kumar
                                   S.No.156
