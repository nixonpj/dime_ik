<pre id="pre_1">                                   ITA No. 135 of 2009
                                    GA No.1292 of 2009

                            IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA

Constitutional Writ/Civil Appellate/Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction

                                      ORIGINAL SIDE




SRI MANOJ KUMAR SINGH                             Plaintiff/Petitioner/Applicant

    Versus

THE ASSISTANT COMMISSIONER OF INCOME TAX          Defendant/Respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1"> &amp; ANR.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
For Plaintiff/Petitioner : Mr. S.K.Mukherjee, Advocate


For Defendant/Respondent : Mr. P.Dudhoria, Advocate


BEFORE:
</p>
<p id="p_3">The Hon'ble JUSTICE SUBHRO KAMAL MUKHERJEE

The Hon'ble JUSTICE SANKAR PRASAD MITRA

Date : 28th October, 2009.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">          The   Court   :    Mr.    Dudhoria,   learned   advocate,   appears   for   the

 respondent and accepts the notice of the application for condonation of

 delay.
</p>
<p id="p_6">          After hearing Mr. Mukherjee, learned Advocate appearing for the

 appellant, Mr. P.Dudhoria, learned Advocate appearing for the respondent

 and considering the averments contained in the application for condonation

 of delay in filing the appeal, we are satisfied that the appellants were

 prevented by sufficient cause from presenting the memorandum of appeal

 in time.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  2</span><p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">      Therefore, delay in filing the appeal is condoned. The application

for condonation of delay stands allowed. Office is directed to register

the appeal if the appeal is otherwise in form.

</p><p id="p_9">      There will be no order as to cost.
</p>
<p id="p_10">      Put up the appeal for hearing for admission under the same heading

on 4 November 2009.
</p>
<p id="p_11">      All parties concerned are to act on a xerox signed copy of this

order upon the usual undertakings.




</p><p id="p_12">                             (SUBHRO KAMAL MUKHERJEE, J.)



                             (SANKAR PRASAD MITRA, J.)

G/
