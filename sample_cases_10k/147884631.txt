        Let photocopy of the receipt and other papers handed

over to the Court by the learned advocate representing the

appellant in MAT 1180 of 2018 indicating deposit of a sum of

Rs.12 lakhs with the learned Registrar General, in terms of

our order dated 24th January, 2019 - within the timeframe as

indicated therein - be kept with the record. The learned

Registrar General shall keep the said amount deposited in a

short term fixed deposit account to be opened in any

nationalised bank and keep the same renewed from time to

time, until further orders of this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        In order to expedite hearing of both the appeals, the

learned advocate representing the appellant in MAT 1180 of

2018 undertakes to prepare informal paper books which shall

be made ready and filed within a period of three weeks from

date.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                       3</span>


<p id="p_5">       List the appeals and the connected applications under
