<p id="p_1">2.   17.8.2010                It is submitted that the petitioner retired

                 on 31.8.2007. After his superannuation a memo of

                 charge dated 24.10.2008 was sought to be served

                 under Rule 43(b) of the Bihar Pension Rules which

                 was quashed by this Court in C.W.J.C. No. 14651 of

                 2009 noticing that it was for an incident more than

                 four years prior to the date of issuance of the

                 charges. Subsequently, the respondents have served

                 a fresh notice of a departmental proceeding initiated

                 against the petitioner on a charge dated 23.12.2006

                 purporting to convert the same to one under Rule

                 43(B)   of     the    Bihar     Pension     Rules    on

                 6.5.2010/11.5.2010.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              He submits from Paragraph-5 of the writ

                 application that this memo of charge was never
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             2</span>




           served upon the petitioner during his service tenure.

</p><p id="p_3">                      Let the State file its counter affidavit

           within a period of four weeks.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                      Pending   disposal    of    this   application,

           further proceedings in pursuance of the memo of

           charge dated 23.12.2006 and the order dated

           6.5.2010/11.5.2010 purported to be under Rule

           43(b) of the Pension Rules shall remain stayed.




</p><pre id="pre_2">P. Kumar                                         ( Navin Sinha, J.)
