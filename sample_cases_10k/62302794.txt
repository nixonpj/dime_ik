                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.12907 of 2016
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -229 Year- 2015 Thana -SULTANGANJ District- BHAGALPUR
                 ======================================================
                 1. Santosh Kumar Mandal S/o Rajgir Mandal Resident of Village-
                 Dholpahari, P.S.- Asharganj, District- Bhagalpur.


                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar


                                                                           .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr. Davendra Kumar Pandey
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Jitendra Kumar Roy-I(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR
                 SRIVASTAVA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   31-03-2016                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

                 learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  Petitioner seeks bail in a case registered for the

                 offence punishable under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_1">Section 392</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  It appears that except confessional statement of co-

                 accused and criminal antecedent of one case, there is nothing

                 against the petitioner, who is languishing in jail custody since

                 22.10.2015.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances as

                 well as submissions of the parties, let the petitioner be released on

                 bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten Thousand) with
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">       Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.12907 of 2016 (2) dt.31-03-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                 2/2</span>




                   two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of Chief

                   Judicial Magistrate, Bhagalpur in connection with Sultanganj P.S.

                   Case No. 229 of 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                         (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
SHAHZAD/-
</p>
