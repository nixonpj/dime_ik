                        "Whether   the   employer   ­   employee  
                        relationship   ever   existed   between   Sh.  
                        Pritam Singh S/o Sh. Ram Ratan and the  
                        management and if so whether his  services  
                        have   been   terminated   illegally   and   /   or  
                        unjustifiably by the management; and if so,  
                        to   what   relief   is   he   entitled   to   and   what  
                        directions are necessary in this respect?"


</p><p id="p_3">2.            Vide endorsement no. 1 to the aforesaid Order a copy of the 

              order was sent to the workman with a direction to file a  

              statement of claim with the relevant documents and list of  

              witnesses in the Labour Court mentioned above within 15  

              days. He was also directed to serve a copy of the statement of 

              claim on all the parties involved. It was not so done by the 

              workman. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">3.            Pursuant   to   notice   of     reference   issued   by   the   court   Mr.

              S.K.   Singh,   AR   of   the   workman   appeared   on

              13.03.2013 and  sought adjournment for filing statement of 

              claim. Case was adjourned for 15.05.2013. None appeared  

              on   behalf   of   the   workman   on   15.05.2013,   17.07.2013,

              21.08.2013 and 16.09.2013 to file statement of claim of the 

              workman       despite     last   opportunity,   and   last   and   final  

              opportunity.     Sufficient   opportunities   have   been   given   to  

              the workman  to file statement of claim but he has  not filed  

              the statement of claim.  The workman appears to have lost  


Page 2 to 4                                                         (ANAND SWAROOP AGGARWAL)
                                                                     POLC - XI : KKD. : DELHI.
</p><p id="p_6"> Pritam Singh Vs.  M/s. FAIR WEALTH SECURUTIES  LTD.                                    ID No.  388/12



              interest in the present industrial   dispute   regarding   which  

              reference has been made to the court.



</p><p id="p_7">4.            In these circumstances, no statement of claim has come on 

              record.   In   the   absence   of   any   statement   of   claim   by   the  

              workman this court finds it unable to answer the reference in 

              question.   Hon'ble   Supreme   Court   of   India   in   the   case  

              reported   as    Virendra   Bhandari     Vs.     Rajasthan   State  

              Road  Transport Corporation &amp; others 2002 (3) LLJ 256 has 

              observed as under : 
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                     "4.       A   perusal   of   the   award   made   on   the  
                     earlier occasion will clearly indicate that there is  
                     no adjudication of the dispute at all. All that was  
                     stated   was   that   the   concerned   party   had   not  
                     appeared before the Tribunal and in such an event,  
                     the   Tribunal   should   have   noted   its   inability   to  
                     record the finding on the issue referred to it not  
                     that the dispute itself does not exist. When there is  
                     no adjudication of the matter of merits, it cannot  
                     be   said   that   the   industrial   dispute   does   not  
                     exist ........." 
</blockquote>


<p id="p_9">5.            Thus, this court is left with no option but to terminate the  

              present proceedings   at this stage and return the reference  

              unanswered   /   undecided   leaving   it   for   the   appropriate  

              Government   to send the reference again in case the party  

              raising the dispute approaches the appropriate Government.  



</p><p id="p_10">6.            A  copy  of  the  award  be sent to the concerned Office of
