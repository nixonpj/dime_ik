<div class="doc_title">Aarti Devi And Another vs State Of Punjab And Others on 19 April, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">Criminal Misc. No. M-11312 of 2011                                    1




      In the High Court of Punjab and Haryana, at Chandigarh.


                  Criminal Misc. No. M- 11312 of 2011

                     Date of Decision: 19.4.2011


Aarti Devi and Another
                                                            ...Petitioners
                                    Versus
State of Punjab and Others
                                                          ...Respondents


CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE KANWALJIT SINGH AHLUWALIA.


Present: Mr. Maninder Singh Bajwa, Advocate
         for the petitioners.


Kanwaljit Singh Ahluwalia, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">          Petitioners-Aarti Devi and Ajay     Kumar are present in the

Court.   Aarti Devi has stated that she has passed 10+2 examination.

She has further stated that she was born on 20.5.1992. It is further

stated by the petitioners that they have solemnized marriage according

to their own volition against the wishes of their parents. The petitioners

seek protection to their life and pray that they may not be harmed by

those who have not accepted their marriage.

</p><p id="p_2">          Issue notice of motion.
</p>
<p id="p_3">          On asking of the Court, Mr. B.S. Sra, Additional Advocate

General, Punjab, accepts notice on behalf of respondents No.1 to 3. A

copy of the petition has been supplied to him.

</p><p id="p_4">          Respondent       No.2-Commissioner      of   Police,    District

Jalandhar, is directed to assess threat perception to the petitioners and
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Criminal Misc. No. M-11312 of 2011                                   2</span>




if required, ensure necessary vigil that no harm is caused to the life of

the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_5">          With the observations made above, the present petition is

disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_6">          A copy of this order, duly attested by the Special Secretary of

this Court, be furnished to Mr. B.S. Sra, Additional Advocate General,

Punjab, for onward transmission and compliance.


</p><p id="p_7">                                           (Kanwaljit Singh Ahluwalia)
                                                                Judge
April 19, 2011
"DK"
</p>
