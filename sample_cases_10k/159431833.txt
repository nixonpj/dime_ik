<pre id="pre_1">                          IN THE COURT OF SH. VIRENDER KUMAR GOYAL
                      ADDL SESSIONS JUDGE: FAST TRACK COURT 
                                                  ROHINI:DELHI



SC No. 41/1
Unique Identification No. 02404R0018792013 
State 
Versus

1)            Bhagalu
              Son of Sh. Rameshwer Mahawat
              R/o Gali No.9, 
              Mahawat Colony Hind Vihar,
              Prem Nagar­III, Delhi. 

2)            Kissan
              Son of Sh.Rameshwer Mahawat
              R/o H.No. A­471, 
              Mahawat Colony,
              Prem Nagar­III, Delhi. 

3)            Bambiya
              Son of Sh. Jungri Mahawat
              R/o H.No. A­432, Gali No.8,
              Mahawat Colony Hind Vihar,
              Prem Nagar­III, Delhi. 

              FIR No. 60/12
              PS - Aman Vihar
              U/s. 308/34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>


              Date of institution of the case: 15/03/2013
              Arguments heard on: 07/06/2013
              Date of Decision: 07/06/2013 

              JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">              This case was registered on the statement of one Batru U/s. 308/34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>. 

Prior to that, DD No. 22A was recorded on 09/03/2012 at about 1.52 p.m. day about 

quarrel in Hind Vihar, Mahawat Colony, Prem Nagar III, H.NO. 14.



</p><p id="p_2">SC No. 41/1                                                 1/5
               During investigation, rough site plan was prepared. Accused Bambiya, 

Bhagelu   and   Kishan   were   arrested.   Personal   search   of   accused   Bhagelu   was 

conducted. Accused Kishan and Bambiya also made disclosure statements. MLCs of 

injured persons were also collected. On completion of investigation, charge­sheet was 

filed U/s. 308/34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">IPC</a>, which was committed to the court of Session on 08/03/2013 

and was received on 15/03/2013. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">              Charge   U/s.   308/34   of   <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a>   was   framed   against   all   the   three   accused 

persons, to which, they pleaded not guilty and claimed trial. 

</p><p id="p_4">              To prove its case, prosecution has examined PW1 to PW6 in all. 

              As   none   of   the   witness   has   supported   the   case   of   the   prosecution 

regarding   identity   of   the   accused   persons   as   the   assailants,   hence,   prosecution 

evidence was closed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">              As   there   is   nothing   on   record   to   be   put   to   the   accused   persons, 

accordingly, recording of their statements U/s. 313 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_5">Cr.P.C</a>. has been dispensed with. 

</p><p id="p_6">              PW1 HC Ajeet Singh is of formal nature. He was working as duty officer 

at PS Aman Vihar on 12/03/2012.  On receipt of rukka, he recorded FIR of this case 

Ex. PW1/A.  He also recorded Kayami DD No. 20A and also made endorsement on 

the rukka vide Ex. PW1/B. 
</p>
<p id="p_7">              Complainant Batru has been examined as PW2.   He has stated that on 

09/03/2012,  at about 1.00 p.m. noon, he was present at his  house.    On that day, 

cemented road of their gali was being constructed and the labourers of the thekedar 

were doing the work of preparation of cemented road.  On seeing the road not being 

properly constructed, he came out of his house and asked the thekedar "Bhai aap road 

ko kahi pura or kahi adhoora karke chod dete hain". 
</p>
<p id="p_8">              PW2 has further deposed that thereafter, he came at the place, where road 

was being constructed and due to his walking on the road, the road got damaged, at 

which,   the   thekedar   and   his   labourers,   who   were   10­15   in   numbers,   had   started 

quarreling with him and also gave beatings to him. When he raised alarm, his wife 


SC No. 41/1                                                             2/5
 Rajshani   Devi   alongwith   his   cousin   Dalip   and   nephew   Bully   also   reached   there. 

Other persons from the surrounding area also reached there.   When these persons 

came   to   save   him,   labourers/contractor   also   started   beating   them   with   lathi   and 

dandas and other instruments of road making, due to which, he alongwith his wife, 

Dalip and Bully had sustained injuries. Somebody informed the police, at which, PCR 

van came and took them to SGM hospital.  After sometime, police had come in the 

hospital and made inquiries from him. At that time, they had not got recorded their 

statements due to some social pressure.  On the next day, he was discharged from the 

hospital.
</p>
<p id="p_9">              PW2 has further deposed that on 12/03/2012, he reached at PP and his 

statement was recorded Ex. PW2/A. PW2 has specifically stated that accused persons 

present in the court had not caused any injuries to them. As PW2 has not supported 

the case of the prosecution, so, he has been cross examined by Ld. APP, but even 

then,   he   has   not   supported   the   case   of   the   prosecution   regarding   identity   of   the 

accused persons. 
</p>
<p id="p_10">              Similarly, PW3 is Madan Mahawat. He has stated that on 09/03/2012, at 

about 1.00 p.m. noon, on hearing the noise of quarrel, he came out of his house and 

saw that a quarrel was going on between the labourers of the contractor and Batru, 

his wife and other family members.   When he tried to intervene, he also sustained 

injuries   in   the   quarrel.   Somebody   informed   the   police.     After   causing   injuries, 

labourers ran away from the spot. After sometime, PCR van came there and took 

PW2 Batru, his wife and other relatives to hospital, who had sustained injuries. PW3 

Madan Mahawat has specifically stated that accused persons present in the court had 

not caused injuries to anyone.  
</p>
<p id="p_11">              PW3   has   further   deposed   that   on   20/05/2012,   police   came   and   made 

inquiries from him.  At that time, police had obtained his signatures on some blank 

papers, which are  Ex. PW3/A and Ex. PW3/B i.e. arrest memos of accused Kishan 

and Bambaiya. He has voluntarily stated that these documents were blank, when his 


SC No. 41/1                                                               3/5
 signatures were obtained on them. PW3 has not supported the case of the prosecution 

regarding   the   identity   of   accused   persons   as   assailants.   He   has   also   been   cross 

examined   by   learned   APP,   but   even   then,   he   has   not   supported   the   case   of   the 

prosecution  regarding the identity of accused persons as the same, who had caused 

injuries to them. 
</p>
<p id="p_12">              PW4 is Dalip.  He has deposed that on 09/03/2012, he was present at is 

house. At about 1.00 p.m. noon, on hearing the noise of quarrel in the gali, where 

cemented road was being constructed, he came out and saw that a quarrel was going 

on between the labourers of the contractor and his cousin Batru i.e. PW2.  On seeing 

the same, he tried to intervene, but he also sustained injuries in the quarrel.  At that 

time, Bully, who is son of his sister, also reached there,who also tried to intervene to 

save them, but he also sustained injuries in that quarrel.   Other persons from the 

surrounding areas had also gathered there. Due to the beatings given by the labourers, 

he alongwith Bully, PW2 Batru and his wife had sustained injuries in the quarrel. 

Somebody had informed the police.   After causing injuries to them, labourers ran 

away from there.  PCR van came there and took them to SGM hospital. 

</p><p id="p_13">              PW4 Dalip has further deposed that on 20/05/2012, police met him and 

made inquiries from him and recorded his statement.   He has also not supported the 

case of the prosecution regarding identity of the accused persons as the assailants in 

any manner, hence, he has also been cross examined by the learned APP, but even 

then,   he   has   not   supported   the   case   of   the   prosecution   regarding   identity   of   the 

accused persons as the same, who had caused injuries to them. 

</p><p id="p_14">              PW5 Bully Mahawat and PW6 Rajshanni have also deposed the same 

facts as of PW4 Dalip.   They have also not supported the case of the prosecution 

regarding identity of the accused persons, hence, they have been cross examined by 

learned APP, but even then, they have also not supported the case of the prosecution 

regarding identity of the accused persons. 
</p>
<p id="p_15">


</p><p id="p_16">SC No. 41/1                                                               4/5
               In  view  of  above, as neither complainant nor  public  witnesses/injured 

have supported the case of the prosecution in any manner regarding the identity of the 

accused persons as the assailants, who had caused injuries to them, prosecution has 

not been able to prove offence U/s. 308/34 of <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_6">IPC</a> in any manner, accordingly, all 

the accused are acquitted for the same. 
</p>
<p id="p_17">

</p><pre id="pre_2">Announced in the open court                                    (Virender Kumar Goyal)

today on 7th of June, 2013                              Additional Sessions Judge
                                                        Fast Track Court, Rohini Courts,Delhi. 




SC No. 41/1                                                           5/5
