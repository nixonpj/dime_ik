<div class="doc_title">Criminal Side vs Unknown on 9 September, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF UTTARAKHAND AT NAINITAL
              ORIGINAL JURISDICTION

                  Dated: Nainital: September 9, 2014

             First Bail Application No.857 of 2014
             Order on the bail application of accused

Criminal Side
  Sandeep @ Sanni,
  S/o Daljeet Singh,
  R/o Telipura Chaukhandi,
  Police Station - Tanda,
  District Rampur (U.P.).

                                             .....Applicant
                                Versus
   State of Uttarakhand                   .......Opp. Party
____________________________________________________________

Hon'ble Sudhanshu Dhulia, J. (Oral)
</pre><p id="p_1">     Heard Mr. D.C.S. Rawat, Advocate for the applicant and
Mr. Navneesh Negi, learned Brief Holder for the State of
Uttarakhand.
</p><p id="p_2">     The applicant is in jail since 10.07.2013 having been
implicated in F.I.R. No. 54 of 2012, which has been
registered under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">Sections 379</a>/<a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_2">411</a> of I.P.C. at Police Station
Kashipur, District - Udham Singh Nagar.
</p><p id="p_3">     Applicant is not named in the First Information Report.
Charge-sheet has been filed under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_3">Section 379</a>/<a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_4">411</a> of I.P.C.
in which maximum punishment is three years.
</p><p id="p_4">     Considering the overall evidence which is presently
available before this Court, particularly considering the fact
that the applicant is in jail since July, 2013, the applicant
has been able to make out a case for bail. The bail
application is accordingly allowed.
</p><p id="p_5">     Let the applicant (Sandeep @ Sanni) be enlarged on bail
in the aforesaid crime on his executing a personal bond and
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               2</span>




two reliable sureties each of the equal amount to the
satisfaction of the Magistrate concerned.
</p><p id="p_6">     It is made clear that any observation made by this
Court is only for the purpose of grant of bail. It shall not be
taken into consideration at all in any other proceedings.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                  (Sudhanshu Dhulia, J.)
                                      09.09.2014
ML
