<div class="doc_title">None Present For The vs State &amp; Ors on 5 September, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                                  S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO. 4213/2014.
                                         Lachhi Ram    Vs.     State &amp; Ors.
                                                    Order dated 05.09.2014
                                    1/2

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN

                             AT JODHPUR.

                             :: O R D E R ::

             S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO. 4213/2014.
                     Lachhi Ram     Vs.           State &amp; Ors.
                                     ..

Date of Order                            ::::          5th September, 2014.

                               PRESENT

             HON'BLE Dr. JUSTICE VINEET KOTHARI

Appearance:

None present for the petitioner
                                     --
BY THE COURT:</pre>

<p id="p_1">1.       The lawyers are observing strike which is contrary to

various Supreme Court decisions. Name of Mr. Shardul Bishnoi,

as the counsel for the petitioner, is shown in the cause list.

</p><p id="p_2">2.       Nobody is present for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_3">3.       Perused the record. The case is listed at Sl. No. 2 in the

cause list under the category of "For Fresh Admission Without

Stay".
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.       The writ petition has been filed by the petitioner in this

Court on 28.05.2014 with the following prayers:-

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">               "It    is,   therefore,          most   humbly    and
         respectfully prayed that this writ petition may
         kindly be allowed and by an appropriate writ,
         order or direction:
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">      (i)      the respondents may kindly be directed to
               grant the first selection scale to the
               petitioner on completion of 9 years of
                                     S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO. 4213/2014.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                                           Lachhi Ram    Vs.     State &amp; Ors.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">                                                      Order dated 05.09.2014
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                      2/2</span>

                   service while counting the service of the
                   petitioner from the date of his initial
                   appointment.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_5">           (ii)    the respondents may kindly be directed to
                   grant the arrear of salary to the petitioner
                   along with 18% interest.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_6">           (iii)   Any other appropriate writ, order or
                   direction which this Hon'ble Court deems
                   just and proper may kindly be passed in
                   favour of the petitioner."
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_7">     5.    Having perused the record of the case and having gone

     through the averments made in the writ petition by the

     petitioners, this Court is satisfied that the writ petition is

     premature. The petitioner should first approach the respondents

     and should pursue his representation.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_8">     6.     Accordingly, the writ petition is disposed of with a direction

     and liberty to the petitioner to approach the respondents to

     pursue his representation Annexure-8 dated 03.01.2014 and the

     respondents are expected in law to decide the representation of

     the petitioner in accordance with law, preferably after giving an

     opportunity of hearing to the petitioner, within a period of three

     months from today.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_9">     7.    A copy of this order be sent to the concerned parties

     forthwith.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_10">                                                (Dr. VINEET KOTHARI), J.
</blockquote><p id="p_5">/Mohan/
