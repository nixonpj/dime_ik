      THIS WRIT PETITION IS FILED UNDER <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">ARTICLE 227</a> OF
THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA PRAYING TO ISSUE A WRIT OF

MISC.CASE NO.39/2016.
                               -2-
                                           WP No.33632/2018


     THIS WRIT PETITION COMING ON FOR PRELIMINARY
HEARING, THIS DAY, THE COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING:

                          ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">H.G.RAMESH, J. (Oral):
</p>
<p id="p_2">1.    Learned Counsel appearing for the petitioner submits

that the writ petition may be disposed of by directing the

Court of the VI Additional City Civil Judge, Bengaluru City

(CCH-11) to dispose of the proceeding in Misc.No.39/2016

filed by the petitioner under Order IX Rule 13 of CPC to set

aside the decree dated 17.06.2015 made in the suit in

OS No.4909/2011 expeditiously. It is stated that hearing of

the miscellaneous petition is concluded and only the order

has to be pronounced.
</p>
<p id="p_3">2.    In view of the above, the aforesaid Court is directed

to dispose of the miscellaneous petition in Misc.No.39/2016

expeditiously and in any event within one month from the

date of receipt of a copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_4">      Petition disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                               Sd/-
</p><p id="p_6">                                              JUDGE
