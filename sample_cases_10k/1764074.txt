<p id="p_1">SLP(C) Nos.7918-7951/2015:
</p>
<p id="p_2">          Learned   counsel    for   the   petitioners     has   drawn   the
attention of this Court to order dated 29.09.2016 which records
that no application of substitution of legal heirs has been filed
in respect of certain respondents relating to the special leave
petitions. He submits that it appears to be typographical error to
the extend that in respect of SLP(C) Nos. 7921-7932 of 2015, the
reference to respondent Nos.22 to 27 should read as respondent
Nos.22 and 27 as would be apparent from order dated 19.01.2016. He
further   submits   that   since   these   respondents    were   supporting
parties, now, he does not seek to bring the legal heirs on record
and seek deletion of the said respondents.
</p><p id="p_3">          Ordered accordingly.
</p><p id="p_4">          Amended memo of parties be filed within two weeks from
today.
</p>
<p id="p_5">SLP(C) No. 7903-7916/2015 :
</p>
<p id="p_6">          Application for deletion of respondent Nos.7, 20, 21, 24,
27 &amp; 29 is allowed at the risk and cost of the petitioners.
</p>
