<p id="p_1">            TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.J

                               The petitioner, who is serving as a Math Master under the

            Punjab School Education Department, has filed the instant writ petition

            raising a grievance that he has not been released his salary from July, 2011

            to February, 2012.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               Learned counsel for the petitioner would contend that on

            8.7.2011 the petitioner was transferred from Govt. High School, Zora

            Singha to Govt. Girls High School, Narot Mehra against the vacant post of

            Math Master. On 11.7.2011 the Head Mistress of the school, where the

            petitioner was transferred, wrote to the D.E.O., Gurdaspur that there is no

            vacant post in the school as two Math Masters on regular basis are already

            working. On 12.7.2011 the D.E.O (S.E.), Gurdaspur wrote back to the Head

            Mistress with instructions that the petitioner be allowed to join on a

            demanded post. Accordingly, on 13.7.2011 the petitioner joined on his

            transferred place of posting.            Thereafter, the respondent-department

            sanctioned posts in District Gurdaspur including two posts of Math Masters

            in the month of April, 2012 and it is only thereafter that the petitioner was

            released his salary. Counsel would argue that the petitioner cannot be made
