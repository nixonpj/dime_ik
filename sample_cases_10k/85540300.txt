ORAL ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">1.	It appears that under Order
dated 21.02.2013, certain information regarding Special Civil Suit
No.220 of 2003 was called for. It further appears that the Registry
has received instruction from In-charge Principal District and
Sessions Judge vide letter dated 01.03.2013 that the said Suit has
been disposed on 31.03.2009 by judgment and order passed by learned
Additional Senior Civil Judge, Ahmedabad (Rural). 
</p>
 


 

 


<p id="p_2">2.	Any details as to whether any appeal
against the said decision is filed or not is not available on record.
</p>

 


 


 
	  


<p id="p_3">	Today, when the petition is called out, no
	one is present from the side of petitioner or from the side of
	respondent.
</p>
 


 


 


 


 
	 


<p id="p_4">	4.	Since the petition is listed in the cause
	list under Code 315 (For Orders) category, instead of passing any
	order, hearing is adjourned. 
</p>	
	 


	
	  


<p id="p_5">	Office is directed to place the matter for
	hearing in regular board Since the Suit proceedings appear to have
	been disposed of. 
</p>	
	 


	
	  


<p id="p_6">	Learned advocate for the contesting parties
	shall inquire and inform the Court as to whether any appeal against
	the said judgment is filed or not. S.O. to 23. 10.2013. 
</p>	

 


 


 


<p id="p_7">(K.M.THAKER, J.) 

 

Girish
