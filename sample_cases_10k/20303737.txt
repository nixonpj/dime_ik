  17039, 36286, 36287, 36303, 36358, 36419, 36935,
    36981, 36987, 37005, 37008, and 37021 of 2015

COMMON ORDER :

      As the issue involved in all these Writ Petitions is

one and the same, they are being heard together and

disposed of by this Common Order.

     Heard both sides.

     Sri   V.Ramachander      Goud,     learned   Standing

Counsel for JNTU on instructions submits that similar

issue was considered and dismissed by this Court in

WP.Nos.39315/2017 and batch on 27.11.2017.

     In view of the same and for the reasons alike in the

Common       Order    dated    27.11.2017     passed    in

WP.Nos.39315/2017 and batch, these writ petitions are

also dismissed. No order as to costs.

     As a sequel thereto, miscellaneous petitions, if any,

pending in these writ petitions, shall stand closed.



                          _______________________________
                            A.RAJASHEKER REDDY, J
