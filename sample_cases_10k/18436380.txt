<div class="doc_title">Babu Lal Khati vs State Of Raj And Ors on 11 May, 2017</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                     1</span>



                    S.B. Civil Writ Petition No.5025/2017

Date: May 11, 2017
              HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ALOK SHARMA

Mr. Ashindra Gautam, for the petitioner.
Ms. Manju Joshi, Dy.GA for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">      The issue in the petition is with regard to the convening of the


newly constituted marketing Committee Dooni for election of the


Chairman and Vice Chairman as per Rule 32 of the Rajasthan


Agricultural Produce Markets Rules, 1963 (hereinafter `the Rules of


1963').
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">      On the matter coming up before this court on 11-4-2017,


copy of the petition was supplied to the Additional Advocate General


and adjourned to 24-4-2017. On the aforesaid date ten days time


was allowed for filing reply to petition. The matter was fixed for 4-


5-2017.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">      However the matter has been listed today. Still reply to petition


has not been filed and time is being sought for the purpose.


</p><p id="p_7">      I find no good ground to grant another indulgence and


adjourn the matter for filing of the reply as the matter in issue of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                  2</span>

importance relating to the election of the Chairman and Deputy


Chairman of the Market Committee.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">       The District Collector Tonk is directed to remain present


before this court on 12-5-2017 at 11.00 AM to show cause as to


why Rule 32 of the Rules of 1963 is not being complied with.


</p><p id="p_10">       A copy of this order be supplied to Ms. Manju Joshi for


conveying the same to the District collector Tonk and ensure


compliance of the order.
</p>
<p id="p_11">

</p><p id="p_12">                                                 (Alok Sharma), J.
</p><p id="p_13">arn/
 All corrections made in the order have been
incorporated in the order being emailed.


Arun Kumar Sharma, Private Secretary.
 </p>
