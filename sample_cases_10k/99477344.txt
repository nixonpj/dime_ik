</pre><p id="p_19">                These petitions have been filed, seeking a direction for approval
and registration of students admitted against the lapsed seats.
</p>
<p id="p_20">                2. When these matters are taken up for hearing, the learned
counsel for the petitioner-colleges has submitted that nothing survives for
further adjudication in all these petitions, as the relief sought for by the
petitioner-colleges has become infructuous, pursuant to ratification of
admissions of students against the lapsed seats.
</p><p id="p_21">                3. In view of the above submission, this Court is of the view
that nothing survives for further adjudication in these matters. Hence, these
petitions are closed as having become infructuous. No costs. Consequently,
connected miscellaneous petitions are closed.
</p>
