      04.10.
951    2018                          C.A.N. 11823 of 2017
 ML
AGM
                                               in
 Ct                                   S.A.T. 296 of 2016
02



                                      Sudhir Kumar Saha
                                             Versus
                                     Anil Kumar Saha &amp; Ors

               Mr. Dr. Indrajit Mondal,
               Mr. Amit Banerjee,
                                               ... For the Appellant/Plaintiff.

                      This appeal lies on the following substantial
               questions of law :-
               1. Whether the Court of Appeal below failed to consider
                    that the plaintiff had filed his documents in relation
                    to title and due to mistake they were not marked as
                    exhibits and, therefore, the plaintiff was entitled to
                    lead additional evidence on the same.
               2.    Whether the Court of Appeal below erred in
                    rejecting   an    application       for   additional   evidence
                    without recording a positive finding as to whether
                    such document has any relevance on the issues
                    involved in the suit and becomes necessary for the
                    purpose of the delivery of the judgment?
               3. Whether       the    Court       of   Appeal   below     erred   in
                    surreptitiously jumping to a conclusion that the
                    appeal is bereft of any merit without recording its
                    specific reasons and/or findings thereupon?
                      The office is directed to bring the Lower Court
               Records by Special Messenger at the cost of the
                            2


appellant. Such cost shall put in by next Friday, i.e.,
12th October, 2018. After the arrival of the Lower Court
Records, the office shall verify the same and, if found in
order, shall put the same in this regard.
        The appellant is directed to prepare requisite
numbers of informal paper book within two weeks after
reopening of this Court after Puja Vacation.
        The petitioner is directed to serve the copy of the
injunction application upon the respondents by speed</pre>
<p id="p_1">post and shall file affidavit-of-service on the returnable
date.
</p><p id="p_2">        The application is made returnable two weeks
after the reopening of this Court following the Puja
vacation before the appropriate Bench.
</p>



<p id="p_3">(Shekhar B. Saraf, J)                (Harish Tandon, J)
