<div class="doc_title">Sanjay Singh vs The State Of Bihar on 14 December, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.71400 of 2018
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-671 Year-2018 Thana- KHAGARIA District- Khagaria
                 ======================================================
                 Sanjay Singh, Son of Munna Prasad Singh @ Munna Singh, resident of

                 Village - Ram Nagar, Police Station - Gangour, District - Khagaria.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar.
                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr. Sanjana
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr. Amrendra Prasad
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE BIRENDRA KUMAR
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   14-12-2018                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                  Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in a case

                  registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/471092/" id="a_1">Sections 272</a>,

                  <a href="/doc/285163/" id="a_2">273</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and Section 30(a) of the Bihar

                  Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_4">Excise Act</a>, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               From possession of co-accused Sanjay Chaudhary,

                  liquor was recovered. Sanjay Chaudhary disclosed the name of

                  the petitioner and another person as co-accused that they had

                  fled away from there. Petitioner has stated on oath that he has

                  got no criminal antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               Considering the fact that there is no substantial

                  material against the petitioner, let the petitioner, above named,

                  in the event of his arrest or surrender before the Court below
            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.71400 of 2018(2) dt.14-12-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                    within a period of thirty days from the date of receipt of the

                    order, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.20,000/-

                    (rupees twenty thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                    each to the satisfaction of the learned court below where the

                    case is pending in connection with Khagaria (Gangour) Police

                    Station Case No. 671 of 2018, subject to the conditions as laid

                    down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_5">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure

                    as well as condition that the petitioner shall fully cooperate with

                    the investigation and trial of the case, failing which the court

                    below shall be at liberty to cancel the bail bond of the petitioner.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                                         (Birendra Kumar, J)
    Kundan/-
</p>
