<div class="doc_title">Mr.Sushil Solanki vs Issue Notice Of Writ Petition As ... on 5 August, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                                1


   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                       JODHPUR
                             ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">              S.B.CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.8187/2015

             Date of order             :           5.8.2015

              HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SANDEEP MEHTA

Mr.Sushil Solanki, for the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">                            &lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;


       Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and perused the

material available on record.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       It is reported that the petitioner has 20 months' service

to go and now in the eve of his service tenure, he has been

transferred from Chhotu to Juni Undari.

</p><p id="p_4">       Learned counsel drew attention of this Court to the order

dated 12.7.2015, whereby the transfer orders of numerous

other teachers, who were having less than two years of service

remaining, have been cancelled. Learned counsel contends

that   the    petitioner   submitted       a   representation   to   the

appropriate authority for cancelling his transfer and for being

provided similar relief but till date, no action has been taken

thereupon. He thus prays that the impugned transfer order

(Annex.2) dated 28.6.2015 deserves to be quashed.

</p><p id="p_5">       The matter requires consideration.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       Issue notice of writ petition as well as stay petition to the

respondents returnable in six weeks.

</p><p id="p_7">       The effect and operation of the order (Annex.2) dated
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    2</span>


      28.6.2015 shall remain stayed qua the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_8">           In   the   meantime,   the   appropriate   authority   shall

      objectively consider the petitioner's representation and decide

      the same keeping in view the order dated 12.7.2015, whereby,

      the transfer orders of a number of teachers, who are to retire

      in the next two years have been cancelled.



</p><p id="p_9">                                                (SANDEEP MEHTA), J.
</p>
<p id="p_10">/tarun/
