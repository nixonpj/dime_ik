<div class="doc_title">Suresh vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 9 December, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">               1                            M.Cr.C.No.12806/2015

                   (Suresh and Others Vs. State of M.P.)
09.12.2015
     Shri Raj Shrivastava, Advocate, for the applicants.
     Shri Prabal Solanki, Public Prosecutor, for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard.
</p><p id="p_2">     This is the first application for regular bail under
<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C. on behalf of applicants Suresh,
Naresh and Sukhdev.
</p><p id="p_3">     The applicants are in custody since 17.11.2015           in
connection with Crime No.181/15 registered at Police Station
Sirsod, district Shivpuri (M.P.) for the offence under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">Sections
323</a>, <a href="/doc/594493/" id="a_3">294</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_5">506-B</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_7">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_8">326</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_4">     It is alleged that on 14.09.2015 at about 7.30 PM
complainant Ramswaroop Jatav along with his younger brother
Karan Singh were going to their home from the bore-well, on
the way, accused Lakshman and Rai Singh said Karan Singh
that thier names have been told by him falsely for steeling the
pipe. When Karan said that he has not named them and the
complainant Ramswaroop asked about what has happened,
the accused persons abused him. When complainant stopped
them from abusing, accused Rai Singh, Lakshman running to
their home.    Lakshman armed with axe, applicant Suresh
armed with lathi came there and they started beating the
complainant and Karan Singh.     Complainant sustained injury
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                  2                               M.Cr.C.No.12806/2015</span>

on his elbow and on the right thumb and in the middle finger.
When his cousin Kalyan and aunt came to the scene, the
accused applicants Naresh        and Sukhdev caused injuries to
them.     Sukhdev inflicted injury by axe to her aunt, who
sustained injuries on the right wrist and middle finger. Naresh
inflicted injury to Kalyan by lathi on his head and on the left
wrist and shoulder. Witnesses Chiddha and Prahlad came to
the scene and saved them.
</p><p id="p_5">        On behalf of the applicant it is submitted that on the
report of accused Ram Singh, criminal case has been
registered     against   Pappu       Jatav,   Kashiram,   Kalla   and
complainant Ramswaroop under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_9">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/594493/" id="a_10">294</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_11">341</a>, <a href="/doc/175699/" id="a_12">336</a>,
<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_13">506-B</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_14">34</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_6">        It is also stated that the injury caused to Ramswaroop
and Kusuma are not grievous in nature.
</p><p id="p_7">        Learned Public Prosecutor for the State opposed the
application.
</p><p id="p_8">        Considering the facts that there has been dispute
between the parties and injuries are not caused in vital part of
the body, the application for regular bail is allowed and it is
directed that the applicants shall be released on bail on their

furnishing a personal bond in a sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rs. Fifty

Thousand only) each with one solvent surety each in the like
amount to the satisfaction of the Trial Court for securing their
presence before      the said Court on all the dates of hearing
fixed in this regard during trial.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                  3                             M.Cr.C.No.12806/2015</span>

<p id="p_9">        This order will remain operative subject to compliance of
the following conditions by the applicants:-
</p><p id="p_10">  1. Applicants will not influence the prosecution witnesses.
</p><p id="p_11">  2. Applicants swill help the progress of the Trial.
</p><p id="p_12">  3. Applicants will not indulge in any similar offence during the
     course of Trial.
</p><p id="p_13">  4. If at all the applicants are found to breach any of the above
     conditions, the learned Trial Court would be at liberty to
     reconsider on the question of bail.
</p>
<p id="p_14">             Certified copy as per rules.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                  (S.K. Palo)
 mani                                               Judge
