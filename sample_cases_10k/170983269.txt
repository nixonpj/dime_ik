</p><p id="p_3">as                              C.R.R. 3930 of 2014

              In Re:- An application under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal
              Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
              In the matter of : Pradip Biswas &amp; Anr.      ... Petitioners.


              Mr. Kallol Kr. Basu.              ...for the Petitioners.


</p><p id="p_5">                   It is submitted that the parties have amicably settled the

              matrimonial dispute resulting in the registration of the impugned

              criminal case.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                   Let this matter appear as 'Contested Application' two weeks

              hence.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                   Copy of the revisional application be served upon the

              opposite party No.1 through the learned Public Prosecutor, High

              Court, Calcutta and upon the opposite party no.2 through

              registered speed post with acknowledgement due card and file

              affidavit of service on the next date of hearing.

</p><p id="p_8">                   There shall be stay of the proceeding in G. R. Case No.3172

              of 2013 pending before the learned Additional Chief Judicial

              Magistrate, Basirhat, 24-Paraganas, North arising out of Harora

              Police Station Case No.303 of 2013 dated 05.08.2013 under

              <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Sections 498A</a>/<a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_3">406</a>/<a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_4">354</a> of the Indian Penal Code for a period of

              three weeks or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                2</span>


         Parties are at liberty to file joint petition of compromise.

         Liberty to pray for extension, modification, variation and/or

    vacating of the interim order upon notice to the other side.



</p><p id="p_9">                                     (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
