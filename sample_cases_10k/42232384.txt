                         JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Petitioner is a registered owner of a car bearing

registration No.KL-09 V 296, which was purchased after

availing a loan from the 1st respondent bank.               Admittedly

repayment      was    defaulted.    Consequentially        action   was

initiated, evident from Exts.P1 and P2 issued under sections

7 and 34 of the Kerala Revenue Recovery Act. On receipt of

the same, petitioner has submitted Ext.P3 representation

before   the    1st   respondent      bank,     which      is    pending

consideration and according to the learned counsel, a

direction to dispose of the same would suffice the situation.

</p><p id="p_2">     2. Having heard learned counsel for petitioner and

learned Senior Government Pleader as well as learned

Standing Counsel for the 1st respondent bank, there will be

a direction to the 1st respondent to consider Ext.P3 in

accordance with law and attain finality at the earliest

possible time and at any rate within a month from the date
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">W.P.(C) No. 7638 of 2018       2</span>



of receipt of a copy of this judgment. Till such time,

proceedings pertaining section 7 shall be kept in abeyance.

</p><p id="p_3">      Writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
