                           W.P. 30534(W) of 2016

                              Swapan Sardar
                                    Vs.
</p><p id="p_2">                      The State of West Bengal &amp; Ors.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
      Mrs. Srijani Bhattacharyya
                     ..... for the petitioner

      Mr. Asim Kumar Ganguly,
      Mr. Bellal Sheikh
                      .... for the State



      Report is filed by the learned Counsel appearing on behalf of the

State pursuant to the order dated 12.01.2017. Let this report be kept

with record.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      From the said report it appears that enquiry was conducted

with regard to cause of death of the victim and it was found that the

victim was suffered from ailments and was admitted to various

hospitals and finally expired in Peerless Hospitex Hospital and

Research Center Limited. Cause of death was stated to be systematic

lupus erythe matosus with multi organ failure.       It also appears from

the said report that death was a natural one and, therefore, graver

offence of murder as alleged by the petitioner was not included in the

charge-sheet.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      As the investigation has resulted       in filing of a police report

before the Criminal Court, it shall be open to the petitioner to appear
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                    2</span>


before the Criminal Court as victim of crime and agitate his grievances

therein in accordance with law, if so advised.

</p><p id="p_6">      With the aforesaid observation, the writ petition is disposed of.

      There will be no order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_7">      Photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, shall be

given to the parties as expeditiously as possible on compliance of all

necessary formalities.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                  (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
