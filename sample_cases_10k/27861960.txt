                     SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 1525 of 2016
         ==========================================================
                   PRATAPSINH SIDUBHA JADEJA &amp; 5....Petitioner(s)
                                     Versus
                      STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 6....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MR BHARAT T RAO, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1 - 6
         ADVANCE COPY SERVED TO GP/PP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         NOTICE SERVED BY DS for the Respondent(s) No. 5 , 7
         NOTICE UNSERVED for the Respondent(s) No. 6
         ==========================================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N.V.ANJARIA

                                    Date : 07/02/2017
                                     ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">              Pursuant to order dated 27.01.2017, all newly added
         parties except the deceased respondent then joined, have
         been served as per the affidavit filed by the petitioner. As far
         as respondent no.6, it is stated that he was not present at his
         residence and on being contacted telephonically, respondent
         no.6 stated that he was away from the house on account of
         occasion of marriage in their family. In the circumstances,
         fresh notice to the respondent no.6 returnable on 16.02.2017.
         Petitioner is permitted to serve the notice by Registered
         Speed Post at his cost.
</p>
<p id="p_2">              The parties may file reply before the next date.
</p>
<p id="p_3">              Stand over to 16.02.2017.</p><pre id="pre_2">
