                               JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      The petitioner, who is working as Junior Superintendent at

PH Division of the respondent Water Authority, is aggrieved by the

fact that in Ext.P3 order, which shows the names of qualified Junior

Superintendents, who are promoted as Divisional Accounts

Officers, his name has not been included. The specific case of the

petitioner is that, his seniority has not been correctly worked out

by the respondent and, in this connection, he has preferred Ext.P2

representation before the 2nd respondent. The limited prayer of the

petitioner in the writ petition is for a direction to the 2nd respondent

to   consider    and     pass    orders     on    Ext.P2      representation,

expeditiously, after hearing the petitioner.




</p><p id="p_2">      2.    I have heard the learned counsel appearing for the

petitioner as also the learned Standing Counsel appearing on

behalf of the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_3">


</p><p id="p_4">      On a consideration of the facts and circumstances of the case

as also the submissions made across the bar, I dispose the writ

petition with a direction to the 2nd respondent to consider and pass

orders on Ext.P2 representation, after hearing the petitioner,

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               -2-</span>
W.P.(C). No. 25126 of 2015



within a period of two months from the date of receipt of a copy of

this judgment. The petitioner shall produce a copy of the writ

petition together with a copy of this judgment before the 2nd

respondent for further action.
</p>
