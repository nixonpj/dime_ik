<div class="doc_title">Dilip Oraon vs The State Of Jharkhand on 30 April, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                 B.A. No. 244 of 2015
                                        -------
            Dilip Oraon                             .... Petitioner
                                       -Versus-
            The State of Jharkhand                 ..... Opposite Party
                                         -------
            CORAM :        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.C.MISHRA
                                         -------
            For the Petitioner :            : M/s. Jitendra Shankar Singh
            For the State :                 : M/s. Hardeo Prasad Singh, A.P.P.
            For the Informant :             : M/s. Rajesh Kumar
                                         -------

4/ 30.04.2015</pre><p id="p_1">.        Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned counsel for the
             State.
</p><p id="p_2">                      The petitioner has been made accused in connection with Lohardaga
             (Mahila) P.S. Case No.25 of 2014, corresponding to G.R No.319 of 2014, for
             the offence under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_1">Sections 376</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_3">379</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506</a> of the Indian Penal Code
             and <a href="/doc/230360/" id="a_5">Sections 4</a> / <a href="/doc/84602/" id="a_6">5</a> of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.
</p><p id="p_3">                      There is direct allegation against the petitioner to have committed rape
            upon the informant and also to have sexually exploited her on the false
            pretext of marrying her.
</p><p id="p_4">                      In the facts of this case, I am not inclined to release the petitioner on
            bail. Accordingly, the prayer for bail of the petitioner Dilip Oraon, is hereby
            rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                                            (H.C.Mishra, J.)
D.S.
</p>
