<div class="doc_title">Sri Atma Deep Dutta vs University Of Burdwan &amp; Ors on 21 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                       1

  21.07.2017
  10/skp.

                  W.P. 14345(W) of 2017
Sri Atma Deep Dutta vs. University of Burdwan &amp; Ors.

       Mr. Debjit Mukherjee,
       Mrs. Susmita Chatterjee         .... for the petitioner.
      Mr. N. C. Bihani,
      Ms. Papiya Banerjee Bihani   ... for Burdwan University.


       Mr. Debjit Mukherjee, learned counsel appears for the writ petitioner and complains of
defective and/or faulty marking in the B.Sc. (Hons.) Geography Paper -IV for which the writ
petitioner appeared in the 2014-2015 Examination.
       On behalf of the respondent/University of Burdwan, Mr. N. C. Bihani, learned counsel</pre>
<p id="p_1">appears and questions the maintainability of the relief as claimed by the writ petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">       Having heard the parties and considering the materials placed, this Court is of the view
that this is a fit case to be remitted to the respondent no. 3, i.e., the Controller of Examinations,
University of Burdwan to answer the grievances of the writ petitioner on merits in accordance
with law.
</p><p id="p_3">       It is expected that the Respondent No. 3 shall take a decision in the matter, if necessary,
upon giving an opportunity of hearing to the petitioner within a period of two weeks from the date
of communication of this order.
</p><p id="p_4">       W.P. No. 14345(W) of 2017 stands disposed of.
</p><p id="p_5">       Affidavit of service filed in Court today be kept on record.
       Urgent xerox certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the parties on usual
undertaking.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                   (Subrata Talukdar, J.)
