<div class="doc_title">Kanti Devi vs The State Of Bihar on 6 February, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.32362 of 2016
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -118 Year- 2016 Thana -GANDHIMAIDAN District- PATNA
                 ======================================================
                 Kanti Devi wife of Ajay Kumar Resident of Village- Kalyanpur, P.S.-
                 Kadirganj, District- Patna

                                                                                 .... ....   Petitioner
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar
                 2. P.N.B. Patna Zone through its G.M.

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Rajani Kant Singh, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Sri Arbind Kumar Pandey
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ARUN KUMAR
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">7   06-02-2017                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

                 learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 Petitioner apprehends his arrest in connection with a

                 case registered under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_2">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_4">471</a> and <a href="/doc/1128948/" id="a_5">120(B)</a> of

                 the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Allegation in brief is that the amount of pre-matric

                 scholarship are being defalcated by some fictitious persons in

                 connivance with government school officials.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioner

                 submits that no any such amount was ever withdrawn by this

                 petitioner from her account. It is said that total defalcated money

                 to the tune of Rs.15 lacs and odd has been recovered in cash in

                 school premises and the Principal of the said School, Rajiv Ranjan
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.32362 of 2016 (7) dt.06-02-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                      has been granted anticipatory bail earlier by a Co-ordinate Bench

                      of this Court by order dated 11.8.2016 passed in Cr. Misc.

                      No.26558 of 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                      Learned Additional Public Prosecutor appearing on

                      behalf of the State also does not point out any material coming

                      against the petitioner in the case diary.

</p><p id="p_6">                                       Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, in

                      the event of arrest/surrender within four weeks from the date of

                      receipt of this order to the concerned court, let Kanti Devi, the

                      petitioner be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of

                      Rs.10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                      each to the satisfaction of learned Susri Supriya Goswami, J.M. Ist

                      Class, Patna in connection with Gandhi Maidan P.S. Case No.118

                      of 2016, subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_6">Section

                      438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                                      (Arun Kumar, J)
N.H./-
</p>
