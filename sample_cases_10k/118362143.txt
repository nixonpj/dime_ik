                             Mr. Manoj Tandon, Adv (For R. No. 4)
</p><p id="p_4">                                   -----
</p><p id="p_5">       10/Dated 3rd December, 2016
</p><p id="p_6">       1.     This Interlocutory Application has been preferred for impleadment of the
       opposite parties in contempt Case (Civil) No. 958 of 2013.
</p><p id="p_7">       2.     Having heard counsel appearing for both sides and looking to the facts
       and circumstances of the case, this Interlocutory application is allowed and
       following impleadments shall be carried out in this contempt application in red ink
       during course of the day:-
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">              (a)    Shri R.K. Srivastav, Chairman, Jharkhand Urja Vikas Nigam
                     Limited is permitted to be joined as Opposite Party No. 3.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">              (b)    Pratya Amrit, Chairman, Bihar State Electricity Board as
              Respondent No. 4.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">              (c )   Shri K.K. Jha, Director Finance/Accounts, Jharkhand Urja
              Vikas Nigam Ltd. as Opposite Party No. 5.
</blockquote><p id="p_8">       3.     The nature of contempt is not going to be changed to the contrary by
       virtue of this amendment and this contempt will be decided in accordance with
       law.
</p><p id="p_9">       Cont. Case(Civil) No. 958 of 2013
</p><p id="p_10">       4.     Notice to the newly joined parties.
</p><p id="p_11">       5.     Mr. Om Prakash Tiwari, Advocate waives Notice on behalf of Opposite
       Party No.s 3 and 5 and seeks time to get instruction.
</p><p id="p_12">       6.     Mr. Manoj Tandon, Advocate, waives Notice on behalf of Opposite Party
       No. 4 and seeks time to get instruction from his client.
</p><p id="p_13">       7.     Copy of the contempt case and affidavits filed will be given to the
       Advocates of the newly joined parties.
</p><p id="p_14">       8.     This matter is adjourned to be listed on 20th January, 2017.
</p>

<p id="p_15">                                                                     (D.N.Patel, J.)


s.m.
