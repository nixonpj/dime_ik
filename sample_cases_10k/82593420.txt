<div class="doc_title">An Application For Anticipatory ... vs 1. Sk. Ramjan And on 20 April, 2017</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                    1</span>

     20.04.2017                       C.R.M. 2975 of 2017.
</p><p id="p_2">      ALLOWED
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">73</span>
 c
     In the matter of : An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Code of Criminal
     Procedure filed on 10th April, 2017.
</p><p id="p_3">                                   And
     In the matter of :    1.     Sk. Ramjan and
</p><p id="p_4">                           2.     Rejina Begum @ Bibi.
</p><p id="p_5">                                                         Petitioners.</p><pre id="pre_1">
     Ms. Pampa Dey (Dhabal)
                                         .....    For the Petitioners.
     Mr. Debojyoti Deb
                                         ......     For the State

</pre><p id="p_6">            Apprehending arrest in course of investigation of Indas Police Station FIR No. 13
     of 2017 dated 11.01.2017 under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">sections 498A</a>/<a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_3">304B</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code, the
     petitioners (in-laws of the victim) have applied for anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_7">            Having heard the learned advocates for the parties and on perusal of the case
     diary, it appears that there is dearth of sufficient material therein to suggest direct
     involvement of the petitioners in the alleged crime.
</p><p id="p_8">            We are, thus, of the considered view that custodial interrogation of the petitioners
     is not necessary for effective and meaningful investigation of the FIR and that they are
     entitled to direction, as prayed for in the application.
</p>
<p id="p_9">            CRM 2975 of 2017 stands allowed with the direction that in the event of arrest,
     the petitioners viz. Sk. Ramjan and Rejina Begum @ Bibi, shall be released on bail upon
     furnishing bond of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand) each with two sureties of like
     amount each, one of whom must be local to the satisfaction of the arresting officer and
     subject to the condition as laid down in sub-section (2) of <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_5">section 438</a> of the Code of
     Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_10">            Certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given on priority basis.
</p>


<p id="p_11">                                                         ( Dipankar Datta, J.)


                                                        (Debi Prosad Dey, J. )
