</p><p id="p_3">                             *****
A.K. SIKRI, C.J. (ORAL)

            No doubt the respondent herein had got few promotions

in his service career. At the same time, it is also not in dispute that

he got the promotion of Sub Station Operator (later on designated as

Junior Engineer) in 1973 from which post he got promoted as Junior

Engineer Grade-I in only 1997.     Thus, on the post of Sub Station

Operator, he remained stagnated for the period of almost 24 years.

Going by these considerations, the learned Single bench has held

that the respondent herein would be entitled to the benefit of grant of

ACP scales which are admissible after 10 years and 20 years of

stagnation in a particular post. The respondent fairly fulfilled these

conditions and the learned Single Judge has rightly granted the

benefit of ACP after completion of 10 years and 20 years of service.

The matter is squarely covered by Division Bench judgment of this

Court in its judgment dated 4.4.2008 titled as "Ramesh Dahiya Vs.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> LPA No.1759 of 2012 (O&amp;M)                           -2-</span>




Uttar   Haryana      Bijli   Vitran   Nigam   Limited"    reported   in

2008(3) RSJ 186.
</p>
<p id="p_4">            We, thus, do not find any merit in this appeal. Hence the

same is hereby dismissed on merits as well as on delay.</p><pre id="pre_2">




                                                   (A.K. SIKRI)
                                                  CHIEF JUSTICE



                                              (RAKESH KUMAR JAIN)
JANUARY 8, 2013                                     JUDGE
