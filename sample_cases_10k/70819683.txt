                    In the High Court At Calcutta
                        Civil Appellate Jurisdiction
                               Appellate Side

                                 S.A. 215 of 2009
                                       With
                               C.A.N. 8857 of 201
                                       With
                                C.A.N. 30 of 2015

                             Sanjib Banerjee &amp; Ors.
                                      Vs.
                         Shiba Prosad Karmakar &amp; Anr.


             Mr. Arup Banerjee,
             Mr. Arun Kumar Saha
                        ....for the appellant-applicant

                           In Re.: C.A.N. 3182 of 2016


                  This is an application at the instance of the appellants in the
              second appeal for impleadment of Prabir Karmakar as the
              respondent in the pending appeal.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                  The appeal arises out of a partition suit. In paragraph 10 of
              this application, the petitioners have categorically mentioned that
              from an affidavit-in-opposition filed by the respondents in C.A.N. 30
              of 2015, they for the first time came to learn that during the
              pendency of the appeal, the respondent no. 2 by a registered deed of
              gift dated May 19, 2014 has transferred his share in the suit
              property in favour of his son, Prabir Karmakar.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              2</span>


<p id="p_2">      A copy of this application has been served on the learned
advocate representing the respondents in the second appeal before
this Court. However, none appears on behalf of the respondents to
oppose this application.
</p><p id="p_3">      Considering the averments made in this application, I find that
the transferee of a portion of the suit property, namely Prabir
Karmakar, being the son of the donor respondent no. 2, is entitled
to be added as a party respondent in the pending appeal.
</p><p id="p_4">      Accordingly, the application of the appellants succeeds and
Prabir Karmakar, son of Gurupada Karmakar, residing at village-
Chakbhura, Post Office- Kaiti, P.S. Madhabdihi, District-Burdwan is
added as the respondent no. 3 in the appeal.
</p><p id="p_5">      The Department is directed to carry out necessary amendment
in the memorandum of appeal filed in the second appeal by
impleading the said Prabir Karmakar as the respondent no. 3.
</p><p id="p_6">      With the above directions, C.A.N. 3182 of 2016 stands disposed
of.
</p><p id="p_7">      There shall, however, be no order as to costs.
</p>


<p id="p_8">                                        (Ashis Kumar Chakraborty, J.)
