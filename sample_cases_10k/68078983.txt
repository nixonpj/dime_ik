                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.2735 of 2018
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -92 Year- 2017 Thana -BUXAR RAIL P.S. District- BHOJPUR
                 ======================================================
                 Subham Kumar, Son of Dinesh Prasad Sav, Resident of Mohalla-
                 Khusrupur, P.S.- Khusrupur, District- Patna.

                                                                                 .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar.

                                                              .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance:
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Ashhar Mustafa, Advocate.
                                            Mr. Ashish Kumar Ranjan, Advocate.
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Dashrath Mehta, APP.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRAKASH CHANDRA
                 JAISWAL
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   23-01-2018                     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   The petitioner seeks bail in Buxar Rail P.S. Case No.

                 92 of 2017 registered under Section 30(a) of the Bihar Excise

                 Amendment Act, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   29 litres of foreign liquor is said to have been

                 recovered from the possession of the petitioner at the platform of

                 Buxar Railway Station.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

                 that no incriminating article has been recovered from conscious

                 physical possession of the petitioner. As a matter of fact, the

                 petitioner was travelling on the train as a bonafide passenger and
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.2735 of 2018 (2) dt.23-01-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       2/2</span>




                        when alighted at the platform of Buxar Railway Station, the police

                        demanded illegal graft from him and on failure to cough up his

                        demand, he has falsely implicated the petitioner in the case

                        planting recovery of the liquor from his possession. The petitioner

                        has no criminal antecedent. The petitioner has been languishing in

                        custody since 09.12.2017.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                           Considering the facts aforesaid, the above named

                        petitioner is directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail

                        bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the like

                        amount each to the satisfaction of learned 4th ADJ-cum-Spl.

                        Judge (Excise), Ara in connection with Buxar Rail P.S. Case No.

                        92 of 2017.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                               (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
Mishra/-
</p>
