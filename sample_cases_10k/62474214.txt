<p id="p_4">              Without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case,

the instant petition is disposed of with a direction to respondent No.2-

complaints (Annexure P-6, P-10 to P-12), already moved by the

petitioner, expeditiously, in accordance with law.</p><pre id="pre_2">
