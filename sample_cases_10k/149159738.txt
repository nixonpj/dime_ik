<div class="doc_title">Pushpendra Singh Jadon vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 9 October, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">     THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
               CRR-2466-2017
     (PUSHPENDRA SINGH JADON Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


Order Number : 1
Jabalpur, Dated : 09-10-2017
      Shri Pramod Thakre, learned counsel for the
petitioner.
      Shri C.K.Mishra, learned GA for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      This revision has been preferred against the order
dated 17.8.2017 passed by First ASJ Narsinghpur in
S.T.No.312/2015. It is stated that the petitioner's
application for exemption under <a href="/doc/1009979/" id="a_1">Section 317</a> of Cr.P.C
has been dismissed. The application of Jagdish and
Pushpendra along with other accused Ravindra was also
dismissed on the same day in Cr.R.No.2362/2017 vide
order dated 15.9.2017. This Court has allowed the
revision and directed the applicant to remain punctual
before the trial Court and he shall appear on the dates as
fixed by the trial Court condoning his absence and
recalling the arrest warrant issued against Ravindra.
</p><p id="p_2">      Petitioners Jagdish &amp; Pushpendra had also filed
medical certificates with regard to their ailment.
</p><p id="p_3">      Considering the parity of the petitioners to that of
accused Ravindra Patel @ Ravi Patel, this revision is
allowed. The absence of petitioners Jagdish &amp;
Pushpendra before the trial Court is condoned.
</p><p id="p_4">      The issuance of warrant of arrest on 17.8.2017 is
 recalled.
</p><p id="p_5">      Petitioners are directed to remain present before
the trial Court on each and every date of hearing and
shall cooperate with the trial.
</p><p id="p_6">      With the above observations, this revision is
disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                               (SUSHIL KUMAR PALO)
                                       JUDGE
