<p id="p_1">(Per
: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE PARESH UPADHYAY)
 

 


 
	  

Rule.
</p><p id="p_2">	Learned APP, Mr.Pandya, waives service of rule on behalf of
	respondent-State.
</p>	 

 
	
	  

<p id="p_3">By
	way of this application, the applicant-convict has prayed that he be
	enlarged on temporary bail on the ground that he wants to attend his
	ailing mother.
</p>	 

 
	
	  

<p id="p_4">From
	the record, it transpires that along with the application, no
	medical papers are annexed to support the case coupled with the fact
	that in October, 2011, the present applicant was already enlarged on
	temporary bail.
</p>	 

 
	
	  

<p id="p_5">Considering
	the totality of the facts, discretion does not deserve to be
	exercised.
</p>	 

 
	
	  

<p id="p_6">Hence,
	application is rejected. Rule discharged.
</p>
