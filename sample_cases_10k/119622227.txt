<p id="p_1">Prayer: Contempt Petitions are filed under <a href="/doc/974692/" id="a_1">Section 11</a> of the Contempt of
Courts Act, 1971, praying to punish the respondent for  his wilful
disobedience of the order of this Court, dated 16.11.2016, made in
W.P(MD)Nos.21868, 21869 and 21870 of 2016 respectively.   
</p>
<p id="p_2">!For Petitioner     : Mr. E.V.N.Siva

^For Respondent  : Mr. S. Gunaseelan Muthiah, 
                                                   Government Advocate          
        
                                        
:COMMON ORDER      

        It is represented by the learned Government Advocate that the order
passed by this Court dated 16.11.2016, in W.P(MD)Nos.21868, 21869 and 21870   
of 2016 respectively are complied with.  Recording the above, the Contempt
Petitions are closed.   
</p>

Mr.S.Muruganantham,   
District Educational Officer,
