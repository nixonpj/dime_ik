<div class="doc_title">Shankar vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 20 March, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                             MCRC-3661-2017
                    (SHANKAR Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


20-03-2017

     Shri K.S.Rajput, learned counsel for the applicant.
     Shri A.N.Gupta, learned Panel Lawyer for the respondent-State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard.
</p><p id="p_2">     This is first bail application filed on behalf of the applicant under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section
439</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
</p><p id="p_3">    The applicant is in custody since 10/02/2017, in connection with Crime
No.307/2016 registered at Police Station Khalwa District Khandwa(M.P.), for
the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_2">Sections 363</a>,<a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_3">366</a>,<a href="/doc/1677485/" id="a_4">376(2)(n)</a> of IPC read with
Section 5/6 of Laingik Apradhon Se Balkon Ka Sanrakshan Adhiniam.
</p><p id="p_4">     Allegedly, applicant took the minor prosecutrix from the guardianship of
her parents, kept her with him and during stay committed rape upon her and
thereafter she was recovered on 10/02/2016 from the possession of the
applicant.
</p><p id="p_5">     Learned counsel for the applicant submits that applicant is innocent and
he has been falsely implicated in this case. It is also submitted that
prosecutrix herself accompanied the applicant. The date of birth of the
prosecutrix as per school record is 21/08/1999 and according to that record
on the date of incident i.e 16/11/2016 the age of prosecutrix was 17 years two
months and 25 days. In her statement under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_5">Section 164</a> of Cr.P.C she has
stated that she was living with the applicant as his wife. He is ready to
furnish bail as per the order, abiding with all conditions imposed by the
Court. On these grounds, learned counsel for the applicant prays for
grant of bail to the applicant.
</p><p id="p_6">      Per-contra, learned panel lawyer for the respondent-State opposes the
bail application.
</p><p id="p_7">       After hearing arguments of the parties and looking to the facts,
 circumstances of the case, age of prosecutrix and role of prosecutrix herself, I
am of the considered view that it would be appropriate to release the
applicant on bail, therefore, without commenting on the merits of the case,
application of the present applicant namely, Shanker under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_6">Section 439</a> of
the Cr.P.C. seems to be acceptable. Consequently, it is hereby allowed.
</p><p id="p_8">       It is directed that applicant be released on bail on his furnishing bail
bond in the sum of Rs.40,000/- (Rupees Forty Thousand) with one surety
of the same amount, to the satisfaction of the JMFC concerned or Trial Court
for his appearance before them on the dates given by the concerned Court. It
is directed that applicant shall comply the provisions of <a href="/doc/259682/" id="a_7">Section 437(3)</a> of
Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_9">Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                                                              (H.P. SINGH)
                                                                 JUDGE
