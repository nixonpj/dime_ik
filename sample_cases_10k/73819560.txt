<div class="doc_title">Md. Idris vs The State Of Bihar on 7 January, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.171 of 2015
                       Arising Out of PS.Case No. -112 Year- 2012 Thana -DHURAIYA District- BANKA
                 ======================================================
                 Md. Idris
                                                                     .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Amrendra Kumar Jha
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Madhuranand Jha(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   07-01-2015               Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                             The petitioner is apprehending arrest in a case registered

                 for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_2">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_4">471</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120B</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_6">504</a> and <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_7">323</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                             The accusation is of taking loan for purchase of the

                 tractor on the basis of forged documents.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that

                 the petitioner admits taking of the loan and he is making

                 repayment of the same, the proof with regard to the same has been

                 brought on record as Annexure-2. The petitioner undertakes that

                 the entire loan amount with interest will be deposited within

                 stipulated time frame fixed by the bank.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above named

                 petitioner be released on anticipatory bail, in the event of arrest or
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                       Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.171 of 2015 (2) dt.07-01-2015</span>


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2</span>




                 surrender before the learned Court below within a period of 12

                 weeks from today, on furnishing bail bond of `10,000/- (ten

                 thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                 satisfaction of the learned C.J.M., Banka in connection with

                 Dhoraiya P.S. Case No. 112 of 2012, subject to the conditions as

                 laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_9">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                              The bail bonds of the petitioner will be accepted by the

                 learned court below provisionally till the entire loan amount with

                 interest is being deposited.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                         (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Amrendra/-
</p>
