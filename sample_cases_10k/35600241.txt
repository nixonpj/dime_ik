<div class="doc_title">Arbind Singh Kushwah vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh Thr on 10 April, 2018</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                   1</span>
                                THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                                         M.Cr.C. No. 12295/2018
                                  Arbind Singh Kushwah Vs. State of M.P.
</p>
<p id="p_2">          Gwalior Dated :10.04.2018
                     None for the applicant.
</p><p id="p_3">                     None for the State.
</p><p id="p_4">                     Case diary provided by the office of AG is perused.
                     Default pointed out by the Registry is reported to be not
          cured.
</p><p id="p_5">                     The applicant has filed this 3rd repeat application u/S 439,
          <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>. for grant of bail. The applicant has been arrested on
          10.11.2017 by Police Station Kotwali Datia, District Datia in
          connection with Crime No.497/2016 registered in relation to the
          offences punishable u/Ss. 302, 147, 149, 120B of <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>.
</p><p id="p_6">                     This repeat bail application is filed after rejection of the
          earlier one vide order dated 03.01.2018 passed in M.Cr.C. No.
          27047/2017 by finding that though implication of applicant is based
          on statement recorded Sec. 27 of the <a href="/doc/1953529/" id="a_3">Evidence Act</a> of the co-
          accused Ajay but the said revelation in the said confessional
          statement is supported by eye-witness Pritam who saw the
          applicant behind the wheels of the offending vehicle which was
          used to crush the deceased to death.
</p><p id="p_7">                     Applicant has been absconding for long before being
          arrested on 10.11.2017 and therefore has delayed the proceedings
          and trial. Admitting the applicant to bail would amount to grant of
          premium to default and therefore, present application stands
          dismissed with liberty to come again after examination of main
          eyewitnesses or if the trial gets further delayed.
</p>
