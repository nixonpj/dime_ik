<div class="doc_title">Ismail Sk vs State Of Jharkhand on 27 February, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                   Cr. Revision No. 977 of 2012

       Ismail Sk. S/o Taijul Sk., R/o Village-Singadda, P.O-Malpahari, P.S.-Pakur
       (M), Dist.-Pakur (Jharkhand)                        ...... Petitioner
                               Versus
       The State of Jharkhand                              ...... Opposite Party
                              ---------
</pre><p id="p_1">       CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AMITAV K. GUPTA
</p><p id="p_2">                              ---------</p><pre id="pre_2">
       For the Petitioner            :     Mr. Gautam Kumar, Advocate
                                           Mr. Shashi Kumar Verma, Advocate
       For the State                 :     A.P.P.
                              ---------

       07/Dated: 27/02/2015
               Admit.
               Call for the lower court records.
</pre><p id="p_3">               Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the petitioner
       has been convicted for the offence under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Section 498-A</a> and <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a> of the I.P.C.
       and sentenced to be detainede in the remand home for a period of one year.
</p><p id="p_4">               Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the entire
       allegation is false as alleged cruelty harassment pertains to only one day
       and there is no allegation that prior to said date the informant was ever
       subjected to cruelty or torture by the petitioner. That the informant was
       carrying pregnancy of 4 months at the time of alleged marriage and the
       marriage itself is null and void which the courts below have failed to
       appreciate. That all the witnesses are interested and related and, no
       independent witnesses have supported the allegation; that the petitioner is
       in custody since 26.11.2014.
</p><p id="p_5">               Learned A.P.P has opposed the prayer for bail. .
</p><p id="p_6">               Regard being had to the facts and circumstances of the case,
       petitioner above named is directed to be released on bail during the
       pendency of the revision on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees ten
       thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of
       Principal Magistrate, Juvenile Justice Board, Pakur in connection with Pakur
       (Mahila) P.S. Case No. 147 of 2008 corresponding to G.R. No. 685 of 2008
       (T.R. No. 08 of 2011), subject to the condition that one of the bailors shall be
       father of the petitioner and he shall file an undertaking to ensure proper
       care and supervision and well being of the petitioner and will also produce
       the petitioner before the Probation Officer on 15 th day of each month. The
       Probation Officer shall submit the report regarding the conduct of the
       petitioner to the Board.
</p><p id="p_7">               Put up this case after receipt of the lower court records.
</p>
