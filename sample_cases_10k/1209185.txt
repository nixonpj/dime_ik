<div class="doc_title">/Dr.Bijay Gupta @ Dr.Bijay Kumar ... vs State Of Bihar on 4 July, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Cr.Misc. No.32524 of 2008
   Dr.Bijay Gupta @ Dr.Bijay Kumar Gupta, son of Late Chandi Pd., village and PS Haveli
Kharagpur, Dist. Munger, at present posted as Medical Officer, Sadar Hospital, Hajipur, Vaishali
                                         - Petitioner.
                                            Versus
                                        State Of Bihar


3      4.7.2011</pre><p id="p_1">                         Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                           counsel appearing on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                        The petitioner is aggrieved by order, dated 9.1.2008

                           passed by the C.J.M, Munger in Sangrampur PS Case No.

                           131/2002 by which cognizance has been taken for the offences

                           under <a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_1">sections 409</a>, <a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_2">201</a>, <a href="/doc/1442106/" id="a_3">423</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_4">120B</a> of the Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                        Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

                           petitioner was transferred out of the Centre, where the expired

                           medicine was found four months prior to the inspection and as

                           such he cannot be held responsible for the presence of expired

                           medicines in the stock.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                        These aspects of the matter can only be considered at

                           the appropriate stage of the case. For the present this court does

                           not find any illegality in the impugned order. This application is

                           dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><pre id="pre_2">         haque                                                   ( Sheema Ali Khan, J.)
 </pre>
