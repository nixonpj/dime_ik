<div class="doc_title">Anmol Agarwal vs Sushant Kumar Agarwal (Huf) &amp; Ors on 18 May, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                    GA No.1044 of 2018
                                     CS No.201 of 2015

                          IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                      Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                                  ORIGINAL SIDE


                                            ANMOL AGARWAL

                                             -Versus-

                                            SUSHANT KUMAR AGARWAL (HUF) &amp; ORS.

                                                                               Appearance:
                                                                   Mr. Bajrong Manot, Adv.

      BEFORE:
      The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Date : 18th May, 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">               The Court : This is a joint application filed by the

plaintiff      and    the     defendants      for    recording      compromise     with     a

prayer for addition of Nav Technology Pvt. Ltd. as defendant No.6.

in view of the fact that the parties have entered into a lawful

agreement and arrived at a compromise during the pendency of this

proceeding      and    the    Court    is    satisfied      with   the   nature     of    the

agreement entered into by and between the parties duly recorded in

the    Terms    of    Settlement      annexed       to    the   petition,   the    suit    is

decreed on the basis of the Terms of Settlement filed by the

parties and duly signed by the parties.

</p><p id="p_4">               The added defendant is represented by Mr. Bajrang Manot,

Advocate.       The vakalatnama and the affidavit of competence filed

in    Court,    is    taken    on    record.        The    added   defendant      has    also
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    2</span>


consented to the orders being passed in terms of the settlement

annexed to the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_5">          Under   such   circumstances   there   shall   be   an   order   in

terms of prayers (a), (b), (c) and (e) of the plaint.

</p><p id="p_6">          The department is directed to carry out the amendments

on or before 15th June, 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_7">          The parties have agreed that if any belongings of the

defendant No.2 and his mother are in the almirah presently in

possession of the plaintiff, the plaintiff shall hand over such

movable, if any, to the defendant No.2.

</p><p id="p_8">          The department is directed to draw up the decree as

expeditiously as possible.
</p>
<p id="p_9">          G.A.No.1044 of 2015 stands disposed of.

          C.S.No.201 of 2015 is accordingly decreed on compromise.




</p><p id="p_10">                                                    (SOUMEN SEN, J.)

A/s.
</p>
