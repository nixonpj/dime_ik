         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

         R/CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 10201 of 2018

==========================================================
                   RASUL NATHU MITHU DAFER SINDHI
                               Versus
                          STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR SHAKEEL A QURESHI(1077) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1,2
for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
MR L. B. DABHI, APP, for the RESPONDENT(S) No.1
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.P.DHOLARIA

                               Date : 18/06/2018

                                ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">1.          This application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code
of Criminal Procedure for regular bail in connection with an offence
being C.R.No.I- 21 of 2018 registered with Adalaj Police Station,
Gandhinagar for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_2">Sections 395</a> and
<a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a> of the Indian Penal Code and Section 135 of the G.P.Act.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.          Learned advocate appearing on behalf of the applicants
submits that considering the nature of offence, the applicants may
be enlarged on regular bail by imposing suitable conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.          Learned Additional Public Prosecutor appearing on
behalf of the respondent-State has opposed grant of regular bail
looking to the nature and gravity of the offence.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4.          Learned         advocates    appearing   on   behalf     of   the
respective parties do not press for further reasoned order.
</p>

<p id="p_5">5.          I have heard learned advocates appearing on behalf of



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   Page 1 of 3</span>
            R/CR.MA/10201/2018                                   ORDER



the respective parties and having considered the fact that the
recovery of robbed articles to the tune of Rs.53,000/- had already
been recovereded. From that count, this Court is inclined to use
judicial discretion for enlarging him on bail.
</p>

<p id="p_6">6.              In the facts and circumstances of the case and
considering the nature of allegations made against the applicants
in the FIR, without discussing the evidence in detail, prima facie,
this Court is of the opinion that this is a fit case to exercise the
discretion and enlarge the applicants on regular bail. Hence, the
present application is allowed and the applicants are ordered to be
released on regular bail in connection with an offence being
C.R.No.I-21 of 2018 registered with Adalaj Police Station, on
executing a personal bond of Rs.20,000/- each (Rupees Twenty
thousands only) with one surety of the like amount to the
satisfaction of the learned Trial Court and subject to the conditions
that they shall;
</p>

<blockquote id="blockquote_1">     [a]        not take undue advantage of liberty or misuse liberty;
     [b]        not act in a manner injuries to the interest of the
                prosecution;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">     [c]        surrender passport, if any, to the lower court within a
                week;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">     [d]        not leave India without prior permission of the Sessions
                Judge concerned;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">     [e]        report before the concerned Police Station on
                alternate       Monday     of     every   English   calendar
                month, till final disposal of the sessions case
                against them;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_5">     [f]        furnish latest address of residence to the Investigating
                Officer and also to the Court at the time of execution of
                the bond and shall not change the residence without
                prior permission of this Court;
</blockquote>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                    Page 2 of 3</span>
<blockquote id="blockquote_6">        R/CR.MA/10201/2018                              ORDER




</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_7">7.          The concrened authority shall release the applicants
only if they are not required in connection with any other offence
for the time being. If breach of any of the above conditions is
committed, the Sessions Judge concerned will be free to issue
warrant or take appropriate action in the matter. Bail bond to be
executed before the learned Lower Court having jurisdiction to try
the case. It will be open for the concerned Court to delete, modify
and/or relax any of the above conditions, in accordance with law. At
the trial, learned Trial Court shall not be influenced by the
observations of preliminary nature, qua the evidence at this stage,
made by this Court while enlarging the applicants on bail. Rule is
made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_8">            Direct service is permitted.</blockquote>



<p id="p_7">                                                 (R.P.DHOLARIA, J)
