<div class="doc_title">R.Saravanan @ Jothi vs The Superintendent Of Police ... on 31 August, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED : 31.08.2015
CORAM
THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE P.N.PRAKASH
Crl.O.P.No.20633 of 2015

R.Saravanan @ Jothi						... Petitioner
Vs.

1.The Superintendent of Police (North),
   Office of the Superintendent of Police,
   Puducherry.

2.The Sub-Inspector of Police,
   Orleanpet Police Station,
   Orleanpet,
   Puducherry.							... Respondents

Prayer: Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Cr.P.C., praying to direct the third respondent to register FIR (First Information Report) on the petitioner's complaint dated 11.06.2015 by strictly following ratio/guidelines laid down/framed by the Hon'ble Apex Court in Lalitha Kumari's case reported in 2013 (6) CTC 353.

	For Petitioner	:  	Mr.Prakash Adiapadam
	For Respondents	:	Mr.Thangavel
					Additional Public Prosecutor (Pondicherry)	
	
O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">		This Criminal Original Petition has been filed praying to direct the second respondent to register an FIR on the petitioner's complaint dated 11.06.2015. 
</p><p id="p_2">									  P.N.PRAKASH,J.
</p>
<p id="p_3">											      ds
</p><p id="p_4">		2. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned Additional Public Prosecutor (Pondicherry) appearing for the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_5">		3. The learned Additional Public Prosecutor submits that a petition enquiry is pending on the complaint dated 11.06.2015 given by the petitioner.   
</p><p id="p_6">		4. In view of the submission made by the learned Additional Public Prosecutor, the second respondent Police is directed to conduct enquiry on the complaint given by the petitioner and take action in accordance with law laid down by the Hon'ble Supreme Court in Lalita Kumari V. Government of Uttarpradesh and others reported in (2014) 2 SCC1.
</p>
<p id="p_7">		5. With the above direction, this Criminal Original Petition is closed.		
</p><p id="p_8">										31.08.2015
ds
To:
</p><p id="p_9">1.The Superintendent of Police (North),
   Office of the Superintendent of Police,
   Puducherry.
</p>
<p id="p_10">2.The Sub-Inspector of Police,
   Orleanpet Police Station,
   Orleanpet, Puducherry.		
</p>
<p id="p_11">3.The Public Prosecutor, Pondicherry.		      Crl.O.P.No.20633 of 2015
          </p>
