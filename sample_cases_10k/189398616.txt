<div class="doc_title">Deepak Kumar vs The State Of Bihar C.B.I., Pat on 16 April, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.46402 of 2012
                 ======================================================
                 Deepak Kumar Son of Sri Nakul Babu @ Nakul Singh @ Anukul Singh and
                 proprietor of M/s Sharda Construction, S.S. Lane, Mithapur, Patna and
                 presently resident of Mohalla Ashiyana Nagar, Plot No.139/C [Phase-1],
                 P.S. Shastri Nagar, District &amp; Town Patna
                                                                        .... .... Petitioner
                                                   Versus
                 The State of Bihar C.B.I., Patna
                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner    : None
                 For the Opposite Party: Mr. Bipin Kumar Sinha (SC, CBI)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VIKASH JAIN
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   16-04-2015              The present application has been filed for quashing the

                 order dated 10.09.2012 passed in Special Case No.33 of 2004 by

                 which the learned Special Judge, CBI-III, Patna has dismissed the

                 discharge petition filed by the petitioner in connection with R.C.

                 No.30 (A) of 2004 instituted under <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_1">sections 120-B</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>

                 and <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/86648814/" id="a_6">Section 13</a> (2) read with

                 <a href="/doc/86648814/" id="a_7">Section 13(1)</a> (d) of the <a href="/doc/1331755/" id="a_8">Prevention of Corruption Act</a>.

</p><p id="p_2">                            2.     None appears on behalf of the petitioner when the

                 case is called out.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                            3.     Mr. Bipin Kumar Sinha, learned Standing Counsel

                 for the C.B.I., states that stage of the case has changed since

                 charges have already been framed and out of 34 witnesses, seven

                 have already been examined.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                              4.       In the above circumstances, this Court is not
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.46402 of 2012 (2) dt.16-04-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




                     inclined to interfere with the impugned order. The petition stands

                     dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                                       (Vikash Jain, J)
B.T/-
</p><pre id="pre_2">  U        T
