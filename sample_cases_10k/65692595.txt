                                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

                                                    DATED : 30.01.2019

                                                          CORAM

                                 THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N. ANAND VENKATESH

                                                Crl.O.P.No.2390 of 2019

                      K.Balaji Krishnan                                            ... Petitioner

                                                            Vs.
                      1.The State represented by
                      The Inspector of Police,
                      Mamallapuram Police Station,
                      Kancheepuram District.

                      2.Kumar                                                      ... Respondents

                      PRAYER: Criminal Original Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of
                      Criminal Procedure, to call for records in connection with FIR No.685 of
                      2018 on the file of the respondent police, Mamallapuram Police Station, and
                      quash the same against the petitioner.

                                   For Petitioner        : Mr.K.Sudalai Kannu
                                   For R1                : Mr.M.Mohamed Riyaz,
                                                           Additional Public Prosecutor.

                                                         ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                            This Criminal Original Petition has been filed seeking to quash the

                      FIR in Crime No.685 of 2018 on the file of the first respondent police.




</p><p id="p_2">                            2.The respondent police has registered an FIR for the offence under

                      <a href="/doc/1270101/" id="a_2">sections 279</a> and <a href="/doc/1402213/" id="a_3">337</a> of IPC and subsequently, the same has been altered to

                      <a href="/doc/1270101/" id="a_4">section 279</a>, <a href="/doc/1402213/" id="a_5">337</a> and <a href="/doc/409589/" id="a_6">304(A)</a> IPC.
</p><p id="p_3">http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                               2</span>

                                                                             N.ANAND VENKATESH, J.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                                                                   mpa/rpl
</p><p id="p_5">                               3.The learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the

                      petitioner has been falsely implicated in this case and his vehicle is not

                      even involved in the said accident. The ground raised by the petitioner has

                      to be investigated by the respondent police and this Court cannot conduct a

                      mini investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                               4.This Court is inclined to interfere with the FIR at this stage.


</p><p id="p_8">                               5.This Criminal Original Petition is disposed of with a direction to the

                      first respondent police to complete the investigation and file a final report

                      within a period of three months from the date of receipt of a copy of this

                      order.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                                                                        30.01.2019

                      Index: Yes/No
                      mpa/rpl

                      To

</p><p id="p_10">                      1.The Inspector of Police,
                        Mamallapuram Police Station,
                        Kancheepuram District.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                      2.The Public Prosecutor,
                        High Court, Madras.
