<div class="doc_title">Malik Ram Maravi vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 13 January, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                          MCRC-235-2016
            (MALIK RAM MARAVI Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


13-01-2016</pre>
<p id="p_1">Shri B.K.Mishra, Advocate for the applicant.
Shri S.K.Shrivastava, PL for the non-applicant.
This is first bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C. for
offences under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_2">Sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/1559723/" id="a_3">366-A</a>, <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_4">376</a> of I.P.C &amp; <a href="/doc/200125/" id="a_5">Sections 3</a> and
<a href="/doc/230360/" id="a_6">4</a> of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012 in
connection with Crime No.679/2015 registered at Police Station
Motinala, District Mandla.
</p><p id="p_2">Learned counsel for the applicant contends that it is a case of
consent which reflected from the statement of the prosecutrix
recorded under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_7">Section 164</a> of Cr.P.C. After completion of the
investigation, challan has already been filed in this case.
Considering the aforesaid, prayer is made to enlarge the applicant
on bail..
</p><p id="p_3">On due consideration of the facts and circumstances of the case,
this Court deems it proper to grant bail to the applicant.
Accordingly, this application is allowed. Applicant Malik Ram
Maravi be released on bail on his furnishing a personal bond in
sum of Rs.50,000/- with one solvent surety in the like amount to
the satisfaction of JMFC concerned or CJM for his appearance in
Trial Court on the dates so fixed by that Court during trial. It is
directed that applicant shall comply with the provisions of <a href="/doc/259682/" id="a_8">Section
437(3)</a> Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_4">Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                               (J.K. MAHESHWARI)
 JUDGE
