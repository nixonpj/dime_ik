<div class="doc_title">Raben Sah vs The State Of Bihar on 19 September, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.44011 of 2017
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -57 Year- 2017 Thana -SRI NAGAR District- MADHEPURA
                 ======================================================
                 Raben Sah, Son of Chhutahru Sah, Residents of Village- Parmanandpur,
                 P.S.- Srinagar, District- Madhepura.

                                                                                 .... ....   Petitioner
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner     : Mr. Shailendra Kumar Singh, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party : Mr. Sri Nawal Kishore Prasad (APP)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE JITENDRA MOHAN SHARMA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   19-09-2017                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel representing the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner apprehends his arrest in connection

                 with Srinagar P.S. Case No. 57 of 2017, registered for the offences

                 punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1208971/" id="a_5">353</a>, <a href="/doc/308927/" id="a_6">225</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_7">333</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_8">504</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_9">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Allegedly, the petitioner and other co-accused started

                 abusing and assaulting the informant when he was filling the paper

                 of memo of arrest of Dilip Sah, the petitioner and others assaulted

                 the informant, a police officer, brutally and thereafter sensing the

                 assemblance of other police personnel they fled away. The

                 informant was brought for treatment.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Submission is of false implication and that specific
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.44011 of 2017 (2) dt.19-09-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                       allegation is against Ramesh Sah who has assault with farsa on the

                       head of the informant, against the petitioner there is general and

                       omnibus allegation and, as such, the petitioner deserves

                       sympathetic consideration.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                        The learned A.P.P. opposes the prayer of pre-arrest

                       bail.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                        In the facts and circumstances as stated above, I am

                       not inclined to grant privilege of pre-arrest bail to him and

                       accordingly his such prayer stands rejected in connection with

                       Srinagar P.S. Case No. 57 of 2017, pending in the Court of

                       learned A.C.J.M.-II, Madhepura.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                                                 (Jitendra Mohan Sharma, J.)
Rajiv/-
</p>
