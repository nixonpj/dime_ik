                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
         CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR EXTENSION OF TEMPORARY BAIL)
                                 NO. 22754 of 2016
                 In CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 20971 of 2016
         ==========================================================
                MAHAVIRSING HARINANDANSING KUSHVA....Applicant(s)
                                      Versus
                       STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance: MS.AKSHITABA SOLANKI, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No.
         1
         MR JK SHAH, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         ==========================================================
          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.P.BHATT
                            Date : 07/09/2016
                                         ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">         1.     Rule. Learned APP waives service of Rule on behalf of the respondent-State.
</p><p id="p_2">         2.     The present application is filed by the applicant seeking extension of
         temporary bail for a period of 30 days on the ground of attending the marriage
         ceremony of his niece.
</p><p id="p_3">         3.     Heard the learned counsel for the applicant as well as the learned APP for
         the respondent-State and perused the application which is supported by an
         affidavit.
</p><p id="p_4">         4.     Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the present case and for
         the reasons stated in the application, the same is partly allowed and the
         temporary bail period granted earlier is extended for a period of one week on
         same terms and conditions. In addition to the conditions imposed earlier, the
         applicant shall remain present before the concerned Trial Court as and when
         required and shall cooperate in the trial proceedings. During that period, the
         applicant shall not abuse the liberty granted to the applicant and shall maintain
         law and order. The applicant shall surrender before the Jail Authority
         immediately after the aforesaid period is over. Rule is made absolute
         accordingly. Direct Service is permitted.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                                                (P.P.BHATT, J.)
