                HIGH COURT OF CHHATTISGARH, BILASPUR

                           MCRCA No. 1250 of 2018

      Chandresh Thakkar, aged about 45 years, S/o- Late Purushottam
        Thakkar, R/o Vasundhara Nagar, Raipur, Chhattisgarh.

                                                                ---- Petitioner

                                     Versus

      State Of Chhattisgarh Through SHO, P.S. Kotwali, Mahasamund,
        Chhattisgarh

                                                               ---- Respondent</pre>




<p id="p_1">For Applicant                        Mr. Vivek Sharma, Advocate
For Respondent /State                Mr. PK Bhaduri, Govt. Advocate


                              Order On Board By

                 Hon'ble Mr. Justice Prashant Kumar Mishra

26/9/2018

</p><p id="p_2">1.   Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">2.   This is the second application under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Code of Criminal
     Procedure for grant of anticipatory bail to the applicant, who is
     apprehending his arrest in connection with Crime No.244/2008,
     registered at Police Station Kotwali Mahasamund for the offence under
     <a href="/doc/940928/" id="a_2">Sections 407</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_3">120B</a> of IPC and <a href="/doc/158335608/" id="a_4">Sections 3</a>, <a href="/doc/361626/" id="a_5">7</a> of the Essential Commodities
     Act.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">3.   The first bail application of the applicant was dismissed, as withdrawn,
     on 5.9.2012.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">4.   Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, this Court is not
     inclined to extend the benefit of <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_6">Section 438</a> of Cr.PC to the applicant.


</p><p id="p_9">5.   Accordingly, the bail application is dismissed.

</p><p id="p_10">                                               Sd/-
</p>
<p id="p_11">                                     (Prashant Kumar Mishra)

                                              Judge

Shyna
