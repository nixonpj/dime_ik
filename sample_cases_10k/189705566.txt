<p id="p_1">Writ Petition No. 6742 of 2018

Petitioner :             Sharad son of Maroti Mankar, aged about 51

                         years, Occ: Service, resident of Masal (Bk),

                         Tahsil Chimur, District Chandrapur

                         versus

Respondents :            1) The Vice-Chairman/Member/Secretary,

                         Scheduled Tribe Caste Certificate Scrutiny

                         Committee, Gadchiroli

</p><p id="p_2">                         2) The Superintendent of Police, Chandrapur



Ms Preeti D. Rane, Advocate for petitioner

Ms Kalyani Deshpande, AGP for respondents

                         Coram : Sunil B. Shukre &amp; S. M. Modak, JJ

                         Dated : 31st January 2019

Oral Judgment (Per Sunil B. Shukre, J)

</p><p id="p_3">1.               Heard learned counsel for the parties. Rule. Heard forthwith

by consent of parties.
</p>
<p id="p_4">2.               The ground relied upon by the petitioners for issuance of

direction to respondent no.1- Committee is that real cousin brother of

petitioner Shubham and real nephew of petitioner Sharad                      has been

granted validity         as he belonging to "Mana" scheduled tribe by                 the


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">     ::: Uploaded on - 02/02/2019                     ::: Downloaded on - 02/02/2019 22:54:14 :::</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                         3</span>




respondent no. 1-Committee on 31.3.2010.             There is no dispute about

the grant of validity to the blood relation of the petitioners on paternal

side and, therefore, going by the settled principles of law, we see no

reason as to why the petition should not be allowed.

</p><p id="p_5">3.               Writ Petition is allowed.   The impugned order is hereby

quashed and set aside. It is directed that respondent no. 1-Committee

shall issue the validity certificate to the petitioners as they belonging to

"Mana" Scheduled tribe within four weeks from the date of receipt of this

order.
</p>
<p id="p_6">4.               Rule is made absolute in the above terms. No costs.
