<p id="p_1">           Challenge in this petition is to the order dated

10.3.2011 whereby the petitioners have been summoned to

face the trial under <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_1">Sections 148</a>/<a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">341</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_3">307</a>/<a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_4">302</a> IPC.

</p><p id="p_2">           Counsel for the petitioners has argued that the

charge has been framed without passing a speaking order.

Subsequently, the challan has been presented and all the

documents have been gone into while framing the charge.

</p><p id="p_3">           No merits. Dismissed.
</p>
