<div class="doc_title">WP(C)/33242/2011 on 17 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                    W.P.(C) No.33242 of 2011




06.   17.08.2018            This Court has passed the following orders on
                   24.12.2014 and 20.01.2015.
                                    "24.12.2014 - Heard Mr.R.K.Kar, learned
                           counsel on behalf of the petitioner.
                                    At the first instance, let a copy of the writ
                           petition along with annexures be furnished to Mr.
                           Muduli, learned Addl. Standing Counsel for the
                           State-opposite party.
                                    The State-opposite party would lay
                           necessary instructions with Mr. Muduli, on the
                           averments made in the writ petition, more
                           particularly, with regard to the report of the
                           Superintending Engineer, Electrical Circle (R &amp;
                           B), Bhubaneswar (vide Annexure-6) to the writ
                           petition) addressed to the Chief Engineer in Chief
                           (Civil), Odisha, Bhubaneswar and the action, if
                           any taken on the basis thereof.
                                    List this matter immediately after the
                           ensuing Winter holidays."

                                   "20.01.2015 - Mr. R.K.Mohapatra, learned
                           Govt. Advocate for the State-opp. Party prays for
                           and is granted ten days' time to obtain
                           instruction. On being queried by us, he has
                           apprised this Court that, in the meanwhile, two
                           letters have been addressed to the Chief Engineer
                           (Buildings) R &amp; B, Odisha, Bhubaneswar seeking
                           instructions, which are not forthcoming. We
                           make it clear that in case instructions are not
                           received by the next date, this Court may have to
                           order personal presence of the Chief Engineer
                           (Buildings) R &amp; B, Odisha, Bhubaneswar.
                                   List it after ten days."


                            Today, learned counsel for the State submits
                   that he has no instruction in the matter.
                            The matter to come up on 28.08.2018 on which
                   date, the Chief Engineer (Buildings) R &amp; B, Odisha,
                   Bhubaneswar is directed to remain present before this
                   Court in person along with the necessary information.
                            2




                    Free copy of this order be handed over to the
          learned    counsel   for   the   State   for   necessary
          communication.



                                      .................................
                                        K.S.JHAVERI,
                                       CHIEF JUSTICE


                                      ..................................
                                          I.MAHANTY, J.
</pre>
