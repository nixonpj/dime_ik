<pre id="pre_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
            S.B. Criminal Misc. 4th Bail No. 8544/2018

Devilal S/o Bherulal Jat, Aged About 32 Years, Village Jevana ,
P.s. Fatehnagar, Distt. Udaipur (Presently Lodged In Distt. Jail
,udaipur)
                                                      ----Petitioner
                              Versus
State Of Rajasthan, Through PP
                                                    ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)      :   Mr. Dinesh Godara, Mr. Pappu
                           Sangwa.
For Respondent(s)      :   Mr. A.S. Rathore, PP.



            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SANDEEP MEHTA

                              Order

13/09/2018

     Heard learned counsel for the parties. Perused the material

available on record.
     The instant fourth bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.

has been preferred on behalf of the petitioner who is in custody in

connection with F.I.R. No.185/2015, registered at Police Station

Fatehnagar, District Udaipur for the offences under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_2">Section 147</a>,

<a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_3">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_4">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_5">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_6">323</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_7">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_8">325</a> and <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_9">302</a> IPC.


     After rejection of the third bail application filed on behalf of

the petitioner by order dated 05.07.2018, statement of Dr.

Mahesh, who conducted postmortem upon the body of the

deceased Toliram has been recorded by the trial court. The doctor,

in his cross examination, candidly conceded that he did not call

and examine the documents regarding treatment of the deceased
                                                                     (2 of 2)           [CRLMB-8544/2018]


                                   who admittedly passed away after more than two months of the

                                   alleged assault.


                                        In this background and considering the overall facts and

                                   circumstances as available on record, this Court is inclined to

                                   accept this fourth bail application.

                                        Accordingly, the fourth bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_10">Section 439</a>

                                   Cr.P.C. is allowed and it is directed that the petitioner Devilal

                                   arrested in connection with the F.I.R. No.185/2015, registered at

                                   Police Station Fatehnagar, District Udaipur shall be released on bail

                                   provided he furnishes a personal bond of Rs.50,000/- and two

                                   surety bonds of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned

                                   trial court with the stipulation to appear before that Court on all

                                   dates of hearing and as and when called upon to do so.




                                                                               (SANDEEP MEHTA), J.
</pre>
