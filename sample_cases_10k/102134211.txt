                                                                Date of order: 27.02.2018

<a href="/doc/139957210/" id="a_1">A.V. Gupta                     V.                  M.K.Bhandari and another</a>.
Coram:
     Hon'ble Mr Justice Badar Durrez Ahmed, Chief Justice
     Hon'ble Mr Justice Sanjeev Kumar, Judge.
Appearance:
For the Petitioner/appellant (s)    : M/s Ajay Sharma &amp; Aditya Gupta, Advocate
For the Respondent (s)              : Mr Sanjeev Padha, GA.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                     Mr D.C. Raina, Sr.A dvocate with
                                     Mr F.A. Natnoo, Advocate.
</p><p id="p_2">                                     Ms Sindu Sharma, ASGI.
</p>
<p id="p_3">1.     The learned counsel appearing on behalf of the Government of Jammu and
Kashmir has not provided the latest status with regard to the procurement and
commissioning of PET Scan machine in the Super Speciality Hospital at Jammu.
The direction was given as far back as on 20.12.2017. In response, the affidavit of
Dr.Pawan Kotwal, IAS, Principal Secretary to Government, Health and Medical
Education Department, Government of Jammu and Kashmir dated 16.02.2018 has
been filed wherein it has been indicated that on 05.02.2018 a reminder was sent to
the Government of India, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, New Delhi to
accord sanction for the project of setting up of State Cancer Institute in
Government Medical College, Jammu along with first installation fund so that the
work on the project could be started at the earliest and PET Scan machine could
be installed. It is further stated that the response from the Government of India is
awaited.
</p>
<p id="p_4">2.     Mrs. Sindu Sharma is present on behalf of the Union of India. She was
directed to ensure that the proposal which had been earlier sent by the State
Government is expedited. Unfortunately, she has not filed any status report with

  _________________________________________________________________________________________
CPPIL No. 03/2017                                                                   Page 1of 2
 regard to the same. She submits that she was hampered because of the non-supply
of the paper-book in these proceedings. That impediment has been removed as the
paper-book has been supplied. She shall ensure that the status report is filed
within one week.
</p>
<p id="p_5">3.       Mr. D.C.Raina, appearing on behalf of the Public Service Commission, on
instructions, submits that two posts, one for Oncology and one for Surgical
Oncology had been assigned to them and in respect of Surgical Oncology, no
candidate was found to have made the minimum grade specified, as a result of
which fresh process would have to be started for the same. As regards Oncology,
selection has already been recommended.
</p>
<p id="p_6">4.       Re-notify on 12.03.2018.
</p>
