                               Disputed question of fact as to whether possession had been taken

            from Atma Ram or Mani Ram, Ram Niwas or Parmeshwari Devi, is required

            to be determined in this Contempt petition. It will not be appropriate to

            enter into the said disputed question of fact in Contempt proceeding which

            is required to be determined by a Civil Court or by raising the issue before

            the appropriate authorities.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               No ground is made out to entertain the present Contempt petition.

                               Dismissed.</p><pre id="pre_2">
