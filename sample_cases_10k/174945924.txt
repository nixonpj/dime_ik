            Mr. Subir Pal.................................For the State.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                  Let affidavit in opposition to the writ petition be filed by
            11th April, 2014. Reply, if any, by 22nd April, 2014.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                  So far as the prayer for interim order is concerned, in view
            of the statements and grounds, particularly statements in
            paragraphs 20 and 21 and ground nos. II, III, IV and V, I am of
            the view that a prima facie case has been made out for granting
            interim order. Therefore, let there be an interim order in terms
            of prayer "g" to the writ petition till 6th May, 2014 or until
            further order whichever is earlier.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                  Liberty to mention upon notice.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                  Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for,
            be supplied to the parties.
</p>
