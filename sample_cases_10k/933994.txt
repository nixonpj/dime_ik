<p id="p_1">(Per
: HONOURABLE THE ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE MR. M.S.SHAH)
 

 


 

 


 

	This
appeal is directed against the judgment and order dated 9th
July, 2008, of the learned Single Judge, dismissing the appellant's
petition challenging the order of removal from the Office of
Sarpanch, under Section 57 of the Gujarat Panchayats Act, 1993, as
confirmed by the Development Commissioner in appeal.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.	The
Check Dam constructed during the period when the appellant was
holding the office of Sarpanch was washed away in the next monsoon. 
The District Development Officer, District Panchayat, Ahmedabad,
issued show cause notice, inter-alia indicating that the petitioner
had not produced vouchers for a sum of Rs. 38,000/- and odd amount. 
After considering the petitioner's reply, the District Development
Officer came to the conclusion that the appellant had not produced
any vouchers for Rs. 36,110/- out of the total amount of Rs. 89,570/-
withdrawn by the appellant for the purpose of construction of the
Check Dam.  The District Development Officer as well as the
Development Commissioner gave concurrent findings of fact against the
appellant, and the learned Single Judge has not interfered with the
same.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_3">3.	We
see no infirmity in the order of the learned Single Judge. The appeal
is therefore, summarily dismissed.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_4">4.	As
the appeal is dismissed, the civil application also stands disposed
of.				
</p>
