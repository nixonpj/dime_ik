<div class="doc_title">Somabhai Paramabhai Gavariya &amp; vs State Of Gujarat on 18 January, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                 R/CR.MA/23683/2016                                              ORDER



                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
          CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR REGULAR BAIL) NO. 23683 of 2016
         ==========================================================
                 SOMABHAI PARAMABHAI GAVARIYA &amp; 1....Applicant(s)
                                      Versus
                         STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         DELETED for the Applicant(s) No. 2
         MR JIGAR G GADHAVI, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR PRANAV TRIVEDI, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         ==========================================================
          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI

                                      Date : 18/01/2017
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">         1.           The present application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>
         of the Code of Criminal Procedure for regular bail in
         connection with an offence being C.R.No.I- 109 of 2016
         registered with Pratij Police Station, District: Sabarkantha, for
         the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/872363/" id="a_2">Sections 143</a>, <a href="/doc/1154131/" id="a_3">147</a>, <a href="/doc/791362/" id="a_4">148</a>, <a href="/doc/777535/" id="a_5">149</a>,
         <a href="/doc/1983271/" id="a_6">302</a>, <a href="/doc/770961/" id="a_7">323</a>, <a href="/doc/267625/" id="a_8">324</a>, <a href="/doc/366823/" id="a_9">337</a>, <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_10">504</a>, <a href="/doc/387648/" id="a_11">34</a> etc. of<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_12"> the Indian Penal Code</a> and
         Section 135 of Gujarat Police Act.
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2.            Mr. Jigar Gadhavi, learned advocate appearing on
         behalf of the applicant submits that considering the nature of
         offence, the applicant may be enlarged on regular bail by
         imposing suitable conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         3.           Learned Additional Public Prosecutor appearing on
         behalf of the respondent-State has opposed grant of regular
         bail looking to the nature and gravity of the offence.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         4.           Learned advocates appearing on behalf of the
         respective parties do not press for further reasoned order.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                     R/CR.MA/23683/2016                                                  ORDER




</pre><p id="p_5">         5.              I have heard learned advocates appearing on
         behalf of the respective parties and perused the papers of
         investigation and read the affidavit filed by the original
         complainant opposing present application. I have also gone
         through the order dated 09.12.2016 passed in Criminal Misc.
         Application No. 30839 of 2016 passed by Co-ordinate Bench
         of this court granting bail to the co-accused namely Sonabhai
         @    Sunilbhai          Parabhabhai          Govariya          against          whom             the
         allegation was that he had given stick blow to the deceased. I
         have also considered the fact that investigation is over and
         charge sheet is filed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">         6.              In the facts and circumstances of the case and
         considering the nature of allegations made against the
         applicant in the FIR, without discussing the evidence in detail,
         prima facie, this Court is of the opinion that this is a fit case to
         exercise the discretion and enlarge the applicant on regular
         bail. Hence, the present application is allowed and the
         applicant is ordered to be released on regular bail in
         connection with an offence being C.R.No.I-109 of 2016
         registered          with        Pratij        Police           Station,             District:
         Sabarkantha, on executing a personal bond of Rs.10,000/-
         (Rupees Ten thousand only) with one surety of the like
         amount to the satisfaction of the learned Trial Court and
         subject to the conditions that he shall;
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">              [a]        not take undue advantage of liberty or misuse
                         liberty;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">              [b]        not act in a manner injurious to the interest of the
                         prosecution;</blockquote>
<p id="p_7">              [c]        surrender passport, if any, to the lower court within

                         R/CR.MA/23683/2016                                            ORDER



                             a week;
</p><p id="p_8">                  [d]        not leave the State of Gujarat without prior
                             permission of the Sessions Judge concerned;
                  [e]        mark presence before the concerned Police Station
                             on every Monday of each English calendar month
                             for      a period of three months and thereafter
                             alternate Monday for a period of six months
                             between 11:00 a.m. And 2:00 p.m.;
</p><p id="p_9">                  [f]        furnish latest and permanent address of residence
                             to the Investigating Officer and also to the Court at
                             the time of execution of the bond and shall not
                             change the residence without prior permission of
                             this Court;
</p>
<p id="p_10">         7.                  The Authorities will release the applicant on
         regular bail only if he is not required in connection with any
         other offence for the time being. If breach of any of the above
         conditions is committed, the Sessions Judge concerned will be
         free to issue warrant or take appropriate action in the matter.
         Bail bond to be executed before the learned Lower Court
         having jurisdiction to try the case. It will be open for the
         concerned Court to delete, modify and/or relax any of the
         above conditions, in accordance with law. At the trial, learned
         Trial Court shall not be influenced by the observations of
         preliminary nature, qua the evidence at this stage, made by
         this Court while enlarging the applicant on bail. Rule is made
         absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p><p id="p_11">                             Direct service is permitted.
</p>
