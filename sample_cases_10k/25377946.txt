                                Santu Mondal @ Joyrup Mondal &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_2">                                      Vs.

</p>
<p id="p_4">

                   None appears for the petitioners when the matter is called on for

             hearing. No accommodation is prayed for.

</p><p id="p_5">                   On 10th July, 2019 and 11th July, 2019 also none appeared for

             the petitioner when the matter was called on for hearing nor any

             accommodation was prayed for and as such in order to give one more

             chance hearing was adjourned for the day.

</p><p id="p_6">                   Repeated non-appearances of the petitioner indicates that the

             petitioner is not interested to proceed with the instant revision, so, it is

             useless to drag this case unnecessarily.

</p><p id="p_7">                   Accordingly, the instant revision is dismissed for default.

                   Order of stay, if any, stands vacated.

                   Department is directed to communicate this order to the learned

             court below.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">                                                                  ( Md. Mumtaz Khan, J. )
