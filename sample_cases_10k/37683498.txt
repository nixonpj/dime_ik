<p id="p_1">CORAM:       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JASWANT SINGH

Present:     None.
</p>
<p id="p_2">JASWANT SINGH, J (ORAL)

             By filing the present second appeal, plaintiff-appellant has

prayed for setting aside the judgments &amp; decree of both the courts below

whereby the suit for specific performance of agreement to sell filed by him

has been dismissed and the findings affirmed in appeal.

</p><p id="p_3">             From the perusal of the file, it is apparent that the present case

was earlier dismissed for non-prosecution vide order dated 13.9.2011 and

thereafter restored vide order dated 21.11.2011. After 21.11.2011, on two

occasions, adjournments were sought. Even today, none has appeared for

the appellant.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             It appears that the appellant has lost interest in the present

appeal and does not want to pursue the same.

</p><p id="p_5">             Dismissed in default.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><pre id="pre_2">August 17, 2012                                     ( JASWANT SINGH )
manoj                                                    JUDGE
