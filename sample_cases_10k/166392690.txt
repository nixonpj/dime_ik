P.S.           :     Chanakaya Puri

JUDGMENT
</pre><p id="p_1">a,  Serial No. of the case              :     02403R0097112004
b.  Name of complainant                 :     Larry Carroll
c.  Date of offence                     :     30.05.2000
d.  Name of the accused                 :     (1) Mushtaq Din Mahrul Dubani
    with parentage and address                S/O Din Mahmad Savji Dubani
                                              R/o A-4/203, Vaishali Nagar
                                              S.V. Road, Jogeshwari, Mumbai

                                              (2) Kasam Hasam Samnani
                                              S/O Hasambhai Savji Samnani
                                              R/O B-33, Srishtha Complex, Sec-5
                                              Auradha Nagar Road, Thane,
                                              MUmbai

e.       Offence complained             :     420/468/471/511<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>
f.       Plea of the accused            :     Not Guilty
g.       Final Order                    :     Acquitted
h.       Date of such order             :     17.01.2008

BRIEF REASONS FOR THE DECISION


</p><p id="p_2">1.    The case was registered on written complaint dated 30.05.2000 of High
     Commission of Canada. Allegations are that both the accused had applied for
     visitor visa to the said High Commission. However the documents annexed with
     the application were found to be fake. Accused were arrested. Documents were
     seized.
</p><p id="p_3">2. After completion of investigation, challan was filed. Accused were summoned.
</p><p id="p_4">     Sec. 207 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_3">Cr.P.C</a>. was complied with. Charge was framed against the accused on
     16.11.05 for the offences u/s 420/511<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a> &amp; 468/471/34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_5">IPC</a>.      The accused



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                       Page 1 of 2</span>
                                                                FIR NO. 164/2000
                                                             P.S. Chanakaya Puri



  pleaded not guilty and claimed trial.
</p>

<p id="p_5">3. Prosecution examined PW1 S.Y. More and PW-2 Dharmabir.
</p>

<p id="p_6">4. PW-1 appeared from passport office, Mumbai and proved report EX.PW.1/B,C,D
  and E. PW-2 had accompanied the IO to the High Commission. Accused were
  arrested in his presence.
</p><p id="p_7">5. Today matter was fixed for remaining Prosecution Evidence.      IO/SI Mahesh
  Prakash was present.        Summons to complainant were received unserved with
  report that he has left India. No purpose would have been served by examining
  other witnesses as allegation of cheating could only be proved by complainant.
  Prosecution evidence was closed.
</p>

<p id="p_8">6. Statement of accused was also dispensed with.
</p>

<p id="p_9">6. IN view of the above both the accused are acquitted. Bail bonds are cancelled
  and sureties are discharged. File be consigned to record room.
</p>



<p id="p_10">                                                          (SANJAY BANSAL)
                                                   METROPOLITAN MAGISTRATE:
</p><p id="p_11">                                                                  NEW DELHI.
</p><p id="p_12">Announced in open Court on
this 17th January 2008
