GURNAM SINGH AND OTHERS
VS.
DEVINDER SINGH @ GHAINT AND OTHERS

Present : Mr. Ashwani Arora, Advocate
          for the applicants-appellants.

             Mr. Lalit Garg, Advocate
             for respondent No. 3

                        ****
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             The present application is for reviewing the judgment dated

07.03.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             After hearing learned counsel for the parties, this Court is of the

view that the income of the deceased has been wrongly taken at Rs.5000/-

per month and the income should have been taken at Rs.12000/- per month,

as he had done B.Sc (IT) from Sikkim Manipal.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             In view of the above fact, the review application is allowed and

judgment dated 07.03.2018 is reviewed to the extent that the appellants are

entitled to compensation as mentioned below:-

</p><pre id="pre_2">  Sr.                 Heads                                Calculations
  No.
(i)       Income                          Rs.12000/- per month
(ii)      40% of (i) above to be added as Rs.12000+Rs.4800=Rs.16800/- per month
          future prospects=
(iii)     1/2 of (ii) deducted as personal Rs.16800-Rs.8400=Rs.8400/- per month
          expenses of the deceased=
(iv)      Compensation after multiplier of Rs.8400X 12 X 18= Rs.18,14,400/-
          18 is applied
(v)       Compensation       under     the Rs.30,000/-
          Conventional heads
(vi)      Total Compensation awarded      Rs.18,44,400/-



17.09.2019                                               (RITU BAHRI)
G Arora                                                    JUDGE
