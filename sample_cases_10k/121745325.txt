                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   28-02-2018                      This case has been listed today under the heading

                 "To Be Mentioned" at the instance of learned counsel for the

                 petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                    It has been submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioners that petitioners were granted the privilege of

                 anticipatory bail vide order dated 05.02.2018, passed in this case,

                 however, it appears that in the said order in para - 5 in satisfaction

                 portion, in place of ACJM, Patna, CJM , Saran at Chapra has

                 inadvertently been mentioned.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                    In view of the submission, order dated 05.02.2018

                 is modified to the extent that satisfaction portion of the said order

                 be read as "to the satisfaction of ACJM, Patna" in place of CJM,
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.3915 of 2018 (3) dt.28-02-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                       Saran at Chapra.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                           Let this order be communicated to the concerned

                       court below.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                                   (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
sunil/-
