<p id="p_1">2   13-01-2015                       Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">                                     This writ application has been filed for direction

                 to the court below to consider and decide the application filed by

                 the plaintiff-petitioner under Order 39 Rule 1 and 2 on 15.10.2011.

                 From the order sheet as well as during the course of the hearing,

                 the learned counsel for the petitioner has fairly submitted that the

                 said injunction application was earlier heard by the learned court

                 below and in the fitness of things the Advocate Commissioner was

                 appointed by order of the court for local inspection. It further

                 appears from the order sheet that the Pleader Commissioner has

                 submitted his report on 18.02.2014 and the subsequent order sheet

                 discloses that the injunction petition has been taken up and heard

                 by the learned court below.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court CWJC No.11325 of 2014 (2) dt.13-01-2015</span><p id="p_3">




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2</span>




</p><p id="p_4">                                             In view of the backdrop of the aforesaid facts and

                        circumstances of the case, this Court is not persuaded to pass any

                        specific order as prayed by the petitioner. Suffice would be to

                        observe that the learned court below will dispose of the injunction

                        petition in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                             The revision application is, accordingly, disposed

                        of.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                                          (V. Nath, J)
