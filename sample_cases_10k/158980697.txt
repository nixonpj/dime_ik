                                     ORAL ORDER

               The   present   civil   application   is   filed   by   the 

         applicant­original petitioner for amendment with prayer 

         to   allow   the   applicant   to   produce   on   record   the 

         impugned order as prayed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">               Heard      learned   advocates   appearing   for   the 

         respective parties.
</p>
<p id="p_4">               In   the   facts   and   circumstances,   the   application 

         deserves   to   be   granted.   It   is   well   settled   that 

         normally   the   amendment   has   to   be   granted   in   order   to 

         enable   the   parties   to   put   complete   facts   before   the 

         Court in order to enable the Court to resolve the issue 

         in the matter.</p><pre id="pre_2">

         The applicant­original petitioner is directed to carry 

         out amendment within two days and serve amended copy to 

         other side.
</p>
