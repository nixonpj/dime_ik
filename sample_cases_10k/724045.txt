                              JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">      The writ petition is filed with the following prayer:-

</p><p id="p_2">             i)   issue a writ in the nature of mandamus or such
        other appropriate writ, order or direction commanding the
        respondents not to demolish the compound wall and conduct
        survey of the property covered by Exts.P1 to P4 documents.

Essentially the petitioner claims the benefits under the Kerala

Stay of Eviction Proceedings Act, 1999. It is made clear that any

further action in the matter shall only be after considering the

petition in the light of the provisions of the said Act. Needless

also to say that the petitioner will also be afforded an opportunity

for hearing in the process.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      The writ petition is disposed of as above.




</p><p id="p_4">                                          (KURIAN JOSEPH, JUDGE)
