                      this Court to issue a writ of mandamus directing the respondents to consider the

                      representation of the petitioner dated 20.11.2018 and reinstate the petitioner in

                      service as Conductor in the respondent Corporation will all attendant benefits.

                                    For Petitioner       : Mr. K.Nagarajan

                                                           ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                            Ms. Rajeni Ramadoss, learned standing counsel takes notice for the

                      respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

</p><p id="p_3">                            2. The writ petition has been filed seeking direction to the respondents to

                      consider the representation of the petitioner dated 20.11.18. The representation

                      of the petitioner is that once the approval was not granted for the dismissal of

                      the petitioner from service by the appropriate authority, the petitioner ought to

                      have been reinstated. In support of the said contention, the order passed by the

                      Special Deputy Commissioner of Labour in A.P. No.47/14 is relied upon, wherein

                      the approval has been rejected by the said authority for the dismissal of the

                      petitioner from service. Though the petitioner submitted a representation on

                      20.11.2018 to reinstate him, no orders have been passed on the said

                      representation. Hence, the present petition has been filed.



</p><p id="p_4">                            3. A perusal of the records reveal that the approval petition has been

                      rejected as early as on 13.4.2018. Once the approval petition is rejected the

                      necessary corollary is that the petitioner ought to have been automatically

                      reinstated. Even though the rejection order has been passed on 13.4.18, till date

                      the petitioner has not been reinstated. Inspite of the representation submitted

                      by the petitioner, reinstatement has not been made. There is no reason as to
http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                3</span>

                      why the order of reinstatement has not been passed despite the order of

                      rejection of the approval petition.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                              4. In view of the limited relief sought for, this Court is of the view that it

                      would be suffice to direct the respondents to consider the representation dated

                      18.11.2018, submitted by the petitioner, in the light of the order passed in A.P.

                      No.47/14 and the observation made by this Court to pass orders on the said

                      representation within a particular time frame.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                              5. In the above circumstances, this writ petition is disposed of directing the

                      respondents to consider the representation dated 18.11.2018, submitted by the

                      petitioner, in the light of the order passed in A.P. No.47/14 and pass orders

                      thereon in accordance with law within a period of four weeks from the date of

                      receipt of a copy of this order.</p><pre id="pre_2">
