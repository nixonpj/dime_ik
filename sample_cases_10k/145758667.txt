                                          ...for the petitioner.

<p id="p_4">


                          In view of the order proposed to be made, no previous

                 service is required to be effected on the opposite party. The

                 petitioner will, however, remain obliged to immediately forward

                 copies of the petition and this order to the opposite party.

</p><p id="p_5">                          The petitioner has filed a suit for recovery of possession

                 against a perceived trespasser. The petitioner complains of the City

                 Civil Court not taking up the suit despite the written statement not

                 having been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                          CO 1324 of 2017 is disposed of by requesting the X Bench,

                 City Civil Court at Calcutta to take up and dispose of Title Suit No.

                 1275 of 2013 and any pending application therein in their turn

                 without granting any adjournments to any of the parties.

</p><p id="p_7">                          There will be no order as to costs.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                             2</span>


         Urgent certified website copies of this order, if applied for,

be made available to the parties upon compliance with the requisite

formalities.
</p>

</p><p id="p_9">                            (Sanjib Banerjee, J.)
