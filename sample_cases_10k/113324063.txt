<pre id="pre_1">ORDER SHEET

                                 WP No. 944 of 2013

                          IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA

                         Constitutional Writ Jurisdiction

                                   ORIGINAL SIDE


   ALOKE SAHA                                      Petitioner

       Versus

   STATE OF WEST BENGAL AND ORS.                   Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">   BEFORE:
</p>
<p id="p_2">   The Hon'ble JUSTICE DEBASISH KAR GUPTA

   Date : 24th September, 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">               For Petitioner        : Mr. Sakti Pada Jana, Advocate

               For Respondents       : Mrs. Chama Mookherji, Advocate
                                       Ms. Pratima Pal, Advocate


                     The Court : This writ application is directed against an

    order passed by the respondent no. 2 under Memo. No. 242/1(5)LCIC-1092-

    LS/08 dated December 30, 2009.    By virtue of the impugned order, respondent

    no. 2 rejected the claim of the petitioner for approval of his appointment

    as a non-teaching staff (Clerk) of Saraswat Kshatriya Vidyalaya, District -

    Kolkata.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                         Let Affidavit-in-opposition be filed three weeks after

    reopening of Court after Puja vacation; Reply, if any, thereto be filed

    within one week thereafter.    Liberty is given to the parties to mention the

    matter for early hearing.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                                   (DEBASISH KAR GUPTA, J.)

    msen
