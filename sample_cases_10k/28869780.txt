     In Re:- An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">section 438</a> of the Code of
 Criminal Procedure filed on 23.12.2016 in connection with Khardah Police
 Station Case No.828/2016 dated 14/11/2016 under <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_2">Section 354</a> of the Indian
 Penal Code and 12 of POCSO Act, 2012 ;

           And
    In the matter of:-
    Ashim Dey @ Bapi @ Ashim Kumar Dey
                                                 ...
</pre><p id="p_1">                                                                          Petitioner
   Mr. Debasis Kar,
   Mr. Shyamal Krishna Deb
                                                                 ..for the Petitioner

   Mr. Dipankar Pramanick
                                                                     .. for the State
   Mr. Arup Sarkar

                                                     .. for the de facto complainant


    The 164 statement of the victim girl, warrants grant of anticipatory bail to

the petitioner herein. Accordingly, the petitioner, namely, Ashim Dey @ Bapi @

Ashim Kumar Dey, in the event of his arrest, be released on anticipatory bail

upon furnishing bond of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties of Rs.5,000/- each, to

the satisfaction of the arresting authority and subject to the conditions imposed

under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_3">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>


<p id="p_2">    In view of the aforesaid, this application is allowed and disposed of.
