<div class="doc_title">Uttim Bhagat &amp; Ors vs State Of Bihar on 23 July, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                        Cr.Misc. No.21498 of 2009
                     1.UTTIM BHAGAT S/o Late Musa Bhagat
                     2.Sudama Bhagat S/o Late Musa Bhagat
                     3.Radha Bhagat S/o Late Musa Bhagat
                     4.Ram Raj Bhagat S/o Late Musa Bhagat, all resident of
                     Village Chauraw, P.S. Kuchaikote Dist.Gopalganj.
                                  Versus
                     1.STATE OF BIHAR
                               -----------
</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   23.7.2009.                      Counsel for the petitioner is permitted to make

                  necessary correction in the prayer portion of the petition.

</p><p id="p_2">                                    Heard counsel for the petitioner, the State and the

                  counsel appearing for the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                    Offence        alleged        is     under       <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Section

                  341</a>,<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>,<a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_3">504</a>,<a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_4">354</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1507082/" id="a_6">Section 3</a> (i) (x) of

                  SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities )Act.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                    Counsel for the petitioners submits that after one

                  month of the alleged occurrence F.I.R. has been instituted making

                  false allegation. Date of occurrence is 3.2.2009 and F.I.R. was

                  instituted on 14.2.2009. It has further been stated that prior to

                  institution of the F.I.R. the informant has filed Complaint case no.242

                  of 2009 with regard to same occurrence. The allegations which are

                  there in the F.I.R. is completely missing in the complaint case. This

                  indicate that the entire prosecution case is false.

</p><p id="p_5">                                    Considering      the     aforesaid   fact,   prayer   for

                  anticipatory bail on behalf of the petitioners is allowed. The

                  petitioners namely 1. Uttim bhgat 2. Sudama Bhagat 3. Radha Bhagat

                  and 4. Ram Raj Bhagat are directed to surrender before the

                  C.J.M.Hajipur Vaishali within four weeks from today and on their so
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              2</span>




        surrendering they shall be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bond of

        Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount

        each to the satisfaction of C.J.M.Hajipur, Vaishali in connection with

        Hajipur (SC/ST) P.S.Case no.111 of 2009 subject to the condition as

        laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_7">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                 (Mridula Mishra, J)
Sss/-
</p>
