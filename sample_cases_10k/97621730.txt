<div class="doc_title">Sudeep Singh Rajput vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 7 September, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                           MCRC-14254-2017
             (SUDEEP SINGH RAJPUT Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


07-09-2017

      Shri Abhinav Dubey, learned counsel for the applicant.
      Shri C.K.Mishra, learned Govt. Advocate for the State.
      Heard. Case diary perused.
      This is the first bail application filed by the applicant/accused
under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Cr.P.C for grant of anticipatory bail who
apprehends his arrest in connection with Crime No.87/2017 registered
at Police Station-Dolariya, District Hoshangabad, under <a href="/doc/764237/" id="a_2">Sections 394</a>
of the Indian Penal Code.
       Learned counsel for the applicant submits that applicant has
been arrayed in this case on the basis of memorandum of the co-
accused while complainant in his affidavit has stated that the applicant
was not involved in the incident. The stolen property has been
recovered, therefore, the applicant's arrest for the purpose of
investigation is also not required. He further submits that there is no
criminal antecedents of the applicant and applicant is a permanent
resident of district Hoshangabad. Applicant shall abide by all the
conditions and directions which may be imposed by the Court. Hence,
he prays for grant of anticipatory bail.
       The prayer is opposed by learned counsel for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Considering the overall facts and circumstances of the case, but
without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, application
is allowed. It is directed that if applicant Sudeep Singh Rajput
surrenders before the Arresting Authority /Investigating Officer in
relation to aforementioned Crime No.87/2017, within a period of 10
days from today, she shall be released on bail on his furnishing a
 personal bond in the sum of Rs.25,000/- with one solvent surety in the
like amount to the satisfaction of the Arresting Authority/Investigating
Officer.
</p><p id="p_2">      The applicant shall make himself available for interrogation by a
Police Officer as and when required. He shall further abide by the
other conditions enumerated in sub-section (2) of <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_3">Section 438</a> of
Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_3">      C.C. as per rules.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                                                       (J. P. GUPTA)
                                                           JUDGE
