<p id="p_1">                                   This petition is filed to withdraw the case in Crime No.286

                      of 2015 on the file of the second respondent and transfer the same to

                      any other competent agency preferably to the third respondent.
</p>


<p id="p_2">                                   2.The   learned   Government        Advocate    (Crl.side)    would

                      submit that the investigation in Crime No.286 of 2015 on the file of the

                      second respondent has been completed and the respondent No.2 laid

                      the charge sheet before the concerned Magistrate on 21.08.2015 and

                      the same was taken cognizance in S.C.No.85 of 2014.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                            3.Recording    the   said   submission       made      by    the    learned

                      Government Advocate (Crl.side), since the prayer sought for in this

                      petition   has   become    infructuous,   this    petition   is    dismissed    as

                      infructuous. Consequently, connected miscellaneous petition is                 also
