<p id="p_1">25   09-01-2015                Perused the office note dated 08.01.2015.

</p><p id="p_2">                               It appears that appellant nos.4 and 5 and respondent

                  nos.5, 6, 15, 16 were shown minor on the date of institution of the

                  first appeal in the year 1983. Likewise respondent nos.12(b) and

                  12 (c) were shown minor on the date of their substitution in the

                  year 1992. In view of the fact that now because of lapse of time

                  the appellants and respondents have already attained majority, the

                  Court can take judicial notice of this fact. Therefore, the appellants

                  are not required to file any affidavit. Accordingly, the appellants

                  and the aforesaid respondents are declared as major.

</p><p id="p_3">                               So far appellant nos.4 and 5 are concerned, the

                  learned counsel Mr. Ajay Kumar Sharma submitted that he will

                  file vakalatnama within one month and for the respondents, who

                  have been declared major, the appellants shall file the necessary
