<div class="doc_title">Travancore Coir Mats &amp; Matting vs The Joint Director on 31 August, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">       

  

  

 
 
  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 27102 of 2010(K)


1. TRAVANCORE COIR MATS &amp; MATTING
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE JOINT DIRECTOR,
                       ...       Respondent

2. OFFICER-IN-CHARGE

3. OFFICER-IN-CHARGE

                For Petitioner  :SRI.A.KRISHNAN

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice ANTONY DOMINIC

 Dated :31/08/2010

 O R D E R
                      ANTONY DOMINIC, J.
                         ----------------------
                  W.P.(C) No.27102 OF 2010
                       ---------------------------
            Dated this the 31st day of August, 2010

                         J U D G M E N T
</pre><p id="p_1">                         ~~~~~~~~~~~

      Petitioner claims to be the President of the Association of

Exporters of coir products. In this Writ Petition what is sought

to be challenged is Ext.P1, a public notice issued stating that

the official activities of Export Inspection Agency- Kochi, Sub

Offices at Beach Road, Calicut and Sarada Shopping Complex

(3rd Floor) Alleppey will be stopped and the offices will be closed

w.e.f.31.8.2010.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

</p><p id="p_3">      2.    The venue of an office of the Export Inspection

Agency is a policy matter, which is to be decided by the

authorities themselves. Petitioner has no case that shifting of

office from Alleppey to Quilon as notified in Ext.P1 is against

any of the statutory provisions.        Therefore, interference in a

proceedings under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India is not

called for.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      Accordingly, the Writ Petition is dismissed.



</p><p id="p_5">                                    (ANTONY DOMINIC, JUDGE)

ps/2/09

</p>
