                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

         CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR REGULAR BAIL) NO. 26459 of 2016

                           In CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. 1438 of 2016

         ==========================================================
                NAYANKUMAR @ NARESH HUKAMBHAI OAD....Applicant(s)
                                   Versus
                       STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MR HIREN P VYAS, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR PINAKIN M RAVAL, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR.K.L.PANDYA, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         ==========================================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.G.SHAH

                                        Date : 07/10/2016


                                            ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">            1. Rule. Learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives service
              of notice of Rule on behalf of the respondent- State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">            2. By way of the present application u/s.389 of the Code of
              Criminal Procedure, 1973, the applicant herein original
              accused has prayed to suspend the sentence imposed by
              the learned 3rd Additional Sessions Judge Court, Godhara,
              in Sessions Case No. 39 of 2015 vide judgment and order
              of conviction and sentence dated 15.09.2016, by which,
              the    applicant        has     been        convicted         for     the       offence
              punishable under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_1">Section 376</a> of the Indian Penal Code
              and      sentenced         to       undergo           10       years          rigorous

                 R/CR.MA/26459/2016                                             ORDER



              imprisonment with fine of Rs.50,000/- and in default to
              undergo simple imprisonment for 1 month.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            3. Heard learned Senior Counsel Mr. Yatin Oza with learned
              advocate Mr.Pinakin Raval on behalf of the applicant and
              learned APP Mr.K.L.Pandya on behalf of respondent State
              and perused the record and proceedings of the case.
</p>

<p id="p_4">            4. Considering the overall facts and circumstances of the
              case and sentence imposed by the learned 3rd Additional
              Sessions Judge Court, Godhara, the present application
              requires consideration and the same is allowed. The
              sentence imposed by the learned 3rd Additional Sessions
              Judge Court, Godhara, in Sessions Case No. 39 of 2015
              vide judgment and order of conviction and sentence
              dated 15.09.2016 is hereby suspended. Prayers in terms
              of Paragraph No.9(A) and (B) are granted. The applicant
              shall be released on regular bail on executing fresh bond
              of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) and one
              surety of like amount to the satisfaction of the Trial Court
              and subject to the conditions that he shall;
</p>
<p id="p_5">              (a) surrender passport, if any, to the lower court within a
              week;
</p>
<p id="p_6">              (b) not leave the India without prior permission of the
              Trial Court;
</p>
<p id="p_7">              (c) furnish the present addresse of residence to the I.O.
              and also to the Court at the time of execution of the bond
              and shall not change the residence without prior
              permission of this Court;
</p>
<p id="p_8">         6. Application is disposed of. Rule is made absolute. Direct
         service is permitted, today.</p><pre id="pre_2">
