<div class="doc_title">C482 No. 1309/2012 vs Unknown on 7 December, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">C482 No. 1309/2012
Hon'ble Servesh Kumar Gupta, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">       Mr. Manish Arora, Advocate, for the
applicants.
</p><p id="p_2">       Mr. KS Rautela, AGA, for the State.
       Heard.
</p><p id="p_3">       It appears that applicant no. 1 is father
and applicants no. 2 to 4 are brothers of bride
Momina, whose marriage was solemnized on
4.3.2010 with Mashrur, who is brother of Jabir
Hasan, private opposite party no. 2. Ms.
Momina was allegedly subjected to atrocities
by her husband and other members of her in-
laws family in connection with demand of
dowry. So, an FIR was lodged on 4.2.2012
against Mashrur and other accused persons
belonging to in-laws family of Ms. Momina.
After      investigation,    police    submitted
chargesheet against the accused persons.
</p><p id="p_4">       A cross-FIR was lodged by Jabir Hasan,
brother of groom on 4.2.2012 itself at 5.15 pm
alleging an incident to have taken place at 2 pm
on 3.2.2012. Investigation into the same also
culminated into submission of chargesheet
against the present applicants.
</p><p id="p_5">       Admit.
</p><p id="p_6">       Issue notice to private opposite party no.
2 calling his counter affidavit within four
weeks.
</p><p id="p_7">       List thereafter.
</p><p id="p_8">       Having considered the facts and
circumstances of the case and on going through
the contents of the FIR, it is directed that
further proceedings of Criminal Case No.
467/<a href="/doc/846421/" id="a_1">2012, State v. Zakir &amp; Others</a>, under
<a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_2">Section 452</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_4">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_5">325</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_6">307</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_7">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_8">506</a> IPC,
pending before the Court of Additional Chief
Judicial Magistrate, Laksar, District Haridwar
shall remain stayed until further orders of this
Court.
</p><p id="p_9">                     (Servesh Kumar Gupta, J.)
                            7.12.2012
Prabodh
  </p>
