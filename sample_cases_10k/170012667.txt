IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

DATED:  04.09.2018

CORAM

THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE S.S.SUNDAR

W.P.No. 22850 of 2018


Srinivasa Charitable Trust
Rep by its Secretary
M. Rajendra Naidu							...Petitioner


						Vs.


1.	The Principal Secretary,
	Department of School Education
	Secretariat, Government of Tamil Nadu,
	Chennai - 600 009.

2.	The Chief Educational Officer,
	Director of School Education, DPI,
	College Road, Chennai - 600 006.			     ..Respondents


Prayer: Writ petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India praying for issuance of a Writ of Mandamus, directing the respondents to consider the representation of the petitioner's Trust dated 06.07.2018 in accordance with law and grant sanction of minority status to Sri Srinivasa Vidyashram (CBSE) School, Vellore.


			For Petitioner	 : M/s. R. Sankara Subbu					For Respondents 	 :  Mrs. V. Annalakshmi
						    Government Advocate


				         O R D E R		
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	The Writ Petition is filed for issuing a direction to the respondents to consider the representation of the petitioner dated 06.07.2018 and grant minority status to Sri Srinivasa Vidyashram (CBSE) School, Vellore.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2. The learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the petitioner would be satisfied, if a direction is issued to the respondents to consider the representation of the petitioner-Trust, dated 06.07.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. Having regard to the limited scope of the prayer that is now sought before this Court by the learned counsel counsel for the petitioner, this Court, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, directs the respondents to consider the representation of the petitioner-Trust dated 06.07.2018 and pass appropriate orders on merits and in accordance with law, after providing an opportunity to the petitioner, within a period of four weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4. With the above direction, the Writ Petition is disposed of. No costs.
</p>
