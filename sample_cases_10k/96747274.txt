<div class="doc_title">Md. Jafar Imam Ansari @ Jafar Imam vs The State Of Jharkhand &amp; Anr on 4 October, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">         IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI

                                A.B. A. No. 6026 of 2018
         1.Md. Jafar Imam Ansari @ Jafar Imam
         2.Hassan Imam Ansari @ Hasan Imam @ Hasan Imam Ansari
                                                  ...... Petitioners
                                 Versus
         The State of Jharkhand &amp; Anr.                  ...... Opposite Party


         CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
                                  ---------
</pre><p id="p_1">         For the Petitioners :Mr. Pran Pranay, Advocate

         For the State      :A.P.P
                          ...........
</p><p id="p_2">         02/Dated: 04/10/2018
                  Learned counsel for the petitioners has submitted that present case is

         counter-blast of Sector-12 P.S. Case No. 101 of 2016 dated 17.12.2016 for the

         offence under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">section 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_6">325</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_7">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_8">379</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_9">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1947545/" id="a_10">448</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_11">504</a> I.P.C

         in which petitioners have sustained grievous injury.

</p><p id="p_3">                  Let notice be issued upon O.P. No. 2 under registered cover with A/D as well as

         ordinary process, for which requisites etc must be filed within two weeks.

</p><p id="p_4">                  It appears that petitioner has filed Cr.M.P. No. 1034 of 2018 before this Hon'ble

         Court.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                  Office is directed to call for case diary of Sector-12 P.S. Case No. 101 of 2016

         dated 17.12.2016 and injury report, if any, from the court concerned.

</p><p id="p_6">                  List this case on 04.12.2018 along with record of Cr.M.P. No. 1034 of 2018. On

         that date both the parties are directed to remain physically present before this Court, so

         that in their presence, possibility of reconciliation may be made.

</p><p id="p_7">                  Till then no coercive step shall be taken against the petitioners in connection with

         Complaint Case No. 564 of 2017 pending in the Court of Sri B. Oraon, learned J.M, Ist

         Class, Bokaro.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><pre id="pre_2">Satyarthi/-                                                        (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
 </pre>
