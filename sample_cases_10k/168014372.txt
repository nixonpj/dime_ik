<div class="doc_title">Syed Tayyab Pasha, And 7 Others vs The State Of Telangana, And ... on 27 September, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: G Sri Devi</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                         HON'BLE JUSTICE G. SRI DEVI


                       CRIMINAL PETITION No.6169 of 2019


ORDER:
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            This Criminal Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C.

seeking to quash the proceedings in Crime No.24 of 2019 of

the SHO, WPS Police Station, D.D., Hyderabad, registered

against the petitioners/Accused for the offences punishable

under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Sections 498-A</a>, <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_3">406</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a> of IPC and <a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_5">Sections 4</a> and <a href="/doc/194071/" id="a_6">6</a>

of Dowry Prohibition Act.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">            Though learned counsel for the petitioners/Accused

filed the present petition for quashing of proceedings in Crime

No.24 of 2019, he restricts his prayer seeking a direction to

the Investigating Agency to follow the procedure prescribed

under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_7">Section 41-A</a> Cr.P.C. and the guidelines prescribed by

the Apex Court in <a href="/doc/2982624/" id="a_8">Arnesh Kumar v. State of Bihar</a> and

another1.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">            The learned Additional Public Prosecutor would submit

that the directions of the Apex Court have to be followed and

implemented.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">            In that view of the matter, this Criminal Petition is

disposed of directing the police to abide by the directions of

the Apex Court as set out in Arnesh Kumar's case (supra).

However, no coercive steps shall be taken against the

petitioners/Accused.</p><pre id="pre_2">

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">1</span>
    AIR 2014 SC 2756
                             2                        GSD, J
                                              Crl.P.No.6169 of 2019




</pre><p id="p_8">     As a sequel, miscellaneous petitions pending, if any,

shall stand closed.
</p>

                                 JUSTICE G. SRI DEVI
27.09.2019.</p>
