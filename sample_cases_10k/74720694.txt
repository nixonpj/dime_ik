           SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 17122 of 2013

================================================================
                AMRELI MUNICIPALITY....Petitioner(s)
                            Versus
           SAURASTRA SHRAMIK SANGH &amp; 1....Respondent(s)

Appearance:
MR DEEPAK P SANCHELA, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1

================================================================

        CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE PARESH UPADHYAY

                                 Date : 11/03/2014


                                  ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.    Learned advocate for the petitioner Municipality states
that, it was contended before the Industrial Tribunal that in
view of the directions of the Authorities of the Government
with regard to the set up, etc., but the same is not accepted by
the Tribunal. It is submitted that in this background, the
presence    of     those        higher    Authorities,       like     Director   of
Municipalities and the State of Gujarat in Urban Housing and
Urban Development Department, may be necessary for
effective adjudication of these petitions. He prays that these
two   Authorities          be   permitted          to   be   joined     as   party
respondents. Request is accepted.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.    In view of above, it is ordered that, the following
Authorities be joined as party respondents No.3 and 4,

</p>
