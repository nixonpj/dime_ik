<div class="doc_title">Mahaveer vs State Of Rajasthan Through P P on 21 April, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1"> HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN BENCH AT
                      JAIPUR
S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous IIIRD Bail Application No. 5242 / 2017
Mahaveer
                                                            ----Petitioner
                                 Versus
State Of Rajasthan Through P P
                                                          ----Respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)   : Mr. Rajveer Singh
For Respondent(s) : Mr. N.S. Dhakad, PP
_____________________________________________________
   HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE KANWALJIT SINGH AHLUWALIA
                                 Order
21/04/2017

            This is third bail application filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

Cr.P.C. for grant of interim bail to the petitioner in a case arising

out of FIR No.205/2016, registered at Police Station Nagar, District

Bharatpur for offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1926477/" id="a_2">Sections 489B</a> and <a href="/doc/257920/" id="a_3">489C</a>

IPC, to enable him to attend the last ceremony of his father, who

had died on 16.04.2017.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            In compliance of the order dated 19.04.2017, Mr. N.S.

Dhakad, ld. Public Prosecutor, has placed on record the report

received from Police Station Nagar, District Bharatpur.           The true

copy of the said report is taken on record.

</p><p id="p_3">            Considering that the Police has verified the factum of

the death of the father of the present petitioner, the present

application for grant of interim bail is, hereby, accepted and the

petitioner is ordered to be released on interim bail for a period of

ten-days.    The    petitioner   shall   furnish   bail   bonds    to   the
                                 (2 of 2)
                                                        [CRLMB-5242/2017]

satisfaction of the trial court. The trial court shall insist that the

sureties to be furnished by the petitioner shall be registered

sureties and shall be verified by a Revenue Officer not below the

rank of the Tehsildar.    The trial court in the bail bonds/surety

bonds shall specify the date and time when the petitioner shall

surrender before the trial court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


</p><p id="p_5">                               (KANWALJIT SINGH AHLUWALIA)J.
</p>
<p id="p_6">Heena/106
