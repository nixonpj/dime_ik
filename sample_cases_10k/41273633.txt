<div class="doc_title">M.Sheela Margret vs C.Kingston Premkumar on 22 September, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED: 22.09.2016
CORAM:
THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE K.K.SASIDHARAN
Transfer C.M.P.No.11 of 2016
And 
C.M.P.No.224 of 2016

M.Sheela  Margret						... 	Petitioner

Versus

C.Kingston Premkumar					... 	Respondent


	This Petition filed under Section 24 of C.P.C. praying to withdraw D.O.P.No. 1256 of 2015 on the file of the Additional Family Court, Coimbatore and transfer the same to any other appropriate Court having competent jurisdiction.
***
		For Petitioner	:	Mr.N.Umapathi
		For Respondent	:	Mr.S.Vinoth Kumar

O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	The respondent in D.O.P.No. 1256 of 2016 on the file of Additional Family Court, Coimbatore, is the petitioner in this transfer petition.
</p>


<p id="p_2">	2.  Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned counsel for the respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.  According to the petitioner, she is a permanent resident of Chennai and it would not be possible for her to travel to Coimbatore for the purpose of defending the proceedings pending before the Additional Family Court, Coimbatore.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4.	The learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the petitioner wanted to transfer the proceedings before the Family Court at Chennai.
</p>
<p id="p_5">	5.	The learned counsel for the respondent fairly submitted that the respondent has no objection in transferring the proceedings to a Court at Chennai.
</p>
<p id="p_6">	6.	In view of the submissions made on behalf of the parties, the proceedings in D.O.P.No. 1256 of 2015 is withdrawn from the file of Additional Family Court, Coimbatore and is transferred to the file of Family Court, Chennai, for disposal on merits.
</p>

<p id="p_7">	7.  The Transfer Civil Miscellaneous Petition is allowed as indicated above.  No costs.  Consequently, connected Miscellaneous Petition is closed.
22.09.2016
vsg

To

</p><p id="p_8">1.  The  Additional Family Court, Coimbatore.
</p>
<p id="p_9">2.  The   Family Court, Chennai.
</p><p id="p_10">K.K.SASIDHARAN, J.
</p>
<p id="p_11">vsg









Transfer C.M.P.No.11 of 2016
And 
C.M.P.No.224 of 2016










22.09.2016
