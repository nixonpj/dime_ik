<div class="doc_title">Md. Sajjad vs State Of Bihar on 9 September, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                               Cr.Misc. No.26455 of 2009
                                        MD. SAJJAD
                                           Versus
                                    STATE OF BIHAR
                                         -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">05   09.09.2009               Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              Sole petitioner has renewed prayer for grant of bail

                  in a case registered under <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_1">Section 414</a> of the Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                              Earlier he had moved this court for bail in Cr. Misc.

                  No.   47554 of 2008 which was rejected with the following

                  observations:
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                      However, the petitioner is given liberty to
                        renew prayer for bail after seven months, if the trial of
                        the case, in the mean while, is not taken up.</blockquote><p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                              In the present proceeding, this court under order

                  dated 12.08.2009, called for the status report which has been

                  sent to and received. The report is at Flag-'X'. It appears

                  therefrom that charges have already been framed but not a single

                  witness has been till date examined in this case. Petitioner is in

                  custody since 21.09.2008.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                              In the facts and circumstances of the case, petitioner

                  above named, is directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail

                  bond of Rs. 10,000/- ( ten thousand) with two sureties of the like

                  amount each to the satisfaction of the         CJM Darbhanga in

                  connection with Keoti P.S.Case no. 114 of 2008 subject to the

                  following conditions:
</p>
<p id="p_7">                              (I)             One of the bailors should be the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">               2</span>




                  own/close family member of the

                  petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_8">       (II)       Petitioner shall continue to appear in

                  person before the court below on each

                  date fixed at the trial. In case of

                  default in such appearance on two

                  consecutive dates, the trial court shall

                  have liberty to cancel the bail bond

                  and secure his arrest in accordance

                  with law.
</p>
<p id="p_9">


</p><pre id="pre_2">Sym/                    (Kishore K. Mandal, J.)
 </pre>
