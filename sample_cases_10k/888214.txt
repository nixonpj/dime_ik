<p id="p_1">(Per
: HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. S.J. MUKHOPADHAYA)
 

 
 


 

	Apart
from the question of maintainability of appeal under Clause 15 of the
Letters Patent against an order passed in a First Appeal preferred
under the Workmen's <a href="/doc/1113485/" id="a_1">Compensation Act</a>, we find that the appellant
having not deposited the amount as contemplated under the provisions
of the Workmen's <a href="/doc/1113485/" id="a_2">Compensation Act</a>, learned Single Judge was of the
view that the Court below was completely justified in imposing
penalty, as it was essentially a consequential one.  Having no
illegality or infirmity found in the order passed by the learned
Single Judge, we have no ground to take a different view.  Appeal 
and Civil Application stand dismissed.  
</p>
