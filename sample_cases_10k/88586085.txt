BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 25.04.2017  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.SUBRAMANIAN             

C.M.S.A.No.43 of 1999 

K.Rajendran                             ... Appellant/Appellant/Petitioner
                
Vs.
        
S.N.Revathi                           ... Respondent/Respondent/Respondent 

Prayer: Appeal filed under Section 100 of the Code of Civil Procedure,
against the judgment and decree dated 23.03.1999 passed in C.M.A.No.75 of 
1994 on the file of the II Additional District Judge, Madurai, in confirming
the judgment and decree dated 30.06.1994 passed in H.M.O.P.No.30 of 1992 on  
the file of the I Additional Subordinate Judge, Madurai.

!For Appellant  : Ms.D.Sathiya Sri

^For Respondent : No appearance         


:JUDGMENT   
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        This appeal is nearly 18 years old. The original proceedings were
initiated as early as in 1992. The respondent is yet to be served.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2. On an earlier occasion, on 04.04.2017, I have directed the learned
Counsel for the appellant to take steps to serve a private notice on the
respondent to her residential address.
</p><p id="p_3">        3. The learned Counsel for the appellant would submit that she is
unable to get the residential address of the respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4. No useful purpose will be served by keeping this appeal pending
forever without service. Therefore, this Civil Miscellaneous Second Appeal is
dismissed for non-prosecution. No costs.
</p>


<p id="p_5">To
</p><p id="p_6">1.The II Additional District Judge, Madurai.
</p>
<p id="p_7">2.The I Additional Subordinate Judge, Madurai.
</p>
<p id="p_8">3.The Section Officer,
   V.R.Section,
   Madurai Bench of Madras High Court,
   Madurai..
