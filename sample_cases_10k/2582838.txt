<div class="doc_title">Sandeep Kumar And Another vs State Of Punjab And Others on 19 December, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">  IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT
                  CHANDIGARH

             Criminal Misc. No. M-38266 of 2011 (O&amp;M)
             Date of decision: 19.12.2011.

Sandeep Kumar and another                       ..Petitioners

                       Versus

State of Punjab and others                     ..Respondents

CORAM:         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE NAWAB SINGH

Present:       Petitioners in person with
               Mr. Sandeep Arora, Advocate.

NAWAB SINGH, J. (ORAL)
</pre><p id="p_1">               Notice of motion.
</p><p id="p_2">               Mr.   Sudhir   Nehra,   Additional     Advocate
General, Punjab appears on behalf of State of Punjab.
</p><p id="p_3">               The newly wed couple Sandeep Kumar and
Pinky have preferred this petition alleging that the marriage
was against the wishes of the parents, they apprehend
danger to their lives and that Police protection be provided
to them.
</p><p id="p_4">               Already application (Annexure P-8) to the
above said effect has been made by the petitioners to the
Senior Superintendent of Police, Hoshiarpur.
</p><p id="p_5">               It is directed that the Senior Superintendent
of Police, Hoshiarpur shall take action on the application
(Annexure P-8) in right earnest as warranted by the facts
and circumstances.
</p><p id="p_6">               Petition is disposed of accordingly.
</p>


<pre id="pre_2">19.12.2011                             (NAWAB SINGH)
neetu                                     JUDGE
