<div class="doc_title">Ashok Ram vs The State Of Bihar on 24 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.40847 of 2016
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -66 Year- 2015 Thana -MAHILA PS District- GOPALGANJ
                 ======================================================
                 Ashok Ram Son of Ramashray Ram, Resident of village- Mohammadpur,
                 Police Station- Mohammadpur, District- Gopalganj.

                                                                                 .... ....   Petitioner.
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Lokesh Kumar Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Sri Sakir Ahmad
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR
                 SRIVASTAVA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   24-11-2016                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

                 learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 Petitioner is languishing in jail custody since 26-07-

                 2016 in a case registered under Section-376 &amp; other minor

                 sections<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 There is case and counter case between the parties

                 and both the parties are levelling allegation against each others.

                 The informant of the present case claims that the petitioner having

                 entered into her house, made attempt to commit rape on her but

                 she anyhow eluded herself from clutches of the petitioner and

                 gave one stab blow, which caused injury on the private part of the

                 petitioner whereas; the petitioner claims that on the alleged date of

                 occurrence, the present informant as well as her family members
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.40847 of 2016 (3) dt.24-11-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        caught him and husband of the informant caused injury on his

                        private part.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                           Considering the above-said facts and circumstances

                        of the case as well as submission of the parties, let the petitioner

                        named above, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                        10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each

                        in connection with Gopalganj (Mahila) P.S. Case No. 66 of 2015

                        to the satisfaction of learned Sub Divisional Judicial Magistrate,

                        Gopalganj.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                              (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
</p>
