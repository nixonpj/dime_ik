<div class="doc_title">Narayan Paswan vs The State Of Bihar on 9 April, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.11872 of 2015

                         Arising Out of PS.Case No. -351 Year- 2014 Thana -NARPATGANJ
                                                 District- ARRARIA
                     ======================================================
                     Narayan Paswan son of Misrilal Paswan R/o Village Madhura Dakshin,
                     P.S. Narpatganj, District Araria

                                                                          .... ....   Petitioner
                                                     Versus
                     The State of Bihar                             .... .... Opposite Party
                     ======================================================
                     Appearance :
                     For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Anil Prasad Singh, Adv.
                     For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. M.Dayal, APP
                     ======================================================
                     CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRABHAT KUMAR JHA
                     ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2       09-04-2015               Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner seeks bail in Narpatganj P.S. Case No.

                     351/14, registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>,

                     <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">354B</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 It is alleged that the petitioner has tried to outrage the

                     modesty of the prosecutrix. There is delay of 16 days in filing the

                     case. The petitioner is in jail since 20.01.2015.

</p><p id="p_4">                                 Considering the facts aforesaid, the petitioner above

                     named is directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bond of

                     Rs. 10,000/- (Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                     each to the satisfaction of the learned J.M. 1st Class, Araria in

                     connection with Narpatganj P.S. Case No. 351/14.


</p><p id="p_5">                                                                (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J.)
Rakhi/-
</p>
