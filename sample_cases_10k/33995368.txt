<p id="p_1">5   01-09-2015                   Learned counsel for the parties are present.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 It appears that broadly the petitioner has been paid

                 his admitted dues except for arrears of difference of salary and

                 pension on account of revised U.G.C. pay-scale with effect from

                 01.01.1996. The stand in the supplementary counter affidavit filed

                 on behalf of the University today is that due to lack of release of

                 fund from the State Government, nobody has been paid the dues

                 on the said heads.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Learned counsel for the State submits that he will

                 have to take instructions with regard to such stand of the

                 University. If the position is that funds have not been released, he
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court CWJC No.22714 of 2011 (5) dt.01-09-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                        shall also take instructions as to by what time the State

                        Government proposes to release the amount since already more

                        than nine and a half years have elapsed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                        Accordingly, as prayed for by learned counsel for the

                        State, the matter be listed after one week under the heading 'For

                        Admission'. Learned counsel for the University shall ensure that

                        the money transferred to the principal of the College for payment

                        to the petitioner is also actually paid to him within the said period.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                                      (Ahsanuddin Amanullah, J)



Anjani/-
</p>
