<p id="p_1">          The petitioner is the accused in Crime No. 894 of

2016 of Kuruppampady Police Station.                   He is involved in

offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_2">376</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_3">506(i)</a> of IPC

and <a href="/doc/1227639/" id="a_4">Section 5(1)</a> read with <a href="/doc/1832444/" id="a_5">Sections 6</a> and <a href="/doc/736248/" id="a_6">14</a> of the

Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.

</p><p id="p_2">          2.   The allegation against him is that in the

Month of February, 2015, he physically assaulted the victim

and forcefully had sexual intercourse with her. He had taken

nude photographs of the victim and threatened the victim

that it would be published in social media. Coercing the

victim on this ground, he committed rape on her.                     The

petitioner was arrested on 27.4.2016.

</p><p id="p_3">          3.   Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_4">          4.   The     learned        counsel       for    the petitioner

submitted that for the last 68 days, the petitioner is in

B.A.No. 4851 of 2016
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               -2-</span>



custody.
</p>
<p id="p_5">           5.    The learned Public Prosecutor opposed the

application. The victim girl is aged about 17 years.

</p><p id="p_6">           6.    Considering    the      entire    facts  and

circumstances and the stage of investigation, bail is granted

to the petitioner with the following strict conditions.

</p><p id="p_7">          a.    The petitioner shall be released on bail on

    his executing a bond for  1 lakh (Rupees One Lakh

    only) with two solvent sureties for the like sum to the

    satisfaction of the Special Court concerned.

</p><p id="p_8">          b.    The sureties shall produce documents to

    establish their identity and solvency.      The learned

    Special Judge shall not insist on solvency certificate,

    but other documents can also be perused to find out

    whether the sureties are solvent or not.

</p><p id="p_9">          c.    The petitioner shall appear before the

    Investigating Officer on all Mondays between 9.00

    a.m. and 10.00 a.m. until final report is filed.

B.A.No. 4851 of 2016
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                               -3-</span>



          d.    The petitioner shall not enter the limits of

    Kuruppampady Police Station for a period of six

    months except for the purpose of complying with the

    conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_10">          e.    The petitioner shall surrender his passport

    if any. If he does not have a passport, he shall file

    an affidavit before the learned Special Judge to that

    effect.
</p>
<p id="p_11">          f.    The petitioner shall not intimidate or

    attempt to influence the witnesses.

</p><p id="p_12">          g.    The petitioner shall not involve in any

    offence pending trial of this case.
</p>
<p id="p_13">     In case any of the above conditions mentioned above is

violated, the learned Special Judge having jurisdiction is free

to cancel bail without referring to this Court.

</p><p id="p_14">                                               Sd/-</p><pre id="pre_2">
