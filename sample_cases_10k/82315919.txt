<div class="doc_title">Amit Kumar @ Sanny vs The State Of Bihar on 7 August, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.49183 of 2019
                      Arising Out of PS. Case No.-376 Year-2019 Thana- JAKKANPUR District- Patna
                 ======================================================
                 AMIT KUMAR @ SANNY Son of Sri Manoj Kumar Resident of Ashok
                 Nagar, Road No. 01, Anuradha Sadan Gali, P.S.- Kankarbagh, District - Patna.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Amrit Abhijat
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Gauri Shankar Gupta
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR UPADHYAY
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   07-08-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                   counsel appearing on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                Petitioner is in custody in connection with Special

                   Case No. 5630 of 2019 arising out of Jakkanpur P.S. Case No.

                   376 of 2019 for the offence under section 30(a)/38/41 of Bihar

                   Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>, 2016

                                Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that in the

                   instant case total 106.200 liters of foreign liquor was seized. He

                   submits that the liquor was seized from the Tempo in which the

                   petitioner was sitting and not from the conscious and exclusive

                   possession of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                The petitioner is in custody since 26.6.2019.

                                Considering the fact that nothing was recovered from

                   exclusive possession of the petitioner, the Court is inclined to
              Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.49183 of 2019(2) dt.07-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                         2/2</span>




                       grant bail to the petitioner, let the petitioner, named above, be

                       enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten

                       thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                       satisfaction of learned Special Judge, Excise, Patna, in

                       connection with Special Case No. 5630 of 2019 arising out of

                       Jakkanpur P.S. Case No. 376 of 2019 subject to the condition

                       that both the bailors should be elected representative of the local

                       body.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                                             (Anil Kumar Upadhyay, J)

Ravi/-
</p>
