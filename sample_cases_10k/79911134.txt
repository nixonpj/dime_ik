<div class="doc_title">Unknown vs None Present For The Revisionist on 7 May, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">CLMA No. 4306 of 2013
      In
CLR No. 39 of 2012



Hon'ble Prafulla C. Pant, J.

<p id="p_1">      None present for the revisionist.
      Shri Dharmendra Barthwal, Advocate, present
for respondents.
</p><p id="p_2">      Learned counsel for the respondents
submitted that the condition mentioned in the
interim order dated 24.05.2012, has not been
complied with the revisionist, even after the time
was extended by this court on 02.04.2013.
</p><p id="p_3">      In the above circumstances, it is clarified that
interim order dated 24.05.2012, modified vide
order dated 05.12.2012, stands vacated and the
execution court may proceed with the execution of
the decree.
</p><p id="p_4">      List this revision for final hearing in the week
commencing 27th of May, 2013.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                                  (P.C.Pant,J.)
07-05-2013
