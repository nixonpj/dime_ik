<div class="doc_title">Upendra Kumar Poddar &amp; Anr vs State Of Bihar on 4 March, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                      Cr.Misc. No.1455 of 2009
                    1. Upendra Kumar Poddar, son of Late Dewki Poddar
                    2. Saroj Devi, wife of Upendra Kumar Poddar
                       Both are resident of Village-Bagha, Ward No. 1, P.S.-Town,
                       District-Begusarai ................................Petitioners.
                                                   Versus
                       The State of Bihar ............................Opposite Party.
                                                 -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2.   04. 03. 09.             Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                             The petitioners apprehend their arrest in connection with

                   Begusarai (Town) P.S. Case No. 334/08 for alleged offence under

                   Sections 323, 379, 380, 384, 307, 498 A and 504/34<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian

                   Penal Code</a> and 3/4 of the <a href="/doc/1763444/" id="a_2">Dowry Prohibition Act</a>.

</p><p id="p_3">                             It is submitted that all the allegations are general in nature.

                   It is further submitted that husband of the informant earlier in custody

                   later on he has been granted regular bail Petitioners are father-in-law

                   and mother-in-law of the complainant.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             Learned counsel appearing on behalf of complainant-

                   informant and filed a Vakalatnama and submits that the complainant is

                   still ready to live with matrimonial home.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that if the

                   complainant is ready to come in matrimonial home they will ready to

                   keep her with full dignity.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                             Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, it is

                   directed that in the event of arrest or surrender within a period of four

                   weeks from today, the petitioners namely, Upendra Kumar Poddar and

                   Saroj Devi shall be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of

                   Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              -2-</span>




       each to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Begusarai, in

       connection with Begusarai (Town) P.S. Case No. 334/08, subject to

       the conditions as laid down under Sub Section 2 of Section 438<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3"> of

       Code</a> of Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><pre id="pre_2">m.p.                                             (Shailesh Kumar Sinha, J.)
 </pre>
