<div class="doc_title">Preeti And Another vs State Of Haryana And Others on 3 July, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">Crl. Misc. No. M-18903 of 2012 (O&amp;M)                                 -1-

      In the High Court of Punjab and Haryana at Chandigarh


                          Crl. Misc. No. M-18903 of 2012 (O&amp;M)
                          Date of Decision: 03.7.2012.


Preeti and another                                    ......Petitioners



                                   Versus



State of Haryana and others                       .......Respondents


CORAM: HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA


Present:    Petitioners in person along with
            their counsel, Mr. S.S.Kharb, Advocate.

                          ****
SABINA, J.
</pre>

<p id="p_1">            Petitioners have filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of

the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 for issuance of directions to

respondents No. 2 and 3 to protect their life and liberty from the

hands of respondents No. 4 to 13.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioners has submitted that

the marriage of the petitioners was solemnized on 26.6.2012.

Certificate (Annexure P-3) in this regard has been issued by Shri

Shanni Mandir, Mansa Devi Road, Panchkula. Photographs of

marriage ceremony are annexed as Annexure P-4. He has further

submitted that the petitioners have moved a representation to

Superintendent of Police, Sonepat (Annexure P-5) but no action has

been taken so far.
</p>
<p id="p_3">              Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on the

merits of the case, Superintendent of Police, Sonepat-respondent
 Crl. Misc. No. M-18903 of 2012 (O&amp;M)                            -2-

No.2 is directed to look into the representation moved by the

petitioners (Annexure P-5) and if required, necessary protection be

provided to them.
</p>
<p id="p_4">            Petition stands disposed of in the above terms.




</p><p id="p_5">                                              (SABINA)
                                               JUDGE

July 03, 2012
Gurpreet
 </p>
