</pre><p id="p_2">                     Affidavit of service filed in Court today be kept on record.

                     Having heard the learned advocates for the parties and upon perusing the instant

              application, it appears that this matter cannot be disposed of finally, without calling for

              affidavits.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                      Accordingly, let affidavit in opposition be filed within a fortnight after Christmas

              vacation. Reply, if any, within a fortnight thereafter.

</p><p id="p_4">                     The matter shall appear under the heading, 'For Final Disposal', in the monthly

              combined list to be published for the month of February 2011.

</p><p id="p_5">                     Any step taken by the concerned District Primary School Council, during the

              pendency of the instant writ petition, shall not cause any prejudice to the rights of the writ

              petitioner, should he succeed finally.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                              (Biswanath Somadder, J.)
