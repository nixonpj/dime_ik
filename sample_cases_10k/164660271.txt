                                      Nilima Pramanik

<p id="p_1">                             Affidavit of service filed on behalf of the
                petitioner be kept on the record.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             This writ application is filed by the petitioner
                on the ground of in action in the part of the Managing
                Committee      of   Srirampur   Agricultural   High   School,
                District - Purba Medinipur in the matter of giving
                appointment to the petitioner in the post of Assistant
                Headmaster of the said School.
</p><p id="p_3">                             According to the petitioner the panel for
                appointment of Assistant Headmaster in the School in
                question was approved by the respondent No. 3 under
                Memo No. 1266-S dated September 13, 2011 but no step
                is taken by the Managing Committee of the said School
                to issue letter of appointment.
</p><p id="p_4">                             It is submitted by the learned Advocate
                appearing on behalf of the respondents No. 4 and 5 that
                the erstwhile Managing Committee did not take any step
                to give effect to the panel under reference.
</p><p id="p_5">                             In view of the above I find that once the
                petitioner has been selected for appointment to the post
                of Assistant Headmaster of the School in question there
                is not legal bar and/or impediment to issue letter of
                appointment in her favour by the respondent no. 4.
</p><p id="p_6">                             Therefore, I direct the respondent No. 4 to
                issue letter of appointment in favour of the petitioner in
                the   post    of    Assistant   Headmaster     of   Srirampur
                Agricultural High School, District - Purba Medinipur
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      2</span>



within a period of four weeks from the date of
communication of this order.
</p><p id="p_7">               The respondent No. 3 is also directed to act in
accordance with law after the letter of appointment is
issued in favour of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_8">               This writ application is, thus, disposed of.
               There will be however, no order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_9">      Urgent photostat certified copy of this order be supplied to the
parties, if applied for, subject to compliance with all necessary
formalities.
</p>



<p id="p_10">                                 ( Debasish Kar Gupta, J.)
