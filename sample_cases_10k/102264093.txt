<div class="doc_title">Chandgiram Bymal vs State (Education Department) Ors on 23 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                            1.


   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                 JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR

                                   O R D E R

              S.B. Civil Writ Petition No.16236/2016
                         (<a href="/doc/153565893/" id="a_1">Chandgiram Bymal
                                 v.
                    State of Rajasthan &amp; Ors</a>.)

Date of Order:23/11/2016

                           PRESENT
        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE KANWALJIT SINGH AHLUWALIA

Mr. B.B.L. Sharma, for the petitioner.
Mr. Vinay Pandey, Asst.GC, for Mr. S.K. Gupta, AAG, for
the respondents.</pre>


<p id="p_1">       Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       Issue notice to the respondents.

       On     asking       of     the       Court,       Mr.   Vinay     Pandey,

appearing for Mr. S.K. Gupta, AAG, accepts notice on

behalf of the respondents. Copy of the petition has

been served upon him.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       Mr. Vinay Pandey, Asst.GC, appearing for Mr. S.K.

Gupta, AAG, on behalf of respondents, has assured this

Court       that     the    case       of        the    petitioner     shall     be

considered in the light of the order dated 03.10.2016

passed      by     the     Co-ordinate           Bench    of   this    Court     in

Harishankar Verma Vs. The State of Rajasthan &amp; Others,

vide     S.B.       Civil       Writ    Petition          No.12450/2016.       The

learned       advocate       appearing            for    the   petitioner       has

submitted          that    the     present         writ     petition    may      be

disposed of, in CWP 15687/2016 terms of the statement

made by Mr. Vinay Pandey, Asst. GC, appearing on behalf

of Mr. S.K. Gupta, AAG, for the respondents.

</p><p id="p_4">       Ordered accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       Upon        disposal       of        main        petition,     the      stay

application, filed therewith does not survive and the

same is also disposed of.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   2.</span><p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">     As a parting note, this Court shall put a word of

caution that while deciding representation, petitioner

may be posted at the place where post is lying vacant

or    administrative    exigencies          so    demand.    However

respondents shall ensure that education of the students

is   not   disturbed   due   to        transfer   in   mid   academic

session.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                             (KANWALJIT SINGH AHLUWALIA),J.


Govind/-
</p>
