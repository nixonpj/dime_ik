<p id="p_2">                                                       CRA 298 of 2010
                                                                With
                                                           CRAN 824 of 2014
                                                                With
                                                            CRA 348 of 2010
       Re: An Application for Bail in connection with appeal under <a href="/doc/1116025/" id="a_1">Section 389(1)</a> of the Code of
           Criminal Procedure, 1973 being CRAN 824 of 2014 filed on 19th February,
           2014.
</p>
<p id="p_3">           In the matter of:
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                          Md. Nausad Khan @ Nausad Khan
                          @ Md. Nausad @ Billu.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                                         ......Appellant/Petitioner.
</blockquote><p id="p_4">              Ms. Rituparna De,
             Mr. Debojyoti Das.
</p><p id="p_5">                           ........For the Appellant/Petitioner.
             Mr. Souvik Mitter.
</p><p id="p_6">                           .......For the State.
</p><p id="p_7">                                 Re: CRAN 824 of 2014
                This is an application for bail in connection with the main Appeal being CRA 298 of 2010.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                 Heard the learned Counsel for the appellant/ petitioner and the learned Counsel for the State. In

     view of the fact that the appellant/petitioner has served almost eight years and three months in custody, let

     the appellant/petitioner have the privilege of suspension of sentence during the pendency of this appeal.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                In the circumstances, we direct that this appellant/ petitioner be released on bail to the

     satisfaction of the learned Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Kolkata in the aforementioned case.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                The bail shall be granted on furnishing a bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand) with two

     sureties of the like amount each one of whom must be local surety.
</p>

<p id="p_11">                The application being CRAN 824 of 2014 is allowed.
</p>

<p id="p_12">                Urgent photostat certified copy of this Order, if applied for, be supplied

     expeditiously after complying with all necessary legal formalities.
 ( Tapen Sen, J. )



( Tarun Kumar Gupta, J.)
