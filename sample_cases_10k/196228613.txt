<div class="doc_title">Manoj Kumar Singh vs State Of Bihar on 24 April, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                          Cr.Misc. No.8718 of 2009
                          MANOJ KUMAR SINGH, S/O SRI MUNINDRA SINGH, R/O
                          VILLAGE-BARARAKH, P.S.-SURSAND, DISTRICT-SITAMARHI
                                            ............PETITIONER.
                                   Versus
                               STATE OF BIHAR
                                              ............OPPOSITE PARTY.
                                -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2   24.04.2009               Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel

                  for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             Case is under <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_1">Sections 467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_2">468</a>, <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_3">406</a>, <a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_4">409</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_5">420</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">120B</a> of

                  the Indian Penal Code. The petitioner is said to be nominee of Mohan

                  Singh who is holding account of bank and had taken over draft which he

                  did not pay in time. Said Mohan Singh, against whom the allegation has

                  been levelled, has been granted bail by this Court by order passed on

                  8.2.2005 in Cr.Misc. No. 4619 of 2005.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             In the circumstances, considering the fact that the account

                  holder whose nominee is the petitioner, has been granted bail by this

                  Court, the application is allowed. Let petitioner, namely, Manoj Kumar

                  Singh be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (rupees

                  ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                  satisfaction of S.D.J.M. Pupri at Sitamarhi in connection with Sursand

                  P.S. Case No. 12 of 2003.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


</p><pre id="pre_2">        Arvind/                                   (J. N. Singh, J.)
 </pre>
