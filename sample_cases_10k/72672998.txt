                                                          Date of decision:25.01.2016

            Satish Kumar                     .... Petitioner

                       Versus

            State of Punjab                  .... Respondent

            CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JASWANT SINGH

            Present:            Mr. Pritam Saini, Advocate for the petitioner.

                                Mr. R.S. Randhawa, Additional Advocate General, Punjab.

                                Mr. Damandeep Singh, Advocate for the complainant.
                                          *****

            Jaswant Singh, J. (Oral)

<p id="p_1">                                Prayer is for grant of regular bail to the petitioner under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section

            439</a> Cr.P.C in case FIR No.29 dated 10.02.2015 under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1317063/" id="a_3">465</a>,

</p><p id="p_2">                                The complainant in the present case is the learned Judicial

            Magistrate First Class, Rupnagar and he is one of the contesting witnesses.

</p><p id="p_3">                                Learned State Counsel, on instructions from ASI Inderpal

            Singh, states that role of the petitioner is that he is attesting witness to the

            said forged agreement to sell.

<p id="p_4">                                After arguing for some time, learned counsel for the petitioner

            prays for permission to withdraw the present petition.

</p><p id="p_5">                                Dismissed as withdrawn.
