<div class="doc_title">An Application For Bail Under ... vs In Re:- Rashik Mandal on 4 October, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                    1

   37
04.10.2016</pre>
<p id="p_1">   sm
Allowed
                            CRM No. 8099 of 2016

              In the matter of an application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the
              Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 27.09.2016 in connection with
              Gazole Police Station Case No.507 of 2016 dated 30.08.2016
              under <a href="/doc/1595149/" id="a_2">sections 493</a>/<a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_3">376(ii)(i)</a>/<a href="/doc/140515/" id="a_4">313</a>/<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120B</a>/<a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_6">506</a> of the Indian Penal
              Code and <a href="/doc/1832444/" id="a_7">section 6</a> of the Protection of Children from Sexual
              Offences Act.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                        And

             In Re:- Rashik Mandal    ...... Petitioner.

             Mr.Sujan Chatterjee     ... for the petitioner

             Mr.Partha Pratim Das... for the State.


</p><p id="p_3">                    Heard the learned advocates appearing on behalf of the

             parties. Perused the case diary.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                    While the petitioner is a young boy aged about 21 years, the

             de facto complainant is a girl aged about 17 years +.


</p><p id="p_6">                    We have gone through her statement recorded under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_8">section

             164</a> CrPC and other materials collected during investigation.


</p><p id="p_7">                    We find that both of them had a love-affair and on promise to

             marry, the petitioner cohabited with her and then resiled from his

             promise.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">                    The petitioner is in custody for about 34 days.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    2</span>


         Now, having regard to the fact that when no case is made out

  from the side of the State that, if the petitioner is released on bail, he

  is likely to abscond or his further custodial detention is still necessary

  for the purpose of investigation, the prayer for bail stands allowed.


</p><p id="p_10">         Let the petitioner be released on bail to the satisfaction of the

  learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Malda upon furnishing a Bond of

  Rs.10,000/-, with two sureties of Rs.5,000/- each, one of whom must

  be local.
</p>
<p id="p_11">         Accordingly, this application for bail is disposed of.




(Ashim Kumar Roy, J.)                      (Malay Marut Banerjee, J.)
 </p>
