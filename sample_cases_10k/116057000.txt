<div class="doc_title">Basanti Gupta And Another vs Abhijit Banerjee on 7 July, 2017</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         1</span>

   Serial 96.
</p><p id="p_2">   July 7, 2017
      SG
                     CO 3012 of 2016
                           with
                     CAN 5898 of 2017


                  Basanti Gupta and another
                                       versus
                     Abhijit Banerjee


             Mr Bhaskar Ghose
             Mr Uttiya Ray
                                 ... for the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Mr Abhisek Banerjee
                           ... for the opposite party.
</p>

<p id="p_4">      The petition is directed against the order dated July 12, 2016 by which the
petitioning plaintiffs' application for amendment of the plaint has been rejected
on the ground that such amendment would change the nature and character of
the suit.
</p>

<p id="p_5">      The opposite party is represented and says that the allegations sought to
be incorporated are contrary to the statements made by the plaintiffs in some
other suit filed by the opposite party herein. It is not in dispute that the proviso
to Order VI Rule 17 of the Code did not come into operation in this case as the
trial had not commenced.
</p>

<p id="p_6">       As is evident from the amendment appliciation appended to the present
petition and the schedule to such amendment application, the plaintiffs were
merely trying to embellish a statement already contained in the plaint.       As to
whether such additional allegations sought to be incorporated by way of
amendment are justified or can be established, has to await the response to such
additional allegations in the additional written statement to be used by the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                             2</span>


defendant; the material sought to be incorporated by way of amendment cannot
be perceived to change the nature or character of the suit.
</p>

<p id="p_7">      Accordingly, CO 3012 of 2016 is allowed by setting aside the order
impugned dated July 12, 2016 and by allowing the amendment application. The
amendment should be carried within four weeks of the presentation of this order
before the trial court. The defendant will be entitled to file an additional written
statement within six weeks of the receipt of a copy of the amended plaint. The
trial court should thereafter take expeditious steps to dispose of the suit in
accordance with law.
</p>

<p id="p_8">      CAN 5898 of 2017 is disposed of in view of the order above.
</p>

<p id="p_9">      There will be no order as to costs.
</p>



<p id="p_10">                             (Sanjib Banerjee, J.)
