For Respondent(s)       :    Mr. D.K. Bhardwaj
                             Mr. Rishi Raj Singh, PP



         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE G R MOOLCHANDANI

                                Order

12/09/2018

           Investigating officer Shri Sangram Singh is present and

has submitted that he has issued notice to Smt. Chhaju,

Moolchand, Gopal and Sitaram but they have not turned up and

are not co-operating, their co-operation is needed for completion

of the investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_6">           Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that all the

petitioners are prepared to extend their sincere co-operation to
                                                           (2 of 2)                       [CRLMP-2834/2012]



                                   the investigating agency but they are apprehending arrest, so

                                   orders be passed that no coercive action will be taken against the

                                   petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                                It has been pointed out that arrest of the petitioners in

                                   item no.37, S.B. Criminal Misc. Petition No.2815/2012 titling Gopal

                                   Meena and Ors, has been stayed on 19.11.2012 and this fact is

                                   not disputed by the rival side.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                                Perusal of record reveals that in S.B. Criminal Misc.

                                   Petition No.2815/2012, Gopal Meena and Ors versus State, which

                                   is a connected matter, this Court on 19.11.2012 has ordered that

                                   the petitioner shall not be arrested in FIR No.475/2012 and rest of

                                   the matters pertain to FIR Nos.474/2012 and 301/2012 Police

                                   Station J.D.A.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                                Learned counsel for the petitioner is directed to instruct

                                   his clients to extend full co-operation to the investigating agency

                                   for completion of the investigation, meanwhile, the petitioners

                                   shall not be arrested in connection with the FIR No.474/2012

                                   Police Station J.D.A. and FIR No.301/2012, Police Station J.D.A.,

                                   investigation of which has subsequently been transferred to

                                   Shivdaspura.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                                                List on 11.10.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                                                                               (G R MOOLCHANDANI),J
