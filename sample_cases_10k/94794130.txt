CORAM

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.M. SUNDRESH

W.P.No.19200 of 2015 and
M.P.Nos.1 and 2 of 2015

 M/s. Sri Vayalur Murugan                    
 Transport  Rep. by its Partner  K.Sathaiah  
 Having Office at No.4  Driver Colony  E.H.
 Road  Korrukupet  Chennai-21.				 ... Petitioner

Vs -


1    The General Manager                           
     (OPS) TNSO  O/o. Indian Oil Corporation 
     Limited  Indian Oil Bhawan  139  Mahathma 
     Gandhi Road  Nungambakkam  Chennai-34.

2    The Chief Terminal Manager 
     Tondairpet Terminal  Indian Oil Corporation 
     Limited  Ennore High Road  Chennai-81.		...  Respondents

Prayer : Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India praying to issue Writ of certiorari calling for the records of the Second Respondent herein pertaining to the impugned order of termination of contract and blacklisting of all the Tank Trucks of Petitioner Firm passed against the Petitioner firm and the Partners in and by order bearing Ref.No. TNP/OPS/BL/167 dated 06.06.2015 and to quash the said order.
	For Petitioner          	: Mr.AR.L.Sundaresan, senior counsel
	For R1 to R3		: Mr.M.Ravindran Senior Counsel
					  for Mr.V.Anantha Natarajan



ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">	By consent, the writ petition itself is taken up for disposal.
</p>	
<p id="p_2">	2. The petitioner has come forward to file this writ petition to quash the order bearing Ref.No. TNP/OPS/BL/167 dated 06.06.2015  passed by the Second Respondent herein pertaining to the impugned order of termination of contract and blacklisting of all the Tank Trucks of Petitioner Firm against the Petitioner firm and the Partners.
</p>	
<p id="p_3">	3. The learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioner submitted that in view of the admitted position, the period of contract itself is over.
</p>	
<p id="p_4">	4. In view of the submission made by the learned senior counsel for the petitioner, no further order is required in this petition. 
</p>
<p id="p_5">	5. Writ petition stands closed. Consequently, connected miscellaneous petitions are also closed. No costs.</p><pre id="pre_2">
