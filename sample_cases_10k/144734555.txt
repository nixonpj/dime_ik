<div class="doc_title">Abdul Shahanshah @ Shamshad &amp; Anr vs The State Of Bihar on 14 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.25164 of 2017
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -32 Year- 2017 Thana -AHIAPUR District- MUZAFFARPUR
                 ======================================================
                 1. Abdul Shahanshah @ Shamshad.
                 2. Md. Naushad.
                    Both sons of Md. Zaki @ Zaki Ahmad, Resident of Village- Benibad,
                    Police Station- Gaighat (Benibad O.P.), District- Muzaffarpur.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar.

                                                        .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr.
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAJENDRA KUMAR
                 MISHRA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                             ----------
</p>
<p id="p_2">4   14-07-2017                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the

                 learned A.P.P. for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                The petitioners seek bail in connection with Ahiyapur

                 P.S. Case No.32 of 2017 registered under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_1">Section 392</a> of the

                 Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioners

                 submits that the petitioners are not named in the F.I.R. In course

                 of investigation, one Satyam Kumar alias Ranu was apprehended

                 by the police in connection with Ahiyapur P.S. Case No.66 of

                 2017 in which he disclosed the name of the petitioners and others

                 as his associates indulged in the present crime. Thereafter, the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.25164 of 2017 (4) dt.14-07-2017</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                2/2</span>




                  petitioners were remanded in the present case on 20.02.2017 and,

                  thereafter, they were further remanded in three other cases, as

                  detailed in paragraph-3 to this application.

</p><p id="p_5">                                 Having regard to the facts and the circumstances of the

                  case, the petitioners, above named, are directed to be released on

                  bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-(Rupees Ten Thousand)

                  each with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction

                  of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Muzaffarpur, in connection with

                  Ahiyapur P.S. Case No.32 of 2017. Out of the two sureties, one

                  must be close relative of the petitioners, who will file an affidavit

                  giving genealogy as to how he/she is related with the petitioners.

                  The bailors will undertake to furnish information to the court

                  about any change in the address of the petitioners.


</p><p id="p_6">                                                          (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J)
    P.S./-
</p>
