IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED :12.01.2016
CORAM:
THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE R.SUBBIAH
Crl.O.P.No.783 of 2016

Kannan			 		..Petitioner

Vs.,

The State rep by
Station House Officer,
Virudhachalam Police Station,
Cuddalore District.
(crime No.428/2005)				..Respondent 

Prayer: Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Criminal Procedure Code praying to direct the court below to consider the petitioner's recall warrant petition on the same day in connection with C.C.No.103/2007 on the file of the Additional District Judge-II, Cuddalore@ Vridhachalam. 
 
	For Petitioner   	: Mr.R.Sethuvarayar
	For Respondent	: Mr.C.Emalias, APP 

* * * * *
ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	This Criminal Original Petition has been filed seeking a direction to the Court below to consider the petitioner's recall warrant petition on the same day in connection with S.C.No.103/2007 on the file of the Additional District Judge-II, Cuddalore @ Vridhachalam. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2.It is stated in the petition that the petitioner is  the fifth accused in S.C.No.103 of 2007 on the file of the learned Additional District and Sessions Judge-II, Cuddalore at Vridhachalam.  During the trial, the case in C.C.No.103 of 2007 was split up against this petitioner and the same was taken on file as S.C.No.243 of 2015 and the same is still pending. During the trial,  the petitioner was absconded and the learned Additional District and Sessions Judge-II, Cuddalore at Virdhachalam issued Non Bailable Warrant against this petitioner/A5 on 12.09.2013. However, the main Accused 1, 3 to 4 in S.C.No.103 of 2007 were acquitted by the trial Court on 21.12.2015. Hence, the benefit of acquittal in the above case may be given to this petitioner/A5. Therefore, the petitioner has filed the present criminal original petitioner to  re-call the warrant issued by the trial court dated 12.09.2013 against this petitioner. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.The learned Additional Public Prosecutor submitted that the petitioner is absconding for a long period and hence, this petition may not be  entertained at this stage. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4.I find force in the submission made by the learned Additional Public Prosecutor.  Since the petitioner is an absconding accused for a long period in S.C.No.243 of 2015 on the file of the learned Additional District and Sessions Judge-II, Cuddalore at Virdhachalam,  this court is not inclined to consider the prayer made by the petitioner at this stage.  Hence, this criminal original petition is dismissed. 
</p><p id="p_5">12.01.2016 
ari


To,

</p><p id="p_6">1.The Station House Officer,
   Virudhachalam Police Station,
   Cuddalore District.
</p>
<p id="p_7">2.The Public Prosecutor,
   High Court, Madras. 
</p>






















<p id="p_8">R.SUBBIAH, J.
</p><p id="p_9">ari
