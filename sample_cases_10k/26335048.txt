<div class="doc_title">Prabhat Kumar Mishra vs State Of Bihar &amp; Anr on 15 September, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.43847 of 2015
                 Arising Out of PS.Case No. -840 Year- 2014 Thana -MADHEPURA COMPALINT CASE District-
                                                         MADHEPURA
                 ======================================================
                 Prabhat Kumar Mishra
                                                                   .... .... Petitioner/s
                                              Versus
                 State of Bihar &amp; Anr
                                                              .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Sanjay Sinha, Adv.
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Smt. Sucheta Yadav(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   15-09-2015               Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                             The petitioner being the husband of the complainant is

                 apprehending arrest in a complaint case in which process has been

                 directed to be issued after cognizance being taken for the offences

                 punishable under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Section 498A</a> of the Indian Penal Code and

                 <a href="/doc/751411/" id="a_2">Section 3</a>/<a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_3">4</a> of the Dowry Prohibition Act.

</p><p id="p_3">                             The accusation is of torture for non-fulfilment of the

                 dowry demand.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             As jointly prayed for, let the matter be referred to the

                 Mediation Centre of Bihar State Legal Services Authority.

</p><p id="p_5">                             At the first instance both sides shall appear before the

                 mediation centre on 6th of October, 2015 along with a copy of this

                 order and other relevant documents, thereafter, the mediation

                 centre will fix further dates.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.43847 of 2015 (2) dt.15-09-2015</span><p id="p_6">

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_7">                                   Put up this matter on 7th of December, 2015 at 4.15 PM

                     in Chambers when it is expected that the petitioner and informant

                     shall appear in person.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                    In the meantime, let no coercive steps be taken against

                     the petitioner in connection with Complaint Case No. 840C of

                     2014 pending in the court of learned SDJM, Madhepura.



</p><p id="p_9">                                                (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Amrendra/-
</p>
