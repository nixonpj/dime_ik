<div class="doc_title">Krishna Devi vs The State Of Bihar on 7 December, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">       Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.42325 of 2012 (2) dt.07-12-2012




                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.42325 of 2012
                    ======================================================
                    Krishna Devi
                                                                       .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                    The State Of Bihar
                                                                  .... .... Opposite Party/s
                    ======================================================
                    CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                    SINGH
                    ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">02/   07-12-2012                          Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and

                          the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                          The petitioner is apprehending her arrest in a

                          case registered for the offences punishable under

                          <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_5">324</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_6">307</a> and <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_7">379</a> of the

                          Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                          The accusation is of making assault. All the

                          injuries have been found to be simple except a hair line

                          crack on the skull of Sanjeev who alleged to have been

                          assaulted by Kamlesh, who is not the petitioner before

                          this Court. Moreover, there is a counter version of the

                          occurrence also and the petitioner is a lady.

</p><p id="p_4">                                          Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above

                          named petitioner be released on anticipatory bail in the

                          event of her arrest or surrender before the learned court

                          below within a period of twelve weeks from today, on
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.42325 of 2012 (2) dt.07-12-2012</span>




                   furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with

                   two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of

                   learned SDJM, East Muzaffarpur in connection with

                   Gaighat P.S. Case No. 259 of 2011, subject to the

                   conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_8">Section 438(2)</a> of the

                   Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                  (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J.)
DKS/
 </p>
