<div class="doc_title">Jagat Kishore Singh &amp; Ors. vs The State Of Bihar on 27 January, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                               Cr.Misc. No.44716 of 2010
                                       LALAN SINGH
                                            Versus
                                  THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                              with
                               Cr.Misc. No.2050 of 2011
                            JAGAT KISHORE SINGH &amp; ORS.
                                            Versus
                                  THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                          -----------
</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   27.01.2011            Heard learned counsel for the petitioners as well as

                 learned counsel for the state assisted by learned counsel for

                 the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                          Both the above-said Cr. Misc. Cases arise out of the

                 same case i.e. Muffasil P.S. Case No. 71 of 2010 registered

                 under sections- 147, 148, 149, 452, 323, 379 &amp; 307<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the

                 Indian Penal Code</a>     as also ection-27 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_2">Arms Act</a> and

                 accordingly, both the above-said Cr. Misc. Cases are being

                 disposed of by this common order.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                          Admittedly, 13 persons have been named in the F.I.R.

                 with specific allegation against each of them to this effect that

                 they made indiscriminate firing causing fire-arm injury to

                 different persons.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                          It is evident from perusal of Annexure-4 of this Cr.

                 Misc. Case that having similar allegation, 4 co-accused persons

                 have already been granted privilege of bail by another bench of

                 this court vide order dated 23.08.2010 passed in Cr. Misc. No.

                 31119 of 2010.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              -2-</span><p id="p_5">




                 Allegedly, the petitioner in Cr. Misc. No. 44716 of 2010

        made firing causing fire-arm injury to one, Shashi Ranjan.

        Annexure-2 to this Cr. Misc. Case reveals that the aforesaid

        Shashi Ranjan has received simple injury caused by fire-arm.

        Similarly, petitioners in Cr. Misc. No. 2010 of 2011 made

        indiscriminate firing causing fire-arm injury to Laddu Devi,

        Naulesh, Wali Paswan and Rabo Devi. Admittedly, injured,

        Laddu Devi, Naulesh and Wali Paswan have sustained simple

        injury. So far as the injured, Rabo Devi is concerned, the nature

        of her injury is not clear in her injury report but admittedly, she

        has received fire-arm injury on her little finger which is not vital

        part of the body. So far as petitioner Nos. 5 to 6 of Cr. Misc. No.

        2050 of 2011; are concerned, there is no allegation of firing

        against them.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                 Considering the facts and circumstances as well as

        submissions of the parties, the petitioners, namely, Lalan Singh

        (in Cr. Misc. No. 44716 of 2010), Jagat Kishore Singh, Upendra

        Singh, Vijay Singh, Ashok Singh, Uchit Singh and Sintu Singh

        (in Cr. Misc. No. 2050 of 2011) are directed to be released on

        bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs 10,000/- (ten thousand) each

        with two sureties of the like amount each in connection with

        Muffasil P.S. Case No. 71 of 2010 to the satisfaction of Chief

        Judicial Magistrate, Nawada.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><pre id="pre_2">AKV/-                                (Hemant Kumar Srivastava,J.)
