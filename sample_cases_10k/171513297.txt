<div class="doc_title">Bablu @ Haalim Khan @ Kharaha vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 2 September, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                          CRR-2043-2015
     (BABLU @ HAALIM KHAN @ KHARAHA Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


02-09-2015
Shri Raj Roop Patel, Advocate for the applicant.
Ms. Hemlata Kshatriya, Panel Lawyer for the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Heard on admission.
</p><p id="p_2">The revision seems to be arguable, hence admitted for final
hearing.
</p><p id="p_3">As learned Panel Lawyer accepts notice on behalf of
respondent/State, hence, no notice is required to be issued.
Heard on I.A.No.16718/2015, an application filed by the
applicant for suspension of sentence and grant of bail.
The applicant has been convicted under <a href="/doc/986100/" id="a_1">Section 401</a> of IPC and
sentenced to suffer rigorous imprisonment for one year with fine of
Rs.500/-, with default stipulation.
</p><p id="p_4">Learned counsel for the applicant submits that there are
contradiction, omissions and improvements in the statements of
prosecution witnesses. It is further submitted by learned counsel
for the applicant that the applicant was on bail during trial as well
as during appellate period and he had never misused the liberty
granted to him. There is no likelihood of coming up of this revision
for final hearing in near future, hence, the sentence of the
applicant be suspended and he be released on bail.
Learned Panel Lawyer for the State opposed the prayer for bail.
On due consideration of the facts and circumstances of the case
and the nature of allegation, but without expressing any opinion
on the merits of the case, the application is allowed. Execution of
jail sentence of applicant Bablu @ Haalim Khan @ Kharaha is
suspended till pendency of this revision. He is directed to be
enlarged on bail on furnishing a personal bond in the sum of
Rs.20,000/-(Rupees Twenty Thousand Only) and a surety
 bond in the like amount to the satisfaction of trial Court for his
appearance before Registry of this Court firstly on 16/5/2016 and
on such other dates, which will not be less than the period of eight
months, as may be in this regard.
</p><p id="p_5">It is hereby clarified that sentence of fine amount is not
suspended.
</p><p id="p_6">Records of the Courts below be called for.
List the case for final hearing in due course.
C.C as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                               (SUBHASH KAKADE)
                                                     JUDGE
