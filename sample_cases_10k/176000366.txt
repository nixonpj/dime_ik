<p id="p_1">             The matter was settled between the parties vide order of this

Company Limited has brought a crossed-cheque of HDFC Bank bearing

of appellant No.2-Rajdev Singh which has been handed over to Mr. Raj

Kumar, Clerk to Mr. Gurinderjit Singh, Advocate.
