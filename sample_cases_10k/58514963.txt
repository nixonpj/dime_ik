<pre id="pre_1">       R/CR.MA/9061/2018                                          ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

            R/CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 9061 of 2018

==========================================================
                     DINESHBHAI BADHABHAI MAHIDA
                                Versus
                          STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR GAURAV CHUDASAMA(5660) for the PETITIONERS
Ms MONALI BHATT, APP (2) for the RESPONDENT
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.H.VORA

                              Date : 11/05/2018

                                  ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.    Heard     Mr.        Chudasama,       learned      advocate,    for   the
applicant and learned APP, for the respondent-State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.    This application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of
Criminal Procedure for regular bail in connection with F.I.R.
registered at C.R. No. I - 22/2018 with Umrala Police Station
for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_2">Sections 143</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_3">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_4">148</a>,
<a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_5">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_6">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_7">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_8">326</a>, <a href="/doc/1402213/" id="a_9">337</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_10">504</a> of the IPC and u/s 135 of the
G.P. Act.
</p>

<p id="p_3">2.1   The     gist   of     the   case     is     that   on   17.4.2018,    the
complainant heard some shouts near the house of his cousin
brother Punjahai and when the complainant rushed there, he
saw that the applicants were assaulting his cousin brother and
his family.      When the complainant intervened,                     he also
received injuries. Hence, present FIR.
</p>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    Page 1 of 3</span>
<p id="p_4">           R/CR.MA/9061/2018                                        ORDER



</p><p id="p_5">3.      Considering the police papers supplied by learned APP
during the course of hearing, it appears that six injured
persons now are discharged from the Hospital.                       There is a
cross     complaint           and   therefore,      there   is   allegation   of
aggression and counter aggression.                    The offences alleged
against the applicants are triable by Magisterial Court and
considering the pendency before the concerned Magisterial
Court, there is no likelihood of commencement and conclusion
of trial within near future and therefore, refusal of bail will
amount to pre-trial conviction, which is prohibited by law.
Therefore,        present       Criminal      Misc.   Application     deserves
consideration.
</p>

<p id="p_6">4.      Hence, the application is allowed and the applicants are
ordered to be released on bail in connection with C.R. No. I -
22/2018 with Umrala Police Station on executing a bond of
Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand only) each with one
surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial Court
and subject to the conditions that they shall;
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">        [a]    not take undue advantage of liberty or misuse
               liberty;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">        [b]    not act in a manner injurious to the interest of the
               prosecution;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">        [c]    not leave the territory of India without prior
               permission of the Sessions Judge concerned;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">        [d]    appear before the Investigation Officer concerned,
               as and when required for investigation purpose and
               attend the Court concerned regularly.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_5">        [e]    furnish the present address of residence along with
               the proof to the I.O. concerned and also to the
               Court at the time of execution of the bond and shall


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                      Page 2 of 3</span>
          R/CR.MA/9061/2018                          ORDER



              not change the residence without prior permission
              of Sessions Court concerned;
</blockquote>
<p id="p_7">5.      The competent authority will release the applicants only
if they are not required in connection with any other offence
for the time being. If breach of any of the above conditions is
committed, the Sessions Judge concerned will be free to take
appropriate action in the matter. Bail bond to be executed
before the lower court having jurisdiction to try the case. It
will be open for the concerned Court to delete, modify and/or
relax any of the above conditions in accordance with law. At
the trial, the trial court shall not be influenced by the
observations of preliminary nature, qua the evidence at this
stage, made by this Court while enlarging the applicants on
bail.
</p>

<p id="p_8">6.      Rule made absolute to the aforesaid extent. Direct
service is permitted.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                                   (S.H.VORA, J)
SHEKHAR P. BARVE
