<div class="doc_title">Ganga Mahto vs State Of Bihar on 5 May, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             Cr. Misc. No.7689 of 2009
           Ganga Mahto, son of late Karoo Mahton, resident of Hassanpur, Chaien Tola, P.S.-
              Kadamkuan, District- Patna.                         . . . . Petitioner.
                                        Versus
                                  STATE OF BIHAR
                                       -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">2/    05.05.2009             Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Petitioner seeks bail in a case which has been registered for

                    the offences under Sections461 and 379<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>.

</p><p id="p_3">                               The First Information Report has been registered against

                    unknown regarding theft of one T.V., Stabilizer and some other

                    articles from the office of the Principal of a College.

</p><p id="p_4">                               Name of the petitioner has come on the basis of

                    confessional statement of one Paddu Rajak, who told that he had sold

                    the stolen articles to the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               Submission is that the recovery was made from the house of

                    the petitioner but at the time of recovery he was not present. Petitioner

                    had no knowledge that the articles were stolen one.

</p><p id="p_6">                               Considering the facts and circumstances of the case,

                    petitioner, named above, is directed to be released on bail on

                    furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties

                    of the like amount each to the satisfaction of Sri P.K. Singh, Judicial

                    Magistrate, 1st Class, Patna / concerned court in connection with

                    Sultanganj P.S. case no. 101 of 2004.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                                                        (Shyam Kishore Sharma, J.)
     Uday/
 </p>
