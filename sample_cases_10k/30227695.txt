<p id="p_1">            SANJAY KISHAN KAUL, C.J. (ORAL)

                               Learned counsels for the parties agree that the matter in issue
            is covered by the decision of this Court in ARB-129-2013 and
            connected matters decided on 11.10.2013 and pray for a reference of
            disputes in terms of the operative portion of that order which reads as
            under:-
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                      "In view of the aforesaid facts and
                                      circumstances, Justice R.K. Nehru, a
                                      retired Judge of this Court, is appointed as
                                      sole arbitrator to enter upon the references
                                      and adjudicate the disputes inter-se the
                                      parties. The fee of the arbitrator will be
                                      governed by the High Court Rules."</blockquote>

<p id="p_2">                                 The present petition is also, thus, allowed in the aforesaid
            terms leaving the parties to bear their own costs.
</p><p id="p_3">                                  A copy of this order be sent to the arbitrator.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                                               (SANJAY KISHAN KAUL)
                                                                   CHIEF JUSTICE
