                     Arising Out of PS. Case No. -212 Year- 2014 Thana -KHARAGPUR District- MUNGER
                 ======================================================
                 Amar Khirari @ Amar Khirhari, Son of Ganesh Khirari, Resident of Village
                 - Bhaiya Ram Tola, P.S. - Kharagpur, District - Munger.
                                                                          .... .... Petitioner
                                                  Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                     .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s       :   Mr. Pranav Kumar, Advocate

<p id="p_1">2   03-03-2015                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner is an accused in a case under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections

                 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_6">384</a>, <a href="/doc/1183069/" id="a_7">386</a>, <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_8">427</a>, <a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_9">452</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_10">379</a>, <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_11">380</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_12">325</a>, <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_13">354</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_14">504</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_15">506</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that there

                 is a case and counter case and the petitioner too have sustained

                 injuries. He further submits that the injury sustained by the

                 prosecution side is not serious in nature. He submits that two of

                 the co-accused, namely, Bipin Khirhari and Guddu Kumar, have

                 already been granted anticipatory bail. The petitioner is in custody

                 since 30.09.2014.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 In the facts and circumstances of the case, the

                 petitioner is directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bond

                 of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                  each to the satisfaction of Sub-Divisional Judicial Magistrate,

                  Sadar, Munger in connection with Kharagpur P.S. case no. 212 of

                  2014.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                          (Samarendra Pratap Singh, J.)
