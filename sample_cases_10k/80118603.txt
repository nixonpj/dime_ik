<div class="doc_title">Bhairo Rai &amp; Anr vs The State Of Bihar on 2 March, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.8738 of 2015
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -324 Year- 2014 Thana -BUXAR MUFFSIL District- BUXAR
                 ======================================================
                 1. Bhairo Rai Son of Late Indradeo Rai resident of village - Gamharia, P.S.
                 Buxar (M), District - Buxar.
                 2. Rabindra Rai S/o Late Indradeo Rai resident of village - Gamharia, P.S.
                 Buxar (M), District - Buxar.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar.

                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Jitendra Pandey, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Subhash Chandra Mishra(APP)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   02-03-2015                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the

                 State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioners are apprehending their arrest in a

                 case registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_1">Sections 406</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">354B</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and 3/4 of the

                 Prevention of Witch Practices Act, 1999.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 It is alleged that co-accused Binod Ram (non-

                 petitioner) and Dinanath Rai (non-petitioner) realized gold and

                 cash for curing the daughter of the informant and the

                 petitioners alleged to have conspired.

</p><p id="p_4">                           It is submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioners

                 that the trust of accusation is against co-accused Binod Ram

                 and Dinanath Rai. Statement has been made in para-3 of the

                 petition that petitioners have no criminal antecedent.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.8738 of 2015 (2) dt.02-03-2015                2</span>




</p><p id="p_5">                                       Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above

                      named petitioners be released on anticipatory bail in the event

                      of their arrest or surrender before the learned court below

                      within a period of twelve weeks from today, on furnishing bail

                      bonds of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten Thousand) each with two sureties of

                      the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned Chief

                      Judicial Magistrate, Buxar in connection with Buxar (M) P.S.

                      Case No. 324 of 2014, subject to conditions as laid down under

                      <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_6">Section 438(2)</a> of the Indian Penal Code.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                 (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
P.K./-
</p>
