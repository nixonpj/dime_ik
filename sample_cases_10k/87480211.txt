<div class="doc_title">Bishwanath Ram vs The State Of Jharkhand on 6 January, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">          IN       THE      HIGH   COURT         OF    JHARKHAND      AT RANCHI
                                 A. B. A. No. 591 of 2014
          Bishwanath Ram                                ...... ...  Petitioner
                                      Versus
          The State of Jharkhand                           ..... ...  Opposite Party
                                   --------
                 CORAM       :     HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H. C. MISHRA
                                   ------
          For the Petitioner       :        Mr. Gautam Kumar, Advocate
          For the State            :        A.P.P.
                                       ------

6/ 06.01.2015</pre><p id="p_1">            Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel for the
          State.
</p><p id="p_2">                         The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in connection with Borio
          P.S. Case No. 216 of 2012 corresponding to G.R. No. 472 of 2012, for the
          alleged offence under <a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_1">Section 409</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_3">                         The petitioner is a forest official and there is allegation against him
          and other co-accused persons of defalcation of Rs. 46,58,162/-.
</p><p id="p_4">                         Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the petitioner
          has been falsely implicated in this case and the FIR has been lodged after some
          delay.
</p><p id="p_5">                         In the facts of this case, I am not inclined to grant anticipatory bail
          to petitioner, Bishwanath Ram. Accordingly, his prayer for anticipatory bail is
          rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                                           ( H. C. Mishra, J.)
  R.Kr.
</p>
