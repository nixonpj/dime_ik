<p id="p_1">                                The claim in the present writ petition is for grant of higher pay

           scale to the petitioner from the date of acquiring higher qualification, i.e.,

           3.12.1963. The petitioner joined as J.B.T. Teacher.

</p><p id="p_2">                                It is a matter of record that the petitioner, vide Annexure P-1,

           dated 19.3.1996 has been granted the higher pay scale w.e.f. 12.12.1973,

           whereas it should have been granted from 3.12.1963.

</p><p id="p_3">                                There is no dispute to the proposition of law on this point as

           such like persons are entitled to the pay scale from the date of attaining the

           higher qualification. Since the petitioner joined on 3.12.1963, he is entitled

           to higher pay scale from such date and not from 12.12.1973 as has been

           mentioned in Annexure P-1.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                Accordingly, order (Annexure P-1) is modified and the date of

           granting higher pay scale shall be treated as 3.12.1963 instead of

           12.12.1973.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                The writ petition is accordingly disposed of.
