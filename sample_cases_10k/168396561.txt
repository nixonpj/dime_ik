                         Versus

Appearance:
MR PRABHAV A MEHTA(2009) for the Petitioner(s) No. 1

<p id="p_1">     Heard Mr.Prabhav Mehta, learned advocate for the
petitioner and Ms.Vyoma Jhaveri, learned advocate for
the respondents.
</p>

<p id="p_2">     Learned advocate for the respondents pointed out
that the Co­ordinate Bench has passed the following
order on 12.03.2019            in Special Civil Application
No.2095 of 2018.
</p>

<blockquote id="blockquote_1">       "Rule returnable on 06.05.2019.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">      Learned advocate for the petitioner submits that the
      issue pertains to recovery of concessional rates
      incurred by the petitioner on the ground that it
      being registered with the Registrar of News Paper of
      India (RNI). It is the case that the petitioner had
      lost   its   registration  and   for   some  period,
      concessional rates were not available and hence,
      recovery was ordered. Learned advocate submits that
      even on today, registration with the RNI has
      continued. He draws attention of this Court to the
      latest certificate, wherein the registration is till
