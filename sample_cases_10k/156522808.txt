                 1. Ganauri Manjhi Son of Shri Krishna Manjhi Resident of Village : Hander
                 Mohanpur Mushahari, P.S. Gauri Chak, District - Patna.

<p id="p_1">2   03-04-2018         Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned APP for

                 the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                       The petitioner seeks bail in a case instituted for the offences

                 under Section-30(A) of the Bihar Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>,

                 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                       Prosecution case, in short, is that 30 liters wine is recovered.

                       It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioner that the

                 petitioner is in custody since 14-01-2018 and has got no criminal

                 antecedent. Charge-sheet/prosecution report has been submitted

                 in this case. There is no allegation of tampering of witnesses

                 alleged against the petitioner. It is alleged that 30 liters wine is

                 recovered. There is no compliance of <a href="/doc/1137396/" id="a_2">Section 100</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_4">                       On behalf of the State, it is submitted that the petitioner is
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.17044 of 2018 (2) dt.03-04-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   Page 2 of 2</span>




                        named in the F.I.R/complaint case.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                            Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances,

                        it is directed that the petitioner, above named, be released on bail

                        on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two

                        sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                        Special Judge, Patna in connection with Gopalpur P.S. Case No.

                        08 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                         (Sudhir Singh, J)
A.K.V./-
