<p id="p_1">          This revision petition has been preferred challenging

the order dated 6.1.2009 vide which ld. Trial Court, while

dismissing the petition under Sec.125 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">CrPC</a>, also imposed

penalty of Rs.10,000/- on the petitioner under Sec.340 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_2">CrPC</a>.

</p><p id="p_2">2.        TCR was summoned and perused. The grievance of


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               1</span>
 the petitioner is that in the affidavit, there was some

typographical mistake regarding the gainful employment of the

petitioner and while dismissing the petition, ld. Trial Court has

imposed a cost of Rs.10,000/- without any basis and that order is

illegal and without jurisdiction. On behalf of respondent No.1, it

has been submitted that ld. Trial Court was justified in imposing

the cost on the petitioner for her misrepresentation that she was

not having any means to maintain herself and under the

provisions of <a href="/doc/1888376/" id="a_3">Section 126(3)</a> CrPC, just cost could be imposed

on her. However, it has been submitted by ld. Counsel for the

respondent no.1 that in view of the status of the parties, the cost

of Rs.10,000/- being unjust, he is ready to accept lessor amount

as per financial capacity of the petitioner. Ld. Counsel for the

petitioner has offered to pay Rs.4000/- towards cost to the

respondent No.1 which respondent No.1 as well as his counsel

both have agreed. In the circumstances, the revision petition is

partly allowed and impugned order dated 6.1.2009 passed by ld.

MM is modified to the extent that petitioner shall pay Rs.4000/-

to the respondent No.1 towards cost. TCR alongwith copy of the

order be sent back and revision file be consigned to Record

Room.</p><pre id="pre_2">

Announced in the open Court

12.5.2009                            ( PRATIBHA RANI )
                                     ASJ-01(Central)/Delhi
