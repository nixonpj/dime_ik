                                Date of Decision: December 22, 2008


Savita @ Pal Kaur
                                                     .....PETITIONER(S)

                                  VERSUS


State of Punjab
                                                    .....RESPONDENT(S)</pre>

<pre id="pre_2">                              .     .      .


CORAM:            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AJAI LAMBA


PRESENT: -        Mr. Harit Sharma, Advocate, for the
                  petitioner.

                  Mr. Kamaldeep Singh Sidhu,                 Deputy
</pre><p id="p_1">                  Advocate General, Punjab.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                              .     .      .
</p>
<p id="p_4">AJAI LAMBA, J (Oral)

              This petition has been filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section

439</a> Cr.P.C. for grant of bail to the petitioner(s) in

case FIR No.152 dated 8.7.2008 lodged for commission

of offence punishable under Section(s) 419, 420, 467,

468,   471,    120-B    <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>       with     Police    Station,    City,

Rajpura.
</p>
<p id="p_5">              Learned counsel states that the petitioner

(a lady) has been in custody since 17.7.2008. Challan

has already been presented. No useful purpose would

be served by further custody of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_6">              It has been pointed out that co-accused,

Harjit Singh, has been allowed bail by the Additional

Sessions Judge, Patiala.
</p><p id="p_7"> Crl. Misc. No. M-26309 of 2008                                         [2]



                I have considered the issue.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                Learned counsel for the respondent-State

has not disputed the fact that the petitioner is in

custody     since      17.7.2008.        Challan      has    already         been

presented and therefore, the investigation has been

concluded.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                Conclusion         of    trial   is    likely     to         take

substantial time.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                The petition is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                Bail     to      the    satisfaction        of   the         Chief

Judicial Magistrate, Patiala.</p><pre id="pre_3">

                                                             (AJAI LAMBA)
December 22, 2008                                               JUDGE
