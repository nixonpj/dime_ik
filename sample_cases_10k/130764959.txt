                           GA No. 1426 of 2014
                            CS No. 16 of 1994
                      IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                  Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction


                          BAJRANGLAL CHORARIA
                                 Versus
                       UNIT TRUST OF INDIA &amp; ORS.


    BEFORE:
    The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN
    Date: 20th August 2014.
                                                                 Appearance:
                                         Mr. Abhrajit Mitra, Senior Advocate
                                                     Mr. Sakya Sen, Advocate
                                          ...for Respondent No.3/petitioner.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                              Mr. Reetobroto Mitra, Advocate
                                                       ...for the plaintiff.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
    The Court:        The   basis   of    this   application   is   an   order

passed on 23rd March 2010 in CS No. 16 of 1994.                However, Mr.

Reetobroto   Mitra,   learned   counsel     appearing   on   behalf   of   the

plaintiff submits that the cause title appearing in the said order

is not the cause title of the suit and accordingly it had escaped

the attention of the plaintiff.          The plaintiff is seeking to file

an application for restoration of the suit.

    That there are mistakes in the order dated 23rd March 2010

cannot be disputed.     Either the suit number is wrong or the cause

title is wrong.
</p>
<p id="p_3">    In view thereof, this application is dismissed.             However, in

the event no step is taken for recalling of the order dated 23rd
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 2</span>


March 2010 within a period of fortnight from date, the petitioner

shall be entitled to move afresh on the self-same cause of action.




</p><p id="p_4">                                             ( SOUMEN SEN, J. )

S. Kumar
A.R.(CR)
