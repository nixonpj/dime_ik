</pre><p id="p_23">            Learned counsel for the appellant has submitted that pursuant to the order
         dated 09.08.2019 notice has been issued upon the respondents, but it appears
         from the office note that respondent no.3 has not been found on the given
         address though Mr. Subodh Kumar Dubey, Advocate undertakes before this
         Court to appear on behalf of the respondent no.3.
            So far service of notice against respondent no.6 is concerned, service report
         as well as A/D has not been received.
</p><p id="p_24">            So far service of notice against respondent no.7 is concerned, he could not
         be traced as per the service report at flag- X7.
            So far service of notice against respondent no.9 is concerned, he has also
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       -2-</span>
               not appeared today though learned counsel, Mr. Subodh Kumar Dubey on
               behalf of respondent no.9.
</p><p id="p_25">                  So far service of notice against respondent no.10 is concerned, he could not
               be identified by the process server.
</p><p id="p_26">                  So far service of notice against respondent no.12 is concerned, she has
               been served notice through her husband but she has not appeared.
                  So far service of notice against respondent no.15 is concerned, the same
               could not be served as the person has shifted to Jamshedpur and Kolkata.
                  So far service of notice against respondent no.16 is concerned, the service
               report has not been received.
</p><p id="p_27">                  Under the aforesaid circumstances, learned counsel for the appellant, Mr.
               Bhaiya Vishwajeet is directed to serve notice upon these respondents before
               the learned court below by filing requisites etc. before the learned court below
               in the Title Partition Suit No.117 of 2017 pending before the court of Civil
               Judge, Senior Division-I, Jamshedpur by the next date.
                  In the meantime, parties shall maintain status quo which shall be
               automatically vacated the day on which the parties shall appear before this
               Court.
</p><p id="p_28">                  Parties are free to exchange their counter affidavits and pleadings, if any.
                  I.A. No. 283 of 2019 prayed for status quo is hereby disposed of.
                  The learned court below is directed to ensure that all the parties of the suit
               must appear before this Court in the present miscellaneous appeal.
</p>
