<div class="doc_title">Karuppaiah vs The Superintendent Of Police on 20 January, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 20.01.2017  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.SELVAM           
and 
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.KALAIYARASAN             

W.P(MD)No.984 of 2017   

Karuppaiah                                      ..  Petitioner

Vs.

1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Sivagangai District.

2.The Inspector of Police,
   Singampunari Police Station,
   Sivagangai District.                                 ..  Respondents

PRAYER: Writ Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India
praying for the issuance of a Writ of Mandamus directing the second
respondent herein to provide necessary police protection during the conduct
of the Annathanam Function during the coming Taipoosam Festival Day on  
09.02.2017 at in the petitioner's own premises situated at Survey Number
364/19 measuring an extent of 0.03.75 situated at Vengaipatti Village,
Sivagangai District on the basis of the petitioner's representation dated
02.01.2017.

!For Petitioner                 : Mr.B.Jameel Arasu 

For Respondents         : Mr.M.Govindan,         
                                  Special Government Pleader.
        
:ORDER  
</pre><p id="p_1">[Order of the Court was made by A.SELVAM, J.]  
        This writ petition has been filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_2">Article 226</a> of the Constitution
of India praying to direct the respondents to give police protection on the
eve of Annathanam Function in the place mentioned therein, by way of issuing
a writ of mandamus. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.Mr.M.Govindan, learned Special Government Pleader, has taken notice 
for the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.It is seen from the records that on 02.01.2017 the petitioner has
given a representation to the respondents and the same has not been
considered.  Under the said circumstances, the following order can be passed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4.In fine, this writ petition is allowed in part without costs and the
second respondent viz., Inspector of Police, Singampunari Police Station,
Sivagangai District is directed to look into the representation dated
02.01.2017 and take appropriate action in accordance with law.
</p>

<p id="p_5">To

</p><p id="p_6">1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Sivagangai District.
</p>
<p id="p_7">2.The Inspector of Police,
   Singampunari Police Station,
   Sivagangai District.
</p><p id="p_8">.
