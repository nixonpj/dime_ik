<p id="p_1">		The learned counsel for the petitioners seeks permission of this Court to withdraw the Petition and he has also made an endorsement  to that effect.  
</p>  
<p id="p_2">		2. In view of the endorsement made by the learned counsel for the petitioners, the Writ Petition is dismissed as withdrawn.  However, liberty is granted to the petitioners to file an appeal under Section 152 Sub Clause 2 of the Tamil Nadu Cooperative Societies Act, 1983 in proper format before the Joint Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Kanchipuram Region (i.e.) the second respondent herein within a period of two weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of this Order.  No costs.  Consequently, connected Miscellaneous Petition is closed.  </p><pre id="pre_2">
