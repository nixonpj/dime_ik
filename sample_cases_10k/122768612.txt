 Crl.M.C. 7914/18                     -:2:-


                                    ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">       Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_2">       2.     Petitioners are accused in C.P. No.13 of 2018 on the file of

the Judicial Magistrate of First Class-I, Ernakulam registered for the

offence under <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_2">Section 308</a> IPC. Second respondent is the victim. It is

submitted that the matter has been settled and the proceedings in the

trial court may be quashed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       3.     Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and the

second respondent and the learned Public Prosecutor.

</p><p id="p_4">       4.     I    have   perused   the   affidavit   filed   by   the   second

respondent, who has entered appearance through counsel. I am

satisfied that the matter has been settled and no public interest is

involved in this case. There is no impediment for granting the prayer

of the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       In the result, this Crl.M.C is allowed. The proceedings in C.P.

No.13 of 2018 on the file of the Judicial Magistrate of First Class-I,

Ernakulam are quashed. If any material objects have been produced

in this case, the trial court may pass appropriate orders for their

disposal.
</p>
