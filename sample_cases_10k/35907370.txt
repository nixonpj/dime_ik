<div class="doc_title">Hc Kishan Chand vs Unknown on 10 May, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH



                                         C.W.P No. 8687 of 2012
                                         Date of decision : May 10, 2012


HC Kishan Chand,
                                              ...... Petitioner

                        v.
State of Punjab and others,
                                              ...... Respondents

                       ***
</pre><p id="p_1">CORAM : HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE AJAY TEWARI
                       ***
Present :      Mr. H.S.Baath, Advocate
               for the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">                                 ***
</p><p id="p_3">1.   Whether Reporters of Local Newspapers may be allowed to see the
     judgment ?
</p><p id="p_4">2.   To be referred to the Reporters or not ?
</p><p id="p_5">3.   Whether the judgment should be reported in the Digest ?
</p><p id="p_6">                                 ***

AJAY TEWARI, J (Oral)

               The claim in this writ petition is that the petitioner is a Head

Constable and has been transferred out of his district                 which is

impermissible under the rules since the post of Head Constable is a District

cadre post.
</p>
<p id="p_7">               In my opinion, it would be in the interest of justice if the

petitioner moves a self contained representation to respondent No.2 who

would decide the same by passing a speaking order within three months of

the receipt thereof. Ordered accordingly.

</p><p id="p_8">               Disposed of in the above terms.
</p>

</p><pre id="pre_2">                                           ( AJAY TEWARI           )
May      10,    2012.                           JUDGE
