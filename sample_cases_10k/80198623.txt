                      At Chandigarh



                                            CRM-M-10630-2019 (O&amp;M)
                                            Date of Decision:- 8.3.2019


Madhu Sharma and others                                       ... Petitioners
                                Versus


State of Punjab and others                                    ... Respondents



CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE GURVINDER SINGH GILL


Present:-   Mr. Ravi Chaddha, Advocate, for the petitioners.

            *****

GURVINDER SINGH GILL, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            By way of filing this petition, the petitioners seek issuance of

direction to respondents No.2 and 3 so as to protect their life and liberty and

also to ensure that they are not unnecessarily harassed.

</p><p id="p_2">            Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the

petitioners are the parents and sister of Deepak Kumar who along with his

wife has been involved in FIR No. 72, dated 9.12.2018, registered at Police

Station Hajipur, under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1317063/" id="a_2">465</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120-B</a> IPC. It has

been submitted that although the petitioners have nothing to do with the

present FIR but they are unnecessarily being harassed by the police and are

being pressurized to make the accused surrender before the police.

</p><p id="p_3">            Having heard the learned counsel for the petitioners, the petition

is disposed of and Superintendent of Police, Hoshiarpur (respondent No.2) is
