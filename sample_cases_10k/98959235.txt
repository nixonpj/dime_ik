<p id="p_1">  (PER : HONOURABLE
MR.JUSTICE R.P.DHOLARIA)
 


 


 


[1]	RULE.
</p><p id="p_2">Mr.H. L. Jani, learned APP waives service of rule for the respondent
  State.
</p> 

 


 


<p id="p_3">[2]	The
present application has been preferred by the applicant   convict,
through jail, seeking temporary bail for the purpose of agriculture
operations.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_4">[3]	We have
read the application and perused the documents on record. From the
papers,  it  reveals that the convict has  served the sentence of
more than 4 years and 8 months, during which period he has enjoyed
two spells of temporary bail and once of furlough leave. 
</p>
 


 


 


 


 


<p id="p_5">[4]	In view
of the aforesaid facts and circumstances of the case, we are inclined
to use judicial discretion in favour of the applicant   convict to
release him on temporary bail. The application is partly allowed. The
applicant   convict is ordered to be released on temporary bail for
a period of 14 days from the date of actual release on his furnishing
personal bond of Rs.10,000/- [Rupees Ten Thousand Only] with the Jail
Authorities. Upon completion of the temporary bail period, he shall
surrender before the Jail Authorities.  Rule is made absolute to the
aforesaid extent. 
</p>
 

 

 

 


 


<p id="p_6">(AKIL KURESHI, J.) 

 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 


(R.P.DHOLARIA,J.)
