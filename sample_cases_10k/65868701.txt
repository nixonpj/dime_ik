<div class="doc_title">Shakuntla &amp; Another vs State Of Punjab &amp; Others on 21 November, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">CWP No.22920 of 2012                                                    -1-

      IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                   AT CHANDIGARH


                                          CWP No.22920 of 2012
                                          Date of decision:21.11.2012

Shakuntla &amp; another
                                  ...... Petitioners

            Vs.


State of Punjab &amp; others
                                  ..... Respondents


CORAM:- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.

Present:    Mr. S.K. Arora, Advocate for the petitioners.

            .....

Tejinder Singh Dhindsa, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            The     petitioners     had     joined     the   Punjab   Education

Department as JBT Teachers on regular basis in the year 1977. All the

petitioners have since retired upon attaining the age of superannuation

on various dates between the years 2009-2012.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            The grievance raised by the petitioners in terms of filing the

instant writ petition is that the benefit of proficiency step up on

completion of 32 years of regular service under the Assured Career

Progression Scheme dated 25.09.1998 (Annexure P-1) has not been

granted to them.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            Learned counsel appearing for the petitioners contends that

the sole basis denying such benefit to the petitioners is that in the year

2007, the petitioners had been promoted as Head Teachers but they had
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> CWP No.22920 of 2012                                               -2-</span>

chosen to forego such promotion benefit. Counsel submits that such

issue already stands settled by this Court in terms of judgment dated

20.01.2011 passed in CWP No.21287 of 2010 (Ram Kumar Kapoor

Vs. State of Punjab &amp; others). Counsel further submits that even a legal

notice dated 12.10.2011 (Annexure P-9) already stands served upon the

respondent-authorities.
</p>
<p id="p_4">            In view of the facts noticed hereinabove, I deem it

appropriate to dispose of the present writ petition with a direction to the

respondent-authorities to consider the claim of the petitioners and to

take a final decision on the legal notice dated 12.10.2011 (Annexure P-

</p><p id="p_5">9) strictly in accordance with law and by passing a speaking order

within a period of three months from the date of receipt of a certified

copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_6">            Disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><pre id="pre_2">November 21, 2012                   (TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA)
harjeet                                     JUDGE
