                      CHANDIGARH.

                                                          CWP No.13027 of 2016

                                                  Date of Decision:-08.07.2016

Harjit Singh.

                                                                   ......Petitioner.

                                        Versus

The State of Punjab &amp; Ors.

                                                                ......Respondents.

CORAM:- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JASWANT SINGH

Present:-   Mr. S.S. Behl, Advocate for the Petitioner.

                                 ***
</pre>
<p id="p_1">JASWANT SINGH, J.(ORAL)

            Petitioner retired on attaining the age of superannuation w.e.f.
31.10.2002 from the rank of Clerk-cum-JBT Teacher with the Education
Department, Punjab.
</p><p id="p_2">            He claims that he had prior to joining the education department
in the year 1972, worked w.e.f. 1.9.1965 to 6.6.1972 with a private
government aided school, namely, Ramgarhia Higher Secondary School,
Amritsar.
</p><p id="p_3">            The prayer is for counting of the said period towards
pensionary benefit.
</p><p id="p_4">            The claim appears to be highly belated, however, the counsel
for the petitioner prays for permission to withdraw the present petition to
file a fresh one by placing on record the documents to show his status with
the previous employer.
</p><p id="p_5">            Dismissed as withdrawn with aforesaid liberty.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                 ( JASWANT SINGH )
                                                      JUDGE
July 08, 2016
Vinay
