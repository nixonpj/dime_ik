<p id="p_1">Coram:       Hon'ble Mr. Justice Ravi Shanker Jha, Chief Justice.
</p><p id="p_2">             Hon'ble Mr. Justice Rajiv Sharma


Present      Mr. Surinder Thakur, Advocate, for the petitioner.


Ravi Shanker Jha, Chief Justice

             This petition has been filed by the petitioner praying for a

direction to the State authorities to take into consideration the

recommendations of the Local Health Authorities and open a Health Sub

Centre in village Behroon, Post Office Hariana, District Hoshiarpur. It is

stated that inspite of the fact that a health centre is required to be opened by

the State at the village concerned as per the Health Policy and inspite of the

favourable recommendations for that purpose by the Local Authorities, no

action is being taken by the authorities at the State level.

</p><p id="p_3">             Mr. Suveer Sheokand, learned Additional Advocate General,

Punjab, appearing for the State, to whom advance copy of the petition has

been supplied, submits that the matter shall be examined and the decision

shall be taken expeditiously by the authorities in accordance with law.

</p><p id="p_4">             In view of the aforesaid undertaking and without commenting

on the merits of the case, the petition is disposed of with a direction to the

respondents to take a decision in respect of opening of Health Sub Centre

within a period of four weeks from the date of receipt of copy of the petition
