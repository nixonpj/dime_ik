<div class="doc_title">Bhaskar Kumar @ Bhaokar Kumar @ ... vs The State Of Bihar on 28 August, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.46955 of 2019
                          Arising Out of PS. Case No.-69 Year-2018 Thana- PIPRA District- Patna
                 ======================================================
                 Bhaskar Kumar @ Bhaokar Kumar @ Bhaskar Prasad, Son of Suresh Prasad,
                 Resident of Village - Behrawan Chak, P.S.- Pipra, District- Patna

                                                                                     ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar.
                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :        Mr. Ravi Shanker Pankaj
                 For the Opposite Party/s :        Mr. Raj Ballabh Singh
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   28-08-2019             This application has been filed by the petitioner for grant

                   of anticipatory bail in connection with Pipra P.S. Case No. 69 of

                   2018, for the offences under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/162506/" id="a_3">447</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>,

                   <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_6">325</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_7">307</a> and <a href="/doc/1402213/" id="a_8">337</a> of IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                           Prosecution case is that petitioner and other accused

                   persons came variously armed and on the abatement of accused,

                   he assaulted the informant by rod on his head, causing injury

                   and thereafter, other accused persons have also assaulted,

                   causing injuries.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                           Submission of learned counsel for the petitioner is that

                   there is a land dispute between the parties and for that he has

                   falsely been implicated in this case.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                           Heard learned APP also, who opposed the prayer for

                   anticipatory bail by stating that the process under Section 82
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.46955 of 2019(3) dt.28-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_9">Cr.P.C</a>. has already been exhausted.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Having heard both sides, in view of the facts and

                     circumstances, as stated above, this application is disposed of

                     with a direction to the petitioner to surrender before the learned

                     court below and make prayer for regular bail, which will be

                     considered by the learned court below on the basis of materials

                     available on the record as well as the nature of injury found on

                     the person of injured, and if, possible to be disposed of on the

                     same day.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                                               (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
Sunil Shukla/-
</p>
