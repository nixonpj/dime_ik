<div class="doc_title">Annpurna Gurjar Daughter Of Shri ... vs State Of Rajasthan on 2 July, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Alok Sharma</div>

<pre id="pre_1">      HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                  BENCH AT JAIPUR

              S.B. Civil Writ Petition No.11093/2019

Annpurna Gurjar Daughter of Shri Ranveer Singh Gurjar, aged
about 18 years, resident of Village Habibwala, Post Office
Saithal, Tehsil Dausa, District Dausa, Rajasthan.
                                                                     ----Petitioner
                                   Versus
1.     State of Rajasthan, through Secretary, Department of
       Medical Education, Government Of Rajasthan, Jaipur.
2.     Chairman,    Neet      Ug     Medical       And      Dental     Admission/
       Counseling Board-2019, And Principal And Controller,
       S.m.s. Medical College And Attached Hospitals, Jaipur.
3.     Senior Director, National Eligibility Cum Entrance Test Ug-
       2019, Through Central Board Of Secondary Education,
       Shiksha Kendra-2, Community Centre, Preet Vihar, Delhi.
                                                                ----Respondents</pre>


<pre id="pre_2">For Petitioner(s)        :     Mr.Himanshu Jain
For Respondent(s)        :     Mr.Harshal Tholia for Dr.V.B. Sharma,
                               AAG
                               Mr.Angad Mirdha.
                               Mr.Prakash Gupta, DLR, SMS Medical
                               College, Jaipur, present in person.



            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ALOK SHARMA

                                    Order

02/07/2019



</pre><p id="p_1">          The case of the petitioner is that she was successful in


the NEET UG, 2019 Examination. However for reasons beyond


control she could not register online for the first round of


counselling where Colleges were to be allotted on merit cum


preference to pursue the MBBS Course. It has been submitted that

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    (Downloaded on 04/07/2019 at 10:37:43 PM)</span>
                                             (2 of 3)                      [CW-11093/2019]


in   terms   of   instructions        for     writing      the      NEET     UG    2019,


Examination in the schedule for admissions appended thereto, it


has been provided that fresh application forms will be opened


before the second round of counselling as also before mop-up


round. It has been submitted that in the circumstances it be


directed that the petitioner be allowed to register online for the


second round of counselling.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">             Mr.Harshal      Tholia      appearing         for      the    respondents-


Chairman, NEET UG Medical and Dental Admission/ Counselling


Board, 2019 on instructions of Mr.Prakash Gupta, DLR, SMS


Medical College, Jaipur, who is present in person, submitted that


indeed the successful candidates at the NEET UG Examination,


2019 who had failed to register online in the first instance for the


first round of counselling will be allowed to register online for the


second   round     of     counselling.         He      submitted          that   however


information with regard to the dates for filling of the online


application form for second round of counselling will be uploaded,


on the website of Medical Education Department, after the Board's


meeting on 12th July, 2019.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                        (Downloaded on 04/07/2019 at 10:37:43 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_5">                                                                                (3 of 3)                [CW-11093/2019]


                                              In view of the submission of Mr.Harshal Tholia on


                                   instruction of Mr.Prakash Gupta, DLR, SMS Medical College, Jaipur


                                   which resolves the petitioner's grievance I would dispose of this


                                   petition accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                              Let the information with regard to online registration for


                                   the second round of counselling be uploaded on the website of the


                                   Medical Education Department immediately after the Board's


                                   meeting by 12th July, 2019. Pursuant thereto the petitioner shall


                                   be free to register online for the second round of counselling and


                                   seek admission on her merit cum preference on a vacant seat.



</p><p id="p_8">                                                                                                 (ALOK SHARMA), J

                                   Himanshu Soni/375/184
