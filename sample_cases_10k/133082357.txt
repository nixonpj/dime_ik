<p id="p_9">

          Heard the learned Counsel appearing on behalf of the parties.


          It is submitted at the Bar that neutral place may be chosen so that the

child can be brought there for visitation of her father. Thereafter it has been

decided by the parties that the child can be brought to the house of Sougata

Bhattacharjee, Sristhi Flat, 2nd Floor, near Konnagore Railway Station (house of

opposite party's brother) and in the house of Nila Ganguly, 82, R.N. Tagore Road,

Kolkata-35 near Dakshineswar Temple (house of petitioner's maternal uncle). The

child shall be taken to the respective houses at every alternative Saturday in

between 3 P.M. to 7 P.M., which shall be the visitation hours. The first visitation

shall be made in the house of the maternal uncle of the present petitioner.



</p><p id="p_10">          With this observation and direction, the civil revisional application is

allowed and the order of the learned Court below is hereby modified to this extent.



</p><p id="p_11">          Urgent photostat certified copy of this Order, if applied for,

be supplied expeditiously after complying with all necessary legal

formalities.
