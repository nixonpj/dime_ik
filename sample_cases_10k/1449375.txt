<div class="doc_title">Balram Mishra vs State Of Bihar on 10 July, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Cr.Misc. No.24798 of 2008
                                     BALRAM MISHRA
                                             Versus
                                      STATE OF BIHAR
                                           -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2   10.7.2008.               Offence alleged is under <a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_1">Section 304B</a>/<a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_2">201</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian

                   Penal Code. Complaint case no.1335 of 2004 was filed by              the

                   informant who is brother of the deceased alleging therein that his

                   sister Ruby Kumar was murdered for demand of dowry. The name of

                   this petitioner is also included in the F.I.R. but subsequently during

                   investigation the witnesses said that the petitioner is not the family

                   member and he has no concern. The informant also filed a petition in

                   the court supporting this fact.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             Considering the aforesaid fact prayer for anticipatory bail is

                   allowed. The petitioner namely Balram Mishra is directed to surrender

                   before the S.D.J.M. West Muzaffarpur within four weeks from today

                   and on his so surrendering he shall be enlarged on bail on his

                   furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties

                   of the like amount each to the satisfaction of S.D.J.M. West

                   Muzaffarpur in connection with Kurdhani P.S.Case no.156 of 2004

                   subject to the condition as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_4">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_3">                                             (Mridula Mishra, J)
           Sss/-
</p>
