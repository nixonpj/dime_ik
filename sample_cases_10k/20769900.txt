<pre id="pre_1">         C/SCA/18783/2019                                       ORDER




          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

             R/SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 18783 of 2019

==========================================================
           ASSET RECONSTRUCTION COMPANY (INDIA) LTD.
                            Versus
                      STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR. VN. SEVAK(3791) for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
MS. JYOTI BHATT, ASSISTANT GOVERNMENT PLEADER for the
Respondent(s) No. 1
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE BIREN VAISHNAV

                             Date : 21/10/2019

                              ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1      Notice for final disposal. Learned AGP Ms.Jyoti Bhatt,

waives service of notice for the respondent - State.



2      By way of the present petition, the petitioner has prayed

for the following relief :
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">       "10 (b)    To direct the Respondent no.2 - District
       Magistrate, Vadodara to decide the Securitisation
       Application No. 251 of 2018 filed on 06/12/2018 by the
       petitioner as expeditiously as possible."
</blockquote>
<p id="p_2">3      Having heard submissions made at bar and considering

the provisions of <a href="/doc/57488768/" id="a_1">Section 14</a> of the SARFAESI Act, it appears

that   the     respondent    -   authority       has   not    decided          the

application dated 06.12.2018 preferred by the petitioner




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 Page 1 of 2</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                        Downloaded on : Wed Oct 23 22:27:34 IST 2019</span>
           C/SCA/18783/2019                             ORDER



under <a href="/doc/57488768/" id="a_2">Section 14</a> of the SARFAESI Act despite lapse of 10

months.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

4       Under the circumstances, the respondent - authority is

directed to decide the said application preferred by the

petitioner as expeditiously as possible but, not later than 3

weeks from the date of receipt of copy of this order. Needless

to remind the respondent - authority that there is a

mandatory requirement of law to decide such application

within a period of 30 days.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

5       In view of the above observation and direction, present

petition stands disposed of. Direct service is permitted.



</p><p id="p_5">                                            (BIREN VAISHNAV, J)
Bimal
