<div class="doc_title">Bharat Lal Singh vs Collectariate on 3 February, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                W.P.(S) No. 7213 of 2013
        Bharat Lal Singh, S/o Late Chinta Mani Singh, resident of Village- Sundari,
        P.O. Sundari, P.S. Torpa, Dist. Khunti                           ...... Petitioner
                                           Versus
        1. State of Jharkhand
        2. Deputy Commissioner, Ranchi, P.O., P.S. and Dist. Ranchi
        3. District Panchayat Raj Officer, P.O., P.S. and Dist. Ranchi
        4. Block Development Officer, Lapung, P.O. and P.S. Lapung,
        Dist. Ranchi                                                      ......Respondents
                                          ---------
        CORAM:        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SUJIT NARAYAN PRASAD
                                          ---------
        For the Petitioner         : Mr. Amit Kumar Sinha &amp; Sharad Kaushal, Advocates
        For the Respondents       : Mr. Ajit Kumar, Adv.
                                          ---------
        02/Dated: 3rd February, 2015
               The petitioner has approached this Court for release of his salary for the
        period February 2010 till 31.10.2013 i.e. the date of retirement.
               The grievance of the petitioner is that he was regularly transferred from
        one Block to other as Panchayat Sewak against his appointment dated
        14.12.1989</pre><p id="p_1"> and he rendered his duty till his superannuation i.e. on 31.10.2013.
</p><p id="p_2">        The further submission has been made on behalf of the petitioner that no
        departmental proceeding was ever initiated against him, but without any rhyme
        and reason the salary for the period from February 2010 till 31.10.2013 has not
        been released in spite of the recommendations made before the authorities
        concerned.
</p><p id="p_3">               The writ petition was filed on 02.12.2013, but no counter affidavit has been
        filed till date.
</p><p id="p_4">               Counsel for the respondents has fairly submitted that the grievance of the
        petitioner will be looked into and decision will be taken in accordance with law
        within specified period.
</p><p id="p_5">               writ petition is disposed of with consent of both parties. Petitioner is at
        liberty to approach before respondent no. 2 i.e. Deputy Commissioner, Ranchi,
        raising all his grievances along with all supporting documents regarding arrears of
        salary for the period from February 2010 till 31.10.2013 within three weeks from
        the date of receipt of copy of this order, the concerned respondent shall take
        decision in accordance with law within eight weeks thereafter and if respondent
        will dispute the same the reason shall be communicated to him by passing
        reasoned order within period aforesaid.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                            (Sujit Narayan Prasad, J.)
Tarun
