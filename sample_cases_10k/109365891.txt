<div class="doc_title">Smt Geeta Bai vs Om Prakash And Ors on 19 September, 2016</div><p id="p_1">                                                                S.B. CWP No. 12217/2016

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         1</span>

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT JAIPUR BENCH
                             JAIPUR

                                       ORDER


                   S.B. Civil Writ Petition No. 12217/<a href="/doc/79356415/" id="a_1">2016
                     Smt. Geeta Bai vs. Om Prakash &amp; Ors</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">Date of order: 19th September, 2016.

</p><p id="p_3">           HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE KANWALJIT SINGH AHLUWALIA

Mr. Rajneesh Gupta, for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">             The present petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_2">Article 227</a> of the

Constitution of India, praying that a direction be issued to the District

Judge, Karauli to expeditiously decide the election petition No.51/2015

preferred by the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">             The learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that he

had filed the election petition in the year 2015 and till today, the same has

not made much headway.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">             After hearing the learned counsel for the parties, the present

petition is disposed of with the direction to the District Judge, Karauli, to

decide the election petition No.51/2015 titled as <a href="/doc/79356415/" id="a_3">Geeta Bai vs. Om Prakash

&amp; Ors</a>., preferred by the petitioner within a period of six months from the

date of receipt of certified copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_9">                                             (KANWALJIT SINGH AHLUWALIA), J.
</p><p id="p_10">Mak/-
