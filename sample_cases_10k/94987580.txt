             IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT BOMBAY
                        BENCH AT AURANGABAD

                        WRIT PETITION NO.2618 OF 2019

                     BHARAT LAXMAN FERE AND OTHERS
                                 VERSUS
                  THE STATE OF MAHARASHTRA AND OTHERS

Mr.R.P.Bhumkar, Advocate for the petitioners
Mr.M.A.Deshpande, AGP for the respondents/State

                                                 CORAM :       PRASANNA B. VARALE &amp;
                                                               S.M.GAVHANE,JJ.
</pre><pre id="pre_2">                                                 DATED :       26.02.2019
P.C. :-

.              Heard learned counsel for the petitioners.


</pre><p id="p_1">2.             Limited grievance is raised in the petition. The
petitioners           are      retired          employees        of      Zilla        Parishad,
Osmanabad.          They        were        working       on     the      post        of      Civil
Engineer Assistant. Representation is submitted to the
Chief     Executive            Officer,         Zilla     Parishad,            Osmanabad           on
07.09.2018.           Copy      of       the    same     is    placed         on     record        at
Exh.H. By way of said representation these petitioners
prayed      for       interest           on    the      default        payment.           Learned
counsel       submitted              that      in   identical          circumstance               the
Division          Bench             of    this         Court      in       writ         petition
No.9054/2010 and 2868/2011 allowed the writ petitions.
</p>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">     ::: Uploaded on - 28/02/2019                              ::: Downloaded on - 21/03/2019 22:47:50 :::</span>
<p id="p_2">                                            (2)                                   wp2618.19

</p><p id="p_3">3.              Considering this fact, the petition is disposed
of with direction to the Chief Executive Officer, Zilla
Parishad, Osmanabad to decide the representation dated
07.09.2018 as expeditiously as possible and not later
than 12 weeks from the date of order of this Court, if it
is already not decided till date, needless to state on
the     merits        of     the     representation       and      considering             the
order of the Division Bench dated 22.08.2011. We further
make      it        clear       that    this     Court     has       not       made        any
observation on the merits of the representation and this
issue of assignment of merit is left for consideration
and decision by the authority namely the Chief Executive
Officer, Zilla Parishad, Osmanabad.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4.              With the above direction, the writ petition is
disposed of.
</p>
