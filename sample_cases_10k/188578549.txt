<pre id="pre_1">       R/CR.MA/12008/2018                                    ORDER




          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

          R/CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 12008 of 2018

==========================================================
     ALTAFHUSAIN GULAM RASUL SHAIKH THROUGH SHAMINBANU
                     ALTAFHUSAIN SHAIKH
                           Versus
                      STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
KARTIKKUMAR K JOSHI(8042) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1
for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 2
MS JIRGA JHAVERI, APP for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
==========================================================
 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.P.BHATT

                              Date : 28/06/2018
                               ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.    Rule. Learned APP waives service of notice of
Rule on behalf of the respondent-State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.    The present application has been filed by the
applicant-accused to release him on temporary bail
for a period of two weeks on the ground of attending
'Akika     Mubarak'         ceremony      of    his   daughter   namely
Shabana.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.    Heard the learned advocate for the applicant-
accused    and      learned     APP    for      the   respondent-State.
Perused the application and the jail remarks.
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.    Considering the material placed on record, the
cause shown in the application appears to be genuine.
In the facts and circumstances, this Court is of the
view that the applicant-accused is required to be
released on temporary for a period of three days.
</p>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  Page 1 of 2</span>
<p id="p_5">             R/CR.MA/12008/2018                                             ORDER



</p><p id="p_6">5. Accordingly, the applicant-accused is ordered to
be released on temporary bail for a period of three
days        from       the       date     of      his      actual     release,      on
executing            personal           bond    of       Rs.10,000/-(Rupees         Ten
Thousand          only)          with    local       surety       before    the    Jail
authority            and     on        usual    terms       and    conditions       and
further subject to the following conditions:
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                [a]       The applicant shall mark his presence
                before           the    concerned         Police     Station       once
                during the temporary bail period;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">                [b]       The applicant shall not leave Bharuch
                City during the temporary bail period;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">                [c]       The applicant shall not misuse liberty
                in     any       manner        during      the     temporary       bail
                period. The applicant shall surrender before
                the        Jail         Authorities         on     completion        of
                temporary bail period without fail.
</blockquote>

<p id="p_7">6.      Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
Direct service today is permitted.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                                                       (P.P.BHATT, J)
BDSONGARA
