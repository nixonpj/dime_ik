<p id="p_1">1.      Rule. Learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives service of Rule

on behalf of the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">2.      By way of this petition, convict has prayed to release him on parole

leave on the ground of managing financial assistance to his family.


</p><p id="p_4">3.      Learned Additional Public Prosecutor has opposed this petition and

produced jail record of the convict.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">4.      I have heard learned advocate appearing for the applicant and

learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the respondent. I have gone

through the jail record of the convict. It appears from the jail record that

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  Page 1 of 2</span>
            R/SCR.A/7641/2018                                  ORDER



whenever he was released on parole leave, he had surrendered in time and

his jail conduct is found to be good.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">5.       In the peculiar facts and circumstances of the case, the petition

deserves consideration. The convict shall be released on parole leave for

a period of Ten days, from the date of his actual release, on usual terms

and conditions with an understanding that no further extension will be

granted. The convict to surrender before Jail Authority on completion of

parole leave, without fail. Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.


</p><p id="p_9">                Direct service is permitted.
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">                                                         (A.J. SHASTRI, J)
