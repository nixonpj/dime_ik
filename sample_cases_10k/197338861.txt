<p id="p_1">2    17-01-2017                  Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

                  petitioner is ready to pay the amount along with the interest. He

                  further submits that this matter be referred to the National Lok

                  Adalat as there is a possibility of settling the dispute.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 Learned counsel for the Bank submits that if the

                  Bank would find that the proposals of the petitioner are viable

                  and possible only then the Bank will be able to come forward.

                  He further submits that in the meantime, the petitioner must give

                  his proposal in black and white before the Bank for proper

                  consideration.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Let this case be referred to the National Lok Adalat

                  as per the request of the petitioner for resolution and settlement

                  of dispute.
</p>
