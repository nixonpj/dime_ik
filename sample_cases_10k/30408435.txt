<p id="p_1">      The petitioner is running a madrassa. According to the petitioner,

the Central Government has framed a scheme for giving financial

assistance to madrassas, which conduct other formal education also.

According to the petitioner, the petitioner satisfies all the conditions for

grant of financial assistance as per the scheme. Although similarly

placed institutions have been given that benefit, the madrassa run by

the petitioner has been discriminated in the grant of financial

assistance as per the scheme, is the complaint of the petitioner in this

writ petition. The petitioner has submitted Ext.P6 application in this

regard. The petitioner seeks       a direction to the 2nd respondent to

consider Ext.P6 and pass orders thereon expeditiously.

</p><p id="p_2">      2.    I have heard the learned Government Pleader also.

</p><p id="p_3">      3.    In the facts and circumstances of the case, I dispose of this

writ petition with a direction to the 2nd respondent to consider and pass

orders on Ext.P6, as expeditiously as possible, at any rate, within one

month from the date of receipt of a certified copy of this judgment,

after affording an opportunity of being heard to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                                                           Sd/-
