<div class="doc_title">Mrs. Sudha Srivastava vs M/S Labmate (Asia) Pvt. Ltd on 10 February, 2011</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Sh. Narinder Kumar</div>

<pre id="pre_1">            IN THE COURT OF SH. NARINDER KUMAR 
          ADDIGIONAL DISTRICT JUDGE­02 (WEST); DELHI

C. S. No. 269/10

Date of Institution:     : 28.09.2010
Date of order            : 10.02.2011

In the matter of:­

Mrs. Sudha Srivastava,
Wife R. K. Prasad,
 R/o A­249/1, Hari Nagar,
Clock Tower,
New Delhi                                            ......Plaintiff 

                   VERSUS
M/s Labmate (Asia) Pvt. Ltd.
Baid Mehta Complex,
C­Block 1st Floor, 183, Mount Road,
Chennai,
Through its Director/Managing Director/Authorised Signatory
Also at 
M/s Labmate (Asia) Pvt. Ltd.
Flat No. 16, Ashoka Apartment,
Commercial Complex,
Ranjeet Nagar, 
New Delhi
Through its Director/Authorised person
                                                      ......Defendant

              SUIT FOR POSSESSION AND FOR DAMAGES

JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      This  suit  has been  filed  by the plaintiff­landlord against  the tenant for 


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         1</span>
 possession of the suit property i.e. Flat No. 106, Ashoka Apartment, Commercial 

Complex, Ranjeet Nagar, New Delhi and for recovery.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.    Today when the suit has been taken up, counsel for plaintiff and Sh. B. K. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">Poddar, Power of Attorney of the defendant submitted that parties have arrived 

at amicable settlement.  Sh. Narender Chauhan, counsel for the plaintiff and Sh. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">B. K. Poddar, Power of Attorney holder of the defendant have made statements 

recorded separately.  The statements read as under:­

        "Statement of Sh. Narender Chauhan, counsel for plaintiff.
        Without Oath
                The   defendant   company   has   amicably   and   voluntarily  
        arrived   at   settlement   with   the   plaintiff   regarding   the   subject  
        matter in dispute in this suit.   I have instructions in this regard  
        from the plaintiff.  Husband of the plaintiff has also come present  
        to court.
</p><p id="p_5">                As a result of amicable settlement, defendant company has  
        undertaken that it shall vacate the suit premises i.e. Flat no.106,  
        Ashoka   Apartment,   Commercial   Complex,   Ranjeet   Nagar,   New  
        Delhi, as shown in red colour in the site plan Ex C­2, and deliver  
        its vacant possession to the plaintiff,  on or before 30.09.2012.
</p><p id="p_6">                As regards the claim for recovery, defendant company has  
        undertaken that it shall pay to the plaintiff, use and occupation  
        charges @ Rs.20,000/­ p.m. w.e.f.01.09.08 to 30.09.2010 and Rs.
        27,000/­   p.m.   w.e.f.   01.10.2010   to   30.09.2012   and   actual   flat  
        maintenance   charges   to   M/s.   Ashoka   Apartment   Welfare  
        Association until the delivery of possession of the premises to the  
        plaintiff.
</p><p id="p_7">                With payment of the aforesaid amount to the plaintiff and  
        with   delivery  of   vacant  possession  of   the  suit   premises  to her,  
        there shall be no dispute left between the parties.   Suit may be  
        disposed of accordingly as having been compromised."
</p>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                             2</span>
<p id="p_8">           Statement of Sh. B.K. Poddar, Power of Attorney Holder of the  
          Defendant   company,  Aged about  30 years, R/o H.No.44, Press  
          Enclav e, Uttam Nagar, New Delhi. 
</p><p id="p_9">          On SA 
                I am General Power of Attorney holder as per power of  
          attorney Ex.C­1.   I have signed application Ex.C­X on behalf of  
          the defendant.  It has also been signed by counsel for plaintiff.
</p><p id="p_10">                The   defendant   company   has   amicably   and   voluntarily  
          arrived   at   settlement   with   the   plaintiff   regarding   the   subject  
          matter in dispute in this suit.  
</p><p id="p_11">                As a result of amicable settlement, I, as General Power of  
          Attorney   holder   of   the   defendant   company,   undertake   that   the  
          defendant company shall vacate the suit premises on or before  
          30.09.12, Flat no.106, Ashoka Apartment, Commercial Complex,  
          Ranjeet Nagar, New Delhi, as shown in red colour in the site plan  
          Ex C­2, and deliver its vacant possession to the plaintiff,   on or  
          before 30.09.2012.
</p><p id="p_12">                As regards the claim for recovery,  I, as General Power of  
          Attorney   holder   of   the   defendant   company,   undertake   that   the  
          defendant company shall pay use and occupation charges @ Rs.
          20,000/­   p.m.   w.e.f.01.09.08   to   30.09.2010   and   @   Rs.27,000/­  
          p.m. w.e.f. 01.10.2010 to 30.09.2012 plus actual flat maintenance  
          charges to M/s. Ashoka Apartment Welfare Association until the  
          delivery of possession of the premises to the plaintiff.
</p>
<p id="p_13">          With payment of the aforesaid amount to the plaintiff and with  
          delivery of vacant possession of the suit premises to her, there  
          shall be no dispute left between the parties.  Suit may be disposed  
          of accordingly as having been compromised."
</p>
<p id="p_14">3.      From the aforesaid statement made in Court, this Court is satisfied that 

parties have amicably and voluntarily settled the subject matter in dispute in this 

suit.   The undertakings given by Sh. B. K. Poddar, Power of Attorney of the 

defendant   are   accepted.         Suit   of   the   plaintiff   is   decreed   in   terms   of   the 

settlement arrived at today.  Parties shall bound by their statements.  Statements 

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                                  3</span>
 shall be made part of the decree.  Decree sheet be prepared and file be consigned 

to record room. </p><pre id="pre_2">



Announced in Open Court 
on 10.02.2011                                      (Narinder Kumar )
                                         Additional District Judge­01(West)
                                                      Delhi
