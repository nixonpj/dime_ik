<div class="doc_title">Deepak Tiwari @ Arpit Tiwari vs State Of U.P. on 11 July, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Ajit Singh</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 80
 

 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION No. - 27707 of 2019
 

 
Applicant :- Deepak Tiwari @ Arpit Tiwari
 
Opposite Party :- State Of U.P.
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Raghvendra Prakash
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.
 

 
Hon'ble Ajit Singh,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Heard learned counsel for the applicant, learned A.G.A. for the State and perused the material on record. 
</p> 
<p id="p_2">By means of this application, the applicant who is involved in Case Crime No. 44 of 2019, under <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_1">Sections 395</a> and <a href="/doc/844968/" id="a_2">412</a> I.P.C., Police Station-Gambhirpur, District-Azamgarh, is seeking enlargement on bail during the trial.
</p> 
<p id="p_3">Learned counsel for the applicant submitted that initially an F.I.R. was lodged under <a href="/doc/764237/" id="a_3">Section 394</a> I.P.C. and later on the information of the informant five persons were alleged to have committed the offence and the offence was converted to <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_4">Section 395</a> I.P.C. The alleged recovery of 2 notes of Rs. 500/- which has been shown from the applicant's possession was false one. There was no public witness of the alleged recovery. No identification parade was conducted. He next submitted that the applicant is absolutely innocent and has been falsely implicated in the present case due to some ulterior motive. He further submitted that other co-accused Digvijay Singh @ Bholu Singh has already been granted bail by another Bench of this Court vide order dated 19.06.2019 passed in Criminal Misc. Bail Application No. 24544 of 2019, copy of the bail order produced by learned counsel for the applicant is taken on record. He lastly submitted that the applicant is languishing in jail since 16.03.2019 and in case he is released on bail, he will not misuse the liberty of bail and will cooperate in trial. 
</p> 
<p id="p_4">The prayer for bail has been vehemently opposed by learned A.G.A. 
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Keeping in view the nature of the offence, evidence, complicity of the accused, severity of the punishment, submissions of learned counsel for the parties and without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, this Court is of the view that the applicant is entitled to be enlarged on bail during the pendency of the trial. 
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Let the applicant, Deepak Tiwari @ Arpit Tiwari be released on bail on his executing a personal bond and furnishing two sureties each in the like amount to the satisfaction of the court concerned in Case Crime No. 44 of 2019, under <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_5">Sections 395</a> and <a href="/doc/844968/" id="a_6">412</a> I.P.C., Police Station-Gambhirpur, District-Azamgarh subject to the following conditions:- 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">1. The applicant will continue to attend and co-operate in the trial pending before the court concerned on the date fixed after release.
</p> 
<p id="p_8">2. He will not tamper with the witnesses. 
</p> 
<p id="p_9">3. He will not indulge in any illegal activities during the bail period. 
</p> 
<p id="p_10">It is further directed that the identity, status and residence proof of the sureties be verified by the authorities concerned before they are accepted. 
</p> 
<p id="p_11">In case of breach of any of the above conditions, the trial court will be at liberty to cancel the bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_12">Order Date :- 11.7.2019
 
sweta
