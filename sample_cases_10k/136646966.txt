</p><p id="p_3">            Not on board. Mentioned. Upon mentioning taken on board.



</p><p id="p_4">2.          On 13th November, 2019 this application had come up for first

time. As learned A.P.P. did not have police papers with her on that day, the

matter could not be heard finally. Inadvertently, the Court has passed the

order deciding the application finally.



</p><p id="p_5">3.          Both learned Counsel for the applicant and learned A.P.P. urged to

treat the said order as ad-interim order and requested for keeping the matter

for final hearing.
</p>
