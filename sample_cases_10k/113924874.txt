<p id="p_1">     The petitioner has approached this Court with the

following prayers :
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">           "(a) To declare that the petitioner is entitled to be

           considered for appointment as Notary along with the

           applicants, who were interviewed from 25.02.2013 to

           27.02.2013 and the conduct on the part of the 1st and

           3rd respondents in not considering the petitioner for

           appointment as NOTARY along with the applicants

           interviewed   from    25.02.2013    to   27.02.2013    in

           unconstitutional    and   against     the     petitioners

           fundamental rights under <a href="/doc/367586/" id="a_1">Article 14</a> and <a href="/doc/1199182/" id="a_2">21</a> of the

           Constitution.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">           (b) Issue a writ of mandamus or other appropriate writ,

           order or direction directing the 1st and 3rd respondents

           to consider the petitioner for appointment as NOTARY

           along with the candidates who are being interviewed by

           the 3rd respondent interview Board (constituted as per

           Rule 7A of the Notaries Rules) from 25.02.2013 to

           27.02.2013;
</blockquote>
