<div class="doc_title">Magma Fincorp Limited vs Jitendra Singh &amp; Anr on 27 October, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                             ORDER SHEET
                          EC No.710 of 2015
                    IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                            ORIGINAL SIDE


                        MAGMA FINCORP LIMITED
                                Versus
                        JITENDRA SINGH &amp; ANR.


    BEFORE:
    The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>
<p id="p_1">    Date : 27th October, 2017.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
     The Court : The report filed by the office of the Deputy

Sheriff shows that although the warrants were duly forwarded to

the concerned police authority for due execution and production of

the judgment debtors but the said warrants have not been executed

and the department has not received the compliance report.

</p><p id="p_3">     Under such circumstances, the returnable date of the warrants

of arrest is extended till 1st December, 2017.

</p><p id="p_4">     The jurisdictional Superintendent of Police and the Officer-

in-Charge of the local police station are directed to ensure the

execution of the warrants and production of the judgment debtors

on the returnable date.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     The   Deputy   Sheriff   shall   communicate   this   order   to   the

jurisdictional Superintendent of Police for immediate compliance.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                       (SOUMEN SEN, J.)

R.Bhar
