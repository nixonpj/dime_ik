<div class="doc_title">No. 121 Of 2019 Dated 25.4.2019 ... vs Allowed on 17 September, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                1


17.09.2019</pre>
<pre id="pre_2"> tkm/ct 28                       C.R.M. 8564 of 2019
sl no. 389
</pre><p id="p_1">             In Re : An application for bail under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal
             Procedure filed on 16.9.2019 in connection with Manikchak P.S Case
             no. 121 of 2019 dated 25.4.2019 under <a href="/doc/409589/" id="a_2">section 304</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the IPC
                                  And
Allowed
             In Re : Paban Kumar Das @ Paban Das                   ........ petitioner

                   Mr. S. Chakraborty
                                    ...... for the petitioner
                   Mr. Anwar Hossain
                   M. B. Hasna
                                    ...... for the State

                   It is submitted on behalf of the petitioner that the incident
             occurred in the course of scuffle and he did not intend to murder
             the victim.
</p><p id="p_2">                   Learned lawyer for the State opposes the prayer for bail
             and submits that the petitioner assaulted the victim resulting in
             his death.
</p><p id="p_3">                   Having considered materials on record and bearing in mind
             the nature of allegations in the light of the submission that the
             incident occurred in the course of a scuffle and that he did not
             intend to murder the victim and in view of the period of detention
             suffered by the petitioner i.e. 61 days, we are inclined to grant
             bail to the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_4">                   Accordingly, the petitioner be released on bail upon
             furnishing a bond of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties of like
             amount each, one of whom must be local, to the satisfaction of
             the learned CJM, Malda on condition that the petitioner shall
             appear before the trial court on every date of hearing and shall
             not intimidate witnesses or tamper with evidence in any manner
             whatsoever.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  2</span>


<p id="p_5">      In the event he fails to appear before the trial court without
justifiable cause, the trial court shall be at liberty to cancel his
bail automatically without reference to this court.
</p><p id="p_6">      The application being CRM 8564 of 2019 is disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_7">(Jay Sengupta, J.)                          (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
 </p>
