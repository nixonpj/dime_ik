                                                  JUDGMENT
</pre><p id="p_1">                          (Judgment of the Court was delivered by Huluvadi G.Ramesh,J.)

                          When the matter is taken up today, learned Special Government Pleader,

            appearing for the appellants/State, has submitted that in compliance of the order passed

            by the learned single Judge in the writ petition, which is impugned in this Writ Appeal,

            the Government has issued G.O.(P)No.287, Rural Development &amp; Panchayat Raj (PA6)

            Department, dated 26.06.2018, ordering to reckon 50% of both part time and full time

            services in respect of the respondent, for calculation of pension.

</p><p id="p_2">                          2. In view of the compliance of the order passed by the learned single

            Judge, this Writ Appeal is dismissed, for having become infructuous. No costs.

            Consequently, the connected C.M.P.No.18200 of 2018 also stands dismissed
