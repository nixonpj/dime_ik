<div class="doc_title">Manoj Choudhary vs The State Of Bihar on 22 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.24383 of 2018
                 ======================================================
                 Manoj Choudhary son of Late Shiv Durga Choudhary, resident of Village-
                 Jabra, P.S. -Bausi,

                                                                   ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
           1.    The State of Bihar through Secretary Food and Consumer Protection
                 Department, Old Secretariat, Patna.
           2.    The Sub-Divisional Officer Banka.
           3.    The Block Supply Officer, Banka.

                                                           ... ... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s   :    Mr. Vijay Anand, Advocate
                 For the Respondent/s   :    Mr. Arvind Ujjwal
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ASHUTOSH KUMAR
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   22-05-2019                 The father of the petitioner, who was a PDS

                   licensee, died below the age of 58 years but only in the

                   year 2012. In the same year, the petitioner made an

                   application before the concerned authority for grant of

                   such license on compassionate ground. Along with the

                   license, the petitioner also annexed all the necessary

                   documents including the relinquishment of the claim of

                   the other relatives of his father who are also entitled to

                   be granted license on compassionate ground. Till the mid

                   of 2019, no decision has been taken. Despite several

                   correspondences of the petitioner with the concerned
 Patna High Court CWJC No.24383 of 2018(2) dt.22-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           2/3</span>




          authority, the case of the petitioner has remained

          pending for consideration.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                      The learned counsel for the petitioner has

          submitted that though many years have passed by since

          his father predeceased, nonetheless, the concerned

          authority was required to pass an order on such

          representation by the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                      Under the aforesaid circumstances, this Court

          deems it appropriate to direct the petitioner to make a

          fresh representation before the Sub-Divisional Officer,

          Banka (respondent No. 2) annexing the application and

          all other necessary facts and documents as also the

          correspondences            between        the   petitioner   and   the

          concerned respondent within a period of six weeks from

          today. On receipt of such representation/application, the

          concerned respondent shall pass a reasoned order in

          accordance with law within a further period of six weeks

          thereafter.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                      With the aforesaid observation/direction, the
             Patna High Court CWJC No.24383 of 2018(2) dt.22-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       3/3</span>




                      writ petition stands disposed off.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                                      (Ashutosh Kumar, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
