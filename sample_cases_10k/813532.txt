<p id="p_1">2.   29.6.2010             Heard learned counsel appearing on behalf of

                 the petitioners and learned counsel appearing on behalf

                 of the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                           The petitioners are in custody in connection

                 with Dumraon P.S. Case No. 100/2010 for offence

                 punishable under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1208971/" id="a_2">353</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian

                 Penal Code and Section Â¾B of the Bihar Lottery and

                 Gambing Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                           Taking into consideration the submission of

                 learned counsel that the antecedent of these petitioners

                 are clean and they have never been involved in any

                 other case and that the petitioners have remained in

                 custody since 20.5.2010, let the petitioners, namely, (1)
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                2</span>




            Kundan Chaurasia @ Kundan Kumar Chaurasia, (2)

            Monu @ Monu Gupta, (3) Raj Kishore Singh, (4) Krishna

            Kumar Jaiswal, (5) Rakesh Kumar Jaiswal and (6)

            Laxman Prasad Gond be released on bail upon each of

            them furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- (ten

            thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to

            the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Buxar

            in connection with Dumraon P.S. Case No.           100 of

            2010, subject to the condition that a close relative of the

            petitioners would stand as one of the bailors and who

            would indicate his relation with the said petitioner by

            filing an affidavit.   The said bailor would be under a

            duty to inform the Court below in a case the petitioners

            get involved in any further case of similar nature after

            their release and whereupon the Court concerned shall

            be at liberty to cancel the bail bonds of the defaulting

            petitioner and for taking him into custody on grounds of

            misuse.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
