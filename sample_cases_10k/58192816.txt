<div class="doc_title">Kanchan Devi And 02 Others vs State Of U.P. And 03 Others on 5 April, 2018</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Ramesh Sinha, Dinesh Kumar Singh-I</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 1
 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. WRIT PETITION No. - 8529 of 2018
 
Petitioner :- Kanchan Devi And 02 Others
 
Respondent :- State Of U.P. And 03 Others
 
Counsel for Petitioner :- Pavan Kumar Srivastava
 
Counsel for Respondent :- G.A.
 
Hon'ble Ramesh Sinha,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Hon'ble Dinesh Kumar Singh-I,J.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">Heard Sri P.K.Srivastava,learned counsel for the petitioners, Sri N.K.Verma, learned A.G.A. for the State and perused the impugned FIR as well as material brought on record.
</p> 
<p id="p_3">This petition has been filed by the petitioners with a prayer to quash the FIR dated 23.2.2018, registered as Case Crime No.57 of 2018, under <a href="/doc/1667388/" id="a_1">Sections 419</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a> I.P.C., Police Station Kotwali, District Azamgarh.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that the petitioners are innocent and have been falsely implicated in the present case with malafide intention. He further submits that the allegations levelled against the petitioners are absolutely false, frivolous and baseless. He further submits that identically situated co-accused person, namely, Pankaj Kumar Shukla has already challenged the FIR of the present case by means of filing Crl. Misc. Writ Petition No.7837 of 2018, which has been disposed of by Coordinate Bench of this Court vide order dated 3837 of 2018 staying the arrest of the said co-accused, copy of which has been produced by learned counsel for the petitioner the same is taken on record, hence, the petitioner is also entitled for the same relief.
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Learned A.G.A. opposed the prayer for quashing of the FIR, but could not dispute the aforesaid fact as argued by learned counsel for the petitioner. 
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Considering the submissions advanced by learned counsel for the parties and nature of the allegations, it is directed that the petitioners shall not be arrested in above mentioned case, till the submission of the police report under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">section 173(2)</a> Cr.P.C. but they shall co-operate with the investigation of the case. 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">With the above direction, this petition is finally disposed of. 
</p> 

 
<p id="p_8">(Dinesh Kumar Singh-I,J.)   (Ramesh Sinha, J.) 
 
Order Date :- 5.4.2018/NS
