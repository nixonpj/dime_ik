<div class="doc_title">Rohit Yadav vs The State Of Jharkhand on 27 November, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                B.A. No. 9262 of 2015
     Rohit Yadav                      .... Petitioner
                        Versus
     The State of Jharkhand                      ...     Opposite Party
                        ---
</pre><p id="p_1">     CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY
</p><p id="p_2">                        ---
</p><p id="p_3">     For the Petitioner : Mr. Anurag Kashyap, Advocate
     For the State      : Mr. Satish Kumar Keshri, APP
</p><p id="p_4">                        ---
</p><p id="p_5">     Order No. 02              Dated 27th November , 2015

              Heard Mr. Anurag Kashyap , learned counsel appearing for the
     petitioner and Mr. Satish Kumar Keshri, learned APP appearing for the
     State.
</p><p id="p_6">              Petitioner is an accused in connection with Meharma (Balbadda)
     P.S. Case No. 317 of 2015 [G.R. No. 1546 of 2015] registered for the
     offences punishable u/s 452,386,363,511,323,504 and 34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">I.P.C</a>.
</p><p id="p_7">              It has been submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioner
     that the the main allegation of demanding ransom and assaulting is
     against co-accused Suchit Yadav who happens to be brother of the
     petitioner. It has also been submitted that no specific allegation has
     been made against the petitioner and that the petitioner is in custody
     since 13.8.2015.
</p><p id="p_8">              Learned counsel for the State has opposed the prayer for bail of
     the petitioner and has referred to the Fardbeyan while stating that the
     three accused persons, who are brothers, have created fear and
     nuisance in the entire village.
</p><p id="p_9">              Regard being had to the fact that the main allegation has been
     levelled against co-accused Suchit Yadav, the petitioner, named above,
     is directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-
     (Rupees Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the
     satisfaction of learned Judicial Magistrate, Godda in connection with
     Meharma (Balbadda) P.S. Case No. 317 of 2015 [G.R. No. 1546 of
     2015].
</p>

<p id="p_10">                                          (RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY, J.)
MK
