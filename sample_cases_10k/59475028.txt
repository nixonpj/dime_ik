<div class="doc_title">S.Ponsingh Mohanram vs The Secretary To Government on 18 May, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

DATED: 18.05.2017

CORAM

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.GOVINDARAJ


Writ Petition No.12845 of 2017

S.Ponsingh Mohanram				...Petitioner 
			         Vs.


1.The Secretary to Government
   Labour &amp; Employment Department
   Fort St.George, Chennai-9

2.The Director of Industrial safety and Health
   Chennai-14					..Respondents


	Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India praying to issue a Writ of Mandamus directing the 1st respondent to dispose of the representation of the petitioner dated 18.04.2017 before filling up the post of Director of Industrial Safety and Health. 

	For Petitioner	  : Mr.A.Edwin Prabakar

	For Respondents     : Mr.A.N.Thambidurai, Spl.Govt.Pleader







		   
O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	The Writ Petitioner is working as Additional Director of Industrial Safety and Health in the Department of Labour and Employment. According to the petitioner, the post of Director of Industrial Safety and Health will fall vacant on 31.05.2017 in view of the retirement of the present incumbent and appointment should be made in advance.  There are several contestants for the said post and this petitioner is also fully eligible to the post as he is already holding the post of Additional Director of Industrial Safety and Health. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2. The learned counsel for the petitioner would submit that the post of Director of Industrial Safety and Health is the selection post and while the Government is considering the candidates for selection to that post, the petitioner should also be considered along with others, because without saying, by virtue of the petitioner is also eligible to be considered for promotion.  The learned counsel further submits that however, the petitioner apprehends that he may not be considered for the said post. Therefore, the petitioner made a representation to the respondent on 18.04.2017 and requests disposal of the representation by the respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. Considering the submission made, a direction is issued to the respondents to dispose of the representation of the petitioner dated 18.04.2017 and also to consider him along with other persons on merits for the post of Director of Industrial Safety and Health, if the post is going to be filled up before 31.05.2017. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4. The Writ Petition is disposed of with the above direction. No costs. 
</p>

nvsri.
