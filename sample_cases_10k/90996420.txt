<div class="doc_title">An Application For Anticipatory ... vs Satyendra Samanta &amp; Ors on 27 November, 2013</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Nishita Mhatre</div>

<p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                    1</span>


27, 2013.
</p><p id="p_2">                      C.R.M. 13564 of 2013

            In the matter of: An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the
            Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 05.10.2013.
</p><p id="p_3">                                  And
            In the matter of: Satyendra Samanta &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_4">                                           Petitioners.
</p><p id="p_5">                               -versus-
</p><p id="p_6">                            The State of West Bengal
                                          Opposite party
            Mr. Siddhartha Sarkar
                          For the petitioners.
</p><p id="p_7">            Mr. Rudradipta Nandy
                                   For the State.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                  The petitioners, apprehending arrest in connection with Shyampur Police

            Station Case No.212 of 2008 dated 30.09.2008 under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Sections 498A</a>, <a href="/doc/92983/" id="a_3">306</a>, <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the

            Indian Penal Code, have come to this Court for anticipatory bail.

</p><p id="p_9">                  We have heard the learned Advocates for the parties and have seen the

            case diary and other relevant record. This is a case of the year 2008.        The

            petitioners have been absconding since then. The prayer has been made for

            issuance of the W.P and A. In our opinion, the petitioners are not entitled to any

            relief.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                  Hence, the application for anticipatory bail is rejected.


</p><p id="p_11">                                             (Nishita Mhatre, J.)



                                       ( Indrajit Chatterjee, J.)
