<p id="p_1">            The State counsel has stated at the Bar that the case is at the

    fag end. The statement of accused under <a href="/doc/767287/" id="a_1">Section 313</a> Cr.P.C. has been

    recorded. The offence is under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_2">Section 376</a> IPC.

</p><p id="p_2">            So, no ground for grant of bail is made out.

            Consequently, the petition stands dismissed.
