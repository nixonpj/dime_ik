<p id="p_1">7   29-08-2018               Learned counsel for the petitioner; State and L.N.

</p><p id="p_2">                 Mithila University (hereinafter referred to as the 'University')

                 have assisted the Court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             2. On 07.08.2018, for the reasons recorded in the

                 order, cost of Rs. 10,000/- was imposed on the State but as an

                 indulgence, it was also observed that if the affidavit was filed by

                 the next date, the order imposing cost shall stand withdrawn.

</p><p id="p_4">                             3. Supplementary counter affidavit on behalf of

                 respondents no. 1 and 2 is on record. However, the same has

                 been filed on 09.08.2018, whereas the indulgence given by the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court CWJC No.4666 of 2018 (7) dt.29-08-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                        Court was only till the next date i.e., 08.08.2018. Thus, cost of

                        Rs. 10,000/- has to be deposited in terms of the order by the

                        authorities concerned and further the respondent no. 2 was to fix

                        responsibility on the officers so responsible.

</p><p id="p_5">                                      4. In view thereof, the Court finds that there is non

                        compliance of the order dated 07.08.2018, relating to payment of

                        cost.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                      5. Accordingly, let show cause be filed on behalf of

                        respondent no. 2, as to why appropriate action be not taken for

                        such non compliance.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                       6. The matter be listed on 11th September, 2018.


</p><p id="p_8">                                                                     (Ahsanuddin Amanullah, J.)



P. Kumar
