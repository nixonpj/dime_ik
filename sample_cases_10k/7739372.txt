<div class="doc_title">Ramu Paswan vs The State Of Bihar on 28 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.52047 of 2018
                   Arising Out of PS. Case No.-349 Year-2018 Thana- GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL COMP.
                                                    District- Sitamarhi
                 ======================================================
                 Ramu Paswan S/o Late Shivdhari Paswan, R/o Vill.- Gandhinagar, Ward No.
                 9, P.S.- Bairgania, District- Sitamarhi.

                                                                             ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :    Mr. Pushpendra Kumar Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s :    Mr. Surendra Prasad Singh
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRAKASH CHANDRA
                 JAISWAL
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   28-08-2018                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                  APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               The petitioner seeks bail in connection with

                  Complaint Case no. C-2/349 of 2018 registered for the offence

                  punishable under Section 30(A) of the Bihar Prohibition and

                  <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               35.100 lts. of Nepali Saufi wine is said to have been

                  recovered from possession of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                               It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

                  that no incriminating article has been recovered from conscious

                  physical possession of the petitioner. He has no concern with

                  the seized liquor. He has been falsely implicated in this case by

                  the personnel of Excise Department merely on suspicion while
               Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.52047 of 2018(2) dt.28-08-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                           2/2</span>




                       he was passing through the place of occurrence at the time of

                       occurrence and real culprits managed to escape dumping the

                       aforesaid liquor there seeing them. There is no compliance of

                       <a href="/doc/1137396/" id="a_2">Section 100</a> Cr.P.C. He has no criminal antecedent. He has been

                       languishing in custody since 28.06.2018.

</p><p id="p_5">                                    In the facts and circumstances of the case, the above

                       named petitioner is directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing

                       bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of

                       the like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned A.D.J.-II-

                       cum-Special Judge, <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_3">Excise Act</a>, Sitamarhi in connection with

                       Complaint Case no. C-2/349 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                             (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
rohit/-
</p>
