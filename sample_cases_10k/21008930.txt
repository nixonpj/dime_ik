       IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA AT BANGALORE

           DATED THIS THE 22ND DAY OF APRIL, 2013

                            BEFORE

        THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ASHOK B. HINCHIGERI

       WRIT PETITION Nos.16996-16997/2013 (KLR-RES)

BETWEEN:

1.     Sri M.Srinivas,
       S/o late S.V.Muniswamappa,
       Aged about 58 years,
       Kachohalli Village,
       Dasanapura Hobli,
       Bangalore North Taluk.

2.     Smt.K.Jayalakshmi,
       W/o Ramegowda,
       Aged about 50 years,
       Kachohalli Village,
       Dasanapura Hobli,
       Bangalore North Taluk.                        ...Petitioners

             (By Sri Keshava Murthy.H.B, Advocate)

AND:

1.     The Deputy Commissioner,
       Bangalore Urban District,
       Bangalore - 560 001.

2.     The Tahsildar,
       Bangalore North Taluk,
       Bangalore - 560 001.

3.     The Deputy Director for Land Records
       and Technical Asst. to Deputy Commissioner,
       In the Office of the D.C.,
                                 2


      Urban District,
      Bangalore - 560 001.                          ... Respondents

                 (By Smt.M.C.Nagashree, HCGP)

     These writ petitions are filed under Articles 226 and 227 of
the Constitution of India praying to issue a writ of mandamus to
R-1 to 3 to consider the representation dated 31.12.2012 and
31.12.2012 made by the petitioners as Annexure-Q and
Annexure-R and etc.

      These writ petitions coming on for preliminary hearing this
day, the Court made the following:

                            ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Smt.M.C.Nagashree, the learned High Court Government

Pleader is directed to take notice for the respondents.


</p><p id="p_2">      2. The petitioners' grievance is that the first respondent's

order, dated 29.7.2011 (Annexure-M) is not being implemented

by the respondent No.2.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">      3. Sri Keshava Murthy H.B., the learned counsel for the

petitioners submits that the petitioners' representations, dated

31.12.2012 (Annexures-Q and R) submitted to the respondent

Nos.2 and 3 have remained unconsidered.


</p><p id="p_5">      4.   Smt.M.C.Nagashree,       the   learned     High   Court

Government Pleader appearing for the respondents submits that
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  3</span>


the second respondent shall implement the first respondent's

order, dated 29.7.2011 within four months.


</p><p id="p_6">       5. I dispose of these petitions recording the submission

made    on   behalf   of   the   respondents   that   the   Deputy

Commissioner's order, dated 29.7.2011 would be implemented

within four months' time. No order as to costs.



</p><p id="p_7">                                                   Sd/-
</p><p id="p_8">                                                  JUDGE




MD
