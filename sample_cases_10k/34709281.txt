<div class="doc_title">Aftab Alam Alias Monu vs The State Of Jharkhand on 15 July, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                B.A. No.4143 of 2015

             Md. Aftab Alam @ Monu @ Aftab Alam                .....   Petitioner
                                    Versus
             The State of Jharkhand                            ....      Opposite Party

             CORAM:        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.C. MISHRA

             For the Petitioner          :       Mr. Rakesh Kumar
             For the State               :       A. P.P.
                                         -----

2/15.07.2015</pre><p id="p_1">        Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel for the State.
</p><p id="p_2">                    Petitioner has been made accused for the offence under <a href="/doc/1183069/" id="a_1">Sections 386</a>, <a href="/doc/1688284/" id="a_2">387</a>,
             <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_4">471</a>, <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_5">414</a> / <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_7">120-B</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1961417/" id="a_8">Sections
             25</a>(1-B)(a)/26/35 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_9">Arms Act</a>, in connection with Lower Bazar P.S. Case
             No.98 of 2015, corresponding to G.R. No.1487 of 2015.
</p><p id="p_3">                    Petitioner was apprehended with firearms at the bus stand along with other
             co-accused persons while they had gone for realising levy.
</p><p id="p_4">                    In the facts and circumstances of the case, I am not inclined to release the
             petitioner on bail. Accordingly, prayer for bail stands rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                                                    (H. C. Mishra, J)
   R.Kumar
 </p>
