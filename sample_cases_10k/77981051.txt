<div class="doc_title">Sonal Wife Of Sanjay Joshi And ... vs Unknown on 12 June, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">         C/MCA/348/2014                                     ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

     MISC.CIVIL APPLICATION (FOR TRANSFER) NO. 348 of 2014

================================================================
   SONAL WIFE OF SANJAY JOSHI AND DAUGHTER OF KANAIYALAL
                  NANJIBAHI VYAS....Applicant(s)
                            Versus
           SANJAY BIPINCHANDRA JOSHI....Opponent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
VIRAL K SHAH, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MR VAIBHAV A VYAS, ADVOCATE for the Opponent(s) No. 1
================================================================

        CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE RAVI R.TRIPATHI

                              Date : 12/06/2014


                               ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">     Present application is filed by the wife seeking transfer
of Civil Misc. Application No.114 of 2013 pending in the Court
of learned District Judge, Jamnagar to the Court of learned
District Judge, Amreli or to any court situated near to the
Court of learned District Judge, Amreli.
</p>

<p id="p_2">     Heard learned advocate Mr. Tatvadip Jani for learned
advocate     Mr.      Viral   K.    Shah.         The   matter      requires
consideration.
</p>

<p id="p_3">     Rule.      Learned advocate Mr. Niraj Soni for learned
advocate Mr. Vaibhav A. Vyas appearing for the opponent
waives service of Rule.
</p>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   Page 1 of 2</span>
<p id="p_4">          C/MCA/348/2014                               ORDER



      At the request made by learned advocates for the
parties, the matter is taken up for final hearing.
</p>

<p id="p_5">      Taking into consideration that fact that wife is residing
at Rajula and there are other proceedings pending at Rajula,
it will be in the interest of justice if this Civil Misc. Application
No.114 of 2013 is also transferred to Rajula.
</p>

<p id="p_6">      Learned advocate appearing for the opponent expresses
an apprehension/anxiety that father of the wife i.e. father-in-
law of the husband was a public prosecutor and that may
influence the proceedings in the Court at Rajula.
</p>

<p id="p_7">      This Court is of the opinion that the apprehension is far-
fetched and also misplaced.
</p>

<p id="p_8">      In view of the same, this application is allowed.         Civil
Misc. Application No.114 of 2013 pending in the Court of
learned District Judge, Jamnagar is transferred to the Court of
learned District Judge, Rajula, District-Amreli. Rule is made
absolute.
</p>



<p id="p_9">                                            (RAVI R.TRIPATHI, J.)
ila
