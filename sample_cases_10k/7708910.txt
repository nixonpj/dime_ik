       IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA
         CIRCUIT BENCH AT DHARWAD

  DATED THIS THE 25 t h DAY OF JULY, 2013

                      BEFORE

       THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE N.ANANDA

    CRIMINAL PETITION No.10975 OF 2013

BETWEEN:

     SHANKAR S/O. TULAJAPPA RATHOD
     AGE: 23 YEARS, OCC: COOLIE WORK,
     R/O. ARASHINKERI, TQ: &amp; DIST: KOPPAL
                                       ... PETITIONER
(By Sri. D L LADKHAN ADV.)

AND:

     STATE OF KARNATAKA
     BY RURAL POLICE STATION,
     KOPPAL,
     REPTD. BY STATE PUBLIC PROSECUTOR,
     HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA,
     CIRCUIT BENCH, DHARWAD
                                    ... RESPONDENT
(By Sri.K.S.PATIL, HCGP)

      THIS CRIMINAL PETITION IS FILED U/S 439 OF
CR.P.C. SEEKING TO ISSUE DIRECTION TO THE LEARNED
JMFC    COURT,    KOPPAL,  IN   CRIME  NO.74/2013
REGISTERED BY KOPPAL RURAL POLICE STATION, U/S
302, 201 OF <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> TO ENLARGE ACCUSED NO.4, WHO IS IN
JUDICIAL   CUSTODY    AND   TO   ISSUE  NECESSARY
                                 :2:




DIRECTION TO THE JAIL AUTHORITIES TO ENLARGE
ACCUSED NO.4 IN CRIME NO.74/2013 REGISTERED BY
KOPPAL RURAL POLICE STATION U/S 302, 201 OF <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>.

      This criminal petition coming on for Orders
this day, the Court made the following:

                           O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       The petitioner is arrayed as accused No.4 in

Crime        No.74/2013         registered     for    offences

punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_3">Sections 302</a> and <a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_4">201</a> of the

Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">       2.     The    case      of   prosecution   rests   upon

circumstantial evidence.            The investigation records

would reveal that prosecution has relied on the

confession statement said to have been made by

accused No.4 and recovery of his motor cycle.


</p><p id="p_4">       3.     As    per   investigation    records,   accused

No.4    is   alleged      to   have    made   a   confessional

statement that he had used his motor cycle to flee

away from the place of incident and the said motor
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            :3:</span>




     cycle was seized on the information volunteered

     by accused No.4. The investigating officer has not

     collected any material to show that accused No.4

     was seen fleeing away from the place of incident

     on   his       motor     cycle.         Therefore,         confessional

     statement said to have been made by accused

     No.4         cannot    be        considered        as   prima     faice

     evidence.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">             4.     The petition is accepted. The petitioner

     (accused No.4) is                released on       bail,    subject   to

     following conditions:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">          I.        The petitioner (accused No.4) shall
                    execute       a     bond      for    a   sum     of
                    Rs.50,000/-         (Rupees     Fifty    Tho usand
                    only) and offer two sureties for the
                    like    sum       to    the    satisfaction      of
                                                                           *Corrected V.C.O.
</blockquote><p id="p_7">*Committal          sessions Court;                                        dtd: 2.08.2013


          II.       The petitioner shall not intimidate or
                    tamper with prosecution witnesses;
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                         :4:</span>




   III.   The petitioner shall regularly attend
          the court.
</p>
