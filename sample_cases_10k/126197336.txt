                      contained in para 7(vi) of the order dated February 20, 2013 was

                      body has been duly recognized by the competent authority, in that

                      case, such Managing Committee, with permission of the

                      competent authority, shall be at liberty to take loan etc. against

                      its immovable property in the best interest of the institution.
