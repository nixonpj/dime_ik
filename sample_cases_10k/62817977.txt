<div class="doc_title">Mr.R.S.Gill vs Unknown on 23 April, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                                             [1]

3
            S.B.CRIMINAL MISC.4th BAIL APPLICATION NO.8795/2014
                    (Hari Ram     Vs. State of Rajasthan)

                             Date of order : :     23.04.2015

                     HON'BLE MS.JUSTICE NIRMALJIT KAUR


         Mr.R.S.Gill, counsel for the petitioner.
         Mr.Pankaj Awasthi, counsel for the State.

                                        &lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;

               This is the fourth bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.

         The petitioner was arrested in connection with FIR No.38/2013,

         Police Station Mathania, District Jodhpur for the offence under

         <a href="/doc/704277/" id="a_2">Section 8</a>/<a href="/doc/325366/" id="a_3">18</a> of the NDPS Act.

               11 witnesses have already been examined. The matter is at

         the fag end. This Court does not deem it proper to release the

         petitioner on bail at this stage.

               Accordingly, the bail application is dismissed. However, the

         trial court is directed to expedite the trial.



                                                          (NIRMALJIT KAUR), J.
