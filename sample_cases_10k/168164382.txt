                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.14045 of 2012
                 ======================================================
                 1. Manju Devi W/O Sri Binod Prasad Singh Resident Of Village- Bakhari,
                 P.S.- Motipur, District- Muzaffarpur

                                                                     .... ....   Petitioner
                                                 Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar
                 2. Ram Chandra Rai S/O Late Dukhit Rai R/O Village- Bakhari, P.S.-
                 Motipur, District- Muzaffarpur
                 3. Manoj Rai S/O Ram Chandra Rai R/O Village- Bakhari, P.S.- Motipur,

                 5. Md. Majhar S/O Md. Ayub R/O Village- Bakhari, P.S.- Motipur,
                 District- Muzaffarpur
                 6. Brahmdeo Kumar S/O Rijhan Rai R/O Village- Bakhari, P.S.- Motipur,
                 District- Muzaffarpur

                                                      .... .... Opposite Parties
                 ======================================================

                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE JYOTI SARAN
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   06-04-2012            Heard Mr. J.P. Verma, learned counsel for the petitioner and

                 Mr. Shardanand Jha, learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the

                 State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                          The petition is directed against the order dated 24.12.2011

                 passed by Ms. Kalpna Srivastava, learned Judicial Magistrate 1 st

                 Class, Muzaffarpur in Protest-cum-Complaint No. 978 of 2010

                 arising from Motipur P.S. Case No. 143 of 2009, whereby the

                 learned Magistrate after considering the entire matter has been

                 pleased to dismiss the complaint under <a href="/doc/443138/" id="a_1">Section 203</a> of the Code of

                 Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                          Facts of the matter in brief is that the petitioner herein had
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.14045 of 2012 (2) dt.06-04-2012</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                        2</span>




                         initially filed a complaint case bearing No. 1353 of 2009 on

                         22.08.2009 alleging disappearance of her brother-in-law one year

                         back on 12.09.2008 and which knowledge the complainant

                         gathered only when the accused arrived for taking possession of

                         the piece of land on the basis of a sale deed said to have been

                         executed by the said brother-in-law of the informant. The said

                         complaint was registered as an F.I.R. under <a href="/doc/99487/" id="a_2">Section 156(3)</a> of the

                         Code of Criminal Procedure giving rise to Motipur P.S. Case No.

                         143 of 2009 and the police upon investigation submitted final

                         form, holding the case untrue against the accused.

</p><p id="p_4">                                  The petitioner being aggrieved by the submission of final

                         form, filed protest-cum-complaint case which was registered as C-

                         No. 978 of 2010 and which has since been dismissed under

                         <a href="/doc/443138/" id="a_3">Section 203</a> of the Code by the impugned order dated 24.12.2011

                         considering the materials, the allegations made and the delay in

                         raising the issue. I find no infirmity in the order passed by the

                         Court below.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                  The application is dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                                                           (Jyoti Saran, J)

Bibhash/-
</p>
