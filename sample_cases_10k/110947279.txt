  UPON hearing the counsel the Court made the following
                     O R D E R
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Heard the learned counsels for the parties and

perused the relevant material.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      Permission             to        place/file           additional

facts/documents is granted.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      Exemption from filing O.T. is granted.

      We find no merit in the present Special Leave

Petitions.          The      Special       Leave    Petitions      are

accordingly dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      In     view     of    our    above   order    the     settlement

arrived at between the Bank and the Borrower would

stand nullified and consequentially O.A. No.149 of 2011

filed by the Bank before the Debts Recovery Tribunal at

Jabalpur     (“DRT”    for        short)   shall    stand     revived.

Keeping in view the amount that is the subject matter

of recovery in the said O.A. (No.149 of 2011), the sum

of Rs.15 crores which has been received by the Bank

pursuant to the settlement (by and between the Bank and

the Borrower) now set aside shall remain with the Bank

and abide by such quantification of dues that may be

made by the DRT.           We direct the DRT to expedite the

proceedings and conclude the same within six months

from today.    We make it clear that as the order of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                           3</span>


DRT dated 9th April, 2013 holding the proceedings to be

maintainable has not been challenged till date before

any forum the same may be understood to have attained

finality in law.   We further direct that the enquiry as

directed by the High Court in terms of the impugned

order be carried out and be concluded as expeditiously

as possible and action, as required, in accordance with

law will follow.
</p>
