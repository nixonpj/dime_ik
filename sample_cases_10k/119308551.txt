                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.50359 of 2014
                    Arising Out of Laheriasarai P.S. Case No.356 Year- 2014 Thana -LAHERIASARAI District-
                                                          DARBHANGA
                 ======================================================
                 Sanjeet Kumar @ Sangeet Kumar, son of Deepak Purvey, resident of
                 Mohalla - Belwaganj, P.S. Laheriasarai, Distt. Darbhanga.
                                                                         .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                    .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s   :    Mr. Girish Chandra Jha, Adv.
                 For the State          :    Mr. Bharat Lal, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA PRAKASH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   16-12-2014                   Heard learned counsel for the Petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The Petitioner seeks bail in a case instituted for the

                 offence under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                Considering that apart from extra judicial confession of

                 the Petitioner of having killed his own brother, there is no other

                 material against him and he has fair antecedent, let the Petitioner

                 above named, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                 5,000/- (Five thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each

                 or any other surety to be fixed by the Court below to the

                 satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Darbhanga, in connection

                 with Laheriasarai P.S. Case No. 356 of 2014 subject to the

                 following conditions: (i) That one of the bailors will be a close

                 relative of the Petitioner who will give an affidavit giving

                 genealogy as to how he is related with the Petitioner. The bailor
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.50359 of 2014 (2) dt.16-12-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                2/2</span>




                  will also undertake to inform the Court if there is any change in

                  the address of the Petitioner. (ii) That the affidavit shall clearly

                  state that the Petitioner is not an accused in any other case and if

                  he is, he shall not be released on bail. (iii) That the bailor shall

                  also state on affidavit that he will inform the court concerned if the

                  Petitioner is implicated in any other case of similar nature after his

                  release in the present case and thereafter the court below will be at

                  liberty to initiate the proceeding for cancellation of bail on the

                  ground of misuse. (iv) That the Petitioner will give an undertaking

                  that he will receive the police papers on the given date and be

                  present on date fixed for charge and if he fails to do so on two

                  given dates and delays the trial in any manner, his bail will be

                  liable to be cancelled for reasons of misuse. (v) That the Petitioner

                  will be well represented on each date and if he fails to do so on

                  two consecutive dates, his bail will be liable to be cancelled.



</p><p id="p_4">                                                                    (Anjana Prakash, J)
    S.Ali/-
</p>
