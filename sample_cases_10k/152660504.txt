<p id="p_1">             Prayer in this petition is for quashing of the FIR on the basis of

the compromise.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Vide order dated 02.12.2016, it was directed to the

complainant/victim to approach the Investigating Officer with regard to

cancellation of report.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Learned State counsel, on instructions from HC Rajinder Pal,

submits that cancellation report has already been submitted before the trial

Court and the same has been accepted on 04.03.2017.

</p><p id="p_4">             In view of the above, this petition is dismissed as infructuous.
