         following reliefs, as the acquittal appeal is pending
         in the Court.

                     direct      the Learned          4th    Additional           (Ad-

                     applicant;

<p id="p_5">                       The Passport is surrendered at the Sessions
         Court, i.e. 4th Additional (Ad-hoc) Sessions Judge,
         Anand.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                       Learned          APP       has          not        pointed           out       any
         material or pleadings militating against granting of
         the   prayer,         as      the     passport            at      present           is     only
         required       for      the     purpose            of     its       renewal            and        no
         foreign travel is contemplated.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                       The counsel for the applicant has assured
         that passport will be surrendered once it is renewed
         and in case if any foreign travel is contemplated, a
         fresh    application            will       be       made       for      releasing            the
         renewed passport, if it is renewed by the authority.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                       The counsel's submission qua exemption to
         the applicant from surrendering the passport cannot
         be accepted, as looking to the earlier orders and the
         pendency of the Criminal Appeal, this Court is of the
         considered view that the passport is ordered to be
