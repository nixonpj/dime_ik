<div class="doc_title">Mr. Ramit Mehta vs An Application Has Been Filed By ... on 4 March, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                S.B. CIVIL CONTEMPT PETITION NO.586/2014
                                    IN
                  S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO. 996/2003

         Date of Order :: 4.3.2015

                    HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ARUN BHANSALI


         Mr. Ramit Mehta, for the petitioner/s.

                                        -----

               An application has been filed by the petitioners seeking

         impleadment of another party as respondent No.4.

               It is inter-alia indicated that the respondents No.1 to 3 in

         their reply have specifically pointed out that the amount of

         compensation directed to be paid by the judgment dated

         26.11.2013, is to be paid by the Defence Estate Officer, Jodhpur

         and communication sent by the said Estate Officer has also been

         placed on record seeking time to do the needful. However,

         despite passage of sufficiently long time, nothing has been done

         by   the   said   respondent   No.4,   which   amounts   to   willful

         disobedience of the order passed by this Court.

               In view of the submissions made, the application is

         allowed. The respondent No.4 as prayed in the application is

         impleaded as party respondent.

               Amended cause title filed by the petitioners is taken on

         record.

               Issue notice to the respondent No.4. Alongwith copy of the

         contempt petition, a copy of the application filed by the

         petitioners seeking impleadment of respondent No.4 be also sent

         alongwith the notice.

               List on 20.3.2015.

                                                   (ARUN BHANSALI), J.
</pre>
