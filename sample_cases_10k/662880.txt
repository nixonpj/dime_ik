<div class="doc_title">Md.Rashid Sah @ Md.Rashid vs State Of Bihar on 7 October, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">                        IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                       Cr.Misc. No.28698 of 2010
                          Md.Rashid Sah @ Md.Rashid, S/o Late Sobrati Sah, R/o Village-
                          Hardas Bigha, P.S.-Khurrupur, District- Patna.
                                                                         ...........Petitioner.
                                                   Versus
                                             STATE OF BIHAR

                                                    -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2.      07.10.2010                        Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                     the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  2.      Petitioner   is    an    accused    for   offence

                      punishable under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_1">section 376</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  3.      The allegation against the petitioner is that he

                      raped his ex-wife, who had taken divorce from him three months

                      back and the period of iddat was going on. It is also found that the

                      victim lady was 28 years old and she was alone at her rented

                      quarter when she was raped by the petitioner regarding which the

                      victim lady had given specific statements.

</p><p id="p_4">                                  4.      In the said circumstances, this court does not

                      find any merit in this case at this stage. Accordingly, this petition

                      is dismissed with liberty to the petitioner to renew his prayer for

                      bail after completion of one year in custody.



</p><pre id="pre_2">Sujit                                                                 (S.N. Hussain, J.)
