                 Rohan Krishnamoorthy                                      ...    Respondent


                 Prayer: Transfer Civil Miscellaneous Petition filed under Section 24 of
                 the Civil Procedure Code, to withdraw and transfer H.M.O.P. No.208 of
                 2019 pending on the file of Subordinate Court, Tambaram to the file of
                 IV Additional Family Court, Chennai.


                                      Petitioner       : Ms.V.Pushkala

                                      Respondent       : Mr.S.Anand

                                                    ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">                          The transfer civil miscellaneous petition has been filed to

                 withdraw and transfer H.M.O.P. No.208 of 2019 pending on the file of

                 Sub Court, Tambaram, to the file of IV Additional Family Court,

                 Chennai.
</p>

<p id="p_2">http://www.judis.nic.in
                    Page 1/4
                                                                  Tr. C.M.P. No.773 of 2019 and
                                                                        C.M.P. No.21611 of 2019



</p><p id="p_3">                          2. When the matter came up for hearing today, learned counsel

                 appearing for the respondent submitted that, the respondent, has no

                 objection for transferring H.M.O.P. No.208 of 2019 pending on the file

                 of Sub Court, Tambaram, to the file of IV Additional Family Court,

                 Chennai.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                          3. In view of the same, H.M.O.P. No.208 of 2019 pending on the

                 file of Sub Court, Tambaram, is withdrawn from the said court and

                 transferred to the file of IV Additional Family Court, Chennai. The

                 learned IV Additional Judge, Family Court, Chennai, is expected to

                 conclude the trial, as expeditiously as possible, without adjourning the

                 matter beyond thirty working days, at any point of time, in order to

                 bring the issue to a logical end.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                          4. With the above direction, the Transfer Civil Miscellaneous

                 Petition is ordered. No costs. Consequently, the connected civil

                 miscellaneous petition is closed.</p><pre id="pre_2">
