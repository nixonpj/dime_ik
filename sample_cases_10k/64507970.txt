<p id="p_3">                                      CORAM  :  SMT. VASANTI A. NAIK &amp;

    Oral Judgment : (Per Shri A.S.Chandurkar,J.)

                 By this petition, the petitioner challenges the communication dated

    30/04/2001  by which  the  Educational  Officer (Secondary)  has refused to

     240A-J-WP-64-03                                                                                 2/3


    approve the appointment of the petitioner on the post of part time Librarian




                                                                                            
    on account of  a ban on recruitment of non-teaching staff. 
</p>



                                                                    
<p id="p_5">    2.           The   petitioner   came   to   be   appointed   on   the   post   of   part   time




                                                                   
    Librarian pursuant to permission being granted by the Education Officer on

    23/11/2000 to fill in the said post.  After advertising the post, the petitioner




                                                     
    was  duly   appointed.     The   approval   was   refused   only   on   the   ground   that

    there was a ban on recruitment.
</p>                                      
                                     
<p id="p_6">    3.           This Court while admitting the writ petition, passed an interim

    order   on   30/06/2003   directing   the   respondent   No.3   to   grant   provisional
             


    approval to the appointment of the petitioner if she was otherwise eligible.
</p>          



<p id="p_7">    4.           Having   heard   Shri   Maheshwari,   the   learned   counsel   for   the





    petitioner as well as Shri N. Joshi, learned Assistant Government Pleader for

    respondent Nos.1 to 3,  we are of the view that the petitioner is entitled for





    being granted approval subject to she be found eligible.   Her appointment

    was after grant of permission by the respondent No.3 to fill in the post.   It is

    to   be   noted   that   since   passing   of   the   interim   order,   the   petitioner   has

    continued in employment.  Hence the following order is passed : 
</p>
<p id="p_8">    (i)         The   communication   dated   30/04/2001  issued   by  the   Education
                Officer refusing to grant approval is set aside.
</p>
