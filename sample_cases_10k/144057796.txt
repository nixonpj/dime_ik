<p id="p_1">_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)   : Mr. Sudhir Kumar Jain
For Respondent(s) : Mr. R.R. Gurjar-P.P.
_____________________________________________________
                HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA
                              Order
12/09/2017

     Petitioners have filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Code of

Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking anticipatory bail in F.I.R. No.

58/2013 registered at Police Station Manpur, Distt. Dausa for

offences under <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_2">Sections 143</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_3">307</a> of Indian Penal Code, 1860 and

subsequently charge sheet was filed under <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_4">section 143</a> and <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_5">302</a> of

Indian Penal Code, 1860


     Learned counsel for the petitioners has submitted that

petitioners were students, at the time of the alleged occurrence.

Petitioners had joined investigation and were found innocent.

Challan was presented against the co-accused of the petitioners.

Petitioners have been summoned to face the trial on an application

moved by the prosecution under <a href="/doc/435819/" id="a_6">Section 319</a> Code of Criminal

Procedure, 1973. Petitioners are ready to surrender before the
                                    (2 of 2)
                                                            [CRLMB-11029/2017]

trial Court and face the trial.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      Learned State Counsel on the other hand has opposed the

petition.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      In the present case, there is land dispute between the

parties. During investigation, it transpired that father of the

petitioners   and   their   uncles     were   liable   to   be   challened.

Consequently, challan was presented against the father and

uncles of the petitioners. Since, petitioners were found innocent,

during investigation, it would be just and expedient to order the

release of the petitioners on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      Accordingly this petition is disposed of with a direction that

in case petitioners surrender before the trial Court within fifteen

days from today, they be released on bail subject to the

satisfaction of the trial Court.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                                                 (SABINA)J.
</p>
