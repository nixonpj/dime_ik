<div class="doc_title">Chaitanya Mandal vs Unknown on 10 May, 2018</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                             1</span>

</p><p id="p_2">330.    10.05.2018
                                         C.R.M. 2438 of 2018
Aloke    Court 28

                     In Re : An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of
                     the Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 08.05.2018 in connection
                     with Tamluk P.S. Case No. 585 of 2017 dated 28.12.2017 under
                     <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>/<a href="/doc/1517318/" id="a_3">436</a>/<a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506(2)</a>/<a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_5">427</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                     And

                       In the matter of: Chaitanya Mandal
                                                                ...Petitioner
                              Mr. Deep Chaim Kabir
                              Mr. Mazhar Hossain Chowdhury
                                                     ... for the petitioner

                              Ms. Faria Hossain
                              Ms. Debjani Sahu
                                                      ... for the State



         allowed             It is submitted on behalf of the petitioner that there is a

                     landlord and tenant dispute between the parties and he has been

                     falsely implicated in the instant case.

</p><p id="p_4">                             Learned counsel for the State opposes the prayer for bail.

                              Having considered the materials in the case diary and

                     bearing in mind the nature of allegations in the light of the

                     aforesaid submissions made on behalf of the petitioner, we are

                     inclined in granting anticipatory bail to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_5">                              Accordingly, we direct that in the event of arrest, the

                     petitioner shall be released on anticipatory bail upon furnishing

                     bond of Rs.10,000/-(Rupees Ten Thousand only) with two sureties

                     of like amount, to the satisfaction of the arresting officer and also

                     subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_6">Section 438(2)</a> of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                            2</span>


Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973.

</p><p id="p_6">           The application for anticipatory bail is, accordingly,

allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">           Urgent Photostat Certified copy of this order, if applied for,

be supplied expeditiously after complying with all necessary legal

formalities.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

(Ravi Krishan Kapur, J.)             (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
