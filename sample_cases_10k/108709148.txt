<div class="doc_title">State vs . Inderjeet Singh on 19 July, 2014</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Mr.Devender Nain</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE COURT OF SH. DEVENDER NAIN
        METROPOLITAN MAGISTRATE: WEST­05, DELHI


                            FIR No. 440/2005


Case ID: R1330402006
State Vs. Inderjeet Singh
PS Tilak Nagar
U/s 380/411 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> 


Date of Institution of case               :     25.01.2006



Date of Judgment                          :     19.07.2014


JUDGMENT:
</pre>
<pre id="pre_2">a)     Date of offence                    :     28.06.2005

b)     Offence complained of              :     U/s   380/411 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>

c)     Name of Accused, his               :     Inderjeet Singh,
       parentage &amp; residence                    S/o Amar Singh,
                                                R/o   5­C/19A,   West  
                                                Block, Vishnu Garden, 
                                                Delhi.

d)     Plea of Accused                    :     Compounded

e)     Final order                        :     Convicted

FIR No. 440/05                                                          1 of 3
PS Tilak Nagar
</pre><p id="p_1"> BRIEF FACTS AND REASONS FOR DECISION:
</p>
<p id="p_2">                 Accused as well as complainant / victim wish to compound 

the offence punishable u/s 411 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">IPC</a> and permission has been sought from 

the Court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                 I have examined the parties.   All the parties have entered 

into   a   compromise   without   any   force,   fear   or   coercion.     Separate 

statements of parties recorded in this regard.  In view of the statements, 

offence punishable u/s 411 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a> stands compounded.  Accused is hereby 

acquitted for the offence u/s 411 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_5">IPC</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                 Accused further voluntarily submits that he wishes to plead 

guilty   to   the   offence   punishable   u/s   380   <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_6">IPC</a>.     The   consequences   of 

pleading guilty have been explained to the Accused.   Accused submits 

that he is pleading guilty without any fear, force or coercion and further 

submit   that   lenient   view   may   be   taken   against   him.     Accordingly, 

Accused is hereby convicted for offence punishable u/s 380 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_7">IPC</a>.</p><pre id="pre_3">




Announced in the Open Court                               (DEVENDER NAIN)
on 19th day of July, 2014                               MM(WEST­05):DELHI




FIR No. 440/05                                                                         2 of 3
PS Tilak Nagar
 ORDER ON SENTENCE 

</pre><p id="p_5">                 Arguments on the point of sentence heard on behalf of both 

the sides and I have perused the record of the case and considering the 

age   of   convict   and   the   fact   that  convict   belong   to   the  poor   strata   of 

society and only earning member of his family and family members will 

suffer   great   hardship   if   hard   view   is   taken   against   him,   I   am   of   the 

opinion that the sentence for the period already undergone will meet the 

ends of justice.   I accordingly sentence the convict Inderjeet Singh S/o 

Amar Singh for the period already undergone alongwith fine of Rs.500/­ 

for offence punishable u/s 380 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_8">IPC</a> IDSI 15 days alongwith TRC.  Fine 

paid.     Bail   Bond   stands   cancelled   and   Surety   be   discharged,   if   any. 

Original  documents,  if any, be returned  to the rightful  person  against 

acknowledgment and after cancellation of endorsement, if any.  

</p><p id="p_6">                 File be consigned to the Record Room after due compliance.</p><pre id="pre_4">




Announced in the Open Court                                   (DEVENDER NAIN)
       th
on 19  day of July, 2014                                    MM(WEST­05):DELHI




FIR No. 440/05                                                                               3 of 3
PS Tilak Nagar
