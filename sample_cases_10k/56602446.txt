                  State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  The Petitioners seek bail in Dariyapur P.S. Case No.

                  150 of 2013 instituted under Section(s) 396<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> Indian Penal Code</a>

                  pending before the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Saran at Chapra.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  Considering that the circumstantial material suggests

                  that the Petitioners were involved in this serious case of dacoity
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">     Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.35108 of 2014 (3) dt.20-01-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                               2/2</span>




                 with murder, I am not inclined to grant them bail.

</p><p id="p_4">                                  Prayer for bail is rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                  The Trial Court is directed to expedite the trial for

                 which reason the Trial Court shall send a list of the witnesses

                 fixing specific dates for their examination along with a copy of

                 this order to the concerned Superintendent of Police, who is

                 directed to ensure the attendance of the witnesses on the date fixed

                 so that there is no further delay in the trial.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                           (Anjana Prakash, J)
