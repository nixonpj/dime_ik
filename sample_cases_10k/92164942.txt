<div class="doc_title">Juna And Another vs State Of Punjab And Others on 22 April, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA, CHANDIGARH

                                                                      CRM-M-4954 of 2014
                                                                      Date of Decision :22.04.2014

                 Juna and another                                              ..........Petitioners

                                               Versus


                 State of Punjab and others                                   ..........Respondents

                 CORAM HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE R.P.NAGRATH

                 Present:       Mr.Mohd. Salim, Advocate for the petitioners.

                                Mr.A.S.Kler, AAG Punjab.

                                  ***********
                 R.P.NAGRATH ,J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                This petition has been filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. with a prayer for

                 seeking directions to the official respondents to protect the life and liberty of the

                 petitioners from the hands of respondents No.4 to 7 by way of providing adequate

                 security and restraining them not to interfere in the married life of the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_2">                                Response     by way of affidavit of Varinder Singh, PPS, DSP, Sub

                 Division Dasuya, District Hoshiarpur, has been filed by learned State counsel which

                 is taken on record.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               Learned State counsel on instructions from SI Ashwani Kumar, submits

                 that statements of petitioner No.1 and respondents No.4 to 7 were recorded and there

                 is no threat perception from the private respondents to the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_4">                               In view of the above, no further indulgence is required by this Court and

                 the instant petition is disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               If, there is still apprehension to the life or liberty of petitioners, they are

                 at liberty to make fresh representation to the SSP concerned.



</p><pre id="pre_2">                 22.04.2014                                                 (R.P.NAGRATH)
                 anil                                                          JUDGE




Anil Kumar
2014.04.24 09:34
