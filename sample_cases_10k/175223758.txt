                               PRESENT

          THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE B.SUDHEENDRA KUMAR

     TUESDAY, THE 04TH DAY OF JUNE 2019 / 14TH JYAISHTA, 1941

                     Bail Appl..No. 3657 of 2019

 AGAINST THE ORDER/JUDGMENT IN CRMC 889/2019 of DISTRICT COURT &amp;
                      SESSIONS COURT,KOLLAM

      CRIME NO. 804/2019 OF Kundara Police Station , Kollam


PETITIONER/1ST ACCUSED:

             AJESH RAJAN@FEBIN
             AGED 18 YEARS
             S/O.RAJAN, JOSEPH BHAVAN,
             PADAPPAKKARA, PERAYAM CHERRY,
             PEREAYAM VILLAGE, KOLLAM-691601

             BY ADVS.
             SRI.K.SIJU
             SMT.S.SEETHA


RESPONDENTS/STATE:

      1      STATE OF KERALA
             REPRESENTED BY PUBLIC PROSECUTOR,
             HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM-31.

      2      THE STATION HOUSE OFFICE,
             KUNDARA POLICE STATION,
             KOLLAM DISTRICT-691501


             SMT.V.SREEJA,PUBLIC PROSECUTOR


THIS BAIL APPLICATION HAVING COME UP FOR ADMISSION ON 04.06.2019,
THE COURT ON THE SAME DAY PASSED THE FOLLOWING:
                                -2-

Bail Appl..No. 3657 of 2019




                          ORDER
</pre>


<p id="p_1">     The    petitioner    is   the    1st   accused     in        Crime

No.804/2019 of Kundara Police Station registered for the

offences punishable under Sections 143, 147, 148, 323,

324 and 326 read with <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_1">Section 149</a> IPC.

</p><p id="p_2">     2.    The petitioner has filed this application under

Section 438 Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3.    Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     4.    The learned Public Prosecutor has submitted

that the petitioner has no criminal antecedent. It appears

that the defacto complainant sustained injury on his right

wrist in the incident.    Iron rod was the weapon used to

inflict injury on the injured. The said iron rod was

recovered from the place of occurrence. Therefore, no

recovery is to be effected from the petitioner. In the said

circumstances,      the   custodial     interrogation        of     the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  -3-</span>

Bail Appl..No. 3657 of 2019


petitioner is not necessary. That apart, the petitioner is

aged only 18 years. He has also no criminal antecedent.

In the said circumstances, I am inclined to grant pre-

arrest bail to the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     In the result, this bail application stands allowed and

the respondents are directed to release the petitioner on

bail in the event of his arrest in the above said crime, on

condition   of     the   petitioner     executing   a    bond   for

Rs.35,000/- (Rupees thirty five thousand only), with two

solvent sureties, each for the like sum to the satisfaction

of the 2nd respondent before whom the petitioner shall

surrender within ten days, if not arrested in the meantime

and subject to the following further conditions:

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">    1. The       petitioner   shall    report   before   the
        Investigating      Officer     on   every   Sunday
        between 9.00 a.m. and 11.00 a.m. for
        three months and thereafter, as and when
        required by the Investigating Officer for
        interrogation.
</blockquote><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                  -4-</span>

<p id="p_6">Bail Appl..No. 3657 of 2019


</p><p id="p_7">     2. The       petitioner   shall   not   intimidate   or
          influence the witnesses or in any way
          tamper with the investigation.
</p><p id="p_8">     3. The petitioner shall not get involved in any
          other offence during the pendency of this
          case.
</p>
<p id="p_9">
</p><p id="p_10">                                               Sd/-
</p>
<p id="p_11">                                   B.SUDHEENDRA KUMAR

                                              JUDGE
Nkr/04.06.19
