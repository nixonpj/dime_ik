<div class="doc_title">State Of M.P. Th.Collector,Sagar vs Krishi Pandit Shrimant Seth ... on 1 December, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                         S.A. No. 291/2004
01.12.2014
      Shri      A. Namdeo,     learned     Panel   Lawyer    for   the
State/appellant.
      Heard on the question of admission.
      The appeal is admitted on the following substantial
questions of law:-
          (1)      Whether   the    appellate   court    below
          grossly erred in decreeing the suit in favour of
          the respondent/plaintiff totally ignoring the fact</pre>
<p id="p_1">          that there was no document or any evidence to
          indicate that the respondent was the owner of
          the land in question ?
</p><p id="p_2">          (2)      Whether in view of section 58 of the
          Land Revenue Code, the plaintiff is not
          required to pay land revenue and premium of
          the disputed land to the State Government ?
          (3)      Whether lower appellate court is correct
          in holding that the disputed land belongs to
          Municipal Council        and therefore no amount
          towards land revenue or premium               can be
          recovered from the plaintiff ?
          (4)      Whether in the facts and circumstances
          of the case the conclusion recorded by the
          court below is justified ?
      Issue notice to the respondent on payment of P.F. within
a week.
</p><p id="p_3">      C.C. as per rules.
</p><p id="p_4">                                                   (R.S. Jha)
