<div class="doc_title">Vishnu Adarsh Shishu Shiksha ... vs Brajesh Kumar Singh on 17 September, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                   Writ Appeal 912/2012
17.9.2012
       Shri N.S.Ruprah, Advocate for the appellant.
       Shri Rajendra Pandey, Advocate for respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">Nos.1&amp;2.
</p><p id="p_2">       Shri P.K.Kaurav, Deputy Advocate General for
respondent Nos. 3&amp;4.
</p><p id="p_3">       It is submitted by learned counsel for the appellant
that for allowing grand-in-aid to the appellants, matter was
referred to the State Government by the appellants but till
date   no   decision   has   been   taken    by   the   State
Government. So far as salary is concerned, it has been
paid to the respondent Nos.1&amp;2 in compliance of the
order dated 9.8.2007 passed in Writ Petition No.94/1999
but the learned Single Judge has directed the appellants
herein to pay salary to respondent No.1 at par with the
employees who were getting grant-in-aid from the State
Government. It is further submitted that till date the State
Government has not decided the matter of respondent
Nos. 1&amp;2 for release of grant-in-aid and recognized these
two persons for the aforesaid purposes.
</p><p id="p_4">       Considering the short controversy involved in this
case, we direct Shri P.K.Kaurav, Deputy Advocate
General to seek instructions from respondent Nos.3&amp;4
about the status of matter in respect of release of grant-in-
aid to respondent Nos.1&amp;2.
</p><p id="p_5">       Shri P.K.Kaurav prays two weeks' time in this
regard.
</p><p id="p_6">        Prayer is allowed.
</p><p id="p_7">       Be listed for hearing after two weeks.
       In the meantime, the order passed by Single Bench
directing attachment of properties of the appellant for
payment of salary shall remain stayed.</p><pre id="pre_2">



       (Krishn Kumar Lahoti)              (Smt.Vimla Jain)
             Judge                            Judge
amit
