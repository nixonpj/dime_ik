<p id="p_1">              Present petition under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. for issuance of

respondent No.3.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Learned counsel for the petitioner contended that for the

redressal of his grievance, petitioner made a representation (Annexure P1)

to respondent No.2-Commissioner of Police, Amritsar, but till date no action

has been taken thereon.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            Notice of motion.

<p id="p_4">            On asking of the Court, Mr. Pawan Sharda, Senior Deputy

Advocate General, Punjab accepts notice on behalf of respondents No.1 &amp; 2.

A complete copy of paper-book be provided to him during the course of the

day.
</p>

directions to respondent No.2-Commissioner of Police, Amritsar to look into
