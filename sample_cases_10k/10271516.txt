<p id="p_1">3         08-10-2014                        Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                         learned counsel for the respondents.

</p><p id="p_2">                                            This writ application under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the

                         Constitution of India is directed against the order dated 22.07.2014

                         passed in Eviction Suit No. 06 of 1992 whereby the Court of

                         Additional Munsif -IIIrd, Jamui rejected the amendment petition

                         of the defendant/petitioner to amend the written statement.

</p><p id="p_3">                                            I find no illegality in the impugned order for

                         interference under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_2">Article 227</a> of the Constitution of India.

                         Accordingly, this writ application is dismissed.


</p><p id="p_4">                                                      (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J.)
