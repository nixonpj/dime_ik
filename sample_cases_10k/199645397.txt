  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

 Dated :28/07/2011

 O R D E R
                              S.SIRI JAGAN, J.

                         W.P.(C).No. 9146 of 2011
                       ==================
                  Dated this the 28th day of July, 2011
                              J U D G M E N T
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       The petitioner challenges proceedings initiated by the 2nd

respondent through        the 1st respondent for recovery of the loan

amounts defaulted by the petitioner. The petitioner does not dispute

the liability to pay or the quantum. The petitioner only seeks facility to

pay off the amounts due in instalments.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       2.     This is opposed by the learned counsel for the 2nd

respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       3.     Having heard both sides, taking a lenient view, I dispose of

this writ petition with the following directions;

</p><p id="p_4">       The petitioner shall pay an amount of ` 7 lakhs within one month.

Balance amounts due shall be paid in eight equal monthly instalments

starting from 1.11.2011. Every subsequent instalment shall be paid on

the first working day of every succeeding month. If the petitioner pays

the initial amount of ` 7 lakhs and the instalments on the due dates

without default, coercive proceedings for recovery of the amounts shall

be kept in abeyance. However, if the petitioner commits default in

payment of the initial amount of `7 lakhs or any of the instalments, it

would be open to the respondents to continue proceedings as now

initiated without having to issue any fresh notice or proceedings in that

regard.
</p>
