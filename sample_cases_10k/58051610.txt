<p id="p_1">      The learned advocate appearing for the petitioner seeks
permission to withdraw this petition. Permission is granted as
prayed for. This petition stands disposed of as withdrawn.
Notice is discharged. Ad-interim relief, if any, stands vacated
forthwith. Since the petition is withdrawn, the detaining
authority is directed to apply the mind with regard to nature
of offences and likelihood of breach of public order, if any,
before passing any order of detention against the petitioner.
If as and when order of detention is passed, it will be open for
the petitioner to challenge the same in accordance with law.
</p>
