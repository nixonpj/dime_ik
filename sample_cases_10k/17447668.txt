                    CIVIL APPLICATION (FOR STAY) NO. 11143 of 2015

                             In SECOND APPEAL NO. 217 of 2015

                                                TO
                            CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 11147 of 2015
                                                In
                               SECOND APPEAL NO. 221 of 2015
         ==========================================================
                        THE COLLECTOR &amp; 2....Applicant(s)
                                   Versus
               FAKIRMAHAMMAD USMANBHAI SHEKH &amp; 6....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MS AMITA SHAH, AGP for the Applicant(s) No. 1 - 3
         MR MA BUKHARI, ADVOCATE for the Respondent(s) No. 2 - 7
         ==========================================================

                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.D.KOTHARI

                                       Date : 21/01/2016
                                     COMMON ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.   Earlier this Court has issued Rule by order dated
         30.10.2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2.   Heard learned advocates for the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         3.   Upon considering the submissions made by learned
         advocates       for     the   parties,        the       following            order          is
         passed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         4.   The       applicants        shall           pay      to       the        original
         claimants-respondents Rs. 50,000/-, in each case, -
         out of the compensation amount Rs.2,00,000/- awarded

         by the two Courts below within 6 weeks from today.
         Rest of the amount i.e. Rs. 1,50,000/-, in each case,
         shall be kept in FDR in the name of Nazir of the
         District Court, Patan for the period of three years
         and quarterly interest accrue on the FDR shall be paid
         to    the      original        claimants-respondents                    till       further
         order is passed in this regard.
</p>
<p id="p_5">         5.      Till further is passed, the FDR be renewed from
         time to time and for renewal of the FDR only, no
         further of this Court is required.
</p>
<p id="p_6">         6.      With joint suggestion by the learned advocates
         for the parties, it is ordered that it would be open
         for the applicant-Government to                            deposit the amount
         i.e. Rs. 50,000/-, in each case, before Nazir of this
         Court.
</p>
<p id="p_7">         7.      As directed above, if the applicants instead of
         directly pay the amount to the claimants-respondents
         deposit the amount before this Court, on depositing
         the     amount,         it     would   be        open     for       the      claimants-
         respondents to withdraw the amount from the Nazir of
         this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_8">         8.      On above conditions the judgments of the Courts
         below are stayed till further order.
</p>
<p id="p_9">         9.      In view of above, these applications are allowed.
         Rule made absolute to the above extent.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                                                                               (R.D.KOTHARI, J.)
         BD Songara
