<pre id="pre_1">                                APOT No. 442 of 2008
                                G.A. No.3559 of 2008
                                C.S. No. 197 of 2008

                   IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA

   Constitutional Writ/Civil Appellate/Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction

                         ORIGINAL SIDE


PARAS MARKETING COMPANY PVT LTD                     Plaintiff/Petitioner/Applicant

    Versus

THE NATIONAL AVIATION COMPANY OF INDIA L            Defendant/Respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">TD &amp; ANR


BEFORE:
</p>
<p id="p_2">The Hon'ble JUSTICE PINAKI CHANDRA GHOSE

The Hon'ble JUSTICE SANKAR PRASAD MITRA

Date : 24th November, 2008.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">                               The Court :- There will be an order in terms

of prayer (a) of the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_5">          We have heard learned Counsel for the parties at length. We do

not find any reason that at this stage we should interfere with the

order     so   passed   by    the   learned    Trial   Judge   since   the   matter   is

awaiting for final disposal on 8th December, 2008. Hence, we do not

want to disturb the position, which is prevailing at this stage. We

only direct the respondent to keep a separate account in respect of

transaction      fees    as    also    at     the   earlier    stage   prevailing     on

commission      basis   concerning      the    petitioner      until   the   matter   is

decided by the Trial Court.
</p>
<p id="p_6">          Accordingly, both the appeal and the application are disposed

of by the foregoing order. Undertakings are discharged.
</p><p id="p_7">          All parties concerned are to act on a signed copy of the minutes

      of this order on the usual undertakings.

</p><p id="p_8">           Urgent xerox certified copy of this order, if applied for, be

      supplied to the parties subject to compliance with all requisite

      formalities.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">                                  (PINAKI CHANDRA GHOSE, J.)



                                  (SANKAR PRASAD MITRA, J.)
