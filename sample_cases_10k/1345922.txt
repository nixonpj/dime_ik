<div class="doc_title">Bigan Baitha vs The State Of Bihar &amp;Amp; Anr on 30 August, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Cr.Misc. No.8333 of 2009
                  BIGAN BAITHA, SON OF LATE LALCHAND BAITHA, RESIDENT
                  OF VILLAGE SHASTRINAGAR, BAGHA, DISTT. WEST
                  CHAMPARAN.
                                                        ------- PETITIONER
                                     Versus
                  1. THE STATE OF BIHAR
                  2. MANISH KUMAR TULSYAN, SON OF SRIKAILASH NATH
                  TULSYAN, RESIDENT OF VILLAGE NARAIPUR KOTHI, P.S.
                  BAGAHA, DISTT. WEST CHAMPARAN.
                                                      -------- OPPOSITE PARTIES.
                                          -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2.   30.08.2010                Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               It has been submitted that after a complaint was filed

                    on 5.8.2003, some witnesses were examined but he court

                    found that a report from the Circle Officer was necessary upon

                    which a report was called for by the Magistrate on 28.2.2004.

                    Subsequently, the successor in office without waiting for said

                    report has taken cognizance in the matter on 27.8.2008. The

                    petitioner is aggrieved by the act of the Magistrate since it has

                    been submitted that the court below has no power to recall its

                    own order and once a Magistrate had put off issuance of

                    summons against the accused persons, no further action should

                    have been taken without report of the Circle Officer.

</p><p id="p_3">                               Considering the same, the application is allowed and

                    the order dated 27.8.2008 passed by Judicial Magistrate,

                    Bagaha in Complaint Case No. C-632 of 2003 by which he has

                    taken cognizance in the matter is hereby set aside.



</p><p id="p_4">                                             ( Anjana Prakash, J.)
       S.Ali
