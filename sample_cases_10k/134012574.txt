<div class="doc_title">Jagtar Singh And Another vs State Of Punjab on 14 October, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                       AT CHANDIGARH

                     Criminal Misc.-M No. 26990 of 2011 (O&amp;M)
                     Date of decision: 14.10.2011

Jagtar Singh and another
                                                     .. Petitioners

               Vs.

State of Punjab                                      .. Respondent

CORAM:         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJESH BINDAL

Present:       Mr. O. P. Kamboj, Advocate for the petitioners.
               Mr. P. S. Bajwa, Deputy Advocate General, Punjab.
               Mr. N. S. Sodhi, Advocate for the complainant.


                                  ...
</pre><p id="p_1">Rajesh Bindal J.
</p><p id="p_2">               While issuing notice of motion on 6.9.2011, learned counsel
for the petitioners stated that husband of the complainant was arrested and
released on regular bail. By recording that contention, notice of motion
was issued for today and interim stay of arrest was also granted to the
petitioners.
</p><p id="p_3">               Learned counsel for the State submitted that husband of the
complainant has not been arrested till date.
</p><p id="p_4">               Considering the fact that a wrong statement was made before
the court on 6.9.2011, the petitioners do not deserve to be heard on merits.
</p><p id="p_5">               Accordingly, the present petition is dismissed.
</p>



<p id="p_6">                                                           (Rajesh Bindal)
                                                                      Judge

14.10.2011
