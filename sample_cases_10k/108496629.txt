<div class="doc_title">Sk. Abbas Uddin &amp; Anr vs Unknown on 10 December, 2013</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Joymalya Bagchi</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                                        1


10.12.2013</pre><p id="p_1">.
</p><p id="p_2">674.
as
                             C.R.M. 15490 of 2013


              In Re:- An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of
              the Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 25.11.2013 in connection
              with Chanditala P.S. Case No.324 of 2013 dated 05.09.2013 under
              Section 4D of the West Bengal Land Reforms Act.


              In the matter of : Sk. Abbas Uddin &amp; Anr.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                              ... petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
              Mr. Ainul Haque.                     ....For the petitioners.

              Mr. Ranabir Roy Chowdhury.              .....For the State.


</p><p id="p_5">                   Heard the learned Counsels appearing on behalf of the

              parties.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                   Having considered the materials in the case diary and in view

              of the nature of allegations, we are of the view that both the

              petitioners may be granted anticipatory bail but their movement

              requires to be regulated by stringent conditions.

</p><p id="p_7">                   Accordingly, we direct that in the event of arrest the

              petitioners shall be released on anticipatory bail upon furnishing

              bond of Rs.5,000/- each with one surety of like amount each to

              the satisfaction of the arresting officer and also subject to the

              conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_2">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of

              Criminal Procedure, 1973 and on further condition that they shall

              not enter into Chanditala Police Station until further order.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              2</span>


        This application for anticipatory bail is, thus, disposed of. 



</p><p id="p_8">                                            (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)


                                            (Subrata Talukdar, J.)
