<div class="doc_title">Linan New Sanlian Lighting ... vs Arya Filaments Pvt. Ltd. on 5 December, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                               COMP-32-2014
 (LINAN NEW SANLIAN LIGHTING ELECTRIC CO. LTD. THRU. POWER OF ATTORNEY HOLDER MR.

                   PANKAJ SACHDEVA Vs ARYA FILAMENTS PVT. LTD.)


05-12-2016

Shri Jitendra Verma, learned counsel for the petitioner.
Shri Vijayesh Atre, learned counsel for the respondent has</pre>
<p id="p_1">fairly pointed out that vide Gazette Notification dated
25.11.2016 the Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provision)
Repeal Act, 2003 has come into operation w.e.f. 1.12.2016.
In view of this the protection to the respondent under the <a href="/doc/438563/" id="a_1">SICA
Act</a> is no longer available.
</p><p id="p_2">Learned counsel for the petitioner prays for time to file reply to
the IA No.8705/2016.
</p><p id="p_3">List on 30.1.2017.
</p>
