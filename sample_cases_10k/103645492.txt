<pre id="pre_1">                             1




IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA AT BENGALURU

        DATED THIS THE 17TH DAY OF AUGUST 2015

                        BEFORE

      THE HON'BLE Mr. JUSTICE L.NARAYANA SWAMY


              CRIMINAL PETITION NO.1004/2015

BETWEEN:

1.     ANAND,
       S/O LATE MUNNISWAMY,
       AGED ABOUT 50 YEARS,
       OCCUPATION: COOLIE,
       R/O. NO.183, 23B,
       JADAL NAIDU, JAYANAGAR BADAVANE,
       PERIYANAYAKANAPALYAM,
       KOYAMBATURU TALUK AND DISTRICT.

2.     ANANDI,
       W/O ANAND,
       AGED ABOUT 42 YEARS,
       OCCUPATION: COOLIE,
       R/A. NO.183, 23B,
       JADAL NAIDU, JAYANAGAR BADAVANE,
       PERIYANAYAKANAPALYAM,
       KOYAMBATURU TALUK AND DISTRICT.

                                    ...PETITIONERS

     (BY SRI. PRASANNA KUMAR.P.DAROJI, ADV.)
                                2




  AND:

  THE STATE OF KARNATAKA,
  REP BY T. NARASIPURA POLICE STATION,
  MYSORE DISTRICT,
  REPRESENTED BY SPP,
  AMBEDKAR VEEDI.
  HIGH COURT BUILDING,
  BANGALORE - 560001.
                            ...RESPONDENT

  (BY SRI. K. NAGESHWARAPPA, HCGP,)

    THIS CRIMINAL PETITION IS FILED UNDER
  SECTION 439 OF CR.P.C PRAYING TO ENLARGE THE
  PETITIONERS ON BAIL IN S.C.NO.122/2013 OF PRL.
  SESSIONS JUDGE, MANDYA, FOR THE OFFENCES
  P/U/S 302 R/W 34 OF <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> AND ETC.,


     THIS PETITION COMING ON FOR ORDERS, THIS
DAY, THE COURT PASSED THE FOLLOWING:


                        ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and the

learned Government Pleader.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     2.    Petitioners are accused in Cr.No.240/2012 on

the file of T.Narasipura Police Station, Mysuru District.

After concluding investigation, charge-sheet has been filed

for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Section 302</a> r/w <a href="/doc/1507082/" id="a_3">Section
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                3</span></a>




<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of IPC. The petitioner is in judicial custody since 3

years. Hence, regular bail application has been filed under

<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_5">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">      3.    Case on hand relates to the alleged murder of

Smt. Rajathi @ Arogyameri. On 25/08/2012 the accused

persons were apprehended and given voluntary statement

before T.Narasipura CPI that, they took one Smt. Rajathi @

Arogyameri to show the temple of Kabbalamma and at

Kurifarm Dhanagur, Malavalli Taluk. The accused persons

made the deceased Rajathi to consume the liquor and

thereafter committed her murder and taken away her

valuable articles.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      4.    Learned Government Pleader has vehemently

opposed the bail application on the ground that prima facie

case is forthcoming in regard to this petitioner committing

the murder of Smt. Rajathi, aged about 48 years. On

25/08/2012 the petitioners-accused took one Smt. Rajathi

@ Arogyameri to show the temple of Kabbalamma and at
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                            4</span>




Kurifarm      Dhanagur,            Malavalli   Taluk     and   made    the

deceased Rajathi to consume the liquor and thereafter

committed her murder and taken away her valuable

articles. The CPI of T.Narasipura recovered the gold articles

of deceased and other connected material objects upon the

alleged information of the accused. Hence, he requests this

court to dismiss the bail application.


</p><p id="p_6">     5.       Perused the records.
</p>
<p id="p_7">     6.       The        earlier    bail   application    filed   by   the

petitioners        in     SC.No.122/2013         was      dismissed    on

26/02/2014.             The petitioners are in judicial custody for

more than 3 years.             The petitioners are the permanent

residents     of        Tamilnadu      State   having     roots   in   the

community and have undertaken to obey any condition

which may be imposed on them.                    Hence, they pray for

allowing the bail petition. Thus the apprehension of the

learned Government Pleader could be suitably met with by
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                     5</span>




imposing proper conditions. Hence, the bail application is

to be allowed, subject to the following conditions:

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                           ORDER
     The petition is allowed.</blockquote><p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">     Petitioners are to be enlarged on bail, in Crime No.

240/2012 of T.Narasipura Police Station, Mysuru District

subject to the following conditions:

</p><p id="p_10">  1) Petitioners shall execute personal bond for a sum
     of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) with
     one surety for like sum to the satisfaction of the
     concerned Court;
</p><p id="p_11">  2) Petitioners shall not tamper or attempt to tamper
     any of the prosecution witnesses.
</p><p id="p_12">  3) Petitioners shall not hold out threats to the
     prosecution witnesses or lure them in any
     manner.
</p><p id="p_13">  4) Petitioners shall not involve in any criminal
     activities.
</p><p id="p_14">  5) Petitioners   shall   attend       the   Sessions        Court
     regularly on all dates of hearing and shall co-
     operate with trial.
</p><p id="p_15">  6) Petitioners   shall   mark         attendance       at     the
     Jurisdictional Police Station once in a month on
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                   6</span>



      every Second Sunday between 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
      for a period of two years from the date of his
      release or till the disposal of the case, whichever
      is earlier.
</p><p id="p_16">  7) If   the   petitioners   violate   any   one   of   the
      conditions, the prosecution is at liberty to seek
      for cancellation of bail from the concerned
      Sessions Court.
</p>
<p id="p_17">

</p><p id="p_18">                                               Sd/-
</p><p id="p_19">                                              JUDGE

MS*
