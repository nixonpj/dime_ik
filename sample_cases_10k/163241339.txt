            CRM No.M-4462 of 2014, seeking concession of anticipatory bail arising

            out of the common FIR No.749 dated 12.06.2013, under <a href="/doc/1046310/" id="a_1">Sections 15</a>/<a href="/doc/833486/" id="a_2">61</a>/<a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_3">85</a>

            of NDPS Act and 420 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a>, registered at Police Station City Hissar.

</p><p id="p_3">                               Counsel for the parties have been heard at length.

                               As per the prosecution version, the police party was on patrol

            duty on Sirsa Chungi and a message was received at about 01.00 am in

            the night that a Scorpio vehicle bearing registration No. HR-10-K-5419 was

            coming from Hansi side.              The police party made an effort to stop the

            vehicle at the Naka but the driver did not stop and thereafter the vehicle

            was apprehended after some time, upon which two persons alighted from

            the car and ran away. From the vehicle in question, a recovery of 320 Kg

<p id="p_4">                               The alleged recovery of contraband is of 320 Kgs of poppyhusk.

            In the light of such huge commercial quantity, this Court is of the

            considered view that the petitioners are not entitled to the concession of

            pre-arrest bail.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               Both the petitions are accordingly dismissed.
