                 ======================================================
                 1. Dewanti Devi W/o Samdeo Pandey @ Samdeo Pandey
                 2. Urmila Devi, W/o Late Baldeo Pandey
                                                                               .... ....   Appellant/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                              .... .... Respondent/s

                 For the Appellant/s        :    Mr. Satyapal Singh

                 SRIVASTAVA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   15-06-2016                Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              This Cr. Appeal is admitted for hearing.

                              Call for the lower court record of Sessions Trial No.

                 168 of 2002 arising out of Buxar (M) (Industrial) P.S. Case No. 73

                 of 2002 from the court of learned VIth Additional Sessions Judge,

                 Buxar.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              Appellants were summoned by the trial court u/S 319<a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1"> of

                 the Code of Criminal Procedure Code</a> and subsequently, after

                 completion of trial, appellants were convicted for the offence
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court CR. APP (SJ) No.421 of 2016 (2) dt.15-06-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        punishable under<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2"> Section-324 of the Indian Penal Code</a> and they

                        were sentenced to undergo simple imprisonment for six months.

                        After conviction, both the appellants were taken into custody but

                        they were released by the trial court on provisional bail vide order

                        dated 20-05-2016.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                      Learned counsel, appearing for the appellants, seeks

                        confirmation of the aforesaid provisional bail, granted to the

                        appellants by the trial court vide order dated 20-05-2016.

</p><p id="p_5">                                      Accordingly, the provisional bail, granted to the

                        appellants by the trial court vide order dated 20-05-2016 is hereby

                        confirmed and it is ordered that the appellants shall remain on

                        similar bail bond till disposal of this Cr. Appeal.

</p><p id="p_6">                                       List this matter after receipt of the lower court record

                        under appropriate heading.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                              (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
</p>
