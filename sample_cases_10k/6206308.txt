--------------------------

       SHIBU AGED 47 YEARS

       THIS WRIT PETITION (CIVIL)  HAVING COME UP FOR ADMISSION ON

WP(C)NO.30832/2015

APPENDIX

PETITIONERS EXHIBITS


P1-TRUE COPY OF THE TEMPORARY PERMIT ISSUED TO THE PETITIONER ON THE
ROUTE ANAPPARA-ANGAMALY-PERUMBAVOOR VALID FROM 24.6.2015 TO 23.10.2015

P2-TRUE COPY OF THE 4 MONTHS TEMPORARY PERMIT APPLICATION SUBMITTED BY
THE PETITIONER BEFORE THE RESPONDENT DATED 3.10.2015

P3-TRUE COPY OF THE COVERING LETTER SUBMITTED ALONG WITH THE
APPLICATION DATED 3.10.2015.


RESPONDENTS EXHIBITS

NIL




                                               TRUE COPY




SKS                                            P.A. TO JUDGE



                   V.CHITAMBARESH, J.

             --------------------------------------
               W.P(C)NO. 30832 OF 2015
            ---------------------------------------
          Dated this the 9th day of October, 2015

                          JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     The validity of Ext.P1 temporary permit issued to the

petitioner expires on 23.10.2015. Ext.P2 application for

reissue of temporary permit is pending.

</p><p id="p_2">     2.   I direct the respondent to consider          Ext.P2

application with notice to the petitioner. The needful shall

be done within two weeks from the date of receipt of a copy

of this judgment.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3.   The petitioner shall be permitted to operate

service as before till orders are passed on Ext.P2 application

as directed above.
</p>
<p id="p_4">          The writ petition is disposed of as above.




</p><p id="p_5">                               V.CHITAMBARESH, JUDGE
