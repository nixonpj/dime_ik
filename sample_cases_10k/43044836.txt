                                          Second Appeal No.310 of 2012

                     Radha Mohan Ram
                                                                        .... .... Appellant/s
                                                    Versus
                     Muneshwar Choudhary &amp; Ors

                     Appearance :
                     For the Appellant/s    : Mr. Vinod Shanker Modi

                     ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3     02-12-2013             Admit.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             This Second Appeal shall be heard on the following substantial

                     question of law :-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                         (1) Whether the Judgment of both the Courts below are vitiated

                              because of the fact that both the Courts below on flimsy and

                              unacceptable ground have rejected the agreement, ext. '3' when

                              it was the specific case of the plaintiff that this is the

                              Kirayanama by which the defendant was inducted in the suit

                              premises as tenant for 6 years. Therefore, there was relationship

                              of landlord and tenant but both the Courts below without

                              framing any issue on that question held that there is no

                              relationship of landlord and tenant?</blockquote><p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">                             Issue appeal notice to the respondents. The appellant shall file

                     the necessary requisites within 2 weeks. Peremptory.

</p><p id="p_5">                             Call for the lower Court record.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><pre id="pre_2">Sanjeev/-                                                      (Mungeshwar Sahoo, J)
