<div class="doc_title">Kishan Lal vs Sandeep Kumar @ Deepak &amp; Ors on 31 July, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                                     FAO No. 5081 of 2011
                                     Date of decision : 31.07.2012


Kishan Lal
                                                 ....Appellant

                                   V/s

Sandeep Kumar @ Deepak &amp; ors.
                                                 ....Respondents</pre>

<p id="p_1">BEFORE : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJAN GUPTA

Present:     Mr. Tarundeep Kumar, Advocate for the appellant.

</p><p id="p_2">             Ms. Rohini Bedi, Advocate for
             Mr. R.S. Madan, Advocate for respondent no. 2.

RAJAN GUPTA J. (ORAL)

             Learned counsel for the appellant who is owner-cum-driver

of the offending vehicle submits that matter has been settled between

the parties, they being residents of the same village. Thus, in view of

same no cause of action may survive in this appeal.

</p><p id="p_3">             In view of statement made by learned counsel for the

appellant, it appears that this appeal requires no further adjudication.

Disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             In the eventuality, however, appellant feels the necessity of

pressing the appeal, he shall be at liberty to move an application for

revival of the same.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">July 31, 2012                                            (RAJAN GUPTA)
Ajay                                                         JUDGE
