<pre id="pre_1">                                      1                           MCRC-32894-2019
         The High Court Of Madhya Pradesh
                   MCRC-32894-2019
                     (PRADEEP Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)

2
Gwalior, Dated : 26-08-2019
       Shri Kumar Gaurav Sharma, learned counsel for the applicant.
       Shri    Vikrant      Sharma,       learned   Public   Prosecutor   for   the
respondents/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Case-diary is perused.
</p><p id="p_2">       Learned counsel for the rival parties are heard.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       This is first application u/S. 439 <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a> filed by the applicant for grant
of bail. He has been arrested on 18.7.2019 by Police Station Banmore district
Morena in connection with Crime No.243/2019 registered in relation to the
offences punishable u/S.34 (2) of the M.P. Excise Act.
</p><p id="p_4">       Learned counsel for the applicant submits that as per prosecution
story, 123.95 bulk liters of wine was seized from the possession of the
applicant for which he was having no license. The charge sheet has already
been filed and there is no requirement of custodial interrogation of the
applicant. He is having no criminal antecedents. He is permanent resident of

district Morena and there is no chance of his absconding. On these grounds,
he prayed for grant of bail to the applicant.
</p><p id="p_5">       Learned Public Prosecutor for the State opposed the application but
has acceded the fact that the applicant is having no criminal antecedents and
prayed for its rejection.
</p><p id="p_6">       Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances of the case, but
without expressing any opinion on merits of the case, this application is
allowed and it is directed that the applicant be released on bail on furnishing a
personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rs. Fifty thousand Only) with
two solvent sureties of the like amount to the satisfaction of the concerned
trial Court.
</p><p id="p_7">       This order will remain operative subject to compliance of the following
                                       2                          MCRC-32894-2019
conditions by the applicant:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                1. The applicant will comply with all the terms and
      conditions of the bond executed by him;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                2. The applicant will cooperate in the investigation/trial, as
      the case may be;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">                3 . The applicant will not indulge himself in extending
      inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted with the
      facts of the case so as to dissuade him/her from disclosing such
      facts to the Court or to the Police Officer, as the case may be;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">                4. The applicant shall not commit an offence similar to the
      offence of which he is accused;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_5">                5. The applicant will not seek unnecessary adjournments
      during the trial; and
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_6">                6. The applicant will not leave India without previous
      permission of the trial Court/Investigating Officer, as the case may
      be.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_7">       A copy of this order be sent to the Court concerned for compliance.
       C.c. as per rules.</blockquote><pre id="pre_2">
