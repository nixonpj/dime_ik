<p id="p_6">                           OMP No. 283 of 2017

                           Mr. Hamender Chandel, Advocate has placed on

            record a reply on behalf of the Municipal Corporation,


            Shimla, to the plaintiffs' application for modifying the

            previous order(s) recorded by this Court, whereby She is

            restrained from raising any further construction upon the



            suit property. With the apposite application, the plaintiff has

            also appended the relevant building plan and the apposite




            sanction purveyed thereto. However, in the reply instituted





            thereto by Mr. Hamender Chandel, Advocate, there is a





            specific averment, of transfer of the area, whereat the suit

            property is located, from the jurisdiction of the TCP vis-à-vis

            the jurisdiction of the Municipal Corporation. Though, the

            records in respect thereto, were also enjoined to be

            transferred, yet records with respect to the apposite sanction

            meted by the TCP department vis-à-vis the plaintiffs'

            building plans      remain not transmitted to the Municipal

            Corporation. Also it is averred in the application, of, notice

            being      issued   upon   the   plaintiff,    for    hers      carrying

            unauthorized construction upon the suit land. Since in view

            thereof, it would not be permissible, at this stage, to modify
