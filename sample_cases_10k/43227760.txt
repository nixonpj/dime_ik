O R D E R

<p id="p_1">	The writ petition has been filed seeking to quash the impugned proceedings of the 2nd respondent in Nada.Na.Ka.No.5208/2009/A2 dated 21.07.2016 and  consequently direct the 3rd respondent to refix the fair rent to the petitioner land as per G.O.Ms.No.298, Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowment Department, dated 20.07.2010.  
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2. The learned Special Government Pleader appearing for the respondents would submit that as against the impugned order, there is an provision for appeal to the Commissioner and sought dismissal of the writ petition. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. The learned counsel appearing for the petitioner would submit that the petitioner may be permitted to prefer an appeal before the Commissioner and the appellate authority may be directed to pass orders within a time frame.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4. In view of the above, this Court without going into the merits of the writ petition, permits the petitioner to file an appeal before the first respondent within a period of 15 days from the date of receipt of a copy of this order and on receipt of the same, the first respondent shall consider and dispose of the appeal preferred by the petitioner on merits and in accordance with law and pass orders as expeditiously as possible without taking into consideration the period of limitation.  The writ petition is disposed of accordingly. The Registry is directed to return the original impugned order dated 21.07.2016 to the petitioner. Consequently, the connected miscellaneous petition is closed. No costs.</p><pre id="pre_2">
