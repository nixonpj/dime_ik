<div class="doc_title">Present: None For The vs Unknown on 14 March, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                                     FAO No. 1658 of 2000



            Chet Ram v. Rakesh

            Present:           None for the appellant


                                     ......
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                               Present case was burnt in the fire which broke out on
            30.1.2011.The matter was widely reported in the press and appeals had been
            issued through general notice to the bar to supply copies of case papers to
            help the court to reconstruct files. Several counsel have cooperated in the
            exercise and supplied copies. Cases which are reconstructed are being
            posted for hearing but there are also many instances where paper books
            have not been supplied inspite of notices to counsel.
</p><p id="p_2">                               It has also been reported that the status of the case as to
            whether the case is pending or decided is also not available in computer as
            the same is not entered in the Mozilla. The case is brought for hearing with
            such limited information as it available. The cause list contains the case
            number, names of the parties/ counsel but none has appeared at the time of
            calling.
</p><p id="p_3">                               Dismissed in default with liberty to revive petition as and when
            an application for restoration of the case alongwith paper book of the main
            case is filed. A copy of the order be sent to appellant as well as counsel for
            the parties. The Court Master to ensure that disposal of the case is entered in
            the ISHICO (Mozilla). Case be consigned to record.
</p><p id="p_4">                               Copy of this order be sent to the appellant.
</p>
