<div class="doc_title">Mohd. Anis Kuraishi vs State Of U.P. And Another on 3 February, 2015</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Ramesh Sinha</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 41
 

 
Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 319 of 2015
 

 
Applicant :- Mohd. Anis Kuraishi
 
Opposite Party :- State Of U.P. And Another
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Subodh Pandey
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- Govt. Advocate
 

 
Hon'ble Ramesh Sinha,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Heard Sri Subodh Pandey, learned counsel for the applicants and Sri  Nikhil Srivastava, learned A.G.A. 
</p> 
<p id="p_2">This application under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. has been filed for quashing the chargesheet dated 17.05.2008 of Case No. 196 of 2013 (<a href="/doc/1245810/" id="a_2">State vs. Mohd. Hasib Kuraishi</a>) under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_3">Sections 498-A</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506</a> I.P.C. and 3/4 D.P. Act, Police Station Faridpur, District Bareilly, pending before the Additional Judicial Magistrate, Faridpur, Bareilly.
</p> 
<p id="p_3">It has been submitted by learned counsel for the applicant that the marriage between brother of applicant no.1 and opposite party no.2 was solemnized in the year 2006. He further submits that the husband of opposite party no.2 has died natural death. The applicant is Devar of opposite party no.2.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">It has been contended by learned counsel for the applicant that the applicant is the family member of husband of opposite party no.2 and the allegation levelled against him is wholly vague and no specific allegation has been levelled against him. Learned counsel for the applicant has placed reliance on the judgment of the Apex Court in the case of Geeta Mehrotra Vs. State of U.P. and others reported in 2012 (10) ADJ 464. 
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Notice on behalf of opposite party no. 1 has been accepted by learned A.G.A. 
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Issue notice to opposite party no.2 returnable within four weeks at the address given in the application. 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">Opposite party no.2 may file counter affidavit within four weeks. Learned A.G.A. may also file counter affidavit within the same period. Rejoinder affidavit may thereafter be filed within two weeks. 
</p> 
<p id="p_8">List immediately after expiry of the aforesaid period before appropriate Bench. 
</p> 
<p id="p_9">Till the next date of listing, further proceedings of the aforesaid case against applicant, namely, Mohd. Anis Kuraishi shall remain stayed. 
</p> 
<p id="p_10">Order Date :- 3.2.2015
 
Ajay
