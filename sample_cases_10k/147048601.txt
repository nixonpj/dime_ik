ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">     1.By way of interim arrangement, the petitioner of
     Special Civil Application No. 15510 of 2004 is directed
     to appear before the Development Commissioner on 11th
     January, 2005 with all documents to establish the meeting
     conducted after ruling atmosphere on 30th November, 2004.
     The Development Commissioner shall examine the documents
     and also the report from the District         Development
     Officer. As a result of this exercise if he comes to the
     conclusion that the meeting (i.e. drawing of lots) was
     conducted by the petitioner namely Amrutbhai Patel, he
     shall examine the same and shall pass appropriate order
 in accordance with law.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.However, if the documents are not produced or if
the Development Commissioner is of the opinion that
drawing lot was not done properly, the report of the same
shall be produced before this Court on 13th January,
2005.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3.The Court appreciates the efforts made by Mr.
Kamal Trivedi learned Additional Advocate General     for
accepting the Court's suggestion and conducting       the
proceedings in truly    democratic  manner.  S.O.      to
13/1/2005.
</p>

<p id="p_4">[K.S. JHAVERI, J.]
