<div class="doc_title">Suman Kanti Mitra vs State &amp; Ors on 22 February, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                        1

179   22.02.                     WP 3216(W) of 2017
an     2017
                                  Suman Kanti Mitra
                                       Versus
                                     State &amp; ors.


               Mr. Kalyan Kr. Chakraborty
               Mr. Kashinath Bhattacharjee         ... for the Petitioner


               Mr. Ratul Biswas
               Mr. Kaushik Chowdhury               ... for the State.



                          The petitioner's mother was a primary school

               teacher.    She   was   declared    to   be   permanently

               incapacitated in the year 2009 by the concerned Medical

               Council. She subsequently passed away.

                          The petitioner applied for being appointed on

               compassionate ground.        The proposal for approval of

               appointing the petitioner as primary school teacher was

               forwarded to the Directorate of School Education, W.B.,

               presently the Commissionerate of School Education. The

               Deputy Director of School Education (R/P), W.B. by a

               Memo dated 5.8.2016 informed the Chairman, Malda

               District Primary School Council that the proposal for

               appointing the petitioner as a primary school teacher has

               been received by the Deputy Director of School Education

               (R/P), W.B. but no recommendation of the Chairman,
                             2


Malda District Primary School Council was found in the

records. The Deputy Director of School Education (R/P),

W.B. requested the Chairman, Malda District Primary

School Council to inform him as to whether the Chairman

had recommended the name of the petitioner for approval

as primary teacher on compassionate ground.            Since,

there was no response from the end of the Chairman,

Malda District Primary School Council, the petitioner

made    a     representation    dated   02.11.2016   to   the

Chairman, Malda District Primary School Council.          The

learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioner

submits that the said representation has also elicited no

response.

            I have heard the learned counsel for the parties.

            I direct the Chairman, Malda District Primary

School Council being respondent no. 8 to dispose of the</pre>

<p id="p_1">representation of the petitioner dated 02.11.2016 in the

light of the memo dated 05.08.2016 issued by the Deputy

Director of School Education (R/P), W.B. by a reasoned

order in accordance with law and applicable rules and

regulations within a period of four weeks from the date of

communication of this order after giving an opportunity of

hearing to the petitioner or his authorized representative.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                           3</span>


</p><p id="p_2">          The decision so taken by respondent no. 8 shall

be communicated to the Deputy Director of School

Education (R/P), W.B. as also to the petitioner within a

fortnight from the date of the decision.




</p><p id="p_3">          Since no affidavit-in-opposition has been called

for, the allegations contained in the writ petition are

deemed not to be admitted.
</p>
<p id="p_4">          WP 3216(W) of 2017 is, accordingly, disposed of.

          Affidavit-of-service filed today be kept with the

record.
</p>
<p id="p_5">          There will be no order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_6">          Urgent certified website copy of this order, if

applied for, shall be given to the parties as expeditiously

as possible on compliance of all necessary formalities.




</p><p id="p_7">                                      ( Arijit Banerjee, J. )
 </p>
