<div class="doc_title">Md.Naushad Alam vs State Of Bihar on 6 January, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                      Cr.Misc. No.36985 of 2010
        MD.NAUSHAD ALAM SON OF LATE MD. JAMIL FAROEE.
                                Versus
                         STATE OF BIHAR .
                               -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">2.    06.01.2011              Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              Torture for demand of dowry is doubted on the ground

                   of nature of demand from a laborer class person i.e. of a color

                   T.V., Fridge, Godrej Almirah and Hero Honda Motocycle and

                   Rs. 50,000/- cash even after seven years of marriage. Further no

                   injury was found though she was dashed from first floor of the

                   house. Specific submission is that she is not willing to reside with

                   petitioner and to justify her desertion the false case is lodged.

</p><p id="p_3">                              Taking this into consideration, in the event of arrest or

                   surrender within one month from the date of receipt/production of

                   a copy of this order in connection with Complaint Case

                   No.1647(c) of 2009 above named petitioner shall be released on

                   bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with

                   two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                   Chief Judicial Magistrate, patna subject to the conditions as laid

                   down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_1">section 438(2)</a> of Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                                         ( Mandhata Singh, J. )
     Devendra/
