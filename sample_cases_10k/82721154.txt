                            COURT: DELHI

Unique Case ID No. 02402C0272952010
In the matter of:

Sh. Ramveer Singh 
S/o Sh. Mansha Ram,
R/o H. No. 540/I­2/8, 
Sangam Vihar, New Delhi­62.

Through:
General Secretary, 
All India General Mazdoor Trade Union (Regd.),
170, Bal Mukund Khand, Giri Nagar,
New Delhi­19.
                                                              ......... Workman 
VERSUS:

M/s. Boutique International,
D­14/1, Okhla Industrial Area,
Phase­II, New Delhi­20.
                                                                        ......Management 
 


Date of Institution                  :       27.08.2010
Date of reserving award              :       14.03.2011
Date of pronouncement                :       14.03.2011

                                     AWARD
                A   reference   no.   F.   24   (92)/Lab./SD/2010/11431   dated 
18.08.2010</pre><p id="p_1"> was sent by Sh. S. K. Dahiya, Under Secretary (Labour), Govt. 
of   NCT   of   Delhi   for   adjudication   and   disposal   of   an   industrial   dispute 
between the aforesaid workman and the management by formulating the 
following issue:
</p>
<p id="p_2">ID No. 563/10                                                                           1/2
                          "Whether   the   services   of   Sh.   Ramveer 
                         Singh S/o Sh. Mansha Ram have been 
                         terminated illegally and/or unjustifiably 
                         by   the   management;   if   yes,   to   what 
                         relief is he entitled and what directions 
                         are necessary in this respect?"
</p>

<p id="p_3">2.              A notice of aforeseaid reference was sent to the workman for 
27.11.2010 but he did not appear.  Today Sh. Anil Rajput has appeared on 
behalf   of   the   workman   and   has   stated   that   the   workman   has   not   been 
contacting   him   for   a   long   time   and   therefore,   he   is   unable   to   file   his 
statement of claim.   Statement of AR for the workman Sh. Anil Rajput is 
recorded in this regard separately.
</p>

<p id="p_4">3.              In view of above statement of AR for the workman, it seems 
that   the   workman   is   not   interested   to   pursue   this   case   any   further, 
therefore, a no dispute award is passed against him and the reference is 
answered accordingly.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                A copy of the same be sent to the appropriate Government for 
its publications as per rules. </p><pre id="pre_2">


ANNOUNCED IN THE OPEN COURT                    (CHANDRA SHEKHAR)
ON 14th MARCH, 2011                                          POLC.V:KKD:DELHI




ID No. 563/10                                                                               2/2
