                     behalf of the respondent No. 2, which has been shared and also

                     served on the learned counsel for National Council for Teacher

                     Education (Eastern Region).

                          Education (Eastern Region) is, now, directed to file a clear and

                          categorical affidavit on the issue of inspection from the position

                          has to be an inspection of each and every institution in the

                          emerging in the State of Bihar and there is no sighing away

                          from the fact of inspection as part exercise to identify sub-
