<p id="p_1">02. 08.09.2014               The Petitioner seeks quashing of the order of

                   cognizance dated 06.11.2013 passed by the Judicial

                   Magistrate, 1st Class, Patna in Complaint Case No. 2775(C)

                   of 2012 by which she has not proceeded against the

                   private Opposite Parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             Considering that the Petitioner will have an

                   ample opportunity during trial for summoning additional

                   accused if there is any material against them, I am not

                   inclined to interfere in the matter at this stage.
