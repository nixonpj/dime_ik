<p id="p_1">      This revision is filed by the accused, convicted

concurrently for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_1">Section 138</a> of

the Negotiable Instruments Act, for short, the 'N.I.Act'. The

revision petitioner/accused and the complainant together have

filed a petition stating that they have settled the disputes and

a composition of the offence had been arrived at. The counsel

on both sides, representing the respective parties, seek for

setting aside the conviction and sentence imposed against the

accused.     Having regard to the composition arrived, as

evidenced by the petition filed, and the submissions made by

the counsel of both sides, the revision is allowed, setting aside

the conviction and sentence imposed against the revision

petitioner/accused for the offnece punishable under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_2">Section

138</a> of the N.I.Act., and he is acquitted of that offence.
