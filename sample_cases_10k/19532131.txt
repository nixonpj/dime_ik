<div class="doc_title">Inderjeet Singh And Another vs State Of Punjab And Others on 20 April, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                           AT CHANDIGARH

                                      Criminal Misc. No. M- 5962 of 2011
                                      Date of decision:- 20.04.2011

Inderjeet Singh and another

                                                        ...Petitioners

                             Versus

State of Punjab and others

                                                        ...Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">CORAM: HON'BLE MS. JUSTICE RITU BAHRI


Present:-     None for the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_2">              Mr. Munish Kumar, AAG Punjab.
</p>
<p id="p_3">RITU BAHRI J.(Oral)

              This petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. is for directing respondent

Nos. 2 and 3 for providing adequate security and protection to the life and liberty

of the petitioners from the hands of respondent No.4 and further not to harass

and interfere in the peaceful married life of the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_4">              Counsel for the State on instructions from ASI Harjit Singh informs

the Court that the petitioners have made statements before the police that they

do not require any protection.
</p>
<p id="p_5">              In view of this fact, no further orders are required to be passed in

this criminal miscellaneous petition.
</p>
<p id="p_6">              In case the petitioners need any protection in future, they can

approach before the authorities as per the instructions dated 10.8.2010 issued

by the Home Department, Punjab.
</p>
<p id="p_7">              The criminal miscellaneous petition is disposed of accordingly.




</p><pre id="pre_2">April 20, 2011                                       ( RITU BAHRI )
Vijay Asija                                              JUDGE
