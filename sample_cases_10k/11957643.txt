Hon'ble Barin Ghosh, C.J.

<p id="p_1">       Mr. Vipul Sharma, Advocate for the appellant.
       Mr. A.S. Gill, Government Advocate, with Mr. Amit
Kapri, Brief Holder for the State of Uttarakhand / respondent
No. 1.
</p><p id="p_2">       Mr. Kurban Ali, Advocate for respondent Nos. 2 &amp; 3.
       This is an appeal by the victim against a judgment of
acquittal of offence punishable, amongst others, under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_1">Section
376</a> of I.P.C. There has been some delay in preferring the
appeal. An opportunity was given for filing objection thereto.
No objection has been filed. We have considered the averments
made in the Application for condonation of delay and, being
satisfied with the reasons for the delay, allow the Application.
</p><p id="p_3">       Admit.
</p><p id="p_4">       Call for the records.
</p>


<p id="p_5"> (Servesh Kumar Gupta, J.) (Barin Ghosh, C.J.)
                      16.12.2013
