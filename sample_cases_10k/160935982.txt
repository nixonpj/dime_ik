<pre id="pre_1">                               1/4




IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA, BENGALURU

   DATED THIS THE DAY 19TH OF JANUARY 2018

                           BEFORE

    THE HON'BLE Dr.JUSTICE VINEET KOTHARI

                  C.M.P.No.211 OF 2015

BETWEEN:

M/s. Chabbra's Associates
A Registered Firm having its office
At No.11-1-776/14, Chikalguda,
Secundarabad - 560 061,
Represented by its
Power of Attorney Holder
Mr.K.Satish.                                    ...Petitioner

(By Sri Nataraj R, Advocate)

AND:

Superintending Engineer
JNNURM,
Mysore City Corporation,
Mysore - 570 022.                             ...Respondent

(By Sri Udaya, Advocate for Sri H.C.Shivaramu, Advocate)

      This petition is filed under <a href="/doc/596725/" id="a_1">Section 11(a)</a> of the
Arbitration and <a href="/doc/1306164/" id="a_2">Conciliation Act</a>, 1996 praying to appoint
Retired District Judge or any other competent person as a
sole arbitrator to adjudicate the dispute between the parties
in terms of the tender document and refer the parties to
arbitration and etc.

      This petition, coming on for admission, this day, the
Court made the following:
                                   Date of Order 19-01-2018 C.M.P.No.211/2015
                                                  M/s. Chabbra's Associates Vs.
                                            Superintending Engineer, JNNURM

                                  2/4

                              ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">Mr.Nataraj R, Adv. for Petitioner
Mr.Udaya, Adv. for
Mr.H.C.Shivaramu, Adv. for Respondent


        The     petitioner    -    M/s.Chabbra's              Associates

Contractors, having executed the contract for the

respondent            Jawaharlal        Nehru       National         Urban

Renewal         Mission      (JNNURM)          in     the      agreement

Annexure-A dated 29.04.2011 and certain disputes

arose        about    payments     not     being       made        by     the

respondent, a Government Organization.

</p><p id="p_2">        2.     The     agreement         between          the       parties

undisputably contains the Arbitration Clause, which is

Clause No.4.1, which is reproduced below:

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                "4.1 The procedure for arbitration shall be
        as follows:
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_2">              (a) In case of dispute or difference arising
        between the Employer and the Contractor relating
        to any matter arising out of or connected with this
        Agreement it shall be settled in accordance with
                                Date of Order 19-01-2018 C.M.P.No.211/2015
                                               M/s. Chabbra's Associates Vs.
                                         Superintending Engineer, JNNURM

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                3/4</span>

        the Arbitration and <a href="/doc/1306164/" id="a_3">Conciliation Act</a>, 1996. The
        disputes or differences shall be referred to a Sole
        Arbitrator. The Sole Arbitrator shall be appointed
        by agreement between the parties; failing such
        agreement, by the Appointing Authority (any one
        of the Organizations as per list enclosed in
        Annexure)."
</blockquote>

<p id="p_3">   3.      The    petitioner     contractor         M/s.Chabbra's

Associates served notice              on the respondent vide

Annexure-B dated 30.05.2014, it has remained un-

responded. The parties have thereafter failed to appoint

a mutually agreed Arbitrator in the said dispute

between the parties. Hence, the petitioner approached

this Court under Section11 of the Arbitration and

<a href="/doc/1306164/" id="a_4">Conciliation Act</a>, 1996.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">        4. Learned counsel for the Respondent still prays

for time to file statement of objections.                It has been

refused, as for a long period, for about two years, the

Respondent has not raised any valid objection to the

said C.M.P. before this Court.
</p><p id="p_6">                                         Date of Order 19-01-2018 C.M.P.No.211/2015
                                                        M/s. Chabbra's Associates Vs.
                                                  Superintending Engineer, JNNURM

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                        4/4</span>


</p><p id="p_7">        5. Having heard the learned counsels, this Court

is of the opinion that the Arbitrator in the present case

deserves to be appointed under <a href="/doc/1841764/" id="a_5">Section 11</a> of the Act.


</p><p id="p_8">        6. Accordingly, the present petition under <a href="/doc/605764/" id="a_6">Section

11(6)</a>        of       the   Act    is         allowed       by      appointing

Mr.R.B.Garasangi, Former District Judge, Karnataka

State, to act as an Arbitrator in the present dispute.


</p><p id="p_9">        7.        A    copy   of   this        order     be      sent     to    the

Arbitration Centre, Khanija Bhavan, Bengaluru, for

proceeding further in the matter, on administrative side

and also to Mr.R.B.Garasangi, on the address available

with the said Arbitration Centre, Bengaluru.



</p><p id="p_10">                                                    Sd/-
</p><p id="p_11">                                                   JUDGE
Cm/-
</p>
