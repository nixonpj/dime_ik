<p id="p_1">         1.     Having regard to the concurrent findings recorded by the
         courts below and the cruel manner, in which, the buffaloes
         were being transported in three trucks with their mouths and
         legs tied, I see no good reason to interfere with the impugned
         orders.
</p>

         over to the Panjrapole, almost 14 have died. The reason
