<div class="doc_title">Bablu Rikhiyasan vs The State Of Jharkhand on 18 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                 Cr.M.P. No. 1464 of 2019

1. Bablu Rikhiyasan
2. Suraj Rikhiyasan                        .....   Petitioners
                          Versus
The State of Jharkhand                     ..... Opp. Party
                        ---------
</pre><p id="p_1">CORAM:      HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
</p><p id="p_2">                          ---------
</p><p id="p_3">For the Petitioners    : Mr. Aman Shekhar, Advocate.
</p><pre id="pre_2">For the State          : A.P.P.
                    ---------
02/Dated: 18/05/2019

</pre><p id="p_4">         This application has been filed for modification of order
dated 25.04.2019 passed in B.A. No. 264 of 2019.
</p><p id="p_5">         It appears that under order dated 25.04.2019 passed in
B.A. No. 264 of 2019, petitioners were admitted on bail on
certain conditions and one of the conditions is that "Petitioners
are further directed that alongwith the bail bonds, they will
furnish their Cell numbers and xerox copy of Aadhar Cards in
the court below".
</p><p id="p_6">         It is further submitted that so far petitioner no. 2 is
concerned, he has been released from the custody.
</p><p id="p_7">         It is further submitted that petitioner no. 1 has no
Aadhar Card. So the conditions may be modified.
</p><p id="p_8">         In view of the submissions, the order dated 25.04.2019
passed in B.A. No. 264 of 2019 with respect to petitioner no. 1
only is modified to the extent that "Petitioner no. 1 is further
directed that alongwith the bail bonds, he will furnish his Cell
number and xerox copy of Voter Card in the court below".
</p><p id="p_9">         Rest part of the order dated 25.04.2019 passed in B.A.
No. 264 of 2019 shall remain intact.
</p><p id="p_10">         Cr.M.P. No. 1464 of 2019 stands allowed.
         Let a copy of this order be communicated to the court
below.
</p>

<p id="p_11">                                     (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
Sunil/
