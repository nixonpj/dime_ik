            THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SUNIL THOMAS

WEDNESDAY,THE 12TH DAY OF DECEMBER 2018 / 21ST AGRAHAYANA, 1940

                     Bail Appl..No. 8189 of 2018

CRIME NO. 2395/2018 OF KOTHAMANGALAM POLICE STATION , ERNAKULAM



PETITIONER/1ST ACCUSED:


             THANVEER ALIYAR
             AGED 23 YEARS
             S/O.ALIYAR, KALAPARAMBIL HOUSE, NELLIKUZHI,
             KOTHAMANGALAM, ERNAKULAM.

             BY ADVS.
             KUM.SHAHINA HAMEED
             SRI.NEERAJ NARAYAN




RESPONDENTS/STATE &amp; COMPLAINANT:

      1      STATE OF KERALA
             REP. BY THE PUBLIC PROSECUTOR, HIGH COURT OF KERALA,
             ERNAKULAM, PIN - 682 031.

      2      THE STATION HOUSE OFFICER
             KOTHAMANGALAM POLICE STATION, ERNAKULAM DISTRICT -
             682 031.




             SR.PP C.N PRABHAKARAN


THIS BAIL APPLICATION HAVING COME UP FOR ADMISSION ON 12.12.2018,
THE COURT ON THE SAME DAY PASSED THE FOLLOWING:
 Bail Appl..No. 8189 of 2018
                                            2




                           ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        The petitioner herein apprehending arrest in

Crime        No.2395/2018       of     Kothamangalam          police

station for offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">sections 323</a>,

<a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_2">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_3">326</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code, seeks

bail.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.    According    to    the     prosecution,        due   to

previous enmity, the petitioner herein along with the

co-accused         wrongfully        restrained        the   defacto

complainant and assaulted him. It is stated that the

first accused hit with an iron bangle. When his friend

intervened, he was beaten up with a stick Thereafter,

the petitioner herein along with the another accused

caught hold of his shirt and pushed him down. There

is a further allegation that the accused fisted and

stamped the defacto complainant.

</p><p id="p_3">        3.    It   seems    that      the       main    overtact   is
 Bail Appl..No. 8189 of 2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  3</span>

attributed to the other accused. The role played by

the petitioner herein seems to be rather less. Having

considered these and also the fact that the petitioner

has no criminal antecedents, I am inclined to grant

pre-arrest bail to the petitioner on the following

conditions:
</p>
<p id="p_4">     (i)    Petitioner   shall   appear   before   the

investigation officer within ten days from today and

shall undergo interrogation.      Thereafter, if he is

proposed to be arrested, he shall be released on bail

on executing a bond for a sum of Rs.30,000/-

(Rupees thirty thousand only) with two sureties for

the like sum each.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     (ii)   He shall appear before the investigating

officer as and when called for and co-operate with the

investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_6">     (iii) He shall not threaten, coerce or intimidate

the defacto complainant or the witnesses.

</p><p id="p_7">     (iv) He shall not get involved in any other

crime.
</p><p id="p_8"> Bail Appl..No. 8189 of 2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                        4</span>

       Anticipatory bail application is allowed as above.



</p><p id="p_9">                                      Sd/-
</p><p id="p_10">                                SUNIL THOMAS, JUDGE
R.AV
