IN
			THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

				MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI
			
		
	

 


 

 


Date : 16/09/2013
 


 

 


ORAL ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">1.0	Rule.
</p><p id="p_2">Mr. LR Poojari, learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives service
of notice of rule on behalf of the respondent-State. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_3">2.0	This
application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal
Procedure, 1973 in connection with FIR registered as
C.R. No. I-135 of 2012 before Sanand Police Station, Ahmedabad Rural
for the offences
punishable under <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_2">Sections 380</a> and <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_3">114</a> of the Indian Penal Code. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_4">3.0	Learned
advocate appearing on behalf of the applicant submits that the
charge-sheet is filed and the applicant is in jail from 28.06.2013.
It is further submitted that considering the nature of offence, the
applicant may be enlarged on regular bail by imposing suitable
conditions.
</p> 


 


 
	 
		 


<p id="p_5">		4.0	I
		have heard learned advocates appearing on behalf of the respective
		parties and considered the role played by the present applicant.
</p>		 


		 
		
	

 


<p id="p_6">5.0	In
the facts and circumstances of the case and considering the nature of
allegations made against the applicant in the FIR
and role attributed to the applicant coupled with the facts that
charge sheet is filed and  applicant is in jail from 28.06.2013, I am
of the of the opinion that this is a fit case to exercise the
discretion and enlarge the applicant on regular bail. Hence, the
present application is allowed and the applicant is ordered to
be released on regular bail in connection with an offence
being C.R.No.I 135 of 2012 registered with Sanand Police
Station, Ahmedabad Rural on executing a personal bond of
Rs.25,000/- (Rupees  Twenty Five Thousands only) with one
local surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the learned
Trial Court and subject to the conditions that the applicant shall;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_7">[a]
	not take undue advantage of his liberty or misuse his liberty;
</p> 


<p id="p_8">[b]
	not act in a manner injuries to the interest of the prosecution;
</p> 


<p id="p_9">[c]
	surrender his passport, if any, to the lower court within a week;
</p> 


<p id="p_10">[d]
	not leave the State of Gujarat without prior permission of the
Sessions Judge concerned;
</p> 


<p id="p_11">[e]
	mark presence before the concerned Police Station every Friday
initially for a period of three months and thereafter on any day of
the first week of every English Calendar Month for a period of one
year only, between 10:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m.;
</p> 


<p id="p_12">[f]
	furnish latest address of his residence to the Investigating Officer
and also to the Court at the time of execution of the bond and shall
not change the residence without prior permission of this Court;
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_13">6.0	The
Authorities will release the applicant only if he is  not required in
connection with any other offence for the time being. If breach of
any of the above conditions is committed, the Sessions Judge
concerned will be free to issue warrant or take appropriate action in
the matter. Bail bond to be executed before the learned Lower Court
having jurisdiction to try the case. It will be open for the
concerned Court to delete, modify and/or relax any of the above
conditions, in accordance with law. 
</p>
 


 


 
	 
		 


<p id="p_14">		7.0	At
		the trial, learned Trial Court shall not be influenced by the
		observations of preliminary nature, qua the evidence at this stage,
		made by this Court while enlarging the applicant on bail.
</p>	

 


 


 


<p id="p_15">8.0	Rule
is made absolute to the aforesaid extent. Direct service is
permitted. 
</p>
 


 


 


 


 


<p id="p_16">(A.J.DESAI, J.)
