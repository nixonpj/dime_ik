</pre><p id="p_3">            The present appeal has been filed on 19.10.2016 and in
      spite of twice time granted by Lawazima Board on 30.11.2016
      and 08.03.2017, defects have not been removed.
</p><p id="p_4">            On 20.08.2018 following order has been passed:
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                        "As prayed for, as a last chance, three weeks'
                 time is granted to the learned counsel for the appellants
                 for removal of surviving defects.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                        Put up this case on 19.09.2018".
</blockquote>

<p id="p_5">            The defect regarding non filing of limitation petition as well
      as other minor defects has also not been removed.
</p><p id="p_6">            The appellants are original plaintiffs, who have instituted
      the suit for setting aside the ex parte decree in Title Suit No.56 of
      1979. They have lost before both the courts below.
</p><p id="p_7">            From the fact mentioned above, it is evident that the
      appellants are not interested in disposal of the case rather they
      are interested in keeping the matter defective.
</p><p id="p_8">            Accordingly, the present second appeal is dismissed as not
      pressed.
</p>
