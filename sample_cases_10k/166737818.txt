       The High Court Of Madhya Pradesh
                   CRR-68-2007
                            (MOHD.SHAMIM Vs RADHAYSHAYAM)

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">3</span>
Jabalpur, Dated : 12-07-2018
       None for the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">       Earlier also on 28.6.2018 and 5.7.2018 the case was adjourned as
no one appeared on behalf of the petitioner. In the order dated 5.7.2018
it was specifically observed that if no one would appear on behalf of the
petitioner, this Court shall be at liberty to vacate the interim relief
granted to the petitioner vide order dated 29.1.2007.
</p><p id="p_3">      Today also no one had appeared before this Court.
      Under the circumstances, the interim relief granted vide order
dated 29.1.2017 is vacated and the learned Trial Court is requested to
proceed with the Trial in Complaint case No.92/1999 Rahdey Shayam
Vs. Mohd.Shamim and another.
</p><p id="p_4">      A copy of this order be placed before the learned Trial Court for
necessary action.
</p><p id="p_5">      List this case after three weeks.
</p>
