<p id="p_1">1.            Rule.           Mr.L.B.Dabhi,         learned      Public       Public
Prosecutor       waives         service    of       Rule   on    behalf      of   the
respondents.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.            By way of the present application, the applicant
herein - undertrial prisoner, has prayed to extend his
temporary bail, which was granted and extended by this Court
vide order dated 12/09/2014 in Criminal Misc. Application
No.14434 of 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.            The applicant was released on temporary bail and
now, he is seeking extension of temporary bail. Considering
the facts and circumstances of the case,                        I am inclined to



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                      Page 1 of 2</span>
          R/CR.MA/15428/2014                        ORDER



extend temporary bail for a period of three weeks on the same
terms and conditions.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4.            Considering the above facts and circumstances of
the case, the present application is allowed. Temporary bail,
which has been earlier granted and extended by this Court
vide order dated 12/09/2014 in Criminal Misc. Application
No.14434 of 2014 is hereby extended for a period of three
weeks, on the same terms and conditions. Rule is made
absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p><p id="p_5">              Direct service is permitted today.
