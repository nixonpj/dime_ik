Advocate General, Punjab, accepts notice on behalf of the respondent-State.

on bail and diligently attending the Trial Court. He, however, absented

w.e.f. 10.05.2016 as he was admitted in the Drug De-Addiction Centre at

Nangal for one year and no process issued against him thereafter.

already been closed by that time and the matter was pending for
