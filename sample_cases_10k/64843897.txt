<div class="doc_title">Javed vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 27 March, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    MCRC-2733-2017
             (JAVED Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


27-03-2017

Shri Vivek Singh, learned counsel for the applicant.
Shri Pankaj Wadhwani, learned counsel for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Case-diary is available.
</p><p id="p_2">Arguments heard.
</p><p id="p_3">This is first application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C.
for grant of bail.
</p><p id="p_4">The present applicant was arrested by Police Station-
Barwani, District-Barwani, in Crime No.102/2017,
under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_2">Sections 376</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_3">506</a>, <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_4">376(D)</a> of IPC.
According to prosecution story, the present applicant
committed rape on prosecutrix by threatening her to
disclose the video clip which he made earlier when he
had physical relationship with her.
Learned counsel for the applicant submits that
prosecutrix was 22 years of age and she was a
consenting party. There is no injury found on her body
and the report was lodged when she was seen by two
other persons.
</p><p id="p_5">Learned counsel for the State opposed the application.
After taking all the facts and circumstances of the case
into consideration and without commenting on merits
of the case, this application is allowed.
It is directed that the present applicant shall be
released on bail on his furnishing a personal bond in
 the sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rs. Fifty Thousand only)
and one solvent surety of the like amount to the
satisfaction of the concerned Magistrate for his
appearance on all the dates of hearing as may be
directed in this regard during trial.
He is further directed that on being so released on
bail, he would comply with the conditions enumerated
under <a href="/doc/259682/" id="a_5">section 437(3)</a> Cr.P.C. meticulously.
Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                      (ALOK VERMA)
                                         JUDGE
