                                          ORDER

<p id="p_1">Date : 13-09-2019

      Heard Mr. P Bora, learned counsel for the accused-petitioners as well as Mrs. SH
Borah, learned Additional Public Prosecutor, appearing for the State of Assam.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      This is an application, filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Cr.PC. seeking pre-arrest bail
of the accused-petitioners, namely, 1. Abdul Mannan Sk. and 2. Hafizur Rahman, in
                                                                                     Page No.# 2/2

connection with Dhubri PS Case No. 1306/2019 registered under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">Sections
341</a>/<a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_3">427</a>/<a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_4">379</a>/<a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506</a> of the IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      Perused the case diary produced before this court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      On perusal of the materials in the case diary, it does not appear to this court that
there is any such material requiring custodial interrogation of the accused-petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      The petitioners are already on interim pre-arrest bail granted vide order, dated
19.08.2019. There is no instance to indicate that during this period, the petitioners have
misused the liberty granted to them while availing interim pre-arrest bail. In the
meantime, the petitioners have appeared before the Investigating Police Officer and got
their statements recorded.
</p>
<p id="p_6">      In view of the above facts, this court is inclined to grant the privilege of pre-arrest
bail to the petitioners and accordingly, the interim pre-arrest bail granted to the accused-
petitioners, on 19.08.2019, is made absolute on the same terms and conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_7">      This petition stands disposed of accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_8">      Return the case diary.
</p>


<p id="p_9">                                                                      JUDGE
