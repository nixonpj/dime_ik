<p id="p_1">C.M. No.12520 of 2017

              This application has been moved for placing on record legal

notice dated 12.05.2017 as Annexure P-9.
</p>
<p id="p_2">              C.M is allowed and Annexure P-9 is taken on record.

CWP No.16719 of 2017

              Learned counsel for the petitioner, at the very outset, submits

that the petitioner is entitled to be considered for promotion in view of

letter dated 23.09.2011. He further submits that in this regard, not only the

representation has been made but a legal notice dated 12.05.2017

(Annexure P-9) has also been served upon the respondents but in spite of

that, no action has been taken so far. Learned counsel further submits that

the petitioner would be satisfied, in case, the directions are issued by this

Court to the respondents to consider the legal notice dated 12.05.2017.

</p><p id="p_3">              Keeping in view the limited prayer and without commenting

anything on the merits of the case, the present petition is disposed of with

a direction to respondent No.1 to consider legal notice dated 12.05.2017
