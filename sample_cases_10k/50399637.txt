ORAL ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">	Registry has to
verify the service report by the concerned police station. Respondent
No.1 is directed to call upon the person, who has endorsed such
service on next date. List the matter on 28.6.2013. It seems that
service report is not proper. Therefore, learned advocate for the
petitioner is praying for fresh notice. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_2">	Let there be fresh
notice. Learned
advocate for the applicant prays for direct service, but requested
that he may be allowed to serve the notice through trial Court.
Request is allowed. Applicant has to handover the direct service to
the registry of the trial Court, who shall serve such notice to the
complainant in a pending matter before such Court either on the
adjourned date or on any date prior to the next date of hearing i.e.
28.6.2013. Ad-interim relief is extended till the next date of
hearing. 
</p>
