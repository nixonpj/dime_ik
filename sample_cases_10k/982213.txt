<div class="doc_title">Amir Naim @ Raja And Others vs State Of U.P.And Another on 18 January, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">Court No. - 49

Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 34318 of 2009

Petitioner :- Amir Naim @ Raja And Others
Respondent :- State Of U.P.And Another
Petitioner Counsel :- Shashi Dhar Pandey
Respondent Counsel :- Govt.Advocate

Hon'ble Rajesh Dayal Khare,J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">Heard learned counsel for the applicants and the learned AGA for the State-
respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">Learned counsel for the applicant has submitted that the present matter is a
matrimonial matter and the said matter can be well considered by the
Mediation and Conciliation Centre of this Court. It is further contended that
there are chances of reconciliation between the husband and wife, therefore,
the matter may be referred to the Mediation Centre.
</p>
<p id="p_3">It is directed that the applicant shall deposit a sum of Rs.8,000/- within two
weeks from today with the Mediation and Conciliation Centre of which 50%
shall be paid to the opposite party No.2 for appearance before the Mediation
Centre.
</p>
<p id="p_4">The matter is remitted to the Mediation Centre with the direction that the
same may be decided after giving notices to both the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_5">It is directed that the Mediation Centre shall decide the matter expeditiously
preferably within a period of four months. Thereafter the case shall be listed
before the appropriate Court on 19.04.2010.
</p>
<p id="p_6">Till the next date of listing, further proceedings against the applicants in crime
case no. 46 of 2009 under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Sections 498-A</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_3">504</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506</a> IPC and 3/4
<a href="/doc/1763444/" id="a_5">Dowry Prohibition Act</a>, PS Mahila Thana Rackbganj, District Agra pending
before the Special Chief Judicial Magistrate, Agra shall be kept in abeyance,
provided the applicants deposit the amount as stated above.
</p>
<p id="p_7">After depositing the aforesaid amount, notice shall be issued to the parties and
in case, the aforesaid amount is not deposited within the aforesaid period, the
interim protection granted above shall automatically be vacated.
</p>
<p id="p_8">Order Date :- 18.1.2010
shailesh
 </p>
