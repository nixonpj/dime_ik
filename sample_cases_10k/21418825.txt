<p id="p_1">            Learned       advocate       for    the   appellant   seeks
permission to withdraw present appeal with a liberty to
approach concerned Sessions Court, as the appellant has
moved present application directly to this Court without
approaching concerned Sessions Court.
</p><p id="p_2">            In light of above statement, present appeal stands
disposed of with a liberty to file fresh bail application under
<a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">section 439</a> of the Criminal Procedure Code before the
concerned Sessions Court, who in turn will decide the same in
accordance with law and without influenced by the present
order passed by this Court.
</p>



<p id="p_3">                                                        (S.H.VORA, J)
