<div class="doc_title">Smt. Krishna Bai Agrawal Thr Power ... vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh Thr on 8 September, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                             WP-5848-2015
(SMT. KRISHNA BAI AGRAWAL THR POWER OF ATTORNEY HOLDER MUKESH AGARWAL Vs
                      THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH THR)


08-09-2015
Shri Sarvesh Sharma, learned counsel for the petitioner.

Shri Praveen Newaskar, learned GA for respondents 1 and 2.</pre>

<p id="p_1">At the request of the petitioner's counsel, the case is taken up out
of turn from the list of motion hearing cases along with WP
5859/2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">In the course of arguments on admission, on asking the petitioner's
counsel whether he has filed any reply to the show cause notice of
respondent No.4 dated 25/8/2015 (Annexure P/1) on which he
submits that he has given reply in the shape of notice of advocate
dated 28/8/2015 (Annexure P/11) to the respondent No.4. On
asking him how the notice sent for demand of justice could be
treated to be reply of the show cause notice Annexure P/1, on
which, from his brief, by referring some papers he said that reply to
the show cause notice was filed on behalf of the petitioner in the
office of respondent No.4 on 2/9/2015, i.e. subsequent to filing this
petition. On asking the petitioner's counsel whether any pleading in
this regard has been amended in this petition on which he seeks
for a short adjournment till tomorrow to file an appropriate
application for amendment in the petition so also to place aforesaid
documents reply dated 2/9/2015 to the show cause notice (
Annexure P/1) on record.
</p>
<p id="p_3">Considering such submissions, office is directed to list this petition
                th
tomorrow ( 09        September, 2015) along with the record of WP
5859/2015.
</p>


<pre id="pre_2">(U.C. MAHESHWARI)                              (SUSHIL KUMAR GUPTA)
       JUDGE                                           JUDGE
