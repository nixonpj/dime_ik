<p id="p_1">8     14-12-2012                        This case was heard under Order 41 Rule 11 C.P.C. on

                         12.12.2012 and the order was reserved. Thereafter on the next

                         day, it was mentioned that in fact, the another Second Appeal

                         being S.A. No.447 of 2010 filed by the vendor of the present

                         appellant is still pending, therefore, the learned counsel submitted

                         that it will be better that both the Second Appeals may be heard

                         together.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                        In view of the above facts, the office is directed to list

                         Second Appeal No.447 of 2010, if it is defect free along with this

                         Second Appeal under the heading for hearing under Order 41 Rule

                         11 C.P.C. after X-mas Holidays. The order dated 12.12.2012 is

                         hereby recalled.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
