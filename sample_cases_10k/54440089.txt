<p id="p_1">       The prayer in this review petition is to review the judgment delivered

by this Court on 3.4.2012 disposing of W.P.(C) No.1801 of 2012 along with

a batch of other cases with the observation that the clearance certificates

issued pursuant to the interim orders passed by this Court in each of the

cases shall be treated as regular for all purposes. The main and the only

ground which was urged before me during the course of arguments today is

that the dispute involved in the writ petition has nothing to do with the issue

of a clearance certificate, that no interim order had been passed therein

directing grant of a clearance certificate and therefore, the order disposing

of the writ petition may be reviewed and recalled. The learned Government

Pleader appearing for the respondent did not oppose the said request and

submitted that the judgment may be reviewed and the writ petition posted

afresh for hearing as per roster.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        I accordingly allow the review petition and review and recall the

judgment delivered by this Court on 3.4.2012 disposing of W.P.(C) No.1801

of 2012.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       Registry is directed to post W.P.(C) No.1801 of 2012 as per roster

next week.
</p>
