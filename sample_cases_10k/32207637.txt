<p id="p_1">5.2010
                            C.R.A 143 of 2010

                        Re: C.R.A.N 1472 of 2010


         In the matter of an application for bail under <a href="/doc/985477/" id="a_1">Section 389</a>
                        Of the Code of Criminal Procedure

                  In re: Gopal Saha
                                  ......... appellant/convict

          Mr. Jaymalya Bagchi,
          Mr. Nilendra Narayan Ray
                             ......... For the appellant/convict

          Mr. Debabrata Nandy           ......       For the State

                    Mr. Bagchi appearing as learned counsel for the appellant/convict refers to the fact that
          the present appellant/convict after having been found guilty under <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_2">Sections 326</a>/<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">307of</a> Indian
          Penal Code has been sentenced to suffer imprisonment for 10 years.
</p><p id="p_2">                    Inviting attention of the court to the relevant materials it is submitted that the entire
          case cropped up out of on intra-family dispute and the present appellant/convict does not have
          any criminal antecedent.
</p><p id="p_3">                    After taking into consideration all relevant facts and circumstances, the present
          appellant/convict, who has been inflicted with a term imprisonment, be out on bail of
          Rs.10,000/- with two sureties of Rs.5,000/- each of whom one must be local. Such bail bonds
          are to be furnished to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Coochbehar to whom
          a copy of the order be sent at once.
</p><p id="p_4">                    Sentence inflicted upon the present appellant/convict do remain suspended during
          pendency of the appeal.
</p><p id="p_5">                    This disposes of C.R.A.N 1472 of 2010.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                          (S.P. Talukdar,J.)



                                        (Prabhat Kumar Dey,J.)
