<div class="doc_title">Rajesh Gope vs The State Of Bihar on 2 August, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                            Criminal Miscellanious No.23246 of 2011
                                            Rajesh Gope
                                                Versus
                                       The State Of Bihar
                                   ----------------------------------
</pre><p id="p_1">02.      02.08.2011           Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                      learned counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  There is clear allegation against this petitioner

                      about forceful commitment of rape is denied on the ground

                      that no sign of violence is found on the person of the

                      victim including her private part nor spermatozoa is found

                      in pathological report. Specifically it is said that due to

                      differences otherwise petitioner has been implicated.

</p><p id="p_3">                                   Accordingly, let the above named petitioner

                      be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-

                      (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to

                      the satisfaction of the IIIrd Additional Sessions Judge,

                      Hilsa, Nalanda in connection with S. Tr. No.244 of 2011

                      arising out of Karai Parsurai P. S. Case No.06 of 2011.


</p><pre id="pre_2">Vikash                                                 (Mandhata Singh, J.)
