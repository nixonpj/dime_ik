  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

1. ALEYKUTTY K.,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE STATE OF KERALA,
                       ...       Respondent

2. THE DIRECTOR OF PUBLIC INSTRUCTIONS,

 Dated :14/09/2007

 O R D E R

                W.P. (C). NO. 33564 OF 2006
               ---------------------------------------
         Dated this the 14th day of September, 2007.


                         J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Petitioner is essentially aggrieved by Exhibit P5 order

passed by the Deputy Director of Education, Palakkad in the

matter of reversion from the post of Primary School Headmistress.

</p><p id="p_2">      2.    Though several contentions are taken, since the

petitioner has an effective remedy by way of revision before the

Government as rightly pointed out by the learned Government

Pleader, it is not necessary for this Court at this stage to go into

all these matters. It is submitted that the petitioner is due to

retire by March 2008.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      In the above circumstances, this writ petition is disposed of

as follows:
</p>
<p id="p_4">      In the event of the petitioner filing a revision before the

Government within a period of one month from today, the same

shall be treated to have been filed in time. Thereafter, the matter

shall be disposed of by the first respondent with notice to the

petitioner and any affected party, within a period of another two

months.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                       KURIAN JOSEPH, JUDGE.
