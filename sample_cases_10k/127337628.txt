AMIT RAWAL, J. (ORAL)
</pre><p id="p_1">               The petitioners have filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the

Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 for issuance of directions to official

respondents to protect their life and liberty from the hands of private

respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">               The petitioners are stated to be major and have solemnized

marriage on 01.01.2019 which is against the wishes of their parents. The

factum of marriage has been pleaded with reference to photographs

(Annexure P-3). The petitioners have an apprehension at the hands of

private respondent Nos.4 to 6 and in this regard, they have submitted a

representation     dated     01.01.2019      (Annexure     P-4)   to   the   Senior

Superintendent of Police, Patiala, Punjab/respondent No.2, but no action has

been taken so far.
</p>
<p id="p_3">               The present petition is disposed of with the direction to the

Senior Superintendent of Police, Patiala, Punjab/respondent No.2 to look
