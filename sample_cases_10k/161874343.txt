<p id="p_1">11. 24-08-2018                This application has been preferred seeking

                 transfer of Matrimonial Case No. 1083/2015 from the court

                 of learned Principal Judge, Family Court, Patna to the

                 learned Principal Judge, Family Court, Bhagalpur.

</p><p id="p_2">                              In course of hearing, learned counsel for the

                 opposite party has agreed for transferring the matter from the

                 court of learned Principal Judge, Family Court, Patna to the

                 court of learned Principal Judge, Family Court, Bhagalpur.

</p><p id="p_3">                              In the aforesaid view of the matter, this court

                 directs transfer of Matrimonial Case No. 1083/2015 from the

                 court of learned Principal Judge, Family Court, Patna to the

                 court of learned Principal Judge, Family Court, Bhagalpur

                 within the period of 15 days from the date of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">       Patna High Court MJC No.2740 of 2016 (11) dt.24-08-2018                             2</span>




                       receipt/production of a copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_4">                                      This application stands disposed of, accordingly.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                        (Rajeev Ranjan Prasad, J)
