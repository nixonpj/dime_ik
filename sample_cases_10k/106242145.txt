                APPLICATION U/S 166 &amp; 140 M.V. ACT 1988
                      FOR GRANT OF COMPENSATION
AWARD

</p><p id="p_5">       1.         The present claim petition is filed by petitioner stating that he 

          suffered grievous injuries in an accident occurred on 23.07.2011. 

          As per the contents of the FIR No. 640/11, driver of the vehicle i.e. 

          DL­1LK­2065 (TATA 407) negligently drove the vehicle and hit 

          the   petitioner   as   well   as   his   motorcycle   due   to   which   petitioner 

          sustained grievous injuries.  
</p>
<p id="p_6">       2.         Copies of the claim petition alongwith annexures supplied to 

          the   driver   and   owner   of   the   of   the   offending   vehicle   and   to   the 

          counsel for the insurance company.  
</p>
<p id="p_7">       3.         The   insurance   company   has   given   an   offer   stating   that 

          company is ready to pay Rs.6,10,000/­ as full &amp; final settlement of 

          the claim.
</p>
<p id="p_8">       4.         Ld.counsel   for   the   insurance   company   submits   that   the 

          petitioner/   injured has suffered grievous injuries which is evident 

          from   the   contents   of   the   MLC   issued   by   the   GTB   Hospital. </p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                                                    K.Venugopal
                                                                    P.O. MACT (North­East), Delhi
MAC Petition No. 454/11                                                                  Page 2/3
</pre><p id="p_9">       Petitioner also submits that he has received grievous injuries.  Offer 

      made   by   the   insurance   company   was   accepted   by   the   petitioner. 

      Statements   of   the   petitioner   and   counsel   for   insurance   company 

      recorded separately.   
</p>
<p id="p_10">  5.         In   view   of   the   statements   made   by   the   parties   and   the 

      material based  on  record, I am of the opinion  that award  for  an 

      amount of Rs.6,10,000/­ can be passed in favour of the petitioner. 

      Accordingly,   petitioner   is   awarded   Rs.6,10,000/­   towards   full   &amp; 

      final   compensation.   Since   the   award   is   passed   with   the   mutual 

      consent of the parties no interest is granted. Insurance company is 

      directed to pay the award amount to the  petitioner by 03.07.2013. 

      In the event insurance company does not pay the award amount by 

      next date of hearing, further penal interest @12% p.a., shall be paid 

      by insurance company till actual realization.  

</p><p id="p_11">  6.         Copy of the award be given to the insurance company for 

      compliance. 
</p>
