<div class="doc_title">Satyendra Singh @ Sarak Singh vs The State Of Bihar on 4 February, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.4771 of 2016
                           Arising Out of PS.Case No. -189 Year- 2015 Thana -CHIRAIYA District-
                                             EASTCHAMPARAN(MOTIHARI)
                 ======================================================
                 1. Satyendra Singh @ Sarak Singh S/o Late Palat Singh, Resident of
                 Village - Jaipal Tola, P.S. - Chiraiya, District - East Champaran.

                                                                                  .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Nafisuzzoha, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Pushpa Sinha 2(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   04-02-2016                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                     State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The      petitioner       being      the      neighbour           of   the

                     informant is apprehending his arrest in a case registered

                     for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_3">379</a>,

                     <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a> of the Indian Penal Code and 3(1) (x) of the

                     S.C./S.T.(Prevention of Atrocities) Act.

</p><p id="p_3">                                The      prosecution          case      is    that     the        in   the

                     background of fight between the sons of the petitioner and

                     informant, the petitioner made assault to the informant

                     and abused by calling caste name.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                It is submitted by the learned counsel for the

                 petitioner that for the occurrence of 31.08.2015 the First

                 Information       Report       was      registered          on   05.09.2015.           The
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4771 of 2016 (2) dt.04-02-2016                                  2</span>




                      petitioner is neighbour of the informant. The informant has

                      retracted from the initial version and filed a petition to that

                      effect before learned court below. Statement has been made in

                      para-3      of    the    petition     that       petitioner   has   no   criminal

                      antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                                Considering the genesis of the occurrence

                      and the retracted version of the informant, let the above named

                      petitioner be released on bail in the event of his arrest or

                      surrender before the learned court below within a period of

                      twelve weeks from today, on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.

                      10,000/- (Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                      each to the satisfaction of learned Sri Uma Shankar, J.M.,

                      Sikrahana at Dhaka, East Champaran in connection with

                      Chiraiya P.S. Case No. 189 of 2015, subject to conditions as

                      laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_5">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal

                      Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                                 (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
P.K./-
</p>
