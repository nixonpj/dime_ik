<pre id="pre_1">                                    WP No. 822 of 2010

                              IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA

                            Constitutional Writ Jurisdiction

                                        ORIGINAL SIDE



THE KARANPURA DEVELOPMENT CO. LTD.                              Petitioner

     Versus

THE LD. 2ND INDUSTRIAL TRIBUNAL, W.B.,                         Respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">KOLKASTA-1.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
BEFORE:
</p>
<p id="p_3">The Hon'ble JUSTICE TAPEN SEN

Date : 1st July, 2010.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                      The Court :   Affidavit of Service is          taken on record.       There

is no appearance on behalf of the Respondents despite service.

</p><p id="p_6">                      One of the points argued by the learned Counsel for the

Petitioner is that the State of West Bengal has no jurisdiction inasmuch as

it       is   not   the   appropriate   Government   in   so   far   as    the   Petitioner      is

concerned which, according to him, is the Central Government undertaking

and therefore, the appropriate Government would be the Central Government.

</p><p id="p_7">                      Issue Notice to the Respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                      The requisites etc. must be filed by the Petitioner in the

Department within a period of ten days.                 The matter shall be re-listed on

the 4th of August 2010.             Till the 11th of August 2010 there shall be an

interim order in terms prayed for at prayer (e)

                      Department    and   all   parties    concerned       are   to   act   on    a

Photostat signed copy of this Order on usual undertakings.




</p><p id="p_9">                                                                          (TAPEN SEN, J.)
 M.Sen
