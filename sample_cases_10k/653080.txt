<div class="doc_title">Rishi Yadav &amp; Ors. vs The State Of Bihar on 25 August, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                        Criminal Miscellaneous No.25635 of 2011
              1. Rishi Yadav, S/o Mithilesh Yadav.
              2. Ravi Yadav, S/o Nand Yadav.
              3. Pappu Yadav, S/o Vijay Yadav.
                                          Versus
                                   The State of Bihar
                                    ---------------------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">02.   25.08.2011            Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the

                   State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                            The petitioners are apprehending their arrest in a

                   case registered under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>,

                   <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_6">307</a>, <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_7">308</a>, <a href="/doc/1183069/" id="a_8">386</a> and <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_9">324</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                            It has been submitted that the occurrence took

                   place on account of differences between the owners of

                   transport companies and subsequently the matter has

                   been compromised between them.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                            Considering the same as also the petitioners

                   have fair antecedents, let the petitioners, above named be

                   released on anticipatory bail in the event of arrest or

                   surrender before the learned Court below within a period

                   of four weeks from the date of receipt of the order on

                   furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 5,000/- (Five Thousand) each

                   with two sureties of the like amount each or any other

                   surety as fixed by the Court to the satisfaction of Chief

                   Judicial Magistrate, Nalanda at Biharsharif in connection

                   with Noorsarai P.S. Case No. 13 of 2011 subject to the

                   conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_10">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                     2</span>




of Criminal Procedure      as also subject to the following

conditions:- (i) That one of the bailors will be a close

relative of the petitioners who will give an affidavit giving

genealogy as to how he is related with the petitioners. The

bailor will also undertake to inform the Court if there is

any change in the address of the petitioners. (ii) That the

affidavit shall clearly state that the petitioners are not an

accused in any other case and if they are they shall not be

released on bail. (iii) That the bailor shall also state on

affidavit that he will inform the court concerned if the

petitioners are implicated in any other case of similar

nature after their release in the present case and

thereafter the court below will be at liberty to initiate the

proceeding for cancellation of bail on the ground of

misuse. (iv) That the petitioners will give an undertaking

that they will receive the police papers on the given date

and be present on date fixed for charge and if they fail to

do so on two given dates and delays the trial in any

manner, their bail will be liable to be cancelled for reasons

of misuse. (v) That the petitioners will be well represented

on each date and if they fail to do so on two consecutive

dates, their bail will be liable to be cancelled.

</p><p id="p_5">          Initially the Magistrate concerned will release

the petitioners on provisional bail and thereafter notice

the informant and if it is confirmed that the informant has
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                               3</span>




           compromised the matter with the petitioners without any

           coercion then he will confirm the bail of the petitioners on

           conditions as mentioned above.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                       (Anjana Prakash, J.)
Vikash/-
</p>
