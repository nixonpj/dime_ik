                               JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Petitioner is a stage carriage operator operating on the

route Medical College - Kundungal - Mankavu with stage carriage

bearing registration No.KL 11/S 1611. According to the

petitioner, the timing of the service was lastly issued on

22.03.2012.      Therefore, seeking variation of the timing, the

petitioner has submitted Ext.P1 application before the respondent

which is pending consideration.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       2.   Having heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

learned Government Pleader, there will be a direction to the

respondent to consider Ext.P1 and pass orders at the earliest and

at any rate within two months from the date of receipt of a copy

of this judgment, after providing notice of hearing to the

petitioner and all interested or affected persons.

</p><p id="p_3">       The Writ Petition is disposed of as above.


</p><p id="p_4">                                                      Sd/-
</p><p id="p_5">                                           SHAJI P. CHALY, JUDGE


ttb 22/02
