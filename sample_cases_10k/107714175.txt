</pre><p id="p_1">       I.A. No. 5168 of 2016
</p><p id="p_2">       1.     Present interlocutory application has been preferred under <a href="/doc/100581/" id="a_1">section 5</a> of
       the Limitation Act, 1963, for condonation of delay of 108 days in preferring this
       Letters Patent Appeal.
</p><p id="p_3">       2.     Having heard counsels for the both sides and looking to the reasons
       stated in the interlocutory application, especially in paragraph No.s 4 to 9, it
       appears that there are reasonable grounds for condonation of delay.
</p><p id="p_4">       3.     In view of these facts, we, hereby, condone the delay in preferring this
       Letters Patent Appeal. Accordingly, I.A. No. 5168 of 2016 is allowed and
       disposed of.
</p><p id="p_5">       L.P.A. No. 166 of 2016
</p><p id="p_6">       4.     This Appeal is Admitted.
</p><p id="p_7">       5.     Counsel for Respondent No.s 3 to 5 are waiving Notice of Admission.
</p><p id="p_8">       6.     Notice upon rest of the respondents to be served through ordinary
       process, for which requisites etc. to be filed within one week.
</p><p id="p_9">       7.     Registry is directed to enlist this appeal in the 2nd week of March, 2019.
</p>



<p id="p_10">                                                                    (D.N.Patel, J.)
