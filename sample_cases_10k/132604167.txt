            For the Pollution Control Board: Mr. A.K. Pandey, Adv.

            Order No. 05          ------ Dated 19th September, 2012

                      Learned counsel for the Pollution Control Board

            submitted that proceedings in these matters have been

            stayed by Hon'ble Supreme Court in Transfer Petitions.

</p><p id="p_5">                      In view of above, no need to list these matters

            till the transfer petitions are decided by the Hon'ble

            Supreme Court and if any order is passed by the Hon'ble

            Supreme Court for transferring these petitions, the same

            be complied with by the Registry.

</p><p id="p_6">                      However, on deciding the transfer petition by

            the Hon'ble Supreme Court, the counsel for the petitioner

            may submit copy of that order and if needed, the matter

            may be listed in Courts.
</p>
