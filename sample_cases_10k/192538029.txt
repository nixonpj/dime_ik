DATED: 01.08.2016  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.K.SASIDHARAN            
and 
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE B.GOKULDAS            

W.P.(MD) No.10084 of 2015  
and 
M.P.(MD) No.1 of 2015 


Asaithambi                                                                      ... Petitioner

-vs-

1.The Assistant Director of Town Panchayats 
   Regional Office, Sivagangai District

2.The Executive Officer
   Sayalkudi Town Panchayat 
   Ramanathapuram District 

3.The President
   Sayalkudi Town Panchayat 
   Kadaladi Taluk
   Ramanathapuram District 

4.Abdul Kani                                                            ... Respondents

PRAYER: Writ Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India
for issuance of writ of certiorarified mandamus to call for the records
pertaining to the impugned order in Na.Ka.No.84/2014, passed by the 2nd
respondent, dated 06.05.2015 and quash the same and consequently direct the 
2nd respondent to allot Shop No.11, situated at Sayalkudi Bus Stand, Kadaladi
Taluk, Ramanathapuram District to the petitioner.

!For Petitioners        :       No appearance

^For Respondents        :       Mr.Aayiram K.Selvakumar  
                          Government Advocate for R1 &amp; R3 
                        Mr.R.Anandharaj for R2 
                        Mr.J.Senthil Kumariah for R4

:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">   [Order of the Court was made by K.K.SASIDHARAN, J.]  
When the writ petition is taken up for hearing, none appeared on behalf of
the petitioner.  The writ petition is dismissed for non-prosecution.  No
costs.  Consequently, connected miscellaneous petition is closed.
</p>
