<div class="doc_title">Phagulal vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 18 April, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                         MCRC-6491-2016
               (PHAGULAL Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


18-04-2016

      Shri Narendra Nikhare, learned counsel for the applicant.
      Shri S.K. Shrivastava, learned P.L. for the respondent/State.</pre>

<p id="p_1">      This is the first bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C.

for offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1783355/" id="a_2">sections 9</a>, <a href="/doc/454573/" id="a_3">39(1)(ba)</a>, <a href="/doc/1771214/" id="a_4">39(2)</a> and <a href="/doc/672187/" id="a_5">51</a> of

the Wild Life Protection Act, 1972 in connection with Forest

Crime No.38934/2014 registered at Police Station Van

Parikshetra Adhikari Barghat, Dakshin Samanya Vanmandal,

Seoni, District Seoni.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      Learned counsel for the applicants does not wish to press

the bail application on behalf of applicant no.1 Phagulal and seeks

liberty to renew the prayer after two months.

</p><p id="p_3">      Now the bail application is being considered on behalf of

applicant nos.2 to 9.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      So far as applicant nos.2 to 9 are concerned, it is said that

they have received 3 Kgs. of beef of Chital. However, they have

not hunted wild life animal. After receiving the flesh, they have

distributed among themselves. The applicants are in custody

since last 2Â½ months, therefore, prayer is made to enlarge the
 applicants on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      On due consideration of facts and circumstances of the

case, this court deems it proper to grant bail to the applicants

namely Santosh, Devi Singh, Durga Prasad, Anil, Bodh

Singh, Ravindra, Kharak Singh and Surendra. They be

released on bail on thier furnishing a personal bond in a sum of

Rs.25,000/- each with a solvent surety in the like amount to the

satisfaction of JMFC concerned or CJM for their appearance in

the trial Court on the dates so fixed by that Court during the

trial. It is directed that the applicants shall comply the provisions

of <a href="/doc/259682/" id="a_6">Section 437(3)</a> of Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">      Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                                            (J.K. MAHESHWARI)
                                                   JUDGE
