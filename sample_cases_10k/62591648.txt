KULDIP SINGH J. (ORAL)

</pre><p id="p_5">             Learned Advocates are not appearing today in Court on account

of sad demise of two young Bar Members of this Court in a motor vehicle

accident.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             File perused.
</p>
<p id="p_7">             Petitioners, who are stated to be major, claim that they have

married against the wishes of their parents on 24.1.2019.            True typed

translated copy of marriage certificate has been placed on file as

Annexure-P-3. The photographs of the marriage have been placed on file

as   Annexure-P-4 (Colly.). True and correct relevant extracted typed

copies of Aadhar Cards, showing date of birth of both petitioners, have

been placed on file as Annexure-P-1 and Annexure-P-2 respectively.

</p><p id="p_8">             It is claimed that the petitioners have moved an application

dated 24.1.2019 before the police authorities for protecting their life and

liberty as they apprehend danger to their life from the private respondents,

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> CRM No. M-3963-2019 (O/M)                                                  -2-</span>

true typed copy of which is placed on file as Annexure-P-5.

</p><p id="p_9">             In these circumstances, the present petition is disposed of with

a direction to Superintendent            of Police,   Hisar-respondent No. 2, to

consider the application of the petitioners dated 24.1.2019 (Annexure-P-5)

and assess the threat perception to the life and liberty of the petitioners and

if it is found that there is danger to the life of the petitioners, necessary steps

be taken to protect the life and liberty of the petitioners. However, this order

shall not be construed as a recognition of valid marriage between the

parties.
</p>
