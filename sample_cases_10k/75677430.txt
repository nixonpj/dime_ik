               DARSHANABEN KISHORBHAI PATEL
                           Versus
                     STATE OF GUJARAT

Appearance:
MR LUV S SHAH(9365) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1

================================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.P.DHOLARIA

                            Date : 10/08/2018

                             ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">      Learned   advocate   Mr.   Jal   Unwala   assisted   by 
learned   advocate   Ms.   Nirali   Oza   for   the   applicants 
submits   that   the   original   partnership   deed   is   with 
the Registrar of Firms, Ahmedabad. He further submits 
that in the original partnership deed, the share of 
the complainant - Anilbhai Shankarbhai Patel is 60%, 
whereas   the   share   of   the   applicant   -   Kishorbhai 
Shankarbhai   Patel   is   40%   and   there   is   no   forgery 
committed by the applicants. He submits that this is 
a totally concocted complaint which is required to be 
quashed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      In   view   of   aforesaid   factual   scenario,   let 
learned Additional Public Prosecutor Mr. L. B. Dabhi 
call   for   the   original   partnership   deed   from   the 
Registrar   of   Firms,  Ahmedabad   through   the   concerned 
Investigating Agency.
</p>
