<div class="doc_title">Yogendra Ravidas vs The State Of Jharkhand &amp; Anr on 24 April, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">          IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                             B.A. No. 334 of 2019
          Yogendra Ravidas                        ..... Petitioner
                                  Versus
          The State of Jharkhand &amp; Anr.           .....  Opp. Party
                                     -----
</pre>
<p id="p_1">          CORAM:   HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
</p><p id="p_2">                                   -----
</p><p id="p_3">     For the Petitioner : Mr. N.P.Thakur, Advocate
     For the State             : A.P.P
</p><p id="p_4">                                  -----
</p><p id="p_5">06 /Dated: 24/04/2019

          Pursuant to order dated 19.02.2019, both the parties are
physically present before this court alongwith their learned counsel.
</p><p id="p_6">          In order to test the bona fide of the petitioner, he is directed to pay
Rs. 4,00,000/(Four lacs)- to O.P.N.2 as ad interim co compensation.
</p><p id="p_7">          Petitioner is further directed to deposit Rs. 2,00,000/-(two lacs), as
first installment in the court below latest by 10.06.2019.Thereafter,       O.P.
No.2 will appear before the court below and file application for
withdrawal of the amount and the court below is directed to release the
aforesaid amount in favour of O.P. No.2.
</p><p id="p_8">          Out of the said amount, O.P.No.2 will use Rs. 1 lacs for her
personal expenses and rest amount of Rs. 1 lac will fixed under FDR
Scheme in any nationalized bank for a period of Five years and also
deposit the original certificate of the said deposit in the court below.
</p><p id="p_9">           Petitioner is further directed to deposit Rs. 2,00,000/-(two lacs),
as second installment in the court below latest by 07.08.2019.
</p><p id="p_10">          In that view of the matter, the provisional bail earlier granted to
the petitioner till 03.05.2019 is further extended till 03.09.2019.
</p><p id="p_11">          List this case on 20.08.2019. On that date, both the parties are
directed to remain physically present before this court, so that in their
presence, further order may be passed.
</p><p id="p_12">          Meanwhile, office is directed to call for a report as to whether the
amount amount is deposited by the petitioner or not ?
</p><p id="p_13">          However, it is made clear that during investigation and trial of the
case, petitioner will not influence, intimidate or gain over either the
informant-O. P. No.2 or her witnesses and if he will found indulge in the
aforesaid act, it will be opened for the informant-O.P. No.2 to file an
application for cancellation of bail bond of the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_14">          Let a copy of this order be communicated to the trial court through
FAX.
</p>

<p id="p_15">                                                 (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
Raman/-
</p>
