                A.B.A. No. 6102 of 2017

Md. Raushik @ Md. Inzman            ..... Petitioner
                        Versus
The State of Jharkhand              ..... Opp. Party
                         ---------
</pre><p id="p_1">CORAM:      HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
</p><p id="p_2">                          ---------
</p><p id="p_3">For the Petitioner      : Mr. Pravin Kumar, Advocate.
</p><pre id="pre_2">For the State           : A.P.P.
                     ---------
08/Dated: 08/05/2018

</pre><p id="p_4">         In terms of order dated 26.02.2018, this Court has
directed to list this case before appropriate Bench after
taking necessary permission from Hon'ble the Acting Chief
Justice.
</p><p id="p_5">         Thereafter, it appears that in B.A. No. 3828 of 2017
and another analogous matters, the Hon'ble Division Bench
of this Court on 09.04.2018 has clarified that which matter
is tied up and which matter is to be listed before regular
Bench.
</p><p id="p_6">         Thereafter, under order dated 13.04.2018, Hon'ble
Coordinate Bench of this Court has directed to place the
matter before the Hon'ble the Acting Chief Justice for
passing appropriate orders in light of Hon'ble Division
Bench Judgment.
</p><p id="p_7">         Thereafter, office has placed this case before this
Court today without taking permission from Hon'ble the
Acting Chief Justice.
</p><p id="p_8">         Under the said circumstance, Joint Registrar (List &amp;
Computer) is directed to take necessary permission from
Hon'ble the Acting Chief Justice in light of the judgment
passed by the Hon'ble Division Bench in B.A. No. 3828 of
2017.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                                   (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
Sunil/
