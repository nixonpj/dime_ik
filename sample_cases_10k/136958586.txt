                       AT CHANDIGARH

                         Date of Decision: 15.01.2018
                                    ****
Rani Kaur                                                          ..... Petitioner
                                       Versus

State of Punjab                                                  ..... Respondent

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SUDIP AHLUWALIA

Present:     Mr. H.S. Rakhra, Advocate for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">             Mr. Luvinder Sofat, AAG, Punjab.
</p>
<p id="p_3">SUDIP AHLUWALIA J. (ORAL)

             This is a petition for regular bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. on

behalf of the petitioner, who was arrested on 10.03.2017 in case FIR No.10

dated 10.03.2017, under <a href="/doc/591351/" id="a_2">Sections 489-A</a>, <a href="/doc/1926477/" id="a_3">489-B</a>, <a href="/doc/257920/" id="a_4">489-C</a>, <a href="/doc/193831/" id="a_5">489-D</a> and <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_6">420</a> of

the IPC read with <a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_7">Section 25</a>/<a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_8">54</a>/<a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_9">59</a> of the Arms Act, registered at Police

Station Sadar Budhlada, District Mansa.

[2].         It is submitted by Ld. State counsel that after an earlier order

was passed on 06.12.2017, the only two private witnesses in the case have

already been examined. Those witnesses do not appear to have supported

the prosecution case.
</p>
<p id="p_4">[3].         Co-accused, namely, Pritam Singh has already been granted

bail in CRM-M-21645-2017.
</p>
<p id="p_5">[4].         In the given circumstances, the interim bail granted to the

petitioner earlier is, hereby, confirmed.

</p><pre id="pre_2">[5].         Disposed off.


15.01.2018                                         (SUDIP AHLUWALIA)
Bhumika                                                  JUDGE
