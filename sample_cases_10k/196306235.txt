                             3




                         ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Respondent      No.2   is   present     with    corpus

Ms.Ranjana M.Kumawat, daughter of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">     2. The marriage certificate is produced. It is taken

on record and as per the said certificate on 05.04.2017,

the marriage is performed. Respondent No.2 states that

he will properly treat the corpus as wife.

</p><p id="p_3">     3. In view of the above, we would have taken

serious action against the petitioner for making false

statement and suppression.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     4. However, the learned Counsel Mr.M.N.Nehru,

states that the petitioner is present and he withdraws

all allegations made against his daughter and he also

undertakes that the petitioner shall not create any

disturbance in the matrimonial life of the corpus, his

daughter Ms.Ranjana M.Kumawat.

</p><p id="p_5">     5. In view of the above, we leave it at that.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             4</span>




</p><p id="p_6">       6. As the corpus has married voluntarily and the

same is verified, no further order deserves to be passed

and the petition is dismissed. The parties shall abide by

the declaration made before this Court.

</p><p id="p_7">       7. Mr.A.S.Kulkarni, learned Counsel is permitted

to file his appearance for respondent No.2.
