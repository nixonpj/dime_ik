<div class="doc_title">Hasmat Ara &amp; Anr vs State Of Jharkhand on 18 July, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                             B.A. No.4525 of 2012

          1. Hasmat Ara
          2. Imamuddin Ansari                               .....   Petitioners
                                      Versus
          The State of Jharkhand                            ....      Opposite Party

          CORAM:        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.C. MISHRA

          For the Petitioners         :       Mr. Peeyush Krishna Choudhary
          For the State               :       A. P.P.

                                   -----
2/18.7.2012</pre><p id="p_1">      Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned counsel for the State.
</p><p id="p_2">                 Petitioners have been made accused for the offence under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a>
          of the Indian Penal Code in connection with Godda (M) P.S. Case No.124 of 2012,
          corresponding to G.R. No.381(A) of 2012.
</p><p id="p_3">                 The case relates to murder of the father of the informant.            Both the
          petitioners are named in the FIR.
</p><p id="p_4">                 It appears that after investigation, police submitted final form as mistake of
          fact, but the Court below took cognizance against the petitioners under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_3">Section
          302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the IPC.
</p><p id="p_5">                 In the facts and circumstances of the case, particularly in view of the fact
          that the police had submitted final form in favour of the petitioners, I am inclined to
          release the petitioners on bail. Accordingly, the petitioners Hasmat Ara and
          Imamuddin Ansari are directed to be released on bail, on furnishing bail bonds of
          Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand) each with two sureties of like amount each to
          the satisfaction of learned Sub-Divisional Judicial Magistrate, Godda, in
          connection with Godda (M) P.S. Case No.124 of 2012, corresponding to G.R.
          No.381(A) of 2012.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                                          (H. C. Mishra, J)
R.Kumar
 </p>
