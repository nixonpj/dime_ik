<div class="doc_title">Abhay Kumar Rai vs The State Of Bihar on 9 July, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.26738 of 2015
                  Arising Out of PS.Case No. -134 Year- 2015 Thana -MOHANIA District- BHABHUA (KAIMUR)
                 ======================================================
                 1. Abhay Kumar Rai Son of Shio Shanker Rai Resident of village -
                 Bharkhar, P.S. Mohania, District - Kaimur ( Bhabhua )

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr. Pancham Lal Jaiswal
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Ashraf Ansari(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE KISHORE KUMAR MANDAL
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   09-07-2015                     Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   The petitioner apprehends his arrest in connection

                 with Mohania P.S. Case No. 134 of 2015 registered under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections

                 420</a> and <a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_2">409</a> of the IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   It is a case where the petitioner was supplied paddy

                 for milling and delivery thereof to the Food Corporation of India

                 at the agreed rate. The petitioner defaulted in doing so                           and

                 thereby committed misappropriation of the Government fund in

                 the sum of Rs. 29,01,850.40/-.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   It has been submitted that diverse orders have been

                 passed by this Court in identical/similar matters wherein after

                 deposit of certain percentage of amount as also considering the

                 fact that there is an agreement reached by and between the parties
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.26738 of 2015 (2) dt.09-07-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                           2/3</span>




             and in few cases proceeding under the PDR is also filed against

             the accused, they have been privileged with anticipatory bail.

</p><p id="p_5">                               Learned counsel for the Bihar State Food &amp; Civil

             Supplies Corporation Ltd., Patna ( for short 'the Corporation') has

             not disputed the aforesaid contention.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                               Regard being had to the above, I direct that the

             petitioner, abovenamed, in the event of his arrest/surrender before

             the learned Court below within a period of five weeks from today,

             shall be released on provisional bail for a period of 06 months

             from the date of release on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 15,000/-

             (Fifteen thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to

             the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Kaimur at

             Bhabhua in connection with Mohania P.S. Case No. 134 of 2015

             subject to the condition as laid down under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_3">Section 438</a> (2) of the

             <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_4">Cr.P.C</a> with further condition that one of the bailers shall be the

             own/close family members of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_7">                        The petitioner shall produce receipt of deposit of 15%

             of the      misappropriated amount depicted in the F.I.R. through

             demand draft/bank instrument                 or otherwise in favour of the

             Corporation alongwith the bail bonds.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                        If the petitioner deposits the remaining 10% of the

             misappropriated amount within 06 months                       and furnish
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.26738 of 2015 (2) dt.09-07-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                                      3/3</span>




                        receipt/document with a prayer for confirmation of the bail, the

                        learned court below shall confirm the provisional bail.




</p><p id="p_9">                                                (Kishore Kumar Mandal, J)
Pankaj/-
</p>
