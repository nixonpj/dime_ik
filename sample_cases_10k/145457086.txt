<div class="doc_title">Kashi Kumar vs State Of Bihar &amp; Anr on 20 August, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                          Cr.Misc. No.2480 of 2010
                  KASHI KUMAR S/O LATE TAMOL MAHTO
                                   Versus
               1. THE STATE OF BIHAR
               2. ARUN THAKUR S/O LATE SONELAL THAKUR
                                  -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">2.   20.08.2010               No one appears on behalf of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">                              The petitioner has sought quashing of the order of

                   cognizance on the ground that the complaint is evidently

                   frivolous as also that there was an earlier case of similar

                   nature still pending.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              Issue notice to the opposite party no. 2 to show

                   cause as to why this application be not admitted/disposed off

                   at the stage of admission itself, for which requisites etc. under

                   registered cover with A/D as well as ordinary process must

                   be filed within 10 days, failing which this application shall

                   stand rejected without further reference to the Bench.

</p><p id="p_4">                              Rule returnable three months.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><pre id="pre_2">         Fahad.                                    ( Anjana Prakash, J )
