<p id="p_1">6   09-04-2019                   After some arguments, the learned counsel for the

                   petitioners seeks to withdraw the present writ petition, faced

                   with the issue of the present writ petition being barred by the

                   principals of delay and laches as also in view of the fact that the
            Patna High Court CWJC No.2371 of 2016(6) dt.09-04-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     petitioners had not complied with the earlier order of this Court

                     and had approached the District Teachers Appointment

                     Appellate Authority, Darbhanga much after expiry of the time-

                     line fixed by this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 Accordingly, the present writ petition is dismissed as

                     withdrawn.
</p>
