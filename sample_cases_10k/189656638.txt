                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.19856 of 2017
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -1532 Year- 2015 Thana -COMPLAINT CASE District- BANKA
                 ======================================================
                 Santosh Kumar Mandal S/o Sri Sharwan Mandal Resident of Village- Bela,
                 P.O.- Gulani Kushaha, P.S. Shambhuganj, District- Banka.
                                                                          .... .... Petitioner
                                                  Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar.
                 2. Soni @ Komal Kumar aged about 21 years W/o Santosh Kumar Mandal
                    D/o Naresh Mandal Resident of Village- Rampur, P.S.- Sangrampur,
                    District- Munger.
                                                                   .... .... Opposite Parties
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Pawan Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Pawan Kumar Chaurasiya
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAJENDRA KUMAR
                 MISHRA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">4   13-10-2017                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned

                 A.P.P. for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner apprehends his arrest in connection with

                 Complaint Case No. 1532 of 2015, registered under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections 323</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/1868826/" id="a_2">498(A)</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_3">Section 4</a> of the Dowry

                 Prohibition Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that on the

                 joint prayer of the parties, the matter was referred to the Mediation

                 Centre, Patna High Court, Patna for mediation and settlement of

                 dispute between the petitioner and O.P. No.2, who are husband and

                 wife, but in spite of best and sincere efforts, the dispute between the

                 parties could not be resolved through the process of mediation. Further

                 submission is that while petitioner was ready to keep his wife (O.P.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.19856 of 2017 (4) dt.13-10-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_4">                        No.2), but O.P. No.2 refused to go with the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_5">                                        Learned counsel appearing on behalf of O.P. No.2

                        submits that she is not ready to live with the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_6">                                        Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case,

                        let the above named petitioner, be released on bail, in the event of his

                        arrest or surrender before the learned Court below within a period of

                        four weeks from today, on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten

                        thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction

                        of the S.D.J.M., Banka in connection with Complaint Case No. 1532

                        of 2015, subject to the condition as laid down under <a href="/doc/1763444/" id="a_4">Section 438</a> (2) of

                        the <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_5">Cr.P.C</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                                                (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J)
manish/-
</p>
