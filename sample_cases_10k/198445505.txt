<p id="p_2">            Md. Yamin Ali                 ... for the petitioner.


            Mr. Krishna Das Poddar   ... for the Opposite Parties.



</p><p id="p_3">                            Re : CAN 4930 of 2016

                   This is an application for restoration of this

           revisional application which was dismissed for default

           on April 15, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                   Perused the application.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                   It appears that sufficient cause has been shown

           which prevented the petitioner from appearing before

           the Court when the matter was called on for hearing.

</p><p id="p_6">                   CAN 4930 of 2016 is therefore allowed.

                   C.O. No. 727 of 2006 is restored to its original

           file and number.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                            Re : CAN 9349 of 2018

                   This is an application for substitution of the

           legal heirs and representatives of the deceased opposite
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    2</span>


party no. 2 Md. Nashim Alam, who died on April 16,

2012 after setting aside abetment on condonation of

delay.
</p>
<p id="p_8">         Perused the application.
</p>
<p id="p_9">         It appears that the petitioner has sufficiently

explained the delay in not filing the application within

time. The delay in filing the said application is therefore

condoned.
</p>
<p id="p_10">         CAN 9349 of 2018 is therefore allowed.

         Let the legal heirs and representatives of the

deceased opposite party no. 2 whose particulars have

been set out at paragraph no. 2 of the application be

substituted in his place.
</p>
<p id="p_11">         The   department   is    directed   to   carry   out

necessary amendments in the cause title of the

revisional application.
</p>
<p id="p_12">         Mr. Krishna Das Poddar, learned advocate

appearing for the plaintiffs/opposite parties undertake

to file Vakalatnama on behalf of the substituted

opposite parties within a week.

</p><p id="p_13">               Re : C.O. 727 of 2006

         Let the matter appear in the Combined Monthly

List of January, 2019 under the heading 'Assigned
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                    3</span>


Motion'.
</p>
<p id="p_14">        Urgent Photostat certified copy of this order, if

applied for, be supplied to the parties subject to

compliance with all requisite formalities.



</p><p id="p_15">                                    (Biswajit Basu, J.)
