             IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT
                        CHANDIGARH

                                          CRM-M-31033 of 2018.
                                          Decided on:-July 25, 2018.


Gurdev Singh and others
                                                            .........Petitioners.
                                 Versus
State of Punjab and others

                                                            .........Respondents.


CORAM:       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE HARI PAL VERMA.

             *****

Present:-    Ms. Ruchi Sekhri, Advocate and
             Mr. B.S. Sra, Advocate
             for the petitioners.

HARI PAL VERMA, J. (ORAL)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Prayer in this petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.PC is for

issuance of a direction to respondents No.1 and 3 to protect the life and

property of the petitioners at the hands of private respondents No.6 to 9 and

their accomplices and to take legal action against respondent No.5 and his

fellow officials for misusing their position and authorities as police officials

under political influence of respondents No.7 and 8.

</p><p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioners states that for the protection

of life and property of the petitioners, they have already made a representation

dated 16.07.2018 (Annexure P-5) to various authorities including respondent

No.3-Senior Superintendent of Police, Ferozepur. She further states that at

this stage, the petitioners confine their prayer qua protection of their life and

liberty only and petitioners would be satisfied if a direction is issued to

respondent No.3 to consider the said representation viz. a viz. their prayer for

protection of the life and liberty only.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                Notice of motion to respondents No.1 and 3 only.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                On asking of this Court, Mr. Tanvir Joshi, AAG, Punjab accepts

notice on behalf of these respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                Let requisite number of copies of paper book be given to learned

State counsel during the course of the day.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                Record reveals that the parties are already in civil litigation and,

therefore, this fact cannot be ignored lightly. Be that as it may, but without

endorsing the contents as submitted in representation (Annexure P-5), the

respondent No.3 is directed to look into the contents of said representation

only to the extent of protection of life and liberty of the petitioners and to act

in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                In case it is found that the allegations of threat to life and liberty

are found to be false, the respondent No.3 shall be competent to take further

legal course.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                Disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_2">
