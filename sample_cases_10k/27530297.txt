                   BENCH AT JAIPUR

      S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail No. 15778/2018

Ummed Singh S/o Shri Girdharilal B/c Jat, R/o Raghunathpura
Police Station Gudhagaurji Distt. Jhunjhunu. At Present In Distt.
Jail, Jhunjhunu.
                                                        ----Petitioner
                              Versus

                                                  ----Respondent</pre>



<p id="p_1">For Petitioner(s)      :   Mr. Harendra Singh
For Respondent(s)      :   Mr. Ram Ratan Gurjar, P.P.



</p><p id="p_2">                HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA

                           Judgment

12/12/2018

     Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Code of

Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking regular bail in F.I.R. No.

423/2018 registered at Police Station Kotwali Jhunjhunu, Distt.

Jhunjhunu, for offences under <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_2">Sections 384</a>, <a href="/doc/1030257/" id="a_3">388</a>, <a href="/doc/970675/" id="a_4">389</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120-B</a> of

Indian Penal Code, 1860.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the

petitioner is in custody since 18.09.2018. Challan has already

been presented in the Court and conclusion of trial may take time.

</p><p id="p_4">     Learned State Counsel on the other hand has opposed the

petition.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     Keeping in view the facts and circumstances of the case, it

would be just and expedient to order the release of the petitioner

on bail.
</p><p id="p_6">                                                                    (2 of 2)            [CRLMB-15778/2018]


                                        Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on merits of the

                                   case, this petition is allowed. Petitioner be admitted to bail subject

                                   to satisfaction of the Trial Court.


</p><p id="p_7">                                                                                           (SABINA)J.
</p>
