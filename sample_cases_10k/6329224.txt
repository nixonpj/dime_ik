      IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD


          R/SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 14241 of 2018


===============================================
              BHUPENDRA ALIAS BHOPO ISHVARBHAI PARMAR
                                  Versus
                             STATE OF GUJARAT
===============================================
Appearance:
MR MAULIN G PANDYA(3999) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1
DS AFF.NOT FILED (N)(11) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 2,3
DR.VENUGOPAL PATEL, AGP (1) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
===============================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J. SHASTRI

                             Date : 19/09/2018

                                  ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">[1]   Rule. Learned AGP waives service of notice of Rule on
behalf of the respondent - State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">[2]   The present petition is filed under apprehended action of
detention by the authority. The Court on 17.09.2018 was pleased
to issue Notice making it returnable on 19.09.2018. Today, when
the   matter    has        come   up    for       hearing,   learned   Assistant
Government       Pleader      placed      on       record    a   communication
indicating that there is no proposal of detention of the present
petitioner under the PASA in respect of C.R.No.III-122 of 2018
registered with Chaklasi Police Station, Kheda for the offences
mentioned therein.          The said communication is submitted by
Police Inspector, LCB Kheda, Nadiad, informing the office of the
Government Pleader that there is no proposal of detention of the
present petitioner under the PASA in respect of offences which
are mentioned. The said communication is taken on record.
</p>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    Page 1 of 2</span>
<pre id="pre_2">              C/SCA/14241/2018                                     ORDER



[3]    Hence,         in    light   of   aforesaid     communication,     learned
</pre><p id="p_3">advocate appearing for the petitioner does not press the present
petition at this stage. It is clarified that in view of aforesaid
instructions since the petition is withdrawn, the detaining
authority shall not detain the petitioner under PASA in respect of
complaints which are mentioned in the present petition. It is
further clarified that this order is restricted to present FIR which
is brought before the Court. In view of this, petition stands
disposed of as not pressed. Rule is discharged. No order as to
costs.
</p>

<p id="p_4">       Direct service today is permitted.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                             (A.J.SHASTRI ,J.)

dharmendra
