<div class="doc_title">Vinay Sahu vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 8 March, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                        MCRC-21514-2015
               (VINAY SAHU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


08-03-2016

Shri G.M. Burrows, learned counsel for the applicant.
Shri Vijay Soni, learned Panel Advocate for the respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">State with the case diary.
</p><p id="p_2">They are heard.
</p><p id="p_3">This is first application u/s. 439 of the <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>. for grant of bail
to the applicant.
</p><p id="p_4">Applicant has been arrested in connection with Crime
No.471/2015 registered at Police Station- Kareli, District-
Narsinghpur for the alleged offences punishable under <a href="/doc/704277/" id="a_2">Section
8</a>/<a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_3">20-B</a> of N.D.P.S. Act.
</p><p id="p_5">From the possession of the applicant, prosecution alleges that
36 kgs. of Ganja was recovered. Similarly, 13 kgs. of Ganja
was recovered from the possession of the co-accused Raja
Teli.
</p><p id="p_6">After having heard learned counsel for the parties at length
and considering the material available on the record and in
the case diary, in the opinion of this Court, no case for bail is
made out.
</p><p id="p_7">Accordingly, this bail application stands rejected and closed.
</p>


<p id="p_8">                                                             (S.K. SETH)
                                                                JUDGE
