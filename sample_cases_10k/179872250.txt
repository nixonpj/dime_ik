<div class="doc_title">Renu Devi &amp; Anr vs The State Of Bihar on 21 December, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.73703 of 2018
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-62 Year-2018 Thana- KANHAULI District- Sitamarhi
              ======================================================
           1. Renu Devi, W/o-Thaga Rai
           2. Thaga Rai, S/o Late Sugani Rai
              Both Resident of Village- Bharsar, P.S.-Kanhauli, District-Sitamarhi.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :        Mr.Sanjay Kumar, Adv.
                 For the Opposite Party/s :        Mr.Pradeep Narain Kumar, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   21-12-2018           Heard learned Counsel for the petitioners and learned APP

                   for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                         The petitioners are apprehending arrest in a case registered

                   for       the        offences        punishable          under         <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections

                   341</a>/<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>/<a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>/<a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_4">354</a>/<a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_5">379</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_6">307</a>/<a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_7">504</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                         The prosecution case got initiated on the Fardbeyan of Shiv

                   Shankar Rai, recorded by S.I. Manoranjan Kumar, Sonbarsa

                   Police Station on 18.05.2018 at 16.30 Hours at emergency ward

                   of P.H.C., Sonebarsa to the effect that on the same date at 12.00

                   PM, four accused persons, including petitioner no.2, Thaga Rai

                   were constructing house over the land of the informant. On

                   protest being made, petitioner no.2 assaulted the brother of the

                   informant, namely, Gauri Shankar Rai with an iron dabia,
 Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.73703 of 2018(2) dt.21-12-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                             2/3</span>




          whereas co-accused, Chandan Rai assaulted with farsa on the

          head of the informant. It is further alleged that when the wife of

          the informant came to rescue him, then co-accused, Manish

          Kumar assaulted her with an iron rod, whereas, petitioner no.1,

          Renu Devi assaulted to the informant and others with a stick

          (danda). The accused persons also alleged to have taken away

          Rs.3000/- cash from the pocket of the informant and a gold

          locket from his neck.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                 It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioners that in

          the background of land dispute, the accusation has been

          levelled. There is a counter version of the occurrence also, being

          Kanhauli P.S. Case No. 78/2018. The impugned order reflects

          that the injuries have not been found grievous. A statement has

          been made in paragraph no.3 of the petition that the petitioners

          are not having any criminal antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                 It is submitted by learned APP for the State that the

          petitioners are named in the FIR with specific accusation of

          assault.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                       Considering the genesis of the occurrence, being

          levelled in the background of land dispute and there is a counter

          version of the occurrence, coupled with statement made in

          paragraph no.3 of the petition that the petitioners are not having
             Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.73703 of 2018(2) dt.21-12-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                         3/3</span>




                      any criminal antecedent, let the above named petitioners be

                      released on anticipatory bail, in the event of arrest or surrender

                      before the learned Court below within a period of 12 weeks

                      from today, on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (ten

                      thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                      satisfaction of the learned CJM, Sitamarhi, in connection with

                      Kanahauli P.S. Case No.62/2018, subject to the conditions as

                      laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_9">Section 438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C.



</p><p id="p_7">                                              (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Ashwini/-
</p>
