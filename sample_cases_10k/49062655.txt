     THIS WRIT PETITION IS FILED UNDER ARTICLES 226 &amp; 227

JUDGE &amp; JMFC, BHADRAVATHI, SHIMOGA, PRODUCED AT ANN-B
&amp; MODIFY THE ORDER DIRECTING THE PETITIONER TO PAY A
SUM OF RS.500/- &amp; CALL FOR RECORDS.


     THIS WRIT PETITION COMING ON FOR ORDERS, THIS DAY,
THE COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING :
                                 2

                            ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     This    Court    on    taking   note    of   the   nature    of

contentions put forth was of the view that notice to the

respondent    would    be    ordered    only      subject   to   the

petitioner depositing a sum of Rs.5,000/- before this Court

in one week. Despite the said order, the petitioner has not

deposited the amount though the time was once again

granted on 08.08.2013.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">     In that view of the matter, the petition is dismissed.
