                               SARPANCH SHRI
                                    Versus
                           DALSHAJI RAMAJI THAKOR
Appearance:
MR ANKIT Y BACHANI(5424) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1
NOTICE UNSERVED(8) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.M.THAKER

                               Date : 10/04/2018

                                ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">     Heard Mr. Bachani, learned advocate for the petitioner.

     Mr. Bachani, learned advocate for the petitioner

submitted that with a view to taking out appropriate

application under the provisions of Industrial Disputes

(Gujarat) Rules, 1966 and to file appropriate application

before learned Labour Court under said Rules the petitioner

does not press the petition and the petitioner has instructed

him to withdraw the petition so that appropriate application

can be filed before the learned Labour Court.

</p><p id="p_2">     Mr. Bachani, learned advocate for the petitioner

submitted that in view of the instruction by the petitioner he

withdraws the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     If the petitioner files any application under Rule 26A of



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   Page 1 of 2</span>
           C/SCA/15514/2016                               ORDER



Industrial Disputes (Gujarat) Rules, 1966 then learned

Labour Court will consider and decided the same in

accordance with law on the basis of the evidence and

material available on record.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       With the aforesaid clarification the petition stands disposed of

as not pressed / withdrawn. Notices is discharged. Ad-interim /

interim relief if any, stands vacated forthwith.

</p><p id="p_5">                                                      (K.M.THAKER, J)
