  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.N.UNNIKRISHNAN

The Hon'ble MR. Justice K.K.DENESAN

 Dated :13/02/2007

 O R D E R
                        K.K. DENESAN, J.

             Dated this the 13th February, 2007



                         J U D G M E N T
</pre>


<p id="p_1">     The petitioners have filed Ext. P17 representation



before   the   1st  respondent   requesting   to   remove   certain



discrepancies   and  anomalies  in  the  order  passed  by  the



Government   as   per   G.O.(Rt.)   No.   35/2006/SWD   dated   11-
</p>


<p id="p_2">10-2006.   Govt. Pleader submits that Ext. P17 has been



received in the office of the 1st  respondent and that a



communication   has   been   sent   to   the   2nd      respondent



directing it to offer  remarks.
</p>


<p id="p_3">     2.   It   is   necessary   that   urgent   decision   shall   be



taken   on   Ext.   P17.     Hence,   there   shall   be   an   order



directing the 1st respondent to consider Ext. P17,  take



appropriate   decision   and   communicate   the   same   to   the



petitioners   within   six   weeks   from   the   date   of   receipt



of a copy of the judgment.  Counsel for the petitioners



submits   that   after   the   issuance   of   the   Govt.   Order



dated   11-10-2006   some   of   the   offending   clauses   have



undergone   changes   and   therefore   the   petitioners   would



like   to   file   a   supplementary   representation   so   as   to



bring   to   the   notice   of   the   Government   the   situation


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">WPC No.2825 /2007                -2-</span>


that   prevails   in   the   light   of   such   development.     The



petitioners   are   free   to   file   any   such   supplementary



representation.     If   such   representation   is   received,



the same shall be considered along with Ext. P17.
</p>


<p id="p_4">        The   petitioners   shall   produce   a   copy   of   the



judgment along with a copy of the writ petition before



the 1st respondent for compliance.
</p>




<p id="p_5">                                                  K.K. DENESAN

                                                      JUDGE

jan/


</p>
