<div class="doc_title">Mohammed Shakir @ Ghoda vs State on 9 January, 2017</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
            S.B.Criminal Misc. Second Bail No. 138 / 2017
Mohammed Shakir @ Ghoda S/o Shri Yusuf Khan, Raajanagar,
Kishanpol, P.S. Surajpol, Udaipur. (presently Lodged in Central Jail,
Udaipur).
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                                        ----Petitioner
                               Versus
The State of Rajasthan

</p><p id="p_3">                                                     ----Respondent
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)   : Mr. Ram Singh Rawal

For Respondent(s) : Mr. Vikram Singh Rajpurohit - PP

_____________________________________________________
             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI
                         Judgment / Order
09/01/2017

     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as learned

Public Prosecutor and also perused the material on record.

</p><p id="p_4">     The petitioner has been arrested in FIR No.44/2016 of Police

Station, Ghantagarh, District Udaipur for the offence punishable

under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">Section 379</a> IPC. He has preferred this criminal misc.

second bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_2">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_5">     Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that after

rejection of the first bail application, charge-sheet has been filed

against the petitioner. It is submitted that offence for which the

charge-sheet has been filed against the petitioner, is triable by

Magistrate and the trial of the case will take long time.

</p><p id="p_6">     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.
</p><p id="p_7">                                (2 of 2)
                                                       [CRLMB-138/2017]

      Having regard to the totality of the facts and circumstances

of the case, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail to the accused

petitioner under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_3">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_8">      Accordingly, this criminal misc. second bail application filed

under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_4">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is allowed and it is directed that

petitioner - Mohammed Shakir @ Ghoda S/o Shri Yusuf Khan shall

be released on bail in connection with FIR No.44/2016 of Police

Station, Ghantagarh, District Udaipur provided he executes a

personal bond in a sum of Rs.50,000/- with two sound and solvent

sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of learned trial

court for his appearance before that court on each and every date

of hearing and whenever called upon to do so till the completion of

the trial.
</p>
<p id="p_9">


</p><p id="p_10">                                               (VIJAY BISHNOI)J.
</p>
<p id="p_11">Abhishek Kumar
S.No.102
