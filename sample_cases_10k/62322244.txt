                      JODHPUR
                S.B. Civil Misc. Appeal No. 8/2003

The New India Assurance Co.ltd.
</p>


<p id="p_2">                                                                  ----Appellant

                                   Versus

Laxman Lal
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                ----Respondent




For Appellant(s)         :     Mr. RK Mehta with Mr. Shyam
                               Toshniwal
For Respondent(s)        :
</p>


<p id="p_4">     HON'BLE DR. JUSTICE PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI

                                    Order

23/09/2019

</p><p id="p_5">1.         This misc. appeal under <a href="/doc/307321/" id="a_1">Section 30</a> of the Workmen's

<a href="/doc/1113485/" id="a_2">Compensation Act</a> has been preferred by the appellant-Insurance

Company claiming the following relief:-
</p><p id="p_6">         "It is, therefore, prayed that this appeal be allowed
   impugned judgment and award be set aside and the
   application filed under <a href="/doc/380348/" id="a_3">Section 22</a> of Workmen's
   <a href="/doc/1113485/" id="a_4">Compensation Act</a> be ordered to be dismissed with cost.
</p>
<p id="p_7">     The unfortunate accident happened on 1.6.1997                   where the

employee died due to the accident.                 A meagre amount of Rs.
</p>
<p id="p_8">1,68,915/- is awarded as compensation.
</p>
<p id="p_9">     As per the pleadings of the appellant,                     no substantial

question of law arises in the present appeal.
</p>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    (Downloaded on 30/09/2019 at 08:24:19 PM)</span>
<p id="p_10">                                                                               (2 of 2)            [CMA-8/2003]



                                        Consequently, the appeal against the meagre compensation

                                   of Rs. 1,68,915/- is dismissed.
</p>


<p id="p_11">                                                                (DR. PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI),J

                                    ns. 55-1/-
