<p id="p_1">            In compliance with the order dated 25.05.2017 passed by this

Court in CWP No.11725 of 2017, whereby, directions were issued to the

respondents without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case to

consider the legal notice dated 22.03.2017 submitted by the petitioner for

grant of permit for operation of the Light Motor Vehicle, which was ordered

to be considered and decided within a period of two months from the date of

receipt of certified copy of the order. This order dated 25.05.2017 was duly

served upon the respondent. Respondent has filed reply to the contempt

petition by way of affidavit of the State Transport Commissioner, Punjab,

dated 15.10.2018, according to which, on consideration of the legal notice

of the petitioner, the claim has been rejected vide order dated 21.07.2017

(Annexure R-1).
</p>

COCP-2726-2017



             In view of the above, the present contempt petition has been

rendered infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             Learned counsel for the petitioner states that the present

contempt petition has not been rendered infructuous in the light of the

judgment dated 20.12.2012, especially paras 115 to 123 thereof.

</p><p id="p_5">             Be that as it may, the fact remains that the order which has been

passed by this Court was limited to the extent of consideration of the legal

notice as submitted by the petitioner, which on consideration has been duly

take note of and an order has been passed by the respondent. In case the

petitioner is aggrieved of the decision taken by the respondent, she may

avail of her remedy in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             Disposed of as infructuous.
</p>
