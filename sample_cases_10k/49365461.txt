<div class="doc_title">Ram Bilash Rai vs Kamal@Kamal Rai@Kamal Rai &amp; Or on 22 June, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">       Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.13803 of 2012 (3) dt.22-06-2012
                                                   1


                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Criminal Miscellaneous No.13803 of 2012
                    ======================================================
                    Ram Bilash Rai, s/o late Lochan Rai, resident of village Khalak Beg, P.S.
                    Hajipur Sadar, district Vaishali           .... Petitioner
                                                     Versus
                    1. Kamal @ Kalam Rai @ Kamal Rai, s/o Shri Baskit Rai, r/o Chakaima
                       @ Chakaiya @ Chakaua, P.,S. Hajipur, district Vaishali
                    2. The State of Bihar                      .... Opposite Parties
                    ======================================================
                    CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE GOPAL PRASAD
                    ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3 22-06-2012                Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.
</p><p id="p_2">                            This is a petition for cancellation of bail of opposite party no
               1, who was granted bail by order, dated 14.03.2012 in Cr. Misc. No.
               11117 of 2012. The cancellation of bail petition has been filed on the
               ground that the petitioner has suppressed the fact that he has earlier
               moved regular bail, which has been dismissed and has filed the first
               information report which does not mention the name of petitioner
               amongst the person, who fired, hence, is forged and fabricated.
</p><p id="p_3">                            It has been pointed out on behalf of the petitioner that a
               regular bail petition, being Cr. Misc. No. 41238 of 2011, was earlier
               filed on 05.12.2011 by opposite party no. 1 had been rejected by order,
               dated 06.02.2012, by a Bench of Hon'ble Mr. Justice Jyoti Saran.
               Further, the first information report filed in Cr. Misc. No. 11117 of
               2012 also appears to be forged as the name of this petitioner amongst
               the persons who fired is missing. Thought the first information report
               filed in Cr. Misc. No. 41238 of 2011 mentions that this petitioner was
               amongst the persons, who fired at the deceased. However, the first
               information report filed in Cr. Misc. No. 11117 of 2012 the name of
               the petitioner was not amongst the persons who fired, which is
               apparent from the comparison of the first information report filed by
               this petitioner in Cr. Misc. Nos. 11117 of 2012 and Cr. Misc. No.
               41238 of 2011.
</p><p id="p_4">                            Hence, having regard to the facts and circumstances of the
               case, issue notice to opposite party no. 1 why the bail granted to the
               petitioner be not cancelled. The requisites for notice against opposite
               party no. 1 must be filed within one week under ordinary process as
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.13803 of 2012 (3) dt.22-06-2012</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2</span>


               well as under registered cover with A/D and as also through the
               Presiding Officer of the trial Court.
</p><p id="p_5">                            Put up after receipt of the notice.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                    (Gopal Prasad, J)
SA/-
</p>
