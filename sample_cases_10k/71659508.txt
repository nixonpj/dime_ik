<p id="p_1">2   12-12-2017                     Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   The petitioners apprehend their arrest in Complaint

                   Case No.606(c) of 2016 for the offences allegedly committed by

                   the petitioners under <a href="/doc/695990/" id="a_1">Section 364(A)</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_2">120B</a> of the Indian Penal

                   Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   The learned counsel for the petitioners submits that

                   Harendra Narayan Choubey, father of the victim made allegation

                   against the petitioners that his son had gone with his vehicle but he

                   did not return till 06.11.2012. During the course of investigation, it
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.55827 of 2017 (2) dt.12-12-2017</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                             2</span>




             transpired that the informant concealed his son and lodged a false

             case against his brother and sons of his brother, the petitioners.

             The police after investigation submitted final form finding the case

             false and recommending for action against the informant under

             <a href="/doc/534484/" id="a_3">Section 182</a> and <a href="/doc/172919/" id="a_4">211</a> I.P.C. but the informant filed complaint case

             in the year 2016. The complaint was treated as protest petition and

             after inquiry under <a href="/doc/1149595/" id="a_5">Section 202</a> of the Cr.P.C., the learned court

             below found prima facie case under <a href="/doc/695990/" id="a_6">Section 364(A)</a> and other

             Sections of the <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_7">I.P.C</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             Considering the facts that on the accusation made by

             the informant against his brother and sons of his brother, the

             police found the case false but the informant again filed complaint

             which was treated as protest petition. The victim has already

             returned.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Considering the facts aforesaid and the nature of

             allegation made against the petitioners, the petitioners, above

             named, in the event of their arrest or surrender before the learned

             court below within a period of four weeks from the date of

             receipt/production of a copy of this order are directed to be

             enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-(Ten

             thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

             satisfaction of learned Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate 1st ,
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">             Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.55827 of 2017 (2) dt.12-12-2017</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                                         3</span>




                         Danapur (Patna) in connection with                 Protest Complaint Case

                         No.606(c)/2016 arising out of Bihta P.S. Case No.386 of 2012,

                         subject to conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_8">Section 438(2)</a> of the

                         Code of Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                                     (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J)
Saurabh/-
