                                  CRIMINAL APPEAL (DB) No.969 of 2012
                       Arising Out of PS. Case No.-24 Year-2008 Thana- BARHARA District- Bhojpur
                 ======================================================
                 1. Ram Lal Singh, son of Late Kalaktar Singh, Resident of village Shaligram
                 Singh Ke Tola, Niknam Tola, P.S. Barhara in the district of Bhojpur
                 2. Chhote Singh, son of Sri Bachcha Singh, resident of village Niknam Tola
                 P.S. Barhara in the district of Bhojpur

                                                                                  ... ... Appellant/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State Of Bihar

                                                           ... ... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :

                 For the Appellant/s     :        Mr.Prabhu Narayan Sharma

                 For the Respondent/s    :        Ms. S.B.Verma , A.P.P.

                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAKESH KUMAR
                         and
                         HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">                 (Per: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAKESH KUMAR)

8   18-06-2019                   The appeal has been listed under the heading "For

                   Order (On Office Note)". By order dated 10.12.2015 on the

                   interlocutory application i.e. I.A. No. 1982 of 2015 filed on

                   behalf of appellant no. 2 - Chhote Singh under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 389(1)</a>

                   of the Code Of Criminal Procedure, 1973 for provisionally

                   releasing appellant no. 2 on bail to attend marriage of his

                   daughter, the appellant no. 2 was granted provisional bail till

                   18.12.2015 with direction to surrender on or before 18.12.2015.

                   Office note suggests that report from the court below indicates

                   that appellant no. 2 - Chhote Singh has not surrendered. In
 Patna High Court CR. APP (DB) No.969 of 2012(8) dt.18-06-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            2/3</span>




          interlocutory application i.e. I.A. No. 1982 of 2015 in

          paragraph- 4 a specific stand was taken that marriage of his

          daughter was to be solemnized on 14.12.2015 with one Kamlesh

          Singh. Along with the petition a photo copy of marriage card

          was also enclosed. Since the appellant no. 2 has not surrendered

          within time specified by this court, it would be desirable to

          direct the concerned Superintendent Of Police, Bhojpur at Ara

          to enquire as to whether as per statement made in the

          interlocutory application marriage of daughter of appellant no. 2

          was fixed or not or solemnized or not. In the meanwhile, issue

          non bailable warrant of arrest against appellant no. 2 namely

          Chhote Singh, S/o Sri Bachcha Singh, resident of village -

          Niknam Tola, P.S.- Barhara, District - Bhojpur who was

          convicted and sentenced in Sessions Trial No. 295 of 2009

          {arising out of Barhara P.S. Case No. 24 of 2008} for the

          offence under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Section 302</a> of the Indian Penal Code, 1860 as

          well as <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_3">Section 27</a> of the Arms Act, 1959. The court below i.e.

          learned Additional Sessions Judge - III, Ara, Bhojpur is

          required to ensure attendance of appellant no. 2.

</p><p id="p_2">                       The Superintendent of Police, Bhojpur at Ara after

          conducting enquiry regarding the fact of marriage of so- called

          daughter of appellant no. 2 is required to submit its report
                Patna High Court CR. APP (DB) No.969 of 2012(8) dt.18-06-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                           3/3</span>




                         within a period of eight weeks from today.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                      List this appeal under the same heading i.e. "For

                         Order (On Office Note)" on 3rd September, 2019.



</p><p id="p_4">                                                    (Rakesh Kumar, J)


                                                  ( Anil Kumar Sinha, J)
praful/-
</p>
