Date

ORAL
ORDER</pre> 

 

 


 

<p id="p_1">1.	Heard
Mr. Ashish Dagli, learned advocate for the petitioner.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.	In
this petition under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> and <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_2">227</a> of the Constitution of India,
the petitioner has complained of inaction on part of the higher
authority, namely, respondent No.2 herein in not taking any action
pursuant to a representation dated 15.12.2009, by which, a grievance
was made against Member of the Panchayat of ward No.5 of Village
Mandasar, Taluka: Chotila of District: Surendranagar with regard to
withdrawal of certain amount illegally. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_3">3.	From
the perusal of the above representation, it transpires that the above
representation was duly submitted on 15.12.2009 to District
Superintendent of Police, Surendranagar, but till today the
petitioner is not given any reply nor any action is taken thereon.  
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_4">4.	In
view of the above, the District Superintendent of Police,
Surendranagar, is directed to consider the representation and take
decision in accordance with law and appraise the petitioner about
outcome of the representation.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_5">5.	With
the aforesaid observations and directions, this petition is disposed
of at this stage.
</p> 

<p id="p_6">	Direct
service is permitted.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_7">[ANANT
S. DAVE, J.]
