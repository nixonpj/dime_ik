<p id="p_1">Harsimran Singh Sethi, J.(Oral)

             In the present writ petition, the prayer is for the release of

retiral benefits and also the arrears of 3rd ACP scale.

</p><p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioner states that the petitioner

retired as a Sweeper from Municipal Council, Bhiwani on 30.06.2018 and

after retirement, nothing has been paid to her so far without any valid

justification. Learned counsel further states that there is no proceeding

which is pending against the petitioner either departmental or criminal

which would entitle the respondents to withhold the pensionary benefits.

</p><p id="p_3">             Learned counsel for the petitioner states that for the relief,

which has been claimed in the present writ petition, the petitioner has served

the respondents with a legal notice dated 21.08.2018 (Annexure P-3) and

the petitioner will be satisfied, at this stage, in case a time bound direction is

given to the respondents to decide the legal notice by passing a speaking

order.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                                       1 of 2
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                   ::: Downloaded on - 10-02-2019 00:52:25 :::</span>
 CWP No. 1762 of 2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                           2</span>

             Without going into the merits of the case or expressing any

opinion to the entitlement of the petitioner in respect of the claim prayed in

the present writ petition or in the legal notice, the present writ petition is

disposed of with a direction to the respondents to pass an appropriate

speaking order on the claim raised by the petitioner in the legal notice dated

21.08.2018 (Annexure P-3) within a period of three months from the date of

receipt of certified copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             In case after the order, it is found by the respondents that the

petitioner is entitled for any monetary benefits, the same should also be

released to her within a period of next three months.

</p><p id="p_7">             The writ petition stands disposed of.
</p>
