<div class="doc_title">Satya Pal Anand [Sr. Citizen] vs Bal Niketan Nyar, Adj &amp; Other ... on 10 March, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                               Writ Petition No.2842/2006

10.03.2014
                   Petitioner in person.
                   Smt.Shobha Menon, Senior Advocate with Shri Rahul
             Choubey, Advocate for the respondents.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                   When counsel for the respondents raised preliminary
             objection relying on the decision of the Apex Court in AIR 1985 SC
             257 and AIR 2003 SC 1883, she was called upon to answer the issue
             noticed in the order dated 22.2.2006. She was specifically asked
             whether any decision of the Supreme Court or for that matter this
             High Court has had occasion to interpret section 3(2) of the M.P.
             Accommodation Control Act, 1961. She prays for time to examine
             this aspect.   The request for adjournment is opposed by the
             petitioner, who appears in person.          Nevertheless, by way of
             indulgence, we are inclined to give some time to learned counsel for
             the respondents to prepare on this question of law.
</p><p id="p_2">                   Petitioner, who appears in person, insisted for orders on
             I.A.No.10284/2012,        I.A.No.13881/2012,       I.A.No.12193/2012,
             I.A.No.14871/2012 and I.A.No.669/2013.         However, since we are
             deferring the hearing of the main matter and only if the same is to be
             admitted, the question of considering interim applications would
             arise for which reason we are not taking up the hearing of those
             applications today.    We place on record the submission of the
             respondents that the apprehension of the petitioner of being
             dispossessed from the suit premises is misplaced in view of the
             interim order passed in F.A.No.1037/2012 dated 17.12.2012.
</p><p id="p_3">                   As aforesaid, the main matter will proceed on 1.4.2014 and the
             companion applications will be heard thereafter.
</p><p id="p_4">                   List the matter on top of the list.
</p><p id="p_5">                   Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">               (A.M.Khanwilkar)                           (K.K.Trivedi)
                 Chief Justice                               Judge
      Writ Petition No.2842/2006

HS
 </pre>
