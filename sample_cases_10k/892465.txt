  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

1. MR.N.R.SATISH, 46 YEARS,
                      ...  Petitioner
2. MR.H.MUHAMMAD ABDUL SATHAR SAIT,

                        Vs



1. STATE OF KERALA, REPRESENTED BY
                       ...       Respondent

The Hon'ble MR. Justice ANTONY DOMINIC

 Dated :24/03/2009

 O R D E R
                      ANTONY DOMINIC, J.
                    ================
                 W.P.(C) NO. 9043 OF 2009 (A)
                =====================

           Dated this the 24th day of March, 2009

                         J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     Ext.P3 is a Government order which provides that flats

having value less than Rs.5 lakhs have been exempted from

stamp duty subject to other conditions specified therein.    In this

writ petition, what the petitioners submit is that they are entitled

to the benefit of Ext.P3 Government order and on that basis, they

presented Exts.P5(a) and P5(b) sale deeds before the 2nd

respondent for registration and that the 2nd respondent has

declined to register the documents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     2.    The issue similar to this have been considered by this

court and Ext.P4 is the judgment rendered by this Court in WP(C)

No.3230/09, where the Registering Authority was directed to

examine the eligibility of the petitioners for the benefit of the

Government order, and if found to be eligible, to register the

document.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3.    The facts of this case being identical consistent with

the above judgment, I dispose of this writ petition also directing

that the 2nd respondent shall examine the eligibility of the

WPC 9043/09
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 :2 :</span>



petitioners for the benefit of Ext.P3 Government order and if they

are found to be eligible for the same, shall register the original of

Exts.P5(a) and P5(b) on its presentation.

</p><p id="p_4">      Petitioners may produce a copy of this judgment before the

2nd respondent for compliance.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      Writ petition is disposed of as above.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                       ANTONY DOMINIC, JUDGE
