                                 2

                           JUDGMENT
</pre><p id="p_1">     This writ petition is filed seeking directions to

the respondents to take appropriate action on Ext.P2

complaint preferred by the petitioner.


</p><p id="p_2">     2. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner

and the learned Government Pleader.


</p><p id="p_3">     3. It is submitted by the learned counsel for the

petitioner that the vehicles are being unauthorizedly

parked and washed near the temple premises and anti

social activities are also going on in the premises

of the temple. It is stated that the said activities

are in contravention of Ext.P1 judgment issued by the

Division Bench of this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">     4.   Instructions have been made available by the

Sub Inspector of Police, Cheranalloor Police Station,

stating that pursuant to Ext.P2 compliant preferred

by the petitioner, patrolling has been effected in

the temple premises and it was found that on the

dates,    when      patrolling   was   conducted,   no   illegal

parking is noticed in the premises and no anti social
 WP(C).No. 1780/19

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 3</span>

activities were also found. It is stated that in case

specific instances of violation of Ext.P1 are pointed

out by the petitioner, necessary steps shall be taken

by the respondents in accordance with the law.


</p><p id="p_6">      The   said    submission       is   recorded,     the   writ

petition is closed.
</p>
