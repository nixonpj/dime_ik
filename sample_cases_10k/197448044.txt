                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.41855 of 2019
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-39 Year-2019 Thana- SITAMARHI District- Sitamarhi
                 ======================================================
           1.     DINESH PRASAD SINGH Son of Late Shiv Shankar Prasad Singh
                  Resident of Village- Bathnaha Tole Chakwa, P.S.- Bathnaha, District-
                  Sitamarhi (Name of Village wrongly mentioned in the F.I.R. as Panthpakar
           2.    Kanhai Singh @ Deepak Kumar Son of Dinesh Prasad Singh Resident of
                 Village- Bathnaha Tole Chakwa, P.S.- Bathnaha, District- Sitamarhi (Name
                 of Village wrongly mentioned in the F.I.R. as 'Panthpakar').

                                                                                   ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Prabhat Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Md. Matloob Rab
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">4   27-09-2019                    Heard learned counsel for the parties.

</p><p id="p_2">                           Petitioners are apprehending their arrest in connection

                   with Sitamarhi P.S.Case No.39 of 2019 , registered for offences

                   punishable under <a href="/doc/1293807/" id="a_1">Sections 461</a>, <a href="/doc/1904534/" id="a_2">462</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_3">379</a> of the Indian Penal

                   Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                           As per the FIR, the informant had gone out of in

                   connection with the treatment of his mother and handed over

                   the shop to one Deepak Chaurasiya and when he returned, he

                   found the lock of the shop broken and the computer, Invertor

                   batra, laser printer and other articles valued of Rs.02 lac were

                   missing and further stated that the printer which was kept in
             Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.41855 of 2019(4) dt.27-09-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                      the box valued of Rs.2,40,000/- was also missing. He has named

                      the petitioner no.2 as accused.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                              Submission of the learned counsel for the petitioners is

                      that    they have been made accused only on the basis of the

                      suspicion and there is no recovery from their house or from

                      their possession. They are ready to abide by any condition

                      imposed upon them.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                              Heard learned A.P.P. also.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                              Having heard both sides and in view of the facts and

                      circumstances, as stated above, this application is allowed and

                      the provisional bail granted to the petitioners dated 09.07.2019

                      is confirmed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                   With the above direction, this application is allowed.




</p><p id="p_8">                                              (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
chn/-
</p>
