<div class="doc_title">M/S. Anmol Biscuits Limited vs M/S. Suruchi Biscuits Pvt.Ltd on 14 December, 2015</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Soumen Sen</div>

<pre id="pre_1">ORDER SHEET

                            GA No.3875 of 2015
                            With
                            CS No.311 of 2015
                        IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                     Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                              ORIGINAL SIDE



                                                 M/S. ANMOL BISCUITS LIMITED
                                                             Versus
                                                 M/S. SURUCHI BISCUITS PVT.LTD.


   BEFORE:
   The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN
   Date : 14th December, 2015.


                                                         Mr.Suvasish Sengupta, Ms.Sanchalita
                                                         Sarkar, Mr. Udit Agarwal, Advocates
                                                         for the petitioner/plaintiff.
                                                         Mr. Sayantan Bose, Mr.Bimalendu Das,
                                                         Advocates for the defendant.



              The Court : The defendant has filed an affidavit of undertaking to

the effect that being aware of the order dated 5th November, 2015 passed by this

Court, the defendant/respondent immediately informed and/or instructed all its</pre>

<p id="p_1">distributors,      super    stockiest      and   sales      personnel    not   to    sell    any   biscuit

manufactured by the defendant company bearing the trade mark "Butter Bite" in

compliance    of    the    said    order     dated    5th    November,    2015       and    some   of   the

distributors were also informed through e-mail and by personal service. It is

also    undertaken        that    pursuant       to   receipt       of   such       communication       the

defendant/respondent company has also stopped manufacturing the product "Butter

Bite" with immediate effect and has caused its Advocate-on-Record to publish a

public notice in newspaper, namely, "Bartaman" on December 4, 2015 thereby

notifying such manufacture having been discontinued by the defendant/respondent

after   receipt    of     the    said   notice    from      the   plaintiff's       Advocate-on-Record.

Further undertaking is given that the defendant shall not undertake anything
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            2</span>


that comes in conflict with the business of any other organization in the same

trade.
</p>
<p id="p_2">           Affidavit of undertaking filed in Court is taken on record.

           In   this   application   the   petitioner   has   prayed   for   appointment

Special Officer to take possession and custody of the infringed materials from

the custody of the defendant. It is alleged that in spite of giving undertaking,

the defendant is continuing with the distribution of its product under the trade

mark "Butter Bite" in the market. It is further alleged that a large stock of

infringed goods are lying at the office of the respondent at Delhi Road,

Chakundi, Dankuni, Hooghly, West Bengal, Pin - 712310.

</p><p id="p_3">           In order to avoid future complications in the matter, I appoint Mr.

Sayan Roy Chowdhury, Advocate, a member of Bar Library Club as Special Officer

to inspect the stock of goods at the office of the respondent at Delhi Road,

Chakundi, Dankuni, Hooghly, West Bengal, Pin - 712310 and shall file a report on

the returnable date. The Special Officer shall be paid an initial remuneration

of 1000 GMs by the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">           I make this application returnable on 21st December, 2015.

           It is made clear that the Special Officer shall not interfere with

the running of the business by the defendant and this order shall not be

publicized by the plaintiff to anyone in the trade.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                                   (SOUMEN SEN, J.)
