<div class="doc_title">Birendra Kumar Bhatia vs The State Of Jharkhand on 24 February, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">             IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                        Cr. M.P. No. 226 of 2014
              Birendra Kumar Bhatia                   ...      ...      Petitioner
                                  Versus
              The State of Jharkhand                    ...    ...    Opp. Party
                                  -----
              CORAM:        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE R.R. PRASAD
                                  -----
              For the Petitioner        : Mr. Shailesh, Advocate
              For the State             : APP.
                                  -----
        02/24.02.2014</pre><p id="p_1">.      Learned counsel appearing for the petitioner submits that one
              Dharamraj Yadav lodged a case as Dhanbad P.S. Case No.659 of 2011,
              alleging therein that this petitioner and other accused persons, in conspiracy
              with each other, got the land of the informant, transferred in the name of Pratap
              Parui on the basis of forged documents.
</p><p id="p_2">                            The matter was taken up for investigation. Subsequently, said
              Pratap Parui to whom the land was sold by this petitioner, has also lodged the
              case as Dhanbad (Bank More) P.S. Case No.985 of 2011 wherein the same
              allegation has been reiterated that the petitioner, in connivance with other
              accused persons including government officials, got the land, belonging to the
              informant of the first case, transferred fraudulently in his favour and thereby,
              the second first information report cannot be maintained in view of the decision
              rendered in a case of <a href="/doc/1974324/" id="a_1">T.T. Antony vs. State of Kerala &amp; Ors</a>. reported in
              [(2001) 6 Supreme Court Cases 181] and also in a case of <a href="/doc/1164619/" id="a_2">Babubhai vs. State
              of Gujarat and others</a> [(2010) 12 SCC 254].
</p><p id="p_3">                            In view of the submission, let notice be issued to the informant,
              who be impleaded as Opp. Party No.2, on filing requisites etc. under registered
              cover with A/D by Friday i.e. on 28.02.2014.
</p><p id="p_4">                            Let this matter be listed in anticipation of the appearance of the
              Opp. Party No.2.
</p><p id="p_5">                            Till then, no coercive action shall be taken against the petitioner
              above named, in connection with Dhanbad (Bank More) P.S. Case No.985 of
              2011 (G.R. No.4015 of 2011), pending in the court of learned Chief Judicial
              Magistrate, Dhanbad.
</p><p id="p_6">                            Let a copy of this order be communicated through Fax at the cost
              of the petitioner.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                                    (R.R. Prasad, J.)

Ravi/
 </p>
