BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               
DATED: 03.05.2018  
CORAM   
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.M.BASHEER AHAMED               
W.P(MD)No.10498 of 2018   
and 
W.M.P(MD)No.9602 of 2018   
A.Roshini                                                       ..Petitioner            
                                                
                Vs.     

1.The Principal Secretary to Government,
   Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department,  
   St. Fort George,
   Chennai.

2.The Director,
   Directorate of Rural Development
   and Panchayat Raj Department, 
   Panagal Maligai,
   Saidapet,  Chennai.

3.The District Collector,
   Madurai,
   Madurai District.                                            ..Respondents 

PRAYER: Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of  
India, Section 397 r/w 401 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, praying for a
writ of Mandamus, to direct the second respondent to pass orders on the
appeal petition, dated 28.04.2018 preferred by the petitioner challenging the
proceedings of the third respondent in Na.Ka.No.2206/2018/Ou.Va-2, dated
23.04.2018.
!For Petitioner         : Mr.R.J.Karthick
^For Respondents        : Mr.K.Chellapandian,
                                     Additional Advocate General
                                     Assisted by Mr.V.Anand,
                                     Government Advocate

:ORDER  
</pre><p id="p_1">        This writ petition has been filed praying for issuance of writ of
mandamus directing the second respondent to pass orders on the appeal 
petition, dated 28.04.2018 preferred by the petitioner challenging the
proceedings of the third respondent, in Na.Ka.No.2206/2018/Ou.Va-2, dated
23.04.2018.
</p><p id="p_2">        2.It is admitted that there is a dispute in respect of fixing the
seniority between the directly recruited persons and the promotees. Earlier
writ petition filed by the petitioner before this Court was questioning the
inter-seniority list and the same is pending.  Further, it is admitted that
the petitioner has filed an appeal under Rule 37 of the Tamil Nadu
Subordinate Service Rules, before the second respondent for considering his
objections in respect of fixation of seniority.
</p><p id="p_3">        3.The learned Additional Advocate General appearing for the respondents
state that the second respondent may be given  time to dispose of the appeal
preferred by the petitioner in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4.It is left open to the petitioner to move the said appeal before the
second respondent while the second respondent heard the objections of the
petitioner herein.
</p><p id="p_5">        5. Considering the above facts, this Court is inclined to dispose of
the writ petition and accordingly, dispose of the same directing the second
respondent by granting three months time from the date of receipt of a copy
of this order to dispose of the objections raised by the petitioner before
the second respondent.   No costs.  Consequently, connected miscellaneous 
petition is also closed.
</p>


<p id="p_6">To
</p><p id="p_7">1.The Principal Secretary to Government,
   Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department,  
   St. Fort George, Chennai.
</p><p id="p_8">2.The Director,
   Directorate of Rural Development
   and Panchayat Raj Department, 
   Panagal Maligai,Saidapet,  Chennai.
</p>
<p id="p_9">3.The District Collector,
