                        IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA

                                  ORIGINAL SIDE



                         SREI EQUIPMENT FINANCE LTD.
                                    Versus
                                 HAMSHEERA


  BEFORE:
  The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>
<p id="p_1">  Date : 23rd December, 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                          Appearance:
</p><p id="p_4">                                                                Mr. K.K. Panday, Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
      The Court: This is an execution application for enforcement of an

award/decree dated 7th February, 2014. The application is less than two years

old and hence no notice is required to be served.           The award is not under

challenge. The sum sought to be realised is Rs.52,57,588/- together with further

interest.
</p>
<p id="p_6">      There shall be order in terms of prayer (c) of column 10 of the tabular

statement. Mr. Koushik Chowdhury Advocate Bar Library Club is appointed as

Receiver in terms of prayer (a) of column 10 of the tabular statement at an initial

remuneration of 800 GMs. to be paid by the petitioner. The Receiver may engage

an agent. The Receiver shall file report by the returnable date.

</p><p id="p_7">      The jurisdictional Superintendent of Police as well as the officer in charge

of the local police station is to render all assistance to the receiver or his agent.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                          2</span>


</p><p id="p_8">      The matter is made returnable on January 20, 2015.

      The jurisdictional Superintendent of Police, the officer in charge of the local

police station and all concerned shall act on a website certified copy of this order

which shall be supplied to the parties on an urgent basis subject to their

compliance with all requisite formalities.




</p><p id="p_9">                                                         (SOUMEN SEN, J.)
