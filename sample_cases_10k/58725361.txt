<div class="doc_title">Triveni Paswan &amp; Anr vs State Of Bihar on 14 May, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              Cr.Misc. No.15979 of 2008
                                 TRIVENI PASWAN &amp; ANR
                                        Versus
                                    STATE OF BIHAR
                                     -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">02/   14-05-2008              Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned A.P.P.

</p><p id="p_2">                   for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              The petitioners are being prosecuted in a case under Section

                   498(A), 304(B) and other<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> Sections of the Indian Penal Code</a>.

</p><p id="p_4">                              As per the allegations the petitioners are father-in-law and

                   mother-in-law of the victim lady. It is said that the dead body was

                   found in a pond.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                              Learned counsel submitted that as per the postmortem report

                   the death took place due to shock and asphyxia caused by drowning.

                   He further submitted that the petitioners are father-in-law and mother-

                   in-law of the victim lady and they are in custody since 17.1.2008. He

                   submitted that all the allegations are against the husband.

</p><p id="p_6">                                    In the facts and circumstances of the case, the above

                   named petitioners, namely, Triveni Paswan and Jasmatia Devi are

                   directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-

                   (rupees ten thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each

                   to the satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Gaya in connection

                   with Tekari P.S. Case No. 108 of 2007.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><pre id="pre_2">      S.Sb/-                                                  (Madhavendra Saran, J.)
 </pre>
