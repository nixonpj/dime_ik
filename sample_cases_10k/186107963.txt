<p id="p_5">
    The Court : Mr. Basu, learned advocate appears on behalf

of owners of the vessel. He submits, his client has been

negotiating a settlement for a while and some further time be
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           2</span>


granted to achieve result. He seeks adjournment of two weeks.

Mr. Bose, learned senior advocate appears on behalf of added

defendant, opposes prayer for adjournment and presses for

orders      on    his     client's    application,      GA   3171   of   2018,   in

which, inter alia, prayer has been made for appointment of

Receiver in respect of the vessel with direction to sell it

by       public        auction   or   private    treaty      and    consequential

prayers. Mr. Banerjee, learned advocate appears on behalf of

plaintiff and submits, direction for addition of party was

made      on     5th    March,   2019.   He     prays   for    adjournment       for

amendment being carried out and thereafter service of amended

copies of plaint upon appearing defendants.

</p><p id="p_6">         List on 7th May, 2019. Amendment is to be carried out in

the meantime and service effected.
