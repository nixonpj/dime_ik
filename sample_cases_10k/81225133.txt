</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 13-11-2019

      Heard Mr. J. Chopra, learned counsel for the applicant. None appears on call for the
Opposite Party.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      At the outset, the learned counsel for the applicant submits that notwithstanding the
judgement and order dated 30.07.2018 passed by this Court in the connected Tr.P.(C) No.
6/2018, he has no objection if any order is passed in this application by this Bench.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                                  Page No. 2/2

      The learned counsel for the applicant has submitted that the parties have settled their
matter amicably out of the Court vide Deed of Agreement dated 22.10.2019. Accordingly, the
applicant seeks to withdraw the connected Tr.P.(C). No. 6 of 2018. The said prayer, being not
objected to, is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     This application stands disposed of.
