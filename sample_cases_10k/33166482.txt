<div class="doc_title">Murti Manjhi vs The State Of Bihar on 25 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.50788 of 2016
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -8 Year- 2016 Thana -SIWAN MUFFASIL District- SIWAN
                 ======================================================
                 Murti Manjhi Son of Manjur Manjhi Resident of Village- Mahadeva Dusad
                 tola, P. S. Muffassil (Mahadeva), District Siwan.

                                                                                 .... ....   Petitioner
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                               .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Ranjeet Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. Bisheshwar Ram
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SUDHIR SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   25-11-2016                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel appearing on behalf of the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in connection

                 with Siwan Muffasil (Mahadeva) P.S. Case No. 08 of 2016 for the

                 offence instituted under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1947545/" id="a_3">448</a> and <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the

                 Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              The allegation against the petitioner is that he along

                 with other co-accused assaulted the informant and when his wife

                 and son came for rescue, they were also assaulted by the accused

                 persons, causing injury to them.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                              It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioner that

                 there is no allegation of tampering of witnesses alleged against

                 the petitioner. It is a case and counter case between the parties
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.50788 of 2016 (2) dt.25-11-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                      vide Annexure-4 to the present application. The other co-accused

                      have been granted anticipatory bail vide Cr. Misc. No. 33430 of

                      2016 and Cr. Misc. No. 37528 of 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                    On behalf of the State, it has been submitted that the

                      petitioner is named in the F.I.R.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                    Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, let

                      the petitioner, above named be released on anticipatory bail in the

                      event of his arrest or surrender before the learned court below

                      within a period of six weeks from today, in connection with

                      Siwan Muffasil (Mahadeva) P.S. Case No. 08 of 2016 on

                      furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-(Ten Thousand) with two

                      sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                      C.J.M., Siwan, subject to the conditions as laid down under

                      <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_6">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.



</p><p id="p_7">                                                                        (Sudhir Singh, J.)
Amit/-
</p>
