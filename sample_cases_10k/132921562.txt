<div class="doc_title">Pramod Sah vs The State Of Bihar on 19 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.39734 of 2017
                              Arising Out of PS.Case No. -39 Year- 2017 Thana -DARPA District-
                                              EASTCHAMPARAN(MOTIHARI)
                  ======================================================
                  Pramod Sah son of Prabhu Sah, resident of village - Sukhalahiya, P.S.
                  Darpa, Distt. - Motihari, East Champaran.
                                                                        .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                  The State of Bihar
                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                  ======================================================
                  Appearance :
                  For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr. Rajesh Kumar
                  For the Opposite Party/s    : Mr. Brajendra Nath Pandey
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAKESH KUMAR
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2.   19-08-2017                 Heard Sri Rajesh Kumar, learned counsel for the

                  petitioner and learned Addl. Public Prosecutor.

</p><p id="p_2">                                The sole petitioner, who is in custody since

                  18-05-2017 in Darpa P.S. Case No. 39 of 2017 registered for

                  offence under <a href="/doc/471092/" id="a_1">Sections 272</a>, <a href="/doc/285163/" id="a_2">273</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and

                  Section 30(A) of Bihar Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_4">Excise Act</a>, 2016, has

                  prayed for grant of bail.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                By way of referring to statement made in paragraph -

                  3 of the petition, it was submitted by learned counsel for the

                  petitioner that petitioner is having clean antecedent and he has

                  falsely been implicated on an accusation as if petitioner alongwith

                  one another while moving on a motorcycle and seeing police party

                  threw a bag containing about 216 liter of Nepali liquor, kept in

                  pouches.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.39734 of 2017 (2) dt.19-08-2017</span><p id="p_4">

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_5">                                    Considering the nature of accusation, clean antecedent

                    as well as period of custody, let the petitioner namely Pramod Sah

                    be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail-bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten

                    thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                    satisfaction of learned 7th Additional Sessions Judge cum Special

                    Judge, Excise, East Champaran, Motihari in connection with

                    Darpa P.S. Case No. 39 of 2017.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                                      (Rakesh Kumar, J.)
Anay
