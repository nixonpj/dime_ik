<p id="p_1">            I heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

Government Pleader.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2.    Petitioner seeks instalment facility.           Writ petition is

disposed of as follows.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      Proceedings against the petitioner will be kept in abeyance if

the petitioner pays the entire amount of tax due with additional tax

in six equal monthly instalments, first of which shall be payable on

or before 25.4.2009 and the further instalments shall be paid on or

before the 25th working day of the succeeding months.                 If the

petitioner makes default in payment of any of the instalments, the

petitioner will lose the benefit of this judgment and the respondent

will be free to proceed against the petitioner in accordance with

law. It is made clear that the petitioner cannot operate the vehicle

without payment of tax with additional tax.

</p><p id="p_4">                                                                        Sd/-
</p><p id="p_5">                                                  (K.M.JOSEPH, JUDGE)
