<p id="p_2">	ADMIT.

<p id="p_3">	The following substantial
question of law arises for determination:
</p> 


<p id="p_4"> Whether,
in the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Tribunal was
justified in directing the adjudicating authority to denovo consider
the levy of Central Excise duty and interest under Rule 96ZQ of the
Central Excise Rules, 1944 read with <a href="/doc/85233116/" id="a_1">Section 11A</a> of the Central
Excise Act, 1944 despite non challenge to the order fixing Annual
Production Capacity (APC) which included length of gallery while
computing APC? 
</p> 


<p id="p_5">	To be heard along with Tax
Appeals No.1555 of 2007, 1742 of 2007 and 830 of 2009.
