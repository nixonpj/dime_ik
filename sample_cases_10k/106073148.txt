=============================================================

         CORAM: HONOURABLE            Mr. JUSTICE B.N. KARIA
                     25th October 2018

IA ORDER

      This Civil Application has been moved for condonation of

delay of 405 days occurred in preferring the Second Appeal which

has been preferred against the judgment and decree dated 22nd

February 2017 passed by the learned Addl. District &amp; Sessions

Judge, Arvali at Modasa in Regular Civil Appeal No. 20 of 2014.

</p><p id="p_3">      Having heard learned advocates for the respective parties

and upon perusal of the application, it appears that sufficient

cause has been shown for condonation of delay, which deserves

consideration in light of the averments made and those pleaded

before this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               Page 1 of 2</span>
</p><p id="p_5">        C/SA/20945/2018                             IA ORDER



     Resultantly, this Civil Application is allowed. Delay of 405

days occurred in preferring Second Appeal is hereby condoned.

Rule made absolute to the aforestated extent.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                   [B.N Karia, J.]

Prakash
