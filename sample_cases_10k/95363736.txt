<p id="p_1">                               This order shall dispose of the above titled writ petitions

            together as similar set of facts and question of law are involved in these

            petitions.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               The cases had been directed to be heard alongwith CWP No.2

            of 1992. The said writ petition has been decided by the Division Bench of

            this Court on 28.1.1998 holding that Rule 9 (3) of the Punjab Recruitment

            of Ex-Servicemen Rules, 1982 are valid and could not be               held to be

            discriminatory.         The challenge contained in the writ petitions is with

            reference of the said rule and seeks for quashing of the letter Annexure P/2

            dated 25.5.1992. The petitioners cannot obtain any benefit in terms of the

            decision in CWP No. 2 of 1992 and consequently these writ petitions are

            dismissed.
</p>
