<p id="p_1">                 (Per: HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE)

2   27-03-2018           This writ petition has been filed for release of a vehicle

                  (Motorcycle) bearing Registration No. BR-24Q-2235, which has

                  been seized in connection with Dehri Nagar (Indrapuri) P.S.

                  Case No. 962/2017 due to violation of the <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>. The

                  prayer made in the writ petition is to release the vehicle in

                  question pending finalization of the confiscation proceedings

                  and criminal case.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                        It is common ground that in various cases, identical in

                  nature, pending finalization of the confiscation proceedings

                  vehicles have been directed to be released by this Court on

                  various conditions and we see no reason to make a deviation in
            Patna High Court CWJC No.5374 of 2018(2) dt.27-03-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                    the present case.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                          Keeping in view the aforesaid, it is directed that pending

                    finalization of the confiscation proceedings and aforesaid

                    criminal case, the aforesaid vehicle in question shall be released

                    to the petitioner, on his furnishing two surety bonds to the

                    satisfaction of District Magistrate, Rohtas at Sasaram and

                    further undertaking to produce the vehicle as and when directed

                    by the authority concerned and not to alienate or deal with the

                    vehicle in question or create a third party interest during the

                    pendency of the confiscation proceedings and criminal case or

                    prejudice the right of the State in confiscation proceeding. The

                    aforesaid vehicle shall be released within one week from the

                    date of furnishing of the sureties.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                          With the aforesaid, the writ petition stands allowed and

                    disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                             (Rajendra Menon, CJ)


                                           (Rajeev Ranjan Prasad, J)
P.K.P./-
</p>
