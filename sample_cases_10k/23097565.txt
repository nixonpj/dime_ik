<p id="p_1">JASWANT SINGH, J.(ORAL)


            The petitioner is a Junior Engineer (Civil) with the Municipal

Corporation, Amritsar. His grievance is that in May 2013 (Annexure P-2),

his case for grant of revised higher pay scale w.e.f. 01.12.2011, as also for

re-designation as Assistant Engineer and having completed ten years regular

service of Junior Engineer was sent to the Government and no sanction to

date has been received.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Upon notice, learned State Counsel submits that the requisite

relief has been granted to the petitioner as vide order dated 29.07.2016 the

petitioner has been designated as Assistant Corporation Engineer w.e.f.

01.12.2011. It is also ordered that his salary also be fixed accordingly and

dues paid to him immediately. A copy of the order dated 29.07.2016 has

been produced in Court and the same is taken on record as Annexure

Mark-A.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            Faced with the aforesaid circumstances, learned Counsel for the

petitioner submits that the present petition has become infructuous.

</p><p id="p_4">            Dismissed as infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_5">            It is hoped that the Municipal Corporation, Amritsar would be

kind enough to release the dues to the petitioner as ordered.</p><pre id="pre_2">
