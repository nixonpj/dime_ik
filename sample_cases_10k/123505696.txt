<p id="p_1">CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE KULDIP SINGH
Present:-    Mr. Jitender Dhanda, Advocate for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">             Mr. Ramesh Kumar Ambavta, AAG, Haryana.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Mr. R.S.Mamli, Advocate for complainant.
</p><p id="p_4">1.           Whether the Reporters of local newspaper may be allowed to
             see the judgment ?
</p><p id="p_5">2.           To be referred to the Reporter or not.
</p><p id="p_6">3.           Whether the judgment should be reported in the digest ?



KULDIP SINGH J. (ORAL)

             Learned counsel for complainant has filed the vakalatnama in

Court today. The same is taken on record.
</p>
<p id="p_7">             Learned State counsel on instructions from SI Nihal Singh,

contends that petitioner has joined the investigation and is no longer

required for further investigation and that State has no objection to the grant

of anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_8">             However, petition has been seriously and strongly opposed by

learned counsel for complainant. He has referred to the allegations in the

FIR and the suicide note (Annexure P-1) and has sated that Kapil Sharma is

one of the person who is running Lala Chat Bhandar and is the main abettor.

</p><p id="p_9">             I have gone through the suicide note where the petitioner is not

specifically named. The merits of the case are yet to be examined.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                             1 of 2
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    ::: Downloaded on - 06-01-2019 07:18:56 :::</span>
              CRM-M-49706-2018 (O&amp;M)                                  -2-



</pre><p id="p_10">             Learned counsel for the complainant has further argued that the

complainant-party is being threatened for which they have filed an

application before the police. If it is so, police can always take corrective

measure.
</p>
<p id="p_11">             However, since the petitioner is not required for further

investigation by the police, interim order dated 14.11.2018 is made

absolute.
</p>
<p id="p_12">             Petition stands disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_3">
