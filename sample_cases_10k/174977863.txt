<p id="p_1">       Rule. Learned APP waives service of notice of
Rule for and on behalf of the respondent-State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       This     application          is     filed     for     extension           of
temporary bail which was granted by a co-ordinate
Bench of this Court by an order dated 20.05.2015. I
have     considered            the      averments        made       in          this
application and the documents with the compilation.
</p>


<p id="p_3">       Looking         to      the        averments         made     in          the
application,          this     application          is   required          to     be
partly allowed. Accordingly, it is partly allowed.
The temporary bail granted earlier by a co-ordinate


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                     Page 1 of 2</span>
           R/CR.MA/10491/2015                                       ORDER




Bench     of     this      Court    is    extended       for   a   period    of
1(one) week from the date of expiry of temporary bail
granted        by    earlier       order      on   the    same     terms    and
conditions. The convict shall surrender to the jail
authorities on expiry of temporary bail period.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
Direct service is permitted.
</p>
