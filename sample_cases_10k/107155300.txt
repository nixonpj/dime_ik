<div class="doc_title">Shivani Devi vs The State Of Bihar on 20 August, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.34096 of 2019
                        Arising Out of PS. Case No.-339 Year-2018 Thana- RAMPUR District- Gaya
                 ======================================================
                 SHIVANI DEVI Wife of Late Manu Singh Resident of Village - Gewal Bigha
                 Panpar, P.S.- Rampur, District- Gaya

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Vinod Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Amarendra Prasad
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   20-08-2019                    This application, for grant of anticipatory bail, arises

                   out of Rampur P.S. Case No. 339 of 2018, disclosing offences

                   under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_5">379</a>, <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_6">427</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_7">34</a> of the

                   Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Allegation against the petitioner and several other

                   accused persons including one Vicky Singh is that they entered

                   inside the house of the informant and took away Rs. Twenty

                   Five Thousand and on protest co-accused Vicky Singh stabbed

                   the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Submission of learned counsel for the petitioner is

                   that petitioner is a lady and has falsely been implicated in this

                   case and no specific allegation has been attributed to her.

</p><p id="p_4">                                 Heard learned A.P.P. also.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                  Having heard both sides, in view of the above facts,
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.34096 of 2019(2) dt.20-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     this application is allowed. Let the petitioner, above named, in

                     the event of her arrest or surrender before the court below within

                     six weeks, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                     25,000/- (Rupees Twenty five Thousand) each with two sureties

                     of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned Chief

                     Judicial Magistrate, Gaya, in connection with Rampur P.S. Case

                     No. 339 of 2018, subject to the condition laid down under

                     <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_8">Section 438</a> (2)<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_9"> of the Code</a> of Criminal Procedure.



</p><p id="p_6">                                             (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
sunilkumar/-
</p>
