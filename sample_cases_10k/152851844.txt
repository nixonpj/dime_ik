<p id="p_1">         Notice, returnable on 13th August, 2014. Mr.L.R.Poojari,
learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives service of notice for
the respondent-State. Till then no coercive steps shall be taken
against the applicant. It is, however, clarified that the investigation
is not stayed. Direct Service for respondent No.2 is permitted
through the concerned police station.
</p>
<p id="p_2">         The Investigation Officer shall file a report considering the
earlier reports, which were filed by the competent Investigating
Officers upon similar complaints filed by the First Informant, which
is forming part of the record of this application on the returnable
date.
</p>
