<p id="p_1">	When
the petition was called out and taken up for hearing in first round /
session learned advocates for the petitioners and respondents were
not present.  Any request for passover or adjournment was also not
made. However, with a view to giving opportunity of hearing to the
petitioners and respondents, the hearing was differed to second
session. 
</p>
 


<p id="p_2">	Even
in second call when the petition is called out and taken up for
hearing, learned advocates for the petitioners and respondents are
not present. Any request for passover or adjournment is also not
made. 
</p>
 


<p id="p_3">	Therefore,
petition is dismissed for non-prosecution. Notice is discharged.
Ad-interim / interim relief, if any, granted earlier stands vacated
forthwith.
</p>
