CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE AJAY KUMAR TRIPATHI
ORAL JUDGMENT
Date: 18-04-2016

                After having heard learned counsel for the petitioners and

   counsel for the State as well as having perused the stand of the
          Patna High Court CWJC No.631 of 2016 dt.18-04-2016

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                  2/2</span>




                 respondents in the counter affidavit, obviously, the promotion

                 granted to the petitioners by the previous incumbent, who is

                 Respondent No. 6 in the present writ application, has been done in

                 gross violation of the directive, dated 12.08.2014, issued by the

                 General Administration Department, Government of Bihar.
</p>
<p id="p_15">                              The Court is informed that appropriate show-cause has

                 already been issued to the Respondent No. 6 in this regard and the

                 matter will be examined critically.
</p>
<p id="p_16">                              Be that as it may, the Court would not like to interfere with

                 Annexure-3, because that will amount to giving premium to an illegal

                 decision and a decision in the face and teeth of the ban imposed by

                 the General Administration Department, dated 12.08.2014.
</p>
<p id="p_17">                              However, now since the said situation has changed. The

                 order, dated 12.08.2014 has been quashed. Respondent authorities

                 will proceed in the matter afresh as per the prevalent rules in this

                 regard with regard to such promotion.
</p>
<p id="p_18">                              Writ application is dismissed.
</p>



<p id="p_19">                                              (Ajay Kumar Tripathi, J)
SKM/-
