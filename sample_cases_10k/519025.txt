  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 24971 of 2009(N)


1. NAMEETA KUNAPALLY MANUEL,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE UNIVERSITY OF CALICUT,
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.E.NARAYANAN

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice V.GIRI

 Dated :28/<a href="/doc/1390448/" id="a_1">08/2009

 O R D E R
                          V.GIRI, J
                        -------------------
                     W.P.(C</a>).24971/2009
                        --------------------
          Dated this the 28th day of August, 2009

                        JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     Petitioner has completed her VIIIth semester B.Tech

examination and     dissatisfied with the marks obtained in

one paper in the said examination, she had applied for

revaluation.   She is    aggrieved that the result       of the

revaluation has not been published so far. Ext.P2 application

for revaluation is pending before the respondent.



</p><p id="p_2">2.   I heard learned standing counsel for the University

also.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">3.   Accordingly, writ petition is disposed of directing the

respondent to publish the results of the revaluation sought

for by the petitioner, as early as possible, at any rate, within

a period of eight weeks from the last date of receipt of the

application for revaluation provided the same is       in order

and otherwise correct.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                            V.GIRI,
                                            Judge
