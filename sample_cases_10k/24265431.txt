<div class="doc_title">Anoop Kumar Gupta vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 29 July, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                          WP-6242-2012
          (ANOOP KUMAR GUPTA Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


29-07-2015
      None for the petitioner, even the case is called in second
round.
      Shri S.K. Agrawal, learned counsel for the respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">No.2.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the respondent
No.2 has contended that the controversy involved in this petition
has already been decided by the judgment of the Supreme Court
in the matter of <a href="/doc/23755334/" id="a_1">Nagar Panchayat Kurwai &amp; Another vs.
Mahesh Kumar Singhal &amp; others</a>, 2013 (12) SCC 342.
</p><p id="p_3">      The petitioners in this petition has challenged levy of
parking fees, temporary entry fees from the buses and vehicles
entering within the limits of Nagar Panchayat. The aforesaid
point has already been decided by the Supreme Court in the
aforesaid judgment.
</p><p id="p_4">      This court has also dismissed another writ petition
No.10449/2012 after relying on judgment of Supreme Court
quoted above.
</p><p id="p_5">      In this view of the matter, this petition is dispose of.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                                      (S.K. GANGELE)
                                                           JUDGE
