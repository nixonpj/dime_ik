For Respondent(s) : G.S. Fauzdar, Spl. PP for JVVNL

       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAMCHANDRA SINGH JHALA
                                 Order
13/06/2017

            These two bail applications have been filed

under     <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section     439</a>     CrPC    in    connection      with    FIR

No.432/2016 registered at Police Station Niwai, District

Tonk for the offences under <a href="/doc/132967048/" id="a_2">Section 136</a> of Electricity

Act.
</p>
<p id="p_4">            Learned    counsel       for   the    petitioner(s)     has

contended     that     the    petitioners        have   falsely    been
                                  (2 of 2)
                                                            [ CRLMB-8175/2017]



implicated in the matter. He has further contended that

the    co-accused      has    already       been   granted         bail.     The

learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that

the petitioners are in judicial lock up since long and

conclusion of trial will take time. Hence they should be

released on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail

application.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and

learned      Public    Prosecutor       appearing      for         JVVNL     and

carefully perused the relevant material made available

on record.
</p>
<p id="p_7">             Looking to the overall facts and circumstances

of the case, but without expressing any opinion on the

merits and demerits of the case, I deem just and proper

to enlarge the petitioners on bail.

</p><p id="p_8">             Therefore, bail applications are allowed and

it    is    directed   that    accused       petitioner(s)            Gutya      @

Uganta S/o Ramswaroop and Ramkaran @ Karan S/o Rodu

shall be released on bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_3">Section 439</a> CrPC in

connection with aforesaid FIR provided they furnishes

personal bond of Rs.50,000/- each, with two sureties of

Rs.25,000/- to the satisfaction of the trial court with

the stipulation that they shall appear before that Court

on    all   subsequent   dates    of        hearing   and     as    and     when

called upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">                                 (RAMCHANDRA SINGH JHALA) J.
</p>
<p id="p_11">Mak/-
