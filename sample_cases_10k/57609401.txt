<p id="p_1">		Pursuant
to the order passed by this Court dated 03/05/2010, Mr.Anis Mankad,
Joint Secretary, Revenue Department is personally present before the
Court and has explained the procedure and difficulties faced by
department while holding the auction.  Looking to the efforts
put-forward by the Collector and its department, it is found just and
proper.  Therefore, since there is no buyer, it will not be proper to
dispose of the properties in a hurry, with a view to fetch the best
market price.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">		Mr.Mithani,
learned Advocate for the petitioner submitted that petitioner will
make a representation and Department will consider the same in
accordance with law.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_3">		As
the efforts made by the Department is found just and proper, presence
of Mr.Anis Mankad, Joint Secretary, Revenue Department, who is
present before the Court, is dispensed with and with a hope that,
subordinate department to the Revenue Department will take up the
matter and will accept and decide the representation of the
petitioner in accordance with law within a period of one week from
today.  For reporting compliance matter is adjourned to 09/08/2010.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_4">(K
S JHAVERI, J.)
