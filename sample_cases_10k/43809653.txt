<p id="p_1">4.   21-May-10                Mr. Yaduvansh Giri presses for an interim order

                 for stay of the order under appeal on the ground that the writ

                 court has interfered with notice issued for no confidence

                 motion but without taking note of subsequent event that in the

                 meeting writ petitioner also participated and the appellant was

                 elected as Deputy Chief Councilor of Sitamarhi Municipality.

                 It has further been submitted that against a similar impugned
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              3</span>




         order whereby the Chief Councilor was affected, the appellant

         preferred LPA No. 525 of 2010 and the Chief Councilor

         preferred LPA No. 670 of 2010 and both the appeals were

         allowed by a Division Bench on 20th of April, 2010.

</p><p id="p_2">                       A prayer has been made to adjourn the matter on

         the ground of difficulty of Mr. S.B.K. Mangalam, counsel for

         the private respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                       Let this matter be listed under the same heading

         after vacation.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                       Till then the status quo shall be maintained.

                       Learned counsel for the State is ready with

         records. They should be kept ready on the next date.


</p><p id="p_5">                                    (Shiva Kirti Singh, J.)



                                     (C. M. Prasad, J. )
