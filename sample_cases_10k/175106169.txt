            IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA
                    DHARWAD BENCH

        DATED THIS THE 12TH DAY OF JANUARY, 2015

                        BEFORE

         THE HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE RATHNAKALA

           CRIMINAL PETITION NO. 100024/2015

BETWEEN:

1.   JAMBAIAH S/O HOSAGERAPPA
     AGED ABOUT 52 YEARS,
     AGRICULTURIST,
     R/O. THIMALAPURA VILLGE,
     BELLARY TALUK AND DISTRICT

2.   SMT. DURUGAMMA W/O JAMBAIAH
     AGED ABOUT 45 YEARS, AGRICULTURIST,
     R/O. THIMALAPURA VILLGE,
     BELLARY TALUK AND DISTRICT
                                           ... PETITIONERS

(BY SRI : T HANUMAREDDY, ADVOCATE)

AND :

THE STATE BY SUB-INSPECTOR OF POLICE,
KUDITHINI POLICE STATION, BELLARY,
REP.BY SPP HIGH COURT OF
KARNATAKA, DHARWAD.
                                          ... RESPONDENT

(BY SRI K.S. PATIL, HIGH COURT GOVERNMENT PLEADER)

     THIS CRIMINAL PETITION IS FILED U/S 439 OF CR.P.C.
SEEKING TO ENLARGE THE PETITIONERS ON BAIL IN CRIME
NO.156/2014 ON THE FILE OF THE KUDITHINI P.S., TQ &amp; DIST.
BELLARY, FOR THE OFFENCES P/U/S 498A, 304B R/W 34 OF
<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>.
                                   2




    THIS PETITION COMING ON FOR ORDERS THIS DAY, THE
COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING:

                            ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      The petitioners herein, and their son i.e., accused

No.1, are arrayed as accused Nos. 2 and 3 in Crime

No.156/2014 of Kuduthini Police Station for the offences

punishable under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Sections 498A</a> and <a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_3">304B</a> read with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">Section

34</a> of the Indian Penal Code, 1860.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

</p><p id="p_3">      2. The   allegation   is,   accused   No.1   has   married

Soujanya, daughter of the complainant, in the month of April

2014. The accused persons used to torture the deceased for

various reasons. On 20.11.2014 accused No.1 took his wife

on his motorcycle for medical check up and while returning

he stopped his vehicle, pushed the deceased to a canal and

thereby finished off her.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">      3. Sri T. Hanumareddy, learned counsel for the

petitioner submits that they are falsely implicated, they have

not committed any offence as alleged. There is no allegation

on any overt act against them. In fact, it was an accident.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                3</span>




At the time of the incident accused No.1 had lost control over

the vehicle and had drowned in the channel water along with

his wife. He was admitted to the hospital. Petitioners are in

custody from 26.11.2014.           The incident occurred on

24.11.2014 and complaint is lodged on 25.11.2014.        They

are permanent residents of cause title address and are

owning properties.     They will fully co-operate with the

Investigation Officer during further investigation. Under the

circumstances, they may be enlarged on bail.



</p><p id="p_6">      4. Sri K.S. Patil, learned High Court Government

Pleader opposes the petition and prays for rejection of

petition.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">      5. Having regard to the facts of the case and that the

nature of the allegation do not call for any more custodial

interrogation of the petitioners, petitioners are having deep

roots in the society, and therefore, their continued detention

till filing of the charge sheet is not warranted, petition is

allowed, subject to the following conditions :
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                               4</span>




</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">    i)    Petitioners   are   enlarged    on   bail    in   Crime

          No.156/2014 of Kuduthini Police Station on

          executing a self bond for Rs.1,00,000/- each

          with one surety for the likesum.

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">    ii)   They shall co-operate with the Investigation

          Officer   during        the    further      course   of

          investigation.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">


</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">                                                Sd/-</blockquote><p id="p_9">
</p><p id="p_10">                                               JUDGE
