 WP(C).No. 7545 of 2019

                                          2



                                  JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">        The petitioner has approached this court seeking for the
  following reliefs:
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">             "i) to issue a writ of mandamus or such other writ or
             direction or order commanding the 2 nd respondent to issue
             Clearance Certificate to the petitioner as contemplated
             under Rul 5 (3) (iii) of the Kerala Abkari Shops Disposal
             Rules 2002, on remitting 50% of the amount mentioned in
             Ext.P3 Chalan.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">             ii)     to issue a writ of Mandamus or such other writ or
             direction or order declaring that the petitioner is entitled
             for Clearance Certificate as contemplated under Rule 5
             (3) (iii) of the Kerala Abkari Shops Disposal Rules, 2002
             on remitting 50% of the amount mentioned in Ext.P3
             Chalan."</blockquote><p id="p_2">


</p><p id="p_3">        2.         In the light of Ext.P4, there shall be a direction to the

  second respondent to issue Chalan to the petitioner for an

  amount equivalent to 50% of the amount mentioned in Ext.P2

  Chalan and to issue clearance certificate in the event of

  remitting the said amount.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        The writ petition is disposed of as above.
