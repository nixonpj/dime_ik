                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

              CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO.
                                 16620 of 2017

                         In CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. 156 of 2014

         =============================================
                        DINESHBHAI RAJABHAI BAGDA....Applicant(s)
                                        Versus
                         STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
         =============================================
         Appearance:
         THROUGH JAIL for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MS MONALI BHATT, ADDL. PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the
         Respondent(s) No. 1
         =============================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.P.BHATT

                                        Date : 04/07/2017

                                          ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">         1.     Rule. Learned APP waives service of rule on behalf of the
         respondent-State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2.     The present application is filed by the applicant-prisoner
         through jail for releasing him on temporary bail for a period of
         30 days on the ground of agriculture activities during monsoon
         season.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         3.     Heard        the       learned       APP       for       the        respondent-
         State and perused the application.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         4.     Considering the material placed on record, the cause
         shown in the application appears to be genuine. In the facts
         and circumstances, this Court is of the view that the applicant
         is required to be released on temporary bail for a period of ten
         days.</p><pre id="pre_2">



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                              Page 1 of 2</span>

HC-NIC                                     Page 1 of 2      Created On Sun Jul 23 12:19:24 IST 2017
                   R/CR.MA/16620/2017                                             ORDER




</pre><p id="p_5">         5.     Accordingly, the applicant is ordered to be released on
         temporary bail for a period of ten days from the date of his
         actual release on his executing a personal bond and local
         surety in the sum of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand Only)
         before the Jail Authorities on usual terms and conditions.
         During the period of temporary bail, the applicant shall not
         abuse the liberty granted to the applicant and shall maintain
         law and order. The applicant shall surrender before the Jail
         Authorities on completion of bail period without fail.
</p>
<p id="p_6">         6.     The present application is disposed of accordingly. Rule is
         made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p>
<p id="p_7">         7.     Registry is directed to send a writ of this order to the
         concerned Jail Authority forthwith by FAX.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                                                          (P.P.BHATT, J.)



         NEHA
