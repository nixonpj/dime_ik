<div class="doc_title">Smt. Kamla Devi Agarwal vs Unknown on 28 August, 2008</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Sanjib Banerjee</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                               GA No. 2748 of 2008
                               PLA No. 166 of 2008
                          IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                                  ORIGINAL SIDE
                                IN THE GOODS OF :
                  SMT. KAMLA DEVI AGARWAL, DECEASED



BEFORE:

 The Hon'ble JUSTICE SANJIB BANERJEE</pre>

<p id="p_1">     Date : 28th August, 2008.
</p>
<p id="p_2">Mr. G.S.Gupta, Advocate appears.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       The Court :- This is an application for directing the Manager

of     Bank of Baroda, Ganesh Chandra Avenue branch to permit the

locker no. 396 at such branch to be opened and for the contents

thereof to be inventorised.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     The applicant/alleged executor says that the alleged testatrix

maintained such locker and there may be substantial valuables in

such locker.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       The bank has been served, but is not represented. Ms. Indrani

Dhar, Adv. Of Bar Library is appointed special officer for the

purpose of making an inventory of the contents of locker no. 396

at the Ganesh Chandra Avenue branch of Bank of Rajasthan. The

inventory will be made within a period of three weeks from date.

The bank Manager will depute a responsible officer of the bank to

be present at the time of inventory and the inventory list will be

signed on behalf of the bank and on behalf of the applicant and

countersigned by the special officer.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 2</span>


</p><p id="p_6">      The special officer will be paid a remuneration of 600 GMs

and the special officer will stand discharged upon filing his

report and making over copies thereof to the bank and to the

applicant. The bank will not permit any articles to be removed

from the locker without previous leave of Court or prior to the

grant of the Probate, if any.
</p>
<p id="p_7">      GA No. 2748 of 2008 is disposed of without any order as to

costs.
</p>
<p id="p_8">      Urgent certified photostat copy of this order, if applied

for, be supplied to the parties subject to compliance with all

requisite formalities.
</p>
<p id="p_9">


</p><p id="p_10">                                           (SANJIB BANERJEE, J.)
