                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.48029 of 2019
                    Arising Out of PS. Case No.-76 Year-2018 Thana- BHAGWANPUR District- Vaishali
                 ======================================================
                 Sujeet Kumar @ Thapachi Son of Basudeo Rai @ Basado Rai Resident of
                 village- Chakmakra, P.S.- Hajipur Sadar, District- Vaishali.

                                                                                 ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr.Umesh Chandra Verma
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mr.Ramchandra Singh
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SUDHIR SINGH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   01-08-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               The petitioner seeks bail in a case registered under

                   <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_1">Section 395</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               The prosecution case, in short, is that six persons

                   variously armed, entered in the agency of the informant and

                   took away cash, gold and other belongings.

</p><p id="p_4">                               It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioner that

                   there is no allegation of tampering of witnesses alleged against

                   the petitioner. The petitioner is in custody since 15-11-2018.

                   Charge sheet in this case has already been submitted. The

                   petitioner is not named in the FIR. His name transpired in this

                   case on the basis of confessional statement of co-accused. There

                   is no recovery of any incriminating article from possession of
             Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.48029 of 2019(2) dt.01-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                      the petitioner. He has not been put on test identification parade. .

                      It has further been submitted that the petitioner may be released

                      after completion of one year in custody.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                    On behalf of the State, it is submitted that the

                      petitioner is not named in the F.I.R. The petitioner has got

                      criminal antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, it

                      is directed that after completion of one year in custody, the

                      petitioner, above named, be released on bail on furnishing bail

                      bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the

                      like amount each to the satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                      Vaishali at Hajipur in connection with Bhagwanpur P.S. Case

                      No. 76 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                                   (Sudhir Singh, J)
A.K.V.//-
</p>
