    S/L. 29.
</p><p id="p_2">pril 29, 2014.            C.R.M. No. 15773 of 2013
           MNS.
</p><p id="p_3">                                         Re: C.R.A.N. 1672 of 2014
                                   (relaxation and/or modification)

                     In re: Tarakeswar Patwa
                                                     ...Petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                     Mr. Ansuman Bera
                                               ...for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                     Mr. Rudradipta Nandy
                                               ...for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                     The petitioner was enlarged on anticipatory bail by order dated December
            20, 2013, inter alia, on condition that the petitioner would meet the Investigating
            Officer once in a week until further orders.
</p><p id="p_7">                     This is an application for relaxation of condition of anticipatory bail.
                     Mr. Rudradipta Nandy, learned advocate appearing for the State, informs
            this Court that the charge-sheet has been submitted.
</p><p id="p_8">                     Thus, we are of the opinion that it is no more necessary for the petitioner to
            meet the Investigating Officer once in a week.
</p><p id="p_9">                     Therefore, the condition asking the petitioner to meet the Investigating
            Officer once in a week stands waived.
</p><p id="p_10">                     The application for relaxation of condition of anticipatory bail, being CRAN
            1672 of 2014, is, thus, allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                                         (Subhro Kamal Mukherjee, J.)



                                        (Shib Sadhan Sadhu, J.)
