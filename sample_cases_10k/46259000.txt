                               Criminal Miscellaneous No.23577 of 2013

                 1. Pradeep Kr. Bishwash S/O Jagarnath Bishwash Resident Of Village
                 Majhuwa (Rangdaha) P.S. Forbesganj District- Araria

                                                                             .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                          Versus
                 1. The State Of Bihar

                                                      .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s       :       Mr.
                 For the Opposite Party/s       :     Mr.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   24-07-2013                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                    State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 Petitioner     is        languishing   in   custody        since

                    15.03.2013 in a case registered under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">section 379</a> of the

                    Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 It is alleged that motorcycle of the informant was

                    stolen by the petitioner. The petitioner has also confessed

                    his guilt.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 It is submitted by the learned counsel for the

                    petitioner that petitioner has committed no offence and

                    nothing has been recovered from his possession. It is stated
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.23577 of 2013 (2) dt.24-07-2013</span>


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                         that petitioner is also accused in four other cases.

</p><p id="p_5">                                     Considering the nature of accusation and the period

                         of custody, let the above named petitioner be released on

                         bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand)

                         with two sureties of like amount each to the satisfaction of

                         C.J.M. Araria in connection with Forbesganj P.S. Case no.

                         52 of 2013. The learned court below will cancel the bail

                         bonds of the petitioner if he gets involved substantially in

                         same and similar offence or he defaults on four consecutive

                         occasions.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                                (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)

M.Rahman/-
</p>
