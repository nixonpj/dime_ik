<p id="p_1">For Petitioner                     Shri Faisal Akhtar, Advocate



             Hon'ble Shri Justice Prashant Kumar Mishra

                             Order On Board

05/12/2017

</p><p id="p_2">1.      Learned counsel for the petitioner would submit that the petitioner

        has not been accorded benefit of pay under FR-22A (1), even

        though the said benefit has been extended to similarly placed

        employees working in the Chhattisgarh Power Generation

        Company Ltd, the petitioner is thus deprived of one increment of

        Rs. 340/-. He would draw attention of the Court to the pay fixation

        slip of one Shri Jitendra Nath Sikdar, Junior Engineer vide

        Annexure P-12.
</p><p id="p_3">          2.   Considering the limited issue involved in the writ petition relating

              to payment of one increment of Rs. 340/-, instead of keeping this

              petition pending, ends of justice would be served, if the writ

              petition is disposed of with a direction that in the event, petitioner

              prefers representation before the competent authority claiming

              benefit of increment under FR-22A (1), as has been extended to

              similarly placed employees Shri Jitendra Nath Sikdar and Shri

              K.D. Diwan, within a period of 4 weeks from today, the competent

              authority shall decide the representation by speaking order, at the

              earliest, preferably within a period of 3 months from the date of

              submission of the representation.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">         3.   Accordingly, the writ petition is disposed of.


</p><p id="p_6">         4.   It is made clear that this Court has not expressed any opinion on

              the merits of the case and the respondent authorities shall decide

              the matter, on its own merits, strictly in accordance with law,

              without treating any observation made in this order as opinion on

              the merits of the case.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                                                     Sd/-
</p>
