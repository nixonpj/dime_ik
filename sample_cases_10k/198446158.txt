<pre id="pre_1">                                  1



13.08.2014
C.L.-116(KB)



                               W.P. 5201 (W) of 2012


                               Alima Hossain
                                    Versus
                            State of West Bengal &amp; ors.


                          Mr. Rudranil De,
                          Mr. Ziaul Haque        ... For the petitioner.



                         By an earlier order of this Court dated 4th July,

               2012, the respondent no.9 was directed to consider the</pre>

<p id="p_1">               issue of equivalence of the Matriculation Examination of

               Bihar or Jharkhand with the Madhyamik Examination in

               the State of West Bengal.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                         Mr. De, learned advocate appearing for the

               petitioner submits that till date the said direction has not

               been complied with and the petitioner was not granted

               any opportunity of hearing and no order has been

               communicated by the said respondent no.9.

</p><p id="p_3">                         Mr.    De    further   submits   that   though    the

               respondents were directed to file an opposition by the

               order dated 17th December, 2012, no such affidavit-in-

               opposition has been filed till date.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      2</span>


          No one appears on behalf of the respondents

and no instruction is forthcoming as regards the grievance

of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">          List this matter as 'For Orders' in the Daily

Supplementary List of this Court on 17th September,

2014.
</p>
<p id="p_5">          The petitioner is directed to communicate this

order to the respondent no.9 along with a copy of the

earlier order of this Court dated 4th July, 2012 and to file

an affidavit of service on the returnable date.



</p><p id="p_6">                              (Tapabrata Chakraborty, J.)
