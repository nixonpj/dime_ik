<div class="doc_title">Kulvendra Singh vs State on 30 August, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                  1

     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                         JODHPUR

                            ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       S.B. CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL PETITION NO.7628/2016
            Kulvendr Singh Vs. The State of Rajasthan

      Date of order           :          30.08.2016

           HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE PANKAJ BHANDARI

Dr.A.A.Bhansali for the petitioner.
Mr.Deepak Choudhary, PP for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                            &lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;


</p><p id="p_3">1.          The petitioner has moved this bail application

under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_4">2.          The allegation in this FIR pertains to offences under

<a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1947545/" id="a_4">448</a>, <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_5">354</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_6">147</a> IPC and Sections 3(2)(iv)

and 3(1)(xi) of the SC ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act.

</p><p id="p_5">3.          The contention of the counsel for the petitioner is

that the complainant has earlier also lodged FIR No.178/2016

and husband of the complainant has also lodged FIR

No.177/2016 and the facts of the present FIR are akin to that

alleged in the earlier FIRs and pertains to the same dispute. It

is also contended that there is a revenue dispute pending

between the complainant and the accused petitioner, as a

result of which, two FIRs have been filed. It is also alleged that

there is no grievous injury on the body of the complainant and

there are only bruises and swellings.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                      2</span>

</p><p id="p_6">    4.            Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail

    application. His contention is that the accused petitioner has

    criminal antecedent and he has forcefully tried to take

    possession of the agricultural land and plot.

</p><p id="p_7">    5.            I have considered the rival contentions of the

    parties.
</p>
<p id="p_8">    6.            Considering the fact that there are revenue dispute

    amongst the parties, as also the fact that the accused

    petitioner is in custody and is not required for further

    investigation and the injuries sustained by the complainant are

    also simple in nature, I am inclined to grant bail to the present

    petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_9">    7.            Consequently, the bail application is allowed. It is

    ordered that the accused-petitioner, Kulvendr Singh s/o Shri

    Harfool Singh arrested in connection with F.I.R. No.137/2016,

    Police Station Kesarisingpur         shall be released on bail;

    provided he furnishes a personal bond of Rs.50,000/- and

    two surety bonds of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of

    the learned trial court with the stipulation to appear before

    that Court on all dates of hearing and as and when called

    upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">                                              (PANKAJ BHANDARI), J.
</p>
<p id="p_12">skant/-
