<p id="p_1">2   01-04-2015            Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                          The petitioner apprehends his arrest in a case under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">section

                 420</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_2">section 138</a> of N.I. Act.

</p><p id="p_3">                           The complainant alleged that the petitioner borrowed

                 rupees five lacs from him and handed over two cheques, but both

                 the cheques were dishonoured on being presented.

</p><p id="p_4">                            It is submitted that the petitioner never borrowed any

                 money. The cheques of the petitioner were lost, and for that the

                 petitioner filed informatory application.

</p><p id="p_5">                           Considering the aforesaid facts and the fact that the case
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.40869 of 2014 (2) dt.01-04-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




                   relates to borrowing and lending of money, it is directed that in the

                   event of arrest or surrender in the court below within four weeks

                   from the date of receipt/production of a copy of this order, the

                   above named petitioner shall be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail

                   bond of Rs.10,000/-(ten thousand) with two sureties of the like

                   amount each to the satisfaction of Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class,

                   Patna City in connection with Complaint Case No. 1353(c) of

                   2013, subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_3">section 438(2)</a>

                   Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                             (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J)


B.Roy/-
</p>
