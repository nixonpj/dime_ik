<div class="doc_title">Purushotam Lal Sukhwal vs State Of Raj. &amp; Ors on 17 August, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                           JODHPUR
   --------------------------------------------------------


           CIVIL WRIT (CW) No. 13125 of 2012

                 PURUSHOTAM LAL SUKHWAL
                         VS
                   STATE OF RAJ. &amp; ORS.


                  Date of Order : 17.8.2016


             HON'BLE MR. SANGEET LODHA,J.


        None present for the petitioner.


                              ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">                              -----
</p>
<p id="p_2">

          This   petition,   questioning   the   notice   dated

    23.10.11 issued by the Commissioner,Municipal Council,

    Udaipur, directing the petitioner to remove the illegal

    construction raised, was filed on 5.12.12.

</p><p id="p_3">          The Registry pointed out the defects on 10.12.12.

    Even after lapse of more than 3 years, the petitioner has

    not cared to remove the defects.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    (2)</span>

</p><p id="p_4">             Today,      nobody   is   present   on   behalf   of   the

       petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             It appears that the petitioner has lost interest in

       the litigation.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             The writ petition is, therefore, dismissed for non-

       prosecution.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                          ( SANGEET LODHA ),J.
</p>
