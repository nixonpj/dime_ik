<p id="p_1">2   07-04-2014                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel representing the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  The petitioner seeks bail in a case registered under

                 sections 25 (1-B) A, 26, 35 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_1">Arms Act</a>.

</p><p id="p_3">                                   Allegation is that one nokia mobile was recovered

                 from the possession of the petitioner and from para-3 of the

                 petition, it appears that the petitioner has no criminal antecedent.

                 No fire arm has been recovered from the possession of the

                 petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  Considering the facts and circumstances of the

                 case, the petitioner above named is directed to be released on bail

                 on furnishing of bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/-(ten thousand) with two

                 sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned

                 Chief Judicial        Magistrate, Jehanabad in connection with
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.7276 of 2014 (2) dt.07-04-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                       Mehandia P.S.Case No. 95 of 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                (Shyam Kishore Sharma, J)

Sudha/-
