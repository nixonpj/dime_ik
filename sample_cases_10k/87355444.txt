<p id="p_1"> ct no. 33
  Sl. 6
    sp


                          CPAN 613 of 2019
                                In
                      W.P. No. 8904 (W) of 2017

                Debabrata Ghosh &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_2">                         Versus
               Pravat Kumar Bose &amp; Ors.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
             Mr. Pinaki Dhole,
             Mrs. Ananya Neagi
                             ...........for the contemnors



                  None appears on behalf of the petitioners.

                  The Deputy Director of Agriculture (Admn.),

             Paschim Medinipore has passed a final order on the

             August 1, 2018. A copy whereof is handed over to this

             Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                  It appears from the order that the Deputy

             Director has only imparted training to the petitioners

             and was in no way connected with the engagement of

             the petitioners in any gram panchayat.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                          2</span>


      It   is     further      submitted        that     the   Block

Development Officer, Government of West Bengal at

Kharagpur-I Block, P.O. - Satkui, Matkatpour, Dist. -

Paschim      Medinipur             had   earlier    engaged      the

petitioners for election duty and census work.

</p><p id="p_5">      In those circumstances, let this order be kept

with the record.
</p>
<p id="p_6">      The petitioners are entitled to take steps in

accordance        with       law    to    approach       the   BDO,

Government of West Bengal for any relief.

</p><p id="p_7">      With the aforesaid directions, the contempt

application being no. CPAN 613 of 2019 is disposed

of.
</p>
<p id="p_8">      The alleged contemnors shall make over a copy

of this order to each of the writ petitioners.

</p><p id="p_9">      Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if

applied    for,   be     given      to   the   parties    on   usual

undertaking.
</p>
<p id="p_10">

</p><p id="p_11">                                          (Rajasekhar Mantha, J.)
