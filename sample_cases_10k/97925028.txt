<p id="p_5">
                Affidavit of service filed in Court today be taken on

             record.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                Having heard the learned advocates for the parties and

             upon perusing the instant application, it appears from the

             averments that the petitioner has stated to be in the

             business of pisciculture in 9 No. Autina Gram Panchayat,

             Police Station, Tapan, situated in the district of Dakshin

             Dinajpur, for the last 50 years along with his two brothers.

                Perusing the writ petition further, it appears that the

             concerned local authorities had once upon a time allowed

             the writ petitioner to conduct pisciculture in a local

             tank/pond within the jurisdiction of 9 No. Autina Gram

             Panchayat. In paragraph 7, the petitioner apprehends that

             the local panchayat authority may lease out the tank-in-

             question to their "yes man".
</p>
<p id="p_7">                The writ Court, merely on the basis of such averments
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                       2</span>


cannot embark into a fishing and roving enquiry, especially

when the writ petition is mainly grounded on conjecture and

surmise. However, in the interest of justice, the writ petition

can be disposed of, at this stage itself, with a direction upon

the concerned authority to ensure that if a lease is to be

executed in respect of the local tank/pond for pisciculture,

such lease should be on the basis of an auction bid only and

the same should be conducted in a transparent and fair

manner and strictly in accordance with law.

   Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied

for, be given to the learned advocates for the parties.




</p><p id="p_8">                                 (Biswanath Somadder, J.)
