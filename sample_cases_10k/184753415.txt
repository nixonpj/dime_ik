Present:     Mr. Amaninder Preet, Advocate for the petitioner.

JASWANT SINGH, J (ORAL)

</pre><p id="p_1">             Petitioner-Shireen Roselein and her father-Salvester Fazal

Masih, by filing the present petition, are seeking the transfer of a suit filed

by the respondent-plaintiff/Asif at Faridkot seeking to restrain petitioner

No.2-Salvester Fazal Masih from re-marrying petitioner No.1-Shireen

Roselein to anybody else, in view of the alleged marriage between Asif and

Shireen Roselein on 16.3.2013 at Faridkot Church. The transfer is sought

from Faridkot to Kharar, District Mohali.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             After arguing for some time, learned counsel for the petitioners

prays for withdrawal of the present petition.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Dismissed as withdrawn.
</p>
