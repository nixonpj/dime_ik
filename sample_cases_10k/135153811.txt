<div class="doc_title">Ms Kiran Garments Through Partner ... vs Smt Nirmala Chawda on 6 January, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                             Civil Revision No. 09/2013

           M/s Kiran Garments through partner Smt. Kiran Saha
                                                                    ....Petitioner(s)
                                              Versus
           Smt. Nirmala Chawda                                   ... Respondent(s)

           CORAM:        THE HON'BLE MR JUSTICE P.P. BHATT
                                          --------
           For the Petitioner(s) : Mr. Shailesh, Advocate.
           For the Respondent(s): Mr. Vishal Kumar Tiwari, Advocate.
                                   -------

5/05.01.2015</pre><p id="p_1">:          Learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the
        petitioner is ready and willing to settle the case with the help of
        conciliation/mediation that may be undertaken by the Jharkhand State
        Legal Services Authority (JHALSA).           It is further submitted that the
        petitioner has also an offer of Rs. 35,00,000/- for the purpose of shop in
        question. It is also submitted that while recording the deposition, the
        respondent has also shown inclination for sale of the property in question.
</p><p id="p_2">              In view of the above position, for the purpose of having effective
        conciliation/mediation between the parties, the petitioner as well as the
        respondent shall appear in person before the Member Secretary, JHALSA
        on 6.2.2015 at 11:30 A.M. The Member Secretary, JHALSA shall make
        necessary arrangement for conciliation/mediation between the parties.
</p><p id="p_3">              Put up this case on 24.2.2015.
</p><p id="p_4">              Let the outcome of the mediation/conciliation be sent to this court
        on or before the next date i.e. on 24.2.2015.
</p><p id="p_5">              Since the possibility of amicable settlement is being explored with
        the help of mediation/conciliation, JHALSA, it expected from the parties,
        especially from the respondent, that he will not take any further steps in
        execution of the property in question till the next date i.e. on 24.2.2015.
</p><p id="p_6">              Let this order be communicated to the court below concerned
        through FAX at the cost of the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_7">              A copy of this order be also sent to the Member Secretary,
        Jharkhand State Legal Services Authority.
</p>

<pre id="pre_2">Anu/-                                                             (P.P. Bhatt, J.)
 </pre>
