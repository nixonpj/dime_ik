<pre id="pre_1">                              1


 IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA AT BANGALORE

           DATED THIS THE 03RD DAY OF JULY, 2012

                          BEFORE

        THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE SUBHASH B. ADI

              CRIMINAL PETITION NO:3190/2012

BETWEEN:

SRI MUKUNDA K G
S/O SRI GOPALAIH
AGED ABOUT 37 YEARS
R/O R H CIRCLE, BEHIND DCM
NITUVALLI, DAVANAGERE
                                  ...PETITIONER
(BY SRI. H. MUJTABA, ADV.,)


AND:

THE STATE OF KARNATAKA
REP BY THE K T J POLICE STATION
DAVANAGERE
                               ...RESPONDENT
(BY SRI SATISH R GIRJI, HCGP)


       THIS CRIMINAL PETITION IS FILED UNDER SECTION 439
CR.P.C. PRAYING TO ENLARGE THE PETITIONER ON BAIL IN
FIR NO.181/2011 OF K.T.JAMBANNANAGAR POLICE STATION,
DAVANAGERE FOR THE OFFENCES P/U/S 302, 304(B) R/W 34
OF <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> AND SECTION 3 AND 4 OF D.P. ACT.


       THIS CRIMINAL PETITION COMING ON FOR ORDERS THIS
DAY, THE COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING:
                                  2

                             ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Petitioner    has    been        charge-sheeted      by    K.T.

Jambannanagar Police, Davanagere, in Crime No.181/2011

for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Sections 302</a>, <a href="/doc/409589/" id="a_3">304(B)</a>,

<a href="/doc/1868826/" id="a_4">498(A)</a>, <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_5">114</a> r/w <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">Section 34</a> of IPC and <a href="/doc/751411/" id="a_7">Sections 3</a> and <a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_8">4</a> of

Dowry Prohibition Act.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">     2. Though the complainant alleges that the accused

strangulated the deceased in connection with the dowry

demand, however, Post Mortem report reveals that the death

is due to hanging. The opinion of the Doctor is that "the

death of the deceased is Asphyxia due to constriction of

major blood vessels and airways in the neck as a result of

hanging."
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">     3. Since the charge-sheet has been filed and this

accused has been in judicial custody for quite sometime, I

find that the petitioner could be enlarged on bail subject to

certain conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">     4. Accordingly, the petition is allowed.          Petitioner is

enlarged on bail subject to the following conditions:


</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">             (i)   The   petitioner    shall   execute   a
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                3</span>

       personal bond for Rs.25,000/- with two

       solvent sureties for the like sum to the

       satisfaction of the Trial Court.


</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">            (ii) The petitioner shall regularly attend

       the Court on hearing dates.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">            (iii) The petitioner shall not tamper

       with the prosecution witnesses.




</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_5">                                           Sd/-</blockquote><p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">                                          JUDGE

PMR*
 </p>
