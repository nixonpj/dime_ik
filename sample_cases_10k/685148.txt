<p id="p_1">Heard
	learned advocate Mr. NV Anjaria on behalf of petitioner, learned
	advocate Ms. AU Amin appearing on behalf of respondent no. 1 and
	learned advocate Mr. RK Pandya appearing for respondent no. 3. The
	notice is served to respondent no. 2 but no appearance is filed.
</p>	  

<p id="p_2">Today,
	learned advocate Mr. Pandya appearing for respondent no. 3 placed on
	record affidavit in reply.  Copy thereof is served to learned
	advocate Mr. Anjaria and learned advocate Ms. Amin, which is taken
	on record.
</p>	  

<p id="p_3">The
	public notice issued by respondent no. 3 under section 3(A) of the
	Act dated 7/1/2010.  Against which, reply was filed by petitioners
	on 21/1/2010 and were remained present in personal hearing on
	22/1/2010.  
</p>	
	  

<p id="p_4">The
	present petition is filed with a grievance that till date
	petitioners were not aware about facts that in respect to objection
	raised by petitioners whether decision has been taken by respondent
	no. 3 or not?  But today, learned advocate Mr. Pandya placed on
	record affidavit in reply, wherein according to him at page 107
	decision has been taken by authority on 12/4/2010.
</p>	  

<p id="p_5">He
	fairly admitted before this Court that decision taken by respondent
	no. 3 has not been communicated by respondent no. 3 to any of
	petitioner.  Therefore, it is directed to respondent no. 3 to
	communicate decision dated 12/4/2010 to each petitioner, which has
	been taken by respondent no. 3 in respect to objection raised by
	petitioners within a period of 10 days from date of receiving copy
	of present order.
</p>	  

<p id="p_6">It
	is open for petitioner to challenge aforesaid order if so advise
	dated 12/4/2010 by filing comprehensive petition including same
	cause of action before appropriate forum in accordance with law. 
	The decision taken by respondent no. 3 dated 12/4/2010 has not been
	implemented till date as per submission made by learned advocate Mr.
	Pandya.  Let respondent no. 3 may wait upto challenge such order by
	petitioners before appropriate forum in accordance with law.  It is
	further directed to respondent no. 3 not to take further steps in
	respect to implementation of decision dated 12/4/2010 for a period
	of three weeks only.
</p>	  

<p id="p_7">In
	view of above observation and direction, present petition is
	disposed of without expressing any opinion on merits with a liberty
	to petitioner to challenge decision of respondent no. 3 dated
	12/4/2010 by filing comprehensive petition on the basis of same
	cause of action.
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_8">								(H.K.RATHOD,
