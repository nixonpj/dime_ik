<p id="p_1">Ashok Bhushan, Ag, CJ.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

learned Government Pleader. No notice is being issued to

respondents 5 and 6, in view of the order, which is being

passed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     2. The petitioner has filed this Writ Petition alleging

police harassment. The petitioner's case is that although

an injunction has already been granted in O.S.No.79/2015

in favour of the petitioner, the Police authorities are calling

the petitioner and pressurising to withdraw the suit.

</p><p id="p_4">     3. The learned Government Pleader, after obtaining

instruction, submitted that before the petitioner obtained

the injunction order, a complaint was filed by the

petitioner against the 6th respondent in which connection,

the petitioner was called and a statement was recorded. It

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">   WP(C).6038/15             2</span>

is further submitted that on finding that the dispute is of

civil nature, the matter was closed.

</p><p id="p_5">      In view of the above facts, we do not find that there

is any police harassment in the case. Recording the

submission of the learned Government Pleader, the Writ

Petition is closed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                  ASHOK BHUSHAN
                                ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE
