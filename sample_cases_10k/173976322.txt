<div class="doc_title">Mohammad Abdul Rasul vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 30 November, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                          MCRC-20744-2015
           (MOHAMMAD ABDUL RASUL Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


30-11-2015

Shri Ashish Rawat, counsel for the applicant.
Shri G.S.Thakur, Panel Lawyer for the State/ respondent.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
This is the Seventh bail application of the applicant, whereas few
of his previous bail applications were dismissed on merits.
The applicant is in custody since 13.11.2013 relating to crime
No.178/2013 registered at Police Station Sabhapur, District Satna
for offence punishable under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Sections 498-A</a>, <a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_2">304-B</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of IPC.
After considering the submissions made by the learned counsel for
the parties and looking to the facts and circumstances of the case,
without expressing any view on the merits of the case, I am of the
view that no new ground or change in circumstance has been
shown by the applicant, so that view taken on 10.9.2015 may be
changed.
</p><p id="p_2">Consequently, application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_4">section 439</a> of Cr.P.C. filed by the
applicant Mohammad Abdul Rasul relating to aforesaid crime is
hereby dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                                          (N.K. GUPTA)
                                                             JUDGE
