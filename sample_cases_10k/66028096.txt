                                     Miscellaneous Appeal No.1128 of 2017
                  ======================================================
                  Mrs. Ila wife of Lt. Col. Sanjeev Kumar, D/o Dr. Ramadhin Prasad, Resident
                  of Anand Garh, Police Station- Barari, District- Bhagalpur.

                                                                                ... ... Appellant/s
                                                      Versus
                  Lt. Co. Sanjeev Kumar S/o Sri Anandi Prasad Yadav, Resident of Fatehpur,
                  Police Station- Zeromile, District- Bhagalpur, at present at Rani Talab, Police
                  Station- Zero Mile, District- Bhagalpur.

                                                            ... ... Respondent/s
                  ======================================================
                  Appearance :
                  For the Appellant/s    :        Mr.Pranav Kumar
                  For the Respondent/s   :        Mr.Alok Kumar Shahi
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                          and
                          HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ARVIND SRIVASTAVA
                                        ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">                  (Per: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH)

11   15-10-2019                 The appellant is present along with her counsel.

</p><p id="p_2">                    However, none appears for the respondent.

</p><p id="p_3">                                I.A. No. 7160 of 2018/ 1 of 2018

                                The above mentioned I.A. has been filed for

                    condonation of delay of 1 year 2 months 1 day (though in the

                    I.A. it has been stated that delay is of 164 days) in filing the

                    present appeal.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                M.A. No. 1128 of 2017

                                The appellant, being the wife of the sole respondent

                    has preferred this Miscellaneous Appeal against the judgment

                    and decree dated 08.06.2017 passed by the learned Principal

                    Judge, Family Court, Bhagalpur in Matrimonial Case No. 150 of
 Patna High Court MA No.1128 of 2017(11) dt.15-10-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            2/3</span>




          2014, whereby the matrimonial suit of the respondent filed with

          a prayer for dissolution of his marriage with the appellant on the

          ground of cruelty has been decreed and without any application

          preferred by the appellant, the claim of maintenance or

          permanent alimony has been rejected.

</p><p id="p_5">                       Learned counsel for the appellant submits that the

          calculation with regard to the period of limitation is incorrect in

          the I.A. application and the period of limitation calculated by

          the office is correct. Hence, he seeks permission to make

          necessary correction in the I.A. He is permitted to make such

          correction.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                       Considering the grounds taken in the Interlocutory

          application showing the sufficient cause for not filing the appeal

          within stipulated period, the delay of 1 year 2 months 1 day in

          the filing the present appeal is hereby condoned.

</p><p id="p_7">                       I.A. No. 7160 of 2018/ 1 of 2018 stands disposed of.

                       Let the appeal be admitted.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                       Lower Court record has already been received.

                       Let fresh notice be issued to the sole respondent

          under ordinary process as well as under registered cover with

          A/d for which requisites etc. must be filed by 8.11.2019, failing

          which the memo of appeal shall stand dismissed without further
          Patna High Court MA No.1128 of 2017(11) dt.15-10-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     3/3</span>




                   reference to the Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                Let the appeal be listed for hearing on 3rd December,

                   2019.
</p>
<p id="p_10">

</p><p id="p_11">                                          (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)


                                            ( Arvind Srivastava, J)
anil/-
</p>
