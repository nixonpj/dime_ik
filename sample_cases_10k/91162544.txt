        IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
         R/CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 7354 of 2019
==========================================================
                MOHAN YASHODANANDAN ARORA
                            Versus
                      STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
MS ARCHANA R ACHARYA(2475) for the Applicant(s) No. 1
for the Respondent(s) No. 2
PUBLIC PROSECUTOR(2) for the Respondent(s) No. 1
==========================================================
CORAM:                  HONOURABLE MS JUSTICE SONIA GOKANI
                              Date : 03/05/2019
                               ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">1.             This is a petition, seeking quashment of the
FIR       being      I-C.R.       No.      138/2018,            registered            with
Varachha         Police        Station,         Surat,         for    the       offence
punishable under <a href="/doc/1721129/" id="a_1">Sections 338</a>, <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_2">114</a> and <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_3">504</a> of the
Indian Penal Code, 1860, which, of filing of the
charge-sheet, has culminated into Criminal Case No.
13174/2016,          which       is    pending          before       the    Court         of
learned        JMFC,          Surat,     where,          the    matter         is       for
recording of plea.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.             Since,          there      is      an      alternative              remedy
available to the petitioner, he shall exhaust the
same and if, he is not satisfied, he may approach
this Court once again. This Court has not entered
into       the     merits        of      the          matter.    DISPOSED             OFF,
accordingly.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                                                                 (SONIA GOKANI, J)
UMESH/-
</p>
