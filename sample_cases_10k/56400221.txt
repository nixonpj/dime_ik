<div class="doc_title">Hemant Dashputre vs Jiwaji University on 19 May, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                          WP-8159-2011
                (HEMANT DASHPUTRE Vs JIWAJI UNIVERSITY)


19-05-2017

       None for petitioners.
       None for respondent.</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Petitioners have approached this Court for the relief
seeking classification as Lab Technicians alleging discrimination.
</p><p id="p_2">       On notice, respondent has filed counter affidavit and
submitted that there is no provision under statute No.31 for
classification from Class IV post to Class III post. Petitioners, as
a matter of fact, did not fulfill the essential qualifications while
appointed as Lab Attendant; Class IV. Employees, who have
been appointed as Lab Assistant; Class III, were eligible and after
due selection were appointed, as the Executive Council had
directed to fill-up the vacant posts of Lab Technicians as per
rules. Therefore, for want of any rule or authority with the
respondent for classification of Class IV employees to Class III,
no relief much less effective relief can be granted to the
petitioners.
</p><p id="p_3">       In the opinion of this Court, the grievance of the petitioners
is not found to be sustainable in the eyes of law. Under such
circumstances, writ petition is found to be devoid of merits.
Accordingly, dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                                     ROHIT ARYA)
                                                       JUDGE
Arun*
