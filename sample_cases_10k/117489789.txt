<p id="p_1">2   31-08-2015          Heard learned counsel for the informant as well as learned

                 Additional P.P.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                        The petitioner seeks bail in Bhabhua (Sonhan) P.S. Case

                 No. 249/2015, registered for the offences punishable under

                 Section 302 and other<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> Sections of the Indian Penal Code</a>.

</p><p id="p_3">                        The informant named the petitioner but no specific

                 allegation of assault is made against the petitioner. The informant

                 alleged that Ajay Singh stabbed his brother causing his death.

                 Similarly situated co-accused Sanjay Singh has already been

                 enlarged on bail vide order passed in Cr. Misc. No. 27862/2015.

</p><p id="p_4">                         Considering the facts aforesaid, the petitioner above named

                 is directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                 10,000/- (Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.39295 of 2015 (2) dt.31-08-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                       to the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                       Bhabhua, Kaimur, in connection with Bhabhua (Sonhan) P.S.

                       Case No. 249/2015.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                                                         (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J.)
