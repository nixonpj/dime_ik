                 1. Bachan Yadav
                 2. Subodh Yadav @ Sibodh Kumar
                 3. Faresh Yadav @ Faresh Kumar &amp;
                 4. Shambhu Yadav

                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s

                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Kunwar Narayan Jamuar

                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   11-02-2015                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and

                 learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  Petitioners apprehend their arrest in connection with

                 Atri P.S. Case No. 197 of 2014 registered for the offence under

                 Sections-341, 323, 307 and 504/34<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  The specific allegation of giving Khanti blow on the

                 head of the informant is against co-accused Vijay Yadav, who is

                 not the petitioner here. So far as petitioners are concerned; there is

                 omnibus allegation of assault against them. The informant has

                 sustained two lacerated wounds on his head and the opinion

                 regarding the aforesaid injuries was given by the doctor as

                 dangerous to life. Moreover, there is case and counter case

                 between the parties and a Panchayati was also held between both
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.5718 of 2015 (2) dt.11-02-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                           Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances

                        as well as submission of the parties, particularly, taking note of

                        this fact that no specific overt act has been attributed against these

                        petitioners and admittedly, the alleged occurrence took place on

                        account of previous land dispute, petitioners, named above, in the

                        event of their arrest or surrender within four weeks from the date

                        of receipt/production of copy of order in the court below, shall be

                        released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs 10,000/- (ten

                        thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each in

                        connection with Atri P.S. Case No.               197 of 2014   to the

                        satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Gaya subject to

                        conditions as laid down u/S 438(2) of the Cr. P.C.

</p><p id="p_5">                                               (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
</p>
