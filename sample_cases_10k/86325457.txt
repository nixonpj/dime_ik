                   BENCH AT JAIPUR

           S.B. Civil Contempt Petition No. 1818/2017

1.     Kailash Chand Meena S/o Shri Kanhaiya Lal Meena, R/o
       Village And Post Chhapoli, Tehsil Udaipurwati Distt.
       Jhunjhunu Raj. (Since Deceased)
1/1.   Smt. Manju W/o Late Shri Kailash Chand Meena, aged
       about 39 years
1/2.   Sandeep S/o Late Shri Kailash Chand Meena, aged about
       22 years
1/3.   Rajeev S/o Late Shri Kailash Chand Meena, aged about
       15 years, through Natural Guardian Through His Mother
       Smt. Manju
1/4.   Poonam D/o Late Shri Kailash Chand Meena, aged about
       17 years, through Natural Guardian Mother Smt. Manju
       All petitioners are resident of village and Post Chhapoli,
       Tehsil Udaipurwati, District Jhunjhunu
                                                          ----Petitioners
                                  Versus
1.     Shri Kuldeep Ranka, IAS, Managing Director, Rajasthan
       State   Road      Transport    Corporation,    Parivahan     Marg,
       Chomu House, Jaipur Rajasthan
2.     Smt.    Jyoti    Chauhan,      Executive    Officer     Mechanical,
       Rajasthan       State   Road   Transport    Corporation,     Jaipur
       Rajasthan
3.     Sh. Kalyan Shai Meena, Chief Manager, Rajasthan State
       Road Transport Corporation, Sikar Depot, Sikar Raj.
4.     State    Of     Rajasthan,     Through      Principal    Secretary,
       Department Of Transport, Secretariat, Jaipur Raj.
                                                        ----Respondents</pre>


<pre id="pre_2">For Petitioner(s)         :    Mr. SK Singodiya
For Respondent(s)         :    Mr. Vinayak Joshi



              HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ALOK SHARMA

                                  Order

19/09/2018
                                 (2 of 3)           [CCP-1818/2017]


</pre><p id="p_1">          Non compliance of the order dated 1.3.2017 in S.B.


Civil Writ Petition No. 13630/2008 is alleged in this petition.


Thereunder the termination of Kailash Chand Meena was quashed


and set-aside. However, owing to the death of aforesaid Kailash


Chand Meena, during the pendency of his petition the Court


directed that benefits due to him consequent to his termination


being set-aside be calculated by the respondents on the basis of


treating him to be reinstated and in service till his death i.e.


30.9.2012 and be released to his LRs.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">          Mr. Vinayak Joshi appearing for the respondents at the


outset submitted that in compliance with the court's order, Chief


Manager, Sikar Depot, RSRTC vide order dated 10.9.2018 has


drawn a cheque bearing No. 387379 dated 7.9.2018 drawn on


State Bank of India for a sum of Rs. 7,39,134/-    favouring the


widow of late Kailash Chand Meena covering all due benefits to


deceased Kailash Chand Meena in terms of the court's order dated


1.3.2017. He submitted that for the purposes of family pension in


the service book of the deceased Kailash Chand Meena, no


nominee has been notified. However for grant of family pension on
                                                                        (3 of 3)              [CCP-1818/2017]


                                   an appropriate application being received, it would be considered


                                   in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                                              Mr.   SK    Singodiya   submitted   that     respondents     be


                                   directed to consider a dependent of the deceased Kailash Chand


                                   Meena for appointment on compassionate basis. That however is


                                   not the subject matter of this petition.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                              In    the   facts   recorded   above,   no   case   of   willful


                                   disobedience     of the court's order dated 1.3.2017 can be made


                                   out. The contempt petition stands dismissed accordingly. Notices


                                   stand discharged.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                                                                       (ALOK SHARMA),J

                                   DK
