<p id="p_1">      The petitioners are retired employees of the Palakkad

Municipality. Their complaint in this writ petition is that retirement

benefits due to the petitioners have not yet been disbursed to

them.    Today it is submitted that all amounts due to the

petitioners have been paid. Accordingly, the writ petition is

closed.
