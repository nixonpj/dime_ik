<p id="p_1">     br                     C.O. 4174 of 2013


                        Mr. Subrata Bhattacharyya,
                                       ....... For the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
                   Sri Subrata Bhattacharyya, learned counsel appears on

             behalf of the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                   None appears on behalf of the opposite party/husband.

                   The matter appears under the heading "Section 24

             Contested Application" today.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                   Sri Bhattacharyya points out that from the order dated

             7th April, 2014, the opposite party/husband sought time to

             file affidavit-in-opposition. However, till date neither any

             affidavit-in-opposition has been filed nor served upon the

             petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                   Sri Bhattacharyya pleads that the matter be taken up

             for hearing peremptorily.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                   This Court directs Sri Bhattacharyya to serve notice on

             the   learned    counsel     for   the    opposite   party/husband

             intimating that the matter will be taken up for peremptory

             consideration    under     the     same    heading   "Section   24

             Contested Application" on 17th September, 2014.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                     2</span>


    The parties are directed to exchange affidavits in terms

of the order of this Court dated 7th April, 2014 in the

meantime.
</p>
<p id="p_7">    Sri Bhattacharya is further directed to furnish proof of

communication of the order passed by this Court today on

the opposite party/husband on the next date.

    Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied

for, supplied to the learned counsel for the petitioners upon

compliance of all necessary formalities.




</p><p id="p_8">                               ( Subrata Talukdar, J. )
