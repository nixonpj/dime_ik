THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.S.SUNDAR

Usha Sharma                                             .. Petitioner
                                            Vs.

1.The Vice Chancellor,
   Madurai Kamaraj University,
   Madurai District.

2.Madurai Kamaraj University,
   Represented by its
   The Registrar,
   Madurai District.                                    .. Respondents                  
                
PRAYER: Writ petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India,
to issue a Writ of Certiorarified Mandamus, to call for the records
pertaining to the impugned circular dated 02.03.2017 on the file of the
Respondent No.2 and quash the same as illegal and consequently to direct the
respondent No.2 to permit the petitioner to reside in the living Quarters
within the Respondent University Campus preferably the Lecturers Quarters
within the time stipulated by this Court.


!For Petitioner         : Mr. T.Lajapathi Roy

For Respondent          : R.M.Makesh Kumaravel  
                                            Standaing Counsel for R2


:ORDER

<p id="p_1">                The learned counsel appearing for the petitioner seeks permission
of this Court to withdraw this Writ petition and he has also made an
endorsement to that effect.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                2.In view of the same, this Writ petition is dismissed as
withdrawn. No costs. Consequently, connected Miscellaneous petition is
closed.
