<div class="doc_title">Shankar Rai vs The State Of Bihar on 18 April, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.1118 of 2013
                 ======================================================
                 Shankar Rai S/O Namdhari Rai Resident Of Village- Rustampur, P.S.-
                 Rustampur (O.P.), District- Patna.

                                                                     .... ....   Petitioner.
                                                 Versus
                 The State Of Bihar

                                                      .... .... Opposite Party.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">3   18-04-2013              Heard learned counsels for the petitioner

                 and the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                            The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in

                 a case registered for the offences punishable under

                 <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_1">Section 414</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                            One stolen tractor was recovered when Lal

                 Mohan Rai was apprehended who disclosed the name of

                 his other associates including the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                            It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioner that the recovery was not made from the

                 petitioner       and    except    confession        of     co-accused,

                 there     is   nothing     against       the   petitioner.            It   is

                 further submitted that apprehended accused person

                 has been granted regular bail.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                            This Court sees no reason for the learned

                 court     below      not   to    consider      to   give        the    same

                 privilege to the petitioner if he surrenders within
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.1118 of 2013 (3) dt.18-04-2013</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                  a period of six weeks from the date of receipt of

                  copy of this order in connection with Fatuha P.S.

                  Case No. 63 of 2012 pending in the court of the

                  learned S.D.J.M., Patna City, Patna.

</p><p id="p_6">                               With this observation, the application is

                  disposed off.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                               Let the order be faxed to the learned court

                  below at the cost of the petitioner.




</p><pre id="pre_2">U.K./-                                      (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
 </pre>
