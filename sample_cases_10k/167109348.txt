<p id="p_1">     Sri. Lakshmikanth, Advocate appearing on behalf

of the respondents submits that he has no objection to

set-aside the order of the learned Senior Civil Judge,

Kundapura, on I.A. No.8 filed under Order 23 Rule 1 of

Code of Civil Procedure, dated 18.06.2012 in O.S. No.

72/2006. In the instant case, the plaintiff has filed an

application i.e. I.A. No.8 seeking for dismissal of the

O.S.No.72/2006 as withdrawn. It is stated in the

affidavit filed in support of this application that

O.S.No.12/2010 is also pending before the Court of

Senior Civil Judge, Kundapura. The subject matter of

O.S. No. 12/2010 and O.S. No. 72/2006 are one and

the same. It is further stated in the affidavit that in

order to avoid the multiplicity of suits and conflict of

decisions, this plaintiff wants to withdraw the suit.


</p><p id="p_2">     Since other side has no objections, Order passed

on I.A.No.8 filed under Order 23 Rule 1 of Code of Civil

Procedure, dated 18.06.2012 in O.S. 72/2006 is hereby
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                4</span>

set-aside. Accordingly, this Civil Revision Petition is

disposed off.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">      Since the main matter has been disposed off, I.A.

No.   1/2012    filed   for   stay   does   not   survive   for

consideration. Hence, it is dismissed.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                   SD/-
</p><p id="p_6">                                                  JUDGE
