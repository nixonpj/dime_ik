  70.
27.01.2014</pre>
<p id="p_1">   aa.
</p><p id="p_2">                             CRR No. 91 of 2014

             In re : Simanta Bauri and others
                                                      ..... Petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Re : An application under <a href="/doc/1571667/" id="a_1">Section 401</a> of the Code of
                  Criminal Procedure, 1973.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             Mr. Sabir Ahmed
                                                     ..... For the Petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
                    The case of the petitioners is that they were wrongly

             convicted for commissioner of alleged offence under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">Sections

             341</a>/<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>/<a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and was sentenced

             to undergone rigorous imprisonment for one month each by

             judgment dated 2.3.2013 passed by the learned Additional Chief

             Judicial Magistrate, Bishnupur, District - Bankura.

</p><p id="p_6">                    It is further submitted that the learned Sessions Judge

             also confirmed said judgment of conviction of sentence on wrong

             premises by an order dated 19.9.2013 passed in Criminal

             Appeal No. 11 of 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                    The petitioner is directed to serve a copy of the

             application to the opposite party no. 1/State through the office

             of the learned Public Prosecutor, High Court, Calcutta and also

             to opposite party no. 2       by registered/speed post with

             acknowledgement due intimating that the matter will appear in

             the monthly combined list of March 2014 under the heading
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                         2</span>


'Contested Application' and to file an affidavit of service on the

next date of hearing.
</p>
<p id="p_8">       In view of the undertaking that the petitioner no. 3 will

surrender before the learned court below within 2 weeks from

this date, the impugned order of conviction and sentence is

hereby stayed till 31st March 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_9">       Parties are at liberty to pray for extension, modification

and/or vacating the order of stay with notice to the other side.

</p><p id="p_10">       Let entire lower court records, namely, the records of

criminal appeal being Criminal Appeal No. 11 of 2013 and

criminal case being G.R. Case No. 220 of 2008 be called for from

the court below. The office to do needful.




</p><p id="p_11">                                 ( Tarun Kumar Gupta, J. )
 </p>
