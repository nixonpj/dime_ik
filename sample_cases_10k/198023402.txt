	 VIRABHAI RAMBHAI RATHODV/SSTATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	 
	

 
 


	 


	R/CR.MA/3014/2013
	                                                                    
	                           ORDER

 
	  
	  
		 
			 

IN
			THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
		
	

 


CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR REGULAR BAIL) NO. 3014 of 2013
 

================================================================
 


VIRABHAI RAMBHAI RATHOD  &amp;
 2....Applicant(s)
 


Versus
 


STATE OF
GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
 

================================================================
 

Appearance:
 

MR
PRAVIN GONDALIYA, ADVOCATE for the Applicants
 


Mr.
Maulik Nanavati, APP, for respondent
 

================================================================
 


	 
		  
		 
		  
			 
				 

CORAM:
				
				
			
			 
				 

HONOURABLE
				MR.JUSTICE ANANT S. DAVE
			
		
	

 


 

 


Date : 05/03/2013
 


 

 


ORAL ORDER</pre>
 

 


 


<p id="p_1">	This
application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal
Procedure in connection with first information report registered at
C.R.No.I-19 of 2013  with Kodinar police station for the offences
punishable under <a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_2">Sections 304B</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p> 

<p id="p_2">	Learned
counsel for the applicants submits that the applicants are
father-in-law, mother-in-law and brother-in-law of the deceased.
That, substantially allegations are against the husband about mental
and physical cruelty and in the year 2009 an agreement was reached
between the husband and the deceased to which applicant Nos. 1, 2 and
3 were signatories and witnesses. So far as the allegations of mental
and physical cruelty and demand of dowry are concerned, nothing
specifically is stated against the applicants who stay separately but
in the nearby locality in a small village.
</p> 

<p id="p_3">	The
learned Additional Public Prosecutor has opposed grant of bail
looking to the nature and gravity of offence.
</p> 

<p id="p_4">	In
the facts and circumstances of the case as recorded above, and
considering the role  attributed to the applicants,   without
discussing the evidence in detail, prima facie, this Court is of the
opinion that this is a fit case to exercise the discretion to enlarge
the applicants on bail.
</p> 

<p id="p_5">	The
parties do not press for further reasoned order.
</p> 

<p id="p_6">	In
the facts and circumstances of the case, the application is allowed
and the applicants are ordered to be released on bail in connection
with  first information report registered at C.R.No.I-19 of 2013 
with Kodinar police station  on their executing a bond of Rs.10,000/-
(Rupees ten thousand only) each with one surety of the like amount to
the satisfaction of the trial court and subject to the conditions
that they shall
 

     
i.	not take undue
advantage of their liberty or misuse their liberty;
</p> 
	  

<p id="p_7">not
	act in a manner injuries to the interest of the prosecution;
</p>	  

<p id="p_8">surrender
	their passports, if any, to the lower court within a week;
</p>	  

<p id="p_9">not
	leave the State of Gujarat without prior permission of the Sessions
	Judge concerned;
</p>	  

<p id="p_10">mark
	their presence at the concerned police station on the first Sunday
	of every month between 10 a.m. And 3 p.m for six months only.
</p>	  

<p id="p_11">furnish
	the present  address of their residence to the I.O. and also to the
	Court at the time of execution of the bond and shall not change the
	residence without prior permission of this Court;
</p>
 

<p id="p_12">	The
Authorities will release the applicants only if they are not required
in connection with any other offence for the time being. If breach of
any of the above conditions is committed, the Sessions Judge
concerned will be free to issue warrant or take appropriate action in
the matter. Bail bond to be executed before the lower court having
jurisdiction to try the case.  It will be open for the concerned
Court to delete, modify and/or relax any of the above conditions in
accordance with law. At the trial, the trial court shall not be
influenced by the observations of preliminary nature, qua the
evidence at this stage, made by this Court while enlarging the
applicants on bail.
</p> 

<p id="p_13">	Rule
is made absolute to the aforesaid extent. D.S. Permitted.
</p> 

<p id="p_14">(Anant
S. Dave, J.)
 

(swamy)
