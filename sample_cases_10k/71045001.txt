<div class="doc_title">Hardas vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 20 December, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                             CRA-910-2014
                   (HARDAS Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


20-12-2016

Shri Aseem Kumar Dixit, learned counsel for the appellants.

Shri R.N. Yadav, learned Panel Lawyer for the respondent-State.</pre>

<p id="p_1">An escort comprising Head Constable-318 Ayub Khan and
 Constable-538 Ajay Singh has produced appellant Kishori @ Kissu
 before this court in custody from the Sub-Jail Banda, district Sagar in
 compliance with the production warrant issued by this court. He is duly
 identified by his counsel. His presence is marked.
As per the record and the statement made by Shri Assem Kumar Dixit,
 learned counsel, appellant Kishori @ Kissu is on bail in the present
 case and that he has been under trial prisoner in another case.
In view of the above, a note be made on his production warrant that he
 is on bail in the present case. It is also mentioned on it that if he is not
 released in the concerned case, then he be produced before this court
 on 17.04.2017. Appellant Kishori @ Kissu is made to understand that if
 he is released on bail, then he shall mark his presence before the
 Registry of this court on 17.04.2017.
</p><p id="p_2">Appellant Kishori @ Kissu be sent back with the same escort.
A typed copy of this order-sheet be given to the escort for official
 purpose.
</p><p id="p_3">List this appeal with connected criminal appeals for analogous final
 hearing in due course.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                (RAJENDRA MAHAJAN)
                                                       JUDGE
 ac/-
