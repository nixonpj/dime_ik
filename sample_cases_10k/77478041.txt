 WP(C).No. 36643 of 2018             2

                                   JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                  This writ petition is filed by the petitioner

        seeking the following reliefs:-



</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                  "i) To issue a writ in the nature of mandamus or
                  any other appropriate writ or order or direction
                  commanding the respondent to issue a residential
                  certificate as applied through Ext.P3 application
                  to the petitioner within a time frame to be fixed
                  by this Hon'ble Court.
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_2">                  ii) To grant cost of this case from the respondent


</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                  iii) To mould and grant such other reliefs, as
                  this Hon'ble court shall deem just and proper in
                  the interest of justice, including costs."
</blockquote>


<p id="p_2">        2.        The grievance of the petitioner is that, even

        though Ext.P3 application is submitted on 10.10.2018,

        seeking    residential     certificate,     the   same   is   not

        considered     by    the   Secretary   of     the   respondent

        Panchayat      and      therefore,     seeks        appropriate

        directions.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">        3.    Having heard learned counsel for the petitioner,

        and in view of the limited nature of order I propose

        to pass, notice to the respondent is dispensed with.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> WP(C).No. 36643 of 2018         3</span>

        There will be a direction to the Secretary of the

        respondent Panchayat to consider Ext.P3 application

        in accordance with law and attain finality at the

        earliest, and at any rate, within a month from the

        date of receipt of a copy of this jdugment, after

        providing opportunity of hearing to the petitioner or

        any other interested persons.



</p><p id="p_5">              The writ petition is disposed of, accordingly.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                      Sd/-
</p>
