<div class="doc_title">Srimati Rampari Devi vs State Of Bihar &amp; Ors. on 23 September, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              Civil Review No. 144 of 2010
                                               In
                   (CIVIL WRIT JURISDICTION CASE NO. 14892/2008)
                                   Srimati Rampari Devi
                                            Versus
                                 State Of Bihar &amp; Ors.
                                 ----------------------------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">04.   23.09.2011             This review application has been filed on

                   behalf of Srimati Rampari Devi, the then Mukhiya

                   of   Gram     Panchayat      Raj   Bilaspur,   P.S.   -

                   Bhagwanpur, District - Siwan for review of the

                   order, dated 04.03.2010, passed in C.W.J.C. No.

                   14892 of 2008, in which review petitioner had

                   been impleaded as a party Respondent No. 6.

</p><p id="p_2">                             The Writ petitioner, Bidyawati Kumari, in

                   the cause title of the writ petition had given wrong

                   address     of   all   the   respondents,      including

                   Respondent No. 6. The name of the district in case

                   of all respondents had been mentioned as Saran

                   at Chapra in place of Siwan, though respondents

                   belong to Siwan District.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             The review petitioner's grievance is that

                   on account of wrong address, in the Writ

                   Application, no opportunity could be availed by the

                   then Mukhiya of Gram Panchayat Bilaspur,

                   Respondent No. 6 and as such relevant facts

                   could not be brought before this Court. Petitioner

                   could get an order passed in her favour in absence
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                         2</span>




      of the concerned respondents, including the

      review petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">               Prayer of the review petitioner is for

      review of the order, dated 04.03.2010, passed in

      C.W.J.C. No. 14892 of 2009, whereby Mukhiya

      and Panchayat Secretary of Panchayat Raj

      Bilaspur in the district of Saran at Chapra have

      been directed to make payment of entire arrears of

      salary to the writ petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">               Considering the fact that review petitioner

      could not get any opportunity to bring all relevant

      fact before the Writ Court, the Order, dated

      04.03.2010, passed in C.W.J.C. No. 14892 of

      2008 is recalled. Counsel for the opposite party

      (writ petitioner) is present in Court and order is

      passed in his presence.
</p>
<p id="p_6">               The Writ Application is directed to be

      placed for admission and proper adjudication of

      this case, before appropriate Bench, obtaining the

      permission of Hon'ble the Chief Justice.




</p><pre id="pre_2">SKM                                    (Mridula Mishra, J.)
 </pre>
