<p id="p_1">                                Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the

            Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking anticipatory bail in FIR

            No. 132 dated 16.8.2014 under <a href="/doc/886598/" id="a_2">Section 365</a>, <a href="/doc/1030257/" id="a_3">388</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_4">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120-B</a> of

            the Indian Penal Code, 1860 and <a href="/doc/114117546/" id="a_6">Section 3</a>/<a href="/doc/91704683/" id="a_7">4</a> of the Immoral Traffic

            (Prevention) Act, 1956, registered at Police Station City-1 Abohar,

            District Fazilka.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Prosecution story, in brief, is that on 15.8.2014, at

            about 2.00 P.M., complainant had gone to Sardarpura to meet his

            friend Ramdarshan. When the complainant was standing near

            Malout Chowk to board a bus, a Verna car without any registration

            number, came there. The car was being driven by Sarabjeet Singh

            alias Kala. Jasvir Kaur @ Sonu was sitting beside Sarabjeet Singh.

            On the rear seat of the car, Sandeep Singh and Kamal were sitting.

            Sarabjeet Singh and Sandeep Singh forcibly made the complainant

            to sit in the said car.            Complainant was taken to the house of
