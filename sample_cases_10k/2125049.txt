                                   O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">            Dated this the 27th day of November 2019

       The petitioner is the 2nd accused in S.C.No.83/2018

and M.C.No.4/2019 on the file of the Assistant Sessions

Court, Tirur, for having allegedly committed the offences

punishable under Sections 143, 147, 148, 341, 323, 324,

308, 427 read with <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_1">Section 149</a> of the I.P.C.                                The parent

case was registered as S.C.No.337/2012 on the file of the

above      Court       and       vide     Annexure          A2      judgment       dated

13.02.2017 all the accused, except two, faced trial and

they     were     found      not       guilty        and    acquitted.            It     is

submitted       that       the   matter        has    been        settled    with       the

defacto complainants, respondents 2 and 3 herein, who

appeared through a Counsel, have filed affidavits stating

that the matter has been settled amicably and they have

no existing grievance against the petitioner.                                A report

to     that     effect       has        also    been        obtained        from        the

investigating             officer,        through           the     Senior        Public

Prosecutor.               Although      <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_2">Section        308</a>,        <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">I.P.C</a>.    is        also

included,       there       is    no    material           to    indicate     that      an

offence under <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_4">Section 308</a> is committed.                            The injuries are

not serious and no public interest is involved.
 Crl.M.C.No.8010 of 2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         3</span>


      Under the circumstances, the Crl.M.C. is allowed and

the       entire          proceedings         in         S.C.No.83/2018     and

M.C.No.4/2019         on    the   file       of    the     Assistant   Sessions

Court, Tirur, stands quashed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5">Section 482</a> of Cr.P.C.

and the petitioner is set at liberty.
