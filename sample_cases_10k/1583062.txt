<div class="doc_title">Gayani Paswan vs The State Of Bihar on 4 August, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                         IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Cr.Misc. No.25257 of 2011
                             Gayani Paswan son of Yadunandan Paswan
                                                Versus
                                        The State Of Bihar
                                              -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2.   4.8.2011                Heard learned Counsel for the petitioner and the

                State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                            The petitioner seeks bail in a case instituted for the

                offence under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_5">326</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_6">307</a> and <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_7">302</a>

                of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                            Considering that the petitioner is in custody since

                9.5.2011 and there is no specific overt-act alleged against him

                and he has fair antecedents, let the petitioner above named,

                be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 5,000/-(Five

                thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each or any

                other surety to be fixed by the court concerned to the

                satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Vaishali,

                Hazipur in connection with Bidupur P.S. Case No.192 of 2010,

                subject to the conditions (i) That one of the bailor shall be

                Mahesh Paswan, uncle of the petitioner and the other bailor

                will be a close relative of the petitioner who will give an

                affidavit giving genealogy as to how he is related with the

                petitioner. The bailor will undertake to furnish information to

                the Court about any change in address of the petitioner. (ii)

                That the affidavit shall clearly state that the petitioner is not an

                accused in any other case and if he is he shall not be

                released on bail, (iii) That the bailor shall also state on
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    -2-</span>




            affidavit that he will inform the court concerned if the petitioner

            is implicated in any other case of similar nature after his

            release in the present case and thereafter the court below will

            be at liberty to initiate the proceeding for cancellation of bail

            on ground of misuse, (iv) That the petitioner will give an

            undertaking that he will receive the police papers on the given

            date and be present on date fixed for charge and if he fails to

            do so on two given dates and delays the trial in any manner,

            his bail will be liable to be cancelled for reasons of misuse, (v)

            That the petitioner will be well represented on each date if he

            fails to do so on two consecutive dates, his bail will be liable to

            be cancelled.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


</p><pre id="pre_2">Narendra/                        ( Anjana Prakash, J. )
