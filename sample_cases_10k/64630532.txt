                   CRM No.19536 of 2016 in

Ashu @ Aas Mohd. &amp; others                    Vs.   State of Haryana

Present: Mr.Saleem Ahmed, Advocate
         for applicant/appellant No.1-Ashu @ Aas Mohd. in
         CRM No.19522 of 2016.

           Mr. Ashok Tyagi, Advocate
           for applicant/appellant No.8-Sagru in
           CRM No.19536 of 2016.

           Mr. Surender Singh Pannu, DAG, Haryana for the State.

           Mr.Anil Ghanghas, Advocate for
           Mr.Sunil Panwar, Advocate for the complainant.

<p id="p_1">          Learned counsel for the State has filed affidavits of Shri

Devi   Dayal,   Deputy Superintendent, District                Jail,   Gurgaon,

mentioning the periods of imprisonments undergone by the

applicants/appellants No.1 and 8 namely Ashu @ Aas Mohd. and

Sagru respectively. The same are taken on record.

</p><p id="p_2">          Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_3">          This order will dispose of CRM No.19522 of 2016 filed by

Ashu @ Aas Mohd.- applicant/appellant No.1 as also CRM

No.19536 of 2016 filed by Sagru- applicant/appellant No.8. Both

the respective applicants/appellants No.1 and 8 seek suspension

of their sentences of imprisonments during pendency of the

appeal.
</p>
<p id="p_4">          FIR in the case has been registered on the statement of

Salmuddin.      He stated that there was an old enmity between

them and Nanhe Khan, father of applicant/appellant No.1-Ashu @

Aas Mohd., as also Aslam Khan (appellant No.3), Rafik (appellant

No.4), Akram (appellant No.9), Rashid (appellant No.12) and

Azad (appellant No.13).       The said enmity was compromised in

the Panchayat about ten days earlier to the incident that had

occurred on 01.09.2009. The brother of the complainant namely

Saddam (PW-5) was standing in front of the house of his uncle

i.e. father's sister's husband namely Ali on 01.09.2009, then

Aslam (appellant No.3), Rafik (appellant No.4), Azad (appellant

No.13), Kalu (i.e. Ashu @ Aas Mohd.-applicant/appellant No.1),

Akram (appellant No.9), Aasiq, Rashid (appellant No.12), Sagru

(applicant/appellant    No.8),     Fakru,     Deenu     (appellant   No.6),

Arshad (appellant No.2), Israil (appellant No.11), Saddam,

Shehjad (appellant No.10) and Sajid (appellant No.5) armed with

deadly weapons like 'lathi', 'jaily', 'farsi' etc. came there and

proclaimed that they had quarrelled with them and got the

dispute settled by putting pressure on the 'baradari'. Now they

(assailants) would teach them a lesson. The complainant was

also present there and he asked them not to fight. It, however,

had no effect.      The assailants become furious then Aslam

(appellant No.3) inflicted a 'lathi' blow on the left shoulder of the

complainant. Rafik (appellant No.4) inflicted a 'lathi' blow on his

left arm.   Arshad (appellant No.2) inflicted a 'lathi' blow on his

ear pit and Rashid (appellant No.12) inflicted a 'lathi' blow on the

ankle of the left leg of the complainant.           In the incident apart

from the complainant, his brother Saddam, Mustaq, Jakir,

Rukmu, Shahud (PW-1) also suffered injuries.

         On the statement of Salmuddin initially FIR for the

offences under Sections 323, 324 read with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_1">Section 34</a> IPC was

registered. Later report of the doctor was received and <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_2">Section

325</a> as also <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_3">Section 307</a> IPC were added.
</p>
<p id="p_6">         The learned trial Court referred to the deposition of

Salmuddin     (PW-2)       and      noticed       the     role   of     the

applicants/appellants No.1 and 8. It is brought out that Kalu alias

Ashu (applicant/appellant No.1) grappled with Sahud (PW-1). The

accused caused injuries in the following manner; Nanhe Khan

inflicted a 'farsa' blow on the head of Sahud (PW-1).             Arshad

(appellant No.2) inflicted a 'lathi' blow (covered with an iron pole)

on the right eye of Sahud. Aslam (appellant No.3) inflicted a pipe

blow on both the wrists of Sahud. Sehjad (appellant No.10) and

Sajid (appellant No.5) inflicted fist blows; besides, kicked Sahud.

Aslam (appellant No.3) inflicted a 'lathi' blow on the left shoulder

of Sahud. Rafik (appellant No.4) inflicted a 'lathi' blow on the

left arm of Sahud. Arshad (appellant No.2) inflicted a 'lathi' blow

on the left temporal of Sahud. Rashid (appellant No.12) inflicted

a 'lathi' blow on the left ankle of Sahud.
</p>
<p id="p_7">         No injuries are attributed to applicants/appellants No.1

and 8- Ashu @ Aas Mohd. and Sagru on the person of injured

Sahud (PW-1).      Sahud (PW-1) also stated that accused also

caused injuries to Rukmuddin, Mustak, Saddam, Jakir and

Rahisan. Nanhe Khan is attributed a 'pharsa' blow on the head of

Sahud (PW-1) and on account of the said injury on the person of

</p>
<p id="p_8">          The applicants/appellants No.1 and 8 -               Ashu @ Aas

Mohd. and Sagru have been sentenced to undergo rigorous

imprisonment for a period of seven years; besides, pay a fine of

Rs.25000/- each and in default of payment of fine, the convict

committing      the    default   is      to    further   undergo   rigorous

imprisonment for three months for the offence under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">Section 307</a>

read with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">Section 34</a> IPC; besides, they have been sentenced to

varying terms for the offences under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_7">Section 323</a> read with

<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">Section 34</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_9">Section 324</a> read with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_10">Section 34</a> and <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_11">Section 325</a>

read with <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_12">Section 34</a> IPC.         All the sentences of imprisonment

have been ordered to run concurrently.
</p>
<p id="p_9">          In terms of the affidavits that have been filed in Court

today, the applicant/appellant No.1 - Ashu @ Aas Mohd. has

undergone actual imprisonment of two years, one month and

nineteen days as on 13.07.2016 out of the sentence of seven

years and applicant/appellnat No.8 - Sagru has undergone actual

imprisonment of one year and six months as on 13.07.2016 out

of the sentence of seven years. The sentences of imprisonments

of Fajru (appellant No.7) and Isrile (appellant No.11) have been

suspended by this Court vide order dated 03.02.2016 passed in

CRM No.39526 of 2015; besides, sentences of imprisonments of

Arshad (appellant No.2), Aslam Khan (appellant No.3), Sajid

(appellant No.5), Akram (appellant No.9), Shazad (appellant

No.10), Rashid (appellant No.12) and Azad (appellant No.13)

have been suspended by this Court vide order dated 14.06.2016

passed in CRM Nos.12832, 28677 of 2015; 7499, 5311 and 9477

of 2016. Therefore, it would be just and expedient in the facts and

circumstances to suspend the sentences of imprisonments of

applicant/appellant No.1-Ashu @ Aas Mohd. and applicant/appellant

No.8-Sagru respectively during pendency of the appeal.

</p><p id="p_10">            Accordingly, the criminal miscellaneous applications are

allowed and the sentences of imprisonments of the applicant/

appellant No.1-Ashu @ Aas Mohd. and applicant/appellant No.8-

Sagru shall, during pendency of the appeal remain suspended

subject to their furnishing personal bonds and surety each to the

satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Nuh.</p><pre id="pre_2">
