                              Criminal Miscellaneous No.4086 of 2015
                   Arising Out of PS.Case No. -82 Year- 2014 Thana -TARAPUR District- MUNGER
            ======================================================
            1. Shashi Kumar Son of Manoj Kumar Singh, Resident of Village -
            Mudheri, P.S. - Haveli Kharagpur, District - Munger.
            2. Diwakar Kumar Yadav Son of Brahmadeo Yadav Resident of Villae -
            Dulhar, P.S. - Asarganj, District - Munger.

                                                                           .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
            1. The State of Bihar

                                                             .... .... Opposite Party/s
            ======================================================
            Appearance :
            For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Jyoti Ranjan Jha
            For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Umesh Lal Verma (App)
            ======================================================
            CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
            SINGH
            ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   30-01-2015               Heard learned counsels for the petitioners and the

                 State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             The petitioners are apprehending arrest in a case

                 registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_1">Section 384</a> of

                 the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             It is alleged that through mobile extortion demand

                 of rupees one lac was made and life threatening was given to

                 the informant. Name of the petitioners sprang up in the

                 confession of co accused Mithun Paswan.

</p><p id="p_4">                             It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioners that the alleged mobile through which extortion
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4086 of 2015 (2) dt.30-01-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                           2/3</span>




                 demand was made is not registered in the name of the

                 petitioners. Statement to the aforesaid effect has been made

                 in paragraph 10 of the petition. More over, even assuming

                 the accusation, no offence under <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_2">section 384</a> IPC is made

                 out. Statement has been made in paragraph 3 of the petition

                 that the petitioners have no criminal antecedent.

</p><p id="p_5">                               Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above

                 named petitioners be released on anticipatory bail in the

                 event of arrest or surrender before the learned Court below

                 within a period of twelve weeks from today on furnishing

                 bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) each with two

                 sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned JM

                 Ist Class, Munger in connection with Tarapur P.S. Case No.

                 82 of 2014 subject to the conditions as laid down under

                 <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_3">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                               The bail bonds of the petitioners will be accepted

                 on filing of an affidavit by the petitioners before the learned

                 court below that they will regularly cooperate with the

                 investigation. The said affidavit will be transmitted by the

                 learned court below to the concerned I.O. Non cooperation

                 in investigation by the petitioners will give liberty to
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4086 of 2015 (2) dt.30-01-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                                    3/3</span>




                          prosecution to file application for cancellation of bail bonds

                          by learned court below.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">                                                 (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Anil/-
</p>
