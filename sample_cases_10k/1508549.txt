<p id="p_1">Respondent
	no.2-Additional Registrar (Appeals), Cooperative Societies,
	Gandhinagar, is directed to decide the matter afresh taking into
	consideration the observations made by this Court (Coram : Hon'ble
	Mr.Justice D.A. Mehta) in judgment dated 31st July 2007-,
	more particularly in paragraph nos.13 and 14 of the judgment, at the
	earliest, preferably by 30-8-08.
</p>	 


	  

<p id="p_2">At
	the request of Mr.B.S. Patel, learned advocate appearing for
	respondent no.4, the averments made in paragraph no.11 of the
	affidavit-in-reply filed by one Nita Chaudhari be also taken into
	consideration.  It will be in the fitness of things if respondent
	no.2 personally hears the representative of the petitioner and
	respondent no.4. 
</p>	
	  

<p id="p_3">Taking
	into consideration the nature of dispute between the parties, it
	will be an act of appreciation if respondent no.2 is able to find
	out an amicable solution to the problem by putting both the parties
	together and explaining them about co-existence. 
</p>	
	 


	  

<p id="p_4">At
	the request of Mr.B.S. Patel, learned advocate for respondent no.4,
	the present arrangement to continue till then. 
</p>	
	 


	  

<p id="p_5">A
	copy of this order be made available to Mr.A.A. Dave, learned
	Assistant Government Pleader, for its onward communication for
	compliance.
</p>	 


<p id="p_6">			The
	matter be listed for hearing on 04th August 2008. 
</p>
