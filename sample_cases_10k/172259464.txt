                                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

                                                    DATED : 18.03.2019

                                                          CORAM

                                    THE HONOURABLE Mr.JUSTICE G.K.ILANTHIRAIYAN

                                                  Crl.O.P.No.7123 of 2019

                     R.Muniraj                                                  ... Petitioner

                                                            Vs
                     The Inspector of Police,
                     Krishnagiri Taluk Police Station,
                     Krishnagiri Taluk,
                     Krishnagiri District.                                      ... Respondent

                     Prayer: Criminal Original Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of Criminal
                     Procedure Code, to direct the respondent authority to give Police protection
                     and permission to conduct Cultural Programme (Adal Padal Nigalchi) in the
                     event of Bethalapalli Village Arulmigu Sree Mandu Mariamman Temple Festival
                     at Krishnagiri Taluk &amp; District on 23.03.2019 between 06.00 p.m. to 11.00 p.m
                     by considering the petitioner's representation dated 12.03.2019.
                                         For Petitioner     :      Mr.A.Sathishkumar

                                         For Respondent     :      Mr.C.Raghavan
                                                                   Government Advocate [Crl. Side]

                                                          ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                           Seeking permission to conduct cultural programme in the event of

                     Bethalapalli Village Arulmigu Sree Mandu Mariamman Temple Festival at

                     Krishnagiri District, the petitioner made a representation dated 12.03.2019 to

                     the respondent. As the same has not been considered, the present Criminal


http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                             2</span>

                     Original petition has been filed.
</p>



<p id="p_2">                             2. Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the

                     learned Government Advocate [Crl. Side] appearing for the respondent.
</p>



<p id="p_3">                              3. The learned Government Advocate, on instructions, would submit

                     that there is likelihood of arising law and order problem.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                              4. A mere apprehension cannot be a ground to reject the

                     representation. There is absolutely no material before this Court to hold that

                     there will be a law and order problem in the event of permission being granted.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                     In such view of the matter, the petitioner is permitted to conduct cultural

                     programme in the event of Bethalapalli Village Arulmigu Sree Mandu Mariamman

                     Temple Festival at Krishnagiri District however with the following conditions:-
</p>


<p id="p_6">                              (a) The cultural programme in connection with the event of
                     Bethalapalli Village Arulmigu Sree Mandu Mariamman Temple Festival at
                     Krishnagiri District shall be conducted on 23.03.2019 between 06.00 p.m. and
                     10.00 p.m.
</p><p id="p_7">                              (b)There should not be any kind of obscene dance or vulgar dialogues
                     during the performance, by anyone of the participants.
</p><p id="p_8">                              (c) Double meaning songs should not be played so as to spoil the minds
                     of students and youths.
</p>

<p id="p_9">http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                               3</span>

</p><p id="p_10">                                (d) No dance or songs, touching upon any political party or religion or
                     community or caste shall be played.
</p><p id="p_11">                                (e) No flex boards in support of any political party or communal leader,
                     shall be erected at the premises of the programme.
</p><p id="p_12">                                (f) The function shall not affect either religious or communal harmony
                     and shall be conducted without any discrimination based on caste.
</p><p id="p_13">                                (g) If there is violation of any one of the conditions imposed, the
                     concerned police officer is at liberty to take necessary action, as per law and
                     stop such performance forthwith; and
</p><p id="p_14">                                (h) Similarly, the police is directed to stop the dance programme, if it
                     is played beyond the permitted time limit.
</p><p id="p_15">                                (i) The respondent is directed to issue necessary permission,
                     incorporating the above conditions.
</p>

<p id="p_16">                                5. With the above directions, this Criminal Original Petition stands

                     allowed.
</p>
