<div class="doc_title">Shahid Qamar Alias Kamar vs The State Of Jharkhand And Anr on 31 July, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI

                         A. B.A. No. 2112 of 2013

           Shahid Qamar @ Kamar                   .........Petitioner
                        Vs.
           The State of Jharkhand &amp; Anr........Opposite parties

CORAM:     HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PRASHANT KUMAR

           For the Petitioner:             Mr. Shekhar Prasad Sinha
           For the State:                  Mr. Tapas Roy ,APP
           For the O.P. No. 2:             Mr. K.S. Nanda

3/31.07.2013</pre><p id="p_1">:     Anticipatory bail application filed by petitioner Shahid
     Qamar @ Kamar is moved by Sri Shekhar Prasad Sinha and opposed
     by Sri Tapas Roy, learned Additional P.P. and Sri K.S. Nanda, learned
     counsel for the O.P. No. 2.
</p><p id="p_2">                  At the out set, Sri Shekhar Prasad Sinha submits that he is
     ready to keep the complainant ( O.P. No. 2) as his wife with all honour
     and dignity and for that purpose he is ready to give written
     undertaking in the court below.
</p><p id="p_3">                  Learned counsel for the O.P. No. 2 submits that the
     complainant is also ready to reside with the petitioner            in his joint
     family as his wife provided he give undertaking that he will keep her
     with all respect and dignity and will not torture her in future for
     demand of dowry.
</p><p id="p_4">                  In view of the aforesaid facts and circumstance, I allow
     this application and direct the petitioner to surrender in the court
     below on 14.08.2013, on that date the O.P. No. 2 shall also remain
     present in the court below, and in that event           the court below is
     directed to enlarge the petitioner, above named, on bail on furnishing
     bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-(Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like
     amount each to the satisfaction of learned S.D.J.M., Koderma in
     connection with Complaint Case No. 231 of 2012, subject to the
     condition as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_1">section 438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_5">                   The court below is further directed to send the O.P. No. 2
     with the petitioner after taking a written undertaking that he will not
     torture her in future and keep her as his wife with all respect and
     dignity. If in future O.P. No. 2 has any complaint against the behaviour
     of petitioner, she is entitled to file an application in the court below in
     that respect and if such application is filed, the court below will
     enquire into the matter and pass order including the order for
     cancellation of bail of petitioner.
</p>



<p id="p_6">                                                ( Prashant Kumar,J.)

     Sharda/-
</p>
