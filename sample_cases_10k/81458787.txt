                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.17947 of 2013
                  ======================================================
                  1. Shashi Bhushan Jha S/O Late Raj Kumar Jha Resident Of Village-
                  Goraul, P.O- Goraul, District- Vaishali.

                                                                    .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                  1. The State Of Bihar Through Vigilance.

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s

                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                  SINGH
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">3    04-07-2013               Heard learned       counsel for the petitioner and the
                    State.
</p><p id="p_2">                              Petitioner is languishing in            custody since
                    11.01.2013 in a case registered for the offence punishable
                    under sections 7, 13(2) r/w 13(1) (d) of the <a href="/doc/1331755/" id="a_1">P.C. Act</a> .
</p><p id="p_3">                              It is alleged that the petitioner being clerk        in the
                    school was trapped while accepting bribe of Rs. 2500/- for
                    payment of G.P.F. amount of the complainant.
</p><p id="p_4">                                 Considering the period of custody of the
                      petitioner, let the above named petitioner be released on
                      bail   on furnishing         bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten
                      thousand) with two sureties of like amount        each to the
                      satisfaction of Special Judge, Vigilance (Trap) Patna in
                      connection      with    Spl Case No. 2/13     arising      out of
                      Vigilance P.S. Case No.02/2013.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                        (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
M.Rahman/-
</p>
