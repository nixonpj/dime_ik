<p id="p_1">11   20-11-2018                     Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that the

                   present case is squarely covered by the order dated 17.01.2017

                   passed in C.W.J.C. No.21025 of 2011 (<a href="/doc/155664644/" id="a_1">Shailendra Roy vs. The
           Patna High</a> Court CWJC No.12977 of 2014(11) dt.20-11-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                      2/2</span>




                   State of Bihar and Ors.) and other analogous cases, which has

                   been affirmed upto the Hon'ble Supreme Court in Special Leave

                   to Appeal (C) No.21961 of 2017.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   In such view of the matter, let the respondent

                   authorities examine the case of the present petitioners vis-a-vis

                   the case of the petitioners of C.W.J.C. No.21025 of 2011 and

                   other analogous cases and if it is found that the facts, prayer and

                   law of all the cases are identical, in such circumstance, the

                   present petitioners will be given the same benefit as has been

                   given to the petitioners of C.W.J.C. No.21025 of 2011 and other

                   analogous cases.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   With the aforesaid observations and directions, this

                   writ petition is disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                                                            (Shivaji Pandey, J)
