                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.20881 of 2012
                 ======================================================
                 Vijai Choudhary
                                                                      .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                 The State Of Bihar
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                                           ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   18-07-2012                  Petitioner is apprehending his arrest in a case

                 registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/285163/" id="a_1">Sections 273</a> of

                 the I.P.C. and 47(a) of the <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_2">Excise Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The recovery is of country made liquor from one

                 Arvind Kumar who disclosed that he was carrying the illicit

                 liquor to deliver it in the house of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_3">                                It    is   submitted   by   learned    counsel       for    the

                 petitioner that recovery is not from the petitioner. Moreover the

                 accusation was found false during investigation, but differing

                 with the same, cognizance has been taken.

</p><p id="p_4">                               Considering the same, let the petitioner namely

                 Vijai Choudhary, in the event of his arrest or surrender before

                 the Court below within a period of 12 weeks from today, be

                 released on anticipatory bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                 10,000/-(ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.20881 of 2012 (2) dt.18-07-2012</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                     each to the satisfaction of learned A.C.J.M., Hilsa, Nalanda in

                     connection with Ekangar Sarai P.S. Case No. 66 of 2011.




</p><p id="p_5">                                               (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
