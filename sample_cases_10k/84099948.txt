                                             CHANDIGARH

                                                        CWP No. 25665 of 2013 (O&amp;M)
                                                        Date of Decision: 22.11.2013.

            Amrit Kaur &amp; others                                           --Petitioners

                                           Versus

            State of Punjab &amp; others                                      --Respondents

            CORAM:- HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.

            Present:-          Mr. Vikas Chatrath, Advocate for the petitioners.

                               ***
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.J
                               The petitioners, who retired from the Education Department,
            State of Punjab are seeking directions to the respondent-authorities to
            permit them to re-exercise their option for the grant of revised pay scales
            w.e.f. 01.01.1986 in the light of notification/circular dated 24.12.1992 at
            Annexure P-2.
</p><p id="p_2">                               Counsel submits that in an identical issue, directions have
            already been issued by this Court in judgment dated 25.02.2010 in CWP No.
            3285 of 2010 titled as Chaman Lal and others Vs. State of Punjab and
            others, at Annexure P-7 and which was further clarified vide order dated
            01.03.2013 passed in CM No. 2866 of 2013.
</p><p id="p_3">                               The present writ petition is disposed of with a direction to
            respondents No. 2 and 3/competent authority to take a final decision on the
            legal notice dated 9.9.2013 (Annexure P-6) strictly in accordance with law
            and in the light of judgment rendered in Chaman Lal's case (supra) and
            further clarified by this Court on 01.03.2013. A speaking order in this
            regard be passed within a period of 4 months from the date of receipt of a
            certified copy of this order.
</p><p id="p_4">                               Writ petition disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                        (TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA)
                                                                  JUDGE
            November 22, 2013.
