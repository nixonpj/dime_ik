                           B.A. No. 9030 of 2019
                                     ------
</pre><p id="p_1">        Murti Devi              .... ....   .... Petitioner
                                     Versus
        The State of Jharkhand                  ....  .... Opposite Party
</p><p id="p_2">                                     ------
</p><p id="p_3">     CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY
</p><p id="p_4">                                     ------
</p><p id="p_5">        For the Petitioner      :    Mr. Sudhansu Kumar Deo, Advocate
        For the State           :    Mr. Azeemuddin, A.P.P.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                          ------
</p><p id="p_7">02/21.10.2019      Heard the parties.
</p><p id="p_8">                   Petitioner is accused in connection with Devipur P.S. Case No. 94 of
           2019.
</p><p id="p_9">                   It has been alleged that the daughter of the informant was being
           enticed away by the accused but, all were apprehended. In the statement of
           the victim recorded under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 164</a> Cr.P.C. she has stated that she was
           forcibly made to sit on the bike by Shambhu Rana and the petitioner had
           also closed her mouth. The petitioner appears to be a lady and she is in
           custody since 02.06.2019.
</p><p id="p_10">                   Regard being had to the aforesaid facts, the petitioner, named
           above, is directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of
           Rs.10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount
           each to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Deoghar, in
           connection with Devipur P.S. Case No. 94 of 2019.
</p>

<p id="p_11">                                                   (Rongon Mukhopadhyay, J.)
    VK
