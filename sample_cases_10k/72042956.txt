                                              AT CHANDIGARH

                                                   CRM-M-33840 of 2014
                                                   Date of decision:October 29, 2014

            Charanjit Singh
                                                                                   .... Petitioner
                                                      Versus

            State of Punjab.

                                                                                 .... Respondent</pre>

<p id="p_1">            CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JASPAL SINGH

            Present:            Mr. Vikram Anand, Advocate
                                for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Mr. Gurinderjit Singh, DAG, Punjab

                                None for the complainant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            Jaspal Singh, J.(Oral)

                                The only allegation against the petitioner is that he caught hold

            the complainant from his neck and got him descended from motorcycle.

            Neither the petitioner was armed with any weapon nor any injury is

            attributed to him. Moreover, he has also joined the investigation.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Learned State counsel, on instructions from ASI Lakhwinder

            Singh, has stated at the bar that petitioner has joined the investigation in

            compliance of order dated September 29, 2014 and he is not required for

            further investigation/interrogation.

</p><p id="p_5">                                So, in view of the above, order dated September 29, 2014

            passed by this Court, is hereby made absolute. However, petitioner shall

            continue to abide by the conditions envisaged under Section 438 (2)

            <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                Disposed of.
</p>
