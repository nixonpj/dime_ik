03.09.2013
Item No.41
   SB

                                     W.P. 26165 (W) of 2013


                 Ms. Husn Ara Begum...........for the petitioner.



                 None appears for the State despite service. Affidavit of service filed

             in Court today shall be retained with the records.

                  The   petitioner     is   aggrieved   because   his   application   dated

             15.06.2012 for contract carriage permit on the route Pakriguri to

             Maynabari via Jarai, Volka, KMG, Chepani has not been considered and

             disposed of by the Regional Transport Authority, Jalpaiguri, respondent</pre>

<p id="p_1">             no.2.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                 Having regard to the nature of grievance expressed by the petitioner,

             I find no reason to keep the writ petition pending.         The same stands

             disposed of with a direction upon the respondent no.2 to consider and

             dispose of the petitioner's application within four months from date of

             receipt of a copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                 Needless to observe, if the petitioner's application is rejected, the

             order must have the support of reasons.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                 On the contrary, if the prayer of the petitioner deserves acceptance,

             necessary follow up steps shall be taken without any further delay.

                 There shall be no order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                 Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be

             furnished to the parties as early as possible.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2</span>


    (DIPANKAR DATTA, J.)
 </p>
