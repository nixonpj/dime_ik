<div class="doc_title">Vishnubhai Motiram Patel vs State Of Gujarat &amp; on 22 December, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">      R/SCR.A/5559/2014                                    ORDER




           IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD


     SPECIAL CRIMINAL APPLICATION (DIRECTION) NO. 5559 of 2014

================================================================
                VISHNUBHAI MOTIRAM PATEL....Applicant(s)
                               Versus
                 STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
MR DILIP B RANA, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
================================================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE J.B.PARDIWALA

                            Date : 22/12/2014


                             ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">1.     By this writ application under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">section 482</a> of the Code of
Criminal Procedure, 1973 the petitioner has a grievance to
redress as regards the inaction on the part of the police
authorities in not registering the first information report
pursuant to a complaint lodged by him in writing dated
29.9.2014 addressed to the D.S.P, Mehsana of the offence
punishable under <a href="/doc/1317063/" id="a_2">sections 465</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_4">471</a>, <a href="/doc/112749/" id="a_5">114</a> and <a href="/doc/1128948/" id="a_6">120(B)</a> of
the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.     The Police Inspector of the Unza Police Station, Mehsana
is directed to look into the complaint lodged by the petitioner
herein (Annexure-A to this petition) and consider whether the
same discloses commission of any cognizable offence or not.
Upon perusal of the complaint and other materials, if any, the
Police Inspector is of the view that the same discloses


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                Page 1 of 2</span>
         R/SCR.A/5559/2014                                        ORDER


commission of a cognizable offence, then he shall proceed to
register the first information report forthwith. However, for any
reason, if the Police Inspector is of the view that the complaint
fails to disclose commission of any cognizable offence, then in
such circumstances, he shall inform the petitioner in writing by
assigning reasons in brief within a period of fortnight from
today. I clarify that I have otherwise not gone into the merits of
the matter.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.      With      the       above   observations   and    directions,    this
application is disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_4">        Direct service is permitted.
</p>



<p id="p_5">                                                         (J.B.PARDIWALA, J.)
Vahid
