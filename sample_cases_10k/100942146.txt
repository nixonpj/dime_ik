<p id="p_1">       The   appellant   is   before   this   Court   seeking

enhancement of compensation of the market value as

against the sum awarded by the Reference Court by the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                       :3:</span>



judgment dated 31.3.2000 passed in LAC No.88/2000, on

the file of the Civil Judge &amp; Addl. CJM at Koppal.

</p><p id="p_2">2.   The    land        of    the     appellant   bearing   Sy.No.24

measuring 6.03 guntas of Arakere village, Yelburga taluk

has been acquired for the Hirehalla submergence area

under the preliminary Notification dated 12.1.1995 and

the award came to be passed on 5.1.1998 awarding

Rs.21,500/- per acre.           Aggrieved by the same, on the

reference application made by the applicant, the matter

was referred to the Reference Court, whereby the Reference

Court enhanced the compensation amount to Rs.71,500/-

per acre with statutory benefits.            Being dissatisfied, the

appellant   is     in        appeal     seeking   enhancement     of

compensation.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">3.   Sri. Laxman T. Mantagani, learned counsel appearing

for the appellants placing reliance on the judgment of this

Court in MFA No.2095/2007 (DD 7.9.2010) would contend

that this Court in identical circumstances in respect of

very same notification has enhanced the market value to
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                :4:</span>



Rs.1,00,000/- per acre and the same can be taken as

guiding factor to fix the market value in the present case.



</p><p id="p_5">4.      Learned counsel appearing for the respondents do

not dispute the same.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">5.      Considering the submissions made by the learned

counsel for the parties and perusing the material on

record, it emerges that this Court for similarly situated

lands acquired for the same project has fixed the market

value    at   Rs.1,00,000/-   per    acre   with   proportionate

statutory benefits and costs considering the preliminary

notification dated 28.03.1996.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">     Since the present lands were acquired vide Preliminary

Notification dated 12.1.1995, the market value in the

present case has to be fixed at Rs.95,000/- with de-

escalation at 5% per year. Thus, the appellant shall be

entitled to the market value of Rs.95,000/- per acre with

the    statutory benefits and       the   costs.   However,   the

appellants shall not be entitled to the interest for the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                              :5:</span>



delayed period of 2488 days in filing the appeal before this

Court.
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">6.    Accordingly, this appeal is allowed in terms of the

above.
</p>
