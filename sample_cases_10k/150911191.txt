<pre id="pre_1">          THE HON'BLE SRI JUSTICE T.SUNIL CHOWDARY

                    C.R.P.No.2686 of 2016

ORDER:
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     This revision petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the

Constitution of India assailing the order dated 25.04.2016

passed in I.A.No.123 of 2016 in O.S.No.135 of 2009 on the

file of the Court of V Additional District Judge, Tirupathi.


</p><p id="p_2">2.   As per the note furnished by the Registry, the trial

Court disposed of the suit on 31.07.2017.        In view of the

disposal of the main suit, the purpose of filing this revision

became infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">3.   Hence, the C.R.P. is dismissed as infructuous.            As a

sequel, miscellaneous petitions pending, if any, shall stand

closed.
</p>

                                         T.SUNIL CHOWDARY, J
