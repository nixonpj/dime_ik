<div class="doc_title">Rahul Thakur vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 15 May, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                       1




      THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
              MCRC No.18059 / 2018
                        (Rahul Thakur V/s State of M.P.)
Jabalpur,
Dated:15.05.2018

         Shri Surendra Rajak, learned counsel for the applicant.
         Shri Sharad Sharma, learned Government Advocate for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">         This is first application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code
of Criminal Procedure filed on behalf of the applicant in connection
with Crime No.253/2018 registered by Police Station- Ranjhi,
District Jabalpur (M.P.) for the offence under <a href="/doc/134179606/" id="a_2">Section 34(2)</a> of the
Excise Act. The applicant is in jail since 22.04.2018.
</p><p id="p_2">         Learned counsel for the applicant submits that the applicant has
not commited any crime and has been falsely implicated in the case.
He prays for grant of bail to the applicant.
</p><p id="p_3">         Learned G.A. for the respondent/ State opposes the bail
application.
</p><p id="p_4">         After hearing learned counsel for the parties and going through
the facts and circumstances of present case, in the considered opinion
of this Court, no case for grant of bail is made out by the applicant.
</p><p id="p_5">         Accordingly, the bail application filed by the applicant Rahul
Thakur is hereby dismissed.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                    (Subodh Abhyankar)
                                                         Judge
Vikram

VIKRAM SINGH

17:01:59 +05'30'
