<div class="doc_title">Lakhan Alias Lakhan Singh vs The State Of Jharkhand on 22 October, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">            IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                         B. A. No. 7073 of 2016
                                       ---
            Lakhan @ Lakhan Singh            ...     ...      Petitioner
                                      Versus
            The State of Jharkhand           ...     ...      Opp. Party
                                       ---
      CORAM       : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY
                                      ---
            For the Petitioner        : Mr. A. K. Sahani, Advocate
            For the Opp. Party        : Mr. Tapas Roy, A.P.P.
                                      ---
06/22.10.2016</pre><p id="p_1">     Heard Mr. A. K. Sahani, learned counsel for the petitioner and
           Mr. Tapas Roy, learned A.P.P. for the State.
</p><p id="p_2">                 Earlier the prayer for bail of the petitioner was rejected on
           18.08.2015 in B. A. No. 5928 of 2015.
</p><p id="p_3">                 It appears from the status report that three witnesses have
           already been examined and approximate time which would require for
           the learned trial court to conclude the trial is for a period of six months.
           The complicity of the petitioner is apparent and pursuant to the
           confessional statement Bhujali which was used in the murder of the
           deceased was recovered. The petitioner is in custody since 02.02.2015.
</p><p id="p_4">                 In view of the fact that the trial is already in progress, I am not
           inclined to grant bail to the petitioner which is accordingly rejected.
</p><p id="p_5">                 However, since the petitioner is in custody for more than one
           and half years, the learned trial court is directed to expedite the trial by
           examining the rest of the witnesses and conclude the same within a
           period of four months from the date of receipt/production of a copy of
           this order.
</p><p id="p_6">                                                          (R. Mukhopadhyay, J.)




Umesh/-
</p>
