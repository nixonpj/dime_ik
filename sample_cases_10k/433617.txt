                                  -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2     1.12.2010                   Heard learned counsel for the parties.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  Order of dismissal contained in annexure-1 is

                  totally legal and valid. Convicted person has no right to continue

                  in service. Mere filing of appeal where the conviction has not been

                  stayed by the High Court does not make out a case to give any

                  favourable order till the petitioner is exonerated by the appellate

                  court. There is no question of putting him back in service till the

                  order of conviction is set aside by the appellate court or the order

                  of conviction is stayed by the superior court.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  This writ application has no merit and it is

                  dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><pre id="pre_2">RPS                              (Ajay Kumar Tripathi,J.)
