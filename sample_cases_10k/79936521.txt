                                                            CRM No. 814 of 2011


           In Re: Manoj Sarkar &amp; Ors.                    -Petitioners

           Mr. R.R. Biswas
           Ms. M. Gomes                  -For the petitioners

           Mr. S. Pachhal               -For the State


</pre><p id="p_1">           Re : Application for Anticipatory Bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 27.1.2011.
</p>

<p id="p_2">                   This is an application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_2">Section 438</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure on behalf of

           the petitioners who apprehend arrest in connection with Jalangi P.S. Case No. 776/10 dated 11.12.10 under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_3">Sections

           379</a>/<a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_4">411</a>/<a href="/doc/1976294/" id="a_5">413</a>/<a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_6">414</a>/<a href="/doc/261128/" id="a_7">274</a>/<a href="/doc/1565155/" id="a_8">275</a>/<a href="/doc/1049807/" id="a_9">276</a>/<a href="/doc/513074/" id="a_10">109</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_11">34</a> I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                   We have heard the submissions of the learned Advocates for the petitioners and for the State. We have

           perused the materials produced before us in the case diary and we are of the considered view that this is not a fit case

           for allowing the prayer of the petitioners under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_12">Section 438</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                   Accordingly, we refuse such prayer and reject this application.
</p>



<p id="p_5">                                                                              (Kalidas Mukherjee, J.)



                                                                              (Md. Abdul Ghani, J.)
