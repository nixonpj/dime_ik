                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

                     SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 23372 of 2017

         ==========================================================
                    PRATIK SURESHBHAI UNADKAT....Petitioner(s)
                                     Versus
              GUJARAT PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION &amp; 1....Respondent(s)

         Appearance:
         VENU H NANAVATY, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N.V.ANJARIA

                                       Date : 28/12/2017


                                        ORAL ORDER</pre>




<p id="p_1">               Heard learned advocate Ms. Venu Nanavaty for the petitioner.


</p><p id="p_2">         2.    The petitioner aspires to be considered and appointed on the post of

         Accounts Officer, Class-II and/or Commercial Tax Officer, Class-II. The

         process for recruitment to the said post was initiated pursuant to

         advertisement dated         01.08.2016 by the Gujarat Public Service

         Commission.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">         3.    The petitioner's case is that he appeared in the main examination.

         Thereafter, as required, the petitioner forwarded the application form

         on-line and the receipt for the same was obtained. However, subsequent

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            Page 1 of 3</span>

         to the said events, in the list published, the petitioner was treated as

         ineligible to participate in the recruitment process. The petitioner was

         denied eligibility on the ground that the hard copy of the on-line

         application form alongwith the required documents was not received by

         the GPSC.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">         4.    It was submitted by learned advocate for the petitioner that the

         receipt evidencing the submission of application form was in possession

         of the petitioner and was also sent at the relevant time through post,

         figures on record the acknowledgment of receipt of postal record. It is the

         grievance of the petitioner that the interviews are to commence on

         1.1.2018 and as the petitioner is treated as ineligible, he would not be

         allowed to participate in the interview.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">         5.    Notice returnable on 16.01.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_9">
</p><p id="p_10">         6.    By way of ad-interim relief, the petitioner shall be called in

         interview and shall be interviewed by the interview committee.

         However, it is clarified that result of the interview may be produced

         before this court in this proceedings to be subjected to further orders,

         which may be passed by the court.
</p>
