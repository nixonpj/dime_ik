<div class="doc_title">Rajju Miyan vs The State Of Bihar on 17 January, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.51038 of 2017
                       Arising Out of PS.Case No. -412 Year- 2017 Thana -NAWADA District- NAWADA
                 ======================================================
                 Rajju Miyan ASon of Halim Miyan @ Tola Mian @ Halim Tailor, Resident
                 of Naya Tola Noor Sarai P.S. Noor Sarai District-Nalanda.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Bhavesh Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s  : Smt. Pushpa Sinha
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE MOHIT KUMAR SHAH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   17-01-2018                   At the outset, the learned counsel for the petitioner

                 seeks permission to correct the provision of law mentioned in

                 paragraph-1 of the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 He may do so during the course of the day.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                 learned A.P.P. for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 The petitioner seeks regular bail in connection with

                 Nawada Town P.S. Case No. 412 of 2017 for the offences

                 punishable under Sections 399, 402/34,<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>,

                 under <a href="/doc/1961417/" id="a_2">Sections 25</a>(1-b)1 26 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_3">Arms Act</a> and under Section

                 30(a) of Bihar Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_4">Excise Act</a>.

</p><p id="p_5">                                 The allegation in the present case is that when the

                 police had apprehended the petitioner herein and other accused
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.51038 of 2017 (2) dt.17-01-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                     persons, one country made loaded pistol and one cartridge apart

                     from one mobile were recovered from possession of the petitioner

                     herein.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                       The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

                     there is no recovery of any illicit liquor from the person of the

                     petitioner, hence no offence is made out under the <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_5">Excise Act</a>. It is

                     submitted that the petitioner otherwise has a clean antecedent, but

                     after his arrest in the present case he has been remanded in another

                     case. The petitioner is stated to be in custody since 18.06.2017.

</p><p id="p_7">                                       Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the

                     case, the petitioner, above named, is directed to be released on bail

                     on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- with two sureties of the

                     like amount each to the satisfaction of learned A.D.J. II cum

                     Special Judge, Nawada in connection with Nawada Town P.S.

                     Case No. 412 of 2017.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                                 (Mohit Kumar Shah, J)
BTiwary/-
</p>
