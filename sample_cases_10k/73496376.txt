<div class="doc_title">Dhananjay Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 30 January, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA

                                      Criminal Miscellaneous No.4049 of 2015
                           Arising Out of PS.Case No. -21 Year- 2014 Thana -SAKSOHRA District- PATNA
                   ======================================================
                   Dhananjay Kumar Son of Jayram Prasad Resident of Village- Durjanchak,
                   P.S Saksohra, District- Patna.

                                                                                   .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                           Versus
                   The State of Bihar

                                                                  .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Ashok Kumar Kashyap
                   For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Suresh Pd.Singh(App)
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA PRAKASH
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2     30-01-2015                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner seeks bail in a case instituted for the

                   offences under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">354-b</a>, and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_3">506</a> of the Indian Penal

                   Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Considering that there is allegation against the

                   petitioner that he had committed outrage of modesty with the

                   informant, I am not inclined to grant bail to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   Prayer for bail is rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   However, considering that apart from having caught

                   hold the hand of the informant, there is no other allegation against

                   the petitioner and he may renew his prayer for bail after framing

                   of charge provided if his case is not bifurcated for the purpose of

                   commitment of charge.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                 (Anjana Prakash, J)
Sudha/-
</p><pre id="pre_2">  U       T
