<p id="p_1">         1. Rule. Learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives
         service of Rule on behalf of the respondent-State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2. This petition is moved by the petitioner seeking
         parole leave for a period of 30 days for the purpose
         of medical treatment of his mother.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         3.   Heard learned advocate for the petitioner as well
         as learned Additional Public Prosecutor appearing for
         the respondent-State.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.   Considering the facts and circumstances of the
         case as also the jail record, this Court is of the
         view that the petitioner is required to be released on
         parole leave for a period of one week.
</p>
<p id="p_5">         5.   Accordingly,           the     petitioner            is      ordered           to       be
         released on parole leave for a period of one week from
