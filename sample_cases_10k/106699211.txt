<div class="doc_title">Sandeep Kumar @ Kallu vs State Of Punjab on 26 May, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">Sr. No.211          CRM-3865-2016 in CRA-S-4478-SB-2014

SANDEEP KUMAR @ KALLU                     VS    STATE OF PUNJAB

Present:     Mr. Sanjeev Sharma, Advocate for the applicant-appellant.
             Mr. Sidamkeet Singh Sandhu, AAG, Punjab.

             *****
</pre><p id="p_1">             This is an application moved by applicant under <a href="/doc/985477/" id="a_1">Section 389</a> Cr.P.C. for

suspension of sentence, in case FIR No.73, dated 09.04.2012, in which, he has

been convicted under <a href="/doc/1566465/" id="a_2">Section 21</a> of the Narcotic Drugs and <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_3">Psychotropic

Substances Act</a>, 1985 and sentenced to undergo rigorous imprisonment for a period

of 10 years besides imposition of fine.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the applicant-appellant has submitted that the case

of applicant-appellant is almost covered within the parameters laid down by the

Division Bench of this Court in "<a href="/doc/190060/" id="a_4">Daler Singh v. State of Punjab</a>", 2007 (1) RCR

(Criminal) 316.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             A glance at the custody certificate, already on record, reveals that

applicant-appellant has already undergone actual sentence of 3 years, 11 months

and 17 days as on today, out of the total substantive sentence of 10 years. Appeal

challenging his conviction and sentence has already been admitted by this Court

vide order dated November 04, 2014, which is not likely to mature in a short span

of time.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             Taking into consideration all these aspects of the case but without

expressing any opinion on merits, application is allowed and sentence imposed

upon applicant-Sandeep Kumar @ Kallu shall remain suspended, during the

pendency of appeal, on his furnishing requisite bail bonds as per the satisfaction of

concerned ld. Chief Judicial Magistrate/Duty Magistrate.


</p><pre id="pre_2">May 26, 2016                                           (JASPAL SINGH)
sonika                                                    JUDGE
