<div class="doc_title">Bapi Sardar vs Unknown on 13 January, 2014</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Subhro Kamal Mukherjee</div>

<p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        1</span>



    S/L. 29
January 13, 2014.                            C.R.M. 158 of 2014
       ss.
</p><p id="p_2">                    In re: An application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of
                    Criminal Procedure filed on January 6, 2013 in connection with
                    Bongaon Police Station Case No. 662 of 2013 dated September
                    01, 2013 under <a href="/doc/1566465/" id="a_2">Section 21</a> of the Narcotic Drugs and
                    <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_3">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                    And

                    In the matter of: Bapi Sardar                      ...Petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                          Mr. Prantick Ghosh                    ...for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                          Mr. Rajesh Jana                            ...for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                          Heard the learned advocates appearing on behalf of the
                respective parties.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                          The petitioner is seeking bail in connection with a case
                relating to offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1566465/" id="a_4">Section 21</a> of the Narcotic
                Drugs and <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_5">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                          Having considered the case diary and the materials on
                record, and, particularly, when the charge-sheet has been
                submitted and seized articles are less than commercial quantity,
                we are of the opinion that custodial trial of the accused/petitioner
                is not necessary.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                          Therefore, the accused/petitioner, namely, Bapi Sardar, be
                released on bail upon furnishing a bond of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees five
                thousand) only with two sureties of like amount, one of whom must
                be local, to the satisfaction of the learned Judge, Special Court
                under the Narcotic Drugs and <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_6">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>, 1985
                at Barasat, District: North 24 Parganas.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                          The application for bail is, thus, disposed of.
</p>


<p id="p_11">                                             (Subhro Kamal Mukherjee, J.)



                                                    (Nishita Mhatre, J.)
