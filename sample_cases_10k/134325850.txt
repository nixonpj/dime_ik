03/08/2018, we are of the view that the NEERI should respond to the

Affidavits filed by the Applicant dated 02/08/2018 and 21/08/2018.

The NEERI is further requested to specifically make a clarification as

to   whether   the   land   on   which   the   proposed   transplantation   of   the

trees and plantation is to be done, is suitable for the said purpose or

not.     It   is   the   case   of   the   Applicant   that   the   lands   on   which   the

replantation  /  relocation  of the trees and plantation of the trees is

proposed, is a government land and the Government has no objection

for the replantation / relocation of the trees.  Let the learned AGP to

take instructions in this regard. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">4.                    We   also   expect   the   NEERI   should   verify   the   number   of

trees which are now shown to be fit for transplantation / relocation, is

correct or not and the possibility of their survival.  Let a report to that

effect be filed by NEERI within two weeks.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">5.                    The learned Counsel for the Petitioners is also directed to

file his response to the Affidavits filed by the Applicant.

</pre><p id="p_8">6.                    We also direct the Applicant to file an Affidavit showing in

what manner the transplantation of the trees and plantation will be

done  and  what  steps will be taken  to supervise  the survival of the

plants and trees.  We expect that the most advance technical expertise

and  machinery  will  be  used for  this purpose  as the  survival  of the

transplanted trees and the plantation is the most important aspect of

the matter.  Let an undertaking to that effect be filed by the Applicant

within two weeks.
