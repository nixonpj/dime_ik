            Parveen Kumar.

                                                                                   ......Petitioner.

                                                       Versus

            State of Punjab.

                                                                                 ......Respondent.

            CORAM:- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JASWANT SINGH

            Present:-           Mr. Liaqat Ali, Advocate for the Petitioner.

                                Mr. R.S. Randhawa, Additional Advocate General, Punjab.

<p id="p_1">            JASWANT SINGH, J (ORAL)


                                Prayer is under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.PC for grant of regular bail to

            the petitioner Parveen Kumar in case FIR No.356 dated 31.10.2015 for

            offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">120-B</a> of Indian

            Penal Code registered with Police Station Focal Point, District Ludhiana

            City.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The allegations against the petitioner and his co-accused Rohit

            Jain are of taking loan from various banks on behalf of their customers by

            preparing fake invoices of their firm in the name of their customers but

            factually neither the vehicles are sold/delivered nor the loan amount

            forwarded to the customers while misutilizing the same for their own use.

</p><p id="p_3">                                Petitioner is stated to be in custody since 06.11.2015.
VINAY MAHAJAN
2016.02.23 17:45
I attest to the accuracy and
authenticity of this document
at Chandigarh
             CRM-M No.43455 of 2015                               # 2#

                                At the time of hearing learned Counsel for the petitioner

            submits that the present petition has become infructuous in view of the trial

            Court having granted regular bail to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Dismissed as infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
