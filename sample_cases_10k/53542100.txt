<p id="p_1">2.   11.02.2009.              This application is for modification of the order dated

                   19.9.2008 passed in Cr. Misc. No. 34210 of 2008. Petitioners were

                   granted anticipatory bail but because of some typographical error and

                   other situation as mentioned in the petition, the time allowed to the

                   petitioners for surrendering before the court below has expired.

</p><p id="p_2">                              Considering these facts, the order dated 19.9.2008 passed in

                   Cr. Misc. No. 34210 of 2008 is modified to the extent that the time

                   earlier allowed for surrendering before the court below is extended by

                   three weeks from today. All other terms will remain same as

                   mentioned in the order dated 19.9.2008.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              The order be faxed to the S.D.J.M., Aurangabad if requisite

                   cost is deposited for the same.
</p>
