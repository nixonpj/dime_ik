    the petitioners and to submit a report to this Court.
</p><p id="p_7">         From     perusal    of    the   report   dated    03.05.2018,

    surrender before the court on 25.01.2018 and remanded on
    Observation     Home,         Dumka.    Sanjay       Mandal      was
    surrendered before the court on 17.01.2018. Ajay Mandal
    was arrested by the police and sent to jail custody on
    14.01.2018.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                           -2-</span>


<p id="p_8">         Perused   the   further   report    dated   06.07.2018
submitted by Vikram Anand, learned S.D.J.M., Jamtara,
which reveals that in A.B.A. No. 7334 of 2017, petitioner -
Bijay Mandal @ Bijay Kumar Mandal @ Bijoy Kumar Mandal
has been admitted on anticipatory bail vide order dated
26.06.2018.
</p><p id="p_9">         Further, petitioner Sunil Mandal @ Sunil Kumar
Mandal has been granted anticipatory bail in A.B.A. No.
7319 of 2017 vide order dated 08.05.2018 and his bail
bonds was accepted in this Court on 24.05.2018.
</p><p id="p_10">         Further, petitioner Chandradev Mandal @ Chando
Mandal has surrendered in the court below on 09.05.2018
and sent to judicial custody.
</p><p id="p_11">         No further direction is required to be passed.
         Matter is consigned.
</p>
