<p id="p_1">2   15-09-2017                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in connection

                 with Complaint Case No. 810 of 2015 for the offences instituted

                 under <a href="/doc/1868826/" id="a_1">Sections 498(A)</a> of the IPC and Â¾ of the D. P. Act.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 The prosecution story, in brief, is that the accused

                 persons including the petitioner tortured the complainant due to

                 non-fulfillment of demand of dowry.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 It has been submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioner that the petitioner has got no criminal antecedent. There

                 is no allegation of tampering with the witnesses alleged against the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.45095 of 2017 (2) dt.15-09-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                      petitioner. He has falsely been implicated in the present case due

                      to petty family dispute. The case is triable by the Magistrate. The

                      petitioner has further relied upon the judgment of this Court in the

                      case of Md. Naimul Haque Ansari @ Naimul Haque Ansari &amp;

                      Ors. Vs. The State of Bihar, reported in 2006(3) PLJR 182.

</p><p id="p_5">                                       It has been submitted by learned counsel for the State

                      that the petitioner is named in the complaint case.

</p><p id="p_6">                                       Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, let

                      the petitioner above named, be released on bail in the event of his

                      arrest or surrender before the learned court below within a period

                      of six weeks from today in connection with Complaint Case No.

                      810 of 2015 on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten

                      Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                      satisfaction of the learned S.D.J.M., Patna City, Patna, subject to

                      the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_2">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of

                      Criminal Procedure.
</p>
