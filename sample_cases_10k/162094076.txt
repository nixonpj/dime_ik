<p id="p_1">4   24-07-2012                 Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               This is an application for regular bail in a case

                  registered for the offence under section 20, 22, <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_1">N.D.P.S. Act</a>.

</p><p id="p_3">                               It is alleged in the first information report that 9 kg

                  Ganja was recovered from the three bundle which was kept in

                  the e tempo in which the petitioner were traveling.

</p><p id="p_4">                               Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

                  petitioner has no criminal antecedent and the recovery of ganja

                  is below the commercial quantity. The petitioner is in jail

                  custody since 22 December 2011.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               Considering the facts and circumstances of the case,

                  the petitioner named above is directed to be enlarged on bail

                  on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.19538 of 2012 (4) dt.24-07-2012</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        two       sureties of like amount each to the satisfaction of

                        Special Case No. 50 of 2011 arising out of Bypass P.S. case

                        No. 139 of 2011 subject to the condition that one of the

                        bailors shall be close relative               of the petitioner and other

                        shall be local bailor who shall file an affidavit stating that

                        petitioner has no criminal antecedent. If the petitioner is found

                        involved in such activity again, his bail bond shall be

                        cancelled by the trial court.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                                     (Gopal Prasad, J)
M.Rahman/-
