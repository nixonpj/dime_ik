<p id="p_1">     Heard learned counsels.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">     2.    The trial court on considering the plea of

the complainant was of the view that there was no

authorization   by   the   complainant   company     to

prosecute the petition. That the person who has

deposed on behalf of the complainant company has

not produced any authorization or power of attorney

or any such document to authorize the initiation of the

proceedings. Under these circumstances, the trial

court rejected the complaint.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">     3.    On considering the same, I am of the view

that, no interference is called for. It was the duty of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                            4</span>




the complainant-company to show its legal authority

to maintain a petition before the trial court. It has

failed to do so. Merely by contending so, would not be

sufficient in the eyes of law, unless there is power of

attorney of otherwise. No such material has been

produced before the trial court or even before this

Court. Consequently, I find no ground to entertain the

appeal. Hence, the appeal is dismissed.




</p><p id="p_6">                                            SD/-
</p><p id="p_7">                                          JUDGE
