Dr. Harkesh Sidhu
                                                                .... Petitioner
                                    Versus

                                                              .... Respondents

                                    ****
Present :    Mr. Jagjit Singh, Advocate for the petitioner.
             Ms. Rajni Gupta, Addl. A.G. Punjab.
                                    ****
A. B. CHAUDHARI, J. (ORAL)
</pre><p id="p_1">             The petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C.

read with <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_2">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. seeking anticipatory bail in Complaint Case

No.COMI/1932 of 2011 registered under <a href="/doc/570574/" id="a_3">Sections 166</a>, <a href="/doc/1815602/" id="a_4">167</a>, <a href="/doc/513074/" id="a_5">109</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_6">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1672685/" id="a_7">352</a>,

<a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_8">427</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_9">506</a>, <a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_10">452</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_11">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_12">149</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_13">120-B</a> IPC pending before the JMIC, Phagwara,

District Kapurthala.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Heard learned counsel for the rival parties.

             Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that pursuant to

interim order dated 25.07.2016 the petitioner had appeared before the

concerned JMIC and furnished his bail bonds and cash surety.

</p><p id="p_3">             In that view of the matter and looking to the nature of offence,

interim order dated 25.07.2016 is made absolute.

</p><p id="p_4">             Disposed of accordingly.
</p>
