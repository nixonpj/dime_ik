             Mr. Rishab Lohan, Advocate, for
             Mr. R.N. Lohan, Advocate for respondents No.3 and 5.

</p><p id="p_5">                           ****


Rakesh Kumar Jain, J.(Oral)

             This petition is filed for the issuance of a writ in the nature of

certiorari for quashing the notice/order dated 16/19.08.2017, vide which the

petitioner has been directed to remain present before the Presiding Officer-

cum-Assistant Registrar, for the election of the Vice Chairman of The

Palwal Cooperative Sugar Mills Ltd., on the ground that as per Rule 110 of

the Haryana Cooperative Societies Rules, 1989 (for short, 'the Rules') and

bylaws No.44, a clear 15 days notice of the meeting to conduct the election

of the office of Vice Chairman, has not been given.




</p><p id="p_6">                                  1 of 2
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             ::: Downloaded on - 28-11-2017 23:38:15 :::</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                               -2-</span>

                  Learned counsel for the respondents has submitted that the

impugned order may be set aside and liberty may be given to them to serve a

clear 15 days notice to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_7">                  In view of above, the present petition succeeds and the notice/

order dated 16/19.08.2017 is hereby set aside with a liberty to the

respondents to serve a 15 days clear notice for the purpose of holding a

meeting to elect Vice Chairman of the society.

</p><p id="p_8">                  Disposed of.
</p>
