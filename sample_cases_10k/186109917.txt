<div class="doc_title">Sanjan Kumar Thakur @ Sanjan Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 26 September, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.51531 of 2018
                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -156 Year- 2018 Thana -CHHATAPUR District- SUPAUL
                 ======================================================
                 Sanjan Kumar Thakur @ Sanjan Kumar S/o Rajendar Thakur, resident of
                 Village- Bairia, P.S. Chhatapur, District- Supaul.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Kumar Goutam, Advocate.
                 For the Opposite Party/s  : Mr. Shailendra Kumar -2, A.P.P.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SANJAY PRIYA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   26-09-2018                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               The petitioner seeks bail in Chhatapur P.S. Case No.

                 156 of 2018 instituted for the offence under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_1">Section 379</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the

                 Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             It is alleged in the written report that while the

                 informant was coming from his Customer Service Centre, two

                 unknown persons came and snatched his bag containing Laptop

                 of HP Company, PAN card, Voter-ID Card and other valuable

                 articles as well as Two Mobiles of Nokia Company and LYF. In

                 one Mobile, SIM No. 9102062123, 9570322205 whereas in

                 another Mobile of LYF company, SIM No. 7992430002 were

                 inserted. It is further alleged that four accused persons were

                 involved in snatching the articles and cash of Rs.59,600/- kept in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">       Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.51531 of 2018 (3) dt.26-09-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                   the bag.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Counsel for the petitioner submits that petitioner has

                   clean antecedent. He is in custody since 25.05.2018. He is not

                   named in the written report. His name has come during

                   investigation after receiving CDR report that SIM No.

                   8877497565 was used by the petitioner in the stolen LYF Mobile,

                   belonging to the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                  Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances of

                   the case, prayer for bail of the petitioner is allowed. Let the

                   petitioner above named, be released on bail on furnishing bail

                   bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the like

                   amount each to the satisfaction of learned Additional Chief

                   Judicial Magistrate-IVth, Supaul, in connection with Chhatapur

                   P.S. Case No. 156 of 2018, subject to the condition that both the

                   bailors will be the close relatives of the petitioner.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                                      (Sanjay Priya, J)
    S.Ali/-
</p>
