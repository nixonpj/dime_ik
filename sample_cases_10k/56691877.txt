<div class="doc_title">Poonam &amp; Ors vs Rakesh Kumar &amp; Ors on 10 July, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                        AT CHANDIGARH

213                                      FAO-6249-2014
                                         Date of Decision : 10.7.2018


Poonam and others                                            ...... Appellants

                                  Versus

Rakesh Kumar and others                                    ...... Respondents


CORAM : HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE AJAY TEWARI

             ***
Present :    Mr. Sumit Sangwan , Advocate
             for the appellants.

             Mr. Sachin Bhardwaj, Advocate
             for respondents No.1 and 2.

             Mr. Shivan, Advocate for
             Ms. Manjari Nehru Kaul, Advocate for respondent No.3.

             ***

AJAY TEWARI, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             This appeal has been filed against the Award dated 10.3.2014

passed by Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Bhiwani awarding an amount of

Rs.8,15,600/- with interest at the rate of 6% per annum. Since a very

limited point has been urged the detailed reference to the facts is not

required.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             The first issue raised by the counsel for the appellants is that as

per the Constitution Bench judgment of the Supreme Court in the matter of

<a href="/doc/74988840/" id="a_1">National Insurance Company Ltd. vs. Pranay Sethi and others</a>, 2017(4)

RCR (Civil)1009 under the conventional heads Rs.50,000/- more had to be

granted.
</p>
<p id="p_3">


</p><p id="p_4">                                1 of 2
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             ::: Downloaded on - 22-07-2018 15:28:05 :::</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> FAO-6249-2014                                             -2-</span>



            Counsel for the insurance company has very fairly accepted this

fact.
</p>
<p id="p_5">            The second issue raised by counsel for the appellants is that in

view of the judgment in Pranay Sethi and others' case (supra), 40% had to

be added to the income as future prospects. Counsel for the insurance

company has accepted this also.
</p>
<p id="p_6">            Resultantly, this appeal stands disposed of with the

modification in the Award that Rs.50,000/- more is granted under the

conventional heads and future prospect of 40% had to be added to the

income of the deceased. The apportionment, interest and management

would be as per the direction of the Tribunal.

</p><p id="p_7">            Since the main case has been decided, the pending civil

miscellaneous application, if any, also stands disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_2">
