<p id="p_1">1.	The
present civil applications have been filed for condonation of delay
caused in filing the First Appeal under sec. 5 of the <a href="/doc/1317393/" id="a_1">Limitation Act</a>
on the grounds stated in the applications.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.	Heard
learned AGP Mr.P. P. Banaji. Though served nobody appears  for the
respondent.
</p> 

 


 

 


 

<p id="p_3">3.	In
view of the grounds mentioned for explaining the delay, sufficient
cause can be said to have been made out for condonation of the delay.
 Moreover, in view of the guidelines laid down by the Hon'ble Apex
Court in catena of judicial pronouncements with regard to pragmatic
approach to be adopted in such matters including the judgments
reported in  (2005) 3 SCC 752,  AIR 2000 SC 2306 and  AIR 1996 SC
1623, the present civil applications deserve to be allowed.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_4">4.	The
delay is condoned. The applications stand disposed of as allowed.
Rule is made absolute.
</p>
