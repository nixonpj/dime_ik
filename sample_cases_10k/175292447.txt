<div class="doc_title">Kashinath Choudhary @ Kashi ... vs The State Of Bihar on 20 February, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.10315 of 2019
                       Arising Out of PS. Case No.-414 Year-2018 Thana- DUMRAO District- Buxar
              ======================================================
           1. Kashinath Choudhary @ Kashi Choudhary, Son of- Daulat Choudhary.
           2. Dewanti Devi Wife of- Kashinath Choudhary @ Kashi Choudhary
              Both residents of village - Nandan, P.S. - Dumaraon, District - Buxar.

                                                                                 ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 THE STATE OF BIHAR

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr.Ajit Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mr.Suman Kumari Singh
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   20-02-2019                   This application, for grant of anticipatory bail, arises

                   out of Dumaraon P.S. Case No. 414/18, disclosing offences

                   under <a href="/doc/409589/" id="a_1">Section 304(B)</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_2">                                Petitioners happen to be father in law and mother in

                   law of the deceased and allegation against him is of causing

                   dowry death of the deceased.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Submission of learned counsel for the petitioners is

                   that they are father in law and mother in law respectively and no

                   specific allegation has been attributed to them rather only

                   general and omnibus allegation has been attributed to them.

</p><p id="p_4">                                 Heard learned A.P.P. also.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                  Having heard both sides, in view of the above facts,

                   this application is allowed. Let the petitioners, above named, in
            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.10315 of 2019(2) dt.20-02-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                     the event of their arrest or surrender before the court below

                     within six weeks, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of

                     Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty five Thousand) each with two

                     sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                     Chief Judicial Magistrate, Buxar, in connection with Dumaraon

                     P.S. Case No. 414/18, subject to the condition laid down under

                     <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_2">Section 438</a> (2)<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3"> of the Code</a> of Criminal Procedure.



</p><p id="p_6">                                              (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)

sunilkumar/-
</p>
