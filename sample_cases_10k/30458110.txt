<div class="doc_title">Dorothia Kesaria And Ors vs State Of Jharkhand Through ... on 4 February, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                             W. P. (C) No. 594 of 2017
                                         ...
                Dorothia Kesaria and Ors.                           ...   ...Petitioners
                                  -V e r s u s-
                The State of Jharkhand and Ors.                     ....    ...Respondents

         CORAM: - HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE APARESH KUMAR SINGH
                                     ...
             For the Petitioner      : - M/s Anup Kr. Agarwal and Anuj Burman, Advs.
             For the Resp.-State     :- Mr. Anil Kumar, JC to AG.
             For the Resp-University:- Dr. Ashok Kr. Singh, Advocate.
                                     ...


03/04.02.2017</pre><p id="p_1">         Learned counsel for therespondent-University on instruction
                submits that plea of the petitioners to allow them to face 2 nd
                Semester exam of B.Ed. Course for Session 2015-17 is flawed on
                two counts:- (i) It is submitted that the paper of Childhood and
                Growing Up Paper-I has been evaluated on 80 marks allotting 20
                marks to each Group-A and Group-B question and 40 marks to
                Group-C    question    uniformally   to     all   appearing   students.
                Typographical error allotting 20 marks in Group-C papers in the
                question paper, therefore make no difference to the case of the
                individual students as all students of those papers of 1 st Semester
                B.Ed Course have been evaluated on the basis of 80 marks with 40
                marks for Group-C and (ii) It is further submitted that syndicate of
                Vinoba Bhave University, Hazaribag in its meeting held on
                11.11.2016 has taken a policy decision to promote students of 1 st
                Semester of Session 2015-17 B.Ed. Course, if they fail in only one
                paper. Petitioner have admittedly failed in two papers.
</p><p id="p_2">                      Counsel for the petitioners submits that Rules of other
                Universities are different on that count.
</p><p id="p_3">                      Counsel for the University seeks and is allowed two weeks'
                time to state these facts and their stand on affidavit.
</p><p id="p_4">                      On consideration of these facts, no prima facie case for
                interim order is made out in favour of the petitioners at this stage.
</p><p id="p_5">                      List this case accordingly.
</p>



<p id="p_6">                                                          (Aparesh Kumar Singh, J.)
  Kamlesh/
