<p id="p_1">          Learned counsel for respondent No.2 has filed Vakalatnama in Court

<p id="p_2">          Prayer in the instant petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. has been

under <a href="/doc/1270101/" id="a_2">Sections 279</a>/<a href="/doc/1402213/" id="a_3">337</a>/<a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_4">427</a> IPC, at Police Station Zirakpur, District SAS

Nagar, Mohali, and all subsequent proceedings arising therefrom, on the

basis of compromise (Annexure P-2).
</p>
<p id="p_3">          Pursuant to order dated 01.08.2018 of this Court, the parties

appeared before the trial Court/Illaqa Magistrate on 06.08.2018 to get their

statements recorded. Learned Judicial Magistrate, Ist Class, Dera Bassi, has

submitted her report vide letter bearing No.912, dated 12.09.2018 duly

forwarded by learned District and Sessions Judge, SAS Nagar, vide Endst.

No.9368, dated 12.09.2018.
</p>
