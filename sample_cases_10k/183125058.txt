              HIGH COURT OF JAMMU AND KASHMIR
                         AT JAMMU

                                                 CM No. 8672/2019
                                                 (in PIL No. 19/2012)

Ashish Sharma and Another                                       ...Applicant(s)

                                 Through:- Applicant present in person.
                         v/s

State of J&amp;K and Others                                      .... Respondent(s)

                                 Through:- Mr. A.M. Malik, Dy. AG.


         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJESH BINDAL, JUDGE
Coram:
         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE DHIRAJ SINGH THAKUR, JUDGE

                                        ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">1.           Despite opportunity, objections have not been filed by the non-

applicants to the application.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.           This application is filed seeking release of vehicle, i.e. Tractor

Trolley bearing Registration No. JK02AT-6781 seized by the police of

Police Post, Talab Tillo, Jammu vide Challan No. 119533 on 08.11.2019, on

the ground of violation of order passed by this Court in WPPIL Nos.

19/2012, 7/2014 &amp; 27/2014, as the applicant was allegedly extracting minor

minerals from Tawi Bed area without permission. It was argued that the

applicant has been falsely implicated in the case. He was not at the spot.

Otherwise also the applicant had never indulged in this act earlier.

</p><p id="p_3">3.           Learned counsel for the respondents has stated that he has

received instructions that the vehicle in question has been seized for the first

time.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2                           CM No.8672/2019</span><p id="p_4">




</p><p id="p_5">           4.                  Considering the aforesaid pleas, the vehicle in question is

           ordered to be released in favour of the registered owner, subject to deposit of

           â‚¹ 50,000/- before the Registrar Judicial of this Court with an undertaking

           that the aforesaid vehicle has been seized for the first time for such kind of

           violation and in case the vehicle is seized second time for such kind of

           violation, this Court may take serious view of the matter and will direct

           confiscation of the said vehicle of the applicant.

</p><p id="p_6">           5.                  It is made clear that the Jurisdictional Court having received the

           challan shall decide the same on merits and the amount now ordered to be

           deposited would be refunded in case the applicant is acquitted of the

           charges.
</p>
<p id="p_7">           6.                  Present application is disposed of accordingly.




</p><pre id="pre_2">                                                  (Dhiraj Singh Thakur)          (Rajesh Bindal)
