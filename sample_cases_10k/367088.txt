                              J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">       Petitioner was an HSA(Physical Education). According to him,

persons who were advised subsequent to him are retained in service and

at the same time he has been retrenched. Complaining of this, he has

filed Ext.P5 before the Deputy Director and in this writ petition what is

sought for is a direction to the 2nd respondent to consider Ext.P5.

</p><p id="p_2">       Having regard to the above facts, this writ petition is disposed of

directing that the 2nd respondent shall take up, consider and pass orders

on Ext.P5 with notice to the petitioner, as expeditiously as possible, at any

rate within 6 weeks of production of a copy of this judgment. It is clarified

that this Court has not examined the merits of the contentions raised in

the writ petition.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                              ANTONY DOMINIC, JUDGE
