<p id="p_6">

       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VINIT KUMAR MATHUR

                                Order

14/08/2018

In S.B. Criminal Appeal No. 576/2018 :

</p><p id="p_7">     The instant appeal has been filed under Section 14A(2)

SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act on behalf of the appellant,

who is in custody in connection with FIR No. 20/2018, Police

Station Nai Mandi Ghadsana, District Sri Ganganagar for the

offences under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_1">Sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_2">366</a>, <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_3">376</a>, <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of I.P.C. and Section
                                  (2 of 3)              [CRLAS-576/2018]


3(2)(V) of the SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act against the

order dated 02/05/2018 passed by the Special Judge, SC/ST

(Prevention of Atrocities) Cases, Sri Ganganagar, whereby, the bail

application preferred under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_5">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. on behalf of the

appellant was rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">     Heard. Perused the material available on record.



     It is submitted by learned counsel for the appellant that the

present appellant's name is not reflected in the F.I.R. He further

submits that in the statements recorded under <a href="/doc/447673/" id="a_6">Sections 161</a> and

<a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_7">164</a> of the Cr.P.C of the prosecutrix, the name of the present

appellant is also not there. He further submits that the present

appellant Jai Pal has falsely been implicated in the present case.



</p><p id="p_10">     Thus,   having    regard   to   the    entirety   of   facts   and

circumstances as available on record and upon a consideration of

the arguments advanced, this Court is of the opinion that the

appellant deserves to be enlarged on bail.



</p><p id="p_11">     Consequently, the instant appeal is allowed. The impugned

order dated 02/05/2018 passed by the Special Judge, SC/ST

(Prevention of Atrocities) Cases, Sri Ganganagar is set aside. It is

ordered that the accused-appellant Jai Pal S/o Bhup Ram arrested

in connection with F.I.R. No. 20/2018, Police Station Nai Mandi

Ghadsana, District Sri Ganganagar shall be released on bail;

provided he furnishes a personal bond of Rs. 50,000/- and two

sureties of Rs. 25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial
                                                                     (3 of 3)             [CRLAS-576/2018]


                                   Court with the stipulation to appear before that Court on all dates

                                   of hearing and as and when called upon to do so.


                                   In S.B. Criminal Appeal (Sb) No. 524/2018 :

</p><p id="p_12">                                          Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that he does not

                                   want to press the present bail application at this stage.

</p><p id="p_13">                                          Accordingly, the bail application is dismissed as not pressed

                                   at this stage.
</p>
