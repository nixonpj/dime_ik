                 Present:          Mr. Amit Mehta, Advocate,
                                   for the petitioner.

                                   for the respondent.

<p id="p_3">                 NARESH KUMAR SANGHI, J (Oral)

                                   Learned   counsel     for    the   petitioner    prays   for

                 withdrawal of the present petition to enable the petitioner to

                 challenge the order of summoning by way of criminal revision

                 petition before the Court of Session at the first instance.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   Learned counsel for the respondent has no objection

                 to the above prayer.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   In view of above, the present petition is disposed of

                 with permission to the petitioner to file criminal revision petition

                 challenging the summoning order dated 26.2.2013 before the

                 Court of Session at the first instance.




PRASHANT KAPOOR
2015.09.15 16:50
I attest to the accuracy and
authenticity of this document
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                  CRM-M-13616-2013                         2</span>
                                If the criminal revision petition is filed within three

                 weeks of passing of this order and an application for

                 condonation of delay is also filed, then the application for

                 condonation of delay shall be considered in view of the fact that

                 present petition has been filed.

</p><p id="p_6">                                Interim directions issued by this Court vide order

                 dated 30.4.2013, are hereby vacated.
