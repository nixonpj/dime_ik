                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.41224 of 2014
                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -41 Year- 2013 Thana -HARIJAN PS. District- NALANDA
                                                      (BIHARSHARIFF)
                 ======================================================
                 Ram Naresh Kumar, son of Late Bishun Ram, Resident of Mohalla -
                 Gupatpur ( Garhpar ), Police Station - Laheri, District - Nalanda

                                                                                 .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                      .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr.
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE V.N. SINHA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   10-04-2015                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  Petitioner is apprehending arrest in connection with a

                 case registered for the offences under Section 379, other allied

                 Sections of the Penal Code and Sections 3(i)(x) of the SC/ST

                 (Prevention of Atrocities) Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  It is submitted on behalf of the petitioner that

                 allegations set out in the fardbeyan is wholly false as there is no

                 injury on the person of the informant or any other member of the

                 prosecution party to corroborate the assault and in that light the

                 assertion that petitioner called the informant by his caste name

                 'Chamar' is false as informant has implicated the petitioner and

                 his family members in the present case for his failure to pay back

                 the money which petitioner and his family members deposited in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.41224 of 2014 (2) dt.10-04-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        GTFS Multi Service Ltd. of which informant is also a member.

</p><p id="p_4">                                         Let petitioner, named above, surrender in the court

                        below within a period of four weeks from the date of receipt/

                        production of a copy of this order in the Court below and if there

                        is no injury report on the record, he be admitted to the privilege of

                        bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 5,000/-(Rs. Five Thousand

                        Only) with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction

                        of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Biharsharif, Nalanda in connection

                        with Biharsharif SC/ST P.S. Case No. 41 of 2013, subject to the

                        conditions laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_1">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                            (V.N. Sinha, J.)
Rajesh/-
</p>
