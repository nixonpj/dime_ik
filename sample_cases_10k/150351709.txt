<p id="p_1"> .        Mr. Kulkarni, the learned advocate for petitioners submits
 that, all these petitioners are selected through duly constituted
 selection committee after undergoing the selection process. They
 had applied pursuant to advertisement.           The petitioners had
 passed written examination.          They were interviewed by the
 selection committee and their selection was recommended by the
 selection committee.          They had also undergone medical
 examination. These petitioners have worked for ten years and
 more.
</p>

 Zilla Parishad shall clarify on these aspects.
</p>
