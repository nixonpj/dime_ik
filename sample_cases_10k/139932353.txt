5. Rehabilitation Officer,
   Upper Krishna Project,
   Alamatti,
   Taluk: Basavana Bagevadi,
   District: Bijapur.                      ...Respondents

(By Smt. K.Vidyavathi, Adl. Govt. Advocate)


       This writ petition is filed under Articles 226 &amp; 227
of the Constitution of India with a prayer to direct the
respondents to consider the request made by the
petitioner for an alternative site at Nidagundi
Rehabilitation Centre, in place of the House Site No.307
allotted to him under the sites for the project displaced
persons, in Chimmalagi rehabilitation by considering
the representation made by the petitioner, the copy of
which has been produced herewith at Annexure-A.

      This petition coming on for Preliminary Hearing
this day, the court made the following:


                         ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     In this writ petition, the petitioner has prayed for a

writ of mandamus directing the respondents to consider
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                           :3:</span>




his representation dated 21st August 2012-Annexure-A.

In this representation, the petitioner requested the

respondents for exchange of a site. No prejudice will be

caused to the respondents, if they are directed to

consider   the   representation   at   Annexure-A,      in

accordance with law and as expeditiously as possible.


</p><p id="p_2">      Accordingly, the writ petition is hereby disposed

of.
</p>
