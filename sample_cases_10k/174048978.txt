<p id="p_13">          Post admission notice is waived by Mr.Ajit Kumar appearing

on behalf of the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_14">          We do not feel inclined to give any interim relief to the

appellant-writ petitioner so far as stay of recovery of the balance

amount due from the appellant is concerned, which he is required to
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                     2</span>

deposit by tomorrow (19.03.2015) in terms of the impugned order. We,

however, grant him another two week's time to deposit the same.
</p>
<p id="p_15">            It is made clear that in default of payment of the amount

due, the respondent, Jharkhand Urja Vikas Nigam Limited, shall be at

liberty to resort to coercive measures    as indicated in the impugned

order itself.
</p>
