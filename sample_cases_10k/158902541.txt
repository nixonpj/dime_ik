<div class="doc_title">Akram@Amrain S/O Ramjan B/C Mev vs State Of Rajasthan on 30 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">         HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                     BENCH AT JAIPUR

     S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous 3rd Bail Application No. 6868/2019

Akram@amrain S/o Ramjan B/c Mev, R/o Goyla Police Station
Tawdu Distt. Nooh Mewat (Haryana) (Accused Petitioner Is In
Distt. Jail Alwar)
                                                                    ----Petitioner
                                     Versus
State Of Rajasthan, Through P.p.
                                                                  ----Respondent</pre>


<p id="p_1">For Petitioner(s)           :    Mr. P.C. Thakuriya.
For Respondent(s)           :    Mr. Deshraj Gosingha, P.P.



</p><p id="p_2">             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE INDERJEET SINGH

                                      Order

30/05/2019

</p><p id="p_3">1.      The present bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

Cr.P.C. The petitioner has been arrested in connection with FIR No.

480/2018 registered at Police Station Bhiwadi District Alwar for

the offence(s) under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a>, <a href="/doc/1667388/" id="a_6">419</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_7">120b</a> of

IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_4">2.      Counsel for the petitioner submits that the co-accused Saroj

@ Pushpa Bhardwaj has filed S.B. Criminal Misc. (Petition)

No.2023/2019 before this court wherein on 10.05.2019 FIR was

quashed qua the petitioner therein on the basis of compromise

between the parties. Counsel further submits that the petitioner is

only attesting witness and challan has already been presented in

this matter and conclusion of trial may take long time.

</p><p id="p_5">3.      Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      (Downloaded on 29/06/2019 at 05:13:16 AM)</span>
</p><p id="p_6">                                                                           (2 of 2)                     [CRLMB-6868/2019]



</p><p id="p_7">                                   4.      Considering the contentions put-forth by the counsel for the

                                   petitioner and taking into account the facts and circumstances of

                                   the case and without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

                                   case, this court deems it just and proper to enlarge the petitioner

                                   on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                   5.      Accordingly, this 3rd bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C.

                                   is   allowed    and   it   is   ordered        that      the      accused-petitioner

                                   Akram@amrain S/o Ramjan shall be enlarged on bail provided

                                   he furnishes a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two

                                   sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial

                                   Judge for his appearance before the court concerned on all the

                                   dates of hearing as and when called upon to do so.



</p><p id="p_9">                                                                                             (INDERJEET SINGH),J

                                   MG/14
