<div class="doc_title">Suma Devi &amp; Ors. vs The State Of Bihar on 12 March, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.3764 of 2011
                 ======================================================
                 1. Suma Devi, wife of Bigneshwar Thakur, resident of Singarpur, P.S.
                 Khaira, District- Jamui, presently working as head mistress, Girl's Middle
                 School, Khaira, Jamui.
                 2. Amur Kumar alias Amur Kishore Kumar, son of Ram Kishore Sharma,
                 resident of Village- Dhamna, P.S. Jhajha, District-Jamui.
                 3. Sachidanand Thakur, son of Bigneshwar Thakur, resident of Singarpur,
                 P.S. Khaira, District- Jamui.
                                                                          .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                 The State Of Bihar
                                                                     .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s      : Mr. Prabhat Ranjan Singh, Advocate.
                 For the Opposite Party/s     : Mr. Surendra Prasad Singh, A.P.P.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SHIVAJI PANDEY
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   12-03-2012                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and

                    learned counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 This application has been filed for quashing the

                    order dated 7.5.2010 passed in Khaira P.S. Case no.274 of

                    2009 by the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Jamui by which the

                    learned Magistrate has taken cognizance against the

                    petitioners for the offences under <a href="/doc/1293807/" id="a_1">sections 461</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_2">379</a>, <a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_3">411</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a>,

                    <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_5">406</a> and <a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_6">409</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 According to the First Information Report on

                    29.11.2009 in the morning at about 3 O'clock at Kamui

                    Khaira main road when the informant came out from his

                    house for the purpose of natural call, he saw some persons
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.3764 of 2011 (2) dt.12-03-2012</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




                      moving inside the school building. It has been further alleged

                      that a person came out from inside the northern gate of the

                      school carrying two plastic bags full with rice loaded on his

                      bicycle and behind him one Suma Devi, Principal of Khaira

                      Kanya Middle School was also there. The police was called

                      and accordingly the person who was holding the bicycle was

                      arrested who disclosed the name of other accused persons.

</p><p id="p_4">                                    Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that

                      petitioner no.1 is a headmistress and she is at the verge of

                      retirement. She was not arrested at the spot though she has

                      been named in the First Information Report.

</p><p id="p_5">                                    Looking to the nature of dispute the defence of the

                      petitioners can not be adjudicated at this stage. This

                      application is dismissed. However, the petitioners will be at

                      liberty to raise all the points before the trial court at the

                      appropriate stage.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><pre id="pre_2">Vinay/-                                           (Shivaji Pandey, J)
