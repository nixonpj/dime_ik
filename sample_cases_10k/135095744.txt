                                                     ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                  The present Writ Petition has been filed for the issuance of

                      a Writ of Mandamus, to direct the 3 rd respondent to consider and pass

                      orders on the petitioner's representation dated 15.02.2016 seeking to

                      give adequate protection to the petitioner and execute the construction

                      of a Village Panchayat Service Centre and the additional school

                      buildings of the Government High School at Manavur, Tiruvallur

                      District.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

</p><p id="p_3">                                  2. The petitioner, who is the elected President of Manavur

                      Panchayat in Tiruvallur District, had identified a Grama Natham

                      Poromboke land to an extent of 2736 sq.ft in S.No.199/13 for putting




http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                           3</span>

                      up a building. The Block Development Officer, Thiruvalankadu, and the

                      Tahsildar, Tiruttani,   who are respondent Nos. 1 and 2 herein have

                      identified the land suitable for the purpose of putting up the Village

                      Panchayat Service Centre and given the clearance for the same.



</p><p id="p_4">                                  3.    Mr.V.R.Rajasekar,      Block    Development      Officer,

                      Thiruvalankadu, Mr.R.Venkatesan, Zonal Deputy Tahsildar, and also

                      the petitioner viz., R.C.Dilli Babu, are personally present in this Court.



</p><p id="p_5">                                  4. The Block Development Officer, Thiruvalankadu, states

                      that a third party filed a suit and disturbing the construction. The

                      petitioner also states that the said suit is filed for declaration and the

                      same is pending till today.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                  5. Admittedly, there is no interim order. As the petitioner is

                      unable to put up construction in view of the disturbance caused by the

                      private parties, he sought for police protection and also the help of the

                      respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                  6. It is stated that the building being Service Centre for the

                      Village Panchayat, funds have been granted and the same would lapse




http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                          4</span>

                      if the construction is not completed within a stipulated time. Hence,

                      the petitioner made a request to the third respondent on 15.02.2016

                      seeking a direction to the Police to give adequate protection during the

                      construction of the building.
</p>
<p id="p_10">

</p><p id="p_11">                                  7. Considering the facts and circumstances of the case,

                      this Court directs the 3rd respondent/District Collector, Tiruvallur

                      District, to consider the petitioner's representation dated 15.02.2016

                      and pass appropriate orders, on merits and in accordance with law,

                      after issuing notice to the petitioner and the persons interested in this

                      regard and also affording them an opportunity of personal hearing,

                      within a period of three (3) weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of

                      this order and also directs respondent Nos.1 and 2 to cooperate for

                      completion of the building.
</p>
<p id="p_12">

</p><p id="p_13">                                  With the above directions, this Writ Petition is disposed of.

                      There shall be no order as to costs.
</p>
