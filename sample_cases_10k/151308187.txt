<div class="doc_title">Rajesh Rai vs The State Of Bihar on 2 April, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.19464 of 2019
                    Arising Out of PS. Case No.-241 Year-2018 Thana- HATHAURI District- Muzaffarpur
                 ======================================================
                 RAJESH RAI, Son of Ramvriksha Rai, Resident of Village- Berai, P.S.-
                 Hathauri, District- Muzaffarpur.


                                                                                   ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar


                                                                             ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr. Ram Babu Rai
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr. Amarendra Prasad
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   02-04-2019                   Heard leaned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner is languishing in custody since

                   18.11.2018 in a case registered for the offences punishable

                   under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/162506/" id="a_4">447</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a> and <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_6">302</a> of the Indian

                   Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 The prosecution case as per the fardbayan of Indal

                   Ray recorded by R.P. Ray, S.I. of Ahiyapur P.S. on 17.11.2018 at

                   10.30 A.M. at SKMCH Emergency Ward, Muzaffarpur is to the

                   effect that on 15.11.2018, in the evening, the brother of the

                   informant, Asharfi Rai went to ease out, in the meantime, co-
 Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.19464 of 2019(2) dt.02-04-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            2/3</span>




          accused Yadunandan Rai tried to dash the brother of the

          informant through tractor but the brother of the informant save

          himself by jumping. Subsequently, the matter was resolved

          between the parties. It is further alleged that on 16.11.2018 at

          about 08.00 P.M., all the FIR named accused persons including

          the petitioner along with ten other unknown persons came

          variously armed and co-accused Sanjay Rai, Mohan Rai and Jai

          Nandan Rai ordered to kill the brother of the informant. When,

          Uttim Rai, the brother of the informant came out of his house

          then he was also assaulted by co-accused Sanjay Rai, Mohan

          Rai, Yadunandan Rai and Vijay Rai with iron rod and farsa.

          When other family members of the informant came to rescue,

          they were also assaulted by all the accused persons and when

          nearby people came, all the accused persons started flee away

          from the scene.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                       It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

          that though the petitioner is named in the FIR but specific

          accusation of assault with iron rod and farsa is against co-

          accused Sanjay Rai, Mohan Rai, Jaynandan Rai and Vijay Rai.

          It is further submitted that the investigation has already been

          concluded. A statement has been made in paragraph no.3 of the

          petition that the petitioner is not having any criminal antecedent.
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.19464 of 2019(2) dt.02-04-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       3/3</span>




                                  Learned APP for the State submits that the petitioner

                     is named in the FIR.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                  Considering the fact that the thrust of accusation of

                     assault is not specific against the petitioner and the fact that the

                     investigation has already been concluded, coupled with the

                     statement made in paragraph no.3 of the petition that the

                     petitioner is not having any criminal antecedent, let the above

                     named petitioner be released on bail, on furnishing bail bond of

                     Rs.10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                     each to the satisfaction of learned ACJM-cum-Sub-Judge, 10th,

                     Muzaffarpur in connection with Hathauri P.S. Case No.241

                     of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                            (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Deepak/-
</p>
