<div class="doc_title">Ramsharan Agnihotri vs Y. Sudarshan &amp; Anr on 24 August, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                      1

     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                             AT JODHPUR
      ------------------------------------------------------------

              WRIT CONTEMPT (WCP) No. 138 of 2014
                      Ramsharan Agnihotri
                               VS
                       Y.Sudarshan &amp; Anr.

     Date of Order : 24.8.2016

                        HON'BLE MR. SANDEEP MEHTA,J.

     MR. Nitin Trivedi, for the petitioner
     Dr. RDSS Kharlia, for the respondents.


                                ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">                                -------
</p>
<p id="p_2">

      I.A. No. 4069/2016 seeking exemption from personal

appearance of respondent Registrar, SKRAU, Biakner is allowed

for the reasons mentioned therein.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">      Mr. Kharlia, learned counsel for the respondent University

has placed on record a copy of the order dated 22.8.2016, as per

which in a purported compliance of this Court's order dated

22.10.2013 the petitioner has been regularized in service from

28.7.1994. However, he has been given notional benefit for

intervening    period    from   the       said   date   till   the   date   of

regularisation order.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">      Mr. Trivedi, learned counsel for the petitioner contends

that though the order does not reflect total compliance of this

Court's order but as the order passed in the petitioner's writ

petition is silent on the question of consequential benefits
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                     2</span>

pursuant to regularisation, the petitioner may be allowed to

agitate      this claim by filing a fresh writ petition while assailing

the said order dated 22.8.2016.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">           Consequently the contempt proceedings initiated against

the respondents are hereby dropped. Rule is discharged. The

petitioner is given liberty to challenge the order dated 22.8.2016

by taking recourse to appropriate remedy.




</p><p id="p_9">                                        ( SANDEEP MEHTA ),J.
</p>
<p id="p_10">/sushil/
