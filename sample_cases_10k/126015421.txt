     THIS WRIT PETITION IS FILED UNDER <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">ARTICLE 226</a> AND
<a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_2">227</a> OF THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA, PRAYING TO QUASH
THE DEMAND NOTICE DATED 05.12.2017 (ANNEXURE-A) BY
THE SECOND RESPONDENT AND ETC.

      THIS PETITION COMING ON FOR PRELIMINARY HEARING
THIS DAY, CHIEF JUSTICE MADE THE FOLLOWING:

                            ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner

and the learned Additional Government Advocate appearing for

the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">      2.    By the impugned notice (Annexure-A), a demand

of arrears of royalty and interest thereon was made on the

basis of inspection and verification of accounts.


</p><p id="p_4">      3.    The learned Additional Government Advocate, on

instructions, states that even subsequent notice has been

issued making a similar demand. However, he accepts that so

far as Annexure-A is concerned, a show cause notice was not

served to the petitioner and an opportunity of being heard was

not granted to him before making the demand.


</p><p id="p_5">      4.    In view of this position, we dispose of the petition

by passing the following order:
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               -3-</span><p id="p_6">



</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">     (i) We direct the second respondent to serve a show

        cause notice to the petitioner within a period of one

        month from today;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">     (ii) After granting an opportunity to file a reply and of

        being heard, an appropriate order shall be passed by

        the concerned authority within a period of two months

        from the date on which the show cause notice is

        served to the petitioner;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">     (iii) It is needless to add that the impugned notice dated

          05.12.2017 (Annexure-A) shall not be implemented;

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">     (iv) All issues concerning the liability of the petitioner to

          pay royalty and interest thereon are kept open.




</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_5">                                            Sd/-
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_6">                                       CHIEF JUSTICE




                                             Sd/-</blockquote><p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                                            JUDGE
