<p id="p_1">(Per
: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE RAVI R.TRIPATHI)
 

</p><p id="p_2">1.		The
present application is filed through jail seeking temporary bail for
a period of 30 days so as to complete the construction work of the
house of applicant-convict.  In support of that, a photograph and a
certificate from the Sarpanch of Kalyanpar-Chapar Group Gram
Panchayat dated 25.02.2011 is enclosed along with the application. 
The applicant has also stated that he has mother aged 90 years. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_3">2.		By
now, the applicant-convict has undergone 9 years, 5 months and 2 days
as on 04.03.2011.		
</p> 

<p id="p_4">3.		 RULE.
</p><p id="p_5"> Mr.J.K.Shah, learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives service of
Rule on behalf of the respondent-State.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_6">4.		For
the contents of the application, the application is allowed.  The
applicant-convict is ordered to be released on temporary bail for a
period of 30 days from the date of his release in Criminal Appeal
No.1337 of 2008 pending before this Court, on his executing a
personal bond of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousand Only) with one
surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the Jail
authorities.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_7">5.		The
applicant shall surrender to the Jail authorities on expiry of the
temporary bail period.
</p> 

 


 


<p id="p_8">6.		Rule is made
absolute. 
</p>
 


 


 

 


 

<p id="p_9">							(Ravi
R.Tripathi, J.)
 

 


 

							(P.P.Bhatt,
