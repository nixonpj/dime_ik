                          CHANDIGARH

                                     Criminal Misc. No.M- 40075 of 2017(O&amp;M)
                                               Date of Decision: March 26 , 2018.

Nisha Rani                                     ...... PETITIONER (s)
             Versus
State of Haryana and others                    ...... RESPONDENT (s)


CORAM:- HON'BLE MRS.JUSTICE LISA GILL

Present:     Mr. Ashok Kumar Khubbar, Advocate
             for the petitioner.

             Mr. Anmol Malik, AAG, Haryana.
                          *****

LISA GILL, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             This petition has been filed for directing respondents No.1 to 3 to

take action on the complaints dated 19.09.2017 and 22.09.2017, Annexures P1

and P2, respectively

             As per reply filed by way of affidavit dated 07/12.12.2017 of Mr. Raj

Singh, HPS, Deputy Superintendent of Police, Head Quarter, Kurukshetra on

behalf of respondents No.1 to 4, it is specifically stated that the allegations

levelled by the petitioner were found to be incorrect and no cognizable offence

was found to be made out. The petitioner has moved an application i.e., CRM

No.2314 of 2018 seeking withdrawal of this petition. This application is duly

supported by the petitioner's affidavit.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             In view of the same, this petition is dismissed as withdrawn.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                        ( LISA GILL )
March 26 , 2018.                                            JUDGE
