                 Although the petitioners have prayed for an order on the

              respondents to consider three representations submitted by them,</pre>

<p id="p_1">              there is more than what meets the eyes. The writ petition has

              been presented only after the private respondents instituted a civil

              suit for declaration and injunction against the petitioners and

              thus, their bonafides have to be tested.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                 I am of the view that the prayer for an order for consideration

              of the representations ought to be examined after granting

              opportunity to the parties to exchange their affidavits.

                 Let affidavit-in-opposition be filed by four weeks; reply thereto,

              if any, may be filed within two weeks thereafter.

                 Put up the writ petition in the monthly list of September,

              2014 for 'Final Disposal'.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">                                                              ( Dipankar Datta, J. )
