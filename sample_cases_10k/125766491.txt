<p id="p_1">          Petitioners claim to be the office bearers and

     members   of   one    association         by   name   JCB   Owners

     Association.          According       to       the    prosecution

     allegation, on 11.10.2018, petitioners went to the

     work site of the de facto complainant introduced

     themselves     as    the   members        of    association    and

     demanded ₹5,000/- as fine for engaging JCB works

     below the rates fixed by the association.                     When

     the de facto complainant refused to accept it,

     petitioners obstructed the works of the de facto

     complainant and the 1st accused forcefully took an

     amount of ₹1,000/- from the de facto complainant.

     Complaint      was       laid       and        the    petitioners

     apprehending arrest, seek bail.


</p><p id="p_2">          2.   The specific allegation is that the money

     was extorted from a JCB owner, who had entered

     into an agreement with the willing contractor on

     agreed terms.        Without going into the legality of
 Bail Appl..No. 7503 of 2018

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                       3</span>



     that     contract,      I   feel        that       essentially      the

     allegation against the petitioners do not warrant

     a   custodial      interrogation.              Having      considered

     these facts, I am inclined to grant the benefit of

     pre-arrest bail to the petitioners.


</p><p id="p_3">            Accordingly, anticipatory bail application is

     allowed on the following conditions:


</p><p id="p_4">      (i)       Petitioners       shall           appear       before    the

     Investigating Officer within 10 days from today

     and shall undergo interrogation.                      Thereafter, if

     they are proposed to be arrested, they shall be

     released     on    bail     on        each    of    the    petitioner

     executing a bond for a sum of ₹40,000/- (Rupees

     forty thousand only) with two sureties for the

     like sum each.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">     (ii)       They     shall        not     threaten,         coerce    or

     intimidate        the     defacto        complainant         or     the

     witnesses.
</p><p id="p_7"> Bail Appl..No. 7503 of 2018

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                 4</span>



</p><p id="p_8">     (iii)      They     shall        appear       before       the

     Investigating     officer   on    all     Saturdays    between

     9.00 a.m. and 10.00 a.m. for a period of one month

     from the date of execution of the bond.

</p><p id="p_9">     (iv)       They shall not get involved in any other

     identical offence and the involvement if any, will

     be a ground for cancellation of bail.




</p><p id="p_10">                                                 Sd/-
