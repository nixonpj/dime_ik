                                                         Date of Decision: 04.12.2014.

            Rajesh Kumar                                                   --Petitioner

                                            Versus

            State of Haryana &amp; others                                      --Respondents

            CORAM:- HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.

            Present:-           Mr. Bhim Singh, Advocate for
                                Mr. Nonish Kumar, Advocate for the petitioner.

                                Mr. Sunil Nehra, Sr. D.A.G., Haryana.

                                ***
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.J

                                Challenge in the instant petition was to the order dated

            29.8.2014 (Annexure P-9), passed by the Director, Health Services

            (Malaria), Health Department, Haryana, whereby the petitioner while

            serving as Multipurpose Health Supervisor (Male) had been transferred

            from C.H.C. Samalkha (Panipat) to C.H.C., Punhana (Mewat).

</p><p id="p_2">                                Learned State counsel has produced before this Court a copy of

            the office order dated 25.11.2014 issued by the Director General, Health

            Services, Haryana in terms of which the impugned order dated 29.8.2014,

            has been cancelled.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Writ petition, accordingly, has been rendered infructuous and is

            disposed of accordingly.
</p>
