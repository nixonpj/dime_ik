PRAYER : Writ Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of Constitution of India praying to issue Writ of Mandamus directing the 1st respondent to consider and approve the resolution No.133 dated 28.02.2002 passed by the Madurai Municipal Corporation in respect of the establishment schedule for th posts covered under the said resolution as to the enable the 3rd respondent to implement the said resolution in terms of Section.23(1) of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act 1971 for effecting promotions and filling up vacancies in the posts covered under the resolution from the eligible feeder categories in the Madurai Municipal Corporation Services within a time frame.

<p id="p_1">	The prayer in the writ petition is to issue Writ of Mandamus directing the 1st respondent to consider and approve the resolution No.133 dated 28.02.2002 passed by the Madurai Municipal Corporation in respect of the establishment schedule for th posts covered under the said resolution as to the enable the 3rd respondent to implement the said resolution in terms of Section.23(1) of the Madurai City Municipal Corporation Act 1971 for effecting promotions and filling up vacancies in the posts covered under the resolution from the eligible feeder categories in the Madurai Municipal Corporation Services within a time frame.
</p>
 

<p id="p_2">	2. When the matter was listed earlier on 15.09.2017 and 21.06.2018, there was no representation for the petitioner, hence the matter came to be listed today under the caption 'for dismissal'.  Even today, there was no representation for the petitioner. Hence this writ petition is dismissed for default. No costs.
