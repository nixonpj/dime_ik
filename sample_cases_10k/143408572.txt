                              WP 1024(W) of 2011
                              CPAN 813 of 2012

            (Arsad Ali Gazi &amp; Ors. -vs- Sanjay Bansal)


            Mr. Amit Kumar Pan...for the petitioners


            Mr. Soumitra Bandyopadhyay ...for the alleged contemnor


                     It is submitted by Mr. Bandyopadhyay, learned advocate for the alleged contemnor on instruction that the

            directions contained in the order dated 30th June, 2011 passed in WP 1024(W) of 2011 have been complied with as

            compensation has been paid on 15th January, 2013, which is disputed by Mr. Pan, learned advocate for the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_2">                     Let the matter appear as 'For Orders' on 14th February, 2014 when the alleged contemnor shall file a report of

            compliance in the form of affidavit stating the dates with regard to the issuance of notifications under <a href="/doc/43654/" id="a_1">Sections 4</a> and <a href="/doc/1792838/" id="a_2">6</a>

            of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894.
</p>
