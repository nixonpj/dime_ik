<div class="doc_title">ABLAPL/9586/2018 on 19 July, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                ABLAPL No. 9586 OF 2018




02.   19.07.2018         Upon     hearing   the   learned    counsel   for   the
                   petitioner and the learned counsel for the State, this
                   Court directs the petitioner to appear before the District
                   Mediation Centre, Berhampur, Ganjam on receipt of
                   notice from the said centre on the date and time as
                   would be indicated therein for sitting across the table
                   for mediation in exploring the possibility of resolving the
                   matrimonial dispute amicably. The daughter of the
                   informant be also noticed accordingly for mediation.
                         A set of brief and copy of this order be sent to the
                   Secretary,    District    Legal    Services     Committee,
                   Berhampur, Ganjam for onward action with this order.
                         Learned counsel for the petitioner is directed to
                   file one set of brief for the purpose within five days. The
                   Secretary, District Legal Services Committee is directed
                   to see that the exercise is completed within six weeks
                   hence.
                         As an interim, it is directed that the petitioner
                   shall not be arrested in Tarasing P.S.Case No. 68 of
                   2010 corresponding to G.R. Case No. 383 of 2010
                   pending on the file of learned S.D.J.M., Bhanjanagar till
                   next date of listing.
                         List this ABLAPL on 18.09.2018 for orders.
                         Urgent certified copy of this order be granted on
                   proper application.
