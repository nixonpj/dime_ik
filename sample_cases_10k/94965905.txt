<div class="doc_title">Vijay Kumar Mahale vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 21 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                        WP No.10385/2017
21.12.2016
                      Ms. Pooja Gajra, counsel for the petitioner.
                      Shri Swapnil Gangully, Government Advocate for the
             State.
                      Heard.
                      Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that petitioner
             is transferred at the fag end of his career. He is due for retirement
             in July, 2018.
                      It is seen that petitioner has already preferred representation
             Annexure P/4 and Annexure P/5 before respondent No.2.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                      Considering the aforesaid, I deem it proper to dispose of this
             petition with the following directions:
</p><p id="p_2">                      (i)     Petitioner shall resubmit representation Annexure
             P/4 and P/5 taking all possible grounds against the order dated
             10.7.2017 Annexure P/1 before respondent No.2 within seven
             working days from today, failing which interim protection will not
             be available to him;
</p><p id="p_3">                      (ii)    If representation is preferred by the petitioner within
             aforesaid time, the respondent No.2 shall consider and decide it in
             accordance with law;
</p><p id="p_4">                      (iii)   Till decision of representation, the impugned order
             dated 10.7.2017 Annexure P/1 to the extent it relates to the
             petitioner shall remain stayed.
</p><p id="p_5">                      Petition is disposed of without expressing any merit of the
             case.
</p><p id="p_6">                      C.C. today.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                            (Sujoy Paul)
                                                               Judge
 YS/
