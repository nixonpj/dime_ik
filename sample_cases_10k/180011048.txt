19/04/2017




     This public interest litigation is before us to challenge order

dated 24.1.2017 passed by Collector, Udaipur arising out of the

representation submitted by the petitioners on 03.11.2016.
</p>



<p id="p_18">     The grievance of the petitioners is that the respondents Smt.

Sharda and Shri Bhagwan Lal Meena are raising construction on a

land which is in downskirts of a water channel and further that is

on a land pertaining to restricted hill.
</p>



<p id="p_19">     From perusal of the facts stated in the order passed by the

Collector, Udaipur dated 24.1.2017, it is apparent that the land

in-question is not recorded in revenue records as water channel or

even as restricted hill.        The Collector considered each and every

aspect of the matter and gave finding thereupon, which reads as

follows :-

<p id="p_21">     The findings arrived by the Collector are based upon the

reports given by the competent officers, who submitted that after

having complete physical verification. We do not find any just

reason to interfere with such finding, which is based on revenue

records.   The writ petition, as such, is having no merit, hence

dismissed.
