<div class="doc_title">N.Subramanian vs The Superintendent Of Police on 12 October, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 12.10.2018  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE G.K.ILANTHIRAIYAN           

Crl.O.P.(MD).No.6776 of 2015 
and 
Crl.M.P.(MD)No.1 of 2015 

N.Subramanian                                   ... Petitioner

                                Vs.

1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Virudhunagar District, Virudhunagar.

2.The Sub-Inspector of Police,
   Vembakottai Police Station,
   Virudhunagar District.                               ..Respondents

PRAYER: This Criminal Original Petition has been filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of
Criminal Procedure Code, to direct second respondent to give necessary police
protection to the petitioner's proposed construction of Fireworks factory in
S.No.382, Vembakottai Village, Sivakasi Taluk, Virudhunagar District by
considering the representation dated 26.03.2015.

!For Petitioner : Mr.M.Mohamed Sherbudeen    
For Respondents : Mr.A.P.G.Ohm Chairma Prabhu,           
                                  Government Advocate (Crl.Side)

:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        This direction petition is filed to give necessary police protection to
the petitioner's proposed construction of Fireworks factory in S.No.382,
Vembakottai Village, Sivakasi Taluk, Virudhunagar District by considering the
representation dated 26.03.2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.The petitioner seeks police protection to his proposed construction
of Fireworks factory in S.No.382, Vembakottai Village, Sivakasi Taluk,
Virudhunagar District through a representation dated 26.03.2015.  Now, 3
years have been lapsed and hence, due to the lapse of period, the prayer
sought for by the petitioner has become infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.In view of the same, this criminal original petition is dismissed.
Consequently, connected miscellaneous petition is closed.                       
</p>

<p id="p_4">To
</p><p id="p_5">1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Virudhunagar District, Virudhunagar.
</p>
<p id="p_6">2.The Sub-Inspector of Police,
   Vembakottai Police Station,
   Virudhunagar District.       
</p>
<p id="p_7">3.The Additional Public Prosecutor,
   Madurai Bench of Madras High Court,
   Madurai.
</p>
