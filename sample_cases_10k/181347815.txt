                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.28872 of 2019
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-92 Year-2017 Thana- KALUAHI District- Madhubani
                 ======================================================
                 Nikesh Kumar Jha @ Nikesh Jha Son of Bauan Jha @ Sutai Jha @ Babuvan
                 Resident of Village - Balia, P.S.- Benipatti, Dist.- Madhubani.

                                                                                 ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 THE STATE OF BIHAR

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr.Subhash Kumar Jha
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mr.Aditya Narayan Singh.1
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SUDHIR SINGH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">4   17-08-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                          The petitioner seeks bail in a case registered under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_1">Section

                   392</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                         Allegedly, Rs. 1,03540/- ATMs and other articles were

                   snatched away by some unknown persons.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                         It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioner that there

                   is no allegation of tampering of witnesses alleged against the

                   petitioner. The petitioner is in custody since 17-09-2018. Charge

                   sheet has already been submitted. The petitioner is not named in

                   the FIR. His name transpired in this case on the basis of

                   confessional statement of co-accused. There is no recovery of

                   any incriminating article from possession of the petitioner. The

                   petitioner was not put on test identification parade.
             Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.28872 of 2019(4) dt.17-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                            On behalf of the State, it is submitted that the petitioner is

                      not named in the Complaint Case/F.I.R.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, let

                      the petitioner, above named, be released on bail on furnishing

                      bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of

                      the like amount each to the satisfaction of Sri Maheshwar

                      Dubey, learned         Judicial Magistrate-Ist Class, Madhubani in

                      connection with Kaluahi P.S. Case No.                92 of 2017

                      corresponding to C.R.I. Case No. 1251 of 2017.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                   (Sudhir Singh, J)
A.K.V.//-
</p>
