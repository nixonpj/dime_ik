</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 18-03-2019
(A.S. Bopanna, CJ)

                 Mr. P.D. Nair, learned counsel for the appellant while assailing the order dated
    21.02.2019 passed by the learned Single Judge in WP(C) No.491(AP)/2018 would point out
    that the appellant was before the learned Single Judge in an earlier petition in WP(C)
    No.281(AP)/2018, wherein through the order dated 14.06.2018, the learned Single Judge
    had directed consideration of the representation submitted by the appellant making out a
    grievance as regard to the transfer of the appellant from Ziro to Anini Division. The learned
    counsel would contend that the consideration of the representation, as directed, has not
    been made and despite the order of status quo passed therein, the impugned transfer order
    dated 21.09.2018 was made.
</p><p id="p_2">                 In that view, the matter requires consideration in that regard.
                 Mr. B.D. Goswami, learned Additional Advocate General, Arunachal Pradesh,
    assisted by Mr. Arun Chandra, learned Junior Government Advocate, accepts notice on behalf
    of the respondent Nos.1 to 6 and prays for some time to secure instructions in the matter.
</p><p id="p_3">                 Issue notice on the respondent No.7, returnable in 3(three) weeks. Steps by
    registered post with A/D.
</p><p id="p_4">                 Till then, status quo existing shall continue until further orders are made herein.
</p>
