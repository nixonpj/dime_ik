<div class="doc_title">Shambhu Prasad Rai @ Shambhu Rai vs The State Of Bihar on 19 June, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.11818 of 2012
                  ======================================================
                  Shambhu Prasad Rai @ Shambhu Rai, Son of Parmeshwari Rai.
                                                                     .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                  The State Of Bihar
                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE AKHILESH CHANDRA
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">4    19-06-2012                     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned Additional Public

                  Prosecutor for the State, who is armed with carbon/xerox copy of the case diary.

</p><p id="p_2">                                    The petitioner seeks bail in a case registered under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Sections

                  498A</a>/<a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">302</a>/<a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_3">201</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> and <a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_5">304B</a> of the Indian Penal Code, is one of the named accused in

                  this case of the year 1998 being husband of the deceased daughter of the informant,

                  who died under unnatural circumstance within five years of her marriage after

                  suffering some sort of demand of dowry and torture and her deadbody was also

                  appears disposed of. The petitioner is in custody since 02/01/2012 after evading his

                  arrest for about fourteen years.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case, for the

                  present, I am not inclined to enlarge the petitioner on bail. Accordingly, prayer for

                  regular bail of the above named petitioner, in connection with Hasanpur P.S. Case No.

                  103/1998, pending in the court of learned Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate, Rosera,

                  District - Samastipur, is hereby, refused. Simultaneously, courts below are directed to

                  proceed expeditiously with the trial and avoid undue delay and adjournments, so that,

                  this fourteen years old case come to its logical end at the earliest.




</p><p id="p_4">                                                (Akhilesh Chandra, J)
Praveen-II/-
</p>
