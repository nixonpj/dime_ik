<p id="p_1">          Notice returnable on 15.07.2015.
</p><p id="p_2">          Further affidavit is taken on record.
          Interim relief in terms of para 5 B. on condition that
petitioner shall deposit 50% of award amount before this Court. As
and when such amount is deposited, the same shall be deposited in
Fix Deposit, so as to earn maximum interest. It is made clear that
such deposit and interest shall be subject to outcome of this
petition. If any amount is deposited before any competent
authority, the same shall be considered as set off in such 50%
amount.
</p><p id="p_3">          Petitioner shall file undertaking to deposit remaining 50%
amount, if he does not succeed in this petition.
