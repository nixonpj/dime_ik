<div class="doc_title">Ramveer Rathod vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 14 July, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                           1
                                         M.Cr.C.No. 6309/2015

               (Ramveer Rathore Vs. State of M.P.)
14.07.2015
    Shri Ashok Kumar Jain, Advocate for the applicant.
    Shri R.B.S. Tomar, Public Prosecutor for the respondent/</pre>
<p id="p_1">State.
</p><p id="p_2">    Case Diary is perused.
</p><p id="p_3">    Learned counsel for the rival parties are heard.
</p>
<p id="p_4">    Applicant apprehends arrest in connection with offences
punishable u/Ss. 498A, 323, 506, read with Sec 34 of <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>
registered as Crime No.131/2015 at Police Station Mahila
Thana, District Gwalior.
</p>
<p id="p_5">    Learned Public Prosecutor for the State opposed the
application and prayed for its rejection by contending that on
the basis of the allegations and the material available on
record, no case for grant of anticipatory bail is made out.
</p>
<p id="p_6">    The allegation against the applicant who is the brother-in-
law(Jeth) of the complainant is of causing dowry demand
related cruelty alongwith other relatives and arrest in matters
of this nature may diminish the possibility of settlement in
future and therefore this Court is inclined to extend the benefit
of bail to the applicant but with certain stringent conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_7">    Accordingly, without expressing opinion on merits of the
case, I deem it appropriate to allow this application u/S 438
<a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_2">Cr.P.C</a> in the following terms.
</p>
<p id="p_8">    It is hereby directed that in the event of arrest, the
applicant shall be released on bail on furnishing a personal
bond of Rs.1,00,000/-(Rupees One Lac only) with two
solvent sureties of Rs.50,000/- to the satisfaction of Arresting
Authority.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   2</span>
<p id="p_9">                                                M.Cr.C.No. 6309/2015

           This order will remain operative subject to compliance of
       the following conditions by the applicant :-
</p>
<p id="p_10">       1 The applicant will comply with all the terms and conditions
             of the bond executed by him;
</p><p id="p_11">       2 The applicant will cooperate in the investigation/trial, as the
             case may be;
</p><p id="p_12">       3 The applicant will not indulge himself in extending
             inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted
             with the facts of the case so as to dissuade him/her
             from disclosing such facts to the Court or to the Police
             Officer, as the case may be;
</p><p id="p_13">       4 The applicant shall not commit an offence similar to the
             offence of which he is accused;
</p><p id="p_14">       5 The applicant will not seek unnecessary adjournments
             during the trial; and
       6 The applicant will not leave India without previous
             permission of the trial Court/Investigating Officer, as the
             case may be.
</p><p id="p_15">       7 The applicant will mark his attendance before the
         concerned Police Station once a week till investigation is
         over.
</p><p id="p_16">          A copy of this order be sent to the Court concerned for
       compliance.
</p><p id="p_17">          C.c. as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_18">                                                         (Sheel Nagu)
                                                            Judge
sh/-
</p>
