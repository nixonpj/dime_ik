<div class="doc_title">Mahendra Singh vs Indra on 26 February, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                 1                              CRR-5139-2018
                       The High Court Of Madhya Pradesh
                                  CRR-5139-2018
                                          (MAHENDRA SINGH Vs INDRA)

       5
       Indore, Dated : 26-02-2019
                      Shri Gaurav Rawat, learned counsel for the petitioner.
                      Shri Amit Tripathi, learned counsel for the respondent.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                      Let an amount of Rs. 40,000/- be deposited in the account of
    respondent wife or paid in person by the petitioner husband to meet out the
    school expenses and other day-to-day expenses of child within one week.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                      At this stage, learned counsel for the parties have shown their grace
    requesting to summon both the petitioner husband and respondent wife to
    work out amicable settlement. This Court records its appreciation for the
    gesture shown.
</p><p id="p_3">                      Accordingly, it is further ordered that an amount of Rs. 5,000/- shall
    be paid to the respondent wife enabling her to undertake                      to and fro
    journey(Ratlam-Indore) and stay at Indore on the next date of hearing.
</p><p id="p_4">                      LIst the case on 12.03.2019.
</p>
