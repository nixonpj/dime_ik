<p id="p_1">       The limited prayer in this O.P.(Crl.) is to issue a

direction to the Family Court, Malappuram to keep in

abeyance all coercive steps against the petitioner in Exts.P2

to P4 execution petitions, till the disposal of Ext.P6

application filed by the petitioner before the Family Court.

</p><p id="p_2">     2. Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3. The petitioner has submitted that the petitioner is

prepared to deposit an amount of Rs.75,000/- before the

Family Court within a period of two weeks from today to

show     his     bonafides.           Considering         the    facts     and

circumstances of the case and the submission of the learned

O.P.(Crl.) 21/2017
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             -: 2 :-</span>

counsel for the petitioner, the Family Court, Malappuram is

directed to dispose of Ext.P6, in accordance with law, as

expeditiously as possible and at any rate within a period of

two months from the date of production of a copy of this

judgment before the court by any of the parties. Till that

time, the coercive steps taken in Ext.Nos.P2 to P4 will be

kept in abeyance on condition of the petitioner depositing

Rs.75,000/- (Rupees seventy five thousand only) before the

court below within a period of twenty days from today, as

consented by the learned counsel for the petitioner. If the

amount is deposited, the first respondent shall be at liberty

to withdraw the same from the Family Court concerned.

</p><p id="p_4">       In the result, this O.P.(Crl.) stands disposed of, as

above.
</p>
