 Crl.Rev.Pet.No. 444 of 2018



                              -3-




                          ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">    The petitioners are accused in C.C. No.82 of 2016 on
the files of the court below.       The offences alleged are
punishable under Sections 406, 420, 460 and 468 read with
<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_1">Section 34</a> IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">    2.     The petitioners are aggrieved by order          dated
10-6-2016 passed by the court below framing charge.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3.    Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_4">    4.     It appears from the proceedings of the court
below that the petitioners appeared before the court below
on 10-6-2016. On the very same day, the court below
framed the charge against the accused for the offences
punishable under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_2">Sections 406</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_3">420</a> and <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a> read with
<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">Section 34</a> IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      5.   The learned counsel for       the petitioners    has
submitted that      the petitioners were not granted an
 Crl.Rev.Pet.No. 444 of 2018



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              -4-</span>


opportunity of hearing before framing the charge and in
the said circumstances,       the order of the court below
framing charge is to be set aside.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       6. It is true that the learned Magistrate recorded
"heard". However, it appears that the day on which the
petitioners appeared before the court, the charge was
framed by the court.       It appears from the proceedings
dated 10-6-2016 that the copy of the police report and other
documents were given to the petitioners only on 10-6-2016.
Thereafter, the charge was framed on the same day. In the
said circumstances, it cannot be said that the submission
of the learned counsel for the petitioners that they did not
get reasonable opportunity          to argue   for discharge, is
without any substance. Therefore,        I am of the view that it
is only just and proper to grant an opportunity to the
petitioners to raise their contentions before the court below
and plead for discharge. For the said reason, order dated
10-6-2016    passed by the court below framing charge,
 Crl.Rev.Pet.No. 444 of 2018



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                              -5-</span>


cannot be sustained and consequently, I set aside the same.
</p>
<p id="p_7">           In the result, this Criminal Revision Petition
stands allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_8">       The court below will grant opportunity of hearing to
the petitioners before deciding as to whether the charge is
to be framed or not.
</p>
<p id="p_9">       Dated this the 13th day of February, 2019.
</p>


<p id="p_10">                   Sd/-B. Sudheendra Kumar, Judge.
</p>
