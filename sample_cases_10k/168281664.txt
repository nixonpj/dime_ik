<div class="doc_title">Jitendra Sah vs The State Of Bihar on 15 May, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA

                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.18734 of 2013
                 ======================================================
                 Jitendra Sah son of Sri Rajnath Bhagat
                                                                   .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                 The State Of Bihar
                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                                            ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">2   15-05-2013                     Petitioner is apprehending his arrest in a case

                 registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_6">324</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_7">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_8">379</a>, <a href="/doc/886598/" id="a_9">365</a>, <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_10">406</a> of the I.P.C.

</p><p id="p_2">                                   It is alleged that informant supplied 52 trucks of

                 paddy to the petitioner worth rupees 80 lacks when only rupees

                 7 lacs was paid and balance of rupees 73 lacs was not paid and

                 when demand was made then petitioner assaulted the

                 informant and snatched mobile and rupees 2000/-.

</p><p id="p_3">                                   It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioner that no accusation under <a href="/doc/886598/" id="a_11">Section 365</a> I.P.C. has been

                 levelled in the First Information Report when the accusation

                 arises out of contractual relationship between the parties and

                 the informant has subsequently retracted from the initial

                 version which has been brought on record as Annexure-2.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.18734 of 2013 (2) dt.15-05-2013</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_4">                                         Considering          the     aforesaid   facts,   let   the

                     petitioner, above named, in the event of his arrest or surrender

                     before the Court below within a period of 12 weeks from today,

                     be released on anticipatory bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                     10,000/-(ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                     each to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                     Buxar in connection with Buxar Town P.S. Case No. 101 of

                     2013, subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_12">Section

                     438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                               (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
