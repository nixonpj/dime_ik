<div class="doc_title">Abdul Sattar &amp; Ors vs The State Of Bihar on 24 November, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                        Cr.Misc. No.20784 of 2008
                    1. ABDUL SATTAR, S/o Md. Yasin
                    2. Jamila Khatoon, W/o Abdul Sattar
                    3. Bel Kesh, D/o Abdul Sattar
                    4. Md. Nazir S/o Abdul Sattar
                    5. Md. Najrul @ Md. Najrul Haque
                    All residents of vill. Dumaria Sukhasan, P.S.
                    Barari, Dist. Katihar.

                                  Versus
                     1. THE STATE OF BIHAR
                     2. Afsana Khatoon, W/o Manzoor Alam
                               -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">6/   24.11.2008                  Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The complainant is not present in Court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                  Learned counsel for the petitioners has produced a copy of an

                  order passed by the Family Court, which suggests that there

                  has been a compromise in between the husband and the wife,

                  and the wife has agreed to live with the husband. The Family

                  Court has fixed the next date of hearing on 22nd December,

                  2008 when the husband and the wife have been directed to

                  appear together.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Taking    the   said   state   of   affair   into

                  consideration, the application for grant of anticipatory bail is

                  allowed. Let the above named petitioners, in the event of

                  arrest or surrender within six weeks from today, be enlarged

                  on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand)

                  each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                  satisfaction of Sub-Divisional Judicial Magistrate, Katihar in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             2</span>




         connection with Complaint Case No. 2231 of 2007 subject to

         the conditions as laid down under Section 438(2) of the Code.


</p><pre id="pre_2">S.B.P.                                           (Barin Ghosh, J.)
