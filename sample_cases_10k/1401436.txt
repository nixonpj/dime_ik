<p id="p_1">02.   02.09.2011                The present writ application has been filed for a

                      direction to the respondent authorities to remove the

                      encroachment made by respondent nos. 5 to 7 over the

                      land recorded in the revenue     record as Anabad Sarva

                      Sadharan Land appertaining to Khata No. 104, Plot No.

                      578 situate in Mauza Mangara in Karakat (Gorari) Police

                      Station District Rohtas.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                It is submitted that only the Encroachment Case

                      No. 2/07-08 was initiated against the respondents but it

                      has still not been concluded.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Let the Circle Officer, Karakat (Gorari) dispose

                      of the aforesaid encroachment case within a period of

                      three months of the receipt of the order of this Court if it
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               2</span>




            has not already been disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                      With the above observation, the writ application

            is disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><pre id="pre_2">Amrendra/                                       (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
