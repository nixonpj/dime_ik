<div class="doc_title">Gama Kurmi vs The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors on 24 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.10360 of 2017
                 ======================================================
                 Gama Kurmi
                                                                       .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                 The State of Bihar &amp; Ors
                                                                      .... .... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr. Vitesh Kumar Singh, Adv.
                 For the Respondent/s       :  Mr. R.K. Roy- GP18
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   24-08-2017                Heard Mr. Vitesh Kumar Singh, learned counsel for

                 the petitioner and Mr. Raj Kishore Roy, learned G.P.-18 for the

                 respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              The petitioner is aggrieved by notice dated 22.06.2017,

                 issued under the signature of respondent no.4, the Circle Officer,

                 Jiradei, as contained in Annexure-1, whereby the petitioner has

                 been directed to remove the encroachment over the land

                 appertaining to Khata No. 242, Plot No. 1367, Thana No. 228,

                 area 14 Khata, 10 dhurs, situated in Village Sivpur Sakra, P.S.

                 Aasaw, District-Siwan.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that

                 the notice has been issued without initiating any proceeding under

                 the provisions of the Bihar Public Land Encroachment Act, 1956.

                 Though, the land in question was recorded in the Khatiyan as

                 'Gairmajarua Malik Aam Land', but the ex-landlord settled the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court CWJC No.10360 of 2017 (2) dt.24-08-2017</span>
                                                 2/ 2




                     land in question with the grand-father of the petitioner and since

                     then the petitioner's ancestors are paying rent. The Jamabandi was

                     cancelled, against which the petitioner preferred Jamabandi

                     Cancellation Appeal No. 162 of 2017 before respondent no.1, the

                     Collector, Siwan and the same is still pending.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   Mr. Raj Kishore Roy, learned G.P.-18 prays for some

                     time to file counter affidavit.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   As prayed for, put up this matter after four weeks.

                                   In the meantime, the operation of the notice dated

                     22.06.2017, issued under the Signature of respondent no.4, the

                     Circle Officer, Jiradei, as contained in Annexure-1, shall remain

                     stayed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                                                        (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Amrendra/-
</p>
