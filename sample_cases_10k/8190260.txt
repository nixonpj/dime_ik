<p id="p_1">     The petitioner is the accused in Crime No.1788 of 2016

of Panoor Police Station registered for offences under

Sections 306, 498(A) and 201 r/w <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_1">Section 34</a> IPC.

</p><p id="p_2">     2. The petitioner was granted bail by this Court as per

order dated 10.04.2017 in B.A.No.2138/2017. In the said

order, this Court directed the petitioner to surrender his

passport before the court below. Accordingly, the petitioner

surrendered his passport before the court below.

</p><p id="p_3">     3.  Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     4.   The petitioner is working abroad. Therefore, the

passport of the petitioner is necessary for the petitioner to

go abroad to join for his employment. The petitioner is not

involved in any other offence of similar nature.          The

employment abroad is the only source of livelihood of the

petitioner. In the said circumstances, I am of the view that it

Crl. M.C. No.3506 of 2017

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   -: 2 :-</span>




is only just and proper to direct the court below to release

the passport of the petitioner.               For the said reason,

Annexure-A3 order cannot be sustained and consequently, I

set aside the same.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      5.    In the result, this Crl.M.C. stands allowed, setting

aside Annexure-A3 order and the court below is directed to

release the passport of the petitioner on condition of the

petitioner executing a bond for Rs.1,00,000/- (Rupees One

lakh only) with two solvent sureties, each for the like sum to

the satisfaction of the court below, on undertaking to

produce the passport as and when required by the court.

</p><p id="p_6">      The petitioner shall furnish the address of his office

abroad before getting the passport released from the court

below.
</p>
