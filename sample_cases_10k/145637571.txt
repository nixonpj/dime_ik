<div class="doc_title">Prasunendu Chandra Pandey vs Bihar State Financial ... on 18 July, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                   F.A. No.201 of 2009
                                ------
</pre><p id="p_1">    Prasunendu Chandra Pandey          .... .... .... Appellants
                                Versus
    Bihar State Financial Corporation &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_2">                                       .... .... .... Respondents
</p><p id="p_3">                                ------
</p><p id="p_4">CORAM      : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR CHOUDHARY
</p><p id="p_5">                                        ------
</p><p id="p_6">     For the Appellant            : Mr. P.C. Roy, Advocate
     For the Respondents          : Mr. A.K. Yadav, Advocate
</p><p id="p_7">                                        ------
</p><p id="p_8">     Order No.10 Dated- 18.07.2018
     I.A. No.356 of 2017
           This interlocutory application has been filed with a prayer for
     substitution of the legal representatives of the sole appellant no.1-
     Prasunendu Chandra Pandey.
</p><p id="p_9">           Learned counsel for the appellant submits that the sole appellant
     no.1-Prasunendu Chandra Pandey died on 14.10.2016 leaving behind his
     only three legal representatives whose names, parentage and addresses
     have been mentioned in paragraph no.2 of the interlocutory application. It
     is submitted that the petition for substitution has been filed within time
     on 12.01.2017. It is further submitted by Mr. P.C. Roy, Advocate that he
     has filed power on behalf of the three legal representatives of the sole
     deceased appellant. Hence, it is submitted that the said only three legal
     representatives of the sole appellant no.1-Prasunendu Chandra Pandey be
     substituted in his place as appellant nos. 1 (a) to 1 (c).
</p><p id="p_10">           Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances of the case, the
     prayer to substitute the only three legal representatives of the sole
     appellant no.1-Prasunendu Chandra Pandey, in his place as appellant nos.
     1 (a) to 1 (c) is allowed.
</p><p id="p_11">           Registry is directed to incorporate the names, parentage and
     address of the said three legal representatives of the sole appellant no.1 in
     the cause title of the appeal memo in red ink.
</p><p id="p_12">                 This interlocutory application is disposed of accordingly.
</p><p id="p_13">                                            (Anil Kumar Choudhary, J.)
F.A. No.201 of 2009

                List this appeal under the heading 'for Hearing' after three weeks.
</p>

<p id="p_14">Sonu-Gunjan/-                            (Anil Kumar Choudhary, J.)
