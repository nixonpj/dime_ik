<p id="p_2"> 1.               Heard Mr. Patil, learned Counsel appearing for the

 applicants.

</p><p id="p_4"> 2.               By inviting our attention to an order passed by this

 Court in Writ Petition No. 8614 of 2017 dated 19 th March 2017,

 learned Counsel Mr. Patil submitted that the applicants are

 identically circumstanced to petitioners in Writ Petition No. 8814

 of 2017. While passing the order dated 19 th March 2017, our

 Science stream and the Government Resolution also refers to the

 list of districts and Ashram Schools in these districts.            In view of

 the Government Resolution, the writ petition was disposed of with

 Counsel submitted before us that applicant No.1 Balaji Pandurang

 Chavan is teaching Geography subject, applicant No.3 Kalleshwar

 Subhashrao Choudhari is teaching Physics subject in the Higher

 8614 of 2017.
