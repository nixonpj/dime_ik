                                                     ORDER

<p id="p_1">                           Heard the Learned Counsel for the Petitioner and the Learned

               Standing Counsel for the Respondents 1 &amp; 2.



</p><p id="p_2">                           2. The case of the petitioner is that, the electricity service

               connection Nos.251-509-1448, 251-509-1449, 251-509-1450 &amp; 251-

               509-1455 at No.40/10, Murugesan 2nd Cross Street, East Tambaram,

               Chennai – 600 059 was disconnected on the 3rd respondent and

               therefore, sought for representation of the electricity service connection.

               The alleged representation of the petitioner dated 07.09.2019 not been

               considered by the 1st and 2nd respondent.            Hence, the present Writ

               Petition is filed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">                           3. At the time of admission, this Court ascertained from the

               Standing Counsel from the respondents 1 &amp; 2 that, the disconnection was


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">              2/4</span>
http://www.judis.nic.in
                                                                          W.P.No.27422 of 2019

               not done by them but by the 3rd respondent, who is none other than

               grand mother of the petitioner, due to some personal dispute and

               therefore, this Court directed the 2nd respondent to visit premises of the

               petitioner and find out whether there is any tampering in the fuse board

               done by the 3rd respondent and to rectify the same.      Accordingly, the

               staff of the 2nd respondent, visited the premises and found that the fuse

               carrier was removed by the 3rd respondent unauthorisedly and same was

               restored.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                           4. Now the petitioner is enjoying the electricity service

               connection since the service connection is restored, nothing survives in

               this Writ Petition. If the petitioner wants separate service connection to

               the premises, she shall make proper application to the respondents in

               accordance with the procedure and same may be considered by the 2nd

               respondent as per law.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                           5. Accordingly, the Writ Petition is disposed of. No costs.</p><pre id="pre_2">
