<div class="doc_title">Islamuddin Ahmed vs The State Of Assam on 24 October, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                                                            Page No.# 1/2

GAHC010257292019




                                 THE GAUHATI HIGH COURT
   (HIGH COURT OF ASSAM, NAGALAND, MIZORAM AND ARUNACHAL PRADESH)

                                       Case No. : AB 3651/2019

              1:ISLAMUDDIN AHMED
              S/O LATE JOYNUDDIN, R/O JARPARA (KHORITOL), P.S.-CHHAYGAON,
              DIST-KAMRUP, ASSAM, PIN-781124

              VERSUS

              1:THE STATE OF ASSAM
              REPRESENTED BY THE PUBLIC PROSECUTOR, ASSAM

Advocate for the Petitioner      : MR F HAQUE

Advocate for the Respondent : PP, ASSAM

                                     BEFORE
                    HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HITESH KUMAR SARMA

                                                ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 24-10-2019



This is an application made under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C., seeking pre-arrest bail by the petitioner,
namely, Islamuddin Ahmed, in connection with Chhaygaon Police Station Case No. 935 of 2019,
(corresponding to GR Case No. 2465(K)/2019), registered under <a href="/doc/1868826/" id="a_2">Section 498(A)</a> of the IPC.
I have perused the petition including the copy of the FIR and the documents annexed
therewith.
</p><p id="p_2">Heard Mr. F Hoque, learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and Mr. NJ Dutta, learned Additional Public
Prosecutor.
</p><p id="p_3">On perusal of the statements in the FIR and materials placed before this Court as of now, this
Court is of the view that till a final decision is taken , on perusal of the case diary, the petitioner be

allowed to remain on interim pre-arrest bail.
</p><p id="p_4">                                                                                                 Page No.# 2/2

Accordingly, it is, in the interim, provided that in the event of arrest of the petitioner above
named, in connection with the case aforementioned, he shall be released on furnishing bail
bond of Rs.10,000/-, with a suitable surety of the like amount, to the satisfaction of arresting
authority.
</p><p id="p_5">The direction for pre-arrest bail is subject to the conditions that the petitioner:
</p><p id="p_6">(a) shall appear before the Investigating of concerned Police Station, within seven days from
today and shall co-operate with the investigation;
</p><p id="p_7">(b) shall not hamper with the investigation, or tamper with the evidence of the case;
</p><p id="p_8">(c) shall not, directly or indirectly, make any inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted with
the facts of the case so as to dissuade him from disclosing such facts to the Court or to any police officer.
Call for the case diary.
</p><p id="p_9">List the case on 27-11-2019.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                                                                                 JUDGE



Comparing Assistant
