<div class="doc_title">Prem Pat Pingua @ Premchand Pingua vs State Of Jharkhand on 29 October, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                      B.A. No. 7752 of 2018
            1. Prem Pat Pingua @ Premchand Pingua
            2. Suresh Pat Pingua.....................             Petitioners
                                      Versus
            State of Jharkhand. ............                            Opp. Party
                                      ......
</pre><p id="p_1">            Coram: Hon'ble Mr. Justice Ananda Sen
                                      ......</p><pre id="pre_2">
            For the Petitioners                : Mr. Pravin Kumar, Advocate
            For the State                      : A.P.P.
                                      ......

4/29.10.2018         Heard learned counsel appearing for the petitioners and the
</pre><p id="p_2">            learned counsel for the State, who opposes the prayer for bail.
</p><p id="p_3">                     The petitioners are accused for allegedly committing offence
            punishable under <a href="/doc/786750/" id="a_1">Sections 121</a>, <a href="/doc/1949191/" id="a_2">121A</a>, <a href="/doc/1641007/" id="a_3">124A</a>, <a href="/doc/811548/" id="a_4">153A(1)</a>, <a href="/doc/771276/" id="a_5">153B</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_6">420</a>,
            <a href="/doc/926966/" id="a_7">505(1)(B)</a>, <a href="/doc/1198526/" id="a_8">505(2)</a>, <a href="/doc/1183069/" id="a_9">386</a>, <a href="/doc/1688284/" id="a_10">387</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_11">120B</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_4">                     The petitioners are in custody since 18.06.2018. There is
            allegation that one Ramo Birua self declared himself to be the owner of the
            Kolhan State and thereafter started issuing caste certificates, residential
            certificates, birth and death certificates etc. and disclosed that he will take
            over the Government. He obstructed the government officials from
            performing their duties. It is alleged that these petitioners were his
            associates.
</p><p id="p_5">                     Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that there is no
            specific overt act against these petitioners.
</p><p id="p_6">                     Taking into consideration the aforesaid fact and the period of
            custody and since there is no specific overt act, I am inclined to enlarge
            the petitioners on bail. Accordingly, both these petitioners, named above,
            are directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-
            (ten thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the
            satisfaction   of   Additional   Chief   Judicial   Magistrate,   Chaibasa    in
            connection with Chaibasa Mufassil (Pandrasali) P.S. Case No. 140 of 2017
            [G.R. No. 641/2017].
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                                                        (Ananda Sen, J)
Mukund/-cp. 3
 </p>
