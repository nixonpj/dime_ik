<div class="doc_title">Pawan Kori vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh Thr on 20 October, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                     W.P.No.7333/2015(Habeas Corpus)
      20-10-2015
            Shri Yash Sharma, learned counsel for the petitioner.
            Shri Vishal Mishra, learned Deputy Advocate General for
      the respondents/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">            Corpus, Neelam has been produced before this Court.
            From the documents, it is evident that during pendency
      of the trial arising out of crime No.104/15 for the alleged
      offence punishable under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_1">sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_2">366</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_3">376</a> IPC read
      with <a href="/doc/230360/" id="a_4">section 4</a> of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offence
      Act, 2012 against the petitioner/accused, corpus who was
      prosecutrix was directed to be kept in the Nari Niketan vide
      order dated 06/09/2015 by the Judicial Magistrate, First Class,
      District Datia (Annexure P/1).
</p><p id="p_2">            It is informed by the learned counsel for the petitioner
      that the criminal prosecution has ended in acquittal.
</p><p id="p_3">            Let, copy of the judgment passed by the Court below be
      brought on record.
</p><p id="p_4">            Let, the corpus be sent back to the Nari Niketan,
      Gwalior.
</p><p id="p_5">            The petitioner and the corpus (both) be kept present in
      the Court, on the next date of hearing.
</p><p id="p_6">            List this case on 28/10/2015.
</p>



<pre id="pre_2">      (Sheel Nagu)                                  (Rohit Arya)
          Judge                                        Judge
