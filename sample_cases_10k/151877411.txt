              SANJAY KISHAN KAUL, CHIEF JUSTICE, J.(Oral)

                                 Learned counsel for the appellant seeks to contend before us that

              there was no occasion to grant grace marks earlier, as the appellant had to

              reappear in those papers.           If that be the position, then all grace marks are

              available to be assigned at present, in which case the appellant can get the benefit

              of those one percent grace marks which would clear his examination.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                 However, on perusal of the impugned order we find that there was

              no discussion on this aspect at all, though the learned counsel for the appellant

              contends that plea was raised in the petition though appears not urged before the

Singh Omkar
              learned Single Judge.
</p><p id="p_6">2014.03.20 14:46

              prayer before learned Single Judge who is to contemplate on the issue.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                               At this stage, learned counsel for the appellant seeks to withdraw the

              appeal with liberty to approach learned Single Judge.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                               Dismissed as withdrawn with liberty to move the learned Single

              Judge, in accordance with law.
