<div class="doc_title">Ashim Kumar Dhar &amp; Ors vs Smt.Indrani Roy &amp; Ors on 8 June, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                       Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                                ORIGINAL SIDE

                                GA 2068 OF 2013
                                     WITH
                                 CS 413 of 1989
                           ASHIM KUMAR DHAR &amp; ORS.

                                     Versus

                            SMT.INDRANI ROY &amp; ORS.


  BEFORE:

  The Hon'ble JUSTICE ARINDAM SINHA</pre>

<p id="p_1">  Date : 8th June, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               Mr.Kushal Poddar, Advocate
                                          ..for Valuer
                               Mr.Sarahi Dasgupta, Advocate
                                          ...for applicants




             The Court :-Mr. Poddar appears on behalf of the valuer and submits,

the valuer requires particulars of the description of the property, as has not been

give to him as yet.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            Mr. Dasgupta, learned Advocate appears on behalf of the applicants

and submits, there is no document in the custody of his client which gives such

particulars. The defendant nos. 11 and 13 in person submit, they have been able

to get copies of documents which provide particulars of the property but such

copies are of documents in the Court records.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                          2</span>


</p><p id="p_4">            The defendant nos. 11 and 13 might forward the copy documents

bearing particulars of the description of the property to the valuer. The valuer, it

appears, has already notified his intention to visit the property on 11th June,

2016. The copy documents should be given to the valuer prior to such visit so

that valuer might on receipt of documents and upon visiting the property draw

up and file his report on the adjourned date.

</p><p id="p_5">            List this application on 20th June, 2016 under the same heading.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                        (ARINDAM SINHA, J.)


GH.
</p>
