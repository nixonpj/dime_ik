<div class="doc_title">Sanjay Sahu vs State Of Jharkhand on 1 July, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                  B. A. No. 4112 of 2011

                   Sanjay Sahu                         ...   ...    Petitioner
                                             Versus
                   The State of Jharkhand              ...   ...    ... Opp. Party

                         CORAM: THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PRASHANT KUMAR
                                       ............

                For the petitioner      : Mr. Satish Kumar Keshri, Advocate
                For the State           : Mr. P.K. Sahay, A.P.P.


2 /01.07.2011</pre><p id="p_1">                 Bail application filed by petitioner, Sanjay Sahu in connection with
                Kamdara P.S. Case No.52 of 2006 corresponding to G.R. No.814 of 2006 (S.T.
                No.221 of 2010) pending in the court of learned Sessions Judge, Gumla is
                moved by Mr. Satish Kumar Keshri, counsel for the petitioner and opposed by
                Mr. P.K. Sahay, Additional P.P.
</p><p id="p_2">                              Earlier bail prayer of petitioner rejected vide order dated
                28.06.2010. It is submitted that other co-accused have been granted bail by a
                Bench of this Court vide order dated 12.05.2011. It is also submitted that since
                October 2010, no witness examined. However, from perusal of earlier order, I
                find that in the case diary there are sufficient materials against the petitioner and
                this is a case in which six persons were murdered.
</p><p id="p_3">                              Considering the gravity of offence, I do not find any reason to re-
                consider the prayer of petitioner. Hence this bail application rejected.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                                                                    (Prashant Kumar, J.)
R.K.
</p>
