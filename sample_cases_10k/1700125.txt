ORAL
ORDER</pre> 

 

 
 


 
	  


<p id="p_1">	Counsel for the
	petitioner submitted that investigation is over and charge-sheet is
	filed. He further submitted that from the petitioner, there has been
	no recovery or discovery. Petitioner is unlikely to jump bail, if
	released.
</p>	 


	 
	
	  


<p id="p_2">	In addition to the
	submissions made, I have perused the nature of the allegation
	contained in the FIR and the material collected by the Investigating
	Agency while filing the charge-sheet. 
</p>	
	 


	 
	
	  


<p id="p_3">	In facts and
	circumstances of the case, petitioner is ordered to be released on
	bail in connection with C.R. No. I-56/2011 registered
	before Rander Police Station on his furnishing bond of Rs.
	10,000/-(Rupees Ten Thousand) with one surety of like amount to
	the satisfaction of the lower Court and subject to following
	conditions that he shall:
</p>
 

 


 
	  

<p id="p_4">not
	take undue advantage of his liberty or abuse his liberty;
</p>	  

<p id="p_5">not
	act in a manner injurious to the interest of the prosecution;
</p>	  

<p id="p_6">maintain
	law and order;
</p>	  

<p id="p_7">mark
	his presence before the concerned Police station on every 1st
	and 15th day of English Calendar month between 11:00 am
	to 2:00 pm:
</p>	  

<p id="p_8">not
	leave the State of Gujarat without prior permission of the Sessions 
	Judge concerned;
</p>	  

<p id="p_9">furnish
	the address of his residence at the time of execution of the bond
	and shall not change the residence without prior permission of  this
	Court;
</p>	  

<p id="p_10">surrender
	his passport, if any, to the Lower Court immediately. 
</p>	

 

 


 

<p id="p_11">	If
breach of any of the above conditions is committed,  the Sessions
Judge concerned will be free  to take appropriate action in the
matter.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_12">	Bail
before the Lower Court having jurisdiction to try the case.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_13">	Rule
is made absolute. Application is disposed of accordingly.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_14">	Direct
service is permitted.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_15">(Akil
Kureshi,J.)
 
	 


	(ashish)
