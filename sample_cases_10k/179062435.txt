<div class="doc_title">Vikas Yadav @ Vikas Singh And Ors vs State Of Haryana And Ors on 22 February, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">            IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                         AT CHANDIGARH

                                   Crl. Misc. No.M-5923 of 2017
                                   Date of decision:- 22.02.2017

Vikas Yadav @ Vikas Singh and others                                 ...Petitioners

                            Versus

State of Haryana and others                                        ...Respondents

CORAM: HON'BLE MS. JUSTICE RITU BAHRI

Present:-     Mr. Abhimanyu Singh, Advocate
              for the petitioners.

              ***
</pre>
<p id="p_1">RITU BAHRI J.(Oral)

              Petitioners have filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C for

issuance of direction to respondent No. 1 to 3 not to harass, humiliate and

victimize them at the instance of respondent No. 4 (enstranged wife of

petitioner No. 1).
</p>
<p id="p_2">              The grievance of the petitioners is that they are in private job

and are working on high posts in their respective Companies at Ghaziabad

and Gurgaon. The police is repeatedly harassing other family members by

threatening false implication in false dowry case.

</p><p id="p_3">              Keeping in view the above factual position, the present petition

at this stage is being disposed of by giving a direction to official

respondents to give 7 days prior notice, as contemplated under <a href="/doc/1957834/" id="a_2">Section 160</a>

Cr.P.C, in case, petitioners are sought to be investigated in any complaints

pending with the police.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">February 22, 2017                                  ( RITU BAHRI )
G Arora                                                JUDGE
