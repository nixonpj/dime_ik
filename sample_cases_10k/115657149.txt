<p id="p_1">       The petitioners in these writ petitions are stage carriage

operators. In these writ petitions they seek an order directing the

Secretary of the concerned Regional Transport Authority to issue

clearance certificates releasing their respective stage carriages

without insisting on the surrender or cancellation of the permit.

Pursuant to the interim orders passed by this Court the clearance

certificates have been issued. The respondents have no case that

any amount whatsoever is due from the respective petitioners. It

is also brought to my notice the clearance certificates have been

issued upon being satisfied that no amount is due from any of the

petitioners by way of arrears of motor vehicles tax and that no

departmental action in the form of check reports or otherwise, is

pending against them.
</p>
<p id="p_2">W.P(C).No.12811 of 2011
and connected cases
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  -:2:-</span>

</p><p id="p_3">       2.   In such circumstances I am of the opinion that no

further orders are called for in these writ petitions.     They are

accordingly closed, with the observation that the clearance

certificates issued pursuant to the interim orders passed by this

Court in these cases shall be treated as regular for all purposes.
