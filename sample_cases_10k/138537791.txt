<div class="doc_title">Sukari Devi &amp; Anr vs State Of Bihar on 24 November, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             Cr.Misc. No.36533 of 2008
                     1. SUKARI DEVI W/O LATE BHOLA YADAV
                     2. ANITA DEVI W/O ARUN YADAV
                        ( BOTH ARE R/O VILL-BELA, P.S. KHAIRA,DIST.
                        JAMUI)
                                       Versus
                                STATE OF BIHAR
                                      -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">02   24.11.2008                     Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the

                      State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                    Petitioners are accused in a case registered for the

                      offences under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections 323</a>,<a href="/doc/1868826/" id="a_2">498(A)</a> of the Indian Penal Code and

                      Section Â¾ of the <a href="/doc/1763444/" id="a_3">Dowry Prohibition Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                    It is submitted on behalf of the petitioners that the

                      present complaint has been filed in order to harass the petitioners.

                      Prior to filing of this complaint, the father of the complainant has

                      lodged an F.I.R. with similar allegations however only against the

                      husband of the present complainant. It is further submitted that the

                      petitioner no.1 is an old lady aged about 70 years and suffering

                      from several diseases of serious nature and there is no specific

                      allegation against these two petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                    Considering the above, in the event of arrest or

                      surrender within a period of four weeks from today, the petitioner

                      nos. 1 to 2, namely, Sukari Devi and Anita Devi are directed to be

                      released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- ( ten

                      thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                      satisfaction of the Sub Divisional Judicial Magistrate, Jamui, in

                      connection with Complaint Case no. 793C/ 2007 subject to the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             2</span>




      conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_4">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of

      Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><pre id="pre_2">Sym                               ( Shailesh Kumar Sinha, )
