                                        Dipankar Sikdar

<p id="p_4">                      Mr. Amit Banerjee,
                      Mr. Suranjan Mandal,
                      Mr. Sukumar Bhuniya.

</p>
<p id="p_6">                      Mr. Amal Kumar Sen,
                      Mr. Sabyasachi Mondal.
</p><p id="p_7">                                      ... for the alleged contemnor.
</p>


<p id="p_8">                      The alleged contemnor says that in terms of the order dated
              26th August 2014, the entire payment has been made to the
              petitioner/applicant and hands over a chart containing the
              breakup and the statements of payment. Let the said chart be kept
              with the record.
</p><p id="p_9">                      The learned advocate for the petitioner/applicant says that
              entire interest has not been paid.
</p><p id="p_10">                      On perusing the said chart, this Court does not find any
              substance in submission of the petitioner. A sum of Rs. 30,378/-
              has been paid towards the interest at the rate indicated in the said
              order for the period between 1st April 2014 to 25th September
              2014.
</p><p id="p_11">                      Since the compliance has been made, there is no point in
              keeping the contempt application pending.
</p><p id="p_12">                      The contempt application is, thus, dismissed.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                          2</span>


<p id="p_13">     There shall, however, be no order as to costs.
</p>
