ORDER:
</pre><p id="p_1">                An application u/s 15 (2) of the <a href="/doc/794158/" id="a_1">Payment of Wages Act</a>, 1936 
was   filed   by   the   applicant   stating   that   he   was   working   with   the 
management since 12.03.1993 as electrician. His last drawn wages were a 
sum of  Rs. 6800/­ per month. The management did  not pay his  earned 


PWA No. 7/12                                                                                    1/2
 wages since December, 2011. He demanded the  same due to which the 
management   got   annoyed   and   illegally   terminated   his   services   on 
10.03.2012 on the pretext of closure of the management. The management 
did   not   pay   his   Earned   wages,   retrenchment   compensation,   notice   pay, 
bonus, leave pay etc. The details of his dues against the management have 
been   mentioned   in   Annexure   A.   He   sent   a   demand   notice     to   the 
management   asking   for   his   aforesaid  dues   but   the   management  did  not 
reply. A request is made that directions be given to the management to pay 
the same to him at the earliest. The management be penalized for delay in 
making   the   payment   and   compensation   may   be   awarded   to   him   as   per 
<a href="/doc/1106018/" id="a_2">section 15</a> of Payment of Wages Act, 1936.
</p><p id="p_2">                           A notice of the aforesaid application was sent to the 
management. Today, Shri Rajesh Kumar Verma appeared on behalf of the 
management. He filed his vakalatnama and memo of settlement and receipt 
and stated that matter has been amicably settled. The workman also stated 
that the management has paid him a sum of Rs. 41,849/­ after deducting 
the amount of loan of Rs. 1,49,200/­ from his settlement amount of Rs. 
1,91,049/­. The statement of the applicant was recorded separately in this 
regard.
</p><p id="p_3">             In view of aforesaid facts, it is apparent that matter is amicably 
been   settled   between   the   parties   fully   and   finally.   Hence,   application   is 
disposed off as settled.</p><pre id="pre_2">


ANNOUNCED IN THE OPEN COURT                                        (CHANDRA SHEKHAR)
ON 18th AUGUST' 2012                                               POLC­V:KKD:DELHI 



PWA No. 7/12                                                                             2/2
