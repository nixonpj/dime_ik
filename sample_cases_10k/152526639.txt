<div class="doc_title">Navroop Singh @ Navi And Another vs State Of Punjab on 28 October, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">            Crl. Misc. No. M-31227 of 2013                                         -1-


                  In the High Court of Punjab and Haryana at Chandigarh

                                               Crl. Misc. No. M-31227 of 2013
                                               Date of Decision: 28.10.2013.

            Navroop Singh @ Navi and another                        .......Petitioners


                                                 Versus


            State of Punjab                                         ......Respondent

            CORAM: HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA

            Present:           Mr. T.S.Sangha, Senior Advocate with
                               Mr. J.S.Lalli, Advocate
                               for the petitioners.

                               Mr. Deep Singh, AAG, Punjab.

                               Mr. Amit Jhanji, Advocate
                               for the complainant.

                                    ****

            SABINA, J.
</pre>

<p id="p_1">                               Petitioners have filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a>

            of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking anticipatory

            bail in FIR No. 127 dated 31.7.2013 under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_2">Section 307</a>, <a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_3">452</a>,

            <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_4">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_6">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_7">149</a>, <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_8">427</a>, <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_9">354</a> of the Indian Penal Code, 1860,

            <a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_10">Section 25</a> of the Arms Act, 1959 and <a href="/doc/42436223/" id="a_11">Section 3</a>/<a href="/doc/138583315/" id="a_12">4</a> of Scheduled

            Castes and <a href="/doc/25085007/" id="a_13">Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act</a>, 1989

            (added later on), registered at Police Station Dharamkot, District

            Moga.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               While issuing notice of motion, following order was

            passed by this Court on 20.9.2013:-

</p><p id="p_3">                               "Notice of motion for 28.10.2013.

                               List along with Criminal Miscellaneous No. M-29691
Singh Gurpreet
2013.10.28 16:09
I attest to the accuracy and
integrity of this document
chandigarh
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Crl. Misc. No. M-31227 of 2013                                          -2-</span>


                               of 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               In the meantime, in the event of arrest, petitioners be

                               admitted to interim bail subject to the satisfaction of

                               the Arresting Officer. They shall abide by the

                               conditions envisaged under <a href="/doc/50707649/" id="a_14">Section 438</a> (2)<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_15"> of the

                               Code</a> of Criminal Procedure, 1973."

                               Learned State counsel, who is assisted by Assistant

            Sub Inspector Balwinder Singh, has submitted that in terms of

            the above order, petitioners have joined investigation.

</p><p id="p_5">                               Accordingly, interim bail granted by this Court to the

            petitioners vide order dated 20.9.2013 is made absolute.

</p><p id="p_6">                               Petition stands disposed of accordingly.




</p><p id="p_7">                                                                 (SABINA)
                                                                  JUDGE
            October 28, 2013
            Gurpreet




Singh Gurpreet
2013.10.28 16:09
I attest to the accuracy and
integrity of this document
chandigarh
