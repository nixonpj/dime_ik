<div class="doc_title">Tuntun Agrawal Alias Anup Kumar ... vs The State Of Jharkhand on 21 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">         IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                     Cr.M.P. No. 2892 of 2016
                                 -----------
         1.    Tuntun Agrawal @ Anup Kumar Agrawal.
         2.    Pandey @ Sabua Murmu.
         3.    Shyam Dactor @ Bishwaketu Mandal......Petitioners
                                   Versus
               The State of Jharkhand.           ....Opposite Party
                           -----
         Coram: HON'BLE MR JUSTICE RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY
                                           -----
         For the Petitioners       : Mr. Pandey Neeraj Rai, Advocate
         For the State             : A.P.P.
                                   -----
         I.A. No. 5034 of 2017

02/21.8.2017</pre><p id="p_1">       Heard the parties.
</p><p id="p_2">                   This application has been preferred by the petitioners for
         amending paragraph 1 of the main application in view of subsequent
         development, by virtue of which, charge has been framed against the
         petitioners on 15.12.2016 for the offence under <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_1">section 414</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the
         Indian Penal Code, <a href="/doc/1195854/" id="a_3">Section 4</a> of the Mines and Minerals (Development
         and <a href="/doc/1489134/" id="a_4">Regulation) Act</a>, 1957 and <a href="/doc/1914151/" id="a_5">Section 33</a> of the Indian Forest Act.
</p><p id="p_3">                   It appears that initially the petitioners had prayed for quashing
         the entire criminal proceeding including the order dated 4.5.2016, by
         which cognizance had been taken by the learned court below. During
         pendency of the application, charge has been framed against the
         petitioners on 15.12.2016, which the petitioners intend to challenge
         by incorporating the same in the main application.
</p><p id="p_4">                   Since prayer made in this application has a direct nexus with
         the prayer made in the original application and in order to avoid
         multiplicity of proceeding, this application is allowed.
</p><p id="p_5">                   Let   I.A. No. 5034 of 2017 be treated as a part of the main
         application.
</p><p id="p_6">         Cr.M.P. No. 2892 of 2016
                   List this case under the heading "For Admission" on 11th
         September, 2017.
</p><p id="p_7">                                                  (Rongon Mukhopadhyay,J)
         Rakesh/
 </p>
