                                               Date of Decision: 21.04.2017


Harmeet Kaur and another
                                                               ..... Petitioners

                                          Versus

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE DEEPAK SIBAL

Present:     Mr. Kanwarjit Singh Dhillon, Advocate
             for the petitioners.

             ****
</pre><p id="p_1">DEEPAK SIBAL J. (ORAL)

             Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that the

petitioners have married each other against the wishes of the parents of

petitioner No.1 and on account of this act on their part, they are under

threat of being eliminated at the hands of respondents No. 4 to 6. In

view of the above, seeking protection, a representation dated

17.04.2017 (Annexure P-5) was made on behalf of the petitioners to

the Commissioner of Police, Amritsar, but to no avail.

</p><p id="p_2">             In   the   above    circumstances,      the    petitioners   have

approached this Court through the present petition.

</p><p id="p_3">             Without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

matter, particularly the validity of the alleged marriage, the present

petition is disposed of with a direction to the Commissioner of Police,

Amritsar (respondent No.2) to forthwith consider the aforesaid

representation dated 17.04.2017 (Annexure P-5) made on behalf of the
