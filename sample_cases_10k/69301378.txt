<p id="p_1">     The petitioner is a stage carriage operator in the route

Mamenkara - Munderi (Via) Edakkara, Kalasar, Palunda,

Uppada and Pothukallu. The permit was issued to his stage

carriage bearing registration No.KL-10-S-5782. He obtained

clearance certificate with respect to the above vehicle and

thereafter he sought for replacement of the vehicle by

Ext.P4 by producing the records of stage carriage bearing

registration No. KL-10-S-5607.     However, the Secretary,

RTA has declined consideration of the application for

replacement and on the ground that the outgoing vehicle is

older than the one sought to be replaced.

</p><p id="p_2">     2. This Court had considered the said issue in W.P(C)

No. 3553/2013 dated 06.12.2013 and had held that though

discretion is vested in the respondent under Rule 174(2) of

the Kerala Motor Vehicle Rules, 1989 to assess the merits

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">W.P.(C) No.25927 of 2014 - M        2</span>



and demerits of a vehicle, it cannot be extended to merely

looking at the model or date of registration of the vehicle.

In such circumstances, Ext.P4 shall           be reconsidered in

accordance with law, without looking into the model, but

only considering the viability of the said vehicle to be

operated on the route in question.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            The writ petition is disposed of.
</p>
