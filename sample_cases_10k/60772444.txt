                               WP No.209 of 2003
                              GA No.2624 of 2013
                       IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                                ORIGINAL SIDE


                   RAJESH PAWARVersusUNION OF INDIA
 BEFORE:
The Hon'ble JUSTICE HARISH TANDON
Date : 18th September, 2013.

                                                    Mr.Sagar Bandyopadhyay, Adv.
                                                        Ms.Sema Kar Ghosh, Adv.
                                                         Mr.Niladri Banerjee, Adv.
                                                                ..for the petitioner


       The Court: Despite service there is no representation on behalf of the

respondent. The letter dated 04.09.2013 showing the service of the copy of the</pre>

<p id="p_1">application filed today be kept on record. This is an application in substance for

restoration of the writ petition which was dismissed for default on 25.02.2010.

The cause for non-appearance on the said date is narrated in paragraphs 5, 6

and 7 are found sufficient.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      This Court finds that the petitioner was prevented by sufficient cause for

not appearing on the day when the writ petitioner was dismissed for default.

</p><p id="p_3">      The application is, thus, allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      The writ petition is restored to its original file and number.

      By the said order dated 25.02.2010 interim order which was granted

earlier was also vacated. Since the matter has already restored, this Court does

not find any justification in not passing the interim order.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           2</span>


</p><p id="p_5">      Accordingly, there shall be a stay of the operation of the order of

confiscation and payment of penalty of the balance amount impugned in this

application until further order of this Court.

</p><p id="p_6">      Let the writ petition be listed in the combined monthly list of cases for the

month of December 2013 "For Hearing".
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">                                                            (HARISH TANDON, J.)
