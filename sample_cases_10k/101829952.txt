<div class="doc_title">Mohammed Junaid vs State on 23 February, 2018</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
              S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail No. 2110 / 2018
Mohammed Junaid S/o Usman Gani, Aged About 37 Years, By
Caste Muslim, R/o 2999, Marwadi Ka Dehla, Near Teen Darwaja,
Dalgarwad, Ahmedabad, Gujrat. (At Present Lodged At Central
Jail, Jodhpur)
</p><p id="p_2">                                                         ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State of Rajasthan
</p><p id="p_3">                                                     ----Respondent
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)    : Mr. Bhushan Singh Charan
For Respondent(s) : Mr. KV Vyas,
_____________________________________________________
         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MANOJ KUMAR GARG
                               Order
23/02/2018

     The petitioner has been arrested in connection with FIR

No.59/2009 of Police Station Dangiyawas, Jodhpur for the offence

punishable under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_2">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_4">471</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120B</a> IPC. He has

preferred this bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_6">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_4">     Counsel for the petitioner submits that co-accused namely

Sagar Khan and Shaitan Singh have already been enlarged on bail

by this Court and the case of the present petitioner is similar to

that of the co-accused. The offences are triable by the Magistrate.

The accused-petitioner is in judicial custody and the trial of the

case will take sufficient long time to be concluded. Therefore, the

benefit of bail should be granted to the accused-petitioner.


</p><p id="p_5">     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.
</p><p id="p_6">                                (2 of 2)
                                                       [CRLMB-2110/2018]

     Having regard to the totality of the facts and circumstances

of the case, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail to the accused

petitioner under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_7">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_7">     Accordingly, the bail application filed under Sec.439 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_8">Cr.P.C</a>. is

allowed and it is directed that petitioner Mohammed Junaid S/o

Usman Gani shall be released on bail in connection with FIR

No.59/2009 of Police Station Dangiyawas, Jodhpur provided he

executes a personal bond in a sum of Rs.50,000/- with two sound

and solvent sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of

learned trial court for his appearance before that court on each

and every date of hearing and whenever called upon to do so till

the completion of the trial.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                          (MANOJ KUMAR GARG)J.
</p>
<p id="p_9">Ms/-79
