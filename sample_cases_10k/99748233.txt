<div class="doc_title">Ram Prasad Mahto &amp; Ors vs State Of Bihar &amp; Anr on 10 July, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.22771 of 2016
                         Arising Out of PS. Case No.-1 Year-2012 Thana- JALE District- Darbhanga
                 ======================================================
                 Ram Prasad Mahto &amp; Ors.

                                                                                    ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar &amp; Anr.


                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :        Mr. Md. Soban Asghar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :        Mr. C.Jawahar(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SANJAY PRIYA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   10-07-2018                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that for the

                  same offence three cases have been filed by the informant against

                  petitioner. One case is pending in the Sarpanch Gram Kutchery vide

                  S.DE. no. 109 of 2011. Second case is complaint case no. 2120 of

                  2011 and the third case is Jalley P.S. case no. 1 of 2012 in which

                  cognizance has been taken by the learned CJM, Darbhanga on

                  30.3.2012 and the case is at present pending in the Court of A.A.

                  Sahey J.M. 1st Class, Darbhanga.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that petitioners

                  have filed Cr. Rev. before the Sessions Judge and by the impugned

                  order the learned Additional Sessions Judge, Darbhanga has ordered

                  to club the Jalley P.S. case no. 1 of 2012 with complaint case no.

                  2120 of 2011 and both the records are attached. But the learned

                  Magistrate is proceeding in both the matters separately.
            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.22771 of 2016(3) dt.10-07-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                             Issue notice to the opposite party No.2 to show cause as to

                    why this application be not heard and/or disposed off at the

                    admission stage itself.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             Requisites both under registered cover with A/D as also under

                    ordinary process must be filed within one week, failing which this

                    application shall stand rejected without further reference to a Bench.

</p><p id="p_5">                             Call for Xerox copy of the entire order sheets of Jalley P.S.

                    case no. 1 of 2012 and C.R. No. 2120 of 2011 from the Court of the

                    3rd Addl. Sessions Judge, Darbhanga/concerned court so as to reach

                    this Court positively within a period of two weeks from the date of

                    receipt of copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                             The learned court below will also send report as to whether

                    both the cases are tried separately or they have been amalgamated

                    and whether cognizance has been taken separately in both the cases.

</p><p id="p_7">                             In the meanwhile, further proceeding in the Court below with

                    respect to both the cases will remain stayed.

</p><p id="p_8">                             Put up this case on 31.7. 2018




                                                   (Sanjay Priya, J)
shyambihari/-
</p>
