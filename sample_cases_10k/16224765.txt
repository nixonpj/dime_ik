ORDER</pre><p id="p_1"> (Per : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE RAVI
R.TRIPATHI)
 

 
 


 

	The
present application is filed by the convict through Jail seeking
temporary bail for a period of 60 days so as to  get his right eye
operated.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.	 RULE.
</p><p id="p_3"> Mr.H.K. Patel,  learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives service
of rule on  behalf of the respondent-State.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_4">3.	The learned
APP placed on record certificate dated 20th July 2012
issued by the Medical Officer, Central Jail Dispensary, Ahmedabad
Central Jail.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_5">4.	The
applicant- appellant- original accused has been convicted for the 
offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">sections 302</a> and <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a> of the Indian Penal
Code to undergo imprisonment for life with fine of Rs.6000/- in
default to further undergo imprisonment for 7 months as awarded vide
judgement dated 25th March 2008 by the learned Judge, Fast
Track Court No.4, Gandhidham in Sessions Case No. 29 of 2005.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_6">5.	The learned
APP has invited attention of the Court to the Jail remarks and also
to the application wherein it is mentioned that the convict got his
left eye operated on 28th May 2011 and certificate also
shows that his left eye vision is 6/12 with glasses, whereas his
vision in right eye - counting fingers 4 meters.  The convict
has undergone 7 years, 1 month and 4 days in Jail as on 27th
June 2012. The convict had enjoyed  temporary bail once and furlough
once. He has reported in time.  Nothing adverse is noticed from the
convict's Jail remarks. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_7">6.	For the
contents of the application, the same is allowed. The convict is
ordered to be released on  temporary bail for a period of 30
(thirty) days from the date of his release on his executing a
personal bond of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand only) to the
satisfaction of the Jail authorities.
</p> 
	 


	

 


<p id="p_8">7.	The convict
shall surrender himself to the Jail authorities on expiry of the
above temporary bail period.	 Rule is made absolute. 
</p>
 


 


 

 


 

<p id="p_9">(RAVI
R. TRIPATHI, J.)
 

 


 

 


 

 


 

(PARESH
UPADHYAY, J.)
