          CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 25135 of
                                                   2015

         ================================================================
                   KUTBUDDIN @ RAJA NIAMUDDIN SAIYED....Applicant(s)
                                      Versus
                        STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
         ================================================================
         Appearance:
         MEHUL A SURATI, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MS.NISHA THAKORE, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         RULE NOT RECD BACK for the Respondent(s) No. 2
         ================================================================

                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI

                                          Date : 04/01/2016


                                            ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.     By   way   of   the   present   application,   the   applicant   has   prayed   to 
                release him on temporary bail on the  ground of sickness of his 
                child. In support of the application, medical certificate is produced 
                on record. 
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.     In past, whenever the applicant came to be released on temporary 
                bail,   he   had   surrendered   late   except   on   one   occasion   he   had 
                surrendered in time.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.     Considering the above aspect, I am of the opinion that the present 
                application   requires   consideration   and   accordingly,   the   same   is 
                allowed. The under­trial prisoner is hereby ordered to be released 
                on  temporary bail  for a period of  three weeks from the date of 
                his actual release on usual terms and conditions and on executing
