                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

         CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR EXTENSION OF TEMPORARY BAIL) NO.
                                  13175 of 2017

                        In CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 10226 of 2017
                               In CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. 395 of 2014

         =================================================
                          DHANSHUKH MERUBHAI UMATH....Applicant(s)
                                             Versus
                             STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
         =================================================
         Appearance:
         HCLS COMMITTEE, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MS.AKSHITABA SOLANKI, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR.L.B.DABHI, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         =================================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE B.N. KARIA
                           Date : 24/05/2017
                             ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">         [1]             RULE. Mr.L.B.Dabhi, learned Additional Public Prosecutor
         waives service of notice of rule on behalf of the respondent - State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         [2]             The present application has been preferred                       by the
         applicant - convict seeking extension of temporary bail on the ground
         of applicant's procedure of pension and for getting document and
         certificate.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         [3]             Heard learned advocate for the applicant and learned
         A.P.P. for the respondent - State. Having read the application and
         perused the documents on record, no case is made out to extend the
         temporary bail.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         [4]             In view of the aforesaid facts and circumstances of the
         case, I am not inclined to use judicial discretion in favour of the convict
         to extend the temporary bail. The application stands rejected. Rule is
         discharged.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                                           (B.N. KARIA, J.)

         dharmendra
