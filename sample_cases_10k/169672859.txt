<div class="doc_title">Devi vs Unknown on 6 December, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">C-482 No.2716 of 2019
Hon'ble R.C. Khulbe, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">      Mr. B.S. Negi, learned Advocate for
the applicant.
</p><p id="p_2">      Mr. A.K. Sah, learned A.G.A. for the
State.
</p><p id="p_3">      Heard.
</p><p id="p_4">      This      criminal      miscellaneous
application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of Cr.P.C.
has been filed for an expedite of the
Criminal Revision No.176 of 2016, Anaro
Devi vs. Baisakhi Devi &amp; others, pending
before the Court of 4th Additional District
&amp; Session Judge, Dehradun.
</p><p id="p_5">      From the perusal of the record, the
applicant filed a Criminal Revision
No.176 of 2016, Anaro Devi vs. Baisakhi
Devi &amp; others, before the Sessions Judge
Dehradun,       which      was    ultimately
transferred to the 4 Additional District
                       th

and Session Judge Dehradun, against the
order dated 08.07.2016 passed by the
ACJM, First/ JM, Dehradun, which is
pending since 2016.
</p><p id="p_6">       Considering       the   facts    and
circumstances of the case, I dispose of
this petition by directing the court below
to try and conclude the proceedings of
Criminal Revision No.176 of 2016, Anaro
Devi vs. Baisakhi Devi &amp; others, pending
before the Court of 4th Additional District
and Session Judge, Dehradun, as early
as possible, within a period of two
months from the date of production of the
certified copy of this order.
</p><p id="p_7">      All pending applications also stand
disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                         (R.C. Khulbe, J.)
                           06.12.2019
Balwant
