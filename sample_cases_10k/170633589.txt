<p id="p_1">(BY SRI M Y KATAGI, ADVOCATE FOR R2 )

     THIS MFA IS FILED U/S 173(1) OF MV ACT 1988,
AGAINST THE JUDGMENT AND AWARD DATED:31-03-2008
PASSED IN MVC NO.3058/2005 ON THE FILE OF CIVIL
JUDGE(SR.DN) SAUNDATTI, PARTLY ALLOWING THE CLAIM
PETITION   FOR    COMPENSATION     AND    SEEKING
ENHANCEMENT OF COMPENSATION.
</p>
<p id="p_2">    THIS APPEAL COMING ON FOR CONCILIATION
BEFORE LOK ADALAT THE FOLLOWING ORDER IS PASSED.

</p><p id="p_3">                    CONCILIATION ORDER


     The learned counsel for the appellant and the learned

counsel    for   Respondent   No.2-Insurance   Company    are

present.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">     2. After due deliberations, the parties have arrived at a

settlement. The appellant-claimant has agreed to receive and

the Respondent No.2-Insurance Company has agreed to pay

global compensation of Rs.90,000/- [Rupees Ninety thousand
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              -3-</span>


only) inclusive of interest in addition to what has been

awarded by the Tribunal, in full and final settlement of the

claim. A joint memo is filed on behalf of the parties to this

effect.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">      3.    The respondent No.2-Insurance Company has

agreed to deposit the said amount before the Tribunal, within

six weeks from the date of preparation of award, failing which

the said amount shall carry interest at 9% p.a. from the date

of default, till the date of deposit. The Tribunal shall issue

Fixed Deposit slip to the claimant/s immediately after

deposit.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">      4. Out of the enhanced amount, 50% is agreed to be

invested in Fixed Deposit in the name of the claimant in any

Nationalised Bank/Post office for a period of three years and

the Bank is directed to release the Fixed Deposit amount on

maturity with accrued interest without insisting any further

orders from the Tribunal. The remaining 50% of the amount

shall be released in favour of the claimant.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                             -4-</span>




</p><p id="p_10">     5.    This Miscellaneous First Appeal stands disposed

of in terms of the joint memo. The award of the Tribunal shall

stand modified accordingly. Draw up the award accordingly.




</p><p id="p_11">                                       Sd/-
