                      Prayer: Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., to direct the
                      Inspector of Police, Sholavandan Police Station, Madurai, to execute the Non
                      Bailable Warrant pending against the respondent/accused in C.C.No.1769 of
                      2018 on the file of the learned Metropolitan Magistrate Fast Track Court No.III,
                      Saidapet, Chennai 15.


                                           For Petitioner     : M/s.T.S.Kani
                                                            ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                   This Criminal Original Petition has been filed seeking for a direction

                      to the second respondent to execute the Non Bailable Warrant pending against

                      the first respondent/accused in C.C.No.1769 of 2018 on the file of the learned



http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                               2</span>

                      Metropolitan Magistrate Fast Track Court -III, Saidapet, Chennai.



</p><p id="p_2">                             2.     It is seen from the records that the petitioner has filed a case in

                      C.C.No.1769 of 2018 against the first respondent/accused for the offence under

                      <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_2">Section 138</a> of the Negotiable Instruments Act.       The Court below has issued

                      summon to the accused and appeared before the Court below on 12.06.2018.

                      But the petitioner has not appeared before the Court below on 12.06.2018,

                      hence, the Court below issued Non Bailable Warrant against the accused and

                      posted the case to 16.11.2018. On 16.11.2018, the accused has not appeared

                      before the Court below. Again, the Court below issued Non Bailable Warrant

                      against the accused and posted the case to 05.04.2019. In spite of warrant of

                      arrest issued by the Court below twice, the second respondent police has not

                      executed the NBW pending against the accused. Hence, the petitioner filed this

                      petition.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">                             3.     Considering the facts and circumstance of the case, this Court

                      directs the second respondent to execute the Non Bailable Warrant issued as

                      against the first respondent, within a period of four weeks from the date of

                      receipt of a copy of this Order, if the warrant is pending on the file of the second

                      respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                             3</span>

</p><p id="p_6">                            4.     Accordingly, this Criminal Original Petition is disposed of with the

                      above direction.
</p>
