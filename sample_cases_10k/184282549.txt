<div class="doc_title">Khushwant Kumar Manjhi @ Sonu ... vs The State Of Bihar on 7 August, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.46520 of 2019
                       Arising Out of PS. Case No.-154 Year-2017 Thana- RIVILGANJ District- Saran
                 ======================================================
                 KHUSHWANT KUMAR MANJHI @ SONU KUMAR @ SONU MANJHI
                 @ SONU KUMAR MANJHI Son of Amrika Manjhi Resident of Village -
                 Nawada P.S.- Revilganj, Dist.- Saran at Chapra.

                                                                                   ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :        Mr.Rajesh Roy
                 For the Opposite Party/s :        Mr.Ashok Kumar Singh
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SANJAY PRIYA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   07-08-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                   learned counsel for the State .
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioner seeks bail in Revilganj               P.S. case no.

                   154 of 2017 instituted for the offence under <a href="/doc/653797/" id="a_1">Sections 304B</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a>

                   of the IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                The prayer for bail of the petitioner was earlier

                   rejected by this Court vide order dated 4.12.2018 passed in

                   Cr. Misc. no.        53734 of 2018 with direction to trial court to

                   expedite the trial.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                A supplementary affidavit has been filed on behalf of

                   petitioner stating therein that father of this petitioner died on

                   27.5.2019 and cremated at Revilganj Shamshan Ghat, Gauriya

                   Chapra. The petitioner is only son in his family who will do the
 Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.46520 of 2019(3) dt.07-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                            2/3</span>




          duty of his father with Hindu ritual because two brothers of the

          petitioner have died before death of                 father of petitioner.

          Therefore, he seeks             prayer to release the petitioner on

          provisional bail for a period of three weeks.

</p><p id="p_5">                       Looking into the submission made in para 3 of the

          supplementary affidavit, petitioner, above named, is directed to

          be released on provisional bail for a period of two weeks from

          the date of his release on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-

          (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

          satisfaction of the ACJM 8th Saran at Chapra in connection with

          Revilganj PS case no. 154 of 2017, G.R.No. 4322 of 2017

          subject to the condition that both the bailors shall be the close

          relative of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                       It is made clear that petitioner after performing the

          last rituals of his father shall surrender before the Court below

          within two weeks from the                 date of his release. The Court

          below shall thereafter send surrender certificate to this Court.

</p><p id="p_7">                       In the meantime, call for the legible Xerox copy of

          case diary, status report regarding present stage of the trial of

          above noted case along with Xerox copy of the up-to-date

          entire order sheets          and the time likely to be consumed in

          disposal of the trial, from the Court of the learned ACJM-8th
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.46520 of 2019(3) dt.07-08-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       3/3</span>




                     Saran at Chapra so as to reach this Court positively within a

                     period of two weeks from date of receipt of copy of this order

                                  Put up this case on 27.8.2019 within first five case.



</p><p id="p_8">                                                  (Sanjay Priya, J)
shyambihari/-
</p>
