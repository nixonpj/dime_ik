appellant so that he can prefer the appeal before the concerned Court.

</p><p id="p_3">       As the delay has already been condoned by this Court in the IA (Crl.) No. 353/2018, it is

directed that the learned trial Court or the appellate Court/Sessions Court will not raise any point

of delay.
