                              JUDGMENT

<p id="p_2">      The petitioner operates a quarry having all the requisite

licenses. He apprehended threat from respondents 5 to 8 to his

activities and complained to the police. Ventilating his grievance

that the police have not chosen to act on his complaint, the

petitioner filed this writ petition.

</p><p id="p_3">      2. The learned Government Pleader, on instructions, has

submitted that the contesting respondents have been agitating on

the ground that there is an environmental threat.

</p><p id="p_4">      3. As we see from the records filed along with I.A.14916/17,

the petitioner has the quarrying license subsisting till 28.9.2017.

So the petitioner is entitled to carry on his business till then.
W.P.(C)No.28368 of 2017
                                 :   2 :
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">       4. At this juncture, the learned Government Pleader has also

submitted that the police have already registered a crime, acting

on the complaint made by the petitioner's driver.

</p><p id="p_7">       5. In these circumstances, we clarify that if the petitioner

faces any threat to his person or property, he may as well

approach the police who will act in accordance with law. On the

other hand, if the contesting respondents have any genuine

grievance about the alleged environmental threat, they can

approach the Governmental authorities and ventilate their

grievance.
</p>
<p id="p_8">       With these observations, we dispose of the writ petition.
