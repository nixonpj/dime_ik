            Dated this the 21st day of July, 2010

                              ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     Petitioner who is the subject of a "man missing" case

registered as Crime No.105/2007 before the Pazhayangadi

Police Station, seeks a direction to the Judicial Magistrate of

the First Class, Payyannur to record her statement and to set

her free along with her minor son Muhammed Sahir.

</p><p id="p_2">     2.    The letter dated 19/7/2010 from the learned

Magistrate shows that on the police filing a U.D. report the

learned Magistrate after notice to the de facto complainant in

the case had accepted the case and dropped the proceedings.

If so, the petitioner is not required in connection with the

aforesaid case either before the Pazhzyangadi Police Station

or before the Magistrate. Recording the above this Criminal

M.C. is closed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">


</p><p id="p_4">                                          V.RAMKUMAR, JUDGE
