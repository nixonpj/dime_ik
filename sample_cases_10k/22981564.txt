            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ARUN BHANSALI
                               Order
19/01/2018

     The present writ petitions have been filed by the petitioners,

seeking a direction to the respondents to grant the pay scale of

Sub Inspector to the petitioners on account of second benefit

under selection grade.
</p><p id="p_70">                                    (6 of 6)



      It is submitted that the issue raised in the present writ

petitions is no more res integra as this Court in the case of State

of Rajasthan v. Banne Khan : S.A.W. No. 763/2011, decided on

02.08.2011       and    by   various   other   orders   has   settled   the

controversy.
</p>
<p id="p_71">      It is further submitted that the representation made by the

petitioners has not been properly dealt with by the respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_72">      In view of the submissions made, the writ petitions filed by

the petitioners are disposed of requiring the petitioners to submit

copy of the present writ petitions alongwith a copy of this order to

the appropriate authority. The appropriate authority is directed to

consider and deal with the petitioner's grievances by reasoned

speaking order within a period of eight weeks from the date of

submission of this order.       It is made clear that even thereafter

petitioners' grievance survive, they will be at liberty to take

recourse to appropriate proceedings.
</p>
<p id="p_73">                                                    (ARUN BHANSALI)J.
