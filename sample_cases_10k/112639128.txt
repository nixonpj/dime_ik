                    BENCH AT JAIPUR

     S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 11253/2019

Lalu@lala@rohitash@rajendra S/o Shri Chunni Lal Bairwa B/c
Bairwa, R/o B-99 Ayodhya Nagar Lalpura Vaishali Nagar Ps Karni
Vihar Jaipur (Presently Lodged In Central Jail Jaipur)
                                                                      ----Petitioner
                                       Versus
State Of Rajasthan, through PP
                                                                    ----Respondent</pre>


<pre id="pre_2">For Petitioner(s)            :     Mr. Sudhir Jain.
For State                    :     Mr. Ganesh Saini, PP.



            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PANKAJ BHANDARI

                                        Order

27/08/2019

</pre><p id="p_1">1.     Petitioner has filed this bail application under Section

439 of Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">2.     F.I.R. No.902/2018 was registered at Police Station Muhana,

Jaipur, for offence under Sections 457, 380 of I.P.C.


</p><p id="p_4">3.     It is contended by counsel for the petitioner that no recovery

has been effected from the petitioner. In the earlier cases lodged

against the petitioner, petitioner stands acquitted in fifteen cases.

Most of the cases were prior to the year 2009. It is contended that

petitioner was arrested in one case and was subsequently made

accused in other case. Charge-sheet has been filed. Conclusion of

trial will take time.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">4.     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.


</p><p id="p_7">5.     I have considered the contentions.
</p>
<p id="p_8"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                        (Downloaded on 29/08/2019 at 09:07:40 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_9">                                                                       (2 of 2)                     [CRLMB-11253/2019]


</p><p id="p_10">                                   6.    Considering the contentions put forth by counsel for the

                                   petitioner, I deem it proper to allow the bail application.


</p><p id="p_11">                                   7.    This bail application is accordingly allowed and it is directed

                                   that accused petitioner shall be released on bail provided he

                                   furnishes a personal bond in the sum of Rs.1,00,000/- (Rupees

                                   One Lac only) together with two sureties in the sum of

                                   Rs.50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) each to the satisfaction

                                   of the learned trial court with the stipulation that he shall appear

                                   before that Court and any court to which the matter is transferred,

                                   on all subsequent dates of hearing and as and when called upon to

                                   do so.
</p>
<p id="p_12">
</p><p id="p_13">                                                                                           (PANKAJ BHANDARI),J

                                   CHANDAN /41
