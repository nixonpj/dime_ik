<p id="p_1">  Date : 10th March, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                                                              Appearance:
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                  Mr. Aashif Hussain, Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
      The Court: The award is less than two years old. The said award has now become

deemed decree. It is submitted that the award is not under challenge. The award has

now become enforceable.      It is submitted on behalf of the decree-holder that the

hypothecated asset was repossessed and sold during the pendency during the pendency

of the arbitration proceeding and the present application is filed for realization of the

balance claim under the award. Considering the averments made in the said affidavit in

support of the Tabular Statement there shall be an order in terms of prayer (a). The

judgment debtors are directed to file their respective affidavit of assets on or before the

returnable date.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      The advocate on record for the decree holder is directed to server a copy of this

application along with a copy of this order upon the judgment debtors in course of this

week by speed post with acknowledgement due and shall file the affidavit of service on

the returnable date.
</p>
<p id="p_6">      The application is made returnable on 7th April, 2015.

      Urgent certified website copies of this order, if applied for, be urgently supplied to

the parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.
