                                   ... for the State


             Affidavit of service filed in Court today be kept with the
        record. Service is complete. Accordingly, the writ petition is
        taken up for hearing.
</p><p id="p_2">             Heard the learned advocates of the respective parties.
             Upon hearing the learned advocates of the respective parties,
        I am of the view that there is a scope of investigation into the
        matter. Accordingly, the writ petition is admitted and it shall be
        heard on affidavits.
</p><p id="p_3">             The respondents may file an affidavit-in-opposition to the
        writ petition by two weeks after ensuing the Summer Vacation; and
        reply thereto, if any, may be filed within two weeks thereafter.
</p><p id="p_4">             Let the matter appear for hearing in the combined monthly
        list of July, 2013.
</p><p id="p_5">             As regards interim order, I find from the materials on record
        that the final practice teaching has already been started on 8th
        May, 2013 and the B. Ed. Examination is scheduled to be held on
        and from May 23, 2013. So, if the petitioner is not granted any
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                        2</span>

interim order, he may be seriously prejudiced, there may be loss
of one year, etc.
       This being the position, I am of the view that there is an
extreme urgency in granting the interim order in the nature as
prayed for in prayer (b) of the writ petition appearing at page
</p><p id="p_6">13. The interim order is granted accordingly. Such interim order
shall remain in force till the end of July, 2013 or until further
order, whichever is earlier.
</p><p id="p_7">       For clarification as to the interim order, it is recorded
that   the   respondent   university       shall   allow   the   petitioner   to
participate in the ensuing B. Ed. Examination, which is scheduled
to be held on and from May 23, 2013 onwards provisionally and to
do all the needful so that there may not be any difficulty for the
petitioner to appear at the said examination.
</p><p id="p_8">       However, such interim order shall abide by the decision of
this writ petition.
</p><p id="p_9">       Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied
for, be supplied to the parties at an early date.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                                                            (Prasenjit Mandal, J)
