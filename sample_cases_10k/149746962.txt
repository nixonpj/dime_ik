<div class="doc_title">Mahendran vs The State on 13 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 13.04.2018  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE G.R.SWAMINATHAN              

Crl.O.P.(MD)No.5882 of 2018 

Mahendran                                       ... Petitioner

Vs.

1.The State
   Rep. by the Inspector of Police,
   Kallal Police Station,
   Sivagangai District.
   (Crime No.129/2014)

2.Thirugnam                                          ... Respondents

        Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of
Criminal Procedure, to call for the records in C.C.No.147 of 2014 filed
before the Judicial Magistrate Court, Karaikudi, Sivagangai District and
quash the same. 

For Petitioner           : Mr.S.Vanchinathan
^For R1          : Mr.Prabhu Ramachandran          
                                 Government Advocate (Crl. Side)

        For R2            : Mr.J.Prabhu


:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        Today, when the matter is taken up for hearing, the second
respondent/de-facto complainant is present before this Court.  His identity
is duly verified by the first respondent police also.  The parties have
amicably settled the dispute between themselves.  Therefore, no useful
purpose will be served in permitting the prosecution to continue.  Therefore,
the impugned proceedings relating to C.C.No.147 of 2014 on the file of the
Judicial Magistrate Court, Karaikudi, Sivagangai District, shall stand
quashed and this Criminal Original Petition is allowed accordingly.
</p>

<p id="p_2">To

</p><p id="p_3">1.The Judicial Magistrate,
   Karaikudi,
   Sivagangai District.
</p>
<p id="p_4">2.The Inspector of Police,
   Kallal Police Station,
   Sivagangai District.
</p>
<p id="p_5">3.The Additional Public Prosecutor,
   Madurai Bench of Madras High Court,
   Madurai.
</p>
