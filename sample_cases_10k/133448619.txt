 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR

ORDER

S.B. CRIMINAL MISC. PETITION NO. 2077/2010

SURESH KUMAR VS. UNION OF INDIA &amp; ANOTHER.


DATE OF ORDER                          :                                   22.05.2013



HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE NARENDRA KUMAR JAIN-II

Mr. Suresh Sahni, for the accused-petitioner.
Mr. Tej Prakash Sharma, Special Public Prosecutor, for Respondent No. 1-CBI.</pre>

<p id="p_1">		Learned Special Public Prosecutor appearing on behalf of Respondent No. 1-CBI produced order dated 02.03.2013 passed by Additional Sessions Judge, No. 4, Jaipur District, Jaipur, whereby the revisional court has dismissed the revision petition filed against the order framing charge in Criminal Case No. 2/2011, State Vs. Radhey Shyam &amp; Others for perusal of the Court which is taken on record and submitted that in view of this order, this misc. petition has rendered infructuous.
		Learned counsel for the petitioner has not objected and controverted this fact at this stage.
		Criminal misc. petition is dismissed as having become infructuous.  
</p><p id="p_2">		Stay Application No. 1953/2012 also stands dismissed.
</p> 		 
<p id="p_3">                 (NARENDRA KUMAR JAIN-II),J.
</p><p id="p_4">           Manoj, 
S.No. 33.
</p><p id="p_5">All corrections made in the judgment/order have been incorporated in the judgment/order being emailed.
MANOJ NARWANI
JUNIOR PERSONAL ASSISTANT.
</p>
