<p id="p_1">                     Having considered the report of the District Magistrate,

             Burdwan dated 21st April, 2015 that the godown or warehouse of

             the petitioner does not fall within Sashpur, Satapati, Bandebaj,

             Hanspukur, Medgachi Paikpara area, this writ application has to

             be dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                     All the papers are before this Court. No affidavits were

             invited. The allegations contained in the petition are deemed not

             to have been admitted.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                     This writ application is accordingly dismissed. No order

             as to costs.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                         2</span><p id="p_6">


        Urgent certified photocopy of this order, if applied for, be

made available to the parties upon compliance with the requisite

formalities.
</p>

</p><p id="p_8">                                             (I.P. Mukerji, J.)
