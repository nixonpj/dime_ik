<p id="p_1">            With the trial still to effectively commence, with none out of the 11

prosecution witnesses still examined, without making any comment on the actual

merits of the case for or against the petitioners, even though learned State counsel

on instructions from the police official who is present in Court today to assist him,

submits that a similar case has also been registered against the petitioner,

Mohammad Rahis @ Raju (in CRM-M-32364-2019), the petition is allowed. The

petitioners (in both petitions) shall be admitted to bail upon their furnishing

adequate bail bonds and surety bonds to the satisfaction of the learned trial Court.
