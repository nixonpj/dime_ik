<div class="doc_title">Jaiprakash Tiwari &amp; Others vs State Of Uttarakhand &amp; Others on 22 May, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1"> IN THE HIGH COURT OF UTTARAKHAND AT NAINITAL
  Criminal Miscellaneous Application No. 587 of 2015
                (Under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C.)
Jai Prakash Tiwari
&amp; others                                             ........... Applicants

                                      Versus

State of Uttarakhand &amp; others                        ...... Respondents

Present: Mr. Vipul Sharma, Advocate, present for the applicants.
           Mr. K.S. Rautela, learned Government Advocate, present for the State of
           Uttarakhand.
           Mr. D.C.S. Rawat, Advocate, present for the respondent nos. 3 &amp; 4.


Hon'ble Sudhanshu Dhulia, J. (Oral)
</pre><p id="p_1">1.                 All the applicants as well as respondent nos. 3 and
4 are present before this Court today. The curious facts in this
case are that an F.I.R. was lodged by the police at Police Station
Nehru Colony, Dehradun, wherein there were allegations that
the present applicants were allegedly trying to sell an antique
coin to the respondent nos. 3 and 4 for a hefty amount, an F.I.R.
was registered against the applicants under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Section 420</a> of
I.P.C., on such information being given by the police.
</p><p id="p_2">2.                 Now after investigation, a charge-sheet has been
filed against the applicants under <a href="/doc/326674/" id="a_3">Sections 240</a>/<a href="/doc/105503/" id="a_4">417</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of IPC.
According to the applicants, no case under <a href="/doc/326674/" id="a_6">Section 240</a> of IPC is
made out as it relates to counterfeit coin. There is only an
allegation of attempt to commit "cheating", that too not against
the complainant.
</p><p id="p_3">3.                 Now the alleged victims who are present before this
Court are willing to compound the offence. They have also filed
an affidavit to this effect through their counsels. The applicants
are too present.
</p><p id="p_4">4.                 In view thereof, as an interim measure it is
provided that further proceedings of Criminal Case No. 707 of
2014, under <a href="/doc/326674/" id="a_7">Sections 240</a>/<a href="/doc/105503/" id="a_8">417</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_9">34</a> of IPC pending in the court of
Judicial Magistrate IIIrd, Dehradun shall remain stayed and
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              2</span>




applicants shall not be arrested nor any coercive measure shall
be taken against the applicants.
</p><p id="p_5">5.           List this matter after three weeks in a daily cause
list.
</p><p id="p_6">6.           Personal   presence   of   applicants   as   well   as
respondents is hereby dispensed with.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                   (Sudhanshu Dhulia, J.)
                                               22.05.2015
Ankit
 </p>
