<p id="p_1">8       05-08-2014                      The learned counsel for the petitioners submits that,

                        in pursuance to the advertisement, brought on record at Annexure-

                        1 to the writ application, the petitioners applied for appointment as

                        driver against the posts enumerated in Clause 'Kh' and they never

                        applied seeking appointment for 11 posts of driver as mentioned in

                        the Clause 'K' of the advertisement. However, the learned counsel

                        for the petitioners seeks adjournment to seek specific instruction in

                        this regard and revert to this Court with categorical statement.

</p><p id="p_2">                                        Put up this matter after two weeks.
</p>
