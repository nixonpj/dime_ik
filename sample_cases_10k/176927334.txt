                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.21701 of 2014
                 ======================================================
                 Anil Sah
                                                                      .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Javed Aslam
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Asha Kumari
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRABHAT KUMARJHA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   15-05-2014                Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in Chakia

                 P.S.Case No. 421 of 2012 for the offence allegedly committed

                 under <a href="/doc/471092/" id="a_1">sections 272</a>, <a href="/doc/285163/" id="a_2">273</a>, <a href="/doc/1074140/" id="a_3">284</a>, <a href="/doc/1976294/" id="a_4">413</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_5">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_6">467</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_7">471</a>, <a href="/doc/722115/" id="a_8">477</a> I.P.C. and

                 under <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_9">section 47-A</a> Excise Act and under Section 43 of the

                 Juvenile Justice Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               It is submitted that the petitioner is licencee vendor of

                 composite shop and running his shop without any material.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Learned counsel for the informant has alleged that

                 the liquor was found in the house of Chunnu Singh @ Chandra

                 Bhushan Singh.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                Call for the clear carbon/photo copy of the case diary

                 of Chakia P.S.Case No. 421 of 2012 from the Court of learned

                 Chief Judicial Magistrate, East Champaran at Motihari and put up

                 this case "For Admission" after receipt of the same.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.21701 of 2014 (2) dt.15-05-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_6">                                            Let this order be communicated through Fax at the

                       cost of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


</p><p id="p_8">                                                   (Prabhat KumarJha, J)

Sudha/-
</p>
