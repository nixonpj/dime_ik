<pre id="pre_1"> THE HONOURABLE Dr. JUSTICE B. SIVA SANKARA RAO
            CRIMINAL REVISION CASE No.308 of 2019

ORDER :
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       As there is efficacious remedy provided under Section 126(2)

proviso of <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>. to file application to set aside the ex parte order of

maintenance, before admission before notice to the respondent the

revision can be disposed of as the trial Court can consider if at all to

set aside the ex parte decree on such terms as per the very wording of

the proviso, which may include to sustain the order as interim

maintenance in such event.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       With these observations, the revision is disposed of giving

liberty to file such application under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Section 126(2)</a> proviso <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">Cr.P.C</a>.

within one week from the date of receipt of a copy of the order, before

the learned Magistrate concerned, so that to decide from the above

observations and by entertaining the application without reference to

the three months limitation otherwise provided by virtue of this order,

but for on other merits.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       Miscellaneous petitions pending, if any, shall stand closed.

</p><p id="p_4">                                    ____________________________
                                    Dr. B. SIVA SANKARA RAO, J
28th March 2019
