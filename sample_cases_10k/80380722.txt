<div class="doc_title">State vs Unknown on 3 February, 2012</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Sh. Rajneesh Gupta</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                                       1

    IN THE COURT OF SHRI RAJNEESH KUMAR GUPTA, 
   ADDITIONAL SESSIONS JUDGE­01 (WEST), TIS HAZARI 
                       COURTS, DELHI.
S.C. No. 102/2011
Unique Case I.D. No. 02401R0536442011

FIR No.  268/11
PS       Nangloi
U/s      376/506 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>

State 
Vs.
Manbir @ Pappu
S/o Late Sh. Ram Singh
R/o H. No. 16, Rajendera Park, 
Extn. 5/6, Nangloi, Delhi.                               ... Accused

Date of Institution    :  23.11.2011
Date of arguments      :  03.02.2012
Date of order          :  03.02.2012

JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">           In brief the case of the prosecution is that in the month of 

April 2011 at about 7.00 pm at a house at Mohalla Kamruddin, Nangloi, 

the accused had committed rape upon Ruby @ Jantri (the prosecutrix) 

aged about 16­17 years.  It is further the case of the prosecution that the 

SC No. 102/2011                                                        P­1/4
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                             2</span>

accused   has   threatened   the   prosecutrix   with   threat   to   her   life   if   the 

prosecutrix   disclosed   the   incident   to   anyone.   The   FIR   has   been 

registered U/s 376/506 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>. Police after investigation filed the challan 

U/s 376/506 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">IPC</a> against the accused.  After supplying the documents to 

the accused, the case was committed to the court of Sessions and charge 

U/s 376/506 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a> was framed against the accused on 21.12.11 to which 

accused pleaded not guilty and claimed trial.

</p><p id="p_2">2.           In order to prove its case, the prosecution has examined four 

witnesses.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.           I have heard the Ld. APP for the state and Ld. Counsel for 

the accused and perused the evidence on record.

</p><p id="p_4">4.           PW   1   Raja   Ram   has   deposed   that   he   has   one   daughter 

namely   Ruby   aged   18­19   years   and   three   sons.   He   do   not   know 

anything about this case. He do not know the accused present in the 

court. 
</p>
<p id="p_5">In cross­examination by the Ld. APP, he had denied the suggestion that 

he used to reside in the rented room belonging to the accused and that 

he had employed the prosecutrix with the accused. He had denied that 

the prosecutrix was raped by the accused. He had denied that he had 


SC No. 102/2011                                                                      P­2/4
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                            3</span>

lodged any complaint with the police.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             PW2 Ms. Ruby (the prosecutrix) had deposed that she do not 

know the accused and the accused had not done anything wrong with 

her. She has not studied in any school at Shahjahan Pur. She has denied 

the   suggestion   that   while   staying   in   the   house   of   the   accused,   the 

accused had raped her and threatened her to kill if she disclosed the 

incident to anybody. She has also denied the suggestion that due to fear 

she went to her village in Shahjahan Pur and lodged the complaint with 

the police Station Shahjahan Pur on 17.07.11. She has also denied that 

she had been medically examined and that accused was arrested on her 

pointing out. She had deposed that whatever she stated in her statement 

U/s   164   Cr.   P.C.   before   the   Magistrate   was   tutored   by   the   police 

officials.
</p>
<p id="p_7">             PW3 Ram Beti is the mother of the prosecutrix and she has 

also not supported the case of the prosecution as she has deposed that 

nothing wrong had happened with her daughter and has also denied the 

case of the prosecution in her cross­examination.

</p><p id="p_8">             PW4 W/SI Savita is the investigating officer of the case and 

had deposed that the investigation of the case was handed over to her 


SC No. 102/2011                                                                   P­3/4
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                         4</span>

after registration of the FIR Ex.PW4/A. The father of the prosecutrix 

had lodged the Zero FIR at PS Banda, the copy of which is Ex.PW4/B. 

She has prepared the site plan of the place of incident at the instance of 

the   prosecutrix   which   is   Ex.PW4/N.     On   the   pointing   out   of   the 

prosecutrix, the accused was apprehended vide arrest memo Ex.PW4/O. 

The   prosecutrix   and   the   accused   were   medically   examined   from   the 

SGM Hospital. The statement of the prosecutrix was got recorded U/s 

164 Cr. P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_9">5.          PW1 to PW3 are the material witnesses of the case but they 

have not supported the case of the prosecution. From the evidence led by 

the   prosecution,   no   incriminating   evidence   has   been   come   on   record 

against the accused, so his statement U/s 313 Cr. P.C. was dispensed with.

</p><p id="p_10">6.          In view of the above discussion, the prosecution has failed to 

prove its case against the accused.  Accused is, accordingly, acquitted of 

the offence punishable U/s 376/506 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_5">IPC</a>. He be released forthwith, if not 

wanted in any other case.  
</p>
<p id="p_11">7.          File be consigned to Record Room.
</p>
<p id="p_12">Announced in the open court                   (RAJNEESH KUMAR GUPTA) 
today i.e. on 03.02.12                       Additional Sessions Judge­01(West) 
                                                        Tis Hazari Courts, Delhi. 
</p>
