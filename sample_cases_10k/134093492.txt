<div class="doc_title">National Insurancw Co. Limited vs Bhagwanlal And Ors on 28 July, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT JODHPUR
  --------------------------------------------------------


              SPECIAL APPEAL CIVIL No. 122 of 2006

                   NATIONAL INSURANCW CO. LIMITED.
                                V/S
                            BHAGWANLAL AND ORS.

    Mr. MOHIT CHOUDHARY for Mr. SANJEEV JOHARI, for the
appellant / petitioner

    Mr. GS RATHORE for Mr. DILIP KAWADIA, for the respondent

    Date of Order : 28.7.2008

                      HON'BLE SHRI N P GUPTA,J.
           HON'BLE SHRI KISHAN SWAROOP CHAUDHARI,J.

                                 ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">                                 -----
</p>
<p id="p_2">
           In view of the latest judgment of Hon'ble the

Supreme   Court   in   Kamla   Devi   Vs.   Khushal   Kanwar   &amp;   Anr.,

reported in AIR 2007 SC 663, and of this Court in RSRTC Vs

Vaibhav Kumar &amp; Ors., reported in 2007(1) DNJ (Raj.) 569,

this being an intra court appeal having been filed against

the judgment of learned Single Judge dated 2.12.2005 passed

in civil misc. appeal is not maintainable. The appeal is,

therefore, dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

( KISHAN SWAROOP CHAUDHARI ),J.                   ( N P GUPTA ),J.


/tarun/
