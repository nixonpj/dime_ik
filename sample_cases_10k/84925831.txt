 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN 
 JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR
ORDER 
SB Civil Writ Petition No.14741/2011
Dharmendra Singh Dhabhai versus State of Rajasthan &amp; anr 
21.10.2011
HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MN BHANDARI
Mr Kartar Singh - for  petitioner
BY THE COURT: </pre>
		
<p id="p_1">		This writ petition has been filed against the order of transfer and before that a representation was filed to the respondents giving out that petitioner's wife is also serving in the government. 

		In view of aforesaid, respondents are directed to consider the said representation within a period of fifteen days. It is expected of the respondents to consider the representation of the petitioner sympathetically looking to the grounds given therein. 

		In view of aforesaid, writ petition so as the stay application are disposed of.

(MN BHANDARI), J.
