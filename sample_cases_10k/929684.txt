                      Criminal Miscellaneous No. M-20419 of 2008
                                    Date of Decision: August 13, 2008


Vandana Rani &amp; Another
                                                       .....PETITIONER(S)

                                 VERSUS


State of Haryana &amp; Others
                                                      .....RESPONDENT(S)</pre>

<pre id="pre_2">                             .     .      .


CORAM:        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AJAI LAMBA


PRESENT: -    Mr. Brijender Kaushik,                  Advocate,
              for the petitioners.


                             .     .      .

AJAI LAMBA, J (Oral)

                      It     has       been        pleaded     that    the

petitioners   being     of    legally         marriageable     age,    got

</pre><p id="p_1">married as per their wishes. The marriage, however, has

been solemnized against the desires of respondent Nos.4

and 5. The petitioners are now being threatened by the

said respondents.
</p>
<pre id="pre_3">               The         petition           be     treated      as     a

representation/      complaint.         Station        House    Officer,

Police   Station,    Parao,       District,        Ambala    Cantt.,    is

</pre><p id="p_2">directed to have an enquiry conducted into the facts

given out in the petition. The enquiry be conducted

within 15 days of receipt of a copy of this order. In

case the perception of threat of the petitioners is
 Crl. Misc. No. M-20419 of 2008                             [2]



found reasonable, appropriate steps would be taken to

ensure protection of their life and liberty.

</p><p id="p_3">                     It is further directed that in case any

complaint       or   FIR    has   been   lodged   in   regard    to   the

issues pleaded or raised in this petition, first an

enquiry be conducted so as to verify the allegations

made in the petition and only thereafter proceed with

the enquiry or investigation of the case.

</p><p id="p_4">                  Disposed of with the above directions.</p><pre id="pre_4">


                                                         (AJAI LAMBA)
August 13, 2008                                              JUDGE
