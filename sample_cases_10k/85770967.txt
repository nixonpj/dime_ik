<div class="doc_title">Shubham vs State Of Rajasthan Through Pp on 27 September, 2016</div><p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                1</span>

   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                     JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR
        S.B. Criminal Misc.Bail Application No.12314/2016
                         Shubham vs State
              Date of Order           ::   27/9/2016


                      PRESENT
    HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MAHESH CHANDRA SHARMA



Mr.Surendra Sharma for the petitioner(s)
Mr. RS Shekhawat PP for the State


            This bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>
CrPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Brief facts of the case are that an FIR No.192/2016
was lodged at Police Station Kotwali, Kota for the offences
mentioned     therein,   whereupon       the    investigation    was
commenced.      During    the   course     of   investigation,   the
petitioner(s) was/were arrested. He/she/they moved the bail
application before the court below, which was dismissed.
</p><p id="p_3">            Learned counsel for the petitioner(s) has contended
that the petitioner/s has/have falsely been implicated in the
matter. He/They/She is/are in judicial lock up since long and
conclusion of trial will take time. Hence he/she/they should be
released on bail.
</p><p id="p_4">            Learned PP appearing for the State has opposed the
same.
</p><p id="p_5">            I have heard learned counsel for the parties and
carefully perused the relevant material on record.
</p><p id="p_6">            Looking to the facts and circumstances of the case,
but without expressing any opinion on the merits and demerits
of the case, I deem just and proper to enlarge the petitioner(s)
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                2</span>

on bail.
</p><p id="p_7">           Therefore, this bail application is allowed and it is
directed that accused petitioner(s) Shubham son of Dhanraj
shall be released on bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_2">Section 439</a> CrPC in connection
with aforesaid FIR provided he/she/they furnish(es) a personal
bond of Rs. 2,00,000/- (Rs. Two Lakh) with two sureties of Rs.
1,00,000/- (Rs. One Lakh) to the satisfaction of the trial court
with the stipulation that he/she/they shall appear before that
Court on all subsequent dates of hearing and as and when called
upon to do so.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                           (MAHESH CHANDRA SHARMA), J.
</p>
