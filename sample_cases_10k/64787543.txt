<div class="doc_title">Nawal Kishore Sharma @ Nawal ... vs The State Of Jharkhand on 10 January, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
            A.B.A. No. 2631 of 2018
                        ------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">    Nawal Kishore Sharma @ Nawal Kishor Sharma
                                  ...       Petitioner
                              Versus
</p><p id="p_2">    1. The State of Jharkhand
</p><p id="p_3">    2. Ajay Kumar Singh          ...      Opposite Parties
</p><p id="p_4">                               ------
</p><p id="p_5">CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR CHOUDHARY
</p><p id="p_6">                                      ------</p><pre id="pre_2">
    For the Petitioner          : Mr. A.K. Choudhary, Advocate
    For the State               : Addl. P.P.
                                      ------
    Order No.10 Dated- 10.01.2019

</pre><p id="p_7">          Apprehending his arrest, the petitioner has moved this
    Court for grant of privilege of anticipatory bail in connection with
    Bhelwaghati P.S. Case No.25 of 2016 (G.R. No. 2625 of 2016)
    registered under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_1">sections 406</a>/<a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_2">409</a>/<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_3">420</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_8">          Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and learned
    Addl. P.P. for the State.
</p><p id="p_9">          The Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that there is
    excess withdrawal of Rs.5,68,096/- against the petitioner. It is
    further submitted that the allegation against the petitioner are all
    false. By filing supplementary affidavit dated 29.11.2018, it is
    submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioner that the
    petitioner is ready and willing to deposit the said alleged
    withdrawal amount before the Superintendent of Education,
    Giridih. Hence, it is submitted that the petitioner be given the
    privilege of anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_10">          Learned Addl. P.P. submits that in view of the readiness of
    the petitioner to deposit the excess withdrawal amount, the State
    has no serious objection to the prayer for grant of anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_11">          Considering the submissions of the counsels and the fact as
    discussed above, I am of the opinion that it is a fit case where the
    above named petitioner be given the privilege of anticipatory bail.
    Hence, in the event of his arrest or surrender within a period of
    two months from the date of this order, he shall be released on bail
  on    showing   the   proof   of   deposit   of   Rs.5,68,096/-   with
 Superintendent of Education, Giridih and on furnishing bail bond
 of Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand) with two sureties
 of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned J.M. 1st Class,
 Giridih, in connection with Bhelwaghati P.S. Case No.25 of 2016
 (G.R. No. 2625 of 2016) subject to the conditions laid down under
 section 438 (2) Cr. P.C.
</p>

<p id="p_12">                            (Anil Kumar Choudhary, J.)
Sonu/Gunjan-
</p>
