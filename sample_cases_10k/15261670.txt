                            Date : 19/02/2015
                                ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">         (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE RAVI R.TRIPATHI)


</p><p id="p_2">  1. The present application is filed by the convict seeking
     extension of temporary bail granted by this Court
     vide order dated 21.01.2015 for a period of 30 days.
     The extension sought is also for 30 days on the
     ground that his daughter is not well and she is
     suffering from jaundice.
</p>

<p id="p_3">  2. The applicant is present before the Court and states
     that he is having marriage against the wish and will
     of his parents and there is nobody in the family to


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                Page 1 of 3</span>
     R/CR.MA/3446/2015                                       ORDER



  support him.
</p>


<p id="p_4">3. Learned APP has invited attention of the Court to the
  jail remarks. The convict has undergone 04 years 01
  month and 28 days as on 19.02.2015. During this
  period he has availed temporary bail only on two
  occasions and the third one is going on. Jail conduct
  of the convict is reported to be good.
</p>

<p id="p_5">4. RULE. Learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives
  service of notice of rule on behalf of the respondent-
  State.
</p>

<p id="p_6">5. Taking     into      consideration         the   contents    of   the
  application,          the   matter         requires   consideration.
  Temporary bail granted by this Court vide order
  dated 22.01.2015 is extended for a further period of
  03 weeks from the date of the expiry of earlier bail
  period.
</p>
<p id="p_7">6. The convict shall surrender himself to the Jail
  authorities on expiry of the above temporary bail
  period. Rule is made absolute. Registry is directed to
  send a copy of this order to the Jail Authorities. A
  copy of this order be made available to learned APP
  for its onward communication.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                    (RAVI R.TRIPATHI, J.)
