          IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA,
             CIRCUIT BENCH AT GULBARGA

     DATED THIS THE 29TH DAY OF JANUARY, 2013

                           BEFORE

   THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE ANAND BYRAREDDY

          CRIMINAL PETITION NO.16212 OF 2012

BETWEEN:

Beeranna Vaggi S/o Irappa Vaggi
Age: 52 years, Occ: Agriculture,
R/o Kadaganchi village,
Taluk: Aland, District: Gulbarga.
                                                ... Petitioner
(Shri Avinash A Uploankar, Advocate)
AND:

The State through
Raghavendra Nagar Police Station,
Gulbarga.
                                                ... Respondent
(Shri Sanjay A Patil, Additional State Public Prosecutor)

       This Criminal Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of Code
of Criminal Procedure, 1973, praying to release the petitioner
on bail in the event of his arrest in Crime No.69/2012 of
Raghavendra Nagar Police Station, District: Gulbarga, which is
registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_2">Sections 143</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_3">147</a>,
                                   <a href="/doc/915147/" id="a_4">2</a>




<a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_5">148</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_6">325</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_7">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_8">302</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_9">149</a> of the Indian Penal Code, 1860 and
<a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_10">Sections 25</a> and <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_11">27</a> of the Indian Arms Act.

      This petition coming on for orders this day, the Court
made the following:

                            ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      The learned counsel for the petitioner files a memo

seeking to withdraw the petition, with liberty to press for the

relief before the trial court, at the appropriate stage.


</p><p id="p_2">      The petitioner is permitted to withdraw the petition.
