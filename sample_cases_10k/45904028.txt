53   11.12.2014                W.P.31811(W) of 2014
pg                  [Sunanda Paul (Samanta) vs. The State of West
                                    Bengal &amp; Ors.]

                  Mr. Aniruddha Chatterjee
                  Mr. Subhash Chandra Atha....for the petitioner

                  Mr. Mrinal Kanti Biswas       ......for the State

                  Mr. Ekramul Bari
                  Mr. Syed Mansur Ali.............for the respondent no. 4 &amp; 5</pre>


<p id="p_1">                               Having heard learned advocates appearing

                  for the parties and considering the nature of grievance

                  expressed by the petitioner (she is aggrieved because the

                  official respondents have not acted on the basis of her

                  representation dated September 26, 2014, appearing at

                  page 19 of the writ petition wherein a prayer for her

                  inclusion in the panel in place of a less meritorious

                  candidate has been made), I find no reason to keep the

                  writ petition pending. The writ petition, accordingly,

                  stands disposed of directing the Chairman, District

                  Primary   School       Council,   South   24-Parganas,   the

                  respondent no.5 to consider and dispose of the prayer of

                  the petitioner contained in her representation dated

                  September 26, 2014 after hearing her and in accordance

                  with law, as early as possible but not later than eight

                  weeks from date of receipt of a copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_2">                               If the petitioner's representation deserves

                  acceptance, follow up steps in accordance with law shall

                  be taken without delay. On the contrary, if she is not

                  found entitled to any relief, a reasoned order shall be
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                   2</span>



passed and communicated to her.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            Needless to observe, I have not examined the

petitioner's claim on merit and all points are left open.

</p><p id="p_4">            There shall be no order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_5">           Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if

applied for, be furnished to the parties expeditiously.



</p><p id="p_6">                                  (DIPANKAR DATTA,J.)
