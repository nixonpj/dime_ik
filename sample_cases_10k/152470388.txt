<div class="doc_title">Kirann Devi &amp; Anr vs State Of Bihar &amp; Anr on 25 September, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                               Criminal Miscellaneous No. 13655 of 2010
                  ==================================================
                  Kiran Devi &amp; Anr
                                                                 .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                Versus
                  The State Of Bihar &amp; Anr
                                                            .... .... Opposite Party/s
                  ==================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAKESH KUMAR
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">5.   25-09-2012                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and Sri

                  Jharkhandi Upadhaya, learned Addl. Public Prosecutor.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Despite valid service of notice, opposite party

                  no. 2 has preferred not to appear.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               It was submitted by learned counsel for the

                  petitioner that though in this case, order of cognizance was

                  passed in the year 2008, till date charge has not framed and

                  case is fixed for evidence before charge.

</p><p id="p_4">                               Keeping in view of nature of accusation, this

                  petition requires detailed hearing.

</p><p id="p_5">                               Admit.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                               Call for Lower Court Record in connection with

                  Complaint Case No. 2059 of 2008, Trial No. 3239 of 2009

                  from the court of learned Judicial Magistrate 1 st Class,

                  Muzaffarpur.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.13655 of 2010 (5) dt.25-09-2012</span><p id="p_7">

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_8">                                 Issue notice in hearing matter to opposite party

                no. 2.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                 Notice to go through ordinary post as well as

                registered cover with A/D, for which requisites etc. must be

                filed within two weeks, failing which, the entire petition

                shall stand dismissed without further reference to a Bench.

</p><p id="p_10">                                 During the pendency of this petition, further

                proceeding in Complaint Case No. 2059 of 2008, Trial No.

                3239 of 2009 pending in the court of Judicial Magistrate 1 st

                Class, Muzaffarpur, shall remain stayed.




</p><p id="p_11">                                                            (Rakesh Kumar, J.)
    Anay
