<p id="p_1">    Petition filed under Section 482 Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_2">2. The petitioners are the accused in Crime No.

    1905 of 2012 of Palarivattom Police Station

    registered          for    the     offences      under       <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections

    323</a>,    <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_2">324</a>    &amp;     <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>    of      the    Indian     Penal     Code.

    Respondents 1 to 5 are the victims. It is

    submitted that the matter has been settled and

    the proceedings in the criminal case may be

    quashed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners

    and respondents 1 to 5 and the learned Public

    Prosecutor.
</p>
<p id="p_4">4. I     have     perused         the        affidavits         filed    by

    respondents          1     to       5,     who       have     entered

    appearance          through       counsel.       I   am     satisfied

    that the allegation of settlement is true and

    no public interest is involved. There is no

    impediment          for    granting        the   prayer       of    the

    petitioners.
</p><p id="p_5"> Crl.MC.No. 55 of 2019

                               ..4..
</p>
<p id="p_6">     In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed. The

     proceedings        in   Crime     No.   1905     of   2012   of

     Palarivattom Police Station are quashed. The

     learned magistrate may pass appropriate orders

     for the disposal of the material objects, if

     any, produced in the case.
</p>
