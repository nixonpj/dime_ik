<p id="p_1">     Learned Counsel for the petitioner has submitted

that pursuant to the interim order passed by this Court

directing the respondent to receive the petitioner's

application seeking permission to start English Medium

Primary School and process the same, the respondents

have considered the petitioner's application and have

accorded retrospective permission to start English
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              -3-</span>


Medium     Primary   School    from   1st    Standard   from

academic year 2014-15, hence the writ petition does not

survive for consideration.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">       In view of the said submission and memo filed by

the petitioner to that effect, the writ petition stands

dismissed as having become infructuous.
