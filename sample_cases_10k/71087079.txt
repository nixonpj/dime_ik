<div class="doc_title">Macherla Venkataiah And 3 Others vs The State Of Telangana And 6 Others on 31 December, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: T.Vinod Kumar</div>

<pre id="pre_1">             THE HON'BLE SRI JUSTICE T.VINOD KUMAR

                  WRIT PETITION No.27938 of 2019

O R D E R:
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       The present writ petition is filed questioning the action of

respondents 4 and 5 in interfering into the civil matters and calling the

petitioners to the police station and pressurizing them to settle the matter

with the unofficial respondents 6 and 7.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.     Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned Assistant

Government Pleader for Home.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.     Learned Assistant Government Pleader for Home, on written

instructions, dated 19.12.2019, under the signature of the 5th respondent,

denied the allegations made by the petitioners that respondents 4 and 5

called the petitioners to the police station on 10.11.2019 and 07.12.2019

and harassing them to sit in the police station from morning to evening.

By the said written instructions, it is stated that respondents 4 and 5

never called the petitioners to the police station on the said dates, or on

any other date, nor made them to sit in the police station. It is further

stated that till date no complaint is given by any individual against the

petitioners, nor any case is registered against them. It is also stated that

the respondent police never interfered in the civil disputes of the

petitioners at any point of time.
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.     Having regard to the submissions made by the learned Assistant

Government Pleader, no further orders need be passed in the present writ

petition.
</p>
<p id="p_5">5.     The writ petition is accordingly closed. Pending miscellaneous

petitions, if any, shall also stand closed. No order as to costs.


</p><p id="p_6">                                              _____________________
                                               JUSTICE T.VINOD KUMAR
Date:31.12.2019

GJ
