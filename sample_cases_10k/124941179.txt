                                   Cr.Misc. No.10307 of 2010
                   AKHILESH KUMAR SINGH @ AKHILESH PRASAD SINGH , son
                   of Surya Narayan Singh, resident of vill.-Mau, P.S.-Vidyapati Nagar ,
                   Distt.-Samastipur---------------------------------------------Petitioner.
                                                    Versus
                                          STATE OF BIHAR
                                                ------------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">2.   27.4.2010              Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                            Petitioner has no hand in issuance of connection.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                  Ultimately, it were the consumers who did not make payment.

</p><p id="p_4">                            Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case, in

                  the event of arrest or surrender within a period of one month from the

                  date of communication of this order the above named petitioner shall

                  be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-(Ten

                  thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction

                  of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Darbhanga, in connection with

                  Laheria Sarai P.S. case no.387 of 2009 , subject to the conditions as

                  laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_1">section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.




</p><pre id="pre_2">          Sudip                          ( Mandhata Singh, J. )
