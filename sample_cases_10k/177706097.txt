<div class="doc_title">Santosh Kumar Jatthap vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 15 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                           MCC-2482-2016
          (SANTOSH KUMAR JATTHAP Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


15-11-2016
       Shri B.R. Pandey, counsel for the petitioner.
       Smt. S. Sharma, learned panel lawyer for the respondents/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Counsel for the parties heard on I.A. No.12870/16 filed for
condonation of delay alongwith an affidavit of the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">       According to office note, restoration petition is time barred by
138 days.
</p><p id="p_3">       Looking to the reasons mentioned in the application supported
by an affidavit, the is allowed and delay in filing restoration petition is
hereby condoned.
</p><p id="p_4">       Counsel for the parties also heard on restoration petition.
       This petition is filed for restoration of W.P. No.21871/2015,
which was dismissed on 3/5/2016 for non-compliance of common
conditional order dated 22/2/2016.
</p><p id="p_5">       For the reasons mentioned in the restoration petition, the same
is allowed. W.P. No.21871/2015 is directed to be restored to its
original number, subject to deposit of cost of Rs.500/- (Rupees five
hundred only) by the petitioner before the High Court Legal Services
Committee, Jabalppur, within one month from today.
</p><p id="p_6">       This M.C.C stands allowed and disposed of.
       A copy of this order be retained in the file of W.P.
No.21871/15.
</p><p id="p_7">     C.C as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                            (ASHOK KUMAR JOSHI)
                                                   JUDGE
