<p id="p_1">                           CORAM:         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE HARI PAL VERMA

                           Present:-      Mr. P.S. Sekhon, Advocate
                                          for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                          Mr. Gurinderjit Singh, DAG Punjab.

                                          Mr. M.S. Batth, Advocate
                                          for the complainant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                           HARI PAL VERMA J.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                          Petitioner has filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the

                           Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking anticipatory bail in FIR No. 137

                           dated 5.11.2014, under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_4">148</a> and <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_5">149</a> IPC and offence

                           under <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_6">Section 326</a> IPC (added later on), registered at Police Station

                           Sadar, Rup Nagar.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                         On 13.2.2015, following order was passed by this Court:-

</p><p id="p_6">                                                           "The learned State counsel admits the factum

                                          that the mother of the petitioner received three injuries,

                                          including one grievous injury, as reflected in the MLR

                                          dated 2.11.2014 and the accused Soma, who inflicted the

                                          injury has been admitted to bail.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                                           The   petitioner   is   directed         to   join   the

                                          investigation and shall appear before the Investigating




VIJAY ASIJA
2015.03.23 17:08
I attest to the accuracy and
integrity of this document
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                            Crl. Misc. No. M-3065 of 2015                                   -2-</span>




                                          Officer on 18.2.2015 at 10.00 a.m. in the concerned Police

                                          Station.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                                           Meanwhile, in the event of arrest of the

                                          petitioner by the Arresting Officer, he shall be released on

                                          interim bail subject to the following conditions :-

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                                  1.       That he shall make himself available for
                                                           interrogation by a police officer as and when
                                                           required;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                                                  2.       That he shall not directly or indirectly make
                                                           any inducement, threat or promise to any
                                                           person acquainted with the facts of the case
                                                           so as to dissuade him from disclosing such
                                                           facts to the Court or to any police officer and;
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                                                  3.       That he shall not leave India without previous
                                                           permission of the Court.
</blockquote>
<p id="p_9">                                          List again on 13.3.2015."
</p>
<p id="p_10">                                         Learned State counsel, who is assisted by ASI Darshan Lal,

                           has stated that in terms of the above order the petitioner has joined

                           investigation and at this stage his custodial interrogation is not required.

</p><p id="p_11">                                         In view of the above, the present petition is allowed and

                           interim directions issued by this Court vide order dated 13.2.2015 are

                           made absolute.
</p>
<p id="p_12">                                          However, the petitioner shall continue to join investigation as

                           and when required to do so and shall abide by the terms and conditions,

                           as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_7">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.
