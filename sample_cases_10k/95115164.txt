            IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
                 R/APPEAL FROM ORDER NO. 212 of 2017
                                  With
                     CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 2 of 2017
==========================================================
             QURESHI MOHAMMAD ILIYAS NOOR MOHAMMAD
                              Versus
                 TAIYABBHAI ABIDBHAI KHOKHAWALA
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR DHAVAL D VYAS(3225) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1
MOIZ K RAFIQUE(8043) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
NOTICE SERVED BY DS(5) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 2,3
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE C.L. SONI

                            Date : 26/07/2018
                             ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">1.       This appeal with present application when was called out in
the first session, learned advocate Mr.Vyas for the appellant was
present and requested to permit the appellant to withdraw the
appeal. However, learned advocate Mr.Rafique was not present in
the first session. Thereafter, in the second session when the appeal
is taken up, learned advocate Mr.Vyas is present and requests to
permit the appellant to withdraw the appeal, however, again
learned advocate Mr.Rafique is not present.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.       The Court finds that since learned advocate for the appellant
seeks permission to withdraw the appeal unconditionally, such
permission could be granted. Hence, the appeal from order stands
disposed of as withdrawn. Notice discharged.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3.       since, the appeal from order is disposed of, civil application
shall not survive. Hence, it is disposed of accordingly.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                                                            (C.L. SONI, J)
GUPTA*
