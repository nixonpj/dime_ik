                   The petitioner shall be at liberty to implead the other joint

              holder of the MIS account as additional respondent in the writ

              petition. Since the petitioner is not aware of the address of the

              other joint holder, a paper publication shall be made in two

              newspapers (one English and one vernacular) having wide

              circulation in the country with a gist of the prayers made in this

              writ petition inviting the said joint holder to contest the

              proceedings on 28th July, 2015, when the writ petition shall be

              listed once again under the same heading.
