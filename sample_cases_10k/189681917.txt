     07/05/2014
            Shri Imtiaz Husain, learned counsel for the appellants.
            Shri R.K.Sanghi, learned counsel for the respondent.</pre>
<p id="p_1">            I.A.No.5083/2014 has been filed on behalf of the respondents
     and prayer made in the application is to issue a direction restraining the
     appellant from creating third party interest in the property.
</p><p id="p_2">            Vide order dated 4.12.2012, when the appeal was admitted,
     parties were directed to maintain status-quo and they were also
     restrained from creating third party interest and to alter it from it's
     present form.
</p><p id="p_3">            In the light of the aforesaid order passed, no further orders are
     necessary on I.A.No.5083/2014.
</p><p id="p_4">            I.A.No.5083/2014 is accordingly, dismissed.
</p>
