<div class="doc_title">Saurabh Kumar Tiwari @ Satendra ... vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 19 February, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                  1
                                                         M.Cr.C. No.6174/2019

          THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                         M.Cr.C No.6174/2019
     (<a href="/doc/7104784/" id="a_1">Saurabh Kumar Tiwari @ Satendra Tiwari vs. State of M.P</a>.)
Jabalpur, Dated 19.02.2019

      Shri Sanjeev Kumar Tiwari, learned counsel for the applicant.
      Shri Manoj Kushwaha, learned Panel Lawyer for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard.
</p><p id="p_2">      Case diary is available.
</p><p id="p_3">      This is first application filed on behalf of applicant under
<a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Section 438</a> of CrPC for grant of anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_4">      The applicant apprehends his arrest in connection with Crime
No.01/2019 registered by Police Station Semariya, District Rewa for
offence punishable under <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_3">Section 354</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_5">      It is submitted by counsel for the applicant that the applicant is
innocent and has been falsely implicated in the case. Prosecutrix
appeared before this Court on 12.02.2019 and submitted that she has
no objection if the applicant is granted anticipatory bail. It is further
submitted that the applicant is ready and willing to cooperate with the
investigation and there is no possibility of his absconding or tampering
with the prosecution case. On these grounds prayer is made to enlarge
the applicant on anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_6">      Per contra, learned counsel appearing on behalf of the State has
opposed the application and prayed for its rejection.
</p><p id="p_7">      Looking to the submission made on behalf of the parties and in
view of the statement made by prosecutrix as well as considering
overall facts and circumstances of the case but without commenting on
the merits of the matter, the application is allowed. It is directed that in
the event of arrest or surrender of applicant- Saurabh Kumar Tiwari
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    2</span>
                                                        M.Cr.C. No.6174/2019

@ Satendra Tiwari before the Arresting Authority/Investigating
Officer in relation to the aforementioned crime number within a period
of 15 days from today, he shall be released on bail on furnishing a
personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rs.Fifty Thousand only)
with one surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the Arresting
Officer/ Investigating Officer.
</p><p id="p_8">        The applicant shall make himself available for interrogation by
the Investigating Officer as and when required. He shall further abide
by the other conditions enumerated in sub-Section (2) of Section 438
of Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_9">        If this order is not complied by the applicant/accused within the
said period and conditions fixed by this Court, the bail order shall
become ineffective without further reference to the Bench.
</p><p id="p_10">        Certified copy as per rules.
</p><p id="p_11">                                           (Akhil Kumar Srivastava)
                                                     Judge

anand
