Cl.77.
</p><p id="p_2">January 7,
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">2011</span>

             F.M.A.T.1615 of 2010
                          With
             C.A.N.10300 of 2010


    Mr. Sib Sankar Banerjee,
    Mr. Deepak Kumar Mukhopadhyay..
</p><p id="p_3">               ...for the appellants.
</p>

<p id="p_4">      This matter is taken up for hearing out of turn on
the mentioning of Mr. Sib Sankar Banerjee, the learned
advocate appearing for the appellants, and in view of the
urgency pleaded by him.
</p>

<p id="p_5">      The learned advocate for the appellants is directed
to classify the appeal as an appear from original order, to
insert the name of the learned trial judge in the preamble
of the memorandum of appeal, to delete the unnecessary
certificate appended to the foot of the memorandum of
appeal as also to replace uncalled for list of documents by
a proper list of papers.
</p>

<p id="p_6">      This is an appeal against an order passed by the
learned Civil Judge (Senior Division), Seventh Court,
Alipore in Title Suit No.1880 of 2009 rejecting the
application for injunction.
</p>

<p id="p_7">      The    injunction    application   was   taken   up   for
hearing. The learned advocate for the plaintiffs expressed
his inability to appear in the matter and informed the
court that he had already returned the papers to his
clients.
</p>

<p id="p_8">      The learned advocate for the defendants was, also,
absent.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                    2</span>




<p id="p_9">            The court fixed the hearing of the application again
      at 3.00 O'clock. Both the sides were absent.
</p>

<p id="p_10">            Accordingly, the application for injunction was
      rejected as no one pressed the application for injunction.
</p>

<p id="p_11">            We do not find any infirmity with the order passed
      by the learned judge requiring interference of this court.
</p>

<p id="p_12">            The appeal is, thus, dismissed under Order XLI,
      rule 11 of the Code of Civil Procedure.
</p>

<p id="p_13">            However, this order of dismissal shall not prevent
      the appellants to take appropriate steps in the court
      below in accordance with law.
</p>

<p id="p_14">            In view of the dismissal of the appeal, the
      application for stay filed under C.A.N.10300 of 2010
      becomes infructuous, and the same is, also, dismissed.
</p>

<p id="p_15">            We make no order as to costs.
</p>



<p id="p_16">                          (Subhro Kamal Mukherjee, J.)
Bpg


                            (Tarun Kumar Gupta, J.)
