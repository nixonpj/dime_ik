<p id="p_1">      The petitioner approached the first respondent by Ext.P9

appeal under the Maintenance and Welfare of Parents and

Senior Citizens Act, 2007, as against the order passed by the

Maintenance Tribunal.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      In view of the limited request made by the petitioner,

there shall be a direction to the first respondent to consider

Ext.P9 appeal within one month after issuing notice to the

petitioner as well as to the party respondents.

</p><p id="p_3">      The writ petition is disposed of as above.
