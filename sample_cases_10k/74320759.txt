				MR.JUSTICE ANANT S. DAVE
			
		
	

 


 

 


Date : 30/09/2013
 


 

 


ORAL ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">1.	This petition under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a>
of the Constitution of India is filed by the petitioners with
following prayers:
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_2"> 13(a)	Your Lordships may be
pleased to issue a writ of certiorari and or any other appropriate
writ, direction or order quashing and setting aside the impugned
order dated 19.10.2012 passed by the learned Joint Charity
Commissioner, Vadodara, respondent No.1 herein below an application
Exh.5 in Misc. Application No. 22 of 2011 and Your Lordships may be
pleased to grant the prayers made in the said application Exh.5. 
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_3">2.	It is not in dispute that order
dated 19.10.2012 was passed in the proceedings initiated under
Section 22 of Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950 and Joint Charity
Commissioner, Vadodara has rejected the interim injunction prayer
upon perusal of record and other pendency of the proceedings.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_4">3.	In the above circumstances
extraordinary jurisdiction under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_2">Article 226</a> invoked by the
petitioners against such interlocutory order passed on appreciation
of relevant records and discretionary in nature in absence of any
illegality do not warrant any interference of this Court and
accordingly this petition is summarily rejected.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_5">(ANANT S.DAVE, J.) 

 

SMITA
