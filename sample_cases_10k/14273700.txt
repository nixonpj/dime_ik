<div class="doc_title">C24 No. 19 Of 2010 vs Shiv Kumar on 19 July, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">C24 No. 19 of 2010 
Hon'ble Prafulla C. Pant, J. 
       Mr. M.S. Tyagi, Advocate, present for the
applicant.
       Heard.
       By means of this application moved under
section 24 of Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, the
applicant has sought transfer of Suit No. 44 of
2009, Smt. Apurva @ Neha vs. Shiv Kumar,
under section 8 of Guardian and <a href="/doc/1608688/" id="a_1">Wards Act</a>,
1890, pending in the court of Family Court,
Dehradun, to Family Court, Udham Singh
Nagar.
       Learned counsel for the applicant
submitted that respondent Apurva @ Neha</pre>
<p id="p_1">(divorced wife of the applicant) is resident of
Ludhiana, and she has filed the suit in Dehradun
for custody of the child Km. Nishita.
</p><p id="p_2">       However, address of the respondent shows
that she is resident of 86/34, Saharanpur Road,
Patel Nagar, Dehradun. In the counter affidavit
the respondent has denied the allegations in the
affidavit filed in support of the present
application moved under section 24 of C.P.C.
</p><p id="p_3">       Having heard learned counsel for the
applicant, and after going through the affidavit,
counter affidavit, and rejoinder affidavit
exchanged by the parties, this Court finds that
no sufficient ground has been made out to
transfer the case from Family Court, Dehradun,
to Family Court, Udham Singh Nagar, at this
stage.
</p><p id="p_4">       Therefore, without expressing any opinion
as to the final merits of the case pending
between the parties, and without prejudice to
their rights, this transfer application is
dismissed.</p><pre id="pre_2">
 
                              (Prafulla C. Pant, J.) 
Parul                            19.07.2013 
 </pre>
