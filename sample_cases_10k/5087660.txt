                                PLA No.259 of 2015

                         IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                    Testamentary and Intestate Jurisdiction
                                 ORIGINAL SIDE


                                        IN THE GOODS OF:
                                        KRISHNAMAY MUKHERJEE (DEC)
                                                                             Appearance:
                                                                   Mr. D.N. Sharma, Adv.
       BEFORE:
       The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>

<p id="p_1">       Date : 17th February, 2017.
</p>
<p id="p_2">               The Court : The supplementary affidavit disclosing the

genealogical table is kept with the record.

</p><p id="p_3">               Save and except Smt. Mohua Ghoshal all other legal heirs

who     are   sui   juris   have     been    served   with   the    citations.       The

affidavit of service shows that Smt. Mohua Ghoshal has refused to

accept the special citation.
</p>
<p id="p_4">               The office of the Deputy Sheriff is directed to effect

service upon Smt. Mohua Ghoshal by affixation at a conspicuous

place at the outer wall of the premises and also make an attempt

to serve her personally within two weeks from date.                        The costs,

charges       and   expenses   for    such    service   shall      be   borne   by   the

applicant.
</p>
<p id="p_5">               The matter is adjourned for three weeks.                 The applicant

is directed to file affidavit of service on the adjourned date.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                              (SOUMEN SEN, J.)
A/s.
</p>
