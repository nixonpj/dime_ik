                            JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      The petitioners are aggrieved by the pollution allegedly

caused by the diesel generators which the 7th respondent has

installed in the 6th respondent's property for the purpose of

operating a telecommunication tower.              According to the

petitioners, it would cause pollution problems around the area

where the telecommunication tower is established.                The

petitioners have filed Ext.P5 representation in this regard before

the 3rd respondent. The petitioners seek a direction to the 3rd

respondent to consider and pass orders on Ext.P5 expeditiously.

</p><p id="p_2">      2.    I have heard the learned Standing Counsel appearing

for the 3rd respondent also.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      In the facts and circumstances of the case, I dispose of this

writ petition with a direction to the 3rd respondent to consider and

pass orders on Ext.P5, as expeditiously as possible, at any rate,

within a period of two months from the date of receipt of a copy

of this judgment, after affording an opportunity of being heard to

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">W.P.9c)No.8127/09                 2</span>

the petitioners and respondents 6 and 7. The petitioners shall

forward a certified copy of this judgment along with a copy of

the writ petition to the 3rd respondent for compliance.




</p><p id="p_4">                                      S. SIRI JAGAN, JUDGE
