<div class="doc_title">An Application For Anticipatory ... vs In Re : Kunti Rabidas And Anr on 8 February, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                              1


08.02.17</pre>
<p id="p_1">71/skp.
</p><p id="p_2">Partly allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                    C.R.M. 181 of 2017



In the matter of : An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the
Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 06.01.2017 in connection with English Bazar
Police Station Case No. 872 of 2016          dated 16.10.2016 under <a href="/doc/1243353/" id="a_2">Sections
342</a>/<a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_3">325</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>/<a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_5">379</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_4">                    And

In Re : Kunti Rabidas and Anr.          ..... Petitioners.
</p>

<pre id="pre_2">         Mr. Mounick Ghosh        ... for the petitioners.


     Mr. Antirikhya Basu              .. for the State.



</pre><p id="p_5">         On scrutiny of the case diary produced by State Counsel and the injury report at pages 20 and 30 and bed-head
ticket at pages 2 and 3 so also the 161 statements at pages 7 to 11 of the case diary, calls for rejection of the application
of the petitioner no. 2 herein, namely, Nimai Rabidas.
</p><p id="p_6">         Accordingly, the application of the petitioner no. 2 is rejected and dismissed.
         Sufficient material, however, exists to warrant grant of anticipatory bail to the petitioner no. 1, namely, Kunti
Rabidas, in the event of her arrest upon furnishing bond of Rs. 10,000/- with two sureties of Rs. 5,000/- each to the
satisfaction of the arresting authority subject to the conditions as stipulated under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_7">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of
Criminal Procedure.
</p><p id="p_7">         The application in respect of the petitioner no. 1 is, therefore, allowed and disposed of.
         Accordingly, the application for anticipatory bail is, thus, partly allowed.
          Certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the parties on priority basis.
</p>


<p id="p_8">                                   (Patherya, J.)


                          (Amitabha Chatterjee,J.)
