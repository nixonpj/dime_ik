<div class="doc_title">Manoy Roy vs The State Of West Bengal &amp; Ors on 19 July, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                            1

181   19.7.                        W.P. 6417 (W) of 2011
AGM   2016
                                       Manoy Roy
                                         Versus
                             The State of West Bengal &amp; Ors



              Mr. Debabrata Dhar,
              Mr. Probhat Kumar Roy,
              Ms. Paromita Sahu,
                                    ... For the Petitioner.

                     Learned advocate appearing on behalf of the

              petitioner submits that this writ petition has been filed

              challenging the procedure of recruitment since the

              procedure of recruitment has been suffering from various

              illegalities    and/or     irregularities   and    thereby   the

              candidature of the petitioner has not been considered in

              its proper perspective by the recruitment authority.

                     Record reveals that no affidavit-of-service has been

              filed showing that the respondents or their learned</pre>

<p id="p_1">              advocates have been informed about the filing of this writ

              application, which has been filed in the year 2011.

</p><p id="p_2">                     The petitioner is thus directed to take appropriate

              steps in terms of writ rules and to inform learned

              advocates      for   the   State-respondents,     who   generally

              appears on behalf of the State-respondents.

</p><p id="p_3">                     The matter would be taken up for hearing under the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                         2</span>


heading "Listed Motion" on 3rd August 2016.



</p><p id="p_4">                                   ( Debi Prosad Dey, J.)
