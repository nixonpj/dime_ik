<div class="doc_title">Manjhi Purty vs State Of Jharkhand on 19 October, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">          IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                         B.A. No. 7257 of 2011

          Manjhi Purty                       .....   Petitioner
                                 Versus
          The State of Jharkhand      ....     Opposite Party

          CORAM:      HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.C. MISHRA

          For the Petitioner     :Mr. Amit Kumar
          For the State          :A. P.P.

                             -----
2/19.10.2011</pre><p id="p_1"> Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned A.P.P. for the
          Prosecution.
</p><p id="p_2">                Petitioner has been made accused for the offence under <a href="/doc/919170/" id="a_1">Sections 20</a>
          of the N.D.P.S. Act, in connection with Special Case No. 02 of 2011 arising
          out of Sidgora P.S. Case No. 11 of 2011, corresponding to G.R. Case No. 223
          of 2011.
</p><p id="p_3">                About 20 kg. of Ganga was recovered from one vehicle and it appears
          that the petitioner has been made accused being the owner of the said
          vehicle. The petitioner was not apprehended at the spot.
</p><p id="p_4">                In the facts and circumstances of the case, I am inclined to release the
          petitioner on bail. Accordingly, the petitioner Manjhi Purty is directed to be
          released on bail, on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten
          Thousand) with two sureties of like amount each to the satisfaction of
          learned 1st Additional Sessions Judge, Jamshedpur in connection with Special
          Case No. 02 of 2011 arising out of Sidgora P.S. Case No. 11 of 2011,
          corresponding to G.R. Case No. 223 of 2011.
</p>



<p id="p_5">                                                           (H. C. Mishra, J)
Umesh/-
</p>
