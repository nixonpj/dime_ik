<p id="p_1">        The petitioner was initially not permitted to sit for

the practical examination for the IIIrd year B.Pharm course

on the ground that she had not passed 1st year

Pharmaceutical Chemistry-11 paper. It is admitted that the

petitioner subsequently cleared the paper and was

permitted to sit for the practical examination for IIIrd year

B.Pharm course on the basis of the interim order dated

25-03-2011 in this writ petition. The surviving prayer in the

writ petition is for declaration of results of the practical

examination of IIIrd year B.Pharm course .

</p><p id="p_2">        2. The petitioner pointed out that the results of the

candidates similarly placed were declared pursuant to

Exts.P8 and P9 orders of this court. There cannot therefore

be any objection in the Mahatma Gandhi University

declaring the results of the petitioner for practical

examination for the IIIrd year B.Pharm course.

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">W.P.(C) No.9616 of 2010   2</span>

</p><p id="p_3">        3. The writ petition is disposed of directing the

Mahatma Gandhi University to declare the results of the

petitioner for the practical examination for the IIIrd year

B.Pharm course within a period of 3 weeks from today.

</p><p id="p_4">        The writ petition is disposed of as above.




</p><p id="p_5">                                 V.CHITAMBARESH

                                         JUDGE
