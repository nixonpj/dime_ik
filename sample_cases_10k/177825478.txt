<div class="doc_title">Regional Manager, Oriental ... vs Urpramila Devi &amp; Ors. on 9 October, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Miscellaneous Appeal No.66 of 2013
                 ======================================================
                 Regional Manager, Oriental Insurance Co. Ltd.  .... .... Appellant/s
                                                  Versus
                 Urpramila Devi &amp; Ors.                          .... .... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
            CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRAKASH CHANDRA JAISWAL
                                              ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">11    09-10-2017             Perused the office notes.

</p><p id="p_2">                             On perusal of memo of appeal, it appears that though in

                    paragraph-5 of the grounds of appeal, the appellant has assailed

                    the age and income of the deceased as assessed by the learned

                    lower Court but in the prayer portion it has prayed to remand the

                    appeal for re-assessment and for fresh determination of

                    contributory negligence for the accident claiming that there has

                    been contributory negligence of the two vehicles in the accident

                    and not for enhancement of quantum of compensation awarded by

                    the learned Tribunal. The appellant has not sought the relief

                    against the claimants. In such a situation, cross-objection in the

                    shape of appeal do not apply and the claimants, if so advised, may

                    file an appeal as provided in <a href="/doc/147367599/" id="a_1">Section 173</a> of the M.V. Act. Hence,

                    the cross-objection filed by the claimants is not maintainable and

                    is accordingly dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             List this case under proper heading.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                                      (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
Trivedi/-
