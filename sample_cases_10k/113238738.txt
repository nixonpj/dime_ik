<p id="p_1">RAM CHAND GUPTA, J.(Oral)

             The present petition filed under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C. is for grant
of anticipatory bail to the petitioner in case FIR No.212, dated 27.5.2012,
under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_2">Sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/1559723/" id="a_3">366-A</a>, <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_4">376</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120-B</a> IPC, registered at Police Station
District Jind.
</p><p id="p_2">             It has been contended by learned counsel for the petitioner that
this petition has been rendered infructuous and hence, he does not want to
proceed with the same and the same may be dismissed as such.
</p><p id="p_3">             Dismissed as withdrawn as having been rendered infructuous.
</p>
