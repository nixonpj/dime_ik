<pre id="pre_1">ORDER SHEET

                                W.P.No.225 of 2014
                         IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                           Constitutional Writ Jurisdiction
                                  ORIGINAL SIDE


                                                       PRADIP KUMKAR DUTTA
                                                                      Versus
                                              KOLKATA MUNICIPAL CORPORATION


   BEFORE:

   The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMITRA PAL

   Date : 14th March, 2014.

                                    For Plaintiff/Petitioner : Mr.L.M.Hazra,Advocate
                                                                 Mr.S.Panja,Advocate
                                                               Mrs.M.Hazra,Advocate
                                                For state: Mrs.S.Majumder,Advocate
                                                               Mrs.P.Ghatak,Advocate
                                   For respondent nos.4 &amp; 5 : Mr.D.Trivedi,Advocate</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                                For KMC :Mr. Fazlul Haque,Advocate



                   The Court : In the writ petition, the petitioner has prayed for a

direction upon the authorities of the Kolkata Municipal Corporation to allow him

to repair a portion of the premises occupied by him.

</p><p id="p_2">         It is submitted on behalf of the private respondent nos.4 and 5 that since a

civil suit being T.S.No.1358 of 2006 (Rafique Khanam &amp; Anr. vs. Sri Pradeep

Kumar Dutta &amp; Ors) is pending before the learned 11th Bench, City Civil Court at

Kolkata with regard to the self same premises, no order may be passed.

</p><p id="p_3">         Having heard learned advocates for the parties and considering the facts

and circumstances of the case, since a civil suit is pending, the writ petition is

disposed of by granting liberty to the petitioner to make appropriate application

before the appropriate forum in accordance with law and if so advised.




</p><p id="p_4">                                                                (SOUMITRA PAL, J.)

ssaha
AR(CR)
