<p id="p_1">                        (Per: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE JAYANANDAN SINGH)

9         06-12-2013                        By a reasoned order dated 12.04.2013, a Bench of

                         this Court has set aside the impugned judgment and has convicted

                         the respondents no. 2, 3 and 4. Thereafter, the Bench has called for

                         a report from the Probation Officer, Khagaria in respect of

                         desirability of granting benefit under <a href="/doc/755395/" id="a_1">Section 360</a> Cr. P.C. to the

                         convicted respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                            Reports have been received from the Probation

                         Officer, Khagaria in respect of above respondents which is

                         appropriate to be considered by the same Bench.

</p><p id="p_3">                                            Put up this appeal before the Bench which passed

                         the order and judgment dated 12.04.2013 after obtaining the orders

                         of Hon'ble Chief Justice.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                                                        (Jayanandan Singh, J.)


Shail/-                                              (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J.)
