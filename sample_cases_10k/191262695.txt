<div class="doc_title">Anil Kumar vs State Of Haryana And Others on 11 October, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">CWP No. 20436 of 2012                                           1

     IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT
                        CHANDIGARH.


                              CWP No. 20436 of 2012


                              Date of Decision : October 11, 2012


Anil Kumar
                                               ....   PETITIONER
                  Vs.

State of Haryana and others
                                               ..... RESPONDENTS



CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH



Present :    Mr. Vikas Lochab, Advocate,
             for the petitioner.


AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH, J. (ORAL)
</pre>


<p id="p_1">             Prayer in this petition is for issuance of a writ of

mandamus directing the respondents to grant the benefit of revised

overtime allowance to the petitioner in lieu of revision of pay scales

under notifications dated 07.01.1998 and 30.12.2008. Reliance has

also been placed on the judgment of this Court in CWP No. 15807 of

2008 titled as General Manager, Haryana Roadways, Chandigarh vs.

Azad Singh and others, decided on 5.3.2009 where this Court had

held the employees similar to the petitioner entitled to the benefit as

claimed in this writ petition. L.P.A. preferred against the said
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> CWP No. 20436 of 2012                                              2</span>

judgment has also been dismissed by a Division Bench of this Court

on 23.03.2011(Annexure-P-2). Petitioner has served a legal notice

dated 23.02.2012 (Annexure-P-1) but no decision thereon has been

taken till date.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Counsel for the petitioner, at this stage, contends that the

petitioner would be satisfied if a direction is issued to decide the legal

notice served by the petitioners on the Secretary-cum-Commissioner,

Transport Department, Haryana-respondent No. 1 within some

specified time.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Without going into the merits of the case or commenting

thereon, the present writ petition is disposed of with directions to the

Secretary-cum-Commissioner,        Transport     Department,    Haryana-

respondent No. 1 to consider the claim of the petitioner as put forth in

his legal notice dated 23.02.2012 (Annexure P-1) within a period of

two months from the date of receipt of a certified copy of this order

keeping in view the judgment referred to above. The decision so

taken be conveyed to the petitioner forthwith.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             In case, the claim of the petitioner is granted, the

consequential benefits be released to him within a further period of

two months. In case the claim as made by the petitioner through his

legal notice is not to be accepted, then a speaking and well reasoned

order be passed by respondent No. 1 and the same be conveyed to

the petitioner forthwith.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                    (AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH )
October 11, 2012                             JUDGE
