<p id="p_1">         1.           Heard. Normally, the respondents should
         be heard in the matter of condonation of delay.
         But,    in      the          case   on       hand,          as      the        delay            is
         minimal,         even         if,     notice            is       issued           to       the
         Respondents, the delay being minimal has to be
         condoned in view of the catena of decisions of
         the Apex Court in this regard. Hence, without
         issuing any notice to the respondents, in view of
         the     averments             made        in         the       application                 for
         condonation of delay, same is                                  ALLOWED and the
         delay is condoned.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.           Further, Criminal Misc. Application No.
         3619 of 2016 is for grant of leave for filing
         appeal      against           the    judgment              and       order         of      the
         trial Court, which is also allowed in view of the
