                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                     Criminal Miscellaneous No.44076 of 2014
                       Arising Out of PS.Case No. -809 Year- 2013 Thana -COMPLAINT CASE District- JAMUI
                   ======================================================
                   1. Sanjay Kumar Das Son of Late Premchand Das Resident of village-
                   Bhikhanpur, Gumti No.-12, Anand Bag Colony, P.S.- Ishakchak, District
                   Bhagalpur

                                                                                   .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                   1. The State of Bihar
                   2. Shweta Kumari alias Nutan Devi

                                                                    .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Satya Prakash Parasar
                   For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Sharda Kumari(App)
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                   SINGH
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   15-12-2014                   Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner being husband of the complainant is

                   apprehending arrest in a complaint case in which cognizance has

                   been taken under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">sections 498A</a> and <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a> of the Indian Penal Code

                   and <a href="/doc/751411/" id="a_3">sections 3</a> and <a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_4">4</a> of the Dowry Prohibition Act.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 The basic accusation is of torture for non fulfillment of

                     dowry demands.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

                   that the petitioner is ready to keep the complainant as wife with

                   full dignity and honour. Statement to that effect has been made in

                   paragraph 9 of the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   Let the learned counsel for the petitioner add the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.44076 of 2014 (2) dt.15-12-2014</span>




                      complainant as O.P. No. 2.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                      Issue notice to O.P. No. 2 under ordinary process as

                      well as by registered cover with A/d for which requisites etc. must

                      be filed within two weeks failing which this application as against

                      O.P. No. 2 shall stand rejected without further reference the bench.

</p><p id="p_7">                                      Let the ordinary process of notice be served on O.P.

                      No. 2 through her lawyer appearing before the learned court below

                      through the office of the learned court below.

</p><p id="p_8">                                     Let petitioner and O.P. No. 2 appear before this court

                      in person on 30th March,2015 at 2.10 P.M. in Chambers.

</p><p id="p_9">                                      In the meantime, let no coercive steps be taken against

                      the petitioner in connection with Complaint case no. 809C of 2013

                      pending in the court of the learned S.D.J.M. Jamui.




</p><p id="p_10">                                                 (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Anil/-
</p>
