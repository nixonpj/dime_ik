                            JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">      Petitioner society has been functioning since 3.8.2008.

Conducting Group Deposit and Credit Scheme (GDCS) is one of

the objective of the petitioner society. Petitioner has been

conducting certain number of GDCS since August 2015.

However, the same will expire within two months. Thereupon,

petitioner society has submitted Exts.P2 and P4 seeking sanction

of the respondent in respect of new GDCS to be started.

However, no orders are passed on the same. This constrained

the petitioner to approach this Court by filing this writ petition.

</p><p id="p_2">      Having head learned Counsel for the petitioner and learned

Government Pleader and perusing the pleadings on records,

there will be a direction to the respondent to consider Exts.P2

and P4 in accordance with law and attain finality to the same,

within a period of two months from the date of receipt of a copy

of this judgment.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      The writ petition is disposed of as above.
