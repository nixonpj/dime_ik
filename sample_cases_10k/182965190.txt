<div class="doc_title">Rashmi Tiwarid vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 17 May, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                           WP-5857-2017
             (RASHMI TIWARID Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


17-05-2017

      Shri A.K. Dixit, learned counsel for the petitioner.
      Shri Piyush Jain, learned Panel Lawyer for the
respondents/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard.
</p><p id="p_2">      The petitioner before this Court is presently working as
A.N.M at Community Health Centre, Banda, Distt. Sagar (M.P.).
 She is aggrieved by the inaction on the part of the respondents
to regularize her services and in this regard, she has also
submitted a representation on 06.11.2015 (Annexure P/2), which
is still not decided.
</p><p id="p_3">      The contention of the petitioner is that she was appointed
on the post of A.N.M. on 26.06.2006 and other similarly situated
employees have already been regularized in her case.
</p><p id="p_4">      Since no order has been passed on the aforesaid
representation, in the circumstances, the petition is disposed of
with a direction to the petitioner to submit a fresh representation
alongwith all the relevant documents before the respondent no.2
within a period of two weeks from the date of receipt of certified
copy of this order and the said respondent shall decide the same
in accordance with law within a further period of two months
from the date submission of representation alongwith certified
copy of this order, by speaking and reasoned order.
</p><p id="p_5">      Accordingly, the petition stands disposed of.
      C.C. as per rules.
</p><p id="p_6">      (SUBODH ABHYANKAR)
           JUDGE
