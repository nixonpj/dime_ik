IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                                 CWP No.2411 of 2013
                                 Date of Decision: 04.02.2013


The Varpal Cooperative Labour and Construction Society Limited
                                               ..... Petitioner

                                        Versus

State of Punjab and others
                                                   ..... Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">CORAM:-        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJIV NARAIN RAINA

Present:     Mr. Hemant Saini, Advocate,
             for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">1. To be referred to the Reporters or not?
</p><p id="p_3">2. Whether the judgment should be reported in the Digest?


RAJIV NARAIN RAINA, J.(Oral)

             The claim in this petition is by a Contractor who claims that his

bills have not been discharged even though the construction of Suvida

Centre on the ground floor in the Tehsil Complex Tarn Taran stands

completed in March 2012. The petitioner has served a legal notice dated

26.09.2012 (P-1) which has not been acted upon so far.

</p><p id="p_4">             Learned counsel for the petitioner would be satisfied if a

direction be issued to the respondents to consider and decide the aforesaid

legal notice within a time bound period for release of outstanding payments.

Learned counsel relies on a decision of this Court rendered in CWP

No.14213 of 2008 passed by this Court on 12.09.2008 containing similar

directions as prayed for in this petition.

</p><p id="p_5">             Without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, the
 CWP No.2411 of 2013
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                     -2-</span>


respondents are directed to consider and decide the legal notice dated

26.09.2012 (P-1) within a period of two months from the date of receipt of

copy of this order and in case, the claims are found due, the same be

released within a further period of one month thereafter.

</p><p id="p_6">             The petition stands disposed of with the above directions.




</p><p id="p_7">                                            (RAJIV NARAIN RAINA)
                                                   JUDGE
04.02.2013
manju
