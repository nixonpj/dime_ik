 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED 05.11.2014
CORAM
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.NAGAMUTHU
Crl.OP No.28992 of 2014
1.Maragatham
2.M.Mohanraj
3.Sankari
4.M.Sonia			..	Petitioners
Vs
The State rep. By
The Inspector of Police,
G-6 Chaiyur Police Station,
Kancheepuram District.		        ..     Respondent

Prayer:- Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., to direct the respondent to register a case based on the complaint given by the petitioner dated 26.09.2014. 

	For Petitioner 	  :Mr.K.Jayakumar

	For Respondent      :Mr.M.Maharaja,	
			   Additional Public Prosecutor

			   ORDER
</pre>	
<p id="p_1">	Seeking a direction to the respondent to register a case based on the complaint given by the petitioner dated 26.09.2014, the petitioner has come up with this petition.

S.NAGAMUTHU,J
jbm
</p><p id="p_2">	2.I have heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned  Additional Public Prosecutor appearing for the respondent and I have also perused the records carefully. 	

</p><p id="p_3">	3.A perusal of the complaint of the petitioner dated 26.09.2014 would go to show that there is a dispute in respect of the landed property between the petitioner and the proposed accused. In my considered opinion, the said allegation does not make any cognizable offence warranting investigation. Further, it makes out only a civil dispute. Therefore, the Criminal Original Petition is dismissed however, with liberty to the petitioner to work out his remedies in the manner known to law.
 
05.11.2014
jbm

To

</p><p id="p_4">1.The Inspector of Police,
G-6 Chaiyur Police Station,
Kancheepuram District.
</p>
<p id="p_5">2.The Public Prosecutor,
High Court, Madras.
</p><p id="p_6">Crl.OP No.28992 of 2014</p>
