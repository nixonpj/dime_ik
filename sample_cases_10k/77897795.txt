<div class="doc_title">B.U.Suganya vs The Inspector Of Police on 14 June, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 14.06.2018  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE D.KRISHNAKUMAR             

Crl.O.P(MD)No.9602 of 2015  
                                
B.U.Suganya                                     .. Petitioner/
                                                                 Defacto Complainant

                                                Vs.

The Inspector of Police,
Dindigul All Women Police Station,
Dindigul District.                                      
(Crime No.19/2016)                              .. Respondent/                  
                                                           Complainant

PRAYER: Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of Cr.P.C. praying
to direct the learned Judicial Magistrate No.II, Dindigul, to expedite the
trial in C.C.No.524 of 2017  on the file of the learned Judicial Magistrate
No.II, Dindigul within the stipulated time to be fixed by this Court.
For Petitioner             : Mr.V.Kannan
For Respondent     : Mr.K.Suyambulingabharathi        
                                    Government Advocate (Criminal side)



:ORDER  
</pre><p id="p_1">        This petition is filed for a direction to the learned Judicial
Magistrate No.II, Dindigul to expedite the trial and dispose of the case in
C.C.No.524  of 2017, within a time limit.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned
Government Advocate (Criminal Side) for the respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3. According to the petitioner, after marriage, the petitioner was
living in her matrimonial home with her husband and in-laws in abroad. At
that time, the proposed accused persons have demanded additional dowry and  
also caused mental torture to her and forcibly sent her to India. Therefore,
based on her complaint a case has been registered against the proposed
accused in Crime No.19 of 2016 for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_2">Sections
406</a>, <a href="/doc/1868826/" id="a_3">498(A)</a> of IPC., and <a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_4">Section 4</a> of Dowry Prohibition Act on the file of
the respondent police. After completion of investigation, the respondent
police filed final report and the same was taken as C.C.No.524 of 2017 on the
file of the learned Judicial Magistrate No.II, Dindigul. But the trial Court
unnecessarily and periodically adjourned the case without assigning any valid
reason and therefore, the present petition has been filed before this Court
for seeking a direction to expedite the trial and disposal of the case within
a time frame.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4.  Having been taken into consideration of the related facts and
circumstances, this Court is of view that in the interest of Justice it may
be expedient to issue a direction to the trial Court, as sought for by the
petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">        5. In the light of the above, the learned Judicial Magistrate No.II,
Dindigul, is hereby directed to dispose of the case in C.C.No.524 of 2017, as
expeditiously as possible without loss of further time and without giving
frequent adjournments.
</p>
<p id="p_6">        6. With the above direction, this  Criminal Original  Petition  is
disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_7">To
The Inspector of Police,
Dindigul All Women Police Station,
Dindigul District.
</p>
