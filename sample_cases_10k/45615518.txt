HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 42
 

 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. WRIT PETITION No. - 2864 of 2012
 

 
Petitioner :- Ranjeet And Others
 
Respondent :- State Of U.P. &amp; Others
 
Petitioner Counsel :- Satendra Kumar Upadhyay
 
Respondent Counsel :- Govt. Advocate,Devesh Dixit,S.P.Pandey
 

 
Hon'ble Dharnidhar Jha,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Hon'ble Vijay Prakash Pathak,J.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">Heard  Sri  Satendra Kumar Upadhyay,  learned counsel for the petitioners and  Sri Sanjay Kumar Dubey who   appears for the  respondent no.4 through a  power filed today itself. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">The couple Ranjeet and  Smt. Shalu Sengar  are present in the court room, who are identified by their counsel Sri Satendra Kumar Upadhyay. The age of Smt. Shalu Sengar as noted  in the F.I.R. is 22 years and she  states that  she had married  Ranjeet.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">The police is directed not to arrest the petitioners, namely,    Ranjeet, Dinesh, Om Prakash and Smt. Shalu Sengar  till  submission of charge sheet in case crime no.  91 of 2012 under section 366 I. P.C. police station Hathras Junction district  Mahamaya Nagar. 
</p> 
<p id="p_5">The writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Order Date :- 16.3.2012
