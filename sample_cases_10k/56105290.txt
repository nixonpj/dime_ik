                          JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">      Petitioner on the strength of building permit for the

purpose of constructing residential building wants to extract

ordinary earth from the site covered by the permit.

</p><p id="p_2">      2.    In view of the Government order, no permit is

required for extracting ordinary earth from the site covered by

building permit.    However, for the purpose of transporting

ordinary earth, O(A) forms are necessary. Therefore, there shall

be a direction to the Geologist to issue O(A)-form on being

satisfied with the genuineness of the claim of the petitioner

based on building permit and after ascertaining the quantity to

be removed from the site. Needful shall be done within a period

of ten days from the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment.

However, it is made clear that if the petitioner fails to construct

any residential building, Geologist is free to take appropriate

legal action against the petitioner for contravening the provisions

of the Kerala Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 1967.

</p><p id="p_3">      The Writ Petition is disposed of as above.
</p>
