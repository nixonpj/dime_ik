<div class="doc_title">Ravi Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 4 July, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.40936 of 2019
                   Arising Out of PS. Case No.-308 Year-2019 Thana- PHULWARISHARIF District- Patna
                 ======================================================
                 Ravi Kumar, aged about 22 years, Gender-M, S/o Sri Nilkamal Sharma @
                 Nityanand Sharma @ Nil Kamal R/v- Jamalpur, P.S.- Athmalgola, Barh,
                 District- Patna.

                                                                                   ... ... Petitioner
                                                      Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                          ... ... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner      :       Mr. Vijay Kumar Sinha,
                                                 Mr. S.K. Bhatnagar, Advocates
                 For the State           :       Mr. Harendra Prasad, APP
                 For the Informant       :       Mr. Mritunjay Kumar, Advocate
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VIKASH JAIN
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   04-07-2019                  Learned counsel for the petitioner informs that the case
                   diary in the case of co-accused Nil Kamal @ Nilkamal Sharma @
                   Nityanand Sharma has been called for by a coordinate Bench of this
                   Court vide order dated 03.07.2019 passed in Cr. Misc. No. 41079 of
                   2019 in which interim protection has been granted to the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">                             2. Learned counsel for the informant appears suo motu.
</p><p id="p_3">                             3. In view of the nature of order dated 03.07.2019 passed in
                   Cr. Misc. No. 41079 of 2019, let this case be listed along with the
                   aforesaid Cr. Misc. No. 41079 of 2019 before the appropriate Bench
                   after obtaining orders of Hon'ble the Chief Justice.
</p><p id="p_4">                            4. Till further orders, in the event of petitioner's arrest or
                   surrender within four weeks hereof let the above named petitioner be
                   released on provisional anticipatory bail on furnishing bail bond of
                   Rs.10,000/- [ten thousand] with two sureties of like amount each to
                   the satisfaction of learned Sub-Judge-XV- cum -A.C.J.M., Patna in
                   connection with Phulwarisharif P.S. Case No. 308 of 2019, subject
                   to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C.
                   and with further conditions --
</p><p id="p_5">            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.40936 of 2019(2) dt.04-07-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_6">                                    (i) That one of the bailors shall be a close relative of the
                     petitioner.
</p><p id="p_7">                                     (ii) That the petitioner shall not indulge in any similar
                     offence till conclusion of the trial.
</p><p id="p_8">                                     (iii) That the petitioner shall remain physically present
                     in court on each and every date during trial and in the event of failure
                     on two consecutive dates without sufficient reason, his bail bond
                     shall be liable to be cancelled by the learned Court concerned.
</p><p id="p_9">                                    (iv) That the petitioner shall cooperate with the
                     investigation, if not already concluded, and make himself available as
                     and when so required and in case of failure, the State shall be at
                     liberty to move for cancellation of bail.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                                                                            (Vikash Jain, J)

BT/-
</p>
