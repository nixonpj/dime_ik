<div class="doc_title">Prakash Chandra vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 20 December, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">               Writ Petition No : 21310/2012
           Prakash Chandra Vs. State of MP and others


20.12.2012</pre><p id="p_1">.
</p><p id="p_2">         Shri Anshuman Singh for the petitioner.
         Shri Sanjeev Kumar Singh, Panel Lawyer, for the State
on advance notice.
</p><p id="p_3">         Having heard learned counsel for the parties and taking
note of the fact that inspite of the order passed on 27.7.2012, in
Writ Petition No. 11351/2012, representation of the petitioner
has not been decided till date, the competent authority of the
department is directed to decide the representation after taking
note of the disability expressed by the petitioner within a period
of one month from the date of receipt of certified copy of this
order.
</p><p id="p_4">         While deciding the representation, the physical disability
of the petitioner and the prayer made for certain alternate
adjustment shall be taken note and a decision taken.
</p><p id="p_5">         With the aforesaid, the petition stands disposed of.
         Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
