                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE MIHIR KUMAR JHA
                   ORAL ORDER

20   27-07-2015               Heard learned counsel for the parties at length in respect

                    of C.W.J.C.No. 10417/2008 wherein one of the issues raised by

                    the learned Senior counsel (Mr. Shivendra Kishore) is that since

                    the petitioner Dharamdeo Pathak was appointed on 13.8.1976 on

                    the post of Lecturer in Science despite having a qualification of

                    only Graduation (B.Sc.Hons.) and that he was assigned teaching

                    duty in the Department of Darvagun on 26.1.1981 as also made

                    Garden Manager, whereafter he became a permanent Lecturer on

                    25.2.1981 and eventually earned promotion on the post of Reader

                    in the department of Darvagun on 25.1.1984 as also on the post of

                    Professor in the department of Darvagun on 5.7.1985, he will be

                    entitled for being absorbed on teaching post keeping in view the

                    requirement laid down in the Statutes III under Chapter XVI-B of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_5"> Patna High Court CWJC No.7246 of 2008 (20) dt.27-07-2015                      6</span>




             the Statutes (Number, Grades, Qualifications, Pay Scales, Mode of

             Appointment of Teachers and Officers in the Faculty of

             Ayurveda), published in Bihar University Calendar in 1986, which

             lays down that any person who has already been appointed prior to

             coming into force of the Statute will be deemed to have qualified

             for the post held by him. This argument will eventually bring this

             Court to find out the date of framing of the Statutes.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                       The submission of Mr. Shivendra Kishore is that since it

             has been incorporated in the Bihar University Calendar published

             in the year 1986 it will be deemed to have been framed only in the

             year 1986.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                       This Court, however, wants to be assured about framing

             of the date of the Statutes as contained in Chapter XVI-B of the

             Bihar University Calendar Vol.II.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                       This Court has, accordingly, requested Mr. Y.V.Giri,

             learned Senior counsel appearing on behalf of the Chancellor as

             also Mr. Vikash Ratan Bharti, learned counsel for the University

             as well as Mr. D.K.Sinha, learned Senior counsel for the State, to

             come out with the specific case of the date when the Statute, as

             contained in Chapter XVI-B came into force after getting the

             consent of the State Government and assent of the Hon'ble

             Chancellor.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_6">          Patna High Court CWJC No.7246 of 2008 (20) dt.27-07-2015                          7</span>




<p id="p_5">                                Put up all these cases on 3.8.2015 under the same heading.
</p>


<p id="p_6">                                                                    (Mihir Kumar Jha, J)
surendra/-
