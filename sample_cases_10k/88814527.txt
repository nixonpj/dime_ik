<div class="doc_title">Upendra Yadav &amp; Anr vs The State Of Bihar on 6 November, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.67589 of 2018
                        Arising Out of PS. Case No.-41 Year-2014 Thana- KARPI District- Jehanabad
               ======================================================
            1. Upendra Yadav Son of Kaliharan Yadav @ Kalicharan Yadav
            2. Ram Dhani Yadav Son of Late Gyanchand Yadav Both are
               Resident of Village-Chainpur,P.S. Karpi,Distt.-Arwal

                                                                                    ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :        Mr. Nilesh Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :        Mr. Mr. Manoj Kumar
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE S. KUMAR
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   06-11-2018                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned

                  counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  The petitioners, who are in custody, seek bail in

                  connection with Karpi (O.P. Saher Telpa) P.S. Case No. 41 of

                  2014 registered for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>,

                  <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_2">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_5">324</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_6">504</a> of the Indian Penal Code and

                  subsequently <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_7">Section 302</a> of IPC was also added.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  Allegation against petitioners and other accused

                  persons is of assaulting the informant with lathi danda causing

                  injury to him.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioners

                  that they are innocent and have been falsely implicated in this

                  case. There is general and omnibus allegation against 15 FIR
               Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.67589 of 2018(2) dt.06-11-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                            2/2</span>




                       named accused person. According to postmortem report only

                       one injury has been found on the person of deceased. Petitioners

                       have no criminal antecedent and they are in custody since

                       12.09.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                       Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances of

                       the case, let the petitioner named above be released on bail upon

                       furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with two

                       sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                       Addl. Chief Judicial Magistrate-IV, Arwal, in connection with

                       Karpi (O.P. Saher Telpa) P.S. Case No. 41 of 2014 subject to the

                       conditions that:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                               (1) Bailors should be local having sufficient
                                          immovable property within the jurisdiction of the
                                          court concerned.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                                               (2) Petitioners shall co-operate in the trial
                                          and shall be represented on each and every date
                                          fixed by the court.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                                               (3) If the petitioners tampers with the
                                          evidence or the witnesses of the case, in that case,
                                          prosecution will be at liberty to move for
                                          cancellation of bail of the petitioners.</blockquote><p id="p_6">




</p><p id="p_7">                                                        (S. Kumar, J)
veena/-
</p>
