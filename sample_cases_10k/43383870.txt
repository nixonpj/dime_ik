<div class="doc_title">Jagyalal @ Chotawa vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 1 May, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                       CRR-1022-2017
        (JAGYALAL @ CHOTAWA Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


01-05-2017

Shri S.K.Mishra, Advocate for the applicants/ accused.
       Shri Amit Pandey, P.L. for the respondent-State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Heard on the question of admission.
       Revision is admitted for hearing.
</p><p id="p_2">       Let the records of the two courts below be called
for.
</p><p id="p_3">          Also heard on I.A.No.7954/2017 filed by the
applicants under <a href="/doc/1632932/" id="a_1">section 397(1)</a> of Cr.P.C. for suspension
of their jail sentence dated 17.4.2017 passed by Special
Judge (S.C./<a href="/doc/1806623/" id="a_2">S.T. Act</a>), Sidhi, in Cr.Appeal No.34/2017
arising out of the judgment of conviction and sentence
dated 15.2.2017 passed by the JMFC, Churhat, District
Sidhi, in Cr. Case No.1060/2010, convicting the
applicants under <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_3">section 325</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the I.P.C. and
sentencing each of them to undergo R.I. for 1 year and
fine of Rs.500/-, with default stipulation.
</p><p id="p_4">         Learned counsel for the applicants submits that
during trial the applicants were on bail and they did not
misuse the liberty so far. Fine amount has already been
deposited. Further, the applicants are in jail since the
date of judgment, i.e. 17.4.2017.
</p><p id="p_5">        Looking to the aforesaid facts and circumstances
of the case; I.A.No.7954/2017 is allowed. It is directed
that on deposit of fine amount, if not already deposited,
the execution of jail sentence of applicants Jagyalal @
 Chotawa, Daddi @ Dadiya Saket, Sitaram Saket and
Ramlal Saket shall remain suspended and they be
released on bail on their furnishing a personal bond for a
sum of Rs.25,000/- each with one solvent surety each in
the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial Court for
their appearance before CJM Sidhi on 25.7.2017 &amp;
thereafter on all other such subsequent dates as may be
fixed by that Court in this regard.
</p><p id="p_6">      In case, the applicants are found absent on any date
fixed by the CJM, then the said CJM shall be free to issue
and execute warrant of arrest without referring the
matter to this Court, provided the Registry of this Court
is kept informed.
</p><p id="p_7">          List for final hearing in due course as per the
listing policy.
</p><p id="p_8">         C.C. as per rules.
</p>


<p id="p_9">                                           (J. P. GUPTA)
                                               JUDGE




HS
