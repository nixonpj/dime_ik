</p><p id="p_2">                           1.   Whether reporters of local newspapers may be allowed to see judgment?
</p><p id="p_3">                           2.   To be referred to reporters or not?
</p><p id="p_4">                           3.   Whether the judgment should be reported in the Digest?



                           Present:-     Mr. Amit Dhawan, Advocate for
                                         Mr. B.S. Jaswal, Advocate
                                         for the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                         Mr. R.S. Nain, AAG Punjab.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                           HARI PAL VERMA J.(Oral)

                                         Prayer in the present petition is for quashing of cross case

                           registered vide DDR No.24 dated 25.10.2014, under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_2">324</a>

                           and <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a> IPC (Annexure P-1) in FIR No.162 dated 24.10.2014, under

                           <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">Sections 307</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120-B</a> IPC, registered at Police Station,Mehatpur,

                           District Jalandhar (Annexure P-2) on the basis of compromise dated

                           25.3.2015 (Annexure P-3).
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                         In compliance of the order dated 10.4.2015, this Court has

                           directed the parties to appear before the Illaqa Magistrate/trial Court for

                           recording       their     respective    statements      with      regard     to

                           compromise/settlement.         Pursuant thereto, the parties have appeared

                           before the learned Magistrate on 24.4.2015 for getting their statements



VIJAY ASIJA
2015.05.11 17:15
I attest to the accuracy and
integrity of this document
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                            Criminal Misc. No. M-11536 of 2015                      -2-</span>




                           recorded.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                         The report received from the learned Judicial Magistrate 1st

                           Class, Nakodar, which is annexed with Criminal Misc. No. M-11270 of

                           2015 titled 'Kashmir Singh @ Dr. Nauni and others vs. State of Punjab

                           and others' reveals that the compromise has been effected between the

                           parties without any undue pressure.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                         Learned State counsel does not dispute the aforesaid factual

                           position, rather admit the factum of compromise effected between the

                           parties.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                                         Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                                         Since the parties have entered into a compromise and

                           learned Magistrate has recorded their statements followed by her report,

                           continuation of the trial arising out from the impugned FIR and

                           consequential proceedings would be sheer abuse of the process of law

                           since chances of the conviction are bleak.

</p><p id="p_12">                                         Keeping in view the factum of compromise effected between

                           the parties dated 25.3.2015 and the law laid down by this Court in the

                           case of <a href="/doc/811682/" id="a_6">Kulwinder Singh and others vs. State of Punjab and another</a>

                           2007(3) RCR (Crl.) 1052 (P &amp; H), this petition is accepted and DDR

                           No.24 dated 25.10.2014, under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_7">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_8">324</a> and <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_9">341</a> IPC

                           (Annexure P-1) in FIR No.162 dated 24.10.2014, under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_10">Sections 307</a> and

                           <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_11">120-B</a> IPC, registered at Police Station,Mehatpur, District Jalandhar and

                           the consequential proceedings arising therefrom are hereby quashed.



</p><pre id="pre_2">                           May 08, 2015                             ( HARI PAL VERMA )
                           Vijay Asija                                     JUDGE
