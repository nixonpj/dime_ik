<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                 (Per: HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE)

7   10-04-2019                   Heard learned counsel for the applicant and perused

                    the affidavit of jointness filed today. Learned counsel for the

                    Respondent No. 5 is present. It has been pointed out that in view

                    of the provisions of <a href="/doc/1054471/" id="a_1">Bihar Act</a> 6 of 2019, the provisions of Sub-

                    section (3) of Section 16 of the Bihar Land Reforms (Fixation of

                    Ceiling Area and Acquisition of Surplus Land) Act, 1961 has

                    been repealed. In view of this, this Review Application has

                    become infructuous and is, accordingly, consigned to records.



</p><p id="p_2">                                        (Amreshwar Pratap Sahi, CJ)


                                           ( Rajeev Ranjan Prasad, J)
