<div class="doc_title">Damru Alias Mukhiya vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh Thr on 6 September, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                      1


                                M.Cr.C.No.8394/2017
         (Damru @ Mukhiya Vs. State of M.P.)
06.09.2017
      Mr. Prateep Visoriya, learned counsel for the applicant.
      Mr. Vivek Bhargava, learned Public Prosecutor for the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Case diary is available.
</p><p id="p_2">      This is first bail application filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of
Cr.P.C for grant of bail. The applicant has been arrested on
19.06.2017 in connection with Crime No.83/2017 registered by
Police Station Thareth, District Datia, for offences punishable
under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/520270/" id="a_3">327</a>, <a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_4">452</a>, <a href="/doc/594493/" id="a_5">294</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_6">506</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_7">34</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_3">      It is submitted by the counsel for the applicant that
according to the prosecution case, the applicant along with the
co-accused went to the shop of the complainant and asked for
liquor free of cost. When the complainant refused to give the
same, he was warned by the applicant and the co-accused
persons and was beaten. As per the MLC report, four abrasions
were found out of which one was on the parietal region and the
remaining were on forearm and index or little finger. It is
further submitted that although in the x-ray report, fracture of
metacarpal bone right hand of the complainant was found but
the offence under <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_8">Section 325</a> is bailable. From the MLC report,
the injuries were not caused on any vital part of the
complainant. The applicant is aged about 26 years and he is in
jail from 19.06.2017 and trial is likely to take sufficiently long
time. It is further submitted that the bail application of co-
accused was dismissed by this Court vide order dated
18.08.2017 passed in M.Cr.C.No.7486/2017 by considering the
short period of detention of the said accused and 18 days have
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                 2</span>

       passed after dismissal of the application of the co-accused,
       therefore, this application may be considered. It is further
       submitted that the applicant is ready and willing to abide by all
       the conditions which may be imposed by this Court while
       granting bail. There is no possibility of his absconding or
       tampering with the prosecution case.
</p><p id="p_4">             Per-contra, application is opposed by the State counsel.
             Considering the facts and circumstances of the case and
       without commenting on the merits of the case, the application is
       allowed. It is directed that the applicant be released on bail on
       furnishing a personal bond in the sum of Rs. 40,000/- (Rs.
       Forty Thousand Only) with one surety in the like amount to
       the satisfaction of the Trial Court/Committal Court to appear
       before the Court on the dates given by the concerned Court.
</p><p id="p_5">             It is further directed that the applicant shall appear before
       the SHO, Police Station Thareth, District Datia on 1st of every
       month till final disposal of the trial.
</p><p id="p_6">             This order shall be effective till the end of trial but in case
       of bail jump or in default of non-appearance before SHO, Police
       Station Thareth, District Datia , it shall become ineffective.
</p><p id="p_7">             Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                                                 (G.S. Ahluwalia)
                                                     Judge
bj/-
</p>
