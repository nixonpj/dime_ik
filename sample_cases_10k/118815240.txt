<div class="doc_title">Dhanjee Yadav @ Jhanjhi Yadav @ ... vs State Of Bihar on 7 October, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              Cr.Misc. No.34865 of 2010
                1. Dhanjee Yadav @ Jhanjhi Yadav @ Sheo Bahadur Yadav, Son of Kedar
                   Yadav.
                2. Bithal Yadav, Son of Kedar Yadav.
                   Both residents of Village-Giridhar Baraon, P.S.-Nawanagar, District-
                   Buxar.                                                 -Petitioners.


                                            VERSUS
                    The State of Bihar                                      -Opposite Party.
                                             -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">02     07.10.2010                        Petitioners are in custody in relation to Nawanagar

                        (Sonbarsha) P.S. Case No.56 of 2010 for an offence under Sections 302

                        and other<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> Sections of the Indian Penal Code</a>. <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Section 302</a> IPC was added

                        subsequently because of death of Raj Kumari Devi, the mother of the

                        informant.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                         Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that reading

                        the entire F.I.R. which states assault on various family members there is

                        not a whisper about the presence of the mother of the informant or an

                        assault on her. She was above 80 years old. Two days later she died and

                        taking advantage of that in further statement the informant states that she

                        was also present and one Kedar Yadav assaulted her with lathi because of

                        which she died subsequently. This entire thing is missing from the F.I.R.

</p><p id="p_3">                                         Considering the facts aforesaid, let the petitioners, Dhanjee

                        Yadav alias Jhanjhi Yadav alias Sheo Bahadur Yadav and Bithal Yadav

                        be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten

                        Thousand) each with two sureties of like amount each to the satisfaction

                        of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Buxar in connection with Nawanagar

                        (Sonbarsha) P.S. Case No.56 of 2010.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><pre id="pre_2">     Trivedi/                                  (Navaniti Prasad Singh, J.)
