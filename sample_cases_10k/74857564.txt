<p id="p_2">                 Instant application has been filed by the applicants/petitioners 12 &amp; 13, seeking
     withdrawal of the writ petition.

     applicants/petitioners 12 &amp; 13 shall stand dismissed as withdrawn. Registry to
     update the cause title of the writ petition accordingly. MP is disposed of.

</p>
<p id="p_5">                 Instant application has been filed by the applicant/petitioner No. 06, namely,
     Mudasir Ahmad Mir, seeking withdrawal of the writ petition.
</p><p id="p_6">                 On the set of facts and the grounds urged, coupled with the submissions made
     at the Bar, the instant application is allowed and the writ petition to the extent of
     applicant/petitioner No. 6 shall stand dismissed as withdrawn with liberty to file
     fresh one in case cause survives. Registry to update the cause title of the writ petition
     accordingly. MP is disposed of.
</p><p id="p_7">                 Main petition be listed on 28.03.2019.
