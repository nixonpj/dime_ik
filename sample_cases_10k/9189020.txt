<div class="doc_title">(Cradel Pharmaceuticals Pvt. ... vs Union Of on 6 October, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                        1


        06.10.16
 2      Ct. No.29
Sws.M
                                        W.P. 18705(W) of 2016

                      (Cradel Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. &amp; Anr. vs. Union of
                                        India &amp; Ors. )

                               Mr. Partha Sarathi Sengupta
                               Mr. Padam Khaitan
                               Mr. V. Jhunjhunwala
                                                                 ....for the petitioners
                               Mr. Somnath Bose
                               Mr. Arpa Chakraborty
                                                            ....for the Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">                                Sri Partha Sarathi Sengupta, learned Senior

                    Counsel appears for the petitioners. The respondents/

                    Union of India represented by Sri Somnath Bose, learned

                    Counsel.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Having heard the parties and considering the

                    materials placed, and, in the light of the observations

                    recorded vide its earlier order dated 5th October, 2016 as

                    well as noticing the observations of another Hon'ble Single

                    Bench in the present writ petition vide order dated 3rd

                    October, 2016, this Court is of the considered view that the

                    matter requires to be examined by the Director, National

                    Pharmaceuticals Pricing Authority (for short NPPA), deeper.

</p><p id="p_3">                                Accordingly,   the    Company-petitioners         are

                    granted     the   opportunity    to   file    a   comprehensive

                    representation covering all the points in respect of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                        2</span>


demand raised before the Director, NPPA who shall then

examine the same after giving the Company through its

authorised representative an opportunity of being heard.

</p><p id="p_4">             The Director, NPPA shall then take a reasoned

decision which shall be communicated to the Company.

The entire exercise as indicated above shall be completed

within   a    period       of   eight   weeks   from   the   date   of

communication of this order.            In the event, the reasoned

order of the Director is adverse to the interests of the

Company, the same shall not be given effect to for a further

period of one week thereafter.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             The order impugned dated 10th June, 2016 along

with all consequential orders thereto shall be kept in

abeyance. The Director, NPPA shall be free to consider the

subject matter before him on its own merits in accordance

with law.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             Since no affidavit-in-opposition is used, the

allegations made in the writ petition are deemed not to have

been admitted.
</p>
<p id="p_7">             With the above directions, W.P. 18705(W) of

2016 stands disposed of.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                    3</span><p id="p_8">


</p><p id="p_9">          Urgent certified photocopy of this order, if

applied for, will be made available to the parties subject to

compliance with all requisite formalities.



</p><p id="p_10">                            (Subrata Talukdar, J.)
 </p>
