     IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA AT BANGALORE

       DATED THIS THE 1st DAY OF SEPTEMBER, 2014

                        BEFORE

     THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A.N.VENUGOPALA GOWDA

          WRIT PETITION No.27068/2012 (GM-KSFC)

BETWEEN

SRI GURUPRASAD JOSHI
SRI NARSIMHACHARYA JOSHI
AGED ABOUT 39 YEARS,
FLAT NO. C-1, IIND FLOOR,
"SHREYA RESIDENCY",
NO. 22/2, NAGASHETTYHALLI,
BANGALORE.
                                            ... PETITIONER

(By Sri. C S SURYA KANTH, ADV.)


AND

1.     KARNATAKA STATE FINANCIAL CORPORATION
       BANGALORE RURAL BRANCH,
       NO. 197, IST FLOOR, 2ND STAGE,
       WEST OF CHORD ROAD,
       BANGALORE-86, (REP. BY ITS BRANCH MANAGER)

2.     M/S MOKSHA BUILDERS
       NO. 24, 3RD CROSS, JALADARSHINI LAYOUT,
       OFF NEW BHEL ROAD, SANJAYNAGAR, BANGALORE
       (REP. BY ITS MG. PARTNER
       SRI. B M GIRISH, MAJOR).
                                          ... RESPONDENTS

(BY SRI.GURURAJ JOSHI, ADV. FOR R1)
                            -2-

     THIS WRIT PETITION IS FILED UNDER ARTICLES 226 AND
227 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA PRAYING TO: QUASH THE
E-AUCTION SALE NOTICE DTD.25.7.12, IN SO FAR AS IT
PERTAINS TO E-AUCTION SALE OF FLAT NO.C-1 MEASURING
1550 SQ. FEET AT "SHREYA RESIDENCY" APARTMENT, 1ST
MAIN ROAD, DOLLARS COLONY, 3RD CROSS ROAD, GOWDARA
COLONY, NAGASHETTYHALLI, BANGALORE-94 VIDE ANNEX-E
AND ETC.,

     THIS PETITION COMING ON FOR PRLY. HEARING 'B'
GROUP THIS DAY, THE COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING: -


                       ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Having entered into an agreement of sale with the

second respondent on 6.7.2007, for purchase of Flat-

C1, situated at No.16, PID No.100-268/16, property

No.22/2, 'Shreya Residency', 1st Main Road, Dollars

Colony, 3rd Cross, Gowdara Colony, Nagashettyhally,

Bangalore - 560 094, filed this writ petition, to quash e-

auction sale notice dated 25.7.2012, insofar as it

pertains to the said flat and for a direction to the first

respondent not to take any further proceedings in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              -3-</span>

pursuance of said e-auction sale notice vide Annexure-

E.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     2.    Sri   Gururaj    Joshi,   learned   advocate   for

respondent No.1, submitted that the intended auction

sale vide Annexure-E has not taken place on the

notified day and hence this writ petition has become

infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">     3. In view of the submission made by Sri Gururaj

Joshi and since the sale as notified vide Annexure-E,

has taken place, on 25.7.2012, in respect of Flat-C-1,

situated   at    No.16,    PID   No.100-268/16,    property

No.22/2, 'Shreya Residency', 1st Main Road, Dollars

Colony, 3rd Cross, Gowdara Colony, Nagashettyhally,

Bangalore - 560 094, there being no cause of action, for

the present, it is unnecessary for the petitioner to

prosecute this writ petition.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                              -4-</span><p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">       Reserving liberty to the petitioner, to seek relief, in

case a need arises at a later date i.e. when a fresh

notification is issued and a cause of action arises,

petition is disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                           Sd/-
</p><p id="p_9">                                         JUDGE



AP/-
</p>
