EMPLOYEE STATE INSURANCE CORP &amp; ORS
    VS
KHUSHBOO SPICES AMRITSAR
                  *****
</pre><p id="p_1">Present:    Mr. Adarsh Malik, Advocate
            for the appellant.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                  ****
CM-8875-CII-2016
            Allowed, as prayed for, subject to all just exceptions.
            Annexure A-1 is taken on record.
</p><p id="p_3">CM-8876-CII-2016
IN/AND FAO-5505-2015
            Counsel for the appellants has made an oral prayer for
preponement of the main appeal which is fixed for 19.07.2016 and
hearing the case today.
</p><p id="p_4">            Prayer granted.      Main appeal is taken on board for
hearing.
</p><p id="p_5">            Counsel for the appellants relies upon the Full Bench
judgment of this Court in Employees State Insurance Corporation,
Chandigarh Vs. Oswal Woolen Mills Ltd., Millar Ganj, Ludhiana,
1980 PLR 656, which he asserts has been upheld by the Supreme
Court in Royal Western India Turf Club Ltd. Vs. E.S.I. Corportion
&amp; others, 2016 (2) S.C.T. 138. On this basis, he contends that a
casual worker is an employee and is covered under the provisions of
the <a href="/doc/88376/" id="a_1">Employees State Insurance Act</a>, 1948 and consequently, is
entitled to the benefit of the said Act and the employer, thus, has to
contribute towards the liability as per the Act.
</p><p id="p_6">            Notice of motion for 08.11.2016.
</p><p id="p_7">            Refund of the amount in pursuance to the impugned
order dated 31.03.2015       shall remain stayed till the next date of
hearing.</p><pre id="pre_2">
                                       ( AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH )
May 03, 2016                                   JUDGE
