<div class="doc_title">Tapendra Sharma vs State Of Rajasthan &amp; Ors on 19 February, 2018</div><p id="p_1">      HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                       JODHPUR
               S.B. Civil Misc. Application No.53/2018
                                    In
               S.B. Civil Writ Petition No. 2420 / 2018
Tapendra Sharma
</p><p id="p_2">                                                            ----Petitioner
                                  Versus
State &amp; Ors.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                         ----Respondents
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)    :Mr. Arjun Purohit
For Respondent(s) :Mr. Vikas Choudhary for Mr. SS Ladrecha, AAG
_____________________________________________________
      HON'BLE DR. JUSTICE PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI
                                  Order
19/02/2018

</p><p id="p_4">1.         Learned counsel for the petitioner has preferred this

application for recalling the order dated 16.02.2018 passed by this

Court in S.B. Civil Writ Petition No.2420/2018.

</p><p id="p_5">2.         For the reasons mentioned in the application, the order

dated 16.02.2018 passed by this Court in S.B. Civil Writ Petition

No.2420/2018 is recalled.
</p>
<p id="p_6">3.         After hearing both the parties, the fresh order is passed

which reads as follows :-
</p>
<p id="p_7">"1.        This writ petition has been preferred with the following

prayers:
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">      "The respondents may kindly be directed to consider
      candidature     of    the   petitioner     for    documents
      verification   and   counseling      for   the   purpose   of
      appointment to the post of Teacher Grade III Level II
                                 (2 of 2)


     in the subject of Hindi, General Category pursuant to
     advertisement dated 11.08.2013 and in the event of
     coming in the merit / cut off marks, appointment may
     be given to the petitioner on the above post, the
     same may kindly be given with all the consequential
     as well as monetary benefits also.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">           Any other appropriate writ, order or direction
     which this Hon'ble Court deems just and proper may
     kindly be passed in favour of the petitioner."
</blockquote>


<p id="p_8">2.         Learned counsel for the respondent assures the Court

that verification and consequential consideration of petitioner shall

be made.
</p>
<p id="p_9">3.         In light of the aforesaid submission, the writ petition is

disposed of with a direction to the respondent to consider

appointment of the petitioner on the post of Teacher Grade - III

(Level-II) in pursuance of advertisement year 2013 strictly as per

his merit, eligibility and vacancy available for the concern

recruitment year in his respective category and subject."




</p><p id="p_10">                             (DR. PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI)J.
sudheer
 </p>
