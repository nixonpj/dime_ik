<div class="doc_title">Rajendra Paswan @ Paras Jee @ Abhay ... vs The State Of Bihar on 21 June, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                            Cr.Misc. No.13992 of 2011
            RAJENDRA PASWAN @ PARAS JEE @ ABHAY JEE @ UDAY JEE
                                      Versus
                             THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                     -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">02.      21.06.2011                Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                       state.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   Petitioner is shown one of the members engaged

                       in making success 'Bharat Band Call' from Maoists

                       extremists. They have been shown protesting the act of

                       police by firing even. Submission of the learned counsel for

                       petitioner is that no police personnel or other civilians

                       received any injury, if really, there was firing petitioner is

                       shown named by other members of the mob as one of their

                       associates' and remained in custody since 08.6.2010.

</p><p id="p_3">                                   Considering the facts and circumstances of the

                       case, let the above named petitioner be released on bail on

                       furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with two

                       sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the

                       Addl. Distt. &amp; Sessions Judge Ist, Rohtas at Sasaram in

                       connection with Rohtas P. S. Case No.58 of 2009, Tr. No.50

                       of 2011.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><pre id="pre_2">Vikash                                      (Mandhata Singh, J.)
