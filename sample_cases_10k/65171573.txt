<div class="doc_title">Avinash Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 24 May, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.31465 of 2018
                   Arising Out of PS.Case No. -87 Year- 2018 Thana -LAHERIASARAI District- DARBHANGA
                 ======================================================
                 Avinash Kumar S/o Arun Kumar Paswan @ Arun Kumar Paswan, R/o
                 Chok Laxmi Sagar, P.S.- Sadar, District- Darbhanga.

                                                                              .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                              .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance:
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Fahad Khurshid
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Smt. Nirmala Kumari
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRAKASH CHANDRA
                 JAISWAL
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   24-05-2018                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                 APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  The petitioner seeks bail in Laheriasarai (Beta O.P.)

                 P.S. Case No. 87 of 2018 registered under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1947545/" id="a_5">448</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_6">354B</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_7">379</a> and <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_8">504</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_9">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  Petitioner along with other accused persons are said

                 to have assaulted the informant, his brother and misbehaved with

                 the womenfolk of the house of the informant and petitioner also

                 assaulted his neighbour Nilam Devi on his hand by means of knife

                 on protest made by the informant about taking intoxicant

                 substance by them in his rented room.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

                 that no such occurrence as alleged ever took place. He has no
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.31465 of 2018 (2) dt.24-05-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       2/2</span>




                        concern with the aforesaid occurrence. He has not made any

                        assault. He has been falsely implicated in this case due to

                        animosity. No one had sustained any injury in the alleged assault.

                        Other allegations are general and omnibus in nature. He has no

                        criminal antecedent. He has been languishing in custody since

                        27.02.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                           Considering the facts aforesaid, the above named

                        petitioner is directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail

                        bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the like

                        amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial

                        Magistrate, Darbhanga in connection with Laheriasarai P.S. Case

                        No. 87 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                             (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
Mishra/-
</p>
