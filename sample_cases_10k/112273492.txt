<p id="p_1">       The petitioner is aggrieved with the recovery

proceedings initiated when the first appeal is pending

before the first appellate authority.

answered in favour of another assessee, by Ext.P5

order of the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal. In such

circumstance, there shall be a stay of recovery till the

appeal is disposed of.

by the observations made herein, which is only of a
