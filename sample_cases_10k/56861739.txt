                         In the High Court at Calcutta
                      Criminal Miscellaneous Jurisdiction
                              Appellate Side
Present:-
</p><p id="p_2">The Hon'ble Mr.Justice Jayanta Kumar Biswas
                and
The Hon'ble Mr.Justice Indrajit Chatterjee

                          CRM No.6887 of 2014
                        Tapas Saha @ Tapash Saha
                                   v.
</p><p id="p_3">                              The State of West Bengal

Mr.Siladitya Sanyal
Mr.Brajesh Jha
Md.B.Israin                 .. for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">Mr.Rababir Roychowdhury         .. for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_5">Heard on: June 10, 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_6">Order on: June 10, 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_7">    Jayanta Kumar Biswas, J.- The petitioner in the CRM saying that he is
apprehending arrest in connection with Chandannagore P.S.Case No.26 of 2014
dated February 7, 2014 under ss.420/406/506 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> is seeking bail under s.438
<a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_2">CrPC</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_8">        We have heard advocates for the petitioner and the State and have perused
the case diary.
</p>
<p id="p_9">        The FIR has been registered pursuant to an order passed by the criminal
court under s.156(3).     The complaint was filed on January 21, 2014.       The
accusations are that after the partnership was created in 2004, while the
petitioner became richer and richer, the complainant became poorer and poorer;
and that the facts and circumstances provide reasons to believe that the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                            2</span>


petitioner acquired money and property by committing the offences in the course
of doing the partnership business.          The further accusations are that the
petitioner did not give accounts to the complainant.
</p>
<p id="p_10">      After examining the facts and circumstances of the case, we are of the
opinion that the petitioner is entitled to anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_11">      For these reasons, we allow the CRM and direct that in the event the
petitioner is arrested in connection with the case, he shall be released on bail to
the satisfaction of the arresting officer. Certified xerox.
</p>



<p id="p_12">                                                         (Jayanta Kumar Biswas, J.)



                                                                (Indrajit Chatterjee, J.)


sm.
</p>
