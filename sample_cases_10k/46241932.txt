<div class="doc_title">R.Mahadevi @ Savithiri vs G.Sathiyacheelarajan on 26 July, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                         1




                          BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT

                                              DATED : 26.07.2019

                                                       CORAM:

                                 THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.S.SUNDAR

                                         TR.C.M.P(MD)No.480 of 2018
                                                    and
                                         C.M.P.(MD)No.11116 of 2018


                 R.Mahadevi @ Savithiri                                     : Petitioner


                                                             Vs.


                 G.Sathiyacheelarajan                                       : Respondent


                 Prayer: Petition is filed under Section 24 of Code of Civil Procedure, to
                 withdraw the case in H.M.O.P.No.174 of 2017 on the file of the Sub Court,
                 Srivilliputhur and transfer the same to the Sub-Court, Coimbatore.


                                      For Petitioner     : Ms.Mahalakshmi
                                      For Respondent     : No Appearance


                                                       ORDER
</pre>


<p id="p_1">                             Despite notice being sent to the respondent, it is stated that the

                 notice has been returned with an endorsement 'no such person in the

                 address'.
</p>
<p id="p_2">


http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2</span>

</p><p id="p_3">                          2.However, the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner

                 produced before this Court a copy of memo served on the counsel before

                 the lower Court appearing for the respondent.



</p><p id="p_4">                          3.This transfer civil miscellaneous petition is filed by the wife to

                 withdraw the proceedings in H.M.O.P.No.174 of 2017 on the file of the Sub

                 Court, Srivilliputhur and to transfer the same to the Sub Court,

                 Coimbatore.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                          4.The Marriage between the petitioner and the respondent took

                 place on 27.11.2006. It is stated that the petitioner is also blessed with a

                 female child, who is aged about 12 years and a male child, who is aged

                 about 8 years.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                          5.The respondent has filed H.M.O.P.No.174 of 2017 before the

                 Sub Court, Srivilliputhur for dissolution of marriage on the ground of

                 cruelty. It is further stated that the respondent has earlier filed a petition

                 in H.M.O.P.No.66 of 2014 before the Sub Court, Srivilliputhur for divorce

                 and the same was transferred to the Sub Court, Kovilpatti, as per the

                 order of this Court in transfer civil miscellaneous petition. However, the

                 said petition was not prosecuted and the respondent has now filed fresh

                 petition before the Sub Court, Srivilliputhur in H.M.O.P.No.174 of 2017.



http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                        3</span>

</p><p id="p_9">                           6.The petitioner as a lady living with two children at Coimbatore

                 and the children are studying in school at Coimbatore. Considering the

                 convenience of the wife and as per <a href="/doc/371013/" id="a_1">Section 19</a> of Hindu Marriage Act, the

                 wife/petitioner can seek transfer of proceeding to the place, where she

                 resides. It is not in dispute that the petition filed by the wife is pending

                 before the Subordinate Court, Coimbatore.



</p><p id="p_10">                           7.In the facts and circumstances of the case and to ensure fair

                 justice to the petitioner, this Court is inclined to allow this petition and the

                 H.M.O.P.No.174 of 2017 is withdrawn from the Sub Court, Srivilliputhur,

                 and the same is transferred to the Sub Court, Coimbatore for disposal on

                 merits.
</p>
<p id="p_11">

</p><p id="p_12">                           8. The learned Subordinate Judge, Srivilliputhur is directed to

                 send the records of H.M.O.P.No.174 of 2017 to the file of the Sub Court,

                 Coimbatore, within a period of two weeks from the date of receipt of a

                 copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_13">

</p><p id="p_14">                           9.The learned Subordinate Judge, Coimbatore after obtaining the

                 records on transfer, is directed to dispose of H.M.O.P.No.174 of 2017, on

                 merits and in accordance with law, as expeditiously as possible, preferably

                 within a period of four moths.
</p>
<p id="p_15">

                                                                             S.S.SUNDAR.J.
</p>
<p id="p_16">

</p><p id="p_17">                                                                                          das



</p><p id="p_18">                           10.In view of the above, this Transfer Civil Miscellaneous Petition

                 is allowed. No costs. Consequently, connected miscellaneous petition is

                 closed.</p><pre id="pre_2">
