<div class="doc_title">Purusheshwar Mishra vs Education on 10 February, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                       W.P.(S) No. 237 of 2016
                                   -----
           Purusheshwar Mishra                      ...........Petitioner
                                   Versus
           The State of Jharkhand &amp; Ors.                ....Respondents
                                           ----
           Coram:      HON'BLE MR JUSTICE RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY
                                   ----------
           For the Petitioner      : Mr. Manoj Tandon, Advocate
           For the State           : J.C. to A.A.G.-Mr. H.K. Mehta
                                   -----
05/10.02.2016</pre><p id="p_1">    Mr. Manoj Tandon, learned counsel for the petitioner, has
          submitted that the petitioner being a disabled candidate in the
          unreserved        category should have been considered in the disabled
          category as he had secured more marks than respondent no. 5. He
          has submitted that the post in which the respondent no. 5 is being
          sought to be appointed belongs to unreserved category, to which the
          petitioner falls but without considering the same, respondent no. 5,
          who is a reserved category disabled candidate, has been selected for
          appointment.
</p><p id="p_2">                     Issue notice to respondent no. 5 as to why this application be
          not admitted and/or disposed of at the stage of admission itself, for
          which requisites etc. under registered cover with A/d as well as by
          ordinary process must be filed by 19.02.2016.
</p><p id="p_3">                     Learned J.C. to A.A.G., Mr. H.K. Mehta, is directed to file a
          counter affidavit within a period of four weeks.
</p><p id="p_4">                     List this case on 16.03.2016.
</p><p id="p_5">                     Appointment of respondent no. 5 shall be subject to result of
          the writ petition.
</p>


<p id="p_6">                                                     (Rongon Mukhopadhyay,J)
          Rakesh/-
</p>
