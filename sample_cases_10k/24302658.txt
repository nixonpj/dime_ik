ORAL ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">	Heard
Mr. Upadhyay, learned advocate for applicant and Mr. Pandya, learned
APP. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_2">	Mr.
</p><p id="p_3">Upadhyay, learned advocate for applicant has submitted that present
application may be disposed of as not pressed/withdrawn since the
main matter before the learned trial Court (from which present
proceedings have arisen) has been disposed of and that therefore
there is no cause for prosecuting the main petition i.e. Special
Criminal Application No.2390 of 2012 as well as the application
seeking restoration of Special Criminal Application No.2390 of 2012
and/or this application seeking condonation of delay in filing Misc.
Criminal Application No.4410 of 2013. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_4">	Learned
counsel for applicant also submitted that since the cause for
prosecuting the main petition being Special Criminal Application
No.2390 of 2012 now does not survive, present applications also does
not survive and both the matters i.e. Criminal Misc. Application No.
4411 of 2013 and Criminal Misc. Application No.4410 of 2013 may be
disposed of as not pressed/withdrawn in view of subsequent
development namely the proceedings before the trial Court has come to
an end and therefore now there is no cause to prosecute these
applications and/or the main petition being Special Criminal
Application Nos. 2390 of 2012. 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_5">	In
view of the said statement by learned counsel for applicant, Criminal
Misc. Application No. 4411 of 2013 and Criminal Misc. Application
No.4410 of 2013 stand disposed of as not pressed/withdrawn.
</p> 


 


 


 


 


<p id="p_6">(K.M.THAKER,
J.)
