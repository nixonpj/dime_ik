<pre id="pre_1">                             1


   IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA AT BENGALURU

     DATED THIS THE 27TH DAY OF SEPTEMBER, 2019

                          BEFORE

    THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JOHN MICHAEL CUNHA

          CRIMINAL PETITION No.9231/2016

BETWEEN:

Sri.B.V.Srinivas
Aged about 59 years,
S/o late Venkataiah,
R/at No.79, LIC Colony,
3rd Main, Sriramapura 2nd Stage,
Mysuru - 570 023.
                                               ... Petitioner
(By Sri.K.K.Vasanth, Advocate)

AND:

State by Lokayuktha Police
Karnataka Lokayuktha,
Mysuru - 570 001.
                                             ... Respondent

(By Sri.Venkatesh S.Arabatti, Advocate)

      This Criminal Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of
Cr.P.C praying to set aside the impugned order dated
18.02.2016 passed on the application filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Section
457</a> of Cr.P.C by the III Additional Sessions and Spl. Judge,
Mysuru vide Annexure-D and consequently, allow the
application filed by the petitioner dated 04.01.2016 under
<a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">Section 457</a> of Cr.P.C in Crime No.14/2014 vide Annexure
B.
                                2


      This Criminal petition coming on for Admission, this
day, the Court made the following:

                          ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard learned counsel for petitioner and learned

Standing Counsel appearing for respondent.


</p><p id="p_2">      2.    Learned counsel for respondent has no objection

to release the Passport, Identity Card, Bank Passbook,

Ration Card, Driving Licence etc, standing in the name of

the petitioner, subject to the condition that the petitioner

shall not make use of the said documents to flee from the

country.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">      3.    Since these documents are not the subject

matter of the offence, the petitioner is entitled for release of

the Passport, Election Identity Card, Aadhar Card, Bank

Passbook, Ration Card, Driving Licence standing in his

name. The Passport and Identity Card relating to the other

family members of the petitioner, the same shall be

released to their custody, subject to making necessary

application by the respective claimants in this regard.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  3</span>


</p><p id="p_5">      The trial Court shall release the above documents to

the custody of the petitioner, by taking an undertaking from

the petitioner that he shall not leave the jurisdiction of the

trial Court, till conclusion of the trial.


</p><p id="p_6">      Petition is disposed of in terms of the above order.




</p><p id="p_7">                                              Sd/-
</p><p id="p_8">                                             JUDGE




UN
