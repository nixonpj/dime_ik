</p><p id="p_2">                               This is an application for restoration of miscellaneous appeal which

                     stated that the order was complied with but there was delay. The said delay

                     is bonafide and in this regard an affidavit has also been filed.
</p><p id="p_3">                               Considering the aforesaid ground mentioned in the memo of
                     application and also in the application for condonation of delay, the delay in
                     filing the application is condoned and the application is also allowed.
</p><p id="p_4">                               Office is directed to restore Misc. Appeal No.734/2009 to its original
