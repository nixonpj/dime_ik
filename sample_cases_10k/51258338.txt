<div class="doc_title">Sunita Kumari Gehlot vs K.L. Swami &amp; Ors on 17 October, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">       S.B. CIVIL CONTEMPT PETITION NO.477/2013
        Sunita Kumari Gehlot Vs. K.L. Swami &amp; Ors.

Date of Order                    :           17.10.2013


            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

Mr. Arjun Singh for petitioner.
Mr. Yashpal Khileree, Dy. Government Counsel.
Mr. Devendra Choudhary, respondent No.2 present in
person.


           Learned counsel for the petitioner states that in

pursuance of the directions given by this Court, the

respondents have passed an order dated 17.10.2013

whereby the petitioner has been permitted to join her

duties as ANM on contract basis, hence, the order

passed by this Court has been complied with.              The

copies of orders dated 17.10.2013 passed by the

Additional       Director   (Admn.),   Medical   and   Health

Services, Rajasthan, Jaipur and passed by Chief Medical

and Health Officer, Bikaner are placed before this Court

for perusal.

           In view of the fact that the respondents have

passed the order allowing the petitioner to join her

duties in pursuance of directions given by this Court,

nothing survives in this contempt petition.

           The contempt petition is, therefore, dismissed.

Notices are hereby discharged.

                                       [VIJAY BISHNOI],J.
</pre>
