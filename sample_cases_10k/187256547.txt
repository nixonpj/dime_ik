<p id="p_1">3       30-03-2016                     There is typographical error in paragraph-2 of the

                       application, inasmuch as in place of "Bakhtiarpur P.S. Case No.

                       207 of 2015", "Majhaulia P.S. Case No. 100/2015" has been typed

                       and in place of the offences punishable under Sections

                       "419/420/467/468/471/34<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>", Sections

                       "302, 120B/34<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>" has been typed.

</p><p id="p_2">                                       Let paragraph 2 of the said order be read accordingly.

                                       The time granted to the petitioners to surrender before

                       the Court below is extended by two weeks from today.
