                          IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                         Criminal Miscellaneous No.44777 of 2014
                           Arising Out of PS.Case No. -78 Year- 2013 Thana -SULTANGANJ District- BHAGALPUR
                       ======================================================
                       1. Pappu @ Pappu Mistri Son of Md. Salim resident of Mohalla- Shahjangi
                       (Azim Nagar), P.O.AND P.S.- Habibpur, District- Bhagalpur

                                                                                      .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                              Versus
                       1. The State of Bihar

                                                                    .... .... Opposite Party/s
                       ======================================================
                       Appearance :
                       For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Nilesh Kumar
                       For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. U.L.Verma(App)
                       ======================================================
                       CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE GOPAL PRASAD
                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">4       10-02-2016               Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                       counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner has been arrested in connection with

                       Sultanganj P.S. Case No. 78 of 2013 registered for offence under

                       <a href="/doc/263398/" id="a_1">Sections 22</a> and <a href="/doc/263398/" id="a_2">22</a> of N.D.P.S. Act with regard to huge quantity

                       i.e. 299 packets of Ganja was recovered from the vehicle and the

                       petitioner was driver of the said vehicle.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case, I

                       am not inclined to grant bail to the petitioner. Accordingly, his

                       prayer for bail is rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><pre id="pre_2">         m.p.                                          (Gopal Prasad, J)
