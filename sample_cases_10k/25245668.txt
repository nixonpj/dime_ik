                      PRAYER: C.M.P.No.7916 of 2016 is filed under Section 173 (1) of
                      the <a href="/doc/785258/" id="a_1">Motor Vehicles Act</a>, 1988, to condone the delay of 1497 days in
                      filing the above appeal.



                            C.M.A.SR.No.31369 of 2016 is filed under <a href="/doc/147367599/" id="a_2">Section 173</a> of the

                      Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, against the judgment and decree dated

                      17.08.2011 made in M.C.O.P.No.2210 of 2008 on the file of the

                      Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, IV Judge, Small Causes Court,

                      Chennai.

                                                    ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">                            C.M.P.No.7916 of 2016 is filed to condone the delay of 1497

                      days in filing the above appeal.
</p>


<p id="p_2">                            C.M.A.SR.No.31369 of 2016 is filed against the judgment and

                      decree dated 17.08.2011 made in M.C.O.P.No.2210 of 2008 on the

                      file of the Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, IV Judge, Small Causes

                      Court, Chennai.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                            2.Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and

                      perused the materials available on record.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                            3.According to the petitioner, he could not mobilise funds to

                      file appeal in time and hence, the delay of 1497 days has occurred

                      in filing the appeal. The petitioner has not given any particulars as

                      to how and when he arranged funds to file appeal. The reason given

                      by the petitioner is not valid and sufficient for condoning the delay

                      of 1497 days in filing the appeal. In view of the same, this Court is



http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       3</span>

                      not inclined to condone the delay of 1497 days in filing the appeal.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                      Accordingly,    C.M.P.No.7916   of   2016   stands   dismissed   and

                      consequently, C.M.A.SR.No.31369 of 2016 is rejected. No costs.
</p>
