<p id="p_1">2   08-07-2019                    This writ petition under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the

                      Constitution of India has been filed by the petitioner for

                      directing and commanding the respondent authorities to release

                      the Ertiga ZDI Maruti Suzuki Car bearing Registration

                      No.UP53BJ3779, which has been illegally seized in connection

                      with Mehsi P. S. Case No. 290 of 2018 under Sections 30(a)

                      and 37(b) of the Bihar Prohibition &amp; <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_2">Excise Act</a>, 2016.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  Realizing that the confiscation is a civil proceeding,

                      learned counsel for the petitioner seeks leave to withdraw the
             Patna High Court CR. WJC No.3020 of 2018(2) dt.08-07-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                      present writ petition with liberty to the petitioner to file a civil

                      writ application for the release of Ertiga ZDI Maruti Suzuki Car

                      bearing Registration No.UP53BJ3779 seized in connection with

                      Mehsi P. S. Case No. 290 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  Leave is granted.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  The writ petition is disposed of.
</p>
