</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 22-02-2019

       Heard Mr. L Deka, learned counsel for the petitioner, who submits that the
petitioner is the wife of the Late Prasanna Kr. Das, though the respondent No. 6 is
projecting herself to be the only wife of the Late Prasanna Kr. Das. He submits that the
proof of the petitioner being the wife is in the judgment dated 31.07.2009 passed by the
learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Barpeta in Case No. MR 203/2007 under Section 125
of the Cr.PC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       Issue notice, returnable in 4 (four) weeks.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       Mr. DS Deka, learned counsel appearing on behalf of Mr. R Borpuzari accepts
notice on behalf of respondent Nos. 1 and 2, Ms. B Bora, learned counsel accepts notice
on behalf of respondent No. 3 and Mr. D Nath, learned counsel accepts notice on behalf
of respondent Nos. 4 and 5.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       Petitioner to take steps for service of notice upon respondent No. 6 by registered
post with A/D within 3 (three) days.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       List the matter after 4 (four) weeks.
</p>
