                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                         First Appeal No.127 of 2010

                    Jai Shankar Prakash

                                                   Versus
                    Dr. Sita Ram Singh &amp; Anr.

<p id="p_1">15   08-04-2013                   Heard the learned senior counsel, Mr. Dhruv Narayan

                    appearing on behalf of the appellant in F.A. No.127 of 2010 and

                    the learned senior counsel, Mr. P. N. Shahi appearing on behalf of

                    the respondent no.1 in this aforesaid First Appeal.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  I.A.No.3190 of 2011 has been filed under Order 39

                    Rule 1 and 2 of the C.P.C. praying for restraining the respondent

                    no.1 from making any construction on the purchased land of the

                    appellant measuring 8 katthas i.e. the land claimed by the

                    appellant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  The learned senior counsel, Mr. P. N. Shahi appearing

                    on behalf of the respondent no.1 submitted that no construction is

                    being made on the 8 katthas land claimed by the appellant nor any

                    construction will be made till the disposal of this First Appeal and

                    prayed that this First Appeal along with analogous First Appeal

                    No.119 of 2010 be heard on priority basis on 6th of May, 2013. So
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Patna High Court FA No.127 of 2010 (15) dt.08-04-2013</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       2</span>




                         far this date is concerned, the learned counsels appearing on behalf

                         of other parties and the learned counsels appearing in another First

                         Appeal have got no objection.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                       Admittedly, both the First Appeals are running in the

                         hearing list before this court. Accordingly, the office is directed to

                         list this First Appeal along with analogous First Appeal as first

                         case on 6th of May, 2013 in the Hearing List I. In the meantime, it

                         is expected that the undertaking given by learned senior counsel

                         appearing on behalf of the appellant shall be maintained.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                    (Mungeshwar Sahoo, J)


Saurabh/-
</p>
