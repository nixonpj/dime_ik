<div class="doc_title">Raju Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 28 September, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.40365 of 2016
                       Arising Out of PS.Case No. -178 Year- 2016 Thana -JAKKANPUR District- PATNA
                 ======================================================
                 1. Raju Kumar Son of Late Satya Narayan Singh Resident of Village
                 Konharapar Sipara, P.S.- Beur, District- Patna.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Lovekush Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Dr. Kumar Uday Pratap
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR SRIVASTAVA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   28-09-2016                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

                 learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Petitioner seeks bail in a case registered under

                 Sections-47A/57 of the Bihar Excise (Amendment) Act, 2016.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 Twenty one bottles of Indian made foreign liquor

                 were recovered from possession of the petitioner, while he was

                 alighting from a bus.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Submission on behalf of petitioner is that the

                 petitioner was caught on mere suspicion and as a matter of fact,

                 the persons, who were carrying the aforesaid liquor, had fled away

                 seeing the police and moreover, the petitioner does not have any

                 criminal antecedent who is languishing in jail custody since 18-07-

                 2016.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.40365 of 2016 (2) dt.28-09-2016</span><p id="p_5">

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_6">                                       Considering the above-said facts and circumstances of

                        the case as well as submission of the parties, let the petitioner

                        named above, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                        10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each

                        in connection with Jakkanpur P.S. Case No. 178 of 2016 to the

                        satisfaction of learned Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate, Patna.

</p><p id="p_7">                                              (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
</p>
