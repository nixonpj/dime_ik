                     CHANDIGARH

                                        Criminal Misc. No. M-50262 of 2018
                                        Date of decision: 16.11.2018

Sanjay Dahuja                                             ..Petitioner



                                 Versus



Central Bureau of Investigation                           ..Respondent

CORAM: HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE DAYA CHAUDHARY

Present:     Mr. Sandeep Jasuja, Advocate
             for the petitioner.

             Mr. Sumeet Goel, Standing counsel
             for the respondent-CBI.

               ***
Daya Chaudhary, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             The prayer in the present petition is for extension of time to

visit USA from 19.11.2018 to 01.12.2018 as the trial Court has allowed the

petitioner to visit USA w.e.f. 19.11.2018 to 28.11.2018 as per prayer made

in the prayer clause.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that inadvertently, it

was mentioned in the prayer clause of the application that he may be

allowed to visit USA w.e.f. 20.11.2018 to 28.11.2018 whereas in the body

of the application, the prayer made was w.e.f. 20.11.2018 to 30.11.2018.

</p><p id="p_3">             Notice of motion was issued.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             Mr. Sumeet Goel, Standing counsel for CBI submits that the

application has rightly been declined by considering the prayer clause and

moreover, the trial Court was not having power to review its own order.

</p><p id="p_5">             Heard arguments of learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

<p id="p_7">             On perusal of the application moved by the petitioner, it is

apparent that in the body of the application, the time period to visit USA

was mentioned as 20.11.2018 to 30.11.2018 but the time period mentioned

in the prayer clause was 20.11.2018 to 28.11.2018 for which, he was

allowed to visit by the trial Court. It is an inadvertent mistake on the part of

the counsel, who has drafted the application or it might be a typographical

mistake.
</p>
<p id="p_8">             Accordingly, this petition is allowed and the petitioner is

granted permission to visit USA from 19.11.2018 to 01.12.2018 on the same

terms and conditions, which have been imposed by the trial Court, subject to

payment of cost of `5000/- to be deposited before the trial Court.
