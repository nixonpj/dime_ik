<div class="doc_title">Ram Nath Singh vs The State Of Bihar on 24 June, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Criminal Miscellaneous No.17960 of 2014

                  =====================================================
                 Ram Nath Singh, S/O Bishwanath Singh, resident of village- Purusotampur,
                 Halai, P.S.- Chak Lalse, O.P. Halai, District- Samastipur.

                                                                     .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                 Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                      .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================

                 CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA PRAKASH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   24-06-2014                 Heard learned counsel for the Petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               The Petitioner apprehends his arrest in a case

                 instituted for the offence under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_1">Section 406</a> of the Indian Penal

                 Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             Considering the nature of allegation against the

                 Petitioner who has fair antecedent, it is ordered that in the event of

                 surrender/arrest of the Petitioner, named above, within four weeks

                 from the date of receipt/production of a copy of this order in

                 connection with Complaint Case No. 608 of 2013, Trial No. 282

                 of 2014, he shall be released on anticipatory bail on furnishing bail

                 bond of Rs.5,000/- (Five thousand) with two sureties of the like

                 amount each or any other surety to be fixed by the court below to

                 the satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Vaishali at Hajipur,

                 subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_2">Sections 438(2)</a> Cr.

                 P.C. and (i) That one of the bailors will be a close relative of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.17960 of 2014 (2) dt.24-06-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                2/2</span>




                  Petitioner who will give an affidavit giving genealogy as to how

                  he is related with the Petitioner. The bailor will also undertake to

                  inform the Court if there is any change in the address of the

                  Petitioner. (ii) That the bailor shall also state on affidavit that he

                  will inform the court concerned if the Petitioner is implicated in

                  any other case of similar nature after his release in the present case

                  and thereafter the court below will be at liberty to initiate the

                  proceeding for cancellation of bail on the ground of misuse. (iii)

                  That the Petitioner will be well represented on each date and if he

                  fails to do so on two consecutive dates, his bail will be liable to be

                  cancelled.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                                                                    (Anjana Prakash, J)
    S.Ali/-
</p>
