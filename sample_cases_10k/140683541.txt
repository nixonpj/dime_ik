     (FOR ADMISSION and IA No.137383/2018-RECALLING THE COURTS ORDER)

     Date : 13-11-2018 This petition was called on for hearing today.

     CORAM :
                             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE KURIAN JOSEPH
                             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT GUPTA


     For Petitioner(s)                      Mr. Gagan Sanghi,Adv.
                                            Mr. Rameshwar Prasad Goyal, AOR

     For Respondent(s)                      Mr. Amol Nirmalkumar Suryawanshi, AOR


                              UPON hearing the counsel the Court made the following
                                                 O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                         This is an application for recall of Court’s order.
                         Learned counsel appearing for the applicant(s) submits that in
     view of the judgment dated 28.03.2018 of this Court, the non-
     applicant(s) would make an attempt to revive even the prayers made
     in the writ petition which had already been withdrawn.                                         It      is
     also submitted that their stand regarding the maintainability of
     the                 ‘appeal’    as     stated       in   the   impugned       order,     may    not    be
     entertained by the High Court.
</p><p id="p_2">                         We do not find any ground for either of these apprehensions.
     It            will       be    open    to     the    applicant(s)       to    take     all     available
     contentions                   before    the     High     Court.    It     will   be     open     to   the
     applicant(s) to point out that it was not a statutory appeal but
     only a representation.
</p><p id="p_3">Signature Not Verified

Digitally signed by
NARENDRA PRASAD
Date: 2018.11.14
17:19:57 IST             The application is, accordingly, disposed of.
Reason:
</p>
