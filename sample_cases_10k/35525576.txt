         The High Court Of Madhya Pradesh
                    WP-2126-2018
                (BALRAM DOUDERIYA Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)

8
Gwalior, Dated : 18-07-2019
        Shri Sarvesh Singh Chauhan, learned counsel for the petitioner.
        Shri Arjun Parihar, learned Panel Lawyer for the respondents</pre>
<p id="p_1">No.1,3,5,6,8,9,10/State.
</p><p id="p_2">        Shri Sameer Kumar Shrivastava, learned counsel for the respondent
No.7.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        None present for other respondents.
</p><p id="p_4">        Today matter is listed under caption "Director" for compliance of
order dated 05.10.2018, whereby this Court while dismissing the petition
preferred by the petitioner Balram Douderiya, found the petition
misconceived and therefore, imposed the cost of Rs.20,000/- over the
petitioner. Since then cost has not been deposited in the office of this Court,
therefore, Principal Registrar of this Court has referred the matter to the
District Collector, Bhind vide letter dated 29.01.2019 for recovery of the
amount as per provisions of law.
</p>
<p id="p_5">        Despite the lapse of six months, no response has been received from
the Collector District Bhind and cost has also not been deposited yet.
Therefore, matter is listed under the caption "Direction".
</p><p id="p_6">        Considering the totality of the circumstance, this Court directs the
office of this Court as well as Collector District Bhind to ensure the recovery
of the said amount from the petitioner Balram Douderiya S/o Ramshankar
Douderiya, resident of Lahar District Bhind and after recovery of the said
amount, the same be deposited in the "High Court Legal Service
Committee, Jabalpur/Gwalior". The account number and details shall be
provided by the Principal Registrar of this Court to the Collector, District
Bhind so that amount can be recovered and deposited in the said account.
</p><p id="p_7">        The matter is listed under caption "Direction" on 24.09.2019.
