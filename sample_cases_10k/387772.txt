<p id="p_1">(Per
: HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. K.S.RADHAKRISHNAN)
 

</p><p id="p_2">1.	This
is a PIL seeking a writ of mandamus or a writ in the nature of
mandamus or any other appropriate writ, order or direction quashing
and setting aside the impugned order passed by respondent No.1 in
Revision Application No.23 of 2003 at Annexure A and the order dated
22.7.2003 at Annexure B passed by respondent No.2. By way of interim
relief, it is prayed that implementation and execution of the order
passed by respondent No.1 in Revision Application No.23 of 2003 at
Annexure A and the order dated 22.7.2003 at Annexure B passed by
respondent No.2 be stayed and the position prior to the construction
of the shops be restored.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_3">2.	Ms.
</p><p id="p_4">Manisha Lavkumar, learned AGP appearing for the State Government,
submits that the construction was already completed and the shops
were already leased and the lease amount is being utilized for paying
electricity dues of the municipality. As the construction of shops is
already over and the lease amount is being used for paying
electricity dues of the municipality, we find no reason to entertain
this PIL. The petition is dismissed. Notice is discharged. No order
as to costs.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_5">(K.S.
</p><p id="p_6">Radhakrishnan C.J.)
 

 


 

(Akil
Kureshi, J.)
