<div class="doc_title">Bharat Kumar @ Bharta Ram vs State on 10 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                1


    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR
               RAJASTHAN AT JODHPUR
 --------------------------------------------------------
 CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION NO. 10286
                          OF 2016


PETITIONER:
Bharat Kumar @ Bharta Ram S/o Lichhu ram by caste
Nai, age 26 years, R/o Gram Amarpura, District
Bikaner.
(At present lodged in Central Jail, Bikaner)


                          VERSUS


RESPONDENT:
The State of Rajasthan


Date of Order : 10.11.2016


           HON'BLE MR. VIJAY BISHNOI, J.


Mr.M.K. Garg       ]
Mr. Kuldeep        ] for the petitioner.
Mr. Pankaj Awasthi - PP, for the respondent-State.


                       ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">                        -----
</p><p id="p_2">     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as
learned Public Prosecutor and also perused the material
on record.
</p><p id="p_3">     The     petitioner   has       been   arrested   in   FIR
No.149/2016 of Police Station, Kolayat for the offence
punishable under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_1">Sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_2">366</a>, <a href="/doc/1677485/" id="a_3">376(2)(N)</a>, <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_4">380</a>
IPC. He has preferred this bail application under
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   2</span>


<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_5">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_4">     Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted
that the prosecutrix is major and she has specifically
stated in her statement that she is known to the
petitioner for last so many days. It is submitted that
the prosecutrix went with petitioner at own free will,
however, later on she has filed false FIR against the
petitioner      on   account     of    pressure    of    her     family
members.
</p><p id="p_5">     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail
application.
</p><p id="p_6">     Having regard to the totality of the facts and
circumstances of the case, without expressing any
opinion on the merits of the case, I deem it just and
proper to grant bail to the accused petitioner under
<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_6">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_7">     Accordingly,       this    bail   application       filed   under
<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_7">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is allowed and it is directed that
petitioner Bharat Kumar @ Bharta Ram S/o Lichhu ram
shall be released on bail in connection with FIR No.
149/2016        of   Police    Station   Kolayat        provided    he
executes a personal bond in a sum of Rs.50,000/- with
two sound and solvent sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to
the satisfaction of learned trial court for his appearance
before that court on each and every date of hearing
and whenever called upon to do so till the completion
of the trial.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                                          (VIJAY BISHNOI), J.
</p>

<p id="p_9">Anurag
