<p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
             S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail No. 11125/2018

Khem Chand @ Khema Ram@ Ravi S/o Naryan Lal Bhil, Aged
About 20 Years, Village Tartoli Police Station Abu Road, District
Sirohi (At present lodged in Sub Jail Abu Road)
</p><p id="p_2">                                                       ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State, Through Pp
</p><p id="p_3">                                                     ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)       :   Mr. H.R. Chawla
For Respondent(s)       :   Mr. Deepak Choudhary,P.P.



</p><p id="p_4">            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

                               Order

02/11/2018


     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as learned

Public Prosecutor and also perused the material on record.


</p><p id="p_5">     The petitioner has been arrested in connection with FIR

No.240/2018 of Police Station Abu Road, District Sirohi for the

offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1873755/" id="a_1">Sections 457</a> and <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_2">380</a> I.P.C.         He has

preferred this bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_3">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_6">     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.


     Having regard to the totality of the facts and circumstances

of the case, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail to the accused

petitioner under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_4">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_7">     Accordingly, this bail application filed under Sec.439 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_5">Cr.P.C</a>. is
                                                                    (2 of 2)             [CRLMB-11125/2018]


                                   allowed and it is directed that petitioner Khem Chand @ Khema

                                   Ram @ Ravi S/o Shri Narayan Lal shall be released on bail in

                                   connection with FIR No.240/2018 of Police Station Abu Road,

                                   District Sirohi provided he executes a personal bond in a sum of

                                   Rs.50,000/- with two sound and solvent sureties of Rs.25,000/-

                                   each to the satisfaction of learned trial court for his appearance

                                   before that court on each and every date of hearing and whenever

                                   called upon to do so till the completion of the trial.


</p><p id="p_8">                                                                                     (VIJAY BISHNOI),J

                                   Babulal/190
