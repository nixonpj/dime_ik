<p id="p_1">4   21-01-2014           Heard Mr. R.K. Jain, learned counsel for the petitioner,

                 learned counsel for the State and Mr. Satyavrat Verma, learned

                 counsel for the opposite party No.2.

</p><p id="p_2">                         This revision is directed against the order dated 9.2.2012

                 passed by the Sub Divisional Magistrate, Chakia in Case No.

                 462M of 2001 under <a href="/doc/1405190/" id="a_1">section 145</a> of the Code of Criminal

                 Procedure whereby the learned Magistrate has been pleased to

                 declare the possession of the opposite party No.2 over the disputed

                 land.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                         Mr. Jain, after some arguments seeks permission to

                 withdraw this application to enable the petitioner to take recourse

                 to such remedy as may be available to him in law.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2           Patna High Court CR. REV. No.442 of 2012 (4) dt.21-01-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_4">                             Permission is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             This criminal revision application is disposed of with the

                     liberty aforementioned.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                             I.A.No.889 of 2012 is accordingly disposed of.




</p><p id="p_7">                                                     (Jyoti Saran, J)
Bibhash/-
