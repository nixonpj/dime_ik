Good Conduct Prisoners (Temporary Release) Act, 1962, in order to enable

him to get his wife treated as she is allegedly suffering from appendicular

lump as per the OPD slip, Annexure P3, attached with the petition.

<p id="p_6">                   This petition is disposed of, at this stage, with a direction

that the Jail Superintendent, Patiala, will permit the petitioner to submit

application and forward the same in accordance with law for due

consideration by the competent authority within a period of one week and

communicate the decision of the authorities on the same within 7 days of

receipt of the same from the competent authority. In case no application is

filed by the petitioner, the present petition may be treated as his application

for parole for consideration in accordance with law.
