BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 30.06.2017  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE T.S.SIVAGNANAM            
AND  
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.VELMURUGAN             

W.P.(MD).No.12197 of 2017  

K.P.Moovendran                                          : Petitioner       

Vs.

1.The Commissioner of Police, 
   Madurai City, Madurai.

2.The Inspector of Police,
   Karimedu Police Station,
   Madurai.

3.The Inspector of Police,
   S.S.Colony Police Station,
   Madurai.                                                     : Respondents 

PRAYER: Writ Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India
to issue a Writ of Mandamus directing the respondents to grant permission
based on petitioner representation dated 20.06.2017 to conduct the temple
festival from 14.07.2017 to 16.07.2017 along with cultural dance program on
16.07.2017 at Mudakku Salai,                 Madurai City.

!For Petitioner         : Mr.C.M.Arumugam   

For Respondents         : Mr.K.Guru        
                                                  Additional Government Pleader


:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">[Order of the Court was made by T.S.SIVAGNANAM, J.]   
        The petitioner seeks for issuance of a direction to the respondents to
grant permission to conduct the temple festival from 14.07.2017 to 16.07.2017
along with cultural dance program on 16.07.2017  in connection with a temple
festival and to the said effect, he has submitted a representation to the
respondents on 20.06.2017. Since, the representation has not been considered,
the petitioner is before this Court.
</p><p id="p_2">        2. Considering the fact that the representation is still pending with
the respondents, this Court is not inclined to give any positive direction in
this Writ Petition, but, directs the respondents to consider the
representation of the petitioner dated 20.06.2017 and pass appropriate orders
on merits and in accordance with law within a period of two days from the
date of receipt of a copy of this order.
</p><p id="p_3">        3. With the above directions, the writ petition is disposed of.  No
costs.  
</p>


<p id="p_4">To

</p><p id="p_5">1.The Commissioner of Police, 
   Madurai City, Madurai.
</p>
<p id="p_6">2.The Inspector of Police,
   Karimedu Police Station,
   Madurai.
</p>

   S.S.Colony Police Station,
