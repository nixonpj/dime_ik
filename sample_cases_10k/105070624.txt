<div class="doc_title">Bandhu @ Phoolchandra Lodhi vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 23 July, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                  M.Cr.C. No. 8367/2014
23/07/2014
     Shri Kamlesh Mishra, Advocate for the applicant.
     Dr.       Smt.   Anjali      Gyanani,       PL   for   the
respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard finally.
</p><p id="p_2">     This is the first application filed by applicant under
<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Cr.P.C for grant of bail.
</p><p id="p_3">     Applicant has been arrested on 19/04/2014 in
connection with Crime No. 59/2014 registered at P.S.
Budera, District Tikamgarh for the offence punishable
under <a href="/doc/482448/" id="a_2">sections 376</a> (2)(n), 312, 201 of <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">IPC</a> and <a href="/doc/84602/" id="a_4">section
5</a>/<a href="/doc/1832444/" id="a_5">6</a> of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences
Act, 2012.
</p><p id="p_4">     Learned counsel for the applicant submits that
applicant has been falsely implicated in the case.
Prosecutrix is aged about 18 years. As per x-ray report
her age was 18 years. She lodged the report after two
months of the incident. Trial would take considerable
time to be disposed of finally       therefore, he prays for
grant of bail to the applicant.
</p><p id="p_5">     Learned      counsel   for    the   State    opposes   the
application.
</p><p id="p_6">     On due consideration of the facts and contention
raised on behalf of the parties, I am of the considered
view that it is a fit case to release the applicant on bail.
Therefore, without commenting on the merits of the
case, this application is allowed and it is directed that
 applicant shall be released on bail on his furnishing a
personal bond in a sum of Rs. 35,000/- (Rupees Thirty
Five Thousand only)           with one surety in the like
amount to the satisfaction of committal Court/trial Court
for securing his presence before the said Court on all
the dates of hearing fixed in this regard during trial.
</p><p id="p_7">        Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                                        (G.S. Solanki)
                                            Judge
navin
