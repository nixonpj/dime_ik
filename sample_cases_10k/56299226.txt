<p id="p_3">          . The matter was substantially heard by this Court. The
 matter is placed on board today for further arguments. Dr.
 Birendra Saraf, learned counsel for the petitioners tenders copy
 of the letter dated 18th December 2019, issued by Shri Manoj V.
 Daisaria to the petitioners as well as respondents, thereby
 resigning as Project Management Consultant for the projects
 namely Neelkanth Palm and Neelkanth Regent, which were
 subject matter of the Arbitration, with immediate effect.
</p>
<p id="p_4">          2. The learned counsel for the parties state that they
 would consider the effect of the said letter dated 18 th December
 2019, addressed by Shri Manoj V. Daisaria and make statement
 before this Court on the next date.
</p><p id="p_5">          3. Stand over to 19th December 2019 for directions on
 Supplementary board.
