  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

Bail Appl..No. 7118 of 2008()


1. UNNIKRISHNAN, AGED 34 YEARS,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. STATE OF KERALA, REPRESENTED BY THE
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.B.RENJITHKUMAR

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MRS. Justice K.HEMA

 Dated :21/11/2008

 O R D E R
                             K.HEMA, J.
                  ---------------------------------------------
                         B.A.No.7118 of 2008
                  ---------------------------------------------
               Dated this the 21st November, 2008


                                O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">   This petition is for bail.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     2.    The alleged offence is under Section 55(i) of Abkari

Act. According to prosecution, petitioner was found in possession

of 900 ml. of Indian made foreign liquor on 11.11.2008 and he

was arrested from the spot.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     3.    Learned public prosecutor submitted that he has no

objection in granting bail on conditions.

</p><p id="p_4">     On hearing both sides, I am satisfied that bail can be

granted to the petitioner and hence, the following order is

passed:
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">           Petitioner shall execute a bond for Rs.25,000/- with

           two solvent sureties each for the like sum to the

           satisfaction of the Magistrate Court concerned on the

           following conditions:
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">               (i)     Petitioner          shall       report   before the

                       Investigating Officer on every Monday and

                       Thursday between 10 a.m. and 1 p.m. until

                       further orders.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">BA No.7118/2008                2</span>



</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">               (ii) Petitioner shall not commit any offence

                    while on bail and in case of breach of this

                    condition, bail is liable to be cancelled.


</blockquote>    The petition is allowed.<p id="p_5">




</p><p id="p_6">                                                K.HEMA, JUDGE
