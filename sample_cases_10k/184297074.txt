<pre id="pre_1">ORDER                          EC No.318 of 2015
                          IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                      Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction


                              SREI EQUIPMENT FINANCE LIMITED
                                          Versus
                                  RAKESH KUMAR RAI &amp;ANR

        BEFORE:
        The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>
<p id="p_1">        Date : 29th September, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                                                   Appearance:
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                  Ms.S.Sharma, Advocate.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         The Court :-Learned Counsel appearing on behalf of the decree-holder submits

that the receiver could not take possession of the assets in question from the judgment-

debtor no.1. The judgment-debtor no.1, however, represented before the receiver that the

said the judgment-debtor is willing to make payment by instalment month by month and

on that plea had refused to make over the assets in question to the receiver.       In spite of

service the judgment-debtors are not represented nor they have filed their respective

affidavits of assets. The affidavit of service filed in Court is kept with the record.

</p><p id="p_5">         In such circumstances, the judgment-debtors are directed to file their respective

affidavits of assets in Form no.16A of appendix-E of the Code of Civil Procedure on or

before 30th November, 2015 and the judgment-debtors shall be personally present in

Court for examination on 4th December, 2015.             This order shall not prevent the

judgment-debtors from arriving at a settlement with the decree-holder.

</p><p id="p_6">         Ms. Sharma shall communicate this order to the judgment-debtors by speed post

with AD within one week from date and shall file an affidavit of service on the returnable

date.
</p>
<p id="p_7">         Certified website copies of this order, if applied for, be urgently supplied to the

parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.




</p><p id="p_8">                                                                ( SOUMEN SEN, J. )
