<p id="p_1">(Per : HONOURABLE
MR.JUSTICE AKIL KURESHI)
 

	Tax
Appeal is admitted for consideration of following substantial
question of law:
</p> 


<p id="p_2">"Whether,
on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Appellate
Tribunal was right in law in allowing deduction U/s.80IB (10)
r.w.s.80IB (1) to the assessee when the approval by the local
authority as well as completion certificate was not granted to the
assessee but to the landowner and the rights and the obligations
under the said approval were not transferable, and when the transfer
of dwelling units in favour of the end-users was made by the
landowner and not by the assessee?"
</p>
