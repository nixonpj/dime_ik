<div class="doc_title">Rabindra Nath Banerjee vs The State Of Jharkhand on 23 October, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                            B.A. No.8020 of 2013

          Rabindra Nath Banerjee                   .....   Petitioner
                                     Versus
          The State of Jharkhand                   ....      Opposite Party

          CORAM:       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.C. MISHRA

          For the Petitioner      :       Mr. Indrajit Sinha
          For the State           :       A. P.P.
                                  -----
2/23.10.2013</pre><p id="p_1">    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel for the State.
</p><p id="p_2">                Petitioner has been made accused for the offence under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections
          420</a>/<a href="/doc/1334254/" id="a_2">422</a>/<a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_3">406</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code, in connection with Dhanbad P.S. Case
          No.657 of 2013, corresponding to G.R. No.2805 of 2013.
</p><p id="p_3">                There is allegation against the petitioner to have taken friendly loan of
          Rs.9,50,000/- from the informant and to have issued cheques in lieu thereof, which
          bounced.
</p><p id="p_4">                It is submitted that the petitioner had earlier filed a complaint case against
          the informant and subsequently the present case has been filed by the informant.
</p><p id="p_5">                In the facts and circumstances of the case, I am inclined to release the
          petitioner on bail. Accordingly, the petitioner Rabindra Nath Banerjee is directed to
          be released on bail, on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten
          Thousand) with two sureties of like amount each to the satisfaction of learned
          Chief Judicial Magistrate, Dhanbad, in connection with         Dhanbad P.S. Case
          No.657 of 2013, corresponding to G.R. No.2805 of 2013.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                                        (H. C. Mishra, J)
R.Kumar
 </p>
