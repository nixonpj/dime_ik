  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 32627 of 2010(C)


1. REUBEN ABRAHAM,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. M.G.UNIVERSITY, KOTTAYAM
                       ...       Respondent

2. THE CONTROLLER OF EXAMINATIONS,

                For Petitioner  :SRI.B.K.GOPALAKRISHNAN

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice ANTONY DOMINIC

 Dated :27/10/2010

 O R D E R
                       ANTONY DOMINIC, J.
                    ================
                W.P.(C) NO. 32627 OF 2010 (C)
                =====================

           Dated this the 27th day of October, 2010

                          J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Petitioner completed B.Tech Degree Course. When the

results were declared, he had failed in Computer Programming

of the 5th Semester and Computer Aided Designing (CAD) of the

6th Semester. He applied for revaluation and approached this

Court complaining of delay and seeking orders to expedite the

revaluation.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2.    I heard the Standing Counsel appearing for the

respondent University.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      3.    Having regard to the above, I dispose of the writ

petition directing the University to complete the revaluation

sought for by the petitioner within 8 weeks of production of a

copy of the judgment, provided the application for revaluation has

been received and is otherwise in order.

</p><p id="p_4">      Petitioner shall produce a copy of the judgment before the

2nd respondent for compliance.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                     ANTONY DOMINIC, JUDGE
