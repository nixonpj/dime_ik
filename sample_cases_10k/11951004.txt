<div class="doc_title">Raju Mandal vs The State Of Jharkhand on 2 December, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI

                         B.A. No. 7057 of 2013

           Raju Mandal                ...  ....Petitioner
                              Vs.
           The State of Jharkhand ....Opposite Party

CORAM:     HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PRASHANT KUMAR

           For the Petitioner:         Mr. Raja Ravi Shekhar Singh
           For the State:              Mr. Tapas Roy, APP

5/02.12.2013</pre><p id="p_1">:     Bail application filed by petitioner        Raju Mandal in
     connection with Rajmahal P.S. Case No. 354 of 2012 ( G.R. No. 634 of
     2012) pending in the court of learned Additional Chief Judicial
     Magistrate, Rajmahal is moved by Sri        Raja Ravi Shekhar Singh and
     opposed by Sri Tapas Roy, learned Additional P.P.
</p><p id="p_2">                  This is a case under <a href="/doc/695990/" id="a_1">section 364</a> (A) of the Indian Penal
     Code. Petitioner has been identified by the victim during T.I. Parade.
</p><p id="p_3">                  Under the said circumstance, I am not inclined to enlarge
     the petitioner on bail. Accordingly, his bail application rejected.
</p>



<p id="p_4">                                                    ( Prashant Kumar,J.)

     Sharda/-
