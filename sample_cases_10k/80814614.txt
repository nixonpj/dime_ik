<pre id="pre_1">        HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                    BENCH AT JAIPUR

     S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 10141/2019

Gautam Saini S/o Shri Kailash Saini, Aged About 25 Years, R/o
Lodha Ki Dhani Vill. Nindad Teh. Amer Ps Harmada Dist. Jaipur
Raj. (Presently Confining In Central Jail Jaipur)
                                                                   ----Petitioner
                                    Versus
State Of Rajasthan, Through Pp
                                                                 ----Respondent</pre>


<p id="p_1">For Petitioner(s)         :     Mr. H.S. Sinsinwar.
For Respondent(s)         :     Mr. S.S. Ola, P.P.



</p><p id="p_2">            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE INDERJEET SINGH

                                     Order

02/08/2019

</p><p id="p_3">1.     The present bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

Cr.P.C. The petitioner has been arrested in connection with FIR No.

556/2019 registered at Police Station Harmara, Jaipur West for the

offence(s) under <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_2">Sections 379</a> of IPC &amp; 4/21 MMRD Act.

</p><p id="p_4">2.     Counsel for the petitioner submits that the offences alleged

against the petitioner are triable by Magistrate and the petitioner

is only owner of the vehicle and he is behind the bars since

22.07.2019.
</p>
<p id="p_5">3.     Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

</p><p id="p_6">4.     Considering the contentions put-forth by the counsel for the

petitioner and taking into account the facts and circumstances of

the case and without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

case, this court deems it just and proper to enlarge the petitioner

on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                     (Downloaded on 29/08/2019 at 10:04:13 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_8">                                                                         (2 of 2)                     [CRLMB-10141/2019]



</p><p id="p_9">                                   5.      Accordingly, the bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C. is

                                   allowed and it is ordered that the accused-petitioner Gautam

                                   Saini S/o Shri Kailash Saini shall be enlarged on bail provided

                                   he furnishes a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two

                                   sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial

                                   Judge for his appearance before the court concerned on all the

                                   dates of hearing as and when called upon to do so.



</p><p id="p_10">                                                                                              (INDERJEET SINGH),J
