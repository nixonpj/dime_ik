<div class="doc_title">Kumresh Tiwari vs The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors on 30 November, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                               Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.22321 of 2018
                 ======================================================
                 Kumresh Tiwari son of Govind Tiwari, resident of Village- Thakraha, P.S.
                 Thakraha, District West Champaran.

                                                                         ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
            1. The State of Bihar through the Secretary Food and Civil Supply Government
               of Bihar New Secretariat, Patna.
            2. The District Magistrate West Champaran, Bettiah.
            3. The Sub Divisional Officer, Bagha West Champaran Bettiah.

                                                           ... ... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s   :     Mr. Sanjeev Kumar Mishra
                 For the Respondent/s   :     Mr. S. Raza Ahmad- Aag5
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE MOHIT KUMAR SHAH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   30-11-2018                 The present writ petition has been filed for quashing

                  the order dated 19.09.2017 passed by the Sub Divisional

                  Officer, Bagha West Champaran, Bettiah whereby and

                  whereunder the licence of the P.D.S. Shop of the petitioner has

                  been cancelled.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              The short point raised by the learned counsel for the

                  petitioner is that the Sub Divisional Officer, Bagha West

                  Champaran, Bettiah i.e. Respondent No.3 has proceeded to

                  cancel the licence of the petitioner without supplying a copy of

                  the inquiry report, hence, the petitioner has been precluded from

                  submitting his defence in an appropriate manner resulting in

                  violation of principles of natural justice. Reference in this regard

                  has been made to a judgment of the Division Bench reported in
            Patna High Court CWJC No.22321 of 2018(2) dt.30-11-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                    2013(3) PLJR 706.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the

                    case, the order dated 19.09.2017 passed by the Sub Divisional

                    Officer, Bagha West Champaran, Bettiah is quashed and the Sub

                    Divisional Officer, Jainagar, Madhubani is granted liberty to

                    proceed in the matter after supplying a copy of the inquiry

                    report to the petitioner herein.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 The writ petition is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                             (Mohit Kumar Shah, J)
Tiwary/-
</p>
