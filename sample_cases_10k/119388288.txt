<div class="doc_title">Jaswinderpal vs State Of Punjab on 14 August, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">     IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT
                   CHANDIGARH

                            CRM NO. M-20032 OF 2012(O&amp;M)
                            DECIDED ON : 14.08.2012

Jaswinderpal
                                                   ...Petitioner
                  versus

State of Punjab
                                                   ...Respondent


CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE K. C. PURI

Present : Mr. P. S. Ahluwalia, Advocate,
          for the petitioner.

             Mr. P. S. Grewal, DAG, Punjab.


K. C. PURI, J. (ORAL)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             As per allegations of the prosecution, ATM Card of the

customer was prepared by the bank employee and an amount of

`11,600/- was withdrawn. The petitioner was working as Peon in

the bank. The Manager of the bank has been allowed the

concession of anticipatory bail vide order dated 22.12.2010

passed by this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             The petitioner is in custody since 19.06.2012. The

embezzlement is only of `11,600/-. The trial will take sufficient

long time.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             So, without commenting on the merits of case, the

petition for grant of regular bail stands accepted. The petitioner is

ordered to be released on bail to the satisfaction of trial Court.



</p><pre id="pre_2">AUGUST 14, 2012                               (K. C. PURI)
shalini                                         JUDGE
