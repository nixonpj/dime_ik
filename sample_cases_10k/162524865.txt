                      JUSTICE DINESH MEHTA
                              Order

24/07/2019

     Learned counsel for the petitioner(s) submits that the

controversy involved in the present writ petitions is identical to the

one decided by this court in bunch of writ petitions vide order

dated 19.7.2019 (SBCWP No.9226/2019 - Gurusar Modiya Gram

Seva Sahakari Samiti Ltd. Vs. State of Rajasthan &amp; Ors.)

     Mr. Beniwal, is not in a position to dispute this factual aspect

of the matter.
</p>
<p id="p_69">     Office reported certain defects in some writ petitions,

however, since the controversy involved in the writ petitions on



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                      (Downloaded on 01/09/2019 at 11:59:26 PM)</span>
                                                           (3 of 3)                                [CW-10282/2019]



                                   merit is covered, therefore, the defects pointed out by the

                                   Registry being innocuous one, the same are hereby over ruled.
</p>
<p id="p_70">                                        Hence, these writ petitions are disposed of in terms of the

                                   order dated 19.7.2019 passed in Gurusar Modiya Gram Seva

                                   Sahakari Samiti Ltd. (supra).
</p>
<p id="p_71">                                        The stay applications also stand disposed of accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_72">                                        The Registry is directed to keep photostat copy of this order

                                   in each file mentioned above.
