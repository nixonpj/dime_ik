  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

LA.App..No. 329 of 2007()


1. THE STATE OF KERALA, REP. BY THE
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. P.SASIDHARAN NAIR,
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :GOVERNMENT PLEADER

                For Respondent  :SRI.P.B.SURESH KUMAR

The Hon'ble MR. Justice PIUS C.KURIAKOSE
The Hon'ble MR. Justice C.K.ABDUL REHIM

 Dated :08/04/2009

 O R D E R
          PIUS.C.KURIAKOSE &amp; C.K.ABDUL REHIM, JJ.
                      ------------------------
              L.A.A.Nos.329,1194 &amp; 1209/2007
                      ------------------------

             Dated this the    8th day of April, 2009

                           JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">Pius C.Kuriakose, J.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      All these cases pertain to acquisition of land at Attipra

village for the purpose of    electronics Techno Park.   Relevant

Section 4(1) notification was published on 5/4/1994.     The land

acquisition officer included     lands involved in these cases in

categories 10 and 15 respectively. For the properties included in

category No.10, the land acquisition officer awarded land value

at the rate of Rs.11,812/-.       For the properties which were

included in category No.15 the land acquisition officer awarded

land value at the rate of Rs.2907/- per Are. The reference court

by the impugned judgment refixed the land value of the

properties in category No.15 at Rs.3488/- per Are.       Similarly

value of the properties in category 10 are fixed at Rs.15,355/- .

</p><p id="p_3">      2. Having gone through the impugned judgment and having

heard the submissions of the         senior Government Pleader

Sri.Basant Balaji , learned Standing Counsel for the requisitioning

authority   Sri.P.Ahammed, Smt.Hemalatha, who appeared for

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">L.A.A..Nos.329/07 &amp; conn.cases.  2</span>

some of the claimants and taking into account our own judgment

in other cases, including the L.A.A No.491/2007 pertaining to

acquisition of land in other categories, we are of the view that it

is more or less the correct market value of the properties which

has been arrived at by the learned Subordinate Judge. At any

rate, we do not find any warrant for interfering with the

impugned judgments and decree in these appeals preferred by

the Government.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     All these appeals will stand dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                    PIUS.C.KURIAKOSE,JUDGE




                                     C.K.ABDUL REHIM, JUDGE
