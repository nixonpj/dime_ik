                          J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     The petitioner, a stage carriage operator, has applied for

the issue of a temporary permit on the route Eramalloor -

Elamakkara with stage carriage vehicle bearing Registration

No.KL 7/BA 1330 in the vacancy of stage carriage bearing

Registration No.KL 7/AZ 5422.            According to the petitioner,

though the original permit has lapsed, no temporary permit has

been issued in its place so far. Therefore, the petitioner has

submitted Ext.P2 application for issue of a temporary permit. He

complains that the application has not been considered so far.

</p><p id="p_2">     2.   The learned Government Pleader on instructions

submits that the application submitted by the petitioner would

be considered within a short period of time.

</p><p id="p_3">     This Writ Petition is accordingly disposed of directing the

respondent to consider Ext.P2 application for temporary permit

in accordance with law and to pass appropriate orders thereon,

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">W.P(c) No.17352 of 2012-T       2</span>

as expeditiously as possible and at any rate within a period of

two weeks of the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment.




</p><p id="p_4">                                (K.SURENDRA MOHAN, JUDGE)

rtr/



</p>
