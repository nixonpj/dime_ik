<p id="p_1">CORAM:- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ARUN MONGA

Present:- Mr. Kanwaljit Singh, Senior Advocate with
          Mr. Yogesh Vashista, Advocate,
          for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Mr. P.S.Bajwa, Additional A.G., Punjab.

ARUN MONGA, J.(ORAL)

</p><p id="p_3">1.           Petitioner herein seeks issuance of a writ in the nature

of mandamus directing the respondents to grant her pay fixation

and seniority benefits along with interest in accordance with

judgment dated 10.03.2016.
</p>
<p id="p_4">2.           In the premise, petitioner submitted a representation

dated 11.12.2018 (Annexure P-11), but the same has not been

adverted by the respondents. Hence, the writ petition.

</p><p id="p_5">3.           Notice of motion.
</p>
<p id="p_6">4.           On advance service of the petition, Mr. P.S.Bajwa,

Additional A.G., Punjab appears and accepts notice on behalf of

the State.
</p>
<p id="p_7">5.           Given the nature of order being passed, there is no

necessity to seek return by the respondents as no further
