<pre id="pre_1">                                         1


           IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                      Constitutional Writ Jurisdiction
                                 Appellate Side


Present    :
The Hon'ble Justice Biswanath Somadder


                   W.P. 4387 (W) of 2013
                        Niladri Sekhar Dutta &amp; Ors.
                                       Vs.
                         State of West Bengal &amp; Ors.


For the petitioners         :-Mr. R.J. Bhattacharjee
                              Ms. D. Ghosal


For the Advocate            : Mr. J. Majumder
General                       Ms. D. Roy

Heard on                  : 18.9.2013


Judgment on               : 18.9.2013.

           Biswanath Somadder, J :

               Having heard the learned advocate for the petitioners, it appears

    that the petitioners are essentially seeking a mandatory order from this

    Court, upon the respondent authorities, to commence one year Bridge</pre>

<p id="p_1">    Course for the year 2012, forthwith.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                      2</span><p id="p_2">


      Considering the facts and circumstances of the instant case, there is

no scope for the writ Court to grant such prayer, since it would

tantamount to issuing directions upon the State in a case which essentially

falls within its sole prerogative.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      In such circumstances, the writ petition is liable to be dismissed and

is accordingly dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      Urgent certified photocopies of this order be made available to the

parties, if applied for, subject to the compliance of all requisite formalities.



</p><p id="p_5">                                     ( Biswanath Somadder, J.)
