                 HIGH COURT OF CHHATTISGARH AT BILASPUR

                                 WPS No. 1525 of 2007

             Narad S/o Maina Ram, since dead, hence through L.R's.
       1. Smt. Godawari Wd/o Narad, aged about 32 years.
       2. Ku. Bhesh Naraini D/o Narad, aged about 23 years.
       3. Ku. Dipika D/o Narad, aged about 9 years.
             All are R/o Shankapara, Ward No.5, Supela, District Durg (C.G.).
                                                                     ---Petitioners
                                            Versus
       1. Managing Director, SAIL, Bhilai Steel Plant, Bhilai, District Durg (C.G.).
       2. Asstt. General Manager I/C CR &amp; M (Mech/Strl) SAIL, Bhilai Steel Plant,
          Bhilai, District Durg (C.G.).
       3. Town Administrator (Grih Nirman Advance and Hudco), SAIL, BSP,
          Bhilai Steel Plant, District Durg (C.G.).
                                                                  ---Respondents</pre>



<p id="p_1">      For petitioners :         Shri Vinod Kumar Sharma, Advocate.
      For respondents :         Shri Aditya Bhardwaj on behalf of Shri B.D.Guru,
                                Advocate.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

                         Hon'ble Shri Justice P. Sam Koshy
                                    Order on Board
29/01/2019
</p><p id="p_3">      1.     Learned counsel for the petitioner would submit that the petitioner has

      taken a loan of ₹ 2,58,000/- from Bhilai Steel Plant in which ₹ 3,60,855/- has

      already been recovered from the petitioner. The petitioner was not paid

      salary from the year 1997 onwards and no order of recovery has been

      furnished and supplied to him and no complete information has been given

      and for which the petitioner made a representation to the Managing Director

      of the Company, which has not been considered till this date. He would

      further submit that the petitioner may be allowed to make additional
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                          2</span>

  representation to the Managing Director of the Company raising his

  grievance.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">  2.    The prayer appears to be fair and reasonable.


</p><p id="p_6">  3.    If the petitioner makes additional representation to the Managing

  Director of the Company, the representation of the petitioner shall be

  considered and decided by reasoned order keeping in view the fact that he is

  Technician and an amount of ₹ 3,60,855/- has been recovered from the

  petitioner after affording him an opportunity within a period of three months

  from the date of receipt of copy of this order.


</p><p id="p_7">  4.    With the aforesaid observation, the writ petition stands finally disposed

  of. No order as to cost(s).
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                                                 Sd/-</p><pre id="pre_2">

                                                           (P. Sam Koshy)
Sumit                                                          JUDGE
