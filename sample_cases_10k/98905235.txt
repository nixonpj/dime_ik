                          JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">       The learned counsel for the petitioner

has    submitted        that         the    petitioner      will

vacate    the    premises            on    15.11.2019.      The

learned     counsel       for         the    petitioner     has

further submitted that the petitioner needs

only a direction not to evict the petitioner

from the premises till 15.11.2019.

</p><p id="p_2">       In view of the above submission, it is

directed that the petitioner shall not be

evicted from the premises till 15.11.2019.

I make it clear that the eviction can be

effected     from       16.11.2019          onwards,   if   the

petitioner fails to vacate the premises.
 OP(C).No.2681 OF 2019
