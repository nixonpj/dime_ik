<div class="doc_title">Navneetbhai Lilachandbhai Patel vs Manojkumar Shantilal Jain on 20 June, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">             C/CA/6165/2014                                 ORDER




             IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

                       CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 6165 of 2014
                                        In
       APPEAL FROM ORDER-STAMP NUMBER NO. 218 of 2014
                           With
       APPEAL FROM ORDER-STAMP NUMBER NO. 218 of 2014
                                      With
         CIVIL APPLICATION (STAMP NUMBER) No.5036 of 2014
================================================================
             NAVNEETBHAI LILACHANDBHAI PATEL....Applicant(s)
                               Versus
               MANOJKUMAR SHANTILAL JAIN....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
MR PRAFULL B PATEL, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
================================================================

         CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.H.VORA

                                Date : 20/06/2014


                                 ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.    Vide present application, the applicant prays to condone
the delay on 633 days caused in preferring against the order
dated 11.7.2012 passed by the learned Judge, Court No.24,
City Civil Court, Bhadra, Ahmedabad, below Notice of Motion
Exhibit 6/7 in Civil Suit No.1614 of 2009, whereby learned trial
Judge accepted the Notice of Motion filed at the instance of the
plaintiff.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.    Learned advocate for the applicant ventilates grievance
that the plaintiff created forged documents and Notice of
Motion Exhibit 6/7 was heard in the absence of learned
advocate for the defendant. It appears that the suit has been


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                    Page 1 of 2</span>
             C/CA/6165/2014                                    ORDER



filed in the year 2009 and considering facts of the case and
nature     of     the        allegations   alleged   with   regard    to   the
documentary evidence placed on record by the plaintiff, it
would meet the ends of justice, if learned trial Judge is directed
to expedite the hearing of the suit, so the lis involved in the
suit can be put to an end at the earliest.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3.       Under the circumstances, instead of entertaining the
present application under <a href="/doc/100581/" id="a_1">Section 5</a> of the Limitation Act,
learned trial Judge is directed to expedite hearing of the suit
and decide the suit as early as possible, preferably within one
and half years from the receipt of this order, uninfluenced by
the observation made in the impugned order.
</p>
<p id="p_4">Appeal From Order (Stamp Number) No.218 of 2014 and
Civil Application (Stamp Number) No.5036 of 2014 :
</p>

<p id="p_5">         As the Civil Application for condonation of delay is
disposed of in view of the above direction, the Appeal From
Order (Stamp Number) No.218 of 2014 and Civil Application
(Stamp Number) No.5036 of 2014 do not survive. Hence, they
are disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                              (S.H.VORA, J.)
YNVYAS
