<div class="doc_title">Ajab Singh vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh Thr on 20 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                            1                     MCRC-10849-2018


         THE HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH
                     MCRC-10849-2018
            (Ajab Singh Vs. State of M.P. &amp; anr.)

Gwalior, Dated : 20.04.2018
      Shri A.S. Bhadoriya, counsel for the applicant.
      Shri B.P.S. Chauhan, Public Prosecutor for the
respondent No. 1/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      None for the respondent No. 2 though served.
      Case diary is available.
</p><p id="p_2">      This is first application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C.
for grant of bail.
</p><p id="p_3">      The applicant has been arrested on 18.02.2018 in
connection with Crime No.204/2017 registered by Police
Station Dehat District Bhind for the offence punishable under
<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a> and <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_4">      It is submitted by the counsel for the applicant that
respondent No. 2 has filed a criminal complaint against the
applicant alleging that by affixing the photograph of the
complainant, the applicant has fraudulently executed a sale
deed of his land. It is further submitted that the complainant
is still in possession of the land in dispute and undertakes to
stay away from the complainant as well as from the land and
he would not interfere in the peaceful possession of the land
of the complainant. The applicant is in jail from 18.02.2018
and the trial is likely to take sufficiently long time and there is
no possibility of his absconding or tampering with the
prosecution case.
</p><p id="p_5">      Per contra, application is opposed by the counsel for
the State.
</p><p id="p_6">      Prayer for grant of bail is not opposed by the
                                         2                     MCRC-10849-2018


              complainant as none appears for the complainant in spite of
              service of notice.
</p><p id="p_7">                    Considering the facts and circumstances of the case,
              period of pretrial detention and without commenting on the
              merits of the case, the application is allowed. It is directed
              that the applicant be released on bail on furnishing a
              personal bond in the sum of Rs.40,000/- (Rupees Forty
              Thousand Only) with one solvent surety in the like amount
              to the satisfaction of the Trial Court/Committal Court to
              appear before the Court on the dates given by the concerned
              Court.
</p><p id="p_8">                    This order shall be effective till the end of trial but in
              case of bail jump, it shall be become ineffective.
</p><p id="p_9">                    Certified copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                                                     (G.S. Ahluwalia)
                                                         Judge
Abhi
