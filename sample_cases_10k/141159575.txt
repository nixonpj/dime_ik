                                     Order

24/07/2018



D.B. Civil Writs No.9550/2018

     Heard on the application filed under Order 1 Rule 10 CPC. It

is for impleadment of the applicant as party respondent to the

pending writ petition. It is said to be affecting right of the

applicant.
</p>
<p id="p_12">     The application has been opposed by learned counsel for

petitioner/s. It is submitted that similar applications have been

disposed of by permitting intervention of the applicants thus none

of the applicants has been impleaded as party respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_13">     We have considered the submissions made by learned

counsel for the parties and find that similar applications were

disposed     of   with   permission    of    the   intervention   thus   this

application is also covered by the orders passed in similar

applications. Accordingly, applicant is allowed to intervene and

assist the court. The application stands disposed of.
</p>


<p id="p_14">D.B. Civil Writs No.9335/2018

     Learned counsel for applicant-petitioner does not want to

press the application. It is dismissed as not pressed.
