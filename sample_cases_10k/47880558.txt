11.2010                                C.O.2814 of 2010




                         Mr. Partha Pratim Roy.
</p><p id="p_2">                                        ... For the Petitioner.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                         Since   the    defendant's   application   for   accept-ance   of   written
          statement on condonation of delay is pending for consideration before the learned
          Trial Judge since July, 2009, the plaintiff has come before this Court with this
          application under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the Constitution of India for issuance of direction
          upon the learned Trial Judge for early disposal of the defendant's said
          application.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                         After hearing Mr. Roy, learned Advocate, appearing for the petitioner
          and after considering the materials on record, this Court disposes of this
          revisional application by making a request to the learned Civil Judge, Junior
          Division, 2nd Court at Berhampore, to make an utmost endeavour to dispose of
          the said application filed in other Suit No. 132 of 2004 as early as possible, but
          preferably by 31st January, 2011 without granting any unnecessary adjournment
          to any of the parties.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                         Since neither party can have any grievance against early disposal of
          the suit, this Court feels that there is no necessity to direct the petitioner to serve
          copy of this application upon the opposite parties.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                         However, the petitioner is directed to intimate this order to the
          opposite parties as well as their learned Advocate in the Court below, so that the
          opposite parties are not taken by surprise regarding further progress of the suit
          in terms of the order passed hereinabove.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                         The revisional application is, thus, disposed of.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                 2</span>




<p id="p_8">ac   (Jyotirmay Bhattacharya, J.)
