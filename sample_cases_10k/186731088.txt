<div class="doc_title">State vs 1.Ravinder @ Judi on 9 September, 2009</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Sh. V.K. Khanna</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                                  1             I.D.No.02402R0428302007

     IN THE COURT OF ADDITIONAL SESSIONS JUDGE-IV
              EAST :KARKARDOOMA :DELHI

SC No.11/08
FIR No.258/07
PS Kalyan Puri
U/s 385/307/34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>

                     State   vs   1.Ravinder @ Judi
                                      s/o Narender @ Ninder
                                      r/o 7/474, Trilok Puri ,Delhi.
                                  2. Surjeet
                                      s/o Ranveer Singh
                                      r/o 7/412, Trilok Puri.
                                  3. Sunder
                                      s/o Salek Chand
                                      r/o 8/422, Trilok Puri, Delhi.

                                      Date of institution:   4.7.07
                                      Arguments heard:       9.9.09
                                      Date of decision :     9.9.09

JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">1.          Accused in this case have been tried for the offence

     u/s 385/307/34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>. Briefly stated, the case of the

     prosecution is that on 18.4.07 at 8.30p.m. at 7 Block, Trilok

     Puri Chowk, Delhi accused caused simple injuries upon

     complainant Rakesh Kumar with knife while committing

     extortion under such circumstances and with such an

     intention that by the said injuries had they caused the death

     of injured , they would have been guilty of murder . On
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   2         I.D.No.02402R0428302007</span>

     completion of investigation , chargesheet was filed in the

     court on 23.5.07.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.          After supplying the copies u/s 207 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_3">Cr.P.C</a>., the case

     was committed to the court of Sessions on 7.6.07. Charge

     u/s 385/307/34 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a> was framed against all the accused on

     9.8.07 to which accused pleaded not guilty and claimed

     trial. Prosecution in order to bring home the accusations,

     examined seven witnesses .
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.          Statement of accused u/s 313 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_5">Cr.P.C</a>. was dispensed

     with as complainant and material witnesses did not support

     the case of prosecution . I have heard the submissions

     advanced by ld.defence counsel and ld.Additional PP for the

     state and have perused the material on carefully.

</p><p id="p_4">4.          In nutshell the evidence led by the prosecution is as

     follows. Sh.Rakesh (Pw1) is the complainant who deposed

     that in the year 2007 he was working as waiter in the

     marriage parties. He did     not recollect the exact date or

     month of incident. Incident occurred more than two years
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                             3        I.D.No.02402R0428302007</span>

ago. At around 8.30 p.m. he was present at 7 Block Trilok

Puri Chowk. Accused persons Sujit, Sunder and Judi were

also standing there who were his friends. In the mean while

, one boy came there and gave knife and blade blows on his

stomach, ribs and waist. Accused Surjeet saved him from the

said persons and he took him to his house. Pw1 alongwith

his neighbour went to the LBS hospital, where he was

admitted and given medical aid. Pw1 was discharged from

the hospital after receiving stitches in the night. Police

recorded his statement Ex.PW1/A. Pw1 stated that accused

Ravinder and Sunder had not caused any injury to him. Pw1

stated that accused Ravinder @ Judi, Surjeet and Sunder

had not caused any injury upon him. He was cross examined

by the Ld.APP. In cross examination, Pw1 stated that he

knew Surjeet and Sunder since long and Surjeet had asked

Rs.200/- for drinking liquor from him. HC Sukram Pal

(Pw2) , duty officer placed on record copy of DD No.30-A

Ex.Pw2/A and     FIR No.258/07      Ex.Pw2/B. He      made
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                             4         I.D.No.02402R0428302007</span>

endorsement on the back side of rukka Ex.Pw2/C. Saurabh

Kumar (Pw3) deposed that he was coming from Indrapuram

when he reached at seven block chowk, Trilokpuri. He

found quarrel taking place. He found Rakesh who was

having injuries on his stomach. Pw3 did not know who

caused injuries to him. Pw3 specifically stated that accused

persons present in the court were not there. He was cross

examined by the ld.APP. He admitted that Ex.Pw3/A,

Ex.Pw3/B, Ex.Pw3/C, Ex.Pw3/D, Ex.Pw3/E, Ex.Pw3/F and

Ex.Pw3/G bears his signature at point A. He denied the

suggestion that he had signed the said memos after going

through the aforesaid documents. He denied that he being

the resident of the same locality of all accused persons was

not identifying them as assailant. He denied that he wanted

to save the accused persons or was not telling the truth .

Pw3 denied that accused Surjeet was arrested at his instance

by the police on 20.4.07. Pw3 denied that complainant

Rakesh was beaten by all the accused persons with knife. He
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                              5        I.D.No.02402R0428302007</span>

denied that he had been won over by the accused person or

was not identifying the accused persons deliberately. Pw3

admitted that he knew the accused persons being resident of

the same locality but denied that he was not deposing

against those persons in order to save them. Dr.Parmesh

Sharma (Pw4) from LBS Hospital deposed that one Rakesh

was brought with alleged history of assault on 18.4.07 . On

examination, patient was oriented, conscious .On local

examined there was incised eliptical injury (6x4x0.5) over

lest   side   of   abdomen   and   oozing   of   blood   was

present,incised wound over right side of hip (5x5cm) and

red in colour, incised would over left elbow (1x1cm in size)

and oozing of blood was present. Pw4         prepared MLC

No.3016/07 Ex.Pw4/A. HC Shri Bhagwan (Pw5) testified

that he alongwith HC Rajbir and SI Jaspal reached at LBS

hospital and collected MLC and sealed parcel from the

hospital. IO collected blood stained shirt which was seized

vide Ex.Pw5/A. Accused Sunder was apprehended at the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_5">                                   6          I.D.No.02402R0428302007</span>

     instance of eye witness Saurabh vide memo Ex.Pw3/D and

     his disclosure statement was recorded Ex.Pw3/A. Ct.Kanti

     Prasad (Pw6) stated that Saurabh came to the police station

     and informed that accused Surjeet was present in his house

     and could be apprehended if raided. Pw6 alongwith SI

     Harpal Singh and Saurabh reached at 7/412, Trilokpuri and

     apprehended accused Surjeet . SI Jaspal Singh (Pw7)

     prepared     several   documents    during   the   course   of

     investigation. He prepared rukka Ex.Pw7/A, site plan

     Ex.Pw7/B . He stated that weapon of offence could not be

     traced.
</p>
<p id="p_5">5.             Rakesh, the complainant (Pw1) has deposed that

     accused Ravinder, Sunder         and Surjeet had not caused

     injuries on him. Evidence on record shows that witnesses

     have not supported the case of prosecution and have not

     uttered anything against the accused . In view of the

     evidence on record, this court finds that prosecution has

     failed to bring home the guilt of the accused. Hence,accused
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_6">                              7        I.D.No.02402R0428302007</span>

Ravinder @ Judi , Surjeet and Sunder are hereby acquitted

from the charges against them. Their bail bonds cancelled,

sureties discharged. Case property be confiscated to the

state. File be consigned to RR.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><pre id="pre_2">Announced in the              (VINAY KUMAR KHANNA)
open court on 9.9.09           Additional Sessions Judge
                              04-East/Karkardooma : Delhi
