4/18.12.2012</pre><p id="p_1">                Mother of the petitioner, who was working as Sweeper had
               died in harness on 27.01.2010. However, despite repeated requests,
               personal visits by the petitioner death-cum-retiral dues of the
               deceased, mother of the petitioner have not been released in favour of
               the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">                            Mr. Bhawesh Kumar, learned counsel for the respondents
               submits that let the petitioner move a detailed representation before
               the respondent no. 1 within thirty (30) days from today and an
               appropriate decision, in accordance with law, shall be taken thereon
               by the respondent no. 1 within next sixty (60) days and the amount
               found due shall be released in favour of the petitioner without any
               undue delay.
</p><p id="p_3">                            Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the present
               petition be disposed of in the light of the statements made by Mr.
               Bhawesh Kumar, learned counsel appearing for the respondents.
</p><p id="p_4">                            Ordered accordingly.
</p>
