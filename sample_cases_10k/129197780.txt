<p id="p_1">3   17-08-2015          The plaintiff of Title Suit No. 86 of 2005 has filed the

                 present application aggrieved by the order dated 20.6.2009 passed

                 by Sub-Judge-IV, Darbhanga whereby the application dated

                 9.4.2009 filed by the petitioner under Order VI Rule 17 of the

                 Code of Civil Procedure (for short 'the Code') was considered and

                 rejected after hearing both the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                        The suit was filed for confirming the actual possession and

                 title over the suit land. An application for grant of injunction was

                 filed by the plaintiff which was withdrawn on 25.8.2008. Earlier to

                 this, an Advocate Commissioner was appointed who after making

                 on spot enquiry reported that the defendants had constructed
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court CWJC No.2819 of 2011 (3) dt.17-08-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                        his/their house over the land well prior to the institution of the suit.

                        The amendment requested through Annexure-3 was found not

                        filed in good faith which, if allowed, would change the nature of

                        the suit or the relief prayed for therein. The Court has in particular

                        noticed the motive of the plaintiff in seeking amendment in the

                        plaint.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  In my view, the order passed by the Trial Court does not

                        suffer from any patent illegality meriting interference therewith by

                        this Court under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the Constitution of India.

</p><p id="p_4">                                  The application is dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                               (Kishore Kumar Mandal, J)
Pankaj/-
