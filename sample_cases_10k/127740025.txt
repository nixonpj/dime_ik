<div class="doc_title">Rakesh Kumar Singh vs The State Of Jharkhand on 12 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                A.B.A. No. 1830 of 2018

1. Rakesh Kumar Singh
2. Sanaul Ansari                    ..... Petitioners
                        Versus
The State of Jharkhand              ..... Opp. Party
                         ---------
</pre><p id="p_1">CORAM:      HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
</p><p id="p_2">                          ---------
</p><p id="p_3">For the Petitioners     : Mr. Aashish Kumar, Advocate.
</p><pre id="pre_2">For the State           : A.P.P.
                     ---------
02/Dated: 12/04/2018

        Petitioners    are   apprehending      their   arrest   in
</pre><p id="p_4">connection with Dumka (M) P.S. Case No. 22 of 2018,
corresponding to G.R. No. 171 of 2018, registered under
<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_2">414</a>, <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_4">120B</a> of the I.P.C., Section 4/54(i) of
Jharkhand Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 2004, <a href="/doc/1764008/" id="a_5">Section
54</a> (i) of Jharkhand Minor Mineral Concession (Amended)
Rules, 2017.
</p><p id="p_5">        Learned counsel for the petitioners has submitted
petitioner no. 1 is the owner of the seized truck bearing
registration no. JH-04K-8397 and the petitioner no. 2 is the
driver of the aforesaid truck and they have been falsely
implicated in this case. It is further submitted that co-
accused have been admitted on anticipatory bail by this
Court in A.B.A. No. 1571 of 2018 vide order dated
19.03.2018.
</p><p id="p_6">        Learned APP has opposed the prayer for anticipatory
bail.
</p><p id="p_7">        Be that as it may, I am inclined to admit the
petitioners    on     anticipatory   bail.   The   above   named
petitioners are directed to surrender in the Court below
within four weeks from the date of this order and in the
event of their arrest or surrender, the Court below shall
enlarge the above named petitioners on bail on furnishing
bail bonds of Rs. 15,000/- (Rupees fifteen thousand) each
with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction
of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Dumka, in connection with
Dumka (M) P.S. Case No. 22 of 2018, corresponding to G.R.
No. 171 of 2018, subject to the conditions as laid down
under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_6">Section 438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C. and also on the
following conditions:-
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  -2-</span>


<p id="p_8">(i)      One of the bailers shall be the local resident of
Dumka district, solvent person.
</p><p id="p_9">(ii)     On the date of furnishing bail bonds, petitioner no. 1
namely Rakesh Kumar Singh shall deposit Rs. 15,000/- in
the court below, which shall be subject to the result of the
case and the said deposition will not prejudice the case of
the petitioners during trial.
</p><p id="p_10">(iii)    Petitioners   will   give   an    affidavit   through   their
Parivikar that during course of investigation, they will
cooperate in investigation and they will remain physically
present in the court below as and when their presence is
required.
</p><p id="p_11">(iv)     Petitioners are further directed that along with the
bail bonds, they will furnish their Cell numbers and xerox
copy of AADHAR Cards in the court below.
</p><p id="p_12">(v)      It shall be open for the I.O. of this case that if he
wants to record further statement of the petitioners, he will
file an application through APP in the court below and the
court below shall release the Cell Numbers of the
petitioners to the I.O., thereafter the I.O. will give 72 hours'
notice to the petitioners fixing date, time and place and,
thereafter, petitioners will appear and get their statement
recorded. If petitioners fail to do so, then the I.O. will be at
liberty to file application through APP for cancellation of
bail bonds of the petitioners.
</p><p id="p_13">         Let a copy of order be communicated to the court
below and also handed over to the learned APP for
transmission to the I.O.
</p>



<p id="p_14">                                          (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
Sunil/
