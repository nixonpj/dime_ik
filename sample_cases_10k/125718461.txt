<div class="doc_title">Rameshbhai Rupjibhai Pargi vs State Of Gujarat on 19 March, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">          R/CR.MA/3222/2014                                           ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR REGULAR BAIL) NO. 3222 of 
                                        2014

==============================================================
</pre><p id="p_1">              RAMESHBHAI RUPJIBHAI PARGI....Applicant(s)
                             Versus
                 STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
==============================================================
Appearance:
</p><p id="p_2">MR KARTIK V PANDYA, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MR JK SHAH APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
==============================================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE ANANT S. DAVE


          Date : 19/03/2014
 
ORAL ORDER


       Leave to amend.
</p>
<p id="p_3">1.     This   application   is   filed   under   Section   439   of   the   Code   of 

Criminal   Procedure   in   connection   with   first   information   report 

registered   at   C.R.   No.   I­20   of   2014   with   Santrampur   Police   Station, 

District: Mahisagar,   for the offences punishable under Sections 436, 

323 and 114 of the  Indian Penal Code and Section 135 of the Gujarat 

Police Act.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">2.     Learned   counsel   appearing   for   the   applicant   submits   that 

considering the nature of allegations,   punishment prescribed and the 

applicant  as well as the victim both belong to tribal community and 

cause a scuffle. It is further submitted that the considering the above 

aspects, this application for bail may kindly be considered.


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                      Page 1 of 4</span>
</p><p id="p_6">                R/CR.MA/3222/2014                                          ORDER




</p><p id="p_7">3.       Heard learned APP for the respondent - State who opposed grant 

of bail looking to the nature and gravity of offence. 



</p><p id="p_8">4.       Having heard learned counsel for the parties and perusing the 

record of the case and taking into consideration the above facts, nature 

of   allegations,   role   attributed   to   the   accused   and   punishment 

prescribed   for   the   alleged   offences,   I   am   inclined   to   enlarge   the 

applicant on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">5.       Learned counsel for the parties do not press for further reasoned 

order.
</p>
<p id="p_11">

</p><p id="p_12">6.       In   the   facts   and   circumstances   of   the   case,   the   application   is 

allowed   and   the   applicant   is   ordered   to   be   released   on   bail   in 

connection with first information report registered at C.R. No. I­20 of 

2014 with Santrampur Police Station, District: Mahisagar, on executing 

a bond of Rs.25,000/­ (Rupees Twenty Five Thousand only)   with one 

surety   of   the   like   amount   to   the   satisfaction   of   the   trial   Court   and 

subject to the conditions that he shall;



</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">         (a)       not take undue advantage of liberty or misuse liberty;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">         (b)       not act in a manner injurious to the interest of the 
                   prosecution;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">         (c)       surrender passport, if any, to the lower court within a 
                   week;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">         (d)       not leave the State of Gujarat without prior permission of  
                   the Sessions Judge concerned;
</blockquote>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                         Page 2 of 4</span>
<pre id="pre_2">              R/CR.MA/3222/2014                                         ORDER




       (e)       mark presence at the concerned police station on the first  
</pre><blockquote id="blockquote_5">                 Sunday of every  month between 10.00 a.m. and 3.00 
                 p.m. for three months only;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_6">       (f)       furnish the present address of   residence to the I.O. and  
                 also to the Court  at the time of execution of the bond and 
                 shall not change the residence  without prior permission of  
                 this Court;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_7">       The   applicants   are   directed   to   pay   the   victim   by   way   of  
       damage   caused   to   the   house   of   the   complainant   an  
       amount of Rs.10,000/­ (Rupees Ten Thousand only) without  
       prejudice to the rights and contentions.
</blockquote>
<p id="p_13">7.     The Authorities will release the applicant only if not required in 

connection with any other offence for the time being.



</p><p id="p_14">8.     If   breach   of   any   of   the   above   conditions   is   committed,   the 

Sessions   Judge   concerned   will   be   free   to   issue   warrant   or   take 

appropriate action in the matter.
</p>
<p id="p_15">

</p><p id="p_16">9.     Bail   bond   to   be   executed   before   the   lower   court   having 

jurisdiction to try the case.
</p>
<p id="p_17">

</p><p id="p_18">10.    For modification and/or deletion of any of the conditions herein 

above,   the   applicant/s   will   be   at   liberty   to   approach   the   concerned 

Court   and   such   Court   shall   decide   the   application   for   modification 

and/or deletion of any of the conditions of this order in accordance with 

law.
</p>
<p id="p_19">

</p><p id="p_20">11.    At   the   trial,   the   trial   court   shall   not   be   influenced   by   the 

observations   of   preliminary   nature,   qua   the   evidence   at   this   stage, 


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                       Page 3 of 4</span>
         R/CR.MA/3222/2014                                   ORDER



made by this Court while enlarging the applicant on bail.



</p><p id="p_21">12.   Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.  D.S. Permitted.




</p><p id="p_22">                                                  (ANANT S.DAVE, J.) 
/phalguni/
