<div class="doc_title">Vinod &amp; Ors. vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 11 March, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                           CRA-2122-2005
               (VINOD &amp; ORS. Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


11-03-2016</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Shri Vishal Dhagat, counsel for the appellants.
      Shri Prakash Gupta, Panel Lawyer for the State.
      Heard on I.A. No.1726/2016 application for suspension of
sentence and grant of bail filed on behalf of appellant Vinod
Dubey.
</p><p id="p_2">         Considering the fact that based on the circumstantial
evidence that has come on record with regard to seizure of certain
clothes worn by prosecutrix, the appellant has been convicted. So
also the fact that the appellant has already undergone jail
sentence of about 15 years, we allow the application for
suspension of sentence.
</p><p id="p_3">     Accordingly, I.A.No.1726/2016 is allowed. Appellant Vinod
Dubey is directed to be released on bail and operation of the jail
sentence shall remain suspended on his furnishing a personal
bond in the sum of Rs.40,000/- (Rupees forty thousand only)
with one surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial
court, for his appearance before the registry of this court on
01.12.2016 and on such other dates as may be ordered.
Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">  (RAJENDRA MENON)                                  (SUBHASH KAKADE)
        JUDGE                                             JUDGE
