                 For the Petitioner/s     :        Mr. Manoj Kumar, Advocate

<p id="p_1">2   30-01-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Call for a copy of the case diary of Halsi P.S. Case

                   No. 17 of 2018 from the court of learned Additional Chief

                   Judicial Magistrate, Lakhisarai.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Put up after receipt of the case diary or on 28.02.2019,

                   whichever is earlier.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                The court below shall ensure that the diary is

                   transmitted to this Court on or before 25.02.2019.

</p><p id="p_5">                                In the meantime, in the event of arrest or surrender in

                   the court below, the petitioner is directed to be released on

                   provisional bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (rupees

                   ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                   satisfaction of the learned Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                   Lakhisarai in connection with Halsi P.S. Case No. 17 of 2018,
             Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4203 of 2019(2) dt.30-01-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                         2/2</span>




                      subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 438(2)</a> of

                      the Code of Criminal Procedure.
</p>
