<div class="doc_title">Smt.Meena Sahay &amp; Anr vs Afzal Abdullah &amp; Ors on 15 December, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                            MA No.385 of 2003
                          SMT.MEENA SAHAY &amp; ANR
                                  Versus
                           AFZAL ABDULLAH &amp; ORS
                               -----------
</pre>



<p id="p_1">9   15.12.2008                 In a motor vehicle accident taking place on 2nd June

                  1993, two persons, namely, Dr.Meena Sinha and Dr.T.N.Verma died.

                  The heirs of Dr.verma have filed claim Case No. 63 of 1993 and

                  heirs of Dr.Sinha have filed claim case no.62 of 1993. Both the

                  claims cases were decided by common judgment dated 9th July 2003

                  and award dated 28th July 2003 of the Claims Tribunal concerned.

</p><p id="p_2">                                This appeal (M.A.No.385 of 2003) is against the award

                  passed in Claim Case No. 63 of 1993. Learned counsel for the

                  appellant submits that against the award passed in Claim Case No. 62

                  of 1993, separate appeal bearing M.A.No. 384 of 2003 has been filed.

</p><p id="p_3">                                It appears proper and expedient that both the appeals be

                  heard together. Therefore, this appeal be tagged with the said appeal

                  i.e. M.A.No.384 of 2003 and both the appalls will be listed for

                  analogous hearing.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><pre id="pre_2">        Jay/                                                ( C. M. Prasad, J .)
