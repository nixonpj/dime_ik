<p id="p_1">        The learned counsel for the petitioner seeks permission of this Court
to withdraw this writ petition. He has also submitted a letter dated
01.07.2015 to the Registry, to that effect. In view of the same, the writ
petition is dismissed as withdrawn. No costs. Consequently, the connected
miscellaneous petition is also dismissed.
