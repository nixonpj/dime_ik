                          The matter is required to rest now in view of the detailed

            consideration made by the learned Single Judge in MJC No.386 of

            2010 with regard to the status of the petitioner and her claim.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                          It is evident from reading of Annexure- 8 that the freedom

            fighter pension granted to the petitioner was annulled in the year 1996

            itself. This Court, therefore, will not allow the petitioner the benefit

            of assailing an order, which is more than a decade and half old.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                          Writ is dismissed.
</p>
