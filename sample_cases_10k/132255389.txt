          IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA
             CIRCUIT BENCH, AT DHARWAD

       DATED THIS THE 27TH DAY OF MARCH 2013

                           BEFORE

     THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE DILIP B. BHOSALE

        WRIT PETITION No.76398/2013 (GM-CPC)

BETWEEN:

1.   Topanagouda,
     S/o Honnanagouda Patil,
     Age: 42 years, R/o Hanumanahal,
     Taluk: Dharwad, Dist: Dharwad.
                                                -     Petitioner
(By J.S.Shetty Associates, Advocates)

AND:

1.   Smt. Sushilavva,
     W/o Honnanagouda Patil,
     Age: 66 years, R/o Hanumanahal,
     Taluk: Dharwad, Dist: Dharwad.

2.   Smt. Manjula,
     D/o Honnanagouda Patil,
     Age: 32 years, R/o Hanumanahal,
     Taluk: Dharwad, Dist: Dharwad.
                                            -       Respondents</pre>

<p id="p_1">       This Writ Petition is filed under articles 226 and 227 of
the Constitution of India, praying to quash the order dated
08.02.2013 passed on I.A. filed under Order 14 Rule 5 of C.P.C.
in O.S.No.101/2011 passed by the Prl. Civil Judge (Jr.Dn.) and
Prl. J.M.F.C. Dharwad, produced as Annexure-A.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              2</span>




</p><p id="p_2">      This Writ Petition coming for preliminary hearing on
this day, the court made the following:

PC :
</p>
<p id="p_3">       Learned   Counsel   for   the   petitioner   prays   for

withdrawal of this petition with liberty to petitioner to

challenge the order impugned in the present writ petition

in further proceedings, if the suit filed by the respondent

No.1/plaintiff is decreed against him.


</p><p id="p_4">2.     Petition is disposed of as withdrawn with liberty as

prayed for. All contentions are kept open to be raised in

appeal that will be filed against the judgment and decree

passed by the Court below.
</p>

</p><p id="p_6">                                              Sd/-
</p><p id="p_7">                                             JUDGE
