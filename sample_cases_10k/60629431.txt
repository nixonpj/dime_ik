<p id="p_1">      Petitioner is the 8th accused in crime No.34/CC/97 of

CBCID, Malappuram now pending as C.P.No.39/2011 before the

J.F.C.M-II, Tirur for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a>,

<a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_2">201</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_3">120B</a> I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2.    The petitioner was on bail. Due to non-appearance of

the petitioner, non-bailable warrant was issued against the

petitioner. The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

the petitioner is ready and willing to surrender and apply for

bail. The petitioner, if surrenders and applies for bail on or

before 10/08/2011, the same shall be considered and disposed of

by the learned Magistrate on the same day itself on such terms

and conditions he deems fit.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      3.    Crl.M.C is disposed of as above.</p><pre id="pre_2">
