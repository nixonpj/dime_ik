<div class="doc_title">Jai Prakash Mondal Alias Prakash ... vs The State Of Jharkhand on 31 January, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                        Cr.M.P. No. 209 of 2017
                                       ---
           1. Jai Prakash Mondal @ Prakash Mondal, son of Late Kunju Mondal,
           Resident of Village Jhilua, P.S. Narayanpur, P.O. and District - Jamtara
           2. Shaligram Mondal, son of Late Damodar Mondal, resident of Village
           - Murudih, P.S. Karmatar, P.O. and District - Jamtara
           3. Mahendra Mondal, son of Jhuki Mondal, Resident of Village
           Karithar, P.S. Karmatar, P.O. and District - Jamtara
                                                  ...      ...    Petitioners
                               Versus
           The State of Jharkhand                 ...     ...    Opposite Party
                              ---
     CORAM       : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY
                               ---
           For the Petitioners       : Mr. Kaushik Sarkhel, Advocate
           For the Opp. Party        : Mr. Sudhansu Kumar Deo, A.P.P.
                                     ---
                               I.A. No. 859 of 2017

03/31.01.2017</pre><p id="p_1">    This interlocutory application has been preferred by the
           petitioners for amendment in Para - 1 and prayer portion of the main
           application in view of the subsequent developed by virtue of which
           vide order dated 25.01.2017 proclamation under <a href="/doc/1598801/" id="a_1">Section 82</a> of the
           Cr.P.C. has been ordered to be issued.
</p><p id="p_2">                 In view of the fact that the prayer made in the instant application
           has a direct nexus with the prayer made in the main application and in
           order to avoid multiplicity of proceeding, this application is allowed.
</p><p id="p_3">                 I. A. no. 859 of 2017 shall be treated as a part of the main
           application.
</p><p id="p_4">                              Cr.M.P. No. 209 of 2017
                 Heard Mr. Kaushik Sarkhel, learned counsel appearing for the
           petitioners and Mr. Sudhansu Kumar Deo, learned A.P.P.
</p><p id="p_5">                 In this application the petitioners have prayed for quashing of
           the orders dated 02.01.2017 and 25.01.2017 passed by the learned Chief
           Judicial Magistrate, Jamtara in connection with Karmatanr P. S. Case
           No. 179 of 2016 by virtue of which non-bailable warrant of arrest and
           proclamation under <a href="/doc/1598801/" id="a_2">Section 82</a> Cr.P.C. has been issued.
</p><p id="p_6">                 It appears that none of the impugned orders reflect subjective
           satisfaction on the part of the learned Magistrate as merely on the
           requisition of the Investigating Officer the orders taking coercive steps
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           -2-</span>
          against the petitioners has been passed. In absence of any cogent
          reasons the impugned orders dated 02.01.2017 and 25.01.2017 passed
          by the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Jamtara are, hereby, quashed
          and set aside.
</p><p id="p_7">                This application is allowed.
</p><p id="p_8">                However, the learned Magistrate is at liberty to proceed further
          in accordance with law.
</p><p id="p_9">                Pending I.A., if any, also stands disposed of.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                                                (Rongon Mukhopadhyay, J.)




Umesh/-
</p>
