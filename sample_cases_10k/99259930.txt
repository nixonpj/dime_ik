                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

          CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 31735 of
                                                2017

         ==========================================================
         VINU @ VINAYSING OMPRAKASHSING SIKARVAR THROUGH VINITABEN
                       VINYAYSING SIKHARWAR....Applicant(s)
                                     Versus
                        STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MR SURESH G VAGHELA, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MS. MOXA THAKKAR, ADDL. PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the Respondent(s)
         No. 1
         ==========================================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE J.B.PARDIWALA

                                        Date : 09/02/2018


                                         ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">               Rule returnable forthwith. Ms. Moxa Thakkar, the learned APP

         waives service of notice of rule for and on behalf of the respondent-State.

</p><p id="p_2">               By this application, the applicant, an under-trial prisoner prays for

         temporary bail on the ground of making arrangement for the payment of

         school fees of his two sons.
</p>
<p id="p_3">               The accused has been arrested in connection with I-CR No. 443 of

         2013 registered before the Odhav Police Station, Ahmedabad, for the

         offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a>, <a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_2">143</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_3">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_4">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_5">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_6">323</a> of the

         IPC and Section 135(1) of the G. P. Act.</p><pre id="pre_2">


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                             Page 1 of 2</span>

HC-NIC                                    Page 1 of 2      Created On Fri Feb 09 23:25:42 IST 2018
                     R/CR.MA/31735/2017                                              ORDER



</pre><p id="p_4">                  Taking into consideration the grounds urged in this application, the

         applicant - accused is ordered to be released on temporary bail for a

         period of 10 days from the date of his actual release, on the applicant

         executing a bond of Rs. 5,000/- to the satisfaction of the concerned

         authority, on usual terms and conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                  The applicant shall surrender before the jail authority on or before

         the expiry of the temporary bail period, without fail.

</p><p id="p_6">                  Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent. Direct service is
         permitted.
</p>
