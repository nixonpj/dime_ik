<div class="doc_title">Rakesh vs State Of Kerala on 23 May, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">       

  

  

 
 
  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

Bail Appl..No. 3436 of 2008()


1. RAKESH, AGED 30 YEARS
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. STATE OF KERALA, REPRESENTED BY
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.NAGARAJ NARAYANAN

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice V.RAMKUMAR

 Dated :23/05/2008

 O R D E R
                          V. RAMKUMAR, J.
                 * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
                 Bail Application No. 3436 of 2008
                * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
                         Dated: 23-05-2008

                             O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Petitioner who is the 10th accused in Crime No. 77 of 1997

of Peroorkkada Police Station for       offences punishable under

<a href="/doc/1096965/" id="a_1">Sections 143</a>, <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_2">147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_3">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_4">149</a>, <a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_5">452</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_6">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_7">326</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_8">307</a> and <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_9">427</a> I.P.C.

and whose case is now pending before the                  J.F.C.M.II,

Thiruvananthapuram       seeks a direction to the Magistrate to

enlarge the petitioner on bail on the date of his surrender itself.

</p><p id="p_2">      2.    Consequent on the non-appearance of the petitioner

in C.P. No. 40 of 2008              on the file of the J.F.C.M.II,

Thiruvananthapuram, non-bailable warrants of arrest are pending

against the petitioner.    Anticipatory bail cannot be granted to

nullify the process issued by a court of competent jurisdiction.

There is no reason       why the petitioner should not surrender

before the Magistrate and seek regular bail.     Accordingly, if the

petitioner surrenders before the Magistrate and files an

application for regular bail within two weeks from today, the same

shall be considered and disposed of preferably on the same day

on which it is filed after considering the explanation offered by

the petitioner for his previous non-appearance.

</p><p id="p_3">      With the above observation this application is disposed of .




</p><p id="p_4">                                        V.RAMKUMAR, JUDGE.
</p>
