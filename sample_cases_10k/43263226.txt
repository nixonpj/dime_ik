<div class="doc_title">T.Janaher vs The Chief Educational Officer on 10 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 10.04.2018  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.SURESH KUMAR              

W.P(MD)No.7760 of 2018   


T.Janaher                                                         ... Petitioner


Vs.


1.The Chief Educational Officer,
   Tirunelveli.

2.The District Educational Officer,
   Cheranmadevi @ Thirunelveli,
   Thirunelveli District.

3.The Secretary,
   Chathiram Bharathi Higher Secondary
        School,
   Kadaiyam, Tirunelveli District.                      ... Respondents

Prayer: This Writ Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of
India, to issue a Writ of Mandamus, directing the respondents to sanction
incentive increment for the higher qualification of M.Phil., yearly
increments from January, 2016, Earn Leave Surrender, Provident Fund Advance 
and revised pay as per the VII Pay Commission recommendation to the  
petitioner.

!For Petitioner : Mr.V.Panneer Selvam 
For Respondents : Mrs.S.Srimathy, Spl. G.P.        
                                                For RR1 and 2 

:ORDER          
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        The prayer sought for in this Writ Petition is for a Writ of Mandamus,
directing the respondents to sanction incentive increment for the higher
qualification of M.Phil., yearly increments from January, 2016, Earn Leave
Surrender, Provident Fund Advance and revised pay as per the VII Pay
Commission recommendation to the petitioner. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.Heard Mr.V.Panneer Selvam, learned counsel for the petitioner and
Mrs.S.Srimathy, learned Special Government Pleader appearing for the
respondents 1 and 2.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that though he has
acquired higher qualification, for which, he is entitled to get incentive
increments and the same was requested right from the year 2015, by way of
making several representations, the request of the petitioner has not been so
far  considered by the respondents. .
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4.Heard Mrs.S.Srimathy, learned Special Government Pleader appearing  
for the respondents in this regard.
</p>

<p id="p_5">        5.Since the petitioner's request is to consider his various
representations, especially the representations dated 01.06.2015, 06.05.2016
and 30.10.2017, and pass orders thereon, on merits and in accordance with
law, with regard to the request of the petitioner for the grant of incentive
increments, this writ petition is disposed of at the admission stage, with
the consent of both sides with the following direction.
</p>
<p id="p_6">        6.The third respondent is directed to forward the representations of
the petitioner if not already forwarded and on receipt of such
representations from the third respondent to be forwarded if not already
forwarded, the second respondent shall consider the representations dated
01.06.2015, 06.05.2016 and 30.10.2017 of the petitioner, on merits and in
accordance with law and pass orders thereon, with regard to the request of
the petitioner for grant of incentive increments, within a period of six
weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of this order.
</p>

<p id="p_7">        7.With this direction, this writ petition is disposed of.  No costs.
.
          </p>
