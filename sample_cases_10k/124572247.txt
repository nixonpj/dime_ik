                 ======================================================
                 Bhubneshwar Mandal S/o Sri Bhola Prasad Mandal, R/o Village- Batraha,
                 Ward No. 77, P.S. + District- Saharsa

                                                                                  .... ....   Petitioner

                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   10-11-2015               Heard learned counsels for the petitioners of both the

                 cases and learned Additional P.P. for the State along with learned

                 counsel for the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             The petitioners pray for anticipatory bail in a case under

                 <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1053937/" id="a_2">466</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a> , <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a>, <a href="/doc/756306/" id="a_6">474</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_7">120B</a> of the Indian

                 Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

                 Md.Wasiur Rahman @ Jamiur Rahman that the entire case has

                 been filed on false allegation and, as a matter of fact, the

                 Jamabandi exists in the name of the ancestor of the petitioner,

                 namely, Abdur Rahman. It is further submitted that out of 14

                 accused as many as 11, who are also similarly situated, have been

                 granted anticipatory bail by this Court by order dated 16.10.2015
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.46872 of 2015 (2) dt.10-11-2015                     2</span>
                      in Cr.Misc.No. 48587 of 2015. It is further submitted that another

                      accused person has been granted bail at the level of the Sessions

                      Judge and for this no details have been produced by the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   Learned counsel for the petitioner Bhubneshwar Mandal

                      further submits that the matter in question was examined at the

                      level of the department and in his report the DCLR, Sadar, Saharsa

                      has clearly stated that in the Jamabandi Register II no page has

                      been found to have been separately added, which is the main

                      allegation in the present matter that the Jamabandi Register II

                      having been manipulated by the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_5">                                   On a consideration of the facts and circumstances of the

                      case, it is directed that the petitioners, namely, (1) Md. Wasiur

                      Rahman @ Jamiur Rahman and (2) Bhubneshwar Mandal, in the

                      event of their arrest/surrender in the court below within a period of

                      four weeks from today shall be released on bail on furnishing bail

                      bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand) each with two sureties

                      of the like amount each in connection with Saharsa Sadar P.S.Case

                      No. 358 of 2015 to the satisfaction of Shri R. Chaubey, Judicial

                      Magistrate, 1st Class, Saharsa, subject to the conditions as laid

                      down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_8">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                (Ramesh Kumar Datta, J)
spal/-
</p>
