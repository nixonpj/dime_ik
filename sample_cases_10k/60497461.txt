<p id="p_6">For the Petitioner     : Mr. Akhauri Kamal Kishore Sahay, Advocate.
For the State          : Mr. Nirbhay Kumar Singh, A.P.P.

</p><p id="p_7">                                             -----------
</p>
<p id="p_8">  2.     16.10.2009.               Both    applications bearing Criminal Misc. No.35823 of

                         2009 and Criminal Misc. No.35659 of 2009, arise out of the same P.S.

                         Case and the bail application of both these petitioners have been

                         disposed of by the learned Sessions Judge, Jamui, by a common order

                         dated 8.9.2009. Thus, both of them have been heard together and are

                         being disposed of by this common order.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the learned

                         A.P.P. for the State.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                              -2-</span><p id="p_10">




                 The petitioners are accused in a case registered under

       <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a> and <a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_2">201</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_11">                 It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioners that there

       is no eye witness to the occurrence and at best, it would be stated that

       they had been last seen with the deceased. It has also been submitted

       that the co-accused, Laljit Rajak, with similar allegations, has already

       been granted bail by a Bench of this Court vide order dated 30.5.2006

       passed in Criminal Misc. No.16807 of 2006.           In support of the

       aforesaid submission, learned counsel for the petitioner has produced

       the copy of the aforesaid order. Let it be kept on record.

</p><p id="p_12">                 Having regard to the facts and the circumstances of the case,

       the petitioners, namely, Dukhan Rajak (in Criminal Misc. No.35823

       of 2009) and Kamdeo Yadav (in Criminal Misc. No.36659 of 2009),

       are directed to be released on bail on their furnishing bail bond of

       Rs.10,000/-(Rupees Ten Thousand) each with two sureties of the like

       amount each to the satisfaction of the C.J.M., Jamui, in connection

       with Sono (Charka Pathar) P.S. Case No.23/05.


</p><p id="p_13">                                                         (Dr. Ravi Ranjan,J)

P.S.
</p>
