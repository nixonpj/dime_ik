<div class="doc_title">Upendra Paswan vs The State Of Bihar on 11 August, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Cr.Misc. No.29158 of 2010
                        Upendra Paswan, S/o Late Lali Paswan, R/o Village-
                        Bhawa Dehri, P.S.-Nokha, District- Rohtas.
                                               Versus
                                     THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                             -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">2.      11.08.2010            Petitioner is languishing in jail in a case

                     registered under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the Indian Penal

                     Code and under <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_3">section 27</a> of the Arms Act.

</p><p id="p_2">                              There is a specific allegation of causing rifle

                     injury to the deceased against Dinbahora Paswan and

                     other similarly situated co-accused Ghurhu Kahar has

                     been granted bail vide Cr. Misc. No. 30116/2009.

</p><p id="p_3">                              Considering the aforesaid facts, let petitioner

                     above named, be released on bail on furnishing bail

                     bond of Rs. 10,000/- with two sureties of the like

                     amount each to the satisfaction of learned Additional

                     Sessions Judge-IV, Rohtas at Sasaram in connection

                     with S.T. No. 417 of 2007 arising out of Nokha

                     (Baghaila) P.S. Case No. 171 of 2004.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><pre id="pre_2">Sujit                                                ( Dinesh Kumar Singh, J.)
