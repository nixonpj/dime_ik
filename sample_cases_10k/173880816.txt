<div class="doc_title">Md. Niraley @ Md. Jamal Ashraf &amp; ... vs The State Of Bihar on 5 November, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.40993 of 2014 (2) dt.05-11-2014




                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                     Criminal Miscellaneous No.40993 of 2014
                         Arising Out of PS.Case No. -142 Year- 2014 Thana -BAJPATTI District- SITAMARHI
                   ======================================================
                   1. Md. Niraley @ Md. Jamal Ashraf &amp;
                   2. Md. Nehal.
                                                                      .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                   The State of Bihar
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Dinesh Jha
                   For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Ram Chandra Sahni(App)
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRABHAT KUMAR
                   JHA
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   05-11-2014                  Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioners Md. Niraley @ Md. Jamal Ashraf and

                   Md. Nehal seek bail in Bajpatti P.S.Case No.142 of 2014 under

                   <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>,<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>,<a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_3">354</a>,<a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_4">354(A)</a>,<a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_5">504</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_6">506</a> of the IPC.

</p><p id="p_3">                                Samina Khatoon, the informant made allegation against

                   the petitioners that they dragged her inside the house and tried to

                   outrage her modesty.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that the

                   petitioners are own brothers and the allegations are false and

                   concocted and the husband of the informant had taken loan and on

                   account of repayment of loan, a false case has been lodged. The

                   informant is aged about 50 years and the petitioners are aged about

                   25 and 28 years respectively. On the face of allegation appears to

                   be unbelievable as the informant alleged that petitioners wanted to
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.40993 of 2014 (2) dt.05-11-2014</span>




                       marry with the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                    Learned Additional P.P. however opposed the prayer for

                       bail.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                    It appears that there is, of course, allegation against the

                       petitioners that petitioners have assaulted the informant with fists

                       and slaps and tried to outrage her modesty but the petitioners are

                       in custody for about four months.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                    Having considered the aforesaid facts, let the petitioners,

                       namely, Md. Niraley @ Md. Jamal Ashraf and Md. Nehal be

                       directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of

                       Rs.10,000/-(ten thousand) each with two sureties of the like

                       amount each to the satisfaction of SDJM, Pupri at Sitamarhi in

                       connection with Bajpatti P.S.Case No.142 of 2014.



</p><p id="p_8">                                                                  (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J)
B.Kr./-
</p>
