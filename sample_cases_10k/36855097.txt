<p id="p_1">             Custody certificate filed by learned State counsel is taken on

record. Office to tag the same at the appropriate place.

</p><p id="p_2">             Despite the case having been called twice, the petitioner has

gone un-represented.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Perusal of the file shows that on the previous three dates also,

the petitioner had gone un-represented.          Therefore, it can safely be

presumed that the petitioner or his counsel is no more interested in pursuing

the instant revision.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             Resultantly, this revision is dismissed for want of prosecution.

             A copy of this order be sent to the Chief Judicial Magistrate,

Bhiwani, who shall issue warrants of arrest against the petitioner to undergo

remaining part of the sentence.
</p>
