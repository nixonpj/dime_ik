<div class="doc_title">O.L. G.S.F.Co. Ltd vs Shanti Lal on 9 September, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 In The High Court of Judicature for Rajasthan
Jaipur Bench, Jaipur
O R D E R
S.B. Company Application No.64/1996
In S.B. Company Petition No.7/1978
Official Liquidator Vs. Santi Lal &amp; Anr.

Date    Of    Order    ::   09/09/2010

Hon'ble Mr. Justice Ajay Rastogi

Mr. R.C. Mishra, Official Liquidator in person.
		It is company application u/R.9 of the Company (Court) Rules for making recovery from the respondents arises form the order dt.8.7.1994 passed in Company Petition-7/1978.   A total sum of Rs.5,660/- was to be recovered from the respondents which includes principal and interest. Notices were issued by this Court on 11.10.1996 which were sent to their known addresses but still the respondents could not have been served and the notices were received with the report that they are not residing at their addresses mentioned and thus the ultimate fact remains that despite best efforts made from the office of Official Liquidator respondents could not have been served. </pre>
<p id="p_1">		Taking note of long pendency of the company application and the amount which is to be recovered from the respondents, this Court is of the opinion that no purpose is going to be served to continue such proceedings after 14 years of filing of the application and in these circumstances, the application is devoid of merit and is accordingly dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">    (Ajay Rastogi),J.
