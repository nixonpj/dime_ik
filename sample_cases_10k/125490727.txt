<div class="doc_title">Suman Devi And Anr vs The State Of Bihar on 5 February, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                        Criminal Appeal (SJ) No.4836 of 2018
           Arising Out of PS. Case No.-69 Year-2018 Thana- SC/ST District- Purnia
   ======================================================
</pre><p id="p_1">1. Suman Devi and Anr Wife of Janardan Mandal
</p><p id="p_2">2. Moni Devi Wife of Sanjay Mandal Both Resident of Village - Kouali simra,
   P.S.- Tikapatti, District - Purnea

                                                                      ... ... Appellant/s
                                          Versus
   The State Of Bihar

                                             ... ... Respondent/s
   ======================================================
   Appearance :
</p><p id="p_3">   For the Appellant/s     :        Mr.Mukesh Kumar Jha
   For the Respondent/s    :        Mr.Sri Sadanand Paswan
   ======================================================
   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
   ORAL JUDGMENT
    Date : 05-02-2019

                 The appellants seek pre arrest bail in connection with

    SC/ST P.S. Case No. 69 of 2018, registered for offences

    punishable under <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_1">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">341</a>, <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_3">354</a> and <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a> of the Indian

    Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1507082/" id="a_5">Section 3</a> (i)(xi) of SC/<a href="/doc/53524/" id="a_6">ST Act</a>.

</p><p id="p_4">               Allegation against the appellants and others is of abusing

    the informant by caste name and also of thrashing her on the

    ground.
</p>
<p id="p_5">               It has been submitted on behalf of the appellants that no

    specific allegation has been attributed to the appellants, who are

    ladies and they have falsely been made accused in this case.

</p><p id="p_6">               Heard learned Special P.P. also.

               Having heard both sides, considering the facts and

    circumstances of the case, let the appellants, above named, in the
              Patna High Court CR. APP (SJ) No.4836 of 2018 dt.05-02-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                          2/2</span>




                      event of their arrest or surrender before the Court below, be

                      released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 25,000 (Rs.

                      Twenty Five Thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount

                      each to the satisfaction of learned Additional Sessions Judge -I -

                      cum-Special Judge Purnea, in connection with SC/ST P.S. Case

                      No. 69 of 2018, subject to the conditions laid down under Sectin

                      438 (2) of Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                              Accordingly, this appeal is allowed and impugned order is

                      set aside.
</p>
<p id="p_8">


</p><p id="p_9">                                               (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
sunilkumar/-
