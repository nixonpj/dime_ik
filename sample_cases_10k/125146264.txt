<p id="p_2">     Heard learned counsel for the appellant and also learned

Government Pleader for the respondents.

</p><p id="p_3">     Since enquiry is called for, the learned Single Judge

directed release on liberal terms of furnishing cash or Bank

Guarantee for 50% of the security amount, which is only tax

amount, and simple bond without surety for the balance

amount. We therefore do not find any ground for interference

with the judgment of the learned Single Judge.                             This Writ

Appeal is accordingly dismissed.
