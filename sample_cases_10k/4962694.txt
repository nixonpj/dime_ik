J U D G M E N T :
</pre>

<p id="p_1">1          This   order   shall   dispose   of   an   application   for   the   extension   of 

Succession Certificate granted vide judgment dated 25.10.2012 filed by the 

applicant Sh. Surender.
</p>
<p id="p_2">


</p><p id="p_3">                          Extension petition No. 175 of 2013
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                               :2:</span>




2            It  is stated that deceased namely Smt. Kamla Devi had also left 

behind   a saving bank account bearing No. 00024884 with Central Bank of 

India, Subzi Mandi, Delhi­7.  Hence,   it is prayed that succession certificate be 

accordingly extended.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

3            The   deceased   namely   Smt.   Kamla   Devi   was   the   mother   of   the 

petitioner Sh. Surender.  She expired on 30.03.2011 and her death certificate is 

Ex.  AW­1/1.     The  husband of  the deceased predeceased her and his death 

certificate is Ex. AW­1/2.   Besides the petitioner, the deceased has also left 

behind   three   sons   and   one   daughter   namely   Shyam,   Amit,   Sumit   and   Ms. 

Meenu,   who   are   LRs   in   this   petition   and   they   have   filed   their   reply/no 

objection Ex. AW­1/3 to the extension of Succession Certificate in favour of 

the petitioner.  The deceased made no WILL during his life time.  The copy of 

passbook of saving bank account bearing No. 00024884 with Central Bank of 

India, Subzi Mandi, Delhi­7 is Ex. AW­1/4.   The deceased was ordinarily a 

resident of Delhi.  
</p>
<p id="p_5">

4            Therefore, in view of the above, I am of the considered opinion that 

there   is   no   impediment   for   grant   of   extension   of   Succession   Certificate   in 

favour of Petitioner, Sh. Surender  qua the debts and securities of the deceased 

namely Smt. Kamla Devi.  I accordingly direct that a extension of Succession 

                            Extension petition No. 175 of 2013
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                          :3:</span>

Certificate be issued in favour of Petitioner, Sh. Surender   qua the debts and 

securities of the deceased namely Smt. Kamla Devi,  in terms of  Ex. AW­1/4, 

on filing of requisite court fee and on furnishing an Indemnity Bond with one 

surety within 15 days.   
</p>
<p id="p_6">

5           No further orders are required to be passed in the matter.  File be 

consigned to Record Room.   
</p>
<p id="p_7">
Announced in the open court                 (SANJEEV KUMAR SINGH )
on 28.10.2013                               Administrative Civil Judge (Central)

petitioner. 
</p>

</pre><p id="p_14">               Vide separate judgment announced in the open court today, there is 

no impediment for grant of extension of Succession Certificate in favour of 

Petitioner, Sh. Surender qua the debts and securities of the deceased namely 

Smt.   Kamla   Devi.     I   accordingly   direct   that   a   extension   of   Succession 

Certificate be issued in favour of Petitioner, Sh. Surender qua the debts and 

securities of the deceased namely Smt. Kamla Devi,  in terms of  Ex. AW­1/4, 

on filing of requisite court fee and on furnishing an Indemnity Bond with one
