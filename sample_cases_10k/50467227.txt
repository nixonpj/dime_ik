<pre id="pre_1">ORDER SHEET


                              GA No.1311 of 2010
                                      With
                               CS No.93 of 2009
                        IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                     Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                                 ORIGINAL SIDE


                                          In the matter of:
                                          Vindhya Projects Private Ltd.
                                          Versus
                                          Aloke Kumar Basu &amp; Ors.


Before:
The Hon'ble Justice
Patherya
Date. 26.04.2010

                                                                                      Appearance:
                                                                             Mr. Shakya Sen, Adv.,




     The   Court:       In    view   of    the    memorandum           of    understanding

executed between the parties hereto and the construction of the

building being complete so also the refusal of the owners taking

possession of the flats allotted in their favour but taking

possession      of    flats     mentioned        in        paragraph        25        which    the

respondents     are    not    entitled     to,    there         will   be        an    order    of</pre>

<p id="p_1">status quo in respect of the said flats in the possession of the

respondents till 5th May, 2010.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">     The   respondents        are    directed         to    maintain        status       quo    in

respect    of   the     flats   as    mentioned            in   paragraph         25     of    the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                      2</span>


petition, till 5th May, 2010.        Mr. Debajyoti Datta     Advocate 2nd

floor   Bar   Library   Club,   is   appointed   Receiver   to   ascertain

persons in possession of the said flats mentioned in paragraph

25 of the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
    Matter to appear in the list on 3rd May, 2010.


    Receiver and all parties concerned to act on a photostat

signed copy of this order on the usual undertakings.




</p><p id="p_5">                                             (Patherya, J.)

    SP/
