                        Judgment / Order
05/01/2018

     By way of this petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., the

petitioners have approached this Court seeking quashing of F.I.R.

No. 256/2017 registered at P.S. Ladnu for the offences under

<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">120B</a> IPC.

     The I.O. present in the Court has shown the case diary to

the Court and pointed out that the petitioners have been prima
                                 (2 of 2)
                                                        [CRLMP-4449/2017]

facie found responsible for fraudulently procuring the disputed

power of attorney from Prithivi Singh who was bed ridden because

of injuries received in an accident at the relevant point of time.

     In this background, learned counsel Mr. Solanki does not

press the instant misc. petition which is dismissed as being not

pressed. The stay application stands dismissed.




                                             (SANDEEP MEHTA), J.
