<div class="doc_title">Pawan Kumar Burnwal vs The State Of Bihar on 26 July, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.25876 of 2012 (2) dt.26-07-2012




                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.25876 of 2012
                   ======================================================
                   Pawan Kumar Burnwal
                                                                      .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                   The State Of Bihar
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                   SINGH
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   26-07-2012                   Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner is apprehending arrest in a complaint case

                   in which cognizance has been taken under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_3">379</a>

                   and <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a> of the Indian Penal Code and Section 3/4 of the

                   Prevention of Witch Practice Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 It is alleged that the accused persons abused the

                   informant and made assault with fists and slaps for playing the

                   witch craft on the son of the accused persons as a result of which

                   he remains ill most of the time.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that

                   no injury has been caused and the father of the petitioner lodged

                   Jamui P.S. Case No. 166 of 2011 against the husband and other

                   family members of the complainant making accusation under

                   <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_5">Section 384</a> of the IPC on 29.05.2011 then the present complaint

                   was filed on 28.07.2011.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                     Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above named
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.25876 of 2012 (2) dt.26-07-2012</span>




                     petitioner, be released on anticipatory bail, in the event of arrest or

                     surrender before the learned Court below within a period of 12

                     weeks from today, on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten

                     thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                     satisfaction of the learned Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class, Jamui in

                     connection with Complaint Case No. 955C of 2011, subject to the

                     conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_6">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                                (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Amrendra/-
</p>
