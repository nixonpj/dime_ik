Prayer:  
	Writ petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India, for issuance of a Writ of Certiorarified Mandamus, to call for the records relating to the order of the respondent dated 8.9.2005 in Lr.No.Affln.I(2)/10476/2005 and quash the same only in so far as it relates to grant of affiliation for 10 seats in B.Pharmacy Course from the academic year 2005-2006 instead of from the academic year 2004-2005 and direct the respondent to grant affiliation for increase of 10 seats in B.Pharmacy degree course in the petitioner's college from the academic year 2004-2005.
