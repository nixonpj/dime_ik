                      India praying to issue a Writ of Mandamus directing the first
                      respondent to allow the petitioner to join duty as Town Planning
                      Inspector or any other post equalant to his Master Degree in the
                      second respondent Municipality by considering the representation of
                      the petitioner dated 19.06.2017 within a time frame as fixed by this
                      Court.

                                                    ORDER
</pre>


<p id="p_1">                               The Writ Petition is filed seeking a direction to the first

                      respondent to allow the petitioner to join duty as Town Planning

                      Inspector or any other equalant post in the second respondent

                      Municipality, by considering the representation of the petitioner

                      dated 19.06.2017, within a time frame as fixed by this Court.



</p><p id="p_2">                               2. Heard the learned Counsel appearing for the petitioner,

                      the learned Special Government Pleader appearing for the first

                      respondent and the learned Counsel appearing for the second

                      respondent and perused the materials available on record.



</p><p id="p_3">                               3. The petitioner, seeking reemployment, had submitted a

                      representation on 19.06.2017 to the first respondent and the same

                      is said to be pending.   The learned Counsel for the petitioner has

                      also brought to the notice of this Court the proceedings of the

                      second respondent, dated 10.07.2017, wherein it is stated that the

                      petitioner's representation has been sent to the first respondent for

                      consideration.   Hence, he prays appropriate direction to the first

                      respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                           3</span>

</p><p id="p_5">                            4. The learned Special Government Pleader appearing for

                the    first    respondent         would       submit   that   the   petitioner's

                representation would be considered within a time frame as fixed by

                this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                            5. In such view of the matter, without going into the

                merits of the petitioner's case, the first respondent is directed to

                consider the representation of the petitioner, dated 19.06.2017 and

                pass appropriate orders on merits and in accordance with law and

                also in line with the proceedings of the second respondent dated

                10.07.2017, after affording due opportunity of hearing to the

                petitioner, within a period of eight weeks from the date of receipt of

                a copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                            6. The Writ Petition stands disposed of with the above

                direction. No costs.
</p>
