_____________________________________________________
                   HON'BLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE

     HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH CHANDRA SOMANI
                               Order
08/12/2017

</p><p id="p_2">1.         Overlooking the defects notified by the Registry we

have heard learned counsel for the appellants.

</p><p id="p_3">2.         The appellants keep on harping that in the cut off list

general category candidates were selected who had 38 marks and

OBC candidates were selected who also had 38 marks. The

grievance is that OBC candidates who obtained marks entitling
                                  (2 of 2)
                                                                [SAW-1744/2017]

them to be placed in the general category have to be placed in the

general category and if this is done more number of OBC

candidates would be empanelled. There is no factual basis for this

assertion for the reason the cut off percentage for general

category candidates was 51.67% and for OBC candidates was

45.33%. This has been noted by the learned Single Judge in

paragraph    3   of   the   impugned        order.   The   appellants,   OBC

candidates, had obtained marks between 31.33% and 16.33%

</p><p id="p_4">4.          The writ-appeal is accordingy dismissed in limine.




(DINESH CHANDRA SOMANI),J.                  (PRADEEP NANDRAJOG),C.J.

KKC/2
