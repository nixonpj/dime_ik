                HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH : BENCH AT INDORE

                             Writ Appeal No.149 of 2018

              Indore, Dated:- 27/04/2018

                    Ms. Priyanka Shroff, Advocate for the appellant.
                    Shri Yogesh Mittal, Advocate for the respondent No.1.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                    Shri Rohit Kumar Mangal, Government Advocate for the
              respondent Nos.2, 3 and 4.
</p><p id="p_2">                    Shri Soumitra Bajaj, Advocate for the respondent No.5.
                    Heard.
</p><p id="p_3">                    This writ appeal has been filed against the order dated
              31.1.2018 by which learned Writ Court directed the Competent
              Authority under the M.P. Lok Parisar (Bedakhali) Adhiniyam,
              1974 to conclude the proceedings pending before him, as
              expeditiously as possible.
</p><p id="p_4">                    Shri Yogesh Mittal, Counsel for the respondent No.1 has
              submitted that in pursuance to the direction made by the
              learned Writ Court on 31.1.2018, the proceedings has been
              concluded and Competent Authority has passed the order on
              25.2.2018 and appeal has been filed by the present appellant
              against the order dated 25.2.2018.
</p><p id="p_5">                    In view of the aforesaid, this writ appeal is rendered
              infructuous.
</p><p id="p_6">                    Accordingly,   it   is   dismissed   as   having   rendered
              infructuous.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                   (P. K. Jaiswal)                        (S. K. Awasthi)
                      Judge                                    Judge
