<p id="p_1">Item No.648
  (rejected)
     SB                           C.R.M. 3872 of 2017

               In Re:- An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of
               the Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 04.05.2017

               In the matter of : Ranjit Oraon
                                                     .......petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_2">               Mr. Tapas Kumar Sinha,
               Ms. Rupan Biswas                   ...for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">               Mr. Swapan Banerjee,
               Mr. Suman De
                                                 ...for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                    Apprehending arrest in course of investigation of Kumargram
               P.S. F.I.R. No. 37 of 2017 dated 21.02.2017 under <a href="/doc/1947545/" id="a_2">Sections
               448</a>/<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>/<a href="/doc/1540253/" id="a_4">326</a>/<a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">307</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code, the petitioner has
               applied for anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_5">                    Having heard learned advocates for the parties and perusing
               the materials in the case diary, we are of the view that complicity
               of the petitioner cannot be ruled out and that his custodial
               interrogation is necessary for effective and meaningful progress of
               investigation of the F.I.R.
</p><p id="p_6">                    The application for anticipatory bail is, thus, rejected.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                                          (Dipankar Datta, J.)



                                                             (Debi Prosad Dey, J.)
