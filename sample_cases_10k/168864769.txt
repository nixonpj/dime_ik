                      CHANDIGARH


                            Criminal Misc. No. M-23076 of 2011
                            Date of decision : 18.08.2011
Lachman Singh
                                                            ....Petitioner

                                  V/s

State of Punjab and others
                                                            ....Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">BEFORE : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJAN GUPTA

Present:     Mr. A.S. Dhaliwal, Advocate for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">             Mr. Rajesh Bhardwaj, Addl. Advocate General, Punjab.

RAJAN GUPTA J. (ORAL)

             In this petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., the petitioner

has raised a grievance that FIR was registered on 17.05.2011 but there has

been no progress in the investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             At the outset, learned State counsel, on instructions from S.I.

Des Raj, who is present in court, submits that investigation of the case is

already in progress and two of the accused were arrested by the police

during investigation. He submits that investigation is likely to be completed

shortly whereafter final report shall be submitted before the competent court

of jurisdiction.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             In view of statement made by learned State counsel, no further

orders are required to be passed at this stage by this court.

</p><p id="p_5">             Disposed of.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">August 18, 2011                                             (RAJAN GUPTA)
Ajay                                                             JUDGE
