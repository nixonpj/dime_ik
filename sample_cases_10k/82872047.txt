<div class="doc_title">Mr.Rs Saluja vs By Way Of The Instant Writ Petition on 12 May, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                              1

   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                       JODHPUR

                           ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">           S.B.CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.4866/2015
        Richhpal Bishnoi Vs. State of Rajasthan &amp; Ors.

</p><p id="p_2">         Date of order              :       12.5.2015

            HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SANDEEP MEHTA

Mr.RS Saluja, for the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_3">                            &lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;


     By way of the instant writ petition, the petitioner has

approached this Court assailing the action of the respondents

in ignoring the petitioner's case for promotion to the post of

Range Forest Officer Gr.II and in according promotions to the

persons junior to the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

petitioner be permitted to place a copy of the instant writ

petition before the appropriate authority by way of a

representation and considering the writ petition to be a

representation, the appropriate authority be directed to

consider the petitioner's case in a time bound manner and to

pass a reasoned speaking order thereupon so that the

grievance of the petitioner can be ventilated.

</p><p id="p_5">     The prayer thus made is justified.
</p>
<p id="p_6">     Accordingly, the writ petition is disposed of requiring the

petitioner to submit a copy of the writ petition and all the

documents to the appropriate authority alongwith copy of this
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                       2</span>

      order. Upon the copy of the writ petition being placed before

      it, the appropriate authority shall objectively deal with the

      petitioner's   representation   in   reference   to   the   relevant

      rules/instructions and deal with and decide the petitioner's

      objections by a detailed reasoned order within a period of six

      weeks. In the event of the petitioner's grievances still surviving

      after disposal of his representation, he shall be at liberty to

      avail appropriate legal remedy. Stay petition is also disposed

      of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">            No order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                                  (SANDEEP MEHTA), J.
</p>
<p id="p_9">/tarun/
