<p id="p_1">                                                 ***************

23.06.2014                         Heard learned counsel for the parties.

</p><p id="p_2">                                   Petitioner has filed this civil revision against

                     the order, dated 27.09.2012, passed in Miscellaneous

                     Case No. 13 of 2011, whereby the court of Sub-Judge-

                     III, Gopalganj has rejected the application of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court C.R. No.29 of 2013 (14) dt.23-06-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                           3/4</span>




                     petitioner filed under Order 21, Rule 97 of the Code of

                     Civil Procedure to exclude the Plot No. 450, Khata No. 8

                     from final decree of Title Suit No. 193 of 1997.

</p><p id="p_3">                                   Learned counsel appearing for the petitioner

                     submits that while the application was filed to exclude

                     the Plot No. 450 from the execution of the final decree,

                     passed in Title Suit No. 193 of 1997, but the learned

                     Sub-Judge-III, has gave his finding in the impugned

                     order regarding Plot No. 250, which was not in dispute.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   Learned counsel appearing on behalf of

                     opposite parties submits that there is no illegality in the

                     impugned order.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   On perusal of the impugned order, it appears

                     that while the petitioner has given application to exclude

                     Plot No. 450 in the execution of final decree of Title Suit

                     No. 193 of 1997, but the discussion has been made by

                     the court regarding Plot No. 250.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   In    view      of    the   aforesaid   facts   and

                     circumstances, the impugned order, dated 27.09.2012,

                     passed in Miscellaneous Case No. 13 of 2011 is set

                     aside. This civil revision is allowed with a direction to the

                     trial court to pass a fresh order in Miscellaneous Case

                     No. 13 of 2011 in accordance with law without being

                     prejudiced by the Order of this Court.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">     Patna High Court C.R. No.29 of 2013 (14) dt.23-06-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                               4/4</span>




</p><p id="p_7">                                       Taking into consideration the averments made

                         in the Interlocutory Application No. 2011 of 2013 to

                         condone the delay in filing the Civil Revision, the delay is

                         condoned and accordingly, the interlocutory application

                         No. 2088 of 2013 is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                                      (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J.)
