  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 22832 of 2008(R)


1. P.D.MATHEW, VEHICLE SUPERVISOR
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. KSRTC, REP. BY ITS MANAGING DIRECTOR
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.K.P.RAJEEVAN

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice K.M.JOSEPH

 Dated :29/07/2008

 O R D E R
                             K.M. JOSEPH, J.

               ````````````````````````````````````````````````````
                    W.P.(C) No. 22832 OF 2008 R
               ````````````````````````````````````````````````````
                 Dated this the 29th day of July, 2008

                             J U D G M E N T
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            Petitioner retired as Vehicle Supervisor from the

respondent Corporation on 31.5.2007. The petitioner seeks out of

turn payment of his pensionary benefits to meet the expenses of

his daughter's marriage, which is fixed to be solemnised on

25.8.2008. I heard the learned Standing Counsel also. The writ

petition is disposed of as follows:
</p>
<p id="p_2">            There will be a direction to the respondent to conduct

an enquiry as to whether the claim of the petitioner is genuine.

The enquiry should be concluded within two weeks from the date

of receipt of a copy of this judgment. If the claim of the petitioner

is found genuine, there will be a further direction to the respondent

to disburse the amount due to the petitioner as DCRG, in

accordance with law, within a period of three months from the date

on which the enquiry is concluded.               It is made clear that the

payment will be made only if there is no liability which the

petitioner has to discharge in accordance with law.


</p><p id="p_3">                                                                      Sd/-
</p><p id="p_4">                                                    (K.M.JOSEPH, JUDGE)
