                   Arising Out of PS. Case No.-55 Year-2018 Thana- GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL COMP.
                                                   District- Lakhisarai
               ======================================================
            1. Sajan Kumar Mahto (Chalk), Son of Ram Narayan Mahto.
            2. Vinod Kumar Mahto @ Binod Kumar Mahto, son of Jageshwar
               Mahto, Both Resident of Village- Dhankoul, P.S.- Patepur,
               District- Vaishali.

                                                                            ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :    Mr. Ranjan Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :    Mr. Sri Sunil Kumar Pandey
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE S. KUMAR
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   26-10-2018                   Heard learned counsel for petitioners and learned

                  counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  Petitioners, who are in custody, seek bail in

                  connection with Excise Case No. 55C-2 of 2018 registered for

                  the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1320984/" id="a_1">Sections 30(a)</a>, <a href="/doc/1498946/" id="a_2">32(1)</a>, <a href="/doc/1138600/" id="a_3">38(1)</a> and

                  <a href="/doc/170198/" id="a_4">41(2)</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                   Allegation against petitioners are of recovery of

                  1260 liters of foreign wine from the pickup van in which

                  petitioners were sitting.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                  It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioners

                  that they are innocent and have committed no offence. They

                  have been falsely implicated in this case due to enmity. They are
            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.63342 of 2018(2) dt.26-10-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                    not present at the place of occurrence. They have no concern

                    with the said vehicle and recovery. They have no criminal

                    antecedent and they are in custody since 30.07.2018.

</p><p id="p_5">                                     Considering the aforesaid fact and circumstances of

                    the case, let the petitioners named above be released on bail

                    upon furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) each

                    with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of

                    learned Additional District and Sessions Judge 2nd-cum-Special

                    Judge (Excise), Lakhisarai in connection with Excise Case No.

                    55C-2 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                                     (S. Kumar, J)
Rajiv/Manoj-
</p>
