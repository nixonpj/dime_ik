<div class="doc_title">Deepa Lohia vs Sales Tax Officer on 5 May, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                      1



20   05.5.2016

                                   W.P. 8043 (W) of 2016
     gd
                                          Deepa Lohia
                                              Vs.
                        Sales Tax Officer, Bally Charge &amp; Ors.

                        Mr. Piyal Gupta
                                   ..for the Petitioner

                        Mr. Prithu Dudhoria
                        Mr. S. Mukherjee
                                  ..for the Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">                        The petitioner complains of "C"

                 forms being declined to the petitioner

                 in the manual form on the extraneous

                 advice of a superior officer of the sales

                 tax officer who passed the order.

</p><p id="p_2">                        There is no doubt that the order

                 impugned dated August 31, 2015 is

                 liable to be set aside only on such

                 ground.       A person who has been

                 conferred     the    authority      under    the

                 statute or statutory rules to take a

                 decision cannot be influenced by a

                 superior. However, the practical effect

                 of setting aside this order would be

                 that   an    identical     order     would   be
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                     2</span>


passed without the sales tax officer

foolishly recording the advice from the

superior officer.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        The State has handed over a

proposal      prepared   by   the   relevant

superior officer for the denial of way-

bills and declaration forms to certain

dealers.     The petitioner is now aware

why the superior officer advised the

sales tax officer to not issue the

manual "C" forms to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">        Leave is given to the petitioner to

file   a     supplementary    affidavit   to

question the propriety of the proposal.

Such supplementary affidavit be filed

by     May     9,   2016.      Affidavit-in-

opposition to both the petition and the

supplementary affidavit be filed within

a fortnight thereof; reply thereto, if

any, may be filed before the matter is

taken up. The petition will appear for

hearing in the monthly list of June,

2016.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                     3</span><p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">       The pendency of this petition will

not stand in the way of the petitioner

obtaining       manual   "C"   forms   upon

furnishing such security as may be

reasonably demanded of the petitioner.



</p><p id="p_7">                                  (Sanjib

Banerjee, J.)
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3"> 4</span>
