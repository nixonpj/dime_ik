<p id="p_1">  5.     25.11.2008.                Perused the report of the Joint Registrar, Monitoring Cell, Fast

                       Track Court, attached to this Court dated 21.11.2008; also perused the report

                       of the trial court (5th Addl. Sessions Judge, Muzaffarpur) dated 19.11.2008

                       and the report of the Directorate of Revenue Intelligence, Sub-Regional

                       Unit, Muzaffarpur dated 20.11.2008, wherefrom it appears that prosecution

                       evidence is likely to be concluded after the examination of Prosecution

                       Witness Sri R. Ranjan, Ex-I.O., D.R.I., Muzaffarpur and two independent

                       witnesses.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                    Considering the custody of the petitioner for about 4 Â½ years as

                       under-trial prisoner, I direct the trial court and the Intelligence Officer,

                       D.R.I., Sub-Regional Unit, Muzaffarpur to ensure examination of the

                       aforesaid three witnesses in connection with (D.R.I. Case No. 10 of 2004,

                       giving rise to Tr.No. 29 of 2006) at the earliest, in any case within three

                       months from the date of receipt/production of a copy of this order and

                       conclude the trial within four months and submit a report to this Court.

</p><p id="p_3">                                    This application is, accordingly, disposed of.

                                    Let this order be communicated to the court below through fax by

                       the office of this Court.
