                             CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.66320 of 2019
                      Arising Out of PS. Case No.-336 Year-2019 Thana- KHAJEKALA District- Patna
                 ======================================================
                 SURENDRA CHAUDHARY, aged about 65 years, Gender-Male, Son of Late
                 Gauri Chaudhary, Resident of Village - Gurhatta, nearest Hamam Imambaara,
                 Milan Ghat lane, P.S.- Station - Khajekalan, Distt.- Patna.

                                                                                    ... ... Petitioner
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                          ... ... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner     :         Mr.Jay Ram Prasad, Advocate.
                 For the Opposite Party :         Mrs. Dr. Indiwar Kumari, A.P.P.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SUDHIR SINGH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   21-10-2019                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               The petitioner seeks bail in a case for the offence

                   registered under Sections 37(b)(c),30(a), 38 and 41 of the Bihar

                   Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise (Amendment) Act</a>, 2018.

</p><p id="p_3">                               The prosecution story, in brief, is that total 11.720

                   liters wine and 30 liters toddy is said to have been recovered.

</p><p id="p_4">                               It has been submitted by learned counsel for the

                   petitioner that the petitioner is languishing in custody since

                   07.08.2019. The petitioner has got no criminal antecedent. The

                   petitioner has falsely been implicated in the present case.

                   Charge sheet/prosecution report has been submitted in the

                   present case. There is no allegation of tampering with the
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.66320 of 2019(2) dt.21-10-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     witnesses alleged against the petitioner. It is alleged that total

                     11.720 liters wine and 30 liters toddy is recovered. There is no

                     compliance of Section 100 Cr. P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   On behalf of the State, it is submitted that the

                     petitioner is named in the F.I.R/ Complaint Case.

</p><p id="p_6">                                  Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, let

                     the petitioner above named, be released on bail on furnishing

                     bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Ten Thousand) with two sureties of

                     the like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Special

                     Judge, Excise, Patna, in connection with Special Case No. 7043

                     of 2019 (arising out of Khajekalan P.S. Case No. 336 of 2019).



</p><p id="p_7">                                                  (Sudhir Singh, J)
U.K./-
</p><pre id="pre_2">U      T
