<div class="doc_title">Ram Baran Yadav vs Registrar General Allahabad High ... on 9 August, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">Court No. - 39

Case :- WRIT - A No. - 46870 of 2010

Petitioner :- Ram Baran Yadav
Respondent :- Registrar General Allahabad High Court
Petitioner Counsel :- V. K. Singh,G. K. Singh
Respondent Counsel :- Amit Sthalekar

Hon'ble Dilip Gupta,J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">The relief claimed in the present petition is for considering the
representation filed by the petitioner for sending him back to his
original post at Azamgarh.
</p>
<p id="p_2">The records of the writ petition indicate that the petitioner was
attached with the High Court with effect from 20th April, 2009.
His prayer is for sending him back to Azamgarh since his wife has
fallen ill from January 2010 and is undergoing treatment at All
India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi.
</p>
<p id="p_3">If the wife of the petitioner is undergoing treatment at New Delhi,
it is better for the petitioner to stay at Allahabad than at Azamgarh.
The representation filed by the petitioner, particularly when he has
been on deputation for about one year only should not be directed
to be considered.
</p>
<p id="p_4">The writ petition is, accordingly, dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">Order Date :- 9.8.2010
SK
