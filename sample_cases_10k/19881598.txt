            CRIMINAL MISC. APPLICATION (REGULAR BAIL) NO.8935 of
                                   2016

         =========================================
                               RAMESH CHAITANYA GAUD....Applicant
                                            Versus
                                 STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent
         =========================================
         Appearance :
         MR MATAFER R PANDE, ADVOCATE for the Applicant.

         =========================================

                    CORAM : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI

                                        Date : 10/06/2016
                                         ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">                        The learned 12th Additional Sessions Judge, Surat who
         is in charge of Sessions Case No.232 of 2013 is hereby directed to
         decide application Exh.104 filed by the present applicant under
         Section 317 (2) of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 as
         expeditiously as possible, preferably within a period of two weeks
         from today.
</p>

<p id="p_2">                        The      learned 12th Additional Sessions Judge, Surat is
         hereby further directed to complete the trial of Sessions Case
         No.232 of 2013 within a period of six months from today as prayed
         for by him by his communication dated 9.6.2016.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                        With the above directions, the present application
         stands disposed of. Rule is discharged. Office is directed to send
         writ of this order to the concerned Sessions Judge forthwith.
</p>


<p id="p_4">                                                                              (A.J.DESAI, J.)
