<p id="p_1">                 (Per: HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE)


6   22-08-2014                  This Review Petition has been preferred by the
                 respondent-writ petitioner against the order dated 8th October
                 2013 made by us in Letters Patent Appeal No.1433 of 2011.
</p><p id="p_2">                                Learned counsel Mr. Jitendra Singh has appeared for
                 the review petitioner. He has submitted that the order under
                 review suffers from the apparent error on the face of record. He
                 has submitted that the petitioner had purchased land ad
                 measuring 17 acres 4 decimals. He has further pointed out that
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court C. REV. No.447 of 2013 (6) dt.22-08-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                       the order was made on the premise that the possession of the
                       land was taken over from the petitioner by the State
                       Government. He has submitted that till date the review petitioner
                       is in peaceful possession of the disputed piece of land. The
                       statement has been controverted by one Heman Nath Deva, the
                       Additional District Magistrate (D.E.), Gopalganj. He has
                       produced communication dated 21st November 2013 sent by the
                       Circle Officer, Hathua to the effect that the possession of the
                       land in dispute was taken over in February 2010. Obviously, this
                       is not a contemporary evidence of taking over the possession.
</p><p id="p_3">                                       Learned Additional Advocate General Mr. D. K.
                       Sinha has appeared for the respondent-State of Bihar. He is
                       directed to produce contemporary evidence of taking over the
                       possession of the disputed piece of land in February 2010 as
                       communicated in the letter under consideration.
</p><p id="p_4">                                       Stand over to 5th September 2014.
</p>



<p id="p_5">                                                      (R.M. Doshit, CJ)


                                                (Ashwani Kumar Singh, J)
Sunil/-
