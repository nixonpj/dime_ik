                        M.A.No.767/2015

7.7.2015

      Mr. S.V.Dandwate, counsel for the appellant.

      Mr. Manish Jain, counsel for respondent Nos.1 to 3.</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Heard on I.A.No.4729/15 which is an application filed

by the claimants seeking modification/vacation of the interim

order dated 21.4.2015 passed by this court.

</p><p id="p_2">      Learned     counsel    for       respondents   submits   that

respondents be permitted to withdraw the amount which has

been deposited by the appellant/insurance company in terms

of interim order of this court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      On due consideration, the prayer is allowed and

respondents-claimants are permitted to withdraw the amount

as per the condition of the award.

</p><p id="p_4">      I.A.No.4729/15 is accordingly disposed of.

      C.c. as per rules.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                          (Prakash Shrivastava)
