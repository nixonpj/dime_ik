<p id="p_1">                          The matter is listed under the caption “for dismissal” today i.e. on

                 18.12.2019. There is no representation on the side of the petitioner. Even

                 on the last hearing i.e., on 09.12.2019, there was no representation on

                 the side of the petitioner. It can now be inferred that the petitioner is not

                 interested in prosecuting this writ petition. Accordingly, this writ petition

                 is dismissed for non-prosecution. No costs. Consequently, connected

                 miscellaneous petition is closed.
