<div class="doc_title">Sarvajeet Paswan vs State Of Bihar on 7 July, 2008</div><p id="p_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                           Cr.Misc. No.7616 of 2008
                               SARVAJEET PASWAN
                                    Versus

                                   STATE OF BIHAR
</p><p id="p_2">                                       - - - -
</p><p id="p_3">4.   7.7.2008             None appears for the petitioner. Heard

                learned     Additional   Public    Prosecution   and   I

                have the privilege of being taken through the

                case diary.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                          The petitioner is alleged to have set

                fire   to   the   deceased   who   in   order   to   save

                herself jumped into the well. The support of

                the allegation is there from the case diary as

                also from the post-mortem examination report

                which is copied in       Paragraph 49 of the case

                diary, considering which I am not inclined to

                direct release of the petitioner on bail.

</p><p id="p_5">                          The petition is dismissed.

      Kanth

                                             ( Dharnidhar Jha, J.)
