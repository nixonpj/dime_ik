<p id="p_1">                                            BEFORE
                          HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE HITESH KUMAR SARMA


19-12-2018

       Heard Mr. S. Munir, learned counsel for the petitioners. Also heard Mr. NJ Dutta,
learned Additional Public Prosecutor, Assam for the State of Assam and Mr. Sailendra Sarma,
                                                                                   Page No.# 2/2

learned counsel for the informant (inview of his engagement by the informant, as indicated in
I.A. (Crl.) No.667 of 2018).
</p>
<p id="p_2">       This is an application, filed under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the Cr.PC. seeking pre-arrest bail of
the accused-petitioners, namely, 1) Sri Debashish Ghose, and 2) Sri Sanjib Bhatta, in
connection   with   Panbazar PS Case No. 299/2018                 registered   under   <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_2">Sections
406</a>/<a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_3">420</a>/<a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       Learned counsel for the petitioners has submitted that the matter has been amicably
settled between the parties and they have not misused the liberty granted while availing
interim pre-arrest bail granted vide order, dated 9.8.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       Mr. Sailendra Sarma, learned counsel for the informant has also acceded to such
submission made by the learned counsel for the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       That being so, in the considered view of this Court, the interim pre-arrest bail granted
to the accused petitioners, vide order dated 9.8.2018, deserves to be made absolute.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       The said interim pre-arrest bail granted vide order, dated 9.8.2018, is hereby made
absolute on the same terms and conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_7">       The bail application stands disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_8">       Return the case diary.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                                                              JUDGE




Comparing Assistant
