                                        Miscellaneous Appeal No.354 of 2006

                        Divisional Manager, Oriental Insurance Company Ltd.
                                                                             .... .... Appellant/s
                                                          Versus

<p id="p_1">6     24-09-2013                 Having perused the office note and the report of the District

                         Judge, Jammu placed at Flag-'Q' and considering the objection

                         raised by the appellant-Insurance Company, this Court would

                         deem it necessary that a registered notice be also issued to

                         respondent No.3.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 In the circumstances as prayed, four weeks time is allowed

                         for filing requisites etc. for service of notice on respondent No.3

                         under registered cover with A/D, failing which this appeal as

                         against the concerned respondent shall stand rejected without

                         further reference to the Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">                                                         (Jyoti Saran, J)
