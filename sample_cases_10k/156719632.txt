<div class="doc_title">Rajesh Kumar Sarna vs District Judge Dehradun &amp; Others on 12 January, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">WPMS No.61 of 2015
Hon'ble Sudhanshu Dhulia, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">      Mr. Siddhartha Sah, Advocate with Mr.
Ramji Srivastava, Advocate present for the
petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">      Mr. Vikas Pandey, Brief Holder has
accepted notices on behalf of respondent
Nos.2 &amp; 3.
</p><p id="p_3">      The petitioner has challenged a notice
dated 27.12.2014 given to him by the
respondent No.4 who is a member of the
Committee. The Committee itself has been
formed by the orders of this Court in Second
Appeal No.29 of 2003.
</p><p id="p_4">      In case, petitioner has any grievance
such as the Committee has no jurisdiction or
authority to issue such a notice to him, he
should first go before the Committee and
apprise the Committee about it, which shall
then look into it.
</p><p id="p_5">      Without exhausting this remedy, the
petitioner has straight away come before this
Court against the notice, which is premature.
</p><p id="p_6">      In view thereof, the writ petition is
dismissed in limine.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                       (Sudhanshu Dhulia, J.)
                                     12.01.2015
JKJ
