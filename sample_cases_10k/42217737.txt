<div class="doc_title">Mahavir Singh And Others vs Uttar Haryana Bijli Vitran Nigam ... on 31 January, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                        AT CHANDIGARH.


(261)                            CWP-10904-2018
                                 Date of Decision: January 31, 2019


Mahavir Singh and others                                     .. Petitioner

                                 Versus

Uttar Haryana Bijli Vitran Nigam Ltd. and others             .. Respondents</pre>


<p id="p_1">CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE HARSIMRAN SINGH SETHI

Present:     Mr. Jagbir Malik, Advocate, for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">             Ms. Upasana Dhawan, Advocate, for the respondents.

HARSIMRAN SINGH SETHI, J.(ORAL)

             Present writ petition has been filed by the petitioners claiming

the re-fixation of their pay in view of the orders of this Court passed in

CWP No.5563-A of 1989 and CWP No.16084 of 1997 which were upheld

by the Hon'ble Supreme Court, vide order dated 08.12.2015 (Annexure P-5).

</p><p id="p_3">             Petitioners claim that they are similarly situated with those who

have been granted the relief by this Court but the same has not been granted

to them only on the ground that they have not approached this Court and

were not the petitioners in above mentioned writ petitions.

</p><p id="p_4">             Counsel for the petitioners states that the petitioners will be

satisfied, at this stage, in case, a time bound direction is given to the

respondents to decide the legal notice dated 22.08.2017 (Annexure P-7).

</p><p id="p_5">             Learned counsel for the respondents states that he has no

objection in respect of the prayer made by counsel for the petitioner for

deciding the legal notice in a time bound manner.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                      1 of 2
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                   ::: Downloaded on - 18-02-2019 04:39:27 :::</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                    CWP-10904-2018                                -2-</span>

             Keeping in view the request made and without expressing any

opinion on the merits of the case and entitlement of the petitioners in respect

of the claim made in the writ petition/legal notice, the respondents are

directed to decide the legal notice dated 22.08.2017 (Annexure P-7) within a

period of three months from the date of receipt of a certified copy of this

order. In case it is found that the petitioners are entitled for any relief, the

same shall also be released to them within a period of next three months.

</p><p id="p_8">             Present writ petition stands disposed of .</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                               (HARSIMRAN SINGH SETHI)
