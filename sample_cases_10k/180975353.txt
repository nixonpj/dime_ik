                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.4092 of 2015
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -137 Year- 2012 Thana -COMPLAINT CASE District- SUPAUL
                 ======================================================
                 1. Ram Krishna Poddar @ Munna Son of Ram Swarup Poddar, Resident of
                 Hakpara Saharsa Ward No. 21 Police Station - Saharsa, District - Saharsa.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar.
                 2. Binita Devi Wife of Ram Krishna Poddar @ Munna, Daughter of Dev
                 Narayan Poddar, Residing at Simrahi Tola, Gandhi Nagar, Poice Station -
                 Raghopur, District - Supaul.

                                                                  .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Satish Kumar Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Gajendra Pd.Yadav(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   30-01-2015                Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                              The petitioner being husband of the complainant is

                 apprehending arrest in a complaint case in which cognizance has

                 been taken under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">sections 498A</a> and <a href="/doc/508426/" id="a_2">494</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                              The basic accusation is of torture for non fulfillment of

                  dowry demands and performing second marriage.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Issue notice to O.P. No. 2 under ordinary process as

                 well as by registered cover with A/d for which requisites etc. must

                 be filed within two weeks failing which this application as against

                 O.P. No. 2 shall stand rejected without further reference the bench.

</p><p id="p_5">                                Let the ordinary process of notice be served on O.P.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4092 of 2015 (2) dt.30-01-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                      No. 2 through her lawyer appearing before the learned court below

                      through the office of the learned court below.

</p><p id="p_6">                                     Let petitioner and O.P. No. 2 appear before this court

                      in person on 2.4. 2015 at 2.10 P.M. in Chambers.

</p><p id="p_7">                                     In the meantime, let no coercive steps be taken against

                      the petitioner in connection with Complaint case no.137 of 2012

                      pending in the court of the learned SDJM, Birpur, Supaul.



</p><p id="p_8">                                                 (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Anil/-
</p>
