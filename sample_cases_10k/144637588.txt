<p id="p_1">                                        DATED : OCTOBER 26, 2018

P.C. :
</p><p id="p_2">1        Heard.     The learned Advocate General for the State of
Maharashtra submits that the State Government has already
issued Government Resolution dated 27.09.2018 reducing the
price of milk from Rs.27/- per ltr. to Rs.25/- per ltr.                To that
effect he has produced a copy of the said Government Resolution.
</p>

<p id="p_3">2        In view of the said Government Resolution, the learned
counsel for the Petitioners seek some time to take instructions
from their respective clients.
</p>

<p id="p_4">3        Hence, by consent of the parties, Office is directed to place
the matter on board on 26.11.2018, for directions.
</p>

<p id="p_5">4        Interim relief, if any, granted earlier and which is in force
till today, to continue till next date.
</p>
