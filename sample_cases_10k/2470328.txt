</p><p id="p_14">               Mr. Ray, learned advocate for the applicants in CPAN 380 of 2016 and Mr.
     Chakravarty, learned advocate for the applicants in CPAN 841 of 2016 have
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                   2</span>


      complained that despite forwarding of the names of physically handicapped
      candidates to the Commissioner of School Education, West Bengal (hereafter the
      commissioner) by the Chairperson, South 24-Parganas District Primary School
      Council as far back as in February, 2015, no further action has been initiated at
      the end of the commissioner to approve the names so furnished and to take
      follow up steps for issuance of letters of appointment in their favour.
</p>
<p id="p_15">            Mr. Tapan Kumar Mukherjee, learned senior advocate representing the
      commissioner in CPAN 841 of 2016 and Mr. Siddique, learned advocate
      representing the commissioner in CPAN 380 of 2016, have prayed for some time
      to return with complete instructions.
</p>
<p id="p_16">ACO         Put up the contempt applications on 28th August, 2017 under the heading
      "For Orders" at the top of the list.
</p>
<p id="p_17">            It would be desirable if on returnable date the approval order of the
      commissioner is placed before us.
</p>
