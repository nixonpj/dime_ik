             for   the   applicant,   under   the   instructions,   submitted   that   the

             and that he would accompany  the investigator  to a place where 
             from allegedly the cheque in question was collected by him and will 
             also identify the places of residence and work of Nitin Marathe. 
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">             In   view   of   the   above   statement   of   the   learned   advocate   for   the 
             applicant, it is directed that the custody  of the applicant shall be 
             given to the investigator for two days for the said purpose. Stand 
             over to 07.09.2017."
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                      It appears that pursuant to the said order, an application was
