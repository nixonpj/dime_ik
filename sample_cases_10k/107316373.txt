                                    Criminal Miscellaneous No.2494 of 2018
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -57 Year- 2017 Thana -NARKATIYAGANJ RAIL P.S. District-
                                                 WESTCHAMPARAN(BETTIAH)
                  ======================================================
                  1. Rahul Sarraf @ Rahul Kumar, Son of Sri Ashok Sarraf, resident of
                  Mohalla- Kachahari Road, Ward No. 16, Police Station- Shikarpur, District-
                  West Champaran at Bettiah.

                                                                                  .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                         Versus
                  1. The State of Bihar.

                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                  ======================================================
                  Appearance :
                  For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr. Raghunandan Kumar Singh, Adv.
                  For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Kalyan Shankar, A.P.P.
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRAKASH CHANDRA
                  JAISWAL
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">02   23-01-2018                     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                  APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                    The petitioner seeks bail in Narkatiaganj Rail P.S.

                  Case No.57 of 2017 registered under Section 30 (a) of the Bihar

                  Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                    As per the prosecution case, 43.200 liters of foreign

                  liquor is said to have been recovered from the two bags kept on

                  two motorcycles recovered from the place of occurrence. Three

                  persons managed to escape from the place of occurrence.

</p><p id="p_4">                                    It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner

                  that no incriminating article has been recovered from the

                  conscious physical possession of the petitioner. Though one of the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.2494 of 2018 (02) dt.23-01-2018</span>



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      -2-</span>




                         aforesaid motorcycles is hailing to the petitioner but the petitioner

                         was not driving the same at the time of occurrence rather his

                         cousin had taken the same from him. He has been falsely

                         implicated in this case. He has no criminal antecedent. The

                         petitioner has been languishing in custody since 23.11.2017.

                         .                   In the facts and circumstances of the case, the above

                         named petitioner is directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing

                         bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the

                         like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Special Judge

                         Excise, West Champaran at Bettiah in connection with

                         Narkatiaganj Rail P.S. Case No. 57 of 2017.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                 (Prakash Chandra Jaiswal, J)
Trivedi/-
</p>
