<p id="p_1">     The    petitioners   have    sought      for   the

following reliefs:
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">      A) Direct the respondents to admit
         the petitioners in the first and
         second       respondent      college
         concerned as a regular student
         for III B.E. (Civil Engineering) for
         the academic year 2018-19, in the
         interest of justice and equity.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">      B) Direct the respondents to permit
         the petitioners to write the 5 t h
         and 6 t h semester B.E. (Civil
         Engineering) examination during
         the academic year 2018-19 and
         publish the results of 5 t h and 6 t h
         semester of the petitioners along
         with the B.E. students of the
         same class.</blockquote><p id="p_2">

</p><p id="p_3">     2.    Identical   prayers   were   the    subject

matter of W.P.Nos.52135-52138/2018 before the

Principal Bench at Bangalore. The co-ordinate

bench by detailed order in the said writ petitions

rejected the prayers. The same was taken up in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                          4</span>


appeal     in   W.A.Nos.3344-3347/2018.         The

Division   Bench,   by   order   dated   15.06.2019

dismissed the said appeals confirming the order

of the learned single Judge. Following the said

decisions, these writ petitions are rejected.
