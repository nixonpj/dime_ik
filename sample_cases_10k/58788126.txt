<div class="doc_title">Om Parkash Goyal vs State Of Punjab And Others on 8 May, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB                      AND HARYANA
AT CHANDIGARH

                                 C.W.P. No.8908 of 2013
                                 Date of Decision 08.5.2013

Om Parkash Goyal
                                             --Petitioner
                Vs.

State of Punjab and others

                                              --Respondents</pre>

<p id="p_1">CORAM: Hon'ble Mr.Justice Jasbir Singh,Acting Chief Justice
       Hon'ble Mr.Justice Rakesh Kumar Jain,Judge

Present:   Mr. H.C.Arora,Advocate, for the petitioner

           Mr. P.S.Bajwa, Addl.A.G, Punjab

Jasbir Singh, ACJ: (Oral)

           In this writ petition on 29.4.2013, following order was

passed:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                  "It is grievance of the petitioner that the
           students have been asked to purchase defective note
           books. To say so, a reference has been made to
           letter dated 19.3.2013 (Annexure P-6), wherein it is
           admitted that on checking of those note books,
           deficiency in printing has come to the notice of the
           department concerned.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">                 Notice of motion.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">                  On asking of Court, Mr.J.S.Puri,Additional
           Advocate General, Punjab, accepts notice on behalf
           of respondent Nos.1 to 3 and 5. Counsel for the
           petitioner is directed to furnish four copies of the
           writ petition to Mr,Puri during course of the day.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">                 Notice upon respondents No. 4 and 6 be
           served dasti also.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_5">                 Adjourned to 8.5.2013".
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_6">             Reply has been filed on behalf of respondent Nos.1 to 5.
</blockquote>
<p id="p_2">Relevant portion of the reply reads thus:-

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            C.W.P. No.8908 of 2013                     -2-</span>

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_7">                   "It is further submitted that vide the said order,
            the approval accorded to the private firms, namely
            I.S.Dinesh &amp; Co. Educational Publishers and
            Printers HO PO Box No.813 Road, Near Sheetla
            Maata Mandir,Mai Hiran Gate, Jalandhar, 2.
            J.R.Printing Press, Nabha Gate, Sangrur and 3.
            Ravindera Paper Mart Adda Hoshiarpur, Chowk,
            Jalandhar for supplying the Practical Note Books in
            the market for students studying in Government
            Schools has been withdrawn with immediate effect.
            It has further been ordered that the students studying
            in Government Schools are free to purchase
            practical note books of their own choice from the
            market as per the prescribed syllabus".
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_8">            In view of the decision now taken by the respondent-
</blockquote>
<p id="p_3">authorities, this writ petition has become infrucruous and is

disposed of accordingly.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                                                   (Jasbir Singh)
                                                  Acting Chief Justice

08.5.2013                                        ( Rakesh Kumar Jain )
rr                                                        Judge
 </pre>
