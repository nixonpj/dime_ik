<div class="doc_title">Md. Pampam And Ors. vs The State Of Bihar on 19 January, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                        Cr.Misc. No.44366 of 2010
           1. MD. PAMPAM S/O Md. Faruk.
           2. Md. Pappu, S/O Md. Faruk.
           3. Md. Muzaffar, S/O Md. Faruk.
                    All residents of village Sohrai, P.S. Madhepur (R.S.) &amp;
              District- Madhubani.                         .......Petitioners.
                                  Versus
                          THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                 -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   19.01.2011                 The petitioner has filed the present petition

                   for grant of anticipatory bail in Madhepur P.S. Case No.

                   31 of 2010 registered under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1944660/" id="a_3">384</a> and

                   <a href="/doc/1198526/" id="a_4">505</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               Earlier anticipatory bail petition was disposed

                   off on 14.07.2919 vide Cr. Misc. No. 214324 with the

                   observation that learned court below will examine the

                   claim of the petitioner particularly in the light of the

                   observation of the learned Sessions Judge.

</p><p id="p_3">                               Considering the aforesaid facts, I am not

                   inclined to interfere with the earlier order. However

                   learned counsel for the petitioner submits that bail

                   application of other accused has been favourably decided

                   by learned Sessions Judge.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               Let the learned Magistrate consider this fact

                   while considering regular bail of the petitioner and may

                   dispose off the regular bail on the same day, if possible.

</p><p id="p_5">                               With    the    aforesaid   observations,       this

                   application is disposed off.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               2</span><p id="p_6">




</p><p id="p_7">                         Let the order be communicated through FAX

             at the cost of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><pre id="pre_2">Bhardwaj/-                        ( Dinesh Kumar Singh, J.)
 </pre>
