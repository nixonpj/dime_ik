<pre id="pre_1">                     1



   28.11.14
19 Ct. No.35
sm


                       CPAN 1952 of 2014
                               in
                      W.P. 25695(W) of 2010



                  Mr. S.P. Pahari
                  Mr. A. Pradhan
                  Mr. Tapan Kr. Mahapatra
                                  .....for the applicant
                  Mr. Bidhan Biswas
                         .....for the alleged contemnor



                     The applicant in this contempt

               application prays for cognizance of

               contempt committed by the respondent</pre>

<p id="p_1">               No.2 in the writ petition being the

               alleged contemnor.       By order dated

               16th July, 2014 the petitioner had

               obtained     a     direction        upon    the

               respondents that one post of Secretary,

               Gram Panchayat will be kept vacant till

               the disposal of the writ petition.          The

               State was directed to file affidavit-in-

               opposition, copy of which was to be

               given to the petitioner/applicant by

               28th July, 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_2">

</p><pre id="pre_2">                     Learned       advocate         for    the

               petitioner   submitted         no    copy    of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        2</span>




affidavit-in-opposition     has        been

received.



</pre><p id="p_3">       Mr. S.P. Pahari, learned advocate

appearing on behalf of the applicant

submitted      communication      of    the

directions made in the said order dated

16th   July,   2014    on   the    alleged

contemnor.        However, it does not

appear from the application copy of the

order, directions of which are alleged to

have been violated by the alleged

contemnor, was served.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">       In   the    circumstances,      this

application (CPAN 1952 of 2014) is not

admitted and is accordingly dismissed.



</p><p id="p_6">       Dismissal of this application will

not prevent the applicant from filing a

fresh application provided the same is

maintainable in accordance with law.



</p><p id="p_7">                   (Arindam Sinha, J.)
