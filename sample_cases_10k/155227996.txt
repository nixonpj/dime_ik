                  For the Petitioner/s  : Mr. Arif Raza

<p id="p_1">2   07-12-2012                 Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               2. The petitioner is aggrieved as despite fixation of pay

                   at Rs.21,910/- on 1.1.2006 payable from 1.4.2007 payment has

                   been made to her for the period January, 2006 to July, 2010 on the

                   pre-revised pay scale in respect of her having worked as Assistant

                   Professor in the Department of English, Patna Women's College,

                   Patna University, Patna. The petitioner also filed representation

                   dated 28.9.2012 as contained in Annexure-4, but to no avail.

</p><p id="p_3">                               3. After hearing the parties, this writ petition is disposed
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court CWJC No.21979 of 2012 (2) dt.07-12-2012</span>




                        of with liberty to the petitioner to file a fresh representation

                        before the Registrar, Patna University, Patna (Respondent No. 5)

                        within two weeks hereof. If any such representation is filed, the

                        same shall be considered and disposed of in accordance with law,

                        preferably within a period of six weeks thereafter.

</p><p id="p_4">                                    4. The writ petition stands disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                                                               (Vikash Jain, J)


Chandran
