<div class="doc_title">Santosh Kumar Sharaf vs State Of Bihar on 4 May, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                           CR. APP (DB) No.122 of 2007
                          SANTOSH KUMAR SHARRAF
                                     Versus
                               STATE OF BIHAR
                                    -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">5.    4.5.2009                   Heard learned counsel for the appellant and learned counsel for

                     the State in respect of I.A.No.878 of 2009 through which the appellant has

                     renewed his prayer for bail which was earlier rejected on merits.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 It has been submitted that this appellant has now remained in

                     custody for more than five and half years and he is alleged to have

                     disclosed the place from which the kidnapped boy was recovered but he

                     was interrogated only on suspicion that he is an associate of main accused

                     Umesh Sah who has been acquitted.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Considering the period of custody and other circumstances, the

                     prayer for bail is allowed. During pendency of this appeal, let the above

                     named appellant be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of rs.10,000/-

                     (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction

                     of Additional District &amp; Sessions Judge, P.O. Fast Track Court No.1,

                     Motihari, District-East Champaran in Sessions Trail No.335 of

                     2004(Raxaul P.S.Case No.70 of 2003).</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                 (Shiva Kirti Singh,J.)


     B.Kr.                                       ( Dharnidhar Jha, J. )
 </pre>
