BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 13.08.2018  

CORAM   

THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE G.JAYACHANDRAN           

Crl.M.P.(MD).No.5473 of 2018 
in Crl.O.P.(MD)No.2109 of 2018 

Raja                                            ... Petitioner/Petitioner

-vs-
1.The Director,
   Vigilance and Anti Corruption Department,
   No.293, M.K.N.Road, Alandur,
   Chennai.

2.The Deputy Superintendent of Police,
   Vigilance and Anti Corruption Department,
   Collectorate Complex,
   Virudhunagar, Virudhunagar District.
                                                ... Respondents/Respondents  
Prayer: Criminal Miscellaneous petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of
Criminal Procedure Code, to direct the respondents herein to take immediate
action on the basis of the order passed by this Court, dated 12.02.2018 in
Crl.O.P.(MD).No.2109 of 2018 within the time stipulated by this Court.

!For Petitioner      : Mr.S.Sathya Chidambaram

^For Respondent   : Mr.K.K.Ramakrishnan  
                                         Additional Public Prosecutor

:ORDER  
</pre><p id="p_1">        Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and learned Additional
Public Prosecutor for the state.
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.The learned Additional Public Prosecutor, submits that after
completion of the preliminary enquiry conducted based on the complaint given
by the petitioner herein, the Directorate,  Vigilance and Anti Corruption
Department, Chennai, is ordered to register regular case against Mr.G.Mohan,
former Assistant Engineer and Mr.V.Sakthivel, Retired Assistant Executive
Engineer, Agricultural Engineering Department, Virudhunagar.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.Recording the same, this Criminal Original Petition is disposed of.
The respondents shall proceed and complete the investigation and file final
report within a period of four months.
</p>

<p id="p_4">To
</p><p id="p_5">1.The Director, Vigilance and Anti Corruption Department,
   No.293, M.K.N.Road, Alandur, Chennai. 
</p>
<p id="p_6">2.The Deputy Superintendent of Police,
   Vigilance and Anti Corruption Department,
   Collectorate Complex, Virudhunagar, Virudhunagar District.
</p>
<p id="p_7">3.The Additional Public Prosecutor,
   Madurai Bench of Madras High Court, Madurai.
