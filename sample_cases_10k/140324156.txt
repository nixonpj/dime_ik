<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               2</span>

    THIS MFA FILED U/S 173(1) OF MV ACT AGAINST THE
JUDGMENT AND AWARD DATED 14.08.2015 PASSED IN MVC
NO.314/2012 ON THE FILE OF THE PRINCIPAL DISTRICT JUDGE
AND MEMBER MACT, AT CHIKKAMAGALURU, PARTLY ALLOWING
THE CLAIM PETITION FOR COMPENSATION AND SEEKING
ENHANCEMENT OF COMPENSATION.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     THIS MFA HAVING BEEN REFERRED TO LOK ADALATH
FOR SETTLEMENT, COMING ON FOR ORDERS THIS DAY, K.N.
PHANEENDRA, J. MADE THE FOLLOWING:

</p><p id="p_3">                 CONCILIATION ORDER


      The learned counsel for the appellant and the

representative of the Respondent-Insurance Company

along with its counsel are present.


</p><p id="p_4">      2. This is claimant's appeal as against the Judgment

and Award passed by the Tribunal. By mutual consent, a

sum of Rs.80,000/- (Rupees Eighty Thousand only) is

awarded in addition to the amount awarded by the

Tribunal, in full and final settlement of the claim.     A joint

Memo is filed on behalf of the parties to this effect.


</p><p id="p_5">      3. The Respondent - Insurance Company shall

deposit the said amount before the Tribunal, within Six

weeks from the date of preparation of the Award, failing
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                              3</span>

which the said amount shall carry interest at the rate of

9% P.A. from the date of default, till the date of deposit.

On deposit, the entire enhanced amount is ordered to be

released in favour of the claimant/appellant.


</p><p id="p_6">       4. This Miscellaneous First Appeal stands disposed

of in terms of the joint memo. The award of the Tribunal

shall stand modified accordingly.

</p><p id="p_8">                                         JUDGE
