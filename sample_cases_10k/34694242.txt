                          JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Petitioner  approached     the   Local   Level    Monitoring

Committee to correct details in the draft data bank in respect of

property situated in Survey No.75/12/3 of Kunnamkulam Village

in Thrissur district. According to the petitioner, this property is

a dry land.     According to the petitioner, this property is

converted long before the enactment of Kerala Conservation of

Paddy Land and Wet Land Act, 2008 and it can be identified as a

dry land. However, it is submitted that without verifying the

actual nature of the property, this property is classified as nilam

in the draft data bank.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2.    I am of the view, petitioner has a remedy before the

Local Level Monitoring Committee. Petitioner shall approach the

first respondent for correcting details in the draft data bank. If

the petitioner approaches the first respondent within two weeks

from the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment, first

respondent shall conduct local inspection and find out whether

this can be classified as nilam as on the date of enactment of Act

28 of 2008. If it is found that it cannot be classified as nilam as

W.P(C).No.30789 of 2014W
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 2</span>


on the date of enactment of Act 28 of 2008, necessarily

corresponding changes has to be made in the draft data bank.

Needful shall be done within a period of six weeks from the date

of receipt of such application from the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_3">     The Writ Petition is disposed of as above.
</p>
