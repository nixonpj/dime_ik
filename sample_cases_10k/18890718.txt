                      JODHPUR
              S.B. Criminal Misc(Pet.) No. 835 / 2017
Gangandeep Singh S/o Karnail Singh, By Caste Jatsikh, Resident
of 2 PS (Dhani), Tehsil Padampur, District Sri Ganganagar.
                                                        ----Petitioner
                                Versus
1. The State of Rajasthan

2. Jarnail Singh S/o Labh Singh, By Caste Jatsikh, Resident of 2
PS, Tehsil Padampur, Presently Resident of 33 Fendas Bank Colony,
Sri Ganganagar, District Sri Ganganagar.
                                                  ----Respondents
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)   :   Mr.D.L.Rawla.
For Respondent(s) :     Mr.V.S.Rajpurohit, P.P.
_____________________________________________________
           HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SANDEEP MEHTA
                         Judgment / Order
27/11/2017

     The instant misc. petition has been preferred on behalf of

the petitioner seeking quashing of the proceedings of F.I.R.

No.6/2012 registered at the Police Station Padampur, District

Sriganganagar for the offences under <a href="/doc/1305169/" id="a_1">Sections 255</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>,

<a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a>, <a href="/doc/756306/" id="a_6">474</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_7">120B</a> I.P.C.

     Learned counsel Shri Rawla pointed out that 8 different

Police Officers investigated the case thoroughly and came to a

conclusion that no offence was made out from the allegations

levelled by the complainant. Despite that, the matter was

reopened without any justification. He submits that now, after

further investigation, one again a negative Final Report has been

submitted in the court of learned ACJM, Padampur.
                                 (2 of 2)
                                                       [CRLMP-835/2017]

       In view of this development, it is hereby directed that the

learned ACJM, Padampur shall pass appropriate order on the Final

Report without directing any further investigation and after

providing opportunity of hearing to the complainant. While

deciding the matter, the Magistrate shall objectively consider the

material presented bey the investigating agency in support of the

negative Final Report.

       With these directions, the misc. petition as well as the stay

petition are disposed of.

                                             (SANDEEP MEHTA),J.
