<p id="p_1">            Learned Government Advocate has drawn our
     attention to the notification dated 17.5.2005 issued by
     the Government of Madhya Pradesh, whereby only the
     State Government has been exempted for payment of
     Court fees under <a href="/doc/59438309/" id="a_1">Section 35</a> of the Court Fees Act, 1870
     and in the aforesaid notification, no exemption has
     been     granted   to   the   M.P.    Road   Development
     Corporation.
</p><p id="p_2">            Learned Counsel for the appellant prays and is
     granted two weeks to verify the same from the
     appellant and if there is not notification, he will pay the
     deficit Court fees within reasonable time.</p><pre id="pre_2">
