<div class="doc_title">Lal Babu Yadav vs State Of Bihar on 15 September, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">            IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                        Cr.Misc. No.31402 of 2009
            LAL BABU YADAV, SON OF Late Kapildeo Yadav, Resident
            of Gulal Chak, P.S.-Dulhin Bazar, Distt.-Patna.
                                  Versus
                           THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                 -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">02.   15.09.2009             Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the

                    State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             The petitioner seeks bail in a case instituted for

                    the offences under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_1">Section 498A</a> of the Indian Penal

                    Code and <a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_2">Section 4</a> of the Dowry Prohibition Act.

</p><p id="p_3">                              Taking into consideration the fact that the

                    petitioner is the husband who has remained in custody

                    since 25.5.2009, let the petitioner above named be

                    released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 5,000/-

                    (Five Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each

                    to the satisfaction of Sub-Divisional Judicial Magistrate,

                    Danapur in connection with Complaint Case No. 698(C) of

                    2007 on the condition that one of the bailors will be a

                    close relative of the petitioner who will give an affidavit

                    giving genealogy as to how he is related with the

                    petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                              The petitioner has volunteered to pay a lump

                    sum amount of Rs. 5,000/- (Five Thousand) to the

                    complainant which she shall be entitled to withdraw after

                    such deposit in the court below.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><pre id="pre_2">         Vikash/-                              (Anjana Prakash, J.)
 </pre>
