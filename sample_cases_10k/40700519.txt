                          W.P. (C) No. 5583 of 2017
                                ......
</pre><p id="p_1">      Mukhtar Safi son of Late Nazir Alam, resident of Road No.10/A, House
      No.23-A, Azadnagar, P.O. and P.S.-Mango, District-Ranchi.
</p><p id="p_2">                                                                       .... Petitioner
                                Versus
</p><p id="p_3">      1. The State of Jharkhand.
</p><p id="p_4">      2. Deputy Commissioner, East Singhbhum, Jamshedpur, P.O. and P.S.-Sakchi,
      District-East Singhbhum.
</p><p id="p_5">      3. Mango Notified Area Committee, Jamshedpur through Special Officer,
      office at Mango, P.O. and P.S.-Mango, Town Jamshedpur, District-East
      Singhbhum.
</p><p id="p_6">      4. Special Officer, Mango Notified Area Committee, Jamshedpur office at
      Mango, P.O. and P.S.-Mango, Town Jamshedpur, District-East Singhbhum.
</p><p id="p_7">                                                               ..... Respondents
</p><p id="p_8">                                        ---
</p><p id="p_9">      CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SUJIT NARAYAN PRASAD
</p><p id="p_10">                                       ---
</p><p id="p_11">      For the Petitioners              : Mr. Akshay Kr. Mahato, Advocate
      For the Respondent               : Mr. A. Kumar, Advocate
</p><p id="p_12">                                       ---
</p><p id="p_13">09/04.02.2019        This writ petition is under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of

      India to quash the notice as contained in Annexure-2 issued in pursuance to

      the provision as contained in Section 426 of the Jharkhand Municipal Act,

      2011 for asking the petitioner to give reply as to why the construction over

      the land in question be not held to be unauthorized construction being

      contrary to the provision of the Act.
</p>
<p id="p_14">            Learned counsel for the petitioner, at the outset, has submitted that he

      may be allowed to approach        to the concerned authority to satisfy with

      respect to the provisions contained therein for condoning the deviation or

      unauthorized allotment in order to satisfy the authority to get effected

      building, which has already been constructed.

</p><p id="p_15">            Learned counsel for the respondent-Mango Notified Area Committee,

      Mr. Amarendra Kumar however has submitted with respect to such provision

      of law. Further, it is fairly submitted that the petitioner may be given liberty

      to approach before the authority on the aforesaid provision.
</p><p id="p_16">             Upon hearing learned counsel for the parties and without interfering

      with the impugned order, the writ petition is disposed of by giving liberty to

      the petitioner to approach before the authority with relevant rules if any for

      satisfying the authority if that will be done by the petitioner, the authority will

      take decision in this regard within four weeks from the date of receipt of copy

      of this order in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_17">
</p><p id="p_18">                                                        (Sujit Narayan Prasad, J.)
RKM
