</pre><p id="p_1">06.12.2018


                     Heard Mr. A. C. Buragohain, learned senior counsel appearing for the

              applicant. I have also heard Mr. C. K. S. Baruah, learned Govt. Advocate,

              Assam, appearing for the OPs.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                     This I.A. has been filed with a prayer to correct the typographical error

              that has crept into the order dated 09.11.2018 passed by this Court in Review

              Petition No.70/2018.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                     By referring to the appointment order dated 06.04.2010 issued in favour

              of the applicant, Mr. Buragohain submits that his client was appointed as an

              "Anganwadi Worker" and was serving in the said post till such time her services

              were terminated by the authorities acting under the order dated 14.02.2017

              passed by this Court. Notwithstanding the same, due to the typographical error

              in the order whereby the applicant has been shown to have been appointed

              as "Anganwadi Helper", the order of reinstatement of the applicant as per the
                                                                                    Page No.# 3/3

            direction dated 09.11.2018 of this Court, has now been made in respect of the

            post of "Anganwadi Helper", which is a much lower category post then the one

            against which the applicant was appointed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">                      I have considered the submission of the learned counsel for the parties

            and have also perused the materials available on record.               The order of

            appointment, prima facie, goes to show that the applicant was appointed as

            an "Anganwadi Worker". The said fact has also been noted in the earlier order

            of this Court dated 31.10.2018 and the respondents have not denied the same.

            Therefore, I am convinced that there is an error in the order dated 09.11.2018

            which requires to be corrected. The post of "Anganwadi Helper" mentioned in

            the order dated 09.11.2018 shall be read as "Anganwadi Worker".


</p><p id="p_8">                      With the above clarification this I.A. stands disposed of.




</p><p id="p_9">                                                                        JUDGE



Comparing Assistant
