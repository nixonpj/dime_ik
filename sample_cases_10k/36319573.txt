<div class="doc_title">Mukesh Kumar vs State Of U.P. on 19 August, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Anil Kumar-Ix</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 75
 

 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION No. - 21593 of 2019
 

 
Applicant :- Mukesh Kumar
 
Opposite Party :- State Of U.P.
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Raj Kumar Vaishya,Gopeshwar Sahai Bisaria
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.
 

 
Hon'ble Anil Kumar-IX,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Heard Sri A.K. Bisaria, Advocate holding brief of Sri Raj Kumar Vaishy, learned counsel for the applicant, learned A.G.A. and perused the record. 
</p> 
<p id="p_2">Learned counsel for the applicant contended that the applicant is innocent and has been falsely implicated in the present case. The alleged recovered contraband was not measured by the raiding police party after its recovery but it has been mentioned in the F.I.R. that the applicant told them that he had approximately 1 Kg. Charas in his bag. It is much below the commercial quantity. The recovery of 'charas' has been falsely planted on the applicant. There is no independent witness of the alleged recovery. The applicant has no previous criminal history. The compliance of provisions of <a href="/doc/961083/" id="a_1">section 50</a> N.D.P.S. Act has not been made. The applicant undertakes that he will not misuse the liberty of bail. The applicant is in custody since 29.03.2019.
</p> 
<p id="p_3">Learned A.G.A. vehemently opposed the prayer of bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">Upon hearing learned counsel and perusal of record and considering the complicity of accused, severity of punishment as well as totality of facts and circumstances, at this stage without expressing any view on the merits of the case, this Court is of the opinion that it is a fit case for bail. Hence, the bail application is hereby allowed.
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Let the applicant -Mukesh Kumar be released on bail in Case Crime No.246 of 2019, under <a href="/doc/704277/" id="a_2">Section 8</a>/<a href="/doc/919170/" id="a_3">20</a> N.D.P.S. Act, P.S. Izzatnagar, District Bareilly, on furnishing a personal bond and two sureties each in the like amount to the satisfaction of magistrate/court concerned, subject to following conditions:-
</p> 
<p id="p_6">(i) The applicant will not tamper with prosecution evidence.
</p> 
<p id="p_7">(ii) The applicant will abide the orders of court, will attend the court on every date and will not delay the disposal of trial in any manner whatsoever.
</p> 
<p id="p_8">(iii) The applicant will not indulge in any unlawful activities.
</p> 
<p id="p_9">(iv) The applicant will not misuse the liberty of bail in any manner whatsoever.
</p> 
<p id="p_10">The identity, status and residential proof of sureties will be verified by court concerned and in case of breach of any of the conditions mentioned above, court concerned will be at liberty to cancel the bail and send the applicant to prison.
</p> 
<p id="p_11">Order Date :- 19.8.2019
 
Rahul.
</p>
