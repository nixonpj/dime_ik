<pre id="pre_1">        C/SCA/9338/2016                                  ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

         R/SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 9338 of 2016
==========================================================
                 MAHIPATSINH MOTIBHAI GOHIL
                            Versus
    GUJARAT STATE CO-OPERATIVE AGRICULTURAL AND RURAL
                   DEVELOPMENT BANK LTD
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR VISHWAS K SHAH(5364) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1
RULE SERVED(64) for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.M.THAKER

                          Date : 04/09/2018

                            ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">     Learned   advocate   for   the   petitioner   is   not 
present.   The Office  has  circulated  a Note  dated 
28.8.2018, which reads thus: 
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">     "This is regarding note dtd 27.08.2018 received from Ld.  
     Advocate   for   the   Petitioner,   requesting   to   place   the  
     aforesaid   matter   before   the   Honourable   Court,   for  
     withdrawal of appearance in the aforesaid petition, for  
     the reasons stated therein. Copy of the Said Note is not  
     served to the other side.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">     Therefore,   the   note   along   with   aforesaid   matter   is  
     respectfully placed before the Honourable Court for kind  
     perusal and passing necessary order."</blockquote>


<p id="p_2">     Along   with   the   Note,   the   Registry   has   also 
circulated   letter   dated   27.8.2018   addressed   by 
the   learned   advocate   for   the   petitioner   to   the 
Registrar,  High  Court  of Gujarat.  From  the Note 
it   appears   that   learned   advocate   for   the 
petitioner   has   not   followed   any   procedure 



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               Page 1 of 2</span>
         C/SCA/9338/2016                          ORDER



required   to   be   followed   before   retiring   from 
proceedings i.e. before withdrawing appearance on 
behalf of the Client ( party to the proceedings). 
In   that   view   of   the   matter,   the   submission   / 
request   by   learned   advocate   for   the   petitioner 
cannot   be   accepted.   For   the   purpose   of   present 
proceedings, the Court will consider that learned 
advocate   for   the   petitioner   continuous   to 
represent the petitioner, until learned advocate 
retires   from   the   proceedings   after   following 
prescribed procedure.
</p>

<p id="p_3">      In   this   view   of   the   matter,   either   the 
learned advocate for the petitioner should follow 
the   procedure   for   retiring   from   the   proceedings 
or he should continue to represent the petitioner 
and   appear   in   the   proceedings   on   behalf   of   the 
petitioner until the procedure for retiring from 
the proceedings is completed and leave to retire 
is granted.
</p>


<p id="p_4">      With   aforesaid   clarification,   the   note   is 
disposed of. 
</p>


<p id="p_5">                                              (K.M.THAKER, J)
