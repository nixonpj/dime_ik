<div class="doc_title">Krishna Kumar vs Smt Rajni Lal on 8 November, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">          IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                      W.P.(C) No.3496 of 2015
                                  -----
          Krishna Kumar.                          .......... Petitioner.
                               -Versus-
          Smt. Rajni Lal.                         .......... Respondent.
                                 ------
          CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RAJESH SHANKAR
                                 ------
          For the Petitioner :   Mr. Samavesh Bhanj Deo, Advocate
          For the Respondent:    Mr. Rabindra Prasad, Advocate.
                                 ------
16/08.11.2017</pre><p id="p_1">: Pursuant to the order dated 12th September, 2017, the
          Respondent- Rajni Lal along with her two minor daughters,
          namely, Ritika Bharti, aged about eight years; and Kabya
          Bharti, aged about four years; is present before this Court
          today.
</p><p id="p_2">                Learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned
          counsel for the respondent are also present.
</p><p id="p_3">                However, learned counsel for the petitioner submits that
          due to some medical reason, the petitioner has not been able
          to physically appear before this Court, as directed vide order
          dated 12th September, 2017.
</p><p id="p_4">                Learned counsel for the respondent submits that the
          petitioner is deliberately avoiding his appearance before this
          Court, as he is also not appearing before the court of Judicial
          Magistrate, Asansol leading to issuance of warrant of arrest
          against him in M. Ex. No.05 of 2016.
</p><p id="p_5">                Considering aforesaid facts, let this case be listed under
          the heading "For Admission" on 3rd January, 2018.
</p>


<p id="p_6">                                              (Rajesh Shankar, J.)
Sanjay/
