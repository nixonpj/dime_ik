<p id="p_1">  Date : July 24, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       The Court : The Official Liquidator and the erstwhile directors of the company in

liquidation are both represented. According to the erstwhile directors, substantial money

due to the company in liquidation from Government organisations has been realised by

the Official Liquidator with the assistance of these directors. The directors say that if the

entire money due to the company from the Government organisations come in, the

directors will be in a position to revive the company despite it having gone into

liquidation.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       The application will appear four weeks hence.
</p>
