<p id="p_1">           The petitioner availed a housing loan, a term loan

and two Suvidha loans from the respondent Bank. On default

being committed the respondent initiated proceedings under

Securitisation and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and

<a href="/doc/198257891/" id="a_1">Enforcement of Security Interest Act</a>, 2002 ('SARFAESI' for

short) against the property of the petitioner. The overdue

amounts in the housing loan is Rs.30,288/-, in the term loan is

Rs.1,08,015/- and the 2 Suvidha loans are Rs.32,469/- and

Rs.66,319/-.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     2. The liability and the default are admitted. Considering

the facts of the case and the alleged impecunious

circumstances of the petitioner, this writ petition is disposed

of on the following terms.
</p>
<p id="p_3">           (i)  The total defaulted arrears is said to be

Rs.2,37,091/- as on 1st January 2017.

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  2</span>

W.P.(C)No.62682017




</p><p id="p_4">             (ii)  The respondent shall grant twelve monthly

instalments for the payment of the arrears, starting from

30.3.2017, and continued on the 30th of the successive month.

</p><p id="p_5">             (iii) Recovery proceedings shall be kept in abeyance

on condition that the remittances as per this order are made

without any default.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             (iv)   On the petitioner making two defaults in

repaying the installments as also the regular EMIs, the recovery

steps initiated shall revive and continue.

</p><p id="p_7">             (v) The respondent shall issue a statement of the

future interest accrued on the defaulted arrears every three

months and the same shall be paid along with the next

month's instalment, as granted by this Court, after the

satisfaction of the arrears, as the 13th instalment.

</p><p id="p_8">             (vi) On the petitioner satisfying the entire arrears, as

also the EMI's the recovery proceedings shall be unenforceable

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                      3</span>

W.P.(C)No.62682017

and the petitioner shall be permitted to satisfy the EMI as per

the original agreement.
</p>
<p id="p_9">             Writ Petition is disposed of as above, making it clear

that the respondent will be free to proceed with the recovery

if the above conditions are not complied with.




</p><p id="p_10">                                                 Sd/-
