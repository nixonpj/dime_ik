          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

 CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 7554 of
                                    2015

                  In CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. 450 of 2014

==========================================================
          MUNABHAI MANDANBHAI KARAVADRA....Applicant(s)
                            Versus
              STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
==========================================================
Appearance:
MS SUBHADRA G PATEL, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MR HK PATEL, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
==========================================================

        CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE RAVI R.TRIPATHI
               and
               HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.D.KOTHARI

                              Date : 23/04/2015


                               ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1"> (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE RAVI R.TRIPATHI)



</p><p id="p_2">1.   The present application is filed by the convict
through    his      brother     seeking         temporary   bail     for   a
period of 30 days so as to remain present in the
marriage of his sister's daughter.
</p>

<p id="p_3">2.   Learned        APP     Mr.Patel     invited     attention     of   the
Court to the jail remarks. The convict has undergone 4
years 3 months and 27 days as on 22.4.2015. During
this period, the convict has availed one temporary
bail in the month of February, 2015 at the time of sad


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  Page 1 of 2</span>
           R/CR.MA/7554/2015                                       ORDER



demise of his father's elder brother. He has availed
one furlough and reported in time.                        The jail conduct
is also reported to be good.
</p>

<p id="p_4">3. The matter requires consideration.
</p>

<p id="p_5">4. Rule. Learned APP Mr.Patel waives service of Rule
on behalf of Respondents.
</p>

<p id="p_6">5. For the contents of the application, the same is
partly allowed. The convict is ordered to be released
on temporary bail for a period of one week from the
date of his release, on his executing a personal bond
of   Rs.5,000/-           (Rupees    Five          Thousand   Only)   to     the
satisfaction          of      the   Jail     authorities       and    also   on
observing other usual terms and conditions.
</p>

<p id="p_7">6. The convict shall surrender to the Jail authorities
on expiry of the temporary bail period.
</p>

<p id="p_8">7. Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p>

<p id="p_9">8.       Registry is directed to communicate this order to
the concerned jail authority by fax.
</p>



<p id="p_10">                                                          (RAVI R.TRIPATHI, J.)




                                                              (R.D.KOTHARI, J.)
VATSAL
