                    In the High Court of Punjab and Haryana at Chandigarh


                                                   Crl. Writ Petition No. 1524 of 2014
                                                   Date of Decision: 18.11.2014

            Yogesh                                                        ......Petitioner

                                                   Versus


            State of Haryana and others                                   .......Respondents

            CORAM: HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA

            Present:            A.S.Trikha, Advocate
                                for the petitioner.

                                Mr. Amandeep Singh, AAG, Haryana.

                                      ****
            SABINA, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                Petitioner has filed this petition seeking his transfer

            from Central Jail, Hisar to District Jail, Jhajjar or Rohtak on the

            basis of departmental instructions (Annexure P-3) and medical

            certificate from AIIMS, Delhi.

</p><p id="p_2">                                Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that

            petitioner is suffering from Glaucoma and requires treatment from

            hospital            in Delhi.    Therefore, petitioner be transferred to jail at

            Rohtak or Jhajjar.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Para 3 of the reply submitted by the State, reads as

            under:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                 "That in reply to para No. 3 of the present petition, it is

                                 submitted that petitioner is a habitual pugnacious person

                                 and usually involve in quarrel between the prisoners in

                                 District Jain Gurgaon.     On the recommendations of the

                                 Superintendent District Jail, Gurgaon, petitioner was

                                 transferred from District Jail, Gurgaon to Central Jail

                                 Hisar on administrative ground.     It is further submitted
GURPREET SINGH
2014.11.20 16:23
I attest to the accuracy and
authenticity of this document
Chandigarh
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Crl. Writ Petition No. 1524 of 2014                                           -2-</span>


                                that at present District Jail Rohtak is already overcrowded

                                as against the authorised capacity of 1300, 1401 inmates

                                were confined in Rohtak jail as on 11.11.2014.             The

                                petitioner is a hardcore criminal and his home district is

                                Jhajjar and if he is shifted to District Jail, Jhajjar, he will

                                remain active in his criminal activities with the help of his

                                local associates.     Hence, petitioner cannot allow to

                                discretion to choose the place where he is to be kept."</blockquote><p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                                In view of the reply submitted by the State, no ground

            for transfer of the petitioner from Central Jail, Hisar to District Jail

            Jhajjar or Rohtak, is made out.

</p><p id="p_6">                                Dismissed.
</p>
