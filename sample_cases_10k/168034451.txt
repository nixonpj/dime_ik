<div class="doc_title">WPMS/3215/2019 on 16 October, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">WPMS No.3215 of 2019
Hon'ble Sudhanshu Dhulia, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">       Mr. Abhijay Negi, Advocate for the
petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">       Mr. Yogesh Pandey, Additional CSC, for
the State/respondent nos. 1 to 3.
</p><p id="p_3">       Mr. Sanjay Bhatt, Advocate for respondent
no.4.
</p><p id="p_4">       The petitioner has filed the present writ
petition seeking a writ in the nature of
mandamus directing the respondent no.4 to
complete the counting of votes for Gram
Pradhan, Village Chandpur, Block Kashipur,
District Udham Singh Nagar under the
supervision of respondent no. 4.
</p><p id="p_5">        Learned counsel for the petitioner submits
that in this regard although the petitioner moved
a representation before the authority concerned
but the same is not accepted by the respondent
authorities.
</p><p id="p_6">       Since the superintendence, direction and
control for conduct of all elections to the
Panchayats lies with the Election Commission
under <a href="/doc/1331638/" id="a_1">Article 243-K</a> of the Constitution of India,
in case the petitioner moves a representation
before the authority concerned, let a decision be
taken by the State Election Commission on the
representation of the petitioner forthwith so that
election be conducted in accordance with law.
</p><p id="p_7">       In view of the above, the writ petition
stands disposed.
</p><p id="p_8">       Let a certified copy of this order be
supplied today itself on payment of usual
charges.
</p>


<p id="p_9">                               (Sudhanshu Dhulia, J.)
                                     16.10.2019
Nitesh/
