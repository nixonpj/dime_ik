       IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA AT
                    BANGALORE

   DATED THIS THE 05TH DAY OF SEPTEMBER, 2014

                          BEFORE

   THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANAND BYRAREDDY

    CRIMINAL REVISION PETITION No.835 OF 2010

BETWEEN:

Sri. Adamvali,
Ex-employee of Kissan
Plastic Industries,
Presently Videographer,
Aged about 47 years,
Residing at near Vani School,
Sonashettihalli,
Chinthamani Town.
                                     ...PETITIONER

(By Shri. S. Nagaraj , Advocate)

AND:

Smt. K.R.Susheela,
Wife of Late K.S.Ramesh Kumar,
48 years,
Residing at G.N.R.Extension,
Chinthamani Town.

                                    ...RESPONDENT
                                   2




(respondent served and unrepresented)
                            *****
       This Criminal Revision Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1457888/" id="a_1">Section
397</a> and <a href="/doc/1571667/" id="a_2">401</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, praying
to set aside the impugned order of judgment of conviction and
sentence dated 30.6.2009 passed by the Principal Civil Judge
(Jr.Dn.) and JMFC., Chintamani in C.C.No.675/2008 and set
aside the impugned judgment of confirmation order dated
19.3.2010 passed by the ADHOC Sessions Judge, Fast Track
Court-II, Chintamani in Criminal Appeal No.28/2009.

      This petition coming on for Orders this day, the court
made the following:
                         ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      This court, by its order dated 11.8.2010 had     issued

notice to the respondent. The sentence was suspended subject

to the petitioner depositing another 25% of the compensation

amount and the executing a personal bond in a sum of

Rs.20,000/-.   Thereafter, on 28.2.2011, the petitioner was

required to report compliance of the conditional order dated

11.8.2010. There is no such report on record. On 9.1.2013,

there was no representation and that the matter was posted

before the court again on 26.8.2014.    The counsel for the

petitioner had remained absent.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 3</span><p id="p_2">




</p><p id="p_3">      There is a memo dated 30.1.2013 filed by the Counsel for

the petitioner, to state that the earlier counsel had issued a 'No

Objection Certificate' (NOC), in the above case in respect of

the petitioner, but however, it is found in retrospect that the

counsel had not issued a 'NOC', and therefore, it was held to

be invalid and has requested that the same be discarded.

</p><p id="p_4">      There is no representation on behalf of the petitioner.

The petition is dismissed for default.


</p><p id="p_5">                                            Sd/-
</p><p id="p_6">                                          JUDGE
