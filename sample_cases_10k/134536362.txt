<p id="p_1">1.	Heard
Mr. B.C. Dave, learned advocate for the petitioner. Mr. Dave tenders
draft amendment which is permitted to be carried out forthwith. From
the fact stated in the draft amendment along with Annexure-A, it
transpires that the application for inter district transfer was made
by the petitioner from Amreli to Godhra (Panchmahal), whereas vide
order passed in 2010, it appears from the record that the petitioner
has been transferred from Amreli to Dahod. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.	In
view of the above facts, NOTICE to the respondents returnable on
14.2.2012. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_3">3.	Respondent
No.1 shall file an affidavit in regard to the above submissions made
by the learned advocate for the petitioner and more particularly,
considering the fact that as such the application for inter district
transfer was from Amreli to Godhra (Panchmahal) and not Dahod, the
respondents shall also place on record before this Court by way of an
affidavit as to how the petitioner came to be transferred to Dahod. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_4">4.	Mr.
</p><p id="p_5">Dave, learned advocate for the petitioner is requested to provide an
additional copy to Mr. Rakesh Patel, learned AGP. 
</p>
