<div class="doc_title">Suraj Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 9 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.12990 of 2019
                   Arising Out of PS. Case No.-593 Year-2018 Thana- PATRAKARNAGAR District- Patna
                 ======================================================
                 SURAJ KUMAR, Son of Kamlesh Prasad, Resident of Munna Chak, Police
                 Station - Patrakar Nagar, District - Patna
                                                                      ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                               ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr. Mira Kumari
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mr. Gulnar Begum
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE BIRENDRA KUMAR
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   09-05-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Petitioner seeks bail in a case registered for the

                   offences punishable under <a href="/doc/919170/" id="a_1">Sections 20</a>, <a href="/doc/263398/" id="a_2">22</a> and <a href="/doc/1373137/" id="a_3">24</a> of the

                   Narcotic Drugs and <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_4">Psychotropic Substances Act</a>, 1985.

</p><p id="p_3">                                15 grams of brown sugar was allegedly recovered

                   from possession of the petitioner. Petitioner is in custody since

                   17.10.2018 and he has got no criminal antecedent. Co-accused

                   Sujeet Kumar, from whose possession 18 grams of brown sugar

                   was recovered, has already been allowed bail by this Court vide

                   order dated 06.05.2019 passed in Cr. Misc. No. 11153 of 2019.

                   Investigation of the case is already complete.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Hence, let the petitioner, above named, be released on

                   bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.20,000/- (Rupees Twenty

                   Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.12990 of 2019(3) dt.09-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     satisfaction of learned Court below where the case is pending in

                     connection with Special Case No. 124 of 2018 arising out of

                     Patrakar Nagar Police Station Case No. 593 of 2018, subject to

                     the condition that the petitioner shall fully cooperate with the

                     investigation/trial of the case, failing which the court below

                     shall be at liberty to cancel the bail bond of the petitioner.


</p><p id="p_5">                                                                          (Birendra Kumar, J)

    Kundan/-
</p>
