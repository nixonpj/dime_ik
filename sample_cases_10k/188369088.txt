<div class="doc_title">Kallu @ Dinesh Sahani vs State Of U.P. on 21 November, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Sudhir Agarwal</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 34
 

 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION No. - 15255 of 2019
 

 
Applicant :- Kallu @ Dinesh Sahani
 
Opposite Party :- State of U.P.
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Vijay Prakash Chaturvedi
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- G.A.
 

 
Hon'ble Sudhir Agarwal,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">1. Heard Vijay Prakash Chaturvedi, learned counsel for applicant and learned A.G.A. for State.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">2. This bail application has been moved by the accused-applicant for enlarging him on bail in Case Crime No.6 of 2019, under <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_1">Sections 380</a>, <a href="/doc/1873755/" id="a_2">457</a>, <a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_3">411</a>, <a href="/doc/170198/" id="a_4">41</a> and <a href="/doc/1976294/" id="a_5">413</a> IPC, Police Station-Siddharth Nagar, District-Siddharth Nagar. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">3. Learned counsel for applicant contended that applicant has not been named in the FIR which was lodged by informant-Cheenak Prasad. No independent witnesses were present at the time of preparation of recovery memo. It is contended that incident took place in the night of 2/3-01.2019 while first information report has been lodged after 13 days of alleged incident on 15.01.2019. Applicant has been falsely implicated. He is innocent and has no criminal history. Applicant is in jail since 17.01.2019. There are no chances of applicant fleeing away from judicial process or tampering with prosecution evidence. He undertakes to appear personally on each and every date and also not seek any unnecessary adjournment during trial. In case, he is enlarged on bail, he will not misuse liberty of bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">4. Learned A.G.A. has opposed the prayer for bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_5">5. Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, without expressing any opinion on merits of the case, I think it appropriate to release applicant on bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_6">6. The application is allowed.
</p> 
<p id="p_7">7. Let applicant, Kallu @ Dinesh Sahani involved in aforesaid case be released on bail on their furnishing a personal bond and two sureties each in the like amount to the satisfaction of Court concerned with following conditions:
</p> 
<p id="p_8">(i) The applicant will not temper with the evidence during trial.
</p> 
<p id="p_9">(ii) The applicant will not pressurize/intimidate the prosecution witness.
</p> 
<p id="p_10">(iii) The applicant will appear before Trial Court on the date fixed.
</p> 
<p id="p_11">(iv) The applicant shall report to the Police Station concerned in the first week of each month to show his good conduct and behaviour.
</p> 
<p id="p_12">8. In case of breach of any of above conditions by applicant, the Court below shall be at liberty to cancel his bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_13">Order Date :- 21.11.2019
 
Ashish Pd.
</p>
