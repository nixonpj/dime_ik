<div class="doc_title">Raju Baghel vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 7 February, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                            MCRC-1300-2017
                (RAJU BAGHEL Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


07-02-2017

Shri M.S.Rawat, learned counsel for the applicant.

Shri Rajendra Singh Yadav, learned PP for the respondent/state.</pre>

<p id="p_1">Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">This is third bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C. His first and second

applications were dismissed as not pressed vide vide orders dated 6.1.2017

and 28.11.2016. It is submitted that after rejection of the bail application, the

charge-sheet has already been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">The applicant has been arrested in Crime No. 140 of 2016 registered by Police

Station Antri district Gwalior for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_2">Section 324</a>, <a href="/doc/520270/" id="a_3">327</a>,

<a href="/doc/594493/" id="a_4">294</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506</a> of IPC and 25 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_6">Arms Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_4">According to the prosecution case, the applicant was going to his field. As soon

as he reached near Baghelo Ka Pura Laxman and Raju met. They demanded

Rs.100/- for drinking liquor, when complainant refused, then, Laxman gave a

sword blow which was caught by the complainant. Raju caught hold his waist

and again a blow by means of sword was given by Laxman which was also

caught by the complainant. Sunil and Javed intervened into the matter.
</p>
<p id="p_5">Learned counsel for the applicant submits that applicant has not
committed any offence and he has been falsely implicated in this
case. There is no allegation against the applicant for causing injury
to the complainant. The applicant is in custody since 8.11.2016 and
the charge-sheet has already been filed. There is no possibility of
abscontion. Hence, prayed for grant of bail.
The prayer is opposed by learned PP for the respondent/State.
Case-diary perused.
</p><p id="p_6">As per medical report of Mubin, one injury caused by sharp edged
 weapon on right and left hand each has been found.
Considering that the injury is attributed to co-accused, allegation
against the applicant is that he caught hold the complainant, he is
in custody since 8.11.2016 and the charge sheet has already been
filed, but without commenting anything on the merits of the case,
the application is allowed. It is directed that the applicant shall be
released on bail on his furnishing personal bond in the sum of
Rs.50,000/- (Rupees fifty thousand Only) with one solvent
surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the Trial Court.
This order will remain operative subject to compliance of the
following conditions by the applicant :-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">      1. The applicant will comply with all the terms and
      conditions of the bond executed by him;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">      2. The applicant will cooperate in the investigation/trial,
      as the case may be;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">      3. The applicant will not indulge himself in extending
      inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted
      with the facts of the case so as to dissuade her from
      disclosing such facts to the Court or to the Police Officer,
      as the case may be;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">      4. The applicant shall not commit an offence similar to
      the offence of which she is accused;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_5">      5. The applicant will not seek unnecessary adjournments
      during the trial; and

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_6">      6. The applicant will not leave India without previous
      permission of the trial Court/Investigating Officer, as the
      case may be.
</blockquote>
<p id="p_7">A copy of this order be sent to the Court concerned for compliance.
C.c. as per rules.
</p><p id="p_8">       (D.K. PALIWAL)
           JUDGE




Rks
