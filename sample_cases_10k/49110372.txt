*      IN THE HIGH COURT OF DELHI AT NEW DELHI
+      W.P.(C) 4872/2016 &amp; CM No.20311/2016
       INDERA SINGH                                      ..... Petitioner
                           Through : Mr. O.P. Aggarwal, Advocate
                     versus
       UNION OF INDIA &amp; ORS                       ..... Respondents
                     Through : Mr. Vivek Goyal, Advocate

       CORAM:
       HON'BLE MS. JUSTICE HIMA KOHLI
       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SUNIL GAUR
                    ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">       %            25.05.2016

</p><p id="p_2">1.     The petitioner, who had retired as a Naik (GD) in the respondents
No.4 &amp; 5/SSB in the year 2000, has filed the present petition praying inter
alia for directions to the respondents to grant him the benefits of the first
and second financial upgradation under the ACP Scheme w.e.f. 9.8.1999 in
the scales of Head Constable and Sub-Inspector, respectively, with all other
consequential benefits in terms of the OM dated 8.9.1999.
</p><p id="p_3">2.     Counsel for the petitioner states that his client had submitted a
representation dated 10.3.2010 to the Director General, SSB claiming the
admissible benefits of financial upgradation under the ACP Scheme.
</p><p id="p_4">3.     Counsel for the respondents, who appears on advance copy, states that
the aforesaid representation filed by the petitioner is very sketchy as it does
not set out the material facts.
</p><p id="p_5">4.     In response, Mr. Aggarwal, counsel for the petitioner states that the
petitioner shall submit a substantive representation to the respondents, but




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">WPC No.4872/2016                                                     Page 1 of 2</span>
 they be directed to consider and decide the same expeditiously.
</p><p id="p_6">5.    In view of the said submission, the present petition is disposed of with
liberty granted to the petitioner to submit a substantive representation to the
respondents within two weeks. The said representation shall be considered
by the respondents within twelve weeks thereafter and the decision taken
shall be communicated in writing to the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_7">6.    Needless to state that if the petitioner is aggrieved by the decision that
may be taken, he shall be entitled to seek his remedies, if so advised, in
accordance with law.
</p><p id="p_8">7.    The writ petition is disposed of, along with the pending application.
</p>



<p id="p_9">                                                           HIMA KOHLI, J



                                                           SUNIL GAUR, J
      MAY 25, 2016
