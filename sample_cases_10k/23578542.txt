<p id="p_3">     [2]    The petitioner has filed the present petition being
aggrieved by Condition No.2 by which they have been
directed to given undertaking to the effect that if the appeal
is decided against them, then they shall hand over the vacant
possession to the defendants.
</p><p id="p_4">     [3]    Shri Bhatnagar submits that after dismissing of
appeal if the appellants hand over possession to the
Respondents, then their rights to file second appeal would
adversely affect. The petitioners have right to file second
 appeal under Section 100 of CPC.
</p><p id="p_5">        [4]   By order dated 01.06.2017 while issuing notice
to the Respondents, this Court has stayed the operation of
Condition No.2 of the impugned order . The first appeal
arising out of judgment and decree dated 21.11.2016 is still
pending before the District Judge. Because of the interim
relief, the present petitioners have not given such
undertaking before the Trial Court..
</p><p id="p_6">        [5]   Hence the present petition is disposed of with the
direction to the Additional District Judge to decide the
appeal on its merit, preferably within a period of two
months from today and the Condition No.2 of the impugned
order shall remain stayed till the decision of the appeal.
</p><p id="p_7">        [6]   The petition is disposed of.
