            IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR MODIFICATION OF ORDERS) NO.
                                 4415 of 2014
============================================================
====
          KISHORBHAI CHHITABHAI PARMAR....Applicant(s)
                            Versus
               STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
MR. KSHITIJ P VAKIL, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MS JIRGA JHAVERI, ADDL. PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the Respondent(s)
No. 1
================================================================

        CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.G.SHAH

                               Date : 24/04/2014


                                ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">      Heard learned advocate Mr. K. P. Vakil for the applicant and
learned APP.
</p>

<p id="p_2">      Perused the record as well as police papers produced by the
officer of the ACB office, Vadodara. On perusal of entire record, it
seems that when investigating agency does not want applicant for
remaining investigation, it would be appropriate to modify the
condition No.5 imposed by impugned order dated 28.06.2011
passed by the learned Sessions Judge, Vadodara, by which
applicant    was     ordered    to     mark        his   presence    before   the
Investigating Officer on 1st of every month.
</p>

<p id="p_3">      Therefore, petition is partly allowed. Condition No.5 of the
impugned order dated 28.06.2011 is deleted. All other conditions
will remain in force. Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p>
