                           GA 1624 of 2015
                                With
                           CS 2729 of 1968
                    IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                            ORIGINAL SIDE




                         JAIDEV MULLICK &amp; ORS.

                                  Versus

                         SUBIR MULLICK &amp; ORS.


    BEFORE:

    The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>

<p id="p_1">    Date : 20th July, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">                                             Mr.Sayantan Bose, Adv.
</p><p id="p_4">                                              ..for the plaintiffs.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                         M.rDiddhartha Lahiri, Adv.
</p><p id="p_6">                                  ..for the defendant nos.6(c)&amp;(d).</p><pre id="pre_2">


          The   Court:    Under   the   preliminary    decree     Rebati

Mullick    (since    deceased)    and   Satyanarayan    Mullick    were

entitled to 9/36th       share.

</pre><p id="p_7">     It is submitted on behalf of the legal heirs of the

said deceased that an application for grant of probate is

pending before the City Civil Court. There is no dispute

that out of various properties only one property is left

out to be sold and proceeds thereof to be distributed in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                        2</span>


accordance with the shares determined under the preliminary

decree.
</p>
<p id="p_8">      Mr.Pradip Mitra (Jr.), Advocate, a Member of the Bar

Library Club is appointed as a Receiver over and in respect

of the said property being premises no.155 B, Mahtma Gandhi

Road, Kolkata - 700 007, with a direction to sell the said

property    by   public      auction       upon    consultation    with    the

parties. The Receiver shall collect rent from the tenants

including arrears, if any. The Receiver shall be entitled

to an initial remuneration of 1000GMs. to be paid by the

applicant at the first instance to be adjusted at the time

of distribution of the sale proceeds.

</p><p id="p_9">      This application is disposed of with liberty to apply

for further reliefs.
</p>
<p id="p_10">      The   parties    shall    be     given      liberty    to   participate

during    auction.    This    application         is   disposed   of   without

considering the other prayers. The parties shall be paid

liberty to apply after advertisement.

</p><p id="p_11">      This application accordingly stands disposed of.




</p><p id="p_12">                                                            (SOUMEN SEN, J.)
