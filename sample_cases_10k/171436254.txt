<div class="doc_title">Anil Kumar vs State Of Bihar &amp; Anr on 3 June, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.20714 of 2013 (2) dt.03-06-2013




                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Criminal Miscellaneous No.20714 of 2013
                   ======================================================
                   Anil Kumar S/o Shri Surendra Chauhan, resident of village Amarpur, P.S.
                   Pakriwarawan, District Nawada
                                                                         .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                     Versus
                   1. The State of Bihar
                   2. Sushma Devi W/o Shri Anil Kumar &amp; D/o Shri Manoj Kumar, at
                      present resident of village Jamuawan Beldari, P.S. Wazirganj, District
                      Gaya
                                                                    .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s       : Mr. Madhu Prasun, Advocate
                   For the Opposite Party/s    : Mr. Pramod Kr. Pandey, A.P.P.
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA PRAKASH
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   03-06-2013                  In view of the submission of the Petitioner that he is

                   ready to settle the dispute with the Opposite Party No.2, issue

                   notice to the Opposite Party No.2, for which requisites etc. under

                   registered cover with A/D as well as ordinary process must be

                   filed within 10 days after vacation, failing which this application

                   shall stand rejected without further reference to a Bench.

</p><p id="p_2">                             Notice through ordinary process shall go to the concerned

                   court to be served upon the Counsel appearing on behalf of the

                   Opposite Party.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              Rule returnable six weeks.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                              In the meanwhile, let the petitioner above named be

                   released on provisional anticipatory bail in the event of arrest or

                   surrender before the learned court below within a period of six

                   weeks from the date of receipt/production of a copy of this order

                   in connection with Complaint case No.277 of 2012 on furnishing
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.20714 of 2013 (2) dt.03-06-2013</span>




                      bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the

                      like amount each to the satisfaction of S.D.J.M., Nawada, subject

                      to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_1">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of

                      Criminal Procedure as also condition that one of the bailor will be

                      a close relative of the petitioner who will give an affidavit giving

                      genealogy as to how he is related with the petitioner. The bailor

                      will undertake to furnish information to the Court about any

                      change in address of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   The petitioner volunteers to deposit Rs.500/- (five

                      hundred) per month in the court below firstly within 15 days from

                      the date of receipt of this order and subsequently by 15th every

                      month which the wife of the petitioner will be at liberty to

                      withdraw without prejudice the right of the parties. The court

                      below shall inform the wife of the petitioner about the present

                      order. However, if the Complainant/informant has a Bank account

                      or subsequently opens a Bank account, the money shall be

                      deposited in the account and a receipt to this effect shall be filed

                      by 15th every month in the court below and in case he fails to do so

                      his anticipatory bail would be automatically cancelled.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                    (Anjana Prakash, J)


Narendra/-
</p>
