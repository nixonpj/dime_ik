<div class="doc_title">An Application For Bail Under ... vs In Re.: Khagendra Nath Samanta on 29 March, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                             1


  79.
29.03.2016</pre><p id="p_1">.
</p><p id="p_2">  ap                                 C.R.M. 2364 of 2016
              In the matter of: an application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the
              Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 22.03.2016 in connection with
              Tamluk Police Station Case No. 108 of 2016 under <a href="/doc/1926477/" id="a_2">Sections
              489B</a>/<a href="/doc/257920/" id="a_3">489C</a>/<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_4">120B</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_3">                                              And
              In re.: Khagendra Nath Samanta.           ...... Petitioner

                    Mr. Tapan Dutta Gupta,
                    Mr. Bijoy Bag.                       ... for the petitioner

                    Mr. Dipankar Mahato.                     ..for the State.

                    Heard the learned counsel appearing on behalf of the parties.

                    This is a case, where the petitioner was intercepted on the road

              and from his possession 23 pieces of high quality fake Indian

              currency notes, each of domination of Rs.500, were recovered.

              Thereafter pursuant to his statement, his house was searched and

              from there, four more similar fake notes were recovered.

</p><p id="p_4">                    We have gone through the case diary.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                    Having regard to the nature and seriousness of the allegations

              and materials collected in support of the same and the gravity of the

              offence, in our opinion, this is not a fit case for bail.

</p><p id="p_6">                    Accordingly, the application for bail stands rejected.



</p><p id="p_7">                                                        (Ashim Kumar Roy, J.)



                                                           (C.S. Karnan, J.)
