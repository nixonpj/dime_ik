<div class="doc_title">Mehar Chand Jain vs Keshoram on 6 September, 2013</div>    

 
 
 

<p id="p_1"> IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
AT JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR

D.B. Civil Misc.Application No.146/2013
in
D.B.Civil Special Appeal No.57/2005
Mehar Chand Jain &amp; Ors. vs. Kesho Ram &amp; Ors.
</p>
<p id="p_2">Date of Order : 06.09.2013

HON'BLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. AMITAVA ROY
HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VEERENDR SINGH SIRADHANA


Mr. Rishipal Agarwal, for applicant.
</p><p id="p_3">	Heard Mr.Rishipal Agarwal, learned counsel for the applicant.
</p><p id="p_4">	The instant application is filed to place on record the acceptance of the compliance of the orders dated 05.07.2012 passed by the Hon'ble Apex Court deciding Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s).10100/2008 (Mehar Chand Jain &amp; Ors. vs. Kesho Ram &amp; Ors.) alongwith Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s).14486/2007 (<a href="/doc/132390/" id="a_1">New India Assurance Co.Ltd vs. Mehar Chand Jain &amp; Ors</a>.).  Reference has been made to the order dated 09.10.2012 passed by the coordinate Bench of this Court disposing of D.B.Special Appeal (Civil) No.8/2005 accepting the factum of such compliance vis-a-vis the order dated 05.07.2012 passed in Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14486/2007. The instant application has been filed reiterating the fact of compliance.  
	Upon hearing the learned counsel for the applicant and on a consideration of the recitals made in the application, we accept the averments of the compliance of the order dated 05.07.2012 passed in Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s).10100/2008 (Mehar Chand Jain &amp; Ors. vs. Kesho Ram &amp; Ors.) as well.  
	The application is thus, allowed subject to just exceptions, if any, that may be taken by the other side.   
</p>	 

<pre id="pre_1">(VEERENDR SINGH SIRADHANA),J.          (AMITAVA ROY),C.J.


Mohit  
M/1
</pre><p id="p_5">All corrections made in the judgment/order have been incorporated in the judgment/order being emailed.  Mohit Tak, Jr. P.A.
</p>
