<div class="doc_title">Ajay Kumar vs The State Of Jharkhand on 6 May, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                         B.A. No. 2802 of 2016
              Ajay Kumar                       .... Petitioner
                                 Versus
              The State of Jharkhand                       ... Opposite Party
                                 ---
</pre><p id="p_1">              CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY
</p><p id="p_2">                                 ---
</p><p id="p_3">              For the Petitioner : Ms. Asmita Srivastav, Advocate
              For the State      : Mr. Rakesh Kumar, A. P.P.
</p><p id="p_4">                                 ---
</p><p id="p_5">              Order No. 04                     Dated 06th May 2016

              Heard Mr. Asmita Srivastav, learned counsel appearing for the
     petitioner and Mr. Rakesh Kumar, learned A.P.P., appearing for the
     State.
</p><p id="p_6">              Earlier the prayer for bail of the petitioner was rejected vide order
     dated 03.09.2015 passed in B.A. No. 6559 of 2015.
</p><p id="p_7">              It has been submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioner
     that father of the deceased was examined as P.W. 4 and he was
     declared hostile and that the petitioner is in custody since 22.05.2015.
</p><p id="p_8">              Learned A.P.P. appearing for the State has opposed the prayer
     for bail of the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_9">              In view of the fact that the evidence against the petitioner has
     been diluted on account of deposition of P.W. 4 and considering the
     period of custody of the petitioner, he is directed to be released on bail
     on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand) with two
     sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned Sessions
     Judge, Palamau at Daltonganj in connection with Sessions Trial No. 305
     of 2015 arising out of Hussainabad P.S. Case No. 01 of 2015
     corresponding to G.R. Case No. 03 of 2015.
</p>

<p id="p_10">                                           (RONGON MUKHOPADHYAY, J.)
MK
