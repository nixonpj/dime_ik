<p id="p_1">2   08-11-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the parties.

</p><p id="p_2">                               2.       The petitioner has questioned selection of

                   respondent No. 10 as Anganbari Sevika for Anganbai Center of

                   Ward No. 16 under Gram Panchayat Raj, Meyar, in the District

                   of Nalanda. It is her case that she has better right to be selected

                   against the said post. The petitioner is said to have filed an

                   application before        the Child Development Project Officer,
          Patna High Court CWJC No.19793 of 2019(2) dt.08-11-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                     2/2</span>




                   Noorsarai, Nalanda, questioning the selection in question.

</p><p id="p_3">                               3.       It is the petitioner's grievance that though the

                   said application has been filed in July, 2019, the same has not

                   yet been disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               4. Considering the facts and circumstances of the

                   case, this application is disposed of with a direction that let

                   Child Development Project Officer dispose of the petitioner's

                   application, if filed and still pending, within two months from

                   the date of receipt/production of a copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_5">                               5. This disposes of the application.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                      (Chakradhari Sharan Singh, J)
