<p id="p_1">                                The application sent by Prem Lata to the office of Hon'ble

           the Acting Chief Justice, is treated as criminal writ petition and is

           taken up on judicial side.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                As per the allegations contained in the application, Puja

           daughter of the petitioner has gone disappeared since 09.07.2015.

           The suspicion has been raised against the Chowkidar namely Ved

           Parkash that he has kidnapped the daughter of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_3">                                Today, reply by way of affidavit of Inder Mohan Singh,

           PPS, Senior Superintendent of Police, Fazilka, has been filed. Same

           is taken on record.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                As per reply, FIR No.177 dated 04.09.2015, under

           <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_1">Sections 363</a>, <a href="/doc/1559723/" id="a_2">366-A</a> of IPC, has already been registered at Police

           Station Sadar Fazilka. It is further averred that during investigation

           Chowkidar namely Ved Parkash was found innocent. It is further

           stated that the police has made every attempt to find out the girl and
