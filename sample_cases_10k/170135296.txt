<div class="doc_title">S.Palani vs The Inspector Of Police on 25 January, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

DATED:  25.01.2016

CORAM

THE HON'BLE Mr. JUSTICE  M.M.SUNDRESH

 W.P.No.2444 of 2016

S.Palani								..	Petitioner 

Vs.

The Inspector of Police,
Mugudanchavadi Police Station,
Salem District.						..	Respondent


Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of The Constitution of India praying to issue a writ of mandamus directing the respondent authority to give permission to conduct the cultural programme at Ekkattur Chellandimariamman Koil Thiruvizha on 05.03.2016 by considering the petitioner representation dated 18.01.2016.

		For Petitioner		..	Mr.R.P.Murugan Raja	
		For Respondent		..	Mrs.P.Rajalakshmi, G.A.	
 ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">	Seeking permission  to conduct cultural programme on 05.03.2016 at  Chellandimariamman Koil Thiruvizha, Ekkattur, Edakanasalai Post, Sangagiri Taluk, Salem District, the petitioner made a representation dated 18.01.2016 to the respondent. As the same has not been considered, the present writ petition has been filed.
</p><p id="p_2">	2.	Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the learned  Government Advocate appearing for the respondent.
</p><p id="p_3">	3.	Learned Government Advocate, on instructions, would submit that there is likelihood of law and order problem.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4.	A mere apprehension cannot be a ground to reject the representation. There is absolutely no material before this Court to hold that there will be a law and order problem in  the event of permission being granted. In such view of the matter, the  writ petition is allowed and the petitioner is permitted to conduct cultural programme on 05.03.2016 at Chellandimariamman Koil Thiruvizha, Ekkattur, Edakanasalai Post, Sangagiri Taluk, Salem District, however, with the following conditions:-
</p><p id="p_5">	(a) The cultural programme in connection with  temple Festival at Chellandimariamman Koil Thiruvizha, Ekkattur, Edakanasalai Post, Sangagiri Taluk, Salem District, be held on 05.03.2016 between 06.00 p.m and 10.30 p.m.

</p><p id="p_6">	(b)There should not be any kind of obscene dance or vulgar dialogues during the performance, by anyone of the participants.
</p>
<p id="p_7">	(c)Double meaning songs should not be played so as to spoil the minds of students and youths.
</p>
<p id="p_8">	(d)No dance or songs, touching upon any political party or religion or community or caste be played.
</p>
<p id="p_9">	(e)No flex boards in support of any political party or communal leader be erected.
</p>
<p id="p_10">	(f)The function should not affect either religious or communal harmony and shall be conducted without any discrimination based on caste.
</p>
<p id="p_11">	(g)If there is any violation of any one of the conditions imposed, the concerned police officer is at liberty to take necessary action, as per law and stop such performance; and

</p><p id="p_12">	(h)Similarly, the police is entitled to stop the dance programme, if it exceeds beyond the permitted time.
</p>
<p id="p_13">	  (i)The respondent is directed to issue necessary permission, incorporating the above conditions.
		No costs.
</p>						
<p id="p_14">								 25.01.2016

Index:Yes/No

Note:  Issue order copy today (25.01.2016)
sgl


To
The Inspector of Police,
Mugudanchavadi Police Station,
Salem District.
</p>

<p id="p_15">M.M.SUNDRESH, J.,
sgl












 W.P.No.2444 of 2016
