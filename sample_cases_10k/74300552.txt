IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH.



                             CRM No. M-24317 of 2011

                             Date of Decision : September 20, 2011


Jagjit Singh
                                                       ....   PETITIONER

                             Vs.


State of Punjab
                                                       ..... RESPONDENT



CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH

                     *   *   *

Present :      Mr. Harkeerat Singh, Advocate,
               for the petitioner.

               Mr. P.S.Grewal, DAG, Punjab.


                     *   *   *


AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH, J. (ORAL)
</pre>


<p id="p_1">               Counsel for the petitioner contends that the petitioner is in

custody since 01.04.2011. He further contends that for the offence, which

the petitioner is alleged to have committed, the maximum sentence

provided is six months, out of which, he has already undergone more than

five months. He, therefore, prays for grant of bail.

</p><p id="p_2">               Counsel for the State, on instructions from ASI Amrik Singh,

Police Station Dakha, District Ludhiana, submits that the challan has been

presented in the case.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> CRM No. M-24317 of 2011                                      2</span><p id="p_3">



</p><p id="p_4">              In view of the facts that the petitioner is in custody since

01.04.2011 and has already undergone more than five months, this petition

is allowed.    The petitioner is directed to be released on bail to the

satisfaction of the trial Court.</p><pre id="pre_2">




                                   (AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH )
September 20, 2011                          JUDGE
