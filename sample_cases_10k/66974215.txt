                                    Afroza Bibi &amp; Anr.
                                              Vs.
                                       Nasir Khan

                           Mr. Biswajit Hazra
                                     ..for the Petitioners

                           Mr. Partha Pratim Roy
                                     ..for the Opposite Party


</pre><p id="p_1">                         The grievance of the petitioning defendants is

                  that a second application for mandatory injunction

                  under Section 151 of the Code has been filed by the

                  plaintiff and an order obtained thereon despite an

                  application under Order XXXIX Rule 2A of the Code

                  remaining pending for the perceived violation of the

                  previous order of mandatory injunction granted by the

                  trial court. The present petition is directed against the

                  appellate court affirming the ad interim mandatory

                  injunction granted by the trial court.

</p><p id="p_2">                         Since two courts have taken the same view and

                  the order impugned herein gives cogent grounds in

                  support thereof, there does not appear to be any

                  illegality   or     material     irregularity   with   the   same

                  warranting          interference       in   this   extraordinary

                  jurisdiction.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                         However, since the application under Order

                  XXXIX Rule 2A of the Code is pending before the trial

                  court, the trial court is requested to ensure that the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            2</span>



 same is taken up and disposed of as expeditiously as

 the    business      of   that    court    would     permit     and,

 preferably, within a period of three months from the

 date of receipt of a copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_4">         C.O. 2008 of 2014 is disposed of without any

 order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_5">         Urgent certified website copies of this order, if applied for,

be made available to the parties upon compliance of the requisite

formalities.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                    (Sanjib Banerjee, J.)
