<pre id="pre_1">                                            1




18.12.13

                              W. P. 35409 (W) of 2013


           Mr. Sourav Mitra                     ... For the Petitioner



                 Affidavit of service filed in Court today be kept on record.

                 This writ petition is filed by the writ petitioner for a

           direction upon the respondent authorities to pay interest on</pre>

<p id="p_1">           gratuity for belated payment of the same.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                 Learned   counsel    appearing     for   the   writ   petitioner

           produced a copy of the judgment delivered by a learned Single

           Judge of this Hon'ble Court in W. P. 10750 (W) of 2007, wherein

           after considering various judgements along with a Division

           Bench judgement delivered in FMA 720 to 725 of 2004 and FMA

           731 of 2004, Hon'ble Division Bench of this Court directed the

           authority to pay 10% interest per annum for delayed period. He

           further submits that the law is now well settled. The writ

           petitioner is entitled to get interest @ 10% per annum, which

           was agreed by the learned Counsel for the State before the

           learned Single Judge.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                 Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that same

           order may be passed in this case.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and considered

           the submissions made by him. Now, it is settled that the writ

           petitioner is entitled to get interest against delayed payment of

           gratuity.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                 However, considering the facts and circumstances of this

           case and also taking note of the judgement delivered by the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 2</span>




learned Single Judge as well as the Hon'ble Division Bench of

this Court, I direct the Director of Pension, Provident Fund and

Group Insurance, Government of West Bengal, as also the

concerned Treasury Officer to pay 8% interest per annum on the

gratuity amount in favour of the writ petitioner for the delayed

period starting from the date of retirement till the date of

making payment of gratuity amount. The entire exercise shall be

completed within a period of four weeks from the date of receipt

of a copy of this order failing which additional 2% interest per

annum shall be paid to the writ petition by the concerned

respondent authorities.
</p>
<p id="p_6">      With the above direction, the writ petition is disposed of.

      Urgent certified photocopy of this order, if applied for, be

given to the learned advocates for the parties.




</p><p id="p_7">                          (Ashoke Kumar Dasadhikari, J.)
