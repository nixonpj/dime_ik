<div class="doc_title">Anand Prakash vs The State Of Bihar on 12 December, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.18911 of 2012 (6) dt.12-12-2012




                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.18911 of 2012
                   ======================================================
                   Anand Prakash
                                                                      .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                   The State Of Bihar
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                   SINGH
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">06/   12-12-2012                         Heard learned counsels for the petitioner

                          and the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                         The petitioner is apprehending his arrest

                          in a case registered for the offences punishable under

                          <a href="/doc/1667388/" id="a_1">Sections 419</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a> of the Indian Penal

                          Code and 712/73 of Information and <a href="/doc/1965344/" id="a_6">Technology

                          Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                         It is alleged against the petitioner that by

                          forging the digital signature of the informant, the

                          informant was removed from the post of Director of

                          the company and the share of the informant was

                          transferred by the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                                         It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                          petitioner that the supervising officer has not found

                          the accusation true and a statement has been made in

                          para 38 that the petitioner has no criminal
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.18911 of 2012 (6) dt.12-12-2012</span>




                    antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   Let the learned court below consider the

                    regular bail of the petitioner, if the petitioner

                    surrenders before the learned court below within a

                    period of six weeks from today in connection with

                    Kotwali P.S. Case No. 204 of 2010 pending in the

                    court of learned CJM, Patna.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   With the aforesaid observation, this

                    application is disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                   Let the order be transmitted to the

                    learned court below through fax at the cost of the

                    petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                                  (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J.)
DKS/
 </p>
