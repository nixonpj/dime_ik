<p id="p_2">              Heard learned Counsel for the parties.


</p><p id="p_3">      2.      The contempt as alleged in the present petition is of the order

      dated 26 October 2018 passed by this Court in the aforesaid petition, to

      the extent of the order as made in paragraph 2 of the said order, which

      reads thus:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">               "2.    The learned Advocate for the Respondents on
               instructions from the partner of Respondent NO.1 who is
               present in Court, undertakes to provide bank guarantee in
               the sum of Rs.2.50 Crores within a period of 8 weeks from
               today. He further undertakes on instructions, to pay the
               entire dues of Reliance Home Finance Ltd., on or before 30 th
               November, 2018 independently and not by creating any third
               party rights in respect of any of the flats or project. The
               undertakings are accepted."
</blockquote>
