<div class="doc_title">Smt Narangi &amp; Anr vs State &amp; Ors on 18 November, 2017</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
             S.B. Criminal Misc(Pet.) No. 4108 / 2017
</p><p id="p_2">1. Smt Narangi Wife of Rajendra Kumar, D/o Shri Dungar Das,
Aged About 18 Years

</p><p id="p_3">2. Rajendra Kumar Son of Vaja Ram, Aged About 26 Years, Both
by Caste Meghwal, Residents of Meghwalo Ka Bass, Village Banta,
Tehsil Marwar Junction, District Pali.
</p><p id="p_4">                                                         ----Petitioners
                                  Versus
</p><p id="p_5">1. The State of Rajasthan Through the Secretary, Department of
Home Affairs, Govt of Rajasthan, Jaipur

</p><p id="p_6">2. Superintendent of Police, Pali.
</p>
<p id="p_7">3. SHO of Marwar Junction Police Station, District Pali.

</p><p id="p_8">4. Dungar Das Son Shri Ganesh Das, By Caste Mali, Resident of
Pichka Dhal, Village Banta, Tehsil Marwar Junction, District Pali.
</p><p id="p_9">                                                       ----Respondents
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)    : Mr. Kaushal Sharma.
For Respondent(s) : Mr. VS Rajpurohit PP.
_____________________________________________________
     HON'BLE DR. JUSTICE PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI
                                  Order
18/11/2017

</p><p id="p_10">1.         The      petitioners    have    preferred    this   criminal

misc.petition under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. for issuance of necessary

directions to the official respondents to provide adequate security

and protection to the petitioners on the ground that they are

facing grave threat of life and liberty at the hands of respondents

No.4 and their relatives, in view of solemnization of their marriage

with each other on 13.10.2017.
</p>
<p id="p_11">2.         After hearing the learned counsel for the parties and on
                                  (2 of 2)
                                                         [CRLMP-4108/2017]

an overall consideration of the facts and circumstances of the

case, this Court deems it proper to direct the petitioners to appear

before the Superintendent of Police, Pali, who shall hear the

grievance of the petitioners, and if necessitated, provide adequate

security and protection to the petitioners. It is reflected from the

Annexure-1- Adhaar Card produced by the learned counsel for the

petitioner that the girl is major.
</p>
<p id="p_12">3.         Consequently,      the     present   misc.petition    stands

disposed of. The stay application also stands disposed of.




</p><p id="p_13">                               (DR. PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI)J.
</p>
