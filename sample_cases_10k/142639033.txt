<p id="p_1">                               Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that though there is

           undoubtedly fault with the petitioner in not leading defence evidence

           despite so many opportunities granted, however, some of the adjournments

           were on account of the fact that correct address of the person sought to be

           summoned was not available with the petitioner and on one or two

           occasions, it was simply adjourned on account of paucity of time etc.

                               Though no fault, whatsoever, can be found with the impugned

           orders, however, in the interest of justice, only one opportunity is granted to

           the petitioner to lead all his defence evidence on a date convenient to the

           trial court, subject to payment of costs of Rs.5,000/-.

</p><p id="p_2">                               The petition is disposed of, accordingly.
