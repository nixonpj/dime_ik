<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 10
 

 
Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 132 of 2013
 

 
Applicant :- Sh.Amrendra Singh
 
Opposite Party :- Central Bureau Of Investigation Through D.S.P.,C.B.I.&amp; Ors.
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Vikash Bhatnagar,Ashok Bhatnagar
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- A. Khanna,Ashish Agrawal
 

 
Hon'ble Arun Tandon,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Hon'ble Rajul Bhargava,J.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">Sri V.M. Zaidi, learned Senior Advocate assisted by Sri Vikash Bhatnagar, learned Advocate on behalf of the applicant states that the applicant shall make an application for discharge before the trial court at the first instance, inasmuch as it is his case that he has only tendered legal opinion and that if the opinion was not acceptable to the Bank, it was open to it to refuse to act upon the same. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">We need not enter into the merits of the case set up by the applicant, as he wishes to approach the trial court for discharge at the first instance.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">The present application is accordingly rejected with liberty to the applicant to approach the trial by making an appropriate application for discharge.
</p> 

 
<p id="p_5">(Rajul Bhargava, J.)                           (Arun Tandon, J.)
 
Order Date :- 13.1.2017
 
Sushil/-
</p>
