<div class="doc_title">Crja No. 27 Of 2013 vs Unknown on 30 July, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">CRJA No. 27 of 2013
Hon'ble Barin Ghosh, C.J.
Hon'ble Servesh Kumar Gupta, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">       Mr. Amit Kapri, Brief Holder for the State of Uttarakhand
/ respondent.
</p><p id="p_2">       This is a Jail Appeal, which has been filed 57 days' after
expiry of the time to prefer the appeal. It appears that the appeal
has been preferred by the appellant from jail without any legal
assistance. Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the State
does not oppose condonation of delay of 57 days in preferring
the appeal. The request to register the appeal is treated as an
Application for condonation of delay and, being satisfied with
the reason for the delay, as indicated above, we allow the
Application for condonation of delay in preferring the appeal.
</p><p id="p_3">       Admit.
</p><p id="p_4">       Let the lower court records be called for. Let steps be
taken for preparation of paper-books no sooner the records are
received.
</p><p id="p_5">       Since the appellant has not been able to engage any
counsel, we appoint Mr. U.S. Bhakuni, Advocate, as Amicus
Curiae to conduct the case on behalf of the appellant.
</p><p id="p_6">       It shall be open to the parties for mentioning the appeal
for fixation of an early date of hearing.
</p>



<p id="p_7">(Servesh Kumar Gupta, J.) (Barin Ghosh, C.J.)
                  30.07.2013
G
