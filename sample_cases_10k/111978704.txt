<p id="p_1">	Learned advocate Ms.Ketty
A. Mehta for opponents no.5 to 5.3, and 6 to 18 invites attention of
the Court to Annexure R-1, viz. Further Affidavit filed by one Barjis
Dinsha Bhatporia, respondent no.7 herein, affirmed on 10.04.2013,
wherein it is stated that the property is transferred by  Barjis
Dinsha Bhatporia and five other persons mentioned therein. The
learned advocate further states that the property is also sold to
three other persons.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_2">2.	The learned advocate
states that the first part of the order about setting out a provision
in the  sale deed that the sale is subject to pending litigation was
complied with, but inadvertently this fact was not placed on record
before the learned Judge before whom the suit is pending.  Even that
part is complied with. Adjourned to 04.10.2013.  
</p>
