<p id="p_1">42    29-08-2013             Mr. Ratan Kumar Sinha, the learned counsel for the

                    respondents submits that earlier appeal notice was issued to

                    respondent nos. 7(a) to 7(c) and according to his instruction the

                    notices have been validly served on those respondents, but the

                    service report has not been attached with the records. It has further

                    been submitted by Mr. Sinha that respondent nos. 7(a) to 7(c) are

                    purchasers from the defendant-appellants and they have neither

                    appeared in the court below nor contested the suit.

</p><p id="p_2">                             The learned counsel for the appellants has also not

                    contested the aforesaid submission and has urged that this old

                    appeal be heard on merit.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             Put up this matter after a week under the same heading with

                    the office report regarding the service report of respondent nos.

                    7(a) to 7(c), along with the records of F.A.No.305 of 1985.

</p><pre id="pre_2">B.Roy/-                                                (V. Nath, J)
