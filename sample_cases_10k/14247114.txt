<div class="doc_title">Ripu Shrivastava @ Ripu Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 9 January, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.772 of 2015
                 ======================================================
                 Ripu Shrivastava @ Ripu Kumar
                                                                      .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                  Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Dhurendra Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Anant Kumar(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   09-01-2015               Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                             The    petitioner    is   languishing     in   custody     since

                 27.11.2013 in a case registered for the offences punishable under

                 <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_1">Sections 395</a> and <a href="/doc/1535430/" id="a_2">328</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                             The accusation is of robbing the truck loaded with fish.

                 Name of the petitioner sprang up when the petitioner confessed his

                 involvement       during custody in Pipra P.S. Case No. 203 of

                 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             It is submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioner

                 that there is no recovery from the petitioner nor the petitioner has

                 been put on test identification parade. Though the petitioner is

                 accused in two other cases but statement has been made in

                 paragraph 3 of the petition that the petitioner is on bail in those

                 cases.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Considering the fact that the petitioner has not been put
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.772 of 2015 (2) dt.09-01-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                      on test identification parade till date, let the above named

                      petitioner be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/-

                      (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                      satisfaction of learned JM Ist Class, Sadar, East Champaran at

                      Motihari in connection with Trial No. 5385 of 2014 arising out of

                      Kesariya P.S. Case No. 189 of 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                                 (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Anil/-
</p>
