<div class="doc_title">Satnamsingh vs Panchayat And Rural Development ... on 11 June, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                     HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH BENCH AT INDORE
                                    W.P.No.10009/2018
                Indore
                11.06.2018

                       Shri Arvind Sharma, learned Counsel for the petitioners.
                       Shri Mukesh Parwal, learned GA for the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">                       Heard on IA No. 2386/2018 an application for early hearing and IA No.
                2387/2018 an application for hearing during vacation.
</p><p id="p_2">                       In view of the above, both the applications are allowed and disposed of.
                       Heard on IA No. 2385/2018 an application for grant of interim relief.
                       By order dt. 8.5.2018 this Court has declined the exparte interim relief.
                       Being aggrieved by the aforesaid order, the writ petitioners preferred an
                Writ Appeal No.634/2018. By order dt. 21.5.2018 the Division Bench of this
                Court has set-aside the order dt. 8.5.2018 and directed that till the next date of
                hearing before the writ court the respondents will not reduce the salary of the
                appellants. In number of identical writ petitions, the interim relief has already
                been granted by this Court.
</p><p id="p_3">                       In order to maintain parity as well as keeping in view the order passed
                by the Division Bench dt. 21.5.2018 passed in WA No. 634/2018 till the next
                date of hearing, the respondents are restrained from reducing the salary of the
                petitioners while giving effect to the recommendation of 6th Pay Commission.
</p><p id="p_4">                       CC as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                                      (Vivek Rusia)
                                                                      Vacation Judge



M.Jilla.
</p>
