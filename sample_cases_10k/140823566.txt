<div class="doc_title">Jasuben Chhaganbhai Aud vs State Of Gujarat &amp; 2 on 25 April, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">     C/SCA/5730/2014                                                                                                                                        ORDER



      IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
                             SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION  NO. 5730 of 2014

=============================================
</pre><p id="p_1">                                  JASUBEN CHHAGANBHAI AUD....Petitioner(s)
                                                 Versus
                                   STATE OF GUJARAT  &amp;  2....Respondent(s)
=============================================
Appearance:
</p><p id="p_2">MR SHIVGAR P GOSAI, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
MR JANAK RAVAL, AGP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
=============================================

                     CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI
 
                                                              Date : 25/04/2014
 
                                                                   ORAL ORDER


</p><p id="p_3">1.            By way of the present petition filed under Articles 14, 19 and 226 
              of the Constitution of India, the petitioner has prayed as under:
</p>
<p id="p_4">             "[A] This petition may kindly be admitted.
</p>
<p id="p_5">              [B]           This Hon'ble Court may be pleased to issue a writ of or in 
                            the nature of certiorari or any other appropriate writ, order 
                            or   direction   quashing   and   setting   aside   the   order   dated 
                            16.06.2013   passed   by   respondent   No.   1   Annexure   'A'   in 
                            renewal application of the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                          Your   Lordship   be   pleased   to   issue   a   writ   of 
                            mandamus   or   issue   any   other   writ   order   or   direction   in 
                            nature of mandamus revision application shall be heard and 
                            to be disposed of within 8 weeks or before from the date of 
                            order of Hon'ble Court.
</p>
<p id="p_7">              [C]           Such other(s) and further relief(s) which this Hon'ble Court 
                            may deem fit to be granted in the interest of justice"
</p>


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                                          Page 1 of 2</span>
<p id="p_8">      C/SCA/5730/2014                                                                                                                                        ORDER




</p><p id="p_9">2.            It is the case of the petitioner that respondent No. 3 - Collector, 
              Surat has passed order rejecting the renewal application filed by 
              the   petitioner.   Thereafter,   the   petitioner   has   filed   revision 
              application before respondent No. 1 dated 12.07.2011, which is 
              pending. Therefore, respondent No. 1 be directed to decide  the 
              application as expeditiously as possible.
</p>
<p id="p_10">3.            Considering the above, the following order would meet the end of 
              justice.
</p>
<p id="p_11">              "[a]          Respondent No. 1 is hereby directed to decide the revision 
                            application   dated   12.07.2011   as   expeditiously   as   possible 
                            preferably within a period of 12 months from the date of 
                            receipt pf this order.
</p>
<p id="p_12">4.            In   view   of   the   above   direction,   Mr.   Shivgar   P.   Gosai,   learned 
              advocate for the petitioner does not press this petition and seeks 
              permission   to   withdraw   the   present   petition.   Permission,   as 
              prayed   for,   as   granted.   The   present   petition   is   disposed   of   as 
              withdrawn. Direct service is permitted.
</p>


<p id="p_13">                                                                                                                                    (A.J.DESAI, J.) 
*Kazi...
</p>
