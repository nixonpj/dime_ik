<div class="doc_title">Karamshi Varsubhai Rabari vs State Of Gujarat on 18 April, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: V.P. Patel</div>

<pre id="pre_1">        R/CR.RA/446/2019                              ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

        R/CRIMINAL REVISION APPLICATION NO. 446 of 2019

================================================================
                      KARAMSHI VARSUBHAI RABARI
                                Versus
                          STATE OF GUJARAT
================================================================
Appearance:
MR NK MAJMUDAR(430) for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MS. C.M.SHAH, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
================================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE V.P. PATEL

                            Date : 18/04/2019

                             ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1. Rule. Learned APP Ms. C.M.Shah appears and waives service of
notice of Rule on behalf of Opponent - State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2. The Applicant (Original Accused) has filed this Criminal Revision
Application under Section 397 read with <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 401</a> of the Code of
Criminal Procedure, 1973 for grant of bail during the pendency of
Criminal Revision Application.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3. The Applicant / Accused is charged with having committed
offence under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Sections 406</a>, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">465</a>, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5">467</a>, <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_6">471</a> read with <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_7">Section
120B</a> of the the Indian Penal Code for which complaint has been
lodged being I-CR No. 93/2013 with Kathlal Police Station, Kheda.
The learned Judicial Magistrate First Class, Kathlal, Dist; Kheda
vide order dated 27.04.2018 has convicted the Applicant / Accused
in Criminal Case No.1077 of 2013 and Criminal Case No.17 of
2015, which is confirmed by learned 4th Additional Sessions Judge,
Kheda vide order dated 08.04.2019 in Criminal Appeal No.55 of
2018 and Criminal Appeal No. 59 of 2018 for the offences as stated



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 Page 1 of 3</span>
           R/CR.RA/446/2019                                   ORDER



above.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4. Heard learned Advocate Mr. N.K.Majmudar for the Applicant
and learned APP Ms. C.M.Shah for the Opponent - State of Gujarat.
</p>

<p id="p_5">Submissions of the Parties:
</p><p id="p_6">5. Learned Advocate for the Applicant has submitted that the
Applicant is imposed sentence for a fixed term. That the Applicant
is having immovable properties. That the Applicant is not likely to
flee away. That the hearing of the Criminal Revision Application
will take time. That the there is a prima facie case of acquittal
against    the    Applicant.   Learned     Advocate   for   the   Applicant
therefore prayed to grant bail to the Applicant by imposing certain
conditions as may be deemed fit by the Hon'ble Court. Learned
Advocate for Applicant has relied upon certain judgments.
</p>

<p id="p_7">6. Learned APP resisted the Application. She submitted that the
Applicant Accused is convicted for 3 years imprisonment.               She
therefore prayed to reject the Application.
</p>

<p id="p_8">7. Considering the ratio laid down by the Hon'ble Apex Court in a
judgments in case of <a href="/doc/193152/" id="a_8">Kashmira Singh v. The State of Punjab</a>,
reported in (1997) 4 SCC 291 and <a href="/doc/1766031/" id="a_9">Bhagwan Rama Shinde
Gosai &amp; Ors. v. State of Gujarat</a>, reported in (1999) 4 SCC 421,
the contents made in the Application, the arguments by learned
Advocate for the Applicant and learned APP for the Opponent -
State of Gujarat and also the facts and circumstances of the case
and in the interest of justice, the prayer in terms of para 7 (D) and
(E) is granted.
</p>

<p id="p_9">8. Accordingly, pending the Criminal Revision Application, the
execution of sentence under impugned order is hereby stayed. The
Applicant is ordered to be released on bail in connection with I-CR



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                 Page 2 of 3</span>
          R/CR.RA/446/2019                                  ORDER



No. 93/2013 registered with Kathlal Police Station on his executing
a bond of Rs.25,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand) with one solvent
surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the learned Trial
Court and subject to the conditions that he shall:
</p><p id="p_10">(a) not take undue advantage of his liberty or abuse his liberty.
</p><p id="p_11">(b) maintain law and order and not to indulge in any criminal
activities.
</p><p id="p_12">(c) furnish the address of his residence to the Investigating Officer
and also to the Court at the time of execution of the bond and shall
not change his residence without prior permission of the Trial
Court.
</p><p id="p_13">(d) furnish the list of his immovable properties, whether self-
acquired or ancestral with description, location and value of such
property on an affidavit before the learned Trial court.
</p><p id="p_14">(e) not to leave territory of India without prior permission of the
learned Trial Court.
</p><p id="p_15">(f) provide their contact numbers as well as the contact numbers of
the sureties before the learned Trial Court.
</p><p id="p_16">(g) surrender their passport, if any, to the lower Court, within a
week.
</p>

<p id="p_17">9. If breach of any of the above conditions is committed, the
concerned Sessions Judge will be free to issue warrant or take
appropriate action in the matter.
</p>

<p id="p_18">10.Bail before the lower Court having jurisdiction to try the case.
Rule is made absolute. Direct service permitted.
</p>



<p id="p_19">                                                      (V. P. PATEL,J)
J.N. W
