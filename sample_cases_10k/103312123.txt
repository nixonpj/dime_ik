                        AT CHANDIGARH

Sr. No.121
                                         CRM NO. M-34972 of 2016
                                         DECIDED ON: October 03, 2016



NARINDER SINGH
                                                             ..PETITIONER

                                     VERSUS


STATE OF PUNJAB AND OTHERS
                                                           ...RESPONDENTS


CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE JASPAL SINGH


Present:     Mr. H.S. Kathpal, Advocate,
             for the petitioner.

             *****


JASPAL SINGH, J. (ORAL)
</pre>

<p id="p_1">             This is a petition preferred under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. seeking

direction to respondent Nos.1 to 3 to protect the life and liberty of the

petitioner and his family members at the hands of private respondents.

</p><p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioner contends that though

representation dated August 02, 2016 (Annexure P-4) was moved to Senior

Superintendent of Police, Gurdaspur, but till date no action has been taken

by the concerned authorities. Learned counsel further contends that he feels

satisfied in case a direction is issued to respondent No.2-Senior

Superintendent of Police, Gurdaspur (Punjab) to decide his representation

dated August 02, 2016 (Annexure P-4) within a stipulated period.

</p><p id="p_3">             Notice of motion.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                1 of 2
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             ::: Downloaded on - 08-10-2016 03:24:43 :::</span>
 CRM NO. M-34972 of 2016                                              --2--

</pre><p id="p_4">             At the asking of Court, Mr. RPS Sidhu, AAG, Punjab accepts

notice on behalf of respondent-Punjab. Copy of the petition be supplied to

learned State counsel during the course of day.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             The instant petition is disposed of, with a direction to

respondent No.2- Senior Superintendent of Police, Gurdaspur (Punjab) to

look into the grievances unfolded by the petitioner in his representation

dated August 02, 2016 (Annexure P-4) and if some substance is found, to

take an appropriate action in accordance with law within a period of three

months from the date of receipt of a certified copy of this order.
