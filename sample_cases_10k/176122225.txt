IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA AT BENGALURU

   DATED THIS THE 06TH DAY OF FEBRUARY, 2018

                       BEFORE

 THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SREENIVAS HARISH KUMAR

                M.S.A.NO.44 OF 2017

BETWEEN:

The Land Acquisition Officer
Represented by Commissioner,
Karnataka Housing Board
Cauvery Bhavan, Bengaluru-9
Represented by authorized officer
AEE, Kolar                                  ...Appellant

(By Sri Raghavendra A Kulkarni, Advocate)

AND:

1. P.Gopalappa S/o Ramaswamy
   Dead by his LRS
   a) P.G.Somashekar
      S/o P.Gopalappa
      Age: 51 years,
      Occ: Agriculturist
      R/o Thotalapalya
      Mulbagal Town
      Kolar District-563101            ... Respondent

      This MSA is filed under <a href="/doc/85494952/" id="a_1">Section 54(2)</a> of the Land
Acquisition Act, 1894 against the order dated
26.08.2015 passed in M.A.No.37/2013 on the file of the
I Additional District and Sessions Judge, Kolar, partly
allowing the appeal and setting aside the judgment and
                             2


decree dated 28.08.2013 passed in LAC No.9/2006 on
the file of the II Additional Senior Civil Judge and JMFC,
Kolar, Itinerating at Mulbagal partly allowing the
reference petition filed under <a href="/doc/1517117/" id="a_2">Section 18</a> of the Land
Acquisition Act for enhanced compensation.

     This appeal coming on for orders this day, the
Court made the following:

                        ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Appellant's counsel files a memo stating that the

award passed by the trial Court in LAC No.9/2006 has

been complied with by depositing the amount of

Rs.5,20,040/- before the executing Court and therefore,

the appellant has sought for withdrawal of the appeal.


</p><p id="p_2">     Placing the memo on record, appeal is dismissed

as withdrawn.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">     Refund Court fee as prayed for.




</p><p id="p_5">                                       Sd/-
</p><p id="p_6">                                    JUDGE
