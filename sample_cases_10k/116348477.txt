                                             ..........MANAGEMENT

      Date of institution of the case    : 22.05.2014
      Date for which Award reserved :  13.10.2014
      Date of passing the Award        :   13.10.2014

 Ref No. F.24 (56)/14/SWD/Lab./2729­33 dated 01.04.2014

A W A R D
          Having satisfied regarding existence of an industrial dispute 

between the parties, the Dy. Labour Commissioner,  Government of NCT 


LIR No. 218/2014                                            Page No. 1/2
 of Delhi in exercise of powers conferred by section 10(1)(c) and 12 (5) of 

the   <a href="/doc/500379/" id="a_1">Industrial   Disputes   Act</a>,   1947   (hereinafter   referred   as   'Act')   with 

Labour Department Notification No. F­1/31/616/ESTT./2008/7458 dated 

3rd  March 2009, referred the present dispute to   this Labour Court for 

adjudication with the following terms of reference:

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">       "Whether   Sh.   Gudu   S/o   Sh.   Ram   Pal   tendered   his  
       resignation voluntarily or his services have been illegally  
       and/or unjustifiably terminated by the management, and if  
       yes, to what relief is he entitled?"
</blockquote>
<p id="p_4">2.             Notice of reference was sent to the workman who neither 

appeared in person nor anybody on his behalf appeared despite service of 

notice, nor statement of claim was filed, despite opportunity given.



</p><p id="p_5">3.             It was thus, presumed that workman was not interested in 

pursuing   his   claim   as   he   has   no   dispute   with   the   management   and 

therefore "No Dispute Award" is passed. 

</p><p id="p_6">       Reference is answered accordingly.   Copies of this award be sent 

for publication and case file be consigned to Record Room. 

ANNOUNCED IN OPEN COURT
ON 13th Day of October 2014
                                         (SANJAY SHARMA)
                      PRESIDING OFFICER LABOUR COURT­XIX
                              KARKARDOOMA COURTS, DELHI
