ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">	The revision petitioner herein has come forward with this Civil Revision Petition challenging the impugned order dated 07.12.2013 made in I.A.No.519 of 2013 in C.M.A.No.56 of 2013 on the file of the Sub Court, Poonamallee, dismissing the interim injunction application in respect of second item of the suit property.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2.  The learned counsel appearing for the revision petitioner submitted that the revision petitioner herein as plaintiff filed O.S.No.403 of 2013 on the file of the District Munsif Court, Ambattur, for permanent injunction in respect of second item of suit property, i.e. S.No.253/9.  At the time of filing the suit, the plaintiff/revision petitioner herein also filed I.A.No.1411 of 2013 in O.S.No.403 of 2013 on the file of the District Munsif Court at Ambattur, for interim injunction, wherein, interim injunction had been granted in respect of the first item of the suit property, i.e. S.No.253/7, measuring to an extent of 1 acre, 19 cents and the injunction application was dismissed in respect of the second item of suit property, i.e. S.No.253/9, against which, the plaintiff/revision petitioner herein preferred C.M.A.No.56 of 2013 along with I.A.No.519 of 2013 in C.M.A.No.56 of 2013 for Ad-interim injunction on the file of the Subordinate Judge at Poonamallee, but, the said application in I.A.No.519 of 2013 in C.M.A.No.56 of 2013 was dismissed, against which, the present Civil Revision Petition has been preferred by the revision petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.  At the time of arguments, it is represented by the learned counsel appearing for both sides that in the suit, the written statement was filed, issues were framed and now, the suit is ripe for trial, hence, they wants early disposal of the suit without touching the facts of the case.
</p>

<p id="p_4">	4. According to the learned counsel appearing for both sides, the disputed item of property is S.No.253/9, wherein, no injunction has been granted.  
</p>
<p id="p_5">	5. Considering the requests made by the learned counsel appearing for both sides, the trial Court is directed to dispose of the suit in O.S.No.403 of 2013 within one month from the date of receipt of a copy of this order after giving fair opportunity to both sides.  
</p>
<p id="p_6">	6.  Accordingly, this Civil Revision Petition is dismissed.  No costs.  Consequently, connected miscellaneous petitions are closed.
</p>
