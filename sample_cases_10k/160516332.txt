<div class="doc_title">Shah Enterprises vs State Of Gujarat &amp; 2 on 26 June, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">         C/SCA/10278/2015                                ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

            SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 10278 of 2015

==========================================================
                   SHAH ENTERPRISES....Petitioner(s)
                               Versus
                 STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 2....Respondent(s)
==========================================================
Appearance:
MR.CHIRAG K SUKHWANI, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
MR. PRANAV TRIVEDI, AGP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
==========================================================

        CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N.V.ANJARIA

                            Date : 26/06/2015


                             ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">      The petitioner has filed this petition praying to set aside order
dated 14.11.2011 passed in Arbitration Reference No. 109 of 1998 by
the Gujarat Public Works Contracts Disputes Arbitration Tribunal,
Ahmedabad, whereby the Tribunal disposed of the said Reference
before it holding that it was covered by the Supreme Court decision in
V.A. Tech Esher Wyas Flovel Limited vs. Madhya Pradesh
State Electricity board and anr. [2011 (13) SCCC 261] and that
according to the said decision, the Arbitration Reference was not
maintainable before the Tribunal.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.    Learned advocate for the petitioner Mr. Chirag Sukhwani
submitted that the said decision in V.A. Tech Esher Wyas Flovel
Limited (supra) has been subsequently held to be per incurium in
<a href="/doc/166421160/" id="a_1">Madhya Pradesh Rural Road Development Authority and
another vs. L. G. Chaudhary Engineers and Contractors</a> [2012
(3) SCC 495]. In the said subsequent decision, it was held that the
Tribunal has jurisdiction to entertain the Reference. Learned advocate
for the petitioner further relied on order dated 26.06.2014 passed in
Special Civil Application No. 2632 of 2012, as well as another order
dated 23.04.2015 in Special Civil Application No. 1086 of 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.    Learned Assistant Government Pleader Mr. Pranav Trivedi for
the respondent could not dispute the legal position arising from the
decision in Madhya Pradesh Rural Road Development Authority


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                Page 1 of 2</span>
             C/SCA/10278/2015                            ORDER



(supra).
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.    In the circumstances, the Arbitration Tribunal was not justified
in law in rejecting the Reference.
</p>
<p id="p_5">5.    Accordingly, order dated 14.11.2011 passed in Arbitration
Reference No. 109 of 1998 by the Gujarat Public Works Contracts
Disputes Arbitration Tribunal, Ahmedabad, is quashed and set aside.
The Reference in question is restored back to the Tribunal. The
Tribunal shall proceed with the Reference and decide the same in
accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_6">6.        Rule is made absolute.
</p>



<p id="p_7">                                                    (N.V.ANJARIA, J.)
cmjoshi
