<div class="doc_title">Binod Poddar Alias Vinod Poddar ... vs The State Of Jharkhand on 27 July, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                    B. A. No. 5160 of 2016

       1.Binod Poddar @ Vinod Poddar
       2. Bishnu Yadav                                          ...... Petitioners
                              Versus
       The State of Jharkhand                                   ...... Opposite Party
                     ---------
</pre><p id="p_1">       CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
</p><p id="p_2">                     ---------</p><pre id="pre_2">
       For the Petitioners            :     Mr. Arvind Kr. Choudhary, Advocate
       For the State                  :     A.P.P.
                                ---------

       04/Dated: 27/07/2016
</pre><p id="p_3">               Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned counsel for
       the State.
</p><p id="p_4">               Petitioners have been made an accused for the offence registered
       under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_1">Sections 363</a>/<a href="/doc/1559723/" id="a_2">366A</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code read with Section 8
       of POCSO Act.
</p><p id="p_5">               Learned counsel for the petitioners has submitted that police after
       investigation submitted final form. That the petitioners are in custody
       since 12.01.2016 and 01.04.2016
               Learned A.P.P has opposed the prayer for bail.
               In the facts and circumstances of the case, the petitioners above
       named, are directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.
       10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each
       to the satisfaction of Additional Sessions Judge-1, Godda in connection
       with Pathargama P.S. Case No. 136 of 2015 corresponding to G.R. No.
       2116 of 2015 (Spl Case (P) No. 1 of 2016), subject to the condition that
       one of the bailors shall be local resident within district Godda.
</p>
