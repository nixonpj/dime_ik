</p><p id="p_6">                                                  HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ARUN BHANSALI

                                                                          Order

                                   10/05/2019

                                            It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that

                                   during the pendency of the petition, the petitioner has got relief in

                                   so far as the respondent Nos. 4, 5 and 6 are concerned and,

                                   therefore, to that extent, the petitioner does not want to press the

                                   petition.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                            In view thereof, the names of the respondent Nos. 4, 5 and 6

                                   are ordered to be deleted from the array of parties.

</p><p id="p_8">                                            Amended cause title be filed by learned counsel for the

                                   petitioner during the course of day.

</p><p id="p_9">                                            Office is directed to amend the online record.

                                            Issue notice to respondent Nos. 1 to 3 as per amended cause

                                   title.
</p>
