<div class="doc_title">Under Sections 302/34 Of The ... vs In Re: Fulchand Khan &amp; Ors on 1 October, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                 1

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">    161</span>
<p id="p_1">    sdas

   allowed

                               C. R. M. No. 9371 of 2019

    In Re.: An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the
Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 25.09.2019 in connection with
Nakashipara Police Station Case No. 517 of 2019 dated 17.09.2019
under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">Sections 302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p><p id="p_2">                                     And
       In Re: Fulchand Khan &amp; Ors. ....... petitioners


       Ms. Karabi Roy
                               ...for the petitioners

       Mr. Madhusudan Sur, learned A.P.P.
</p><p id="p_3">                              .... for the State

      Having considered the materials on record and bearing in mind the

extent of complicity of the petitioner i.e. in-laws of the victim in the

alleged crime and in the light of the submission that the physical assault

is not supported by contemporaneous records, we are of the opinion that

the petitioners may be granted anticipatory bail.

</p><p id="p_4">       Accordingly, we direct that in the event of arrest the petitioners

shall be released on bail upon furnishing a bond of Rs.10,000/- each

with two sureties of like amount each, to the satisfaction of the arresting

officer and also be subject to the conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">Section

438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 and on further condition

that they shall appear before the court below and pray for regular bail

within a period of six weeks from date.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                        2</span><p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">     Accordingly application for anticipatory bail is allowed.


(Jay Sengupta, J.)       (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
