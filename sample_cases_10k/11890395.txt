<p id="p_6">                      The order impugned does not call for any interference

          since the trial court observed that the petitioning plaintiffs had not

          indicated the number of police personnel that would be necessary.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                    The order impugned dated March 21, 2014 does not

          preclude the petitioners herein from applying afresh for police help

          upon indicating the number of personnel necessary so that the

          appropriate costs in such regard can be assessed.
</p>


<p id="p_8">                    CO No. 3051 of 2014 is dismissed.
</p>


<p id="p_9">                    There will be no order as to costs.
