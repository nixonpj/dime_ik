<div class="doc_title">Anand Chowksey vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 24 July, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                           WP-4494-2017
             (ANAND CHOWKSEY Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


24-07-2017

Shri D.P.Singh, counsel for the petitioner.
Shri Praveen Newaskar, PP for the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">By way of this petition preferred under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the
Constitution of India, innocuous prayer for deciding the appeal
preferred by the petitioner on 8.5.2017 against the final penalty is
sought.
</p><p id="p_2">Opposite side has no objection if such a direction is given.
Without commenting on the merits of the case, this petition is
disposed of with a direction to the appellate authority i.e.
respondent no.1 to decide the appeal (Annexure P/1) preferred by
the petitioner against the order of penalty dated 17.4.2017
Annexure P/2 by which, one annual increment was with held with
non cumulative effect along with recovery of Rs.19,37,726/-.
Let the said appeal be decided as early as possible preferably
within a period of two months from the date of production of a copy
of this order provided that the petitioner cooperates. It is expected
that the recovery proceedings pursuant to the order of penalty
shall be given effect to after decision in the appeal.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                                                    (SHEEL NAGU)
                                                       JUDGE
