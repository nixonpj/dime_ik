</pre>
<p id="p_1">Ajay Kumar Mittal J.(Oral)

</p><p id="p_2">1.              Learned State Counsel, for the respondents, states that the petitioners

have already withdrawn the amount of compensation, in terms of the award

announced by the Land Acquisition Collector, Panchkula and, therefore, after the

withdrawal of the compensation by the petitioners, the writ petition, challenging

the acquisition, does not survive.
</p>
<p id="p_3">2.              In view of the fact that the writ petitioners have withdrawn the

compensation, as stated by the learned State counsel for the respondents, writ

petition is dismissed.       However, it shall be open to the petitioners to file

appropriate application to revive the petition in case any grievance still subsists

or the statement made by the learned State counsel for the respondents is found to

be incorrect.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                                                      (AJAY KUMAR MITTAL)
                                                          JUDGE


03.04.2013                                             (G.S.SANDHAWALIA)
