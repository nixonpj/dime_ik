<div class="doc_title">Rohit Murali vs 2 R. Gajendran on 8 October, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

DATED: 08.10.2018

CORAM:
THE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE P.N. PRAKASH

Crl.O.P. No. 21657 of 2018 &amp; Crl.M.P. No.11852 of 2018

1	Rohit Murali		
2	Vishal									Petitioners
vs.
1	State represented by the Inspector of Police
	J-13, Taramani Police Station, Chennai 600 113

2	R. Gajendran							Respondents

	Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. seeking to call for the records pertaining to the FIR in Cr. No.374 of 2016 pending on the file of the first respondent police and to quash the same.

		For petitioners	Mr. E. Sounder Rajan
					for M/s. B. Thamayanthi
		For R1		Mrs. M. Prabhavathi, Addl. Public Prosecutor

ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	This Criminal Original Petition has been filed seeking to call for the records pertaining to the FIR in Cr. No.374 of 2016 pending on the file of the first respondent police and to quash the same.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2	On the complaint lodged by Gajendran, the second respondent herein, the first respondent police registered a case in Cr. No.374 of 2016 on 22.03.2016 under <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_2">Sections 294(b)</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_3">506(i)</a> IPC against Rohit Murali and three others, seeking quashment of which, this Criminal Original Petition has been filed on the ground that the parties have arrived at a compromise.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3	Today, Mr. S. Elamvazhudhi, Sub Inspector, J-13, Taramani Police Station is present before this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4	On instructions, the learned Additional Public Prosecutor submitted that the investigation in Cr. No.374 of 2016 has been completed and charge sheet has been filed in C.C. No.2413 of 2018 before the XVIII Metropolitan Magistrate, Saidapet, Chennai, for the offences under <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_4">Sections 294(b)</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506(i)</a> IPC against four accused and that summons have been issued to the accused for their presence.
</p>
<p id="p_5">	5	Under normal circumstances, this Court would have directed the petitioners to file an application to quash the final report. However, in this case, the final report has been filed only for the offences under <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_6">Sections 294(b)</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_7">506(i)</a> IPC. That apart, the de facto complainant and the accused were studying in the Law College, during which period, the incident in question is said to have occurred.
</p>
<p id="p_6">	6	Today, Gajendran, the de facto complainant, has filed an affidavit stating that the matter has been settled and that he has no objection in the proceedings against the accused being quashed. The relevant portion of the affidavit filed by the de facto complainant reads as under:
</p>
<p id="p_7">	2	I submit that I had given a complaint before the 1st respondent police against the petitioners and 2 others and based on my complaint, an FIR was registered in Cr. No.374 of 2016 against the said persons under <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_8">Sections 294(b)</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_9">506(i)</a> IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_8">	3	I submit that later, the 1st respondent police completed the investigation and final report has been filed before the learned XVIII Metropolitan Magistrate, Saidapet, Chennai and the same is pending before the said Court in C.C. No.2413 of 2018 in summons stage.
</p>
<p id="p_9">	4	I submit that I have given consent to quash the proceedings in the above said C.C. No.2413 of 2018 in Cr. No.374 of 2016. I am filing this affidavit out of free will, consent violation and there is no threat, coercion,undue influence or duress.
</p>
<p id="p_10">	In the circumstances, it is humbly prayed that this Hon'ble Court may be pleased to record my affidavit and dispose the Crl.O.P. No.21657 of 2018 by quashing the C.C. No.2413/2018 in Cr. No.374/2016 and pass such further or other orders as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the circumstances of the case and thus render justice.
	
	In view of the above, the proceedings as against all the accused in C.C. No.2413 of 2018 in Cr. No.374 of 2016 is hereby quashed and as a sequel, this Criminal Original Petition stands allowed, on condition that Rohit Murali, Vishal Subash, Mani and Bharath Paranthaman, who are the accused in C.C. No.2413 of 2018 in Cr. No.374 of 2016, shall render community service at the Sub Jail, Saidapet, Chennai  15, for a period of five days commencing from 22.10.2018 to 26.10.2018 from 10 a.m. to 1.30 p.m., under the directions of PRISM, a non Government organisation. Connected Crl.M.P. is closed.
</p>

cad

P.N. PRAKASH, J.
