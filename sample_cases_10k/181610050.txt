         ==========================================================
                         INDRASINH BACHUBHA ZALA....Applicant(s)

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.S. SUPEHIA

                                      Date : 12/05/2017


                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">         1.           Rule. Learned Additional Public Prosecutor waives
         service of rule on behalf of respondent - State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.           The present application has been filed by the under-
         trial prisoner to release himself on temporary bail on the
         ground of making arrangement of school fees of the two minor
         children of the convict. In support of this application, applicant
         has produced copies of the letter given by the school.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         3.           I have gone through the Jail record of the under-trial
         prisoner as well as considered the averments made in this
         application.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances of the case
         and looking to the jail record of the under-trial prisoner, I am of

         mark his presence before the concerned Police Station on
