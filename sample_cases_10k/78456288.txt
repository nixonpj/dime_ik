<div class="doc_title">Monirul Mondal @ Md. Monirul ... vs Unknown on 16 September, 2016</div><p id="p_1">16.09.2016
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 45</span>
 pk
                              CRM No. 7390 of 2016

In Re:- An application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal
Procedure filed on 08.09.2016 in connection with Swarupnagar P.S. Case No.
559/16 dated 15.7.16 under <a href="/doc/5353/" id="a_2">Sections 14</a>/<a href="/doc/173097740/" id="a_3">14C</a> of Foreigners Act.
</p><p id="p_2">                     And
In the matter of:- Monirul Mondal @ Md. Monirul Kukarim Mondal
                                                        petitioner
Mr. Kallol Kumar Basu,
Mr. Rahul Halder                        for the Petitioner

Mr. Navanil De                          for the State


      Heard the learned counsel appearing on behalf of the parties. Perused the

case diary.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that his client is an Indian

citizen and he has been falsely implicated in this case.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      The learned counsel for the State opposed the prayer for bail and submits
that the petitioner is a Bangladeshi National. He had come over to India and
while going back he was apprehended by the police.
</p><p id="p_5">      Since it is claimed by the learned counsel for the petitioner that he has all
the valid documents to prove his Indian Nationality, we are of the opinion, that
the genuineness of those documents may be caused to be verified by the learned
court below.
</p><p id="p_6">      Having regard to above, the application for bail stands rejected.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                    (Ashim Kumar Roy, J.)

                                  (Malay Marut Banerjee, J.)
