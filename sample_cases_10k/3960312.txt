                       CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE AJAY KUMAR
                       TRIPATHI
                       ORAL ORDER

6       21-11-2016                     Let     the     Principal      Secretary,   Department   of

                        Education, Government of Bihar, Patna be impleaded as party

                        respondent No.7 in C.W.J.C. No. 11089 of 2016. This

                        impleadment will mean impleadment in all the writ applications,

                        wherever State is not a respondent in the batch of writs. Since a

                        common question of law arises in all the writs that is the reason

                        for all the writ applications being heard together.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                       Counsel for the petitioner, namely, Rajendra Kishori

                        B.Ed. College, will serve a copy of the writ application. The I.A.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                        No. 9049 of 2016 will be served by the Examination Board upon

                        the Advocate General by tomorrow.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                       The Principal Secretary, Department of Education,

                        Government of Bihar, Patna will revert back to this Court by way

                        of an affidavit within two weeks i.e. on 05th December, 2016.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                 (Ajay Kumar Tripathi, J)
