          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

  CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR EXTENSION OF TEMPORARY
                         BAIL) NO. 2003 of 2015
           In CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION NO. 841 of 2015

================================================================
         ALPESH @ PINTU NARAYANBHAI YADAV....Applicant(s)
                            Versus
                STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
PARTY-IN-PERSON, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MR KL PANDYA, APP for the RESPONDENT(s) No. 1
================================================================

         CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE J.B.PARDIWALA

                           Date : 30/01/2015


                             ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Rule returnable forthwith. The learned APP waives service
of notice of rule for and on behalf of the respondent-State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      By this application, the applicant, an under-trial prisoner,
prays for extension of the period of temporary bail granted to
him vide order dated 13th January 2015 passed in Criminal
Misc. Application No.841 of 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      Taking into consideration the grounds urged in the
application, the period of temporary bail is extended by a
further period of one week from today on the same terms and
conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      The applicant shall surrender before the jail authority on
or before the expiry of the period of one week, without fail.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent. Direct
service is permitted.
</p>
