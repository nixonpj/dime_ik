Court No. 5                                 FA 166 of 2013
(DB-VII)
                                          Smt. Saroj Agarwal
                                                 Versus
07.01.2019                              Smt. Nita Agarwal &amp; Ors.
(ML 14)
                                                with
(S. Banerjee)                             CAN 10666 of 2013



                 Mr. Debdut Mukherjee, Ld. Advocate
                                                                   ... for the appellant


                        Re: CAN 10666 of 2013

                        The applicant is directed to serve copies of the
                application upon all the opposite parties by speed post
                with acknowledgement card within ten days and to file an
                affidavit-of-service on the next date.

                        Let affidavits-in-opposition to the application be filed
                within four weeks from the date of the receipt of the copies
                of the application. Let affidavits-in-reply thereto, if any, be
                filed within two weeks thereafter. Let this matter appear
                under the heading 'Application' in the Combined Monthly
                List of March, 2019.

                        The applicant is directed to communicate the gist of
                the order to all the opposite parties while effecting service
                of the application.


                        Re: FA 166 of 2013


                        Call for the Lower Court Records. The appellant is
                directed to put in the special messenger's costs within ten
                days.

                        The department is directed to examine the Lower
                Court Records and to serve a notice of arrival of the Lower
                Court Records upon the learned advocate for the appellant,
                if on examination the Lower Court Records are found to be
                complete.

                        The appellant is directed to prepare and file out of
                court the requisite number of Paper Books within a period
                of eight weeks from the date of receipt of the notice of the
                arrival of the Lower Court Records.
                        2


      The office is directed to effect service of notice of
appeal upon all the respondents. The petitioner is directed</pre>
<p id="p_1">to put in the postal costs for that within a period of ten
days. In default, put up for orders.
</p>



<p id="p_2">                                 (Dr. Sambuddha Chakrabarti, J.)



                                           (Madhumati Mitra, J.)
