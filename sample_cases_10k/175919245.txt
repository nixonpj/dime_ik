DATE: 04..11.2016
CORAM: 
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.N.PRAKASH
Criminal Original Petition No.24262 of 2016
and 
Crl.M.P.No.11564 of 2016

1.Mhader  @ Abdul Mhader
2.A.K.Abid
3.B.Salman
4.Bilal @ K.Mohammed Bilal
... Petitioners
-Versus-

1.The Inspector of Police,
   Arcot Town Police Station,
   Arcot, Vellore District.

2.Wajhuddin @ Mohammed Wajhuddin
... Respondents


Prayer: This petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C. praying to call for the records relating to the case in Crime (FIR) No.47 of 2016 on the file of the 1st respondent police and to quash the above said case.

For Petitioners
:
Mr.D.Balaji
For Respondents
:
Mr.C.Emalias, APP for R1


ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">	On the complaint lodged by the 2nd respondent, the 1st respondent police have registered a case in Crime No.47 of 2010 on 31.01.2016 for offences under <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_2">Sections 294(b)</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_3">323</a>, <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_4">427</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506(i)</a> of IPC against four accused, who are petitioners herein. Seeking to quash the said case, the present petition has been filed on the ground that the petitioners and the de facto complainant have arrived at a compromise among themselves.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	3. Heard both sides and perused the records carefully.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	4. Today, Mr.P.Ravichandran, Special Sub Inspector of Police, Arcot Town Police Station, Vellore District, is present. The petitioners/A1 to A4 and the 2nd respondent/de facto complainant are also present and they have been identified by the Special Sub Inspector of Police. The petitioners and the de facto complainant have filed a joint compromise memo wherein in para 2 and 3 it is stated as follows:- 
</p>
<p id="p_4">	"2. The petitioners/Accused and the 2nd respondent / de facto complainant submit that both have agreed not to disturb and foist a false complaint against each other in future.  ...	...	...	...	...	...	...	...	...	... ...
</p><p id="p_5">	3. The petitioners/Accused and the 2nd respondent / de facto complainant have voluntarily come forward and file this joint compromise memo without any coercion or undue influence. ....."
</p>
<p id="p_6">	5. Recording the above, the criminal original petition is allowed and the case in Crime No.47 of 2016 on the file of the Inspector of Police, Arcot Town Police Station, Vellore District, is quashed. Consequently, connected MP is closed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">04..11..2016   
kmk


TO

</p><p id="p_8">1.The Inspector of Police, Arcot Town Police Station, Vellore 
   District.
</p>

<p id="p_9">P.N.PRAKASH.J.,  

kmk
