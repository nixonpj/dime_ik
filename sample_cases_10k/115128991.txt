<div class="doc_title">Surendra Prasad Gupta &amp; Ors vs The State Of Bihar on 14 May, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                           Cr.Misc. No.16068 of 2008
                      SURENDRA PRASAD GUPTA &amp; ORS
                                        Versus
                             THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                      -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">2   14.5.2008         This modification petition has been filed for

                modifying the order dated 24.1.2008 as modified by the

                order dated 12.3.2008 in order to further extend the time for

                surrender for a period of four weeks with respect to

                petitioners no. 5 to 8 only.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                         It is stated that the said petitioners could not get

                local bailers within the extended period        on account of

                lawyers' strike. Accordingly, he prays to extend the time.

</p><p id="p_3">                         In the aforesaid circumstances, the order dated

                24.1.2008 passed in Cr. Misc. no.48047 of 2007 is modified

                to the extent that the time for surrender is extended by

                further period of four weeks from today.

</p><p id="p_4">                         The application is accordingly disposed of .

                         Let this order be communicated through FAX on

                payment of prescribed fee by the petitioners.


</p><pre id="pre_2">       shahid                            (Ramesh Kumar Datta, J)
