  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

1. J.BALAYYAN, S/O JAMES NADAR,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. STATE OF KERALA REPRESENTED BY THE
                       ...       Respondent

2. THE JOINT REGISTRAR OF CO-OPERATIVE

3. THE SPECIAL SALE OFFICER,

4. TRIVANDRUM CO-OPERATIVE AGRICULTURAL

5. THE MANAGER, TRIVANDRUM AGRICULTURAL

                For Petitioner  :SRI.ABRAHAM MATHEW (VETTOOR)

                For Respondent  :SRI.D.KISHORE

The Hon'ble MR. Justice K.M.JOSEPH

 Dated :11/06/2007

 O R D E R
                           K.M.JOSEPH, J.

                 - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

              W.P.(C).No. 12254  OF 2007

             - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

                  Dated this the 11th day of June, 2007


                               JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Petitioner   challenges   Ext.P5.                By   Ext.P5,   the


respondent bank has informed the petitioner that the sale of


the   mortgaged   property   was   confirmed   by   the   Joint


Registrar   and   there   is   no   question   of   consideration   of   the


application of the petitioner.
</p>

<p id="p_2">      2.    Learned   counsel   for   the   Bank  submits   that   if   the


petitioner   remits   Rs.   52,542/-   in   lump   including   the


expense, if it is not included in the amount of Rs. 52,542/-,


the respondent bank will re-convey the property.
</p>

<p id="p_3">      3.    Learned   counsel   for   the   petitioner   submits   that


petitioner is ready and willing to pay the said amount.
</p>

<p id="p_4">      Recording the submission of the learned counsel for the


parties,   the     writ   petition   is   disposed   of   directing   the   4th


respondent bank to re-convey the property of the petitioner


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">WPC No.12254/07                        2</span>



sold   and   purchased   by   the   fourth   respondent   bank   and


covered   by Ext.R4(d).  upon   the   petitioner   remitting   a  sum


of   Rs.   52,542/-   and   also   meeting   the   expense   for


re-conveyance,   if   the   amount   of   RS.   52,542/-   does   not


include   the   expenses   of   re-conveyance.     This  direction   will


be complied only if the petitioner remits the amount within


one month from today.
</p>




<p id="p_5">                                                    (K.M.JOSEPH, JUDGE)



sv.
</p>
