<div class="doc_title">Sonu vs State Of U.P. on 4 December, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Ramesh Sinha</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?
 
Court No. - 1
 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION No. - 49332 of 2019
 
Applicant :- Sonu
 
Opposite Party :- State of U.P.
 
Counsel for Applicant :- Vikrant Neeraj
 
Hon'ble Ramesh Sinha,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Heard Sri Vikrant Neeraj, learned counsel for the applicant, Sri Gaurav Pratap Singh, learned A.G.A. for the State and perused the record.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">It has been contended by learned counsel for the applicant that the applicant has been falsely implicated in the present case. It has been further submitted that the FIR was lodged against the applicant and one unknown person but during the course of investigation by the C.C.T.V camera, the involvement of Pawan and Devi Singh was traced out and both of them have been granted bail by coordinate Bench of this Court on 22.8.2019 and 5.7.2019 in Criminal Misc. Bail Application No.34058 of 2019 and 26795 of 2019 respectively. It is stated that the applicant is said to have assaulted the deceased along with other unknown persons by danda who died on account of head injury. The author of the said injury is not known. The applicant is in jail since 12.1.2019. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">Learned AGA opposed the prayer for bail but could not dispute the aforesaid facts as argued by the learned counsel for the applicant.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">Copy of bail granting order to co-accused persons supplied by learned counsel for the applicant is taken on record.
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Keeping in view the nature of the offence, evidence, complicity of the accused, severity of punishment, submissions of the learned counsel for the parties and without expressing any opinion on merits of the case, I am of the view that the applicant has made out a case for bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_6">Let the applicant-Sonu involved in Case Crime No.01 of 2019, u/s 302 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>, P.S. Sasnigate, district Aligarh be released on bail on his furnishing a personal bond of Rs.one lac with two sureties (one should be of his family member) each in the like amount to the satisfaction of the court concerned and the court concerned shall take an undertaking from the sureties that the properties movable/immovable which are the basis of accepting the surety, shall not be disposed of by them during the pendency of the trial with the following conditions:-
</p> 
<p id="p_7">(i) The applicant shall file an undertaking to the effect that he shall not seek any adjournment on the dates fixed for evidence when the witnesses are present in court. In case of default of this condition, it shall be open for the trial court to treat it as abuse of liberty of bail and pass orders in accordance with law.
</p> 
<p id="p_8">(ii) The applicant shall remain present before the trial court on each date fixed, either personally or through his counsel. In case of his absence, without sufficient cause, the trial court may proceed against him under <a href="/doc/77781517/" id="a_2">Section 229-A</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p> 
<p id="p_9">(iii) In case, the applicant misuses the liberty of bail during trial and in order to secure his presence proclamation under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_3">Section 82</a> Cr.P.C. is issued and the applicant fails to appear before the court on the date fixed in such proclamation, then, the trial court shall initiate proceedings against him, in accordance with law, under <a href="/doc/119436650/" id="a_4">Section 174-A</a> of the Indian Penal Code.
</p> 
<p id="p_10">(iv) The applicant shall remain present, in person, before the trial court on the dates fixed for (i) opening of the case, (ii) framing of charge and (iii) recording of statement under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5">Section 313</a> Cr.P.C. If in the opinion of the trial court absence of the applicant is deliberate or without sufficient cause, then it shall be open for the trial court to treat such default as abuse of liberty of bail and proceed against him in accordance with law.
</p> 
<p id="p_11">The trial court is directed to expedite the trial and conclude the same preferably within the period of one year from the date of production of the certified copy of this order before the trial court, if there is no legal impediment.
</p> 
<p id="p_12">It is further directed that the identity, status and residence proof of the sureties be verified by the authorities concerned before they are accepted. 
</p> 
<p id="p_13">In case of breach of any of the above conditions, the trial court will be at liberty to cancel the bail.
</p> 
<p id="p_14">Order Date :- 4.12.2019/Gaurav
