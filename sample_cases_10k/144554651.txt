<p id="p_60">5977, 5985, 5988, 5993, 5997, 5999, 6783, 6784, 6848 &amp; 6890 of 2018 as

the issue involved in all these cases is common.
</p>
<p id="p_61">               At the outset, learned counsel for the State has submitted that

though in some of the writ petitions notice of motion has not been issued but

he has instructions from the Transport Commissioner, Haryana to withdraw

the impugned order passed in all these cases for the purpose of reconsidering

the application of the petitioner(s). It is also submitted that the respondents

have taken a decision that they would not raise any objection regarding

deposit of `1 lac by the petitioner(s) but other issues would be considered

and decided.
</p>
<p id="p_62">               In view of the aforesaid facts and circumstances, all the

petitions are hereby disposed of with a direction to the Transport

Commissioner, Haryana to reconsider the application of the petitioner(s) and

decide the same expeditiously and preferably within a period of 15 days

from the date of receipt of certified copy of this order.
</p>
