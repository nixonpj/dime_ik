                      of 2014.

<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                      3.    Learned Special Government Pleader appearing for the First and

                      Second Respondents, on instructions, has filed a memo stating that

                      though orders have been reserved by the First Respondent in I.A.

                      No. 3 of 2017 in O.A. No. 14 of 2014 after conducting detailed enquiry,

                      the incumbent in that post has subsequently retired from service and

                      that the Deputy Commissioner, Arulmighu Bannari Amman Temple,

                      Bannari, Erode District is holding additional charge of that post. It is

                      informed that the said official would issue notice to the parties and



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                      3/6</span>


http://www.judis.nic.in
                                                                               W.P. No. 18861 of 2019

                      after hearing them final orders would be passed.

</p><p id="p_6">                      4.      In the aforesaid circumstances, the officer holding charge of the

                      post    of   Joint   Commissioner,   Hindu   Religious    and    Charitable

                      Endowments Department, Salem shall issue notice to all the parties

                      concerned including the Tenth Respondent, if an application for

                      impleading is filed by him and after affording full opportunity of

                      hearing to all the parties reasoned orders on merits in accordance with

                      law shall be passed dealing with each of the contentions raised and

                      decision taken shall be communicated to all the parties under written

                      acknowledgement and the report of the compliance shall be filed before

                      the Registrar (Judicial) of this Court by 15.12.2019.



</p><p id="p_7">                      5.      Accordingly, the Writ Petition is disposed on the aforesaid terms.
