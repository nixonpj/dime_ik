IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

DATED 01.10.2018

CORAM
					
THE HONOURABLE Mr.JUSTICE P.N.PRAKASH


CRL.O.P.No.20955 of 2018

1.A.Chinthamani
2.A.Govindharaj			 	                .. Petitioners	
Vs

The State rep. By
The Inspector of Police, 
All Women Police Station,
Harur, Dharmapauri District.				.. Respondent

	Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., praying to issue a direction to the respondent or anyone attached to the respondent police station not to harass the petitioners by compelling them to appear before the respondent Police Station or before the learned Judicial Magistrate, Harur without due process of law. 
	        For Petitioners      :   Mr.S.Kumaradevan
	    
	        For Respondent    :   Mr.C.Raghavan, Government Advocate 

O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	This Criminal Original Petition has been filed to direct the respondent or anyone attached to the respondent police station not to harass the petitioners by compelling them to appear before the respondent Police Station or before the learned Judicial Magistrate, Harur without due process of law under the guise of enquiry.

P.N.PRAKASH, J.
</p><p id="p_2">g s a
	
</p><p id="p_3">	2.Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioners and the learned Government Advocate [Crl. Side] appearing for the respondent.

</p><p id="p_4">	3.When the matter was taken up for consideration, the learned Government Advocate represented that neither a case registered nor a petition enquiry is pending against the petitioners.
	
	Recording the same, this petition is closed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">01.10.2018
gsa
To

</p><p id="p_6">1.The Inspector of Police, 
   All Women Police Station,
   Harur, Dharmapauri District.
</p>
<p id="p_7">2.The Public Prosecutor, 
   High Court, Madras.				
</p>
