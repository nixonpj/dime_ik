<div class="doc_title">Divesh Singh And Anr vs State Of Rajasthan Through Pp on 19 April, 2017</div><p id="p_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT JAIPUR
                     BENCH, JAIPUR

                                ORDER

    S.B. CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION NO.3576/2017

</p><p id="p_2">1. Divesh Singh S/o Shri Sohan Singh B/c Thakur, R/o House No.
1, Phase 2, Sagar Avenue, Ayodhya Bye-pass Road, Bhopal (MP)

</p><p id="p_3">2. Sushma Devi W/o Mohan Singh B/c Thakur, R/o House No. 1,
Phase 2, Sagar Avenue, Ayodhya Bye-pass Road, Bhopal (MP)


                                Versus
State of Rajasthan through P.P.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">Date of Order:                             April   19, 2017

                 HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA

Mr. S.S. Ola, for the petitioners.
Mr. R.R. Baisla, P.P. for the State.


</p><p id="p_6">           Petitioners have filed this petition under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section

438</a> Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 seeking anticipatory

bail in FIR No.1/2017 registered at Police Station Dholpur,

for offences u/S. 498-A and 406<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2"> Indian Penal Code</a> 1860.

</p><p id="p_7">           Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_8">           Petitioners are the husband and mother in-law of

the complainant.
</p>
<p id="p_9">           On 21.03.2017 following order was passed by Co-

ordinate Bench of this Court:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                 "The concerned Investigating Officer is directed

           to remain present before the court alongwith case

           diary on 10th April, 2017.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                   List the matter on 10th April, 2017. Till then

           the accused petitioners shall not be arrested"</blockquote><p id="p_10">

</p><p id="p_11">           Learned   State   Counsel   on   instructions    from   the

Investigating Officer has submitted that the petitioners have

joined investigation and are not required for further investigation.

</p><p id="p_12">           Accordingly, without expressing any opinion on

merits of the case, this petition is allowed. In the event of

arrest, petitioners be admitted on bail subject to satisfaction

of the Arresting Officer.    Petitioners shall join investigation

as and when required by the police and shall not leave the

country without prior permission of the Court.             Petitioners

shall not tamper with the prosecution evidence.



</p><p id="p_13">                                                 (SABINA),J.
</p>
<p id="p_14">Tanwar/-
</p><p id="p_15">P.A.
</p><p id="p_16">Item No.23
