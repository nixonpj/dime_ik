<p id="p_4">                               It is submitted that the impugned order taking cognizance

                  in which his whole grievance clusters around the terms and

                  conditions of guarantees/warranty in the matter or purchasing of a

                  cannon printer. Learned counsel submits that in the nature of the

                  allegations no criminal case would be made out, it may be at best a

                  case of deficiency of service and a case of consumer dispute.

                  this application shall stand rejected without further reference to a

                  Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                               List this case immediately within a week after receipt of

                  service report.
</p>

                  Judge VII-cum-Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate, Patna shall
