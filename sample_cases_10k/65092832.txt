                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.42790 of 2019
                     Arising Out of PS. Case No.-323 Year-2018 Thana- GHORASAHAN District- East
                                                       Champaran
                 ======================================================
                 MAYA DEVI W/o Ramghabala Singh @ Achhayabal Singh R/o Village-
                 Balapur, P.S.- Ghorasahan, District- East Champaran.

                                                                                ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr.Ajay Kumar Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mr.Binod Kumar
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   26-09-2019                    This application, for grant of anticipatory bail, arises

                   out of Ghorasahan P.S. Case No. 323 of 2018, disclosing

                   offences under <a href="/doc/409589/" id="a_1">Sections 304(B)</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_2">                                Petitioner happens to be mother in law of the

                   deceased and allegation against her and others is of causing

                   death of the deceased in connection with demand of dowry.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 Submission of learned counsel for the petitioner is

                   that she is mother in law of the deceased and no specific

                   allegation has been attributed to her rather only general and

                   omnibus allegation has been levelled.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 Heard learned A.P.P. also.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                    Having heard both sides, considering the facts and

                   circumstances, this application is allowed. Let the petitioner,
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.42790 of 2019(3) dt.26-09-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     above named, in the event of her arrest or surrender before the

                     court below within six weeks, be released on bail on furnishing

                     bail bond of Rs. 25,000/- (Rupees Twenty five Thousand) with

                     two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of

                     learned Sub Divisional Judicial Magistrate, Sikarahana at

                     Dhaka, East Champaran, Motihari, in connection with

                     Ghorasahan P.S. Case No. 323 of 2018, subject to the condition

                     laid down under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_3">Section 438</a> (2)<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4"> of the Code</a> of Criminal

                     Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                             (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
sunilkumar/-
</p>
