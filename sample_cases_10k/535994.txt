<p id="p_1">     The writ petition is filed mainly with the following prayer:-

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">          (ii)  issue a writ of mandamus or other appropriate
          writ, order or direction, directing the first respondent to
          restore the water supply to the petitioner's residence
          Consumer No.450 and to consider Ext.P6 complaint and
          pass orders in accordance with law.
</blockquote>
<p id="p_2">There will be a direction to the second respondent to look into

Ext.P6 with notice to the petitioner and pass appropriate orders

thereon in accordance with law within a period of four months

from the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment. The interim

order passed by this court will continue till such time.

</p><p id="p_3">     The writ petition is disposed of as above.
