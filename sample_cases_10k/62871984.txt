03/23.04.2016</pre><p id="p_1">                      Apprehending   their   arrest   in   connection   with   Rajnagar   P.S.   Case 
               No.06 of 2016 instituted under Sections 147/149/341/342/323/353/504 of the 
               Indian   Penal   Code,   the   nine   petitioners  have   moved   this   Court   for   grant   of 
               privileges of anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_2">                               Heard   learned   counsel   appearing   for   the   petitioners   and   learned 
               counsel representing the State.
</p><p id="p_3">                               Learned counsel appearing for the petitioners submitted that there is 
               no any specific allegation of assault against any of the petitioners and even in the 
               entire F.I.R, no source of information has been disclosed by the informant. It was 
               also   submitted   that   out   of   village   rivalry,   names   of   the   petitioners   have   been 
               implicated   in   this   case   even   then   the   petitioners   are   ready   to   pay   the   fine   as  
               awarded by this Court. 
</p><p id="p_4">                               Learned counsel representing the State opposed the prayer for bail.
                               Considering   the   submissions   of   learned   counsels,   I   am   inclined   to 
               grant   privileges   of   anticipatory   bail   to   the   petitioners.   Accordingly,   the   nine 
               petitioners­Nilkant Gope @ Nilkanta Gope,  Gangadhar Mahakur @ Ganga Sagar 
               Mahakur,   Jasbant   Mahakur   @   Jasa   Mahakur   @   Jashabant   Mahakur,   Somnath 
               Gope, Tikaram Gope, Piyush Mandal, Lakhan Mahakur, Bhola Gope @ Bhola Nath 
               Gope and Bhabani Gope are  directed to surrender in the court below within two 
               weeks from today and in the event of their arrest/surrender, the court below is 
               directed   to   enlarge   them   on   bail   on   furnishing   bail   bond   of   Rs.10,000/­   (Ten 
               thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of 
               learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Seraikella  in connection with Rajnagar P.S. Case 
               No.06 of 2016 corresponding to G.R. No.54 of 2016 subject to the conditions as 
               laid down under Section 438(2) of the Code of Criminal Procedure on payment of 
               Rs.5000/­ (Five thousand) by each of the petitioners in the court below.
</p>
