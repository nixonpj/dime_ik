                    CRIMINAL APPELLATE JURISDICTION

                  CRIMINAL APPLICATION NO.984 OF 2019
                                  IN
             ANTICIPATORY BAIL APPLICATION NO.2120 OF 2018

    Umesh Sopan Sonawane                                   .... Applicant

                     versus

    The State of Maharashtra                               .... Respondent

    •    Mr. Prashant S. Hagare, Advocate for the Applicant.
    •    Mr. S. H. Yadav, APP for the State/Respondent.

                                     CORAM : SARANG V. KOTWAL, J.
                                     DATE  : 19th JULY, 2019

    P.C. :


    1.               Leave to amend.       Amendment to be carried out

    forthwith.



    2.               The applicant is seeking modification of the order

    dated 30/1/2019 passed by this Court (Coram: Nitin W. Sambre,

    J.) in ABA No.2120/2018. The applicant was granted anticipatory

    bail in connection with C.R. No.513/2018 registered at Baramati

    City Police Station.          The third clause i.e. clause '(C)' of the

Mamta Kale

 operative part of the order reads thus.

                  "(C)             Till framing of charge, the Applicant shall

                  live outside the jurisdiction of the Police Station."



 3.               The present application is filed for modification of this

 clause because the applicant needs to attend two cases pending

 against him and one case in which he is a witness. The applicant

 has given details of his cases in the memo of this application. They

 are as follows.

                  (i)          Sessions Case No.123/2015 pending before

                  Additional Sessions Judge, Baramati.

                  (ii)         Sessions Case No.86/2016 pending before

                  Additional Sessions Judge, Baramati.

                  In both these cases, applicant is one of the accused.

                  (iii)        Sessions Case No.19/2013 pending before the

                  Additional Sessions Judge, Baramati where the

                  applicant is one of the witnesses and witness summons

                  is issued in his name.

                                           3/3                      911-APPP-984-19

 5.               Thus, it is not disputed that the applicant is required to

 attend these court cases. Therefore, the operative part of the order

 dated 30/1/2019 passed in ABA No.2120/2018 needs to be

 modified. Hence, the following order.

                                          ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                  1.           Till framing of the charge in the case

                  arising out of C.R. No.513/2018, registered at

                  Baramati City Police Station, the applicant shall live

                  outside the jurisdiction of the Police Station except for

                  attending the Sessions Case No.19/2013, Sessions Case

                  No. 86/2016 and Sessions Case No.123/2015, pending

                  before the Additional Sessions Judge, Baramati,

                  whenever his presence is required in those cases.

</p><p id="p_2">                  2.           The application is disposed of in the

                  aforesaid terms.
</p>
