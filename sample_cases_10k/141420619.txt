                     BENCH AT JAIPUR

         S.B.Criminal Misc.(SOS) Application No.1196/2018

                                          in

                     S.B. Criminal Appeal No. 1811/2018

Govind Narayan S/o Shri Ram Karan B/c Balai, Aged About 38
Years,    R/o    Balaion     Ki   Dhani        Tan   Ganwali   Police   Station

                                                                 ----Appellant
                                        Versus
State Of Rajasthan Through Pp, Raj.
                                                               ----Respondent</pre>


<pre id="pre_2">For Appellant(s)            :     Mr.Mahesh Gupta
For Respondent(s)           :     Mr.Sudesh Saini,PP



           HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PANKAJ BHANDARI

                            Judgment / Order

03/10/2018

</pre><p id="p_1">1.   Heard on application for suspension of sentence.


</p><p id="p_2">2.   It is contended by counsel for the appellant that the owner of

the premises, Gangaram has been acquitted. Application for

suspension      of    sentence     of    Co-accused-Lakhan       Jaga    stands

allowed. Case of appellant is on better footing than that of co-

accused-Lakhan Jaga. The appellant was on bail during trial. The

disposal of appeal will take time.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">3.   Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the application for

suspension of sentence.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">4.   Considering the contention of counsel for the appellant the

application for suspension of sentence is allowed.
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                        (2 of 2)                 [CRLAS-1811/2018]


</p><p id="p_8">                                   5.    Accordingly, the application for suspension of sentence is

                                   allowed.     It   is    ordered   that    the    sentence      awarded      to

                                   accused-applicant in Sessions Case No.12/2015 (CIS No.15/2015)

                                   shall remain suspended if he furnishes a personal bond of

                                   Rs.50,000/-       and   two   sureties   of    Rs.25,000/-    each    to   the

                                   satisfaction of the learned trial Court to the effect that he shall

                                   appear before Deputy Registrar (Judicial) on or before 02.11.2018

                                   and as and when called upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_9">
</p><p id="p_10">                                                                                      (PANKAJ BHANDARI),J
