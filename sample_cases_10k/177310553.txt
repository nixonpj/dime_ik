             The Union of India through DRM,
             East Central Railway, Dhanbad            ...Appellant (in all cases)

             1. Maniram Mahto.

                                               -----
</pre><p id="p_1">             Coram: HON'BLE MR JUSTICE RAJESH KUMAR
</p><p id="p_2">                                  -----
</p><p id="p_3">             For the Appellant          : Mr. Abhishek Dubey, Advocate
             For the Respondents        : M/s R. Sahu and Amit Verma, Advocates
</p><p id="p_4">                                  ----</p>
<p id="p_5">                      I.A. Nos.1372, 1371 and 1373 of 2018

20/21.03.2018:        Heard the parties.
</p><p id="p_6">                      By present interlocutory applications, the claimant/respondent
             No.1 prays for withdrawal of 50% of the compensation amount deposited
             by the appellant, in terms of order dated 16.01.2017 passed in the
             present second appeal.
</p><p id="p_7">                      It has been submitted by the counsel for the respondent No.1
             that the land has been acquired in the year 1965 by the Railway
             Authority. There was various litigations and the matter is pending since
             1965 and ultimately vide judgment 17.01.2013 passed in L.A. Reference
             Case Nos.44, 47 and 48/1985, the compensation amount granted to the
             respondent No.1 has been enhanced from Rs.600 per Katha to Rs.1000
             per Katha.
</p><p id="p_8">                      Being aggrieved with that judgment dated 17.01.2013, the
             appellant-Railway has filed present appeal in which as per the direction of
             this Court, 50% enhanced amount has already been deposited.
</p><p id="p_9">                      Learned counsel for the appellants seeks permission to withdraw
             the same.
</p><p id="p_10">                      Permission is granted.
</p><p id="p_11">                      Accordingly, this Court directs the Registrar General to release
             50% of the amount, so deposited by the appellant-Railway in pursuance
             of the order of this Court dated 16.01.2017 in favour of respondent No.1
             on his furnishing requisite security/ indemnity bonds and on an
 undertaking that the said amount shall be refunded, if the appeal is
allowed.
</p><p id="p_12">           With the above directions, these I.A. Nos.1372, 1371 and 1373
of 2018 are, hereby allowed and disposed of.
</p><p id="p_13">           F.A. Nos.201, 202 &amp; 203 of 2013
           As prayed for, list these cases on 14.05.2018.
</p>



<p id="p_14">                                                (Rajesh Kumar, J.)
Ravi/-
