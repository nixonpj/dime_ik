                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT BOMBAY
                    ORDINARY ORIGINAL CIVIL JURISDICTION


                                     SUIT NO. 1489 OF 2000

Green Field Hotels &amp; Estates Pvt. Ltd.                                ...Plaintiff

          Versus

Chandru L. Raheja and Ors.                                            ...Defendants
                                              ----

Mr.Chetan Kapadia a/w. Mr.Aditya Shiralkar I/b. Mr. K.D.Abichandani for
Plaintiff (in all suits).
Mr.F.E.DeVitre,   Senior   Advocate   a/w.   Mr.Raghav   Gupta,   Mr.Kashish
Mainkar I/b. M/s. Wadia Ghandy and Co. for Defendants (in all suits).  
                                      ----

                                               CORAM  : K.R.SHRIRAM, J.
</pre><pre id="pre_2">                                               DATE      : 1 OCTOBER 2018
P.C.:

1         Heard the counsel and the following issues are framed:

                                            ISSUES

</pre><p id="p_1">         1.      Whether defendants prove that the suit is not maintainable
         and   ought   to   be   dismissed   on   the   ground   that   there   has   been
         suppression of material and relevant facts?
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2.     Whether   defendants   prove   that   the   suit   is   bad   for   non-
         joinder of necessary parties?
</p>
<p id="p_3">         3.       Whether   plaintiff   proves   that   defendants   have   in   their  
         possession  and  are  bound  and liable  to  handover  to  plaintiff  the  
         title deeds, documents and papers belonging to plaintiff which  have  
         been listed  in the Schedule Exhibit A to the plaint and also  such  
         other   properties,   title   deeds,   documents,   papers   and   assets   of  
         plaintiff?</p><pre id="pre_3">




Trupti

                                               2/2                                 25-s-1489-2000.doc




</pre><p id="p_4">         4.     Whether plaintiff proves that defendants have wrongfully  
         retained   title   deeds,   documents,   papers,   share   certificates   and  
         assets of plaintiff, a list whereof is given in the Schedule Exhibit A
         to the plaint and such other properties, title deeds, documents  
         and assets of plaintiff and hence plaintiff is entitled to damages as
         may   be   deemed   fit   and   proper   by   the   Hon'ble   Court   after   an  
         inquiry in that behalf on account of the loss and damage caused 
         to plaintiff?
</p>
<p id="p_5">         5.    Whether defendants prove that the documents and records 
         of the entities/ companies including plaintiff which were in the  
         custody of defendants and were to be handed over by the C L  
         Raheja Group, i.e., defendant group have already been handed  
         over to the G L Raheja Group?
</p>
<p id="p_6">         6.      What decree? What order?
</p>

<p id="p_7">2.       Mr.DeVitre for defendants and Mr.Chetan Kapadia for plaintiff state

that parties have already filed their respective affidavit of documents and

given inspection to each other.  Copy of statement of admission and denial

with reasons for denial be exchanged before the end of this week.
</p>
<p id="p_8">3.       Plaintiff to also file its list of witnesses, affidavit in lieu of examination

in  chief  of  the  first witness together  with  compilation  of  documents and

serve a copy thereof upon defendants by 31st October, 2018.
</p>

<p id="p_9">4.       Stand   over   to   29th  November,   2018   for   marking   of   documents,

recording   of   evidence   on   which   date   plaintiffs'   first   witness   shall   remain

present in Court.
</p>


<p id="p_10">                                                                (K.R. SHRIRAM, J.)
