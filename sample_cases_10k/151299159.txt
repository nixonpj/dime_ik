ORAL ORDER</pre>
 


 


 


<p id="p_1">1.	On 12.12.2012,
this Court passed following order : 
</p>
 


 


 


<p id="p_2"> Notice
for final disposal returnable on 26th
December, 2012.  As the notice is
issued for final disposal, at this stage the question of granting
interim relief is deferred. However, in case the affidavit-in-reply
is not placed on record justifying the stand, then the Court may
consider granting of mandatory relief or appropriate relief by way of
interim relief. Direct Service permitted today. 
</p> 


 


 


 


 


<p id="p_3">2.		The record
indicates that the said order is served as per the affidavit filed of
direct service on 13.12.2012.  Till date no reply has been filed
controverting the averments made in this petition.  The Court is of
the considered view that looking to the averments made in this
petition and the order passed by this Court on 12.12.2012, the
interim relief as prayed for in Para No. 6(c) is required to be
granted till the next date of hearing.  Accordingly the matter is
adjourned to 16.01.2013.  By way of ad-interim relief, the
further implementation of the order dated 16.08.2012 is suspended. 
This order was remained in force till the next date of hearing i.e.
16.01.2013.  In case no affidavit-in-reply is filed even after
16.01.2013, the Court on the same line may allow the petition as the
averments made in this petition would remain uncontroverted which
would amount the petitioner to succeed this petition for default on
the part of the State.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_4">3.		 Direct
service is permitted. 
</p>
 


 


 


 


 


<p id="p_5">(S.R.BRAHMBHATT, J.)
