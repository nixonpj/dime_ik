                    Arising Out of PS. Case No.-314 Year-2019 Thana- GAIGHAT District- Muzaffarpur
                 ======================================================
           1.     PAPPU MAHTO Son of Jeebachh Mahto Resident of Village - Loma
                  (Loama), P.S.- Gaighat, Distt - Muzaffarpur.
           2.    Jarban Kumar Son of Dhayani Kamat @ Dhayani Mahto Resident of Village
                 - Banipatti Behra, P.S.- Benipatti, Distt - Madhubani.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Nitu Kumari
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Mritunjay Kumar Nirala
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA MISHRA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   02-12-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioner is in custody since 13.09.2019 in

                   connection with Gaighat (O.P Benibad) P.S. Case No. 314/19

                   corresponding to G.R. No. 1750 of 2019                       for the offence

                   registered under <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_1">Sections 414</a>, <a href="/doc/471092/" id="a_2">272</a>, <a href="/doc/285163/" id="a_3">273</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the Indian Penal

                   Code and <a href="/doc/1320984/" id="a_5">Sections 30</a> (a), 38 and 41 of the Bihar Prohibition

                   and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_6">Excise Act</a>, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that the

                   alleged recoveries were not made from the conscious possession

                   of the petitioners and they are merely a driver and Khalasi of the

                   pick up van. It is further submitted that owner of the pick up
             Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.76307 of 2019(2) dt.02-12-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                      van is one Rambabu Rai and the petitioners were unware of the

                      fact that the wine was loaded on the pick up van.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances of

                      the case, and that the petitioners have no criminal antecedents,

                      let the petitioners, above named, be released on bail on

                      furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with two

                      sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of Special

                      Judge Excise, Muzuffarpur in connection with Gaighat (O.P.

                      Benibad) P.S. Case No. 314 of 2019 corresponding to G.R. No.

                      1750 of 2019.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                 (Anjana Mishra, J)
Jagdish/-
</p>
