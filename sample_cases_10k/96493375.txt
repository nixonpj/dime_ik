<div class="doc_title">Bedamiya Devi &amp; Ors vs The State Of Bihar on 11 January, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.542 of 2018
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -292 Year- 2017 Thana -GANDHIMAIDAN District- PATNA
                 ======================================================
                 1. Bedamiya Devi, W/o Ramjee Nai @ Ramjee Mahto,
                 2. Dhananjay Mahto @ Dhanraj Mahto S/o Sita Ram Nai,
                 3. Rahul Kumar S/o Lai Mahto,
                        All are resident of Lodhipur, P.S.- Gandhi Maidan, District- Patna.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                            .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s : Mr. Diwakar Sinha, Advocate
                 For the State         : Mr. Amit Kumar Rakesh, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ASHWANI KUMAR
                 SINGH
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   11-01-2018               Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned

                 counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             The petitioners seek pre-arrest bail in connection with

                 Gandhi Maidan P.S. Case No.292 of 2017 registered under

                 <a href="/doc/838469/" id="a_1">Sections 452</a>, <a href="/doc/262864/" id="a_2">383</a> and <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_3">427</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                             It is submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioners

                 that the petitioners have no concern with the disputed property. He

                 submitted that the petitioners are neighbour of co-accused

                 Annapurna Devi and as they had intervened into the matter when

                 the dispute had taken place between the informant and the

                 Annapurna Devi, they have also been named in the FIR, but

                 subsequently at the intervention of common friends and well
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.542 of 2018 (2) dt.11-01-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        wishers, the dispute has been resolved between the parties and the

                        informant has also filed a compromise petition in this regard.

</p><p id="p_4">                                    Learned counsel for the State has opposed the prayer for

                        grant of pre-arrest bail to the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_5">                                     Considering the nature of allegation and the submissions

                        made above, in the event of arrest or surrender in the court below

                        within six weeks from today, the petitioners are directed to be

                        released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- (ten

                        thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                        satisfaction of learned Sub Judge-VI-cum-Additional Chief

                        Judicial Magistrate, Patna in connection with Gandhi Maidan P.S.

                        Case No.292 of 2017, subject to the conditions as laid down under

                        <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_5">Section 438(2)</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                 (Ashwani Kumar Singh, J)
Md.S./-
</p>
