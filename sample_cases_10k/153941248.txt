<p id="p_1">12   17-07-2018              Learned counsel for the appellant and the respondents

                  are present.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             Before admitting this appeal for hearing I.A.No.9454 of

                  2015 has been filed on behalf of the appellants under Order 22

                  Rule 4 of the Code of Civil Procedure for expunging the name of

                  respondents no.8, 9 and 12 due to their death in the year, 2008 and

                  to substitute the legal heirs of respondents no. 9 and 12 as detailed

                  in paras 3 and 4 of the aforesaid interlocutory application with

                  submission that sole legal heir, namely, Brijendra Prasad Yadav of

                  respondent no.8, Mani Lal Yadav is already on the record as

                  respondent no.7.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             In support of the said I.A.No.9454 of 1991 the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court FA No.200 of 1991 (12) dt.17-07-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                    2/2</span>




                      I.A.No.9455 of 2015 is also filed under <a href="/doc/100581/" id="a_1">Section 5</a> of the Limitation

                      Act to condone the delay in filing substitution petition i.e.,

                      I.A.No.9455 of 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                      Let the learned counsel for the appellants take steps for sending

                      notice of I.A.No.9454 of 2015 supported by the limitation petition

                      i.e., I.A.No.9455 of 2015 on the proposed legal heirs of

                      respondents no.9 and 12 as detailed in paras 3 and 4 of

                      I.A.No.9454 of 2015 under registered cover with A/D and

                      ordinary process for which requisites etc. must be filed within six

                      weeks from today.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                             (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J)
AnilKrSinha/-
