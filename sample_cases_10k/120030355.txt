<p id="p_4">                         Sri Soumya Majumder, learned senior counsel

             appears for the petitioner and correctly points out that at the

             paragraph of the order of this Court dated 5th September,

             2016 begining with the words "This Court is required to notice

             that the issue of overlapping periods in respect of the principal

             amounts payable under Section 7A of the 1952 Act" be read as

             Section 7Q of the 1952 Act instead of Section 7A of the 1952

             Act.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                         On behalf of the Provident Fund Authorities -

             appearance is put in by Sri Shib Chandra Prasad, learned

             counsel.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                         Noticing the contents of the order dated 5th

             September, 2016, the submission made by Sri Majumdar is

             accepted.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    2</span><p id="p_7">


          Registry to carry out the necessary correction.

          Let this order be treated as part of the order dated

5th September, 2016.
</p>

</p><p id="p_9">                                     (Subrata Talukdar,J.)
