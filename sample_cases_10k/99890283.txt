<div class="doc_title">Smt. Jaya Sharma vs Shyam Soni And Another on 13 August, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR.

S.B.CIVIL MISC. APPEAL No.794/00
Smt.Jaya Sharma vs. Shri Shyam Soni &amp; Anr.

13.08.2009


HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE G.S. SARRAF

Mr.Shobit Tiwari, for the appellant.
None present, for the respondents.
 
 ***
</pre><p id="p_1"> 	 The claimant appellant has filed this appeal against the award dated 24.3.2000 passed by Motor Accident Claims Tribunal, Jaipur City, Jaipur in motor accident claim case no.476/1998.
</p><p id="p_2">	It is not necessary to narrate the facts of the case as the short controversy  is that whether the compensation awarded by the Tribunal in this case is fair and reasonable?
</p><p id="p_3">	The claimant appellant has been awarded a compensation of Rs.3500/- for two simple injuries suffered by her in an accident on 14.6.1998. 
</p><p id="p_4">	I am of the opinion that learned Tribunal has been quite generous in awarding the compensation amount and there is absolutely no ground for enhancement. I am also of the opinion that the award does not deserve to be interferred with as regards the order of interest. 
</p><p id="p_5">	There is no merit in the appeal. 
</p><p id="p_6">	Consequently, the appeal stands dismissed.
</p><p id="p_7">		(G.S. SARRAF), J.
</p>
