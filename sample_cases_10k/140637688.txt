<p id="p_1">                                  This petition has been filed to quash the proceedings in

                      Crime No.285 of 2018 on the file of the first respondent police, as

                      against the petitioner.
</p>


<p id="p_2">                                  2.Today, (i.e., 26.11.2019) when the matter is taken up

                      for hearing the learned Additional Public Prosecutor would submit

                      that the first respondent police has completed investigation in this

                      case and filed a final report before the concerned court and the

                      same was taken cognizance in S.T.C.No.324 of 2018.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                                  3.Since the prayer sought for by the petitioner has

                      become infructous, this Criminal Original Petition is dismissed as

                      infructuous. Consequently, connected miscellaneous petition is

                      closed.
