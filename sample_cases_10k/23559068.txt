<div class="doc_title">An Application For Anticipatory ... vs Mujibar Molla on 11 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                  1

Sl No. 86
11.11.2016</pre>
<p id="p_1">Allowed
 pk
                      C.R.M. 7609 of 2016


In the matter of : An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the
Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 16.09.2016 in connection with Illambazar
Police Station Case No. 197 of 2014 dated 26.12.2014 under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_2">Sections
147</a>/<a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_3">148</a>/<a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_4">149</a>/<a href="/doc/1517318/" id="a_5">436</a>/<a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_6">380</a>/<a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_7">427</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_8">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/535894/" id="a_9">Section 9(b)(ii)</a> of
the Indian Explosives Act.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               And
 In the matter of: Mujibar Molla
                                                        ... Petitioner

Mr. Firoz Edulji,
Mr. Sanjib Kumar Dan               ...       for the petitioner


Mr. Pradyut Saha                         ... for the State.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         On scrutiny of the case diary produced by State counsel, no seizure is
reflected in fact no seizure list is found in the case diary. Charge sheet has
already been submitted. This entitles the petitioner to grant of anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_4">         Accordingly, let the petitioner, namely, Mujibar Molla, be released on
anticipatory bail, in the event of his arrest upon furnishing bond of Rs. 10,000/-
with two sureties of Rs. 5,000/- each to the satisfaction of the court below.
</p><p id="p_5">         In view of the aforesaid, the application is disposed of and the same is
allowed.
</p><p id="p_6">         Certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the parties on priority
basis.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                ( Patherya, J.)



                                       ( Debi Prosad Dey, J. )
