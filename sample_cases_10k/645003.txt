<p id="p_1">	This
Appeal from Order is of the 2007 and is adjourned at least 22 times
for one or the other ground. Even, the interim relief granted
earlier, has been continued since more than one and half year. 
</p>
 

<p id="p_2">	It
is reported that  Shri Ravindra Shah, learned advocate appearing on
behalf of the appellant has filed a sick note. Under the
circumstances and in the interest of justice, as a last chance S.O.
to 28.8.2008. 
</p>
 

<p id="p_3">	On
that day irrespective of sick note or leave note of any of the
learned advocates appearing for the respective parties, the present
Appeal from Order shall be heard and decided on merits. Ad-interim
relief granted earlier to continue till then. 
</p>
