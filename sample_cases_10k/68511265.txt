	Learned counsel for the Poultry Feed Industry/7th respondent as well as the learned counsel for the Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board/ Respondent Nos.1 and 2 state that the 7th respondent's Mill was closed from 30.10.2014 and thus cause of action does not survive.
