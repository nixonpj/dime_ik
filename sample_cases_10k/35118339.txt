<div class="doc_title">M/S.Nre Metcoke Pvt. Ltd. &amp; Ors vs Unknown on 13 January, 2010</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Aniruddha Bose</div>

<pre id="pre_1">    ORDER SHEET                                            SHEET NO.1
                          T.A.No.6 of 2010
                          C.A.No.538 of 2009
                          C.P.No.310 of 2009
                  IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                   Constitutional Writ Jurisdiction
                            ORIGINAL SIDE



                           IN THE MATTER OF:-
                           M/S.NRE METCOKE PVT. LTD. &amp; ORS.




       For Petitioner :Mr.M.S.TIWARI, Advocate.



         BEFORE:
    The Hon'ble JUSTICE ANIRUDDHA BOSE</pre>
<p id="p_1">    Date : 13th January,2010


                     The Court:-The present Judge's Summon has been

taken out for correction of certain clerical error in describing

the name of the transferee company in the order passed on 30th

October, 2009. By this order the Scheme of Amalgamation involving

three companies was granted sanction by this Court. In the order,

however, the name of the transferee company was recorded as M/s.

Shree Salasar Coke (Gujarat) Pvt. Ltd. This Company was, in fact,

one of the applicants at the time of filing of the application

under <a href="/doc/1562602/" id="a_1">Sections 391</a>, <a href="/doc/1187271/" id="a_2">392</a> and <a href="/doc/301194/" id="a_3">394</a> of the Companies Act, 1956.


</p><p id="p_2">                     During   subsistence      of   the   application,

however, the name of M/s. Shree Salasar Coke (Gujarat) Pvt. Ltd.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                         2</span>


has been changed. In this regard an application was taken out for

recordal of such change being C.A.No.538 of 2009. This application

was allowed in substance by an order passed on 18th August, 2009.

In view of the same, the name of the applicant-company should have

appeared in the order sheet as "M/s. NRE METCOKE PVT. LTD. &amp; ORS."

However, the order records the name of the company as "M/s. Shree

Salasar Coke (Gujarat) Pvt. Ltd." The application is being taken

out for correction of the name of the company as it appears in the

order passed on 30th October 2009. This is an error of clerical in

nature.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><pre id="pre_2">                                   Under     these          circumstances,          the       present

application                  is   allowed   in   terms      of    prayer    (a)    of   the   Judge's

Summons.


                                   Department     and       all   parties    are    to    act   on   a

</pre><p id="p_4">photostat signed copy of this order on the usual undertakings.




</p><p id="p_5">                                                                           (Aniruddha Bose, J.)



N. Mukherjee
Assistant Registrar [C.R.]
 </p>
