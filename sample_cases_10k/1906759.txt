  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

Con.Case(C).No. 745 of 2008(S)


1. MR.S.MANILAL, AGED 52 YEARS,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. MRS.DR.SHARMILA MARY JOSEPH,
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.ALIAS M.CHERIAN

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice ANTONY DOMINIC

 Dated :23/06/2008

 O R D E R
                           ANTONY DOMINIC, J.

                         ===============
                 Cont. Case (C) NO. 745 OF 2008 - S
                 ======================

                Dated this the 23rd day of June, 2008

                              J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      The petitioner complains that respondent has disobeyed Annexure

A1 to A3 judgments in the matter of conducting an enquiry under <a href="/doc/201374/" id="a_1">Section

65</a> of the Revenue Recovery Act. According to the petitioner, enquiry into

the means of the petitioner has not been held as directed by this court.

</p><p id="p_2">      2.    I  heard     the   learned   Government   Pleader,   who    has

instructions in the matter.     It is submitted that on 3 occasions viz.

13/3/2008, 26/3/2008 and 8/5/2008, petitioner was afforded opportunity

of hearing and that it is thereafter that Annexure A6 was issued. Going by

the steps taken by the respondents resulting in Annexure A6, I do not find

any deliberate disobedience of the judgments rendering the respondent

liable to be proceeded against. If the petitioner is aggrieved, petitioner

may challenge Annexure A6 on merits.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      Contempt of court case is closed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">                                              ANTONY DOMINIC, JUDGE
