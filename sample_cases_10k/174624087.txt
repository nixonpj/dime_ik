                                         W.P.T.T. 33 of 2010.



           Mr. Sumit Kumar Chakraborty,
           Mr. Debanuj Basu Thakur.................For the petitioner.

           Ms. Seba Roy................................For the respondents.</pre>


<p id="p_1">                 Affidavit in reply filed today is kept with record. Mr. Chakraborty submits
           that the report prepared by the concerned officer is not correct and, according to
           him, the officer concerned did not make any visit.
</p>

<p id="p_2">                 Without entering into the controversy on this aspect, we think that the best
           course of action would be to see that the petitioner resumes his business
           lawfully. As such, a chance should be given, provided the following documents
           are produced before this Court on the next date of hearing:
</p>

<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                 1.    Valid trade licence issued by the municipal corporation ;

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                 2.    Valid rent receipt of the tenanted premises where the business
                       activity is said to have been done;
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">                 3.    Last three years' assessment orders and balance sheets under the
                       <a href="/doc/789969/" id="a_1">Income Tax Act</a>, before cancellation of registration;

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">                 4.    Electricity and telephone bills pertaining to the period before
                       cancellation of registration.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_5">
                 The matter is adjourned till 6th December, 2010.


</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_6">                                                                           (K.J. SENGUPTA,J.)

                                                                (KANCHAN CHAKRABORTY,J.)
 </blockquote>
