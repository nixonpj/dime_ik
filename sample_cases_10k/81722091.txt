<div class="doc_title">Dharmendar Kumar @ Jugnu vs State Of Bihar on 20 April, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">              IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              Cr.Misc. No.1319 of 2009
           DHARMENDAR KUMAR @ JUGNU, S/o. Ram Kumar Singh,
                R/o. Village-Dhanaur, P.S. Katra, Distt. Muzaffarpur.
                                        WITH
                             Cr.Misc. No.15079 of 2009
              LAL PRASAD SAH @ LAL JEE, S/o. Sri Hira Lal Sah,
           R/o. Village- Aunis Zero Mile, P.S. Bisfi, District- Madhubani.
                                       Versus
                              THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                      -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">03   20.04.2009                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the

                    State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                Cr.   Misc.    No.1319      of    2009       on   behalf

                    Dharmendar Kumar @ Jugnu and Cr. Misc. No.15079 of

                    2009 on behalf of Lal Prasad Sah @ Lal Jee have arisen

                    out of the same occurrence registered for the offence under

                    <a href="/doc/399026/" id="a_1">Sections 399</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1784492/" id="a_2">402</a> of the I.P.C. and <a href="/doc/1961417/" id="a_3">Section 25</a>(1-b)a, 26

                    &amp; 35 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_4">Arms Act</a> so both the applications have been

                    heard together and are being disposed of by one order.

</p><p id="p_3">                                On 24.09.2008 the petitioners and others were

                    apprehended with the suspicion that they were trying to

                    commit some serious offence. At the time of their being

                    apprehended one country-made loaded pistol were

                    recovered from the possession of Dharmendar Kumar @

                    Jugnu and one pistol from Lal Prasad Sah @ Lal Jee.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Considering the period of detention in the case,
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                          2</span>




       both the petitioners named above are directed to be

       released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-

       (Ten thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount

       each to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate,

       Muzaffarpur in connection with Ahiyapur P.S. Case

       No.366 of 2008.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                              (Shyam Kishore Sharma, J.)
Mkr.
</p>
