<div class="doc_title">Naseer S/O Parmaal B/C Meo vs State Of Rajasthan on 15 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">          HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                      BENCH AT JAIPUR

       S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 5889/2019

Naseer S/o Parmaal B/c Meo, Aged About 40 Years, R/o Beema,
Police Station Firojpur, Distt. Nuh Mewat (Hariyana) At Present
R/o Village Aandhaka, Tehsil Tijara, Distt. Alwar Raj. (At Present
In Central Jail Alwar)
                                                                    ----Petitioner
                                     Versus
State Of Rajasthan, Through P.p.
                                                                  ----Respondent</pre>


<pre id="pre_2">For Petitioner(s)           :    Mr. Bilal Ahamad
For Respondent(s)           :    Mr. Deshraj Gosingha, PP



             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE INDERJEET SINGH

                                      Order

15/05/2019

</pre><p id="p_1">1.      The present bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

Cr.P.C. The petitioner has been arrested in connection with FIR

No.66/2019 registered at Police Station Naugava, District Alwar

for the offences under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a> of IPC &amp; <a href="/doc/1092263/" id="a_3">Section 3</a>/<a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_4">25</a> of Arms

Act.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">2.      Counsel for the petitioner submits that co-accused Aneesh

has already been released on bail by Co-ordinate Bench of this

Court vide order dated 25.04.2019 in S.B. Criminal Misc. IInd Bail

Application No.5394/2019. Counsel further submits that case of

the petitioner is similar to that of co-accused Aneesh.

</p><p id="p_4">3.      Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

</p><p id="p_5">4.      Considering the contentions put-forth by the counsel for the

petitioner and taking into account the facts and circumstances of

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      (Downloaded on 29/06/2019 at 01:24:08 AM)</span>
                                                                            (2 of 2)                   [CRLMB-5889/2019]


                                   the case and without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

                                   case, this court deems it just and proper to enlarge the petitioner

                                   on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   5.       Accordingly, the bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C. is

                                   allowed and it is ordered that the accused-petitioner Naseer S/o

                                   Parmaal shall be enlarged on bail provided he furnishes a

                                   personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two sureties of

                                   Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial Judge for

                                   his appearance before the court concerned on all the dates of

                                   hearing as and when called upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                                                                                              (INDERJEET SINGH),J

                                   JKP/31
