Raja

Delhi­52


       Date of Institution                       : 03.10.2011
       Date on which award reserved              : 14.2.2012
       Date of passing of award                  : 14.2.2012

                                       AWARD 


1      The reference was received   from the Government of NCT of Delhi 
being reference no.F 24/ID/(48)/11/NWD/(279)/11/Lab./4886­90   dated 
23.9.2011</pre><p id="p_1">   on   the   basis   of   claim   filed   by   the   Workman   before   the 
Conciliation Officer u/s 12(4) of the Industrial Dispute Act, 1947 and after 
giving   appropriate   hearing,   the   Ld.Deputy   Labour   Commissioner   has 
referred the matter alongwith the terms of the reference   to this court as 
follows: 
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                                 Raja Vs M/s Eastern  Steel Engineering
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                             2/3</span>

                             Terms of Reference 
       "Whether   services   of   Sh.Raja   s/o   Sh.Ram   Nath   have   been     illegally 
and/or unjustifiably terminated by the management; and if yes, to what relief  
is he entitled and what directions are necessary in this respect?".
</blockquote>


<p id="p_2">2      After receiving the reference, the workman was directed to file the 
statement of claim which was filed by him primarily stating that he was 
working with the management for the last 5 years and was drawing the 
salary of  Rs.6000/­p.m but  legal benefits were not being provided to him 
by the management. Thereafter, the matter was taken up before the Labour 
Conciliation Officer where matter could not be reconciled/ settled and the 
reference was made to this court.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3      Management   was   served     but   before   the   filing   of   the   written 
statement,  parties have shown their inclination to get their matter settled 
and   accordingly   the   matter   was   settled   between   the   parties   outside   the 
court .
</p> 
<p id="p_4">4      Keeping in view the terms of the settlement arrived at between both 
the parties,  the workman has received Rs.9000/­ by way of cheque bearing 
no.   140435   dt.14.2.2012   drawn   on   Citi   Bank,   from   the   management 
towards full and final settlement of all his  claims pertaining to the present 
reference . Statement of the parties is also recorded  separately to this effect 
and in view of the amicable settlement arrived at between the parties,  there 
is no need of deciding the issues separately. Accordingly, present reference 
is answered by holding that the parties have settled their matter and now 
nothing is due against the management arising out of the present reference. 
A settlement award is passed  to the above effect.
</p>



<p id="p_5">                                                     Raja Vs M/s Eastern  Steel Engineering

5     A copy of this Award be sent to the Deputy Labour Commissioner, 
Government of NCT of Delhi of Disst/Area concerned for publication as per 
rules and judicial file be consigned to Record Room as per rules.
</p>
