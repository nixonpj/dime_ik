<div class="doc_title">Abdul Jalil vs Unknown on 14 November, 2014</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Pranab Kumar Chattopadhyay</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                                       1


14.11.2014</pre>
<p id="p_1"> Sl. No.314
    ad
                         C.R.M 15057 of 2014

        In re: An application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of
Criminal Procedure filed on 29.10.2014 in connection with Falakata
Police Station Case No.135 of 2011 dated 10.05.2011 under <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_2">Section 395</a>
of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1518620/" id="a_3">Sections 25(1)(A)</a>/<a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_4">27</a> of the Arms Act.</p><pre id="pre_2">

                         AND

      In the matter of : Abdul Jalil
                                           .........          Petitioner

Mr. Arnab Saha       .........       For the petitioner

Mrs. Anusuah Sinha       .........               For the State


</pre><p id="p_2">        The petitioner is seeking bail in connection with a case relating

to offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_5">Section 395</a> of the Indian Penal Code and

<a href="/doc/1518620/" id="a_6">Sections 25(1)(A)</a>/<a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_7">27</a> of the Arms Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">       The learned advocate representing the petitioner submits that

the petitioner is in custody for more than 3 years and charge sheet has

already been submitted. The learned Advocate of the petitioner further

submits that other charge sheeted co-accused persons have already

been granted bail.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       The learned Advocate for the State does not dispute the aforesaid

submissions made on behalf of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_5">       Having    considered   the   materials      in   the   case   diary   and

considering the fact that the petitioner is in custody for more than three

years and charge sheet has already been submitted and other charge



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                       1</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                     2</span>


sheeted co-accused persons have already been granted bail, we are of

the opinion that further detention of the accused/petitioner is not

necessary.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       Therefore, the accused/petitioner, namely, Abdul        Jalil   be

released on bail upon furnishing bond of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five

Thousand only) with one local surety of like amount, to the satisfaction

of learned Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate, Alipurduar.

</p><p id="p_7">       The application for bail, thus, stands disposed of.



</p><p id="p_8">                         (Pranab Kumar Chattopadhyay, J.)


                                (Sudip Ahluwalia, J.)
