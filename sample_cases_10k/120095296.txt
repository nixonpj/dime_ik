<div class="doc_title">Jagarnath Oraon vs The State Of Jharkhand on 12 October, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                   B.A. No. 8031 of 2019

                  1. Jagarnath Oraon
                  2. Krishna Oraon                                ...     Petitioners
                                               -versus-
                  The State of Jharkhand                          ...    Opposite Party
                      CORAM :       HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANDA SEN
                                              ----
</pre><p id="p_1">                      For the Petitioners:   Mr. Kripa Shankar Nanda, Advocate
                      For the State     :    Ms. Srabani Sanyal, A.P.P.
</p><p id="p_2">                                              ----
</p><p id="p_3">          4/ 12.10.2019     Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p id="p_4">                           Petitioners are accused for allegedly committing the offence
               punishable under <a href="/doc/695990/" id="a_1">Sections 364</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_2">34</a>, <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_3">302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a>, <a href="/doc/386021/" id="a_5">201</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal
               Code, in connection with Sisai Police Station Case No. 39 of 2017 (G.R.
               No.334 of 2017) corresponding to Sessions Trial No.207 of 2017, pending
               in the Court of learned Additional Sessions Judge VI, Gumla.
</p><p id="p_5">                           Prayer for bail of the petitioners was earlier rejected on
               17.01.2018. Petitioners have renewed their prayer for bail.
</p><p id="p_6">                           During the course of argument, it has been submitted that four
               out of six witnesses have already been examined.
</p><p id="p_7">                           Considering the stage of trial and the fact that prayer for bail
               of the petitioners was earlier rejected, I find no fresh ground to entertain
               the prayer for bail of the petitioners. Accordingly, prayer for bail of the
               petitioners, abovenamed, in connection with Sisai Police Station Case
               No.39 of 2017 (G.R. No.334 of 2017) corresponding to Sessions Trial
               No.207 of 2017, pending in the Court of learned Additional Sessions Judge
               VI, Gumla is hereby rejected.
</p><p id="p_8">                           Trial Court is directed to conclude the trial without giving
               unnecessary adjournments in the case.
</p><p id="p_9">                           This application stands dismissed.
</p>


<p id="p_10">                                                                  ( Ananda Sen, J.)
Kumar/Cp-03
