<p id="p_1">                                             ***************

</p><p id="p_2">02.   09.05.2014                  Heard learned counsel for the parties.

</p><p id="p_3">                                  The Court was initially reluctant to accept the

                      contention of the counsel for the petitioner after having

                      perused the order passed in appeal, by virtue of which

                      the order of removal of the petitioner from the

                      responsibility of Anganbari Sevika by the District

                      Programme Officer, Siwan was upheld.

</p><p id="p_4">                                  Annexure-4, dated 02.03.2012 is the order of

                      the District Programme Officer, removing the petitioner

                      from the responsibility of Anganbari Sevika. A look at the

                      said order would show that it is a readymade order,

                      where the name of the petitioner and the centre number
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court CWJC No.9486 of 2013 (2) dt.09-05-2014</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                          2/2</span>




                    has been filled up, rest of the contents stand typed from

                    before. There can't be a better case of non-application of

                    mind by an administrative authority than the present

                    case. If the first order itself suffers from such a vice, the

                    same cannot stand cured at the appellate stage also,

                    merely because it is a more detailed order.

</p><p id="p_5">                                 Both Annexure-4, dated 02.03.2012, passed

                    by the District Programme Officer, Siwan and the

                    appellate order, dated 07.09.2012, passed by the District

                    Magistrate, Siwan are hereby quashed.

</p><p id="p_6">                                 Writ application is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                                (Ajay Kumar Tripathi, J.)
SKM/-
