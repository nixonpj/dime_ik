<div class="doc_title">An Application For Bail Under ... vs Madhab Sarkar on 8 April, 2014</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Subhro Kamal Mukherjee</div>

<p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                     1</span>

C/L   April 8,                                      C.R.M. 3930 of 2014
</p><p id="p_2">35.    2014
       Bpg.
</p><p id="p_3">                 In the matter of : An application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code
                 of Criminal Procedure filed on March 7, 2014 in connection with
                 Dhupguri Police Station Case No. 93 of 2013 dated March 6, 2013
                 under <a href="/doc/261195/" id="a_2">Sections 419</a>/<a href="/doc/261195/" id="a_3">468</a>/<a href="/doc/261195/" id="a_4">471</a> of the Indian penal Code read with
                 <a href="/doc/5353/" id="a_5">Sections 14(A)(b)</a>/<a href="/doc/173097740/" id="a_6">14C</a> of Foreigners Act;
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                                   And

                 In the matter of :         Madhab Sarkar.
</p><p id="p_5">                                                      ...petitioner.
</p><p id="p_6">                                                Versus
                                         State of West Bengal
                                                       ...opposite party.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                         Mr. Rajdeep Majumdar,
                                         Mr. Diptangshu Basu.
</p><p id="p_8">                                                   ...for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                         Mr. Souvik Mitter.
</p><p id="p_10">                                                         ...for the State.
</p><p id="p_11">                                 This is an application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_7">Section 439</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure praying
                 for release on bail in connection with Dhupguri Police Station Case No.93 of 2013 dated March 6,
                 2013 relating to an offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1667388/" id="a_8">Sections 419</a>/<a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_9">468</a>/<a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_10">471</a> of the Indian Penal Code read
                 with <a href="/doc/5353/" id="a_11">Sections 14(A)(b)</a>/<a href="/doc/173097740/" id="a_12">14C</a> of Foreigners Act.
</p>

<p id="p_12">                                 We have heard the learned advocates appearing for the parties and perused the case
                 diary.
</p>

<p id="p_13">                                 It is submitted on behalf of the petitioner that the petitioner is in custody for more
                 than one year and charge-sheet has been submitted and that further detention of the petitioner is not
                 necessary.
</p>

<p id="p_14">                                 It appears, prima facie, that the accused petitioner to negate the allegation of this
                 prosecution that he has been a foreigner produced certain government documents including the
                 PAN Card. We, therefore, feel that further detention of the petitioner is not necessary.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                         2</span>

<p id="p_15">                Having considered the case diary and the materials on record, and particularly, when
charge-sheet has been submitted and the petitioner is in custody for more than one year, we are of
the opinion that further detention of the petitioner is not necessary.
</p>

<p id="p_16">                As such, the petitioner, namely, Madhab Sarkar, shall be released on bail to the
satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Jalpaiguri, upon execution of a bail bond of Rs.
5,000/- (Rupees five thousand) only with two sureties of like amount, one of whom must be local,
on condition that the petitioner shall not leave the territorial jurisdiction of Dhupguri Police Station
without the leave of the trial court.
</p>

<p id="p_17">                         The application for bail, thus, stands allowed.
</p>

<p id="p_18">                                                      ( Subhro Kamal Mukherjee, J. )



                                                           ( Shib Sadhan Sadhu, J. )
