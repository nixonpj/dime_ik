           IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

         CIVIL APPLICATION (FOR ORDERS) NO. 5410 of 2014
         In SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 5537 of 2014
===========================================================
=====
          PINTOO @ MITHUN RAMESHBHAI MANDUBARE....Applicant
                                   Versus
              COMMISSIONER OF POLICE &amp; 2....Respondents
================================================================
Appearance:
PARTY-IN-PERSON, ADVOCATE for the Applicant.
MS ASMITA PATEL, LD.A.G.P. for the Respondent No. 2
================================================================

        CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.G.SHAH

                           Date : 20/05/2014
                               ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">1.          Heard Party-in-person - Vaishaliben Mithun @ Pintoo
and Ms.Asmita Patel, learned Assistant Government Pleader for
the respondent-State and perused the record of the case. It
appears from the case papers of Smt.S.C.L. Municipal General
Hospital that the applicant is pregnant and Doctor has given
probable date of delivery is 17/05/2014, therefore, it would be
appropriate    to release the husband of               the applicant on
temporary bail.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.          Considering        the   above       aspects,    the     present
application is partly allowed. Detenu - Pintoo @ Mithun
Rameshbhai Mandubare is ordered to be released on temporary
bail for a period of 15 days from the date of his actual release,
on   furnishing     personal    bond    of     Rs.10,000/-   (Rupees    Ten
Thousands) before the Jail authority on condition that the
detenue shall mark his presence before Ramol Police Station on
every fourth day. Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p>
