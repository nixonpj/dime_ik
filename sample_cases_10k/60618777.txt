<div class="doc_title">Prasun Dassani vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 3 February, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                            WP-973-2016
             (PRASUN DASSANI Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


03-02-2016

Shri Vivek Dalal, learned counsel for the petitioner.
Shri Rohit Mangal, learned counsel for the State on
advance notice.
Learned counsel appearing for the petitioner submits that
the petitioner is the owner of the disputed property and in
this regard he has referred to the sale deed enclosed with
the writ petition. He further submits that the extent of the
unauthorized construction has not been ascertained and
straightway the notice (Annexure P/1) has been issued for
removing the alleged unauthorized construction within 24
hours. He further submits that no opportunity of hearing
was given before issuing the said notice.
Issue notice on admission and interim relief to the
respondents, on payment of PF within three days.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Counsel for the petitioner is also permitted to serve
Humdast notice to respondents in addition to the usual
mode of service on payment of separate PF within 3 days.
Counsel for the petitioner is permitted to implead the M.P.
Road Development Corporation within 3 days.
Let the notice be issued to the said newly added
respondent on payment of PF within 3 days.
Till the next date of hearing, the respondents are
restrained from taking coercive action in pursuance to the
impugned notice.
</p><p id="p_2">List along with W.P.No.910/2016 on 19.02.2016.
 CC as per rules.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                   (PRAKASH SHRIVASTAVA)
                           JUDGE
