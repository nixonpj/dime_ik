                             JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">       The    petitioner         is     a    senior      citizen.     He

 seeks an early disposal of S.M.No.1/2019 of

 the     file     of       the   Special          Tahsildar       (LA),

 Ottapalam.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       2.    In the light of the judgment of this

 Court in W.P.(C) No.28398/2017 and connected

 cases       dated     14/3/2018            and    the     guidelines

 issued      by      the     Government           as     per    G.O.(P)

 No.09/2018/RD, dated 22/2/2018, there shall be

 a direction to the Land Tribunal to conclude

 the   S.M.Proceedings            in        accordance         with   law

 within a period of six months after hearing

 the petitioner and all affected persons.

</p><p id="p_3">       The petitioner shall produce a copy of the

 judgment in W.P.(C) No. 28398/2017 along with
 WP(C).No. 9806 of 2019



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                         -3-</span>

 this judgment for compliance.

</p><p id="p_4">      The writ petition is disposed of as

 above.
</p>
