<div class="doc_title">Niranjan Kumar Gupta vs Karam Chand Thakur &amp; Ors on 5 December, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">               IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                           C.R. No.1140 of 2006
                         NIRANJAN KUMAR GUPTA
                                  Versus
                       KARAM CHAND THAKUR &amp; ORS
                                 -----------
</pre><p id="p_1"> 3    05.12.2008             Heard counsel for the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_2">                             This Court is fully satisfied that no error much less

                   any jurisdictional error has been committed by the Court

                   below while passing the impugned order.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             This Court has also perused the certified copy of the

                   entire order sheet and from the same it is clear that whatever

                   delay had been caused after 26.3.1998 when the evidence of

                   the both sides were completed, argument heard and the suit

                   was posted for judgment was entirely the own making of the

                   plaintiff-petitioners. .
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             That being so, this Court would not find any reason

                   to interfere with the impugned order imposing cost for

                   reopening of the case from the stage of evidence of plaintiff,

                   which in effect would amount to causing un called delay of

                   more than ten years in disposal of suit. If the petitioner in

                   capacity of plaintiff wants to examine himself his such prayer

                   could not have been allowed without adequately compensating

                   the defendant-opposite parties by imposing cost on the

                   petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Accordingly, this Civil Revision application being

                   wholly misconceived is hereby dismissed with a direction to

                   dispose of the suit in question of the year 1990 within a period

                   of six months.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">Bibhash                                                  (Mihir Kumar Jha, J.)
