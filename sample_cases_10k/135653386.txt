<div class="doc_title">Subrata Sarkar @ Haka vs Unknown on 3 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                     1


03.4.2018</pre>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">   409</span>
<p id="p_1">    ar                        C.R.R 673 of 2018

            In Re:- An application under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal
            Procedure filed on 27.3.2018 in connection with Taherpur P.S.
            Case No. 23 of 2018 dated 12.02.2018 under <a href="/doc/76352360/" id="a_2">Sections 195A</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of
            the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                         And

            In the matter of : Subrata Sarkar @ Haka ... Petitioner

            Mr. Subrata Sarkar (in person) ...For the petitioner



                 Heard Mr. Subrata Sarkar, appears in person.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                 The petitioner, in person, has moved this application under
            <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_4">Section 482</a> of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 with a prayer
            for quashing of the proceedings in connection with Taherpur P.S.
            Case No. 23 of 2018 dated 12.02.2018 under <a href="/doc/76352360/" id="a_5">Sections 195A</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of
            the Indian Penal Code pending before the learned Additional Chief
            Judicial Magistrate, Ranaght, inter alia, on the ground stated in
            the petition.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                 Mr. Sarkar is directed to serve a copy of this application upon
            the learned Public Prosecutor as well as Mr. Bipro Das Sarkar by
            speed post with acknowledgment due within one week from date.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                 List the matter on 17.4.2018 under the same heading.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                 Since the petitioner is on bail in connection with Taherpur

            P.S. Case No. 89 of 2017 dated 01.4.2017 under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_7">Sections

            302</a>/<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_8">120B</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/1961417/" id="a_9">Sections 25(i)(a)</a>/<a href="/doc/412448/" id="a_10">27</a>/<a href="/doc/177872/" id="a_11">35</a> of
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                           2</span>


the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_12">Arms Act</a>, which is at the stage of framing charge, he is

allowed to attend before the Court of Sessions from his residence

on the date fixed and no interference be made when he shall

attend the Court, as per the direction of the of the Sessions Court.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                 (Shivakant Prasad, J.)
