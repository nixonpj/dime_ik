	Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India praying for a writ of Declaration declaring that the professional services rendered by the doctors of petitioner's medical college hospital under the nature of diagnostic services on X-Ray Films, CT Scan Films, MRI Films and supply of medical gases and implants are during the course of treatment to patients would not amounts to transfer of property in goods or an incident of contract of service under the scope of "works contract" as per <a href="/doc/294137/" id="a_2">Article 366(29A)(b)</a> of the Constitution of India and under Section 2(33) of the TN VAT Act, 2006. 



		   For petitioner  		:  Ms.G.Sumitra
		   For respondents  		:  Mr.P.H.Arvind Pandiyan
				 		   Additional Advocate General 
			   		           for Mr.A.R.Jayapratap
						   Government Advocate (Taxes)


O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	In view of the orders passed in W.P.Nos. 22678 to 22683 of 2012, this writ petition stands closed. However, it would be open to the petitioner to raise all the issues which had been raised in the said writ petition, before the appropriate authority, while filing its reply to the impugned notice issued to it, relating to the payment of tax and penalty, under the provisions of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006. No costs. 
</p>
