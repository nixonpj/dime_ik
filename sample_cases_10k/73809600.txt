Amandeep Kaur and another
                                                               ... Petitioners
                         vs.

                                                             ... Respondents

CORAM:HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJENDER SINGH MALIK

              for the petitioners.

<p id="p_1">VIJENDER SINGH MALIK,J(ORAL)

              Amandeep Kaur and Harpal Singh, the petitioners before me,

claim themselves to be major and claiming that they have married against

the wishes of respondents No.4 to 13 seek protection to their life and

liberty. They apprehend danger to them from the side of respondents no. 4

to 13. They have placed on record marriage certificate and photographs,

Annexures P-3 and P-4 respectively evidencing their marriage. The

petitioners have submitted representation dated 01.12.2012, Annexure P-5

to respondent no.2.
</p>
<p id="p_2">              Without entering upon an exercise to evaluate the evidentiary

value of the documents placed on the file, I dispose of the petition with the

direction to respondent no.2 to decide the representation of the petitioners,

Annexure P-5 within a period of two months and grant them protection, if

any threat to their life and liberty is perceived. It is made clear that this

order shall
 Crl. Misc. No.M-38256 of 2012                            =2=


not be taken to protect the petitioners from legal action for violation of law

if any committed by them.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><pre id="pre_2">December 03,2012                      (VIJENDER SINGH MALIK )
dinesh                                        JUDGE
