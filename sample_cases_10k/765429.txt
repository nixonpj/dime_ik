  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 29839 of 2010(D)


1. S.S.JAYAKUMAR, GEETHA NIVAS,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE KERALA STATE ELECTION COMMISSION,
                       ...       Respondent

2. THE LOCAL ADMINISTRATION,

3. THE RETURNING OFFICER,

4. MR.SOMAN, COUNCILOR, WARD NO.15,

                For Petitioner  :SRI.T.RAVIKUMAR

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice ANTONY DOMINIC

 Dated :28/09/2010

 O R D E R
                       ANTONY DOMINIC, J.
                     ================
                 W.P.(C) NO. 29839 OF 2010 (D)
                 =====================

         Dated this the 28th day of September, 2010

                          J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      The prayer sought in the writ petition is to direct the 1st

respondent to declare that the 4th respondent is not entitled to

contest in the forthcoming election to the Neyyattinkara

Municipality on the ground that he had voluntarily incurred

disqualification.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2.    The pleadings in      the writ    petition show that

proceedings seeking disqualification viz., OP No.12/2009 is

pending consideration of the Election Commission, and it is at that

stage, the election has been declared and this writ petition has

been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      3.    Going by the provisions of the Act, only on declaration

of disqualification by an order of the Election Commission, can a

candidate suffer disqualification. Therefore, at this stage, the writ

petition filed is premature. In that view, I am not persuaded to

grant the relief sought for.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      Writ petition is dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                      ANTONY DOMINIC, JUDGE
