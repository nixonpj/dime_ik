1. The District Collector,
    Tiruvallur District, Tiruvallur.

2. The Commissioner,

    Tiruvallur District, Tiruvallur.				        	.. Respondents 
						
PRAYER:  Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India praying for issuance of a Writ of Certiorari, to call for the records on the file of the 3rd respondent in its proceedings bearing No.Na.Ka.No.9530/10 L dt.24.12.10 and quash the same as illegal, unjust, incompetent, irregular and without jurisdiction and without any justification with respect to the Item 49 of the list made in the Impugned Order.
                  For Petitioner     : <a href="/doc/123770828/" id="a_2">M/s.V.Raghavachari
                  For R1</a> 		      : Mr.K.J.Sivakumar, GA.

                  For RR3 &amp; 4		    : Mr.N.Inbanathan, G.A.

D. KRISHNAKUMAR J.

kmi
O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	In the impugned notification, the period of licence is granted from 01.01.2011 or from the date of agreement, whichever is later, till 30.06.2011.  The said period has now expired and therefore, nothing survives for consideration in the present writ petition.  Accordingly, the writ petition is dismissed as having become infructuous.   No Costs.

02.02.2017
