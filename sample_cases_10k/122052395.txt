                      CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. 615 of 1994
         =========================================
                         STATE OF GUJARAT....Appellant(s)
                                      Versus
                       GAFURKHAN @ JADUGAR S.PATHAN &amp;
                          1....Opponent(s)/Respondent(s)

         Appearance:
         MR HIMANSHU PATEL, APP for the Appellant(s) No. 1

                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.R. SHAH
                         and
                         HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.J.THAKER

                                     Date : 21/09/2015

                                 ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">                    (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.R. SHAH)
                Today, when the present Criminal Appeal is taken up for final
         hearing, Shri B.R. Parikh, learned advocate appearing on behalf of
         respondent no.1 has stated at the bar that respondent no.1-Gaffar
         Khan alias Jadugar Sattarkhan Pathan has expired on 08/09/2009. He
         has produced on record the xerox copy of the death certificate of
         respondent no.1 herein issued by Vadodara Municipality, which is
         directed to be taken on record.
</p><p id="p_2">                In view of the above, the present Criminal Appeal is dismissed
         as having been abated so far as respondent no.1 is concerned.
</p>


<p id="p_3">                                                                          (M.R. SHAH, J.)


                                                                        (K.J. THAKER, J.)
