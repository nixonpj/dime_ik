<p id="p_1">             Learned counsel for the petitioner prays for permission to

withdraw the instant petition. He further prays that in case he makes a

prayer for regular bail, the same may kindly be directed to be considered at

the earliest. In support of his plea he has pleaded that no overt act and no

injury has been attributed to him.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Having regard to the aforesaid, I deem it appropriate to dispose

of the instant petition with a direction that in case the petitioner approaches

the court of competent jurisdiction for regular bail, the same shall be

disposed of as expeditiously as possible but not later than two days from the

date of its filing.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Disposed of.
</p>
