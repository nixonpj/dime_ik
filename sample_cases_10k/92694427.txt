                     Cr.Misc. No.46484 of 2008
                        GANESH RAM &amp; ANR
                              Versus
                         STATE OF BIHAR
                           -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">              For the petitioners : Mr. Mohan Lal Das
              For the State          : Mr N.N.Tiwary, APP
</p><p id="p_2">                               -----
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">2.   15.12.2008             Petitioners are accused in a case under

                  section 302 and other allied sections<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Penal

                  Code</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                            They are named in the First Information

                  Report along with seven others including Sanjay

                  Ram, Mangal Ram and Harendra Ram. It is submitted

                  on behalf of the petitioners that the case of the

                  petitioners    is     similar    to    that    of    Sanjay     Ram,

                  Mangal Ram and Harendra Ram who have already been

                  admitted to bail by another Bench of this Court

                  under the order contained in Annexures-4 to 6.

</p><p id="p_6">                            Let the court below verify the aforesaid

                  submission of the petitioners that their case is

                  similar to that of          Sanjay Ram, Mangal Ram and

                  Harendra Ram including the period of incarceration

                  as    under   trial      prisoners     and    if    the    case    is

                  similar, then the petitioners i.e. Ganesh Ram and

                  Balindra Ram be admitted to bail on furnishing

                  bail bond of Rs. 5,000/- (five thousand) each with

                  two   sureties      of   the    like   amount       each   to     the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                      2</span>




     satisfaction of F.T.C.5, Siwan in S.T.no. 14/08

     arising out of Pachrukhi P.S.case no. 29/05.



</p><p id="p_7">                                      (V.N.Sinha,J)
