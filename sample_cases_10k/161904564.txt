<div class="doc_title">Kamruddin Ansari @ Kamruddin vs State Of Bihar on 16 October, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">                            IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                         Cr. Misc. No.35788 of 2009
                   KAMRUDDIN ANSARI @ KAMRUDDIN, S/o Habibullah Ansari, R/o
                   Village - Baswariya, P.S. Town Bettiah, Distt. West Champaran -Petitioner
                                                Versus
                   THE STATE OF BIHAR ...................................... Opposite Party.
                                                -----------
</pre><p id="p_1">                   For the Petitioner:-         Mr. Bimlesh Kumar Pandey, Advocate.
                   For the State:-              Mr. Nirbhay Kumar Singh, A.P.P.
</p><p id="p_2">                                                -----------
</p>
<p id="p_3">2.   16.10.2009.                     Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                       learned A.P.P. for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                     The petitioner has been made accused in a

                       case registered under <a href="/doc/361626/" id="a_1">Section 7</a> of the Essential

                       Commodities Act.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                     It has been stated in the first information

                       report that 50 lt. of kerosene oil was recovered from a

                       rickshaw and the rickshaw puller had stated that the

                       kerosene oil was given by Vijay Raut to the petitioner,

                       Kamruddin Ansari @ Kamruddin. Persons of the locality

                       have stated that they are engaged in black marketing of

                       kerosene oil. It is further submitted that nothing has been

                       recovered from the possession of the petitioner, rather

                       recovery is from a rickshaw and the petitioner has been

                       falsely implicated in this case. Petitioner is stated to be in

                       judicial custody since 23.07.2009.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                     Having regard to the facts and circumstances
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                        2</span>




     of the case, I direct the petitioner, Kamruddin Ansari @

     Kamruddin to be released on bail on his furnishing bond

     in the sum of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two

     sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the

     Chief Judicial Magistrate, Bettiah, West Champaran in

     connection with Bettiah Town P.S. Case No. 143/09.


</p><pre id="pre_2">dk                         ( Dr. Ravi Ranjan, J. )
 </pre>
