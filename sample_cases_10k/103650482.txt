<div class="doc_title">Ravinder Singh Sethi @ Rinku Sethi ... vs State Of Punjab Etc on 20 January, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">             IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT
                           CHANDIGARH

                                           CRM No. M-37054 of 2014 (O&amp;M)
                                           Date of Decision: 20.01.2017

Ravinderpal Singh Sethi @ Rinku Sethi
&amp; others                                                       --Petitioners
                             Versus
State of Punjab &amp; another                                      --Respondents
CORAM:- HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.

Present:-    Mr. Ashish Bakshi, Advocate for
             Mr. Ravi Malhotra, Advocate for the petitioners.

             Mr. Varun Sharma, A.A.G., Punjab.

        ***
</pre><p id="p_1">TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA.J

             The instant petition was preferred under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C

seeking quashing of FIR No. 54 dated 28.06.2008 under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_2">Sections 307</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>,

<a href="/doc/1208971/" id="a_4">353</a>, <a href="/doc/1440821/" id="a_5">332</a>, <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_6">333</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_7">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_8">149</a> I.P.C., registered at Police Station Division No.3,

District Jalandhar.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Counsel appearing for the petitioners apprises the Court that in

the trial that ensued, the petitioners already stand convicted by the Trial

Court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Counsel, as such, makes a statement that he would not be

pursuing the instant petition.

</p><p id="p_4">             Dismissed as not pressed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                            (TEJINDER SINGH DHINDSA)
                                                      JUDGE
20.01.2017
