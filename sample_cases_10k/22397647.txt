<div class="doc_title">Sumati Chandra Jain vs Bhaskar A Sawant And Others on 6 January, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
BENCH AT JAIPUR

D.B. CIVIL RESTORATION APPLICATION NO.1213/2011
IN
D.B. CIVIL CONTEMPT PETITION NO.634/2011
IN
D.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.6406/1997

SUMATI CHANDRA JAIN 
Vs.
 SHRI BHASKAR A. SAMANT &amp; ORS.

DATE: 06.01.2012

HON'BLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. ARUN MISHRA
HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE NARENDRA KUMAR JAIN-I


Mr. Rahul Agarwal, for the applicant/petitioner.
                                  ****
</pre><p id="p_1">		There is delay of 42 days in filing the restoration application. 
</p><p id="p_2">		For the reasons stated in the application under <a href="/doc/100581/" id="a_1">Section 5</a> of the Limitation Act, delay of 42 days is condoned. Application under <a href="/doc/100581/" id="a_2">Section 5</a> of the Limitation Act stands allowed. 
</p><p id="p_3">		The petition was dismissed for non-compliance of the order of this Court dated 21.09.2011. The case was dismissed due to mistake of the counsel, for which litigant may not suffer.   
</p><p id="p_4">		Considering the aforesaid aspect, we are inclined to restore D.B. Civil Contempt Petition No.634/2011 to its original number, subject to the condition that the petitioner shall pay a sum of  Rs.250/- as  cost and  make  compliance of the order dated 21.09.2011.  In  case,  petitioner  deposits Rs.250/-  as 
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">:2:</span>
cost  with  the  Rajasthan  State  Legal  Services Authority and make compliance of the order dated 21.09.2011 within 15 days from today, the petition shall stand restored to its original number.
</p><p id="p_5">		The restoration application stands disposed of accordingly.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">(NARENDRA KUMAR JAIN-I),J.         (ARUN MISHRA),CJ.


/KKC
</pre>
