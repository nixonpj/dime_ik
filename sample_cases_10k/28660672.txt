CORAM   

THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE M.M.SUNDRESH

and M.P.(MD) No.1 of 2015 

Ana.Lakshmanan @ Thanneermalai                                       .. Petitioner

Vs 

1.State rep. by
  The Inspector of Police ? Crime,
  North Police Station,
  Karaikudi,
  Sivagangai District.

2.Ana.Soamasundaram                                                   .. Respondents

         Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">section 482</a> of the Code of
Criminal Procedure, praying to call for the records in Crime No.73 of 2014
dated 05.04.2014 on the file of the 1st Respondent Police and to quash the
same. 

!For Petitioner                 : Mr.RST.Ravanan @ Ramasubramanian

For 1st Respondent              : Mrs.Prabha 
                                  Government Advocate (Criminal side)

:ORDER  
</pre><p id="p_1">        This Criminal Original Petition has been filed seeking quash of the
proceedings  in Crime No.73 of 2014 dated 05.04.2014 on the file of the 1st
Respondent Police. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">        2.Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned
Government Advocate (Criminal side) for the 1st respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.The learned Government Advocate (Criminal side) would submit that the
case has been transferred to Devakottai Police Station.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4.Recording the same, this Criminal Original Petition is dismissed with
liberty to the petitioner to file fresh petition. Consequently, connected
M.P.(MD) No.1 of 2015 is also dismissed.
</p>


<p id="p_5">To
</p><p id="p_6">1.The Inspector of Police,
  North Police Station,
  Karaikudi,
  Sivagangai District.
</p>

  Madurai Bench of Madras High Court,
