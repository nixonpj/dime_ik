            brought by the petitioner also failed and was dismissed, vide judgment dated

            19.03.2014 passed by learned Additional Sessions Judge, Ferozepur (for short

            'appellate Court').
</p>
<p id="p_8">                               To challenge the afore-stated judgments/order of the courts below,

            the petitioner has approached this Court by way of instant criminal revision

            petition under <a href="/doc/1457888/" id="a_6">Sections 397</a>/<a href="/doc/1571667/" id="a_7">401</a> of the Criminal Procedure Code, 1973.

</p><p id="p_9">                               Learned counsel for the petitioner has very candidly stated that he

            has nothing to say as regards findings of conviction recorded by the learned

            trial Court and affirmed by the learned appellate Court but he prays for

            leniency as regards quantum of sentence.

</p><p id="p_10">                               Notice of motion.
</p>
<p id="p_11">                               On the asking of the Court, Mr. G.S.Sidhu, Assistant Advocate

            General, Punjab, accepts notice on behalf of the respondent-State.

</p><p id="p_12">                               As agreed, the petition is taken on board for final disposal.

                               As afore-stated, learned counsel for the petitioner does not dispute

            the findings of conviction recorded by the courts below and his only prayer is

            for leniency as regards quantum of sentence awarded to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_13">                               It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner and is not

            disputed by the learned State Counsel that the petitioner is 66 years old, has a

            mentally derailed son and a bed ridden wife to take care of and that he is the

<p id="p_14">                               In the circumstances, judgment of conviction passed by the learned

            trial Court and affirmed by the learned appellate Court, is maintained.

            However, order of sentence is modified and substantive sentence awarded to

            the petitioner is reduced to rigorous imprisonment for one year. Sentence as

            regards fine and default clause is maintained.

            sentence, the petition fails and is dismissed.
