<div class="doc_title">Mr. Pawan Mishra vs Mr. P.S. Saun on 3 June, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">WPCRL No. 612 of 2014


U.C. Dhyani, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">        Mr. Pawan Mishra, Advocate for the
petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">        Mr. P.S. Saun, Dy. Advocate General for
the respondent State.
</p><p id="p_3">        Issue notice to respondent no. 3. Steps be
taken within a week.
</p><p id="p_4">        List after the notice is served upon the said
respondent.
</p><p id="p_5">        An FIR was lodged against seven named
accused, including the petitioner, for the offences
punishable under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_2">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_3">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_4">471</a>,
<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120B</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_6">        Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and
learned Dy. Advocate General.
</p><p id="p_7">        This Court is taken through the contents of
FIR by learned Dy. Advocate General. It is the
contention of learned counsel for the petitioner that
the bank draft was issued by the respondent bank
in the name of Shubh Premier, who did not deliver
the car to the petitioner. Director and other
employees of Shubh Premier are also arrayed as
accused persons in the impugned FIR.
</p><p id="p_8">          It is directed that the petitioner shall
contact the I.O. of the case on 10.06.2014, and on
such subsequent dates, as may be instructed by him
(I.O.). It is provided, as an interim measure, that
no coercive means shall be taken against the
petitioner during the course of interrogation and
investigation, till the next date of listing, so long as
she cooperates with the Investigation Agency.
</p><p id="p_9">         CLMA no. 5679 of 2014 stands disposed
of accordingly.
</p><p id="p_10">         Let a copy of this order be supplied to
learned counsel for the petitioner within 48 hours
on payment of usual charges.
</p>


<p id="p_11">                                   (U.C. Dhyani, J.)
                                       03.06.2014
