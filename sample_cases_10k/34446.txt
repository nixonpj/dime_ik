                         J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Petitioner is aggrieved by Exhibit P1 proceedings under

<a href="/doc/612240/" id="a_1">Section 14B</a> of the Employees Provident Fund and Miscellaneous

Provision Act.   Since the petitioner has a remedy by way of

appeal before the Tribunal, this writ petition is disposed of as

follows.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2. In case the petitioner has not so far filed an appeal, it

will be open to him to file an appeal within a period of two

months from today, in which case, the same shall be treated to

have been filed in time. Thereafter, the Appellate Authority shall

consider the matter with notice to the petitioner and the appeal

shall be disposed of expeditiously.       It will be open to the

petitioner to seek appropriate interim orders before the Appellate

Authority.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">                                       KURIAN JOSEPH, JUDGE.
