<p id="p_1">(Per
: HONOURABLE THE ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE MR.BHASKAR BHATTACHARYA)
 

 


 

	This
is an application seeking permission to withdraw Rs. 5 lac deposited
by the appellant pursuant to an order passed by the Division Bench
while admitting the appeal.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.	It
appears that by the award impugned in the appeal, a sum of Rs.
16,81,200/- with interest was awarded with the finding that the
offending vehicle was not insured.  Consequently, the Tribunal
directed the owner of the vehicle to pay the amount and absolved
insurance company of its liability.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_3">3.	We
find that the owner has come up with an appeal and on an application
for stay submitted by such owner, a Division Bench of this Court
granted interim stay on condition that the appellant should deposit a
sum of Rs. 5 lac by way of interim measure as condition of issue of
notice.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_4">4.	Pursuant
to such direction, the appellant has deposited the said amount of Rs.
5 lac, and the main appeal and the injunction application for stay is
fixed after vacation.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_5">5.	In
the meantime, the respondent claimant has come up with the present
application thereby praying for permission to withdraw the amount of
Rs. 5 lac deposited by the appellant before the Tribunal.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_6">6.	Learned
Advocate appearing on behalf of the appellant submits before us that
he has no objection if the claimant-applicant before us is permitted
to withdraw the sum of Rs. 5 lac deposited by his client.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_7">7.	In
such circumstances, we allow this application by permitting the
claimant to withdraw the amount of Rs. 5 lac deposited before the
Tribunal below.  It is needless to mention that this order will be
subject to the final decision in the appeal, and in the event the
appeal succeeds in the long run, the claimant will be bound to refund
this amount to the appellant.  With the above observation this
application is disposed of.  Let the application for stay come up for
hearing on the day already fixed.
</p> 

<p id="p_8">					(Bhaskar
Bhattacharya, Actg. C.J.)
 

 


 

					(J.B.
</p><p id="p_9">Pardiwala, J.)
