</p><p id="p_7">         2.            Learned AGP Mr. Raval at outset, raised objection

         against the entertainability of the petition on the ground that

         the petition is filed after long delay of more than 30 years.



</p><p id="p_8">         3.            Learned advocate Ms. Makwana for the petitioners

         however submitted that the lower authority has passed the

         order contrary to the provisions of law and contrary to the

         resolution dated 16/03/1982 prevailing at the relevant time

         and therefore delay in approaching the Court should not come

         in the way of the petitioner in challenging the impugned

         orders.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">         4.            The Court, however finds that the petitioners have

         been indolent for more than 30 years long period in

         challenging        the impugned orders. If as per the case of the

         petitioners the impugned orders passed by the authority

         below are illegal or not in accordance with law, the petitioners

         should have approached the Court within a reasonable time.</p><pre id="pre_2">
