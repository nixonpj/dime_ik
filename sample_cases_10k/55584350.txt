<p id="p_1">           The representation Annexure P-8 is not the reflection of

the grievance of the petitioners. The petition is totally vague and does

not specify as to how the petitioners claim seniority over and above

the private respondents. The petition has also been filed without

making an effective demand on the official respondents. In these

circumstances, I am not inclined to interfere at this stage, but deem it

appropriate to dispose of the instant writ petition with liberty to the

petitioners to file a representation making grievance that has been

raised in the petition. In case such a representation is made within a

period of one month from today, the official respondents shall be

obliged to look into it and they would dispose of the same as

expeditiously as possible, preferably within a period of four months

thereafter. The petitioners in the eventuality of any grievance
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> C.W.P.No.23091 of 2011            -2-</span>

subsisting thereafter are at liberty to challenge the decision of the

respondents.
</p>
<p id="p_2">          Petition disposed of.
