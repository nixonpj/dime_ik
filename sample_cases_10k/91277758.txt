            1. Manoj Singh @ Manoj Kumar Singh, Son of Rama Shankar
               Singh, Resident of Village- Keshopur, Police Station-Barahara,
               District-Bhojpur.
            2. Raju Lala     Son of Law Kush Lala, Resident of Village-
               Kalyanpur Pakri, Police Station-Bihiya, District-Bhojpur.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr. Rahul Nath

                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   14-12-2018                  Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                          The petitioners seek regular bail in connection with

                  Excise Case No.1591 of 2018 (arising out of Barhara P.S.Case

                  No.394 of 2018) registered for offences punishable under

                  Sections 30(a) of the Bihar Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>, 2016<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2"> of

                  the Indian Penal Code</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                          Allegation against the petitioner is about recovery of 38

                  ltrs. of liquor from the Bolero vehicle and the petitioner no.1 is

                  the Driver and the petitioner no.2 is the Khalasi.

</p><p id="p_4">                          Submission of the learned counsel for the petitioner is

                   that they have nothing to do with the seized articles and though

                   the petitioner no.1 is accused in one more case but it does not
             Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.73884 of 2018(2) dt.14-12-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                         2/2</span>




                      relate to similar type of case. He is in custody since 4.10.2018.

</p><p id="p_5">                              Heard learned A.P.P. also.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                              Having heard both sides and in view of the facts and

                     circumstances, as stated above, let the petitioner, above named,

                     be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.25,000/-

                     (Twenty Five Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                     each to the satisfaction of the learned 4th A.D.J., Bhojpur at Ara

                     in connection with Excise Case No.1591 of 2018 (arising out of

                     Barhara P.S.Case No.394 of 2018).
</p>
<p id="p_7">                              With the aforesaid observation, this application is

                        allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_8">


</p><p id="p_9">                                               (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
chn/-
</p>
