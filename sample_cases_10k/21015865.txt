<div class="doc_title">Rohit Manjhi &amp; Anr vs The State Of Bihar on 13 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.46728 of 2018
                       Arising Out of PS.Case No. -109 Year- 2018 Thana -BUNIADGANJ District- GAYA
                 ======================================================
                 1. Rohit Manjhi , Son of Sanjay Manjhi
                 2. Sanjay Manjhi, Son of Rameshwar Manjhi, both resident of Village-
                 Dudhaula, P.S.- Buniyadganj, Dist.- Gaya.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                        Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar.

                                                                .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s    : Mr.P.R.Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. S.Ehteshamuddin
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   13-08-2018               Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             Petitioners apprehend their arrest in Buniyadganj

                 P.S.Case No.109 of 2018 registered for the offences under

                 <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>,<a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_3">354</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_4">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506</a> and <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of I.P.C.

</p><p id="p_3">                             Allegation against the petitioner no.1is that on the point

                 of pistol he entered inside the house of the informant and

                 misbehaved with the daughter-in-law of the informant.

</p><p id="p_4">                             Submission of the learned counsel for the petitioners is

                 that petitioner no.2 is father of petitioner no.1 and earlier wife of

                 petitioner no.2 had lodged the F.I.R. bearing Buniyadganj

                 P.S.Case No.107/18 against the informant and his family members

                 on 6.6.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             Heard learned A.P.P. also.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">          Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.46728 of 2018 (2) dt.13-08-2018</span><p id="p_6">

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




</p><p id="p_7">                                    Having heard both sides and in view of the facts and

                      circumstances, as stated above, let the petitioners, above named, in

                      the event of their arrest or surrender before the court below within

                      a period of six weeks from today, be released on bail on furnishing

                      bail bonds of Rs.25,000/- each with two sureties of the like

                      amount each to the satisfaction of A.C.J.M.-8th, Gaya, in

                      Buniyadganj P.S. Case No.109 of 2018 subject to the conditions as

                      laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_7">Section 438(2)</a> of Cr.P.C. and (1) one of the

                      bailors must be a local person having sufficient immovable

                      property within the jurisdiction of the concerned court. (ii) the

                      petitioners will not induce any witness or tamper with the

                      evidence and the petitioners shall co-operate in the investigation

                      of the case and make himself available as and when required by

                      the police, otherwise, the prosecution is free to move for

                      cancellation of their bail bonds.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                                  (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
AnilKrSinha/-
</p>
