<div class="doc_title">Kochu Khadeeja T.I vs The Land Revenue Commissioner on 29 July, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">       

  

  

 
 
  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C).No. 22814 of 2008(N)


1. KOCHU KHADEEJA T.I.,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE LAND REVENUE COMMISSIONER
                       ...       Respondent

2. THE DISTRICT COLLECTOR,

3. THE TAHSILDAR,

                For Petitioner  :SRI.DINESH R.SHENOY

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice V.GIRI

 Dated :29/<a href="/doc/1390448/" id="a_1">07/2008

 O R D E R
                          V.GIRI, J
                        -------------------
                    W.P.(C</a>).22814/2008
                       --------------------
            Dated this the 29th day of July, 2008

                       JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">      Petitioner is currently working as Village Officer at

Pulloot village in Thrissur.              She is a physically

handicapped person. Petitioner is entitled to be promoted

to the post of Deputy Tahsildar.             She was actually

promoted under Ext.P2 order.             But for some personal

reasons, she relinquished her service, as evidenced by

Ext.P3 dated 3.7.2007, for a period of one year. That

period is since over      and now the petitioner is only

desirous to be considered for the post of Deputy Tahsildar.

Petitioner is aggrieved that after the expiry of the period

of relinquishment,     her juniors have been promoted.

Petitioner ought to have been considered for promotion,

after the expiry of the period of relinquishment. These

aspects have been highlighted in Exts.P5               and P6

representations      before       the     Tahsildar, the third

respondent and the Land Revenue Commissioner, the first

respondent, respectively.
</p>
<p id="p_2">W.P.(C).22814/2008
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             2</span>

</p><p id="p_3">2.   Having heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

learned senior Government Pleader, I am of the view

that the petitioner's request ought to be considered.



</p><p id="p_4">3.   In the result, writ petition is disposed of directing

the first respondent to consider Exts.P5 and P6 and do

the needful in the matter of promoting the petitioner to

the post of Deputy Tahsildar, if her claim under Ext.P6

is found to be correct, within one month from the date

of receipt of a copy of this judgment. First respondent

shall also pass orders as regards the petitioner's claim

for increment with effect from 1.5.2008. Petitioner shall

be heard before a decision is taken on the petitioner's

request.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">4.   Petitioner shall produce a copy of the writ petition

along with the copy of the judgment before the first

respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">                                      V.GIRI,
                                      Judge
