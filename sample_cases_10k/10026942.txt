<div class="doc_title">Sunita Devi &amp; Ors vs The State Of Bihar on 2 May, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.13477 of 2017
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -79 Year- 2016 Thana -MADHEPUR District- MADHUBANI
                 ======================================================
                 1. Sunita Devi, W/o - Amarnath Yadav.
                 2. Punit Kumar Yadav, S/o- Amarnath Yadav.
                 3. Kamal Narayan Yadav, S/o Late Dahur Yadav.
                    All Resident of village- Bishanpur, Police Station- Madhepur, District-
                    Madhubani.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioners.
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar.

                                                       .... .... Opposite Party.
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioners : Mr.
                 For the State       : Mr.
                 For the Informant   : Mr.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAJENDRA KUMAR
                 MISHRA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                           ----------
</p>
<p id="p_2">2   02-05-2017            Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the learned

                 A.P.P. for the State as also the learned counsel for the informant.

</p><p id="p_3">                          The petitioners apprehend their arrest in connection with

                 Madhepur P.S. Case No.79 of 2016/G.R. No.1758 of 2016

                 registered under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_3">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506</a> and <a href="/doc/1868826/" id="a_5">498(A)</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the

                 Indian Penal Code besides <a href="/doc/751411/" id="a_7">Sections 3</a>/<a href="/doc/1023340/" id="a_8">4</a> of the Dowry Prohibition

                 Act.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                          The accusation is of torturing the informant for non

                 fulfillment of the dowry demand by her husband and other in-laws

                 and removing her from the matrimonial house.

</p><p id="p_5">                          Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioners
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">       Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.13477 of 2017 (2) dt.02-05-2017</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                 2/2</span>




                   submits that the petitioners are mother-in-law, brother-in-law and

                   grand father-in-law respectively of the informant and they have

                   falsely been implicated in this case only to put undue pressure.

</p><p id="p_6">                             Having considered the facts and the circumstances of the

                   case, let the petitioners, above named, in the event of their arrest

                   or surrender by them within six weeks from today, be enlarged on

                   bail on their furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-(Rupees Ten

                   Thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                   satisfaction      of    the     Additional        Chief   Judicial   Magistrate,

                   Jhanjharpur, District-Madhubani, in connection with Madhepur

                   P.S. Case No.79 of 2016/G.R. No.1758 of 2016, subject to the

                   conditions laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_9">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_7">                                                           (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J)
    P.S./-
</p>
