                       In The High Court At Calcutta
                            Criminal Miscellaneous Jurisdiction
                                      Appellate Side


Present : The Hon'ble Mr Justice Jayanta Kumar Biswas
                           and
          The Hon'ble Mr Justice Indrajit Chatterjee

                                  C.R.M. No.3615 of 2014
                                    Khorshed Mistri @ Sk
</p><p id="p_2">                                             -vs-
</p><p id="p_3">                                  The State of West Bengal

        Mr. Sumanta Das                                            ...for the petitioner

        Mr. Saibal Bapuli                                              ...for the State

Heard on: July 7, 2014

Order on: July 7, 2014

        Jayanta Kumar Biswas, J : The petitioner in the CRM saying that he is
apprehending arrest in connection with Chapra P.S. Case No.812 of 2013 dated
December 3, 2013 under s.498A <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> is seeking bail under s.438 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_2">CrPC</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         We have heard advocates for the petitioner and the State and have perused the
case diary produced before us. It has been submitted that the petitioner is the husband
of the de facto complainant. The accusations are of tortures.
</p>
<p id="p_5">        After examining the case diary materials and the nature of the accusations, we
are of the view that it will be appropriate to grant the petitioner anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_6">        For these reasons, we allow the CRM and direct that in the event the petitioner
is arrested in connection with the case, he shall be released on bail to the satisfaction of
the arresting officer. Certified xerox.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                                            (Jayanta Kumar Biswas, J)



                                                              (Indrajit Chatterjee, J)
