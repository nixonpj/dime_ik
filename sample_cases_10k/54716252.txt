<div class="doc_title">Dhanraj vs State Of Rajasthan Through P P on 29 November, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1"> HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN BENCH AT
                      JAIPUR
          S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail No. 16326 / 2017
Dhanraj S/o Shri Mohan Lal B/c Gurjar, R/o Village Kariri Police
Station Nainwa, District Bundi, At Present in Sub Jail, Nainwa,
District Bundi.
                                                              ----Petitioner
                                   Versus
State of Rajasthan Through PP.
                                                            ----Respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)     : Mr.Harendra Singh
For Respondent(s) : Mr.R.R.Singh Rathore,PP
_____________________________________________________
             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PANKAJ BHANDARI
                                   Order
29/11/2017

</p><p id="p_2">1.    Petitioner has filed this bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of

Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">2.    F.I.R. No.331/2017 was registered at Police Station Nainwa,

District Bundi for offence under <a href="/doc/513777/" id="a_2">Sections 451</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_5">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_6">325</a>

and <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_7">308</a> I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_4">3.    It is contended by the counsel for the petitioner that there is

cross FIR in this case. Four persons were injured from the

petitioner    party   and   two    persons     were     injured   from   the

complainant      party.   None    of   the   injuries   sustained   by   the

complainant is dangerous to life. Petitioner is not required for

further investigation. Co-accused has been given benefit of bail by

this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_5">4.    Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

</p><p id="p_6">5.    Considering the contentions put-forth by the counsel for the
                                 (2 of 2)
                                                        [CRLMB-16326/2017]

petitioner, I deem it proper to allow the bail application.

</p><p id="p_7">6.   This bail application is accordingly allowed and it is ordered

that the accused-petitioner shall be released on bail provided he

furnishes a personal bond of Rs. 50,000/- (Rupees Fifty thousand

only) and two sureties in the sum of Rs.25,000/- (Rupees Twenty

Five thousand only) each to the satisfaction of the learned trial

court with the stipulation to appear before that Court and any

court to which the matter is transferred, on all dates of hearing

and as and when called upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                           (PANKAJ BHANDARI), J.
</p>
<p id="p_9">teekam/54
