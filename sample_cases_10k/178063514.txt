<pre id="pre_1">ORDER SHEET
                                WP No.996 of 2015
                        IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                          Constitutional Writ Jurisdiction
                                 ORIGINAL SIDE

                                                           M/S. SHANTI BUILDERS
                                                                          Versus
                                                      KOLKATA PORT TRUST &amp; ORS.

   BEFORE:
   The Hon'ble JUSTICE DIPANKAR DATTA
   Date: 9th September, 2015
                                                                 Mr. Arik Banerjee, Adv.
                                                                        ..for the petitioner
                                                                 Mr. Somnath Bose, Adv.
                                                                    ..for the respondents</pre>

<p id="p_1">                In pursuance of a tender floated by the Port Trust, the petitioner
    deposited in excess of Rs.7,87,000/- in March, 2014. However, the Port Trust
    could not hand over possession of the land, forming the subject matter of the
    tender, to the petitioner. The petitioner facing such situation approached the
    Port Trust requesting it to return the said amount with interest. The Port
    Trust has agreed to return the principal amount without interest.
</p><p id="p_2">                In view of the above, the writ petition is disposed of directing the
    Port Trust to return the principal amount to the petitioner by two weeks from
    date positively.
</p><p id="p_3">                In so far as awarding interest is concerned, I make no order;
    however, the Chairman of the Port Trust shall, upon granting an opportunity
    of hearing to the petitioner, decide whether it is entitled to interest on the
    principal amount or not.      If the prayer is declined, the order must be
    supported by reasons; on the contrary, if the Chairman decides in favour of
    the petitioner, the amount awarded on account of interest shall be released
    without any delay. Let this exercise be completed within three weeks from the
    date of communication of this order.
</p><p id="p_4">                There shall be no order for costs.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                                     (DIPANKAR DATTA, J.)
AKGoswami
