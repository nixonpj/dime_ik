                   Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.12836 of 2011
    ===========================================================
</pre><p id="p_1">    Kiran Cold Storage, a partnership Firm having its place of Business at Growth
    Centre, N.H. 31, Maranga, Purnea through its Partner Pankaj Kumar Nayak, S/O

    Purnea

                                                                   .... .... Petitioner
                                         Versus
</p><p id="p_2">    1. The Bihar State Electricity Board through its Chairman, Vidyut Bhawan,
    Bailey Road, Patna
</p><p id="p_3">    2. The General Manager Cum Chief Engineer, Kosi Electric Supply Area,
    Saharsa
</p><p id="p_4">    3. The Electrical Superintending Engineer, Electric Supply Circle, Purnea
</p><p id="p_5">    4. The Electrical Executive Engineer, MRT, Purnea
</p><p id="p_6">    5. The Electrical Executive Engineer, Electric Supply Division, Purnea
</p><p id="p_7">    6. The Assistant Electrical Engineer Mrt, Supply Division II, Purnea

                                                         .... .... Respondents
    ===========================================================
           Appearance :
</p><p id="p_8">           For the Petitioner/s : Mr. SURAJ SAMDARSHI
           For the Respondent/s  : Mr. VINAY KIRTI SINGH
    ===========================================================
    CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VIKASH JAIN
    ORAL JUDGMENT
    Date: 02-11-2018

                             The present writ petition has been filed for the

        following reliefs -
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                      "(i)     For quashing of the inspection report dated
                      22.07.2011 as being wholly illegal and without authority of
                      law.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                      (ii)   For restoration of electric supply which has been
                      disconnected by the respondent authorities in pursuance of
                      the inspection dated 22.07.2011.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                      (iii) For a declaration that the provisional assessment
                      order dated 25.07.2011 is illegal and arbitrary.</blockquote>
<p id="p_9">                      (iv) For a declaration that any inspection carried out
          Patna High Court CWJC No.12836 of 2011 dt.02-11-2018




                               contrary to the circular of the Board dated 18.02.2010 is
                               void ab initio.
</p><p id="p_10">                               (v) For a declaration that in case the data has been down
                               loaded to the satisfaction of the respondent authorities
                               three months back, the petitioner cannot be penalized for
                               twelve     months      and       penalty   has   to    be   reduced
                               proportionately for the purpose of <a href="/doc/132967048/" id="a_1">Section 126</a> of the
                               Electricity Act, 2003 and for any other relief or reliefs to
                               which the petitioner is found entitled to in the facts and
                               circumstances of the case."
</p>

<p id="p_11">                                    2. Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that

                 during the pendency of the writ petition, the grievance of the petitioner

                 has been redressed and as such the same need not be pressed.
</p>
<p id="p_12">                                    3. Learned counsel for the respondents appears and he

                 has no objection in this regard.
</p>
<p id="p_13">                                     4. The writ petition stands disposed of.
</p>


<p id="p_14">                                                                                     (Vikash Jain, J)


Md. Ibrarul/-
</p>
