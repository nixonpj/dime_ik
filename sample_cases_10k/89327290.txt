                          JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">               Petitioner and     certain others have filed a

representation before the Panchayath to surrender a pathway to

the Panchayath so that according to them the Panchayath will

take necessary steps for tarring the said pathway and

maintaining the same.     Ext.P2 is the said representation.    It

seems that nothing has been done in the matter by the

Panchayath.   The only relief now sought for is to direct the

Panchayath/3rd  respondent     to  consider   Ext.P2  and   pass

appropriate orders as early as possible.

</p><p id="p_2">          Having   taken    into  consideration  the  facts   and

circumstances of the case, the writ petition is disposed as

follows:-
</p>
<p id="p_3">          The    third   respondent     shall  consider   Ext.P2

representation and pass appropriate orders as early as possible.




</p><p id="p_4">                                A.M. Shaffique
