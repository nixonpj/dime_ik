<div class="doc_title">Binod Yadav &amp; Anr. vs The State Of Bihar on 12 September, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             Cr.Misc. No.21233 of 2011
                        1. Binod Yadav son of Bishwanath Yadav
                       2. Bishwanath Yadav son of Deonandan yadav
                                       Versus
                                 The State Of Bihar
                                      -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">3.   12.09.2011                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as learned

                      Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  Petitioners are named in the First Information Report with

                      allegation that they alongwith other accused persons assaulted the

                      informant as well as informant's son as a result of which informant's

                      son died in course of his treatment.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that deceased

                      had sustained only 2 injuries and, moreover, having similar allegations

                      co-accused Manoj Yadav and others have already been granted privilege

                      of anticipatory bail by another bench of this Court.

</p><p id="p_4">                                  Taking into consideration that there is general allegation of

                      assault against the petitioners and other accused and having, more or

                      less, similar allegations other co-accused have already been granted

                      privilege of anticipatory bail, let the petitioners be released on bail on

                      furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10, 000/-(ten thousand) each with two

                      sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial

                      Magistrate, Bettiah, West Champaran in connection with Jogapatti P.S.

                      Case No. 102 of 2011.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                              (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J.)

       Saif /-
</p>
