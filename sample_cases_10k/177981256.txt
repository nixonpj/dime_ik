<div class="doc_title">Arif @ Monu vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 16 March, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                            MCRC-3527-2017
                  (ARIF @ MONU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


16-03-2017

              Shri K.N. Fakhruddin, Advocate for the applicant.
                 Shri Ajay Tamrakar, Panel Advocate for the
respondent/State with case diary.</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Heard.
</p><p id="p_2">              This is a repeat application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the
        Cr.P.C. for grant of bail to applicant.
</p><p id="p_3">              Applicant has been arrested in connection with Crime
        No.401/2007 registered at Police Station-Piplani, District-
        Bhopal, for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_2">Section 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_3">302</a>,
        <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_4">120-B</a>, <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of IPC and <a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_6">Section 25</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_7">27</a> of the Arms Act.
        Applicant is in jail since 19.2.2013.
</p><p id="p_4">              His earlier application was rejected on 8.4.2015 and
        since then, I do not find any change in the circumstances so
        as to take a different view of the matter.
</p><p id="p_5">             Accordingly, this repeat bail application stands rejected
and closed.
</p>


<p id="p_6">                                                                 (S.K. SETH)
                                                                   JUDGE




irfan
