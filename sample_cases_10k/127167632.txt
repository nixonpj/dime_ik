                     AT CHANDIGARH
112
                                    CWP No.5772 of 2017
                                    Date of Decision : 21.03.2017

Rajiv Kumar                                               ...... Petitioner

                                    Versus

State of Haryana and others                               ...... Respondents


CORAM : HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE AJAY TEWARI
                     ***
Present :    Mr. Namit Khurana, Advocate
             for the petitioner.
                           ***

AJAY TEWARI, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             This petition has been filed for directing the respondents to

issue appointment letter to the petitioner against the posts left unfilled in the

quota of Ex-Servicemen belonging to Scheduled Caste category.

</p><p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the petitioner states that the facts of the

present case are covered by the decision of this Court in the matter of <a href="/doc/165000537/" id="a_1">Raj

Kumar and others vs. UHBVN Ltd. and another</a>, passed in C.W.P.

No.21086 of 2016, decided on 06.10.2016 (Annexure P-12).

</p><p id="p_3">             In the circumstances, the petition stands disposed of in the

same terms as in C.W.P. No.21086 of 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_4">             Since the main case has been decided, the pending civil

miscellaneous application, if any, also stands disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                               ( AJAY TEWARI )
March 21, 2017                                     JUDGE
