<p id="p_1">Kurian Joseph, J.

<p id="p_2">     These appeals are filed aggrieved by the enhancement of

compensation in the matter of acquisition for the Kochi

International Airport, Nedumbassery. It has been held by this

Court in L.A.A. No. 290/2003 that enhancement has to be limited

to 45% over and above the value awarded by the Land

Acquisition Officer. Therefore, these appeals are partly allowed.

The claimants shall be entitled to have the enhancement only to

the tune of 45% over and above the value fixed by the Land

Acquisition Officer. In view of the decision of the Supreme Court

in <a href="/doc/1177203/" id="a_1">Sunder vs. Union of India</a> (2001(3) KLT 489)], the

claimants shall be entitled to interest on solatium.
