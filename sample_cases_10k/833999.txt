<p id="p_1">1.	The
petitioner has for a direction  to modify the Government Resolution
dated 29th March 1988 so as to permit the first respondent
to place the names of the petitioners at serial No.50, 151 and 152 of
the seniority list and to give them all consequential benefits.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.	No
interim relief was granted in the matter. By efflux of time the
petition has become infructuous. Therefore the petition is disposed
of as having become infructuous. Rule is discharged with no order as
to costs. Liberty to apply in case of difficulty.
</p>
