<p id="p_1">	Heard
	learned Counsels appearing for the respective parties.
</p>	 


	
	  


<p id="p_2">	This
	petition is filed by the petitioner   the President with Taluka
	Panchayat, Santalpur with a prayer to give directions to respondents
	No.1, 2 and 3 herein namely the State of Gujarat, the District
	Development Officer   Patan and Taluka Development Officer - Patan
	to cancel the procedure and Resolution No.67 of General Meeting of
	Taluka Panchayat, Santalpur on various grounds, so stated in the
	petition.  
</p>	
	 


	
	  


<p id="p_3">	However,
	subsequent events have taken place as recorded in the order of even
	date in Special Civil Application No.3613/2013 and a Revised Budget
	for the year 2012   2013 has been passed in the Meeting dated
	03.05.2013 of Taluka Panchayat, Santalpur.  
</p>	
	 


	
	  


<p id="p_4">	In
	the aforesaid circumstances, the prayers made in this petition will
	not survive and hence, the petition stands disposed of.  Notice
	discharged. 
</p>	

 


 


 


<p id="p_5">								Sd/-
</p> 


<p id="p_6">(ANANT S. DAVE, J.)
