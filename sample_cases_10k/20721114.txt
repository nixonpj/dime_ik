<div class="doc_title">Rakesh And Ors. vs The State Of U.P Thru Secy., Home ... on 8 March, 2013</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Abdul Mateen, Ashwani Kumar Singh</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD, LUCKNOW BENCH
 
 

?Court No. - 25
 

 
Case :- MISC. BENCH No. - 1982 of 2013
 

 
Petitioner :- Rakesh And Ors.
 
Respondent :- The State Of U.P Thru Secy., Home Deptt., Lucknow And Ors.
 
Petitioner Counsel :- Shiv Pal Singh
 
Respondent Counsel :- Govt. Advocate
 

 
Hon'ble Abdul Mateen,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Hon'ble Ashwani Kumar Singh,J.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the learned Additional Government Advocate.
</p> 
<p id="p_3">Through the instant writ petition the petitioners have prayed for quashing of FIR relating to Case Crime No.06/2013, under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_1">Sections 363</a> and <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_2">366</a> IPC, Police Station Kaiserganj, District Bahraich.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">Learned counsel for the petitioners has produced a girl and a boy identifying them to be  Vijay Kumar Yadav-petitioner no. 2 and Smt. Suman Devi-petitioner no. 3 (alleged victim) and has submitted that they being major have married each other on their own and, as such, in view of the law laid down by the Hon'ble Supreme Court in the case of <a href="/doc/1364215/" id="a_3">Lata Singh vs. State of U.P. and another</a> reported in (2006) 5 Supreme Court Cases 475, the FIR, which has been lodged, is sham and is liable to be quashed. On being asked,  Suman Devi stated that she is illiterate; her age is 19 years and she has married with Vijay Kumar Yadav on her own.
</p> 
<p id="p_5">Accordingly, Vijay Kumar Yadav is directed to produce Smt. Suman Devi before the investigating officer of the aforesaid case within ten days from today for getting her statement under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_4">Section 164</a> Cr.P.C. recorded before the Magistrate concerned on the same day while affixing her colour photograph. Immediately after recording her statement, the investigating officer shall hand over Smt. Suman Devi to Vijay Kumar Yadav.
</p> 
<p id="p_6">The Magistrate concerned is directed to transmit to this Court a photocopy of the aforesaid statement in a sealed cover at an early date.
</p> 
<p id="p_7">Let notice be issued to opposite party no. 3, returnable at an early date.
</p> 
<p id="p_8">List after service.
</p> 
<p id="p_9">Till next date of listing, it is hereby directed that arrest of the petitioners in the aforesaid crime number shall remain stayed, provided they cooperate with the investigation, which shall go on.
</p> 
<p id="p_10">It is clarified that if Vijay Kumar Yadav fails to produce Smt. Suman Devi before the investigating officer within the time indicated above, benefit of this order would not be available to the petitioners.
</p> 
<p id="p_11">Order Date :- 8.3.2013
 
Chauhan/-
</p>
