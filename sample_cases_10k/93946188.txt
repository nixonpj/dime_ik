       These Writ Petitions are filed under Articles
226 and 227 of the Constitution of India praying to

       These Petitions coming on for Orders, this
day, the Court made the following:-


                         ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">       Learned counsel for the petitioner has filed a

memo seeking leave of this Court to withdraw these

writ   petitions   on   the   ground     that   these   writ

petitions   have    been      rendered    infructuous    on

account of efflux of time.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">       The aforesaid memo is taken on record.


       Accordingly, these writ petitions are dismissed

as having become infructuous.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             3</span>



</p><p id="p_4">        In view of dismissal of the main petitions,

pending interlocutory application does not survive

for     consideration.   Accordingly,   the   same   is

dismissed.
</p>
