<p id="p_1">04.   06.11.2015             The Petitioner seeks cancellation of bail granted

                   to the private Opposite Parties No. 2 to 4 by the Sessions

                   Judge, Munger by order dated 20.05.2015 passed in

                   A.B.P. No. 314 of 2015 arising out of Jamalpur P.S. Case

                   No. 35 of 2015 on the ground that they are threatening

                   him.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             This Court had called for a report with regard to

                   it from the Officer In-charge of the concerned Police

                   Station who has reported that there is substance in the

                   submission of the Petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             The Application is disposed off with a direction

                   to the Court below to pass appropriate orders for
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">       Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.43148 of 2015 (4) dt.06-11-2015                  2</span>




                        canceling the bail of the private Opposite Parties on

                        ground of misuse of privilege of bail within a period of

                        four weeks from the date of receipt of this order.




</p><p id="p_4">                                                             (Anjana Prakash, J.)
