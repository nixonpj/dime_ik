<div class="doc_title">Ashok Kumar Singh And Others vs State Of U.P. And Another on 18 June, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">Court No. - 3

Case :- U/S 482/378/407 No. - 2741 of 2010

Petitioner :- Ashok Kumar Singh And Others
Respondent :- State Of U.P. And Another
Petitioner Counsel :- Sunil Kumar Singh
Respondent Counsel :- Govt. Advocate

Hon'ble Shri Narayan Shukla,J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned A.G.A.
</p>
<p id="p_2">The petitioners have challenged the order dated 24.4.2010, passed by the
Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate III, Hardoi in Case No.6272 of 2009
Case Crime No. 402A of 2008 whereby they have been summoned to face
trial under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>,<a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>,<a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>,<a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_4">324</a>,<a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_5">504</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_6">506</a> I.P.C., Police Station Sandi,
district Hardoi on the protest application moved by opposite party no.2.
</p>
<p id="p_3">On a perusal of the application as well statement of opposite party no. 2 at
this stage offence committed by the petitioners cannot be denied. Therefore, I
am not inclined to interfere with the proceedings. However, keeping in view
of the fact that final report had already been submitted by the Police and the
petitioners have been summoned on protest application, I hereby provide that
if the petitioners appear before the Court below and move an application for
bail, the same shall be considered and disposed of expeditiously, if possible
on the same day.
</p>
<p id="p_4">With the aforesaid observations, the petition is disposed of finally.
</p>
<p id="p_5">Order Date :- 18.6.2010
Tripathi
