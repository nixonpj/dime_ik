                          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

         CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR MODIFICATION OF ORDERS) NO. 15690 of 2017

         =======================================================================
                       SAJID NOORMAHOMADBHAI KACHARA....Applicant(s)
                                               Versus
                              STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
         =======================================================================
         Appearance:
         MS GAYATRIBA B JADEJA, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MS CM SHAH, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         =======================================================================

              CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.Y. KOGJE

                                             Date : 23/06/2017
         ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">         1.             RULE.           Learned APP Ms.C.M.Shah waives service

         of Rule on behalf of the respondent-State.


</p><p id="p_2">         2.             This application is filed for modification of

         the condition imposed by the Sessions Judge, Amreli in

         order     dated        17.06.2017         in      Criminal           Misc.Application

         No.364 of 2017 in connection with Sessions Case of CR

         NO.I-17 of 2015, Amreli City Police Station.


</p><p id="p_3">         3.             Learned         Advocate     for      the      applicant           submitted

         that the condition imposed by the Sessions Court while

         granting      temporary          bail is of enlarging the applicant

         under Police escort for a period of three days at the

         cost of the applicant.                    The applicant is desirous to

         attend "Khatna" ceremony, which according to invitation

         card     is    between          22.06.2017        to      24.06.2017.                 Learned

         Advocate for the applicant submitted that the cost of the

         escort is cumbersome and cannot be borne by the applicant

         as he is belonging to lower strata of society.


</p><p id="p_4">         4.            Learned APP furnishes the jail record, which

         indicates that on previous occasions, the applicant has

         been   enlarged        on     temporary         bail.         However,          she     draws

         attention of the Court that on several occasions, the

         applicant has reported late and therefore, insists that

         the    applicant       cannot     be     permitted            to     be     enlarged            on

         temporary bail without any escort.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">         5.            Considering the submissions of both the sides,

         it would be appropriate to permit the applicant to be

         enlarged     on     temporary      bail         for    a    period         of    two       days

         between 24.06.2017 to 26.06.2017.                        However, the applicant

         be    provided      with      Police     escort        to     the      extent         of    one

         Police Constable instead of regular Police escort party.

         The cost of one Police Constable escort will be borne by

         the applicant.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">         6.            With      the     aforesaid             direction,           order        dated

         17.06.2017 stands modified to the aforesaid extent.                                         The

         remaining conditions to continue to operate.


</p><p id="p_9">         7.            The application is disposed of.                             Rule is made

         absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p>
