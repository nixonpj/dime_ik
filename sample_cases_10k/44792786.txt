<div class="doc_title">Jasvinder Kaur vs State Of Punjab And Others on 28 May, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">    IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT
                   CHANDIGARH

           Civil Writ Petition No.4230 of 2013 (O&amp;M)
            Date of Decision : May 28, 2013


Jasvinder Kaur                                   .....Petitioner
       versus
State of Punjab and others                       .....Respondents

CORAM : HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE SURYA KANT.
        HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE R.P.NAGRATH.

Present : Mr.Ajit Pal Singh Rehan, Advocate, for
          Mr.Mohit Jaggi, Advocate, for the petitioner.
                        -.-

1. Whether Reporters of Local papers may be allowed to see the
   judgment?
2. To be referred to the Reporters or not?
3. Whether the judgment should be reported in the Digest?
                       ---
Surya Kant, J. (Oral)
</pre><p id="p_1">            The petitioner while challenging the order dated
8.10.2012, seeks a mandamus for allotment of a plot
measuring 250 square yards on the strength of Rehabilitation
Policy dated 1.6.2001 (Annexure P-4) which was formulated as
a special scheme for the residents of village Lambian whose
land was acquired for development of Sector-69, SAS Nagar
Mohali.
</p><p id="p_2">            A bunch of cases involving the rehabilitation policy
of village Lambian was referred by us to the Officers'
Committee comprising (i) Secretary-cum-Legal Remembrancer
to Government of Punjab (Chairman); (ii) Chief Administrator,
GMADA      (Member)      and    (iii)   the   Additional   Deputy
Commissioner (General), SAS Nagar, Mohali (Member), vide
our order dated 17.10.2012 passed in CWP No.12433 of 2011
 CWP No.4230 of 2013 (O&amp;M)                                     [2]

(Nasib Singh versus State of Punjab and others) and other
connected cases. Though the Officers' Committee has
submitted its report in respect of the cases which were initially
sent, however, some more such like matters have been sent to
the said Committee subsequently. Accordingly, we dispose of
this writ petition with liberty to the petitioner to submit her
claim before the above-mentioned Officers' Committee who
shall then dispose of the same after hearing the respondents
and in accordance with law.          The objection regarding
genuineness of the documents relied upon before us can be
raised before the said Committee.
</p><p id="p_3">             The parties shall appear before the Committee on
15.7.2013 at 11.00 a.m.
             The Committee shall decide the matter within a
period of four weeks thereafter.
</p><p id="p_4">             Dasti.</p><pre id="pre_2">
                                                     $




                                             (SURYA KANT)
                                                 JUDGE

                                                     $




May 28, 2013                                 (R.P.NAGRATH)
  Mohinder                                        JUDGE
