<div class="doc_title">Mandeep Singh vs State Of Punjab on 6 July, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA

                           AT CHANDIGARH



                                          Criminal Misc. No.M-49529 of 2017
                                                Date of Decision: 06.07.2018

Mandeep Singh
                                                               ...Petitioner(s)
                                    Versus
State of Punjab
                                                             ...Respondent(s)
CORAM:- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE HARI PAL VERMA

Present:-    Mr. P.S. Ahluwalia, Advocate
             for the petitioner.

             Mr. Surinder Pal Singh Tinna, Addl.A.G., Punjab.
             *****

HARI PAL VERMA, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Prayer in this petition filed under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C. is for

grant of pre-arrest bail to the petitioner in case FIR No.92 dated 04.11.2017

for offence punishable under <a href="/doc/92983/" id="a_2">Sections 306</a>, <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> IPC registered at Police

Station GRP Sirhind, District Fateharh Sahib

             Learned counsel for the petitioner states that pursuant to order

dated 22.12.2017 of this Court, the petitioner has joined investigation. He

further submits that vide order dated 2.7.2018, the trial Court has also

framed charges against the accused including the petitioner and the case is

now fixed for prosecution evidence on 17.07.2018. He has produced copies

of the challan and the order dated 02.07.2018 passed by the trial Court.

</p><p id="p_2">             Learned State counsel submits that the IO has not turned up to

assist him in the case.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">                                 1 of 2
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">              ::: Downloaded on - 08-07-2018 11:58:29 :::</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2"> Criminal Misc. No. M-49529 of 2017                                     -2-</span>




            I have heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_5">            Considering the fact that the trial Court has framed charges

and the case is now fixed for prosecution evidence on 17.07.2018, the

custody of the petitioner is no more required in the case. Accordingly, the

present petition is allowed and the interim order dated 22.12.2017 is made

absolute. However, the petitioner shall abide by the terms and conditions

as envisaged under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_4">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.
</p>
