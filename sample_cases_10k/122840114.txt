<div class="doc_title">An Application For Anticipatory ... vs Rokia Bibi &amp; Anrs on 25 June, 2013</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Nishita Mhatre</div>

<p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        1</span>


</p><p id="p_2">13.
           C.R.M. 7255 of 2013

      In the matter of : An application for anticipatory bail under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of the
      Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 17.05.2013.
</p><p id="p_3">                            And
      In the matter of : Rokia Bibi &amp; Anrs.
</p><p id="p_4">                                     Petitioners.
</p><p id="p_5">                         -versus-
</p><p id="p_6">                      The State of West Bengal
                                     Opposite party.
</p><p id="p_7">      Mr. Banibrata Datta
                             For the petitioners.
</p><p id="p_8">      Mr. Saibal Bapuli,
      Mr. Soumik Ganguly
                              For the State.
</p>

<p id="p_9">           The petitioners, apprehending arrest in connection with Dhubulia Police

      Station Case No.133 of 2013 dated 22.04.2013 under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Sections 498A</a>, <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_3">302</a>, <a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of the

      Indian Penal Code, have come to this Court for anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_10">           We have perused the case diary. We do not think that this is a fit case to

      grant anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_11">           The application for anticipatory bail is, thus, rejected.
</p>

<p id="p_12">                                 (Nishita Mhatre, J.)



                           ( Kanchan Chakraborty, J.)
