<div class="doc_title">Chinta Devi &amp; Ors. vs The State Of Bihar on 27 February, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">       Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4017 of 2013 (2) dt.27-02-2013




                       IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Criminal Miscellaneous No.4017 of 2013
                    ======================================================
                    1. Chinta Devi
                    2. Kundan Chaudhary
                    3. Triloki Chaudhary
                                                                        .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                    The State Of Bihar
                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                    ======================================================
                    CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                    SINGH
                    ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">02/   27-02-2013                          Heard learned counsels for the petitioners

                          and the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                          The petitioners are apprehending their arrest

                          in a case registered for the offences punishable under

                          <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_1">Sections 307</a> and <a href="/doc/1535430/" id="a_2">328</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                          It is alleged that the husband of the

                          informant has some illicit relationship with petitioner no.

                          1 being his neighbour when it was objected by the

                          informant then she was assaulted by her husband and

                          family members and administered poison forcibly.

</p><p id="p_4">                                          It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                          petitioners       that    the     accusation   of   assault   and

                          administering poison are against the husband and family

                          members whereas the petitioners are neighbours of the

                          husband of the informant.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.4017 of 2013 (2) dt.27-02-2013</span><p id="p_5">




</p><p id="p_6">                                   Considering the aforesaid facts, let the above

                   named petitioners be released on anticipatory bail in the

                   event of their arrest or surrender before the learned court

                   below within a period of twelve weeks from today, on

                   furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) each

                   with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                   satisfaction of learned CJM, Bhojpur at Ara in

                   connection with Udwant Nagar P.S. Case No. 172 of

                   2012, subject to the conditions as laid down under

                   <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_4">Section 438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                                  (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J.)
DKS/
 </p>
