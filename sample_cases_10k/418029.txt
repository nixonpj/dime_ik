<p id="p_1">     Ext.P3 is an application made by the petitioner before the 3rd

respondent seeking measurement of the property covered by Ext.P1

sale deed. Orders have not been passed on Ext.P3 and therefore,

the writ petition is filed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     2.    Taking into account the case of the petitioner regarding

the pendency of Ext.P3 before the 3rd respondent, I dispose of the

writ petition directing the 3rd respondent to consider and pass

orders on Ext.P3. This shall be done as expeditiously as possible, at

any rate, within six weeks of production of a copy of this judgment.

</p><p id="p_3">     The writ petition is disposed of as above.



</p><p id="p_4">                                       (ANTONY DOMINIC, JUDGE)
