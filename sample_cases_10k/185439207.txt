<pre id="pre_1">                              ORDER SHEET
                          EC No. 1192 of 2015
                     IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                 Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction
                             ORIGINAL SIDE



                             L &amp; T FINANCE LTD.

                                    Versus

                          SANJAY KUMAR SINGH &amp; ANR.


    BEFORE:

    The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>

<p id="p_1">    Date : 19th November, 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                                                 Appearance
                                                         Mr. P. Sinha, Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
     The Court : The award is less than two years old. The award

is not under challenge. The award has now become enforceable. It

is submitted by Mr. Paritosh Sinha, learned Counsel appearing on

behalf of the decree holder that the hypothecated asset is not

traceable.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     In view thereof, the judgment debtors are directed to file

their affidavits of assets in Form No. 16A, Appendix-'E' of Code

of Civil Procedure disclosing the particulars of the assets and

properties held by and/or belonging to them on or before the

returnable    date   in   which   they   shall   specifically   disclose   the

whereabouts of the asset in question, failing which warrant of

arrest be issued against the said judgment debtors.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   2</span>


</p><p id="p_5">       The Advocate-on-Record for the decree holder is directed to

serve a copy of this application along with a copy of this order

upon the judgment debtors in course of this week by speed post

with acknowledgement due and shall file the affidavit of service

on the returnable date.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       The application is made returnable on January 7, 2016.



</p><p id="p_7">                                                    (SOUMEN SEN, J.)



dg2.
