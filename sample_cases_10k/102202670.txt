     This Review Petition is filed under Section 114 R/w.

                       ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      This Review Petition is filed by review petitioner

for review of the Judgment dated 7th July 2015, passed

in M.F.A. No.3959/2015 (MV), by this Court.


</p><p id="p_2">     2.    Heard Shri. R. Lakshman, for Shri.H.B.

Somapur,    learned    counsel      appearing   for   review

petitioner, for quite some time and gone through the

grounds urged in the memorandum of review petition

and also the judgment under review.


</p><p id="p_3">     4.    After perusal of the grounds urged in the

memorandum of review petition and also the judgment
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             3</span>




under review, we do not find any justification or good

ground as such made out by the review petitioner for

interference.   Hence, the review petition filed by

petitioner is liable to be dismissed as devoid of merits

and accordingly, it is dismissed.
