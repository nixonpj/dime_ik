<div class="doc_title">Mahendra Prasad vs The State Of Bihar &amp;Amp; Anr. on 19 January, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA

                               Cr.Misc. No.1777 of 2011

                                   MAHENDRA PRASAD
                                        Versus
                              THE STATE OF BIHAR &amp; ANR.


2   19.01.2011</pre><p id="p_1">                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                 learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               This is an application seeking restoration of

                 Cr.Misc. no. 14355 of 2009 dismissed for non-prosecution of

                 order dated 25-10-2010.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               The said application was preferred seeking

                 quashing of order dated 25-10-2008 passed by the Chief

                 Judicial   Magistrate,    Begusarai,   taking   cognizance   in

                 Mansoorchak P.S. Case no.12 of 2008 for the offences under

                 <a href="/doc/48127346/" id="a_1">Sections 161</a>, <a href="/doc/108500659/" id="a_2">162</a>, <a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_3">409</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_4">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_5">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_6">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_7">471</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_8">120B</a> of the

                 Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               After some arguments learned counsel for the

                 petitioner seeks permission to withdraw this application with

                 a liberty to take due steps       before the court below in

                 accordance with law to get another case between the parties,

                 for same alleged offences, clubbed or simultaneous

                 proceeding, as the case may be.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               Permission is granted.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                            2</span><p id="p_6">




                      The application stands disposed of with the

         above liberty.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                 (Akhilesh Chandra, J.)


AAhmad
 </p>
