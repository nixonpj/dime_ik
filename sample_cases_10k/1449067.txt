<p id="p_1">Heard
	learned advocate Mr. YJ Patel on behalf of applicant.
</p>	  

<p id="p_2">Rule
	issued by this Court on 2/8/2010 made it returnable on 27/8/2010. In
	this application, in all total nine opponents are there, out of them
	rule remain unserved for respondent no. 9 Dhanji Rupa Mer as
	expired.  The respondent no. 9 is original respondent no. 10 in
	proceeding before Trial Court.  For rest of respondent nos. 2 to 8,
	rule is served but no appearance is filed by any of respondent and
	no advocate is engaged by them.  The respondent no. 1 is served by
	affixing.  
</p>	
	  

<p id="p_3">The
	present application is preferred by applicant with a prayer to
	condone delay of 1106 days in filing Second appeal.  The respondent
	nos. 2 to 8 original plaintiff though served, no appearance is filed
	and no advocate is engaged by them.  Therefore, in their absence,
	present application is decided by this Court.
</p>	  

<p id="p_4">I
	have considered submission made by learned advocate Mr. Patel and
	also considering averment made in this application supported with
	affidavit.  The delay of 1106 days occurred in filing present appeal
	is condoned by this Court in the interest of justice as sufficient
	cause is shown by applicant to the satisfaction of this Court.
</p>	  

<p id="p_5">Accordingly,
	Rule is made absolute.
</p>
