<div class="doc_title">Rizwan @ Md. Rizwan &amp; Ors vs The State Of Bihar on 16 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.46160 of 2018
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -408 Year- 2018 Thana -KISHANGANJ District- KISANGANJ
                 ======================================================
                 1. Rizwan @ Md. Rizwan, S/o Late Anish Rahman,
                 2. Md. Faiz @ Md. Faiz Alam, S/o Anwarul Haque,
                 3. Md. Firoz @ Firoz Alam, S/o Anwarul Haque,
                 4. Rehan @ Raehan Ahmad, S/o Rizwan,
                 All R/o Vill.- Churipatti, P.O.+P.S. and District- Kishanganj.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                              .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Sanjay Kumar Giri, Advocate
                                             Mr. Dhirendra Kumar Singh, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s  : Mr. Ashok Kumar, APP
                 For the Informant        : Mr. Ram Prawesh Kumar, Advocate
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SANJAY PRIYA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   16-08-2018                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioners, counsel for

                 the Informant and the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioners apprehend their arrest in Kishanganj

                 P.S. Case No.408 of 2018 instituted for the offence under

                 <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a> and <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_5">379</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                Counsel for the petitioners has submitted that prior to

                 the instant case, petitioner No. 1 has filed Kishanganj P.S. Case

                 No.407 of 2018 against the informant, in which four persons have

                 sustained injury. Out of them, petitioner No.3 has sustained

                 grievous injury.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                The instant case has been lodged by the informant
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.46160 of 2018 (2) dt.16-08-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                            2/3</span>




             after lodging the case by petitioner No.1. The informant has

             alleged that petitioners assaulted him with iron rod causing injury

             on his ear. The injury report of the informant has been enclosed as

             Annexure-3 which shows that he has sustained simple injury

             caused by hard and blunt substance.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                              The injury reports of petitioners have been enclosed

             as Annexure-4 series which shows that petitioners also have

             sustained injuries. The injury found on the person of petitioner

             No.3 was grievous in nature.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                              Counsel for the informant has appeared and opposed

             prayer for anticipatory bail of petitioners.

</p><p id="p_7">                               Considering the facts and circumstances of the case,

             prayer for anticipatory bail of the petitioners is allowed. In the

             event of surrender/arrest of the petitioners, named above, within

             six weeks from today, in connection with Kishanganj P.S. Case

             No.408 of 2018, they shall be released on anticipatory bail on

             furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) each with two

             sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

             Chief Judicial Magistrate, Kishanganj, subject to the conditions

             as laid down under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_7">Section 438</a> (2) Cr. P.C. with further

             conditions (1) bailors should be local having sufficient immovable

             property within the jurisdiction of the court concerned, (2)
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">       Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.46160 of 2018 (2) dt.16-08-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_4">                                                  3/3</span>




                   petitioners shall cooperate in the trial and shall be present on each

                   and every date fixed by the court and their absence on two

                   consecutive dates without proper and reasonable reason will be

                   liable to cancel their bail bond and (3) if petitioners tamper with

                   the evidence or the witnesses of the case, in that case, prosecution

                   will be at liberty to move for cancellation of bail of the petitioners.



</p><p id="p_8">                                                                      (Sanjay Priya, J)
    S.Ali/-
</p><p id="p_9">    Rohit Kr.
</p>
