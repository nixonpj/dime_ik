<p id="p_1">                 In this writ petition a direction has been sought to be





    issued to the respondents to take a decision in a time bound

    manner to convene DPC for induction of the petitioner into the

    cadre of Himachal Pradesh Administrative Service in terms of the

    judgment passed by this Court in CWP(T) No. 12130 of 2008 and

    CWP(T) No. 12131 of 2008.
</p>

<p id="p_3">    2.           The writ petition though is at initial stage, however,

    learned counsel submits that the petitioner would feel content in




                                                                       .
</p>
<p id="p_4">    case the writ petition is disposed of with a direction to the

    respondents to constitute a Departmental Promotion Committee





    and consider the petitioner for induction into Himachal Pradesh

    Administrative Service, if otherwise eligible. Learned Additional

    Advocate General is also not averse to any such orders if passed

    in this writ petition.
</p>
<p id="p_5">    3.                                to
                 Therefore, without adverting to the given facts and

    circumstances and the law points raised in this writ petition, the

    same is disposed of with a direction to respondents No. 1 to 3 to

    constitute the Departmental Promotion Committee to examine



    the claim of the petitioner qua his induction into the cadre of

    Himachal Pradesh Administrative Service within two months from




    today.
</p>




<p id="p_6">    4.           Pending     application(s),   if   any,     shall    also     stand

    disposed of.
