<div class="doc_title">Bhrigudeo Sharma &amp; Ors vs State Of Bihar on 12 August, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">        IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                CR. APP (DB) No.41 of 2009
              1. BHRIGUDEO SHARMA
              2. ASHUTOSH SHARMA
              3. SIDHARTH SHAMA
              4. GYANESHWAR SHARMA
                                 Versus
                          STATE OF BIHAR .
                                -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">04/   12-Aug-2010                 Heard learned counsel for appellants No.1 and 2

                    &amp; learned counsel for the State in respect of prayer of these

                    appellants for bail renewed through I.A. No.801 of 2010.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  It has been submitted that prayer has been

                    renewed in the light of observation in the order dated 10.04.2009

                    and there is no likelihood of the appeal being heard in near

                    future.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  In the facts of the case, prayer of the appellants

                    no.1 and 2 is allowed. During the pendency of this appeal, let

                    appellants, namely, (1) BHRIGUDEO SHARMA and (2)

                    ASHUTOSH SHARMA be released on bail on furnishing bail

                    bond of Rs.10,000/- each with two sureties of the like amount

                    each to the satisfaction of Additional District &amp; Sessions Judge,

                    FTC-V, Chapra in Sessions Trial No.427 of 2006.</p><pre id="pre_2">




                                            (Shiva Kirti Singh, J.)



       perwez                           (Hemant Kumar Srivastava,J.)
