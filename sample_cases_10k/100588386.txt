<div class="doc_title">Dhanalakshmi vs Rajkumar on 24 June, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED: 24.06.2016
CORAM
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE C.T.SELVAM
Transfer Civil Miscellaneous Petition No.323 of 2016
and
C.M.P.No.8969 of 2016


Dhanalakshmi
W/o.Rajkumar							... Petitioner


vs


Rajkumar
S/o.Sriraman							... Respondent


	Transfer Civil Miscellaneous Petition filed under Section 24 of the Civil Procedure Code, 1906, praying to withdraw H.M.O.P.No.64 of 2015 on the file of Sub Court, Chidambaram and transfer the same to the file of Family Court, Villupuram, to be tried along with H.M.O.P.No.175 of 2015.

			For Petitioner	:	Mr.V.S.Sivasundaram
			For Respondent	:	No appearance
							
*****


O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	Even though the respondent has been served and the name of the respondent appears in the cause list, the respondent has not chosen to appear before this Court either in person or through a pleader.  Hence, the matter is decided on merits.	
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2. Petitioner has filed the present petition seeking transfer of  H.M.O.P.No.64 of 2015 on the file of Sub Court, Chidambaram, to the file of  Family Court, Villupuram, to be tried along with H.M.O.P.No.175 of 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. Learned counsel for petitioner submits that the respondent/husband has filed H.M.O.P.No.64 of 2015 on the file of Sub Court, Chidambaram, seeking divorce and the petitioner/wife has filed H.M.O.P.No.175 of 2015  on the file of Family Court, Villupuram, seeking restitution of conjugal rights. Learned counsel submits that the petitioner/wife has to take care of her aged parents and hence, she finds it difficult to travel to Chidambaram to attend the hearing of the case filed by the respondent/husband. Since the petitioner/wife has filed H.M.O.P.No.175 of 2015 on the file of Family Court, Villupuram, seeking restitution of conjugal rights, it would be desirable to have both the matters together. Submitting as above, learned counsel prays for transfer.
</p><p id="p_4">	4. Considering the submissions made, this Court directs transfer of H.M.O.P.No.64 of 2015 on the file of Sub Court, Chidambaram, to the file of  Family Court, Villupuram, to be tried along with H.M.O.P.No.175 of 2015.
</p>
<p id="p_5">	The Transfer Civil Miscellaneous Petition is disposed of with the above direction. No costs. Consequently, connected miscellaneous petition is closed.
</p>
