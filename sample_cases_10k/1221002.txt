                        Vs

         Dated this the 15th day of September, 2010

                          J U D G M E N T
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     The petitioner is working as Assistant Project Officer in the

department of Animal Husbandry at Regional A.I. Centre,

Kannur.    The petitioner is desirous to have a transfer to a

vacancy which has arisen at Central Veterinary Store,

Kozhikode, as Assistant Director which is stated to be an

equivalent post. Claiming that benefit, the petitioner has filed

Ext.P3 representation before the 2nd respondent. Petitioner seeks

a direction to the 2nd respondent to consider and pass orders on

Ext.P3 as expeditiously as possible.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     I have heard the learned Government Pleader also. Having

heard both sides, I dispose of this writ petition with a direction

to the 2nd respondent to consider and pass orders on Ext.P3 as

expeditiously as possible, at any rate within a period of one

month from the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment.




</p><p id="p_3">                                                             S. SIRI JAGAN
                                                                 JUDGE
