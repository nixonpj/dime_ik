<div class="doc_title">Haider &amp; Others vs State Of U.P.,Thru. ... on 20 July, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">Court No. - 20

Case :- MISC. BENCH No. - 6751 of 2010

Petitioner :- Haider &amp; Others
Respondent :- State Of U.P.,Thru. Superintendent Of Police,Bahraich &amp; Ors.
Petitioner Counsel :- J.N. Singh
Respondent Counsel :- G.A.

Hon'ble Raj Mani Chauhan,J.
</pre><p id="p_1">Hon'ble Virendra Kumar Dixit,J.
</p>
<p id="p_2">Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned A.G.A. for the State as
well as perused the record.
</p>
<p id="p_3">This petition under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India has been filed by
the petitioner against the impugned First Information Report dated 26.4.2010
lodged by the Opposite Party No. 3 at Crime No. 522/10, under <a href="/doc/619940/" id="a_2">Sections
363</a>/<a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_3">366</a> IPC registered at Police Station Kotwali City, District Bahraich and
also for direction to the opposite parties not to arrest the petitioners in
pursuance of the said impugned First Information Report.
</p>
<p id="p_4">The submission of learned counsel for the petitioners is that the accused-
petitioners are not named in the First Information Report. Their name came
into light in the statement of the prosecutrix before the learned Magistrate
recorded under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_4">Section 164</a> Cr. P.C. The prosecutrix has not levelled any
specific allegation against the petitioners. The complainant has falsely roped
the accused-petitioners in the First Information Report on false grounds.
</p>
<p id="p_5">Learned A.G.A. opposed the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_6">We have gone through the contents of the impugned First Information Report
which disclose the commission of cognizable offence as such it cannot be
quashed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">The Writ Petition is, therefore, dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_8">However, keeping in view the facts and circumstances of the case as well as
the old age of the Petitioner No. 1 (Haider), it is provided that if the Petitioner
No. 1 (Haider) appears before the court below and moves any application for
bail, the same will be heard and decided by the courts below expeditiously.
</p>
<p id="p_9">Order Date :- 20.7.2010
Santosh/-
</p>
