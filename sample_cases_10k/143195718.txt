<div class="doc_title">Mariyam Institute For Higher ... vs State Of Uttarakhand And Another on 6 August, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1"> IN THE HIGH COURT OF UTTARAKHAND AT NAINITAL
              Writ Petition No.1239 of 2013 (M/S)

Mariyam Institute for Higher Studies and Allied Courses.
                                              ......Petitioner
                                    Versus
State of Uttarakhand and another                           ......Respondents

Mr. Arvind Vashisth and Mr. Pradeep Hairiya, Advocates for the petitioner.
Mr. U.K. Uniyal, Advocate General with Mr. Paresh Tripathi, Advocate for the State/
Chancellor.
Mr. B.D. Upadhyaya, Senior Advocate assisted by Mrs. Mamta Bisht, Advocate for the
Kumaon University.


                          Dated: August 6, 2013

Hon'ble V.K. Bist, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.     It is not the stand of the respondents that the
petitioner institution has not fulfilled the requirements,
which      are      required     for    affiliation.      If   the    petitioner
institution has not fulfilled the requirements, which are
required      for    grant     of   affiliation,     in    that      event,   the
University/State Government may make a request to
N.C.T.E. for withdrawal of the recognition, but unless and
until the recognition is not withdrawn by the N.C.T.E., the
University is obliged to grant affiliation to the petitioner
institution as per provision of Section 14 (6) of the N.C.T.E.
Act. Under the N.C.T.E. Act, the State Government has no
role to play to grant affiliation in view of the judgment in
the case of State of Maharashtra Vs. Sant Danyaneshwar
Shikshan Shasthan, Mahavidyalaya and others, reported in
2006 (9) SCC 11.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.     In this case, though application for affiliation was
filed by the petitioner institution few months ago, but the
University did not act on the said application and
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               2</span>




constituted the Inspection Team belatedly, and sent the
report to the Chancellor.
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.     Considering the fact that the matter has been sent for
the consideration of the Chancellor with recommendation, I
direct the respondent University to grant provisional
affiliation to the petitioner institution without any delay. In
the meantime, the University is also directed to permit the
petitioner institution to participate in the counseling
provisionally.
</p>
<p id="p_5">5.     List this matter on 20.08.2013 along with connected
matters.
</p>
<p id="p_6">6.     Interim relief application (CLMA No.5491 of 2013)
stands disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_7">7.     Let a certified copy of this order be supplied to the
learned counsel for the parties, within 24 hours, on
payment of usual charges.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                              (V.K. Bist, J.)
Arti                                             06.08.2013
 </pre>
