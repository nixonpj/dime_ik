                                    (DR. (MRS.) NEELLIMA SINGH Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)

                      1
                      Jabalpur, Dated : 19-12-2018
                              Mr. R.K Shrivastava, learned counsel for the petitioner.
                              Mr. Ajay Shukla, learned counsel for the State.
                              The present petition has been filed by the petitioner herein, wherein no
                      particular order has been challenged but she is claiming appointment on the
                      post of Associate Professor in Medicine as her selection is in waiting list

                      No.1.
                              Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the representation has
                      been preferred by the petitioner to the respondent No.3 and the said</pre>
<p id="p_1">                      representation is Annexure-P/9 at page 21. The said representation is decided
                      within a time bound period.
</p><p id="p_2">                              Under the circumstances, the present petition is disposed of with a
                      request to the respondent No.3 to decide the representation of the petitioner
                      within three weeks' from the date on which the certified copy of this order is
                      placed before him.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              This Court has not expressed any opinion on the merits of the case and
                      the authority is requested to decide the representation strictly in accordance
                      with law.
</p><p id="p_4">                              With the above directions, the petition is finally disposed of.
                              Certified copy as per rules.
</p>
