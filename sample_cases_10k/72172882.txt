<p id="p_1">           Petitioner namely Rupchand Odharmal Sevkani in Special
Civil Application No. 12464 of 2019, who has constructed the
property in question, has now requested the Kalol Nagarpalika to
regularize the use of construction since permission was granted for
residential purpose whereas the building is used of commercial
purpose.
</p>
<p id="p_2">           Notice returnable on 21.08.2019.
</p>
<p id="p_3">           Meanwhile, the Kalol Nagapalika shall not demolish the
property in question.
</p>

</p>
