<div class="doc_title">Dr. Kamlesh Kumar Pandey vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 29 November, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                   M.Cr.C. No.3587/2011

29.11.2012.
      Shri A.M.Trivedi, learned senior counsel with Shri
S.K. Mishra, learned counsel for petitioner.
      Shri Sameer Chile, learned GA for respondent /State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Learned counsel for petitioner submits that the main
controversy is about payment of amount of transportation.
As per submission, it was paid on 22.04.06 to the
transporter through co-accused Suryabhan Singh for which
a written receipt is sought to be filed.
</p><p id="p_2">      Learned GA appearing for the State wants to verify
the payment to the transporter. So, learned counsel for
petitioner has handed over copy of receipt to learned Govt.
Advocate. Learned GA is directed to verify the same. In
the meanwhile, learned counsel for the petitioner is also
directed to file the same.
</p><p id="p_3">      Let the receipt/payment be verified within four
weeks.
</p><p id="p_4">      List the matter in the last week of January, 2013.
      I.R.to continue till next date of hearing.
      C.c.as per rules.
</p><p id="p_5">                                     (M.A.Siddiqui)
                                      Judge.
</p>
