                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

          CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 21317 of
                                             2015

         ==========================================================
                  PRUTHVISINH VANRAJSINH SARVAIYA....Applicant(s)
                                    Versus
                       STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MR JIGNESH L HAJARE, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR KP RAVAL, LD.ADDL.PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the Respondent.
         ==========================================================

                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE J.B.PARDIWALA

                                     Date : 05/11/2015


                                      ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.          Rule.      Mr.K.P.Raval,        learned        Additional            Public
         Prosecutor waives service of Rule on behalf of the respondent.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.          By way of the present application, the applicant has
         prayed to release him on temporary bail on the ground of
         sickness of his wife.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.          Learned advocate appearing on behalf of the
         applicant states that the applicant may be released on
         temporary bail by imposing suitable conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_4">         4.          Learned Additional Public Prosecutor appearing for
         the respondent has opposed this application.
</p>

<p id="p_5">         5.          I have heard learned advocates appearing on


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                          Page 1 of 2</span>

HC-NIC                                 Page 1 of 2      Created On Sat Nov 07 02:35:51 IST 2015
                   R/CR.MA/21317/2015                                            ORDER



         behalf of the respective parties and considered the jail record
         of the applicant and considered the sickness of his wife.
</p>

<p id="p_6">         6.            Considering the overall facts and circumstances of
         the case, I am of the opinion that the application requires
         consideration and the same is allowed. The applicant shall be
         released on temporary bail up to 18/11/2015, on executing
         personal bond of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees Five Thousands) before
         the Jail authority and on usual terms and conditions. The
         applicant shall mark his presence once in a week before the
         concerned Police Station. The applicant to surrender before
         Jail Authority on completion of temporary bail, without fail.
         Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                       Direct service is permitted.
</p>
