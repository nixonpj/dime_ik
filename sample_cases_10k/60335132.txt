          on   the   ground   that   none   of   the   family   members   of   the

          petitioner is seriously ill and the surety of the petitioner, who

          is the father aged about 77 years, is not in a position to keep

          control on the petitioner since he is an old aged person. 



          3]               It   is   the   first   time   for   the   petitioner/accused   to

          apply for a furlough leave.  The reason put forth for rejecting

          the application may be good if the demand was for release

          on parole leave.   But, it cannot be justified in rejecting the

          demand   for   furlough   leave.   The   authorities   can   impose

          suitable   conditions   so   as   to   ensure   the   return   of   the

          petitioner/   accused   in   prison   after   expiry   of   the   period   of

          leave



          4]               In the result,   the writ petition is allowed.   The

          order dated 22.06.2017 rejecting the furlough leave is hereby

          quashed   and   set   aside.     The   petitioner   is   entitled   to   be

          released   on   furlough   leave   for   the   said   period   upon   such

          terms   and   conditions   as   the   authority   may   deem   fit   and

          proper to impose including to see that the petitioner/accused
