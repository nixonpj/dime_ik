<p id="p_1">CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ARVIND SINGH SANGWAN

Present:     Petitioners in person.
</p>
<p id="p_2">ARVIND SINGH SANGWAN J. (Oral)

             The petitioners have approached this Court seeking

protection of their life and liberty on the premise that petitioner No.1

has performed marriage with petitioner No.2 against the wishes of her

parents and other family members and the petitioners are apprehending

threat to their life and liberty at their instance.

</p><p id="p_3">             The petitioners, who are present in the Court, have

submitted that a representation dated 22.04.2019 was made to the

Senior Superintendent of Police, Hoshiarpur (Annexure P5), seeking

necessary protection but no action has been taken so far in the matter.

</p><p id="p_4">             Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             Without examining the question of validity or otherwise of

marriage of the petitioners, the present petition stands disposed of with

a direction to the Senior Superintendent of Police, Hoshiarpur to look

into grievance of the petitioners expressed in the representation

(Annexure P5) and take appropriate action, if any, warranted in the
