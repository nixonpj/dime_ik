<p id="p_1">Item no. 44
Ct. No.29
CHC
                             C.R.A.N.3399 of 2019
                                         in
                              C.R.M.5727 of 2017


                  In Re: An application for relaxation of condition of bail.



                  In the matter of: Sanjib Islam Gazi @ Sanjib Gazi
                                                          ...petitioner



                 Mr. Ali Ahsan Alamgir,
                 Ms. Riya Das
                                              ...for the petitioner


                 Mr. Ajay Choubey,
                 Mr. Amajit De
                                  ...for the State



                 This is an application for relaxation of condition of bail. While

              granting bail to the petitioner this Court imposed certain

              conditions in the order dated 13.07.2017.

                 It is submitted by the petitioner that on all occasions he has

              complied with the conditions imposed by this Court and he

              appeared before the learned trial court on all dates fixed.

                 This submission has not been opposed by the learned advocate

              appearing for the N.C.B.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                           2</span><p id="p_2">


    Since the petitioner has complied with the order passed by this

Court, we deem it appropriate to allow the application for

relaxation of bail and waive the condition so imposed by the earlier

order dated 13th July, 2017.

    Accordingly, the application being C.R.A.N.3399 of 2019

stands disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

(Subhasis Dasgupta, J.)                (Sahidullah Munshi, J.)
