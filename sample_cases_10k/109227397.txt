<div class="doc_title">Rungta College Of Engineering &amp; ... vs State Of Chhattisgarh on 8 July, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                                                                                       NAFR

                   HIGH COURT OF CHHATTISGARH, BILASPUR

                                        WPC No. 1157 of 2015

         Rungta College Of Engineering &amp; Technology, Bhilai, Po Kurud PS Jamul, Tehsil Bhilai
         Nagar, District Durg, Chhattisgarh

                                                                                ---- Petitioner

                                              Versus

    1. State Of Chhattisgarh Through - Secretary, Chhattisgarh Transport Department,
       Mahanadi Bhawan, Naya Raipur, Raipur, Chhattisgarh.

    2. Regional Transport Officer Bhanpuri, Opp. Banjari Mata Mandir, Raipur Chhattisgarh

                                                                            ---- Respondents</pre>




<p id="p_1">For Petitioner                     -        Shri Sourabh Dangi, Advocate
For Respondent/State                -       Shri Satish Gupta, Government Advocate



                                          Order On Board

08/07/2015

Heard.
</p>

<p id="p_2">1. Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the petitioner has moved application before
the respondent No.2/Regional Transport Officer, Bhanpuri for grant of permit to ply vehicle on
02/12/2014 but the application has remained pending without any decision on it.
</p>

<p id="p_3">2. Learned counsel for the State submits that if such application is pending before the
respondent No.2, the application shall be decided in accordance with law.
</p>

<p id="p_4">3. Without commenting upon the merits of the case, it would be proper to dispose off this
petition with a direction to respondent No.2 to decide the petitioner's application as early as
possible preferably within a period of three months from the date of receipt of copy of this
order.
</p>

<p id="p_5">4. With the aforesaid directions, the petition is disposed off.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                                    Sd/-
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                                          Manindra Mohan Shrivastava

                                                                  JUDGE

Rekha
