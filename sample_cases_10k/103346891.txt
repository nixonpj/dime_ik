                                       ORIGINAL SIDE

                                         Versus

     BEFORE:
     The Hon'ble JUSTICE BISWANATH SOMADDER
     The Hon'ble JUSTICE MOUSHUMI BHATTACHARYA

                                                                                       respondent.</pre>



<p id="p_1">               The Court : A perusal of the report of the Registrar, Original Side, dated 2nd

February, 2018 reveals that the directions as contained in our order dated 15th January,

2018 have been complied with. As such, let there be a stay of operation of the judgment

and order dated 23rd November, 2017 passed in C. S. 279 of 2014 (Ashok Kumar Nath

Vs. Union of India &amp; Others) till disposal of the appeal. There shall also be an order in

terms of prayer (a), as contained in G. A. 4043 of 2017.

</p><p id="p_2">               The appeal shall be listed under an appropriate heading in the monthly

combined list to be published for the month of March, 2018.

</p><p id="p_3">               The application for stay, being G. A. 4043 of 2017, stands disposed of

accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


</p><p id="p_5">                                                        (BISWANATH SOMADDER, J.)
