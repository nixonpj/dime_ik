                          JUDGMENT

<p id="p_1">     The main prayer in the suit is for a declaration that the

plaintiffs are the absolute owners in possession of the plaint

schedule property irrespective of a registered document

executed in the name of the first defendant. The court below

has correctly found on the basis of pleadings that court fee

has to be paid under Section 25 (b) of the Kerala Court Fees

and Suit Valuation Act, 1959.        The court below has only

deputed an Advocate Commissioner to assess the market value

of the property which is a permissible exercise under Section

19 of the Kerala Court Fees Suit Valuation Act. The impugned

order does not suffer from any error or jurisdiction as to

warrant interference under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the Constitution of

India.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     The Original Petition fails and is dismissed.


</p><p id="p_3">                                        V. CHITAMBARESH
