<p id="p_1">Ramachandran Nair, J.

<p id="p_2">      Heard counsel for the review petitioner. In our view, Review

Petition  was    absolutely           unnecessary            because          the  Regulatory

Commission itself has stated that the judgment is being complied with

by incorporating appropriate clauses in the new Code being made. We

clarify the judgment by stating that instead of amendment to the

existing Code, it would be sufficient if the Regulatory Commission

complies with the judgment while passing the new Code. Time for

compliance of the judgment is extended by two months from now.
