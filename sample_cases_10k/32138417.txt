 In the High Court of Judicature at Madras

Dated:  01.04.2015
Coram:

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.JAICHANDREN
and
THE HONOURABLE MS.JUSTICE K.B.K.VASUKI
H.C.P.No.2746 of 2014
R.Gunasekaran							..  	Petitioner

vs.
1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Namakkal District, Namakkal.
2.The Inspector of Police,
   Town Police Station,
   Namakkal.
3.R.Prakash
4.Govindaraju							   .. 	Respondents

Prayer : Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India praying for issuance of a writ of habeas corpus directing the respondents 1 and 2 to produce Miss G.Lavanya aged about 27 years before this Court and set her at liberty.

			For Petitioner		..  	Ms.Dr.S.R.Kalyani
			For Respondents 1 &amp; 2	..  	Mr.A.N.Thambidurai, Addl.P.P.

						O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	The petitioner/Gunasekaran and his wife along with the learned counsel on record are present. 

</p><p id="p_2">	2.The detenu, Lavanya aged about 27 years is produced by the second respondent/Inspector of Police, Town Police Station, Namakkal before this Court.  When we enquired her, she stated that she got married to the third respondent/R.Prakash who 
M.JAICHANDREN.,J.
</p><p id="p_3">and
K.B.K.VASUKI,J.
</p>
<p id="p_4">DP

is already a married man and she is only willing to live with him and she is not willing to leave him and go with her parents.  The same is informed to her parents also.

</p><p id="p_5">	3.In view of the above stated position, this Court is inclined to allow her to go to the place of her choice. Recording the statement so made by detenu Lavanya,  no further order is warranted.  Hence, the Habeas Corpus Petition stands closed.

</p><pre id="pre_2">									 (M.J.,J.)     (K.B.K.V.,J.)
								 	      01.04.2015
DP

To
1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Namakkal District, Namakkal.
2.The Inspector of Police,
   Town Police Station,
   Namakkal.	
</pre><p id="p_6">3.The Public Prosecutor, High Court, Chennai.
