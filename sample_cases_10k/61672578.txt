                         AT CHANDIGARH

Sr. No.121
                                               CR-6083-2018 (O&amp;M)
                                               Date of decision: 12.09.2018

Sharanjit Kaur and another                                  ....Petitioners

                          versus

Harwinder Singh                                             ....Respondent

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE DEEPAK SIBAL

Present:     Petitioner No.2-in-person for herself and
             on behalf of petitioner No.1.

                   * * *

DEEPAK SIBAL, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Petitioner No.2, who appears in person on her behalf as also as

General Power Attorney holder of petitioner No.1, while seeking

withdrawal of the present petition, prays for issuance of a direction to the

Appellate Court to take a final decision on the application filed by the

petitioners alongwith their appeal seeking interim protection during the

pendency of the appeal.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Accepting the prayer made on behalf of the petitioners, the

present petition is permitted to be withdrawn with a request to the Appellate

Court to take a final decision on the stay application filed by the petitioners

alongwith their appeal, in accordance with law, expeditiously but not later

than one month from the date of receipt of a certified copy of this order.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                                     (DEEPAK SIBAL)
                                                        JUDGE
