  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

1. THE KUMARAMANGALAM GRAMA PANCHAYAT,

                        Vs

                       ...       Respondent

 Dated :28/07/2008

 O R D E R

                    ----------------------------------
           R.P.NO. 1033 of 2007 IN W.P.C.33177/2004
                    ----------------------------------
              Dated this the 28th      day of July , 2008

                               O R D E R
</pre>



<p id="p_1">       Having regard to the submissions addressed by the learned

counsel for the petitioner and Sri.C.P.Ravikumar, the learned counsel

for respondents 1 and 2 and having considered the documents to

which my attention was drawn to, particularly Ext.R3(a), R3(b) and

R3(e), I am of the view that Annexure - I judgment disposing of the

writ petition in terms of Annexure II interim order was not proper. I

recall Annexure - I judgment and post the writ petition for hearing.

</p><p id="p_2">       The Registry will post the writ petition before the appropriate

court at the earliest, if possible on top of the hearing list next week

itself.
</p>

</p><p id="p_4">                                                PIUS C. KURIAKOSE
