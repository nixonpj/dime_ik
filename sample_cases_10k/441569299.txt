                                Criminal Revision No.1702 of 2010
                     Fazlur Rahman Khan @ Fazlu Rahman Khan, S/o-Late Lall
                     Mohamad Khan, R/v-New Islampur, Panderpala, P.S.-Bhuli,
                     District-Dhanbad, State-Jharkhand.
                                                                           ....Petitioner.
                                                  Versus
                     The Union of India through C.B.I.                  ....Opposite Party.
                                     ----------------------------------
</pre><p id="p_1">11.   05.12.2011               Letter D. No. 6829/2011/SEC-IIA dated 9th

                   August, 2011 of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India has been

                   received. From perusal of the order dated 29th July, 2011

                   passed by the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India it appears that

                   the petition for special leave to appeal together with the

                   application for bail Petiton for Special to Appeal (CRL.) No.

                   3836 of 2011 with Criminal Miscellaneous Petition No. 9887

                   of 2011 (application for bail) have been dismissed and

                   consequent upon the dismissal of the petition for special leave

                   to appeal above mentioned the order of the Hon'ble Supreme

                   Court of India dated 6th May, 2011 passed in the matter

                   granting bail to the petitioner, Fazlur Rahman Khan has been

                   vacated.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              The copy of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India

                   be sent to the Court concerned through FAX.
