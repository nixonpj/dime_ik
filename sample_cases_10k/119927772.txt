<p id="p_1">1. Whether Reporters of local papers may be allowed to see the judgment?
</p><p id="p_2">2. To be referred to the Reporters or not?

Ajay Tewari, J. (Oral):
</p>
<p id="p_4">            This is a petition for issuing directions to get the complaint of

the petitioner investigated by any independent agency. It is further stated

that the petitioner has made complaints Annexure P-2 and P-3.

</p><p id="p_5">            In my considered opinion it would be in the interest of justice if

the respondent No.3 looks into the said complaints and take whatever action

he deems fit thereon within a period of two weeks from the date of receipt

of a certified copy of this order. Ordered accordingly.

</p><p id="p_6">            Petition stands disposed of.
