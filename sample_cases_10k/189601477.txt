<div class="doc_title">Smt. Shanti Devi &amp; Ors vs Sri Ravi Kumar &amp; Ors on 27 April, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Miscellaneous Appeal No.534 of 2011
                  ======================================================
                  Smt. Shanti Devi &amp; Ors
                                                                      .... .... Appellant/s
                                                   Versus
                  Sri Ravi Kumar &amp; Ors
                                                                     .... .... Respondent/s
                  ======================================================
                  Appearance :
                  For the Appellant/s    :    Mr. Mukesh Prasad Singh
                  For the Respondent/s     : Mr.
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR
                  SRIVASTAVA
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">12   27-04-2017                The office has reported that in compliance of order

                  dated 09.02.2017, the appellants have filed requisites for issuance

                  of notice upon the respondent no.1 without process fee of Rs.50/-

                  whereas notification dated 21.09.2016 of the Patna High Court

                  regarding the amendment in Chapter XIII Part-III with regard to

                  Fees and Cost of the Patna High Court Rules, 1916, says that a

                  fee of Rs.50/- is chargeable for issuance of one time process.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Submission on behalf of the appellants is that the

                  aforesaid notification dated 21.09.2016 goes to show that fee of

                  Rs.50/- is chargeable only for issuance one time process and if due

                  to any reason, the second time process is issued then the requisite

                  fee is not chargeable.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               The notification dated 21.09.2016 goes to show that in

                  Part-III of Chapter-XIII of the Patna High Court Rules, certain

                  amendments were made and the aforesaid               amendments were
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court MA No.534 of 2011 (12) dt.27-04-2017</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                     made effective from the date of publication of the aforesaid

                     amendments, i.e., from 21.09.2016. The amended Rule-2(i) of

                     Part-III of Chapter -XIII of the Patna High Court Rules says that a

                     fee of Rs.50/-only as one time process fee irrespective of the

                     number of respondents shall be charged in all the cases.. The word

                     'one time" has been used in the aforesaid rule and the aforesaid

                     word indicates that process fee can be charged only for one time.

                     Moreover, Rule 3(ii) of part-III of Chapter-XIII of the Patna High

                     Court Rules says that no fee shall be chargeable for serving or

                     executing any processes issued a second time in consequence of

                     an adjournment made otherwise than at the instance of the party.

                     The aforesaid provision also goes to show that the fee is not

                     chargeable for issuance of process second time.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   Admittedly, the appellants have deposited process fee

                     once on 21.11.2016 and, therefore, in my view, there is no need to

                     deposit the court process fee of Rs.50/- again by the appellants.

</p><p id="p_5">                                   In the aforesaid manner, the aforesaid issue raised by

                     the office is resolved.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   The office must issue processes in the light of previous

                     order of this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                          (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
N.K/-
