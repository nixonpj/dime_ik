<div class="doc_title">Pinki Yadav vs Dilip Choudhary on 27 March, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                            Conc. No. 206/17

27.03.2017
       Shri Anil Ojha with Shri Manish Yadav, learned counsel for the
applicants.
       Issue notice to the respondents on payment of process-fee by</pre>
<p id="p_1">ordinary as well as by registered/AD mode today itself. Notices be
made returnable within a week.
</p><p id="p_2">       This Court, on 10/03/17 has granted anticipatory bail to the
applicants. It has been alleged in the contempt petition that inspite of
best efforts on behalf of the applicants, the respondents have declined
to accept the order passed by this Court and they are regularly visiting
the house of the applicants.
</p><p id="p_3">       This Court, in order to sort out the matter, has requested the
learned Govt. Advocate (Shri Romesh Dave) to inform the contemnors
and to inform the Town Inspector, Tukoganj Police Station to mark
their presence so that the matter is sorted out. The request made
before Lunch to the Govt. Advocate, to which, he, in turn has informed
this Court that the contemnors for the reasons best known to them,
are not present and, therefore, formal notice is being issued in the
matter.
</p><p id="p_4">       Prima-facie, there appears to be non-compliance of order
passed by this Court. The concerned Superintendent of Police shall
also remain present on the next date of hearing, under whom the
police station falls.
</p><p id="p_5">       Learned Govt. Advocate is directed to inform him about the
matter.
</p><p id="p_6">       List on 06/04/17.
</p><p id="p_7">       C.c. as per rules.
</p><p id="p_8">                                               (S.C.SHARMA)
                                                 JUDGE
