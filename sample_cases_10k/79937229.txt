</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 03-12-2019

     Heard Mr. L. N. Dihingia, learned counsel for the petitioner. Also heard Mr. Sudipta Dutta,
learned Standing Counsel, Forest Department appearing for the respondent nos. 1, 2 &amp; 3.
</p>
<p id="p_2">     This application is filed alleging non-compliance of the order dated 25.03.2019 passed
by this Court in the writ petition, W.P.(C) No. 4442/2018 and the order dated 09.09.2019
passed in connected I.A. (Civil) No. 2839/2019.
</p>
<p id="p_3">     By the order dated 25.03.2019, a direction was given to the respondent authorities to
complete the departmental proceeding against the petitioner within a period of 4 (four)
months and to pass appropriate consequential orders. It was directed that the petitioner is
discharged from any liability, then the consequential benefits be accorded to him.
</p>
<p id="p_4">     Mr. Dutta, learned Standing Counsel, Forest Department has placed on record an order
dated 27.11.2019 passed by the Divisional Forest Officer (DFO), Kachugaon Division,
Gossaigaon. By the order dated 27.11.2019, the departmental proceedings brought against
the petitioner has been dropped. In such view of the matter, as a corollary of the said order
the respondent authorities are to take necessary steps for giving the petitioner the
consequential benefits entitled to him.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     It is submitted by Mr. Dutta that "Forest" is a transferred subject and in the writ petition,
W.P.(C) No. 4442/2018, the Divisional Forest Officer, Kachugaon Division, Gossaigaon was not
a party. As the transferred subject of "Forest" is within the authority of the Bodoland
Territorial Council (BTC), the matter of entitlement of the petitioner will be processed by the
BTC. Thus, by observing that the petitioner is at liberty to file an appropriate representation
before the said BTC authorities, this application stands closed.
</p>
