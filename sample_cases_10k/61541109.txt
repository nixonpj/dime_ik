</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 03-04-2019

           In the affidavit sworn on 28.02.2018 and filed on 05.03.2018 by the Director of Education,
Bodoland Territorial Council, (in short, 'BTC'), it is categorically stated that out of total 2125 posts,
1441 Nos. of posts have already been filled up and the remaining posts shall be filled up by TET
candidates if and when the stay order dated 14.02.2014 passed in WP(C) No. 697/2014 is vacated by
the High Court.
</p>
<p id="p_2">          On perusal of the records of WP(C) 697/2014, it is seen that the said Writ Petition No.
697/2014 was dismissed on 14.07.2015 being infructuous and by the said order, the interim order
passed therein was also vacated.
</p>
<p id="p_3">          As such, the Director of Education, BTC, Kokrajhar, respondent No. 4, shall personally
appear before this Court on 25.04.2019 at 10:30 AM and shall apprise the Court as to why
appropriate order under the provisions of law should not be passed against him for making a false
statement before the Court.
</p>
