<p id="p_2">IN THE COURT OF SH. DINESH BHATT, ADJ-03, (NORTH)/DELHI




Date of Institution:-20/01/10
Date of Decision:-24/02/10


Civil Suit No.:-10/10
Sh. Suresh Chand Kathuria
R/o 245, Kalyan Vihar,
Delhi                                                ..........Plaintiff
VERSUS
Sh. Naresh Kumar
M/s Cheap Store
5644, Gandhi Market,
Sadar Bazar, Delhi
Also At:
</p><p id="p_3">D-145, Ajay Enclave,
(Ajanta Cinema), New Delhi                           ..........Defendant


JUDGMENT:-
</p>

<p id="p_4">        This is a suit U/o 37 CPC for recovery of an amount of Rs. 5 lakhs
on the basis of cheque issued by Sh. Naresh Kumar for M/s Cheap Store,
defendant in favour of the plaintiff.
</p><p id="p_5">                                                                  Contd.......
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                     -2-</span>

<p id="p_6">The chaque dt. 1.11.2009 was dishonoured and intimation received vide
cheque returning memo dt. 14.12.2009 of Punjab National Bank. Plaintiff
had also served legal notice dt. 21/12/09. the UPC receipt and Regd./AD
receipts have been filed on record. Since no appearance have been filed nor
any leave to defend have been filed. The plaintiff is entitled to the suit
amount.
</p><p id="p_7">       Accordingly, the suit of the plaintiff is decreed for a sum of Rs. 5
lakhs against the defendant. Decree sheet be prepared. File be consigned to
Record Room.
</p>

<p id="p_8">Announced in the open court                (DINESH BHATT)
on 24/02/10                                ADJ/Delhi/24/02/10
