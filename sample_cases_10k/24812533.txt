      0401WP5960.05-Judgment                                                                 2/3


     O R A L    J U D G M E N T (Per Smt. Vasanti    A    Naik, J.)




                                                                                       
                                                               
                      By this writ petition, the petitioner challenges the orders

     of the Deputy Director of Education and the Education Officer, dated

     16/02/2005 and 30/08/2005 respectively directing that no permission




                                                              
     be granted to the minority institution to fill the posts of teaching and

     non-teaching staff unless they absorb the surplus teachers in the school

     run by the minority institution.  By the impugned communication dated




                                               
     01/09/2005,   the   Education   Officer   had   directed   the   petitioner-
</p>                             
<p id="p_2">     institution not to appoint a junior clerk on the vacancy arising due to

     the promotion of Shri Abdul Majid on the post of senior clerk, without
                            
     securing the prior permission of the Education Officer.  When the writ

     petition came up for admission and hearing on 27/01/2006, this court
      

     issued Rule and also granted interim relief in terms of prayer clauses
   



     (B) and (D).  The interim relief is operating in favour of the petitioner,

     till date.    
</p>




<p id="p_3">                      It   is   well   settled   that   a   minority   institution   cannot   be

     directed to absorb surplus teachers.  This court has held in the judgment

     reported   in  2014     (4)     ABR   521   (Canossa   Society,   Mumbai   .vs.
</p>




<p id="p_4">     Commissioner, Social Welfare, Pune) and relied on by this court from

     time   to   time   that   surplus   employees   cannot   be   thrust   on   a   minority

     institution.  In view of the position of law, as laid down by this court in

     the judgment reported in 2014  (4)  ABR 521, the petitioner cannot be

     directed to absorb the surplus teachers.  In any case, it is stated in view




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">    ::: Uploaded on - 06/01/2017                               ::: Downloaded on - 07/01/2017 01:35:19 :::</span>
      0401WP5960.05-Judgment                                                                       3/3


     of the settled position of law, the government has in the recent past not




                                                                                             
     directed any minority institution to absorb the surplus teachers. 
</p>



                                                                     
<p id="p_5">                      Since the purpose of filing the writ petition stands served

     in   view   of   the   interim   order   and   since   it   is   held   by   this   court   that




                                                                    
     surplus   teachers   cannot   be   thrust   on   a   minority   institution,   the   writ

     petition could be disposed of.  Hence, in view of the law laid down by




                                                  
     this   court   in   the   judgment   reported   in  2014     (4)     ABR   521,  the
                             
     impugned communication of the Education Officer is set aside. Rule is

     made absolute in the aforesaid terms with no order as to costs. 
</p>
