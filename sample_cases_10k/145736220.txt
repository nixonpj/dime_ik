      IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

      SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION No 7894 of 2004
      --------------------------------------------------------------
      RAJNIKANT ALIAS RAJUBHAI JINABHAI CHAUHAN
     Versus
      PARAMHANS MANIBHAI DESAI
      --------------------------------------------------------------
      Appearance:
      1. Special Civil Application No. 7894 of 2004
           MR HJ NANAVATI for Petitioner No. 1-2
           MR NIRAV C THAKKAR for Respondent No. 1, 2 &amp; 3
           MR BY MANKAD for Respondent No. 4
      --------------------------------------------------------------

               CORAM : HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE K.S.JHAVERI

               Date of Order: 06/10/2004

 ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">      	RULE.
</p><p id="p_2">      	Mr.   Nirav  Thakkar  waives  service  of rule on
      behalf of respondent nos.  1, 2 and 3  and  Mr.    Mankad
      waives service of rule on behalf of respondent no.4.
      	Both  the  sides  shall maintain status-quo as on
      today, till the Contempt Application, pending before  the
      Appellate Court  is  heard and decided.  However, it will
      be open for respondent nos.  1, 2 and 3  to  request  the
      Appellate   Court   to   hear  and  decide  the  Contempt
      Application as early as  possible.    As  and  when  such
      request  is  made,  the  Appellate  Court  shall hear and
      decide the said application within a period of two months
      from the date of the receipt of the application.    After
      the  said  application  is  heard  and  decided,  present
      respondent nos.  1, 2 &amp; 3 shall not change the status-quo
      without the permission of this Court.  It  is  also  made
      clear that respondent no.  4 shall not be restrained from
      taking   any  action  under  Gujarat  Municipalities  Act
      against the parties.
</p>      
<p id="p_3">      				[K.S. JHAVERI, J.]
      /phalguni/

</p>
