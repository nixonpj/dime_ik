<div class="doc_title">Sujeet Kumar @ Sujit Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 2 August, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA

                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.45401 of 2018
                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -138 Year- 2018 Thana -KADAM KUAN District- PATNA
                 ======================================================
                 1. SUJEET KUMAR @ SUJIT KUMAR S/o Krishna Prasad @ Krishna
                 Prasad Yadav, R/o Konitola, Pareo, P.S.- Bihta, District- Patna.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                             .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s : Mr.Surya Swetabh, Advocate
                                        Mr. Sharad Shekhar, Advocate

                 For the Respondent : Mr. Ajit Kumar, A.P.P.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   02-08-2018                Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             The petitioner seeks anticipatory bail in connection with

                 Kadamkuan P.S. Case No.138 of 2018, registered for offences

                 punishable under <a href="/doc/1439698/" id="a_1">Sections 419</a>, <a href="/doc/1439698/" id="a_2">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1439698/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/1439698/" id="a_4">468</a> and <a href="/doc/1439698/" id="a_5">120</a> (B)/34<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_6"> of

                 the Indian Penal Code</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              As per F.I.R., allegation against the petitioner is that in

                 place of the petitioner one another co-accused, Chandan Patel was

                 appearing in the Bihar Police Sub Inspector Examination, 2018.

</p><p id="p_4">                               Submission of the learned counsel for the petitioner is

                 that the petitioner has no knowledge about the same and he has

                 been falsely implicated in this case and <a href="/doc/1439698/" id="a_7">Section 419</a> if<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_8"> the Indian

                 Penal Code</a> is bailable. It is further submitted that the petitioner
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">           Patna High Court Cr.M isc. No.45401 of 2018 (2) dt.02-08-2018</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        has no criminal antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                       Learned A.P.P. opposes the prayer for bail.

                                      Having heard both sides and in view of the facts and

                        circumstances, as discussed above, let the petitioner, above

                        named, surrenders be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of

                        Rs. 25,000/- (Twenty five thousand) with two sureties of the like

                        amount each to the satisfaction of learned Additional Chief

                        Judicial Magistrate, Patna in connection with                Kadamkuan

                        P.S.Case No. 138 of 2018, subject to the conditions as laid down

                        under <a href="/doc/1439698/" id="a_9">Section 438</a> (2) of <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_10">Cr.P.C</a>. with condition that one of the

                        bailors of the petitioner shall be a local person having sufficient

                        immovable property within the jurisdiction of the concerned court.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                   (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
Sudha/-
</p>
