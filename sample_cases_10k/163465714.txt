FOLLOWING:



                           ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Having heard learned counsel for the parties and

having perused the material placed on record, this Court is

unable to find any reason to consider interference in the order

dated 03.01.2018, as passed by the Trial Court under Order

VII Rule 14 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, receiving

the original of the document, whose photocopy has already

been produced on record.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      The    objections   sought   to    be   raised    by   the

defendant/petitioner could be raised at the appropriate stage,

but at the present stage, the order receiving the document on
                                           W.P.No.17504/2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             -3-</span>




record, where in the suit even the issues have not been

framed, remains unexceptionable.

</p><p id="p_3">      No case of jurisdictional error being made out, the

petition stands dismissed.
</p>
