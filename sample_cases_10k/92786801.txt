                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.79094 of 2018
                   Arising Out of PS. Case No.-143 Year-2018 Thana- GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL COMP.
                                                    District- Sitamarhi
                 ======================================================
                 Lakhendra Rai @ Lakhindar Rai Son of Chhote Rai, Residence of Village-
                 Basaha, Police Station - Bajpatti, Distt.- Sitamarhi.

                                                                             ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                 The State Of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :     Mr. Pushpendra Kumar Singh, Advocate.
                 For the Opposite Party/s :     Mr. Ram Bachan Singh, A.P.P.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SANJAY PRIYA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   16-01-2019                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner seeks bail in Complaint Case No. G-

                   O/143 of 2018, instituted for the offence under Sections 30(a)

                   of Bihar Prohibition &amp; <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>, 2016.

</p><p id="p_3">                                    It is alleged in the written report that 72 liters of

                   Nepali Soufi has been recovered from possession of the

                   petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                       Petitioner is in custody since 10.11.2018 having

                   clean antecedent.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                    Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances

                   of the case, prayer for bail of the petitioner is allowed. Let the

                   petitioner above named, be released on bail on furnishing bail

                   bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of the like
               Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.79094 of 2018(2) dt.16-01-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                           2/2</span>




                        amount each to the satisfaction of learned A.D.J. IInd-cum-

                        special Judge, <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_2">Excise Act</a>, Sitamarhi, in connection with

                        Complaint Case No. G-O/143 of 2018, subject to the condition

                        that both the bailors will be the close relatives of the petitioner.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                                            (Sanjay Priya, J)
S.Ali/-
</p>
