                              JUDGMENT
</pre><p id="p_1">Raman, J.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      According to the petitioner-Shaju Sherief,       he married the

daughter of the 7th respondent and their marriage was performed

under the <a href="/doc/4234/" id="a_1">Special Marriages Act</a> on 3rd March, 2008. She is alleged

to be under the illegal custody of the 7th respondent, the father of the

alleged detenue. The7th respondent-father appeared before us. We

have interacted with him and he stated that his wife, the mother of

the alleged detenue, was in the hospital and she was discharged only

yesterday, that is why the alleged detenue could not be produced

today. He also agreed to perform the customary form of marriage,

after his daughter's examination. The petitioner submits that as such

he has no grievance to be redressed. Since the parties have reached

an amicable settlement, we close this writ petition.


</p><p id="p_3">                                                  P.R.RAMAN,
                                                    Judge.
