                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                     Criminal Miscellaneous No.41854 of 2017
                          Arising Out of PS.Case No. -416 Year- 2015 Thana -MADHUBANI TOWN District-
                                                           MADHUBANI
                   ======================================================
                   1. Md. Sahid Son of Late Md. Rafik, R/o Village- Jhanjharpur, Madarsa
                   Choak, P.S.- Jhanjharpur, District- Madhubani.

                                                                                  .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                          Versus
                   1. The State of Bihar

                                                                  .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Manish Kumar No 13
                   For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Smt Pushpa Sinha
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE VINOD KUMAR SINHA
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   21-09-2017                      Heard the parties.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                    This application is for grant of regular bail in

                   connection with Madhubani Town P.S.Case No.416 of 2015 dated

                   14.9.2015 corresponding to G.R.No.2650 of 2015 for the offences

                   under <a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_1">Sections 395</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_2">397</a> of the Indian Penal Code and <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_3">Section

                   27</a> of the Arms Act and Section Â¾ of Explosive Substance Act.

</p><p id="p_3">                                    The petitioner is not named in this case and his name

                   appeared on the basis of confession of the other co-accused

                   persons. The petitioner is in custody for about two years and it

                   further appears that he has criminal antecedent of involving in

                   three other cases also.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                    Submission of the learned counsel for the petitioner

                   is that the petitioner is in custody since 14.10.2015 and other
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.41854 of 2017 (3) dt.21-09-2017</span>




             accused persons have been granted bail by this Court.

</p><p id="p_5">                              Heard learned A.P.P. also.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                              In this case, a report was called for from the learned

             Trial Court and from perusal of the same it appears that the charge

             had been framed on 12.5.2016, no witness has been examined up-

             till-now, 08 accused persons have absconded and the case is fixed

             for appearance.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                              Having heard both sides and in view of report, at this

             stage, I am not inclined to grant bail to the petitioner rather the

             learned trial court is directed to complete the appearance within a

             period of one month.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                              At the same time, the S.P., Madhubani is directed to

             get the absconding accused persons appeared in the trial court

             within a period of one month and once the appearance is

             completed or after one month, the learned trial court will proceed

             with the evidence even after separating the case of the petitioner.

             Once the evidence commenced, the petitioner is at liberty to renew

             his prayer for bail before the learned trial court and considering

             the period of custody, he will release the petitioner on bail bond of

             Rs.25,000/- (Twenty Five Thousand) with two sureties of the like

             amount each to the satisfaction of Additional Sessions Judge VI,

             Madhubani in connection with Madhubani Town P.S.Case No.416
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.41854 of 2017 (3) dt.21-09-2017</span>




                     of 2015 dated 14.9.2015 giving rise to S.T.No.107 of 2016 but

                     before releasing the bail, he will verify the address of the

                     petitioner as well as genuineness of address of the bailor.

</p><p id="p_9">                                      With the aforesaid observation, this application is

                     disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_10">

</p><p id="p_11">                                                (Vinod Kumar Sinha, J)
chn/-
</p>
