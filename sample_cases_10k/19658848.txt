            IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                          AT CHANDIGARH

                                           CRM No. M-25978 of 2017 (O/M)
                                           Date of decision : 10.8.2017

Rajinder Kumar and others                                   ....... Petitioners

                                         Versus

State of Punjab and others                                  ....... Respondents</pre>

<p id="p_1">CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE KULDIP SINGH

Present:-    Mr. Sandeep Arora, Advocate, for the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_2">1.           Whether the Reporters of local newspaper may be allowed to
             see the judgment ?
</p><p id="p_3">2.           To be referred to the Reporter or not.
</p><p id="p_4">3.           Whether the judgment should be reported in the digest ?
</p><pre id="pre_2">             -.-                      -.-

KULDIP SINGH J. (ORAL)

             Heard.

</pre><p id="p_5">             It comes out that there is a dispute of petitioners with their

brother and the police had launched security proceedings under <a href="/doc/1914745/" id="a_1">Section 107</a>,

<a href="/doc/1228992/" id="a_2">151</a> Cr.P.C. against both the parties. There is no illegality in the same and

there is no ground to quash the said proceedings. Hence, present petition is

dismissed.
</p>
