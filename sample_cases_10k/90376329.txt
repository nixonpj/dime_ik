<div class="doc_title">Gagan &amp; Ors vs State Of Punjab on 29 November, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">                                    201
              IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                                                           CRM-M-26944-2014
                                                        Date of Decision:29.11.2014

           Gagan and others                                                         ... Petitioners

                                                              Versus

           State of Punjab                                                         ... Respondent

           CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANUPINDER SINGH GREWAL

           Present : Mr. P.S.Sekhon, Advocate
                     for the petitioner (s).

                            Mr. Neeraj Sharma, AAG, Punjab.

           ANUPINDER SINGH GREWAL, J. (ORAL)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                            The petitioners are seeking anticipatory bail in FIR No.80 dated

           15.07.2014 under <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_1">Sections 307</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">120-B</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_7">148</a> and <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_8">149</a>

           IPC registered at Police Station City Budhlada, District Mansa.

</p><p id="p_2">                            The Co-ordinate Bench of this Court, on 11.08.2014, had passed

           the following order:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                        "Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that the
                            case is of version and cross-version.           Further, as per the
                            complainant, the petitioners were said to have caused injuries
                            with blunt weapons and, that too, on non-vital parts of the injured
                            persons.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                                        Notice of motion to Advocate General, Punjab for
                            29.11.2014.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                                        In the meantime, the petitioner shall appear before the
                            Investigating Officer on 18.08.2014 at 11.00 a.m. and join the
                            investigation. In the event of his arrest, he be admitted to interim
                            bail   by   the   Investigating    Officer/Arresting    Officer   to    his
                            satisfaction. They shall, however, abide by all the conditions as
                            envisaged by <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_9">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C."
</blockquote>



<p id="p_3">ANJU
2014.11.29 15:11
I attest to the accuracy and
authenticity of this document
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            CRM-M-26944-2014                                                  -2-</span>


                            Learned counsel for the State on instructions from ASI Parveen

           Kumar submits that the petitioners have joined the investigation.

</p><p id="p_4">                            Therefore, the interim bail granted to the petitioners, vide order

           dated 11.08.2014 is made absolute. The petitioners shall appear before the

           Investigating Officer as and when called upon to do so. They shall abide by

           all the conditions as envisaged under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_10">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.</p><pre id="pre_2">



           November 29, 2014                             (ANUPINDER SINGH GREWAL)
