MR HITESH N ACHARYA(2302) for the PETITIONER(s) No. 1

 CORAM: HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. R.SUBHASH REDDY

                              Date : 30/10/2018

                        ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1"> (PER : HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. R.SUBHASH REDDY)


</p><p id="p_2">1.            Pursuant to notice issued in Form-I vide
order dated 9.10.2018, the respondent nos.4,5 and
6 are present and their presence is dispensed
with until further orders.
</p>

<p id="p_3">2.            On instructions, it is stated by learned
Assistant Government Pleader that their efforts
to trace out the service book have failed, as
such, they are taking steps for reconstructing
the        service          book     by          obtaining    necessary
information and particulars from the applicant.</p><pre id="pre_2">
It    is      represented          that      the     respondents     will


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   Page 1 of 2</span>
            C/MCA/805/2017                                             ORDER



reconstruct            the    service            book     at       the   earliest
possible          time       and        submit          the        proposals    to
</pre><p id="p_4">implement the directions issued by the learned
Single       Judge.         The    learned            Assistant       Government
Pleader         also        made    a        request          to     direct    the
applicant herein to provide necessary particulars
of his service so as enable the respondents to
reconstruct           the     service           book     of    the       applicant
herein.
</p>

<p id="p_5">3.            Learned         counsel            for    the        applicant    to
give       instructions            to      the        applicant       herein    to
approach          the       authorities               with     the       necessary
particulars            of    the    service             and    the       documents
which he possesses so as to enable the respondent
nos.4 to 6 to reconstruct the service book. The
service book to be reconstructed within a period
of two weeks. Thereupon, the proposals shall be
made       for          payment          of       necessary          amounts    in
compliance           of      the    directions               issued      by    this
Court. This matter be placed for further orders
on 30.11.2018.
</p>


<p id="p_6">                                                        (R.SUBHASH REDDY, CJ)




                                                         (VIPUL M. PANCHOLI, J)
