                           JUDGMENT
</pre>

<p id="p_1">        Petitioner has approached this Court being faced with

the proceedings initiated by the respondent Bank under the

<a href="/doc/52229129/" id="a_1">SARFAESI Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2.   Petitioner had taken a loan for Rs.26,00,000/-. The

petitioner could not repay the monthly installments in time on

account of certain financial difficulties. In the meantime,

proceedings had been taken by the respondent Bank under the

<a href="/doc/52229129/" id="a_2">SARFAESI Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      3.   Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the

petitioner is ready and willing to pay the outstanding dues in

installments and will also pay the regular installments, if some

time is granted.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      4.   Having regard to the fact that the petitioner had

expressed the willingness to repay the amount in installments,

W.P.(C) No. 7757 of 2015

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                   -2-</span>



I am of the view that some indulgence can be shown to the

petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">        Accordingly, this writ petition is disposed of as under:

</p><p id="p_6">        (i)   The petitioner shall pay the overdue amount with

accrued interest in ten equal monthly installments starting

from 30/01/2017.
</p>
<p id="p_7">        (ii)  In addition to payment of the aforesaid amount,

petitioner shall also remit the monthly installments as per the

agreed terms.
</p>
<p id="p_8">        (iii) If there is default in payment of any of the aforesaid

amounts, it shall be open for the respondent Bank to take such

steps in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                                Sd/-
</p><p id="p_10">                                           A.M. SHAFFIQUE
