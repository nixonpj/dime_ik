<div class="doc_title">An Application For Bail Under ... vs Sk. Samsad on 7 March, 2011</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Ashim Kumar Roy</div>

<pre id="pre_1">  20.
07-03-11</pre>
<p id="p_1">                         CRM No. 722 of 2011

           In the matter of an application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of
           Criminal Procedure filed on 21.1.11 in connection with Park Street Police
           Station Case No. 89 dated 26.04.2009 under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_2">Sections 392</a>/<a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_3">397</a> of the
           Indian Penal Code read with <a href="/doc/1961417/" id="a_4">Section 25(1b)</a> (a)/27 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_5">Arms Act</a>.

</p><p id="p_2">                                   And

           In the matter of : Sk. Samsad

                 Mr. Bibhasaditya Chakrabarty ... for the petitioner

                 Mr. Asimes Goswami, ld. P.P.
</p><p id="p_3">                 Mrs. Hasi Saha               ... for the State

                 Heard the learned Advocate appearing on behalf of the petitioners

           as well as the learned Advocate appearing on behalf of the State. Perused

           the case diary.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                 This petitioner on being arrested in connection with a case relating

           to the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_6">Sections 392</a>/<a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_7">397</a> of the Indian Penal

           Code read with <a href="/doc/73862/" id="a_8">Section 25</a> (1b) (a)/27 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_9">Arms Act</a> which was

           registered vide Park Street Police Station Case no. 89 dated 26.04.2009,

           was released on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                 However, after submission of the charge sheet, petitioner did not

           appear in the court and accordingly warrant of arrest was issued against

           him on 1.10.2009. It further appears that on execution of warrant of

           arrest, he was produced in court on 16.10.2010 and that is nearly more

           than one year after the issuance of warrant of arrest. It is submitted that

           petitioner is not a resident of West Bengal      and during trial he was

           involved in another case; that is also in connection with an offence of

           dacoity.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                         2</span><p id="p_6">




      Considering the above facts, in our opinion, the question of granting

bail to the petitioner deserves no consideration. Accordingly, prayer for

bail is rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                              (Ashim Kumar Roy, J.)



                              (Mrinal Kanti Sinha, J.)
