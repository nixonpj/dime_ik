<div class="doc_title">Kukma vs Parvindersingh on 14 July, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                                     ~1~
       HIGH COURT OF MADHYA PRADESH: BENCH: INDORE
                   NATIONAL LOK ADALAT
                           MCC No. 1704/2018
Indore, Dated 14/7/2018
          Shri Subodh Choudhary learned counsel for applicant.
         Heard.
         This MCC has been filed for restoration of MA No. 1331/17
which has been dismissed in default for non compliance of the
Common Conditional Peremptory Order dated 13/12/17. Since
there is a delay of 184 days in filing the appeal, therefore, IA No.
4872/18 which is an application for condonation of delay has been
filed.
         Counsel for applicant submits that dismissal of the appeal
was at the pre-notice stage, therefore notice to respondents is not</pre>
<p id="p_1">required. He further submits that the default could not be cured
within time on account of bonafide mistake of counsel. He further
submits that non compliance was unintentional and if appeal is not
restored then the applicants/claimants will suffer serious prejudice.
</p><p id="p_2">         On due consideration IA No. 4872/18 is allowed and delay in
filing the appeal is condoned.
</p>
<p id="p_3">         Accordingly MCC is allowed and MA No. 1331/2017 is
restored to its original position.</p><pre id="pre_2">



   (Justice Prakash Shrivastava)               (Avinash Sirpurkar)
           MEMBER                                  MEMBER


BDJ
