<div class="doc_title">Sajani Devi vs The State Of Bihar on 18 August, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No. 32197 of 2016
                      Arising Out of PS.Case No. -13 Year- 2016 Thana -GOPALPUR District- BHAGALPUR
                  ======================================================
                  Sajani Devi Wife of Pawan Mandal, resident of Village Kathchira, Police -
                  Station- Gopalpur, District Bhagalpur.

                                                                                  .... ....   Petitioner
                                                        Versus
                  The State of Bihar

                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party
                  ======================================================
                  Appearance :
                  For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr. Vikram Singh
                  For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Amit Kumar Rakesh
                  ======================================================
                  CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAKESH KUMAR
                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2.   18-08-2016                  Heard Sri Vikram Singh, learned counsel for the

                  petitioner and Sri Amit Kumar Rakesh, learned Addl. Public

                  Prosecutor.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The sole petitioner, who is in custody in Gopalpur

                  P.S. Case No. 13 of 2016 registered for offences under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections

                  147</a>, <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_4">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_6">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_7">325</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_8">307</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_9">504</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_10">576</a> of the Indian

                  Penal Code and subsequently, <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_11">Section 302</a> of the Indian Penal

                  Code was added, has prayed for grant of bail.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Learned counsel for the petitioner, by way of referring

                  to Annexure - 1 to the petition i.e. copy of F.I.R., submits that in

                  the F.I.R., save and except the allegation that petitioner was also

                  present at the place of occurrence, there is no specific accusation

                  against the petitioner, whereas, the informant had specifically
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.32197 of 2016 (2) dt.18-08-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                    alleged regarding overt act against some of the accused persons.

                    So far as petitioner is concerned, there is nothing. He submits that

                    it can hardly be a case of general and omnibus allegation.

</p><p id="p_4">                                    Learned Addl. Public Prosecutor though has opposed

                    the prayer for bail, he does not dispute the submission of the

                    learned counsel for the petitioner that there is no specific

                    accusation against the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                    Keeping in view the nature of accusation as well as

                    the fact that the petitioner is lady, let the petitioner namely Sajani

                    Devi be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail-bond of Rs. 10,000/-

                    (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                    satisfaction of learned Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                    Naugachia (Bhagalpur) in connection with Gopalpur P.S. Case

                    No. 13 of 2016.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                                      (Rakesh Kumar, J.)
Anay


 U        T
