30    08.04.                            C.O. 1182 of 2015
AGM   2015


                            Eden Realty Ventures Pvt. Ltd.
</p><p id="p_2">                                       Versus
                              Sri Debasish Ghosh &amp; Anr

               Mr. Uday Narayan Betal,
                                  ... ... For the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
                     This is an application under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">article 227</a> of the

               Constitution    of       India   at   the     instance   of   the

               defendant/petitioner seeking an order for expeditious

               disposal of an application for injunction pending since

               16.8.2014.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                     From the certified copy of the order sheets annexed

               to this revisional application, it appears that the next date

               is fixed on 11.05.2015.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                     The trial Court is requested to take up the said

               application for injunction on the date, so fixed, and shall

               make efforts to dispose of the suit as expeditiously as

               possible without granting unnecessary adjournments to

               either of the parties and preferably within three weeks

               therefrom in accordance with law.

</p><p id="p_6">                     With these observations, the revisional application

               is disposed of. No order as to costs.




</p><p id="p_7">                                                           ( Harish Tandon, J.)
