             for the petitioners.
1.    Whether Reporters of Local papers may be allowed to see the judgment?

                                            *****
SURYA KANT, J. (Oral)
</pre><p id="p_1">              The petitioners allege that the Gram Panchayat's land
comprising Khasra No.306/7 has been encroached upon and no action is
being taken by the Gram Panchayat for removal of such encroachment.
Under Section 7 of the Punjab Village Common Lands (Regulation) Act,
1961, as applicable to the State of Haryana, the residents of the village are
competent to initiate such proceedings against the encroachers of the Gram
Panchayat's land.
</p><p id="p_2">              We, thus, relegate the petitioner to the above stated remedy. If
such a petition is filed, the same shall be decided expeditiously in
accordance with principles of natural justice and preferably within 6
months from the date of receipt of a certified copy of this order.
</p><p id="p_3">              Disposed of.
</p>
