<pre id="pre_1">                                  AP No.94 of 2014

                         IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA

                         Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction

                                  ORIGINAL SIDE




                   SREI EQUIPMENT FINANCE LIMITED
                               Versus
            SHREE MAHALAXMI CORPORATION PRIVATE LIMITED


  BEFORE:

  The Hon'ble JUSTICE SANJIB BANERJEE

  Date : 8th April, 2014.



                                                                        Appearance:
                                                                Mr. S. Banerjee, Adv.

                                                     Mr. Siddhartha Banerjee, Adv.



            The Court : The respondent is represented and cannot dispute that a</pre>

<p id="p_1">sum of in excess of Rs.3.90 crore is due to the petitioner only on account of

defaulted instalments.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Since the respondent has indicated the location of the two assets

covered by the agreement, the receiver will take possession of such assets, keep

them at a place to be provided by the petitioner upon ensuring that the petitioner

cannot use the same, invite offers for sale of the assets in such newspapers as
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                           2</span>


the petitioner may suggest and report to Court when the matter appears next six

weeks hence.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             The receiver will be paid a further remuneration of 1500 GM and all

expenses on account of the receiver's travel, accommodation and the like will be

borne by the petitioner and added to the claim in the pending reference.

</p><p id="p_4">             The petitioner suggests that it may be useful to sell the assets at site

on 'as in where is' basis since the dismantling thereof and transportation may

denude them of their value. The receiver is directed to invite offers for sale of the

assets on 'as is where is basis'.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             Urgent certified website copies of this order, if applied for, be

supplied to the parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.




</p><p id="p_6">                                                   (SANJIB BANERJEE, J.)

</p>
