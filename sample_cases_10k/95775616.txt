<div class="doc_title">Kailash Paswan vs State Of Bihar on 31 May, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             Cr.Misc. No.11173 of 2010
                                 KAILASH PASWAN
                                         Versus
                                  STATE OF BIHAR
                                       -----------
</pre>


<p id="p_1">5   31.5.2010             Heard counsel for the petitioner and the State as well

                as counsel appearing on behalf of the informant.

</p><p id="p_2">                          The petitioner is an accused in a case under <a href="/doc/162506/" id="a_1">sections

                447</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_2">504</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of the Penal Code and <a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_6">section 27</a> of the

                Arms Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                          Counsel for the petitioner submits that there is a case

                and counter case and in fact the house of the petitioner has been

                burnt by the prosecution side. The petitioner is in custody since

                more than 6 months.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                          Counsel for the informant submits that the opinion

                regarding injury of Sugni Devi is reserved by the Doctor.

</p><p id="p_5">                          Put up this case on 21.6.2010 within top ten cases in

                order to enable counsel for the informant to produce injury

                report of Sugni Devi.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                          In case no injury report is received till then, the matter

                would be heard on its own merits.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><pre id="pre_2">    KHAN                                                   (S.P.Singh,J)
 </pre>
