                             ...for the petitioners.

<p id="p_4">              Mr. Dipankar Mahato

<p id="p_5">       The petitioners accused of offences under ss.498A/326/307/34/302
<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> and in custody from April 6,2013 (the second petitioner) and April
8,2013 (the other petitioners) are seeking bail under s.439 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_2">CrPC</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       We have heard advocates for the petitioners and the State and
perused the case diary containing the statement of the victim girl recorded
by the attending doctor. The girl died after a few days. She stated that her
saree caught fire from a kerosene lamp. We think it will be appropriate to
release the petitioners on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_7">       For these reasons, we allow the CRM and direct that the petitioners
shall be released on a Rs.10,000 bond ( for each) with two sureties(for each)
of Rs.5,000 each ( one surety should be local)- all to the satisfaction of the
Chief Judicial Magistrate, Purulia; and that if released, they shall remain
present in court proceedings on all occasions. Certified xerox.</p><pre id="pre_1">


                                         (Jayanta Kumar Biswas, J)



sh                                            ( Subal Baidya,J)
