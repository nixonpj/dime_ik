CA/13777/2011	 2	ORDER 
 
 

	

 

IN
THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

APPLICATION - FOR FIXING DATE OF HEARING No. 13777 of 2011
 

In


 

SPECIAL
CIVIL APPLICATION No. 29008 of
2007 
 
=================================================
 

AMIRKHAN
S KHOKHAR - Petitioner(s)
 

Versus
 

DEPUTY
EXECUTIVE ENGINEER - Respondent(s)
 

=================================================
 
Appearance : 
MR
HARSHAL M SHAH for Petitioner(s) : 1, 
MR HK PATEL AGP for
Respondent(s) : 1, 
=================================================
 
	  
	 
	  
		 
			 

CORAM
			: 
			
		
		 
			 

HONOURABLE
			MR.JUSTICE K.M.THAKER
		
	

 

Date
: 01/02/2012  
ORAL ORDER</pre> 

 

<p id="p_1">	Heard
Mr. Shah, learned advocate for the applicant.
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_2">2.	The
applicant has taken out present application seeking below mentioned
direction:-
</p> 

 


 


<p id="p_3">"4b	May
Lordships be pleased to fix the SCA No.29008 of 2007 at the earlies."
</p> 

 


 

<p id="p_4">3.	This
Court [Coram: Hon'ble Mr. Justice H.K.Rathod (as His Lordship then
was)] admitted the writ petition vide order dated 11.1.2008. While
admitting the petition and passing the order dated 11.1.2008, the
Court, inter alia, observed that :-
</p> 

 


 


<p id="p_5">"2.	I
have considered the submissions made by both the learned advocates.
The question involved in the present petition requires detailed
examination. Hence, Rule. Expedited." 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_6">4.	Now,
the applicant wants that the hearing of the writ petition may be
fixed on early date. Differently put, the applicant wants out of turn
hearing in priority over the petitions which are senior and older and
where other workers are still awaiting adjudicating of their claims.
Since the Court has already passed the order expediting the hearing,
any other or further or different order is not required. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_7">5.	Office
will do the needful for placing the petition for final hearing in the
cause list of Final Hearing in its due course, as per its seniority. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_8">	With
the aforesaid clarification and direction, the application stands
disposed of. 
</p>
 

 


 

<p id="p_9">							(K.M.Thaker,
J.) 

 

kdc
 

	



</p>
