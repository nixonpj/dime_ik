<p id="p_1">                    Having heard the learned counsels of the parties,
            this Court is of the view that this appeal be heard on the
            following substantial questions of law :
</p><p id="p_2">            1.      As to whether the learned court below has erred in
                 law in considering the deeds starting from the year 1954
                 to 1966 (exhibited documents) bypassing legal effect of
                 the rest legal evidence on record ;
</p>

<p id="p_3">            2.      As to whether <a href="/doc/523729/" id="a_1">Section 45</a> of the Transfer of Property
                 Act   has    been   rightly   incorporated   taking   the
                 background of the dispute between the parties.
</p>

<p id="p_4">                          After the aforesaid substantial questions of law
                 have been formulated, the learned counsels of the
                 parties submitted that the appeal be taken up for
                 hearing on 28th June 2013.
</p><p id="p_5">                          Accordingly, the matter is adjourned till 28th
                 June 2013.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                                                  (Asim Kumar Ray, J. )
