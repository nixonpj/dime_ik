<div class="doc_title">Magma Fincorp Ltd vs Abraham C Kuruvila &amp; Anr on 20 April, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">ORDER SHEET

                                    AP No.134 of 2016
                            IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                             Ordinary Original Civil Jurisdiction


                                      MAGMA FINCORP LTD.
                                         Versus
                                      ABRAHAM C KURUVILA &amp; ANR.


  BEFORE:
  The Hon'ble JUSTICE BISWANATH SOMADDER
  Date : 20th April, 2016.

                                          Mr. Anuj Singh, Advocate for the petitioner.
                                          Mr. Abhishek Gupta, Ms. M. Kelra, Advocates for the
                                          respondents.</pre>




<p id="p_1">              The Court : Let the affidavit of service filed in Court today be taken on

record.
</p>
<p id="p_2">               This application under <a href="/doc/1120409/" id="a_1">section 9</a> of the Arbitration and <a href="/doc/1306164/" id="a_2">Conciliation Act</a>,

1996 has been taken out at the post award stage. The award is under challenge in a

separate proceeding initiated by the respondents under <a href="/doc/1722761/" id="a_3">section 34</a> of the Arbitration and

<a href="/doc/1306164/" id="a_4">Conciliation Act</a>, 1996. In the aforesaid factual backdrop, the finance company is now

before this Court praying, inter alia, for a receiver to be appointed in respect of the asset

in question and also for an injunction restraining the respondents from disposing of

and/or transferring and/or encumbering and/or letting out and/or charging or in any

way dealing with or changing the identity of the asset in question.

</p><p id="p_3">          Considering the fact that a <a href="/doc/1722761/" id="a_5">section 34</a> application is pending, this Court is of the

view that in the facts and circumstances of the instant case, the interest of the petitioner

will be well protected if there is an order of injunction in terms of prayer (b) of the

petition.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                              2</span><p id="p_4">


</p><p id="p_5">             This application is accordingly disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_6">             Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be supplied to

the parties upon compliance of all requisite formalities.




</p><p id="p_7">                                                 (BISWANATH SOMADDER, J.)
