ID No. 828/14     Munna Kr. Vs. M/s Innocean Worldwide Communication Pvt. Ltd. &amp; Anr.     Page No. 1 out of  3 
                     Date of Institution :   10.02.2014
                    Date of Judgment  :   17.11.2014


                                         A W A R D


1.</pre><p id="p_1">                  The Secretary (Labour), Government of NCT of Delhi 

vide   its   order   No.   F­24(670)/Lab./SD/2013/1256   dated   21.01.14 

referred an industrial dispute between the above mentioned parties 

to the Labour Court against the illegal or unjustified termination of 

workman. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.                  After   receiving   the   reference,   Ld.   AR   for   present 

workman/claimant has appeared for contesting the industrial but 

despite   given   repeated   opportunities   to   the   workman   none   has 

appeared   on   behalf   of   the   workman   for   filing   of   statement   of 

claim.   The   non   appearance   of   the   workman   shows   that 

workman/claimant   is   not   interested   in   pursuing   his   claim. 

Therefore, in view of the facts and circumstances, the workman 

remains with no dispute with the management. Hence, no dispute 

award is hereby passed. The reference is answered accordingly.  

</p><p id="p_3">3.                  Copy of the award be sent to the Secretary (Labour), 

Government of National Capital Territory of Delhi for necessary 


ID No. 828/14     Munna Kr. Vs. M/s Innocean Worldwide Communication Pvt. Ltd. &amp; Anr.     Page No. 2 out of  3 
 action. The award be also sent to server (www.delhicourts.nic.in). 

File be consigned to record room.
</p>
<p id="p_4">Announced in the Open Court 
on 17th November,  2014               
                                                (SANJEEV KUMAR)
                                        Presiding Officer, Labour Court,
                                             Karkardooma Courts, Delhi.
