THE STATION HOUSE OFFICER,

     THIS CRIMINAL PETITION IS FILED U/S.439
CR.P.C PRAYING TO RELEASE THE PETITIONER ON
BAIL IN S.C.NO.50/2012 (IS ARISING OUT OF CRIME
No.2/2012) OF KUSHALANAGAR POLICE STATION,
KUSHALANAGAR POLICE STATION, KUSHALANAGARA,
SOMWARPET TALUK, MADIKERI DISTRICT, NOW CASE
IS PENDING ON THE FILE OF THE FAST TRACK
COURT, MADIKERI, FOR THE OFFENCE PUNISHABLE
UNDER SECTIONS 354, 324, 292, 506, 376 &amp; 417 OF
<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>.


    THIS CRIMINAL PETITION COMING ON               FOR
ORDERS THIS DAY, THE COURT MADE                    THE
FOLLOWING:

                      ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">     On a complaint given by one Rajani, the

jurisdictional   police   registered   a    case    in

Cr.No.2/2012 for the offence punishable under

<a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_2">Sections 354</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/1704109/" id="a_4">292</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506</a>, <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_6">376</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/105503/" id="a_7">417</a> of IPC.

Investigation is completed and charge-sheet is filed

and the matter is pending before the Fast Track

Court, Kodagu District in S.C.No.50/2012.


</p><p id="p_2">     2.    The petitioner is in custody for the last

nine months. There are no circumstances warranting
                               3                Crl.P.3681/2012



the continuance of the petitioner in custody. Even

according to the complaint, it is revealed that the

relationship       between   the     petitioner       and      the

complainant is very old.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">     3.      For     the   reasons    stated      above,       the

following:
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                             ORDER

     The petition is hereby allowed. The petitioner is

enlarged on bail, subject to the following conditions:


</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">     i)      Petitioner shall execute a personal
             bond for a sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees
             Fifty   thousand     only)   with    a    local
             solvent surety for the like-sum to the
             satisfaction of the Trial Court.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">     ii)     The petitioner shall not leave the
             jurisdiction of the Court without prior
             permission from the Trial Court.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                             4             Crl.P.3681/2012



</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">      iii)   On all hearing dates, the petitioner
             shall be present before the Trial Court.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_5">      iv)    The petitioner shall not, in any manner,
             tamper with the prosecution witnesses.
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_6">      Ordered accordingly.
</blockquote>
