THE HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE R.MAHADEVAN

and M.P.No.1 of 2014

					       Vs
1.The District Collector
   Vellore.

2.District Revenue Officer,
   Collector Office Vellore - 9

3.Revenue Divisional Officer,
   Vellore, 

4.Tahsildar,   
   Ambur, Vellore District

5.Superintendent of police,
   Vellore,

6.Inspector of police,
   Ambur Town,
   Vellore District.                                                                .. Respondents		     	                                                                         
PRAYER : Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India, praying to issue a Writ of Mandamus, directing the respondents to follow due process of law and not to indulge in any illegal act of dispossession in respect of the property in House No.143, Old No.15, 16, New No.15, Devuchetty Street, Ambur Town.
			
			For Petitioner      : Mr.R.Subramanian
       				For Respondents  : Mr.J.Ramesh,
           						Additional Government Pleader    

 O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	The main prayer in this Writ Petition is to the effect that due process of law to be complied with for taking any action with regard to possession of the property in question. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2.After due notice, the learned Additional Government Pleader appearing for the respondents submitted that the petitioner will not be dispossessed and appropriate action by following due process of law will be taken. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3.Such statement made by the learned Additional Government Pleader is recorded and this Writ Petition stands disposed of. No costs. Consequently, connected Miscellaneous Petition is closed. 
</p>
