<pre id="pre_2">                                         DATED :       7th JUNE, 2019
ORAL ORDER:


</pre><p id="p_1">               Perused the order of this Court dated 22 nd July,

2016. The Division Bench of this Court granted four weeks

time as a last chance to file reply.                               Today, learned

A.G.P. submitted before this Court that parawise remarks

are received by the office of the Government Pleader. The

petitioners are before this Court with the prayer of

directions           to      extend    benefits    to        them         like        other

employees of Balgruhas in the State of Maharashtra, as

well as benefits and privileges enjoyed by the employees

of Children Health Society doing                  similar work.



</p><p id="p_2">2.             It     is      the   submission    of     Mr.       Patil,         learned

Counsel for the petitioners that there is a judicial

decision on the issue of entitlement and the said issue

is already decided.                   The petitioners are only seeking

extension of these benefits who are similarly situated

                                           (3)                                 2087.16wp

with those petitioners whose entitlements are admitted

and    orders         are      passed.   It    is   also   submission            of     Mr.

Patil, learned Counsel for the petitioners that the State

Government has taken a decision in view of the judgment

of this Court.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">3.              Considering the submission of Mr. Patil, learned

Counsel as well as the order of this Court dated 22nd

July, 2016, we expect an affidavit in reply on behalf of

responsible             officers     of       the   State       who       are        party

respondents to the petition, namely, respondent Nos. 2,3

and 4. We are surprised to see casual approach of the

State authorities in not filing the reply in this Court

for      considerable long period in spite of the order of

this Court dated 22nd July, 2016. We only hope that now

better sense would prevail over the authorities and grant

two weeks time, as a last chance to learned A.G.P. to

file reply.
</p>
