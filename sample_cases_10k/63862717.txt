<div class="doc_title">AO/464/2019 on 16 November, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">    AO No. 464 of 2019
    Hon'ble Ramesh Ranganathan, C.J.
    Hon'ble Alok Kumar Verma, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">             Sri Parikshit Saini, learned counsel for the
appellant.
</p><p id="p_2">           Admit.
</p><p id="p_3">           Sri Parikshit Saini, learned counsel for the
appellant, is permitted to take out notice on the respondents
by registered post acknowledgement due, and to file proof
of service within three weeks.
</p><p id="p_4">           The first respondent's son, now aged 9 years, has
been under the care of the appellant herein (the maternal
grand-father of the child) ever since 2013, i.e. for the past
six years.
</p><p id="p_5">           The effect of the order under appeal is that the
custody of the child is now required to be given to the first
respondent.
</p><p id="p_6">           We wish to ascertain from the child as to where
he wishes to stay pending disposal of this appeal.
</p><p id="p_7">           Post on 2nd December, 2019 in the daily cause
list, on which date the appellant herein shall produce the
child before this Court.
</p><p id="p_8">           In the meanwhile, the order under appeal, to the
extent the appellant herein was ordered to give custody of
the child to his natural father within 30 days, shall remain
stayed.
</p>
<p id="p_9">    (Alok Kumar Verma, J.)(Ramesh Ranganathan, C.J.)
         16.11.2019            16.11.2019
    Rathour
