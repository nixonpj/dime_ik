       THIS WRIT PETITION IS FILED UNDER <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">ARTICLE 227</a> OF
THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA PRAYING TO QUASH THE ORDER
DATED 26.07.2017 PASSED ON I.A.NO.6 IN O.S.NO.202/2015

       THIS WRIT PETITION COMING ON FOR PRELIMINARY

                                  -2-
                                              WP.No.36050/2017


                                ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">H.G.RAMESH, J. (Oral):
</p>
<p id="p_2">1.     Heard. This writ petition by the defendant is directed

against an interlocutory order dated 26.07.2017 whereby

the trial court has dismissed her application-I.A.No.6 filed in

the suit in O.S.No.202/2015 on the ground that it had

become infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_3">2.     The grievance of the petitioner/defendant in the

aforesaid application was that a document produced by the

respondent/plaintiff did not bear the proper stamp duty.

The trial court has observed that proper stamp duty and

penalty thereon was subsequently paid, and therefore, the

application-I.A.No.6 became infructuous.

</p><p id="p_4">3.     I find no error in the impugned order to warrant

interference.   The writ petition is accordingly dismissed.

However, this order will not come in the way of the

petitioner/defendant       to    make   any    other   appropriate

application in the suit.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       Petition dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                               Sd/-
</p><p id="p_7">                                              JUDGE
hkh.
