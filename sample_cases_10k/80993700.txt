<div class="doc_title">Firoz vs State on 19 December, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Vijay Bishnoi</div>

<p id="p_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                        JODHPUR
     S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 15706/2019

</p><p id="p_2">1.       Firoz S/o Meerbux Mansuri Musalman, Aged About 25
         Years, Guljar Nagar, Bhilwara, P.s. Bhimganj, Bhilwara.
         (At Present Lodged In District Jail Bhilwara).
</p><p id="p_3">2.       Saddam S/o Meerbux Mansuri Musalman, Aged About 24
         Years, Guljar Nagar, Bhilwara, P.s. Bhimganj, Bhilwara.
         (At Present Lodged In District Jail Bhilwara).
</p><p id="p_4">                                                                  ----Petitioners
                                    Versus
State, Through P.p.</p><pre id="pre_1">
                                                                 ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)         :     Mr. A.S. Rathore
For Respondent(s)         :     Mr. A.R. Choudhary, P.P.



             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

                          Judgment / Order

19/12/2019


</pre><p id="p_5">       Heard learned counsel for the petitioners as well as learned

Public Prosecutor and also perused the material on record.

</p><p id="p_6">       The petitioners have been arrested in FIR No.454/2019 of

Police Station Kotwali, District Bhilwara for the offences punishable

under <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_3">325</a>, <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_4">308</a> of IPC. They have preferred

this bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_7">       Learned counsel for the petitioners has submitted that after

rejection of the first bail application of the petitioners, charge-

sheet has been filed in the matter.
</p>
<p id="p_8">       Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.




<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                     (Downloaded on 19/12/2019 at 09:09:24 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_9">                                                                        (2 of 2)                     [CRLMB-15706/2019]


                                        Having regard to the totality of the facts and circumstances

                                   of the case, without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

                                   case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail to the accused

                                   petitioners under Section 439 Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_10">
</p><p id="p_11">                                        Accordingly, this bail application filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5">Section 439</a>

                                   Cr.P.C. is allowed and it is directed that petitioners Firoz S/o

                                   Meerbux Mansuri Musalman and Saddam S/o Meerbux Mansuri

                                   Musalman shall be released on bail in connection with FIR

                                   No.454/2019 of Police Station Kotwali, District Bhilwara provided

                                   each of them executes a personal bond in a sum of Rs.50,000/-

                                   with two sound and solvent sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the

                                   satisfaction of learned trial court for his appearance before that

                                   court on each and every date of hearing and whenever called upon

                                   to do so till the completion of the trial.



</p><p id="p_12">                                                                                             (VIJAY BISHNOI),J

                                    108-Taruna/-
</p>
