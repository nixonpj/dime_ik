                                             AT CHANDIGARH

                                                     CRM No. M-3114 of 2014.
                                                     Date of Decision : 31.03.2014.


              Shiv Kumar                                          ...Petitioner

                                                Versus

              G.S. Grover                                         ...Respondent


              CORAM :          HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE T.P.S. MANN.


              Present:         Mr. H.S. Saini, Advocate for
                               Mr. Onkar Pal, Advocate for the petitioner.

                               ***

              T.P.S. Mann, J. (Oral)

<p id="p_1">                               This order be read in continuation of earlier order

              passed by this Court on 13.02.2014.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               As per the office report, service upon the respondent

              stands effected through his brother's wife. Despite the same there

              is no representation on his behalf.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               Learned    counsel   for   the   petitioner   submits   that

              pursuant to the directions issued by this Court on 13.02.2014, the

              petitioner surrendered before the appellate Court and was

              admitted to ad-interim bail.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                               Heard.    The petition is allowed.    The petitioner shall

              remain on bail during the pendency of the appeal before the lower

              appellate Court subject to his furnishing fresh bail bonds to its

              satisfaction.</p><pre id="pre_2">



              March 31, 2014.                                     (T.P.S. MANN)
              kanchan                                                 JUDGE



Kanchan
