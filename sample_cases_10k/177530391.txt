<div class="doc_title">Saurabh Singh @ Saurabh Kumar vs The State Of Bihar on 10 July, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.42510 of 2019
                        Arising Out of PS. Case No.-339 Year-2018 Thana- RAMPUR District- Gaya
                 ======================================================
                 Saurabh Singh @ Saurabh Kumar Son Of Pramod Singh @ Pramod Ram
                 Resident of Village-Gewal Bigha, Painper, P.S-Rampur, District-Gaya.

                                                                                  ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr.Jagannath Prasad
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr.Binay Krishna(App245)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SUDHIR SINGH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   10-07-2019                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                           The petitioner seeks bail in a case registered under

                   <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_5">379</a>, <a href="/doc/222396/" id="a_6">427</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                         The prosecution case in short is that the accused persons

                   were assembled near the house of informant with ulterior

                   motive. While the informant objected them, they having deadly

                   weapons in their hands entered his house and assaulted the

                   informant badly, as a result of which, injury was sustained by

                   the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                         It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioner that the

                   petitioner has got no criminal antecedent. There is no allegation

                   of tampering of witnesses alleged against the petitioner. The

                   alegation of assault is against co-accused Vicky Kumar. There is
             Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.42510 of 2019(2) dt.10-07-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                      no allegation of assault against the petitioner nor any injury is

                      said to have been caused by him.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                            On behalf of the State, it is submitted that the petitioner is

                      named in the Complaint Case/F.I.R.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, let

                      the petitioner, above named, be released on bail on furnishing

                      bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two sureties of

                      the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned Exclusive

                      Special Judge, SC/ST, Gaya in connection with Rampur P.S.

                      Case No. 339 of 2018.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                                   (Sudhir Singh, J)
A.K.V.//-
</p>
