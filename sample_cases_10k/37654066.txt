<div class="doc_title">Babuanand Singh &amp; Anr vs The State Of Bihar on 6 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.14485 of 2018
                        Arising Out of PS. Case No.-131 Year-2017 Thana- PUNPUN District- Patna
               ======================================================
            1. Babuanand Singh,
            2. Bhushan Singh, Both Sons of Late Shivdhari Singh, Resident of
               Village Rajghat Nawada, P.S.- Pipara, District- Patna.

                                                                                   ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar.

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr. Pankaj Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr. Sri Parmanand Prasad
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ANIL KUMAR UPADHYAY
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   06-04-2018                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the

                  counsel appearing on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Petitioners are apprehending arrest in connection with

                  Punpun P.S. Case No. 131 of 2017 for the offence under

                  <a href="/doc/1667388/" id="a_1">sections 419</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_3">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_4">468</a> and <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_5">471</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal

                  Code.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               From the written complaint, it appears that Jamabandi

                  was created in favour of the petitioners and way back in 1968-

                  69 now in connection with the said land a Title Suit bearing no.

                  5323 of 2014 is pending before the Sub-Judge-III, Patna.

</p><p id="p_4">                               In the aforesaid backdrop of the fact, when the Title

                  Suit is pending the petitioners deserve anticipatory bail as the

                  petitioners have no criminal antecedent as mentioned in para-3
              Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.14485 of 2018(2) dt.06-04-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                          2/2</span>




                      of the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                   Considering the background of the instant case, let the

                      petitioners, named above, in the event of arrest or surrender

                      before the court below within a period of four weeks from the

                      date of receipt/production of a copy of this order, be enlarged on

                      bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) with

                      two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of

                      learned S.D.J.M., Patna in connection with Punpun P.S. Case

                      No. 131 of 2017 subject to the condition as laid down under

                      <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_7">section 438(2)</a> of the Cr.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                              (Anil Kumar Upadhyay, J)
Ravi/-
</p>
