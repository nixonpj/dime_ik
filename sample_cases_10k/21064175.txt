<p id="p_4">

                     It appears from the report of the Additional

           Stamp Report dated December 5, 2017 that the decree

           has not been filed and the appeal has been valued at

           Rs. 1,683/-. On perusal of the photocopy of the

           certified copy of the decree annexed to the application,

           it appears that the suit is valued at Rs. 38,00,100/-.

</p><p id="p_5">                     In view of such circumstances, the appellants

           are directed to take steps for removal of the defects

           pointed out by the Additional Stamp Reporter in his

           aforesaid report.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                     Matter to appear in the list fortnight hence with

           a revised report of the Additional Stamp Reporter.
