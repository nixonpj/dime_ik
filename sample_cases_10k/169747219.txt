                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

              CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR REGULARIZATION OF LATE
                                 SURRENDER) NO. 30270 of 2016
                In SPECIAL CRIMINAL APPLICATION NO. 6973 of 2016
         ===========================================================
         =====
                      JAYESH RAMJIBHAI PARMAR....Applicant(s)
                                     Versus
                       STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 2....Respondent(s)
         ================================================================
         Appearance:
         THROUGH JAIL for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MS. JIRGA JHAVERI, APP,for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         ================================================================
          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI

                                          Date : 15/11/2016
                                            ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">         1.     Rule. Learned APP waives service of notice of Rule on behalf of 
                the respondent­ State of Gujarat.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.     By   way   of   the   present   application,   the   applicant   has   prayed   to 
                regularize three days late surrender before the authority when he 
                was released on parole leave since his mother was sick. 
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.     Perused the jail record. His jail conduct found to be good. In past, 
                he   had  surrendered  in  time   when   he   was   released   on   parole   / 
                furlough leave or temporary bail.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.     Considering the above aspect, I am of the opinion that the present 
                application   requires   consideration   and   accordingly,   the   same   is 
                allowed. Late surrender of three days is hereby regularized and 
                the  same shall be treated as if he  was on parole leave.  Rule is 
                made absolute to the aforesaid extent. </p><pre id="pre_2">
