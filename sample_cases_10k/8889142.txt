<p id="p_1">          When this matter has been taken up for hearing, the

learned Public Prosecutor has submitted that the complaint/

final report will be filed after completing the investigation

within two months. The above submission of the learned

Public Prosecutor stands recorded. In view of the above

submission, it is directed that the final report/ complaint

shall be filed within the above said period of two months

before     the   court   concerned   after   completing   the

investigation as submitted by the learned Public Prosecutor.
</p>


<p id="p_2">          With the above direction, this Crl.M.C. stands

closed.
</p><p id="p_3"> Crl.MC.No. 7127 of 2018

       Needless to state that the petitioners shall co-

<p id="p_4">operate with the investigation.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       I make it clear that this order will not stand in the

way of the petitioners in resorting to the remedies available

to them in accordance with law, if they are aggrieved by

the complaint/ final report.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       Dated this the 13th day of December 2018.
