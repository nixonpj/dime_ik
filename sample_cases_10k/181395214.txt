<p id="p_1">GURVINDER SINGH GILL, J.(Oral)

             The petitioner has approached this Court seeking grant of

anticipatory bail in respect of a case registered vide FIR No.163 dated

29.6.2016 at Police Station Mahesh Nagar, District Ambala under <a href="/doc/119436650/" id="a_1">Section

174-A</a> of Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Vide order dated 21.12.2018, while issuing notice of motion,

the petitioner was directed to appear before the trial Court and trial Court

had been directed to release the petitioner on interim bail.

</p><p id="p_3">             The learned State counsel, upon instructions from ASI Kulvir

Singh, has informed that the petitioner has indeed appeared before the trial

Court pursuant to interim directions issued by this Court.

</p><p id="p_4">             In view of the aforestated position and bearing in mind the

nature of offence and that the petitioner has already caused appearance
