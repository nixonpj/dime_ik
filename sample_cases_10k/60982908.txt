<div class="doc_title">Surendra Kumar vs State Of Rajasthan on 24 July, 2019</div><p id="p_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                        JODHPUR
   S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 8706/2019

Surendra Kumar S/o Shri Balwant @ Balram, Aged About 26
Years, By Caste Jat, Resident Of Ward No. 7, Near New Indan
Gas Godam, Suratgarh, District Sriganganagar. (At Present
Lodged In Central Jail Bikaner).
</p><p id="p_2">                                                                     ----Petitioner
                                   Versus
State Of Rajasthan, Through P.p.
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                   ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)          :    Mr. Kuldeep Sharma
For Respondent(s)          :    Mr. Laxman Solanki, PP



             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

                           Judgment / Order

24/07/2019

       Heard learned counsel for the parties and perused the

mate
    The     petitioner(s)       has/have          been          arrested    in   FIR

No.108/2019 of Police Station Chatargarh, Distt. Bikaner for

the offence(s) punishable under Section(s) 8/15 of the <a href="/doc/1727139/" id="a_1">NDPS

Act</a>.    He/she/they        has/have          preferred           this/these      bail

application(s) under Section 439 Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_4">       Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that in

this matter, narcotic contraband i.e. poppy straw weighing 13

kgs alleged to have been recovered from the petitioner is

below commercial quantity.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       Learned    Public       Prosecutor        has       opposed         the   bail

application(s).
</p>
<p id="p_6"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    (Downloaded on 01/09/2019 at 11:54:25 PM)</span>
</p><pre id="pre_1">                                                                        (2 of 2)                    [CRLMB-8706/2019]


                                        Having   regard       to    the      totality       of    the    facts     and

</pre><p id="p_7">                                   circumstances of the case, without expressing any opinion on

                                   the merits of the case, I deem it just and proper to grant bail

                                   to the petitioner(s) under Section 439 Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_8">                                        Accordingly, this/these bail application(s) filed under

                                   <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_2">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is/are allowed and it is directed that

                                   petitioner(s) - Surendra Kumar S/o Shri Balwant @ Balram shall

                                   be released on bail in connection with FIR No.108/2019 of

                                   Police   Station     Chatargarh,            Distt.        Bikaner       provided

                                   he/she/they execute(s) a personal bond in the sum of

                                   Rs.50,000/-   with     two      sound          and     solvent       sureties    of

                                   Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of learned trial court for

                                   his/her/their appearance before that court on each and every

                                   date of hearing and whenever called upon to do so till the

                                   completion of the trial.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                                                                           (VIJAY BISHNOI),J

                                    113-msrathore/-
</p>
