<p id="p_2">     Learned Counsel appearing for the petitioner, after

arguing the matter for sometime, submits that the writ

petition may be disposed of by directing the Assistant

Commissioner, Kolar Sub-Division, Kolar, to dispose of the

proceeding in RRT (T):CR/107/2009-10 expeditiously.



</p><p id="p_3">2.   Learned Counsel appearing for respondent Nos.6 and

7 submits that the Assistant Commissioner has not disposed
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             -3-</span>
                                          WP No.45395/2012



of the proceeding till today. Accordingly, the Assistant

Commissioner is directed to dispose of the proceeding

expeditiously and in any event before the end of October

2014.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">3.      Learned Counsel on both sides submit that their

clients will appear before the Assistant Commissioner, Kolar

Sub-Division, Kolar, on 02.09.2014. Their submission is

placed on record. In view of this, no notice need be issued

to the petitioner and respondent Nos.6 and 7. The writ

petition stands disposed of in terms stated above.
