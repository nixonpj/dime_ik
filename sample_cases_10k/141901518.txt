                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.56082 of 2015
                   Arising Out of PS.Case No. -137 Year- 2015 Thana -PATNA CITY CHOWK District- PATNA
                 ======================================================
                 1. Guddan @ Sahil aged about 24 Years S/o Satendra Prasad A/P R/o Kali
                    Sthan, Renter in the house of Umesh Yadav,P.s Chowk, District Patna.,
                    Permanent Residence : Ashok Nagar, Road, No. 8A, P.s Kankerbag,
                    District Patna.

                                                                               .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                            .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     : Mr. Kritu Verma
                 For the Opposite Party/s : Mr. U.L.Verma(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR SINGH
                                                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   10-12-2015                        Petitioner is apprehending his arrest in a case

                 registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/796352/" id="a_1">Sections 366(A)</a> of

                 the Indian Penal Code.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                      The accusation is of kidnapping the minor

                 daughter of the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                      It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioner that the victim in her 164 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_2">Cr.P.C</a> statement got her

                 age recorded as 18 years when Court assessed as 16 years,

                 whereas she stated that she was in love with the petitioner and

                 she performed marriage in the Court.

</p><p id="p_4">                                      Considering the age of the victim approaching

                 majority and her statement under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_3">Section 164</a> Cr.P.C, it is a

                 case for consideration of prayer for regular bail, if the petitioner

                 surrenders within a period of six weeks in connection with
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.56082 of 2015 (2) dt.10-12-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                       2/2</span>




                         Chowk P.S. Case No. 137 of 2015, pending in the Court of

                         learned Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class, Patna City, Patna.

</p><p id="p_5">                                               With the observations above, the application

                         stands disposed off.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                                                           (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
