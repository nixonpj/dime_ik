                                       I.A. No. 519 of 2018

06.   22.06.2018            This is an application for appropriate order.

                         Mr. Pradeepta Kumar Behera and his associate,
                   learned counsels file Vakalatnama of the complainant-
                   Jyoshna Haldar as well as her affidavit which are taken on
                   record.
                         The deponent has mentioned in paragraph 4 of the
                   affidavit, "the deponent/complainant has already received
                   the deposit amount as alleged against the petitioners in this
                   case".
                         Copy of the affidavit has been served on the learned
                   counsel for the State.
                         Learned counsel for the State shall take specific
                   instruction regarding the truthfulness of the averments
                   taken and when the deposit amount was received by the
                   complainant and in what way and whether there is any
                   documentary evidence in that respect.
                            List this matter on 03.07.2018.
skb


                         A free copy of the order be handed over to the
                   learned counsel for the State.


                                                                 .......................
                                                                 S. K. Sahoo, J.
