 application for condonation of delay, we would pass directions for

 exchange of Affidavits and adjourn the matter for a period of one week.

</p><p id="p_2">           After preliminary hearing of this application, we are of the view

    the date of appearance of respondent no.1 is fixed before the Hon'ble

    Trial Court on 27.1.2011, we would stay the execution of the

    impugned order under appeal till the returnable date of filing of

    Affidavits.
</p>
