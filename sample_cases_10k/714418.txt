                               O R D E R

<p id="p_1">      This application is filed for seeking an order directing Special

Judge -I for CBI Cases at Ernakulam to release the 2nd accused in

C.C. No. 7/2008 on bail on his surrender. I do not want to go to the

merits of the case at this stage. But it is sufficient if a direction is

given to the effect that if the petitioner herein surrenders before the

court and moves an application, the Special Judge -I, CBI Cases

shall consider the same on the same date itself and pass

appropriate orders on his bail application. I make it very clear that

the petitioner has to surrender and move the application for bail

sufficiently on the early hours of the working day so as to give

breathing time to the Judge to give notice, hear and pass

appropriate orders.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      The Crl.M.C is disposed of accordingly.
</p>
