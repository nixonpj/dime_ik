</p><p id="p_2">       2. The net tax effect of Rs.2,40,746/- which is

the subject matter of this appeal, is less than

Rs.4,00,000/-      as    prescribed      under     Instruction

No.2/2005. Hence, the appeal is not maintainable.


</p><p id="p_3">       3. Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed on the

ground       that the    net tax effect is         less    than

Rs.4,00,000/-, without pronouncing any opinion on

the merits of the case.
