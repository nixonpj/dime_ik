5   13-07-2012                 Though served, the respondent has not entered

</p><p id="p_2">                               Re. Interlocutory Application No. 3401 of 2012:
                               This application under <a href="/doc/100581/" id="a_1">section 5</a> of the Limitation Act
                 is filed by the State of Bihar and Others for condonation of delay
                 of two years and 103 days occurred in filing the Letters Patent
                 Appeal.
</p><p id="p_3">                               In spite of the considerable delay and there is hardly
                 explanation, we allow this application on the sole ground that in
                 related matters the appeals are admitted and are pending for
                 hearing.
</p><p id="p_4">                               The delay is condoned.
</p><p id="p_5">                               Interlocutory Application stands disposed of.
