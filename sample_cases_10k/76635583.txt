<div class="doc_title">Devkishan Gurjar vs State on 3 November, 2017</div><p id="p_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                        JODHPUR
              S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail No. 9672 / 2017
Devkishan Gurjar Son of Shri Jethmal Ji, By Caste Gurjar, Resident
of Nai Abadi, Kankroli, District Rajsamand.
(Lodged in Central Jail, Udaipur)
</p><p id="p_2">                                                         ----Petitioner
                               Versus
State of Rajasthan
</p><p id="p_3">                                                     ----Respondent
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)    : Mr. S.K. Verma.
For Respondent(s) : Mr. K.V. Vyas, PP.
_____________________________________________________
                HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE P.K. LOHRA
                                Order
03/11/2017

       Instant bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is filed by

petitioner arising out of FIR No.288/2017 registered with ACB

Outpost Rajsamand for offences under <a href="/doc/324254/" id="a_2">Section 7</a>, <a href="/doc/1101716/" id="a_3">13(1)(d)</a> and

<a href="/doc/1259316/" id="a_4">13(2)</a> of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">Section 120-B</a>

IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that he

has been falsely implicated in the matter.     Learned counsel has

submitted that neither there was any demand by petitioner, nor

any recovery of tainted money is made from him.           With these

pleas, learned counsel has urged that the only offence against

petitioner is under <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_6">Section 120-B</a> IPC, therefore, he may be

enlarged on bail.
</p><p id="p_5">                                 (2 of 2)
                                                       [CRLMB-9672/2017]

       Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail plea of

petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       I have learned counsel for the petitioner, learned Public

Prosecutor and perused the case diary.

</p><p id="p_7">       Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case,

without expressing any opinion on merits of the case, I feel

persuaded to enlarge the petitioner on bail.

</p><p id="p_8">       Accordingly, the bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_7">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is

allowed and it is ordered that accused-petitioner, Devkishan Gurjar

S/o Shri Jethmal Ji, arrested in connection with FIR No.288/2017

registered with ACB Outpost Rajsamand, may be released on bail;

provided he furnishes a personal bond of Rs.50,000/- with two

surety bonds of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of       learned

trial Court with the stipulation to appear before that Court on all

dates of hearing and as and when called upon to do so.




</p><p id="p_9">                                                    (P.K. LOHRA)J.
</p>
