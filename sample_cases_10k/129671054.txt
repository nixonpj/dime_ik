<div class="doc_title">Arbind Kumar vs State Of Bihar on 14 January, 2010</div><pre id="pre_1">                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              Cr.Misc. No.39404 of 2009
                       ABHIMANYU KUMAR @ MUKESH KUMAR .
                                           Versus
                                    STATE OF BIHAR .
                                             with
                              Cr.Misc. No.37552 of 2009
              1. ARBIND KUMAR S/O BALESHWAR PRASAD
                R/OVILL NARSINGHPUR, P.S. BARBIGHA,
                 DISTT- SHEKHPURA
                                           Versus
                               1. THE STATE OF BIHAR
                                         -----------
</pre>

<p id="p_1">3/    14/01/2010              Heard learned counsel for the petitioners

                   and learned counsel for the State of Bihar.

</p><p id="p_2">                              The defence of the petitioners in a police

                   case under Sections- 409, 419, 420, 422, 423, 467,

                   468, 471, 474 and 478<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of      Indian Penal Code</a> that

                   notwithstanding the allegations, subsequently, the

                   entire construction work had been completed by them

                   and now there existed a dispute with regard to

                   approximately Rs.6,00,000/- of the bill only, are

                   matters to be more appropriately considered in any

                   application for regular bail, without being prejudiced

                   by the present order.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                              The applications are rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><pre id="pre_2">KC/                                                    ( Navin Sinha, J.)
