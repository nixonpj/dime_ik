<div class="doc_title">K K Tripati vs State Of Gujarat &amp; on 27 September, 2006</div><pre id="pre_1">SCA/20047/2006               1/3                        ORDER




       IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

     SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION No. 20047 of 2006


=================================================
            K K TRIPATI - Petitioner(s)
                       Versus
       STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 1 - Respondent(s)
=================================================
Appearance :
MR BP GUPTA for Petitioner(s) : 1,
Mr. L.B. Dabhi, AGP for Respondent(s) : 1,
None for Respondent(s) : 2,
=================================================
          CORAM : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE H.K.RATHOD


                    Date : 27/09/2006




ORAL ORDER</pre>




<p id="p_1">   1.Against        the    order   of        recovery    of

       Rs.1,40,862.36 from the amount of gratuity

       passed by the State Government, petitioner

       approached    the   Tribunal.    In   Miscellaneous

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">       Application NO. 11 of 2005 in Appeal NO. 178</span>

       of 2002, Tribunal passed following order on

       8.9.2006:
</p><pre id="pre_2"> SCA/20047/2006                          2/3                                ORDER




       "Opponent          No.1    had         passed      an    order    dated

</pre><p id="p_2">       18.5.2002 ordering recovery of Rs.1,40,862.36

       ps. From the amount of                       gratuity payable to

       the       applicant       Shri         Tripathi      as     retirement

       benefits. This order had been quashed by this

       Tribunal's order dated 13th October 2004. It

       was       however       stated     in        our    order    that   the

       respondent (i.e. The present opponent no.1)

       did       not    have     the     auhtority          to     order   any

       recovery from a retired government servant

       and such auhtority rested only with the G

       overnment of Gujarat. The amount of recovery

       withheld from the gratuity has however not

       been paid to the applicant so far.

       At the time of hearing the representative of

       the       opponent      has     presented           an    order   dated

       29thMay          2006    passed         by    the    Government      of

       Gujarat ordering recovery of a like amount

       from the gratuity payable to the applicant.

       In        view    of     that      order,          the    applicant's

       application for payment of this amount to him

       by opponent no.1 i.e. Director, Employment
 SCA/20047/2006                        3/3                        ORDER




       and Training does not survive.                   Further we

       have no jurisdiction to look into merits of

       an order passed by the Government of Gujarat

       as this jurisdiction does not rest in this

       Tribunal.             In the result, we are unable to

       grant the relief claimed by the applicant. We

       therefore pass the following order

                        ORDER

          The application is dismissed."




</p><p id="p_3">   2.In          view   of    these   facts,     tribunal   rejected

       application.
</p>
<p id="p_4">   3.Therefore,               issue     notice     returnable     on

       7.11.2006.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                        (H.K. Rathod,J.)

Vyas
