<div class="doc_title">Subramanian vs Sundarambal on 1 June, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">       

  

   

 
 
  
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

DATED : 01.06.2015

CORAM

	  THE HONOURABLE THIRU JUSTICE M. DURAISWAMY
				
C.R.P.(PD)No.1590 of 2015 


1.Subramanian	
2.Selvan				                                             ... Petitioners

  

v.



1.Sundarambal
2.Ramamurthi 					                      ... Respondents

 


	 Civil Revision Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1331149/" id="a_1">Article 227</a> of the Constitution of India,  to set aside the fair and decreetal order dated 27th January, 2015, passed in I.A.No.1357 of 2014 in O.S.No.297 of 2005 on the file of the District Munsif of Attur, and allow this revision petition.


		For Petitioner       :  Mr.R.Aravind 





O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	   Aggrieved over the fair and final  order passed in I.A.No.1357 of 2014 in O.S.No.297 of 2005 on the file of District Munsif Court, Attur,  the plaintiffs have filed the  above   Civil Revision Petition.
</p> 
<p id="p_2"> 	2.  The  plaintiffs filed the  suit  in  O.S.No.297 of 2005 for permanent injunction.   The said suit is being contested by the defendants on various grounds.  When the suit was taken up for trial, the plaintiffs filed the present application  in I.A.No.1357 of 2014 seeking for appointment of an Advocate Commissioner  to note down the physical features.     The said suit is pending from the year 2005 and after a lapse of 9 years, the plaintiffs have filed the present application  for appointment of an Advocate Commissioner that too, after the commencement of the trial.    
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. The only issue that has to be decided in a suit for permanent injunction is with regard to the  possession of  the parties.   When the suit is pending for more than 9 years, the plaintiffs have not given any reason for filing an application at a belated stage.  The case of the plaintiffs can be decided only  by oral and documentary evidences.  The Advocate Commissioner cannot collect evidence on behalf of the parties.   
</p>	

<p id="p_4">	4.   In these circumstances,  the trial court has rightly dismissed the application.  I do  not find any error or irregularity in the order  passed by the trial court.  The   Civil Revision  Petition is devoid of merits and is liable to be dismissed.  Accordingly, the   Civil Revision petition  is   dismissed.    No costs. </p><pre id="pre_2">
 
01.06.2015
Index     :   No
Internet :   Yes

Rj

 
To

The   District Munsif Court, 
Attur,   












 M. DURAISWAMY,J.,
Rj
