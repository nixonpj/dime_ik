<div class="doc_title">Devendra Rawat vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 9 July, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">M.Cr.C.No.5303/2014            Devendra Rawat Vs. State of M.P.


09.07.2014
      Smt. Uma Kushwah, Advocate for the applicant.
      Shri J.M. Sahni, PL for the respondent/ State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard on the bail application.
</p><p id="p_2">      Perused the case diary
      This is second application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C.
      First   application   was   dismissed    vide    order   dated
18.06.2014 passed in M.Cr.C.No.4900/2014.
</p><p id="p_3">      The     applicant has been arrested in Crime No.53/2014
registered at Police Station, AJAK, District Datia, for the offences
punishable under <a href="/doc/203036/" id="a_2">Sections 354</a>, <a href="/doc/594493/" id="a_3">294</a> and <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">506-B</a> of IPC and 3(1)(x)
of SC/<a href="/doc/53524/" id="a_5">ST Act</a>.
</p><p id="p_4">      As per the prosecution case, complainant was suffering
from abdominal pain. She went to take medicine from the
applicant. Applicant has given some tablet and asked her to
come again after one hour to give an injection. When
complainant again visited the room of applicant after one hour.
Applicant asked to lay on bed and after giving the injection
applicant caught hold her hand and pressed her breast when
complainant objected buttons of her blouse got broken and
bangles were also broken. When she raised alarm then applicant
threatened her by giving abuses in the name of her caste.
</p><p id="p_5">      It is submitted by learned counsel for the applicant that
applicant has falsely been implicated. He has not committed any
offence. He is in custody since 01.06.2014. Enmity of the
applicant was going with Vishnu Mahesh and in the report lodged
        M.Cr.C.No.5303/2014            Devendra Rawat Vs. State of M.P.
</p>

<p id="p_6">       by the complainant Vishnu Mahesh has been named as eye-
       witness of the incident. Charge-sheet has been filed. Therefore,
       the applicant be released on bail.
</p><p id="p_7">              Learned counsel for the State submits that no new ground
       is made out for grant of bail and charge-sheet has not been filed
       so far, hence, prayed for rejection.
</p><p id="p_8">              It is true that Vishnu Mahesh has been named as an eye-
       witness of the incident but he is not a relative or friend of the
       complainant. No new ground is made out after dismissal of the
       first bail application, hence, applicant is not entitled to get the
       benefit of bail.
</p><p id="p_9">              Accordingly, it is hereby rejected.
</p>


<p id="p_10">                                                          (D.K.Paliwal)
                                                             Judge
bj/-
</p>
