<div class="doc_title">Muskan Varsheny And 3 Others vs State Of U.P. And 3 Others on 7 November, 2019</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: Naheed Ara Moonis, Anil Kumar-Ix</div>

<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 46
 

 
Case :- CRIMINAL MISC. WRIT PETITION No. - 23350 of 2019
 

 
Petitioner :- Muskan Varsheny And 3 Others
 
Respondent :- State Of U.P. And 3 Others
 
Counsel for Petitioner :- Arvind Kumar
 
Counsel for Respondent :- G.A.
 

 
Hon'ble Naheed Ara Moonis,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">Hon'ble Anil Kumar-IX,J.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and the learned A.G.A. for the State. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">By means of the present writ petition, the petitioners have invoked extraordinary jurisdiction of this Court with a prayer to quash the FIR dated 18.8.2019 lodged at Case Crime No. 476 of 2019, under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_3">506</a>, <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_4">392</a> of IPC, police station Civil Lines, district Aligarh
 
Learned counsel for the petitioners submitted that the impugned first information report has been lodged by respondent No.4 as a counter blast to the first information report lodged by Petitioner No. 1, Muskan Varsheny against respondent No. 4 and other family members under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_5">Sections 498-A</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_6">323</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_7">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_8">506</a> IPC and 3/4 of <a href="/doc/1763444/" id="a_9">Dowry Prohibition Act</a>, police station Delhi Gate, district Aligarh on 06.7.2019. He further submitted that apart from the bald allegations made in the impugned F.I.R., no evidence is forthcoming even prima facie indicating at the complicity of the petitioners in the commission of the alleged crime and hence, the impugned F.I.R. is liable to be quashed.
</p> 
<p id="p_4">Per contra, learned A.G.A. contended that the allegations made in the first information report cannot be aborted at this stage. The petitioners will have sufficient opportunity to rebut the allegations.
</p> 
<p id="p_5">From perusal of the F.I.R., prima facie cognizable offences is made out at this stage against the petitioners, therefore, we do not find any cogent reason to quash the first information report. The prayer for quashing the first information report is refused. 
</p> 
<p id="p_6">However, in the facts and circumstances of the case and the submissions advanced on behalf of the petitioners, it is directed that the petitioners shall not be arrested in the aforesaid case till the submission of police report under <a href="/doc/1763444/" id="a_10">Section 173</a> (2) <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_11">Cr.P.C</a>., subject to the restraint that they shall co-operate with the investigation and shall appear as and when called upon to assist in the investigation. 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">With the above direction, this petition is finally disposed of. 
</p> 
<p id="p_8">Order Date :- 7.11.2019
 
Ishrat
