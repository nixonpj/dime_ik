  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

WP(C) No. 1326 of 2007(G)


1. K.ANILKUMAR,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE DEPUTY DIRECTOR OF PANCHAYATHS,
                       ...       Respondent

2. THE DIRECTOR OF PANCHAYATHS,

3. STATE OF KERALA, REPRESENTED BY

                For Petitioner  :SRI.M.SASINDRAN

                For Respondent  : No Appearance

The Hon'ble MR. Justice K.K.DENESAN

 Dated :25/01/2007

 O R D E R
                        K.K. DENESAN, J.



                = = = = = = = = = = = = = = =

                  W.P.(C) No. 1326 OF 2007 G

                = = = = = = = = = = = = = = =



              Dated this the 25th January, 2007



                         J U D G M E N T
</pre>


<p id="p_1">     The   petitioner   is   working   as   Upper   Division   Clerk



presently   in   Kalikavu   Grama   Panchayat   in   Malappuram



District.   He is a native of Kollam District and wants



to get inter-district transfer to that district.  It is



stated   that   he   is   the   seniormost   person   among   the



applicants who desire to get inter-district transfer to



Kollam.     It   is   contended   that   the   applicants   who   are



juniors   to   him   have   already   been   favoured   with   inter-
</p>


<p id="p_2">district   transfer   orders,   but   the   petitioner's   claim



has   not  been  properly  considered.    Ext.  P4  application



is pending with the 2nd  respondent who is the authority



competent to consider the same.
</p>


<p id="p_3">     2.   This   writ   petition   was   posted   to   enable   the



Govt.   Pleader   to   seek   instructions   from   the   2nd



respondent.     It   appears   that   Ext.   P4   has   not   been



considered or  disposed of.
</p>


<p id="p_4">     3.   In   the   above   circumstances,   it   is   only



appropriate   that   the   2nd      respondent   considers   the



request   of   the   petitioner   for   inter-district   transfer


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">WPC No. 1326/2007                   -2-</span>


as   prayed   for   in   Ext.   P4,   particularly   against   any   of



the   existing   vacancies   or   any   other   vacancy   which   is



likely to arise in the near future in Kollam District.
</p>


<p id="p_5">Needless to state that the 2nd respondent shall consider



applications   filed   by   other   Upper   Division   Clerks   also



along with Ext. P4 and decision shall be taken on Ext.
</p>


<p id="p_6">P4   in   accordance   with   the   principles   laid   down   by   the



Government   in   the   matter   of   inter-district   transfers.
</p>


<p id="p_7">Orders shall be passed on Ext. P4 within six weeks from



the date of receipt of a copy of the judgment.
</p>


<p id="p_8">        The   petitioner   shall     produce   a   copy   of   the   writ



petition   also   before   the   2nd          respondent   so   that



effective orders can be passed on Ext. P4.
</p>




<p id="p_9">                                                     K.K. DENESAN

                                                         JUDGE

jan/


</p>
