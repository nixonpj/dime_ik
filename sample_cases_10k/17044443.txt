<p id="p_5">

             Heard the learned counsel for the respective parties.

             On perusal of the impugned order, I find that the learned Trial Court mainly relied on the

   principle that a liberal approach should be adopted for condonation of delay. He has done the

   same thing but one point that has been lost sight of the learned Court that the frugal amount of

   Rs.500/- is not enough for condoning the delay in such case.

</p><p id="p_6">             Therefore, the present opposite party is hereby directed to pay a sum of Rs.5,000/- as cost

   instead of Rs.500/-. If he has already paid the said amount of Rs.500/- in that case the same may

   be adjusted. He is directed to pay the said amount directly to the opposite party by name by way

   of a banker's cheque.
</p>
<p id="p_7">             With this direction, this revisional application stands dispose of.

             Urgent, photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be given to the parties.


</p><p id="p_8">                                (Siddhartha Chattopadhyay, J.)
