                            ORDER
</pre>


<p id="p_1">    Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_2">2. The petitioner was the second accused in SC

    No. 661 of 2015 on the file of the Additional

    Sessions      Judge-III,     Kozhikode.      The     accused

    allegedly        committed       the     offences        under

    <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_2">Sections 308</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_3">324</a> &amp; <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_4">506</a> of the Indian Penal

    Code. As the petitioner was absconding, only

    the    others    were   tried.    They    were     acquitted

    under <a href="/doc/19163/" id="a_5">Section 232</a> Cr.P.c. The case against the

    petitioner,       who   surrendered      later,     is    now
 Crl.MC.No. 1490 of 2018

                                  ..3..
</p>
<p id="p_3">    pending as SC No. 944 of 2016. Respondents 3 &amp;

    4 are the victims. It is submitted that the

    matter      has       been    settled.     The    proceedings

    against him are sought to be quashed in the

    light of the acquittal of the co-accused and

    settlement.
</p>
<p id="p_4">3. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner

    and the learned Public Prosecutor.

</p><p id="p_5">4. I have perused Annexure C judgment by which

    the     co-accused           were     acquitted     and   the

    affidavits filed by respondents 3 &amp; 4, who

    have entered appearance through counsel. I am

    satisfied that the matter has been settled and

    no purpose will be served by the trial of the

    petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_6">    In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed. The

    proceedings in SC No. 944 of 2016 on the file

    of     the        Additional          Sessions     Judge-III,

    Kozhikode are quashed. The learned magistrate

    may pass appropriate orders for the disposal
 Crl.MC.No. 1490 of 2018

                                 ..4..
</p>
<p id="p_7">     of the material objects, if any, produced in

     the case.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                               Sd/-
