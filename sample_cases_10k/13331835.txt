<p id="p_1">                                              On 30-09-2019, the appellants were permitted to file applications for
                                      amendment of the appeals and they were also directed to examine the
                                      affidavits filed by the respondents to the effect that they have not been
                                      gainfully employed and thereafter take necessary steps for complying with the
                                      provisions of Section 17(b) and submit a report in this regard.
</p><p id="p_2">                                              When the matter is taken up today, we find that the report as well as
                                      the order dated 30-09-2019 has not been complied with.
</p><p id="p_3">                                              By way of last indulgence, one opportunity is granted to the appellants
                                      to comply with the order dated 14-02-2007 as well as the order dated 30-09-
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                      2019. In case of non-compliance, the court would be constrained to dismiss
                                      the appeal for non-compliance of the said order.
</p><p id="p_5">                                              In the meanwhile, the record of the labour court be also sent for
                                      posthaste.
</p><p id="p_6">                                              List this case in the week commencing 16-12-2019.
</p>
