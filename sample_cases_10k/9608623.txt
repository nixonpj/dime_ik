                                                        Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                                 .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Dilip Kumar Roy

                 ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">3   14-10-2015              Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.
</p><p id="p_2">                            The petitioner seeks bail in a case instituted under
                 Sections 25 (1-B) A and 26 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_1">Arms Act</a>.
</p><p id="p_3">                            Allegation against the petitioner is that from his
                 possession one country made pistol and one live cartridge were
                 recovered.
</p><p id="p_4">                            It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioner that he
                 has got no criminal antecedent. He is in custody since 26.5.2015.
                 Charge sheet has been submitted in the case.                               There is no
                 allegation of tampering of evidence against the petitioner. As per
                 prosecution case, one country made pistol and one live cartridge
                 are said to have been recovered from his possession. The said
                 recovery is denied by the petitioner.
</p><p id="p_5">                            On behalf of the State, it is submitted that the petitioner is
                 named in the F.I.R.
</p><p id="p_6">                            Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, let the
                 above named petitioner be released on bail on furnishing bail bond
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.40417 of 2015 (3) dt.14-10-2015</span>


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                 2/2</span>




                   of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand only) with two sureties of the like
                   amount each to the satisfaction of Sri Nitin Kaushik, learned
                   Judicial Magistrate, Ist Class, Samastipur, in connection with
                   Tajpur P.S. Case no. 156 of 2015.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                                   (Sudhir Singh, J)

sudip/-
