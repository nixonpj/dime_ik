</pre><p id="p_1">	An application has been filed to dispose of writ petitions in the light of judgment of the Division Bench dated 08.01.2015 given in bunch of special appeals led by Mohan Lal Kadwasra Vs. Raj. State Road Transport Corp. &amp; Ors., D.B. Civil Special Appeal No.793/2014. The issue raised herein against the order of termination was considered and decided favourable to the petitioners. It is also stated that after the judgment of the Division Bench, even the Single Bench had also decided the writ petition bearing No.3947/2014 on 12th February, 2015 by covering the petition by the judgment of the Division Bench. 
</p><p id="p_2">	Learned counsel for non-petitioners could not contest the fact aforesaid. 
</p><p id="p_3">	In view of above, these writ petitions are disposed of and ordered to be governed by the judgment in the case of Mohan Lal Kadwasra (supra).  
</p><p id="p_4">						            [M.N.BHANDARI], J.
