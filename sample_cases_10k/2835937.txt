COMMON JUDGMENT

(Judgment of the Court was delivered by M.M.SUNDRESH, J.)

	These writ appeals are directed against the order  dated 01.07.2010 made in M.P.Nos.2, 2 and 2 of 2010 in W.P.Nos.13916, 13915 and 13917 of 2010, whereby the learned single Judge dismissed the miscellaneous petitions.

	2.On a perusal of the relief sought for, it appears that the direct recruitment for the year 2010 has been over long time back. Therefore, nothing survives for adjudication in these writ appeals and the writ appeals are dismissed as infructuous. No costs.  Consequently, connected miscellaneous petitions are closed.
