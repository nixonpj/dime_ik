<div class="doc_title">Ram Milan @ Mullu Sahu vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 3 December, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                           CRR-1024-2008
          (RAM MILAN @ MULLU SAHU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


03-12-2015

Shri A.D. Mishra, counsel for the applicants.
Shri B.P. Pandey, Public Prosecutor for the respondent No. 1/</pre>
<p id="p_1">State.
</p><p id="p_2">The applicant No.3 Babulal @ Shrangar Singh Baheliya is not
present in person. No report has been received about the
service of perpetual arrest warrant issued against him. Also
notice issued to the surety of the respondent No. 3 is
returned back without any note, though it was mentioned in
the order dated 11.08.2015 issued by S.P., Umariya that
service be done to the surety, as well as applicant No. 3.
S.P., Umariya, is directed to submit his comments as to why
the notice issued to the surety is returned back without any
service report and delinquent service officer be properly
punished.
</p><p id="p_3">Copy of the order be provided to the learned Public
Prosecutor so that it may be communicated to S.P., Umariya.
Let fresh warrant of arrest be issued against the applicant
No.3 Babulal @ Shrangar Singh Baheliya to secure his
presence before this Court on the next date of hearing.
</p><p id="p_4">     Name of the surety be mentioned on the warrant so that
Serving Officer may take assistance of the surety.
</p><p id="p_5">     Let a fresh show cause notice be issued to the surety as to
why his bail bonds may not be forfeited and recovered. He be
directed to remain present before this Court on next date of
hearing and to submit a reply to the show cause notice.
</p><p id="p_6">     Case be listed for appearance of applicant No. 3 and the
 surety on 22/02/2016 .
</p>
<p id="p_7">                         (N.K. GUPTA)
                            JUDGE
