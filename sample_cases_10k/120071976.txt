<p id="p_1">	Having
heard the learned counsel for the applicant and learned APP and
considering the overall facts and circumstances of the case and the
apprehension of the applicant, though vehemently denied by the
learned APP, I deem it just and proper to direct that the
investigation of C.R. No.I-56 of 2011 and C.R. No.I-57 of 2011
registered with Himmatngar Rural Police Station be carried out by the
Circle Police Inspector of the concerned police station.
</p> 

<p id="p_2">	With
the aforesaid direction, this application is disposed of.
