                                     Date of decision: 21.03.2017

Anjali                                                        .....Petitioner
                            versus
Haryana Urban Development Authority and others
                                                              ......Respondents

CORAM: Hon'ble Mr.Justice Kuldip Singh

Present:     None for the petitioner
             Ms.Bhupinder Kaur, Advocate for the respondents

1. Whether Reporters of Local Newspapers may be allowed to see the

Kuldip Singh, J. (Oral)

<p id="p_1">             Written statement on behalf of the respondents filed in Court is

taken on record. From perusal of the same, it comes out that Family Pension

Payment Order No.1781 dated 28.2.2017 for pensionary benefits has been

sanctioned subject to condition for submission of requisite documents i.e.

self photographs, copy of bank pass book, copy of Aadhar Card, copy of

death certificate and affidavits regarding the fact that the Petitioner is neither

remarried nor shall remarry in future and neither drawing medical from any

other department nor is in the service in any department.

</p><p id="p_2">             In view of the matter, the present petition has become

infructuous and is disposed of accordingly. The petitioner shall supply all

the requisite documents to the respondents.
</p>
