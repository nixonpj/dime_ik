Ram Milan                                              ....Petitioner.

                          Versus

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SHEKHER DHAWAN

Present:    Mr. Jasdev Singh Mehndiratta, Advocate (Legal Aid Counsel)
            for the petitioner.

            Mr. Ramandeep Sandhu, Senior Deputy Advocate General,

Shekher Dhawan, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">            Present revision petition under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 401</a> of the Criminal

Procedure Code is directed against the judgment 04.03.2013 passed by

learned Additional Sessions Judge (Fast Track Court), Patiala, whereby the

appeal, filed by the petitioner, seeking enhancement of the sentence

awarded to respondents No.2 and 3-accused, vide judgment of conviction

and order of sentence dated 17.03.2010 passed by learned Additional Chief

Judicial Magistrate, Patiala, was dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            Learned counsel representing the petitioner (who was

appointed as Legal Aid Counsel by this Court) contended that after filing of

the petition, the petitioner never came forward to this Court for assisting the

Court and, as such, his appointment as Legal Aid Counsel in this case was

made. He has made strenuous efforts to find out the correct address of

respondents No.2 and 3, but could not find out the same, so the service upon

respondents No.2 and 3 could not be effected.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             Otherwise also, both the Courts below have already appreciated

the entire facts and evidence and there is no other ground for interference in

the findings given by the Courts below. Moreso, there is a huge delay of 289

days in filing the present revision petition which has remained unexplained.

Hence, the present petition is dismissed.</p><pre id="pre_2">
