<div class="doc_title">Shridhama vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 5 December, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                          MCRC-13909-2016
               (SHRIDHAMA Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


05-12-2016

Shri P.S. Bhadoriya, counsel for the applicant.
Dr. Anjali Gyanani, Government Advocate for the State/
respondent.</pre>
<p id="p_1">Arguments heard. Case diary perused.
</p><p id="p_2">This is first bail application under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> of Cr.P.C. filed on
behalf of applicant Shridhama, who is apprehending his arrest in
relation to Crime No.119/2016 registered at Police Station Barohi,
District Bhind (MP) for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">Sections
354-A</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_3">506</a>, <a href="/doc/594493/" id="a_4">294</a> and <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_5">506</a> of IPC.
</p><p id="p_3">It is submitted that in this case custodial interrogation is not
required and before the alleged incident quarrel was taken place
between the complainant and the applicant. Hence this
application be allowed.
</p><p id="p_4">Learned Public Prosecutor appearing for the State vehemently
opposes the application and prayed for rejection of the same.
Considering the overall facts and circumstances of the case and
looking to the fact that if the applicant is arrested then possibility
of settlement between the parties shall be ruined, hence, this
application is allowed.
</p><p id="p_5">It is directed that in the event of arrest or surrender of applicant
Shridhama before the Arresting Authority / Investigating Officer
in relation to aforementioned Crime No.119/2016, within a period
of 15 days from today, he shall be released on bail on his
furnishing a personal bond in the sum of Rs.25,000/- (Twenty
Five Thousand Only) with one solvent surety in the like amount
 to the satisfaction of the Arresting Authority / Investigating
Officer.
</p><p id="p_6">The applicant shall make himself available for interrogation by a
Police Officer as and when required and will co-operate in the
investigation. He will further abide by the other conditions
enumerated in sub-section (2) of <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_6">Section 438</a> of Cr.P.C.
C.C. as per rules.
</p>


<p id="p_7">                                                 (J. P. GUPTA)
                                                     JUDGE
