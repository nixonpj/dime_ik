<p id="p_2">     Learned counsel appearing for the petitioners

submits that the writ petitions may be disposed of by

directing respondent No.1 to treat the legal notice dated

25th September 2013 (Annexure-E) as a representation of

the petitioners and to consider the same in accordance

with law and to pass appropriate orders thereon. I find

no legal impediment to grant the prayer.    Accordingly,

respondent No.1 is directed to treat the notice dtd.

25.09.2013 as a representation of the petitioners and to

appropriate orders thereon, expeditiously and in any

event within two months from the date of receipt of a

copy of this order. The petitioners shall furnish a copy of

this order along with a copy of the aforesaid notice to

enable   respondent   No.1    to   consider   the   same   in

accordance with law and within the time stipulated

above.
