<p id="p_1">38   14-11-2017               Heard learned counsel for the parties on I.A. No.5140

                  of 2014, which is a petition under Order XXII Rule 4 read with

                  Section 151 of the Code of Civil Procedure.               Prayer is for

                  substitution of legal heirs of respondent No.25 Birendra

                  Chaudhary, who died on 29.04.2014 leaving behind the heirs

                  mentioned in para-2 of the petition. The sons of respondent No.25

                  are already on the record.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              In the circumstances, the prayer for substitution of all

                  the legal heirs of respondent No.25, mentioned in para-2 of the

                  petition, stands allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">                                                             (Birendra Kumar, J)
