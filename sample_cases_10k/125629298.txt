<div class="doc_title">Gopal vs State (Home Department)Ors on 19 February, 2013</div><pre id="pre_1">    

 
 
 

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR 
RAJASTHAN BENCH AT JAIPUR.

O R D E R

D.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.21010/2012.

Gopal 
Vs.
State of Rajasthan &amp; Ors. 

Date of Order :	            February 19, 2013.

HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE MOHAMMAD RAFIQ
HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE JAINENDRA KUMAR RANKA

Shri Anshuman Saxena for the petitioner. 
Smt.Rekha Madnani, Deputy Government Counsel.
****
BY THE COURT:-</pre>
<p id="p_1">		This writ petition has been filed by the petitioner, who is serving sentence of life imprisonment for offence u/S.302 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> pursuant to the judgment of the Additional District &amp; Sessions Judge, Aklera, District Jhalawar dated 26/03/2004. 			Contention of the learned counsel for the petitioner is that petitioner already served out sentence of life imprisonment much more than 1/3rd of his substantive sentence entitling him to be sent to the open air camp as per Rule 4(c) of the Rules of 1972, which provides that a prisoner shall be eligible for admission to an Open Air Camp, if he has served one third term of his substantive sentence including remission.
</p><p id="p_2">		Learned Deputy Government Counsel has opposed the writ petition and submitted that no such application has been submitted by the petitioner to the jail authorities for considering his case for transfer to the open air camp. 
</p>
<p id="p_3">		We have heard learned counsel for the parties and perused the material available on record. 
</p><p id="p_4">		In the light of the aforesaid, this petition is disposed of directing the Superintendent,  Central Jail, Kota that as and when petitioner submitted any such applciation to him, forward his application to the competent authority, who shall consider and decide his case for transfer to the open air camp in accordance with law within a period of six weeks. 
</p><p id="p_5">		A copy of this order be sent to the Superintendent,  Central Jail, Kota for compliance. 
</p>
       
<p id="p_6">(JAINENDRA KUMAR RANKA), J.   (MOHAMMAD RAFIQ), J.
</p>
<p id="p_7">Anil/34

All corrections made in the judgment/order have been incorporated in the judgment/order being e-mailed
Anil Kumar Goyal
Sr.P.A. Cum J
</p>
