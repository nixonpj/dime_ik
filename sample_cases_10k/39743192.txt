<div class="doc_title">Umesh Ram vs The State Of Bihar on 21 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                     IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.33412 of 2019
                  Arising Out of PS. Case No.-215 Year-2019 Thana- GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL COMP.
                                                District- East Champaran
                 ======================================================
                 Umesh Ram son of Mahendra Ra, Resident of Village- Laxmipur, P.S.-
                 Turkauliya, District- East Champaran

                                                                              ... ... Petitioner
                                                   Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                          ... ... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner     :     Mr. Umesh Chandra Verma, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party :     Ms. Gulnar Begum, APP
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE ASHWANI KUMAR SINGH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   21-05-2019                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                  The petitioner seeks bail in connection with Excise

                   Case No.215 of 2019 registered under Section 30(a) of the Bihar

                   Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_1">Excise Act</a>, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  Considering the nature of allegation made in the

                   FIR, the period undergone by the petitioner in custody and the

                   statement made in paragraph-3 of the application that he has no

                   criminal antecedent, he is directed to be released on bail on

                   furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (rupees ten thousand) with

                   two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of

                   learned 9th Additional Sessions Judge-cum-Special Judge,

                   Excise, East Champaran, Motihari in connection with Excise
               Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.33412 of 2019(2) dt.21-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                          2/2</span>




                        Case No.215 of 2019 subject to the following conditions:-

</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                        (a) that the petitioner shall attend court in
                                        accordance with the conditions of the bond
                                        executed,
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                                        (b) that the petitioner shall not commit an offence
                                        similar to the offence of the present case, and
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                                        (c) that the petitioner shall not directly or indirectly
                                        make any inducement, threat or promise to any
                                        person acquainted with the facts of the case so as to
                                        dissuade him from disclosing such facts to the
                                        Court or to any police officer or tamper with the
                                        evidence.</blockquote><p id="p_4">


</p><p id="p_5">                                                  (Ashwani Kumar Singh, J)

Md. S/-
</p>
