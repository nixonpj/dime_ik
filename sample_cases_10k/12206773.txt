<div class="doc_title">Manish @ Chhotu Birtharia vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh Thr on 6 October, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                                   MCRC 11045/2017
                      Manish alias <a href="/doc/14898986/" id="a_1">Chhotu Birtharia vs. State of MP</a>
06/10/2017
     Shri Gaurav Mishra, counsel for the applicant.
      Shri Prakhar Dhengula, Public Prosecutor for the
respondent- State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      This is fifth application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_2">Section 439</a> of CrPC for
grant of bail. First and second applications were dismissed as
withdrawn by orders dated 03/04/2017 and 22/05/2017 passed
in MCRC 2851/2017 and MCRC 5210/2017. Third and fourth
applications were rejected on merits by orders dated
18/07/2017 and 23/08/2017 passed in MCRC 7263/2017 and
MCRC 9308/2017.
</p><p id="p_2">      The applicant has been arrested on 14/02/2017 in
connection with Crime No.122/2017 registered at Police
Station Dabra, District Gwalior for the offence punishable
under <a href="/doc/704277/" id="a_3">Section 8</a>/<a href="/doc/1566465/" id="a_4">21</a> of NDPS Act.
</p><p id="p_3">      It is submitted by the counsel for the applicant that the
applicant is in jail from 14/02/2017 and more than seven
months have passed but the trial is moving at snail's speed. The
independent witnesses of the seizure have not supported the
prosecution case. The trial is likely to take sufficiently long
time. The applicant is ready and willing to abide by any
condition which may be imposed by this Court.
</p><p id="p_4">      Per contra, the application is opposed by the State
Counsel.
</p><p id="p_5">      Considering the period of detention of the applicant and
without commenting on the merits of the case, this Court is of
the considered opinion, that it is a fit case for grant of bail. The
       application is allowed and it is directed that the applicant be
      released on bail on furnishing a personal bond in the sum of
      Rs.2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lacs) with one surety in the like
      amount to the satisfaction of trial Court/committal Court, to
      appear before the Court on the dates given by the concerned
      Court.
</p><p id="p_6">           It is further directed that the applicant shall appear before
      the S.H.O., Police Station Dabra, District Gwalior On 1 st and
      15th of every month, during the pendency of the Trial.
</p><p id="p_7">           This order shall remain in force till the conclusion of
      Trial. Needless to mention that in case of bail jump or in case
      of failure on the part of the applicant to appear before the
      concerning Police Station on the specified dates, this order
      shall become inoperative.
</p><p id="p_8">           Certified Copy as per rules.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                                            (G.S. Ahluwalia)
                                                  Judge
MKB
