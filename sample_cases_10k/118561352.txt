<div class="doc_title">Nareshkumar Gharyanomal ... vs State Of Gujarat on 7 July, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                 R/CR.MA/14634/2016                                                    ORDER




                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

         CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR REGULAR BAIL) NO. 14634 of 2016

         ==========================================================
              NARESHKUMAR GHARYANOMAL RAMRAKHIYANI....Applicant(s)
                                   Versus
                       STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MR DARSHAN P DAVE, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR JK SHAH, APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         ==========================================================

          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.P.BHATT

                                         Date : 07/07/2016


                                          ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.           The present application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>
         of the Code of Criminal Procedure by the applicant for
         regular bail in connection with First Information Report
         being C.R.No.II-3466 of 2015 registered with Odhav Police
         Station, District:-Ahmedabad for the offences punishable
         under <a href="/doc/114117546/" id="a_2">Sections 3</a>, <a href="/doc/91704683/" id="a_3">4</a>, <a href="/doc/120962339/" id="a_4">5</a>, <a href="/doc/172962030/" id="a_5">6</a>, <a href="/doc/139656816/" id="a_6">7</a> and <a href="/doc/8796492/" id="a_7">9</a> of the Immoral Traffic
         (Prevention) Act.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.           Heard learned counsel for the applicant and
         learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the respondent-State.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.           Learned advocate for the applicant submits that
         the role of the present applicant is similar to that of co-
         accused     persons          namely,      Vikramsinh             Bhanvarsinh               Rao,
         Deepak Babubhai Soni, and Divya @ Tanya Nareshkumar


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                Page 1 of 4</span>

HC-NIC                                     Page 1 of 4        Created On Fri Jul 08 03:39:24 IST 2016
                  R/CR.MA/14634/2016                                                    ORDER



         Ramrakhiyani have been enlarged on bail by this Court as well
         as by the coordinate bench of this Court vide its orders dated
         24.02.2016, 02.03.2016 and 10.06.2016 passed in Criminal
         Misc. Application Nos.4816 of 2016, 5420 of 2016 and 11543
         of 2016 and, therefore, on the ground of parity, the applicant
         may be enlarged on regular bail.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.           Learned Additional Public Prosecutor appearing on
         behalf of the respondent-State has opposed grant of regular
         bail and submitted that from the charge-sheet papers, there is
         a    prima   facie      case       against        the    present          applicant            for
         committing the alleged offence and, therefore, looking to the
         nature and gravity of the offence, this application may be
         dismissed.
</p>

<p id="p_5">         5.           Regard being had to the above submissions and
         considering the nature of allegations made against the
         applicant in the First Information Report and the fact that
         similarly situated co-accused have been enlarged on bail by
         this Court as well as by the coordinate bench of this Court
         vide its orders dated 24.02.2016, 02.03.2016 and 10.06.2016
         passed in Criminal Misc. Application Nos.4816 of 2016, 5420
         of 2016 and 11543 of 2016, this Court is of the opinion that
         this is a fit case to exercise the discretion and enlarge the
         applicant on regular bail.
</p>

<p id="p_6">         6.           Hence, the present application is allowed and the
         applicant is ordered to be released on regular bail in
         connection with an offence being C.R.No.II-3466 of 2015
         registered       with        Odhav           Police         Station,              District:-</p><pre id="pre_2">
         Ahmedabad,           on      his     executing          a      personal            bond         of

         of the like amount to the satisfaction of the learned trial court,
         and subject to the following conditions, that the applicant
         shall;
</p>

<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                         [a] not take undue advantage of liberty or misuse
                         liberty;
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_2">                         [b] not act in a manner injurious to the interest of
                         the prosecution;
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_3">                         [c] surrender passport, if any, to the lower court
                         within a week;
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_4">                         [d] not leave the State of Gujarat without prior
                         permission of the Sessions Judge concerned;
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_5">                         [e] mark presence before the concerned Police
                         Station on every Monday of each English calendar
                         month         for   a    period         of     three         months             and
                         thereafter, on alternate Monday for a period of six
                         months, between 11:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m.;
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_6">                         [f] furnish         latest and permanent                       address           of
                         residence to the Investigating Officer, and also to
                         the Court at the time of execution of the bond, and
                         shall not change the residence without prior
                         permission of this Court;
</blockquote>

<p id="p_8">         7.            The Authorities will release the applicant only if he
         is not required in connection with any other offence for the

         warrant or take appropriate action in the matter. Bail bond to

         jurisdiction to try the case. It will be open for the concerned
         Court to delete, modify and/or relax any of the above
         conditions, in accordance with law. At the trial, learned trial
         court        shall      not        be   influenced          by     the      observations              of
         preliminary nature, qua the evidence at this stage made by
         this Court while enlarging the applicant on bail.
