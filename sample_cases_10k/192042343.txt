<div class="doc_title">Ninshu @ Ajay Sen vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 5 November, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                            MCRC-15431-2015
               (NINSHU @ AJAY SEN Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


05-11-2015
      Shri Sanjiv Kumar Singh, learned counsel for the applicant.
      Smt. Shobhna Sharma, leanred P. L. for respondent/ State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">      Heard.
</p><p id="p_2">      This is first application filed by the applicant under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>
of Cr. P. C. for grant of bail to the applicant, as he has been arrested
on 21.6.2015, in connection of Crime No.92/15, registered at P. S.
Mauganj District Rewa for the offence punishable under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_2">Sections
392</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of IPC
      Allegation against the applicant is that he along with other co-
accused had snatched money of Rs.20,000/-
</p><p id="p_3">      The applicant is in jail since 21.6.2015 from the possession of
the applicant an amount of Rs.800/- has been recovered.
</p><p id="p_4">      Looking to nature and facts of the case, without expressing any
opinion on merits of the matter petition is allowed.
</p><p id="p_5">       It is directed that on furnishing personal bond of Rs.50,000/- (Rs.
Fifty thousand) by the applicant along with one solvent surety in the like
amount to the satisfaction of the trial Court the applicant NINSHU @ AJAY SEN
be released on bail.
</p><p id="p_6">       The applicant shall abide by the following conditions of <a href="/doc/1682361/" id="a_4">Section
437</a> (3) of Cr. P. C. as under:
</p><p id="p_7">       (a) that such person shall attend in accordance with the
conditions of the bond executed under this Chapter;
</p><p id="p_8">       (b) that such person shall not commit any offence similar to the
offence of which he is accused, or suspected of the commission of
which he is suspected and;
</p><p id="p_9">       (c) that such person shall not directly or indirectly make any
inducement, treat or promise to any person acquainted with the facts
of the case as to dissuade him from disclosing such facts to the Court
or to any police officer or tamper with the evidence.
</p><p id="p_10">        C. C. as per rules.
</p><p id="p_11">                                                                    (S. K.
</p><p id="p_12"> Gangele)
           JUDGE
