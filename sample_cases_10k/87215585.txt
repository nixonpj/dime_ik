<div class="doc_title">Tufani Yadav vs The State Of Bihar on 19 June, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.34399 of 2019
                      Arising Out of PS. Case No.-73 Year-2019 Thana- BASANTPUR District- Siwan
                 ======================================================
                 Tufani Yadav Son of Late Naga Yadav Resident of Village-Babhanauli, P.S.-
                 Basantpur, District-Siwan, State-Bihar.

                                                                                 ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 THE STATE OF BIHAR Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :      Mr.Ajeet Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s :      Mrs.Asha Kumari
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE SUDHIR SINGH
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   19-06-2019                    Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   APP for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                         The petitioner seeks bail in a case instituted for the

                   offences under <a href="/doc/471092/" id="a_1">Sections 272</a>, <a href="/doc/285163/" id="a_2">273</a>, <a href="/doc/4266/" id="a_3">308</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_4">34</a> of I.P.C. and Sections

                   30(a)/38/41 of the Bihar Prohibition and <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_5">Excise Act</a>, 2016.

</p><p id="p_3">                         Prosecution case, in short, is that 1209 liters wine is

                   recovered.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                         It has been submitted on behalf of the petitioner that the

                   petitioner is in custody since 14.02.2019and has got no criminal

                   antecedent. Charge sheet/prosecution report has been submitted

                   in this case. There is no allegation of tampering of witnesses

                   alleged against the petitioner. It is alleged that 1209 liters wine

                   is recovered. There is no compliance of <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_6">Section 100</a> Cr.P.C.

                   Other co-accused has been granted bail vide Cr.Misc.No.29426
              Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.34399 of 2019(3) dt.19-06-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                         2/2</span>




                       of 2019.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                             On behalf of the State, it is submitted that the petitioner is

                       named in the F.I.R./complaint case.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                    Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstances, it

                       is directed that the petitioner, above named, be released on bail

                       on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.10,000/- (Ten thousand) with two

                       sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                       Assistant Sessions Judge II-cum-Special Judge, Excise, Siwan

                       in connection with Basantpur P.S. case No.73 of 2019, G.R.

                       No.204 of 2019.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                                    (Sudhir Singh, J)
Narendra/-
</p>
