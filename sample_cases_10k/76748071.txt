<div class="doc_title">Sadiq Khan vs State on 4 August, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                            [1]


               S.B.CRIMINAL MISC. 2nd BAIL APPLICATION NO.6884/2016

                         (Sadiq Khan      Vs.     State of Rajasthan)


                              Date of order : :     04-08-2016


                        HON'BLE MS.JUSTICE NIRMALJIT KAUR


          Mr. Kuldeep Sharma, for the petitioner.
          Mr. O.P. Rathi, Public Prosecutor.

                                         &lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;&lt;&gt;

                The present bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

          Cr.P.C. The petitioner has been arrested in connection with FIR

          No.126/2016, Police Station Ahore for the offence under <a href="/doc/1873755/" id="a_2">Section 457</a>,

          <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_3">376</a>/<a href="/doc/1185693/" id="a_4">511</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a> IPC.

                It is evident from the statement under <a href="/doc/497457/" id="a_6">Section 164</a> Cr.P.C. that

          all the family members were in the house when the present petitioner

          came and tried to commit rape. The incident is surrounded with the

          suspicion.

                Taking into account the facts and circumstances of the case, this

          Court deems it just and proper to release the petitioner on bail.

                Accordingly, the bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_7">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is

          allowed and it is ordered that the accused-petitioner Sadiq Khan S/o

          Habbas Khan shall be enlarged on bail provided he furnishes a

          personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two sureties of

          Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial Judge for his

          appearance before the court concerned on all the dates of hearing as

          and when called upon to do so.



                                                           (NIRMALJIT KAUR), J.
</pre>
