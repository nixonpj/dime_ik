for respondent for proper communication of the order.

</p><p id="p_9">2.          Learned counsel for the petitioner states that he will be satisfied

petitioner to respondent No.3 is considered and appropriate decision is

taken regarding the payment of interest on delayed payment of retiral

benefits.    The respondent No.3 shall consider the demand made through

the legal notice under Annexure P-3 and shall pass the appropriate order

within a period of 8 weeks from the date of receipt of copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_10">3.          The writ petition is disposed of at the stage of admission,

dispensing with notice to the respondents.
