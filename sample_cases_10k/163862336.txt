                                 2


     This MFA is filed under <a href="/doc/93665491/" id="a_1">Section 173(1)</a> of the Motor Vehicles
Act against the judgment and award dated 13.7.2009 passed in
MVC.No.6849/2008 on the file of the VI Addl.Judge, Court of Small
Causes, MACT, Bangalore, dismissing the petition for
compensation.

      This Appeal coming on for hearing this day, the Court
delivered the following :

                          JUDGMENT
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard the learned counsel for respective parties.

</p><p id="p_2">     2. The Tribunal has dismissed the claim petition filed by

the claimant on account of the injuries sustained in the accident

that occurred on 24.3.2008 at about 12.30 p.m. when she was

crossing the road in front of Vijayanagar Club. At that time, the

rider of Activa Honda bearing registration No.KA-02-EW-813,

came in a rash and negligent manner and dashed against her, by

recording some reasons.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">     Having regard to the material on record, I am of the opinion

that matter requires reconsideration at the hands of the Tribunal.

Accordingly, the impugned order is set aside and matter is

remitted to the Tribunal to dispose of the matter in accordance
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 3</span>


with law after affording opportunity to both the parties to lead

additional evidence, if any.    However, it is made clear that

claimant is not entitled for interest from the date of dismissal of

claim petition by the Tribunal till fresh award is passed on

remand.    All contentions are kept open to be urged by the

parties.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      Appeal is disposed of.
</p>
