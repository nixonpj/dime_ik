      IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT
                     CHANDIGARH


                          RSA No. 2484 of 1989 (O&amp;M)
                          Date of decision: April 8, 2013

Dharamvir Singh
                                                           ...Appellant
                                Versus
State of Haryana and another
                                                           ...Respondents

CORAM:- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE A.N. JINDAL


Present:     None for the appellant.

             Mr. Ajay Gupta, Additional Advocate General, Haryana.

A.N. JINDAL, J. (Oral)
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             None appears on behalf of the appellant, despite the fact that

the case was called several times. Under the circumstances, this Court is

left with no option but proceed to decide the appeal with the assistance of

the learned counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             The plaintiff/appellant (hereinafter referred to as 'the plaintiff')

filed a suit for declaration that termination of his services was illegal,

malafide and unjustified, which was dismissed. The appeal preferred by

him also met the same fate. Hence the present Regular Second Appeal.

</p><p id="p_3">             Having scrutinized the impugned judgments, it appears that

both the courts below have concurrently returned the findings that the

appointment of the plaintiff being temporary he had no right to the post and

the State had a right to terminate his services by giving him one month's

notice. It was further held that the termination of the plaintiff was valid and

no fault could be found in the same.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> RSA No. 2484 of 1989                                              2</span><p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">           No substantial question of law arises for determination in this

appeal.
</p>
<p id="p_6">           No merits. Dismissed.
</p>
<pre id="pre_2">April 8, 2013                               (A.N. JINDAL)
prem                                              JUDGE
