<div class="doc_title">Surendra Son Of Ram Kishan, B/C ... vs The State Of Rajasthan on 20 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">           HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                       BENCH AT JAIPUR

     S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 6245/2019

Surendra Son Of Ram Kishan, B/c Bairwa, Resident Of Karjoda, Police
Station Ayana, District Kota, At Present Tenant At Shivnagar House
No. 58, Nayapura, Kota (Raj.) (At Present In Central Jail, Kota)
                                                                     ----Petitioner
                                   Versus
The State Of Rajasthan, Through P.p.
                                                                   ----Respondent</pre>


<p id="p_1">For Petitioner(s)        :    Mr. Nitin Kuamr Sharma
For Respondent(s)        :    Mr. Sumer Singh Ola, PP



               HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE INDERJEET SINGH

                                       Order

   20/05/2019

</p><p id="p_2">   1.    The present bail application has been filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 439</a>

   Cr.P.C. The petitioner has been arrested in connection with FIR No.

   159/2019 Registered at Police Station Borkheda, Kota City for the

   offence(s) under <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_2">Sections 380</a>, <a href="/doc/1293807/" id="a_3">461</a>, <a href="/doc/1185693/" id="a_4">511</a> of IPC.

</p><p id="p_3">   2.    Counsel for the petitioner submits that co-accused Vinod

   Kumar has already been released on bail by this court vide order

   dated 24.04.2019 and his case is similar to that of co-accused-

   Vinod Kumar.
</p>
<p id="p_4">   3.    Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

</p><p id="p_5">   4.    Considering the contentions put-forth by the counsel for the

   petitioner and taking into account the facts and circumstances of

   the case and without expressing any opinion on the merits of the

   case, this court deems it just and proper to enlarge the petitioner

   on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_6"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                       (Downloaded on 29/06/2019 at 02:29:28 AM)</span>
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                          (2 of 2)                   [CRLMB-6245/2019]


</p><p id="p_8">                                   5.     Accordingly, the bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C. is

                                   allowed and it is ordered that the accused-petitioner Surendra

                                   Son Of Ram Kishan,             shall be enlarged on bail provided he

                                   furnishes a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two

                                   sureties of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial

                                   Judge for his appearance before the court concerned on all the

                                   dates of hearing as and when called upon to do so.


</p><p id="p_9">                                                                                            (INDERJEET SINGH),J
