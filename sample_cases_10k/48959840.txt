<pre id="pre_1">    HON'BLE SRI JUSTICE A.RAJASHEKER REDDY
             WRIT PETITON No.27882 of 2018

ORDER:
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     This writ petition is filed aggrieved by the inaction of the

4th respondent in      applying the G.O. Ms.No.8, dated 23-01-

2018 to commute sentence of petitioner's son by way of

remission on par with others, who have been convicted in

S.C.No.337 of 2005 on the file of Sessions Judge, Guntur along

with him.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      It is stated that the grievance of the petitioner is already

pending before the 1st respondent by way of representation

dated 18-07-2018, it is for the 1st respondent to take decision

on the same.
</p>
<p id="p_4">       In view of the same, without expressing any opinion on

merits, the 1st respondent is directed to dispose of the

representation    of   the   petitioner    dated   18-07-2018   in

accordance with law within a period of two months from the

date of receipt of a copy of this order.

</p><p id="p_5">       With the above direction, the writ petition is disposed of.

As a sequel to the disposal of this petition, miscellaneous

petitions, if any, pending shall stand closed.


</p><p id="p_6">                                      _________________________
                                      A.RAJASHEKER REDDY,J
28-08-2018
