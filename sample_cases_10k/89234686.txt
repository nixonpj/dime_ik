                  Arising Out of PS.Case No. -149 Year- 2015 Thana -MAGADH MEDICAL COLLEGE District-
                                                            GAYA
                 ======================================================
                 1. SATENDRA KUMAR @ SATYENDRA KUMAR S/o Rameshwar
                 Ram, resident of village- Vaksu Bigha, P.s. Vishnupad, District- Gaya

                                                                              .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                               .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Vinod Kumar
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Binod Kumar(App)
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR SRIVASTAVA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   25-01-2016                   Heard learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

                 learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                               Petitioner is said to have been caught with stolen

                 motorcycle but submission on behalf of the petitioner is that the

                 petitioner does not have any concern with seized motorcycle.

                 Moreover, petitioner does not have any criminal antecedent and

                 the impugned order does not show as to whether the seized

                 motorcycle was stolen property or not.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               Considering the above-said facts and circumstances of

                 the case as well as submission of the parties, let the petitioner

                 named above, be released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                 10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each

                 in connection with Magadh Medical P.S. Case No. 149 of 2015
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.1952 of 2016 (2) dt.25-01-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Gaya.

</p><p id="p_4">                                               (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
