<p id="p_1">            The present civil application is filed
seeking condonation of delay. Rule was issued by
this Court on 4.7.2014 returnable on 4.8.2014. On
4.8.2014,       learned      advocate           Mr.Bhatia   had     filed
sick     note        and,     therefore,           the     matter    was
adjourned today.
</p>

<p id="p_2">            Learned         advocate           Mr.Bhatia    places     on
record     the      extract     from       the    Postal    Department
showing that the R.P.A.D. sent to respondent no.2



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 Page 1 of 2</span>
              C/CA/12077/2013                                              ORDER



is already served but so far as respondent no.1
is concerned, it is not served. Learned advocate
is         yet     to          receive        the         acknowledgement            due
receipt. So far as respondent no.3 is concerned,
learned advocate Mr.Rathin P Raval appears for
respondent no.3. So far as respondent no.1 is
concerned,             the       Postal        Department            website       shows
that        `article            is    sent      back'.         Learned       advocate
requested             that           fresh        notice           be    issued       to
respondent no.1 only with a permission to effect
direct service. Request is granted.
</p>

<p id="p_3">                  Registry            is    directed           to       issue      fresh
process of rule to respondent no.1 returnable on
19.9.2014. Direct service is permitted. It will
be         open    for         the     applicant              to    effect        direct
service by registered post A.D. or any other mode
of         service        in         addition            to   regular       mode      of
service.
</p>
