<div class="doc_title">Panara Kukabhai Ramabhai ... vs State Of Gujarat on 23 January, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">       R/CR.MA/17132/2013                                  ORDER



         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR REGULAR BAIL) NO. 17132 of 2013

================================================================
     PANARA KUKABHAI RAMABHAI (CHU.KOLI ) &amp; 5....Applicant(s)
                           Versus
               STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
MR BS KHATANA, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1 - 6
MR KL PANDYA, ADDITIONAL PUBLIC PROSECUTOR for the Respondent(s)
No. 1
================================================================

        CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE ANANT S. DAVE

                            Date : 23/01/2014
                             ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">  1. Learned Counsel for the applicants does not press
     this application qua the applicant No.1 - Panara
     Kukabhai        Ramabhai   (Ch     Koli),    applicant     No.2   -
     Panara Labhubhai Ramabhai (Ch Koli) and applicant
     No.3 - Panara Chaturbhai Talshibhai (Ch Koli).
</p>

<p id="p_2">     Hence, this application stands disposed of as not
     pressed qua the applicants No.1, 2 and 3.                  Rule is
     discharged qua the applicants No.1, 2 and 3 only.
</p>

<p id="p_3">  2. This application is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the
     Code     of     Criminal   Procedure       in   connection     with
     First      Information      Report       registered   as      I-C.R.
     No.67/2013 with Dhrangadhra Taluka Police Station,
     Surendranagar for the offences punishable under
     <a href="/doc/534021/" id="a_2">Sections 323</a>, <a href="/doc/534021/" id="a_3">147</a>, <a href="/doc/534021/" id="a_4">148</a>, <a href="/doc/534021/" id="a_5">149</a>, <a href="/doc/534021/" id="a_6">302</a> and <a href="/doc/534021/" id="a_7">307</a> of the



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                Page 1 of 4</span>
     R/CR.MA/17132/2013<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_8">                                            ORDER



  Indian Penal Code</a>.
</p>



<p id="p_4">3. Learned       Counsel        appearing          for     the    applicants
  submits            that       the       chargesheet            is       filed.
  Considering the cause of death of the deceased,
  the role attributed to the applicants, the nature
  of injuries and the statement of the witness, it
  is submitted that the applicants No.4, 5 and 6 may
  be enlarged on            bail.
</p>

<p id="p_5">4. Heard       learned      APP       Mr.        K.L.    Pandya        for    the
  respondent-State.
</p>

<p id="p_6">5. Having      heard     learned        Counsels         for   the     parties,
  perusing the record of the case and taking into
  consideration the facts of the case, nature of
  allegations, role attributed to the applicants, by
  imposing suitable conditions, I deem it just and
  proper to enlarge the applicants on bail.
</p>

<p id="p_7">6. Learned Counsels for the parties do not press for
  further reasoned order.
</p>

<p id="p_8">7. In the facts and circumstances of the case, this
  application is allowed and the applicant No.4 -
  Panara Jayantibhai Bachubhai (Ch Koli), applicant
  No.5     -    Panara      Babubhai            Somabhai   (Ch        Koli)   and
  applicant          No.6   -    Panara         Jadavbhai      Ramabhai       (Ch
  Koli)        are    ordered      to     be      released       on    bail    in
  connection             with         First        Information            Report
  registered as I-C.R. No.67/2013 with Dhrangadhra
  Taluka Police Station, Surendranagar, on executing

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                  Page 2 of 4</span>
       R/CR.MA/17132/2013                                       ORDER



  a bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand Only)
  EACH with one surety of the like amount to the
  satisfaction of the Trial Court and subject to the
  conditions that they shall;
</p>

<p id="p_9">  a) not take undue advantage of liberty or misuse
  liberty;
</p><p id="p_10">  b) not act in a manner injurious to the interest
  of the prosecution;
</p><p id="p_11">  c) surrender their passports, if any, to the lower
  court within a week;
</p><p id="p_12">  d) not leave the State of Gujarat without prior
  permission of the Sessions Judge concerned;
</p><p id="p_13">  e) mark presence at the concerned Police Station
  on the first Sunday of every month between 10.00
  a.m. and 3.00 p.m. for three months only;
</p><p id="p_14">  f) furnish the present address of their residence
  to the Investigating Officer and also to the Court
  at the time of execution of the bond and shall not
  change the residence without prior permission of
  this Court;
</p>

<p id="p_15">8. The authorities will release the applicants only
  if     not      required    in       connection       with   any     other
  offence for the time being.
</p>

<p id="p_16">9. If    breach        of   any    of     the   above    conditions      is
  committed, the Sessions Judge concerned will be
  free to issue warrant or take appropriate action
  in the matter.
</p>

<p id="p_17">10.        Bail bond to be executed before the lower
  court having jurisdiction to try the case.
</p>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_3">                                  Page 3 of 4</span>
<p id="p_18">            R/CR.MA/17132/2013                                               ORDER




</p><p id="p_19">   11.          At the trial, the Trial Court shall not be
       influenced               by    the     observations           of    preliminary
       nature, qua the evidence at this stage, made by
       this Court while enlarging the applicants on bail.
</p>

<p id="p_20">   12.          For modification and/or deletion of any of
       the conditions hereinabove, the applicants are at
       liberty to approach the concerned Court and such
       Court          shall            decide             the   application          for
       modification                  and/or      deletion       of        any   of   the
       conditions of this order in accordance with law.
</p>

<p id="p_21">   13.          Rule made absolute qua applicants No.4, 5 and
</p><p id="p_22">       6.     Direct Service is permitted.
</p>

<p id="p_23">                                                                           Sd/-
</p><p id="p_24">                                                                 (ANANT S. DAVE, J.)
CAROLINE
