<div class="doc_title">Killol Arun Udani vs Registrar Of Companies on 19 September, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                  ORDER SHEET
                                 CP No.870 of 2016
                         IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                                Original Jurisdiction
                                  ORIGINAL SIDE


               TRACK TWO INFO SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. (STRUCK OFF)
                                    AND
                             KILLOL ARUN UDANI
                                  VERSUS
                       REGISTRAR OF COMPANIES, W.B.


  BEFORE:
  The Hon'ble JUSTICE SOUMEN SEN</pre>
<p id="p_1">  Date : 19th September, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                                                           Appearance:
</p><p id="p_3">                                                               Mr. A.K. Upadhyay, Adv.
</p><p id="p_4">                                                                      Mr. D. Saha, Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                                             Ms. Tripti Mukherjee, Adv.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

             The Court : The petition is for resurrecting a company which has been

struck off the list of functioning companies under <a href="/doc/1733325/" id="a_1">Section 560</a> of the Companies Act,

1956 for the statutory records and documents not having been filed.

</p><p id="p_7">             The Registrar has filed a statement indicating therein that a total amount

of Rs.1,45,600/- on account of filing fees and additional filing fees would be due from

the company to the Central Government if the statutory records not filed earlier were to

be filed now. The petitioner accepts such position and is agreeable to pay the amount.

</p><p id="p_8">             Subject to the petitioner paying a sum of Rs.1,56,000/- to the Registrar of

Companies within a fortnight from date towards filing fees, additional filing fees and

penalty, the Registrar will restore the name of the company to the list of active

companies and allow access of the relevant portal to the company and its authorised

officers. The money will be deposited in the same account of the Central Government
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                              2</span>


where filing fees and penalties in such regard are usually received. In default of the

payment within the time indicated, CP No.870 of 2016 will stand dismissed with costs.

</p><p id="p_9">             For a period of two years from date, the company will have to file its records

and documents with the Registrar within the time permitted by statute; or else, the

Registrar will be authorised to strike off the name of the company upon previous notice

to it.
</p>
<p id="p_10">             The petition under <a href="/doc/1155573/" id="a_2">Section 560(6)</a> of the Act is disposed of as above without

any further order as to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_11">             Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be supplied to

the parties subject to compliance with all requisite formalities.




</p><p id="p_12">                                                         (SOUMEN SEN, J.)


B.Pal./
 </p>
