<p id="p_6">                      CAN 3349 of 2016 and CAN 3233 of 2016 are applications for addition of parties at the
               instance of certain residents of Satyam Towers.
</p><p id="p_7">                      None appears to oppose the applications.
                      Having heard Mr. Roy, learned advocate appearing for the applicants and considering the
               earlier order dated 24th March, 2016, the applications stand allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_8">Office                Office shall incorporate the names of the applicants as additional petitioners in the cause
               title of the writ petition and delete the particulars of the petitioner no.1 in terms of the said order
               dated 24th March, 2016. The cause title of the writ petition shall be recast with the petitioner no.2
               as the petitioner no.1.
</p>
<p id="p_9">ACO                   After the aforesaid amendments are effected, the writ petition shall be listed on next
               Monday (27th November, 2017) under the heading "For Orders".
</p>
