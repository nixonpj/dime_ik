<p id="p_1">02.    28.06.2012                      The Petitioners seek quashing of the order dated

                          28.06.2011 passed by the Chief Judicial Magistrate,

                          Bettiah, West Champaran in Tr. No. 1062 of 2011 arising

                          out of Yogapatti P.S. Case No. 313 of 2010 by which he

                          has disagreed with the final report taking cognizance

                          under <a href="/doc/1279834/" id="a_1">Sections 376</a>, <a href="/doc/1185693/" id="a_2">511</a>, <a href="/doc/162506/" id="a_3">447</a> and <a href="/doc/839778/" id="a_4">380</a> IPC and 27 <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_5">Arms

                          Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                       Since it is well within the power                of the

                          Magistrate to disagree with the final report, I am not

                          inclined to interfere in the matter. The application is

                          dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_3">
</p><p id="p_4">                                                                (Anjana Prakash, J.)
      Vikash/-
</p>
