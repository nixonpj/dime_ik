CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.M.SUNDRESH             
and 
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N.SATHISH KUMAR             

Writ Appeal(MD)No.89 of 2015 

R.Subramanian                                                   ... Appellant

Vs.
1.The District Registrar,
   Office of the District Registrar,
   District Court Complex,
   Trichy.

2.The Joint Sub Registrar No.III,
   Office of the Joint Sub Registrar Office No.3,
   Trichy.                                                              ...  Respondents

        Appeal filed under Clause 15 of the Letters Patent against the order
dated 23.07.2014 made in W.P.(MD)No.16732 of 2012 on the file of this Court.

!For Appellant          : Mr.V.Singan

^For Respondents                : Mr.T.R.Janarthanam, 
                                                Additional Government Pleader.
                

:JUDGMENT   
</pre>
<p id="p_1">(Judgment of the Court was delivered by M.M.SUNDRESH,J)   

                It is submitted by the learned Additional Government Pleader that
after the order passed by the learned single Judge, proceedings under Section
47-A has been initiated. Therefore, nothing survives for adjudication in this
appeal. However, learned counsel for the appellant submitted that pending
adjudication under Section 47-A proceedings, document has to be released.  It
constitute a separate cause of action. Therefore, liberty is granted to the
appellant to agitate the above said issue in the manner known to law. The
respondents shall send communication to the appellant indicating pendency of
the 47-A proceedings. If it is not already issued, the respondents shall send
the same within a period of four weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of
this order.
</p><p id="p_2">                2.With the above observation, the writ appeal is disposed of. No
costs.
</p>


<p id="p_3">To

   Office of the District Registrar,
   District Court Complex, Trichy.
</p>
