<div class="doc_title">P.Chinnasamy vs The Superintendent Of Police on 18 June, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 18.06.2018  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE D.KRISHNAKUMAR             

W.P.(MD) No.12865 of 2018  

P.Chinnasamy                                                    ... Petitioner

                                     Vs.

1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Trichy, Trichy District-635 001.

2.The Inspector of Police,
   Siruganur Police Station,
   Lalgudi Taluk,
   Trichy District-621 109.                                     ...Respondents 

         Writ petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India, to
issue a writ of Mandamus, directing the respondents to grant permission to
perform the program of Adal Padal on 18.06.2018 night at about 07.00 pm to
12.00 am in connection with temple festival at ?Sri Maha Mariyamman, Sri
Vaduvachiyamman &amp; Sri Vinayagar Temple festival? Dhiranipalayam Village, 
Dhiranipalayam Post, Lalgudi Taluk, Trichy District based on the petitioner's
representation dated 11.06.2018.

!For Petitioner         : Mr.S.Mayilnathan
For Respondents : Mr.P.Kannidevan,        
                                          Additional Government Pleader.


:ORDER  
</pre><p id="p_1">                This Writ Petition has been filed seeking a direction to the
respondents to grant permission to perform the program of Adal Padal on
18.06.2018 night at about 07.00 pm to 12.00 am in connection with temple
festival at ?Sri Maha Mariyamman, Sri Vaduvachiyamman and Sri Vinayagar  
Temple festival? Dhiranipalayam Village, Dhiranipalayam Post, Lalgudi Taluk,
Trichy District based on the petitioner's representation dated 11.06.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                2.Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the
learned Additional Government Pleader appearing for the respondents.  By
consent of both parties, this writ petition is taken up for final disposal at
the stage of admission itself.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                3.According to the petitioner, he made a representation on
11.06.2018 to the respondents, seeking permission to conduct Adal Padal
program scheduled to be held on 18.06.2018 and on the said representation, no
order has been passed by the respondents.  Therefore, he filed this writ
petition.
</p>
        
<p id="p_4">                4.The learned Additional Government Pleader, who takes notice on
bahalf of the respondents, would submit that the petitioner made a
representation only on 11.06.2018 and he approached this Court, seeking for
permission to perform program on 18.06.2018 (today).  Hence, the said
representation of the petitioner cannot be considered.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                5.In the light of the above submission made by the learned
Additional Government Pleader, this Court cannot entertain this writ petition
and the same is liable to be dismissed.  However, this order will not stand
in the way of considering the petitioner's representation dated 11.06.2018.
The second respondent is directed to consider the petitioner's representation
dated 11.06.2018 on merits and in accordance with law.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                6.With the above observation, this writ petition is dismissed.
No costs.
</p>

<p id="p_7">To
</p><p id="p_8">1.The Superintendent of Police,
   Trichy, Trichy District-635 001.
</p>
<p id="p_9">2.The Inspector of Police,
   Siruganur Police Station,
   Lalgudi Taluk,
   Trichy District-621 109.     
</p>
