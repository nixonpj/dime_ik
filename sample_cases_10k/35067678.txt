<p id="p_1">   HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                    JODHPUR
S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 10198/2019

Rajendra Kumar S/o Sh. Krishan Lal, Aged About 29 Years,
B/c Jat, R/o Khetawali Dhani 99 Rd Tehsil Rawatsar District
Hanumangarh. (Presently Lodged In Sub Jail, Nohar)
</p><p id="p_2">                                                                  ----Petitioner
                                    Versus
State, Through Pp
</p><p id="p_3">                                                                ----Respondent


For Petitioner(s)        :        Mr. R.S. Gill
For Respondent(s)        :        Mr. Vikram Sharma, P.P.



</p><p id="p_4">          HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI

                                     Order

26/08/2019


     Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

the learned Public Prosecutor and perused the material

available on record.
</p>
<p id="p_5">     The petitioner has been arrested in FIR No. 396/2015 of

Police Station Rawatsar for the offences punishable under

<a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a> and <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_2">302</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_3">34</a> of IPC. He has preferred this interim

bail application under Section 439 Cr.P.C.

</p><p id="p_6">     Having   regard         to     the    totality       of    the   facts   and

circumstances of the case and looking to the nature of

accusation and gravity of the offence, without expressing any

opinion on the merits of the case, I am not inclined to grant

interim bail under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_4">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. to the petitioner.



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    (Downloaded on 28/08/2019 at 11:46:39 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_7">                                                                     (2 of 2)                     [CRLMB-10198/2019]


                                        Accordingly, the interim bail application preferred by the

                                   petitioner under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_5">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is rejected.


</p><p id="p_8">                                                                                        (VIJAY BISHNOI),J

                                    115-Taruna/-
</p>
