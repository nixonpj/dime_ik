             IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

    CRIMINAL MISC.APPLICATION (FOR TEMPORARY BAIL) NO. 15615 of
                                       2014

================================================================
    SHRI GAURAVBHAI HEMANTBHAI CHAUHAN THRU, MANISHA GAURAV
                      CHAUHAN....Applicant(s)
                             Versus
                 STATE OF GUJARAT....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
MR EE SAIYED, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
MR LB DABHI APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
================================================================

            CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE A.J.DESAI

                                 Date : 30/09/2014
ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1         RULE. Learned APP Mr. L.B. Dabhi, waives service of rule on
behalf of the respondent - State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2         By way of the present application, the applicant - original accused
has prayed to release him on temporary bail on the ground of his own
illness     as he is suffering from heart diseases.       In support of his
application he has produced a certificate issued by Krishna Hospital &amp;
ICU, Viramgam.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3         I have heard the learned Advocate Mr. E.E. Saiyed for the
applicant and Mr. L.B. Dabhi, learned APP, for the respondent - State.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4         Considering the ground advanced by the applicant for of temporary
bail, the present application requires consideration. Hence, the present


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                     Page 1 of 2</span>
            R/CR.MA/15615/2014                                ORDER




application is allowed. The applicant is ordered to be released on
temporary bail on 5.10.2014 for a period of three weeks on executing a
personal bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees ten thousand only) before the
concerned Jail Authority and on other usual terms and conditions. He can
mark his presence before the concerned Police Station once in a week
between 9.00 a.m and 5 .00 p.m. The applicant - accused shall surrender
before the Jail Authority on completion of the temporary bail period
without fail.
</p>

<p id="p_5">5        Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent only. Direct Service
is permitted.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                                            (A.J.DESAI, J.)
