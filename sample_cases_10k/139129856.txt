<p id="p_1">2   29-08-2014                    Since Cr.Misc.No. 25518/2014 wherein Chhatish
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.25518 of 2014 (2) dt.29-08-2014                             2</span>




             Kumar happens to be petitioner, Cr.Misc.No.28162/2014 wherein

             Thengu Kumar @ Dengu Kumar happens to be petitioner and

             Cr.Misc.No. 31438/2014 wherein Sone Lal Mahto @ Sone Lal

             Kumar happens to be petitioner commonly originate from

             Mokamah P.S. Case No. 265/2013 have been heard analogously

             and are being disposed of by a common order.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              During night patrolling, all the three petitioners along

             with motorcycle were apprehended. During course of search from

             the possession of petitioner, Thengu Kumar @ Dengu Kumar as

             well as Chhatish Kumar country made loaded pistol has been

             recovered while from the possession of Sone Lal Mahto @ Sone

             Lal Kumar one live cartridge along with mobile were recovered

             and on account thereof, they have been booked.

</p><p id="p_3">                              Contentions on behalf of the petitioners are that they

             have got clean antecedents and are under custody since

             29.11.2013. So far motorcycle is concerned, from the seizure list,

             it is evident that it has not been shown from whose possession the

             same was recovered. However, even accepting the prosecution

             case, there happens to be joint possession.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                              Learned APP opposed the prayer for bail.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                               Accordingly,         petitioners,   namely,   1.   Chhatish

             Kumar, 2. Thengu Kumar @ Dengu Kumar and 3. Sone Lal
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.25518 of 2014 (2) dt.29-08-2014                 3</span>




                  Mahto @ Sone Lal Kumar are directed to be released on bail on

                  furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- each with two sureties of the

                  like amount each to the satisfaction of ACJM, Barh, Patna in

                  connection with Mokamah P.S. Case No. 265/2013.</p><pre id="pre_2">



                                           (Aditya Kumar Trivedi, J)
    perwez
