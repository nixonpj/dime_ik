IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

Dated:  23.10.2017

CORAM

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.V.MURALIDARAN

W.P.No.16164 of 2013

Dr. S. Peer Mohamed 						... Petitioner

Vs.
 
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal 
  Sciences University,
Madavaram Milk Colony, Chennai-51.
Rep. by its Registrar. 						... Respondent

Prayer: Writ Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India for a Writ of Mandamus directing the respondent to consider the petitioner to the post of Professor under Career Advancement Scheme as per the circular No. 7346/R.3/2013 dated 08.04.2013 in accordance with law and select me on the basis of eligibility.

		For Petitioner 	:Mr.R.Syed Mustafa
		For Respondent	:Mr.V.Gangadatharan

ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">	The above writ petition is filed directing the respondent to consider the petitioner to the post of Professor under Career Advancement Scheme as per the circular No.7346/R.3/2013 dated 08.04.2013. The relief sought for in the present writ petition has been granted to the petitioner in WP.No.9207 of 2013. Hence, this writ petition is closed as infructuous.
</p>
