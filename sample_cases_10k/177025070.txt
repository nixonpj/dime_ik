<p id="p_1">a)    Date of offence             :   18.02.2006

</p><p id="p_2">b)    Offence complained of       :   28/112 D.P. Act 

</p><p id="p_3">c)    Name of accused, his        :   Ajay Prakash
      parentage and residence         S/o Late Ved Prakash,
                                      R/o 723, Church Mission

</p><p id="p_4">d)    Plea of accused             :   Pleaded guilty


                                                               1 of 3
                                                                             DD No. 37/06
                                                                          PS Lahori Gate
                                                                      U/s 28/112 D.P. Act
                                                                   State Vs. Ajay Prakash




</p><p id="p_5">e)     Final order                                :      Convicted

BRIEF FACTS AND REASONS FOR DECISION:
</p>
<p id="p_6">               Accused submits that he wishes to plead guilty of the offence
u/s   28/112   D.P.   Act.     The   consequences   of   pleading   guilt   have   been
explained to the accused.   However, accused submits that he is pleading
guilt without any fear, force or coercion and further submits that lenient
view   may   be   taken   against   him.     Separate   statement   of   accused   also
recorded in this regard.  
</p>
<p id="p_7">             In view of the statement, I am satisfied that accused is pleading
guilty voluntarily and without any fear, force or coercion.  
</p>
<p id="p_8">             Plea of guilt of accused is accepted and accused is convicted
for offence u/s 28/112 D.P. Act.
</p>

               Arguments on the point of sentence heard.
</p>

28/112 D.P. Act IDSI 01 day.  Fine paid.  The premises should be locked if
there is no license. Bail Bond stands cancelled and Surety be discharged, if
any.  Original documents, if any, be returned to the concerned persons after
placing  the  photocopy  of  the  same  on  record.    File  be  consigned  to  the
Record Room after due compliance. 
</p>
