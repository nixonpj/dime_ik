                              B. A. No. 3639 of 2013
        Parmanad Singh                                  ..... ... Petitioner
                                     Versus
        The State of Jharkhand                            ..... ... Opposite Party
                                  --------
               CORAM       :   HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H. C. MISHRA
                                  ------
        For the Petitioner        :        Mr. Gaurav, Advocate
        For the State             :        A.P.P.
                                    ------

 2/ 26.04.2013</pre><p id="p_1">         Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned A.P.P. for the
        Prosecution.
</p><p id="p_2">                       The petitioner has been made accused for the offence under <a href="/doc/329571/" id="a_1">Section
        392</a> of the Indian Penal Code, in connection with Kolebira P.S. Case No. 47 of
        2012 corresponding to G.R. No. 239 of 2012.
</p><p id="p_3">                       The case relates to robbery.
</p><p id="p_4">                       In the facts of this case, I am inclined to enlarge the petitioner,
        Parmanad Singh, on bail. Accordingly, the petitioner, named above, is directed
        to be released on bail, on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand),
        with two sureties of the like amount each, to the satisfaction of learned Chief
        Judicial Magistrate, Simdega, in connection with Kolebira P.S. Case No. 47 of
        2012 corresponding to G.R. No. 239 of 2012.
</p>

<p id="p_5">                                                                  ( H. C. Mishra, J.)
R.Kr.
