          IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

              SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 558 of 2014

================================================================
           ASHISHBHAI BHARATBHAI MAKVANA....Petitioner(s)
                               Versus
         GUJARAT STATE ROAD TRANSPORT CORPORATION &amp;
                         2....Respondent(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
MR GK RATHOD, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
MR MUKESH H RATHOD, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
MR HARDIK C RAWAL, ADVOCATE for Respondent(s) No. 1 - 2
MR DM DEVNANI, ASSTT. GOVT. PLEADER for Respondent(s) No. 3
================================================================

         CORAM: HONOURABLE SMT. JUSTICE ABHILASHA
                KUMARI

                             Date : 30/01/2014


                               ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">   1.   In   this   petition,   under   <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article   226</a>   of   the 

        Constitution of India, the petitioner has, inter 

        alia,   prayed   for   the   issuance   of   a   Writ   of 

        Mandamus   or   any   other   appropriate   Writ   or 

        direction to the respondent - Gujarat State Road 

        Transport Corporation, to appoint the petitioner 

        on   compassionate   grounds   as   per   the   policy   of 

        the respondent - Corporation. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">   2.   At   the   outset,   Mr.Mukesh   H.Rathod,   learned 


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  Page 1 of 3</span>
       C/SCA/558/2014                              ORDER



     advocate   for   the   petitioner   and   Mr.Hardik 

     C.Rawal, learned advocate for respondents Nos.1 

     and 2, have jointly submitted that the present 

     petition is identical to the group of petitions, 

     being Special Civil Application No.3977 of 2013 

     and connected matters, which have been disposed 

     of   by   order   dated   30.11.2013;   therefore,   the 

     present petition may be disposed of in terms of 

     the said order. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">3.   Learned advocate for the petitioner is directed 

     to provide to the Registry, a copy of the common 

     oral order dated 30.11.2013, passed in Special 

     Civil Application No.3977 of 2013, and connected 

     matters, which shall be placed on the record of 

     the petition. 
</p>
<p id="p_6">
</p><p id="p_7">4.   Mr.D.M.Devnani,   learned   Assistant   Government 

     Pleader for respondent No.3 - State of Gujarat, 

     has also made submissions along the same lines.


</p><p id="p_8">5.   In   view   of   the   joint   request   made   by   learned 

     counsel   for   the   respective   parties,   the 

     following order is passed:
</p>
<p id="p_9">


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                           Page 2 of 3</span>
</p><p id="p_10">         C/SCA/558/2014                                  ORDER



           The petition is disposed of in light of the 

           clarifications,   observations   and   directions 

           contained   in   the   common   oral   order   dated 

           30.11.2013,   passed   in   Special   Civil 

           Application   No.3977   of   2013   and   connected 

           matters.   The   order   made   therein   and   the 

           directions contained in the aforesaid order 

           will   be   applicable   to   the   petitioner,   as 

           well, and his case shall be governed by the 

           said order. Notice is discharged. 




</p><p id="p_11">                                          (SMT. ABHILASHA KUMARI, J.)
