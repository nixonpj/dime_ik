<div class="doc_title">Rambabu Sah vs The State Of Bihar on 29 November, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.75682 of 2019
                       Arising Out of PS. Case No.-51 Year-2014 Thana- SAKRI District- Madhubani
                 ======================================================
                 Rambabu Sah Son of Dashrath Sah Resident of Village-Hariraha, P.S.-
                 Andharamath, District-Madhubani.

                                                                                   ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr. Amar Nath Yadav, Advocate
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr. Navin Kumar Pandey, A.P.P.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE JUSTICE SMT. ANJANA MISHRA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   29-11-2019                  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned

                   Additional Public Prosecutor on behalf of the State.

</p><p id="p_2">                                The petitioner is in custody since 27.05.2014, in

                   connection with Sakri P.S. Case No. 51 of 2014 corresponding

                   S.T. No. 46 of 2015, for the offences under <a href="/doc/1865807/" id="a_1">Sections 216</a>, <a href="/doc/94452/" id="a_2">414</a> of

                   I.P.C. and 25(1-b)a, 26, 35 of <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_3">Arms Act</a> and in the instant case

                   charge has been framed against the petitioner under <a href="/doc/468585/" id="a_4">Sections

                   400</a>, <a href="/doc/812083/" id="a_5">411</a>, <a href="/doc/1865807/" id="a_6">216(A)</a> of the Indian Penal Code and 25(1-AA) of the

                   Arms Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that in

                   view of the fact that the petitioner had been in custody in

                   connection with other cases, but since he has been released in

                   those cases only recently, the petitioner has now preferred the

                   present bail application. Learned counsel for the petitioner
             Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.75682 of 2019(3) dt.29-11-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                        2/2</span>




                      submits that as and when required the petitioner shall appear in

                      the Court below and cooperate in the Trial. It is further

                      submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioner that in the

                      instant case charges were framed only on 19.01.2016 and there

                      is no specific allegation against the present petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                                   In    view      of     the     aforementioned   facts   and

                      circumstances, let the petitioner, above named, be enlarged on

                      bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (Ten Thousand)

                      with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of

                      the learned Additional Sessions Judge, Jhanjharpur, in

                      connection with Sessions Trial No. 46 of 2015, arising out of

                      Sakri P.S. Case No. 51 of 2014.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                 (Anjana Mishra, J)
uma/-
</p>
