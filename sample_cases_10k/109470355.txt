                 ======================================================
                 1. Narendra Singh S/o Late Ram Gopal Singh
                 2. Yogendra Singh S/o Late Ram Gopal Singh

                 District- Rohtas.

                                                                              .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 1. The State of Bihar

                                                                   .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Jai Prakash Singh, Adv.

                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRABHAT KUMAR

                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   14-12-2016               Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             The petitioners apprehend their arrest in Kargahar P.S.

                 Case No. 277/2014, registered for the offences punishable under

                 Section 354 and other sections<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>.

</p><p id="p_3">                             The informant named the petitioners and other staff of

                 the bus and alleged that they were eve-teasing the informant and

                 other school-going girls.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                             Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that on the

                 same day the petitioner lodged Kargahar P.S. Case No. 275/14

                 against some boys of the same village who assaulted the conductor

                 and the driver of the bus. It is further submitted that the present

                 case has been lodged at the instance of those boys, but it appears
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.46839 of 2016 (2) dt.14-12-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                        that the victim is minor girl and she has made specific allegation

                        against the petitioners that the petitioners and other staff of the bus

                        were eve-teasing.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                     Considering the facts aforesaid, I am not inclined to

                        enlarge the petitioners above-named on anticipatory bail.

                        Accordingly, the same is rejected.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                                   (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J.)
Vinita/-
