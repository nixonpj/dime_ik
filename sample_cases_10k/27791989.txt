<div class="doc_title">Pritam Chandel S/O Shri Lallu Lal ... vs State Of Rajasthan Through Pp on 15 March, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">       HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN
                   BENCH AT JAIPUR

          S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail No. 2675/2019

Pritam Chandel S/o Shri Lallu Lal B/c Mali, Aged About 33 Years,
R/o 251, Kamla Nehru Nagar, Hasanpura-C, Jaipur Raj.
                                                                  ----Petitioner
                                   Versus
State Of Rajasthan Through Pp, Raj.
                                                                ----Respondent</pre>


<pre id="pre_2">For Petitioner(s)        :     Mr. Sanjay Joshi
For Respondent(s)        :     Mr. Sanjeev Kumar Mahla, PP



           HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE INDERJEET SINGH

                                    Order

15/03/2019


</pre><p id="p_1">1.   The present bail application has been filed under Section 438

Cr.P.C. in connection with FIR No.0083/2019 registered at Police

Station Sadar, Jaipur (West) for the offence(s) under Sections 420

and 406 of I.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_2">2.   Counsel for the petitioner submits that the complainant has

initiated criminal proceedings against the petitioner under <a href="/doc/1823824/" id="a_1">Section

138</a> of the Negotiable Instruments Act which are still pending.

Counsel further submits that the petitioner has been falsely

implicated in this matter.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.   Learned Public Prosecutor has opposed the bail application.

</p><p id="p_4">4.   Taking into account the facts and circumstances of the case

and without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, this

court deems it just and proper to allow the anticipatory bail

application.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                    (Downloaded on 29/06/2019 at 10:46:48 PM)</span>
</p><p id="p_6">                                                                              (2 of 2)                     [CRLMB-2675/2019]


</p><p id="p_7">                                   5.        Accordingly, the anticipatory bail application is allowed. The

                                   S.H.O/I.O/Arresting Officer, Police Station Sadar, Jaipur (West),

                                   Jaipur in F.I.R. No.0083/2019 is directed that in the event of

                                   arrest of the petitioner, he shall be released on bail, provided he

                                   furnishes a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000/- with two

                                   sureties in the sum of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the

                                   S.H.O/I.O/Arresting Officer of the concerned Police Station on the

                                   following conditions:-
</p>
<p id="p_8">
</p><p id="p_9">                                   (i)       that   the   petitioner      shall     make        himself     available   for

                                   interrogation by a police officer as and when required;


</p><p id="p_10">                                   (ii)      that the petitioner shall not directly or indirectly make any

                                   inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted with the

                                   facts of the case so as to dissuade him from disclosing such facts

                                   to the court or any police officer, and


</p><p id="p_11">                                   (iii)     that the petitioner shall not leave India without previous

                                   permission of the court.
</p>
<p id="p_12">


</p><p id="p_13">                                                                                                 (INDERJEET SINGH),J
