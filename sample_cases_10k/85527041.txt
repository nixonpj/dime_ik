                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

          SPECIAL CRIMINAL APPLICATION (PAROLE LEAVE) NO. 6404 of 2015

         ==========================================================
               NARAYANSHASHTRI GURUOMPRAKASHDASJI....Applicant(s)
                                   Versus
                     STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 2....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MS HANSA V PATEL, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No. 1
         MR LB DABHI APP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         ==========================================================

                CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.H.VORA

                                       Date : 30/10/2015


                                        ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.    Rule. Learned A.P.P. Mr.Dabhi waives service of Rule for
         the respondent - State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.    By way of the present petition, the petitioner - convict
         challenges the order dated 27.10.2015 whereby, his parole
         leave application for performing Babri function scheduled to be
         held from 04.11.2015 to 07.11.2015 has been refused on
         account      of     non-receipt      of         police    opinion          from        Police
         Commissioner, Vadodara City and the petitioner has enjoyed
         furlough leave in the month of March, 2015.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.    In Court's considered opinion, none of the reasons
         mentioned in the impugned order seem to be legal and
         genuine and, therefore, the competent authority is directed to
         release the convict on parole leave for the period from

HC-NIC                                     Page 1 of 2       Created On Sat Oct 31 03:00:03 IST 2015
                     R/SCR.A/6404/2015                                            ORDER




         03.11.2015 to 10.11.2015 on his furnishing personal bond of
         Rs.5000/- to the jail authority and on usual terms and
         conditions. The convict shall surrender before the jail authority
         immediately after completion of aforesaid period of parole
         leave.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.       With the above, present petition stand disposed of. Rule
         is made absolute to the aforesaid extent. Direct service is
         permitted.
</p>
