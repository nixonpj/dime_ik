GAHC010238622019




                               THE GAUHATI HIGH COURT
   (HIGH COURT OF ASSAM, NAGALAND, MIZORAM AND ARUNACHAL PRADESH)

                                     Case No. : AB 3328/2019

             1:MD. BAHARUL ISLAM
             S/O LATE ABDUL RAHIM, R/O VILL-DHING GAON, P.O.-DAGAON, P.S.-
             JARIA, DIST-NAGAON, ASSAM

             VERSUS

             1:THE STATE OF ASSAM
             (NOTICE THROUGH THE PUBLIC PROSECUTOR, ASSAM)

Advocate for the Petitioner    : MR. N DAS

Advocate for the Respondent : PP, ASSAM




                                     BEFORE
                    HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HITESH KUMAR SARMA

                                             ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">Date : 26-09-2019

This is an application, filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 438</a> Cr.PC, praying for pre-arrest bail of the
petitioner, namely, Md. Baharul Islam, in connection with Batadrava PS Case No.
290/2019 under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_2">Sections 420</a>/<a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_3">406</a>/<a href="/doc/1326844/" id="a_4">409</a> IPC.
</p><p id="p_2">Heard Mr. N. Das, learned counsel for the petitioner as well as Mr. H. Sarma, learned
Additional Public Prosecutor for the state respondent.
Perused the petition as well as the annexures furnished therewith.
On perusal of the FIR itself, this court is of the view that till a final decision is taken on this
application, on perusal of the materials in the case diary, the petitioner be allowed to remain
on interim pre-arrest bail till the date fixed.
Call for the case diary.
</p><p id="p_3">List the matter on 13.11.2019.
</p><p id="p_4">The petitioner shall appear before the investigating police officer within 7 (seven) days from
                                                                                      Page No.# 2/2

today, and, in the event of his arrest, he shall be released on furnishing a bail bond of Rs.
15,000/- with one suitable surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the arresting
authority, on the following conditions:
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                    (1)       The petitioner shall not leave the territorial jurisdiction of the
                    aforesaid police station, without prior written permission from its officer-in-
                    charge,
                    (2)       The petitioner shall not hamper with the investigation, or tamper
                    with the evidence of the case, and
                    (3)       The petitioner shall not, directly or indirectly, make any
                    inducement, threat or promise to any person acquainted with the facts of
                    the case so as to dissuade him from disclosing such facts to the Court or
                    to any police officer.</blockquote>




<p id="p_5">                                                                        JUDGE



Comparing Assistant
