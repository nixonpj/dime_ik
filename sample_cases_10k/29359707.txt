<div class="doc_title">Des Raj vs State Of Punjab And Others on 7 January, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                            Criminal Misc. No. M-53758 of 2018 (O&amp;M)
                            Date of decision : January 07, 2019


Des Raj                                                ....Petitioner
                            versus

State of Punjab and others                                     ....Respondents


Coram:        Hon'ble Mr. Justice Fateh Deep Singh


Present :     Mr. Rohan Sharma, Advocate, for the petitioner
              Mr. Saurav Khurana, DAG, Punjab, for the State


Fateh Deep Singh, J. (Oral)
</pre>

<p id="p_1">              On hearing counsel for both the sides and perusal of records,

the present petition stands disposed of with the directions to the official

respondents to ensure protection of life and personal liberty of the petitioner

and his family members at the hands of private respondents no. 4 to 7 and

to ensure impartial investigation into the FIR preferably by an officer of

adequate experience and to ensure that the culprits so responsible are

proceeded against in accordance with law.                   The needful be done

expeditiously preferably within a period of 15 days of the receipt of certified

copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_2">              Petition stands disposed of.</p><pre id="pre_2">


                                                        ( Fateh Deep Singh )
