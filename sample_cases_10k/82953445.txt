<div class="doc_title">Rauf &amp; Ors vs State on 8 September, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                  [1]

      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR
                 RAJASTHAN AT JODHPUR
     --------------------------------------------------------


CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL (CRLMB) No. 8009 of 2016


1.      Rauf S/o Nisar Ahmad @ Babu Khan

2.      Insaf S/o Nisar Ahmad @ Babu Khan

3.      Sajid S/o Nisar Ahmad @ Babu Khan

4.      Fazlu Khan S/o Nisar Ahmad @ Babu Khan

     All b/c Musalman (Siphai), residents of Merta City,
Tehsil Merta, District Nagour (Raj.)
                                  Vs.
The State of Rajasthan


        Date of Order         :          08.09.2016


           HON'BLE MS. NIRMALJIT KAUR, J.

Mr. Ranjeet Joshi, for the petitioners
Mr. K.V. Vyas, Public Prosecutor, for the State
Mr. S. Ali, for the complainant.


                             ORDER
</pre><p id="p_1">                             --------
</p>
<p id="p_2">        The present bail application has been filed under

<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of Cr.P.C. The petitioners have been

arrested in connection with FIR No.272/2016, Police

Station Merta City, District Nagour for the offence

under <a href="/doc/513777/" id="a_2">Section 451</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_5">307</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> IPC.

</p><p id="p_3">        It was a case of free fight. Both sides have

received injuries. The injuries are stated to be simple
                                      [2]

          in nature. A cross case has also been registered. Thus,

          it is yet to be established as to which side is aggressor.

</p><p id="p_4">               Taking into account the facts and circumstances of

          the case, this Court deems it just and proper to release

          the petitioners on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_5">               Accordingly, the bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_7">Section 439</a>

          of Cr.P.C. is allowed and it is ordered that the accused-

          petitioners (1) Rauf S/o Nisar Ahmad @ Babu Khan (2)

          Insaf S/o Nisar Ahmad @ Babu Khan (3) Sajid S/o

          Nisar Ahmad @ Babu Khan (4) Fazlu Khan S/o Nisar

          Ahmad @ Babu Khan shall be enlarged on bail provided

          they furnish a personal bond in the sum of Rs.

          50,000/- each with two sureties of Rs. 25,000/- each to

          the satisfaction of the learned trial Judge for their

          appearance before the court concerned on all the dates

          of hearing as and when called upon to do so.


</p><p id="p_6">                                           (NIRMALJIT KAUR ),J.
</p>
<p id="p_7">


praveen
