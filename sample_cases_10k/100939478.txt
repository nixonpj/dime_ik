<div class="doc_title">Ch.Satyanarayana, Kukatpally, ... vs State Of Ap., Rep. Pp., And Anr., on 8 August, 2018</div><div class="doc_bench">Bench: U.Durga Prasad Rao</div>

<pre id="pre_1">      HON'BLE SRI JUSTICE U.DURGA PRASAD RAO


           CRIMINAL PETITION No.4484 OF 2013

ORDER:
</pre>
<p id="p_1">      In this petition filed under <a href="/doc/1233094/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., the

petitioner seeks to quash the proceedings against him in

C.C.No.456 of 2011 on the file of VII Chief Metropolitan

Magistrate, Hyderabad.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">2.    When the matter came up for hearing, this Court called

for information as to the present stage of C.C.No.456 of 2011.

Accordingly, the Metropolitan Sessions Judge, Hyderabad, in

his    letter   in    Dis.No.11884/MSJ/HYD/2018,           dated

04.08.2018, submitted information to the effect that the said

case was disposed of on 20.02.2013.


</p><p id="p_4">3.    In that view, this criminal petition which was filed

against C.C.No.456 of 2011 has become infructuous and it is

accordingly dismissed as infructuous.


</p><p id="p_5">      As a sequel, miscellaneous petitions pending, if any, shall

stand closed.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                   ______________________________
                                    U.DURGA PRASAD RAO, J
08.08.2018
SS
