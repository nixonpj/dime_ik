                                                  Versus

                                                      .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                 SINGH
                                  ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   11-01-2013                    Petitioners are apprehending their arrest in a

                 case registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_3">341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_5">324</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_6">307</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_7">379</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_8">504</a> of the I.P.C.

</p><p id="p_2">                                  Accusation of assault on the                head      of the

                 informant by farsa is against Mukesh Giri and when the

                 daughter of the informant came then she was also assaulted

                 with lathi and danda and Rs. 3000/- of the informant was

                 snatched by petitioner Ramesh Giri.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                  It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioners that injuries of the informant and his daughter have

                 been found to be abrasion, simple, caused by hard and blunt

                 substance which negates the accusation made in the F.I.R.

</p><p id="p_4">                                  Considering      the     aforesaid     facts,       let    the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> 2           Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.49201 of 2012 (3) dt.11-01-2013</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                     2/2</span>




                     petitioners, above named, except petitioner No. 1 namely

                     Lalbabu Giri, in the event of their arrest or surrender before the

                     Court below within a period of 12 weeks from today, be

                     released on anticipatory bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.

                     10,000/-(ten thousand) each with two sureties of the like

                     amount each to the satisfaction of learned Chief Judicial

                     Magistrate, Gopalganj in connection with Mohammadpur P.S.

                     Case No. 64 of 2012.
</p>
<p id="p_5">


</p><p id="p_6">                                               (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
Shageer/-
</p>
