<p id="p_1">           The petitioner has filed Exts.P4 and P5 appeals against

Exts.P2 and P3 orders of assessment under the Kerala Value

Added Tax Act, 2003. The appeals are accompanied by Exts.P6

and P7 petitions for stay of recovery of the disputed tax. The

petitioner is aggrieved by the coercive steps threatened during

the pendency of the appeals and the petitions afore-stated.

</p><p id="p_2">           2. I direct the second respondent to consider Exts.P6

and P7 petitions with notice to the petitioner within a period of

one month.     The coercive steps pursuant to Exts.P2 and P3

orders shall be put on hold till orders are passed on Exts.P6 and

P7 petitions as directed above.
</p>
<p id="p_3">           3.   The petitioner shall produce a copy of the writ

petition with the judgment before the second respondent for

compliance.
</p>
<p id="p_4">           The Writ Petition is disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><p id="p_6">                                            V.CHITAMBARESH,
                                                  Judge.
