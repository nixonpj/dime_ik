<div class="doc_title">State vs . Joginder Singh on 9 November, 2011</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Mr.Devender Nain</div>

<pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE COURT OF SH. DEVENDER NAIN
       METROPOLITAN MAGISTRATE: NORTH足06, DELHI


                           FIR No. 338/1999


Case ID: R085895/2005
State Vs. Joginder Singh
PS Lahori Gate
U/s 420/468/471 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> 


Date of Institution of case              :     02.05.2000



Date of Judgment                         :     09.11.2011


JUDGMENT:
</pre>


<pre id="pre_2">a)     Date of offence                   :     10.06.1999

b)     Offence complained of             :     420/468/471 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">IPC</a>

c)     Name of Accused, his              :     Joginder Singh,
       parentage &amp; residence                   S/o Sh. Hawa Singh,
                                               R/o H. No. 193/1, Gali 
                                               No.   8,   Jeewan   Park,  
                                               Shrispur, Delhi

d)     Plea of Accused                   :     Pleaded guilty

FIR No. 338/99                                                           1 of 3
PS Lahori Gate
 e)     Final order                                   :       Convicted

</pre><p id="p_1">BRIEF FACTS AND REASONS FOR DECISION:
</p>
<p id="p_2">                 Accused   submits   that   he   wishes   to   plead   guilty   to   the 

offence punishable u/s 420/468/471 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3">IPC</a>.  The consequences of pleading 

guilty have been explained to the accused.  Accused submits that he is 

pleading guilty without any fear, force or coercion and further submits 

that   lenient   view   may   be   taken   against   him.   Separate   statement   of 

accused recorded in this regard.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                 In view of the statement made by the accused, I am satisfied 

that accused is pleading guilty without any force, pressure or coercion. 

Accordingly,   accused   is   hereby   convicted   for   offence   punishable 

420/468/471 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_4">IPC</a>.</p><pre id="pre_3">




Announced in the Open Court                                 (DEVENDER NAIN)
       th
on 09  day of November, 2011                     MM(NORTH足06):DELHI




FIR No. 338/99                                                                          2 of 3
PS Lahori Gate
 ORDER ON SENTENCE 

</pre><p id="p_4">                 Arguments on the point of sentence heard on behalf of both 

the sides and I have perused the record of the case and considering the 

age of convict  and  the fact  that  convict  belongs  to the poor strata of 

society and only earning member of his family and family members will 

suffer   great   hardship   if   hard   view   is   taken   against   him,   I   am   of   the 

opinion that the sentence for the period already undergone will meet the 

ends of justice.  I accordingly sentence the convict Joginder Singh, S/o 

Sh. Hawa Singh for the period already undergone alongwith fine of Rs.

5000/足   IDSI   05   months   with   TRC.     Fine   paid.     Bail   Bond   stands 

cancelled and Surety be discharged, if any.  Original documents, if any, 

be returned to the rightful person against receiving and after cancellation 

of endorsement, if any.  
</p>
<p id="p_5">                 File be consigned to the Record Room after due compliance.</p><pre id="pre_4">




Announced in the Open Court                                    (DEVENDER NAIN)
on 09th day of November, 2011                     MM(NORTH足06):DELHI



FIR No. 338/99                                                                               3 of 3
PS Lahori Gate
