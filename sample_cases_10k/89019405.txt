<pre id="pre_1">                                     APO No. 23 OF 2011
                                            WITH
                                        WP 1050 of 2010
                             IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                                  Civil Appellate Jurisdiction
                                       ORIGINAL SIDE

                                    THE SUN BATTERY &amp; CO.

                                                Versus

                                    CESC LIMITED &amp; ORS.

  BEFORE:

  The Hon'ble JUSTICE KALYAN JYOTI SENGUPTA

  The Hon'ble JUSTICE ASIM KUMAR RAY

  Date : 14th March, 2011.

                                                             Mr.Manabendra Thakur, Adv.
                                                             Mr. S.K. Ghosh, Adv.
                                                                 .....for appellant.
                                                             Mr. R.M. Rakshit, Adv.
                                                                ......for respdt. 3.

              The Court :- The CESC Limited has submitted a report suggesting amongst
others the space occupied by the appellant/tenant is about 2 meter x 2 meter. Furthermore,
this space was not suitable from technical point of view because there appears the place is
prone to fire hazards since the unit deals with battery manufacturing chemicals and raw
materials. This portion of the report has not been denied and disputed by any of the parties.
However, the extent of dispute is with regard to the measurement of this space occupied by
Appellant/tenant.     The Landlord in this affidavit described the area would be around
22'(length) x 16' (width). This statement made in paragraph 2 has been denied and disputed
by the appellant/tenant. In view of this contention raised by the appellant we think the better
course of action is to appoint a surveyor engineer who will go to the spot and take
measurement of the area occupied by the tenants.
              Accordingly, Mr. Sarbajit Datta of 37/C Pratapaditya Place, Kolkata 700026
who is an enlisted civil engineer in the list prepared and maintained by Registrar, Original
Side is appointed Special Officer to take measurement of the tenanted room in question and
submit a report in a sealed cover without disclosing the result of measurement to any of the
parties.    The     remuneration,   cost   of   such     measurement   shall   be   borne   by   the
                                                2


landlord/respondent at the first instance. It is made clear if it is found that an area suggested</pre>
<p id="p_1">by the Landlord is abnormally exaggerated while noting the report of the surveyor engineer
concerned then this cost has to be borne by the Landlord alone and in case of the Landlord's
contention appears to be reasonably correct then the cost of the surveyor engineer shall be
borne by other than landlord in accordance with as may be determined by this Court later.
</p><p id="p_2">              The corrected report given by the CESC Limited is kept on record.
              This matter is adjourned for one week.
</p><p id="p_3">              All parties including the Special Officer, surveyor engineer concerned, so
appointed are to act on a photostat signed copy of this order on the usual undertakings.
</p>



<p id="p_4">                                                    (KALYAN JYOTI SENGUPTA, J.)



                                                         (ASIM KUMAR RAY, J.)


GH.
</p>
