<p id="p_6">2.	Considering
the impugned judgment, it appears that the learned trial Judge has
granted decree as prayed for by the plaintiff only on the strength of
oral evidence and there is no documentary evidence placed on record
to substantiate the misappropriation of the amount.
</p> 


 


 


<p id="p_7">3.	In
view of this position, interim relief in terms of para 7(A) is
granted on condition that the appellant shall furnish the solvent
surety of amount of Rs.4 Lacs to the satisfaction of the trial Court,
within two months from today.
</p> 


<p id="p_8">	Rule
