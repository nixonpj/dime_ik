<div class="doc_title">The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors vs Indra Kumar Mishra &amp; Ors. on 24 September, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">      Patna High Court C. REV. No.200 of 2014 (2) dt.24-09-2014




                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                         Civil Review No.200 of 2014
                                                      In
                                Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No. 22339 of 2013
                   ======================================================
                   The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors
                                                                       .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                    Versus
                   Indra Kumar Mishra &amp; Ors.
                                                                      .... .... Respondent/s
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE HEMANT KUMAR
                   SRIVASTAVA
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   24-09-2014                   Earlier this review petition was taken up with MJC No.

                   2047 of 2014 and it was argued on behalf of the State that the

                   similar question, involved in CWJC No. 22339 of 2013, was

                   decided in CWJC No. 3890 of 2006 and on the above-said

                   argument, this court directed the learned counsel of the State to

                   bring on record the order, passed in CWJC No. 3890 of 2006 but

                   today, learned counsel, appearing for the State fairly conceded to

                   this effect that the issue, involved in CWJC No. 3890 of 2006 was

                   not similar to the issue, involved in CWJC No. 22339 of 2013.

</p><p id="p_2">                                 Admittedly, the State wants modification of interim

                   order dated 01-04-2014 passed in CWJC No. 22339 of 2013 on

                   the ground that the aforesaid interim order decides the merit of

                   above-said CWJC No. 22339 of 2013 but from perusal of above-

                   said interim order dated 01-04-2014 passed in CWJC No. 22339

                   of 2013, it appears that the aforesaid order has been passed on the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court C. REV. No.200 of 2014 (2) dt.24-09-2014</span>




                        basis of ratio, given in LPA No. 311 of 2013, decided by Hon'ble

                        Jharkhand High court on 26-03-2014. Therefore, there is an

                        authority of Hon'ble Jharkhand High Court on the issue, involved

                        in CWJC No. 22339 of 2013 and the State could not show any

                        contrary decision to the ratio, laid down by Hon'ble Jharkhand

                        High Court in above-said LPA No. 311 of 2013.

</p><p id="p_3">                                      In the aforesaid circumstances, in my view, there is no

                        need to modify the order dated 01-04-2014 passed in CWJC No.

                        22339 of 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                      Accordingly, this review petition stands dismissed.

</p><p id="p_5">                                              (Hemant Kumar Srivastava, J)
A.K.V./-
</p>
