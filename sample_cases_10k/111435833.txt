<p id="p_1">03. 22.08.2014            The   Petitioners    seek   quashing     of   the   entire

                   proceeding   including     the   order   of   cognizance   dated

                   09.12.2013 passed by the Judicial Magistrate, Rohtas at

                   Sasaram in Complaint Case No. 1222 of 2012.

</p><p id="p_2">                           It is admitted by the parties that a series of

                   criminal cases have been filed by them on account of a

                   land dispute for which a Title Suit is pending and now they

                   agree that they will sort out the differences in the Civil

                   Suit. By such understanding, they agree that all the

                   criminal cases be quashed.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">       Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.6476 of 2014 (3) dt.22-08-2014                     2</span><p id="p_3">




</p><p id="p_4">                                    In view of such, the application is allowed and the

                         entire proceeding including the order of cognizance dated

                         09.12.2013 passed by the Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class,

                         Rohtas at Sasaram in Complaint Case No. 1222 of 2012

                         and Complaint Case No. 1209 of 2012 dated 09.11.2012 as

                         also Chenari P.S. Case No. 146 of 2013 pending before the

                         respective Magistrates, Rohtas are, hereby, set aside.

</p><p id="p_5">                                    The Application stands allowed with the aforesaid

                         observations.
</p>
<p id="p_6">


</p><p id="p_7">                                                               (Anjana Prakash, J.)
