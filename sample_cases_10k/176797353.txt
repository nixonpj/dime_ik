<div class="doc_title">Kotak Nilambahen Shaileshkumar vs Regional Passport Officer &amp; on 20 March, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                   C/SCA/5740/2017                                                   ORDER



                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

                      SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 5740 of 2017
         ===========================================================
                 KOTAK NILAMBAHEN SHAILESHKUMAR....Petitioner(s)
                                     Versus
                 REGIONAL PASSPORT OFFICER &amp; 1....Respondent(s)
         ===============================================================
         Appearance:
         MR MANISH J PATEL, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
         MR KSHITIJ AMIN, CENTRAL GOVT. COUNSEL for the Respondents
         ===============================================================
          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.M.CHHAYA

                                          Date : 20/03/2017


                                           ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.      Heard Mr.Manish J. Patel, learned counsel for the
         petitioner and Mr.Kshitij Amin, learned Central Government
         Counsel for the respondents on advance copy.
</p>

<p id="p_2">         2.      At the outset, learned counsel for the petitioner
         seeks    permission         to   delete            respondent        No.2,        which         is
         granted. Respondent No.2 stands deleted.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         3.      By way of this petition under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the
         Constitution        of      India,      the        petitioner        has      prayed        for
         appropriate        writ,      order        or       direction,          directing           the
         respondent - Passport Authority to change the birth date
         in the passport from 16.08.1972 to 17.08.1972 and to issue
         fresh passport to the petitioner.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         4.      It is a matter of record that the petitioner was
         granted passport being No.E 6 2 7 6 7 8 0, which was valid
         upto 11.09.2013. In the said passport, date of birth is



<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                 Page 1 of 2</span>

HC-NIC                                        Page 1 of 2      Created On Tue Mar 21 00:46:49 IST 2017
                     C/SCA/5740/2017                                                    ORDER




         mentioned as 16.08.1972, instead of 17.08.1972.
</p>


<p id="p_5">         5.       It appears from the record of the petition that the
         petitioner        has    filed       an    application            to     the      respondent
         passport Authority on 17.06.2016. The petitioner relies
         upon the birth certificate issued by Ahmedabad Municipal
         Corporation, wherein date of birth of the petitioner is
         mentioned as 17.08.1972.
</p>

<p id="p_6">         6.       In light of the aforesaid and considering the fact
         that the application filed by the petitioner is pending
         since long, the petitioner shall file fresh representation
         to   the        respondent       -        Passport         Authority           along         with
         certified copy of birth certificate issued by Ahmedabad
         Municipal Corporation as well as any other authenticated
         documents, showing date of birth as 17.08.1972. If any
         such representation/application is filed, the same shall
         be considered by the Passport Authority as per the new
         Passport Rules, which has come into force from 26.12.2016
         and pass appropriate order upon the application filed by
         the petitioner, as expeditiously as possible, preferably
         within a period of 45 days from the date of filing of such
         representation.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                  With    the     above       observations            and       directions,            the
         petition stands disposed of. No costs.
</p>

<p id="p_8">                  Direct Service is permitted.
</p>
