27    19.04.2013</pre><p id="p_1">                         W. P. 10213 (W) of 2013
rrc                            (Kalipada Manna &amp; Anr. Vs. The State of West
                                           Bengal &amp; Ors.)

                            Mr. Prabir Kumar Mitra
                            Ms. Sujasha Mukherji
                                                 .....For the petitioners

                            Mr. Suman Sengupta
                                                    .....For the State


                            Mr. Mitra, learned advocate for the petitioners submits

                   that the first petitioner was illegally detained in custody.

                   According to him, the accused is one Kalipada Mallik and not

                   the first petitioner (Kalipada Manna). He has prayed for stay of

                   trial.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                            I am not inclined to grant any order of stay considering

                   the order passed by the Division Bench of this Court dated

                   11th February, 2013. While praying for bail, the same

                   contention was urged on behalf of the first petitioner. The

                   learned Judges of the Division Bench did not accept such

                   contention. Propriety demands that at least at this stage this

                   Court passes an order which is consistent with the order of

                   the Division Bench. The prayer for interim order is refused.

</p><p id="p_3">                            However, the writ petition shall be heard on affidavits.

                            Let affidavit-in-opposition be filed by 17th May, 2013;

                   reply thereto, if any, be filed by 3rd June, 2013.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                       -:2:-</span>




      Put up the writ petition for final disposal in the Monthly

List of June, 2013.
</p>
<p id="p_4">


</p><p id="p_5">                                      ( Dipankar Datta, J. )
