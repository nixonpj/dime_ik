<div class="doc_title">Zulfikar Ali vs State Of Uttarakhand And Others on 12 April, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">WPCRL No. 330 of 2018
Hon'ble V.K. Bist, J.
</pre><p id="p_1">      There is no representation for the
petitioner
      Mr. Pramod Tiwari, Brief Holder
for the State of Uttarakhand.
</p><p id="p_2">      Petitioner has approached this
Court seeking the following reliefs:
</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">        "I Issue a writ order or direction in the
        nature of certiorari quashing the impugned
        Arising out of First Information Report No.
        0095 of 2018, dated 18-02-2018 U/S 363,
        366, 323, 506 of <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">I.P.C</a>., P.S. Vikas Nagar,
        and District Dehradun (Annexure No. 4 to
        the writ petition).
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">        II.    Issue a writ order or direction in the
        nature of mandamus staying the arrest of
        the petitioner in First Information Report
        No. 0095 of 2018, dated 18-02-2018 U/S
        363, 366, 323, 506 of <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_2">I.P.C</a>., P.S. Vikas
        Nagar, and District Dehradun (Annexure
        No. 4 to the writ petition) and also not to
        take coercive measures against the
        petitioners during the pendency of the
        present writ petition before this Hon'ble
        Court ."
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">2.         Learned     Brief    Holder
appearing for the State submitted that
the final report has been filed in the
matter.
</blockquote><p id="p_3">3.        In view of the above, this
criminal writ petition has rendered
infructuous and the same is dismissed
as such.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   (V.K. Bist, J.)
                                    12.04.2018
Navin
