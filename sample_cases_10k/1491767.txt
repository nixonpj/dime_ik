<div class="doc_title">Sreekumar vs The Station House Officer on 12 February, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">       

  

  

 
 
  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

Bail Appl..No. 340 of 2009()


1. SREEKUMAR, S/O.RAMAN, AGED 33 YEARS,
                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs



1. THE STATION HOUSE OFFICER,
                       ...       Respondent

                For Petitioner  :SRI.P.A.MUJEEB

                For Respondent  :PUBLIC PROSECUTOR

The Hon'ble MRS. Justice K.HEMA

 Dated :12/02/2009

 O R D E R
                          K. HEMA, J.
         =-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
                      B.A. No. 340 of 2009
         =-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
           Dated this the 12thday of February, 2009

                            O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">       Petition for bail.
</p>
<p id="p_2">       2. The alleged offences are under sections 364, 395, 120

</p><p id="p_3">(b) and 411 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a>.        According to    prosecution, accused in

pursuance of a criminal conspiracy to commit decoity, took de

facto complainant by force into a car, which was waiting outside

the railway station, and committed decoity of 9 k. gms. of gold.

</p><p id="p_4">       3. Petitioner is 3rd accused. He was arrested on 19-11-

2008 and he is in custody for the past more than 53 days.

Learned Public Prosecutor submitted that he has no objection in

granting bail on conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_5">       4. On hearing both sides, I am satisfied that bail can be

granted to petitioner on conditions.
</p>
<p id="p_6">       5. Hence, petitioner shall be released on bail on his

executing a bond for Rs.50,000/- with two solvent sureties each

for the like amount to the satisfaction of the learned Magistrate,

on the following conditions:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">             1)   Petitioner   shall  report    before  the

                  Investigating Officer on every alternate

                  day between    10 a.m. and 1 p.m. until

                  further orders.
</blockquote>
<pre id="pre_2">BA 340/09                       -2-

          2)    Petitioner shall not leave the limits of the

</pre><blockquote id="blockquote_2">                police station within which the crime is

                registered    except     with   the    prior

                permission of the learned Magistrate.

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">          3)    Petitioner shall not intimidate or influence

                any witness or tamper with evidence or

                commit any offence while on bail and in

                case breach of this condition, bail is liable

                to be cancelled.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">     This petition is allowed.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_5">

</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_6">                                           K.HEMA, JUDGE.
</blockquote>
