                                               Date of Decision : May 16, 2011


Jatinder @ Jeetu                                              ...... Petitioner
                                     Versus
State of Haryana                                             ...... Respondent

<p id="p_1">CORAM : HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ALOK SINGH
</p><p id="p_2">1. Whether reporters of local news papers may be allowed to see
   judgement ?
</p><p id="p_3">2. To be referred to reporters or not ?
</p><p id="p_4">3. Whether the judgement should be reported in the Digest ?

Present :    Ms. Amarjit Kaur Khurana, Advocate,
             for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">             Ms. Preeti Chaudhary, AAG, Haryana.

</p><p id="p_6">                                   ****

Alok Singh, J (Oral)



             This is an application seeking bail in FIR No. 596 dated

9.10.2010, under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a>/<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_2">120-B</a> of Indian Penal Code, registered at

Police Station Hansi, District Hisar.

</p><p id="p_7">             Learned counsel for the petitioner states that co-accused

Jitender @ Jeetu and Ranjeet Singh have already been enlarged on bail in

the present case by the Co-ordinate Bench of this Court. She further states

that petitioner is similarly situated accused. She has further stated that in the

present case charges have already been framed and trial is not likely to be

concluded in near future and offences are triable by the Magistrate.

</p><p id="p_8">             Considering totality of the facts and circumstances of the case,

the present petition is directed to be allowed. Let petitioner be released on

bail to the satisfaction of the Trial Court.


</p><p id="p_9">                                                       (Alok Singh)
                                                         Judge
May 16, 2011
Anand
