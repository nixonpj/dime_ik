IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS

Dated:  18.4.2018

CORAM

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE N.SATHISH KUMAR

O.P.NO.248 of 2018



Sir John Demonte Trust 
Rep. by its sole Trustee,
Most Reve. Dr.George Antonysamy,
Archbishop of Madras - Mylapore,
Archbishop's House No.41, 
Santhome High Road,
Chennai 600 004 rep. by his
Power of Attorney					  .. Petitioner										
										                   

		This petition is is filed under Section 7 of the Chartitable and <a href="/doc/70224545/" id="a_1">Religious Trust Act</a>, 1920 read with Order XIV Rule 8 of the O.S. Rules, in the interest of justice and equity, to approve the scheme framed by the petitioner Trust in paragraph 10 to 12 to lease the property namely, land in extent of 11.65 grounds 27.939 sq.ft. situated at Block No.86, S.No.3923, Benz Garden at Boat Club road, R.A.Puram, Chennai 600 028 with a frontage of 31'4'' morefully described in the schedule of the petition, as per the aims and objects and the purpose for which the Trust was created and as per the order passed by the Supreme Court, dated 14.12.2011 in Civil Appeal bearing No.3052/2006, by (a) calling for public offers by placing advertisement in newspaper; (b) to scrutinize the offers inconsultation with the property consultant appointed by the petitioner and select the most advantageous offer, in the best comprehensive interest of the petitioner and  (c) to execute rgistered lease deed with the prospective lessee.
 		For  Petitioner	    :  Mrs.Auxilia Peter
		
					****

O R D E R
</pre>
<p id="p_1">		That the petitioner herein be and is hereby permitted to approve the scheme framed by the petitioner trust in paragraph 10 to 12 to to lease the property namely, land in extent of 11.65 grounds 27.939 sq.ft. situated at Block No.86, S.No.3923, Benz Garden at Boat Club road, R.A.Puram, Chennai 600 028 with a frontage of 31'4'' morefully described in the schedule of the petition, as per the aims and objects and the purpose for which the Trust was created and as per the order passed by the Supreme Court, dated 14.12.2011 in Civil Appeal bearing No.3052/2006, by (a) calling for public offers by placing advertisement in newspaper; (b) to scrutinize the offers inconsultation with the property consultant appointed by the petitioner and select the most advantageous offer, in the best comprehensive interest of the petitioner and  (c) to execute rgistered lease deed with the prospective lessee.
</p>
<p id="p_2">		2.  That the petitioner Trust shall adhere to the procedures contemplated under law, while carrying out the tasks.

N.SATHISH KUMAR,  J.
