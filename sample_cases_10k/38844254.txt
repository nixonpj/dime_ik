<div class="doc_title">V.Natarajan vs The Tahsildar on 3 August, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               

DATED: 03.08.2017  

CORAM   

THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE K.KALYANASUNDARAM                

Writ Petition(MD)No.14523 of 2017 

V.Natarajan                                             
                                                                        : Petitioner
-Vs-


1.The Tahsildar,
   Ponnamaravathi Taluk,
   Pudukkottai District.

2.The Executive Officer,
   Sri Konnaiyur Muthu mariyamman Temple,  
   Ponnamaravathi Taluk,
   Konnaiyur, Pudukkottai District.                             
                                                                        : Respondents 


PRAYER:  Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India, to
issue a Writ of Mandamus, directing the respondents 1 and 2 herein to
consider the petitioner's representation dated 05.06.2017 to forthwith permit
the petitioner and his family members in their individual capacity to worship
their family deity A/m.Koppudaiyamman in Sri Koppudaiyamman Temple, situated   
in Konapattu Village, Konapattu Circle, Thirumayam Taluk, Pudukkottai
District, for cooking pongal and sacrificing animals as per their family
customs. 

!For Petitioner                 : Mr.S.Srinivasaraghavan

For Respondents                         : Mr.G.Muthukannan  
                                  Government Advocate (For R1) 
                                : Mr.G.Mathavan (For R2) 



:ORDER  
</pre><p id="p_1">        This writ petition is filed seeking for the issuance of a Writ of
Mandamus, directing the respondents 1 and 2 herein to consider the
petitioner's representation dated 05.06.2017 to forthwith permit the
petitioner and his family members in their individual capacity to worship
their family deity A/m.Koppudaiyamman in Sri Koppudaiyamman Temple, situated   
in Konapattu Village, Konapattu Circle, Thirumayam Taluk, Pudukkottai
District, for cooking pongal and sacrificing animals as per their family
customs. 
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2.Mr.G.Muthukannan, learned Government Advocate takes notice for the 
first respondent.  Mr.G.Mathavan, learned counsel takes notice for the
second respondent.  By consent, this Writ Petition is taken up for final
disposal at the admission stage itself.
</p>
<p id="p_3">        3.The learned counsel appearing for the petitioner would submit that it
would suffice if a direction is issued to the second respondent to consider
the representation of the petitioner, dated 05.06.2017.
</p>
<p id="p_4">        4.Considering the limited scope of the prayer sought for by the
petitioner, this Court, without going into the merits of the matter, directs
the second respondent to consider the representation of the petitioner dated
05.06.2017 and pass appropriate orders on merits and in accordance with law,
after providing opportunity to all necessary parties, as expeditiously as
possible, preferably, within a period of four weeks from the date of receipt
of a copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_5">        5.With the above direction, this Writ Petition stands disposed of.  No
costs.          
</p>        



<p id="p_6">To
</p><p id="p_7">1.The Tahsildar,
   Ponnamaravathi Taluk,
   Pudukkottai District.
</p>
<p id="p_8">2.The Executive Officer,
   Sri Konnaiyur Muthu mariyamman Temple,  
   Ponnamaravathi Taluk,
   Konnaiyur, Pudukkottai District.
</p>
