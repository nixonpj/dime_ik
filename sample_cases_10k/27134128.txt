     IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

               MISC.CIVIL APPLICATION
           (FOR CONTEMPT) NO. 140 of 2014
                          In
      SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 8576 of 2008

======================================
RANJANBEN MANUBHAI MEHTA WIDOW OF LATE MANUBHAI &amp;
                      4....Applicant(s)
                            Versus
  A M TIWARI - PRINCIPAL SECRETARY &amp; 4....Respondent(s)
======================================
Appearance:
MR BIREN A VAISHNAV, ADVOCATE for the Applicant(s) No.1- 5
MR DHAWAN JAYSWAL, AGP for the Respondent(s)1-5
======================================

       CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.R. SHAH
              and
              HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE R.P.DHOLARIA

                          Date : 28/03/2014

                  ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1"> (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE M.R. SHAH)


</p><p id="p_2">1.    Shri Biren Vaishnav, learned advocate appearing on
behalf of the applicants has stated at the bar that so far as the
benefit of higher pay scale/higher grade scale is concerned,
the same has been paid to applicants nos.1, 2, 4 and 5 and on
the basis of the same now the revised pension is to be fixed
and the retirement benefits are required to be calculated. It is
stated that so far as applicant no. 3 is concerned, who is the
heir of the original employee, the benefit of higher pay
scale/higher grade scale has not been given and the arrears is
not paid.
</p>

<p id="p_3">2.    Shri Dhawan Jayswal, learned AGP, under instructions


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                               Page 1 of 2</span>
           C/MCA/140/2014                                     ORDER




from Shri Suresh Kumar L. Dodia, District Education Officer,
Bhavnagar, has stated at the bar that so far as the benefit of
higher pay scale/higher grade scale to applicant no. 3, being
the heir of the original employee-Natverlal Damodar Bhatt, is
concerned, the same shall be paid with arrears within a period
of ten days from today. He has also stated at the bar that so
far as the revised pension and the arrears of revised pension
and the retirement benefits due and payable to the respective
applicants are concerned, the order passed by this Court in
Miscellaneous        Civil   Application        No.   2579/2013   shall   be
complied with.
</p>

<p id="p_4">3.     Concerned respondents are directed to act accordingly
and is directed to see to it that the respective applicants are
paid the arrears of the revised pension/retirement benefits
within a period of one month from today and so far as arrears
of higher grade scale/higher pay scale to applicant no. 3 is
concerned, the same shall be paid within a period of ten days
from today.
</p>

<p id="p_5">4.     In view of the above, Shri Biren Vaishnav, learned
advocate appearing on behalf of the applicants does not press
the present application.
</p>

<p id="p_6">5.     With the above direction and observation, the present
application is dismissed as not pressed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                                          (M.R.SHAH, J.)



                                                       (R.P.DHOLARIA,J.)
Siji
