arrears of rent, and damages against the defendants.

<p id="p_2">     a residential Apartment bearing Apartment

                        ORDER

           Suit is decreed in part with cost.

    Rs.45,150/-. (Rs.2,150/- for the month

    2014.) Further, the plaintiff is entitled for
    damages    @    Rs.21,500/-     p.m.    from
    01.04.2014 to 10.08.2017.
