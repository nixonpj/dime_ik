<div class="doc_title">D.Govindasamy vs The Inspector Of Police on 10 May, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 
IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT MADRAS
DATED : 10.05.2018
CORAM
THE HONOURABLE MRS. JUSTICE S.RAMATHILAGAM

Crl.O.P.No.13435 of 2018

D.Govindasamy								... Petitioner

					          Vs
The Inspector of Police,
Kandili Police Station,
Kandili,
Vellore District. 						               ... Respondent

	Prayer: Criminal Original Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1679850/" id="a_1">Section 482</a> Cr.P.C., to direct the respondent authority to give police protection and permission to conduct cultural programme (Adal Padal) in the event of Muthagoundanur Arulmigu Sree Mariamman Temple Festival at Natrampalli Taluk, Vellore District on 29.05.2018 by considering the petitioner's representation dated 05.05.2018.

 		For Petitioner       	:  	Mr.A.Sathish Kumar 
		For Respondent 		: 	Mrs.P.Kritika Kamal
							Govt. Advocate [Crl.Side] 

O R D E R
</pre><p id="p_1">	Seeking permission to conduct cultural programme in the event of Muthagoundanur Arulmigu Sree Mariamman Temple Festival at Natrampalli Taluk, Vellore District on 29.05.2018, the petitioner made a representation on 05.05.2018 to the respondent.  As the same has not been considered, the present Criminal Original petition has been filed.
</p>
<p id="p_2">	2. Heard the learned counsel appearing for the petitioner and the learned Government Advocate (Crl. side) appearing for the respondent.
</p>
<p id="p_3">	3. Learned Government Advocate (Crl. side), on instructions, would submit that there is likelihood of arising law and order problem.
</p>
<p id="p_4">	4. A mere apprehension cannot be a ground to reject the representation. There is absolutely no material before this Court to hold that there will be a law and order problem in the event of permission being granted. In such view of the matter, the Criminal Original petition is allowed and the petitioner is permitted to conduct cultural programme in the event of Muthagoundanur Arulmigu Sree Mariamman Temple Festival at Natrampalli Taluk, Vellore District on 29.05.2018, however, with the following conditions:-
</p><p id="p_5">	(a) The cultural programme in connection with Muthagoundanur Arulmigu Sree Mariamman Temple Festival at Natrampalli Taluk, Vellore District shall be conducted on 29.05.2018 between 06.30 p.m. and  11.00 p.m.
</p><p id="p_6">	(b)There should not be any kind of obscene dance or vulgar dialogues during the performance, by anyone of the participants.
</p><p id="p_7">	(c) Double meaning songs should not be played as it may spoil the minds of students and youths.
</p><p id="p_8">	(d) No dance or songs, touching upon any political party or religion or community or caste shall be played.
</p><p id="p_9">	(e) No flex boards in support of any political party or communal leader, shall be erected at the premises of the programme.
</p><p id="p_10">	(f) The function shall not affect either religious or communal harmony and shall be conducted without any discrimination based on caste.
</p><p id="p_11">	(g) If there is violation of any one of the conditions imposed, the concerned police officer is at liberty to take necessary action, as per law and stop such performance forthwith; and
</p><p id="p_12">	(h) Similarly, the police is directed to stop the dance programme, if it is played beyond the permitted time limit.
</p><p id="p_13">	(i) The respondent is directed to issue necessary permission, incorporating the above conditions.	
</p>
