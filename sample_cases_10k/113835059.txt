<div class="doc_title">Kesho Saw &amp; Anr vs The State Of Bihar on 21 May, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  Criminal Miscellaneous No.6296 of 2015
                    Arising Out of PS.Case No. -45 Year- 2015 Thana -LAKHISARAI District- LAKHISARAI
                 ======================================================
                 1. Kesho Saw, Son of Ram Charan Saw.
                 2. Sanjay Saw, Son of Kesho Saw.
                    Both resident of Bazar Samiti, P.S. - Kabaiya, District - Lakhisarai.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                            .... .... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :   Mr.
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAJENDRA KUMAR
                 MISHRA
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>
<p id="p_1">                                               ------------
</p>
<p id="p_2">3   21-05-2015             Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and the learned

                 A.P.P. for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                           The petitioners apprehend their arrest in connection with

                 Kabaiya (Lakhisarai) P.S. Case No.45 of 2015 registered under

                 <a href="/doc/1599401/" id="a_1">Sections 341</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_2">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_3">325</a>, <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_4">307</a> and <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_5">504</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_6">34</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_4">                           The allegation against the petitioners is of causing

                 injuries on the fingers of the right hand of the informant, Putul

                 Devi, by rod.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                           Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioners

                 submits that the petitioner no.1 is the gotiya of the informant and

                 there is a case and counter case. It is further submitted that the

                 injuries found on the finger of the right hand of the informant are
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">      Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.6296 of 2015 (3) dt.21-05-2015</span>
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                 2/2</span>




                  simple in nature.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                              Having considered the facts and the circumstances of the

                  case, let the petitioners, above named, in the event of their arrest

                  or surrender by them within four weeks, be enlarged on bail on

                  their furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-(Rupees Ten Thousand)

                  each with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction

                  of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Lakhisarai, in connection with

                  Kabaiya (Lakhisarai) P.S. Case No.45 of 2015, subject to the

                  conditions laid down under <a href="/doc/1692057/" id="a_7">Section 438(2)</a> Cr.P.C.


</p><p id="p_7">                                                           (Rajendra Kumar Mishra, J)
    P.S./-
</p>
