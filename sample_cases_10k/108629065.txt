<p id="p_1">2   11-05-2015               Heard learned counsel for the petitioners and learned
                 State counsel appearing on behalf of the respondent nos.1 to 5.
</p><p id="p_2">                             After having heard the parties and on consideration of
                 the materials available on record, this Court is of the opinion that
                 the issues raised on behalf of the petitioners with respect to the
                 lands under dispute mentioned in the writ petition can be properly
                 gone into and effectively decided by a Civil Court of competent
                 jurisdiction after recording evidence of the parties.
</p><p id="p_3">                             In above view of the matter, the writ petition is
                 dismissed with a liberty to the petitioners to approach the Civil
                 Court of competent jurisdiction by filing a properly framed suit
                 after impleading all necessary parties for redressal of their valid
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">            Patna High Court CWJC No.6710 of 2011 (2) dt.11-05-2015</span>


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2</span>




                        grievances or for grant of appropriate relief(s).
</p><p id="p_4">                                     If such a Civil Suit is filed on behalf of the petitioners
                        within a period of three months from today with a certified copy
                        of the present order, then the same shall be decided on its own
                        merits on the basis of materials/evidence produced by the parties
                        strictly    in     accordance        with    law,   but   without   being
                        prejudiced/influenced by the dismissal of the present writ petition.
</p>


<p id="p_5">                                               (Birendra Prasad Verma, J)
