<div class="doc_title">Sharmila vs State Of Rajasthan &amp; Anr on 30 January, 2018</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
              S.B. Civil Writ Petition No. 1497 / 2018
Sharmila W/o Shri Pramod Kumar, D/o Shri Mahender Singh
Dhaka, Aged About 23 Years, B/c Jat, R/o Ward No.3, Tidiyasar,
Tehsil Nohar, District Hanumangarh, Rajasthan.
</p><p id="p_2">                                                         ----Petitioner
                              Versus
</p><p id="p_3">1. State of Rajasthan Through the Secretary, Department of
Medical &amp; Health, Government of Rajasthan, Secretariat, Jaipur
(Raj.)

</p><p id="p_4">2. Director, State Medical and Family Welfare Institute, Zhalana
Institutional Area, Jaipur (Raj.)
</p><p id="p_5">                                                   ----Respondents
_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)   :Mr. SR Godara
For Respondent(s) :Mr. Rishab Tayal for Mr. KL Thakur, AAG
_____________________________________________________
     HON'BLE DR. JUSTICE PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI
                               Order
30/01/2018

</p><p id="p_6">1.         Learned counsel for the petitioner states that the

respondents are bound to abide by judgment passed by Jaipur

Bench of this Hon'ble Court in Priyanka Saini &amp; Ors. Vs. State

of Rajasthan &amp; Ors. (S.B. Civil Writ Petition No.1766/2017,

decided on 01.05.2017, the operative portion of this judgment,

reads as under :-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">     "Following the view formulated by the coordinate
     Bench in the case of Rajkumar &amp; Ors. (supra), the
     present writ petitions are disposed of by issuing
     following directions:-
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_2">     (i). That the respondents shall issue a public notice in
     'Rajasthan Patrika' and 'Dainik Bhaskar' having largest
                                     (2 of 2)
                                                                  [CW-1497/2018]

     circulation in the State of Rajasthan, within ten days from the
     receipt of certified copy of the order.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_3">     (ii). That the respondents in the public notice shall also
     disclose number of vacancies available in each District.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_4">     (iii). That the respondents in the public notice to be issued in
     pursuance of directions issued above, shall seek option of
     candidates district-wise. Needless to say, a meritorious
     candidate shall first give option for his/her home district and
     then shall give his/her preference for remaining districts serial-
     wise.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_5">     (iv). That the respondents, after meritorious candidates are
     allowed to exercise option for his/her home district and posts
     are filled on that basis, shall allocate remaining posts in other
     districts to successful candidates on the basis of merit.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_6">     (v). It is clarified that no candidate lower in merit will be
     allowed to jump a candidate who is meritorious. So far
     employment is concerned, all candidates in merit list shall be
     allocated a district.
</blockquote>
<blockquote id="blockquote_7">     (vi). That respondents shall device a formula to act in
     consonance with the directions issued by this court above so
     that no meritorious person is left out of the employment."
</blockquote>
<p id="p_7">2.           In light of the afore-quoted judgment, the writ petition

is disposed of with the direction to the respondents to act strictly

in accordance with terms and conditions laid down in the

judgment of Priyanka Saini (Supra).              It is further made clear

that the respondents shall be required to give appointment to the

petitioner on the post of Auxiliary Nursing Midwifey                (A.N.M.)

arising out of advertisement dated 28.01.2016, in case any person

having lower merit than the petitioner has been accorded such

appointment.
</p>


<p id="p_8">                                    (DR. PUSHPENDRA SINGH BHATI)J.
</p><p id="p_9">sudheer
