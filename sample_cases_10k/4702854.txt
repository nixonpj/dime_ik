<div class="doc_title">Baban Yadav vs The State Of Bihar on 11 September, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                    Criminal Miscellaneous No.36947 of 2015
                          Arising Out of PS.Case No. -124 Year- 2015 Thana -BIHIYA District- BHOJPUR
                   ======================================================
                   Baban Yadav Son of late Ram Rekha Yadav Resident of village Doghra,
                   P.S Bihia, District Bhojpur.
                                                                          .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                   The State of Bihar
                                                                     .... .... Opposite Party/s
                   ======================================================
                   Appearance :
                   For the Petitioner/s     :    Mr. Kumar Mritunjay Narain
                   For the Opposite Party/s     : Mr. A.K.Choudhary(App)
                   ======================================================
                   CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE DINESH KUMAR
                   SINGH
                   ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">02/   11-09-2015                               Heard learned counsels for the petitioner

                   and the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                               The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in

                   a case registered for the offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_1">Sections 147</a>,

                   <a href="/doc/763672/" id="a_2">148</a>, <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_3">149</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_4">323</a>, <a href="/doc/1133601/" id="a_5">325</a>, <a href="/doc/752609/" id="a_6">385</a>, <a href="/doc/1688284/" id="a_7">387</a>, <a href="/doc/555306/" id="a_8">504</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_9">506</a>, <a href="/doc/1101188/" id="a_10">379</a> of the Indian Penal

                   Code and 27 of the <a href="/doc/1934415/" id="a_11">Arms Act</a>.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                               The prosecution case is that the informant

                   was dismantling some construction after taking the same on

                   auction from Gammon India Ltd. when the accused persons

                   made protest and asked the informant to vacate the premises. It

                   is alleged that the accused persons demanded extortion of

                   Rs.20,000/- and 100 asbestos sheets and subsequently the

                   accused persons including the petitioner surrounded the

                   informant, co-accused Rakesh Kumar and Madan Yadav on
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">        Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.36947 of 2015 (2) dt.11-09-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                  2/2</span>




                    the point of country made pistol snatched Rs.1,00,000/- and

                    assaulted the informant.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                                 It is submitted by learned counsel for the

                    petitioner that the demolition undertaken by the informant was

                    affecting the land holders adjacent to the construction, hence,

                    they made protest as a result the present case has been lodged. A

                    statement has been made in paragraph 3 of the petition that the

                    petitioner has no criminal antecedent.

</p><p id="p_5">                                                 Learned counsel for the informant does not

                    dispute that the accusation is not specific against this petitioner

                    but submits that the accusation is serious in nature.

</p><p id="p_6">                                                 In view of the above facts and the

                    accusation being not specific, it is a case for consideration of

                    prayer for regular bail, if the petitioner surrenders before the

                    learned court below within a period of six weeks from today in

                    connection with Bihiya P.S. Case No. 124 of 2015 pending in

                    the court of learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Bhojpur at Ara.

</p><p id="p_7">                                                 With      the        aforesaid   observation,   this

                    application is disposed of.
</p>
<p id="p_8">

</p><p id="p_9">                                               (Dinesh Kumar Singh, J)
    DKS/-
</p>
