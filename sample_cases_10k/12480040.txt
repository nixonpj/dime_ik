<div class="doc_title">Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited vs Yad Ram Sharma on 28 April, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">        In the High Court of Punjab and Haryana at Chandigarh

                        R.S.A.No. 307 of 2009
                        Date of decision: 28.4.2011

Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited                            ......Appellant

                         Versus

Yad Ram Sharma                                        .......Respondent


CORAM: HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE SABINA

Present:     Mr. Nitin Rathee, Advocate, for appellant.

             Mr. S.K.Bhardwaj, Advocate, for the respondent.
                   ****

SABINA, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             Plaintiff-appellant had filed   a suit for recovery of `

59,212/-.    The trial Court dismissed the suit vide judgment and

decree dated      24.7.2006.    The said judgment and decree were

upheld in appeal vide judgment and decree dated 22.8.2008 passed

by the Additional District Judge. Hence, the present appeal by the

plaintiff.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the respondent submits that this

appeal has been rendered infructuous as the amount in question has

been paid by the respondent to the appellant. The submission made

by learned counsel for the respondent has not been disputed by

learned counsel for the appellant.

</p><p id="p_3">             Accordingly, this appeal is dismissed as having been

rendered infructuous.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                                                (SABINA)
                                                 JUDGE
April 28, 2011
