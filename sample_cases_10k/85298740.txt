<div class="doc_title">Inder Singh vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 18 November, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                                         1                        Mcrc.11823/16
                              Inder Singh Vs. State of M.P.

       18/11/16
</pre><p id="p_1">              Shri Atul Gupta, Advocate for the petitioner.
              Shri Kamal Jain, Public Prosecutor for the State.
              Shri Dhirendra Singh, Advocate for the victim.
              I.A.No.9305/2016, an application under <a href="/doc/1521964/" id="a_1">Section 301(2)</a> of Cr.P.C. is
       taken up, considered and allowed for the reasons mentioned therein.
</p><p id="p_2">              Case-diary is perused.
</p><p id="p_3">              Learned counsel for the rival parties are heard.
              This is first application under <a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_2">Section 438</a> of Cr.P.C. for grant of
       anticipatory bail.
</p><p id="p_4">              Petitioner apprehends his arrest in connection with offences
       punishable under <a href="/doc/695990/" id="a_3">Section 364</a>, <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_4">120-B</a>/<a href="/doc/37788/" id="a_5">34</a> of I.P.C., registered as Crime No.
       101/2016, by Police Station Civil Lines, District Datia (M.P.).
</p><p id="p_5">              Learned Public Prosecutor for the State opposed the application and
       prayed for its rejection by contending that on the basis of the allegations
       and the material available on record, no case for grant of anticipatory bail
       is made out.
</p><p id="p_6">              The petitioner is alleged with the offence of kidnapping for wrongful
       detention. The abductee is not yet traced out. The investigation is over by
       filing charge-sheet under <a href="/doc/389269/" id="a_6">Section 290</a> of Cr.P.C.
</p><p id="p_7">              The petitioner has criminal antecedents of four cases including the
       case of attempt to murder registered in 2015 itself.
</p><p id="p_8">              In view of above, no case for bail is made out.
              Accordingly, the application stands rejected with liberty to come
       again after conclusion of investigation.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                                                           (Sheel Nagu)
                                                              Judge
(Bu)
