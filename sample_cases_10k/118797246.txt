            1. Gunjan Kumar @ Gujan Kumar S/o Dev Narayan Singh
            2. Kundan Kumar@ Ritesh Kumar@Ritish Kumar S/o Pramod
               Singh@ Pramod Kumar Singh Both Resident of Village- Gospur,
               Pachhiyari Tol, P.S. Dalsingsarai, District- Samastipur.

                                                                                   ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                       Versus
                 The State of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :       Mr. Lakshmindra Kumar Yadav
                 For the Opposite Party/s :       Mr. Sri Satya Nand Shukla
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRABHAT KUMAR JHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   18-12-2018                 Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                               The petitioners seek bail in Ujiyarpur P.S. case No.

                  243 of 2018 registered under <a href="/doc/471092/" id="a_1">Section 272</a>, <a href="/doc/285163/" id="a_2">273</a> of the Indian

                  Penal Code and under Section 30(a) of Bihar Prohibition &amp;

                  <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_3">Excise Act</a>, 2016.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                               The police intercepted a Scorpio vehicle and arrested

                  the petitioners who were sitting inside the vehicle. On search,

                  huge quantity of different brands of IMFL was recovered.

</p><p id="p_4">                               It is submitted that 86.730 liters of IMFL was

                  recovered and for that the petitioners are in custody since

                  05.10.2018. The petitioners have got no criminal antecedent.

</p><p id="p_5">                               Considering the facts aforesaid and nature of

                  allegation made against the petitioners and the fact that
             Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.75343 of 2018(2) dt.18-12-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                         2/2</span>




                     petitioners have got no criminal antecedent and they have

                     remained in jail for about two and half months, the petitioners,

                     above named, are directed to be enlarged on bail on furnishing

                     bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (rupees ten thousand) each with two

                     sureties of the lime amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                     Additional Sessions Judge-III-cum-Special Judge, <a href="/doc/110162683/" id="a_4">Excise Act</a>,

                     Samastipur in connection with Ujiyarpur P.S. case No. 243 of

                     2018.
</p>
<p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">                                               (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J)
BKS/-
</p>
