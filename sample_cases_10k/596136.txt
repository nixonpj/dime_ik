  IN THE HIGH COURT OF KERALA AT ERNAKULAM

                      ...  Petitioner

                        Vs

                       ...       Respondent

 Dated :03/11/2008

 O R D E R
                                  P.R.Raman &amp;
                          T.R. Ramachandran Nair, JJ.
                       ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
                           W.P.(C) No.31829/2008-L
                        ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
                    Dated this the 3rd day of November, 2008.

                                J U D G M E N T
</pre>
<p id="p_1">Raman, J.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      This writ petition is filed by the petitioners claiming themselves to be

devotees. Their grievance is that the property of 71 cents comprised in Survey

No.93/5B of Koothattukulam Village is allegedly encroached upon by the Co-

operative Hospital. The Co-operative Hospital itself is not made a party in this

writ petition. The prayer is for a direction to the District Collector to dispose of

the petition said to have been filed by the petitioners.

</p><p id="p_3">      In identical matters filed by the Devaswom Board, we have relegated the

Devaswom Board to approach the Civil Court.             Hence, we cannot take a

different view. In the circumstances, we decline to exercise the jurisdiction

under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India and the matter is left open to be

decided by a Civil Court and if the petitioner is so advised he can approach the

said forum.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      The writ petition is dismissed.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                              (P.R. Raman, Judge.)



                                       (T.R. Ramachandran Nair, Judge.)
