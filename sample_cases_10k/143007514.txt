<p id="p_1">2   25-07-2016                     Heard learned Senior counsel, Mr. M.N. Parbat,

                   appearing for the petitioners.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                   2.              Perused the order dated 21.12.2015. From perusal of

                   the impugned order, it appears that the security bond has been

                   furnished by the appellants of T.A. No. 59 of 2011 pursuant to the

                   order dated 27.11.2015, whereby the court below had directed the

                   appellant to furnish the security in terms of Order 41 Rule 3

                   C.P.C.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                   3.              Learned Senior counsel for the petitioners submitted

                   that forgery has been committed by the appellants-respondents

                   while furnishing the security bond in the court below. The court

                   below should have decided the same but the court below has

                   called for the record of execution case and has kept it on record.

                   No order has been passed regarding the acceptance or rejection of

                   the security bond furnished by the appellants-respondents.
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Patna High Court CWJC No.5120 of 2016 (2) dt.25-07-2016</span>




</p><p id="p_4">                         4.              In view of the nature of order passed by the court

                         below, in my opinion, the order is only routine order and,

                         therefore, it is not a cause for interference in exercise of

                         supervisory jurisdiction. Thus, this writ application is dismissed.



</p><p id="p_5">                                                   (Mungeshwar Sahoo, J)
brajesh/-
