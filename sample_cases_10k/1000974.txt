<div class="doc_title">Shiv Ji Sharma vs The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors on 20 October, 2011</div><pre id="pre_1">                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             CWJC No.2463 of 2008
                                  Shiv Ji Sharma
                                      Versus
                             The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors
                                     -----------
</pre>
<p id="p_1">3   20.10.2011                   Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that a road has

                     been constructed on his raiyati lands. It is submitted that this fact has

                     been disclosed in the writ application vide Annexure-5 which was

                     obtained by the petitioner through the Right to <a href="/doc/1965344/" id="a_1">Information Act</a>. The

                     said action of laying down the road was done by the Executive

                     Officer, Banmankhi Nagarpalika, Sri Alok Kumar, Junior Engineer

                     and Sri Surendra Prasad, Assistant Engineer.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 Counsel for the State is required to file an affidavit

                     regarding the aforesaid facts. If this Court finds that the facts as

                     disclosed by the petitioner are correct then, the Court may award

                     compensation to the petitioner for the said acts. Counsel for the State

                     may seek instructions from the Commissioner, Purnia Division as well

                     as the Collector, Purnia.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                 Issue notice to respondent nos. 5, 7 and 8 both under

                     registered cover with A/D as well as under ordinary process, requisites

                     etc. for which under both processes must be filed by 4.11.2011 from

                     today failing which this application shall stand dismissed without

                     further reference to a Bench.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                 List this case on appearance of the respondents or after

                     two months, whichever is earlier.
</p>
<p id="p_5">
</p><pre id="pre_2">    Sanjay                                                    (Sheema Ali Khan, J.)
