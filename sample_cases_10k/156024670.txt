<p id="p_1">      The petitioner who was appointed as per Ext.P1 order as

LPSA under the 5th respondent School w.e.f. 28.06.2010 got his

appointment approved on regular scale only with effect from

01.06.2011 onwards. The period from 28.06.2010 to 31.03.2011

was approved only on daily wages and without vacation salary.

Petitioner seeks a direction to approve her appointment with

effect from the date of her appointment i.e., from 28.06.2010

onwards.
</p>
<p id="p_2">      2.   Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner as well as

the learned Government Pleader.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      3.   The issue involved in the case is covered by the

judgment of the Apex Court in <a href="/doc/119645572/" id="a_1">State of Kerala v. Sneha

Cheriyan</a> [2013 (1) KLT 755] and as explained by this Court in

<a href="/doc/334212/" id="a_2">NSS v. State of Kerala</a> [2013 (4) KLT 921], according to which,

petitioner is entitled to get her appointment approved with effect

W.P.(C).No.9687 of 2017
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  2</span>

from the date of her initial appointment i.e., from 28.06.2010

onwards. Accordingly, the writ petition is disposed of directing the

4th respondent to issue fresh orders revising the approval of the

petitioner with effect from 28.06.2010 onwards on regular scale

and to grant the petitioner all consequential benefits including

arrears of pay, fixation of pay etc. All the benefits shall be granted

within a period of 'three months' from the date of receipt of a

copy of this judgment.
</p>
