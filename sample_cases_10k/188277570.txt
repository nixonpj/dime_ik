<div class="doc_title">Sanjay Agarwal vs State Of Rajasthan on 12 July, 2018</div><p id="p_1">     HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                      JODHPUR
                    S.B. Civil Writ No. 9425/2018

Sanjay Agarwal S/o Shri Om Prakash Agarwal, aged about 52
Years, 9 Bank Street, Shiv Badi Road, Bikaner.
</p><p id="p_2">                                                            ----Petitioner
                                  Versus
</p><p id="p_3">1.      State of Rajasthan, to be served through the Joint
        Secretary, Water Resources Department, Government,
        Secretariat, Jaipur.
</p><p id="p_4">2.      The   Chief   Engineer,     Water       Resources   Department,
        Rajasthan, Jaipur.</p><pre id="pre_1">
                                                       ----Respondents


For Petitioner(s)       :      Mr. B.K. Vyas.



               HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE P.K. LOHRA

                                  Order

12/07/2018

</pre><p id="p_5">     Petitioner working on the post of Assistant Engineer with the

Water Resources Department at Bikaner is transferred in the same

capacity from Bikaner to Sub-Division, Headquarter Chattergarh

vide order dated 29th of June, 2018         (Annex.7).      By the order

Annex.7, eighty-five Assistant Engineers are transferred.

</p><p id="p_6">     For challenging order of transfer precisely petitioner has

pleaded in the writ petition that he is suffering from kidney

ailment and he is under constant treatment of specialized doctors

at P.B.M. Hospital, Bikaner.      It is also pleaded in the writ petition

that against the impugned transfer order, petitioner submitted a

representation (Annex.8) before the competent authority to

cancel/rescind his transfer order on compassionate ground.

Learned counsel has also submitted that so far no heed is paid to
                                                                       (2 of 2)             [CW-9425/2018]



                                   his representation and the same is still pending consideration

                                   before the competent authority.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                          In view of submissions, the instant writ petition is disposed

                                   of with a direction to the competent authority to decide the

                                   representation of the petitioner objectively and benevolently with

                                   promptitude preferably within a period of fortnight from the date

                                   of receipt of certified copy of this order strictly in accordance with

                                   law.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                                                                                        (P.K. LOHRA),J
