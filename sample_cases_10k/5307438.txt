<pre id="pre_1">




HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Court No. - 34
 

 
Case :- CRIMINAL REVISION No. - 493 of 1993
 

 
Revisionist :- M.P. Sharma
 
Opposite Party :- State
 
Counsel for Revisionist :- D.N. Wali
 
Counsel for Opposite Party :- Aga
 

 
Hon'ble Sudhir Agarwal,J.
</pre> 
<p id="p_1">1. Called in revised. None appeared to press this revision. In the circumstances, I myself have perused the record.
</p> 
<p id="p_2">2. This criminal revision under Section 401 read with <a href="/doc/1457888/" id="a_1">Section 397</a> Cr.P.C. has been filed aggrieved by order dated 26.3.1993 passed by the Judge, Family Court, Meerut in Case No. 1432 of 1990/495 of 1991, under <a href="/doc/1056396/" id="a_2">Section 125</a> Cr.P.C., District  Meerut, whereby application under <a href="/doc/1056396/" id="a_3">Section 125</a> Cr.P.C. was allowed and maintenance of Rs. 500/- per month has been granted to respondents no.1 and 4 each from the date of filing of application i.e. 24.7.1990. 
</p> 
<p id="p_3">3. Having gone through the impugned order, I do not find any manifest error or otherwise illegality so as to warrant interference. 
</p> 
<p id="p_4">4. Dismissed. 
</p> 
<p id="p_5">5. Interim order, if any, stands vacated.
</p> 
<p id="p_6">6. Certify this judgment to the lower Court immediately. 
</p> 
<p id="p_7">Order Date :- 24.4.2015
 
Ashish Pd. 
</p>
