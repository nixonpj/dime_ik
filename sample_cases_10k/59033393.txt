<p id="p_1">           1. The   petitioner's   wife   expired   on   October   27, 

              2010, while working in the Government Department 

              as   a   Class­IV   employee.   The   petitioner   is 

              claiming   the   benefit   of  Government   Resolution 

              dated July 05, 2011, wherein compensation in lieu 

              of   compassionate   appointment   has   been   permitted 

              by the Government. Reliance has also been placed 

              on   the   subsequent  Government   Resolution  dated 

              October   13,   2015,   wherein   in   ascending   order 

              relationship   of   dependents   of   the   deceased 

              employee has been prescribed.</p><pre id="pre_2">
