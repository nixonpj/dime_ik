<div class="doc_title">Arabinda Ghosh vs Unknown on 10 July, 2015</div><div class="doc_author">Author: Aniruddha Bose</div>

<p id="p_1"><span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                    1</span>



     S/L. 32
July 10, 2015.                          C.R.M. 6130 of 2015
       ss.
</p><p id="p_2">                   In re: An application for bail under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of the Code of
                   Criminal Procedure filed on June 18, 2015 in connection with
                   Nakashipara Police Station Case No. 51/2015 dated January 26,
                   2015 under <a href="/doc/764237/" id="a_2">Sections 394</a>/<a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_3">395</a>/<a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_4">397</a>/<a href="/doc/844968/" id="a_5">412</a> of the Indian Penal Code,
                   1860 and under <a href="/doc/110585/" id="a_6">Sections 25(1)(a)</a>/<a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_7">27</a> of the Arms Act.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                                                   And

                   In the matter of: Arabinda Ghosh                   ...Petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                         Mr. Prabir Majumdar                   ...for the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                         Mr. Arun Maity                            ...for the State.
</p>

<p id="p_6">                         The petitioner is seeking bail in connection with a case relating
                 to offences punishable under <a href="/doc/764237/" id="a_8">Sections 394</a>/<a href="/doc/1119707/" id="a_9">395</a>/<a href="/doc/1865117/" id="a_10">397</a>/<a href="/doc/844968/" id="a_11">412</a> of the Indian
                 Penal Code, 1860 and under <a href="/doc/110585/" id="a_12">Sections 25(1)(a)</a>/<a href="/doc/244673/" id="a_13">27</a> of the Arms Act.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                         Heard Mr. Prabir Majumdar, learned advocate appearing for
                 the petitioner and Mr. Arun Maity, learned advocate appearing for the
                 State. We have perused the case diary.
</p>
<p id="p_8">                         Mr. Majumdar submits that the only allegation so far the
                 present petitioner is concerned is receiving property allegedly stolen.
                 His further submission is    that another co-accused with similar
                 allegations, being Amjad Sk. has been enlarged on bail on 12th June,
                 2015 in C.R.M. 4179 of 2015. The petitioner is in custody since 1st March,
                 2015.
</p>

<p id="p_9">                         Mr. Maity submits that investigation is complete and charge-
                 sheet has been filed in this case. He also could not bring to our notice
                 any material which would implicate the petitioner with complicity in the
                 alleged crime beyond the allegation of receiving stolen property. The
                 petitioner appears to be similarly circumstanced person with Amjad Sk..
</p>

<p id="p_10">                          We accordingly direct the petitioner, namely, Arabinda
                 Ghosh, be released on bail upon furnishing a bond of ` 5,000/- (Rupees
                 five thousand) with two sureties of like amount, one of whom must be
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                      2</span>



local, to the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate at
Krishnagar, District: Nadia, on further condition that the petitioner shall
report to the Officer-in-Charge, Nakashipara Police Station every
alternate day and shall appear before the trial court on each and
every date of trial. In the event, the petitioner fails to comply with any
of these conditions, it shall be open for the trial court to cancel the bail
of the petitioner without any further reference of this Court.
</p>

<p id="p_11">         The application for bail is, thus, allowed.
</p>


<p id="p_12">                                          (Aniruddha Bose, J.)



                                         (Sankar Acharyya, J.)
