<div class="doc_title">Rajesh Hargovindbhai Thakor vs State Of Gujarat &amp; 2 on 29 March, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                   C/SCA/2027/2016                                                ORDER




                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD
                    SPECIAL CIVIL APPLICATION NO. 2027 of 2016
         ==========================================================
                   RAJESH HARGOVINDBHAI THAKOR....Petitioner(s)
                                      Versus
                      STATE OF GUJARAT &amp; 2....Respondent(s)
         ==========================================================
         Appearance:
         MS SUBHADRA G PATEL, ADVOCATE for the Petitioner(s) No. 1
         MR CHINTAN DAVE, AGP for the Respondent(s) No. 1
         RULE SERVED BY DS for the Respondent(s) No. 1 - 3
         ==========================================================
          CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE P.P.BHATT
                            Date : 29/03/2016
                                          ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">         1.    Learned AGP is in receipt of communication dated 29.03.2016 from

         P.I., Vadi Police Station, Vadodara City disclosing that till date, there is no

         proposal for detaining the petitioner under PASA. A copy of the same is to be

         taken on record. Therefore, this petition is disposed of with an observation

         that henceforth respondents should not detain/book the present petitioner

         under PASA for the same set of information which is available with them till

         29.03.2016 with respect to FIRs being I-C.R.No.130/2015 registered at Vadi

         Police Station, Vadodara City as well as I-C.R.No.5/2016 registered at Vadi

         Police Station, Vadodara City.
</p>
<p id="p_2">         2.    With above observation, this petition is disposed of. Rule is

         discharged. Direct Service is permitted. Interim relief if any granted earlier

         stands vacated forthwith.</p><pre id="pre_2">
