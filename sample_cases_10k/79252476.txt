          IN THE HIGH COURT OF KARNATAKA
                    KALABURAGI BENCH

       DATED THIS THE 13TH DAY OF JUNE, 2018

                             BEFORE

       THE HON'BLE MRS. JUSTICE K.S.MUDAGAL

          CRIMINAL PETITION NO.200524/2018

BETWEEN:

Timmappa S/o Eranna
Age: 31 years, Occ: Plumber
R/o Gadwal road, Maddipet
Raichur, Tq &amp; Dist: Raichur-587105
                                                         ... Petitioner

(By Sri. Shivanand V. Pattanshetti, Advocate)


AND:

The State of Karnataka
R/by Addl. SPP Kalaburagi
Bench-585106
(Through Market Yard P.S.)
                                                       ... Respondent

(By Sri. P.S.Patil, HCGP)


       This Criminal Petition is filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> of
Cr.P.C.   praying    to   grant       the   regular    bail     to   the
petitioner/accused    No.2    in      Market    Yard     P.S.    Crime
No.71/2018 for the offence punishable under Sections 143,
                              2         CRL.P.NO.200524/2018




147, 148, 323, 324, 307, 341, 353, 427 read with <a href="/doc/999134/" id="a_2">Section
149</a> IPC and <a href="/doc/194745198/" id="a_3">Section 2</a> of the Prevention of Damage to Public
Property Act, 1984 pending on the file of Prl. Civil Judge
(Sr.Dn.) and CJM Court at Raichur.


      This petition is coming on for Orders this day, the
Court made the following:



                         ORDER
</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Heard.
</p>
<p id="p_2">
</p><p id="p_3">     2.    Petitioner   is   accused   Nos.2    in   Crime

No.71/2018 of Market Yard Police Station, Raichur. The

said case was registered initially against 8 to 10

unknown persons aged about 25 to 30 years, on the

basis of complaint of Suresh.D. PSI, Law and Order,

Market Yard Police Station, Raichur.




</p><p id="p_4">     3.    It is alleged that on 16.05.2018 at 09.30

p.m. when complainant and his staff were on patrol

duty, they found that there was crowd of 50 to 60

persons. Out of them 8 to 10 persons were beating a
                                3      CRL.P.NO.200524/2018




person suspecting him to be child kidnapper. It is

further alleged that when complainant and his staff

tried to disperse the crowd and rescue the victim, the

accused assaulted them with stone, caused injuries and

obstructed in discharging their duties as public servant.



</p><p id="p_5">     4.    In the remand application, the petitioner is

shown as accused No.2. The wound certificate of alleged

victim, available in the C.D. records shows that he has

suffered only simple injuries. The injuries suffered by

two police persons were simple in nature. One of the

injured/police   by     name   Anand-head   constable   is

reportedly discharged from the Hospital.


</p><p id="p_6">     5.    As per the complaint itself a crowd of 50 to

60 persons assaulted with stones to the police and

vandalized the properties. The offences alleged against

the accused are not exclusively punishable with death

or life imprisonment.
</p><p id="p_7">                               4          CRL.P.NO.200524/2018




</p><p id="p_8">      6.    The Investigation Officer himself requested

to remand the petitioner to judicial custody stating that

detention   of   petitioner   is   not   required        for   any

investigation. Therefore, it is a fit case to grant the bail

to the petitioner with suitable conditions. Therefore,

petition is allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_9">

</p><p id="p_10">      7.    Petitioner   is   granted     bail      in     Crime

No.71/2018 of Market Yard Police Station, Raichur,

which is now pending before the Prl. Civil Judge (Sr. Dn)

and CJM Court at Raichur. Petitioner shall be released

on bail in the said case subject to the following:


</p><blockquote id="blockquote_1">                         CONDITIONS


</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_2">      1.    He shall execute personal bond in a sum of
            Rs.25,000/- each and furnish two sureties
            in the likesum to the satisfaction of the
            Magistrate/Trial Court.
</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_3">                               5              CRL.P.NO.200524/2018




</blockquote><blockquote id="blockquote_4">      2.   He shall appear before the Investigation
           Officer/Magistrate as and when required for
           the purpose of investigation/trial.
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_5">      3.   He   shall   not       tamper      the   prosecution
           witnesses    by        threats,     inducement     or
           otherwise.
</blockquote>

<blockquote id="blockquote_6">      4.   He shall mark his attendance before the
           Investigation Officer on alternative Sunday's
           in his office between 10.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m.,
           till the completion of investigation.
</blockquote>



<blockquote id="blockquote_7">                                              Sd/-</blockquote><p id="p_11">
</p><p id="p_12">                                             JUDGE
