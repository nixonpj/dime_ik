<div class="doc_title">Shiv Kumar Saket @ Shibbu vs The State Of Madhya Pradesh on 13 April, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">                             CRA-314-2015
          (SHIV KUMAR SAKET @ SHIBBU Vs THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH)


13-04-2017

     Shri Jitendra Tiwari, learned counsel for the appellants.
     Shri Akshay Namdeo, learned P.L for the respondent/State.</pre>
<p id="p_1">     Shri K.K. Verma, learned counsel for the respondent No. 2.
     Heard on I.A. No. 6785/2017, an application for change of
counsel. Mr. Jitendra Tiwari present in Court has no objection for
change of counsel. Consequently, I.A is allowed. Change of counsel is
permitted.
</p><p id="p_2">       Heard on I.A. No. 13550/2016, an application for suspension of
sentence and grant of bail on behalf of Shiv Kumar Saket convicted for
offence under <a href="/doc/1560742/" id="a_1">Sections 302</a> and <a href="/doc/695990/" id="a_2">364</a> of I.P.C for causing death of
Nagendra Saket.
</p><p id="p_3">       Learned counsel for the appellant points out that the co-accused
Sardar Saket has been granted bail and sentence imposed upon him has
been suspended vide order dated 13.01.2015 and the role attributed to
the appellants and Sardar Saket is similar. The deceased was last seen
in the company of the appellant Shiv Kumar Saket and Manraj.
</p><p id="p_4">       To maintain parity in respect of suspension of sentence as other
co-accused has been granted benefit of suspension of sentence, we
allow the present application for suspension of sentence. If the
appellants execute a personal bond in the sum of Rs.50,000 (Rs.Fifty
Thousand Only) and furnish one solvent surety in the like amount to
the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Satna, the execution
of sentence passed against them shall remain suspended and they shall
be released on bail.
</p><p id="p_5">      The application is allowed.
</p><p id="p_6">      C.C as per rules.</p><pre id="pre_2">
   (HEMANT GUPTA)   (SUSHIL KUMAR PALO)
  CHIEF JUSTICE             JUDGE
