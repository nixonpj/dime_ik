<p id="p_1">	The applicant   orig.
</p><p id="p_2">	petitioner has filed this Misc. Civil Application seeking extension
	of period of abeyance of execution proceedings (Regular Darkhast
	No.8 of 1994) till the period of implementation of Scheme is over
	i.e. 31.3.2011.  
</p>	
	 

   
	
	  


<p id="p_3">	This Court has issued
	notice on 8.4.2010 and direct service was prayed for. However,
	notice could not be served as the opponent could not stay at the
	address given in the cause title. Thereafter, prayer was made to
	permit the applicant to serve the opponent through public
	advertisement. Accordingly, order was passed on 21.4.2010 for
	publication of advertisement.  The advertisement was published in
	'Sandesh' in Gujarati language daily, Bhavnagar Edition dated
	25.4.2010. Despite publication of notice nobody appears on behalf of
	the opponent.  
</p>	
	 

  
	
	  


<p id="p_4">	Since the BIFR has issued
	direction for extension of implementation of the period of the
	sanctioned scheme and tenure of Asset Sale Committee upto 31.3.2011,
	the prayer is made in the present application to extend the period
	of implementation of the scheme is over i.e. 31.3.2011. 
</p>	
	 

  
	
	  

<p id="p_5">Having
	considered the averments made in the application and having heard
	the learned counsel appearing for the applicant, the prayer made in
	this application is granted and the period of abeyance of execution
	proceedings (Regular Darkhast No.8 of 1994) is extended till
	31.3.2011. 
</p>	
	 

  
	
	  


<p id="p_6">	With this direction this
	Misc. Civil Application is accordingly disposed off. 
</p>
