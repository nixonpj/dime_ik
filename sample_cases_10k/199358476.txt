<div class="doc_title">Prabhat Kumar Agrawal vs The State Of Bihar on 7 June, 2012</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                   Criminal Miscellaneous No.22075 of 2012
                   ======================================================
                   Prabhat Kumar Agrawal son of Arun Lal Agrawal, R/O Village
                   Shivajeegali, P.S.Belaganj, District- Gaya.
                                                                         .... .... Petitioner
                                                      Versus
                   The State Of Bihar
                                                                    .... .... Opposite Party
                   ======================================================

                   PRESENT: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE S.N. HUSSAIN

                                     ORDER
</pre>

<p id="p_1">2.   07-06-2012.                 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and

                    learned counsel for the State.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                 The petitioner is an accused in Jakkanpur P.S. Case

                    No.30 of 2012 for offences punishable under <a href="/doc/1252503/" id="a_1">Sections 489</a>,

                    <a href="/doc/1252503/" id="a_2">489(B)</a> and <a href="/doc/1128948/" id="a_3">120(B)</a> of the Indian Penal Code.

</p><p id="p_3">                                 Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that no

                    recovery of any fake note is alleged against the petitioner

                    whereas according to the prosecution case the recovery of fake

                    note have been made from another co-accused Om Prakash.

                    Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that he has got no

                    criminal antecedent and has been falsely implicated in this case

                    due to which he is custody since February, 2012.

</p><p id="p_4">                                 In the aforesaid facts and circumstances of the case,

                    this petition is allowed. Let the above named petitioner be

                    released on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.22075 of 2012 (2) dt.07-06-2012                      2</span>




                       (Rupees ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount

                       each to the satisfaction of the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Patna

                       in connection with Jakkanpur P.S. Case No.30 of 2012.


</p><p id="p_5">                                                     (S.N. Hussain, J.)
ahk/-
</p>
