05.12.2016</pre><p id="p_1">.
</p><p id="p_2">Item No.120
as
                                      C.R.R. 3782 of 2016


              In Re: An application under <a href="/doc/834094/" id="a_1">Section 407</a> of the Code of Criminal
              Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_3">              In the matter of : Smt. Nisha Sharma.
</p><p id="p_4">                                                         ... Petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                   Ms. Trina Mitra.
</p><p id="p_6">                                               ...for the Petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

                   It is submitted on behalf of the petitioner that the

              matrimonial proceeding and the maintenance proceeding are

              pending at Jalpaiguri and, therefore, the criminal proceeding

              instituted by her at Durgapur be also transferred to Jalpaiguri.

</p><p id="p_8">                   Let this matter appear for hearing under the heading

              'Contested Application' one week after X-mas Vacation.

</p><p id="p_9">                   Copy of the application be served upon the opposite

              partyno.1 through learned Public Prosecutor, High Court, Calcutta

              and upon the opposite parties Nos.2 to 6 through registered speed

              post with acknowledgement due and affidavit of service be filed on

              the next date of hearing.
</p>
<p id="p_10">                   There shall be stay of proceeding in G.R. No.1559 of 2012,

              T.R. No.151 of 2013 pending before the learned Judicial Magistrate

              2nd Court Durgapur arising out of Durgapur P.S. Case No.570 of

              2012 dated 14.9.2012 under <a href="/doc/538436/" id="a_2">Section 498A</a> of the Indian Penal
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                             2</span>


Code for a period of two weeks after the Christmas vacation or

until further order, whichever is earlier.

</p><p id="p_11">     Liberty is given to the parties to apply for extension and/or

modification and/or variation and/or vacation of this order upon

notice to the other side.
</p>
<p id="p_12">
</p><p id="p_13">                                         (Joymalya Bagchi, J.)
