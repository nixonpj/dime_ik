<pre id="pre_1">ORDER SHEET
                            WP No. 22 of 2015
                    IN THE HIGH COURT AT CALCUTTA
                      Constitutional Writ Jurisdiction
                             ORIGINAL SIDE

                  KOUSHIK GLOBAL LOGISTICS LTD. &amp; ANR.
                                  Versus
              STATE TRANSPORT AUTHORITY WEST BENGAL &amp; ORS.


  BEFORE:

  The Hon'ble JUSTICE DEBANGSU BASAK

  Date : 13th January, 2015.

                 The Court : The respondent no. 5 has office outside the</pre>

<p id="p_1">territorial jurisdiction of the original side of this Court. In terms of Rule 4

of the Writ Rules of the Court, the writ petition is not maintainable in the

Original Side. Learned Counsel for the writ petitioner seeks leave to convert

the writ petition to the Appellate Side. Such leave is granted. Learned

Counsel for the petitioner will no doubt serve a fresh notice on the

respondent after conversion of the writ petition.

</p><p id="p_2">                 With the conversion of the writ petition to the Appellate

Side, I have no determination over W.P. 22 of 2015. W.P. 22 of 2015 is

therefore released from my list.
</p>
<p id="p_3">

</p><p id="p_4">                                              (DEBANGSU BASAK, J.)
