<p id="p_1">             Learned counsel for the petitioner restricts his prayer to a

direction being issued to respondent no.2 to look into the matter and to

decide the representation stated to have been made by the petitioner Union

on 15.1.2019 (copy Annexure P-3).
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Consequently, without making any comment on the actual

merits of the case, for or against the petitioner, this petition is disposed of

with a direction to the SSP, Patiala, i.e. respondent no.2, to hear the

petitioner union as regards their grievance and to take action if any

warranted as per law, within a period of three months from the date of

receipt of a certified copy of this order.
</p>
<p id="p_3">             If the SSP, Patiala, comes to a conclusion that no action is

required to be taken by the police, he would pass an order giving reasons

therein.
</p>
