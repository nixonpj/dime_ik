         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

        R/SPECIAL CRIMINAL APPLICATION NO. 5690 of 2019

==========================================================
                       FATESINH RAMLAL CHAUHAN
                                 Versus
                           STATE OF GUJARAT
==========================================================
Appearance:
THROUGH JAIL(50) for the Applicant(s) No. 1
 for the Respondent(s) No. 2,3
MR HK PATEL, ADDL PUBLIC PROSECUTOR(2) for the Respondent(s) No.
1
==========================================================

 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE V.P. PATEL

                            Date : 15/05/2019

                             ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">1.   Rule.     Learned APP waives service of Rule on behalf of
respondent-State.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.   This application is filed through jail by the applicant -
accused to grant parole leave in connection with conviction by an
order dated 18.02.2009 passed by the learned Sessions Court,
Porbandar in Sessions Case No.14 of 2008 on the ground of
medical treatment of his daughter.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3.   Heard learned APP for the respondent - state.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4.   Considering the contents of the application, documents
produced on record, arguments made on behalf of the learned APP
for the respondent-State, facts and circumstances of the case and
in the interest of justice, parole leave is required to be partly
allowed and accordingly allowed.
</p>

<p id="p_5">           R/SCR.A/5690/2019                               ORDER




</p><p id="p_6">5.     The applicant is ordered to be enlarged on parole leave for a
period of 7 days from the date of his actual release on his
furnishing a bail bond of Rs.15,000/-(Rupees Fifteen thousand only)
to the jail authority on usual terms and conditions. The applicant
shall surrender to the jail authority on expiry of the parole leave.
Rule is made absolute to the aforesaid extent.
</p>
