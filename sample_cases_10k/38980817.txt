<p id="p_1">                   (PER : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE S.R.BRAHMBHATT)


</p><p id="p_2">         1.           Rule.           Mr.    J.K.      Shah,        learned           Additional
         Public Prosecutor waives service of Rule on behalf of
         the     respondent           State.      By       consent,         Rule        is      fixed
         forthwith.
</p>

<p id="p_3">         2.           This         application             was      mentioned              in       the
         morning       for       urgent         circulation               on       account              of
         requirement          of       the    convict-prisoner,                    who       is         on
         temporary bail to be needed for attending his ailing
         sister whose operation and after care is warranting
         the same. The Court accordingly granted permission

         and this matter was circulated today, as the convict-
         prisoner's             maternal          uncle          through             whom           the
         application is made, said to have come from Rajkot.
         The       jail     remark-sheet           does       not       militate            against
         granting of extension. The medical papers have been
         placed on record, which indicate that patient i.e.
         ailing sister of the convict-prisoner is required to
         be taken to the hospital for removal of stitches.
</p>

<p id="p_4">         3.               When these things are not controverted, we
         are of the view that temporary bail which was granted
         earlier by this Court vide order dated 24.01.2017 in
         Criminal Misc. Application No.2251 of 2017, is hereby
         extended for a further period of one week on the same
         terms and conditions. He shall surrender to the jail
         authority         on     expiry      of      the     extended            bail        period
         before 5:00 P.M. Application is partly allowed. Rule
         is       made    absolute      to    the          aforesaid        extent.           Direct
         service today is permitted.
</p>
