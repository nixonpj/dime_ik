<p id="p_1">                            This petition is filed for a direction to the Principal District Judge Erode,

                      filed by the petitioners on the same day in connection with Crime No.175 of

                      2019 dated 14.03.2019 pending on the file of Inspector of Police Station,

<p id="p_3">                                                                                                    vsn

</p><p id="p_4">                                2. In view of the specific bar under <a href="/doc/82911775/" id="a_2">Section 18</a> of the Scheduled and

                      <a href="/doc/25085007/" id="a_3">Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act</a>, 1989 and also taking into

                      consideration of the possibility of misusing the pious intention of the

                      Parliament against innocent persons, there shall be a direction to the Principal

                      District Judge Erode, to consider the petitioners' bail application, preferably on

                      the same day of their surrender in connection with Crime No.175 of 2019

                      dated 14.03.2019 and pass appropriate orders in accordance with law after

                      affording due opportunity to the victim under Section 15(a) of the SC/<a href="/doc/53524/" id="a_4">ST Act</a>.

                      The petitioners shall surrender before the concerned jurisdictional Court

                      within a period of fifteen days from the date of the receipt of a copy of this

                      Order.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                               3.In view of the above, the Criminal Original Petition stands allowed.
