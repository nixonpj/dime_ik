<div class="doc_title">Oriental Insurance Co Ltd vs Vimlaben Harmanbhai Solanki &amp; 2 on 4 August, 2014</div><pre id="pre_1">         C/FA/2480/2014                                 ORDER




         IN THE HIGH COURT OF GUJARAT AT AHMEDABAD

                      FIRST APPEAL NO. 2480 of 2014

================================================================
             ORIENTAL INSURANCE CO LTD....Appellant(s)
                            Versus
         VIMLABEN HARMANBHAI SOLANKI &amp; 2....Defendant(s)
================================================================
Appearance:
MR R G DWIVEDI, ADVOCATE for the Appellant(s) No. 1
MR A A ZABUAWALA, ADVOCATE for the Defendant(s) No. 3
================================================================

        CORAM: HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE RAVI R.TRIPATHI

                             Date : 04/08/2014


                              ORAL ORDER</pre>


<p id="p_1">     Present First Appeal is filed by the appellant - Oriental
Insurance Co. Ltd. being aggrieved by the judgment and
award dated 30/09/2013 passed by the Motor Accident Claims
Tribunal (Auxi.), Kheda at Nadiad in MACP No.29 of 2009.
</p>

<p id="p_2">     The First Appeal No.2472 of 2014 arising out of MACP
No.31 of 2009 is admitted and both the claim petitions i.e.
MACP No.31 of 2009 and MACP No.29 of 2009 are arising out
of one single accident.
</p>

<p id="p_3">     Learned advocate for the appellant - Insurance Company
submitted that contention of the Insurance Company is that as
the driver of the vehicle was not having valid license, the
Tribunal ought not to have held the Insurance Company liable
to pay compensation.           Learned advocate further invited


<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                 Page 1 of 2</span>
          C/FA/2480/2014                            ORDER



attention of the Court to the statement of the driver himself
i.e Maheshbai Harmanbhai Solanki dated 23/10/2008 before
the police wherein he himself has stated that he was not
having valid license. The matter requires consideration.
</p>

<p id="p_4">      ADMIT.
</p>

<p id="p_5">      To be heard with First Appeal No.2472 of 2014.
</p>



<p id="p_6">                                         (RAVI R.TRIPATHI, J.)
ila
