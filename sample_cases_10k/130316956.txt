<div class="doc_title">Md. Irphan vs The State Of Bihar on 20 May, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                      IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                              CRIMINAL MISCELLANEOUS No.33083 of 2019
                  Arising Out of PS. Case No.-151 Year-2018 Thana- GAMAHARIYA District- Madhepura
                 ======================================================
                 Md. Irphan S/o Late Majnum @ Majloom Mian, R/o village- Chandanpatti,
                 P.S.- Gamhariya, District- Madhepura

                                                                                ... ... Petitioner/s
                                                      Versus
                 The State Of Bihar

                                                        ... ... Opposite Party/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :     Mr.Ranjay Kumar Singh
                 For the Opposite Party/s :     Mr.Mohammed Arif
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE PRABHAT KUMAR JHA
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   20-05-2019                  Heard both sides.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                The petitioner apprehends his arrest in Gamhariya P.S.

                   Case No.151 of 2018 registered under Sections 307, 436 and

                   other minor sections<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1"> of the Indian Penal Code</a>.

</p><p id="p_3">                                The informant named 12 persons including the

                   petitioner Md. Irphan and alleged that Md. Kadir @ Kadil

                   assaulted his son with farsa on his head and others assaulted his

                   son and wife with stick and set blaze his residential house.

</p><p id="p_4">                                Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that there is

                   a counter version being Gahmariya P.S. Case No.152 of 2018.

                   The petitioner is, of course, named in the F.I.R. but no specific

                   allegation is made against the petitioner. Similarly situated co-

                   accused Md. Habib and Mustuph have been granted anticipatory

                   bail by a coordinate bench of this court by order dated
            Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.33083 of 2019(2) dt.20-05-2019
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                       2/2</span>




                     13.03.2019 passed in Cr.Misc.No.15482 of 2019.

</p><p id="p_5">                                  There appears from perusal of F.I.R. itself that, of

                     course, the informant named 12 persons and the name of the

                     petitioner figured at serial no.6 but no specific allegation is

                     made against the petitioner. Similarly situated two other accused

                     persons, namely, Md. Habib and Mustuph have already been

                     granted anticipatory bail.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                                  Having considered the facts aforesaid, the above

                     named petitioner, in the event of his arrest or surrender before

                     the learned court below within a period of four weeks from the

                     date of receipt/production of a copy of this order, is directed to

                     be enlarged on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Ten

                     thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the

                     satisfaction of learned A.C.J.M. IV, Madhepura in connection

                     with     Gamhariya P.S. Case No.151 of 2018, subject to the

                     conditions as laid down under <a href="/doc/1144664/" id="a_2">Section 438</a> (2)<a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_3"> of the Code</a> of

                     Criminal Procedure.
</p>
<p id="p_7">

</p><p id="p_8">                                             (Prabhat Kumar Jha, J)
Harish/-
</p>
