<p id="p_1">      Appellants who are treated as party-in-person as

per   order   dated   27.03.2017     are   absent.   No

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                           :4:</span>


the present Regular Second Appeals, Regular Appeals

are of the year 2002 in terms of the Regular Appeals

and Original Suits are of the year 1996 in terms of the

Original Suit.   It appears that the appellants are not

interested in prosecuting the cases. Hence, appeals are

dismissed for non-prosecution.
