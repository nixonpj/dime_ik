<div class="doc_title">Mahesh Rajwar vs The State Of Jharkhand on 4 April, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1">         IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                         A.B.A.No. 4798 of 2016
         Mahesh Rajwar                          ..... Petitioner
                             Versus
         The State of Jharkhand &amp; Anr.          .... Opposite party
                         ---------

         CORAM:      HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANANT BIJAY SINGH
                         ---------
         For the Petitioner               : Mr. Binod Kumar, Adv.
         For the Opposite party           : APP

                           ---------
05/Dated: 04.04.2017

                    It appears that under order dated 20.3.2017,
       counsel for the petitioner was directed to bring on record
       the enquiry report conducted by the S.B.I.
                    The said enquiry report has been filed by way of
       supplementary affidavit on behalf of the petitioner, which
       reveals the finding of the Enquiry Officer that the complicity
       of the petitioner for appropriation of funds by unauthorized
       debit and credit of various deceased/inoperative accounts of
       different customers on different occasions a total sum of Rs.
       5.03 lacs.
                    As per the first supplementary affidavit dated
       18.3.2017</pre><p id="p_1"> filed on behalf of the petitioner, which reveals
       that the Branch Manager of the SBI, attached/seized the
       amounts of the petitioner Rs.5,30,115 in the account of the
       S.B.I. Marho Branch, as mentioned in para-08.
</p><p id="p_2">                    Learned counsel for the petitioner is directed to
       supply the phone number of the petitioner to the learned
       APP latest by 12.04.2017 and two additional copy of the
       supplementary affidavit, thereafter learned APP supply the
       same to the concerned I.O.
</p><p id="p_3">                    The concerned I.O. is directed will give 72 hours
       notice to the petitioner fixing date, time and place and
       record the statement of the petitioner as to whether the
       Branch Manager, SBI seized the amount of the petitioner of
       Rs.5,30,114, kept in different accounts and also make
 necessary enquiry as to whether             the petitioner for
appropriation of funds by unauthorized debit and credit of
various     deceased/inoperative    accounts     of   different
customers on different occasions to the tune of Rs. 5.03 lacs
or not and submit up-to-date case diary along with the
report.
</p><p id="p_4">            List this case on 28.06.2017.
</p><p id="p_5">            Till then, interim relief granted earlier shall
continue.
</p><p id="p_6">            Let a copy of this order be sent to the court
below and handed over to the learned APP for needful.
</p>

<p id="p_7">                                     (Anant Bijay Singh, J.)
 Fahim/
 </p>
