<p id="p_1">             The petitioner prays for grant of anticipatory bail in FIR

No.69 dated 22.04.2017, registered under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_1">Sections 420</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_2">120-B</a> IPC

at Police Station Doraha, District Ludhiana.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             The operative part of the order dated 07.08.2018, vide

which interim anticipatory bail has been granted to the petitioner, is

reproduced as under:-
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                   "....Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that
             as per allegations in the FIR, complainant Pawan Kumar
             has alleged that he has entered into an agreement with
             Ringing Bells Pvt. Ltd., a proprietorship firm. It is further
             submitted that the petitioner was Manager of the firm and
             co-accused Sumeer Bajaj has issued a cheque, which was
             dishonoured on presentation in the bank. Counsel for the
             petitioner further submits that in fact, the petitioner was
             never entrusted with any amount by the complainant nor
             has misappropriated any amount and as per own case of
