<p id="p_1">                            This writ petition has been filed by the petitioner directing the

                      respondents 1 to 3 to take appropriate action against the 4th and 5th

                      respondents based on the petitioner's complaint dated 13.02.2019 and

                      16.02.2019.
</p>



<p id="p_2">                            2.It is seen that the petitioner's son and other persons are accused in

                      Crime No.41 of 2019, for the offences under <a href="/doc/1258372/" id="a_2">Sections 147</a>, <a href="/doc/1198799/" id="a_3">294(b)</a>, <a href="/doc/886598/" id="a_4">365</a>, <a href="/doc/1011035/" id="a_5">323</a>,

                      <a href="/doc/724142/" id="a_6">324</a>, <a href="/doc/180217/" id="a_7">506(ii)</a> and <a href="/doc/455468/" id="a_8">307</a> of IPC, pending on the file of the 5th respondent. The

                      petitioner already approached the State Human Rights Commission by lodging a

                      complaint and it is pending.     Therefore, the prayer sought for in this writ

                      petition cannot be considered by this Court.
</p>



<p id="p_3">http://www.judis.nic.in
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                                             3</span>



</p><p id="p_4">                            3.However, the State Human Rights Commission is directed to enquire

                      the complaint lodged by the petitioner dated 08.02.2019, as expeditiously as

                      possible.
</p>



<p id="p_5">                            4.In view of the above, this writ petition is disposed of. No costs.
</p>
