<p id="p_1">1.            By this judgment I will dispose of a suit for recovery for the

sum of Rs. 15,246/­ filed by the plaintiff company which is stated to be

registered   under   <a href="/doc/1353758/" id="a_1">Companies   Act</a>,   1956,   against   the   defendant   for   the

outstanding bills which were not paid by the defendant pursuant to land

line   telephone   connection   No.   25992185     which   was   provided   to   the

defendant vide OB No. 001409 dated 04.02.1997.  Thus, an amount of Rs.

15,246/­ is due payable by defendant which he has not paid despite service

despite service of legal demand notice dated 21.05.2005.
</p><p id="p_2"> 2.             Defendant   was   duly   served   by   ordinary   process   and   was

proceeded exparte vide order dated 05.12.2006  as he was not present on

the said date.
</p>
<p id="p_3">3.     Thereafter,   the   plaintiff   company   has   examined   two   witnesses.

PW­1 is Mrs. Narender Kaur, Accounts Officer, Rajouri Garden and PW­2

is Sh. S.K. Arora, Senior Accounts Officer.  
</p>
<p id="p_4">4.     I have heard counsel for plaintiff and perused the record.  PW­1 has

reiterated   contents   of   the   plaint   in   her   examination   in   chief.   She   has

proved on record the documents Ex. PW1/1 and PW1/6 and authority to

file the present suit has been proved vide Ex. PW2/1 by PW­2.  Assertions

made by both the witnesses PW­1 and PW­2 have gone unchallenged and

have   also   been   corroborated   by   various   documents   Ex.   PW1/1   to   Ex.

PW1/6.  Accordingly, the suit of the plaintiff is decreed for the sum of Rs.

15,246/­ with interest @ 12% per annum pendente­lite and future from the

date of filing of the suit till realisation alongwith costs.   Decree sheet be

prepared accordingly and file be consigned to Record Room.

Announced in the open court on 31.08.2007.
</p>
<p id="p_5">

</p><p id="p_6">                                                                 (Sanjeev Aggarwal)
                                                                 Civil Judge : Delhi.</p><pre id="pre_2">
