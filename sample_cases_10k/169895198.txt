<p id="p_1">(By Sri.Sudarshan M., Advocate for R2;
 V/o dated: 12.12.2018 Notice to R1 is D/W)

      This Miscellaneous First Appeal is filed under

Section 173 (1) of <a href="/doc/785258/" id="a_1">MV Act</a>, praying to call for records

and   to   modify   the   judgment          and   award    dated

05.12.2017 passed in MVC No.1263/2015 on the file of

the Court of the IV Additional District Judge and

Member     Motor    Accident       Claims    Tribunal     No.XIII

Vijayapura at Vijayapura and allow this appeal to grant

the compensation amount by Rs.10,80,000/- only as

claimed by the appellant before this Court and etc.,


      This appeal coming on for Conciliation before Lok

Adalat, this day, the following order is passed:
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                  3</span>

</p><p id="p_2">               CONCILIATION ORDER

      The learned counsel for the appellant and the
learned counsel for respondent - Insurance Company
are present.
</p>
<p id="p_3">      2.      After due deliberations, the parties have

arrived at a settlement. The appellant - claimant has

agreed to receive and the respondent - Insurance

Company has agreed to pay global compensation of

Rs.75,000/-      (Rupees    Seventy-Five-Thousand    Only)

inclusive of interest in addition to what has been

awarded by the Tribunal, towards full and final

settlement of the claim. A joint memo is filed on behalf

of the parties to this effect.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">      3.      The respondent - Insurance Company has

agreed to deposit the said amount before the Tribunal,

within six weeks from the date of preparation of the

award, failing which, the said amount shall carry

interest at 9% p.a. from the date of default, till the date

of deposit.
</p><span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                             4</span><p id="p_6">

</p><p id="p_7">      4.   The entire enhanced amount is ordered to be

released in favour of the claimant on deposit.


</p><p id="p_8">      5.   Accordingly,   Miscellaneous    First   Appeal

stands disposed off in terms of the Joint Memo. The

award of the Tribunal shall stand modified. Draw award

accordingly.
</p>
<p id="p_9">
