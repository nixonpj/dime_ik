<p id="p_1"> IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN 
AT JAIPUR BENCH, JAIPUR

S.B. CRIMINAL MISC. BAIL APPLICATION No.9923/2015
Kallu @ Kallan Vs. State of Rajasthan

Date of Order                   :                   1.10.2015

HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PRASHANT KUMAR AGARWAL

Shri  S.M. Khan,  for the  petitioner.
</p><p id="p_2">Shri  Prakash Thakuriya, Public Prosecutor.
</p><p id="p_3">Shri Madhav Mitra, for complainant. 
</p>
<p id="p_4">	Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p id="p_5">	On consideration of submissions made on behalf of the respective parties and the material made available on record as well as the evidence collected during investigation, which has been placed on record by way of copy of the charge-sheet and also  the case diary of the case which was produced before me which includes  such evidence also which has not been produced along with the charge-sheet  but without expressing any final opinion on the merit and de-merit of the case, I am inclined to grant benefit of bail to the accused-petitioner.
	Consequently, the bail application filed under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. is allowed.
	It is ordered that the accused-petitioner Kallu @ Kallan S/o Shri Bashir in F.I.R. No.119/2015, registered at Police Station Manak Chowk, Jaipur shall be released on bail; provided he furnishes a personal bond of Rs.50,000/- and two surety bonds of Rs.25,000/- each to the satisfaction of the learned trial court with the stipulation to appear before that Court on all dates of hearing and as and when called upon to do so.	
</p>
<p id="p_6">			                    (PRASHANT KUMAR AGARWAL),J.
</p><p id="p_7">Chauhan/-34
		All corrections made in the judgment/
		order have been incorporated in the 
		judgment/order being emailed.
</p>
