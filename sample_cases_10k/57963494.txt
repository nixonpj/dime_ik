<div class="doc_title">Anil Yadav And Others vs State Of Haryana And Another on 28 April, 2009</div><pre id="pre_1">Cr.Misc. NO. 32681 M of 2008                      1



IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB &amp; HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH

                                  Cr.Misc. NO. 32681 M of 2008

                                   DATE OF DECISION 28.4.2009

Anil Yadav and others

                                                  ......PETITIONER
                          VERSUS

State of Haryana and another
                                                  ......RESPONDENTS

PRESENT:        Mr. Akshay Bhan, Advocate
                Mr. Rattan Brar, Advocate.



M.M.S.BEDI,J.
</pre>


<p id="p_1">            The petitioners seek the quashing of       FIR No. 203 dated

1.8.2008 u/s 420/467/468/471/120-B,506 <a href="/doc/1569253/" id="a_1">IPC</a> registered at P.S. SGM

Nagar, District Faridabad on the basis of compromise. The said FIR was

registered at the instance of Rohtash, respondent No.2 alleging that the

petitioners had colluded with each other and manipulated and forged an

agreement of sale dated 27.12.2007 with an intention to grab the plot of the

complainant. The parties have now entered into a compromise vide

Annexure P-1 dated 1.12.2008. Rohtash is present in the court and has

submitted   that he does not want to pursue his remedy against the

petitioners as the matter has been compromised.
</p>
<p id="p_2">            After going through the facts and circumstances of this case, I

am of the considered opinion that no useful purpose will be served by

continuing with the criminal proceedings against the petitioners, especially

when the matter has been compromised.
</p>
<p id="p_3">            In view of the matter having been compromised, in the light of

the Full Bench judgment of this Court in <a href="/doc/811682/" id="a_2">Kulwinder Singh and Ors vs
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> Cr.Misc. NO. 32681 M of 2008                      2</span>



State of Punjab and another</a> 2007(3) RCR (Cr.) 1052, the FIR can be

permitted to be quashed.
</p>
<p id="p_4">            In view of the above, the petition is allowed, FIR and the all the

criminal proceedings emanating therefrom are hereby quashed.


</p><pre id="pre_2">April 28   ,2009                               ( M.M.S.BEDI )
TSM                                                 JUDGE
