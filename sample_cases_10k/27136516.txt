<div class="doc_title">Lalan Jha vs The State Of Bihar &amp; Ors on 11 August, 2015</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.1920 of 2014
                 ======================================================
                 Lalan Jha
                                                                       .... .... Petitioner/s
                                                   Versus
                 The State of Bihar &amp; Ors
                                                                      .... .... Respondent/s
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s    :    Mr. Girish Chandra Jha, Adv.
                 For the Respondent/s      : Mr. Ashutosh Singh, A.C. to S.C.1
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE JYOTI SARAN
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">2   11-08-2015          Mr. Girish Chandra Jha, learned counsel for the petitioner

                 and Mr. Ashutosh Singh, A.C. to S.C.1 for the State are present.

</p><p id="p_2">                        The petitioner has questioned the notice dated 13.12.2013 of

                 the Circle Officer, Biraul in the district of Darhanga issued under

                 Section 6(2) of the Bihar Public Land Encroachment Act

                 (hereinafter referred to as 'the Act') in Encroachment Case No. 1

                 of 2013-14 whereby the petitioner has been directed to remove

                 encroachment from Plot No 2117 in village Ram Nagar, P.S.

                 Biraul, Thana No. 317 in the district of Darbhanga which

                 according to the respondents is in a nature of public land and has

                 been encroached by the petitioner.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                        It is submitted that earlier mutation was done in the name of

                 the grand father and after his death the name of the father of the

                 petitioner Durga Nand Jha was mutated vide order passed on

                 6.9.1994, Jamabandi was created and the rent of the land was
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">             Patna High Court CWJC No.1920 of 2014 (2) dt.11-08-2015</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      2/2</span>




                         fixed in favour of the father of the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_4">                                 It is the case of the petitioner that a suit under Section 106

                         of the Bihar Tenancy Act was filed for correction of the survey

                         entry and the foundational facts of the case of the petitioner can be

                         found in the order passed by the prescribed authority in Title Suit

                         No. 5215 of 1997.
</p>
<p id="p_5">                                 It is argued by Mr. Jha that the orders have been passed in

                         the encroachment case without any notice or opportunity of

                         hearing to the petitioner and a specific statement to that effect has

                         been made in paragraphs 21 and 23 of the writ petition.

</p><p id="p_6">                                 Mr. Singh learned counsel for the State has submitted that

                         although a statutory appeal is available to the petitioner against the

                         order passed in the encroachment proceedings and more

                         particularly so where though the land settled with the grand father

                         of the petitioner only admeasures 3 kathas but the order passed in

                         the suit shows that he is in occupation of more than 22 kathas of

                         land.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                 Let a counter affidavit be filed on the issues so raised and

                         enabling Mr. Singh to seek instructions and file counter affidavit

                         put up this case on 1.9.2015 in the same list.

</p><p id="p_8">                                                                            (Jyoti Saran, J)
Bibhash/-
</p><p id="p_9">  U
