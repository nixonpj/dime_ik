<div class="doc_title">Dev Chandra Jha vs The State Of Bihar on 20 December, 2016</div><pre id="pre_1">                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                 Criminal Miscellaneous No.46368 of 2016
                     Arising Out of PS.Case No. -277 Year- 2016 Thana -BAHERA District- DARBHANGA
                 ======================================================
                 Dev Chandra Jha, son of Late Ganesh Jha, resident of Village: Mahinam,
                 P.S. Bahera, District: Darbhanga.

                                                                                .... ....   Petitioner
                                                      Versus
                 The State of Bihar
                                                                  .... .... Opposite Party
                 ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Petitioner/s     :  Mr. Sameer Ranjan, Adv,
                 For the Opposite Party/s   : Mr. Parmeshwar Mehta, A.P.P.
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAKESH KUMAR
                 ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">3   20-12-2016                     Heard Sri Sameer Ranjan, learned counsel for the

                 petitioner and Sri Parmeshwar Mehta, learned Addl. Public

                 Prosecutor.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                                   The sole petitioner, who is in custody in G.O. Case

                 no.19/2016 arising out of Bahera P.S. Case No.277 of 2016

                 registered    for     the    offence      under      <a href="/doc/704277/" id="a_1">Sections       8</a>,<a href="/doc/150605429/" id="a_2">20(b)(ii)(A)</a>,

                 <a href="/doc/170878130/" id="a_3">20(b)(ii)(B)</a>, <a href="/doc/1089586/" id="a_4">25</a> of N.D.P.S. Act, has prayed for grant of bail.

</p><p id="p_3">                                   It was submitted by learned counsel for the

                 petitioner that the petitioner has been taken in custody on an

                 allegation of recovery of 300 gms. of Ganja from a Gumti. He

                 submits that recovered Ganja is lesser than the smaller quantity

                 and the petitioner is having clean antecedent, which has been

                 stated in paragraph-3 of the petition.
</p>
<p id="p_4">                                   Learned Addl. Public Prosecutor has opposed the
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">         Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.46368 of 2016 (3) dt.20-12-2016</span>

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                   2/2</span>




                     prayer for grant of bail to the petitioner.

</p><p id="p_5">                                        However, considering the clean antecedent of the

                     petitioner as well as recovered little quantity of Ganja, let the

                     petitioner, namely, Dev Chandra Jha be released on bail on

                     furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/-( ten thousand) with two

                     sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned

                     Sessions Judge/Special Judge, Darbhanga in connection with G.O.

                     Case no.19/2016 arising out of Bahera P.S. Case no.277 of 2016.



</p><p id="p_6">                                                    (Rakesh Kumar, J)
NKS/-
</p><pre id="pre_2"> U         T
