<div class="doc_title">Writ Petition (Md) No.3078 Of 2018 vs The Tahsildar on 15 February, 2018</div><pre id="pre_1">        

 

BEFORE THE MADURAI BENCH OF MADRAS HIGH COURT               
DATED: 15.02.2018  
CORAM   
THE HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE V.BHARATHIDASAN             

Writ Petition (MD) No.3078 of 2018
Savithri                                                        ...     Petitioner
Vs.
The Tahsildar,
Thirumayam Taluk, 
Pudukottai District.                                    ...   Respondent
         Petition filed under <a href="/doc/1712542/" id="a_1">Article 226</a> of the Constitution of India praying
for issuance of a Writ of Mandamus, to direct the respondent to consider the
petitioner's representation dated 05.01.2018 to enter the petitioner's name
as pattadar effecting necessary charges in the revenue records in respect of
lands in Survey No.441/1 to an extent 0.24.0 ares  Survey No.441/6 and extent
of 0.49.0 ares and Survey No.441/7 and extent of 0.49.0 ares of K.Royavaram
Village,  Thirumayam Taluk,  Pudukkottai District.

!For Petitioner                 : Mr.N.Balakrishnan
For Respondent          :Mr.Aayiram K.Selvakumar,          
                                                Additional Government Pleader.

:ORDER  
</pre>
<p id="p_1">                This writ petition has been filed seeking direction to the
respondent to enter the petitioner's name as pattadar and effect necessary
changes in the revenue records in respect of the lands in Survey No.441/1 to
an extent 0.24.0 ares, Survey No.441/6, to an  extent of 0.49.0 ares and in
Survey No.441/7 to an extent of 0.49.0 ares in K.Royavaram Village,
Thirumayam Taluk,  Pudukkottai District by considering the representation of
the petitioner, dated 05.01.2018.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                2.According to the petitioner, earlier, the Assistant Settlement
Officer, Madurai has passed an order granting patta in favour of the
petitioner in SR.No.231/95, dated 12.02.1996. Since no one has filed appeal
against the same, the said order has become final. In the above
circumstances, the petitioner has submitted a representation dated 05.01.2018
before the respondent seeking mutation in the revenue records.  Since no
order has been passed, the present writ petition has been filed.
</p><p id="p_3">                3.Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, this Court
without going into the merits of the case, directs the respondent to consider
the representation of the petitioner dated 05.01.2018 and pass appropriate
orders, on merits and in accordance with law, within a period of six weeks
from the date of receipt of a copy of this order.
</p><p id="p_4">                4.With the above direction, the writ petition is disposed of. No
costs.
</p>
<p id="p_5">To
The Tahsildar,
Thirumayam Taluk, 
Pudukottai District.
</p>
