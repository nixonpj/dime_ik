<p id="p_1">7   04-11-2015               In view of the fact that the petitioner and O.P. No.2 are

                 willing to resolve the disputes through the process of mediation,

                 let the matter be placed before the Patna High Court Mediation

                 Centre, which will take appropriate steps for fixing a date in the

                 matter.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                             Learned counsel for the petitioner shall appear before

                 the Mediation Centre, with a copy of this order, on 10th November,

                 2015, on which date it is expected that both the petitioner and O.P.

                 No.2 shall have a date fixed for further proceedings before the

                 Mediation Centre.
</p>
<p id="p_3">                             In the meantime and during the pendency of the
