<div class="doc_title">Mohan Lal vs Hvpn And Another on 20 November, 2008</div><pre id="pre_1">             IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT
                          CHANDIGARH

                                       RSA No. 3745 of 2008 (O&amp;M)

                                       Date of Decision: 20.11. 2008


Mohan Lal                                           ...... Appellant


      Versus


HVPN and another                                    ...... Respondents


Coram:       Hon'ble Mr. Justice Ajay Tewari


1. Whether Reporters of local papers may be allowed to see the judgment?
2. To be referred to the Reporters or not?
3. Whether the judgment should be reported in the Digest?


Present:     Mr.Ashok Kaushik, Advocate
             for the appellant.

                   ****

Ajay Tewari, J.
</pre>
<p id="p_1">             This appeal has been filed against the concurrent judgments of

the Courts below dismissing the suit of the plaintiff for a declaration that the

demand of Rs. 1, 04, 279/-for Account No. 25SP-365 and Rs. 1, 48, 278/-

for account No. 25SP-477 are illegal, null and void and with a consequential

decree for permanent injunction restraining the respondents from

disconnecting the power connection of the plaintiff-appellant. Both the

Courts have concurrently found as a fact that the appellant has not been able

to establish that either he has paid the amounts in question or that the same

were not due from him.
</p>
<p id="p_2">             Learned counsel for the appellant has not been able to persuade

me that these findings of fact are either perverse or can be held to be not
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1"> RSA No. 3745 of 2008                  -2-</span>



arising from the material on the record.

</p><p id="p_3">            Consequently, I dismiss this appeal, however, with no order as

to costs.
</p>
<p id="p_4">

</p><p id="p_5">                                                (AJAY TEWARI)
                                                    JUDGE


November 20, 2008
sunita
