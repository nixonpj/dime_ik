<div class="doc_title">WP(C)/19503/2019 on 21 November, 2019</div><pre id="pre_1">                                  W.P.(C) No. 19503 of 2019




      02. 21.11.2019             Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.
                       2.        By way of this writ petition, the petitioner has prayed for
                       transfer of O.A. No. 3862 (C) of 2015 to this Court from the office of
                       the Odisha Administrative Tribunal, Cuttack Bench, Cuttack.
                       3.        Considering the submission of learned counsel for the
                       petitioner and in view of the averments made in the writ petition, the
                       prayer is allowed.
                       4.        The petitioner is permitted to move an application along
                       with a certified copy of this order before the Registry of the Tribunal
                       for transfer of the case records in Original Application to this Court.
                       In the event, an application is moved by the petitioner for transfer of
                       the aforesaid Original Application, the Registry of the Tribunal will
                       transfer the same expeditiously.
                       5.        The writ petition stands disposed of with the aforesaid
                       observation.
                                 Certified copy of this order be granted on proper
                       application.
