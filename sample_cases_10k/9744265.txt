<p id="p_1">1.            Wife of the convict has filed present application praying 
temporary bail of the convict on the ground of serious illness of 
applicant.
</p>

<p id="p_2">2.            Rule.  Learned APP Mr.K.P.Raval waives service of Rule 
on behalf of respondent - State.
</p>

<p id="p_3">3.            Considering   the   jail   remarks   and   application   of   the 
applicant and the medical certificate attached with the application, 
present   application   is   partly   allowed.     Convict   is   ordered   to   be 
released on temporary bail for a period of 21 days from the date of 
his actual release on his furnishing a self surety of Rs.10,000/­ to 
the satisfaction of jail authority and also on condition of observing 

<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                     Page 1 of 2</span>
         R/CR.MA/8027/2014                                         ORDER




other usual conditions.
</p>

<p id="p_4">4.           The   convict   shall   surrender   with   the   jail   authority   in 
time on completion of period of temporary bail.
</p>

<p id="p_5">5.           Rule   is   made   absolute   accordingly.   Direct   service   is 
permitted.
</p>
