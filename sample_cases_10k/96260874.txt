                                   Letters Patent Appeal No.215 of 2018
                                                     In
                              Civil Writ Jurisdiction Case No.3789 of 2017
               ======================================================
               Ramdhani Singh S/o Late Ghura Singh Resident of Village-Bhanpur, P.S.-
               Karghar, District-Rohtas                              ... Appellant
                                                   Versus
            1. The State of Bihar, through the Principal Secretary, Department, of Forest and
               Environment, Bihar, Patna.
            2. The Divisional Forest Officer-cum-Authorized Officer, Rohtas Forest
               Division, Rohtas.at Sasaram.
            3. The Forester Darigaon, Rohtas                         ... Respondents
               ======================================================
                 Appearance :
                 For the Appellant       :     Mr. Ranjeet Choubey, Adv.
                 For the Respondents     :     Mr. Servesh Kr.Singh, AAG XIII
                 ======================================================
                 CORAM: HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE
                         and
                         HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE RAJEEV RANJAN PRASAD
                                       ORAL ORDER</pre>

<p id="p_1">                 (Per: HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE)

3   10-04-2018                 I.A. No. 1169 of 2018

                              Delay of 24 days in filing the memo of appeal is

                  condoned.
</p>
<p id="p_2">                              Identical letters patent appeal in the matter of release

                  of the vehicle has been disposed off on 21.02.2017 by a

                  coordinate Bench of this Court in L.P.A. No. 306 of 2017

                  wherein in paragraph no. 5, following conditions have been

                  issued.
</p>
<blockquote id="blockquote_1">                                       "In the meanwhile, we order that the vehicle in
                               question (tractor) be released provisionally to the
                               satisfaction     of   the    Authorized          Officer-cum-
                               Divisional Forest Officer, Rohtas at Sasaram, on
                               fulfillment of the following conditions :-
</blockquote><p id="p_3"> Patna High Court LPA No.215 of 2018(3) dt.10-04-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_1">                                             2/3</span>




</p><p id="p_4">                                          (i) The appellant shall furnish all the
                                 necessary papers/documents of ownership
                                 and security bonds (which will not include
                                 Bank Guarantee) as may be deemed fit and
                                 proper by the Divisional Forest Officer-cum-
                                 Authorized Officer, Rohtas at Sasaram.
</p><p id="p_5">                                          (ii) The appellant shall undertake, in
                                 writing, that the vehicle, in question, shall
                                 neither be alienated nor be transferred in
                                 favour of any third party during the pendency
                                 of the confiscation proceeding and that the
                                 vehicle, in question, shall be produced as and
                                 when called upon or required in the
                                 confiscation proceeding or otherwise."
                      Even though learned counsel appearing for the

         respondents indicates that in view of the certain order of the

         Supreme Court, a Bank Guarantee should be furnished for

         release of the vehicle.
</p>
<p id="p_6">                      We are of the considered view that in identical matter,

         this Court has already passed order and, therefore, parity should

         be maintained in the present appeal also.

</p><p id="p_7">                      We, therefore, dispose off the matter in terms of the

         order, dated 21.02.2017, passed in L.P.A. No. 306 of 2017. The

         vehicle (tractor) of the appellant bearing registration no. BR-

         24C-1361 shall also be released in connection with Forest Case

         No. 22 of 2017 to the satisfaction of the Divisional Forest
          Patna High Court LPA No.215 of 2018(3) dt.10-04-2018
<span class="hidden_text" id="span_2">                                                      3/3</span>




                  Officer-cum-Authorized Officer, Rohtas at Sasaram, on the

                  conditions referred to hereinabove in paragraph 5 of the order,

                  dated 21.02.2017, passed in L.P.A. No. 306 of 2017.

</p><p id="p_8">                               List after the decision of L.P.A. No. 1647 of 2015.



</p><p id="p_9">                                            (Rajendra Menon, CJ)


                                          ( Rajeev Ranjan Prasad, J)

Shamshad/-
</p>
