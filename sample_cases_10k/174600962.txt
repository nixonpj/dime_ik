<div class="doc_title">Gopal Lal vs State on 1 June, 2017</div><p id="p_1">      HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
                       JODHPUR
       S.B. Suspension Of Sentence(Appeal) No. 518 / 2017
                                   In
                   S.B. Criminal Appeal No.713/2017
Gopal Lal S/o Ghisa Lal Kumhar, R/o Kharol Basti, Kanechhan
Kalan, P.S.-Fuliya, District-Bhilwara. (At Present Appellant is
Lodged At District Jail Chittorgarh)
</p><p id="p_2">                                                        ----Appellant
                                 Versus
State of Rajasthan
</p><p id="p_3">                                                      ----Respondent
_____________________________________________________
For Appellant(s)     : Mr. Manish Pitaliya
For Respondent(s) : Mr. O.P. Rathi, PP
_____________________________________________________
             HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE VIJAY BISHNOI
                         Judgment / Order
01/06/2017

      Heard learned counsel for the parties on the application for

suspension of sentence.
</p>
<p id="p_4">      Learned counsel for the appellant has submitted that as per

the prosecution story, the poppy husk recovered from the

possession of the appellant was 18 kg, which is below commercial

quantity. It is also submitted that the appellant was on bail during

the trial.
</p>
<p id="p_5">      Having considered the totality of facts and circumstances of

the case, I consider it just and proper to suspend the substantive

sentence awarded to the accused applicant.

</p><p id="p_6">      Accordingly, the bail application filed under Sec.389 <a href="/doc/445276/" id="a_1">Cr.P.C</a>. is

allowed and it is ordered that the substantive sentence passed by
                                   (2 of 2)
                                                              [SOSA-518/2017]

the   trial   court   vide   judgment        dated   15.05.2017   in   Case

No.10/2014 (58/2008) against applicant Gopal Lal S/o Ghisa Lal

Kumhar shall remain suspended till final disposal of the aforesaid

appeal, provided he executes a personal bond in the sum of Rs.

20,000/- with two sureties of Rs.10,000/- each to the satisfaction

of the learned trial Judge for his appearance before this court on

03.07.2017 and whenever ordered to do so with the incorporation

in the bond that as and when he will shift his place of residence,

he will intimate to this Court and his lawyer about his new place of

residence.
</p>
<p id="p_7">                                                      (VIJAY BISHNOI)J.
</p>
<p id="p_8">Gaurav
