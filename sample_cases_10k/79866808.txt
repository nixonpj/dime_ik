<div class="doc_title">Sant Kumar vs State Of Rajasthan Through Pp on 15 February, 2017</div><pre id="pre_1"> HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN BENCH AT
                      JAIPUR
           S.B. Criminal Miscellaneous Bail Application No.
                          2299/ 2017
Sant Kumar Son of Shri Sheokaran, by Caste Meena, R/o
Kanwarawas, Police Station, Todaraisingh, District Tonk
(Raj.)
(At present confined in District Jail, Bundi Raj.)


                                         ----Petitioner/Accused
                             Versus
State of Rajasthan Through P.P.
                                                 ----Respondent</pre>
<p id="p_1">_____________________________________________________
For Petitioner(s)   : Mr. Govind Choudhary
Public Prosecutor   : Mr. Rajender Raghav
_____________________________________________________
        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE BANWARI LAL SHARMA
                              Order
                                                      15/02/2017

           The present Misc bail application under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_1">section 439</a>

Cr.P.C. is preferred by the Accused/ petitioner Sant Kumar in

the matter of FIR No. 141/2007 registered at Police Station

Hindoli, District Bundi for offences punishable under <a href="/doc/988620/" id="a_2">Sections

406</a>, <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_3">420</a>, <a href="/doc/105503/" id="a_4">417</a> and <a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_5">120-B</a> IPC.
</p>
<p id="p_2">              Learned    Counsel   for   petitioner   Mr.   Govind

Choudhary submits that petitioner was low paid employee in

the company, main accused is Vinod Kumar who has already

been released on bail, all cases against the present petitioner

were lodged within a period of one week. In FIR No.

207/2006 registered at Police Station Todarai Singh, District
                                 (2 of 3)
                                                         [CRLMB-2299/2017]

Tonk for offences under <a href="/doc/1436241/" id="a_6">Sections 420</a>, <a href="/doc/1985627/" id="a_7">467</a>, <a href="/doc/556166/" id="a_8">468</a>, <a href="/doc/1466184/" id="a_9">471</a> and

<a href="/doc/1897847/" id="a_10">120B</a> IPC, Coordinate Bench of this Court in S.B. Criminal

Miscellaneous Bail Application No. 2194/2007 decided on

23.04.2007 extended the benefit of Anticipatory Bail under

<a href="/doc/1783708/" id="a_11">Section 438</a> Cr.P.C. to present petitioner on same allegations,

the present Bail Application is under <a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_12">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C.,

petitioner is in custody since 05.01.2017, after investigation

charge sheet has already been filed, therefore petitioner may

be released on bail.
</p>
<p id="p_3">           Learned Public Prosecutor Mr. Rajender Raghav

opposed the bail application.
</p>
<p id="p_4">
</p><p id="p_5">           I have considered the submissions made at bar.


           On consideration of submissions made on behalf of

the respective parties and the material made available for my

perusal and without expressing any final opinion on the merit

and de-merit of the case and considering the overall facts and

circumstances of the case, I am inclined to grant benefit of

bail to the accused-petitioner Sant Kumar.


</p><p id="p_6">           Consequently,   the      bail   application   filed   under

<a href="/doc/1290514/" id="a_13">Section 439</a> Cr.P.C. of accused-petitioner Sant Kumar is

allowed.
</p>
<p id="p_7">
</p><p id="p_8">           Therefore, it is ordered that the accused-petitioner

Sant Kumar S/o Sheokaran in FIR No. 141/2007 registered at

Police Station Hindoli, District Bundi, shall be released on
                              (3 of 3)
                                                       [CRLMB-2299/2017]




bail;   provided   he    furnishes      a   personal      bond       of

Rs.1,00,000/- (Rs. One Lakh) and two surety bonds of

Rs.50,000/- (Rs. Fifty Thousand) each to the satisfaction

of the learned trial court with the stipulation to appear before

the Trial Court on all dates of hearing and as and when called

upon to do so.
</p>
<p id="p_9">                                        (BANWARI LAL SHARMA)J.
</p>
<p id="p_10">Charu 128
